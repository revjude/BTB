<span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">1.</span> <span class="asgreek">τέ</span> solitarium, <span class="asitalic">and</span>, denoting a closer affinity than <span class="asgreek">καί</span> between words and sentences which it connects (Bl., §77, 8): [[Matt.28.12|Mt 28:12]], [[John.4.42|Jo 4:42]], [[Acts.2.33|Ac 2:33]], [[Acts.2.37|37]], [[Acts.2.40|40]] [[Acts.10.22|10:22]] [[Acts.11.21|11:21]], al.</span>
