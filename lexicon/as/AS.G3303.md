<span class="asform"><span class="asgreek"><span class="asbold">μέν</span></span>,</span>  <span class="asgramGrp"><span class="aspos">conjunctive particle</span> (originally a form of <span class="asgreek">μήν</span>), usually related to a following <span class="asgreek">δέ</span> or other adversative conjunction, and distinguishing the word or clause with which it stands from that which follows.</span>
It is generally untranslatable and is not nearly so frequent in NT as in cl. Like <span class="asgreek">δέ</span>, it never stands first in a clause.
<span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">1.</span> Answered by <span class="asgreek">δέ</span> or some other particle: <span class="asgreek">μὲν . . . δέ</span>, <span class="asitalic">indeed . . . but</span>, [[Matt.3.11|Mt 3:11]], [[Luke.3.16|Lk 3:16]], al.; with pronouns, <span class="asgreek">ὃς μὲν . . . ὃς δέ</span>, <span class="asitalic">one . . . another</span>, [[Matt.21.35|Mt 21:35]], al.; pl., [[Phil.1.16|Phl 1:16]], [[Phil.1.17|17]]; <span class="asgreek">ὃ μὲν . . . ὃ δὲ . . . ὃ δέ</span>, <span class="asitalic">some . . . some . . . some</span>, [[Matt.13.8|Mt 13:8]]; <span class="asgreek">τοῦτο μὲν . . . τοῦτο δέ</span>, <span class="asitalic">partly . . . partly</span>, [[Heb.10.33|He 10:33]]; <span class="asgreek">μὲν . . . ἔπειτα</span>, [[John.11.6|Jo 11:6]]; <span class="asgreek">μὲν . . . καί</span>, [[Luke.8.5|Lk 8:5]].</span>
<span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">2.</span> <span class="asgreek">μέν</span> <span class="aslatin">solitarium</span>, answered by no other particle: <span class="asgreek">πρῶτον μέν</span> (Bl., § 78, 5), [[Rom.1.8|Ro 1:8]] [[Rom.3.2|3:2]], [[1Cor.11.18|I Co 11:18]]; <span class="asgreek">μὲν οὖν</span> in narrative, summing up what precedes or introducing something further (Bl., § 78, 5), <span class="asitalic">so then</span>, <span class="asitalic">rather</span>, <span class="asitalic">nay rather</span>: [[Luke.11.28|Lk 11:28]] (WH, <span class="asgreek">μενοῦν</span>), [[Acts.1.6|Ac 1:6]] [[Acts.9.31|9:31]], al.; <span class="asgreek">μὲν οὖν γε</span> ([[Phil.3.8|Phl 3:8]], WH): v.s. <span class="asgreek">μενοῦνγε</span>.</span>
