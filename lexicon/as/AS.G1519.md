<span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">I.</span> Of place.</span>
$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">1.</span> After verbs of motion;</span>
$\quad$$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">(a)</span> of entrance into: [[Matt.8.23|Mt 8:23]] [[Matt.9.7|9:7]], [[Mark.1.45|Mk 1:45]], [[Luke.2.15|Lk 2:15]] [[Luke.8.31|8:31]], al.;</span>
$\quad$$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">(b)</span> of approach, <span class="asitalic">to</span> or <span class="asitalic">towards</span>: [[Mark.11.1|Mk 11:1]], [[Luke.6.8|Lk 6:8]] [[Luke.19.28|19:28]], [[John.11.31|Jo 11:31]] [[John.21.6|21:6]], al.;</span>
$\quad$$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">(c)</span> before pl. and collective nouns, <span class="asitalic">among</span>: [[Mark.4.7|Mk 4:7]] [[Mark.8.19|8:19]], [[Mark.8.20|20]], [[Luke.11.49|Lk 11:49]], [[John.21.23|Jo 21:23]], al.;</span>

$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">2.</span> Of direction;</span>
$\quad$$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">(a)</span> after verbs of seeing: [[Matt.6.26|Mt 6:26]], [[Mark.6.41|Mk 6:41]], [[Luke.9.16|Lk 9:16]], [[Luke.9.62|62]], [[John.13.22|Jo 13:22]], al.; metaph., of the mind, [[Heb.11.26|He 11:26]] [[Heb.12.2|12:2]], al.;</span>

$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">3.</span> After verbs of rest;</span>
$\quad$$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">(a)</span> in "pregnant" construction, implying previous motion (cl.; v.
