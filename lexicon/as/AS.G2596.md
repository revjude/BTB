$\quad$$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">(b)</span> of swearing, <span class="asitalic">by</span>: <span class="asgreek">ὄμνυμι κ.</span> (Bl., § 34, 1), [[Heb.6.13|He 6:13]], [[Heb.6.16|16]], cf. [[Matt.26.63|Mt 26:63]].</span>
