<span class="asform"><span class="asgreek"><span class="asbold">σύν</span></span> (old Att. <span class="asgreek">ξύν</span>), </span>  <span class="asgramGrp"><span class="aspos">prep.</span> c. dat., </span>
expressing association, fellowship and inclusion. It gradually gave way to <span class="asgreek">μετά</span>, c. gen. (cf. LS, s.v.; Bl., § 41, 3), and is therefore comparatively infrequent in NT, being rare in Mt<span class="assubscript">4</span>, Mk<span class="assubscript">6</span>, Jo<span class="assubscript">3</span>, and elsewhere (exx. [[Jas.1.11|Ja 1:11]], [[2Pet.1.18|II Pe 1:18]]) only in Lk (Gosp. and Ac) and Paul. <span class="asitalic">With, together with</span>: of companionship and association, [[Luke.2.13|Lk 2:13]], [[John.21.3|Jo 21:3]], [[Acts.10.23|Ac 10:23]], al.; <span class="asgreek">εἶναι σύν τινι</span>, [[Luke.7.12|Lk 7:12]], [[Acts.4.13|Ac 4:13]], [[Phil.1.23|Phl 1:23]], al.; of partisanship, [[Acts.4.13|Ac 4:13]]; <span class="asgreek">οἱ σύν τινι (ὄντες)</span>, of attendants, companions or colleagues, [[Mark.2.26|Mk 2:26]], [[Luke.5.9|Lk 5:9]], [[Acts.5.17|Ac 5:17]], al.; of assistance, <span class="asgreek">ἡ χάρις τ. θεοῦ σὺν ἐμοί</span>, [[1Cor.15.10|I Co 15:10]]; of two or more things together, almost = <span class="asgreek">καί</span>, [[Luke.23.11|Lk 23:11]], [[Acts.3.4|Ac 3:4]]  [[Acts.10.2|10:2]] [[Acts.14.5|14:5]] [[Acts.23.15|23:15]], [[Eph.3.18|Eph 3:18]]; <span class="asgreek">σὺν Χριστῷ ζῆν</span>, [[2Cor.13.4|II Co 13:4]]; <span class="asitalic">besides</span> (FlJ, LXX), <span class="asgreek">σὺν πᾶσι τούτοις</span>, [[Luke.24.21|Lk 24:21]].
In composition: <span class="asitalic">with</span> (<span class="asgreek">συνχαίρω</span>), <span class="asitalic">together</span> (<span class="asgreek">συνωδίνω</span>), <span class="asitalic">altogether</span> (<span class="asgreek">συντελέω</span>).
