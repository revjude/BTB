of motion outwards, separation from (opp. to <span class="asgreek">εἰς</span>; = Lat. <span class="aslatin">e, ex</span>), <span class="asitalic">from out of, from among, from</span>.
<span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">I.</span> Of Place,</span>
$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">1.</span> <span class="asitalic">of motion, out of, forth from, off from</span>: [[John.6.31|Jo 6:31]], [[Acts.9.3|Ac 9:3]], [[Gal.1.8|Ga 1:8]], al.; esp. after verbs of motion, [[Matt.8.28|Mt 8:28]] [[Matt.17.9|17:9]], [[Mark.1.25|Mk 1:25]] [[Mark.7.28|7:28]], [[John.1.33|Jo 1:33]] [[John.20.1|20:1]], [[Acts.12.17|Ac 12:7]], [[Acts.12.17|17]] [[Acts.27.30|27:30]], al.; constr. praeg., <span class="asgreek">σώζειν (διας.) ἐκ</span>, [[Jude.1.5|Ju 5]], [[Acts.28.4|Ac 28:4]]. $\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">3.</span> Of separation or distinction from a number, before collective or pl. nouns: [[Matt.13.47|Mt 13:47]], [[Matt.13.49|49]], [[John.12.1|Jo 12:1]], [[Acts.3.15|Ac 3:15]], [[1Pet.1.3|I Pe 1:3]], al.; after <span class="asgreek">εἷς</span>, [[Matt.10.29|Mt 10:29]], [[Luke.17.15|Lk 17:15]], al.; <span class="asgreek">οὐδείς</span>, [[John.7.19|Jo 7:19]], al.; <span class="asgreek">πολλοί</span>, [[John.11.19|Jo 11:19]], al.; <span class="asgreek">τις</span>, [[Luke.11.15|Lk 11:15]], al.; <span class="asgreek">τίς</span>, [[Matt.6.27|Mt 6:27]], al.; in partitive phrase as subject of sentence, [[John.16.17|Jo 16:17]]; Hebraistically, <span class="asgreek">ἐκ μέσου</span> seq. gen., = <span class="asgreek">ἐκ</span> (Heb. <span class="ashebrew">מִתֹּוךְ</span> <small><sup>[[H8432]]</sup></small>), [[Matt.13.49|Mt 13:49]], al.</span>
$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">4.</span> Of position or direction (so in cl. = <span class="asgreek">ἔξω</span>): <span class="asgreek">ἐκ δεξιῶν</span> (v.s. <span class="asgreek">δεξιός</span>); <span class="asgreek">ἐξ ἐναντίας</span>, [[Mark.15.39|Mk 15:39]] (metaph., [[Titus.2.8|Tit 2:8]]); <span class="asgreek">ἐκ ῥιζῶν</span> (i.e., <span class="asitalic">utterly</span>), [[Matt.11.20|Mt 11:20]].</span>
<span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">II.</span> Of Time,</span>

$\quad$ <span class="asbullet"><span class="asbold">2.</span> Of succession in time: <span class="asgreek">ἐκ δευτέρου</span>, <span class="asitalic">a second time</span>, [[Mark.14.72|Mk 14:72]], al., cf.
