###### R-GSN
$\quad$ Function=Relative pronoun; Case=Genitive; Number=Singular; Gender=Neuter
$\quad$ Relative pronoun Genitive Singular Neuter 
$\quad$ a REFERENCE BACK to a neuter person or thing that something belongs to 
$\qquad$ _"you gave teachings belonginging to the nation \_which\_ is wise"_
