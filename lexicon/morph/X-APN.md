###### X-APN
$\quad$ Function=Indefinite pronoun; Case=Accusative; Number=Plural; Gender=Neuter
$\quad$ Indefinite pronoun Accusative Plural Neuter 
$\quad$ a generalising reference to neuter people or things that are having something done to them 
$\qquad$ _"they gave \_certain\_ nations to them"_
