###### K-GSN
$\quad$ Function=Correlative pronoun; Case=Genitive; Number=Singular; Gender=Neuter
$\quad$ Correlative pronoun Genitive Singular Neuter 
$\quad$ a reference back to a TYPE of a neuter person or thing that something belongs to 
$\qquad$ _"giving things belonging to \_whichever\_ one of those nations is wise"_
