###### K-ASN
$\quad$ Function=Correlative pronoun; Case=Accusative; Number=Singular; Gender=Neuter
$\quad$ Correlative pronoun Accusative Singular Neuter 
$\quad$ a reference back to a TYPE of a neuter person or thing that is having something done to them 
$\qquad$ _"giving him \_whichever\_ one of those nations is wise"_
