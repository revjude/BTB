###### K-APN
$\quad$ Function=Correlative pronoun; Case=Accusative; Number=Plural; Gender=Neuter
$\quad$ Correlative pronoun Accusative Plural Neuter 
$\quad$ a reference back to a TYPE of neuter people or things that are having something done to them 
$\qquad$ _"giving him \_whichever\_ ones of those nations are wise"_
