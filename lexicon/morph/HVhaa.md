###### HVhaa
$\quad$ Function=Verb ; Stem=Hiphil (hence Action=Causative/declarative; Voice=Active); Form=Infinitive ; State=Absolute
$\quad$ "Verb : Hiphil (Causative/declarative, Active) Infinitive Absolute"
$\quad$ causing or confirming an ACTION OR ACTIVITY that is to be done by someone or something 
$\qquad$ _Example: to exalt_
