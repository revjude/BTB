**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּדַד‎</span></big>** **gâdad** <small>*(gaw-dad')*</small> ==**to cut**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (compare **<big>[[H1464|‎גּוּד‎]]</big>**)</small>

$\quad$ assemble (selves by troops), gather (selves together, self in troops), cut selves.
- ***to crowd***
-  also ***to gash*** (as if by pressing into)

**1)** to penetrate, cut, attack, invade
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to penetrate, cut into
$\quad$ **1b)**(Hithpoel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cut oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to gather in troops or crowds
