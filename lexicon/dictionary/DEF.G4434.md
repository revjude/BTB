**<big><span class="asgreek">‎πτωχός‎</span></big>** **ptōchós** <small>*(pto-khos')*</small> ==**poor**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N|G:N]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <greek BETA="PTW/SSW" unicode="πτώσσω" translit="ptṓssō"/> (to crouch)</small>

$\quad$ beggar(-ly), poor.
- akin to **<big>[[G4422|‎πτοέω‎]]</big>** and the alternate of **<big>[[G4098|‎πίπτω‎]]</big>**)
-  a beggar (as cringing), i.e. pauper (strictly denoting absolute or public mendicancy, although also used in a qualified or relative sense
-  whereas **<big>[[G3993|‎πένης‎]]</big>** properly means only straitened circumstances in private), literally (often as noun) or figuratively (distressed)
