**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָוָה‎</span></big>** **ʻâvâh** <small>*(aw-vaw')*</small> ==**to twist**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ do amiss, bow down, make crooked, commit iniquity, pervert, (do) perverse(-ly), trouble, [idiom] turn, do wickedly, do wrong.
- ***to crook***, literally or figuratively

**1)** to bend, twist, distort
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to be bent, be bowed down, be twisted, be perverted
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to twist, distort
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to do perversely
**2)** to commit iniquity, do wrong, pervert
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to do wrong, commit iniquity
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hiphil) to commit iniquity
