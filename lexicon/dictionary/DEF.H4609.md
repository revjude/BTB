**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מַעֲלָה‎</span></big>** **maʻălâh** <small>*(mah-al-aw')*</small> ==**thought**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>feminine of **<big>[[H4608|‎מַעֲלֶה‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ things that come up, (high) degree, deal, go up, stair, step, story.
- ***elevation***, i.e. ***the act*** (literally, a journey to a higher place, figuratively, a thought arising), or (concretely) ***the condition*** (literally, a step or grademark, figuratively, a superiority of station)
-  specifically ***a climactic progression*** (in certain Psalms)

**1)** what comes up, thoughts
**2)** step, stair
$\quad$ **2a)** step, stair
$\quad$ **2b)** steps (of sundial)
$\quad$ **2c)** stories (of heaven)
$\quad$ **2d)** ascent
$\quad$ **2e)** song of ascent
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2e1)** to the three great pilgrim feasts (Psalm titles)
