**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עֲדַר‎</span></big>** **ʻădar** <small>*(aw-dar')*</small> ==**to help**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ dig, fail, keep (rank), lack.
- ***to arrange as a battle***, ***a vineyard*** (to hoe)
-  hence, ***to muster*** and so ***to miss*** (or find wanting)

**1)** to help
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to help
**2)** to hoe
$\quad$ **2a)** (Niphal) to be hoed
**3)** to be lacking, fail
$\quad$ **3a)**(Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **3b1)** to be lacking
$\quad$ $\quad$ **3b1)** to leave lacking
$\quad$ **3b)** (Piel) to leave lacking
