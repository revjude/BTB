**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָמַץ‎</span></big>** **ʼâmats** <small>*(aw-mats')*</small> ==**to strengthen**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ confirm, be courageous (of good courage, stedfastly minded, strong, stronger), establish, fortify, harden, increase, prevail, strengthen (self), make strong (obstinate, speed).
- ***to be alert***, physically (on foot) or mentally (in ***courage***)

**1)** to be strong, alert, courageous, brave, stout, bold, solid, hard
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be strong, brave, bold
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to strengthen, secure (for oneself), harden (heart), make firm, make obstinate, assure
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to be determined, to make oneself alert, strengthen oneself, confirm oneself, persist in, prove superior to
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to exhibit strength, be strong, feel strong
