**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חֵסֵד‎</span></big>** **chêçêd** <small>*(kheh'-sed)*</small> ==**kindness**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H2616|‎חָסַד‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ favour, good deed(-liness, -ness), kindly, (loving-) kindness, merciful (kindness), mercy, pity, reproach, wicked thing.
- ***kindness***
-  by implication (towards God) ***piety***
-  rarely (by opposition) ***reproof***, or (subject.) ***beauty***

**1)** goodness, kindness, faithfulness
**2)** a reproach, shame
