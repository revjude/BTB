**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תָּמִים‎</span></big>** **tâmîym** <small>*(taw-meem')*</small> ==**unblemished**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:A|H:A]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H8552|‎תָּמַם‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ without blemish, complete, full, perfect, sincerely (-ity), sound, without spot, undefiled, upright(-ly), whole.
- ***entire*** (literally, figuratively or morally)
-  also (as noun) ***integrity***, ***truth***

**1)** complete, whole, entire, sound
$\quad$ **1a)** complete, whole, entire
$\quad$ **1b)** whole, sound, healthful
$\quad$ **1c)** complete, entire (of time)
$\quad$ **1d)** sound, wholesome, unimpaired, innocent, having integrity
$\quad$ **1e)** what is complete or entirely in accord with truth and fact (neuter adj/subst)
