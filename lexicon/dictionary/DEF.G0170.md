**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἀκαιρέομαι‎</span></big>** **akairéomai** <small>*(ak-ahee-reh'-om-ahee)*</small> ==**to lack opportunity**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup>

$\quad$ lack opportunity.
- to be inopportune (for oneself), i.e. to fail of a proper occasion
