**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נוּף‎</span></big>** **nûwph** <small>*(noof)*</small> ==**to sprinkle**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ lift up, move, offer, perfume, send, shake, sift, strike, wave.
- ***to quiver*** (i.e. vibrate up and down, or rock to and fro)
-  used in a great variety of applications (including sprinkling, beckoning, rubbing, bastinadoing, sawing, waving, etc.)

**1)** to move to and fro, wave, besprinkle
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to besprinkle, sprinkle
$\quad$ **1b)** (Polel) to wave, brandish (in threat)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to swing, wield, wave
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to wield
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to wave or shake (the hand)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2a)** to wave (hand)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2b)** to shake or brandish against
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2c)** to swing to and fro
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to wave (an offering), offer
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to shed abroad
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to be waved
