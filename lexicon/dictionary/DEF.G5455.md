**<big><span class="asgreek">‎φωνέω‎</span></big>** **phōnéō** <small>*(fo-neh'-o)*</small> ==**to call**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup>

$\quad$ call (for), crow, cry.
- to emit a sound (animal, human or instrumental)
-  by implication, to address in words or by name, also in imitation
