**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎סוּג‎</span></big>** **çûwg** <small>*(soog)*</small> ==**to turn**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ backslider, drive, go back, turn (away, back).
- properly, ***to flinch***, i.e. (by implication) ***to go back***, literally (to retreat) or figuratively (to apostatize)

**1)** to move, go, turn back, move away, backslide
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to backslide, prove recreant to
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to turn oneself away, turn back
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be turned or driven back, be repulsed
