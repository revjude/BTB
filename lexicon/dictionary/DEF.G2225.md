**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ζωογονέω‎</span></big>** **zōogonéō** <small>*(dzo-og-on-eh'-o)*</small> ==**to give life**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from the same as **<big>[[G2226|‎ζῶον‎]]</big>** and a derivative of **<big>[[G1096|‎γίνομαι‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ live, preserve.
- to engender alive, i.e. (by analogy) to rescue (passively, be saved) from death
