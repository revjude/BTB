**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּבַר‎</span></big>** **gâbar** <small>*(gaw-bar')*</small> ==**to prevail**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ exceed, confirm, be great, be mighty, prevail, put to more (strength), strengthen, be stronger, be valiant.
- ***to be strong***
-  by implication, ***to prevail***, ***act insolently***

**1)** to prevail, have strength, be strong, be powerful, be mighty, be great
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be strong, mighty
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to prevail
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to make strong, strengthen
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to confirm, give strength
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to confirm (a covenant)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to show oneself mighty
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to act proudly (toward God)
