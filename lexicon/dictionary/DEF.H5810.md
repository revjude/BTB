**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָזַז‎</span></big>** **ʻâzaz** <small>*(aw-zaz')*</small> ==**be strong**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ harden, impudent, prevail, strengthen (self), be strong.
- ***to be stout*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to be strong
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be strong, prevail
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to make firm, strengthen
