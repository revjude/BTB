$\quad$ Immer.
- § Immer = *"he hath said"*
- ***Immer***, the name of five Israelites

**1)** a priest in David's time
**2)** a priest in Jeremiah's time
**3)** the father of Zadok  the priest
**4)** an ancestor of a priest
