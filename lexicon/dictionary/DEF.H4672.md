**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָצָא‎</span></big>** **mâtsâʼ** <small>*(maw-tsaw')*</small> ==**to find**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [phrase] be able, befall, being, catch, [idiom] certainly, (cause to) come (on, to, to hand), deliver, be enough (cause to) find(-ing, occasion, out), get (hold upon), [idiom] have (here), be here, hit, be left, light (up-) on, meet (with), [idiom] occasion serve, (be) present, ready, speed, suffice, take hold on.
- properly, ***to come forth to***, i.e. ***appear*** or ***exist***
-  transitively, ***to attain***, i.e. ***find*** or ***acquire***
-  figuratively, ***to occur***, ***meet*** or ***be present***
