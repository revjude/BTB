**<big><span class="asgreek">‎παραλέγομαι‎</span></big>** **paralégomai** <small>*(par-al-eg'-om-ahee)*</small> ==**to sail past**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G3844|‎παρά‎]]</big>** and the middle voice of **<big>[[G3004|‎λέγω‎]]</big>** (in its original sense)</small>

- (specially), to lay one's course near, i.e. sail past
