**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָעַף‎</span></big>** **yâʻaph** <small>*(yaw-af')*</small> ==**to faint**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ faint, cause to fly, (be) weary (self).
- ***to tire*** (as if from wearisome flight)

**1)** to be or grow weary, be fatigued, be faint
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be or grow weary, be fatigued, be faint
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hophal) wearied (participle)
