**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָכַח‎</span></big>** **yâkach** <small>*(yaw-kahh')*</small> ==**to rebuke**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ appoint, argue, chasten, convince, correct(-ion), daysman, dispute, judge, maintain, plead, reason (together), rebuke, reprove(-r), surely, in any wise.
- ***to be right*** (i.e. correct)
-  ***reciprocal***, ***to argue***
-  causatively, ***to decide***, ***justify*** or ***convict***

**1)** to prove, decide, judge, rebuke, reprove, correct, be right
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to decide, judge
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to adjudge, appoint
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to show to be right, prove
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to convince, convict
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to reprove, chide
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to correct, rebuke
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hophal) to be chastened
$\quad$ **1c)** (Niphal) to reason, reason together
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithp) to argue
