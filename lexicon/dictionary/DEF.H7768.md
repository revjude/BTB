**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁוַע‎</span></big>** **shâvaʻ** <small>*(shaw-vah')*</small> ==**to cry**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cry (aloud, out), shout.
- properly, ***to be free***
-  but used only causatively and reflexively, ***to halloo*** (for help, i.e. freedom from some trouble)

**1)** (Piel) to cry out (for help), shout
