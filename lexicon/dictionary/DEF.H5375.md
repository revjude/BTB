**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָשָׂא‎</span></big>** **nâsâʼ** <small>*(naw-saw')*</small> ==**to lift: raise**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">נָסָה</span></big>** **nâçâh** <small>*(nawsaw')*</small>; (Psalm 4:6 (**<big>[[H0007|‎אֲבַד‎]]</big>**)), a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ accept, advance, arise, (able to, (armor), suffer to) bear(-er, up), bring (forth), burn, carry (away), cast, contain, desire, ease, exact, exalt (self), extol, fetch, forgive, furnish, further, give, go on, help, high, hold up, honorable ([phrase] man), lade, lay, lift (self) up, lofty, marry, magnify, [idiom] needs, obtain, pardon, raise (up), receive, regard, respect, set (up), spare, stir up, [phrase] swear, take (away, up), [idiom] utterly, wear, yield.
- ***to lift***, in a great variety of applications, literal and figurative, absolute and relative

**1)** to lift, bear up, carry, take
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to lift, lift up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to bear, carry, support, sustain, endure
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to take, take away, carry off, forgive
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be lifted up, be exalted
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to lift oneself up, rise up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be borne, be carried
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to be taken away, be carried off, be swept away
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to lift up, exalt, support, aid, assist
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to desire, long (fig.)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to carry, bear continuously
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to take, take away
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael) to lift oneself up, exalt oneself
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** to cause one to bear (iniquity)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e2)** to cause to bring, have brought
