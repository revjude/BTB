**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἀπολύω‎</span></big>** **apolýō** <small>*(ap-ol-oo'-o)*</small> ==**to release: release**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G0575|‎ἀπό‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G3089|‎λύω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ (let) depart, dismiss, divorce, forgive, let go, loose, put (send) away, release, set at liberty.
- to free fully, i.e. (literally) relieve, release, dismiss (reflexively, depart), or (figuratively) let die, pardon or (specially) divorce
