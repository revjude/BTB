**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אַלּוּף‎</span></big>** **ʼallûwph** <small>*(al-loof')*</small> ==**tame**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:A-M|H:A-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>or (shortened) **<big><span class="ashebrew">אַלֻּף</span></big>** **ʼalluph** <small>*(al-loof')*</small>; from **<big>[[H0502|‎אָלַף‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ captain, duke, (chief) friend, governor, guide, ox.
- ***familiar***
-  ***a friend***, also ***gentle***
-  hence, ***a bullock*** (as being tame
-  applied, although masculine, to a cow)
-  and so, ***a chieftain*** (as notable, like neat cattle)

**1)** tame, docile
**2)** friend, intimate
**3)** chief
