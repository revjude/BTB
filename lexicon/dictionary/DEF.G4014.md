**<big><span class="asgreek">‎περιαιρέω‎</span></big>** **periairéō** <small>*(per-ee-ahee-reh'-o)*</small> ==**to take away**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4012|‎περί‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G0138|‎αἱρέομαι‎]]</big>** (including its alternate)</small>

- to remove all around, i.e. unveil, cast off (anchor)
-  figuratively, to expiate
