$\quad$ Shemiramoth.
- § Shemiramoth = *"name of heights"*
- ***Shemiramoth***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** a Merarite Levite in the second rank in the choir of David
**2)** a Levite in the time of king Jehoshaphat of Judah
