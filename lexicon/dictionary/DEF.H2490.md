**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָלַל‎</span></big>** **châlal** <small>*(khaw-lal')*</small> ==**to bore**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (compare **<big>[[H2470|‎חָלָה‎]]</big>**); also  denominative (from **<big>[[H2485|‎חָלִיל‎]]</big>**)</small>

$\quad$ begin ([idiom] men began), defile, [idiom] break, defile, [idiom] eat (as common things), [idiom] first, [idiom] gather the grape thereof, [idiom] take inheritance, pipe, player on instruments, pollute, (cast as) profane (self), prostitute, slay (slain), sorrow, stain, wound.
- properly, ***to bore***, i.e. (by implication) ***to wound***, ***to dissolve***
-  figuratively, ***to profane*** (a person, place or thing), ***to break*** (one's word), ***to begin*** (as if by an 'opening wedge')
-  ***to play*** (the flute)

**1)** to profane, defile, pollute, desecrate, begin
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to profane oneself, defile oneself, pollute oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** ritually
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1b)** sexually
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be polluted, be defiled
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to profane, make common, defile, pollute
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to violate the honour of, dishonour
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to violate (a covenant)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to treat as common
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to profane (name of God)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to let be profaned
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to begin
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hophal) to be begun
**2)** to wound (fatally), bore through, pierce, bore
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to pierce
$\quad$ **2b)** (Pual) to be slain
$\quad$ **2c)** (Poel) to wound, pierce
$\quad$ **2d)** (Poal) to be wounded
**3)** (Piel) to play the flute or pipe
