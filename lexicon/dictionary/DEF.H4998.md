**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָאָה‎</span></big>** **nâʼâh** <small>*(naw-aw')*</small> ==**be lovely**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be beautiful, become, be comely.
- properly, ***to be at home***, i.e. (by implication) ***to be pleasant*** (or suitable), i.e. ***beautiful***

**1)** to be comely, be beautiful, be befitting
$\quad$ **1a)** (Pilpel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be comely
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be befitting
