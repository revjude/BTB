**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֲמַצְיָה‎</span></big>** **ʼĂmatsyâh** <small>*(am-ats-yaw')*</small> ==**Amaziah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">אֲמַצְיָהוּ</span></big>** **ʼĂmatsyâhûw** <small>*(am-ats-yaw'-hoo)*</small>; from **<big>[[H0553|‎אָמַץ‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3050|‎יָהּ‎]]</big>**; strength of Jah</small>

$\quad$ Amaziah.
- § Amaziah = *"Jehovah is mighty"*
- ***Amatsjah***, the name of four Israelites

**1)** a king of Judah, son Joash, father of Azariah
**2)** a priest of Bethel under Jeroboam II
**3)** father of Joshah, of the tribe of Simeon
**4)** a Levite tabernacle singer in David's day
