$\quad$ Phuvah, Pua, Puah.
- § Puah or Pua or Phuvah = *"splendid"*
- ***Puah*** or ***Puvvah***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** son of Issachar
$\quad$ **1a)** also 'Pua' and 'Phuvah'
**2)** man of Issachar, father of Tola, the judge of Israel after Abimelech
