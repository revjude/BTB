**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עוּז‎</span></big>** **ʻûwz** <small>*(ooz)*</small> ==**to seek refuge**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ gather (self, self to flee), retire.
- ***to be strong***
-  causatively, ***to stregthen***, i.e. (figuratively) ***to save*** (by flight)

**1)** (Hiphil) to take refuge, bring to refuge, seek refuge
