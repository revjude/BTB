**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מַתִּתְיָה‎</span></big>** **Mattithyâh** <small>*(mat-tith-yaw')*</small> ==**Mattithiah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">מַתִּתְיָהוּ</span></big>** **Mattithyâhûw** <small>*(mat-tith-yaw'-hoo)*</small>; from **<big>[[H4991|‎מַתָּת‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3050|‎יָהּ‎]]</big>**; gift of Jah</small>

$\quad$ Mattithiah. n
- § Mattithiah = *"gift of Jehovah"*
- ***Mattithjah***, the name of four Israelites

**1)** a Levite who presided over the offerings
**2)** a Levite appointed by David to minister in the musical service before the ark
**3)** one of the family of Nebo who had a foreign wife in the time of Ezra
**4)** one of the men who stood at the right hand of Ezra when he read the law to the people
