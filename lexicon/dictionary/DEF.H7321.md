**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רוּעַ‎</span></big>** **rûwaʻ** <small>*(roo-ah')*</small> ==**to shout**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ blow an alarm, cry (alarm, aloud, out), destroy, make a joyful noise, smart, shout (for joy), sound an alarm, triumph.
- ***to mar*** (especially by breaking)
-  figuratively, ***to split the ears*** (with sound), i.e. ***shout*** (for alarm or joy)

**1)** to shout, raise a sound, cry out, give a blast
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to shout a war-cry or alarm of battle
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to sound a signal for war or march
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to shout in triumph (over enemies)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to shout in applause
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to shout (with religious impulse)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to cry out in distress
$\quad$ **1b)** (Polal) to utter a shout
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpolel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to shout in triumph
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to shout for joy
**2)** (Niphal) destroyed
