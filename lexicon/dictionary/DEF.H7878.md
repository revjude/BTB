**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שִׂיחַ‎</span></big>** **sîyach** <small>*(see'-akh)*</small> ==**to muse**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ commune, complain, declare, meditate, muse, pray, speak, talk (with).
- ***to ponder***, i.e. (by implication) ***converse*** (with oneself, and hence, aloud) or (transitively) ***utter***

**1)** to put forth, mediate, muse, commune, speak, complain, ponder, sing
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to complain
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to muse, meditate upon, study, ponder
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to talk, sing, speak
$\quad$ **1b)** (Polel) to meditate, consider, put forth thoughts
