**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָמַר‎</span></big>** **ʼâmar** <small>*(aw-mar')*</small> ==**to say**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ answer, appoint, avouch, bid, boast self, call, certify, challenge, charge, [phrase] (at the, give) command(-ment), commune, consider, declare, demand, [idiom] desire, determine, [idiom] expressly, [idiom] indeed, [idiom] intend, name, [idiom] plainly, promise, publish, report, require, say, speak (against, of), [idiom] still, [idiom] suppose, talk, tell, term, [idiom] that is, [idiom] think, use (speech), utter, [idiom] verily, [idiom] yet.
- ***to say*** (used with great latitude)

**1)** to say, speak, utter
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to say,  to answer, to say in one's heart, to think, to command, to promise, to intend
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be told, to be said, to be called
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to boast, to act proudly
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to avow, to avouch
