**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἄγω‎</span></big>** **ágō** <small>*(ag'-o)*</small> ==**to bring**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary verb</small>

$\quad$ be, bring (forth), carry, (let) go, keep, lead away, be open.
- properly, to lead
-  by implication, to bring, drive, (reflexively) go, (specially) pass (time), or (figuratively) induce
