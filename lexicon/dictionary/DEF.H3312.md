$\quad$ Jephunneh.
- § Jephunneh = *"He will be facing"*
- ***Jephunneh***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** a Kenezite and father of Caleb the spy from the tribe of Judah
**2)** son of Jether and descendant of Asher
