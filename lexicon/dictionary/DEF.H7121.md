**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָרָא‎</span></big>** **qârâʼ** <small>*(kaw-raw')*</small> ==**to call: call to**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (rather identical with **<big>[[H7122|‎קָרָא‎]]</big>** through the idea of accosting a person met)</small>

$\quad$ bewray (self), that are bidden, call (for, forth, self, upon), cry (unto), (be) famous, guest, invite, mention, (give) name, preach, (make) proclaim(-ation), pronounce, publish, read, renowned, say.
- ***to call out to*** (i.e. properly, address by name, but used in a wide variety of applications)

**1)** to call, call out, recite, read, cry out, proclaim
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to call, cry, utter a loud sound
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to call unto, cry (for help), call (with name of God)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to proclaim
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to read aloud, read (to oneself), read
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to summon, invite, call for, call and commission, appoint, call and endow
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to call, name, give name to, call by
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to call oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be called, be proclaimed, be read aloud, be summoned, be named
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be called, be named, be called out, be chosen
