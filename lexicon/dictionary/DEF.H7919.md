**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׂכַל‎</span></big>** **sâkal** <small>*(saw-kal')*</small> ==**be prudent**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ consider, expert, instruct, prosper, (deal) prudent(-ly), (give) skill(-ful), have good success, teach, (have, make to) understand(-ing), wisdom, (be, behave self, consider, make) wise(-ly), guide wittingly.
- ***to be*** (causatively, make or act) ***circumspect*** and hence, ***intelligent***

**1)** to be prudent, be circumspect, wisely understand, prosper
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be prudent, be circumspect
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to look at or upon, have insight
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to give attention to, consider, ponder, be prudent
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to have insight, have comprehension
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3a)** insight, comprehension (subst)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to cause to consider, give insight, teach
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4a)** the teachers, the wise
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** to act circumspectly, act prudently, act wisely
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b6)** to prosper, have success
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b7)** to cause to prosper
**2)** (Piel) to lay crosswise, cross (hands)
