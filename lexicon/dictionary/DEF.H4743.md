**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָקַק‎</span></big>** **mâqaq** <small>*(maw-kak')*</small> ==**to rot**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>' a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ consume away, be corrupt, dissolve, pine away.
- ***to melt***
-  figuratively, ***to flow***, ***dwindle***, ***vanish***

**1)** to decay, pine away, rot, fester
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to fester (of wounds)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to rot, rot away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to moulder away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to pine away
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to cause to rot
