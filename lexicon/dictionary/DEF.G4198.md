**<big><span class="asgreek">‎πορεύομαι‎</span></big>** **poreúomai** <small>*(por-yoo'-om-ahee)*</small> ==**to travel**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>middle voice from a derivative of the same as **<big>[[G3984|‎πεῖρα‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ --depart, go (away, forth, one's way, up), (make a, take a) journey, walk.
- to traverse, i.e. travel (literally or figuratively
-  especially to remove (figuratively, die), live, etc.)
