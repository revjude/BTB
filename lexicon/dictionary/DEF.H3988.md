**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָאַס‎</span></big>** **mâʼaç** <small>*(maw-as')*</small> ==**to reject**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ abhor, cast away (off), contemn, despise, disdain, (become) loathe(some), melt away, refuse, reject, reprobate, [idiom] utterly, vile person.
- ***to spurn***
-  also (intransitively) ***to disappear***

**1)** to reject, despise, refuse
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to reject, refuse
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to despise
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be rejected
**2)** (Niphal) to flow, run
