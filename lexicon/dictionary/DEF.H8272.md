**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שַׁרְאֶצֶר‎</span></big>** **Sharʼetser** <small>*(shar-eh'-tser)*</small> ==**Sharezer**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>of foreign derivation</small>

$\quad$ Sharezer.
- § Sharezer or Sherezer = *"prince of fire"*
- ***Sharetser***, the name of an Assyrian and an Israelite

**1)** son of king Sennacherib of Assyria and murderer of his father
**2)** an Israelite whom the people sent to the house of God to pray in the time of the prophet Zechariah and king Darius
