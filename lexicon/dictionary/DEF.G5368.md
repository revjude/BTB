**<big><span class="asgreek">‎φιλέω‎</span></big>** **philéō** <small>*(fil-eh'-o)*</small> ==**to love**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G5384|‎φίλος‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ kiss, love.
- to be a friend to (fond of (an individual or an object)), i.e. have affection for (denoting personal attachment, as a matter of sentiment or feeling
-  while **<big>[[G0025|‎ἀγαπάω‎]]</big>** is wider, embracing especially the judgment and the deliberate assent of the will as a matter of principle, duty and propriety: the two thus stand related very much as **<big>[[G2309|‎θέλω‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G1014|‎βούλομαι‎]]</big>**, or as **<big>[[G2372|‎θυμός‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G3563|‎νοῦς‎]]</big>** respectively
-  the former being chiefly of the heart and the latter of the head)
-  specially, to kiss (as a mark of tenderness)
