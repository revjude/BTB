**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָלָה‎</span></big>** **châlâh** <small>*(khaw-law')*</small> ==**be weak: weak**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (compare **<big>[[H2342|‎חוּל‎]]</big>**, **<big>[[H2470|‎חָלָה‎]]</big>**, **<big>[[H2490|‎חָלַל‎]]</big>**)</small>

$\quad$ beseech, (be) diseased, (put to) grief, be grieved, (be) grievous, infirmity, intreat, lay to, put to pain, [idiom] pray, make prayer, be (fall, make) sick, sore, be sorry, make suit ([idiom] supplication), woman in travail, be (become) weak, be wounded.
- properly, ***to be rubbed*** or ***worn***
-  hence (figuratively) ***to be weak***, ***sick***, ***afflicted***
-  or (causatively) ***to grieve***, ***make sick***
-  also ***to stroke*** (in flattering), ***entreat***

**1)** to be or become weak, be or become sick, be or become diseased, be or become grieved, be or become sorry
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be weak, be sick
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be or become weak, feel weak
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to become sick, become ill
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** (CLBL) to entreat, pray, beg
$\quad$ **1c)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to make oneself sick
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to be made sick
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to be tired
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be made weak, become weak
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to make oneself sick
$\quad$ **1f)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f1)** to make sore
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f2)** to make sick
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f3)** to show signs of sickness, become sick
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f4)** to grieve
$\quad$ **1g)** (Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1g1)** to be made sick
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1g2)** to be wounded
