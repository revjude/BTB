**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָצוֹר‎</span></big>** **Châtsôwr** <small>*(khaw-tsore')*</small> ==**Hazor**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--L|N:N--L]]</small></sup> 
<small>a collective form of **<big>[[H2691|‎חָצֵר‎]]</big>**; village</small>

$\quad$ Hazor.
- § Hazor = *"castle"*
- ***Chatsor***, the name (thus simply) of two places in Palestine and of one in Arabia

**1)** a royal city of northern Palestine allotted to Naphtali
**2)** one of the cities of Judah in the extreme south
**3)** a town north of Jerusalem in which the Benjamites resided after the return from exile
**4)** a site in Arabia
