**<big><span class="asaramaic">‎כֹּר‎</span></big>** **kôr** <small>*(kore)*</small> ==**kor**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from the same as **<big>[[H3564|‎כּוּר‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ cor, measure. Aramaic the same.
- properly, a deep round ***vessel***, i.e. (specifically) a ***cor*** or measure for things dry

**1)** kor, a measure (usually dry)
$\quad$ **1a)** a dry or liquid measure equal to 10 ephahs or baths
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** a dry measure containing 6.25 bushels (220 l)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** a liquid measure of 58 gallons (263 l)
