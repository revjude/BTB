**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָיָה‎</span></big>** **châyâh** <small>*(khaw-yaw')*</small> ==**to live**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (compare **<big>[[H2331|‎חָוָה‎]]</big>**, **<big>[[H2421|‎חָיָה‎]]</big>**)</small>

$\quad$ keep (leave, make) alive, [idiom] certainly, give (promise) life, (let, suffer to) live, nourish up, preserve (alive), quicken, recover, repair, restore (to life), revive, ([idiom] God) save (alive, life, lives), [idiom] surely, be whole.
- ***to live***, whether literally or figuratively
-  causatively, ***to revive***

**1)** to live, have life, remain alive, sustain life, live prosperously, live for ever, be quickened, be alive, be restored to life or health
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to live
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** to have life
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1b)** to continue in life, remain alive
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1c)** to sustain life, to live on or upon
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1d)** to live (prosperously)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to revive, be quickened
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2a)** from sickness
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2b)** from discouragement
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2c)** from faintness
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2d)** from death
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to preserve alive, let live

$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to quicken, revive, refresh
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3a)** to restore to life
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3b)** to cause to grow
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3c)** to restore
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3d)** to revive
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
