**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נוּס‎</span></big>** **nûwç** <small>*(noos)*</small> ==**to flee**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] abate, away, be displayed, (make to) flee (away, -ing), put to flight, [idiom] hide, lift up a standard.
- ***to flit***, i.e. ***vanish away*** (subside, escape
-  causatively, chase, impel, deliver)

**1)** to flee, escape
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to flee
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to escape
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to take flight, m depart, disappear
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to fly (to the attack) on horseback
$\quad$ **1b)** (Polel) to drive at
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpolel) to take flight
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to put to flight
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to drive hastily
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to cause to disappear, hide
