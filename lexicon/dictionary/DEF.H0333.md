**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָטֵר‎</span></big>** **ʼÂṭêr** <small>*(aw-tare')*</small> ==**Ater**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H0332|‎אָטַר‎]]</big>**; maimed</small>

$\quad$ Ater.
- § Ater = *"binder"*
- ***Ater***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** an exiled descendant of Hezekiah
**2)** an exiled Levite porter (perhaps same as 1)
**3)** a chief Israelite who sealed Nehemiah's covenant
