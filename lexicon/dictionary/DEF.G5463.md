**<big><span class="asgreek">‎χαίρω‎</span></big>** **chaírō** <small>*(khah'-ee-ro)*</small> ==**to rejoice**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary verb</small>

$\quad$ farewell, be glad, God speed, greeting, hall, joy(- fully), rejoice.
- to be "cheer"ful, i.e. calmly happy or well-off
-  impersonally, especially as salutation (on meeting or parting), be well
