**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎הוֹדַוְיָה‎</span></big>** **Hôwdavyâh** <small>*(ho-dav-yaw')*</small> ==**Hodaviah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H1935|‎הוֹד‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3050|‎יָהּ‎]]</big>**; majesty of Jah</small>

$\quad$ Hodaviah.
- § Hodaviah = *"praise ye Jehovah"*
- ***Hodavjah***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** a man of Manasseh, one of the heads of the half tribe on the east of Jordan
**2)** a man of Benjamin, son of Has-senuah
**3)** a Levite, who seems to have given his name to an important family in the tribe
