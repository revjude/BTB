$\quad$ Delilah.
- § Delilah = *"feeble"*
- ***Delilah***, a Philistine woman

**1)** the Philistine mistress of Samson who betrayed the secret of his great strength, and by cutting his hair, enfeebled him and delivered him to the Philistines
