**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בּוֹא‎</span></big>** **bôwʼ** <small>*(bo)*</small> ==**to come (in): come**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ abide, apply, attain, [idiom] be, befall, [phrase] besiege, bring (forth, in, into, to pass), call, carry, [idiom] certainly, (cause, let, thing for) to come (against, in, out, upon, to pass), depart, [idiom] doubtless again, [phrase] eat, [phrase] employ, (cause to) enter (in, into, -tering, -trance, -try), be fallen, fetch, [phrase] follow, get, give, go (down, in, to war), grant, [phrase] have, [idiom] indeed, (in-) vade, lead, lift (up), mention, pull in, put, resort, run (down), send, set, [idiom] (well) stricken (in age), [idiom] surely, take (in), way.
- ***to go*** or ***come*** (in a wide variety of applications)

**1)** to go in, enter, come, go, come in
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to enter, come in
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to come
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2a)** to come with
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2b)** to come upon, fall or light upon, attack (enemy)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2c)** to come to pass
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to attain to
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to be enumerated
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to go
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to lead in
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to carry in
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to bring in, cause to come in, gather, cause to come, bring near, bring against, bring upon
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to bring to pass
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to be brought, brought in
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to be introduced, be put
