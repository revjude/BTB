**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מְשֵׁיזַבְאֵל‎</span></big>** **Mᵉshêyzabʼêl** <small>*(mesh-ay-zab-ale')*</small> ==**Meshezabel**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an equiv. to **<big>[[H7804|‎שְׁזַב‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H0410|‎אֵל‎]]</big>**; delivered of God</small>

$\quad$ Meshezabeel.
- § Meshezabeel = *"God delivers"*
- ***Meshezabel***, an Israelite

**1)** ancestor of Meshullam who assisted Nehemiah in rebuilding the wall of Jerusalem
$\quad$ **1a)** perhaps the same as 2 and 3
**2)** a head of the people who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
$\quad$ **2a)** perhaps the same as 1 and 3
**3)** father of Pethahiah and descendant of Zerah the son of Judah
$\quad$ **3a)** perhaps the same as 1 and 2
