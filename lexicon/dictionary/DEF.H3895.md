<small>from an unused root meaning to be soft</small>

- ***the cheek*** (from its fleshiness)
-  hence, ***the jaw-bone***

**1)** jaw, cheek
$\quad$ **1a)** jaw, jawbone (of animal)
$\quad$ **1b)** cheek (of man)
