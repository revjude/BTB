**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָסֵר‎</span></big>** **châçêr** <small>*(khaw-sare')*</small> ==**to lack**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be abated, bereave, decrease, (cause to) fail, (have) lack, make lower, want.
- ***to lack***
-  by implication, ***to fail***, ***want***, ***lessen***

**1)** to lack, be without, decrease, be lacking, have a need
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to lack
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be lacking
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to diminish, decrease
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to cause to lack
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to cause to be lacking
