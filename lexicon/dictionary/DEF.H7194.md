**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָשַׁר‎</span></big>** **qâshar** <small>*(kaw-shar')*</small> ==**to conspire**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bind (up), (make a) conspire(-acy, -ator), join together, knit, stronger, work (treason).
- to ***tie***, physically (***gird***, ***confine***, ***compact***) or mentally (in ***love***, ***league***)

**1)** to bind, tie, bind together, league together, conspire
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to bind, confine
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to league together, conspire
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be bound, be bound up
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to bind on
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to bind fast
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to bind, tie
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to bind to oneself
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) robust, vigorous (participle)
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to conspire
