**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָפָה‎</span></big>** **yâphâh** <small>*(yaw-faw')*</small> ==**be beautiful**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be beautiful, be (make self) fair(-r), deck.
- properly, ***to be bright***, i.e. (by implication) ***beautiful***

**1)** to be bright, be beautiful, be handsome, be fair
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be beautiful
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to beautify
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be beautiful
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael) to beautify oneself
