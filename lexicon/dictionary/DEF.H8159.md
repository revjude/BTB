**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁעָה‎</span></big>** **shâʻâh** <small>*(shaw-aw')*</small> ==**to gaze**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ depart, be dim, be dismayed, look (away), regard, have respect, spare, turn.
- ***to gaze at*** or ***about*** (properly, for help)
-  by implication, ***to inspect***, ***consider***, ***compassionate***, ***be nonplussed*** (as looking around in amazement) or ***bewildered***

**1)** to look at or to, regard, gaze at or about
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to gaze at, regard, behold, look about
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to look away, cause gaze to turn away
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to look in dismay, gaze about (in anxiety)
