**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἄνομος‎</span></big>** **ánomos** <small>*(an'-om-os)*</small> ==**lawless**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:A|G:A]]</small></sup>

-  (by implication, a Gentile), or (positively) wicked
