**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אַחְיוֹ‎</span></big>** **ʼAchyôw** <small>*(akh-yo')*</small> ==**Ahio**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>prolonged from **<big>[[H0251|‎אָח‎]]</big>**; brotherly</small>

$\quad$ Ahio.
- § Ahio = *"brotherly" or "fraternal"*
- ***Achio***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** son of Abinadab who sheltered the ark
**2)** a Benjamite
**3)** another Benjamite of Saul's ancestry
