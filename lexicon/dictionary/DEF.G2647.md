**<big><span class="asgreek">‎καταλύω‎</span></big>** **katalýō** <small>*(kat-al-oo'-o)*</small> ==**to destroy/lodge**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G2596|‎κατά‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G3089|‎λύω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ destroy, dissolve, be guest, lodge, come to nought, overthrow, throw down.
- to loosen down (disintegrate), i.e. (by implication) to demolish (literally or figuratively)
-  specially (compare **<big>[[G2646|‎κατάλυμα‎]]</big>**) to halt for the night
