**<big><span class="asgreek">‎εὐδοκέω‎</span></big>** **eudokéō** <small>*(yoo-dok-eh'-o)*</small> ==**to delight**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G2095|‎εὖ‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G1380|‎δοκέω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ think good, (be well) please(-d), be the good (have, take) pleasure, be willing.
- to think well of, i.e. approve (an act)
-  specially, to approbate (a person or thing)
