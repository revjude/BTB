**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָחַת‎</span></big>** **nâchath** <small>*(naw-khath')*</small> ==**to descend**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be broken, (cause to) come down, enter, go down, press sore, settle, stick fast.
- ***to sink***, i.e. ***descend***
-  causatively, ***to press*** or ***lead down***

**1)** to go down, descend
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to go down, descend
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to descend, descend into (chastisement) (fig.)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to come down into, penetrate
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to cause to descend, press down, stretch (a bow)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to bring down
