**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כּוֹס‎</span></big>** **kôwç** <small>*(koce)*</small> ==**cup**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root meaning to hold together</small>

$\quad$ cup, (small) owl. Compare **<big>[[H3599|‎כִּיס‎]]</big>**.
- ***a cup*** (as a container), often figuratively, ***a lot*** (as if a potion)
-  also ***some unclean bird***, probably ***an owl*** (perhaps from the cup-like cavity of its eye)
