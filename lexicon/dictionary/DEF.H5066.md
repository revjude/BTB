**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָגַשׁ‎</span></big>** **nâgash** <small>*(naw-gash')*</small> ==**to approach: approach**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (make to) approach (nigh), bring (forth, hither, near), (cause to) come (hither, near, nigh), give place, go hard (up), (be, draw, go) near (nigh), offer, overtake, present, put, stand.
- to be or ***come*** (causatively, bring) ***near*** (for any purpose)
-  euphemistically, ***to lie with*** a woman
-  as an enemy, to ***attack***
-  religious to ***worship***
-  causatively, to ***present***
-  figuratively, to ***adduce*** an argument
-  by reversal, to ***stand back***

**1)** to draw near, approach
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to draw or come near
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** of humans
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** of sexual intercourse
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** of inanimate subject
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2a)** to approach one another
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to draw near
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to cause to approach, bring near, bring
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to be brought near
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to draw near
