**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָצַץ‎</span></big>** **châtsats** <small>*(khaw-tsats')*</small> ==**to divide**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup>

$\quad$ archer, [idiom] bands, cut off in the midst.

-  hence, ***to curtail***, ***to distribute*** (into ranks)
