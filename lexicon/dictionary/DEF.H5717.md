**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עֲדִיאֵל‎</span></big>** **ʻĂdîyʼêl** <small>*(ad-ee-ale')*</small> ==**Adiel**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5716|‎עֲדִי‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H0410|‎אֵל‎]]</big>**; ornament of God</small>

$\quad$ Adiel.
- § Adiel = *"ornament of God"*
- ***Adiel***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** a prince of the Simeon of the family of Shimei in the time of king Hezekiah of Judah
**2)** a priest, ancestor of Maasiai
**3)** ancestor of Azmaveth, the treasurer of David
