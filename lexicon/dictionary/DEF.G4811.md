**<big><span class="asgreek">‎συκοφαντέω‎</span></big>** **sykophantéō** <small>*(soo-kof-an-teh'-o)*</small> ==**to extort**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from a compound of **<big>[[G4810|‎σῦκον‎]]</big>** and a derivative of **<big>[[G5316|‎φαίνω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ accuse falsely, take by false accusation.
- to be a fig-informer (reporter of the law forbidding the exportation of figs from Greece), "sycophant", i.e. (genitive and by extension) to defraud (exact unlawfully, extort)
