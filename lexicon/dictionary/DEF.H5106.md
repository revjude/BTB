**1)** to hinder, hold back, forbid, disallow, restrain, frustrate

$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to restrain, forbid, frustrate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to restrain, make averse, discourage
