**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎טוֹבִיָּה‎</span></big>** **Ṭôwbîyâh** <small>*(to-bee-yaw')*</small> ==**Tobijah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">טוֹבִיָּהוּ</span></big>** **Ṭôwbîyâhûw** <small>*(tobee-yaw'-hoo)*</small>; from **<big>[[H2896|‎טוֹב‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3050|‎יָהּ‎]]</big>**; goodness of Jehovah</small>

$\quad$ Tobiah, Tobijah.
- § Tobiah = *"Jehovah is good"*
- ***Tobijah***, the name of three Israelites and of one Samaritan

**1)** head of a family who returned from exile with Zerubbabel but who were unable to prove their connection with Israel
**2)** the Ammonite who with Sanballat opposed the rebuilding of Jerusalem
**3)** a Levite in the reign of Jehoshaphat
**4)** a chief of the returning exiles
