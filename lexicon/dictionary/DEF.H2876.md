**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎טַבָּח‎</span></big>** **ṭabbâch** <small>*(tab-bawkh')*</small> ==**guard**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H2873|‎טָבַח‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ cook, guard.
- properly, ***a butcher***
-  hence, ***a lifeguardsman*** (because he was acting as an executioner)
-  also ***a cook*** (usually slaughtering the animal for food)

**1)** executioner, cook, bodyguard, guardsman
$\quad$ **1a)** cook (who also killed the animal for food)
$\quad$ **1b)** guardsmen, bodyguard
