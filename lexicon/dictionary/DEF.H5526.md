<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">שָׂכַךְ</span></big>** **sâkak** <small>*(saw-kak')*</small>; (Exodus 33:22), a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cover, defence, defend, hedge in, join together, set, shut up.
- properly, ***to entwine as a******screen***
-  by implication, ***to fence in***, ***cover over***, (figuratively) ***protect***

**1)** (Qal) to hedge, fence about, shut in
**2)** to block, overshadow, screen, stop the approach, shut off, cover
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a1)** to screen, cover
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a2)** to cover oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a3)** protector (participle)
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b1)** to screen, cover
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b2)** to cover, defecate (euphemism)
**3)** (Qal) to cover, lay over
**4)** to weave together
$\quad$ **4a)** (Qal) to weave together
$\quad$ **4b)** (Pilpel) to weave, weave together
