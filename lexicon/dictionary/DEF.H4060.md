**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מִדָּה‎</span></big>** **middâh** <small>*(mid-daw')*</small> ==**measure**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>feminine of **<big>[[H4055|‎מַד‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ garment, measure(-ing, meteyard, piece, size, (great) stature, tribute, wide.
- properly, ***extension***, i.e. ***height*** or ***breadth***
-  also ***a measure*** (including its standard)
-  hence ***a portion*** (as measured) or ***a vestment***
-  specifically, ***tribute*** (as measured)

**1)** measure, measurement, stature, size, garment
$\quad$ **1a)** measure, act of measurement
$\quad$ **1b)** measurement, size
$\quad$ **1c)** measured portion, extent
$\quad$ **1d)** garment
**2)** (BDB) tribute
