**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָלַךְ‎</span></big>** **mâlak** <small>*(maw-lak')*</small> ==**to reign**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ consult, [idiom] indeed, be (make, set a, set up) king, be (make) queen, (begin to, make to) reign(-ing), rule, [idiom] surely.
- ***to reign***
-  ***inceptively***, ***to ascend the throne***
-  causatively, ***to induct into royalty***
-  hence (by implication) ***to take counsel***

**1)** to be or become king or queen, reign
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be or become king or queen, reign
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to make one king or queen, cause to reign
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hophal) to be made king or queen
**2)** to counsel, advise
$\quad$ **2a)** (Niphal) to consider
