**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎סָרַר‎</span></big>** **çârar** <small>*(saw-rar')*</small> ==**to rebel**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] away, backsliding, rebellious, revolter(-ing), slide back, stubborn, withdrew.
- ***to turn away***, i.e. (morally) ***be refractory***

**1)** to rebel, be stubborn, be rebellious, be refractory
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be stubborn
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** stubborn, rebel (subst)
