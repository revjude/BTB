**<big><span class="asgreek">‎διασκορπίζω‎</span></big>** **diaskorpízō** <small>*(dee-as-kor-pid'-zo)*</small> ==**to scatter**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G1223|‎διά‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G4650|‎σκορπίζω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ disperse, scatter (abroad), strew, waste.
- to dissipate, i.e. (genitive case) to rout or separate
-  specially, to winnow
-  figuratively, to squander
