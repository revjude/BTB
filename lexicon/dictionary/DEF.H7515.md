**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָפַשׂ‎</span></big>** **râphas** <small>*(raw-fas')*</small> ==**to foul**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ foul, trouble.
- ***to trample***, i.e. ***roil water***

**1)** to stamp, tread, foul by stamping or treading
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to tread down, foul by treading, foul with the feet
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be fouled
