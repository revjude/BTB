**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גֵּר‎</span></big>** **gêr** <small>*(gare)*</small> ==**sojourner**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>or (fully) geyr (gare); from **<big>[[H1481|‎גּוּר‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ alien, sojourner, stranger.
- properly, ***a guest***
-  by implication, ***a foreigner***

**1)** sojourner
$\quad$ **1a)** a temporary inhabitant, a newcomer lacking inherited rights
$\quad$ **1b)** of foreigners in Israel, though conceded rights
