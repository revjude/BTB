**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָכָה‎</span></big>** **nâkâh** <small>*(naw-kaw')*</small> ==**to smite**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ beat, cast forth, clap, give (wounds), [idiom] go forward, [idiom] indeed, kill, make (slaughter), murderer, punish, slaughter, slay(-er, -ing), smite(-r, -ing), strike, be stricken, (give) stripes, [idiom] surely, wound.
- ***to strike*** (lightly or severely, literally or figuratively)

**1)** to strike, smite, hit, beat, slay, kill
$\quad$ **1a)**(Niphal) to be stricken or smitten
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) to be stricken or smitten
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to smite, strike, beat, scourge, clap, applaud, give a thrust
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to smite, kill, slay (man or beast)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to smite, attack, attack and destroy, conquer, subjugate, ravage
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to smite, chastise, send judgment upon, punish, destroy
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to be smitten
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to receive a blow
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to be wounded
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to be beaten
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d4)** to be (fatally) smitten, be killed, be slain
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d5)** to be attacked and captured
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d6)** to be smitten (with disease)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d7)** to be blighted (of plants)
