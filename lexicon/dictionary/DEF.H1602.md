**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּעַל‎</span></big>** **gâʻal** <small>*(gaw-al')*</small> ==**to abhor**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ abhor, fail, lothe, vilely cast away.
- ***to detest***
-  by implication, ***to reject***

**1)** to abhor, loathe, be vilely cast away, fall
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to abhor, loathe
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be defiled
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to reject as loathsome, show aversion
