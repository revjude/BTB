**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יַרְמוּת‎</span></big>** **Yarmûwth** <small>*(yar-mooth')*</small> ==**Jarmuth**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--L|N:N--L]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H7311|‎רוּם‎]]</big>**; elevation</small>

$\quad$ Jarmuth.
- § Jarmuth = *"heights"*
- ***Jarmuth***, the name of two places in Palestine

**1)** a Canaanitish city in the lowlands of Judah with a king and located between Hebron and Lachish
**2)** a Levitical city of Issachar allocated to the Gershonite Levites
