- ***to vomit***

**1)** to vomit up, spue out, disgorge
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to vomit
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to vomit up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** of land spewing out its inhabitants (fig)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** vomiting, disgorging
