**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָפַץ‎</span></big>** **qâphats** <small>*(kaw-fats')*</small> ==**to gather**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ shut (up), skip, stop, take out of the way.
- ***to draw together***, i.e. ***close***
-  by implication, ***to leap*** (by contracting the limbs)
-  specifically, ***to die*** (from gathering up the feet)

**1)** to draw together, close, shut, shut up, stop up
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to shut
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to draw oneself together (of death)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be shut up to death
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) springing, skipping (participle)
