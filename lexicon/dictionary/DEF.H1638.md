$\quad$ break.
- ***to crush***
-  also (intransitively and figuratively) ***to dissolve***

**1)** to be crushed, be broken
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be crushed
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to crush, break (the teeth)
