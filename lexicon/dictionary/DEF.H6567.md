**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּרָשׁ‎</span></big>** **pârâsh** <small>*(paw-rash')*</small> ==**to declare**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ scatter, declare, distinctly, shew, sting.
- ***to separate***, literally (to disperse) or figuratively (to specify)
-  also (by implication) ***to wound***

**1)** to make distinct, declare, distinguish, separate
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to declare, clarify
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) to be distinctly declared
**2)** (Hiphil) to pierce, sting
**3)** (Niphal) scatter
