**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָשַׁל‎</span></big>** **nâshal** <small>*(naw-shal')*</small> ==**to slip**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cast (out), drive, loose, put off (out), slip.
- ***to pluck off***, i.e. ***divest***, ***eject*** or ***drop***

**1)** to slip off, drop off, clear away, draw off
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to slip or drop off
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to draw off
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to clear away
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to clear out completely, clear entirely
