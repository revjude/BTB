**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּקָר‎</span></big>** **bâqâr** <small>*(baw-kawr')*</small> ==**cattle**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N|H:N]]</small></sup>

$\quad$ beeve, bull ([phrase] -ock), [phrase] calf, [phrase] cow, great (cattle), [phrase] heifer, herd, kine, ox.
- ***beef cattle*** or an animal of the ***ox*** family of either gender (as used for plowing)
-  collectively, ***a herd***

**1)** cattle, herd, oxen, ox
$\quad$ **1a)** cattle (generic pl. but sing. in form-coll)
$\quad$ **1b)** herd (particular one)
$\quad$ **1c)** head of cattle (individually)
