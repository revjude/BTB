**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נוּץ‎</span></big>** **nûwts** <small>*(noots)*</small> ==**to flee**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ flee away, bud (forth).
- properly, ***to flash***
-  hence, ***to blossom*** (from the brilliancy of color)
-  also, ***to fly away*** (from the quickness of motion)

**1)** (Qal) to fly, flee
**2)** (Hiphil) to bloom, blossom
