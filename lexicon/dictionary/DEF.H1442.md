$\quad$ blaspheme, reproach.
- ***to hack*** (with words), i.e. ***revile***

**1)** to revile men, blaspheme God
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to revile (between men)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to blaspheme (God)
