**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎דָּשֵׁן‎</span></big>** **dâshên** <small>*(daw-shane')*</small> ==**to prosper**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; also denominatively (from **<big>[[H1880|‎דֶּשֶׁן‎]]</big>**)</small>

$\quad$ accept, anoint, take away the (receive) ashes (from), make (wax) fat.
- ***to be fat***
-  transitively, ***to fatten*** (or regard as fat)
-  specifically ***to anoint***
-  figuratively, ***to satisfy***
- ***  to remove*** (fat) ***ashes*** (of sacrifices)

**1)** to be fat, grow fat, become fat, become prosperous, anoint
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) of prosperity (fig.)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to make fat, anoint
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to find fat (of offering-acceptable)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to take away ashes (from altar)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be made fat
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hothpael) to fatten oneself (of Jehovah's sword)
