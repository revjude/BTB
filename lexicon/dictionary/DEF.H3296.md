$\quad$ Jaare-oregim.
- § Jaare-oregim = *"forests of weaver"*
- ***Jaare-Oregim***, an Israelite

**1)** a man of Bethlehem, father of Elhanan the giant slayer and one of David's mighty warriors; also called 'Jair'
