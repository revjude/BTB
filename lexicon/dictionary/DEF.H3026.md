**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יְגַר שַׂהֲדוּתָא‎</span></big>** **Yᵉgar Sahădûwthâʼ** <small>*(yegar' sah-had-oo-thaw')*</small> ==**Jegar**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--L|N:N--L]]</small></sup> 
<small>(Aramaic) from a word derived from an unused root (meaning to gather) and a derivation of a root corresponding to **<big>[[H7717|‎שָׂהֵד‎]]</big>**; heap of the testimony</small>

$\quad$ Jegar-Sahadutha.
- § Jegar-sahadutha = *"witness heap"*
- ***Jegar-Sahadutha***, a cairn East of the Jordan

**1)** the mound of stones raised as witness between Jacob and Laban, called by Jacob in Hebrew 'Galeed'
