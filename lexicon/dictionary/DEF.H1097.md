**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בְּלִי‎</span></big>** **bᵉlîy** <small>*(bel-ee')*</small> ==**without**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:Neg|H:Neg]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H1086|‎בָּלָה‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ corruption, ig(norantly), for lack of, where no...is, so that no, none, not, un(awares), without.
- properly, ***failure***, i.e. ***nothing*** or ***destruction***
-  usually (with preposition) ***without***, ***not yet***, ***because not***, ***as long as***, etc.

subst
**1)** wearing out
adv of negation
**2)** without, no, not
