**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּלַח‎</span></big>** **pâlach** <small>*(paw-lakh')*</small> ==**to cleave**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bring forth, cleave, cut, shred, strike through.
- ***to slice***, i.e. ***break open*** or ***pierce***

**1)** to cleave, slice
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to cleave, plow
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cleave open or through
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to cause to cleave open
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to slice, pierce
