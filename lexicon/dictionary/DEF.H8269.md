**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שַׂר‎</span></big>** **sar** <small>*(sar)*</small> ==**ruler**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H8323|‎שָׂרַר‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ captain (that had rule), chief (captain), general, governor, keeper, lord,(-task-)master, prince(-ipal), ruler, steward.
- ***a head person*** (of any rank or class)

**1)** prince, ruler, leader, chief, chieftain, official, captain
$\quad$ **1a)** chieftain, leader
$\quad$ **1b)** vassal, noble, official (under king)
$\quad$ **1c)** captain, general, commander (military)
$\quad$ **1d)** chief, head, overseer (of other official classes)
$\quad$ **1e)** heads, princes (of religious office)
$\quad$ **1f)** elders (of representative leaders of people)
$\quad$ **1g)** merchant-princes (of rank and dignity)
$\quad$ **1h)** patron-angel
$\quad$ **1i)** Ruler of rulers (of God)
$\quad$ **1j)** warden
