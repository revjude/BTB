**<big><span class="asgreek">‎γεννάω‎</span></big>** **gennáō** <small>*(ghen-nah'-o)*</small> ==**to beget**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from a variation of **<big>[[G1085|‎γένος‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ bear, beget, be born, bring forth, conceive, be delivered of, gender, make, spring.
- to procreate (properly, of the father, but by extension of the mother)
-  figuratively, to regenerate
