**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מַקֶּבֶת‎</span></big>** **maqqebeth** <small>*(mak-keh'-beth)*</small> ==**hammer**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5344|‎נָקַב‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ hammer, hole.
- properly, ***a perforator***, i.e. ***a hammer*** (as piercing)
-  also (intransitively) ***a perforation***, i.e. ***a quarry***

**1)** hole, excavation, perforation
**2)** hammer, perforator
