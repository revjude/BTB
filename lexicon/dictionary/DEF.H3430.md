<small>from **<big>[[H3427|‎יָשַׁב‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H5011|‎נֹב‎]]</big>**, with a pronominal suffix and a preposition interposed; his dwelling (is) in Nob</small>

$\quad$ Ishbi-benob (from the margin).
- § Ishbi-benob = *"his dwelling is in Nob"*
- ***Jishbo-be-Nob***, a Philistine

**1)** son of Rapha, one of the nation of Philistine giants who attacked David in battle and was slain by Abishai
