**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בְּהֵמָה‎</span></big>** **bᵉhêmâh** <small>*(be-hay-maw')*</small> ==**animal**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root (probably meaning to be mute)</small>

$\quad$ beast, cattle.
- properly, ***a dumb beast***
-  especially ***any large quadruped*** or ***animal*** (often collective)

**1)** beast, cattle, animal
$\quad$ **1a)** beasts (coll of all animals)
$\quad$ **1b)** cattle, livestock (of domestic animals)
$\quad$ **1c)** wild beasts
