**<big><span class="asgreek">‎συνιστάω‎</span></big>** **synistáō** <small>*(soon-is-tah'-o)*</small> ==**to commend**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4862|‎σύν‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2476|‎ἵστημι‎]]</big>** (including its collateral forms)</small>

$\quad$ approve, commend, consist, make, stand (with).
- to set together, i.e. (by implication) to introduce (favorably), or (figuratively) to exhibit
-  intransitively, to stand near, or (figuratively) to constitute
