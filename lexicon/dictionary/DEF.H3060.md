**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יְהוֹאָשׁ‎</span></big>** **Yᵉhôwʼâsh** <small>*(yeh-ho-awsh')*</small> ==**Jehoash**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H3068|‎יְהֹוָה‎]]</big>** and (perhaps) **<big>[[H0784|‎אֵשׁ‎]]</big>**; Jehovah-fired</small>

$\quad$ Jehoash. Compare **<big>[[H3101|‎יוֹאָשׁ‎]]</big>**.
- § Jehoash = *"given by the Lord"*
- ***Jehoash***, the name of two Israelite kings

**1)** son of king Ahaziah and the 8th king of Judah
**2)** son of king Jehoahaz and the 12th king of the northern kingdom of Israel
