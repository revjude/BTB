**<big><span class="asgreek">‎μνάομαι‎</span></big>** **mnáomai** <small>*(mnah'-om-ahee)*</small> ==**to remember**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>middle voice of a derivative of **<big>[[G3306|‎μένω‎]]</big>** or perhaps of the base of **<big>[[G3145|‎μασσάομαι‎]]</big>** (through the idea of fixture in the mind or of mental grasp)</small>

$\quad$ be mindful, remember, come (have) in remembrance.
- to bear in mind, i.e. recollect
-  by implication, to reward or punish
