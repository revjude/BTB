**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פִּי־בֶסֶת‎</span></big>** **Pîy-Beçeth** <small>*(pee beh'-seth)*</small> ==**Pi-beseth**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--L|N:N--L]]</small></sup> 
<small>of Egyptian origin</small>

$\quad$ Pi-beseth.
- § Pi-beseth = *"mouth of loathing"*
- ***Pi-Beseth***, a place in Egypt

**1)** a town of lower Egypt located on the west bank of the Pelusiac branch of the Nile about 40 miles from Memphis
$\quad$ **1a)** same as 'Bubastis' named after the goddess of the same name
