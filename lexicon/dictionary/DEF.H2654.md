**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָפֵץ‎</span></big>** **châphêts** <small>*(khaw-fates')*</small> ==**to delight in**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] any at all, (have, take) delight, desire, favour, like, move, be (well) pleased, have pleasure, will, would.
- properly, ***to incline to***
-  by implication (literally but rarely) ***to bend***
-  figuratively, ***to be pleased with***, ***desire***

**1)** to delight in, take pleasure in, desire, be pleased with
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** of men
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** to take pleasure in, delight in
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1b)** to delight, desire, be pleased to do
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** of God
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2a)** to delight in, have pleasure in
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2b)** to be pleased to do
**2)** to move, bend down
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to bend down
