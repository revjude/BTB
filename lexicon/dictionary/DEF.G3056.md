**<big><span class="asgreek">‎λόγος‎</span></big>** **lógos** <small>*(log'-os)*</small> ==**word**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-M|G:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G3004|‎λέγω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ account, cause, communication, X concerning, doctrine, fame, X have to do, intent, matter, mouth, preaching, question, reason, + reckon, remove, say(-ing), shew, X speaker, speech, talk, thing, + none of these things move me, tidings, treatise, utterance, word, work.
- something said (including the thought)
-  by implication, a topic (subject of discourse), also reasoning (the mental faculty) or motive
-  by extension, a computation
-  specially, (with the article in John) the Divine Expression (i.e. Christ)
