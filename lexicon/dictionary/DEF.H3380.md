**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יְרֻבֶּשֶׁת‎</span></big>** **Yᵉrubbesheth** <small>*(yer-oob-beh'-sheth)*</small> ==**Jerubbesheth**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H7378|‎רִיב‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H1322|‎בֹּשֶׁת‎]]</big>**; shame (i.e. the idol) will contend</small>

$\quad$ Jerubbesheth.
- § Jerubbesheth = *"shame will contend"*
- ***Jerubbesheth***, a symbolic name for Gideon

**1)** a variant name of Jerubbaal (the other name of Gideon [**<big>[[H3378|יְרֻבַּעַל]]</big>**]) substituting the word 'shame' for the name of the pagan god 'Baal'
