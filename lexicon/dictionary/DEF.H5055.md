**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָגַח‎</span></big>** **nâgach** <small>*(naw-gakh')*</small> ==**to gore**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ gore, push (down, -ing).
- ***to butt with the horns***
-  figuratively, ***to war against***

**1)** to push, thrust, gore
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to gore
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to push or thrust at
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to engage in thrusting, wage war
