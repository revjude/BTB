**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּקַשׁ‎</span></big>** **bâqash** <small>*(baw-kash')*</small> ==**to seek**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ ask, beg, beseech, desire, enquire, get, make inquisition, procure, (make) request, require, seek (for).
- ***to search out*** (by any method, specifically in worship or prayer)
-  by implication, ***to strive after***

**1)** to seek, require, desire, exact, request
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to seek to find
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to seek to secure
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to seek the face
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to desire, demand
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to require, exact
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to ask, request
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) to be sought
