**<big><span class="asgreek">‎προσέρχομαι‎</span></big>** **prosérchomai** <small>*(pros-er'-khom-ahee)*</small> ==**to come near/agree**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4314|‎πρός‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2064|‎ἔρχομαι‎]]</big>** (including its alternate)</small>

$\quad$ (as soon as he) come (unto), come thereunto, consent, draw near, go (near, to, unto).
- to approach, i.e. (literally) come near, visit, or (figuratively) worship, assent to
