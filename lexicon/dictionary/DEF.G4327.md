**<big><span class="asgreek">‎προσδέχομαι‎</span></big>** **prosdéchomai** <small>*(pros-dekh'-om-ahee)*</small> ==**to wait for/welcome**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4314|‎πρός‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G1209|‎δέχομαι‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ accept, allow, look (wait) for, take.
- to admit (to intercourse, hospitality, credence, or (figuratively) endurance)
-  by implication, to await (with confidence or patience)
