<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">שֹׁמֵר</span></big>** **Shômêr** <small>*(sho-mare')*</small>; active participle of **<big>[[H8104|‎שָׁמַר‎]]</big>**; keeper</small>

$\quad$ Shomer.
- § Shomer = *"keeper"*
- ***Shomer***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** son of Jehozabad or Shimrith the Moabitess and one of the conspirators who slew king Joash of Judah
**2)** an Asherite, son of Heber
$\quad$ **2a)** also 'Shemer'
