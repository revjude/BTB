**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἐπιδημέω‎</span></big>** **epidēméō** <small>*(ep-ee-day-meh'-o)*</small> ==**to sojourn**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from a compound of **<big>[[G1909|‎ἐπί‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G1218|‎δῆμος‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ (be) dwelling (which were) there, stranger.
- to make oneself at home, i.e. (by extension) to reside (in a foreign country)
