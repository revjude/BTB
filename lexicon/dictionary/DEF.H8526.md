**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תַּלְמַי‎</span></big>** **Talmay** <small>*(tal-mah'-ee)*</small> ==**Talmai**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H8525|‎תֶּלֶם‎]]</big>**; ridged</small>

$\quad$ Talmai.
- § Talmai = *"furrowed"*
- ***Talmai***, the name of a Canaanite and a Syrian

**1)** one of the 3 sons of the giant Anak who were slain by the men of Judah
**2)** son of Ammihud, king of Geshur, later king of Geshur himself, and father of Maacah, the mother of Absalom  and wife of David
