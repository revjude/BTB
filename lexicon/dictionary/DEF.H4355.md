**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָכַךְ‎</span></big>** **mâkak** <small>*(maw-kak')*</small> ==**to sink**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be brought low, decay.
- ***to tumble*** (in ruins)
-  figuratively, ***to perish***

**1)** to be low, be humiliated
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be low, be humiliated
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to sink
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hophal) to be brought low
