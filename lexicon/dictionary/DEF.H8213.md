**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁפֵל‎</span></big>** **shâphêl** <small>*(shaw-fale')*</small> ==**to abase**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ abase, bring (cast, put) down, debase, humble (self), be (bring, lay, make, put) low(-er).
- ***to depress*** or ***sink*** (expectation figuratively, to humiliate, intransitive or transitive)

**1)** to be or become low, sink, be humbled, be abased
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be or become low
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to lay or bring low, humiliate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to set in a lower place, show abasement
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to make low, sit down
