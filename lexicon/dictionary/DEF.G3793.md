**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ὄχλος‎</span></big>** **óchlos** <small>*(okh'los)*</small> ==**crowd**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-M|G:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from a derivative of **<big>[[G2192|‎ἔχω‎]]</big>** (meaning a vehicle)</small>

$\quad$ company, multitude, number (of people), people, press.
- a throng (as borne along)
-  by implication, the rabble
-  by extension, a class of people
-  figuratively, a riot
