**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁפַר‎</span></big>** **shâphar** <small>*(shaw-far')*</small> ==**to polish**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] goodly.
- ***to glisten***, i.e. (figuratively) ***be*** (causatively, make) ***fair***

**1)** to be pleasing, be beautiful, be fair, be comely, be bright, glisten
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be beautiful
