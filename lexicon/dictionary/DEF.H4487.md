**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָנָה‎</span></big>** **mânâh** <small>*(maw-naw')*</small> ==**to count**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ appoint, count, number, prepare, set, tell.
- properly, ***to weigh out***
-  by implication, ***to allot*** or ***constitute officially***
-  also ***to enumerate*** or ***enroll***

**1)** to count, reckon, number, assign, tell, appoint, prepare
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to count, number
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** reckon, assign, appoint
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be counted, be numbered
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be reckoned, be assigned
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to appoint, ordain
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) appointed (participle)
