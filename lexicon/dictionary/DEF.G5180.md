**<big><span class="asgreek">‎τύπτω‎</span></big>** **týptō** <small>*(toop'-to)*</small> ==**to strike**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary verb (in a strengthened form)</small>

$\quad$ beat, smite, strike, wound.
- to "thump", i.e. cudgel or pummel (properly, with a stick or bastinado), but in any case by repeated blows
-  thus differing from **<big>[[G3817|‎παίω‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G3960|‎πατάσσω‎]]</big>**, which denote a (usually single) blow with the hand or any instrument, or **<big>[[G4141|‎πλήσσω‎]]</big>** with the fist (or a hammer), or **<big>[[G4474|‎ῥαπίζω‎]]</big>** with the palm
-  as well as from **<big>[[G5177|‎τυγχάνω‎]]</big>**, an accidental collision)
-  by implication, to punish
-  figuratively, to offend (the conscience)
