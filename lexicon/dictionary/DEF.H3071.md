<small>from **<big>[[H3068|‎יְהֹוָה‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H5251|‎נֵס‎]]</big>** with the prononimal suffix; Jehovah (is) my banner</small>

$\quad$ Jehovah-nissi.
- § Jehovah-nissi = *"Jehovah is my banner"*
- ***Jehovah-Nissi***, a symbolical name of an altar in the Desert

**1)** the name given by Moses to the altar which he built in commemoration of the discomfiture of the Amalekites
