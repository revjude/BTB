**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָצַע‎</span></big>** **qâtsaʻ** <small>*(kaw-tsah')*</small> ==**to scrape**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cause to scrape, corner.
- ***to strip off***, i.e. (partially) ***scrape***
-  by implication, ***to segregate*** (as an angle)

**1)** to scrape, scrape off, cut off
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil) to scrape
**2)** to be cornered, be set in corners
$\quad$ **2a)** (Hophal) to cut off places, make corners
