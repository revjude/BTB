**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁמַע‎</span></big>** **shâmaʻ** <small>*(shaw-mah')*</small> ==**to hear: hear**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] attentively, call (gather) together, [idiom] carefully, [idiom] certainly, consent, consider, be content, declare, [idiom] diligently, discern, give ear, (cause to, let, make to) hear(-ken, tell), [idiom] indeed, listen, make (a) noise, (be) obedient, obey, perceive, (make a) proclaim(-ation), publish, regard, report, shew (forth), (make a) sound, [idiom] surely, tell, understand, whosoever (heareth), witness.
- ***to hear intelligently*** (often with implication of attention, obedience, etc.
-  causatively, to tell, etc.)

v
**1)** to hear, listen to, obey
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to hear (perceive by ear)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to hear of or concerning
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to hear (have power to hear)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to hear with attention or interest, listen to
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to understand (language)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to hear (of judicial cases)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7)** to listen, give heed
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7a)** to consent, agree
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7b)** to grant request
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a8)** to listen to, yield to
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a9)** to obey, be obedient
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be heard (of voice or sound)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be heard of
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be regarded, be obeyed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to cause to hear, call to hear, summon
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to cause to hear, tell, proclaim, utter a sound
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to sound aloud (musical term)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to make proclamation, summon
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d4)** to cause to be heard
n m
**2)** sound
