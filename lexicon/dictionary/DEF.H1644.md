**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּרַשׁ‎</span></big>** **gârash** <small>*(gaw-rash')*</small> ==**to drive out: drive out**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cast up (out), divorced (woman), drive away (forth, out), expel, [idiom] surely put away, trouble, thrust out.
- ***to drive out from a possession***
-  especially ***to expatriate*** or ***divorce***

**1)** to drive out, expel, cast out, drive away, divorce, put away, thrust away, trouble, cast up
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to thrust out, cast out
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be driven away, be tossed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to drive out, drive away
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be thrust out
