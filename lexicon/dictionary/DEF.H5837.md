**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עַזְרִיאֵל‎</span></big>** **ʻAzrîyʼêl** <small>*(az-ree-ale')*</small> ==**Azriel**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5828|‎עֵזֶר‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H0410|‎אֵל‎]]</big>**; help of God</small>

$\quad$ Azriel.
- § Azriel = *"my help is God"*
- ***Azriel***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** a head of a house of the half-tribe of Manasseh beyond the Jordan
**2)** a Naphtalite, ancestor of Jerimoth, the head of the tribe at the time of the census of David
**3)** father of Seraiah, an officer of king Jehoiakim of Judah
