**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָנַע‎</span></big>** **mânaʻ** <small>*(maw-nah')*</small> ==**to withhold**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ deny, keep (back), refrain, restrain, withhold.
- ***to debar*** (negatively or positively) ***from benefit*** or ***injury***

**1)** to withhold, hold back, keep back, refrain, deny, keep restrain, hinder
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to withhold
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be withheld
