**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יְחִזְקִיָּה‎</span></big>** **Yᵉchizqîyâh** <small>*(yekh-iz-kee-yaw')*</small> ==**Jehizkiah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">יְחִזְקִיָּהוּ</span></big>** **Yᵉchizqîyâhûw** <small>*(yekh-iz-kee-yaw'-hoo)*</small>; from **<big>[[H3388|‎יְרוּשָׁא‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3050|‎יָהּ‎]]</big>**; strengthened of Jah</small>

$\quad$ Hezekiah, Jehizkiah. Compare **<big>[[H2396|‎חִזְקִיָּה‎]]</big>**.
- § Hezekiah or Jehizkiah = *"Jehovah has made strong"*
- ***Jechizkijah***, the name of five Israelites

**1)** son of king Ahazby Abi or Abijah and the 12th king of Judah; his reign was characterised by his godly conduct; reigned for 29 years
**2)** head of a family of returning exiles in the time of Nehemiah
**3)** an Ephraimite in the time of Ahaz
