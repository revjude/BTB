**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֶבְיוֹן‎</span></big>** **ʼebyôwn** <small>*(eb-yone')*</small> ==**needy**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:A-M|H:A-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H0014|‎אָבָה‎]]</big>**, in the sense of want (especially in feeling)</small>

$\quad$ beggar, needy, poor (man).
- ***destitute***

**1)** in want, needy, chiefly poor, needy person
**2)** subject to oppression and abuse
**3)** needing help, deliverance from trouble, especially as delivered by God
**4)** general reference to lowest class
