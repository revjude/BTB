**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָתַת‎</span></big>** **châthath** <small>*(khaw-thath')*</small> ==**to to be dismayed**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ abolish, affright, be (make) afraid, amaze, beat down, discourage, (cause to) dismay, go down, scare, terrify.
- properly, ***to prostrate***
-  hence, ***to break down***, ***either*** (literally) ***by violence***, or (figuratively) ***by confusion and fear***

**1)** to be shattered, be dismayed, be broken, be abolished, be afraid
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be shattered, be broken
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be dismayed
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be broken, be dismayed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to be shattered, be dismayed, be scared
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to cause to be dismayed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to dismay, terrify
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to shatter
