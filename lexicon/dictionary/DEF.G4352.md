**<big><span class="asgreek">‎προσκυνέω‎</span></big>** **proskynéō** <small>*(pros-koo-neh'-o)*</small> ==**to worship**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4314|‎πρός‎]]</big>** and a probable derivative of **<big>[[G2965|‎κύων‎]]</big>** (meaning to kiss, like a dog licking his master's hand)</small>

$\quad$ worship.
- to fawn or crouch to, i.e. (literally or figuratively) prostrate oneself in homage (do reverence to, adore)
