**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ψυχή‎</span></big>** **psychḗ** <small>*(psoo-khay')*</small> ==**soul**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-F|G:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G5594|‎ψύχω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ heart (+ -ily), life, mind, soul, + us, + you.
- breath, i.e. (by implication) spirit, abstractly or concretely (the animal sentient principle only
-  thus distinguished on the one hand from **<big>[[G4151|‎πνεῦμα‎]]</big>**, which is the rational and immortal soul
-  and on the other from **<big>[[G2222|‎ζωή‎]]</big>**, which is mere vitality, even of plants: these terms thus exactly correspond respectively to the Hebrew **<big>[[H5315|‎נֶפֶשׁ‎]]</big>**, **<big>[[H7307|‎רוּחַ‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H2416|‎חַי‎]]</big>**)
