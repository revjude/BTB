**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יוֹאָח‎</span></big>** **Yôwʼâch** <small>*(yo-awkh')*</small> ==**Joah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H3068|‎יְהֹוָה‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H0251|‎אָח‎]]</big>**; Jehovahbrothered</small>

$\quad$ Joah.
- § Joah = *"Jehovah is brother"*
- ***Joach***, the name of four Israelites

**1)** son of Asaph and chronicler to Hezekiah
**2)** a Gershonite son or grandson of Zimmah
**3)** a Korhite, 3rd son of Obed-edom and doorkeeper in the time of David
**4)** son of Joahaz and chronicler to king Josiah
