**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἀφίστημι‎</span></big>** **aphístēmi** <small>*(af-is'-tay-mee)*</small> ==**to leave**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G0575|‎ἀπό‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2476|‎ἵστημι‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ depart, draw (fall) away, refrain, withdraw self.
- to remove, i.e. (actively) instigate to revolt
-  usually (reflexively) to desist, desert, etc.
