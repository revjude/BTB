**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָרָה‎</span></big>** **yârâh** <small>*(yaw-raw')*</small> ==**to shoot**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>or (2 Chronicles 26:15) **<big><span class="ashebrew">יָרָא</span></big>** **yârâʼ** <small>*(yaw-raw')*</small>; a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ ([phrase]) archer, cast, direct, inform, instruct, lay, shew, shoot, teach(-er,-ing), through.
- properly, ***to flow as water*** (i.e. to rain)
-  transitively, ***to lay*** or ***throw*** (especially an arrow, i.e. to shoot)
-  figuratively, ***to point out*** (as if by aiming the finger), ***to teach***

**1)** to throw, shoot, cast, pour
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to throw, cast
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to cast, lay, set
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to shoot arrows
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to throw water, rain
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be shot
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to throw, cast
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to shoot
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to point out, show
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to direct, teach, instruct
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c5)** to throw water, rain
