**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎לָמַד‎</span></big>** **lâmad** <small>*(law-mad')*</small> ==**to learn: teach**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (un-) accustomed, [idiom] diligently, expert, instruct, learn, skilful, teach(-er, -ing).
- properly, ***to goad***, i.e. (by implication) ***to teach*** (the rod being an Oriental incentive)

**1)** to learn. teach, exercise in
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to learn
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to teach
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be taught, be trained
