**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎לָבַן‎</span></big>** **lâban** <small>*(law-ban')*</small> ==**to whiten**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; also as denominative from **<big>[[H3843|‎לְבֵנָה‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ make brick, be (made, make) white(-r).
- ***to be*** (or become) ***white***
- ***  to make bricks***

**1)** to be white
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to make white, become white, purify
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to show whiteness, grow white
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hithpael) to become white, be purified (ethical)
**2)** (Qal) to make bricks
