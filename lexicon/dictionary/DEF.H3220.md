**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָם‎</span></big>** **yâm** <small>*(yawm)*</small> ==**sea**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root meaning to roar</small>

$\quad$ sea ([idiom] -faring man, (-shore)), south, west (-ern, side, -ward).
- ***a sea*** (as breaking in noisy surf) or ***large body of water***
-  specifically (with the article), ***the Mediterranean Sea***
-  sometimes a ***large river***, or ***an artifical basin***
-  locally, ***the west***, or (rarely) ***the south***

**1)** sea
$\quad$ **1a)** Mediterranean Sea
$\quad$ **1b)** Red Sea
$\quad$ **1c)** Dead Sea
$\quad$ **1d)** Sea of Galilee
$\quad$ **1e)** sea (general)
$\quad$ **1f)** mighty river (Nile)
$\quad$ **1g)** the sea (the great basin in the temple court)
$\quad$ **1h)** seaward, west, westward
