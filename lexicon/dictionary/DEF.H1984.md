**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎הָלַל‎</span></big>** **hâlal** <small>*(haw-lal')*</small> ==**to shine**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (make) boast (self), celebrate, commend, (deal, make), fool(-ish, -ly), glory, give (light), be (make, feign self) mad (against), give in marriage, (sing, be worthy of) praise, rage, renowned, shine.
- ***to be clear*** (orig. of sound, but usually of color)
-  ***to shine***
-  hence, ***to make a******show***, ***to boast***
-  and thus ***to be*** (clamorously) ***foolish***
-  ***to rave***
-  causatively, ***to celebrate***
-  also ***to stultify***

**1)** to shine
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to shine (fig. of God's favour)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to flash forth light
**2)** to praise, boast, be boastful
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a1)** to be boastful
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a2)** boastful ones, boasters (participle)
$\quad$ **2b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b1)** to praise
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b2)** to boast, make a boast
$\quad$ **2c)** (Pual)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2c1)** to be praised, be made praiseworthy, be commended, be worthy of praise
$\quad$ **2d)** (Hithpael) to boast, glory, make one's boast
$\quad$ **2e)** (Poel) to make a fool of, make into a fool
$\quad$ **2f)** (Hithpoel) to act madly, act like a madman
