**<big><span class="asgreek">‎αἴρω‎</span></big>** **aírō** <small>*(ah'-ee-ro)*</small> ==**to take up**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary root</small>

$\quad$ away with, bear (up), carry, lift up, loose, make to doubt, put away, remove, take (away, up).
- to lift up
-  by implication, to take up or away
-  figuratively, to raise (the voice), keep in suspense (the mind), specially, to sail away (i.e. weigh anchor)
-  by Hebraism (compare **<big>[[H5375|‎נָשָׂא‎]]</big>**) to expiate sin
