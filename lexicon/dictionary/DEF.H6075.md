**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָפַל‎</span></big>** **ʻâphal** <small>*(aw-fal')*</small> ==**to swell**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be lifted up, presume.
- ***to swell***
-  figuratively, ***be elated***

**1)** to lift up, swell, be lifted up
$\quad$ **1a)** (Pual) to swell
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to be bold, be swelled
**2)** to presume, be heedless
$\quad$ **2a)** (Hiphil) to be heedless, show heedlessness
