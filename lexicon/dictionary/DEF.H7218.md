**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רֹאשׁ‎</span></big>** **rôʼsh** <small>*(roshe)*</small> ==**head**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root apparently meaning to shake</small>

$\quad$ band, beginning, captain, chapiter, chief(-est place, man, things), company, end, [idiom] every (man), excellent, first, forefront, (be-)head, height, (on) high(-est part, (priest)), [idiom] lead, [idiom] poor, principal, ruler, sum, top.
- ***the head*** (as most easily shaken), whether literal or figurative (in many applications, of place, time, rank, itc.)

**1)** head, top, summit, upper part, chief, total, sum, height, front, beginning
$\quad$ **1a)** head (of man, animals)
$\quad$ **1b)** top, tip (of mountain)
$\quad$ **1c)** height (of stars)
$\quad$ **1d)** chief, head (of man, city, nation, place, family, priest)
$\quad$ **1e)** head, front, beginning
$\quad$ **1f)** chief, choicest, best
$\quad$ **1g)** head, division, company, band
$\quad$ **1h)** sum
