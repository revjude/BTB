**<big><span class="asgreek">‎συλλαμβάνω‎</span></big>** **syllambánō** <small>*(sool-lam-ban'-o)*</small> ==**to seize/conceive/help**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4862|‎σύν‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2983|‎λαμβάνω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ catch, conceive, help, take.
- to clasp, i.e. seize (arrest, capture)
-  specially, to conceive (literally or figuratively)
-  by implication, to aid
