**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּזַז‎</span></big>** **bâzaz** <small>*(baw-zaz')*</small> ==**to plunder**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ catch, gather, (take) for a prey, rob(-ber), spoil, take (away, spoil), [idiom] utterly.
- ***to plunder***

**1)** to spoil, plunder, prey upon, seize
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to spoil, plunder, despoil
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be spoiled, plundered
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be taken as spoil
