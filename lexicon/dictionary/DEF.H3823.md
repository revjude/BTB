**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎לָבַב‎</span></big>** **lâbab** <small>*(law-bab')*</small> ==**to encourage**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; properly, to be enclosed (as if with fat); by implication (as denominative from **<big>[[H3824|‎לֵבָב‎]]</big>**);  to unheart;  also as denominative from **<big>[[H3834|‎לָבִיבָה‎]]</big>** i.e.</small>

$\quad$ make cakes, ravish, be wise.
- (in a good sense) ***transport*** (with love), or (in a bad sense) ***stultify***
-  ***to make cakes***

**1)** to ravish, become intelligent, get a mind
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to take heart, become enheartened, become intelligent
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to ravish the heart, encourage, make heart beat faster
**2)** (Piel) to make cakes, bake cakes, cook bread
