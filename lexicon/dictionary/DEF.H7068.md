**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קִנְאָה‎</span></big>** **qinʼâh** <small>*(kin-aw')*</small> ==**jealousy**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H7065|‎קָנָא‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ envy(-ied), jealousy, [idiom] sake, zeal.
- ***jealousy*** or ***envy***

**1)** ardour, zeal, jealousy
$\quad$ **1a)** ardour, jealousy, jealous disposition (of husband)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** sexual passion
$\quad$ **1b)** ardour of zeal (of religious zeal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** of men for God
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** of men for the house of God
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** of God for his people
$\quad$ **1c)** ardour of anger
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** of men against adversaries
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** of God against men
$\quad$ **1d)** envy (of man)
$\quad$ **1e)** jealousy (resulting in the wrath of God)
