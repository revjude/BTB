**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נֶשֶׁף‎</span></big>** **nesheph** <small>*(neh'-shef)*</small> ==**twilight**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5398|‎נָשַׁף‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ dark, dawning of the day (morning), night, twilight.
- properly, ***a breeze***, i.e. (by implication) ***dusk*** (when the evening breeze prevails)

**1)** twilight
$\quad$ **1a)** evening twilight
$\quad$ **1b)** morning twilight
