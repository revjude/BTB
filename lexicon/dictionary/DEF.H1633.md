**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּרַם‎</span></big>** **gâram** <small>*(gaw-ram')*</small> ==**to reserve**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; to be spare or skeleton-like; used only as a denominative from **<big>[[H1634|‎גֶּרֶם‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ gnaw the bones, break.
- (causative) ***to bone***, i.e. ***denude*** (by extensive, craunch) ***the bones***

**1)** to cut off, reserve, lay aside, leave, save
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to reserve
**2)** (Piel) to break bones, gnaw bones, break
