**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ὑγιαίνω‎</span></big>** **hygiaínō** <small>*(hoog-ee-ah'-ee-no)*</small> ==**be healthy**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G5199|‎ὑγιής‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ be in health, (be safe and) sound, (be) whole(-some).
- to have sound health, i.e. be well (in body)
-  figuratively, to be uncorrupt (true in doctrine)
