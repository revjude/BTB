**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֵיתָן‎</span></big>** **ʼÊythân** <small>*(ay-thawn')*</small> ==**Ethan**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>the same as **<big>[[H0386|‎אֵיתָן‎]]</big>**; permanent</small>

$\quad$ Ethan.
- § Ethan = *"enduring"*
- ***Ethan***, the name of four Israelites

**1)** an Ezrahite known for his wisdom
**2)** a grandson of Judah, father of Azariah
**3)** a Kohathite descendant of Levi
**4)** a Merarite son of Kushaiah, descendant of Levi
