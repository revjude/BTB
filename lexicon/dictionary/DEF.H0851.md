$\quad$ Eshtemoa, Eshtemoh.
- ***Eshtemoa*** or ***Eshtemoh***, a place in Palestine

Eshtemoa = "I will make myself heard" Eshtemoh = "I shall cause my own ruin: fire of astonishment: I shall soar aloft"
**1)** a Levitical city in Judah
**2)** a man of Judah
