**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁאַר‎</span></big>** **shâʼar** <small>*(shaw-ar')*</small> ==**to remain**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ leave, (be) left, let, remain, remnant, reserve, the rest.
- properly, ***to swell up***, i.e. ***be*** (causatively, make) ***redundant***

**1)** to remain, be left over, be left behind
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to remain
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be left over, be left alive, survive
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1a)** remainder, remnant (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be left behind
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to leave over, spare
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to leave or keep over
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to have left
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to leave (as a gift)
