**<big><span class="asgreek">‎συνευωχέω‎</span></big>** **syneuōchéō** <small>*(soon-yoo-o-kheh'-o)*</small> ==**to feast with**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4862|‎σύν‎]]</big>** and a derivative of a presumed compound of **<big>[[G2095|‎εὖ‎]]</big>** and a derivative of **<big>[[G2192|‎ἔχω‎]]</big>** (meaning to be in good condition, i.e. (by implication) to fare well, or feast)</small>

$\quad$ feast with.
- to entertain sumptuously in company with, i.e. (middle voice or passive) to revel together
