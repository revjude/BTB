**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎צֹחַר‎</span></big>** **Tsôchar** <small>*(tso'-khar)*</small> ==**Zohar**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from the same as **<big>[[H6713|‎צַחַר‎]]</big>**; whiteness</small>

$\quad$ Zohar. Compare **<big>[[H3328|‎יִצְחַר‎]]</big>**.
- § Zohar = *"tawny"*
- ***Tsochar***, the name of a Hittite and of an Israelite

**1)** a Judaite, son of Ashur by wife Helah
**2)** father of Ephron the Hittite
**3)** one of the sons of Simeon; also 'Zerah'
