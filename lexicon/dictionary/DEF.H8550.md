$\quad$ Thummim.
- § Thummim = *"perfection"*
- ***perfections***, i.e. (techn.) one of the epithets of the objects in the high-priest's breastplate as an emblem of complete Truth

**1)** stones provided for the means of achieving a sacred lot
$\quad$ **1a)** used with the Urim, the will of God was revealed
