**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּשַׁל‎</span></big>** **kâshal** <small>*(kaw-shal')*</small> ==**to stumble**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bereave (from the margin), cast down, be decayed, (cause to) fail, (cause, make to) fall (down, -ing), feeble, be (the) ruin(-ed, of), (be) overthrown, (cause to) stumble, [idiom] utterly, be weak.
- ***to totter*** or ***waver*** (through weakness of the legs, especially the ankle)
-  by implication, ***to falter***, ***stumble***, ***faint*** or ***fall***

**1)** to stumble, stagger, totter
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to stumble
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to totter
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to stumble
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be tottering, be feeble
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to cause to stumble, bring injury or ruin to, overthrow
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to make feeble, make weak
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to be made to stumble
$\quad$ **1e)** (Piel) bereave
