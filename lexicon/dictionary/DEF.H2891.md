**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎טָהֵר‎</span></big>** **ṭâhêr** <small>*(taw-hare')*</small> ==**be pure**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; properly, to be bright; i.e. (by implication)</small>

$\quad$ be (make, make self, pronounce) clean, cleanse (self), purge, purify(-ier, self).
- ***to be pure*** (physical sound, clear, unadulterated
-  Levitically, uncontaminated
-  morally, innocent or holy)

**1)** to be clean, be pure
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be clean (physically-of disease)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be clean ceremonially
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to purify, be clean morally, made clean
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cleanse, purify
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1a)** physically
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1b)** ceremonially
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1c)** morally
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to pronounce clean
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to perform the ceremony of cleansing
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be cleansed, be pronounced clean
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to purify oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1a)** ceremonially
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1b)** morally
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to present oneself for purification
