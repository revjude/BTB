**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎סָפַר‎</span></big>** **çâphar** <small>*(saw-far')*</small> ==**to recount**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ commune, (ac-) count; declare, number, [phrase] penknife, reckon, scribe, shew forth, speak, talk, tell (out), writer.
- properly, to ***score*** with a mark as a tally or record, i.e. (by implication) to ***inscribe***, and also to ***enumerate***
-  intensively, to ***recount***, i.e. ***celebrate***

v
**1)** to count, recount, relate
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to count (things)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to number, take account of, reckon
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be counted, be numbered
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to recount, rehearse, declare
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to recount (something), rehearse
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to talk
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to count exactly or accurately
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be recounted, be rehearsed, be related
n m
**2)** enumerator, muster-officer, secretary, scribe
$\quad$ **2a)** enumerator, muster-officer, secretary
$\quad$ **2b)** learned man, scribe
