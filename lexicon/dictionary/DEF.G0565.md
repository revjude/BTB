**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἀπέρχομαι‎</span></big>** **apérchomai** <small>*(ap-erkh'-om-ahee)*</small> ==**to go away**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G0575|‎ἀπό‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2064|‎ἔρχομαι‎]]</big>**</small>
