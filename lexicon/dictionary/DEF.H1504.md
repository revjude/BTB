**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּזַר‎</span></big>** **gâzar** <small>*(gaw-zar')*</small> ==**to cut**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cut down (off), decree, divide, snatch.
- ***to cut down*** or ***off***
-  (figuratively) ***to destroy***, ***divide***, ***exclude***, or ***decide***

**1)** to cut, divide, cut down, cut off, cut in two, snatch, decree
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to cut in two, divide
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to cut down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to cut off, destroy, exterminate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to decree
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be cut off, separated, excluded
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be destroyed,  cut off
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be decreed
