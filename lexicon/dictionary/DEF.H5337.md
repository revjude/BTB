**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָצַל‎</span></big>** **nâtsal** <small>*(naw-tsal')*</small> ==**to rescue**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] at all, defend, deliver (self), escape, [idiom] without fail, part, pluck, preserve, recover, rescue, rid, save, spoil, strip, [idiom] surely, take (out).
- ***to snatch away***, whether in a good or a bad sense

**1)** to snatch away, deliver, rescue, save, strip, plunder
$\quad$ **1a)**(Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to tear oneself away, deliver oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be torn out or away, be delivered
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to strip off, spoil
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to deliver
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to take away, snatch away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to rescue, recover
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to deliver (from enemies or troubles or death)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to deliver from sin and guilt
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to be plucked out
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to strip oneself
