$\quad$ chap(-man), sent to descry, be excellent, merchant(-man), search (out), seek, (e-) spy (out).

**1)** to seek, search out, spy out, explore
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to seek out, select, find out how to do something
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to spy out, explore
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2a)** explorers, spies (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to go about
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3a)** merchant, trader (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to make a search, make a reconnaissance
