**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎זָרַח‎</span></big>** **zârach** <small>*(zaw-rakh')*</small> ==**to rise**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ arise, rise (up), as soon as it is up.
- properly, ***to irradiate*** (or shoot forth beams), i.e. ***to rise*** (as the sun)
-  specifically, ***to appear*** (as a symptom of leprosy)

**1)** to rise, come forth, break out, arise, rise up, shine
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to rise
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to come out, appear
