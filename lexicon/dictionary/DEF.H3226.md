- § Jamin = *"right hand"*
- ***Jamin***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** the 2nd son of Simeon; founder of the family of Jaminites
**2)** a Judaite, the 2nd son of Ram the Jerahmeelite
**3)** a Levite who helped Ezra interpret the Law
