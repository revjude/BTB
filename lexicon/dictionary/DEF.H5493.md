**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎סוּר‎</span></big>** **çûwr** <small>*(soor)*</small> ==**to turn aside: remove**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">שׂוּר</span></big>** **sûwr** <small>*(soor)*</small>; (Hosea 9:12), a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be(-head), bring, call back, decline, depart, eschew, get (you), go (aside), [idiom] grievous, lay away (by), leave undone, be past, pluck away, put (away, down), rebel, remove (to and fro), revolt, [idiom] be sour, take (away, off), turn (aside, away, in), withdraw, be without.
- ***to turn off*** (literal or figurative)

**1)** to turn aside, depart
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to turn aside, turn in unto
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to depart, depart from way, avoid
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be removed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to come to an end
$\quad$ **1b)** (Polel) to turn aside
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to cause to turn aside, cause to depart, remove, take away, put away, depose
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to put aside, leave undone, retract, reject, abolish
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to be taken away, be removed
