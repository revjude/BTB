**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שַׁחַק‎</span></big>** **shachaq** <small>*(shakh'-ak)*</small> ==**cloud**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H7833|‎שָׁחַק‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ cloud, small dust, heaven, sky.
- ***a powder*** (as beaten small)
-  by analogy, ***a thin vapor***
-  by extension, ***the firmament***

**1)** dust, cloud
$\quad$ **1a)** fine dust
$\quad$ **1b)** (thin) cloud
