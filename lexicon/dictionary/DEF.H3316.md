**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יִפְתָּח‎</span></big>** **Yiphtâch** <small>*(yif-tawkh')*</small> ==**Iphtah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--L|N:N--L]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H6605|‎פָּתַח‎]]</big>**; he will open</small>

$\quad$ Jephthah, Jiphtah.
- § Jephthah or Jiphtah = *"he opens"*
- ***Jiphtach***, an Israelite
-  also a place in Palestine

n pr m
**1)** a son of Gilead and a concubine and the judge who defeated the Ammonites; after the victory because of a vow taken before the battle he sacrificed his daughter as a burnt offering
n pr loc
**2)** a city in Judah
