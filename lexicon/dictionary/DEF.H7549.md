**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָקִיעַ‎</span></big>** **râqîyaʻ** <small>*(raw-kee'-ah)*</small> ==**expanse**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H7554|‎רָקַע‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ firmament.
- properly, ***an expanse***, i.e. ***the firmament*** or (apparently) ***visible arch of the sky***

**1)** extended surface (solid), expanse, firmament
$\quad$ **1a)** expanse (flat as base, support)
$\quad$ **1b)** firmament (of vault of heaven supporting waters above)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** considered by Hebrews as solid and supporting 'waters' above
