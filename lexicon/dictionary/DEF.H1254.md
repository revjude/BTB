**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּרָא‎</span></big>** **bârâʼ** <small>*(baw-raw')*</small> ==**to create**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ choose, create (creator), cut down, dispatch, do, make (fat).
- (absolutely) ***to create***
-  (qualified) ***to cut down*** (a wood), ***select***, ***feed*** (as formative processes)

**1)** to create, shape, form
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to shape, fashion, create (always with God as subject)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** of heaven and earth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** of individual man
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** of new conditions and circumstances
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** of transformations
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be created
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** of heaven and earth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** of birth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** of something new
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** of miracles
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to cut down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to cut out
**2)** to be fat
$\quad$ **2a)** (Hiphil) to make yourselves fat
