**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָהַר‎</span></big>** **mâhar** <small>*(maw-har')*</small> ==**to hasten**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be carried headlong, fearful, (cause to make, in, make) haste(-n, -ily), (be) hasty, (fetch, make ready) [idiom] quickly, rash, [idiom] shortly, (be so) [idiom] soon, make speed, [idiom] speedily, [idiom] straightway, [idiom] suddenly, swift.
- properly, ***to be liquid*** or ***flow easily***, i.e. (by implication)
-  ***to hurry*** (in a good or a bad sense)
-  often used (with another verb) adverbially, ***promptly***

**1)**(Qal) to hasten
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to be hurried, be anxious
$\quad$ **1a)** hasty, precipitate, impetuous
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to hasten, make haste
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** hasten (used as adverb with another verb)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to hasten, prepare quickly, do quickly, bring quickly
