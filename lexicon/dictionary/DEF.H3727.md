**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כַּפֹּרֶת‎</span></big>** **kappôreth** <small>*(kap-po'-reth)*</small> ==**mercy seat**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H3722|‎כָּפַר‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ mercy seat.
- ***a lid*** (used only of the cover of the sacred Ark)

**1)** mercy-seat, place of atonement
$\quad$ **1a)** the golden plate of propitiation on which the High Priest sprinkled the seat 7 times on the Day of Atonement symbolically reconciling Jehovah and His chosen people
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** the slab of gold on top of the ark of the covenant which measured 2.5 by 1.5 cubits; on it and part of it were the two golden cherubim facing each other whose outstretched wings came together above and constituted the throne of God
