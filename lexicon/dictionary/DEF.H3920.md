**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎לָכַד‎</span></big>** **lâkad** <small>*(law-kad')*</small> ==**to capture**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] at all, catch (self), be frozen, be holden, stick together, take.
- ***to catch*** (in a net, trap or pit)
-  generally, ***to capture*** or ***occupy***
-  also ***to choose*** (by lot)
-  figuratively, ***to cohere***

**1)** to capture, take, seize
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to capture, seize
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to capture (of men) (fig.)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to take (by lot)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be captured
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be caught (of men in trap, snare) (fig.)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to grasp each other
