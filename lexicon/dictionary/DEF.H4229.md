**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָחָה‎</span></big>** **mâchâh** <small>*(maw-khaw')*</small> ==**to wipe**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ abolish, blot out, destroy, full of marrow, put out, reach unto, [idiom] utterly, wipe (away, out).
- properly, ***to stroke*** or ***rub***
-  by implication, ***to erase***
-  also ***to smooth*** (as if with oil), i.e. ***grease*** or ***make fat***
-  also ***to touch***, i.e. ***reach to***

**1)** to wipe, wipe out
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to wipe
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to blot out, obliterate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to blot out, exterminate
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be wiped out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be blotted out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be exterminated
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to blot out (from memory)
**2)** (Qal) to strike
**3)** (Pual) full of marrow (participle)
