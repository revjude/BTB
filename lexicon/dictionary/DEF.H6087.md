**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָצַב‎</span></big>** **ʻâtsab** <small>*(aw-tsab')*</small> ==**to hurt**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ displease, grieve, hurt, make, be sorry, vex, worship, wrest.
- properly, ***to carve***, i.e. ***fabricate*** or ***fashion***
-  hence (in a bad sense) ***to worry***, ***pain*** or ***anger***

**1)** to hurt, pain, grieve, displease, vex, wrest
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to hurt, pain
$\quad$ **1b)**(Niphal) to be in pain, be pained, be grieved
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to vex, torture
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to cause pain
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to feel grieved, be vexed
**2)** to shape, fashion, make, form, stretch into shape, (TWOT) worship
$\quad$ **2a)** (Piel) to shape, form
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hiphil) to form, copy, fashion
