**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אַחֲרִית‎</span></big>** **ʼachărîyth** <small>*(akh-ar-eeth')*</small> ==**end**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H0310|‎אַחַר‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ (last, latter) end (time), hinder (utter) -most, length, posterity, remnant, residue, reward.
- ***the last*** or ***end***, hence, ***the future***
-  also ***posterity***

**1)** after part, end
$\quad$ **1a)** end, issue, event
$\quad$ **1b)** latter time (prophetic for future time)
$\quad$ **1c)** posterity
$\quad$ **1d)** last, hindermost
