**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּרַת‎</span></big>** **kârath** <small>*(kaw-rath')*</small> ==**to cut: cut**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be chewed, be con-(feder-) ate, covenant, cut (down, off), destroy, fail, feller, be freed, hew (down), make a league (covenant), [idiom] lose, perish, [idiom] utterly, [idiom] want.
- ***to cut*** (off, down or asunder)
-  by implication, ***to destroy*** or ***consume***
-  specifically, ***to covenant*** (i.e. make an alliance or bargain, originally by cutting flesh and passing between the pieces)

**1)** to cut, cut off, cut down, cut off a body part, cut out, eliminate, kill, cut a covenant

$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to cut off
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** to cut off a body part, behead
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to cut down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to hew
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to cut or make a covenant
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be cut off
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be cut down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be chewed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to be cut off, fail
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual)

$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)

$\quad$ **1e)** (Hophal) cut off
