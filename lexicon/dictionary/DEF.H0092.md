**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֲגֻדָּה‎</span></big>** **ʼăguddâh** <small>*(ag-ood-daw')*</small> ==**band**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>feminine passive participle of an unused root (meaning to bind)</small>

$\quad$ bunch, burden, troop.
- ***a band***, ***bundle***, ***knot***, or ***arch***

**1)** band, binding
$\quad$ **1a)** cords, bands, thongs (metaphorical of slavery)
$\quad$ **1b)** bunch of hyssop
$\quad$ **1c)** band of men, troops
$\quad$ **1d)** vault (of the heavens), firmament (binding earth to the heavens)
