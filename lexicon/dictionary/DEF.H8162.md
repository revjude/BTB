**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שַׁעַטְנֵז‎</span></big>** **shaʻaṭnêz** <small>*(shah-at-naze')*</small> ==**mixed stuff**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>probably of foreign derivation</small>

$\quad$ garment of divers sorts, linen and wollen.
- ***linsey-woolsey***, i.e. cloth of linen and wool carded and spun together

**1)** mixed stuff, fabric of mixed weave, linsey-woolsey
$\quad$ **1a)** a kind of cloth forbidden for garments
$\quad$ **1b)** cloth made by weaving linen and wool together
