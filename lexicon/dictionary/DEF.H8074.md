**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁמֵם‎</span></big>** **shâmêm** <small>*(shaw-mame')*</small> ==**be desolate: destroyed**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ make amazed, be astonied, (be an) astonish(-ment), (be, bring into, unto, lay, lie, make) desolate(-ion, places), be destitute, destroy (self), (lay, lie, make) waste, wonder.
- ***to stun*** (or intransitively, grow numb), i.e. ***devastate*** or (figuratively) ***stupefy*** (both usually in a passive sense)

**1)** to be desolate, be appalled, stun, stupefy
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be desolated, be deflowered, be deserted, be appalled
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be appalled, be awestruck
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be desolated, be made desolate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be appalled
$\quad$ **1c)** (Polel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to be stunned
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** appalling, causing horror (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2a)** horror-causer, appaller (subst)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to devastate, ravage, make desolated
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to appal, show horror
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hophal) to lay desolate, be desolated
$\quad$ **1f)** (Hithpolel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f1)** to cause to be desolate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f2)** to be appalled, be astounded
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f3)** to cause oneself desolation, cause oneself ruin
