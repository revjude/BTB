**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כַּרְמִי‎</span></big>** **Karmîy** <small>*(kar-mee')*</small> ==**Carmi**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H3754|‎כֶּרֶם‎]]</big>**; gardener</small>

$\quad$ Carmi.
- § Carmi = *"my vineyard"*
- ***Karmi***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** the 4th son of Reuben and progenitor of the Carmites
**2)** a Judaite, father of Achan, the 'troubler of Israel' in the time of Joshua
