**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קְנַז‎</span></big>** **Qᵉnaz** <small>*(ken-az')*</small> ==**Kenaz**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>probably from an unused root meaning to hunt; hunter</small>

$\quad$ Kenaz.
- § Kenaz = *"hunter"*
- ***Kenaz***, the name of an Edomite and of two Israelites

**1)** son of Eliphaz and grandson of Esau; one of the dukes of Edom
**2)** a brother of Caleb and father of Othniel
