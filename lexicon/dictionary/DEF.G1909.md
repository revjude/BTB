**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἐπί‎</span></big>** **epí** <small>*(ep-ee')*</small> ==**upon/to/against**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:PREP|G:PREP]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary preposition</small>

$\quad$ about (the times), above, after, against, among, as long as (touching), at, beside, X have charge of, (be-, (where-))fore, in (a place, as much as, the time of, -to), (because) of, (up-)on (behalf of), over, (by, for) the space of, through(-out), (un-)to(-ward), with.
- properly, meaning superimposition (of time, place, order, etc.), as a relation of distribution (with the genitive case), i.e. over, upon, etc.
-  of rest (with the dative case) at, on, etc.
-  of direction (with the accusative case) towards, upon, etc.
