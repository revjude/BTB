**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָטָא‎</span></big>** **châṭâʼ** <small>*(khaw-taw')*</small> ==**to sin**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bear the blame, cleanse, commit (sin), by fault, harm he hath done, loss, miss, (make) offend(-er), offer for sin, purge, purify (self), make reconciliation, (cause, make) sin(-ful, -ness), trespass.
- properly, ***to miss***
-  hence (figuratively and generally) ***to sin***
-  by inference, ***to forfeit***, ***lack***, ***expiate***, ***repent***, (causatively) ***lead astray***, ***condemn***

**1)** to sin, miss, miss the way, go wrong, incur guilt, forfeit, purify from uncleanness
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to miss
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to sin, miss the goal or path of right and duty
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to incur guilt, incur penalty by sin, forfeit
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to bear loss
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to make a sin-offering
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to purify from sin
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to purify from uncleanness
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to miss the mark
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to induce to sin, cause to sin
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to bring into guilt or condemnation or punishment
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to miss oneself, lose oneself, wander from the way
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to purify oneself from uncleanness
