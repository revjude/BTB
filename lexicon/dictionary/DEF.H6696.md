**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎צוּר‎</span></big>** **tsûwr** <small>*(tsoor)*</small> ==**to confine**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ adversary, assault, beset, besiege, bind (up), cast, distress, fashion, fortify, inclose, lay siege, put up in bags.
- ***to cramp***, i.e. ***confine*** (in many applications, literally and figuratively, formative or hostile)

**1)** to bind, besiege, confine, cramp
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to confine, secure
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to shut in, beseige
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to shut up, enclose
**2)** (Qal) to show hostility to, be an adversary, treat as foe
**3)** (Qal) to form, fashion, delineate
