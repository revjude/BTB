**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָתַק‎</span></big>** **nâthaq** <small>*(naw-thak')*</small> ==**to tear**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ break (off), burst, draw (away), lift up, pluck (away, off), pull (out), root out.
- ***to tear off***

**1)** to pull or tear or draw off or away or apart, draw out, pluck up, break, lift, root out
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to draw away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to draw or pull off
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to pull or tear away
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be drawn away, be drawn out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be torn apart or in two, be snapped
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be separated
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to tear apart, snap
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to tear out, tear up, tear away
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to draw away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to drag away
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hophal) to be drawn away
