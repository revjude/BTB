**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כֹּחַ‎</span></big>** **kôach** <small>*(ko'-akh)*</small> ==**reptile**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>or (Daniel 11:6) **<big><span class="ashebrew">כּוֹחַ</span></big>** **kôwach** <small>*(ko'-akh)*</small>; from an unused root meaning to be firm</small>

$\quad$ ability, able, chameleon, force, fruits, might, power(-ful), strength, substance, wealth.
- ***vigor***, literally (force, in a good or a bad sense) or figuratively (capacity, means, produce)
-  also (from its hardiness) ***a large lizard***

**1)** strength, power, might
$\quad$ **1a)** human strength
$\quad$ **1b)** strength (of angels)
$\quad$ **1c)** power (of God)
$\quad$ **1d)** strength (of animals)
$\quad$ **1e)** strength, produce, wealth (of soil)
**2)** a small reptile, probably a kind of lizard, which is unclean
$\quad$ **2a)** perhaps an extinct animal, exact meaning is unknown
