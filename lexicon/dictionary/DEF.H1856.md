**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎דָּקַר‎</span></big>** **dâqar** <small>*(daw-kar')*</small> ==**to pierce**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ pierce, strike (thrust) through, wound.
- ***to stab***
-  by analogy, ***to starve***
-  figuratively, ***to revile***

**1)** to pierce, thrust through, pierce through
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to pierce, run through
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be pierced through
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) pierced, riddled (participle)
