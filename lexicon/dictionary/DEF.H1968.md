$\quad$ Heman.
- § Heman = *"faithful"*
- ***Heman***, the name of at least two Israelites

**1)** a wise man to whom Solomon was compared
**2)** son of Joel, grandson of Samuel, and a Levitical singer and author of PS 88
**3)** a seer
