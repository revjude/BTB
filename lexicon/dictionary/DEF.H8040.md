<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">שְׂמֹאל</span></big>** **sᵉmôʼl** <small>*(sem-ole')*</small>; a primitive word (rather perhaps from the same as **<big>[[H8071|‎שִׂמְלָה‎]]</big>** (by insertion of the aleph) through the idea of wrapping up)</small>

$\quad$ left (hand, side).
- properly, ***dark*** (as enveloped), i.e. ***the north***
-  hence (by orientation), ***the left hand***

**1)** the left, the left hand, the left side
$\quad$ **1a)** left
$\quad$ **1b)** left hand
$\quad$ **1c)** north (as one faces east)
