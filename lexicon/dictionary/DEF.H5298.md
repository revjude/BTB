**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נֶפֶג‎</span></big>** **Nepheg** <small>*(neh'-feg)*</small> ==**Nepheg**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root probably meaning to spring forth; a sprout</small>

$\quad$ Nepheg.
- § Nepheg = *"sprout"*
- ***Nepheg***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** one of sons of Izhar and grandson of Kohath of the tribe of Levi in the time of the exodus
**2)** one of the sons of David born to him at Jerusalem
