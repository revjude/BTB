**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָוָה‎</span></big>** **ʼâvâh** <small>*(aw-vaw')*</small> ==**to desire**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ covet, (greatly) desire, be desirous, long, lust (after).
- ***to wish for***

**1)** desire, incline, covet, wait longingly, wish, sigh, want, be greedy, prefer
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel) to desire, crave (food and drink)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hithpael) to desire, long for, lust after (of bodily appetites)
