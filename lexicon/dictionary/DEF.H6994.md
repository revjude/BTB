**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָטֹן‎</span></big>** **qâṭôn** <small>*(kaw-tone')*</small> ==**be small**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (rather denominative from **<big>[[H6996|‎קָטָן‎]]</big>**)</small>

$\quad$ be a (make) small (thing), be not worthy.
- ***to diminish***, i.e. ***be*** (causatively, make) ***diminutive*** or (figuratively) ***of no account***

**1)** to be small, be insignificant
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be insignificant
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to make small, reduce significantly

<small>See Greek: </small>**<big>[[G2427|ἱκανόω]]</big>**
