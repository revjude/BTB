**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רוּד‎</span></big>** **rûwd** <small>*(rood)*</small> ==**to roam**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ have the dominion, be lord, mourn, rule.
- ***to tramp about***, i.e. ***ramble*** (free or disconsolate)

**1)** to wander restlessly, roam
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to roam
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to be restless, show restlessness
