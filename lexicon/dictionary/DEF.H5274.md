**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָעַל‎</span></big>** **nâʻal** <small>*(naw-al')*</small> ==**to lock**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; also denominative from **<big>[[H5275|‎נַעַל‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ bolt, inclose, lock, shoe, shut up.
- properly, ***to fasten up***, i.e. ***with a******bar*** or ***cord***
-  hence, ***to sandal***, i.e. ***furnish with slippers***

**1)** to bar, lock, bolt
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to bar, lock, bolt
**2)** to furnish with sandals, shoe
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to shoe
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hiphil) to give sandals
