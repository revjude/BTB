<small>from the active participle (of the same form as the first word) feminine of an unused root (meaning to entrap) and plural of **<big>[[H6643|‎צְבִי‎]]</big>**; trap of gazelles</small>

$\quad$ Pochereth of Zebaim.
- § Pochereth of Zebaim = *"here the cutting off"*
- ***Pokereth-Tsebajim***, one of the 'servants of Solomon'

**1)** a servant of Solomon whose descendants returned from exile with Zerubbabel
