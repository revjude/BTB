**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עַדְנָה‎</span></big>** **ʻAdnâh** <small>*(ad-naw')*</small> ==**Adnah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5727|‎עָדַן‎]]</big>**; pleasure</small>

$\quad$ Adnah.
- § Adnah = *"pleasure"*
- ***Adnah***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** a Manassite who deserted from Saul and joined David at Ziklag
**2)** a captain in charge of over 300,000 men of Judah in the time of Jehoshaphat
