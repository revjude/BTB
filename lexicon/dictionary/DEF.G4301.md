**<big><span class="asgreek">‎προλαμβάνω‎</span></big>** **prolambánō** <small>*(prol-am-ban'-o)*</small> ==**to take beforehand**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4253|‎πρό‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2983|‎λαμβάνω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ come aforehand, overtake, take before.
- to take in advance, i.e. (literally) eat before others have an opportunity
-  (figuratively) to anticipate, surprise
