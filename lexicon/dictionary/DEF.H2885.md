**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎טַבַּעַת‎</span></big>** **ṭabbaʻath** <small>*(tab-bah'-ath)*</small> ==**ring**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H2883|‎טָבַע‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ ring.
- properly, ***a seal*** (as sunk into the wax), i.e. ***signet*** (for sealing)
-  hence (generally) ***a ring of any kind***

**1)** ring, signet, signet ring
$\quad$ **1a)** signet ring (as symbol of authority)
$\quad$ **1b)** ring (as ornament)
