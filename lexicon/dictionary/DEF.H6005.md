**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עִמָּנוּאֵל‎</span></big>** **ʻImmânûwʼêl** <small>*(im-maw-noo-ale')*</small> ==**Immanuel**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5973|‎עִם‎]]</big>** and with a pronominal suffix inserted; with us (is) God</small>

$\quad$ Immanuel.
- § Immanuel = *"God with us" or "with us is God"*
- ***Immanuel***, a type name of Isaiah's son

**1)** symbolic and prophetic name of the Messiah, the Christ, prophesying that He would be born of a virgin and would be 'God with us'
