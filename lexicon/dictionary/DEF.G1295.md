**<big><span class="asgreek">‎διασώζω‎</span></big>** **diasṓzō** <small>*(dee-as-odze'-o)*</small> ==**to save**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G1223|‎διά‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G4982|‎σώζω‎]]</big>**</small>

- to save thoroughly, i.e. (by implication or analogy) to cure, preserve, rescue, etc.
