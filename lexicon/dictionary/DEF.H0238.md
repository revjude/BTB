**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָזַן‎</span></big>** **ʼâzan** <small>*(aw-zan')*</small> ==**to listen**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; probably to expand; but used only as a denominative from **<big>[[H0241|‎אֹזֶן‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ give (perceive by the) ear, hear(-ken). See **<big>[[H0239|‎אָזַן‎]]</big>**.
- ***to broaden out the ear*** (with the hand), i.e. (by implication) ***to listen***

**1)** to hear, listen
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to hear, listen, give ear
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be obedient, harken
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to hear or listen to prayers (of God)
