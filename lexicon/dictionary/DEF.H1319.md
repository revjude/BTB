**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּשַׂר‎</span></big>** **bâsar** <small>*(baw-sar')*</small> ==**to bear tidings**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ messenger, preach, publish, shew forth, (bear, bring, carry, preach, good, tell good) tidings.
- properly, ***to be fresh***, i.e. ***full*** (rosy, (figuratively) ***cheerful)***
-  ***to announce*** (glad news)

**1)** to bear news, bear tidings, publish, preach, show forth
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to gladden with good news
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to bear news
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to announce (salvation) as good news, preach
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hithpael) to receive good news
