**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יֶרֶד‎</span></big>** **Yered** <small>*(yeh'-red)*</small> ==**Jared**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H3381|‎יָרַד‎]]</big>**; a descent</small>

$\quad$ Jared.
- § Jered or Jared = *"descent"*
- ***Jered***, the name of an antediluvian, and of an Israelite

**1)** son of Mahalaleel and father of Enoch in the 4th generation from Seth
**2)** a descendant of Judah and father of Gedor
