**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מַהֲלַלְאֵל‎</span></big>** **Mahălalʼêl** <small>*(mah-hal-al-ale')*</small> ==**Mahalalel**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H4110|‎מַהֲלָל‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H0410|‎אֵל‎]]</big>**; praise of God</small>

$\quad$ Mahalaleel.
- § Mahalaleel = *"praise of God"*
- ***Mahalalel***, the name of an antediluvian patriarch and of an Israelite

**1)** son of Cainan and the 4th in descent from Adam in the line of Seth
**2)** a descendant of Perez, the son of Judah
