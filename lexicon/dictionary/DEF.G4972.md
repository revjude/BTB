**<big><span class="asgreek">‎σφραγίζω‎</span></big>** **sphragízō** <small>*(sfrag-id'-zo)*</small> ==**to seal**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4973|‎σφραγίς‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ (set a, set to) seal up, stop.
- to stamp (with a signet or private mark) for security or preservation (literally or figuratively)
-  by implication, to keep secret, to attest
