<span class="ashebrew">‎תְּקוֹעַ‎</span> <small>***(te.qo.a)***</small> ==**Tekoa**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--L|N:N--L]]</small></sup> 
- Tekoa or Tekoah = "a stockade"
-   town founded by Ashur son of Hezron
- in the hill country of Judah near Hebron
- rebuilt by king Rehoboam of Judah; birthplace of Amos
- Also named: <i>te.qo.i</i> (<span class='ashebrew'>‎ת‎</span>ְּ<span class='ashebrew'>‎קו‎</span>ֹ<span class='ashebrew'>‎ע‎</span>ִ<span class='ashebrew'>‎י‎</span> "Tekoa" [[H8621]])
