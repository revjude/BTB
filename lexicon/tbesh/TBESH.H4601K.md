<span class="ashebrew">‎מַעֲכָה‎</span> <small>***(ma.a.khah)***</small> ==**Maacah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-F-P|N:N-F-P]]</small></sup> 
- A woman of the tribe of Judah living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Kgs.15.2|1Ki.15.2]]
-  daughter of: Uriel ([[H0222I]]) and Tamar ([[H8559I]]); married to Rehoboam ([[H7346]])
-  mother of: Abijah ([[H0029I]]), Attai ([[H6262I]]), Ziza ([[H2124H]]) and Shelomith ([[H8019G]]); also called Micaiah (KJV: Michaiah) at [[2Chr.13.2|2Ch.13.2]]
- daughter of Absalom, wife of king Rehoboam of Judah, and mother of king Abijam of Judah
