-  mother of: Abijah (**<big>[[H0029I|אֲבִיָּה]]</big>**), Attai (**<big>[[H6262I|עַתַּי]]</big>**), Ziza (**<big>[[H2124H|זִיזָא]]</big>**) and Shelomith (**<big>[[H8019G|שְׁלֹמִית]]</big>**); also called Micaiah (KJV: Michaiah) at [[2Chr.13.2|2Ch.13.2]]
