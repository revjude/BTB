- cubit-a measure of distance (the forearm), roughly 18 in (. 5m). There are several cubits used in the OT, the cubit of a man or common cubit ([[Deut.3.11|Deu.3.11]] ), the legal cubit or cubit of the sanctuary ([[Ezek.40.5|Ezk.40.5]] ) plus others. See a Bible Dictionary for a complete treatment.
