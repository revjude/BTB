<span class="ashebrew">‎אֲחִיתֹ֫פֶל‎</span> <small>***(a.chi.to.phel)***</small> ==**Ahithophel**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at [[2Sam.15.12|2Sa.15.12]]
-  father of: Eliam ([[H0463H]])
-  a counsellor of David, grandfather of Bathsheba (cf [[2Sam.11.3|2Sa.11.3]], [[2Sam.11.23| 23:34]] ), who joined Absalom in revolt against David, and committed suicide when Absalom did not heed his counsel
