<small>*Male • Son of Shomer • A descendant of Asher.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jehubbah</span> was a descendant of Asher mentioned in the genealogy recorded in 1 Chronicles.
- A man from the tribe of Asher living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.34|1Ch.7.34]]; 
- only referred to as Jehubbah **<big>[[H3160|יְחֻבָּה]]</big>**; 
-  son of Shomer **<big>[[H7763|שׁוֹמֵר]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Ahi **<big>[[H0277|אֲחִי]]</big>**, Rohgah **<big>[[H7303|רוֹהֲגָה]]</big>** and Aram **<big>[[H0758|אֲרָם]]</big>**.

Jehubbah is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Asher in 1 Chronicles. He was the son of Shomer \(or 'Shamer'\), who was a descendant of Beriah, one of the sons of Asher.
