Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Reaiah</span> was a Reubenite, the son of Micah, mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Reuben.
- A man from the tribe of Reuben  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.5.5|1Ch.5.5]]; 
- only referred to as Reaiah **<big>[[H7211|רְאָיָה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Micah **<big>[[H4318|מִיכָה]]</big>**; 
-  father of Baal **<big>[[H1168|בַּעַל]]</big>**.

Reaiah is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Reuben \([[1Chr.5.5|1 Chronicles 5:5]]\). He was the son of Micah and a member of the Reubenite clan. The genealogy in 1 Chronicles 5 traces the descendants of Reuben, the firstborn son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\), and provides a record of the families and clans within the tribe. No further details are given about Reaiah's life or role within the tribe. His mention in the genealogy serves to document the lineage of the Reubenites.
