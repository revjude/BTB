<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Amnon</span> was a son of Shimon and a descendant of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah\(?\) living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.20|1Ch.4.20]]; 
- only referred to as Amnon **<big>[[H0550|אַמְנוֹן]]</big>**; 
-  son of Shimon **<big>[[H7889|שִׁימוֹן]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Rinnah **<big>[[H7441|רִנָּה]]</big>**, Ben-hanan **<big>[[H1135|בֶּן־חָנָן]]</big>** , Tilon **<big>[[H8436|תּוּלוֹן]]</big>**.

Amnon was a descendant of Judah through his son Shimon. He is mentioned in the genealogical record of the tribe of Judah in 1 Chronicles 4.
