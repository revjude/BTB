Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Adrammelech</span>, son of Assyrian king Sennacherib, assassinated his father. \([[2Kgs.19.37|2Ki.19.37]]; [[Isa.37.38|Isa.37.38]]\)
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[2Kgs.19.37|2Ki.19.37]]; 
- only referred to as Adrammelech **<big>[[H0152|אֲדְרַמֶּלֶךְ]]</big>**; 
-  son of Sennacherib **<big>[[H5576|סַנְחֵרִיב]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Sharezer **<big>[[H8272|שַׁרְאֶצֶר]]</big>** and Esarhaddon **<big>[[H0634|אֵסַר־חַדּוֹן]]</big>**.

Adrammelech was one of the sons of Sennacherib, the king of Assyria. After Sennacherib's unsuccessful campaign against Jerusalem, he returned to Nineveh. While worshipping in the temple of his god Nisroch, Adrammelech and his brother Sharezer assassinated their father with the sword. They then escaped to the land of Ararat. Sennacherib's son Esarhaddon succeeded him as king. \([[2Kgs.19.37|2Ki.19.37]]; [[Isa.37.38|Isa.37.38]]\).
