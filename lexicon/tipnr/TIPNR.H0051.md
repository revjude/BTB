Abishur was a descendant of Judah, the son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\) and Leah. He is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah in 1 Chronicles 2. Abishur was the son of Shammai, who was the son of Onam, a descendant of Jerahmeel, one of the sons of Hezron \([[1Chr.2.28|1 Chronicles 2:28]]\). 

Abishur married a woman named Abihail, and together they had two sons, Ahban and Molid \([[1Chr.2.29|1 Chronicles 2:29]]\). No further details are provided about Abishur's life or his role within the tribe of Judah. His mention in the genealogy serves to trace the lineage of the tribe and demonstrate the fulfillment of God's promise to Abraham that his descendants would be numerous.
