<small>*Karka • A place on the southern border of Judah*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Karka</span>, a location along the southern border of the tribe of Judah \([[Josh.15.3|Joshua 15:3]]\).
- A location near Azmon<[[H6111]]> only mentioned at [[Josh.15.3|Jos.15.3]]; 
- only referred to as Karka<[[H7173]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ק</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ק</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>\).

Karka is mentioned in [[Josh.15.3|Joshua 15:3]] as one of the places along the southern border of the territory allocated to the tribe of Judah. It is listed between Kadesh-barnea and Hezron, suggesting that it was located in the Negev region, south of Canaan. Its location remains unknown.
