<small>*Male • Son of Naphtali • Son of Naphtali, grandson of Jacob.*</small>

Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Guni</span> was a son of Naphtali and a grandson of Jacob, as listed in the genealogies of Genesis and Numbers.
- A man from the tribe of Naphtali living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, first mentioned at [[Gen.46.24|Gen.46.24]]; 
- referred to as Guni<[[H1476G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ג</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\), or Gunite<[[H1477]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ג</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
-  son of Naphtali<[[H5321G]]>; 
- a brother of Jahzeel<[[H3183]]>, Jezer<[[H3337]]> and Shillem<[[H8006]]>.

Guni is mentioned in [[Gen.46.24|Genesis 46:24]] and [[Num.26.48|Numbers 26:48]] as one of the sons of Naphtali, who was one of the twelve sons of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\). In the genealogy of Jacob's descendants who went to Egypt during the famine, Guni is listed among the sons of Naphtali. The descendants of Guni are referred to as the Gunites in [[Num.26.48|Numbers 26:48]], where they are counted as part of the tribe of Naphtali in the census taken in the wilderness. In [[1Chr.7.13|1 Chronicles 7:13]], Guni is again mentioned in the genealogy of Naphtali's descendants.
