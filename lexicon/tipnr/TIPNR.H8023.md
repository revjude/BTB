The <span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shiloni</span>tes or Shiloni were descendants of Judah living in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile.
- People living at Shiloh or descended from Shelah **<big>[[H7956|שֵׁלָה]]</big>**, only mentioned at [[Neh.11.5|Neh.11.5]]; 
- only referred to as Shilonites **<big>[[H8023|שִׁלֹנִי]]</big>**;
-  progenitors of Zechariah **<big>[[H2148|זְכַרְיָה]]</big>**.

The Shilonites or Shiloni were a group of People from Judah who lived in Jerusalem following the return from Babylonian captivity \(Neh. 11:5\). They are mentioned in the context of a list of leaders and people who resettled in Jerusalem.
