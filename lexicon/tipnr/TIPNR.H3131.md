Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Josiphiah</span> was the father of Shelomith, who was among the exiles who returned with Ezra to Jerusalem.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Ezra.8.10|Ezr.8.10]]; 
- only referred to as Josiphiah **<big>[[H3131|יוֹסִפְיָה]]</big>**; 
-  father of Shelomith **<big>[[H8019|שְׁלֹמִית]]</big>**.

Josiphiah is mentioned only in [[Ezra.8.10|Ezra 8:10]]. He is the father of a man whose descendants returned from the Babylonian exile. His son \(referred to as Ben-Josiphiah, meaning "son of Josiphiah"\) led 160 males who accompanied Ezra on the return journey to Jerusalem. This return occurred during the reign of Artaxerxes, king of Persia. The name appears in a list of family heads who participated in this important return to Jerusalem.
