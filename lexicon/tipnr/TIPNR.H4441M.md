Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Malchijah</span>, son of Harim, repaired a section of Jerusalem's wall \([[Neh.3.11|Neh.3.11]]\).
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.11|Neh.3.11]]; 
- only referred to as Malchijah **<big>[[H4441|מַלְכִּיָּה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Harim **<big>[[H2766|חָרִם]]</big>**.

Malchijah, the son of Harim, was one of the people who helped repair the wall of Jerusalem during the time of Nehemiah. He worked on rebuilding a section of the wall, alongside Hashub, the son of Pahath-Moab \([[Neh.3.11|Nehemiah 3:11]]\).
