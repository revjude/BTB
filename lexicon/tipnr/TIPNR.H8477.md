Man living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tahash</span> was a son of Abraham's brother Nahor and his concubine Reumah.
- A man living at the time of the Patriarchs, only mentioned at [[Gen.22.24|Gen.22.24]];

-  son of Nahor **<big>[[H5152|נָחוֹר]]</big>** and Reumah **<big>[[H7208|רְאוּמָה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Tebah **<big>[[H2875|טֶבַח]]</big>**, Gaham **<big>[[H1514|גַּחַם]]</big>** and Maacah **<big>[[H4601|מַעֲכָה]]</big>**; 
-  half-brother of: Uz **<big>[[H5780|עוּץ]]</big>**, Buz **<big>[[H0938|בּוּז]]</big>**, Kemuel **<big>[[H7055|קְמוּאֵל]]</big>**, Chesed **<big>[[H3777|כֶּשֶׂד]]</big>**, Hazo **<big>[[H2375|חֲזוֹ]]</big>**, Pildash **<big>[[H6394|פִּלְדָּשׁ]]</big>**, Jidlaph **<big>[[H3044|יִדְלָף]]</big>**, Bethuel **<big>[[H1328|בְּתוּאֵל]]</big>**.

Tahash is mentioned in [[Gen.22.24|Genesis 22:24]] as a son of Nahor and his concubine Reumah. Nahor was Abraham's brother, making Tahash Abraham's nephew. Tahash is listed along with his brothers Tebah, Gaham, and Maacah. No other details are provided about Tahash's life or descendants. As a son of Nahor, he was born in Mesopotamia, where Abraham and his family originated.
