Man living before Israel's Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Pasach</span>, a son of Japhlet, who was a descendant of Asher.
- A man from the tribe of Asher living before Israel's Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.33|1Ch.7.33]]; 
- only referred to as Pasach<[[H6457]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ס</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ך</span>ְ\); 
-  son of Japhlet<[[H3310]]>; 
- a brother of Bimhal<[[H1118]]> and Ashvath<[[H6220]]>.

Pasach was a son of Japhlet according to [[1Chr.7.33|1 Chronicles 7:33]]. Japhlet was a descendant of Asher, one of the twelve tribes of Israel. Pasach was listed among Japhlet's sons, who also included Bimhal and Ashvath.
