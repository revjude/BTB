<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ethan</span> the Ezrahite was a wise man and the author of Psalm 89.
- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Kgs.4.31|1Ki.4.31]]; 
- only referred to as Ethan<[[H0387G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ית</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\); 
-  son of Mahol<[[H4235]]>; 
- a brother of Heman<[[H1968G]]>, Calcol<[[H3633G]]> and Darda<[[H1862]]>.

Ethan the Ezrahite was a wise man during the time of King Solomon. He was known for his wisdom, although not as wise as Solomon himself \([[1Kgs.4.31|1 Kings 4:31]]\). Ethan was also a skilled musician and is credited with authoring Psalm 89, a psalm that reflects on God's covenant with David and the challenges faced by the Davidic dynasty \(Psalm 89:1\). The psalm demonstrates Ethan's deep understanding of God's faithfulness and sovereignty.
