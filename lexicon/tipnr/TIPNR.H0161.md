Ohad was one of the sons of Simeon, the second son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\) \([[Gen.46.10|Genesis 46:10]]; [[Exod.6.15|Exodus 6:15]]\). He is mentioned in the genealogies of Simeon, which were recorded when the Israelites were in Egypt and during the Exodus. No further details about Ohad's life or descendants are provided in the Bible. His name is listed among his brothers as part of the family record, highlighting the importance of genealogies in the Israelite tradition.
