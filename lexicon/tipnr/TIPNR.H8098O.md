Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shemaiah</span>, a Levite summoned by Ezra to join the return to Jerusalem.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Ezra.8.16|Ezr.8.16]]; 
- only referred to as Shemaiah **<big>[[H8098|שְׁמַעְיָה]]</big>**.

Shemaiah was a Levite who was invited by Ezra to join the group of exiles returning to Jerusalem from Babylon. According to [[Ezra.8.16|Ezra 8:16]], Ezra sent for leaders and men of understanding, including Shemaiah, to assist in the journey and the re-establishment of the Jewish community in Jerusalem. Shemaiah was among the Levites, along with Eliezer, Arib, Elnathan, Nathan, Zechariah, and Meshullam, who were specifically summoned by Ezra for this purpose. The inclusion of Levites like Shemaiah was crucial for ensuring the proper functioning of the temple and the spiritual life of the community upon their return to Jerusalem.
