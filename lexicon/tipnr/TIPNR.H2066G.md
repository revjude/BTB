<small>*Male • Son of Nathan • Descendant of Judah through Jerahmeel*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zabad</span> was a descendant of Judah through the line of Jerahmeel.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.2.36|1Ch.2.36]]; 
- only referred to as Zabad<[[H2066G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ד</span>\); 
- a son of Nathan<[[H5416J]]>; 
-  father of Ephlal<[[H0654]]>.

Zabad is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah, specifically in the line of Jerahmeel. He was the son of Nathan and the father of Ephlal. Zabad is listed as part of the descendants of Sheshan, who had no sons and gave his daughter in marriage to his Egyptian servant Jarha. This genealogy shows the intermarriage between Israelites and non-Israelites.
