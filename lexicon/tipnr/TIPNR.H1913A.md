Man living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hadoram</span> was a son of Joktan and a descendant of Shem, mentioned in the genealogies of Genesis and 1 Chronicles.

Hadoram is mentioned in the genealogies found in [[Gen.10.27|Genesis 10:27]] and [[1Chr.1.21|1 Chronicles 1:21]] as one of the sons of Joktan, a descendant of Shem, the son of Noah. Joktan's descendants are listed as part of the Table of Nations, which records the spread of Noah's descendants after the flood. The descendants of Joktan are believed to have settled in the southern regions of the Arabian Peninsula. No further details are provided about Hadoram's life or his specific role in the family line. His mention in the genealogies serves to trace the lineage of Shem and to demonstrate the fulfillment of God's promise to Noah that his descendants would spread throughout the earth after the flood.
