<small>*Male • Son of Ashhur • A descendant of Judah through Hezron.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Haahashtari</span> was a descendant of Judah through Hezron, as listed in the genealogy in 1 Chronicles.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.6|1Ch.4.6]]; 
- only referred to as Haahashtari **<big>[[H0326|אֲחַשְׁתָּרִי]]</big>**; 
-  son of Ashhur **<big>[[H0806|אַשְׁחוּר]]</big>** and Naarah **<big>[[H5292|נַעֲרָה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Ahuzzam **<big>[[H0275|אֲחֻזָּם]]</big>**, Hepher **<big>[[H2660|חֵפֶר]]</big>** and Temeni **<big>[[H8488|תֵּימְנִי]]</big>**; 
-  half-brother of: Zereth **<big>[[H6889|צֶרֶת]]</big>**, Izhar **<big>[[H3328|יִצְחַר]]</big>**, Ethnan **<big>[[H0869|אֶתְנַן]]</big>** and Koz **<big>[[H6976|קוֹץ]]</big>**.

Haahashtari is mentioned briefly in [[1Chr.4.6|1 Chronicles 4:6]] as one of the sons of Naarah, who was one of the wives of Ashhur, a descendant of Judah through Hezron. The name Haahashtari appears only once in the Bible, and no other information is provided about him or his descendants.
