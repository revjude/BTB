Tahan is mentioned in [[1Chr.7.25|1 Chronicles 7:25]] as a descendant of Ephraim. He was the son of Telah and the father of Ladan. This genealogy traces the descendants of Ephraim, showing the generations between Ephraim and Joshua. No other details are provided about Tahan's life or role in the tribe. He lived during the time the Israelites were in Egypt.
