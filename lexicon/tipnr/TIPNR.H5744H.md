Obed was a descendant of Judah through the line of Jerahmeel \([[1Chr.2.37|1 Chronicles 2:37]]-38\). He was the son of Ephlal and the father of Jehu. Obed is mentioned in the genealogy of Jerahmeel, which is part of the larger genealogy of the tribe of Judah.
