Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Azariah</span> son of Jehallel was a Merarite Levite who helped purify the temple under King Hezekiah.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[2Chr.29.12a|2Ch.29.12a]]; 
- only referred to as Azariah<[[H5838U]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\); 
-  father of Joel<[[H3100Q]]>.

Azariah son of Jehallel was a Levite of the Merari clan during the reign of King Hezekiah of Judah. When Hezekiah ordered the cleansing and rededication of the temple, Azariah was one of the Levites who helped carry out the purification work \(2Ch.29.12\). No other details are given about his life or responsibilities.
