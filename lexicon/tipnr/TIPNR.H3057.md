Jehudijah, also referred to as the Judahite, is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Judah in 1 Chronicles. She was the wife of Mered, a descendant of Judah. Jehudijah gave birth to three sons: Jered, who was the father of Gedor; Heber, the father of Soco; and Jekuthiel, the father of Zanoah.
