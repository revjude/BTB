<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jehudijah</span> \(or the Judahite\) was the wife of Mered and the mother of Jered, Heber, and Jekuthiel, as recorded in the genealogy of Judah.
- A woman from the tribe of Judah, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.18|1Ch.4.18]]; 
- only referred to as Jehudijah **<big>[[H3057|יְהֻדִיָּה]]</big>**; 
- wife of Mered **<big>[[H4778|מֶרֶד]]</big>**; 
-  mother of Jered **<big>[[H3382|יֶרֶד]]</big>**, Heber **<big>[[H2268|חֶבֶר]]</big>** and Jekuthiel **<big>[[H3354|יְקוּתִיאֵל]]</big>**.

Jehudijah, also referred to as the Judahite, is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Judah in 1 Chronicles. She was the wife of Mered, a descendant of Judah. Jehudijah gave birth to three sons: Jered, who was the father of Gedor; Heber, the father of Soco; and Jekuthiel, the father of Zanoah.
