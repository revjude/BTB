<small>*Male • Son of Zichri • Commander who volunteered for God's service*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Amasiah</span>, the son of Zichri, was a commander who willingly offered himself for the service of the Lord during the reign of King Jehoshaphat.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[2Chr.17.16|2Ch.17.16]]; 
- only referred to as Amasiah **<big>[[H6007|עֲמַסְיָה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Zichri **<big>[[H2147|זִכְרִי]]</big>**.

Amasiah was the son of Zichri and served as a commander during the reign of King Jehoshaphat of Judah. He is described as a man who willingly offered himself for the service of the Lord. This suggests that Amasiah was a devoted follower of God and was committed to serving Him faithfully. As a commander, Amasiah likely played a significant role in Jehoshaphat's army and in the defense of the kingdom of Judah.
