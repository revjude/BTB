Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Raham</span> was the son of Shema and the father or founder of Jorkeam, from the tribe of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.2.44|1Ch.2.44]]; 
- only referred to as Raham **<big>[[H7357|רַחַם]]</big>**; 
- a son of Shema **<big>[[H8087|שֶׁמַע]]</big>**; 
-  father or founder of Jorkeam **<big>[[H3421|יׇרְקְעָם]]</big>**.

Raham is mentioned briefly in the genealogy of the tribe of Judah \([[1Chr.2.44|1 Chronicles 2:44]]\). He was the son of Shema and the father or founder of Jorkeam. No additional information is provided about Raham in the Bible. His mention in the genealogy serves to trace the lineage of certain families within the tribe of Judah.
