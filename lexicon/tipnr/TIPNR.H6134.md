<small>*Male • Son of Ram • Son of Ram, descendant of Judah*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eker</span> was a son of Ram and descendant of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.2.27|1Ch.2.27]]; 
- only referred to as Eker **<big>[[H6134|עֵקֶר]]</big>**; 
-  son of Ram **<big>[[H7410|רָם]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Maaz **<big>[[H4619|מַעַץ]]</big>** and Jamin **<big>[[H3226|יָמִין]]</big>**.

Eker is mentioned in [[1Chr.2.27|1 Chronicles 2:27]] as a son of Ram, who was a descendant of Judah through Hezron.
