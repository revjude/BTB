Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shimei</span> was the son of Pedaiah and nephew of King Jeconiah, mentioned in the genealogy of David's descendants.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.3.19|1Ch.3.19]]; 
- only referred to as Shimei<[[H8096J]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ִׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
-  son of Pedaiah<[[H6305H]]>; 
- a brother of Zerubbabel<[[H2216]]>.

Shimei is mentioned in [[1Chr.3.19|1 Chronicles 3:19]] as a son of Pedaiah and a brother of Zerubbabel. He is listed in the genealogy of King David's descendants, particularly in the line of King Jeconiah \(also known as Jehoiachin\). This genealogy traces the royal lineage after the Babylonian exile, suggesting that Shimei was born during or after the exile.
