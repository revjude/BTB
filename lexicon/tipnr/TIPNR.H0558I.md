<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Amaziah</span>, the son of Hilkiah, was a Levite of the family of Merari during the time of David.
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.6.45|1Ch.6.45]]; 
- only referred to as Amaziah<[[H0558I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>צ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\); 
- a son of Hilkiah<[[H2518I]]>; 
-  father of Hashabiah<[[H2811G]]>.

Amaziah was a Levite who belonged to the family of Merari. He was the son of Hilkiah and lived during the reign of King David. As a Levite, Amaziah would have been involved in the religious duties and services in the tabernacle or temple. The Merarites were responsible for the tabernacle's structural components, such as its frames, crossbars, posts, and bases.
