- A location near Abdon<[[H5658G]]> first mentioned at [[Josh.19.26|Jos.19.26]]; 
- referred to as Mishal<[[H4861]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>\), or Mashal<[[H4913]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ָׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>\).

Mishal, also known as Mashal, was a city allotted to the tribe of Asher \([[Josh.19.26|Joshua 19:26]]\). It was later designated as a Levitical city, given to the Gershonites \([[Josh.21.30|Joshua 21:30]]; [[1Chr.6.74|1 Chronicles 6:74]]\). The Levites, who were not allotted a specific territory, were given cities throughout Israel to dwell in, along with pasture lands for their livestock \(Numbers 35:1-8\). No further details about the location are provided in the Bible.
