Man living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tarea</span>, also called Tahrea, was a son of Micah and grandson of Mephibosheth.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.8.35|1Ch.8.35]]; 
- referred to as Tarea **<big>[[H8390|תַּאֲרֵעַ]]</big>**, or Tahrea **<big>[[H8475|תַּחְרֵעַ]]</big>**; 
-  son of Micah **<big>[[H4318|מִיכָה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Pithon **<big>[[H6377|פִּיתוֹן]]</big>**, Melech **<big>[[H4429|מֶלֶךְ]]</big>** and Ahaz **<big>[[H0271|אָחָז]]</big>**.

Tarea, also known as Tahrea, is mentioned in [[1Chr.8.35|1 Chronicles 8:35]] and 9:41 as a son of Micah and grandson of Mephibosheth. Mephibosheth was a son of Jonathan and grandson of King Saul. These passages list the descendants of Saul, tracing the genealogy of his son Jonathan.
