Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hariph</span> was one of the individuals who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah. \([[Neh.10.19|Neh.10.19]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.10.19|Neh.10.19]]; 
- only referred to as Hariph **<big>[[H2756|חָרִיף]]</big>**.

Hariph is mentioned in the book of Nehemiah as one of the individuals who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah. This covenant was a solemn agreement made by the people of Israel to follow God's laws and commandments. The covenant was sealed by a group of leaders, priests, Levites, and others, including Hariph. No other information is provided about Hariph in the Bible. \([[Neh.10.19|Neh.10.19]]\).
