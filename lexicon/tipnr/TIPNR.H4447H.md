Husband of Hammolecheth The husband of Hammolecheth is not named in the biblical text. He is mentioned indirectly in [[1Chr.7.18|1 Chronicles 7:18]] as the father of Ishhod, Abiezer, and Mahlah. Hammolecheth, whose name means "the queen," was the sister of Gilead, a descendant of Manasseh. The genealogy in 1 Chronicles 7 lists the descendants of Manasseh, including Hammolecheth and her children.
