Jeshua was a town in Judah that was inhabited by the people of Judah after their return from the Babylonian exile \([[Neh.11.26|Nehemiah 11:26]]\). It is mentioned in the context of the resettlement of Jerusalem and the surrounding towns during the time of Nehemiah. The town is listed among other settlements in the southern part of Judah, suggesting that it was located in that region. Its location is unknown.
