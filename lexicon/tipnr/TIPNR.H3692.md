Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Chislon</span> was the father of Elidad, a leader from the tribe of Benjamin. \([[Num.34.21|Num.34.21]]\)
- A man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[Num.34.21|Num.34.21]]; 
- only referred to as Chislon<[[H3692]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ִּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ס</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>לו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\); 
-  father of Elidad<[[H0449]]>.

Chislon is mentioned in [[Num.34.21|Numbers 34:21]] as the father of Elidad, who was appointed as a leader from the tribe of Benjamin. Elidad was one of the representatives chosen to help divide the land of Canaan among the Israelite tribes after the conquest. Each tribe had a designated leader for this task, and Elidad was selected from Benjamin.
