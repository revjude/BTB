Arabian living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Massa</span>, a son of Ishmael, was a grandson of Abraham \([[Gen.25.14|Gen.25.14]]; [[1Chr.1.30|1Ch.1.30]]\).
- A man of the Arabs living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.25.14|Gen.25.14]]; 
- only referred to as Massa **<big>[[H4854|מַשָּׂא]]</big>**; 
-  son of Ishmael **<big>[[H3458|יִשְׁמָעֵאל]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Nebaioth **<big>[[H5032|נְבָיוֹת]]</big>**, Kedar **<big>[[H6938|קֵדָר]]</big>**, Adbeel **<big>[[H0110|אַדְבְּאֵל]]</big>**, Mibsam **<big>[[H4017|מִבְשָׂם]]</big>**, Mishma **<big>[[H4927|מִשְׁמָע]]</big>**, Dumah **<big>[[H1746|דּוּמָה]]</big>**, Hadad **<big>[[H2301|חֲדַד]]</big>**, Tema **<big>[[H8485|תֵּימָא]]</big>**, Jetur **<big>[[H3195|יְטוּר]]</big>**, Naphish **<big>[[H5305|נָפִישׁ]]</big>**, Kedemah **<big>[[H6929|קֵדְמָה]]</big>** and Mahalath **<big>[[H4258|מַחֲלַת]]</big>**.

Massa was one of the twelve sons of Ishmael, the son of Abraham and Hagar. He is mentioned in the genealogies of Ishmael's descendants \([[Gen.25.14|Genesis 25:14]]; [[1Chr.1.30|1 Chronicles 1:30]]\). As a grandson of Abraham, Massa was part of the Ishmaelite tribes that settled in the Arabian Peninsula.
