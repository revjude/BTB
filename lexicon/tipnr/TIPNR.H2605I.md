- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.11.43|1Ch.11.43]]; 
- only referred to as Hanan<[[H2605I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\); 
- a son of Maacah<[[H4601O]]>.

Hanan, the son of Maacah, is mentioned in [[1Chr.11.43|1 Chronicles 11:43]] as one of King David's mighty warriors. The mighty warriors were a group of elite soldiers who served under David and were renowned for their bravery, strength, and military prowess. They played a crucial role in establishing and maintaining David's kingdom, fighting in various battles and campaigns. The mention of Hanan in this list highlights his exceptional skills as a warrior and his loyalty to King David.
