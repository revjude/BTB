Levite living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Bakbakkar</span> was a Levite who resided in Jerusalem.
- A man from the tribe of Levi  living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[1Chr.9.15|1Ch.9.15]]; 
- only referred to as Bakbakkar<[[H1230]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ַּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ק</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ַּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ק</span>ַּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>\).

Bakbakkar is mentioned briefly in [[1Chr.9.15|1 Chronicles 9:15]] as one of the Levites who lived in Jerusalem. The context suggests that this was during the time of the return from Babylonian exile. The Levites were responsible for assisting the priests in the temple service.
