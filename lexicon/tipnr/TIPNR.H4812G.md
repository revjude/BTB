Meraioth was a priest in the lineage of Eleazar, the son of Aaron \([[1Chr.6.6|1Ch.6.6]],7,52\). He is mentioned in the genealogies of the priests and Levites in the books of Chronicles, Ezra, and Nehemiah. In the genealogy of Ezra, a descendant of Meraioth, he is listed as an ancestor of Ezra the scribe \([[Ezra.7.3|Ezr.7.3]]\). Meraioth is also mentioned in the list of priests who resided in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile \([[1Chr.9.11|1Ch.9.11]]; [[Neh.11.11|Neh.11.11]]\).
