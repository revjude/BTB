<small>*Male • David's compatriot • Recorder for David and Solomon.*</small>

David's compatriot living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jehoshaphat</span> served as a recorder for King David and his son Solomon.
- David's compatriot living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at [[2Sam.8.16|2Sa.8.16]]; 
- only referred to as Jehoshaphat **<big>[[H3092|יְהוֹשָׁפָט]]</big>**; 
-  son of Ahilud **<big>[[H0286|אֲחִילוּד]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Baana **<big>[[H1195|בַּעֲנָא]]</big>**.

Jehoshaphat was an official who served as a recorder during the reigns of King David and his son Solomon. The role of a recorder was to keep records of important events and decisions in the kingdom. Jehoshaphat is mentioned alongside other key officials in David and Solomon's administrations.
