<small>*Male • Son of Rhesa • An ancestor of Jesus Christ*</small>

Man living at the time ofExile and return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Joanan</span> is listed as an ancestor of Jesus in the genealogy recorded by Luke \([[Luke.3.27|Luk.3.27]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time ofExile and return, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.27|Luk.3.27]]; 
- only referred to as Joanan<[[G2490]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangGreek'>Ἰωαννᾶς</span>\); 
- a son of Rhesa<[[G4488]]>; 
-  father of Joda<[[G6043]]>.

Joanan is mentioned in [[Luke.3.27|Luke 3:27]] as one of the ancestors of Jesus Christ in the genealogy traced through Joseph, Jesus' legal father. He is listed as the son of Rhesa and the father of Joda, although no further details about his life or role in the family line are provided. The genealogy in Luke's Gospel spans from Jesus back to Adam, emphasizing Jesus' connection to all of humanity.
