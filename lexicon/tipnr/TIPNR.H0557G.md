Levite living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Amzi</span>, a descendant of Merari, was a Levite who served as a musician in the temple.
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.6.46|1Ch.6.46]]; 
- only referred to as Amzi<[[H0557G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>צ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
- a son of Bani<[[H1137H]]>; 
-  father of Hilkiah<[[H2518I]]>.

Amzi was a Levite who belonged to the family of Merari, one of the three main divisions of the Levites. He is mentioned in the genealogy of the Levites in 1 Chronicles 6. As a descendant of Merari, Amzi would have been responsible for the musical duties in the temple, such as singing and playing instruments during worship services.
