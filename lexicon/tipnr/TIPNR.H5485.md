Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Susi</span> was the father of Gaddi, the spy from the tribe of Manasseh.
- A man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[Num.13.11|Num.13.11]]; 
- only referred to as Susi **<big>[[H5485|סוּסִי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Gaddi **<big>[[H1426|גַּדִּי]]</big>**.

Susi is mentioned in [[Num.13.11|Numbers 13:11]] as the father of Gaddi, who was the man chosen from the tribe of Manasseh to spy out the land of Canaan. Susi is not mentioned again in the Bible. His name suggests he was likely a member of the tribe of Manasseh, but no other details are given about his background or life.
