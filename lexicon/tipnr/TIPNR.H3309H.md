Japhia was a town located on the border of the territory allocated to the tribe of Zebulun \([[Josh.19.12|Joshua 19:12]]\). It was situated in the northern part of Canaan, near Gath-hepher and Eth-kazin. The border of Zebulun's territory turned eastward from Japhia toward Gath-hepher and Eth-kazin, extending to Rimmon. Its location remains uncertain.
