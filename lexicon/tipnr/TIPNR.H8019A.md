- referred to as Shelomith<[[H8019A]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ית</span>\), or Shelomoth<[[H8013J]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>\);

-  father of Jahath<[[H3189J]]>.

Shelomith \(or Shelomoth\) was a Levite who belonged to the family of Izhar, son of Kohath. In [[1Chr.23.18|1 Chronicles 23:18]], he is listed as the first of the sons of Izhar. Later, in 1 Chronicles 24:22, Shelomoth is mentioned twice as the head of the father's house of Izhar. This suggests that he was a prominent figure within the Izharite clan of the Levites during the time of King David's organization of the Levitical duties.
