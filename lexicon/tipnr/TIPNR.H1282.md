<small>*Male • Son of Shemaiah • A descendant of David through the line of Solomon.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Bariah</span> was a descendant of King David through Solomon's line.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.3.22|1Ch.3.22]]; 
- only referred to as Bariah<[[H1282]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יח</span>ַ\); 
-  son of Shemaiah<[[H8098B]]>; 
- a brother of Hattush<[[H2407G]]>, Igal<[[H3008I]]>, Neariah<[[H5294G]]> and Shaphat<[[H8202I]]>.

Bariah is briefly mentioned in [[1Chr.3.22|1 Chronicles 3:22]] as one of the descendants of King David through the line of Solomon. The context of the passage is a genealogy tracing the royal lineage from David to several generations after the Babylonian exile. Bariah is listed as one of the sons of Shemaiah, who was a descendant of Shecaniah.
