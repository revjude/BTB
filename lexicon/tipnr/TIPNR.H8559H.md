Tamar was the beautiful daughter of King David and full sister of Absalom \([[2Sam.13.1|2 Samuel 13:1]]; [[1Chr.3.9|1 Chronicles 3:9]]\). She was raped by her half-brother Amnon, who then rejected her \([[2Sam.13.1|2 Samuel 13:1]]-19\). Tamar's tragedy led to Absalom's murder of Amnon and eventual rebellion against David \([[2Sam.13.2|2 Samuel 13:2]]0-39\). Her story illustrates the dysfunctional relationships and violence within David's family.
