<small>*Male • Believer greeted by Paul*</small>

Man living at the time of the New Testament

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Olympas</span> was a believer in Rome to whom Paul sent greetings.
- A man living at the time of the New Testament, only mentioned at [[Rom.16.15|Rom.16.15]]; 
- only referred to as Olympas<[[G3652]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangGreek'>Ὀλυμπᾶς</span>\).

Olympas was a Christian believer who lived in Rome during the time of the apostle Paul. In Paul's letter to the Romans, he sends greetings to Olympas and others, indicating that they were part of the Christian community in Rome \([[Rom.16.15|Romans 16:15]]\). The fact that Paul mentions Olympas by name suggests that he was a prominent or well-known member of the church.
