Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eran</span> was a son of Shuthelah and grandson of Ephraim, who became the head of the Eranite clan.
- A man from the tribe of Ephraim living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[Num.26.36|Num.26.36]]; 
- referred to as Eran **<big>[[H6197|עֵרָן]]</big>**, or Eranite **<big>[[H6198|עֵרָנִי]]</big>**; 
-  son of Shuthelah **<big>[[H7803|שׁוּתֶלַח]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Bered **<big>[[H1260|בֶּרֶד]]</big>**.

Eran was a son of Shuthelah and a grandson of Ephraim \([[Num.26.36|Numbers 26:36]]\). He was the head of the Eranite clan, which was named after him. The Eranites were counted among the descendants of Ephraim during the census taken in the wilderness of Sinai \([[Num.26.36|Numbers 26:36]]\).
