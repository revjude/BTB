Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Uzziah</span>, a descendant of Judah, resettled in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.11.4|Neh.11.4]]; 
- only referred to as Uzziah **<big>[[H5818|עֻזִּיָּה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Zechariah **<big>[[H2148|זְכַרְיָה]]</big>**; 
-  father of Athaiah **<big>[[H6265|עֲתָיָה]]</big>**.

Uzziah was a descendant of Perez, son of Judah. He is mentioned in the list of people who resettled in Jerusalem after returning from the Babylonian captivity. Uzziah is described as a valiant warrior, indicating he was a man of strength and skill. He was among the first Judahites to resettle in Jerusalem and help rebuild the city and its community after the exile.
