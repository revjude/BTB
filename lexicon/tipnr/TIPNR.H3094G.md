Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jehallelel</span> was a son of Elah and a descendant of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.16|1Ch.4.16]]; 
- only referred to as Jehallelel<[[H3094G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ֶּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>\); 
-  father of Ziph<[[H2128J]]>, Ziphah<[[H2129]]>, Tiria<[[H8493]]>, Asarel<[[H0840]]>.

Jehallelel is briefly mentioned in [[1Chr.4.16|1 Chronicles 4:16]] as one of the sons of Elah, who was a descendant of Judah. The genealogy in this chapter traces the lineage of some of the clans and families within the tribe of Judah. Jehallelel is listed along with his brothers Ziph, Ziphah, Tiria, and Asarel. No further information is provided about Jehallelel's life, his occupation, or his role within the clan. The mention of his name in this genealogical record serves to document the family lines and the descendants of Judah.
