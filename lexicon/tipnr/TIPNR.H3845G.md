Libni, also known as Ladan, was a son of Gershon and a grandson of Levi \([[Exod.6.17|Exo.6.17]]; [[Num.3.18|Num.3.18]]; [[1Chr.6.17|1Ch.6.17]],20\). He was the brother of Shimei \([[Exod.6.17|Exo.6.17]]; [[Num.3.18|Num.3.18]]; [[1Chr.6.17|1Ch.6.17]],20; 23.7\). Libni's descendants, the Libnites, were one of the Levitical clans \([[Num.3.21|Num.3.21]]; 26.58\). During the time of David, the Libnites were counted among the Levites and given duties in the service of the tabernacle \([[1Chr.23.7|1Ch.23.7]]-9\). The sons of Libni, Jehiel and Zetham, along with their relatives, were given charge of the treasuries of the house of the Lord \(1Ch.26.21-22\).
