Man living at the time ofExile and return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Joseph</span>, the son of Mattathias, is listed as an ancestor of Jesus Christ in Luke's genealogy.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time ofExile and return, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.24|Luk.3.24]]; 
- only referred to as Joseph **<big>[[G2501|Ἰωσήφ]]</big>**; 
- a son of Mattathias **<big>[[G3161|Ματταθίας]]</big>**; 
-  father of Jannai **<big>[[G2388|Ἰαννά]]</big>**.

Joseph, the son of Mattathias, is mentioned in [[Luke.3.24|Luke 3:24]] as part of the genealogy of Jesus Christ. This genealogy traces Jesus' lineage through his mother Mary's family line. Joseph is listed as the son of Mattathias and the father of Jannai. The genealogy spans several centuries, from Jesus back to Adam, demonstrating Jesus' connection to the ancestral line of David and the fulfillment of messianic prophecies. However, no additional information is provided about Joseph's life or the specific time period in which he lived.
