<small>*Ziddim • A fortified city in Naphtali*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ziddim</span> was a fortified city in the territory of Naphtali \([[Josh.19.35|Jos.19.35]]\).
- A location only mentioned at [[Josh.19.35|Jos.19.35]]; 
- only referred to as Ziddim **<big>[[H6661|צִדִּים]]</big>**.

Ziddim is listed among the fortified cities in the territory allotted to the tribe of Naphtali during the division of the Promised Land under Joshua \([[Josh.19.35|Jos.19.35]]\). It is mentioned alongside other fortified cities such as Zer, Hammath, and Rakkath, suggesting it was a significant defensive location. However, no other details are provided about its history or exact location within Naphtali's territory.
