Man living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Mattatha</span> was an ancestor of Jesus Christ, mentioned in His genealogy \([[Luke.3.31|Luk.3.31]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of United Monarchy, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.31|Luk.3.31]]; 
- only referred to as Mattatha **<big>[[G3160|Ματταθά]]</big>**; 
- a son of Nathan **<big>[[H5416|נָתָן]]</big>**; 
-  father of Menna **<big>[[G3104|Μαϊνάν]]</big>**.

Mattatha is mentioned in the genealogy of Jesus Christ, as recorded in the Gospel of Luke. He is listed as the son of Nathan and the father of Menna \([[Luke.3.31|Luke 3:31]]\). This genealogy traces Jesus' lineage through His mother, Mary, back to David and ultimately to Adam. Mattatha's inclusion in this genealogy highlights the human ancestry of Jesus and His connection to the royal line of David.
