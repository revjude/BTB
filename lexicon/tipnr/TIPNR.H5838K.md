<small>*Male • High Priest • Descendant of Zadok, a priest before Solomon's reign*</small>

Priest living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Azariah</span>, the descendant of Zadok, was a priest before the reign of King Solomon \(1 Ki 4:2; 1 Chr 6:9\).
- A High Priest from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at 1Ch.6.10; 
- only referred to as Azariah **<big>[[H5838|עֲזַרְיָה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Johanan **<big>[[H3110|יוֹחָנָן]]</big>**; 
-  father of Amariah **<big>[[H0568|אֲמַרְיָה]]</big>**.

Azariah, the descendant of Zadok, is mentioned in 1 Chronicles 6:9 as a priest before the reign of King Solomon. Zadok was a prominent priest during the reigns of David and Solomon, known for his loyalty and faithfulness. As the descendant of Zadok, Azariah would have held a significant position within the priesthood. The fact that he is mentioned by name suggests that he played an important role in the religious affairs of the kingdom during Solomon's reign. The priesthood was responsible for overseeing the temple worship, offering sacrifices, and teaching the people the laws of God. As a descendant of Zadok, Azariah would have been part of the line of priests chosen by God to serve in the temple.
