<small>*Female • Sister of Basemath • Daughter of Solomon; wife of Ben-abinadab*</small>

Woman living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Taphath</span> was a daughter of Solomon who married Ben-abinadab, one of Solomon's district governors.
- A woman from the tribe of Judah living at the time of United Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Kgs.4.11|1Ki.4.11]]; 
- only referred to as Taphath<[[H2955]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ט</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>\); 
-  daughter of Solomon<[[H8010]]>; 
- a sister of Basemath<[[H1315G]]> and Rehoboam<[[H7346]]>; 
- wife of Ben-abinadab<[[H1125]]>.

Taphath is mentioned in [[1Kgs.4.11|1 Kings 4:11]] as a daughter of King Solomon. She was given in marriage to Ben-abinadab, who was one of Solomon's twelve district governors responsible for providing food for the royal household. Ben-abinadab's district was Naphoth-dor.
