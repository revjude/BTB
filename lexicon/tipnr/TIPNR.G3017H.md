<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Levi</span>, son of Simeon, an ancestor of Jesus mentioned in Luke's genealogy \(Lk.3.29\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.29|Luk.3.29]]; 
- only referred to as Levi<[[G3017H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangGreek'>Λευΐ</span>\); 
- a son of Simeon<[[G4826H]]>; 
-  father of Matthat<[[G3158H]]>.

Levi, the son of Simeon, is mentioned in the genealogy of Jesus Christ recorded in the Gospel of Luke \(Lk.3.29\). He was an ancestor of Jesus, but no additional information is provided about his life or role in the biblical narrative. Levi lived sometime between the patriarchal period and the Babylonian exile.
