<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Balah</span> was a town allotted to the tribe of Simeon. \([[Josh.19.3|Joshua 19:3]]\)
- A location near Beersheba<[[H0884]]> only mentioned at [[Josh.19.3|Jos.19.3]]; 
- only referred to as Balah<[[H1088]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\).

Balah is mentioned in [[Josh.19.3|Joshua 19:3]] as one of the towns allotted to the tribe of Simeon within the inheritance of Judah. It is listed along with other towns such as Beersheba, Moladah, and Hazar-shual.
