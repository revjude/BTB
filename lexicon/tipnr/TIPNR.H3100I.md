Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Joel</span> is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Reuben as a descendant of Shemaiah \([[1Chr.5.4|1Ch.5.4]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Reuben  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.5.4|1Ch.5.4]]; 
- only referred to as Joel<[[H3100I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>\); 
-  father of Shemaiah<[[H8098D]]>.

Joel is mentioned in [[1Chr.5.4|1 Chronicles 5:4]] as a descendant of Reuben, the firstborn son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\). He is listed in the genealogical record of the tribe of Reuben as the son of Shemaiah and the father of Gog. This genealogy traces the lineage of the Reubenites, who settled east of the Jordan River.
