Jotbah is mentioned in the Bible as the hometown of Meshullemeth, the mother of King Amon of Judah \([[2Kgs.21.19|2 Kings 21:19]]\). Amon, who succeeded his father Manasseh as king, reigned for two years in Jerusalem before being assassinated by his servants. Its location is unknown.
