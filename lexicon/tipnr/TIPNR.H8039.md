<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shimeah</span> \(or Shimeam\) was a Benjamite, son of Mikloth and nephew of Ner.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.8.32|1Ch.8.32]]; 
- referred to as Shimeah<[[H8039]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ִׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\), or Shimeam<[[H8043]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ִׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ם</span>\); 
- a son of Mikloth<[[H4732G]]>.

Shimeah, also known as Shimeam, was a descendant of Benjamin mentioned in the genealogies found in [[1Chr.8.32|1 Chronicles 8:32]] and 9:38. He was the son of Mikloth and the nephew of Ner, who was the father of Kish and grandfather of King Saul. The genealogies in these passages trace the ancestry and descendants of Saul, indicating that Shimeah was part of the royal family of Israel from the tribe of Benjamin.
