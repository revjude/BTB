Absalom's daughter living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tamar</span> was the beautiful daughter of Absalom, named after his tragic sister.
- Absalom's daughter from the tribe of Judah living at the time of United Monarchy, only mentioned at [[2Sam.14.27|2Sa.14.27]]; 
- only referred to as Tamar<[[H8559I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>\); 
- a daughter of Absalom<[[H0053]]>; 
- wife of Uriel<[[H0222I]]>; 
-  mother of Maacah<[[H4601K]]>.

Tamar is mentioned in [[2Sam.14.27|2 Samuel 14:27]] as the beautiful daughter of Absalom. She was likely named after Absalom's full sister Tamar, who was tragically raped by their half-brother Amnon.
