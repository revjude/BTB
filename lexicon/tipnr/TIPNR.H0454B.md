<small>*Male • Son of Neariah • A descendant of David through Jeconiah.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Elioenai</span> was a descendant of David through Jeconiah, mentioned in the genealogy \([[1Chr.3.23|1Ch.3.23]],24\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.3.23|1Ch.3.23]]; 
- only referred to as Elioenai **<big>[[H0454|אֶלְיְהוֹעֵינַי]]</big>**; 
-  son of Neariah **<big>[[H5294|נְעַרְיָה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Hizkiah **<big>[[H2396|חִזְקִיָּה]]</big>** and Azrikam **<big>[[H5840|עַזְרִיקָם]]</big>**; 
-  father of Hodaviah **<big>[[H1938|הוֹדַוְיָה]]</big>**, Eliashib **<big>[[H0475|אֶלְיָשִׁיב]]</big>**, Pelaiah **<big>[[H6411|פְּלָיָה]]</big>**, Akkub **<big>[[H6126|עַקּוּב]]</big>**, Johanan **<big>[[H3110|יוֹחָנָן]]</big>**, Delaiah **<big>[[H1806|דְּלָיָה]]</big>** and Anani **<big>[[H6054|עֲנָנִי]]</big>**.

Elioenai is mentioned in the genealogy of King David's descendants, particularly in the line of Jeconiah \(also known as Jehoiachin\), who was one of the last kings of Judah before the Babylonian exile \([[1Chr.3.23|1Ch.3.23]],24\). Elioenai is listed as one of the sons of Neariah, who was a descendant of Shealtiel, the son of Jeconiah. This genealogy demonstrates the continuation of David's lineage after the exile.
