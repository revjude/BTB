Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Guni</span>, a descendant of Gad, was the father of Abdiel, as mentioned in the genealogy of Gad.
- A man from the tribe of Gad  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.5.15|1Ch.5.15]]; 
- only referred to as Guni **<big>[[H1476|גּוּנִי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Abdiel **<big>[[H5661|עַבְדִיאֵל]]</big>**.

In [[1Chr.5.15|1 Chronicles 5:15]], Guni is mentioned as a descendant of Gad, one of the twelve sons of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\). He is described as the son of Abdiel and the father of Ahi, who was the chief of their ancestral house. This Guni is mentioned in the context of the genealogy of the tribe of Gad, which settled east of the Jordan River.
