<small>*Male • Priest who blew trumpets*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Nethanel</span> was a priest who blew trumpets before the ark of God.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.15.24|1Ch.15.24]]; 
- only referred to as Nethanel **<big>[[H5417|נְתַנְאֵל]]</big>**.

Nethanel was a priest who, along with other priests, blew trumpets before the ark of God when David had it brought to Jerusalem \([[1Chr.15.24|1 Chronicles 15:24]]\). As a priest, his role would have included officiating at religious ceremonies.
