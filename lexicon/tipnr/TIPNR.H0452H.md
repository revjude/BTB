Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Elijah</span> was a Benjamite, one of the sons of Jeroham \([[1Chr.8.27|1Ch.8.27]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.27|1Ch.8.27]]; 
- only referred to as Elijah<[[H0452H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
-  son of Jeroham<[[H3395H]]>; 
- a brother of Shamsherai<[[H8125]]>, Shehariah<[[H7841]]>, Athaliah<[[H6271H]]>, Jaareshiah<[[H3298]]> and Zichri<[[H2147J]]>.

Elijah is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin as one of the sons of Jeroham \([[1Chr.8.27|1Ch.8.27]]\). This genealogy lists the descendants of Benjamin, tracing the lineage of King Saul. Apart from being named as a son of Jeroham, no additional information is provided about this Elijah in the Bible. He should not be confused with the prominent prophet Elijah, as they are different individuals.
