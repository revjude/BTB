Anthothijah is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin, found in 1 Chronicles 8. He is listed as one of the sons of Shashak, who was a descendant of Benjamin through his son Beriah. The genealogy provides a record of the families and clans within the tribe of Benjamin, one of the twelve tribes of Israel. Apart from this mention in the genealogical list, no further information is given about Anthothijah in the biblical text.
