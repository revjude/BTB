Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zerah</span> \(or Zohar\), a son of Simeon, was the ancestor of the Zerahite clan \([[Num.26.13|Num.26.13]]; [[1Chr.4.24|1Ch.4.24]]; [[Num.26.20|Num.26.20]]; Jos.7.17,17; [[1Chr.27.11|1Ch.27.11]],13; [[Gen.46.10|Gen.46.10]]; [[Exod.6.15|Exo.6.15]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Simeon living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, first mentioned at [[Gen.46.10|Gen.46.10]]; 
- referred to as Zerah<[[H2226L]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ֶ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>\), or Zerahite<[[H2227]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\), or Zohar<[[H6714H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>צ</span>ֹ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>\); 
-  son of Simeon<[[H8095G]]>; 
- a brother of Jemuel<[[H3223]]>, Jamin<[[H3226G]]>, Ohad<[[H0161]]>, Jachin<[[H3199G]]> and Shaul<[[H7586H]]>.

Zerah, also known as Zohar, was one of the sons of Simeon, the second son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\) and Leah. He is mentioned in the genealogies of Simeon in [[Gen.46.10|Genesis 46:10]], [[Exod.6.15|Exodus 6:15]], [[Num.26.13|Numbers 26:13]], and [[1Chr.4.24|1 Chronicles 4:24]]. Zerah's descendants formed the Zerahite clan within the tribe of Simeon. In the census taken in the wilderness of Sinai \([[Num.26.20|Numbers 26:20]]\), the Zerahites were counted among the clans of Simeon. The Zerahites are also mentioned in the story of Achan \(Joshua 7:17\), who was from the tribe of Judah but was identified as a descendant of Zerah. In [[1Chr.27.11|1 Chronicles 27:11]],13, Sibbecai, one of David's military commanders, is described as a Zerahite. The repeated mention of Zerah and his descendants in various contexts highlights the importance of family lineage and clan identity within the tribes of Israel.
