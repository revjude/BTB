Man living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Azmaveth</span> the Barhumite was one of David's mighty men, known for his valor \(2 Sam 23:31; 1 Chr 11:33\).
- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at [[2Sam.23.31|2Sa.23.31]]; 
- only referred to as Azmaveth **<big>[[H5820|עַזְמָוֶת]]</big>**.

Azmaveth the Barhumite is mentioned in [[2Sam.23.31|2 Samuel 23:31]] and [[1Chr.11.33|1 Chronicles 11:33]] as one of David's mighty men. The mighty men were a group of elite warriors who served under King David and were renowned for their bravery and military prowess. Azmaveth is described as a Barhumite, indicating his place of origin or family lineage. His inclusion in the list of mighty men suggests that he had distinguished himself in battle and had earned a reputation for his valor and loyalty to King David. The mighty men played a significant role in establishing and maintaining David's kingdom, and their individual acts of heroism were celebrated in Israelite history.
