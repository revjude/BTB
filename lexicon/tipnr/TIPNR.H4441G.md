<small>*Male • Son of Jeatherai • Son of Ethni, a Gershonite Levite.*</small>

Levite living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Malchijah</span>, a son of Ethni, was a Gershonite Levite \([[1Chr.6.40|1Ch.6.40]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.6.40|1Ch.6.40]]; 
- only referred to as Malchijah<[[H4441G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ִּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\); 
- a son of Jeatherai<[[H2979]]>; 
-  father of Baaseiah<[[H1202]]>.

Malchijah was a Levite who belonged to the Gershonite clan, one of the three main divisions of the Levites. He was the son of Ethni and is mentioned in the genealogy of the Levites \([[1Chr.6.40|1 Chronicles 6:40]]\).
