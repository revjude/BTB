Man living at the time before the Flood

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Irad</span> was a descendant of Cain and the father of Mehujael \(Gen.4.18\).
- A man living at the time before the Flood, first mentioned at Gen.4.18; 
- only referred to as Irad **<big>[[H5897|עִירָד]]</big>**; 
- a son of Enoch **<big>[[H2585|חֲנוֹךְ]]</big>**; 
-  father of Mehujael **<big>[[H4232|מְחוּיָאֵל]]</big>**.

Irad is mentioned in the genealogy of Cain's descendants in Genesis 4:18. He was the son of Enoch and the father of Mehujael. Irad represents the fourth generation after Cain. The passage traces the line of Cain down to Lamech. No other details are provided about Irad's life or character. His name may mean "fleet" or "wild ass" in Hebrew.
