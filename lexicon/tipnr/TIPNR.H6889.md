<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zereth</span>, a son of Ashhur \(or 'Ashur'\) and Helah, was a Judahite \([[1Chr.4.7|1Ch.4.7]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.7|1Ch.4.7]]; 
- only referred to as Zereth<[[H6889]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>צ</span>ֶ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>\); 
-  son of Ashhur<[[H0806]]> and Helah<[[H2458]]>; 
- a brother of Haahashtari<[[H0326]]>, Izhar<[[H3328]]>, Ethnan<[[H0869]]> and Koz<[[H6976G]]>; 
-  half-brother of: Ahuzzam<[[H0275]]>, Hepher<[[H2660G]]> and Temeni<[[H8488]]>.

Zereth was a Judahite, the son of Ashhur \(or 'Ashur'\) and his wife Helah. Ashhur was the  father \(ie founder\) of Tekoa, a town in Judah. Zereth is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah in 1 Chronicles 4, along with his brothers Zohar and Ethnan.
