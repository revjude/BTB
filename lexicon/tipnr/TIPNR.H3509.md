Edomite living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jetheth</span>, one of the chiefs of Edom \([[Gen.36.40|Gen.36.40]]; [[1Chr.1.51|1Ch.1.51]]\).
- A man living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.36.40|Gen.36.40]]; 
- only referred to as Jetheth **<big>[[H3509|יְתֵת]]</big>**.

Jetheth is mentioned in the lists of the chiefs of Edom in [[Gen.36.40|Genesis 36:40]] and [[1Chr.1.51|1 Chronicles 1:51]]. He is identified as one of the chiefs who came from the Edomite clans.
