- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.24.16|1Ch.24.16]]; 
- only referred to as Pethahiah<[[H6611G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ְּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\).

Pethahiah was a priest who served as the head of the 19th priestly division during the time of David, according to [[1Chr.24.16|1 Chronicles 24:16]]. The priests were divided into 24 divisions, each responsible for serving in the temple for a week at a time, twice a year. These divisions were established by David to ensure orderly worship and service in the temple.
