Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hezekiah</span> was an ancestor of exiles who returned to Jerusalem.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at [[Ezra.2.16|Ezr.2.16]]; 
- referred to as Hezekiah<[[H2396I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ק</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\), or Hezekiah<[[H3169I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ק</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
- a son of Ater<[[H0333G]]>.

Hezekiah is briefly mentioned as an ancestor in the list of people returned from exile \([[Ezra.2.16|Ezr.2.16]]; [[Neh.7.21|Neh.7.21]]\).
