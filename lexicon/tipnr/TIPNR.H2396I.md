<small>*Male • Son of Ater • Ancestor of exiles who returned to Jerusalem.*</small>

Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hezekiah</span> was an ancestor of exiles who returned to Jerusalem.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at [[Ezra.2.16|Ezr.2.16]]; 
- referred to as Hezekiah **<big>[[H2396|חִזְקִיָּה]]</big>**, or Hezekiah **<big>[[H3169|יְחִזְקִיָּה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Ater **<big>[[H0333|אָטֵר]]</big>**.

Hezekiah is briefly mentioned as an ancestor in the list of people returned from exile \([[Ezra.2.16|Ezr.2.16]]; [[Neh.7.21|Neh.7.21]]\).
