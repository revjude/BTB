<small>*Tappuah\_2 • Town in the Judean Shephelah*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tappuah</span>, a town in the Shephelah \(lowlands\) of Judah \(Josh. 15:34\).
- A location near Enam **<big>[[H5879|עֵינַיִם]]</big>** only mentioned at [[Josh.15.34|Jos.15.34]]; 
- only referred to as Tappuah **<big>[[H8599|תַּפּוּחַ]]</big>**.

Tappuah is mentioned in [[Josh.15.34|Joshua 15:34]] as one of the towns in the Shephelah \(lowlands\) of Judah, along with other towns such as Zanoah, Enam, and Jarmuth. It is listed among the cities allotted to the tribe of Judah during the division of the Promised Land. The exact location of this Tappuah is uncertain, but it is distinguished from the Tappuah on the border of Ephraim and Manasseh.
