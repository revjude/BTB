<small>*Ramoth\_1 • Levitical city in Issachar*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Remeth</span>, also called Ramoth, was a Levitical city allotted to the tribe of Issachar \(Jos. 19:21; 1 Ch. 6:73\).
- A location near Jezreel<[[H3157H]]> first mentioned at [[Josh.19.21|Jos.19.21]]; 
- referred to as Remeth<[[H7432]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>\), or Ramoth<[[H7216G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>אמו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>\).
