<small>*Ramoth\_1 • Levitical city in Issachar*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Remeth</span>, also called Ramoth, was a Levitical city allotted to the tribe of Issachar \(Jos. 19:21; 1 Ch. 6:73\).
- A location near Jezreel **<big>[[H3157|יִזְרְעֵאל]]</big>** first mentioned at [[Josh.19.21|Jos.19.21]]; 
- referred to as Remeth **<big>[[H7432|רֶמֶת]]</big>**, or Ramoth **<big>[[H7216|רָאמוֹת]]</big>**.
