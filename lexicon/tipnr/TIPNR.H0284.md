<small>*Male • Son of Ehud • Son of Ehud, from the tribe of Benjamin*</small>

Man living before Israel's Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ahihud</span> was a son of Ehud, a descendant of Benjamin. \([[1Chr.8.7|1Ch.8.7]]\)
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living before Israel's Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.7|1Ch.8.7]]; 
- only referred to as Ahihud<[[H0284]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יח</span>ֻ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ד</span>\); 
-  son of Ehud<[[H0164G]]>; 
- a brother of Naaman<[[H5283J]]>, Ahijah<[[H0281K]]>, Gera<[[H1617J]]> and Uzza<[[H5798H]]>.

Ahihud is briefly mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin. He was one of the sons of Ehud, who was a descendant of Benjamin through his son Bela. The genealogy traces the lineage of several prominent Benjamite families, but no specific details are given about Ahihud's life or role within the tribe. \([[1Chr.8.7|1Ch.8.7]]\).
