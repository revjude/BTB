<small>*Male • Son of Hebron • Son of Hebron from the tribe of Judah*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tappuah</span> was a son of Hebron, a descendant of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.2.43|1Ch.2.43]]; 
- only referred to as Tappuah<[[H8599A]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ַּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ֻּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ַ\); 
-  son of Hebron<[[H2275I]]>; 
- a brother of Korah<[[H7141J]]>, Rekem<[[H7552I]]> and Shema<[[H8087G]]>.

Tappuah is mentioned in [[1Chr.2.43|1 Chronicles 2:43]] as one of the sons of Hebron. Hebron was a descendant of Judah through Caleb, making Tappuah a member of the tribe of Judah.
