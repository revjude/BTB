<small>*Male • Son of Rehoboam • Son of King Rehoboam and Mahalath*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jeush</span> was a son of King Rehoboam and his wife Mahalath \([[2Chr.11.19|2Ch.11.19]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[2Chr.11.19|2Ch.11.19]]; 
- only referred to as Jeush<[[H3266K]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>עו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ׁ\); 
-  son of Rehoboam<[[H7346]]> and Mahalath<[[H4258H]]>; 
- a brother of Shemariah<[[H8114H]]>, Zaham<[[H2093]]>; 
-  half-brother of: Abijah<[[H0029I]]>, Attai<[[H6262I]]>, Ziza<[[H2124H]]> and Shelomith<[[H8019G]]>.

Jeush is mentioned in [[2Chr.11.19|2 Chronicles 11:19]] as one of the sons born to King Rehoboam, the son of Solomon, and his wife Mahalath. Mahalath was the daughter of David's son Jerimoth and Abihail, the daughter of Eliab, who was David's brother.
