<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shecaniah</span>, a Levite who assisted in distributing tithes during Hezekiah's reign.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[2Chr.31.15|2Ch.31.15]]; 
- only referred to as Shecaniah **<big>[[H7935|שְׁכַנְיָה]]</big>**.

Shecaniah was a Levite who lived during the reign of King Hezekiah of Judah. He was one of the Levites appointed to distribute the tithes and dedicated things brought by the people to the temple. Shecaniah worked faithfully alongside other Levites in ensuring these offerings were properly allocated to the priests and Levites \([[2Chr.31.15|2 Chronicles 31:15]]\).
