<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zerah</span>, a Levite of the Gershonite clan, was the son of Iddo \([[1Chr.6.21|1Ch.6.21]],41\).
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.6.21|1Ch.6.21]]; 
- only referred to as Zerah **<big>[[H2226|זֶרַח]]</big>**; 
- a son of Iddo **<big>[[H5714|עִדּוֹ]]</big>**; 
-  father of Jeatherai **<big>[[H2979|יְאָתְרַי]]</big>**.

Zerah was a Levite who belonged to the clan of Gershon. He was the son of Iddo and is mentioned in the genealogies of the Levites in 1 Chronicles 6. These genealogies trace the lineage of the Levitical families, who were responsible for the religious duties in the temple. As a Gershonite, Zerah would have been part of the Levitical family that was tasked with the care of the tabernacle and its furnishings during the wilderness wanderings.
