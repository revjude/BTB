Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Uzzi</span>, a grandson of Benjamin, was one of the sons of Bela.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.7|1Ch.7.7]]; 
- only referred to as Uzzi<[[H5813I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֻ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ִּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
-  son of Bela<[[H1106A]]>; 
- a brother of Ard<[[H0714H]]>, Naaman<[[H5283H]]>, Ezbon<[[H0675H]]>, Uzziel<[[H5816I]]>, Jerimoth<[[H3406G]]>, Iri<[[H5901]]>, Addar<[[H0146H]]>, Gera<[[H1617I]]>, Abihud<[[H0031]]>, Abishua<[[H0050H]]> and Ahoah<[[H0265]]>.

Uzzi was a descendant of Benjamin, one of the twelve tribes of Israel. He was the son of Bela, who was one of Benjamin's sons. Uzzi is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin, along with his brothers Ezbon, Uzzi, Uzziel, Jerimoth, and Iri. They were all described as heads of their families and mighty warriors. This Uzzi was part of the fighting strength of the Benjamites.
