<small>*Male • Returned builder • A repairer of Jerusalem's wall opposite his house.*</small>

Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hasshub</span> repaired a section of Jerusalem's wall opposite his house. \([[Neh.3.23|Neh.3.23]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.23|Neh.3.23]]; 
- only referred to as Hasshub **<big>[[H2815|חַשּׁוּב]]</big>**.

Hasshub is mentioned in the book of Nehemiah as one of the individuals who helped repair Jerusalem's wall during the time of Nehemiah. He repaired a section of the wall opposite his house, along with Benjamin. This suggests that Hasshub lived in Jerusalem and had a personal stake in the city's restoration. His participation in the rebuilding of the wall demonstrates the involvement of various individuals in the restoration of Jerusalem. \([[Neh.3.23|Neh.3.23]]\).
