Janen is a place name that appears only in the Septuagint, the Greek translation of the Old Testament, in [[Num.21.19|Numbers 21:19]]. It is not mentioned in the Hebrew text or most other translations of the Bible. The Septuagint includes Janen in the list of places the Israelites passed through during their wilderness journey. Its exact location and significance are unknown, and no further information about Janen is provided in the Bible.
