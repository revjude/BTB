<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Rehoboth</span> was a city associated with Saul, an Edomite king \(Gen. 36:37; 1 Ch. 1:48\).
- A location near Edom Zered<[[H2218]]> first mentioned at [[Gen.36.37|Gen.36.37]]; 
- only referred to as Rehoboth<[[H7344H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>בו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>\).

Rehoboth is mentioned as the hometown of Saul, a king of Edom. He is described as "Saul from Rehoboth on the river" \(Gen. 36:37; 1 Ch. 1:48\). This distinguishes it from Isaac's well called Rehoboth. It was likely located along the Euphrates River, but its exact location is unknown.
