Levite living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Baaseiah</span> was a Levite descended from Gershom, the son of Levi.
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.6.40|1Ch.6.40]]; 
- only referred to as Baaseiah<[[H1202]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ַּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ֵׂ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
- a son of Malchijah<[[H4441G]]>; 
-  father of Michael<[[H4317J]]>.

Baaseiah is mentioned in [[1Chr.6.40|1 Chronicles 6:40]] as part of a genealogy of the Levites, specifically the descendants of Gershom, one of the sons of Levi. The Levites were the tribe designated to assist the priests in the tabernacle and temple worship. The genealogy traces the lineage of several prominent Levitical families. Baaseiah is listed as the son of Michael and the father of Malchijah. Beyond this genealogical information, no other details are provided about Baaseiah's life or specific role within the Levitical community. The inclusion of his name in the genealogy serves to establish the continuity and legitimacy of the Levitical priesthood across generations.
