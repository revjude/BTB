Arabian living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jetur</span>, one of the twelve sons of Ishmael \([[Gen.25.15|Gen.25.15]]; [[1Chr.1.31|1Ch.1.31]]\).
- A man of the Arabs living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.25.15|Gen.25.15]]; 
- only referred to as Jetur **<big>[[H3195|יְטוּר]]</big>**; 
-  son of Ishmael **<big>[[H3458|יִשְׁמָעֵאל]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Nebaioth **<big>[[H5032|נְבָיוֹת]]</big>**, Kedar **<big>[[H6938|קֵדָר]]</big>**, Adbeel **<big>[[H0110|אַדְבְּאֵל]]</big>**, Mibsam **<big>[[H4017|מִבְשָׂם]]</big>**, Mishma **<big>[[H4927|מִשְׁמָע]]</big>**, Dumah **<big>[[H1746|דּוּמָה]]</big>**, Massa **<big>[[H4854|מַשָּׂא]]</big>**, Hadad **<big>[[H2301|חֲדַד]]</big>**, Tema **<big>[[H8485|תֵּימָא]]</big>**, Naphish **<big>[[H5305|נָפִישׁ]]</big>**, Kedemah **<big>[[H6929|קֵדְמָה]]</big>** and Mahalath **<big>[[H4258|מַחֲלַת]]</big>**.

Jetur is mentioned in the genealogies of Ishmael in [[Gen.25.15|Genesis 25:15]] and [[1Chr.1.31|1 Chronicles 1:31]]. He is listed as one of the twelve sons of Ishmael, Abraham's son by Hagar. The Ishmaelites, including Jetur and his brothers, are described as living in the region from Havilah to Shur, east of Egypt \(Gen.25.18\).
