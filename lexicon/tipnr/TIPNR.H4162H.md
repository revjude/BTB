Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Moza</span>, a Benjamite, was a descendant of King Saul.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.8.36|1Ch.8.36]]; 
- only referred to as Moza<[[H4162H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>צ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>\); 
- a son of Zimri<[[H2174H]]>; 
-  father of Binea<[[H1150]]>.

Moza was a descendant of King Saul and part of the tribe of Benjamin. He was the son of Zimri and father of Binea \([[1Chr.8.36|1 Chronicles 8:36]]-37, 9:42-43\). These genealogies trace Saul's lineage after his death. No other information is provided about Moza's life or achievements. He should not be confused with Moza, the son of Caleb \(1 Chronicles 2:46\).
