<small>*Male • Returned priest • Priest during the time of Nehemiah.*</small>

Levite living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Malchijah</span> was a priest who served during Nehemiah's time \([[1Chr.9.12|1Ch.9.12]]; [[Neh.11.12|Neh.11.12]]\).
- A priest from the tribe of Levi  living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at [[1Chr.9.12|1Ch.9.12]]; 
- only referred to as Malchijah **<big>[[H4441|מַלְכִּיָּה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Jerahmeel **<big>[[H3396|יְרַחְמְאֵל]]</big>**; 
-  a son of Hammelech **<big>[[H4428|מֶלֶךְ]]</big>**; 
-  father of Pashhur **<big>[[H6583|פַּשְׁחוּר]]</big>**.

Malchijah was a priest who lived during the time of Nehemiah, after the return of the Jews from Babylonian exile. He is mentioned in the lists of priests and Levites who settled in Jerusalem \([[1Chr.9.12|1 Chronicles 9:12]]; [[Neh.11.12|Nehemiah 11:12]]\).
