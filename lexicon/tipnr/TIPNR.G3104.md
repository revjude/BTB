<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Menna</span>, mentioned in Luke's genealogy of Jesus \([[Luke.3.31|Luk.3.31]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.31|Luk.3.31]]; 
- only referred to as Menna **<big>[[G3104|Μαϊνάν]]</big>**; 
- a son of Mattatha **<big>[[G3160|Ματταθά]]</big>**; 
-  father of Melea **<big>[[G3190|Μελεᾶς]]</big>**.

Menna is a name that appears in the genealogy of Jesus Christ recorded in the Gospel of Luke \([[Luke.3.31|Luk.3.31]]\). He is listed as an ancestor of Jesus, but no additional information about his life or role is provided in the biblical text. His name suggests he was likely of Jewish descent.
