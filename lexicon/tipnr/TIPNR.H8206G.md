Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shuppim</span> was a Benjamite descendant, the son of Ir \(or Iri\), mentioned in the genealogy of Benjamin.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin  living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, first mentioned at [[1Chr.7.12|1Ch.7.12]]; 
- only referred to as Shuppim<[[H8206G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ֻׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ִּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ים</span>\); 
-  son of Ir<[[H5893G]]>; 
- a brother of Huppim<[[H2650G]]> and Maacah<[[H4601M]]>.

Shuppim is mentioned in [[1Chr.7.12|1 Chronicles 7:12]],15 as a descendant of Benjamin, specifically the son of Ir \(or Iri\). The genealogy in this passage traces the lineage of the tribe of Benjamin, one of the twelve tribes of Israel. Shuppim is listed along with his brother Huppim as the sons of Ir, who was a descendant of Bela, the firstborn of Benjamin.
