Man living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ahasbai</span> the Maachathite was the father of Eliphelet, one of David's mighty men. \([[2Sam.23.34|2Sa.23.34]]\)
- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, only mentioned at [[2Sam.23.34|2Sa.23.34]]; 
- only referred to as Ahasbai **<big>[[H0308|אֲחַסְבַּי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Eliphelet **<big>[[H0467|אֱלִיפֶלֶט]]</big>**.

Ahasbai is mentioned in the Bible as the father of Eliphelet the Maachathite, who was one of King David's mighty men. The mighty men were a group of skilled warriors who fought alongside David and were renowned for their military prowess.
