Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Azmaveth</span>, a descendant of Saul, son of Jehoaddah..
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.8.36|1Ch.8.36]]; 
- only referred to as Azmaveth **<big>[[H5820|עַזְמָוֶת]]</big>**; 
-  son of Jehoaddah **<big>[[H3085|יְהוֹעַדָּה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Alemeth **<big>[[H5964|עָלֶמֶת]]</big>** and Zimri **<big>[[H2174|זִמְרִי]]</big>**.

Azmaveth is mentioned in [[1Chr.8.36|1 Chronicles 8:36]] and 9:42 as a descendant of Saul, the first king of Israel. He is listed in the genealogy of Saul's family, which is given in two parallel passages in the book of Chronicles.
