<small>*Ir-nahash • Son of Tehinnah • A town associated with the descendants of Eshton*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ir-nahash</span>, a town associated with the descendants of Eshton \([[1Chr.4.12|1 Chronicles 4:12]]\).
- A location in Judah Tribe near Tekoa **<big>[[H8620|תְּקוֹעַ]]</big>** only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.12|1Ch.4.12]]; 
- referred to as Ir-nahash **<big>[[H5904|עִיר נָחָשׁ]]</big>**, or Ir-nahash \(NIV= Ir Nahash\) \(=Alt tag\)  **<big>[[H5892|עִיר]]</big>** **<big>[[H5176|נָחָשׁ]]</big>**; 
-  founded by Tehinnah **<big>[[H8468|תְּחִנָּה]]</big>** - perhaps a person, the son of Tehinnah **<big>[[H8468|תְּחִנָּה]]</big>**.

Ir-nahash is mentioned in the genealogy of the descendants of Judah, specifically in connection with the family of Eshton \([[1Chr.4.12|1 Chronicles 4:12]]\). The passage states that the men of Recah, who were descendants of Eshton, built or founded Ir-nahash. Its exact location remains unknown.
