Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shema</span>, a descendant of Reuben, son of Joel.
- A man from the tribe of Reuben  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.5.8|1Ch.5.8]]; 
- only referred to as Shema<[[H8087H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ֶׁ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>\); 
- a son of Joel<[[H3100J]]>; 
-  father of Azaz<[[H5811]]>.

Shema is mentioned in [[1Chr.5.8|1 Chronicles 5:8]] as a descendant of Reuben, the firstborn son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\). He is identified as the son of Joel and is listed in the genealogy of the Reubenites who lived in the region of Gilead.
