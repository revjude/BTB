<small>*Male • Son of Abiud • Ancestor of Jesus in Matthew's genealogy*</small>

Man living at the time ofExile and return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eliakim</span> was an ancestor of Jesus mentioned in Matthew's genealogy.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time ofExile and return, first mentioned at Mat.1.13; 
- only referred to as Eliakim<[[G1662G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangGreek'>Ἐλιακείμ</span>\); 
- a son of Abiud<[[G0010]]>; 
-  father of Azor<[[G0107]]>.

Eliakim is mentioned twice in Matthew 1:13 in the genealogy of Jesus Christ. He was the son of Abiud and the father of Azor. Eliakim is listed in the third set of fourteen generations, spanning from the time after the Babylonian exile to the birth of Jesus. The genealogy in Matthew's Gospel traces Jesus' lineage through his legal father, Joseph, emphasizing his royal descent from King David.
