<small>*Male • Son of Shimei • Levite of the clan of Merari*</small>

Levite living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Uzzah</span>, a Levite of the Merarite clan, was the son of Shimei.
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.6.29|1Ch.6.29]]; 
- only referred to as Uzzah<[[H5798B]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֻ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
- a son of Shimei<[[H8096M]]>; 
-  father of Shimea<[[H8092G]]>.

Uzzah was a Levite who belonged to the clan of Merari, one of the three main Levite clans. He was the son of Shimei. This Uzzah is mentioned in the genealogy of the Levites, which lists the descendants of Levi who were responsible for assisting the priests and maintaining the tabernacle and temple.
