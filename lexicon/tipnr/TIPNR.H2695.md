Man living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hezro</span> the Carmelite was one of David's mighty men, known for his valor and military prowess.
- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at [[2Sam.23.35|2Sa.23.35]]; 
- only referred to as Hezro<[[H2695]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>צ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>רו</span>ֹ\).

Hezro the Carmelite is mentioned in [[2Sam.23.35|2 Samuel 23:35]] and [[1Chr.11.37|1 Chronicles 11:37]] as one of David's mighty men. The mighty men were a group of elite warriors who served under David and were known for their exceptional bravery and military skills. Hezro is described as a Carmelite, which likely refers to his place of origin, the town of Carmel in Judah.
