<small>*Female • Sister of Ishhod • Daughter of Zelophehad's sister.*</small>

Woman living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Mahlah</span> was the daughter of Zelophehad's sister \([[1Chr.7.18|1Ch.7.18]]\).
- A woman from the tribe of Manasseh living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.18|1Ch.7.18]]; 
- only referred to as Mahlah **<big>[[H4244|מַחְלָה]]</big>**; 
- her mother was Hammolecheth **<big>[[H4447|מֹלֶכֶת]]</big>**; 
- a sister of Ishhod **<big>[[H0379|אִישְׁהוֹד]]</big>** and Abiezer **<big>[[H0044|אֲבִיעֶזֶר]]</big>**.

Mahlah is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Manasseh. She was the daughter of Zelophehad's sister, who is not named in the text.
