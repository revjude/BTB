Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tahan</span> was a son of Ephraim and the ancestor of the Tahanites clan.
- A man from the tribe of Ephraim living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[Num.26.35|Num.26.35]]; 
- referred to as Tahan<[[H8465G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ַּ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\), or Tahanite<[[H8470]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ַּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
-  son of Ephraim<[[H0669G]]>; 
- a brother of Shuthelah<[[H7803G]]>, Becher<[[H1071H]]>, Ezer<[[H5829H]]>, Elead<[[H0496]]> and Beriah<[[H1283H]]>.

Tahan is mentioned in [[Num.26.35|Numbers 26:35]] as a son of Ephraim and the ancestor of the Tahanites clan. The Tahanites were one of the clans of the tribe of Ephraim, descended from Tahan. No other details are provided about Tahan's life or role in the tribe. As a son of Ephraim, he would have been born in Egypt before the Exodus.
