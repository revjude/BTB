Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hasupha</span> was an ancestor of temple servants who returned to Jerusalem from Babylonian exile. \([[Ezra.2.43|Ezr.2.43]]; [[Neh.7.46|Neh.7.46]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at [[Ezra.2.43|Ezr.2.43]]; 
- only referred to as Hasupha **<big>[[H2817|חֲשׂוּפָא]]</big>**.

Hasupha is mentioned as an ancestor of a group of temple servants \(Nethinim\) who returned to Jerusalem from Babylonian captivity. The temple servants were a class of people assigned to assist the Levites in the maintenance and service of the temple. In the lists of returnees recorded in Ezra and Nehemiah, the descendants of Hasupha are mentioned among the temple servants. No further information about Hasupha is provided in the Bible. \([[Ezra.2.43|Ezr.2.43]]; [[Neh.7.46|Neh.7.46]]\).
