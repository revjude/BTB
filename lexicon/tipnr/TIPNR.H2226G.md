Man living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zerah</span>, a son of Reuel and grandson of Esau, was a chief of Edom \([[Gen.36.13|Gen.36.13]],17; [[1Chr.1.37|1Ch.1.37]]\).
- A man living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.36.13|Gen.36.13]]; 
- only referred to as Zerah **<big>[[H2226|זֶרַח]]</big>**; 
-  son of Reuel **<big>[[H7467|רְעוּאֵל]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Nahath **<big>[[H5184|נַחַת]]</big>**, Shammah **<big>[[H8048|שַׁמָּה]]</big>** and Mizzah **<big>[[H4199|מִזָּה]]</big>**.

Zerah was a son of Reuel and a grandson of Esau. He is mentioned in the genealogies of Esau in Genesis 36 and 1 Chronicles 1. Like his cousin Zepho, Zerah was also a chief of Edom, reflecting his leadership position within the Edomite tribe. The Edomites were the descendants of Esau who settled in the region of Seir.
