Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shelemiah</span>, a Levite who repaired a section of Jerusalem's wall.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.30|Neh.3.30]]; 
- only referred to as Shelemiah **<big>[[H8018|שֶׁלֶמְיָה]]</big>**; 
-  father of Hananiah **<big>[[H2608|חֲנַנְיָה]]</big>**.

Shelemiah was a Levite who participated in rebuilding the wall of Jerusalem under Nehemiah's leadership. According to [[Neh.3.30|Nehemiah 3:30]], he repaired a second section of the wall, but the exact location is not specified. His work was part of the collective effort to restore Jerusalem's defenses after the Babylonian exile.
