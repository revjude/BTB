Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shecaniah</span>, a descendant of Parosh, who returned from Babylonian exile.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Ezra.8.3|Ezr.8.3]]; 
- only referred to as Shecaniah<[[H7935J]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\).

Shecaniah was a descendant of Parosh and was among the group of exiles who returned to Jerusalem from Babylon under the leadership of Ezra. He is listed in [[Ezra.8.3|Ezra 8:3]] among the men who went with Ezra to Jerusalem.
