Man living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tarshish</span> was a son of Javan and great-grandson of Noah, associated with the ancient city of Tarshish.
- A man of the Greeks living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.10.4|Gen.10.4]]; 
- only referred to as Tarshish<[[H8659G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ַּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ִׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יש</span>ׁ\); 
-  son of Javan<[[H3120G]]>; 
- a brother of Elishah<[[H0473G]]>, Kittim<[[H3794G]]> and Dodanim<[[H1721]]>.

Tarshish is mentioned in [[Gen.10.4|Genesis 10:4]] and [[1Chr.1.7|1 Chronicles 1:7]] as a son of Javan and grandson of Noah. He was born after the flood and is listed among the descendants of Japheth. Tarshish is associated with the ancient city of Tarshish, a distant port known for its wealth and maritime trade. His descendants likely settled in or near this city, which some identify with Tartessos in Spain or Tarsus in Cilicia.
