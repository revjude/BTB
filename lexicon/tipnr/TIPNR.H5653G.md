Man living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Abda</span> was the father of Adoram \(or 'Adoniram'\), who was in charge of forced labor during Solomon's reign.
- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Kgs.4.6|1Ki.4.6]]; 
- only referred to as Abda **<big>[[H5653|עַבְדָּא]]</big>**; 
-  father of Adoram **<big>[[H0151|אֲדֹרָם]]</big>**.

Abda is mentioned only once in the Bible, in [[1Kgs.4.6|1 Kings 4:6]], as the father of Adoram \(or 'Adoniram'\). He was one of King Solomon's officials, responsible for overseeing the forced labor used in Solomon's building projects.
