Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zebadiah</span> was a greandson of Elpaal and a member of the tribe of Benjamin.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin  living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.15|1Ch.8.15]]; 
- only referred to as Zebadiah<[[H2069G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ד</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\); 
-  son of Beriah<[[H1283I]]>; 
- a brother of Arad<[[H6166B]]>, Ahio<[[H0283H]]>, Shashak<[[H8349]]>, Jeremoth<[[H3406I]]>, Eder<[[H5738]]>, Michael<[[H4317L]]>, Ishpah<[[H3472]]> and Joha<[[H3109G]]>.

Zebadiah is listed in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin as one of the sons of Beriah, son of Elpaal, a descendant of Shaharaim, who was among the Benjamites living in Moab. The genealogy in 1 Chronicles 8 records the descendants of Benjamin, including the family of Elpaal. Zebadiah is mentioned along with his brothers, but no additional details are provided about his life or any specific role he may have played within the tribe.
