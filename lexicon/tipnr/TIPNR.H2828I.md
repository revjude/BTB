Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hashum</span> was one of the individuals who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah. \([[Neh.10.18|Neh.10.18]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.10.18|Neh.10.18]]; 
- only referred to as Hashum<[[H2828I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ֻׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ם</span>\).

Hashum is mentioned in the book of Nehemiah as one of the individuals who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah. This covenant was a solemn agreement made by the people of Israel to follow God's laws and commandments. The covenant was sealed by a group of leaders, priests, Levites, and others, including Hashum.
