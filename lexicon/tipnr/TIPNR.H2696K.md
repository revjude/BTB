The Bible mentions an unnamed wife of Hezron in [[1Chr.2.9|1 Chronicles 2:9]]. She was the mother of Hezron's three sons: Jerahmeel, Ram, and Caleb. No further information is provided about her in the biblical text. Her inclusion in the genealogy highlights the importance of family lineage and the role of women in bearing children to continue the family line.
