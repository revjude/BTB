Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Nethanel</span> was a scribe who helped divide the priests into their divisions during David's reign.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.24.6|1Ch.24.6]]; 
- only referred to as Nethanel<[[H5417J]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>\); 
-  father of Shemaiah<[[H8098H]]>.

Nethanel was a scribe during the reign of King David. He was the son of Shemaiah \([[1Chr.24.6|1 Chronicles 24:6]]\). As a scribe, Nethanel would have been a trained writer and record keeper. He assisted in casting lots to divide the priests into their divisions for service in the temple \([[1Chr.24.6|1 Chronicles 24:6]]\).
