<small>*Male • High Priest • Grandfather of Ezra, descended from High Priests*</small>

Priest living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Azariah</span> was father of Seraiah, father of Ezra. His ancestors served as high priests \(1 Chr 6:13, 14; 9:11; [[Ezra.7.1|Ezr.7.1]]\).
- A High Priest from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.6.13|1Ch.6.13]]; 
- only referred to as Azariah<[[H5838L]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
-  son of Hilkiah<[[H2518H]]>; 
- a brother of Gemariah<[[H1587G]]>; 
-  father of Seraiah<[[H8304H]]>.

Azariah is mentioned in [[1Chr.6.13|1 Chronicles 6:13]]-14, 9:11, and [[Ezra.7.1|Ezra 7:1]] as the descendant of high priests. He was the father of Seraiah, who was the father of Ezra, who returned to Jerusalem to teach the Law.
