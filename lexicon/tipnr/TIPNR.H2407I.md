Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hattush</span>, son of Hashabneiah, repaired a section of Jerusalem's wall. \([[Neh.3.10|Neh.3.10]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.10|Neh.3.10]]; 
- only referred to as Hattush<[[H2407I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ט</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ׁ\); 
- a son of Hashabneiah<[[H2813G]]>.

Hattush, the son of Hashabneiah, is mentioned in the book of Nehemiah as one of the individuals who helped repair Jerusalem's wall during the time of Nehemiah. He repaired a section adjacent to the portion repaired by Jedaiah, the son of Harumaph. Hashabneiah, Hattush's father, is also mentioned as a participant in the wall repair. Hattush's involvement in this important project demonstrates his commitment to the restoration of Jerusalem. \([[Neh.3.10|Neh.3.10]]\).
