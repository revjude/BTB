Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ahi</span> \(or "brother"\) was a son of Shomer \(or 'Shamer'\), a descendant of Asher. \([[1Chr.7.34|1Ch.7.34]]\)
- A man from the tribe of Asher living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.34|1Ch.7.34]]; 
- only referred to as Ahi<[[H0277H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
-  son of Shomer<[[H7763H]]>; 
- a brother of Rohgah<[[H7303]]>, Jehubbah<[[H3160]]> and Aram<[[H0758J]]>.

Ahi, whose name means "brother," is listed in the genealogy of the tribe of Asher. He was the son of Shomer \(or 'Shamer'\), who was a descendant of Asher through Heber. The biblical text does not provide any additional details about Ahi's life, role, or the significance of his name. \([[1Chr.7.34|1Ch.7.34]]\).
