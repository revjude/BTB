Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hasshub</span> was one of the individuals who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah. \([[Neh.10.23|Neh.10.23]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.10.23|Neh.10.23]]; 
- only referred to as Hasshub<[[H2815J]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ּׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>\).

Hasshub is mentioned in the book of Nehemiah as one of the individuals who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah. This covenant was a solemn agreement made by the people of Israel to follow God's laws and commandments. The covenant was sealed by a group of leaders, priests, Levites, and others, including Hasshub. No other information is provided about this Hasshub in the Bible. \([[Neh.10.23|Neh.10.23]]\).
