<small>*Male • David's warrior • Ashterathite, one of David's mighty warriors*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Uzzia</span> the Ashterathite was one of David's mighty warriors.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.11.44|1Ch.11.44]]; 
- only referred to as Uzzia<[[H5814]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֻ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ִּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>\).

Uzzia the Ashterathite is mentioned in the list of David's mighty warriors, also known as David's "Thirty." This list includes the most elite and skilled soldiers who fought alongside David. Uzzia is described as an Ashterathite, which likely indicates he was from the city of Ashtaroth, a Levitical city in the territory of Manasseh.
