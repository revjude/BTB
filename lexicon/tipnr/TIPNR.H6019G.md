Amram was a Levite, the son of Kohath and father of Moses, Aaron, and Miriam \([[Exod.6.18|Exo.6.18]],20; [[Num.26.58|Num.26.58]],59; [[1Chr.6.2|1Ch.6.2]],3; 23.12,13\). He married his father's sister Jochebed \(Exo.6.20\). Amram's family belonged to the Kohathite branch of Levites \([[Num.3.19|Num.3.19]],27; [[1Chr.6.18|1Ch.6.18]]; 24.20; 26.23\). As the father of Moses and Aaron, Amram played a significant role in the history of Israel, although little is recorded about his own life.
