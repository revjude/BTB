<small>*Male • Son of Helez • Son of Helez, a descendant of Judah*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eleasah</span> was the son of Helez, a descendant of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.2.39|1Ch.2.39]]; 
- only referred to as Eleasah **<big>[[H0501|אֶלְעָשָׂה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Helez **<big>[[H2503|חֶלֶץ]]</big>**; 
-  father of Sismai **<big>[[H5581|סִסְמַי]]</big>**.

Eleasah is mentioned in [[1Chr.2.39|1 Chronicles 2:39]]-40 as the son of Helez, who was a descendant of Judah through Jerahmeel. Eleasah is listed in the genealogy of Judah, which traces the lineage from Judah to David. He is noted as the father of Sismai and the grandfather of Shallum.
