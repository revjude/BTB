<small>*Male • Son of Libni • Levite of the family of Merari.*</small>

Levite living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shimei</span> was a Levite of the family of Merari, mentioned in the genealogy of the Levites.
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.6.29|1Ch.6.29]]; 
- only referred to as Shimei **<big>[[H8096|שִׁמְעִי]]</big>**; 
- a son of Libni **<big>[[H3845|לִבְנִי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Uzzah **<big>[[H5798|עֻזָּא]]</big>**.

Shimei is mentioned in [[1Chr.6.29|1 Chronicles 6:29]] as part of the genealogy of the Levites, specifically within the family of Merari. The Merarites were one of the three main divisions of the Levites, along with the Kohathites and Gershonites. As a Levite, Shimei would have had duties related to the tabernacle or temple service, although his specific role is not detailed in the text. The genealogy in this chapter focuses on tracing the lineage of the Levites and their respective families.
