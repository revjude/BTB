Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hashum</span> was an ancestor of exiles who returned to Jerusalem from Babylon. \([[Ezra.2.19|Ezr.2.19]]; 10.33; [[Neh.7.22|Neh.7.22]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at [[Ezra.2.19|Ezr.2.19]]; 
- only referred to as Hashum **<big>[[H2828|חָשֻׁם]]</big>**.

Hashum is mentioned as an ancestor of a group of exiles who returned to Jerusalem from Babylonian captivity. In the lists of returnees recorded in Ezra and Nehemiah, the descendants of Hashum are numbered at 223 \([[Ezra.2.19|Ezra 2:19]]\) and 328 \([[Neh.7.22|Nehemiah 7:22]]\) respectively. Later, in the time of Ezra, some of the descendants of Hashum were found to have married foreign women and pledged to put them away \([[Ezra.10.33|Ezra 10:33]]\). No further information about Hashum is provided in the Bible. \([[Ezra.2.19|Ezr.2.19]]; 10.33; [[Neh.7.22|Neh.7.22]]\).
