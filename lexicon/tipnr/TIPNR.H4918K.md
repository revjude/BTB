Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Meshullam</span>, a Benjamite, was among those who lived in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile \([[1Chr.9.7|1Ch.9.7]]\).
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[1Chr.9.7|1Ch.9.7]]; 
- only referred to as Meshullam<[[H4918K]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ֻׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ם</span>\); 
- a son of Hodaviah<[[H1938I]]>; 
-  father of Sallu<[[H5543D]]>.

Meshullam is mentioned in the list of Benjamites who lived in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile \([[1Chr.9.7|1Ch.9.7]]\). He is listed along with Sallu, Ibneiah, Elah, and Meshullam, who were descendants of Jeshaiah, the son of Hodaviah, the son of Hassenuah.
