<small>*Male • Son of Kish • A Levite of the Merari clan.*</small>

Levite living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jerahmeel</span> was a Levite of the family of Merari, as recorded in the genealogy in 1 Chronicles.
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.24.29|1Ch.24.29]]; 
- only referred to as Jerahmeel **<big>[[H3396|יְרַחְמְאֵל]]</big>**; 
- a son of Kish **<big>[[H7027|קִישׁ]]</big>**.

Jerahmeel is briefly mentioned in the genealogy of the Levites in 1 Chronicles. He is described as a descendant of Merari, one of the three main clans of the Levites. The Merarites were responsible for the transportation and maintenance of the tabernacle's framework and related items during the Israelites' wilderness wanderings. However, no specific details are provided about Jerahmeel's role or life within the Merari clan.
