Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shimei</span> was a descendant of Reuben and the son of Gog, mentioned in the genealogy of the Reubenites.
- A man from the tribe of Reuben  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.5.4|1Ch.5.4]]; 
- only referred to as Shimei<[[H8096L]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ִׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\);

-  father of Micah<[[H4318I]]>.

Shimei, the son of Gog, is mentioned in [[1Chr.5.4|1 Chronicles 5:4]] as part of the genealogy of the tribe of Reuben. He is listed among the descendants of Reuben, the firstborn son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\). The genealogy in this chapter traces the lineage of the Reubenites, emphasizing their ancestry and descendants. Apart from being mentioned in this genealogical record, no other details about Shimei's life or role within the tribe are provided in the Bible.
