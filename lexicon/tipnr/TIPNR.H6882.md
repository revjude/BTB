Zorathites The Zorathites were a clan of Judah descended from Shobal son of Hur \([[1Chr.2.53|1 Chronicles 2:53]]; 4:2\). They are listed among the descendants of Judah who lived in Kirjath-jearim \([[1Chr.2.53|1 Chronicles 2:53]]\). The Zorathites are also mentioned as being related to the Manahethites, another Judahite clan \([[1Chr.2.54|1 Chronicles 2:54]]\).
