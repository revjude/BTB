<small>*Male • Father of Ethan • Father of wise men surpassed in wisdom by Solomon.*</small>

Man living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Mahol</span> was the father of wise men whom Solomon surpassed in wisdom \([[1Kgs.4.31|1Ki.4.31]]\).
- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Kgs.4.31|1Ki.4.31]]; 
- only referred to as Mahol<[[H4235]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>חו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>\); 
-  father of Ethan<[[H0387G]]>, Heman<[[H1968G]]>, Calcol<[[H3633G]]>, Darda<[[H1862]]>.

Mahol is mentioned in the context of describing King Solomon's wisdom. He was the father of Ethan the Ezrahite, Heman, Calcol, and Darda, who were known for their wisdom. However, Solomon's wisdom surpassed that of these men and all the wisdom of Egypt.
