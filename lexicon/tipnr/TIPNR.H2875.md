Man living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tebah</span> was a son of Abraham's brother Nahor and his concubine Reumah.
- A man living at the time of the Patriarchs, only mentioned at [[Gen.22.24|Gen.22.24]]; 
- only referred to as Tebah<[[H2875]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ט</span>ֶ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>\); 
-  son of Nahor<[[H5152H]]> and Reumah<[[H7208]]>; 
- a brother of Gaham<[[H1514]]>, Tahash<[[H8477]]> and Maacah<[[H4601G]]>; 
-  half-brother of: Uz<[[H5780H]]>, Buz<[[H0938G]]>, Kemuel<[[H7055G]]>, Chesed<[[H3777]]>, Hazo<[[H2375]]>, Pildash<[[H6394]]>, Jidlaph<[[H3044]]> and Bethuel<[[H1328A]]>.

Tebah is mentioned in [[Gen.22.24|Genesis 22:24]] as a son of Nahor and his concubine Reumah. Nahor was Abraham's brother, making Tebah Abraham's nephew. Tebah is listed along with his brothers Gaham, Tahash, and Maacah. No other details are provided about Tebah's life or descendants. As a son of Nahor, he was born in Mesopotamia, where Abraham and his family originated.
