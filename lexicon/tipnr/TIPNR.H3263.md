<small>*Male • Son of Shaharaim • A descendant of Shaharaim from the tribe of Benjamin*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jeuz</span> was a Benjamite, born to Shaharaim in the country of Moab \([[1Chr.8.10|1Ch.8.10]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.10|1Ch.8.10]]; 
- only referred to as Jeuz<[[H3263]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>עו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ץ</span>\); 
-  son of Shaharaim<[[H7842]]> and Hodesh<[[H2321]]>; 
- a brother of Jobab<[[H3103J]]>, Zibia<[[H6644]]>, Mesha<[[H4331]]>, Malcam<[[H4445A]]>, Sachia<[[H7634]]>, Mirmah<[[H4821]]>, Abitub<[[H0036]]> and Elpaal<[[H0508]]>.

Jeuz is briefly mentioned in [[1Chr.8.10|1 Chronicles 8:10]] as one of the sons of Shaharaim, a Benjamite. The text states that Shaharaim had children in the country of Moab after he had divorced his wives Hushim and Baara. Jeuz is listed among the children born to Shaharaim in Moab.
