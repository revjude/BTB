<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jehozabad</span>, the son of Shomer, was one of the servants who conspired against and killed King Joash of Judah.
- A man of the Moabites living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[2Kgs.12.21|2Ki.12.21]]; 
- only referred to as Jehozabad **<big>[[H3075|יְהוֹזָבָד]]</big>**; 
- a son of Shomer **<big>[[H7763|שׁוֹמֵר]]</big>**.

Jehozabad, the son of Shomer, was one of the servants who conspired against King Joash of Judah. Along with Jozabad, the son of Shimeath, Jehozabad murdered Joash in his bed as revenge for the execution of Zechariah, the son of Jehoiada the priest. The assassination took place at the house of Millo, on the road that goes down to Silla. Joash's son Amaziah succeeded him as king and executed the conspirators.
