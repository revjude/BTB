Zuar Zuar was an Israelite man of the tribe of Issachar. He was the father of Nethanel, who was chosen as the leader of the tribe of Issachar during the census and organization of Israel in the wilderness \([[Num.1.8|Numbers 1:8]]; 2:5\). Nethanel presented the offering for the tribe of Issachar at the dedication of the tabernacle \([[Num.7.18|Numbers 7:18]],23\). He also led the tribe of Issachar in the order of march through the wilderness \([[Num.10.15|Numbers 10:15]]\).
