<small>*Male • Son of Bela • Descendant of Benjamin, son of Bela*</small>

Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Abishua</span> was a descendant of Benjamin and one of the sons of Bela mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.4|1Ch.8.4]]; 
- only referred to as Abishua **<big>[[H0050|אֲבִישׁוּעַ]]</big>**; 
-  son of Bela **<big>[[H1106|בֶּלַע]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Ard **<big>[[H0714|אַרְדְּ]]</big>**, Naaman **<big>[[H5283|נַעֲמָן]]</big>**, Ezbon **<big>[[H0675|אֶצְבּוֹן]]</big>**, Uzzi **<big>[[H5813|עֻזִּי]]</big>**, Uzziel **<big>[[H5816|עֻזִּיאֵל]]</big>**, Jerimoth **<big>[[H3406|יְרִימוֹת]]</big>**, Iri **<big>[[H5901|עִירִי]]</big>**, Addar **<big>[[H0146|אַדָּר]]</big>**, Gera **<big>[[H1617|גֵּרָא]]</big>**, Abihud **<big>[[H0031|אֲבִיהוּד]]</big>** and Ahoah **<big>[[H0265|אֲחוֹחַ]]</big>**.

Abishua is mentioned briefly in [[1Chr.8.4|1 Chronicles 8:4]] as one of the sons of Bela, who was a son of Benjamin, the patriarch of one of the twelve tribes of Israel. He is listed in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin, which traces the descendants of Benjamin.
