<small>*Male • David's warrior • A Gadite, father of Mibhar, one of David's mighty men.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hagri</span> was the father of Mibhar, one of David's mighty men from the tribe of Gad.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.5.10|1Ch.5.10]]; 
- referred to as Hagri or Hagrite **<big>[[H1905|הַגְרִי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Mibhar **<big>[[H4006|מִבְחָר]]</big>**.

Hagri is mentioned in [[1Chr.11.38|1 Chronicles 11:38]] as the father of Mibhar, who was one of King David's mighty men. Mibhar is described as a Gadite, indicating that Hagri was also from the tribe of Gad. The Gadites were known for their bravery and military prowess, as they were among the first to join David when he was fleeing from King Saul \(1 Chronicles 12:8-15\).
