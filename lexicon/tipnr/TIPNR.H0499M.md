<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eleazar</span> was a priest or Levite during the time of Nehemiah.
- A priest living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.12.42|Neh.12.42]]; 
- only referred to as Eleazar **<big>[[H0499|אֶלְעָזָר]]</big>**.

Eleazar is mentioned in [[Neh.12.42|Nehemiah 12:42]] among the priests and Levites who participated in the dedication of the wall of Jerusalem during Nehemiah's time. He is listed along with other prominent figures such as Maaseiah, Shemaiah, and Uzzi. Eleazar's inclusion in this list suggests he was a significant priest or Levite, but no additional information is provided about his specific role or responsibilities.
