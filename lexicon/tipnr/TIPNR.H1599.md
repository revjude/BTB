Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ginnethon</span>, a priest, was among those who sealed the covenant during Nehemiah's reforms.
- A priest living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at [[Neh.10.6|Neh.10.6]]; 
- referred to as Ginnethon or Ginnethoi \(KJV= Ginnetho, NIV= Ginnethon\) <[[H1599]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ג</span>ִּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ְּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>תו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\).

Ginnethon was a priest who lived during the time of Nehemiah. He is mentioned in [[Neh.10.6|Nehemiah 10:6]] as one of the priests who sealed the covenant, pledging to follow the Law of God. In [[Neh.12.16|Nehemiah 12:16]], he is listed as the head of the priestly family of Meshillemoth during the high priesthood of Joiakim. Ginnethon is also mentioned in [[Neh.12.4|Nehemiah 12:4]] among the priests who returned from the Babylonian exile with Zerubbabel.
