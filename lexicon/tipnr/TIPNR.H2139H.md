<small>*Male • Son of Hammuel • Simeonite descendant of Mishma*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zaccur</span> was a Simeonite mentioned in the genealogy of Mishma.
- A man from the tribe of Simeon living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.26|1Ch.4.26]]; 
- only referred to as Zaccur **<big>[[H2139|זַכּוּר]]</big>**; 
- a son of Hammuel **<big>[[H2536|חַמּוּאֵל]]</big>**; 
-  father of Shimei **<big>[[H8096|שִׁמְעִי]]</big>**.

Zaccur is listed in the genealogy of the tribe of Simeon, specifically as a descendant of Mishma. Mishma was one of the sons of Shaul, who was a son of Simeon. Zaccur is mentioned along with his brothers Hammuel and Zacchur as the sons of Mishma. No further details are provided about Zaccur's life or role within the tribe of Simeon. The inclusion of his name in the genealogical record highlights the importance of family lineage and tribal identity in ancient Israelite society.
