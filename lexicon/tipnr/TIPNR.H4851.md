Man living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Mash</span> \(or Meshech\), a son of Aram, was a grandson of Shem \([[Gen.10.23|Gen.10.23]]; [[1Chr.1.17|1Ch.1.17]]\).
- A man of the Syrians living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.10.23|Gen.10.23]]; 
- referred to as Mash<[[H4851]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ׁ\), or Meshech<[[H4902J]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ֶ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ֶׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ך</span>ְ\); 
-  son of Aram<[[H0758G]]>; 
- a brother of Uz<[[H5780G]]>, Hul<[[H2343]]> and Gether<[[H1666]]>.

Mash, also known as Meshech, was a son of Aram and a grandson of Shem, the son of Noah. He is mentioned in the genealogies of the descendants of Noah's sons after the flood \([[Gen.10.23|Genesis 10:23]]; [[1Chr.1.17|1 Chronicles 1:17]]\). The name Mash is used in Genesis, while Meshech is used in 1 Chronicles, referring to the same individual.
