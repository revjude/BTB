Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jeush</span>, a Benjamite, was a descendant of Bilhan \([[1Chr.7.10|1Ch.7.10]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.10|1Ch.7.10]]; 
- only referred to as Jeush **<big>[[H3266|יְעוּשׁ]]</big>**; 
-  son of Bilhan **<big>[[H1092|בִּלְהָן]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Benjamin **<big>[[H1144|בִּנְיָמִין]]</big>**, Ehud **<big>[[H0164|אֵהוּד]]</big>**, Chenaanah **<big>[[H3668|כְּנַעֲנָה]]</big>**, Zethan **<big>[[H2133|זֵיתָן]]</big>**, Tarshish **<big>[[H8659|תַּרְשִׁישׁ]]</big>** and Ahishahar **<big>[[H0300|אֲחִישַׁחַר]]</big>**.

Jeush is mentioned in [[1Chr.7.10|1 Chronicles 7:10]] as one of the sons of Bilhan, a descendant of Benjamin. He is listed in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin, which is given in the context of the census taken during the reign of King David.
