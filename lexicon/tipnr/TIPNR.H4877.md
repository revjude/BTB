<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Meshobab</span>, a Simeonite leader during the reign of King Hezekiah \([[1Chr.4.34|1Ch.4.34]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Simeon\(?\) living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.34|1Ch.4.34]]; 
- only referred to as Meshobab<[[H4877]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>\).

Meshobab is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Simeon in the book of Chronicles \([[1Chr.4.34|1Ch.4.34]]\). He is listed among the leaders of the Simeonites during the reign of King Hezekiah of Judah. These leaders, including Meshobab, Jamlech, and Joshah, led a group of Simeonites who destroyed the remaining Amalekites and settled in their land.
