Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jair</span> was the father of Mordecai, a Benjamite mentioned in the book of Esther.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Esth.2.5|Est.2.5]]; 
- only referred to as Jair **<big>[[H2971|יָאִיר]]</big>**; 
- a son of Shimei **<big>[[H8096|שִׁמְעִי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Mordecai **<big>[[H4782|מׇרְדְּכַי]]</big>**.

Jair is briefly mentioned in [[Esth.2.5|Esther 2:5]] as the father of Mordecai, one of the main characters in the book of Esther. Jair was a Benjamite, indicating that he belonged to the tribe of Benjamin. The text states that Jair's ancestors, Kish and Shimei, had been exiled from Jerusalem to Babylon during the time of King Jeconiah of Judah.
