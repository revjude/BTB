<small>*Male • Son of Becher • Son of Becher, a Benjamite.*</small>

Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jeremoth</span> was a son of Becher and a member of the tribe of Benjamin, as mentioned in the genealogy in 1 Chronicles.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.8|1Ch.7.8]]; 
- only referred to as Jeremoth **<big>[[H3406|יְרִימוֹת]]</big>**; 
-  son of Becher **<big>[[H1071|בֶכֶר]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Zemirah **<big>[[H2160|זְמִירָה]]</big>**, Joash **<big>[[H3135|יוֹעָשׁ]]</big>**, Eliezer **<big>[[H0461|אֱלִיעֶזֶר]]</big>**, Elioenai **<big>[[H0454|אֶלְיְהוֹעֵינַי]]</big>**, Omri **<big>[[H6018|עׇמְרִי]]</big>**, Abijah **<big>[[H0029|אֲבִיָּה]]</big>**, Anathoth **<big>[[H6068|עֲנָתוֹת]]</big>** and Alemeth **<big>[[H5964|עָלֶמֶת]]</big>**.

Jeremoth is mentioned briefly in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin in 1 Chronicles. He is listed as one of the sons of Becher, who was a son of Benjamin.
