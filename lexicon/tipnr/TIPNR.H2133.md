Zethan was a Benjamite mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin in 1 Chronicles 7. He was a son of Bilhan and a descendant of Jediael, one of the sons of Benjamin. Zethan is listed along with his brothers Jeush and Benjamin. This genealogical record provides insight into the family structure and lineage of the tribe of Benjamin.
