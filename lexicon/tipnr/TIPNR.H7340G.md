<small>*Rehob\_2 • City on the border of Asher*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Rehob</span> was a city on the northern border of the territory allotted to the tribe of Asher \(Jos. 19:28\).
- A location near Hammath<[[H2575A]]> only mentioned at [[Josh.19.28|Jos.19.28]]; 
- only referred to as Rehob<[[H7340G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>חו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>\).
