Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eluzai</span>, a Benjamite warrior, was one of the mighty men who joined David at Ziklag \([[1Chr.12.5|1Ch.12.5]]\).
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.12.5|1Ch.12.5]]; 
- only referred to as Eluzai **<big>[[H0498|אֶלְעוּזַי]]</big>**.

Eluzai is mentioned in the list of Benjamite warriors who joined David at Ziklag when he was fleeing from King Saul \([[1Chr.12.5|1Ch.12.5]]\). He is described as one of the mighty men, indicating his skill and valor in battle. The Benjamites were known for their bravery and prowess as warriors, and Eluzai's presence among David's supporters demonstrates the loyalty and strength he brought to David's cause. The text does not provide any additional information about Eluzai's background, his specific role in David's army, or his later life. His inclusion in the list of David's mighty men highlights the diverse group of warriors who rallied around David during his time of exile and played a crucial role in his rise to power as king of Israel.
