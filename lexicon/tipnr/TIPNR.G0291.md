<small>*Male • Believer greeted by Paul in Rome*</small>

Man living at the time of the New Testament

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ampliatus</span>, a beloved fellow believer greeted by Paul in his letter to the Romans.
- A man living at the time of the New Testament, only mentioned at [[Rom.16.8|Rom.16.8]]; 
- only referred to as Ampliatus **<big>[[G0291|Ἀμπλίας]]</big>**.

Ampliatus is mentioned in [[Rom.16.8|Romans 16:8]] as a believer in Rome whom Paul greets and describes as "my beloved in the Lord." This brief mention suggests that Ampliatus was a dear friend or fellow worker of Paul and a member of the Christian community in Rome.
