Lotan was a son of Seir the Horite and a chief of the land of Edom \([[Gen.36.20|Gen.36.20]],29; [[1Chr.1.38|1Ch.1.38]]\). He had a sister named Timna \(Gen.36.22; [[1Chr.1.39|1Ch.1.39]]\). Lotan is listed among the descendants of Esau who settled in the region of Seir \([[Gen.36.20|Gen.36.20]]-30; [[1Chr.1.38|1Ch.1.38]]-42\). The Horites were the original inhabitants of Seir before Esau's descendants displaced them \(Deut.2.12\). As a chief of Edom, Lotan likely held a position of authority and influence among his people.
