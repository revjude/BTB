Priest living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Amzi</span> was a priest who lived in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile.
- A priest living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.11.12|Neh.11.12]]; 
- only referred to as Amzi<[[H0557H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>צ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
- a son of Zechariah<[[H2148X]]>; 
-  father of Pelaliah<[[H6421]]>.

Amzi was a priest who resided in Jerusalem after the return from the Babylonian exile. He is mentioned in the list of priests and Levites who settled in Jerusalem, as recorded in the book of Nehemiah. As a priest, Amzi would have been responsible for performing religious duties in the rebuilt temple and serving the spiritual needs of the people.
