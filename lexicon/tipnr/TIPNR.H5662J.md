Obadiah was one of the five sons of Izrahiah, a descendant of Issachar \(1 Chronicles 7:3\). His brothers were Michael, Ishiah, Joel, and Ishiah. The sons of Izrahiah were described as mighty men of valor in their generations, indicating their strength and military prowess. No further details about Obadiah's life or role are provided in the Bible. His mention in the genealogy of Issachar highlights the importance of family records and the preservation of tribal heritage.
