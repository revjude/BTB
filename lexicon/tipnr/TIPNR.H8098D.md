Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shemaiah</span>, a descendant of Reuben, son of Joel.
- A man from the tribe of Reuben  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.5.4|1Ch.5.4]]; 
- only referred to as Shemaiah **<big>[[H8098|שְׁמַעְיָה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Joel **<big>[[H3100|יוֹאֵל]]</big>**; 
-  father of Gog **<big>[[H1463|גּוֹג]]</big>**.

Shemaiah is mentioned in [[1Chr.5.4|1 Chronicles 5:4]] as a descendant of Reuben, the firstborn son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\). He is listed in the genealogy of the Reubenites as the son of Joel. The genealogy traces the lineage of the Reubenites who lived in the region of Gilead.
