<small>*Male • Returned builder • Goldsmith who helped repair the wall of Jerusalem.*</small>

Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Malchijah</span>, a goldsmith, repaired a section of Jerusalem's wall near the Levites \([[Neh.3.31|Neh.3.31]]\).
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.31|Neh.3.31]]; 
- only referred to as Malchijah<[[H4441O]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ִּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\).

Malchijah, a goldsmith, was among those who worked on repairing the wall of Jerusalem during the time of Nehemiah. He restored a section of the wall next to the house of the temple servants and the merchants, opposite the Inspection Gate and as far as the room above the corner \([[Neh.3.31|Nehemiah 3:31]]\).
