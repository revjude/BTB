<small>*Biziothiah • A town in the Negev, allotted to Judah*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Biziothiah</span>, a town in the Negev region, was allotted to the tribe of Judah.
- A location near Beersheba<[[H0884]]> only mentioned at [[Josh.15.28|Jos.15.28]]; 
- only referred to as Biziothiah<[[H0964]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ִּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\).

Biziothiah is mentioned in [[Josh.15.28|Joshua 15:28]] as one of the towns in the Negev region that was allotted to the tribe of Judah. It is listed among other cities in the southern part of Judah's territory. The exact location of Biziothiah is not known, and it is not mentioned elsewhere in the Bible.
