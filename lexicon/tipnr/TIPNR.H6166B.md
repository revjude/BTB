<small>*Male • Son of Beriah • A Benjamite, son of Beriah.*</small>

Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Arad</span> was a Benjamite, one of the sons of Beriah.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin  living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.15|1Ch.8.15]]; 
- only referred to as Arad **<big>[[H6166|עֲרָד]]</big>**; 
-  son of Beriah **<big>[[H1283|בְּרִיעָה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Ahio **<big>[[H0283|אַחְיוֹ]]</big>**, Shashak **<big>[[H8349|שָׁשַׁק]]</big>**, Jeremoth **<big>[[H3406|יְרִימוֹת]]</big>**, Zebadiah **<big>[[H2069|זְבַדְיָה]]</big>**, Eder **<big>[[H5738|עֶדֶר]]</big>**, Michael **<big>[[H4317|מִיכָאֵל]]</big>**, Ishpah **<big>[[H3472|יִשְׁפָּה]]</big>** and Joha **<big>[[H3109|יוֹחָא]]</big>**.

Arad is mentioned only once in the Bible, in [[1Chr.8.15|1 Chronicles 8:15]], as one of the sons of Beriah, a Benjamite. He is listed among the descendants of Benjamin who lived in Jerusalem. No further information is provided about Arad in the biblical text. His name means "wild donkey" in Hebrew.
