Naaran, also known as Naarah, was a town on the border between the tribal territories of Ephraim and Benjamin \(Josh. 16:7; 1 Chr. 7:28\). It was located east of Bethel and near Jericho. Naaran is mentioned as one of the cities on the eastern border of Ephraim, along with Gezer, Michmethath, and Taanath-shiloh.
