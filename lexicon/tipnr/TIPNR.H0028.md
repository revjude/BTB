Man living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Abida</span> was a son of Midian and a grandson of Abraham through his wife Keturah.
- A man of the Midianites living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.25.4|Gen.25.4]]; 
- only referred to as Abida<[[H0028]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יד</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>\); 
-  son of Midian<[[H4080G]]>; 
- a brother of Ephah<[[H5891G]]>, Epher<[[H6081G]]>, Hanoch<[[H2585I]]> and Eldaah<[[H0420]]>.

Abida is mentioned in the genealogies of Abraham's descendants through his wife Keturah. He was one of the sons of Midian, who was a son of Abraham and Keturah \([[Gen.25.4|Genesis 25:4]]; [[1Chr.1.33|1 Chronicles 1:33]]\). The Midianites, named after Abida's father, are later mentioned in the Bible as a nomadic people living in the region near Canaan. However, no further information is provided about Abida himself or his specific role within the Midianite clan.
