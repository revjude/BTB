Hasadiah was a descendant of King Jeconiah \(also known as Jehoiachin\) of Judah. He is mentioned in the genealogy of the royal line of Judah recorded in the book of 1 Chronicles. Hasadiah was one of the sons of Zerubbabel, who was a key figure in the return of the Jews from Babylonian exile and the rebuilding of the temple in Jerusalem. No other information is provided about Hasadiah in the Bible. \([[1Chr.3.20|1Ch.3.20]]\).
