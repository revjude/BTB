Jokdeam was a town located in the hill country of Judah, as listed in [[Josh.15.56|Joshua 15:56]]. It was part of the inheritance given to the tribe of Judah during the division of the Promised Land. The town is mentioned only once in the Bible, and no additional details about its history or significance are provided. Its exact location remains unknown.
