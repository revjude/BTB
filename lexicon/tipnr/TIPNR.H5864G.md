Iyim was a place where the Israelites camped during their wilderness journey, as recorded in the book of Numbers \([[Num.33.45|Numbers 33:45]]\). It is mentioned in the list of campsites between Oboth and Dibon-gad. The exact location of Iyim is unknown, and no further information about the site is provided in the Bible.
