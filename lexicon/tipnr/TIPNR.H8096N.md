Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shimei</span> was a Benjamite descendant of Shimei, the son of Elpaal.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.21|1Ch.8.21]]; 
- only referred to as Shimei<[[H8096N]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ִׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
-  father of Jakim<[[H3356G]]>, Zichri<[[H2147H]]>, Zabdi<[[H2067H]]>, Elienai<[[H0462]]>, Zillethai<[[H6769G]]>, Eliel<[[H0447H]]>, Adaiah<[[H5718H]]>, Beraiah<[[H1256]]> and Shimrath<[[H8119]]>.

Shimei is mentioned in [[1Chr.8.21|1 Chronicles 8:21]] as part of the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin. He is listed as a descendant of Shimei, who was the son of Elpaal. This genealogy traces the lineage of several Benjamite families, particularly those who lived in Jerusalem. Apart from being mentioned in this genealogical record, no other details about this specific Shimei's life or role within the tribe are provided in the Bible.
