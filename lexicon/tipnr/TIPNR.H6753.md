Hazzelelponi is a woman mentioned in the genealogy of Judah in 1 Chronicles. She is listed among the descendants of Judah, along with her brothers Jezreel, Ishma, and Idbash. No further information is provided about Hazzelelponi in the Bible, and her name is not mentioned elsewhere in the Scriptures. \([[1Chr.4.3|1Ch.4.3]]\).
