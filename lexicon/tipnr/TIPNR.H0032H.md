<small>*Female • From  Judah\(?\) • Wife of Abishur; mother of Ahban and Molid*</small>

Woman of living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Abihail</span> was the wife of Abishur and the mother of Ahban and Molid, as mentioned in the genealogy of Judah.
- A woman from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.2.29|1Ch.2.29]]; 
- only referred to as Abihail<[[H0032H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יח</span>ַ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>\); 
- wife of Abishur<[[H0051]]>; 
-  mother of Ahban<[[H0257]]>, Molid<[[H4140]]>.

Abihail is mentioned briefly in [[1Chr.2.29|1 Chronicles 2:29]] as the wife of Abishur, who was a descendant of Judah. She is listed in the genealogy of Judah, which traces the lineage of David. Abihail and Abishur had two sons, Ahban and Molid.
