Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hazaiah</span> was an ancestor of Maaseiah, a leader who lived in Jerusalem. \([[Neh.11.5|Neh.11.5]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.11.5|Neh.11.5]]; 
- only referred to as Hazaiah<[[H2382]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
- a son of Adaiah<[[H5718M]]>; 
-  father of Col-hozeh<[[H3626H]]>.

Hazaiah is mentioned in the book of Nehemiah as an ancestor of Maaseiah, a leader who lived in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile. Maaseiah is listed among the descendants of Judah who settled in Jerusalem. No further information is provided about Hazaiah in the Bible. \([[Neh.11.5|Neh.11.5]]\).
