- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at [[2Sam.23.29|2Sa.23.29]]; 
- only referred to as Baanah<[[H1196H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ַּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
-  father of Heleb<[[H2460]]>.

Baanah the Netophathite is mentioned in the lists of David's mighty warriors found in [[2Sam.23.29|2 Samuel 23:29]] and [[1Chr.11.30|1 Chronicles 11:30]]. He is described as being from Netophah, a town in Judah. The mighty warriors were a group of elite soldiers who fought alongside David and were known for their exceptional skill and bravery in battle. Baanah's inclusion in this list indicates that he was a distinguished warrior who had proven his loyalty and valor in service to King David.
