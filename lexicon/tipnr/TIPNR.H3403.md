Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jeribai</span> was listed among David's mighty men, a group of skilled warriors who fought alongside him.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.11.46|1Ch.11.46]]; 
- only referred to as Jeribai<[[H3403]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יב</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
-  son of Elnaam<[[H0493]]>; 
- a brother of Joshaviah<[[H3145]]>.

Jeribai is mentioned briefly in 1 Chronicles as one of David's mighty men. The mighty men were a group of skilled warriors who fought alongside David and played a significant role in his military campaigns and rise to power. Jeribai's inclusion in this list suggests that he was a valiant and trusted soldier in David's army. However, no further details are provided about his specific exploits or background.
