Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zechariah</span>, a descendant of Parosh, who returned to Jerusalem with Ezra \([[Ezra.8.3|Ezr.8.3]]\).
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Ezra.8.3|Ezr.8.3]]; 
- only referred to as Zechariah<[[H2148Q]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\).

Zechariah was a descendant of Parosh who lived during the time of Ezra the scribe. When Ezra led a group of exiles back to Jerusalem from Babylon, Zechariah was among them. He is mentioned in the list of the men who returned with Ezra, grouped under the descendants of Parosh.
