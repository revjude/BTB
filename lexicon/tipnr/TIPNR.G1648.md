Man living at the time ofExile and return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eleazar</span> was an ancestor of Jesus, the son of Eliud and father of Matthan.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time ofExile and return, first mentioned at Mat.1.15; 
- only referred to as Eleazar **<big>[[G1648|Ἐλεάζαρ]]</big>**; 
- a son of Eliud **<big>[[G1664|Ἐλιούδ]]</big>**; 
-  father of Matthan **<big>[[G3157|Ματθάν]]</big>**.

Eleazar is mentioned in Matthew 1:15 in the genealogy of Jesus Christ. He was the son of Eliud and the father of Matthan. Eleazar is listed as part of the lineage from Abraham to Joseph, the husband of Mary, mother of Jesus.
