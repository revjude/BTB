Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Malchijah</span>, a priest, participated in the dedication of Jerusalem's wall \([[Neh.12.42|Neh.12.42]]\).
- A priest living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.12.42|Neh.12.42]]; 
- only referred to as Malchijah **<big>[[H4441|מַלְכִּיָּה]]</big>**.

Malchijah was a priest who lived during the time of Nehemiah. He was among the priests and Levites who participated in the dedication of the wall of Jerusalem after it had been rebuilt. During the dedication ceremony, Malchijah and other priests played musical instruments and offered thanksgiving to God \([[Neh.12.42|Nehemiah 12:42]]\).
