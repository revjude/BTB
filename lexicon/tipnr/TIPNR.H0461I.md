Eliezer is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin in [[1Chr.7.8|1 Chronicles 7:8]]. He was a ; son of Becher, who was one of the sons of Benjamin. The genealogy lists the heads of families and clans in the tribe of Benjamin.
