<small>*Male • Son of Ulam • A descendant of Manasseh through Machir and Gilead.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Bedan</span> was a descendant of Manasseh, listed in the genealogy of Machir and Gilead.
- A man from the tribe of Manasseh living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.17|1Ch.7.17]]; 
- only referred to as Bedan **<big>[[H0917|בְּדָן]]</big>**; 
- a son of Ulam **<big>[[H0198|אוּלָם]]</big>**.

Bedan is briefly mentioned in [[1Chr.7.17|1 Chronicles 7:17]] as a descendant of Manasseh, the son of Joseph. The context of the passage is the genealogy of the tribe of Manasseh, focusing on the descendants of Machir and Gilead. Bedan is listed as the son of Ulam, who was the son of Gilead's daughter.
