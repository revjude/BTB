Edomite living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Mibzar</span> was one of the chiefs of Edom, descended from Esau \([[Gen.36.42|Gen.36.42]]; [[1Chr.1.53|1Ch.1.53]]\).
- A man living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.36.42|Gen.36.42]]; 
- only referred to as Mibzar **<big>[[H4014|מִבְצָר]]</big>**.

Mibzar is mentioned in the lists of the chiefs of Edom in the books of Genesis and 1 Chronicles \([[Gen.36.42|Gen.36.42]]; [[1Chr.1.53|1Ch.1.53]]\). He was one of the chiefs who came from the descendants of Esau, Jacob's brother. The Edomites were the offspring of Esau and lived in the region southeast of Israel. The chiefs of Edom are listed to show the political and social structure of the Edomite people. No specific details are provided about Mibzar's life, reign, or achievements. His inclusion in the list of chiefs suggests he held a position of authority and leadership among the Edomites.
