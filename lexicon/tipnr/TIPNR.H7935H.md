- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.24.11|1Ch.24.11]]; 
- only referred to as Shecaniah<[[H7935H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\).

Shecaniah was a Levite priest who served during the reign of King David. He was the head of the tenth priestly division as organized by David for service in the temple \([[1Chr.24.11|1 Chronicles 24:11]]\).
