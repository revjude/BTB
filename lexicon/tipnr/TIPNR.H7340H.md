<small>*Rehob\_3 • Levitical city in Asher*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Rehob</span> was a Levitical city within the territory of Asher \(Jos. 19:30; 21:31; 1 Ch. 6:75\).
- A location near Helkath **<big>[[H2520|חֶלְקַת]]</big>** first mentioned at [[Josh.19.30|Jos.19.30]]; 
- only referred to as Rehob **<big>[[H7340|רְחֹב]]</big>**.

Rehob was a city allotted to the tribe of Asher \(Jos. 19:30\) and designated as a Levitical city, given to the descendants of Gershon \(Jos. 21:31; 1 Ch. 6:75\). However, the people of Asher did not drive out the Canaanite inhabitants of Rehob, so they continued to live among them \(Jdg. 1:31\). The exact location of this city is uncertain.
