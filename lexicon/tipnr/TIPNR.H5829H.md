Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ezer</span> was a Gadite warrior who joined David at Ziklag when he was fleeing from King Saul.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.12.9|1Ch.12.9]]; 
- only referred to as Ezer<[[H5829H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֵ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>\).

Ezer is mentioned in [[1Chr.12.9|1 Chronicles 12:9]] as one of the Gadite warriors who joined David at Ziklag when he was fleeing from King Saul. The Gadites were known for their bravery and skill in battle, and they were among the first to support David during his time of exile \(1 Chronicles 12:8\). Ezer and his fellow Gadites were described as "mighty and experienced warriors" who were "expert with shield and spear" \(1 Chronicles 12:8\). They played a significant role in helping David establish his kingdom and were known for their loyalty and valor.
