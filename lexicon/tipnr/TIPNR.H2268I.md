Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Heber</span> was a son of Mered, from the tribe of Judah. \([[1Chr.4.18|1Ch.4.18]]\)
- A man from the tribe of Judah\(?\) living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.18|1Ch.4.18]]; 
- only referred to as Heber<[[H2268I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ֶ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>\); 
-  son of Mered<[[H4778]]>\(?\) and Jehudijah<[[H3057]]>; 
- a brother of Jered<[[H3382H]]> and Jekuthiel<[[H3354]]>; 
-  half-brother of: Miriam<[[H4813H]]>\(?\), Shammai<[[H8060I]]>\(?\) and Ishbah<[[H3431]]>\(?\); 
-  father or founder of Soco<[[H7755I]]>.

Heber is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah in 1 Chronicles as a son of Mered. Mered was married to Bithiah, the daughter of Pharaoh, and had two sons, Miriam and Shammai, in addition to Heber. No further information is provided about this Heber in the Bible. \([[1Chr.4.18|1Ch.4.18]]\).
