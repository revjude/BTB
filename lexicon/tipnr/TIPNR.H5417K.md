Nethanel was one of the sons of Obed-Edom who was a Levite \([[1Chr.26.4|1 Chronicles 26:4]]\). His father Obed-Edom had been blessed by God when the ark of the covenant stayed at his house \(2 Samuel 6:11\). Nethanel would have served as a Levite along with his brothers.
