Levite living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Haggiah</span>, a Levite descended from Merari, is mentioned in the genealogy of the Levites in 1 Chronicles.
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.6.30|1Ch.6.30]]; 
- only referred to as Haggiah<[[H2293]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ג</span>ִּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
- a son of Shimea<[[H8092G]]>; 
-  father of Asaiah<[[H6222I]]>.

Haggiah is mentioned in [[1Chr.6.30|1 Chronicles 6:30]] as part of the genealogy of the Levites, specifically the descendants of Merari, one of the three sons of Levi. He is listed as the son of Shimea and the father of Asaiah. The Levites were the tribe chosen by God to assist the priests in the tabernacle and temple services. The descendants of Merari were responsible for the structural components of the tabernacle, such as the frames, crossbars, posts, and bases \(Numbers 3:36\).
