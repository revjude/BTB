Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Malluch</span>, a priest, was among those who sealed the covenant during Nehemiah's time \([[Neh.10.4|Neh.10.4]]; 12.2,14\).
- A priest living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at [[Neh.10.4|Neh.10.4]]; 
- referred to as Malluch **<big>[[H4409|מַלּוּךְ]]</big>**, or Mallichi \(=Ketiv. Qere, KJV= Melicu, NIV= Malluk\)  **<big>[[H4409|מַלּוּךְ]]</big>**.

Malluch was a priest who lived during the time of Nehemiah. He was among the leaders who sealed the covenant between the people of Israel and God, pledging to follow the Law of Moses, keep the Sabbath holy, and support the temple service \([[Neh.10.4|Nehemiah 10:4]]\). Malluch is also mentioned in the list of priests who returned from Babylonian exile with Zerubbabel \([[Neh.12.2|Nehemiah 12:2]]\) and in the list of priestly families during the high priesthood of Joiakim \([[Neh.12.14|Nehemiah 12:14]]\).
