- only referred to as Shelomith<[[H8019I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ית</span>\);

Shelomith is listed in [[1Chr.3.19|1 Chronicles 3:19]] as one of the sons of Zerubbabel. Zerubbabel was the grandson of King Jehoiachin of Judah and served as the governor of Judah during the return from Babylonian exile. He played a key role in rebuilding the temple in Jerusalem. Shelomith is mentioned along with his brothers Meshullam, Hananiah, Hashubah, Ohel, Berechiah, Hasadiah, and Jushab-Hesed.
