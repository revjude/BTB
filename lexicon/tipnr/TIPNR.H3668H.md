Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Chenaanah</span> was a son of Bilhan and descendant of Benjamin. \([[1Chr.7.10|1Ch.7.10]]\)
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.10|1Ch.7.10]]; 
- only referred to as Chenaanah **<big>[[H3668|כְּנַעֲנָה]]</big>**; 
-  son of Bilhan **<big>[[H1092|בִּלְהָן]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Jeush **<big>[[H3266|יְעוּשׁ]]</big>**, Benjamin **<big>[[H1144|בִּנְיָמִין]]</big>**, Ehud **<big>[[H0164|אֵהוּד]]</big>**, Zethan **<big>[[H2133|זֵיתָן]]</big>**, Tarshish **<big>[[H8659|תַּרְשִׁישׁ]]</big>** and Ahishahar **<big>[[H0300|אֲחִישַׁחַר]]</big>**.

Chenaanah is mentioned in [[1Chr.7.10|1 Chronicles 7:10]] as one of the sons of Bilhan, a descendant of Benjamin. He is listed among the heads of families in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin. This Chenaanah is different from the false prophet Zedekiah son of Chenaanah mentioned in 1 Kings 22 and 2 Chronicles 18.
