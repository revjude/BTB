Edomite living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Iram</span> was an Edomite chief, a descendant of Esau \([[Gen.36.43|Gen.36.43]], [[1Chr.1.54|1Ch.1.54]]\).
- A man living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.36.43|Gen.36.43]]; 
- only referred to as Iram **<big>[[H5902|עִירָם]]</big>**.

Iram is listed as one of the chiefs of Edom, a descendant of Esau, in [[Gen.36.43|Genesis 36:43]] and [[1Chr.1.54|1 Chronicles 1:54]]. The passages provide a record of the chiefs who came from Esau, who was also called Edom. Iram is named along with other chiefs such as Magdiel and Kenaz. As a chief, he likely had a leadership role among a clan of the Edomites.
