Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ahi</span>, son of Abdiel, was a chief of the Gadites. \([[1Chr.5.15|1Ch.5.15]]\)
- A man from the tribe of Gad  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.5.15|1Ch.5.15]]; 
- only referred to as Ahi **<big>[[H0277|אֲחִי]]</big>**; 
- a son of Abdiel **<big>[[H5661|עַבְדִיאֵל]]</big>**.

Ahi is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Gad. He was the son of Abdiel and is described as the chief of his father's household. This suggests that Ahi held a leadership position among the Gadites.
