<small>*Male • Son of Ehud • Benjamite exile born in Moab*</small>

Man living before Israel's Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Uzza</span> was a Benjamite born in Moab during the exile.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living before Israel's Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.7|1Ch.8.7]]; 
- only referred to as Uzza<[[H5798H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֻ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>\); 
-  son of Ehud<[[H0164G]]>; 
- a brother of Naaman<[[H5283J]]>, Ahijah<[[H0281K]]>, Gera<[[H1617J]]> and Ahihud<[[H0284]]>.

Uzza was a Benjamite man who was born in the land of Moab. He is mentioned in the genealogy of the descendants of Benjamin, which includes those who were born during the time when many Israelites were living in Moab. The context suggests this was likely during the Babylonian exile. Uzza and his brothers Naaman, Ahijah, and Gera were all born in Moab before Gera moved the family away from there.
