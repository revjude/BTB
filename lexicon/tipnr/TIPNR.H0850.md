Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eshton</span> was the son of Mehir and the father of Beth-rapha, Paseah, and Tehinnah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.4.11|1Ch.4.11]]; 
- only referred to as Eshton<[[H0850]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\); 
- a son of Mehir<[[H4243]]>; 
-  father of Beth-rapha<[[H1051]]>, Paseah<[[H6454G]]>, Tehinnah<[[H8468]]>.

Eshton was a descendant of Judah through Chelub, mentioned in [[1Chr.4.11|1 Chronicles 4:11]]-12. He was the son of Mehir and the father of Beth-rapha, Paseah, and Tehinnah. Tehinnah was noted as the father of Ir-nahash.
