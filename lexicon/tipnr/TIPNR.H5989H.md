Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ammihud</span> was the father of Shemuel, the leader from the tribe of Simeon appointed to help divide the land of Canaan.
- A man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[Num.34.20|Num.34.20]]; 
- only referred to as Ammihud **<big>[[H5989|עַמִּיהוּד]]</big>**; 
-  father of Shemuel **<big>[[H8050|שְׁמוּאֵל]]</big>**.

Ammihud was the father of Shemuel, a leader from the tribe of Simeon. When the Lord commanded Moses to appoint leaders from each tribe to help divide the land of Canaan among the Israelites, Shemuel son of Ammihud was chosen to represent the tribe of Simeon in this important task.
