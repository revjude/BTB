<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zur</span> was a son of Abiel and a descendant of Benjamin.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.8.30|1Ch.8.30]]; 
- only referred to as Zur **<big>[[H6698|צוּר]]</big>**; 
-  son of Abiel **<big>[[H0022|אֲבִיאֵל]]</big>** and Maacah **<big>[[H4601|מַעֲכָה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Kish **<big>[[H7027|קִישׁ]]</big>**, Ner **<big>[[H5369|נֵר]]</big>**, Abdon **<big>[[H5658|עַבְדוֹן]]</big>**, Baal **<big>[[H1168|בַּעַל]]</big>**, Nadab **<big>[[H5070|נָדָב]]</big>**, Gedor **<big>[[H1446|גְדֹר]]</big>**, Ahio **<big>[[H0283|אַחְיוֹ]]</big>**, Zecher **<big>[[H2144|זֶכֶר]]</big>** and Mikloth **<big>[[H4732|מִקְלוֹת]]</big>**.

Zur Zur was a Benjamite, one of the sons of Abiel \([[1Chr.8.30|1 Chronicles 8:30]]; 9:36\). He is listed in the genealogy of Saul, the first king of Israel, who was also from the tribe of Benjamin.
