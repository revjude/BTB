<small>*Male • Issachar leader • Son of Uzzi, an Issacharite*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Izrahiah</span>, a son of Uzzi, was a descendant of Issachar and a clan head.
- A man from the tribe of Issachar living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at 1Ch.7.3; 
- only referred to as Izrahiah **<big>[[H3156|יִזְרַחְיָה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Uzzi **<big>[[H5813|עֻזִּי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Michael **<big>[[H4317|מִיכָאֵל]]</big>**, Obadiah **<big>[[H5662|עֹבַדְיָה]]</big>**, Joel **<big>[[H3100|יוֹאֵל]]</big>** and Isshiah **<big>[[H3449|יִשִּׁיָּה]]</big>**.

Izrahiah is mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:3 as a son of Uzzi, who was a descendant of Issachar. He is listed among the clan heads of Issachar, indicating that he held a leadership position within the tribe. The biblical text notes that Izrahiah had five sons: Michael, Obadiah, Joel, Isshiah, and Isshiah. These sons were described as brave warriors and chiefs, suggesting that Izrahiah's family held significant influence within the tribe of Issachar.
