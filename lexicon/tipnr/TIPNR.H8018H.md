<small>*Male • Returned divorcee • Israelite who married a foreign wife*</small>

Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shelemiah</span>, another Israelite who married a foreign wife during Ezra's time.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Ezra.10.41|Ezr.10.41]]; 
- only referred to as Shelemiah<[[H8018H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ֶׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\).

Shelemiah is listed in [[Ezra.10.41|Ezra 10:41]] among the Israelites who had married foreign women after returning from Babylonian exile. During Ezra's reform, he was one of those who agreed to send away his foreign wife to purify the community.
