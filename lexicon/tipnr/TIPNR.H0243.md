Aznoth-tabor is mentioned in [[Josh.19.34|Joshua 19:34]] as one of the border towns of the territory allotted to the tribe of Naphtali. It is described as being located near Mount Tabor, a prominent mountain in Lower Galilee. The name "Aznoth-tabor" likely means "the ears \(or peaks\) of Tabor," possibly referring to the town's location on the slopes or near the base of the mountain.
