<small>*Male • Son of Shobal • Father of half the inhabitants of Manuhoth.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Haroeh</span> or Reaiah was the father of half the inhabitants of Manuhoth. \([[1Chr.2.52|1Ch.2.52]]; 4.2\)
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.2.52|1Ch.2.52]]; 
- referred to as Haroeh<[[H7204]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\), or Reaiah<[[H7211I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
- a son of Shobal<[[H7732H]]>; 
-  father of Jahath<[[H3189G]]>.

Haroeh, also known as Reaiah, was the father of half the inhabitants of Manuhoth. Manuhoth was likely a town or village in Judah. Haroeh is mentioned in the genealogies of Judah recorded in the book of 1 Chronicles. He was a descendant of Shobal, who was a descendant of Judah's son Hur. No other information is provided about Haroeh or Reaiah in the Bible. \([[1Chr.2.52|1Ch.2.52]]; 4.2\).
