<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Kolaiah</span>, a Benjamite who lived in Jerusalem \([[Neh.11.7|Neh.11.7]]\).

- only referred to as Kolaiah<[[H6964G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>קו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\);
