<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Melchi</span>, mentioned in Luke's genealogy of Jesus \([[Luke.3.24|Luk.3.24]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time ofExile and return, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.24|Luk.3.24]]; 
- only referred to as Melchi **<big>[[G3197|Μελχί]]</big>**; 
- a son of Jannai **<big>[[G2388|Ἰαννά]]</big>**; 
-  father of Levi **<big>[[G3017|Λευΐ]]</big>**.

Melchi is a name that appears in the genealogy of Jesus Christ recorded in the Gospel of Luke \([[Luke.3.24|Luk.3.24]]\). He is listed as an ancestor of Jesus, but no additional information about him is provided in the biblical text. His name suggests he was likely of Jewish descent.
