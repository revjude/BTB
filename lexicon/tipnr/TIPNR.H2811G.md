Hashabiah was a Levite of the Merari clan, the son of Amaziah. He is mentioned in the genealogy of the Levites recorded in the book of 1 Chronicles. The Merarites were one of the three main divisions of the Levites, responsible for various duties in the tabernacle and later in the temple. No other information is provided about this Hashabiah in the Bible. \([[1Chr.6.45|1Ch.6.45]]\).
