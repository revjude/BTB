<small>*Male • Son of Levi • An ancestor of Jesus Christ.*</small>

Man living at the time ofExile and return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Matthat</span> was an ancestor of Jesus Christ, mentioned in His genealogy \([[Luke.3.24|Luk.3.24]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time ofExile and return, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.24|Luk.3.24]]; 
- only referred to as Matthat **<big>[[G3158|Ματθάτ]]</big>**; 
- a son of Levi **<big>[[G3017|Λευΐ]]</big>**; 
-  father of Heli **<big>[[G2242|Ἡλί]]</big>**.

Matthat is mentioned in the genealogy of Jesus Christ, as recorded in the Gospel of Luke. He is listed as the son of Levi and the father of Jorim \([[Luke.3.24|Luke 3:24]]\). This genealogy traces Jesus' lineage through His mother, Mary, back to David and ultimately to Adam. Matthat's inclusion in this genealogy highlights the human ancestry of Jesus.
