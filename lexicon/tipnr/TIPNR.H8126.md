People from Shumath\(?\)

The <span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shumathites</span> were a family from Kirjath-jearim, descendants of Judah through Shobal.
- People from Shumath\(?\), only mentioned at [[1Chr.2.53|1Ch.2.53]]; 
- only referred to as Shumathites<[[H8126]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ֻׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\).

The Shumathites are mentioned in [[1Chr.2.53|1 Chronicles 2:53]] as one of the families from Kirjath-jearim, along with the Ithrites, Puthites, and Mishraites. These families are listed as descendants of Shobal, who was a son of Hur and a grandson of Judah \(1 Chronicles 2:50\). Kirjath-jearim was a city in Judah, known for being the place where the Ark of the Covenant was kept for a period before being brought to Jerusalem by King David \(1 Samuel 7:1-2; 1 Chronicles 13:5-6\).
