Man living before Israel's Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ethan</span> was a son of Zerah and a grandson of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living before Israel's Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.2.6|1Ch.2.6]]; 
- only referred to as Ethan<[[H0387H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ית</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\); 
-  son of Zerah<[[H2226I]]>; 
- a brother of Zabdi<[[H2067G]]>, Heman<[[H1968H]]>, Calcol<[[H3633H]]> and Dara<[[H1873]]>; 
-  father of Azariah<[[H5838I]]>.

Ethan was one of the five sons of Zerah, who was a son of Judah \([[1Chr.2.6|1 Chronicles 2:6]]\). He was a grandson of Judah and is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah's descendants \([[1Chr.2.8|1 Chronicles 2:8]]\).
