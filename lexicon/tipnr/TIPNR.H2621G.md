Hosah was a Levitical city located on the northern border of the territory allocated to the tribe of Asher \([[Josh.19.29|Joshua 19:29]]\). It was situated near Tyre and was one of the cities assigned to the Levites, specifically the descendants of Gershon. Its exact location remains unknown.
