Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hashabnah</span> was one of the individuals who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah. \([[Neh.10.25|Neh.10.25]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.10.25|Neh.10.25]]; 
- only referred to as Hashabnah<[[H2812]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ַׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\).

Hashabnah is mentioned in the book of Nehemiah as one of the individuals who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah. This covenant was a solemn agreement made by the people of Israel to follow God's laws and commandments. The covenant was sealed by a group of leaders, priests, Levites, and others, including Hashabnah. No other information is provided about Hashabnah in the Bible. \([[Neh.10.25|Neh.10.25]]\).
