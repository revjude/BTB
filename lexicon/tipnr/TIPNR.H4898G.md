Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Meshezabel</span>, son of Berechiah, helped repair the wall of Jerusalem during Nehemiah's time \([[Neh.3.4|Neh.3.4]]\).
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.4|Neh.3.4]]; 
- only referred to as Meshezabel **<big>[[H4898|מְשֵׁיזַבְאֵל]]</big>**; 
-  father of Berechiah **<big>[[H1296|בֶּרֶכְיָה]]</big>**.

Meshezabel, the son of Berechiah, is mentioned in the book of Nehemiah as one of the individuals who helped repair the wall of Jerusalem during Nehemiah's time \([[Neh.3.4|Neh.3.4]]\). He worked on a section of the wall next to Meremoth, the son of Uriah.
