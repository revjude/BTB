Edomite living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Manahath</span> was a son of Shobal, a Horite \([[Gen.36.23|Gen.36.23]]; [[1Chr.1.40|1Ch.1.40]]\).
- A man living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.36.23|Gen.36.23]]; 
- referred to as Manahath **<big>[[H4506|מָנַחַת]]</big>**, or Manahathite **<big>[[H4506|מָנַחַת]]</big>**, or "half the Manahathites" \(Alt tag\)  **<big>[[H2680|חֲצִי הַמְּנַחְתִּי]]</big>**; 
-  son of Shobal **<big>[[H7732|שׁוֹבָל]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Alvan **<big>[[H5935|עַלְוָן]]</big>**, Ebal **<big>[[H5858|עֵיבָל]]</big>**, Shepho **<big>[[H8195|שְׁפוֹ]]</big>** and Onam **<big>[[H0208|אוֹנָם]]</big>**.

Manahath was a Horite, a descendant of Seir, who lived in the land of Edom. He was the son of Shobal and is mentioned in the genealogies of the Horites \([[Gen.36.23|Genesis 36:23]]; [[1Chr.1.40|1 Chronicles 1:40]]\).
