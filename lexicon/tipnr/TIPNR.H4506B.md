Manahath was a Horite, a descendant of Seir, who lived in the land of Edom. He was the son of Shobal and is mentioned in the genealogies of the Horites \([[Gen.36.23|Genesis 36:23]]; [[1Chr.1.40|1 Chronicles 1:40]]\).
