Zior is mentioned in the Bible as one of the towns in the hill country of Judah \([[Josh.15.54|Jos.15.54]]\). It is listed alongside other towns such as Humtah, Kirjath-arba \(Hebron\), and Maon in the description of the territory allotted to Judah. However, no other information is provided about the history or significance of Zior. Its exact location is unknown, but it was situated in the hill country of Judah, which was the central mountainous region of the tribe's territory.
