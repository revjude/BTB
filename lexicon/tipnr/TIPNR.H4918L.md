Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Meshullam</span>, a Benjamite, was the son of Shephathiah, the son of Reuel, the son of Ibnijah \([[1Chr.9.8|1Ch.9.8]]\).
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[1Chr.9.8|1Ch.9.8]]; 
- only referred to as Meshullam **<big>[[H4918|מְשֻׁלָּם]]</big>**; 
- a son of Shephatiah **<big>[[H8203|שְׁפַטְיָה]]</big>**.

Meshullam is mentioned in the list of Benjamites who lived in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile \([[1Chr.9.8|1Ch.9.8]]\). He was the son of Shephathiah, the son of Reuel, the son of Ibnijah. This Meshullam is listed separately from the Meshullam mentioned in 1 Chronicles 9:7, indicating that they were two different individuals from the tribe of Benjamin.
