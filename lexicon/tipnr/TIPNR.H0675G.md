Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ezbon</span>, also known as Ozni, was a son of Gad and the head of the Oznite clan.
- A man from the tribe of Gad living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, first mentioned at [[Gen.46.16|Gen.46.16]]; 
- referred to as Ezbon<[[H0675G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>צ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\), or Ozni<[[H0244A]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ׇ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\), or Oznite<[[H0244B]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ׇ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
-  son of Gad<[[H1410G]]>; 
- a brother of Ziphion<[[H6837]]>, Haggi<[[H2291]]>, Shuni<[[H7764]]>, Eri<[[H6179]]>, Arod<[[H0720]]> and Areli<[[H0692]]>.

Ezbon, also referred to as Ozni, was one of the sons of Gad, the founder of the Israelite tribe of Gad \([[Gen.46.16|Genesis 46:16]]; [[Num.26.16|Numbers 26:16]]\). He was born to Gad during the time the Israelites lived in Egypt. Ezbon became the head of the Oznite clan, one of the clans within the tribe of Gad \([[Num.26.16|Numbers 26:16]]\). The Oznites were named after him and were counted among the descendants of Gad in the census taken in the wilderness of Sinai \(Numbers 26:15-18\).
