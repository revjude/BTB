Ezbon, also referred to as Ozni, was one of the sons of Gad, the founder of the Israelite tribe of Gad \([[Gen.46.16|Genesis 46:16]]; [[Num.26.16|Numbers 26:16]]\). He was born to Gad during the time the Israelites lived in Egypt. Ezbon became the head of the Oznite clan, one of the clans within the tribe of Gad \([[Num.26.16|Numbers 26:16]]\). The Oznites were named after him and were counted among the descendants of Gad in the census taken in the wilderness of Sinai \(Numbers 26:15-18\).
