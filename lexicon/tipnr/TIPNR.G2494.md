<small>*Male • Son of Eliakim • An ancestor of Jesus*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jonam</span>, son of Eliakim, was an ancestor of Jesus Christ.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.30|Luk.3.30]]; 
- only referred to as Jonam **<big>[[G2494|Ἰωνάν]]</big>**; 
- a son of Eliakim **<big>[[G1662|Ἐλιακείμ]]</big>**; 
-  father of Joseph **<big>[[G2501|Ἰωσήφ]]</big>**.

Jonam is mentioned in the genealogy of Jesus Christ recorded by Luke \([[Luke.3.30|Luke 3:30]]\). He was the son of Eliakim and an ancestor of Joseph, the legal father of Jesus.
