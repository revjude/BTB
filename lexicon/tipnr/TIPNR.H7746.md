<small>*Male • Brother of Chelub • Brother of Chelub, descendant of Judah.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shuhah</span> was the brother of Chelub and a descendant of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.11|1Ch.4.11]]; 
- only referred to as Shuhah **<big>[[H7746|שׁוּחָה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Chelub **<big>[[H3620|כְּלוּב]]</big>**.

Shuhah is mentioned briefly in [[1Chr.4.11|1 Chronicles 4:11]] as the brother of Chelub, who was a descendant of Judah through Ashhur, the father of Tekoa.
