<small>*Male • Son of Koz • Son of Coz, descendant of Judah*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Anub</span> was a descendant of Judah, mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.8|1Ch.4.8]]; 
- only referred to as Anub<[[H6036]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>\); 
-  son of Koz<[[H6976G]]>; 
- a brother of Zobebah<[[H6637]]> and Harum<[[H2037]]>.

Anub is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Judah, found in 1 Chronicles 4. He is listed as one of the sons of Coz, who was a descendant of Judah through his son Ashhur. The genealogy provides a record of the families and clans within the tribe of Judah, one of the twelve tribes of Israel. Apart from this mention in the genealogical list, no further information is given about Anub in the biblical text.
