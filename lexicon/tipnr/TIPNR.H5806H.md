Azubah was the wife of Caleb, who was a prominent figure in the tribe of Judah. She is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah recorded in [[1Chr.2.18|1 Chronicles 2:18]]-19. The passage states that Caleb had sons by Azubah his wife, and then lists their names as Jesher, Shobab, and Ardon. After this, it notes that Azubah died, and Caleb married Ephrath, who bore him Hur.
