Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jeush</span>, a Benjamite, was a son of Eleasah \(or 'Eshek'\) \([[1Chr.8.39|1Ch.8.39]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.39|1Ch.8.39]]; 
- only referred to as Jeush<[[H3266I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>עו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ׁ\); 
-  son of Eleasah<[[H0501H]]>; 
- a brother of Ulam<[[H0198H]]> and Eliphelet<[[H0467J]]>.

Jeush is briefly mentioned in [[1Chr.8.39|1 Chronicles 8:39]] as one of the sons of Eleasah \(or 'Eshek'\), a descendant of King Saul from the tribe of Benjamin. He is listed in the genealogy of Saul's family, which is given in the context of the Benjamite clans that settled in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile.
