Pelet was a son of Jahdai, according to the genealogy of Judah in [[1Chr.2.47|1 Chronicles 2:47]]. He is listed among the descendants of Caleb, the son of Hezron. No other information is provided about Pelet's life or role. His mention in the genealogy suggests that he was part of the tribe of Judah and lived during the period of Israel's settlement in Canaan or the early monarchy.
