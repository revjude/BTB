People from Ramath\(?\)

The <span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ramathites</span> were people from Ramah who were in charge of King David's vineyards.
- People from Ramath\(?\), only mentioned at [[1Chr.27.27|1Ch.27.27]]; 
- only referred to as Ramathites **<big>[[H7435|רָמָתִי]]</big>**.

The Ramathites are mentioned briefly in the context of King David's administration \([[1Chr.27.27|1 Chronicles 27:27]]\). They were people from the city of Ramah who were appointed to oversee the king's vineyards. This suggests that Ramah was known for its viticulture and that its inhabitants were skilled in tending vineyards. The mention of the Ramathites in this passage highlights the various roles and responsibilities assigned to different groups within David's kingdom.
