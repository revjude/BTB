<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Melea</span>, mentioned in Luke's genealogy of Jesus \([[Luke.3.31|Luk.3.31]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.31|Luk.3.31]]; 
- only referred to as Melea<[[G3190]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangGreek'>Μελεᾶς</span>\); 
- a son of Menna<[[G3104]]>; 
-  father of Eliakim<[[G1662H]]>.

Melea is a name that appears in the genealogy of Jesus Christ recorded in the Gospel of Luke \([[Luke.3.31|Luk.3.31]]\). He is listed as an ancestor of Jesus, but no further information about his life or role is provided in the biblical text. His name suggests he was likely of Jewish descent.
