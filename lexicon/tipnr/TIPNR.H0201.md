Omar was one of the sons of Eliphaz and a grandson of Esau \([[Gen.36.11|Genesis 36:11]], 15; [[1Chr.1.36|1 Chronicles 1:36]]\). He is mentioned in the genealogies of Esau, which list the descendants of Esau and the chiefs of Edom. As a grandson of Esau, Omar was part of the Edomite clan and likely held a position of influence within the tribe.
