Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zoheth</span> was a son of Ishi and a descendant of Judah \([[1Chr.4.20|1Ch.4.20]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah\(?\) living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.20|1Ch.4.20]]; 
- only referred to as Zoheth<[[H2105]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>זו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>\); 
-  son of Ishi<[[H3469H]]>; 
- a brother of Ben-zoheth<[[H1132]]>.

Zoheth is mentioned briefly in [[1Chr.4.20|1 Chronicles 4:20]] as part of the genealogy of the tribe of Judah. He is identified as one of the sons of Ishi, along with his brother Ben-zoheth. The genealogy in this chapter traces the lineage of several clans within the tribe of Judah, focusing on the descendants of Shelah, one of Judah's sons. The mention of Zoheth and his brother in this context suggests that they were significant figures within their clan. However, no further details are provided about Zoheth's life, role, or achievements.
