<small>*Male • Son of Elpaal • Son of Elpaal, a Benjamite*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zebadiah</span> was a son of Elpaal and a member of the tribe of Benjamin.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.17|1Ch.8.17]]; 
- only referred to as Zebadiah **<big>[[H2069|זְבַדְיָה]]</big>**; 
-  son of Elpaal **<big>[[H0508|אֶלְפַּעַל]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Eber **<big>[[H5677|עֵבֵר]]</big>**, Misham **<big>[[H4936|מִשְׁעָם]]</big>**, Shemed **<big>[[H8106|שֶׁמֶר]]</big>**, Beriah **<big>[[H1283|בְּרִיעָה]]</big>**, Shema **<big>[[H8087|שֶׁמַע]]</big>**, Meshullam **<big>[[H4918|מְשֻׁלָּם]]</big>**, Hizki **<big>[[H2395|חִזְקִי]]</big>**, Heber **<big>[[H2268|חֶבֶר]]</big>**, Ishmerai **<big>[[H3461|יִשְׁמְרַי]]</big>**, Izliah **<big>[[H3152|יִזְלִיאָה]]</big>** and Jobab **<big>[[H3103|יוֹבָב]]</big>**.

Zebadiah is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin as one of the sons of Elpaal. The genealogy in 1 Chronicles 8 lists the descendants of Benjamin, including the family of Elpaal.
