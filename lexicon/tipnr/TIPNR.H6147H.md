Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Er</span> was Judah's grandson, ancestor of linen workers at Beth-ashbea.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.21|1Ch.4.21]]; 
- only referred to as Er<[[H6147H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>\); 
-  son of Shelah<[[H7956]]>; 
- a brother of Laadah<[[H3935]]>, Jokim<[[H3137]]>, Joash<[[H3101K]]> and Saraph<[[H8315]]>; 
-  father or founder of Lecah<[[H3922]]>.

Er was the son of Shelah, son of Judah. Unlike his uncle with the same name, he had descendants, and these became the linen workers at Beth-ashbea.
