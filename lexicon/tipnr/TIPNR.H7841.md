<small>*Male • Saul's family • Descendant of Benjamin*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shehariah</span>, a descendant of Benjamin in the genealogy of Saul.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.26|1Ch.8.26]]; 
- only referred to as Shehariah<[[H7841]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
-  son of Jeroham<[[H3395H]]>; 
- a brother of Shamsherai<[[H8125]]>, Athaliah<[[H6271H]]>, Jaareshiah<[[H3298]]>, Elijah<[[H0452H]]> and Zichri<[[H2147J]]>.

Shehariah is mentioned in [[1Chr.8.26|1 Chronicles 8:26]] as a descendant of Benjamin. He appears in the genealogy of King Saul, Israel's first king, who was from the tribe of Benjamin.
