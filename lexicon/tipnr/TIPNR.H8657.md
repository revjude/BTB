<small>*Male • Persian official who plotted against King Ahasuerus*</small>

Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Teresh</span> was one of two Persian officials who plotted to assassinate King Ahasuerus but were discovered by Mordecai.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at [[Esth.2.21|Est.2.21]]; 
- only referred to as Teresh **<big>[[H8657|תֶּרֶשׁ]]</big>**.

Teresh is mentioned in [[Esth.2.21|Esther 2:21]] and 6:2 as one of two eunuchs who served at the door of King Ahasuerus' palace. Along with Bigthan, Teresh plotted to assassinate the king for unknown reasons. However, their plot was discovered by Mordecai, who informed Queen Esther. The matter was investigated and found to be true, leading to the execution of Bigthan and Teresh on the gallows. Mordecai's deed was recorded in the king's chronicles.
