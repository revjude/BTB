<small>*Male • Son of Ishi • Descendant of Judah*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ben-zoheth</span> was a descendant of Judah through Ishi.
- A man from the tribe of Judah\(?\) living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.20|1Ch.4.20]]; 
- only referred to as Ben-zoheth **<big>[[H1132|בֶּן־זוֹחֵת]]</big>**; 
-  son of Ishi **<big>[[H3469|יִשְׁעִי]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Zoheth **<big>[[H2105|זוֹחֵת]]</big>**.

Ben-zoheth is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah as a son of Ishi \(1 Ch. 4:20\). He was part of the clan of Judah that descended from Shelah, one of Judah's sons. No further details about Ben-zoheth's life or role are provided in the biblical text. His inclusion in the genealogy highlights the importance of family lineage and the preservation of ancestral records among the people of Israel.
