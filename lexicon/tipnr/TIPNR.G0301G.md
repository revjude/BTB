<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Amos</span>, an ancestor of Jesus, son of Naum.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time ofExile and return, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.25|Luk.3.25]]; 
- only referred to as Amos<[[G0301G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangGreek'>Ἀμώς</span>\); 
- a son of Nahum<[[G3486]]>; 
-  father of Mattathias<[[G3161G]]>.

Amos is mentioned in [[Luke.3.25|Luke 3:25]] as an ancestor of Jesus Christ in his genealogy. He was the son of Naum.
