<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tahath</span> was a Levite of the Kohathite clan, the son of Assir and father of Uriel.
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.6.24|1Ch.6.24]]; 
- only referred to as Tahath<[[H8480H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ַּ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>\); 
- a son of Assir<[[H0617H]]>; 
-  father of Uriel<[[H0222G]]>.

Tahath is mentioned in [[1Chr.6.24|1 Chronicles 6:24]],37 as a Levite of the Kohathite clan. He was the son of Assir and the father of Uriel. This genealogy lists the descendants of Levi, tracing the ancestry of the prophet Samuel and his sons. As a Kohathite Levite, Tahath would have been involved in the transportation and care of the sacred objects of the tabernacle.
