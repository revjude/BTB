<small>*Male • Son of Shaharaim • A descendant of Benjamin born in Moab.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Mesha</span>, a descendant of Benjamin, was born to Shaharaim in the country of Moab \([[1Chr.8.9|1Ch.8.9]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.9|1Ch.8.9]]; 
- only referred to as Mesha<[[H4331]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יש</span>ָׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>\); 
-  son of Shaharaim<[[H7842]]> and Hodesh<[[H2321]]>; 
- a brother of Jobab<[[H3103J]]>, Zibia<[[H6644]]>, Malcam<[[H4445A]]>, Jeuz<[[H3263]]>, Sachia<[[H7634]]>, Mirmah<[[H4821]]>, Abitub<[[H0036]]> and Elpaal<[[H0508]]>.

Mesha is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin in the book of Chronicles \([[1Chr.8.9|1Ch.8.9]]\). He was one of the sons born to Shaharaim in the country of Moab after he had divorced his wives Hushim and Baara.
