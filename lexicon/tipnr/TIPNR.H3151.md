- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.27.31|1Ch.27.31]]; 
- only referred to as Jaziz **<big>[[H3151|יָזִיז]]</big>**.

Jaziz the Hagrite is mentioned in [[1Chr.27.31|1 Chronicles 27:31]] as the individual responsible for overseeing King David's flocks. He was one of the officials appointed by David to manage various aspects of his kingdom. The Hagrites were a nomadic people who lived in the region east of Gilead, and they were known for their skill in tending livestock. Jaziz's appointment to this position suggests that he had expertise in animal husbandry and was trusted by David to manage this important resource. The text does not provide any further details about Jaziz's life or his role in David's administration.
