<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Malluch</span>, a son of Hashabiah, was a Merarite Levite \([[1Chr.6.44|1Ch.6.44]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.6.44|1Ch.6.44]]; 
- only referred to as Malluch **<big>[[H4409|מַלּוּךְ]]</big>**; 
- a son of Hashabiah **<big>[[H2811|חֲשַׁבְיָה]]</big>**; 
-  father of Abdi **<big>[[H5660|עַבְדִּי]]</big>**.

Malluch was a Levite who belonged to the family of Merari. He was the son of Hashabiah and is mentioned in the genealogy of the Levites \([[1Chr.6.44|1 Chronicles 6:44]]\).
