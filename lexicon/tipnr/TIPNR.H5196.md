<small>*Netaim • A place associated with potters in Judah*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Netaim</span> was a place in Judah where potters lived and worked.
- A location near Gederah **<big>[[H1449|גְּדֵרָה]]</big>** only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.23|1Ch.4.23]]; 
- only referred to as Netaim **<big>[[H5196|נְטָעִים]]</big>**.

Netaim is mentioned briefly in the genealogy of the tribe of Judah \(1 Chr. 4:23\) as a place where potters lived and worked for the king. These potters were said to have dwelt among the plantations and hedges, suggesting that Netaim was a rural area with agricultural land.
