Levi's son living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Kohath</span>, son of Levi, was the ancestor of Moses and Aaron \([[Gen.46.11|Gen.46.11]]; [[Exod.6.16|Exo.6.16]],18\).
- Levi's son from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, first mentioned at [[Gen.46.11|Gen.46.11]]; 
- referred to as Kohath or Kohathite **<big>[[H6955|קְהָת]]</big>**, or Kohathite **<big>[[H6956|קֳהָתִי]]</big>**; 
-  son of Levi **<big>[[H3878|לֵוִי]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Gershon **<big>[[H1648|גֵּרְשׁוֹן]]</big>**, Merari **<big>[[H4847|מְרָרִי]]</big>** and Jochebed **<big>[[H3115|יוֹכֶבֶד]]</big>**; 
-  father of Amram **<big>[[H6019|עַמְרָם]]</big>**, Izhar **<big>[[H3324|יִצְהָר]]</big>**, Hebron **<big>[[H2275|חֶבְרוֹן]]</big>** and Uzziel **<big>[[H5816|עֻזִּיאֵל]]</big>**.

Kohath was the second son of Levi and grandson of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\) \([[Gen.46.11|Gen.46.11]]\). He went to Egypt with Jacob's household \([[Gen.46.11|Gen.46.11]]\) and his descendants were counted in the census of Israel \([[Exod.6.16|Exo.6.16]],18; [[Num.3.17|Num.3.17]],19\). The Kohathites became one of the major clans of the Levites \([[Num.3.27|Num.3.27]]\). They were responsible for the care of the most holy things of the tabernacle \([[Num.3.29|Num.3.29]]-31; 4.4,15\). The Kohathites were given towns to live in among the tribes of Ephraim, Dan and Manasseh \([[Josh.21.5|Jos.21.5]],20,26\). Notable descendants of Kohath included Moses, Aaron, and the priestly line \(Exo.6.18,20\), as well as the musician Heman \([[1Chr.6.33|1Ch.6.33]]\). The Kohathites remained faithful Levites throughout Israel's history \([[1Chr.9.32|1Ch.9.32]]; [[2Chr.20.19|2Ch.20.19]]; 29.12; 34.12\).
