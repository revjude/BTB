Moza was one of the sons of Caleb, the son of Hezron, by his concubine Ephah \([[1Chr.2.46|1 Chronicles 2:46]]\). Caleb was one of the twelve spies sent by Moses to scout out Canaan \(Numbers 13:6\). Along with Joshua, he gave a good report and was permitted to enter the promised land \(Numbers 14:30\). No other details are given about Moza's life. He is not to be confused with the Benjamite Moza mentioned in the genealogies of Saul \(1 Chronicles 8:36-37, 9:42-43\).
