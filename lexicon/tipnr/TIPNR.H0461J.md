<small>*Male • A priest who blew trumpets before the ark.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eliezer</span> was a priest who blew trumpets before the ark of God \([[1Chr.15.24|1Ch.15.24]]\).
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.15.24|1Ch.15.24]]; 
- only referred to as Eliezer **<big>[[H0461|אֱלִיעֶזֶר]]</big>**.

Eliezer is mentioned as one of the priests who blew trumpets before the ark of God when David brought it to Jerusalem \([[1Chr.15.24|1Ch.15.24]]\). He was part of a procession that included Levites carrying the ark, singers, and other musicians. The blowing of trumpets was a significant part of Israelite worship and was used to signal important events.
