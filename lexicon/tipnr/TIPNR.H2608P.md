Hananiah was one of the sons of Heman, a musician in King David's court \([[1Chr.25.4|1 Chronicles 25:4]]\). He and his brothers were appointed by David to prophesy with lyres, harps, and cymbals \(1 Chronicles 25:1\). Hananiah, along with his brothers, was assigned duties in the service of the house of God, according to the order prescribed by King David \([[1Chr.25.23|1 Chronicles 25:23]]\). The sons of Heman were known for their musical skills and were part of the Levitical musicians who ministered in the temple.
