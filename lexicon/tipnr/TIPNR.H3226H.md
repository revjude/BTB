<small>*Male • Son of Ram • Son of Ram, descendant of Judah*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jamin</span> was a son of Ram and a descendant of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.2.27|1Ch.2.27]]; 
- only referred to as Jamin **<big>[[H3226|יָמִין]]</big>**; 
-  son of Ram **<big>[[H7410|רָם]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Maaz **<big>[[H4619|מַעַץ]]</big>** and Eker **<big>[[H6134|עֵקֶר]]</big>**.

Jamin is briefly mentioned in [[1Chr.2.27|1 Chronicles 2:27]] as one of the sons of Ram, along with his brothers Bunah, Oren, and Ozem. Ram was a descendant of Judah through Hezron and Jerahmeel. The genealogy in this chapter traces the lineage of Judah, focusing on the descendants of Hezron. No further information is provided about Jamin's life or his role within the tribe of Judah. His mention in the genealogy suggests that he was an ancestor of some of the families within the tribe, although no specific details are given about his descendants.
