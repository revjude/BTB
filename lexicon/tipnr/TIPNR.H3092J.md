<small>*Male • Son of Nimshi • Father of Jehu, king of Israel.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jehoshaphat</span> was the father of Jehu, who became king of Israel.
- A man of the Kingdom of Israel, living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[2Kgs.9.2|2Ki.9.2]]; 
- only referred to as Jehoshaphat **<big>[[H3092|יְהוֹשָׁפָט]]</big>**; 
- a son of Nimshi **<big>[[H5250|נִמְשִׁי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Jehu **<big>[[H3058|יֵהוּא]]</big>**.

Jehoshaphat was the father of Jehu, who was anointed by Elisha's servant to become king of Israel. Jehu was a commander in the army of Joram, king of Israel, when he was anointed. Jehoshaphat's son Jehu went on to fulfill the prophecy against the house of Ahab by destroying Joram and the royal family of Israel.
