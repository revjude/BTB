One of the descendants of Judah, <span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jonathan</span> was the son of Jada.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.2.32|1Ch.2.32]]; 
- only referred to as Jonathan **<big>[[H3129|יוֹנָתָן]]</big>**; 
-  son of Jada **<big>[[H3047|יָדָע]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Jether **<big>[[H3500|יֶתֶר]]</big>**; 
-  father of Peleth **<big>[[H6431|פֶּלֶת]]</big>** and Zaza **<big>[[H2117|זָזָא]]</big>**.

Jonathan was a descendant of Judah, the son of Jada and brother of Jether. He is mentioned in the genealogies of Judah recorded in [[1Chr.2.32|1 Chronicles 2:32]]-33. No other details are provided about his life. His mention helps trace Judah's descendants down to the time period after the return from Babylonian exile when Chronicles was likely written.
