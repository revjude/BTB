Zillethai is mentioned briefly in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin in 1 Chronicles 8. He is listed as one of the sons of Shimei, who was a descendant of Benjamin through his son Bela. The genealogy in this chapter traces the lineage of several prominent Benjamite families, particularly those who lived in Jerusalem. The inclusion of Zillethai in this record suggests that he was part of a significant family within the tribe of Benjamin. However, no other details are provided about his life or role within the tribe.
