Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Athaliah</span> was a descendant of Benjamin mentioned in the genealogy of Saul's family \(1 Chr 8:26\).
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.26|1Ch.8.26]]; 
- only referred to as Athaliah **<big>[[H6271|עֲתַלְיָה]]</big>**; 
-  son of Jeroham **<big>[[H3395|יְרֹחָם]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Shamsherai **<big>[[H8125|שַׁמְשְׁרַי]]</big>**, Shehariah **<big>[[H7841|שְׁחַרְיָה]]</big>**, Jaareshiah **<big>[[H3298|יַעֲרֶשְׁיָה]]</big>**, Elijah **<big>[[H0452|אֵלִיָּה]]</big>** and Zichri **<big>[[H2147|זִכְרִי]]</big>**.

Athaliah is briefly mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin in 1 Chronicles 8. He is listed among the descendants of Jeroham, who was a member of the family of Saul, the first king of Israel. This genealogical record traces the lineage of Saul and his relatives, providing insight into the family structure of the Benjamite clan from which Israel's first monarch came.
