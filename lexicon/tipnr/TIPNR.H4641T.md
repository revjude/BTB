Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Maaseiah</span>, a descendant of Shiloni, lived in Jerusalem after the exile \([[Neh.11.5|Neh.11.5]]\).
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.11.5|Neh.11.5]]; 
- only referred to as Maaseiah **<big>[[H4641|מַעֲשֵׂיָה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Baruch **<big>[[H1263|בָּרוּךְ]]</big>**.

Maaseiah was a descendant of Shiloni who lived in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile \([[Neh.11.5|Neh.11.5]]\). He is mentioned in the list of people who resettled in Jerusalem, which was compiled by Nehemiah as part of his efforts to repopulate the city \(Neh.11.1-36\). Maaseiah is identified as a descendant of Judah through the lineage of Shiloni. The resettlement of Jerusalem was an important step in the restoration of the Jewish community and the reestablishment of their presence in the holy city.
