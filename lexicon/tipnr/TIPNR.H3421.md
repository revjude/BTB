Man or town at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jorkeam</span> was a descendant of Caleb or a town founded by Raham in the tribe of Judah.
- A man or town of the tribe of Judah at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.2.44|1Ch.2.44]]; 
- only referred to as Jorkeam **<big>[[H3421|יׇרְקְעָם]]</big>**; 
- a son of Raham **<big>[[H7357|רַחַם]]</big>**.

Jorkeam is mentioned briefly in [[1Chr.2.44|1 Chronicles 2:44]] as one of the descendants of Caleb or a town founded by Raham his 'father'. Caleb was from the tribe of Judah and is known for his faithfulness to God during the Israelites' wilderness wanderings \(Numbers 13:30; 14:24\). In the genealogy of Judah recorded in 1 Chronicles 2, Jorkeam is listed as the son of Raham or the town founded by him.
