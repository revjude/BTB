<small>*Male • Son of Josech • Ancestor of Jesus Christ*</small>

Man living at the time ofExile and return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Semein</span> is mentioned in the genealogy of Jesus Christ, as recorded in Luke's Gospel.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time ofExile and return, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.26|Luk.3.26]]; 
- only referred to as Semein **<big>[[G4584|Σεμεΐ]]</big>**; 
- a son of Josech **<big>[[G2501|Ἰωσήφ]]</big>**; 
-  father of Mattathias **<big>[[G3161|Ματταθίας]]</big>**.

Semein is mentioned in [[Luke.3.26|Luke 3:26]] as an ancestor of Jesus Christ. He is listed in the genealogy of Jesus, which traces His lineage back to Adam. Semein is said to be the son of Josech and the father of Mattathias. The genealogy in Luke's Gospel is thought to be the genealogy of Mary, Jesus' mother, while the genealogy in Matthew's Gospel is believed to be that of Joseph, Jesus' earthly father. The inclusion of Semein in this genealogy highlights the importance of family lineage in Jewish culture and emphasizes Jesus' connection to the ancestral line of David, from whom the Messiah was prophesied to come.
