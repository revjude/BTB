Hadad, son of Bedad, was a king of Edom mentioned in [[Gen.36.35|Genesis 36:35]]-36 and [[1Chr.1.46|1 Chronicles 1:46]]-47. He succeeded Husham as king of Edom. Hadad defeated the Midianites in the country of Moab, which suggests that during his reign, Edom had a military conflict with Midian. The city of Avith is mentioned as the place where Hadad reigned, but its exact location is unknown.
