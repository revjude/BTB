<small>*Male • Son of Hebron • Son of Hebron, a Judahite*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shema</span>, a son of Hebron and descendant of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.2.43|1Ch.2.43]]; 
- only referred to as Shema **<big>[[H8087|שֶׁמַע]]</big>**; 
-  son of Hebron **<big>[[H2275|חֶבְרוֹן]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Korah **<big>[[H7141|קֹרַח]]</big>**, Tappuah **<big>[[H8599|תַּפּוּחַ]]</big>** and Rekem **<big>[[H7552|רֶקֶם]]</big>**; 
-  father of Raham **<big>[[H7357|רַחַם]]</big>**.

Shema is mentioned in [[1Chr.2.43|1 Chronicles 2:43]]-44 as one of the sons of Hebron, who was a descendant of Judah. He is listed along with his brothers Korah, Tappuah, and Rekem. Shema's son was named Raham, and Raham's son was Jorkeam.
