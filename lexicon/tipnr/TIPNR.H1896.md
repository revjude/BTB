Hegai was a eunuch who served in the court of King Ahasuerus \(Xerxes I\) of Persia. He was in charge of the king's harem, which was the living quarters for the king's concubines and prospective brides. When Esther was brought into the king's harem, Hegai was responsible for her care and preparation \([[Esth.2.3|Est.2.3]],8\). He provided Esther with cosmetics, special food, and seven choice maidservants from the king's palace \(Est.2.9\). Hegai was also instrumental in advising Esther on how to approach the king and win his favor \([[Esth.2.15|Est.2.15]]\). Esther found favor with Hegai and received the best place in the harem \(Est.2.9\). \([[Esth.2.3|Est.2.3]],8,15\).
