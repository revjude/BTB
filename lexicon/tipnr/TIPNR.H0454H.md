<small>*Male • From  Simeon • A Simeonite leader during Hezekiah's reign.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Elioenai</span> was a Simeonite leader during the reign of King Hezekiah \([[1Chr.4.36|1Ch.4.36]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Simeon\(?\) living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.36|1Ch.4.36]]; 
- only referred to as Elioenai **<big>[[H0454|אֶלְיְהוֹעֵינַי]]</big>**.

Elioenai is mentioned as one of the leaders of the tribe of Simeon during the reign of King Hezekiah of Judah \([[1Chr.4.36|1Ch.4.36]]\). He is listed among the Simeonites who expanded their territory by conquering the land of the Amalekites. This event likely took place during Hezekiah's religious reforms and military campaigns against the enemies of Judah.
