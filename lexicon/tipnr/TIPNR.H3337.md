<small>*Male • Son of Naphtali • Son of Naphtali, founder of the Jezerite clan*</small>

Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jezer</span>, a son of Naphtali, was the ancestor of the Jezerite clan \([[Gen.46.24|Gen.46.24]]; [[Num.26.49|Num.26.49]]; [[1Chr.7.13|1Ch.7.13]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Naphtali living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, first mentioned at [[Gen.46.24|Gen.46.24]]; 
- referred to as Jezer **<big>[[H3337|יֵצֶר]]</big>**, or Jezerite **<big>[[H3340|יִצְרִי]]</big>**; 
-  son of Naphtali **<big>[[H5321|נַפְתָּלִי]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Jahzeel **<big>[[H3183|יַחְצְאֵל]]</big>**, Guni **<big>[[H1476|גּוּנִי]]</big>** and Shillem **<big>[[H8006|שִׁלֵּם]]</big>**.

Jezer was one of the sons of Naphtali, as listed in the genealogies of [[Gen.46.24|Genesis 46:24]], [[Num.26.49|Numbers 26:49]], and [[1Chr.7.13|1 Chronicles 7:13]]. He was born during the period when Israel \(or 'Jacob'\) and his family lived in Canaan, before their migration to Egypt. Jezer became the founder of the Jezerite clan within the tribe of Naphtali. In the census taken in the wilderness of Sinai, the Jezerite clan is mentioned as one of the clans descended from Naphtali \([[Num.26.49|Num.26.49]]\).
