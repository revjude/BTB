- only referred to as Zedekiah<[[H6667I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>צ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ד</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ק</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָּ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\);

Zedekiah was a son of Jeconiah \(also known as Jehoiachin\), the king of Judah who was deported to Babylon by Nebuchadnezzar. He is mentioned in the genealogy of the kings of Judah in 1 Chronicles. Zedekiah had a brother named Assir.
