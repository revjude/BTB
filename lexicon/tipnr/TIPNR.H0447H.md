Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eliel</span> was a descendant of Shimei from the tribe of Benjamin.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.20|1Ch.8.20]]; 
- only referred to as Eliel<[[H0447H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֱ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יא</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>\); 
-  son of Shimei<[[H8096N]]>; 
- a brother of Jakim<[[H3356G]]>, Zichri<[[H2147H]]>, Zabdi<[[H2067H]]>, Elienai<[[H0462]]>, Zillethai<[[H6769G]]>, Adaiah<[[H5718H]]>, Beraiah<[[H1256]]> and Shimrath<[[H8119]]>.

Eliel is mentioned briefly in [[1Chr.8.20|1 Chronicles 8:20]] as one of the descendants of Shimei, who was a member of the tribe of Benjamin. This genealogical record traces the lineage of the Benjamites, providing a list of names without much additional information. Eliel's mention in this context serves to highlight the continuity of the Benjamite lineage and their presence in the post-exilic community.
