<small>*Male • Father of Hushim • Son of Shemer, from the tribe of Asher*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Aher</span> was a son of Shemer, a descendant of Asher. \([[1Chr.7.12|1Ch.7.12]]\)
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.12|1Ch.7.12]]; 
- only referred to as Aher **<big>[[H0313|אַחֵר]]</big>**; 
-  father of Hushim **<big>[[H2366|חוּשִׁים]]</big>**.

Aher is mentioned briefly in the genealogy of the tribe of Asher. He was the son of Shemer, who was a descendant of Asher through Beriah. No further details about Aher's life or role are provided in the biblical text. \([[1Chr.7.12|1Ch.7.12]]\).
