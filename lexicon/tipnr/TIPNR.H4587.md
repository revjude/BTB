Man living before Israel's Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Meonothai</span>, son of Othniel, mentioned in the genealogy of Judah \([[1Chr.4.14|1Ch.4.14]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living before Israel's Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.14|1Ch.4.14]]; 
- only referred to as Meonothai **<big>[[H4587|מְעוֹנֹתַי]]</big>**; 
-  son of Othniel **<big>[[H6274|עׇתְנִיאֵל]]</big>** whose wife was Achsah **<big>[[H5915|עַכְסָה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Hathath **<big>[[H2867|חֲתַת]]</big>**; 
-  father of Ophrah **<big>[[H6084|עׇפְרָה]]</big>**.

Meonothai is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Judah \([[1Chr.4.14|1Ch.4.14]]\). He was the son of Othniel, who was the younger brother of Caleb, the son of Jephunneh. Meonothai was the father of Ophrah, but no further information about his life or role is provided in the biblical text.
