<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Parosh</span>, a clan leader who sealed the covenant of Nehemiah.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.10.14|Neh.10.14]]; 
- only referred to as Parosh<[[H6551J]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ַּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ׁ\).

Parosh was a clan leader in the time of Nehemiah who is listed in [[Neh.10.14|Nehemiah 10:14]] as one of the leaders who sealed the covenant Nehemiah established. The covenant pledged that the people would obey God's law given through Moses, including regulations about marriage, Sabbath keeping, debt cancellation, and financial support of the temple. Parosh represented his clan in affirming this commitment to follow God's ways.
