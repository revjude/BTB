Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jachin</span> \(or Jarib\), a son of Simeon, was the founder of the Jachinites clan within the tribe of Simeon \([[Gen.46.10|Gen.46.10]]; [[Exod.6.15|Exo.6.15]]; [[Num.26.12|Num.26.12]]; [[1Chr.4.24|1Ch.4.24]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Simeon living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, first mentioned at [[Gen.46.10|Gen.46.10]]; 
- referred to as Jachin **<big>[[H3199|יָכִין]]</big>**, or Jachinite **<big>[[H3200|יָכִינִי]]</big>**, or Jarib **<big>[[H3402|יָרִיב]]</big>**; 
-  son of Simeon **<big>[[H8095|שִׁמְעוֹן]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Jemuel **<big>[[H3223|יְמוּאֵל]]</big>**, Jamin **<big>[[H3226|יָמִין]]</big>**, Ohad **<big>[[H0161|אֹהַד]]</big>**, Zerah **<big>[[H2226|זֶרַח]]</big>**  and Shaul **<big>[[H7586|שָׁאוּל]]</big>**.

Jachin, also known as Jarib, is mentioned in several genealogical lists in the Old Testament as a son of Simeon, the second son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\) and Leah. He is first mentioned in [[Gen.46.10|Genesis 46:10]] and [[Exod.6.15|Exodus 6:15]], where he is listed among the sons of Simeon who went down to Egypt with Jacob. 

In [[Num.26.12|Numbers 26:12]], Jachin is identified as the founder of the Jachinites, one of the clans within the tribe of Simeon. This passage records the census taken by Moses and Eleazar in the plains of Moab, which lists the clans of each tribe according to their ancestors. 

Jachin is also mentioned in [[1Chr.4.24|1 Chronicles 4:24]] as part of the genealogy of the tribe of Simeon.
