Mount Lebanon, a mountain range marking the northern border of the Promised Land \([[Judg.3.3|Judges 3:3]]\).

Mount Lebanon is mentioned in [[Judg.3.3|Judges 3:3]] as one of the regions where the Canaanite inhabitants remained after the Israelites settled in the Promised Land. It is described as part of the northern border of the land, along with Baal-hermon and the territory of the Hivites.
