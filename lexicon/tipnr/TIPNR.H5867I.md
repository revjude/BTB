Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Elam</span> was a leader who signed the covenant during Nehemiah's time.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.10.14|Neh.10.14]]; 
- only referred to as Elam<[[H5867I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יל</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ם</span>\).

Elam is mentioned in [[Neh.10.14|Nehemiah 10:14]] as one of the leaders of the people who signed the covenant during the time of Nehemiah. The covenant was a solemn agreement to follow God's law and to support the temple service. Elam's presence among the signers indicates he held a position of responsibility and influence among the returned exiles.
