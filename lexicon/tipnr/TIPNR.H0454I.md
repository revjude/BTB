<small>*Male • Son of Becher • A Benjamite, son of Becher.*</small>

Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Elioenai</span> was a Benjamite, one of the sons of Becher \([[1Chr.7.8|1Ch.7.8]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.8|1Ch.7.8]]; 
- only referred to as Elioenai **<big>[[H0454|אֶלְיְהוֹעֵינַי]]</big>**; 
-  son of Becher **<big>[[H1071|בֶכֶר]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Zemirah **<big>[[H2160|זְמִירָה]]</big>**, Joash **<big>[[H3135|יוֹעָשׁ]]</big>**, Eliezer **<big>[[H0461|אֱלִיעֶזֶר]]</big>**, Omri **<big>[[H6018|עׇמְרִי]]</big>**, Jeremoth **<big>[[H3406|יְרִימוֹת]]</big>**, Abijah **<big>[[H0029|אֲבִיָּה]]</big>**, Anathoth **<big>[[H6068|עֲנָתוֹת]]</big>** and Alemeth **<big>[[H5964|עָלֶמֶת]]</big>**.

Elioenai is listed in the genealogy of the tribe of Benjamin as one of the sons of Becher \([[1Chr.7.8|1Ch.7.8]]\). Becher was a son of Benjamin, making Elioenai a grandson of the patriarch Benjamin. This genealogy records the heads of families and clans within the tribe of Benjamin. Apart from being mentioned as a son of Becher, no additional information is provided about Elioenai's life or role in the Bible.
