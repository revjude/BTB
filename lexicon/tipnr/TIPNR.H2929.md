<small>*Male • Returned from exile • Levite gatekeeper who returned from Babylonian exile*</small>

Levite living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Talmon</span> was a Levite gatekeeper who returned from Babylon and served at the temple.
- A man from the tribe of Levi  living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at [[1Chr.9.17|1Ch.9.17]]; 
- only referred to as Talmon **<big>[[H2929|טַלְמוֹן]]</big>**.

Talmon is mentioned in [[1Chr.9.17|1 Chronicles 9:17]], [[Ezra.2.42|Ezra 2:42]], [[Neh.7.45|Nehemiah 7:45]], 11:19, and 12:25 as a Levite gatekeeper who returned from Babylonian captivity. He and his descendants were responsible for guarding the gates and storerooms of the temple. The gatekeepers were organized by families and rotated their duties. Talmon is listed along with Akkub, Shallum, and Ahiman as the chief gatekeepers. His descendants continued to serve faithfully in this role in the days of Ezra and Nehemiah.
