<small>*Tochen • Town in the territory of Simeon*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tochen</span>, a town allotted to the tribe of Simeon \(1 Chr. 4:32\).
- A location near Ashnah **<big>[[H0823|אַשְׁנָה]]</big>** only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.32|1Ch.4.32]]; 
- only referred to as Tochen **<big>[[H8507|תֹּכֶן]]</big>**.

Tochen is mentioned in [[1Chr.4.32|1 Chronicles 4:32]] as one of the towns allotted to the tribe of Simeon during the division of the Promised Land. It is listed along with other Simeonite towns such as Ain, Rimmon, and Ether. The exact location of Tochen is unknown, but it was likely situated in the Negev region, in the southern part of Judah's territory.
