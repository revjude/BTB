Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zabbai</span>, son of Baruch, repaired a section of Jerusalem's wall.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.20|Neh.3.20]]; 
- only referred to as Zabbai<[[H2079H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ַּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
-  father of Baruch<[[H1263G]]>.

Zabbai, the son of Baruch, is mentioned in the account of the rebuilding of Jerusalem's wall under Nehemiah's leadership. He is listed among the people who worked on repairing the wall after the Babylonian exile. Zabbai repaired a section of the wall, specifically the second section from the house of Eliashib the high priest. His participation in the wall's restoration demonstrates the community-wide effort to rebuild and fortify Jerusalem after the return from exile.
