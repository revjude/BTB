Jehud was a town allotted to the tribe of Dan during the division of the Promised Land \([[Josh.19.45|Joshua 19:45]]\). It was located in the western part of Canaan, in the coastal plain region known as the Shephelah. The town is mentioned only once in the Bible, and no additional information about its history or significance is provided. Its exact location remains uncertain.
