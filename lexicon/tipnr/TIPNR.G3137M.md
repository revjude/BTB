Woman living at the time of the New Testament

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Mary</span> was a woman who worked very hard for the church in Rome \([[Rom.16.6|Rom.16.6]]\).
- A woman living at the time of the New Testament, only mentioned at [[Rom.16.6|Rom.16.6]]; 
- only referred to as Mary<[[G3137M]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangGreek'>Μαρία</span>\).

Mary was a woman mentioned by Paul in his letter to the Romans. He commended her for working very hard for the church in Rome, indicating that she was a dedicated and diligent member of the Christian community there.
