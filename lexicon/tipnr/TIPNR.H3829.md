<small>*Lebonah • A place near Shiloh in Ephraim*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Lebonah</span>, a place near Shiloh in the territory of Ephraim \([[Judg.21.19|Judges 21:19]]\).
- A location only mentioned at [[Judg.21.19|Jdg.21.19]]; 
- only referred to as Lebonah **<big>[[H3829|לְבוֹנָה]]</big>**.

Lebonah is mentioned in [[Judg.21.19|Judges 21:19]] as a place located north of Bethel, south of Lebonah, and on the east side of the highway that goes up from Bethel to Shechem. This description is given in the context of the Israelites' decision to allow the Benjamites to take wives from the daughters of Shiloh during a yearly feast of the Lord.
