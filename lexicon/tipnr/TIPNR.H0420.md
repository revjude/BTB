<small>*Male • Son of Midian • Son of Midian, grandson of Abraham*</small>

Man living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eldaah</span> was a son of Midian and grandson of Abraham.
- A man of the Midianites living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.25.4|Gen.25.4]]; 
- only referred to as Eldaah **<big>[[H0420|אֶלְדָּעָה]]</big>**; 
-  son of Midian **<big>[[H4080|מִדְיָן]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Ephah **<big>[[H5891|עֵיפָה]]</big>**, Epher **<big>[[H6081|עֵפֶר]]</big>**, Hanoch **<big>[[H2585|חֲנוֹךְ]]</big>** and Abida **<big>[[H0028|אֲבִידָע]]</big>**.

Eldaah is mentioned in [[Gen.25.4|Genesis 25:4]] and [[1Chr.1.33|1 Chronicles 1:33]] as one of the sons of Midian, who was a son of Abraham by his wife Keturah. Eldaah is listed among Abraham's descendants through Midian, along with his brothers Ephah, Epher, Hanoch, and Abida. The sons of Midian became the ancestors of various tribes in the region east of Israel.
