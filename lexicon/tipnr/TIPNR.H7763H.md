Man living before Israel's Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shomer</span> \(or Shemer\) was an Asherite descendant, the son of Heber.
- A man from the tribe of Asher living before Israel's Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.7.32|1Ch.7.32]]; 
- referred to as Shomer<[[H7763H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ֹׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>\), or Shemer \(KJV= Shamer, NIV= Shomer\) <[[H8106H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ֶׁ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>\); 
-  son of Heber<[[H2268G]]>; 
- a brother of Japhlet<[[H3310]]>, Hotham<[[H2369G]]> and Shua<[[H7774]]>; 
-  father of Ahi<[[H0277H]]>, Rohgah<[[H7303]]>, Jehubbah<[[H3160]]> and Aram<[[H0758J]]>.

Shomer \(or Shemer\) is mentioned in [[1Chr.7.32|1 Chronicles 7:32]],34 as part of the genealogy of the tribe of Asher. He is listed as one of the sons of Heber, along with his brothers Hotham and Shua. The genealogy traces the descendants of Asher, one of the twelve tribes of Israel, and provides insight into the family structure and lineage within the tribe.
