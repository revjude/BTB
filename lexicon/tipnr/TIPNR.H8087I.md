Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shema</span>, a son of Elpaal and a descendant of Benjamin.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.13|1Ch.8.13]]; 
- only referred to as Shema **<big>[[H8087|שֶׁמַע]]</big>**; 
-  son of Elpaal **<big>[[H0508|אֶלְפַּעַל]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Eber **<big>[[H5677|עֵבֵר]]</big>**, Misham **<big>[[H4936|מִשְׁעָם]]</big>**, Shemed **<big>[[H8106|שֶׁמֶר]]</big>**, Beriah **<big>[[H1283|בְּרִיעָה]]</big>**, Zebadiah **<big>[[H2069|זְבַדְיָה]]</big>**, Meshullam **<big>[[H4918|מְשֻׁלָּם]]</big>**, Hizki **<big>[[H2395|חִזְקִי]]</big>**, Heber **<big>[[H2268|חֶבֶר]]</big>**, Ishmerai **<big>[[H3461|יִשְׁמְרַי]]</big>**, Izliah **<big>[[H3152|יִזְלִיאָה]]</big>** and Jobab **<big>[[H3103|יוֹבָב]]</big>**.

Shema is mentioned in [[1Chr.8.13|1 Chronicles 8:13]] as one of the sons of Elpaal, who was a descendant of Benjamin. He is listed along with his brother Beriah as the heads of the fathers' houses of the inhabitants of Aijalon. Shema and Beriah drove out the inhabitants of Gath, but no additional details are given about this event or Shema's life.
