<small>*Male • Son of Bilhan • Son of Bilhan from the tribe of Benjamin*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tarshish</span> was a son of Bilhan, a descendant of Benjamin.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.10|1Ch.7.10]]; 
- only referred to as Tarshish **<big>[[H8659|תַּרְשִׁישׁ]]</big>**; 
-  son of Bilhan **<big>[[H1092|בִּלְהָן]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Jeush **<big>[[H3266|יְעוּשׁ]]</big>**, Benjamin **<big>[[H1144|בִּנְיָמִין]]</big>**, Ehud **<big>[[H0164|אֵהוּד]]</big>**, Chenaanah **<big>[[H3668|כְּנַעֲנָה]]</big>**, Zethan **<big>[[H2133|זֵיתָן]]</big>** and Ahishahar **<big>[[H0300|אֲחִישַׁחַר]]</big>**.

Tarshish is mentioned in [[1Chr.7.10|1 Chronicles 7:10]] as a son of Bilhan. Bilhan was a descendant of Benjamin, making Tarshish a member of the tribe of Benjamin. This passage lists the sons of Jediael, tracing the genealogy of Benjamin.
