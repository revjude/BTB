Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Elasah</span> was a priest who married a foreign woman during the Exile.
- A priest living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Ezra.10.22|Ezr.10.22]]; 
- only referred to as Elasah **<big>[[H0501|אֶלְעָשָׂה]]</big>**.

Elasah is mentioned in [[Ezra.10.22|Ezra 10:22]] as one of the priests who had married foreign women during the Babylonian exile. This was a violation of God's command for the Israelites to remain separate from the surrounding nations. As part of the religious reforms led by Ezra, Elasah and the other priests pledged to put away their foreign wives and children.
