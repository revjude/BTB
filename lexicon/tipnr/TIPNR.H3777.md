Man living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Chesed</span> was a son of Nahor, Abraham's brother, and Milcah. \([[Gen.22.22|Gen.22.22]]\)
- A man living at the time of the Patriarchs, only mentioned at [[Gen.22.22|Gen.22.22]]; 
- only referred to as Chesed<[[H3777]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ֶּ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ֶׂ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ד</span>\); 
-  son of Nahor<[[H5152H]]> and Milcah<[[H4435G]]>; 
- a brother of Uz<[[H5780H]]>, Buz<[[H0938G]]>, Kemuel<[[H7055G]]>, Hazo<[[H2375]]>, Pildash<[[H6394]]>, Jidlaph<[[H3044]]> and Bethuel<[[H1328A]]>; 
-  half-brother of: Tebah<[[H2875]]>, Gaham<[[H1514]]>, Tahash<[[H8477]]> and Maacah<[[H4601G]]>.

Chesed is mentioned in [[Gen.22.22|Genesis 22:22]] as one of the eight sons of Nahor and Milcah. Nahor was Abraham's brother, making Chesed Abraham's nephew. The passage lists the descendants of Nahor, providing context for the family background of Abraham.
