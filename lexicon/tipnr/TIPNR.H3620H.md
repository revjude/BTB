<small>*Male • Father of Ezri • Overseer of King David's farm workers*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Chelub</span> the Ishmaelite was in charge of King David's farm workers. \([[1Chr.27.26|1Ch.27.26]]\)
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.27.26|1Ch.27.26]]; 
- only referred to as Chelub<[[H3620H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ְּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>לו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>\); 
-  father of Ezri<[[H5836]]>.

Chelub the Ishmaelite is mentioned in [[1Chr.27.26|1 Chronicles 27:26]] as one of King David's officials. He was responsible for overseeing the agricultural workers who tilled the soil. This Chelub is distinguished from the Chelub in 1 Chronicles 4:11 by his Ishmaelite lineage and his role in David's administration.
