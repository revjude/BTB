<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Lahad</span>, a descendant of Judah through Jahath \([[1Chr.4.2|1Ch.4.2]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.2|1Ch.4.2]]; 
- only referred to as Lahad **<big>[[H3855|לַהַד]]</big>**; 
-  son of Jahath **<big>[[H3189|יַחַת]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Ahumai **<big>[[H0267|אֲחוּמַי]]</big>**.

Lahad was a son of Jahath and a descendant of Judah \([[1Chr.4.2|1Ch.4.2]]\). He is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah, but no additional information is provided about him in the Bible. Lahad likely lived during the period of the judges or the early monarchy in Israel.
