Penuel was a son of Hur, according to the genealogy of Judah in [[1Chr.4.4|1 Chronicles 4:4]]. He is listed as one of the descendants of Hur, who was the firstborn of Ephrathah. No other details are provided about Penuel's life or role. His mention in the genealogy suggests that he was part of the tribe of Judah and lived during the period of Israel's settlement in Canaan or the early monarchy.
