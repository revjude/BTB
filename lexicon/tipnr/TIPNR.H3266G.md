Edomite living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jeush</span>, a son of Esau and Oholibamah, was a chief of Edom \([[Gen.36.5|Gen.36.5]],14,18; [[1Chr.1.35|1Ch.1.35]]\).
- A man of the Edomites living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.36.5|Gen.36.5]]; 
- referred to as Jeush<[[H3266G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>עו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ׁ\), or Jeush \(=Qere. Ketiv= Jeish\) <[[H3274]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יש</span>ׁ\); 
-  son of Esau<[[H6215G]]> and Oholibamah<[[H0173]]>; 
- a brother of Jalam<[[H3281]]> and Korah<[[H7141G]]>; 
-  half-brother of: Eliphaz<[[H0464G]]> and Reuel<[[H7467G]]>.

Jeush was one of the sons of Esau, the twin brother of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\), and Esau's wife Oholibamah, as recorded in [[Gen.36.5|Genesis 36:5]],14 and [[1Chr.1.35|1 Chronicles 1:35]]. He is listed among the chiefs of Edom who descended from Esau. As a grandson of Isaac and great-grandson of Abraham, Jeush was part of the Edomite clan that settled in the region of Seir.
