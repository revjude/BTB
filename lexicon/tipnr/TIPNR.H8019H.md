Woman living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shelomith</span>, a descendant of Bani who returned from Babylonian exile.
- A woman living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Ezra.8.10|Ezr.8.10]]; 
- only referred to as Shelomith **<big>[[H8019|שְׁלֹמִית]]</big>**; 
- a daughter of Josiphiah **<big>[[H3131|יוֹסִפְיָה]]</big>**.

Shelomith is mentioned in [[Ezra.8.10|Ezra 8:10]] as a descendant of Bani. He was among the men who returned to Jerusalem from Babylonian exile under the leadership of Ezra. In the list of those who accompanied Ezra, Shelomith is named along with his relatives, numbering 160 males.
