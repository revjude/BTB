Kishion, also called Kedesh, was a Levitical city located in the territory allocated to the tribe of Issachar \([[Josh.19.20|Joshua 19:20]], 21:28\). It was one of the cities given to the Levites, specifically to the Gershonite clan, for their residence \([[1Chr.6.72|1 Chronicles 6:72]]\).
