Obadiah was a descendant of King David, mentioned in the genealogy of David's descendants \([[1Chr.3.21|1 Chronicles 3:21]]\). He was the son of Arnan and the father of Shecaniah. No further details about his life or role are provided in the Bible. His inclusion in the genealogy highlights the importance of family lineage and the continuation of David's dynasty.
