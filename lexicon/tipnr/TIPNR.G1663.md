<small>*Male • Son of Jorim • An ancestor of Jesus Christ.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eliezer</span> was an ancestor of Jesus Christ \([[Luke.3.29|Luk.3.29]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.29|Luk.3.29]]; 
- only referred to as Eliezer **<big>[[G1663|Ἐλιέζερ]]</big>**; 
- a son of Jorim **<big>[[G2497|Ἰωρείμ]]</big>**; 
-  father of Joshua **<big>[[G2424|Ἰησοῦς]]</big>**.

Eliezer is mentioned in the genealogy of Jesus Christ in the Gospel of Luke \([[Luke.3.29|Luk.3.29]]\). He was an ancestor of Jesus, listed in the lineage from David to Joseph, the husband of Mary. The genealogy in Luke traces Jesus' ancestry through his mother Mary.
