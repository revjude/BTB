<small>*Male • Returned builder • Father of Palal, who repaired Jerusalem's wall*</small>

Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Uzai</span> was the father of Palal, who helped repair Jerusalem's wall.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.25|Neh.3.25]]; 
- only referred to as Uzai<[[H0186]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>או</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
-  father of Palal<[[H6420]]>.

Uzai is mentioned only as the father of Palal, who was one of the people responsible for repairing a section of Jerusalem's wall during the time of Nehemiah. Palal repaired the section next to Pedaiah son of Parosh.
