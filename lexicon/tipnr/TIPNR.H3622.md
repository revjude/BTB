Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Cheluhi</span> was among those who married foreign women during the Exile. \([[Ezra.10.35|Ezr.10.35]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Ezra.10.35|Ezr.10.35]]; 
- only referred to as Cheluhi<[[H3622]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ְּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>לו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\).

Cheluhi is briefly mentioned in [[Ezra.10.35|Ezra 10:35]] as one of the men who had married foreign women during the Babylonian Exile. He was among those who agreed to put away their foreign wives and children in response to Ezra's call for repentance and obedience to God's law.
