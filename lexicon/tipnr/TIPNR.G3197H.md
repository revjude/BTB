Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Melchi</span>, an ancestor of Jesus mentioned in Luke's genealogy \([[Luke.3.28|Luk.3.28]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[Luke.3.28|Luk.3.28]]; 
- only referred to as Melchi<[[G3197H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangGreek'>Μελχί</span>\); 
- a son of Addi<[[G0078]]>; 
-  father of Neri<[[G3518]]>.

Melchi is mentioned in the genealogy of Jesus Christ found in the Gospel of Luke \([[Luke.3.28|Luk.3.28]]\). He is listed as an ancestor of Jesus, but the biblical text does not provide any further details about his life or role in the family line. Based on his inclusion in the genealogy, he was likely of Jewish heritage.
