Ophni was a town located in the territory of Benjamin, mentioned in the list of cities and towns allocated to the tribe during the distribution of the Promised Land under Joshua \(Josh. 18:24\). It is listed along with other towns in the region, such as Chephar-ammoni, Ophrah, and Geba. Its location is unknown.
