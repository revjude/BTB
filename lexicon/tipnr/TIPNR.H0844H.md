Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Asriel</span> was a son of Gilead and a descendant of Manasseh \(1 Chr 7:14\).
- A man from the tribe of Manasseh living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.14|1Ch.7.14]]; 
- only referred to as Asriel **<big>[[H0844|אַשְׂרִיאֵל]]</big>**; 
-  son of Manasseh **<big>[[H4519|מְנַשֶּׁה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Machir **<big>[[H4353|מָכִיר]]</big>**.

Asriel is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Manasseh recorded in 1 Chronicles 7. He is listed as a son of Gilead, who was a descendant of Manasseh through his Aramean concubine. This genealogy traces the lineage of Manasseh's descendants, focusing on the clans and families that emerged from his line. Asriel's mention in this context suggests he was a significant figure within the Gileadite clan, although no further details are provided about his life or role.
