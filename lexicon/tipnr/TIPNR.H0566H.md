Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Imri</span> was the father of Zaccur, who helped rebuild the wall of Jerusalem \([[Neh.3.2|Neh.3.2]]\).
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.2|Neh.3.2]]; 
- only referred to as Imri **<big>[[H0566|אִמְרִי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Zaccur **<big>[[H2139|זַכּוּר]]</big>**.

Imri was the father of Zaccur, who is mentioned in [[Neh.3.2|Nehemiah 3:2]] as one of the people involved in rebuilding the wall of Jerusalem. Zaccur worked on repairing the section of the wall next to the high priest Eliashib. No other details are provided about Imri himself in the biblical text. His name means "eloquent" in Hebrew.
