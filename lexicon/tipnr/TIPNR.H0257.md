Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ahban</span> was the son of Abishur, a descendant of Judah. \([[1Chr.2.29|1Ch.2.29]]\)
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.2.29|1Ch.2.29]]; 
- only referred to as Ahban<[[H0257]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\); 
-  son of Abishur<[[H0051]]> and Abihail<[[H0032H]]>; 
- a brother of Molid<[[H4140]]>.

Ahban is mentioned briefly in the genealogy of Judah. He was the son of Abishur and his wife Abihail. Ahban had a brother named Molid. No further information about Ahban's life or role is provided in the biblical text. \([[1Chr.2.29|1Ch.2.29]]\).
