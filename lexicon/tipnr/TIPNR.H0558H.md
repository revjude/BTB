Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Amaziah</span>, the son of Joshah, was a descendant of Simeon.
- A man from the tribe of Simeon\(?\) living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.34|1Ch.4.34]]; 
- only referred to as Amaziah<[[H0558H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>צ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\);

Amaziah was a descendant of Simeon, the son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\). He was the son of Joshah and is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Simeon.
