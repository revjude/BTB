<small>*Male • Son of Hur • Father or founder of Hushah, of Ephrathah.*</small>

Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ezer</span> was the father or founder of Hushah and a son of Hur of Ephrathah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.4|1Ch.4.4]]; 
- only referred to as Ezer<[[H5829G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֵ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>\); 
-  son of Hur<[[H2354G]]>; 
- a brother of Uri<[[H0221G]]>, Shobal<[[H7732H]]>, Salma<[[H8007H]]>, Hareph<[[H2780]]>, Jezreel<[[H3157J]]>, Ishma<[[H3457]]>, Idbash<[[H3031]]>, Hazzelelponi<[[H6753]]> and Penuel<[[H6439H]]>; 
-  father or founder of Hushah<[[H2364]]>.

Ezer is mentioned in [[1Chr.4.4|1 Chronicles 4:4]] as a son of Hur, the firstborn of Ephrathah. Ephrathah was the wife of Caleb, son of Hezron \(1 Chronicles 2:19, 50\). Ezer is identified as the father of Hushah, which could refer to either an individual or a place associated with him.
