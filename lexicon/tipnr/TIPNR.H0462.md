Elienai was a Benjamite mentioned in the genealogy of Shimei \([[1Chr.8.20|1Ch.8.20]]\). He was one of the descendants of Benjamin, the youngest son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\). The genealogy in 1 Chronicles 8 lists the heads of families and clans in the tribe of Benjamin.
