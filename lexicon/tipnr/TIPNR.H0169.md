Ohel was a descendant of Judah, mentioned in the genealogy of Zerubbabel \([[1Chr.3.20|1 Chronicles 3:20]]\). Zerubbabel was a prominent figure in the return of the Jews from Babylonian exile and the rebuilding of the temple in Jerusalem. Ohel is listed among the sons of Zerubbabel, indicating his place in the royal lineage of Judah.
