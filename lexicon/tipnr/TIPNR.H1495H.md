<small>*Male • Son of Haran • Grandson of Caleb through Haran*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Gazez</span> was a grandson of Caleb, born to Haran's son Gazez.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.2.46b|1Ch.2.46b]]; 
- only referred to as Gazez<[[H1495H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ג</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>\); 
- a son of Haran<[[H2771B]]>.

Gazez was a grandson of Caleb son of Hezron, born to Caleb's son Gazez \(1Ch.2.46\). His father, also named Gazez, was the son of Haran, Caleb's concubine. This makes the younger Gazez a great-grandson of Hezron and a member of the tribe of Judah. The text does not provide any additional details about his life or family.
