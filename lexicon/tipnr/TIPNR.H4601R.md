Maacah \(or Maacath\) is the name of a region near Geshur, east of the Jordan River \(Deuteronomy 3:14; [[Josh.12.5|Joshua 12:5]]; 13:11, 13\). The Israelites allowed the Geshurites and Maacathites to remain in the land \([[Josh.13.13|Joshua 13:13]]\). Maacah is also mentioned as one of the regions from which the Ammonites hired chariots and horsemen to fight against David \([[2Sam.10.6|2 Samuel 10:6]], 8; [[1Chr.19.7|1 Chronicles 19:7]]\).
