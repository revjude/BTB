Zuriel Zuriel was the son of Abihail and the leader of the Merarite clan of Levites during the time of Moses \([[Num.3.35|Numbers 3:35]]\). The Merarites were one of the three main divisions of the Levites, responsible for transporting and setting up the framework, pillars, and bases of the tabernacle during the Israelites' journey through the wilderness.
