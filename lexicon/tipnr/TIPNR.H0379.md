Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ishhod</span> was the son of Hammoleketh, who was the sister of Manasseh \([[1Chr.7.18|1Ch.7.18]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Manasseh living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.18|1Ch.7.18]]; 
- only referred to as Ishhod<[[H0379]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ד</span>\); 
- his mother was Hammolecheth<[[H4447G]]>; 
- a brother of Abiezer<[[H0044H]]> and Mahlah<[[H4244H]]>.

Ishhod is mentioned briefly in a genealogy of the tribe of Manasseh \([[1Chr.7.18|1Ch.7.18]]\). He was the son of Hammoleketh, who is described as the sister of Manasseh, the patriarch of the tribe. No other details are provided about Ishhod's life or role within the clan. His name, which means "man of majesty" or "man of glory," is the only information given. The mention of Ishhod in the genealogy helps to trace the lineage of Manasseh and the relationships within his extended family.
