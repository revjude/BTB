Man living before Israel's Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Iru</span> was a son of Caleb, along with his brothers Elah and Naam \([[1Chr.4.15|1Ch.4.15]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living before Israel's Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.15|1Ch.4.15]]; 
- only referred to as Iru<[[H5900]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ירו</span>ּ\); 
-  son of Caleb<[[H3612G]]>; 
- a brother of Achsah<[[H5915G]]>, Elah<[[H0425J]]> and Naam<[[H5277]]>.

Iru is mentioned in [[1Chr.4.15|1 Chronicles 4:15]] as one of the sons of Caleb, son of Jephunneh. He is listed along with his brothers Elah and Naam. The passage is part of a genealogy tracing the descendants of Judah. No other details are provided about Iru's life or family. As a son of Caleb, he was from the tribe of Judah and lived during the period of Israel's settlement in Canaan.
