<small>*Male • From Edom • A chief of Edom, descendant of Esau*</small>

Edomite living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Elah</span> was a chief of Edom, descended from Esau.
- A man living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.36.41|Gen.36.41]]; 
- only referred to as Elah<[[H0425G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\).

Elah is mentioned in [[Gen.36.41|Genesis 36:41]] and [[1Chr.1.52|1 Chronicles 1:52]] as one of the chiefs of Edom, a nation descended from Esau, the brother of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\). The Edomites were the descendants of Esau who settled in the region southeast of Judah. Elah is listed among the chiefs who ruled in Edom before any king reigned over Israel.
