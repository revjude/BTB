<small>*Male • Son of Shual • Son of Shual, an Asherite*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Arah</span>, the son of Shual \('Ulla'\), was a descendant of Asher.
- A man from the tribe of Asher living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.39|1Ch.7.39]]; 
- only referred to as Arah<[[H0733G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>\); 
-  son of Shual<[[H7777B]]>; 
- a brother of Hanniel<[[H2592H]]> and Rizia<[[H7525]]>.

Arah is mentioned in [[1Chr.7.39|1 Chronicles 7:39]] as one of the sons of Shual \('Ulla'\), who was a descendant of Asher. He is listed among the members of the tribe of Asher in the genealogical records.
