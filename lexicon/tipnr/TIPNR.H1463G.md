<small>*Male • Son of Shemaiah • A descendant of Reuben, son of Joel.*</small>

Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Gog</span>, a descendant of Reuben, is mentioned in the genealogy of Reuben in [[1Chr.5.4|1 Chronicles 5:4]].
- A man from the tribe of Reuben  living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.5.4|1Ch.5.4]]; 
- only referred to as Gog **<big>[[H1463|גּוֹג]]</big>**; 
- a son of Shemaiah **<big>[[H8098|שְׁמַעְיָה]]</big>**; 
-  father of Shimei **<big>[[H8096|שִׁמְעִי]]</big>**.

Gog is mentioned in [[1Chr.5.4|1 Chronicles 5:4]] as a descendant of Reuben, the eldest son of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\). He is listed in the genealogy as the son of Joel, who was the son of Shemaiah, the son of Joel, the son of Reaiah, the son of Baal, the son of Reuben.
