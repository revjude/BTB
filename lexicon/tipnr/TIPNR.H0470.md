<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Elika</span> the Harodite was one of David's mighty men \([[2Sam.23.25|2Sa.23.25]]\).
- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, only mentioned at [[2Sam.23.25|2Sa.23.25]]; 
- only referred to as Elika<[[H0470]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֱ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>יק</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>\).

Elika the Harodite is mentioned in the list of David's mighty men, a group of elite warriors who fought alongside King David \([[2Sam.23.25|2Sa.23.25]]\). The mighty men were known for their exceptional bravery, strength, and military prowess. Elika is described as a Harodite, which likely refers to his place of origin or clan affiliation.
