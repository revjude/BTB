Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Manasseh</span>, a descendant of Pahath-moab, divorced his foreign wife during Ezra's reforms \([[Ezra.10.30|Ezr.10.30]]\).
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Ezra.10.30|Ezr.10.30]]; 
- only referred to as Manasseh **<big>[[H4519|מְנַשֶּׁה]]</big>**.

Manasseh was a man from the family of Pahath-moab who had married a foreign woman during the time of Ezra. As part of Ezra's reforms to purify the Jewish community, Manasseh and others who had married foreign wives were required to divorce them \([[Ezra.10.30|Ezra 10:30]]\).
