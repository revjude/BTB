<small>*Male • Son of Hur • A son of Hur in the genealogy of Judah*</small>

Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jezreel</span> was a son of Hur, mentioned in the genealogy of Judah \([[1Chr.4.3|1Ch.4.3]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.3|1Ch.4.3]]; 
- only referred to as Jezreel **<big>[[H3157|יִזְרְעֵאל]]</big>**; 
-  son of Hur **<big>[[H2354|חוּר]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Uri **<big>[[H0221|אוּרִי]]</big>**, Shobal **<big>[[H7732|שׁוֹבָל]]</big>**, Salma **<big>[[H8007|שַׂלְמָא]]</big>**, Hareph **<big>[[H2780|חָרֵף]]</big>**, Ishma **<big>[[H3457|יִשְׁמָא]]</big>**, Idbash **<big>[[H3031|יִדְבָּשׁ]]</big>**, Hazzelelponi **<big>[[H6753|צְלֶלְפּוֹנִי]]</big>**, Penuel **<big>[[H6439|פְּנוּאֵל]]</big>** and Ezer **<big>[[H5829|עֵזֶר]]</big>**.

Jezreel is mentioned in [[1Chr.4.3|1 Chronicles 4:3]] as one of the sons of Hur in the genealogy of Judah, though the text is difficult to interpret. He is listed along with his brothers Ishma and Idbash, as well as their sister Hazzelelponi.
