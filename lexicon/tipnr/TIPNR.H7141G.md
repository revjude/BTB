Edomite living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Korah</span>, son of Esau and Oholibamah \([[Gen.36.5|Gen.36.5]],14\).
- A man of the Edomites living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.36.5|Gen.36.5]]; 
- only referred to as Korah<[[H7141G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ק</span>ֹ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>\); 
-  son of Esau<[[H6215G]]> and Oholibamah<[[H0173]]>; 
- a brother of Jeush<[[H3266G]]> and Jalam<[[H3281]]>; 
-  half-brother of: Eliphaz<[[H0464G]]> and Reuel<[[H7467G]]>.

Korah was a son of Esau and his wife Oholibamah \([[Gen.36.5|Gen.36.5]], 18; [[1Chr.1.35|1Ch.1.35]]\). He was born in Canaan before Esau moved his family to Seir \([[Gen.36.5|Gen.36.5]]-8\). Korah was a grandson of Zibeon the Hivite \([[Gen.36.14|Gen.36.14]]\). He became one of the chiefs of Edom \([[Gen.36.18|Gen.36.18]]\).
