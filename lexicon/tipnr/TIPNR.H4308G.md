Woman living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Matred</span> was the mother of Mehetabel, who was the wife of Hadar, king of Edom \([[Gen.36.39|Gen.36.39]]; [[1Chr.1.50|1Ch.1.50]]\).
- A woman living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.36.39|Gen.36.39]]; 
- only referred to as Matred **<big>[[H4308|מַטְרֵד]]</big>**; 
- a daughter of Mezahab **<big>[[H4314|מֵי זָהָב]]</big>**; 
-  mother of Mehetabel **<big>[[H4105|מְהֵיטַבְאֵל]]</big>**.

Matred was a woman mentioned in the genealogies of the kings of Edom. She was the mother of Mehetabel, who became the wife of Hadar \(or Hadad\), the last king of Edom mentioned before the monarchy of Israel \([[Gen.36.39|Genesis 36:39]]; [[1Chr.1.50|1 Chronicles 1:50]]\).
