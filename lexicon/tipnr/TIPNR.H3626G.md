<small>*Male • Returned builder • Father of Shallum, who repaired the Fountain Gate*</small>

Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Col-hozeh</span> was the father of Shallum \(or 'Shallun'\), who repaired the Fountain Gate. \([[Neh.3.15|Neh.3.15]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.15|Neh.3.15]]; 
- only referred to as Col-hozeh<[[H3626G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ׇּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>־<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
-  father of Shallum<[[H7968]]>.

Col-hozeh is mentioned in [[Neh.3.15|Nehemiah 3:15]] as the father of Shallum \(or 'Shallun'\), who was responsible for repairing the Fountain Gate during the rebuilding of Jerusalem's wall. Shallum, the son of Col-hozeh, is described as the leader of the district of Mizpah. This suggests that Col-hozeh's family held a prominent position in the region.
