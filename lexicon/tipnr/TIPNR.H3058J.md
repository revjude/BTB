Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jehu</span>, the son of Joshibiah, was a descendant of Simeon mentioned in the genealogy recorded in 1 Chronicles.
- A man from the tribe of Simeon\(?\) living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.35|1Ch.4.35]]; 
- only referred to as Jehu<[[H3058J]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>\); 
- a son of Joshibiah<[[H3143]]>.

Jehu, the son of Joshibiah, is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Simeon in 1 Chronicles. He was part of the family of Asiel, a descendant of Simeon.
