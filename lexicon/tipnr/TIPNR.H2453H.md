<small>*Male • David's warrior • Father of Jehiel, one of David's family servants.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hachmoni</span> was the father of Jehiel who, with Jonathan attended King David's sons.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.27.32|1Ch.27.32]]; 
- only referred to as Hachmoni **<big>[[H2453|חַכְמוֹנִי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Jehiel **<big>[[H3171|יְחִיאֵל]]</big>**.

Hachmoni is mentioned in [[1Chr.27.32|1 Chronicles 27:32]] as the father of Jehiel who looked after  King David's sons along with David's counselor, Jonathan.  \([[1Chr.27.32|1 Chronicles 27:32]]\).
