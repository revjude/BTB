Zuph/Zophai Zuph, also called Zophai in [[1Chr.6.26|1 Chronicles 6:26]], was a Levite of the Kohathite clan. He was an ancestor of Elkanah, the father of the prophet Samuel \([[1Sam.1.1|1 Samuel 1:1]]\). In the genealogy of the Kohathites, he is listed as the son of Elkanah and father of Toah \([[1Chr.6.35|1 Chronicles 6:35]]\). Zuph lived in the hill country of Ephraim, where his descendant Samuel was born \([[1Sam.1.1|1 Samuel 1:1]]\).
