<small>*Male • Son of Obed • Son of Obed, a descendant of Judah.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jehu</span> was a descendant of Judah through Obed, as recorded in the genealogy in 1 Chronicles.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at 1Ch.2.38; 
- only referred to as Jehu<[[H3058I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>\); 
- a son of Obed<[[H5744H]]>; 
-  father of Azariah<[[H5838J]]>.

Jehu, the son of Obed, is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah in 1 Chronicles. He was a descendant of Hezron, one of the sons of Perez, who was a son of Judah.
