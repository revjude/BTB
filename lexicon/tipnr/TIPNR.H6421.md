- A priest living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.11.12|Neh.11.12]]; 
- only referred to as Pelaliah **<big>[[H6421|פְּלַלְיָה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Amzi **<big>[[H0557|אַמְצִי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Jeroham **<big>[[H3395|יְרֹחָם]]</big>**.

Pelaliah was a priest who resided in Jerusalem during the time of Nehemiah, according to [[Neh.11.12|Nehemiah 11:12]]. He was the son of Amzi, the son of Zechariah, the son of Pashhur, the son of Malchijah. This lineage indicates that Pelaliah belonged to a priestly family. As a priest living in Jerusalem, he would have had duties related to the temple service and worship.
