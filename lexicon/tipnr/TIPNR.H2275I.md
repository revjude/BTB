Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hebron</span> was a son of Mareshah, a descendant of Caleb from the tribe of Judah. \([[1Chr.2.42|1Ch.2.42]],43\)
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.2.42|1Ch.2.42]]; 
- only referred to as Hebron<[[H2275I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>רו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\); 
- a son of Mareshah<[[H4762I]]>; 
-  father of Korah<[[H7141J]]>, Tappuah<[[H8599A]]>, Rekem<[[H7552I]]>, Shema<[[H8087G]]>.

Hebron is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah in 1 Chronicles as a son of Mareshah. Mareshah was a descendant of Caleb, who was from the tribe of Judah \([[1Chr.2.42|1Ch.2.42]],43\). Hebron is also the name of the town founded by his father Mareshah.
