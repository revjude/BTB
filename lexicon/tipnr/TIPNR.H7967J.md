<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shallum</span> was a son of Josiah, king of Judah, and the brother of Jehoiakim and Zedekiah.

- only referred to as Shallum<[[H7967J]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ַׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ם</span>\);

Shallum, mentioned in [[1Chr.3.15|1 Chronicles 3:15]], was one of the sons of Josiah, the king of Judah. He is listed among Josiah's four sons, along with Johanan, Jehoiakim, and Zedekiah. The order in which the sons are mentioned may not necessarily reflect their birth order. Shallum's brothers, Jehoiakim and Zedekiah, both became kings of Judah, with Jehoiakim succeeding their father Josiah and Zedekiah being appointed by the Babylonian king Nebuchadnezzar II.
