Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Meres</span>, one of the seven princes of Persia and Media who advised King Ahasuerus \([[Esth.1.14|Est.1.14]]\).
- A prince of Persia living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Esth.1.14|Est.1.14]]; 
- only referred to as Meres<[[H4825]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ֶ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ס</span>\).

Meres was one of the seven princes of Persia and Media who had access to King Ahasuerus and were closest to him in rank \([[Esth.1.14|Est.1.14]]\). These princes were consulted by the king when he sought advice on how to respond to Queen Vashti's refusal to appear before him and his guests during a royal feast.
