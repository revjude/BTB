<small>*Male • Benjamite commander • Benjamite commander of 200,000 armed troops*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Eliada</span> was a Benjamite commander who led 200,000 armed troops during the reign of King Jehoshaphat.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[2Chr.17.17|2Ch.17.17]]; 
- only referred to as Eliada<[[H0450I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ד</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>\).

Eliada is mentioned in [[2Chr.17.17|2 Chronicles 17:17]] as a commander from the tribe of Benjamin during the reign of King Jehoshaphat. He is described as a valiant warrior who led a force of 200,000 armed troops. This large number of soldiers highlights the military strength and organization of Judah under Jehoshaphat's leadership. Eliada's inclusion in the list of commanders suggests that he was a skilled military leader trusted by the king.
