Joshbekashah is mentioned in [[1Chr.25.4|1 Chronicles 25:4]] and 25:24 as one of the sons of Heman, a prominent Levitical musician during the reign of King David. Heman, along with Asaph and Jeduthun, was appointed by David to lead the music and worship in the house of the Lord \(1 Chronicles 25:1\). Joshbekashah, along with his brothers, was set apart for the ministry of prophesying with musical instruments \(1 Chronicles 25:2\). He was among those who were assigned duties by casting lots, ensuring a fair distribution of responsibilities \(1 Chronicles 25:8\).
