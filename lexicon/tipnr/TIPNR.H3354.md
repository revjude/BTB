Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jekuthiel</span> was the son of Mered and the  father \(ie founder\) of Zanoah, as recorded in the genealogy of Judah in 1 Chronicles.
- A man from the tribe of Judah\(?\) living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.18|1Ch.4.18]]; 
- only referred to as Jekuthiel **<big>[[H3354|יְקוּתִיאֵל]]</big>**; 
-  son of Mered **<big>[[H4778|מֶרֶד]]</big>** and Jehudijah **<big>[[H3057|יְהֻדִיָּה]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Jered **<big>[[H3382|יֶרֶד]]</big>** and Heber **<big>[[H2268|חֶבֶר]]</big>**;

- father or founder of Zanoah **<big>[[H2182|זָנוֹחַ]]</big>**.

Jekuthiel is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah in 1 Chronicles. He was the son of Mered, who was a descendant of Judah. Jekuthiel's mother was Jehudijah \(or the Judahite woman\), one of Mered's wives. Jekuthiel is described as the  father \(or founder\) of Zanoah, which probably refers to a town associated with his descendants.
