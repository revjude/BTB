Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Paseah</span>, a son of Eshton, who was from the tribe of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.12|1Ch.4.12]]; 
- only referred to as Paseah **<big>[[H6454|פָּסֵחַ]]</big>**; 
-  son of Eshton **<big>[[H0850|אֶשְׁתּוֹן]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Beth-rapha **<big>[[H1051|בֵּית רָפָא]]</big>** , Tehinnah **<big>[[H8468|תְּחִנָּה]]</big>**.

Paseah was a son of Eshton, as listed in the genealogy of Judah in [[1Chr.4.12|1 Chronicles 4:12]]. Eshton was the father of Beth-rapha, Paseah and Tehinnah, who was the father of Ir-nahash. These were the men of Recah, a clan within the tribe of Judah.
