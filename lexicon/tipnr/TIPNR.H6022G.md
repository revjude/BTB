<small>*Male • Son of Elkanah • Levite descendant of Kohath*</small>

Levite living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Amasai</span>, a Levite descended from Kohath, was an ancestor of the prophet Samuel.
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.6.25|1Ch.6.25]]; 
- only referred to as Amasai **<big>[[H6022|עֲמָשַׂי]]</big>**; 
- a son of Elkanah **<big>[[H0511|אֶלְקָנָה]]</big>**; 
-  father of Ahimoth **<big>[[H0287|אֲחִימוֹת]]</big>**.

Amasai was a Levite who belonged to the Kohathite clan. He was a descendant of Kohath, the son of Levi. Amasai is mentioned in the genealogy of the prophet Samuel, indicating that he was one of Samuel's ancestors. As a Levite, Amasai would have been involved in the religious duties and services in the tabernacle or temple.
