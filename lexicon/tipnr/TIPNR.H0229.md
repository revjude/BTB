<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ezbai</span> was the father of Naarai, who was one of David's mighty men.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.11.37|1Ch.11.37]]; 
- only referred to as Ezbai **<big>[[H0229|אֶזְבַּי]]</big>**;

Ezbai is mentioned only once in the Bible, in [[1Chr.11.37|1 Chronicles 11:37]]. He was the father of Naarai, who was one of King David's mighty men. The mighty men were a group of elite warriors who played a significant role in David's military campaigns and in establishing his kingdom \(2 Samuel 23:8-39; 1 Chronicles 11:10-47\). Naarai, son of Ezbai, is listed among these mighty men, indicating that he was a skilled and valiant warrior.
