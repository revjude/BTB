<small>*Male • Son of Amzi • A Levite descendant of Merari.*</small>

Levite living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Hilkiah</span> was a Levite from the family of Merari, mentioned in the genealogy of the Levites.
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.6.45|1Ch.6.45]]; 
- only referred to as Hilkiah<[[H2518I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ק</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ּ\); 
- a son of Amzi<[[H0557G]]>; 
-  father of Amaziah<[[H0558I]]>.

Hilkiah is mentioned in [[1Chr.6.45|1 Chronicles 6:45]] as a descendant of Merari, one of the three sons of Levi. He is listed in the genealogy of the Levites, which traces the lineage of the priestly and temple servants.
