<small>*Male • Returned leader • Levite who led the people in confession*</small>

Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Chenani</span> was a Levite who led the people in confession and worship. \([[Neh.9.4|Neh.9.4]]\)
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.9.4|Neh.9.4]]; 
- only referred to as Chenani<[[H3662]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ְּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\).

Chenani is mentioned in [[Neh.9.4|Nehemiah 9:4]] as one of the Levites who stood on the stairs and led the people in a prayer of confession and worship during the time of Ezra and Nehemiah. He is listed alongside other Levites such as Jeshua, Bani, Kadmiel, Shebaniah, Bunni, Sherebiah, Bani, and Chenani. The prayer they led acknowledged God's faithfulness, recounted Israel's history, and confessed the nation's sins.
