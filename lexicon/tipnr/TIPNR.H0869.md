<small>*Male • Son of Ashhur • Son of Ashhur, the founder of Tekoa.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ethnan</span> was a son of Ashhur \(or 'Ashur'\), the  father \(ie founder\) of Tekoa.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.7|1Ch.4.7]]; 
- only referred to as Ethnan<[[H0869]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ת</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\); 
-  son of Ashhur<[[H0806]]> and Helah<[[H2458]]>; 
- a brother of Haahashtari<[[H0326]]>, Zereth<[[H6889]]>, Izhar<[[H3328]]> and Koz<[[H6976G]]>; 
-  half-brother of: Ahuzzam<[[H0275]]>, Hepher<[[H2660G]]> and Temeni<[[H8488]]>.

Ethnan is mentioned briefly in the genealogy of Judah \([[1Chr.4.7|1 Chronicles 4:7]]\). He was one of the sons of Ashhur \(or 'Ashur'\), who was known as the  father \(ie founder\) of Tekoa, a town in Judah.
