Son of Jashen The son of Jashen is mentioned in [[2Sam.23.32|2 Samuel 23:32]] as one of David's mighty men. His name is not given in the biblical text. The mighty men were a group of skilled warriors who fought alongside David and played significant roles in his military campaigns. They were known for their bravery, strength, and loyalty to David. The son of Jashen is listed among these warriors, indicating his prowess in battle and his dedication to serving King David.
