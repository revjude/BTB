<small>*Male • Son of Shemida • Son of Shemidah, from the tribe of Manasseh*</small>

Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ahian</span> was a son of Shemidah, a descendant of Manasseh. \([[1Chr.7.19|1Ch.7.19]]\)
- A man from the tribe of Manasseh living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.19|1Ch.7.19]]; 
- only referred to as Ahian **<big>[[H0291|אַחְיָן]]</big>**; 
-  son of Shemida **<big>[[H8061|שְׁמִידָע]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Shechem **<big>[[H7928|שֶׁכֶם]]</big>**, Likhi **<big>[[H3949|לִקְחִי]]</big>** and Aniam **<big>[[H0593|אֲנִיעָם]]</big>**.

Ahian is mentioned briefly in the genealogy of the tribe of Manasseh. He was one of the sons of Shemidah, who was a descendant of Manasseh through his son Gilead. No further details about Ahian's life or role are provided in the biblical text. \([[1Chr.7.19|1Ch.7.19]]\).
