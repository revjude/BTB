<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jehoram</span> was a Levite sent by Jehoshaphat to teach the Book of the Law in Judah.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[2Chr.17.8|2Ch.17.8]]; 
- only referred to as Jehoram<[[H3088H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>הו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ם</span>\).

Jehoram was a Levite who was sent by King Jehoshaphat of Judah to teach the Book of the Law in the cities of Judah. He was part of a group of Levites, officials, and priests tasked with instructing the people in the ways of the Lord.
