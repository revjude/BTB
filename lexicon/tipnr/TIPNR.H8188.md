<small>*Male • Priest during David's reign, head of the fourth division*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Seorim</span> was a priest who served during David's reign and was the head of the fourth priestly division.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.24.8|1Ch.24.8]]; 
- only referred to as Seorim **<big>[[H8188|שְׂעֹרִים]]</big>**.

Seorim is mentioned in [[1Chr.24.8|1 Chronicles 24:8]] as a priest who served during the reign of King David. He was the head of the fourth priestly division, as organized by David. The priests were divided into twenty-four divisions, each responsible for serving in the temple for a specific period. This organizational structure ensured a systematic and orderly approach to the priestly duties. Seorim's division would have been responsible for carrying out the assigned temple services when their turn came.
