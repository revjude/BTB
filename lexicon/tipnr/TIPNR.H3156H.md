<small>*Male • Returned leader • A leader of the singers at the wall dedication*</small>

Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jezrahiah</span> was one of the leaders of the singers during the dedication of Jerusalem's wall \([[Neh.12.42|Neh.12.42]]\).
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.12.42|Neh.12.42]]; 
- only referred to as Jezrahiah **<big>[[H3156|יִזְרַחְיָה]]</big>**.

Jezrahiah is briefly mentioned in [[Neh.12.42|Nehemiah 12:42]] as one of the leaders of the singers during the dedication of the wall of Jerusalem. After the completion of the wall's reconstruction under Nehemiah's leadership, a great celebration was held to dedicate the wall. Jezrahiah, along with other leaders, is named as directing the singers and musicians who provided joyful praise during the dedication ceremony.
