Man living at the time of the New Testament

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Patrobas</span>, a believer in Rome to whom Paul sent greetings.
- A man living at the time of the New Testament, only mentioned at [[Rom.16.14|Rom.16.14]]; 
- only referred to as Patrobas<[[G3969]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangGreek'>Πατροβᾶς</span>\).

Patrobas was a Christian in Rome to whom Paul sent greetings in [[Rom.16.14|Romans 16:14]]. He is mentioned along with several others, including Hermes, Hermas, and Philologus, suggesting he was part of the Christian community in Rome. His inclusion in Paul's greetings indicates he was known and appreciated by the apostle.
