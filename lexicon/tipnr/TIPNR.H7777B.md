Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shual</span> \(or Ulla\) was an Asherite descendant, the son of Zophah.
- A man from the tribe of Asher living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.7.36|1Ch.7.36]]; 
- referred to as Shual **<big>[[H7777|שׁוּעָל]]</big>**, or Ulla **<big>[[H5925|עֻלָּא]]</big>**; 
-  son of Zophah **<big>[[H6690|צוֹפַח]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Suah **<big>[[H5477|סוּחַ]]</big>**, Harnepher **<big>[[H2774|חַרְנֶפֶר]]</big>**, Beri **<big>[[H1275|בֵּרִי]]</big>**, Imrah **<big>[[H3236|יִמְרָה]]</big>**, Bezer **<big>[[H1221|בֶּצֶר]]</big>**, Hod **<big>[[H1936|הוֹד]]</big>**, Shamma **<big>[[H8037|שַׁמָּא]]</big>**, Shilshah **<big>[[H8030|שִׁלְשָׁה]]</big>**, Ithran **<big>[[H3506|יִתְרָן]]</big>** and Beera **<big>[[H0878|בְּאֵרָא]]</big>**; 
-  father of Arah **<big>[[H0733|אָרַח]]</big>**, Hanniel **<big>[[H2592|חַנִּיאֵל]]</big>** and Rizia **<big>[[H7525|רִצְיָא]]</big>**.

Shual \(or Ulla\) is mentioned in [[1Chr.7.36|1 Chronicles 7:36]],39 as part of the genealogy of the tribe of Asher. He is listed as one of the sons of Zophah, who was a descendant of Asher through Beriah. The genealogy traces the lineage of various clans and families within the tribe of Asher, providing insight into the tribal structure and organization. In verse 39, Shual is mentioned again, this time with the alternative name Ulla. This suggests that he may have been known by both names or that there was some variation in the transmission of the genealogical records.
