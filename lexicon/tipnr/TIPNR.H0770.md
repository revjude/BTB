<small>*Male • Son of Hananiah • Descendant of David's son Solomon*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Arnan</span> was a descendant of David's son Solomon \(1 Chr 3:21\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.3.21|1Ch.3.21]]; 
- only referred to as Arnan<[[H0770]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>נ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\); 
-  son of Hananiah<[[H2608L]]>; 
- a brother of Pelatiah<[[H6410G]]>, Jeshaiah<[[H3470B]]>, Rephaiah<[[H7509G]]>, Obadiah<[[H5662H]]> and Shecaniah<[[H7935G]]>.

Arnan was a descendant of King David of Israel through his son Solomon, appearing in a genealogy in 1 Chronicles. He was the son of Rephaiah, the son of Jeshaiah, the son of Hananiah, the son of Zerubbabel, who led the first group of exiles back from Babylon.
