<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Elam</span> was a priest or Levite during the time of Nehemiah.
- A priest living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.12.42|Neh.12.42]];

Elam's inclusion in this list suggests he was a significant priest or Levite, but no additional information is provided about his specific role or responsibilities.
