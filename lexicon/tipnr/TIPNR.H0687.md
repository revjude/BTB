Ezer was one of the sons of Seir the Horite, who inhabited the land of Seir before the descendants of Esau \([[Gen.36.21|Genesis 36:21]]; [[1Chr.1.38|1 Chronicles 1:38]]\). He was a Horite chief and is mentioned among the leaders of the Horite clans \([[Gen.36.30|Genesis 36:30]]; [[1Chr.1.42|1 Chronicles 1:42]]\). The Horites were the original inhabitants of Seir, but they were later dispossessed by the descendants of Esau \(Deuteronomy 2:12\). Ezer had three sons: Bilhan, Zaavan, and Akan \([[Gen.36.27|Genesis 36:27]]; [[1Chr.1.42|1 Chronicles 1:42]]\).
