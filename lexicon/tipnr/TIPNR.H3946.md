<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Lakkum</span>, a border town of the tribe of Naphtali \([[Josh.19.33|Joshua 19:33]]\).
- A location near Tabor\_Mount<[[H8396G]]> only mentioned at [[Josh.19.33|Jos.19.33]]; 
- only referred to as Lakkum<[[H3946]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ק</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ם</span>\).

Lakkum is mentioned in [[Josh.19.33|Joshua 19:33]] as one of the border towns of the territory allocated to the tribe of Naphtali. The verse describes the border of Naphtali, stating that it "turned eastward to Aznoth-tabor and went from there to Hukkok, touching Zebulun at the south and Asher on the west and Judah on the east at the Jordan."
