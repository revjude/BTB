- A woman living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Kgs.22.42|1Ki.22.42]]; 
- only referred to as Azubah<[[H5806G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>זו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ב</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
- a daughter of Shilhi<[[H7977]]>; 
- wife of Asa<[[H0609G]]>; 
-  mother of Jehoshaphat<[[H3092I]]>.

Azubah was the wife of King Asa and the mother of King Jehoshaphat, who ruled over the Kingdom of Judah. She is mentioned in [[1Kgs.22.42|1 Kings 22:42]] and [[2Chr.20.31|2 Chronicles 20:31]], which provide the introductory information about Jehoshaphat as he became king. Both passages state that Jehoshaphat's mother's name was Azubah, and identify her as the daughter of Shilhi.
