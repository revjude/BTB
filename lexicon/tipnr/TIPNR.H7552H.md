<small>*Rekem • City in Benjamin*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Rekem</span> was a city allotted to the tribe of Benjamin \(Jos. 18:27\).
- A location near Irpeel<[[H3416]]> only mentioned at [[Josh.18.27|Jos.18.27]]; 
- only referred to as Rekem<[[H7552H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ֶ֫<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ק</span>ֶ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ם</span>\).
