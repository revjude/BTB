Jemnath is a place name that appears only in the Septuagint, the Greek translation of the Old Testament, in [[Josh.15.46|Joshua 15:46]]. It is not mentioned in the Hebrew text or most other translations of the Bible. In the Septuagint, Jemnath is listed among the towns and villages in the lowland region of Judah, near the Mediterranean Sea. Its exact location and significance are unknown, and no further information about Jemnath is provided in the Bible.
