Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shecaniah</span>, a descendant of Jahaziel, who returned from Babylonian exile.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Ezra.8.5|Ezr.8.5]]; 
- only referred to as Shecaniah **<big>[[H7935|שְׁכַנְיָה]]</big>**.

Shecaniah was a descendant of Jahaziel and was part of the group of Jewish exiles who returned from Babylon to Jerusalem with Ezra. He is mentioned in [[Ezra.8.5|Ezra 8:5]] in the list of those who joined Ezra on this journey to restore worship in Jerusalem.
