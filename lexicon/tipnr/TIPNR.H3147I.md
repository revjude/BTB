Jotham is mentioned in [[1Chr.2.47|1 Chronicles 2:47]] as one of the sons of Jahdai. The context of the passage is the genealogy of Caleb, one of the leaders of the tribe of Judah \(1 Chronicles 2:42-55\). 

Caleb's descendants are listed, including his great-grandson Jahdai \([[1Chr.2.47|1 Chronicles 2:47]]\). Jotham is named as one of the six sons of Jahdai, along with Regem, Jotham, Geshan, Pelet, Ephah, and Shaaph \([[1Chr.2.47|1 Chronicles 2:47]]\). 

No further information is provided about Jotham's life, occupation, or role within the family or tribe. His mention serves to complete the genealogical record of Caleb's descendants.
