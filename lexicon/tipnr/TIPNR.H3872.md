<small>*Luhith • A place in Moab*</small>

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Luhith</span>, a place in Moab mentioned in prophecies of Isaiah and Jeremiah \([[Isa.15.5|Isaiah 15:5]]; [[Jer.48.5|Jeremiah 48:5]]\).
- A location near Horonaim **<big>[[H2773|חֹרֹנַיִם]]</big>** first mentioned at [[Isa.15.5|Isa.15.5]]; 
- referred to as Luhith or Luhith **<big>[[H3872|לוּחִית]]</big>**.

Luhith is mentioned in the prophecies of Isaiah and Jeremiah concerning the judgment of Moab. In [[Isa.15.5|Isaiah 15:5]] and [[Jer.48.5|Jeremiah 48:5]], the "ascent of Luhith" is described as a place where the people of Moab will go up with weeping and mourning. The exact location of Luhith is not specified in the Bible, but it is associated with the territory of Moab, east of the Dead Sea. The prophecies indicate that Luhith will be a place of sorrow and distress for the Moabites when God's judgment comes upon them.
