<small>*Male • Levite leader during the Ark's transfer*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Amminadab</span> was a Levite leader from the Uzzielite clan who participated in the transfer of the Ark of the Covenant to Jerusalem.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.15.10|1Ch.15.10]]; 
- only referred to as Amminadab **<big>[[H5992|עַמִּינָדָב]]</big>**.

Amminadab was a Levite leader who belonged to the clan of Uzziel. During the reign of King David, when the Ark of the Covenant was being brought to Jerusalem, Amminadab was one of the Levites appointed to oversee the transportation of the Ark. He, along with his relatives, was sanctified to carry out this sacred task, ensuring that the Ark was handled with the utmost reverence and care.
