<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Lasharon</span>, a Canaanite city whose king was defeated by Joshua \([[Josh.12.18|Joshua 12:18]]\).
- A location only mentioned at [[Josh.12.18|Jos.12.18]]; 
- only referred to as Lasharon **<big>[[H8289|שָׁרוֹן]]</big>**.

Lasharon is mentioned in [[Josh.12.18|Joshua 12:18]] as one of the Canaanite cities whose kings were defeated by Joshua during the conquest of the Promised Land. The verse simply states, "the king of Lasharon, one," without providing any further details about the city or its exact location.
