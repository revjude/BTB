<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Tirhanah</span> was a son of Caleb by his concubine Maacah, mentioned in the genealogy of Judah.

Tirhanah is mentioned briefly in [[1Chr.2.48|1 Chronicles 2:48]] as one of the sons of Caleb by his concubine Maacah. He is listed along with his brothers Sheber and Shaaph. Caleb was a prominent figure from the tribe of Judah, one of the twelve spies sent by Moses to explore the land of Canaan \(Numbers 13:6\). The genealogy in 1 Chronicles 2 traces the descendants of Judah, including Caleb's family.
