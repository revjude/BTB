<small>*Male • Son of Harum • Son of Harum, from the tribe of Judah*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Aharhel</span> was a son of Harum, a descendant of Judah. \([[1Chr.4.8|1Ch.4.8]]\)
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.8|1Ch.4.8]]; 
- only referred to as Aharhel **<big>[[H0316|אֲחַרְחֵל]]</big>**; 
- a son of Harum **<big>[[H2037|הָרֻם]]</big>**.

Aharhel is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah as a son of Harum. He is listed among the descendants of Coz. No further information about Aharhel is provided in the Bible. \([[1Chr.4.8|1Ch.4.8]]\).
