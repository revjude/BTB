<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Lecah</span>, a descendant of Judah through Er \([[1Chr.4.21|1Ch.4.21]]\).
- A man or town of the tribe of Judah at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.21|1Ch.4.21]]; 
- only referred to as Lecah **<big>[[H3922|לֵכָה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Er **<big>[[H6147|עֵר]]</big>**.

Lecah was a son of Er and a descendant of Judah \([[1Chr.4.21|1Ch.4.21]]\). He is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah, specifically in the line of Shelah. No additional information is provided about Lecah in the Bible. He likely lived during the period of the patriarchs or the early settlement of the tribes in Canaan.
