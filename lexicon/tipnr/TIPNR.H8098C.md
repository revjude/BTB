Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Shemaiah</span>, a descendant of Simeon, son of Shimri.
- A man from the tribe of Simeon\(?\) living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.37|1Ch.4.37]]; 
- only referred to as Shemaiah **<big>[[H8098|שְׁמַעְיָה]]</big>**; 
-  father of Shimri **<big>[[H8113|שִׁמְרִי]]</big>**.

Shemaiah is mentioned in [[1Chr.4.37|1 Chronicles 4:37]] as a descendant of Simeon, one of the twelve tribes of Israel. He is identified as the son of Shimri in the genealogy of the Simeonites.
