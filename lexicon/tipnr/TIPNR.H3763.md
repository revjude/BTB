<small>*Male • Son of Dishon • Son of Dishon, descendant of Esau*</small>

Edomite living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Cheran</span> was a son of Dishon and a descendant of Esau. \([[Gen.36.26|Gen.36.26]]; [[1Chr.1.41|1Ch.1.41]]\)
- A man living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.36.26|Gen.36.26]]; 
- only referred to as Cheran<[[H3763]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ְּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ָ֑<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\); 
-  son of Dishon<[[H1787G]]>; 
- a brother of Eshban<[[H0790]]>, Ithran<[[H3506G]]> and Hemdan<[[H2533]]>.

Cheran is mentioned in [[Gen.36.26|Genesis 36:26]] and [[1Chr.1.41|1 Chronicles 1:41]] as one of the sons of Dishon, who was a descendant of Esau. Esau was the son of Isaac and the twin brother of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\). The passages list the descendants of Esau, who became the Edomites, and Cheran is named among the clan leaders.
