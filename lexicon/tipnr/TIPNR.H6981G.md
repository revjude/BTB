<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Kore</span>, a Levite gatekeeper, descendant of Korah \([[1Chr.9.19|1Ch.9.19]]; 26.1\).
- A man from the tribe of Levi living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, first mentioned at [[1Chr.9.19|1Ch.9.19]]; 
- only referred to as Kore<[[H6981G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>קו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>\); 
-  son of Ebiasaph<[[H0043]]>; 
- a brother of Assir<[[H0617H]]>.

Kore was a Levite gatekeeper, a descendant of Korah \([[1Chr.9.19|1Ch.9.19]]\). He belonged to the Korahite clan, which was responsible for guarding the thresholds of the tent of meeting \([[1Chr.9.19|1Ch.9.19]]\). Kore and his relatives were also in charge of guarding the entrance to the temple during David's reign \([[1Chr.26.1|1Ch.26.1]]\). They faithfully carried out their duties as gatekeepers.
