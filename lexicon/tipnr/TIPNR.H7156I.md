Kiriathaim was a Levitical city located in the territory allocated to the tribe of Reuben, as mentioned in [[1Chr.6.76|1 Chronicles 6:76]]. The Levites, who were responsible for the worship and religious duties in Israel, were given cities to live in throughout the territories of the other Israelite tribes.
