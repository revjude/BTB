Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Malchijah</span>, son of Rechab, repaired the Dung Gate during Nehemiah's wall restoration \([[Neh.3.14|Neh.3.14]]\).
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.14|Neh.3.14]]; 
- only referred to as Malchijah **<big>[[H4441|מַלְכִּיָּה]]</big>**; 
- a son of Rechab **<big>[[H7394|רֵכָב]]</big>**.

Malchijah, the son of Rechab, was a ruler of the district of Beth-haccherem. He participated in the rebuilding of Jerusalem's wall under Nehemiah's leadership. Malchijah was responsible for repairing the Dung Gate, one of the gates in the city wall \([[Neh.3.14|Nehemiah 3:14]]\).
