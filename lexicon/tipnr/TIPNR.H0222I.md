Man living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Uriel</span> was the father of the mother of Abijah, king of Judah.
- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, only mentioned at [[2Chr.13.2|2Ch.13.2]]; 
- only referred to as Uriel **<big>[[H0222|אוּרִיאֵל]]</big>**; 
- husband of Tamar **<big>[[H8559|תָּמָר]]</big>**; 
-  father of Maacah **<big>[[H4601|מַעֲכָה]]</big>**.

Uriel, of Gibeah, was the father of Maacah, wife of Rehoboam, king of Judah, and mother of Abijah, who succeeded Rehoboam as king. She is mentioned by name only once in the genealogy of the kings of Judah. No other details are provided about her life or character. Her mention establishes the lineage of King Abijah.
