Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jeiel</span> the Mahavite was listed among David's mighty men in 1 Chronicles.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.11.44|1Ch.11.44]]; 
- only referred to as Jeiel **<big>[[H3273|יְעִיאֵל]]</big>**; 
-  son of Hotham **<big>[[H2369|חוֹתָם]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Shama **<big>[[H8091|שָׁמָע]]</big>**.

Jeiel the Mahavite is mentioned in the list of David's mighty men in 1 Chronicles. He was one of the warriors who fought alongside David and demonstrated exceptional valor and military skill. However, no additional information is provided about Jeiel's background or specific accomplishments.
