Ziphron is mentioned in the Bible as a place on the northern border of the Promised Land \([[Num.34.9|Num.34.9]]\). It is listed between Zedad and Hazar-enan in the description of the boundaries of the land that the Israelites were to inherit. However, no other details are provided about its history or exact location.
