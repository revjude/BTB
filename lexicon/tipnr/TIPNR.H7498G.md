Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Raphah</span> \(or Rephaiah\) was a son of Binea and a descendant of King Saul.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.8.37|1Ch.8.37]]; 
- referred to as Raphah<[[H7498G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>\), or Rephaiah<[[H7509K]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
-  son of Binea<[[H1150]]>; 
- a brother of Eleasah<[[H0501H]]> and Azel<[[H0682A]]>.

Raphah, also known as Rephaiah, is mentioned in the genealogy of King Saul's descendants \([[1Chr.8.37|1 Chronicles 8:37]]; 9:43\). He was the son of Binea and a member of the tribe of Benjamin. The genealogy traces Saul's lineage after his death, showing that his family line continued even though he was no longer king.
