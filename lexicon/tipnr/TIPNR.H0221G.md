<small>*Male • Son of Hur • Father of Bezalel, the skilled craftsman*</small>

Man living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Uri</span> was the father of Bezalel, the skilled craftsman chosen by God to construct the tabernacle and its furnishings.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, first mentioned at [[Exod.31.2|Exo.31.2]]; 
- only referred to as Uri **<big>[[H0221|אוּרִי]]</big>**; 
-  son of Hur **<big>[[H2354|חוּר]]</big>**; 
- a brother of Shobal **<big>[[H7732|שׁוֹבָל]]</big>**, Salma **<big>[[H8007|שַׂלְמָא]]</big>**, Hareph **<big>[[H2780|חָרֵף]]</big>**, Jezreel **<big>[[H3157|יִזְרְעֵאל]]</big>**, Ishma **<big>[[H3457|יִשְׁמָא]]</big>**, Idbash **<big>[[H3031|יִדְבָּשׁ]]</big>**, Hazzelelponi **<big>[[H6753|צְלֶלְפּוֹנִי]]</big>**, Penuel **<big>[[H6439|פְּנוּאֵל]]</big>** and Ezer **<big>[[H5829|עֵזֶר]]</big>**; 
-  father of Bezalel **<big>[[H1212|בְּצַלְאֵל]]</big>**.

Uri, the son of Hur from the tribe of Judah, is mentioned in [[Exod.31.2|Exodus 31:2]], 35:30, and 38:22, as well as 1 Chronicles 2:20, as the father of Bezalel. Bezalel was the skilled craftsman chosen by God and filled with the Spirit of God to construct the tabernacle and its furnishings, including the ark of the covenant. Uri's grandson, Bezalel, is also mentioned in the genealogy of Judah in 1 Chronicles 2:20, emphasizing the family's important role in the construction of the tabernacle. Apart from being the father of Bezalel, no other details are provided about Uri's life or his own role in the community.
