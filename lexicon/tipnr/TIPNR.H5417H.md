Nethanel was the fourth son of Jesse and thus a brother of King David \([[1Chr.2.14|1 Chronicles 2:14]]\). He was born in Bethlehem. No other details are recorded about his life in the Bible. As a son of Jesse, he would have grown up as a shepherd alongside his brothers.
