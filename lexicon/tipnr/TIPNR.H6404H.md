Pelet was a Benjamite warrior who, along with his brother Berachah, joined David at Ziklag, according to [[1Chr.12.3|1 Chronicles 12:3]]. They were among the mighty warriors who helped David in battle and were known for their skill with the bow. The brothers were sons of Azmaveth, suggesting they came from a family of warriors. Their support of David likely came during the time when he was fleeing from King Saul and gathering a loyal following. The mention of Pelet and his brother highlights the diverse group of warriors who supported David and helped him establish his kingdom.
