Moserah was a place where the Israelites camped during their wilderness wanderings after leaving Egypt \(Deut. 10:6\). It was located in the wilderness of Sinai, but its exact location is unknown. According to Deuteronomy 10:6, Aaron died and was buried at Moserah, and his son Eleazar succeeded him as high priest. The Israelites journeyed from Beeroth Bene-jaakan to Moserah, and then from Moserah to Gudgodah.
