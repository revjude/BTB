Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Parosh</span>, a man who made repairs near the house of Pedaiah son of Parosh.
- A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[Neh.3.25|Neh.3.25]]; 
- only referred to as Parosh<[[H6551I]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ַּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ׁ\); 
-  father of Pedaiah<[[H6305J]]>.

Parosh was a man mentioned in [[Neh.3.25|Nehemiah 3:25]] who made repairs to the wall of Jerusalem. He worked on the section of wall near the house of Pedaiah son of Parosh, which was next to the angle and the tower projecting from the upper palace near the court of the guard.
