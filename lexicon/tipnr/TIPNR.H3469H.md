Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ishi</span> was a son of Shimon, a descendant of Judah \([[1Chr.4.20|1Ch.4.20]]\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.4.20|1Ch.4.20]]; 
- only referred to as Ishi<[[H3469H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\); 
-  father of Zoheth<[[H2105]]>, Ben-zoheth<[[H1132]]>.

Ishi is mentioned in a genealogy of the descendants of Judah \([[1Chr.4.20|1Ch.4.20]]\). He was the son of Shimon, who was from the clan of Judah. No other details are provided about Ishi's life or role within the clan. The genealogy focuses on tracing the lineage of Judah's descendants, and Ishi is listed as part of this family line. His name, which means "my husband" or "my man," is the only information given in the text.
