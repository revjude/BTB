<small>*Male • Father of Shamsherai • A Benjamite, son of Jeshaiah.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jeroham</span> was a Benjamite, the son of Jeshaiah, mentioned in the genealogy of Benjamin in 1 Chronicles.
- A man from the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.8.27|1Ch.8.27]]; 
- only referred to as Jeroham **<big>[[H3395|יְרֹחָם]]</big>**; 
-  father of Shamsherai **<big>[[H8125|שַׁמְשְׁרַי]]</big>**, Shehariah **<big>[[H7841|שְׁחַרְיָה]]</big>**, Athaliah **<big>[[H6271|עֲתַלְיָה]]</big>**, Jaareshiah **<big>[[H3298|יַעֲרֶשְׁיָה]]</big>**, Elijah **<big>[[H0452|אֵלִיָּה]]</big>** and Zichri **<big>[[H2147|זִכְרִי]]</big>**.

Jeroham is mentioned briefly in the genealogy of Benjamin in 1 Chronicles as the son of Jeshaiah. He is listed among the descendants of Benjamin who lived in Jerusalem.
