<small>*Male • Son of Omri • Descendant of Judah living in Jerusalem*</small>

Man living at the time of Exile and Return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ammihud</span>, a descendant of Judah, was among those who lived in Jerusalem after the exile.
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at [[1Chr.9.4|1Ch.9.4]]; 
- only referred to as Ammihud **<big>[[H5989|עַמִּיהוּד]]</big>**; 
- a son of Omri **<big>[[H6018|עׇמְרִי]]</big>**; 
-  father of Uthai **<big>[[H5793|עוּתַי]]</big>**.

Ammihud was a descendant of Judah mentioned in the genealogical records of those who lived in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile. He is listed among the descendants of Perez, one of the sons of Judah. The record in 1 Chronicles 9 provides insight into the resettlement of Jerusalem following the return from exile.
