<small>*Male • Son of Jonathan • Son of Jonathan, descendant of Jerahmeel*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Zaza</span> was a son of Jonathan and a descendant of Jerahmeel from the tribe of Judah.
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.2.33|1Ch.2.33]]; 
- only referred to as Zaza<[[H2117]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ז</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>\); 
-  son of Jonathan<[[H3129G]]>; 
- a brother of Peleth<[[H6431H]]>.

Zaza is mentioned in the genealogy of Judah, specifically as a son of Jonathan and a descendant of Jerahmeel. Jerahmeel was one of the sons of Hezron, who was a grandson of Judah. The genealogy in 1 Chronicles 2 traces the lineage of Judah's descendants, including the family line of Jerahmeel. Zaza is listed along with his brother Peleth as the sons of Jonathan.
