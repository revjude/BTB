Man living at the time ofExile and return

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Matthan</span> was the grandfather of Joseph, the husband of Mary, the mother of Jesus \(Mat.1.15,15\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah living at the time ofExile and return, first mentioned at Mat.1.15; 
- only referred to as Matthan<[[G3157]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangGreek'>Ματθάν</span>\); 
- a son of Eleazar<[[G1648]]>; 
-  father of Jacob<[[G2384G]]>.

Matthan is mentioned in the genealogy of Jesus Christ, as recorded in the Gospel of Matthew. He was the son of Eleazar and the father of Jacob, who was the father of Joseph, the husband of Mary, the mother of Jesus \(Matthew 1:15\). Matthan's inclusion in this genealogy highlights the legal lineage of Jesus through His earthly father, Joseph, tracing back to Abraham and David.
