Ziza is mentioned briefly in [[1Chr.4.37|1 Chronicles 4:37]] as part of the genealogy of the tribe of Simeon. He is identified as the son of Shiphi and is listed among the descendants of Simeon who lived during the reign of King Hezekiah of Judah. The genealogy in this chapter traces the lineage of several Simeonite clans and highlights their leaders and the territories they occupied. The mention of Ziza and his father Shiphi suggests that they were significant figures within their clan. However, no further details are provided about Ziza's life, role, or accomplishments.
