<small>*Male • From  Reuben • Head of a Reubenite family.*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jeiel</span> was the head of a family in the tribe of Reuben, as recorded in the genealogy in 1 Chronicles.
- A man from the tribe of Reuben  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.5.7|1Ch.5.7]]; 
- only referred to as Jeiel **<big>[[H3273|יְעִיאֵל]]</big>**.

Jeiel is mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Reuben in 1 Chronicles. He was the head of a family within the tribe, but no further details are provided about him or his family in the biblical record.
