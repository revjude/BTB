Baalath is mentioned in [[Josh.19.44|Joshua 19:44]] as one of the towns included in the territory allocated to the tribe of Dan. It is listed along with other towns such as Eltekeh, Gibbethon, and Gath-rimmon. The exact location of Baalath within Dan's territory is not specified, but it was likely situated in the coastal plain region of Canaan.
