Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Jeroham</span> was a Benjamite warrior who joined David at Ziklag when he was fleeing from King Saul.
- A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.12.7|1Ch.12.7]]; 
- only referred to as Jeroham **<big>[[H3395|יְרֹחָם]]</big>**; 
-  father of Joelah **<big>[[H3132|יוֹעֵאלָה]]</big>**, Zebadiah **<big>[[H2069|זְבַדְיָה]]</big>**.

Jeroham is mentioned in 1 Chronicles as one of the Benjamite warriors who joined David at Ziklag when he was seeking refuge from King Saul. He is described as a brave warrior and is listed alongside his fellow Benjamite, Joelah. These warriors helped David during his time of exile and played a significant role in his eventual rise to power as king of Israel.
