Man living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Paruah</span>, the father of Jehoshaphat, who was one of Solomon's twelve district governors.
- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Kgs.4.17|1Ki.4.17]]; 
- only referred to as Paruah<[[H6515]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>רו</span>ּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ַ\); 
-  father of Jehoshaphat<[[H3092H]]>.

Paruah was the father of Jehoshaphat, who served as one of the twelve district governors under King Solomon, according to [[1Kgs.4.17|1 Kings 4:17]]. Each governor was responsible for providing provisions for the royal household for one month of the year. Jehoshaphat son of Paruah was assigned to the hill country of Ephraim.
