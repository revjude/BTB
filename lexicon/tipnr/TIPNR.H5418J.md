Nethaniah was the father of Jehudi, an official in the court of King Jehoiakim of Judah \([[Jer.36.14|Jeremiah 36:14]]\). Jehudi was sent by the officials to summon Baruch to read the scroll of Jeremiah's prophecies to them. The officials then told Jehudi to bring the scroll to the king.
