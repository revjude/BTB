People from Mahav\(?\)

The <span class='tipnrhighlight'>Mahavites</span> were represented among David's mighty men \([[1Chr.11.46|1Ch.11.46]]\).
- People from Mahav\(?\), only mentioned at [[1Chr.11.46|1Ch.11.46]]; 
- only referred to as Mahavites<[[H4233]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ח</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ו</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ים</span>\).

The Mahavites are mentioned in the context of listing David's mighty men, a group of elite warriors who fought alongside him. In [[1Chr.11.46|1 Chronicles 11:46]], the Mahavites are listed among these warriors, suggesting that at least one of David's mighty men came from a place or clan called Mahavim. However, no further information is provided about their identity or location.
