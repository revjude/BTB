Woman living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Maacah</span>, the mother of Kish, the father of Saul \([[1Chr.8.29|1Ch.8.29]]; 9.35\).
- A woman from the tribe of Benjamin  living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Chr.8.29|1Ch.8.29]]; 
- only referred to as Maacah<[[H4601N]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ַ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֲ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>כ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
- wife of Abiel<[[H0022G]]>; 
-  mother of Kish<[[H7027G]]>, Ner<[[H5369G]]>, Abdon<[[H5658J]]>, Zur<[[H6698I]]>, Baal<[[H1168B]]>, Nadab<[[H5070J]]>, Gedor<[[H1446I]]>, Ahio<[[H0283I]]>, Zecher<[[H2144]]>, Mikloth<[[H4732G]]>.

Maacah was the wife of a man variously called Jeiel and elsewhere called Abiel or Ner \([[1Chr.8.29|1Ch.8.29]]; 9.35\). He is mentioned in the genealogy of Benjamin, specifically in the line of Saul. Her husband re-established Gibeon as an important city and religious centre.
