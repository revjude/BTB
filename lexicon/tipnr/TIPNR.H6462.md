<small>*Male • Son of Ithran • Descendant of Asher*</small>

Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Pispa</span>, a descendant of Asher, mentioned in the genealogy of the tribe of Asher in 1 Chronicles.
- A man from the tribe of Asher living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at [[1Chr.7.38|1Ch.7.38]]; 
- only referred to as Pispa<[[H6462]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ִּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ס</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>פ</span>ָּ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ה</span>\); 
-  son of Ithran<[[H3506H]]>; 
- a brother of Jephunneh<[[H3312H]]> and Ara<[[H0690]]>.

Pispa is mentioned in [[1Chr.7.38|1 Chronicles 7:38]] as one of the sons of Jether, who was a descendant of Asher. Asher was one of the twelve sons of Israel \(or 'Jacob'\) and a founder of one of the tribes of Israel. The genealogy in 1 Chronicles 7 traces the lineage of the tribe of Asher, listing the heads of families and their descendants. Pispa is mentioned alongside his brothers Jephunneh and Ara.
