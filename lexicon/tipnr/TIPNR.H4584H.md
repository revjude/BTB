Man living at the time of Divided Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Maon</span>, a son of Shammai, was a descendant of Caleb \(1Ch.2.45,45\).
- A man from the tribe of Judah  living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at 1Ch.2.45; 
- only referred to as Maon<[[H4584H]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>עו</span>ֹ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ן</span>\); 
- a son of Shammai<[[H8060H]]>; 
- founder of Beth-zur<[[H1049]]>.

Maon was a descendant of Caleb, the son of Hezron, from the tribe of Judah. He was the son of Shammai and is mentioned in the genealogy of Caleb's descendants \(1 Chronicles 2:45\).
