Man living at the time of United Monarchy

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Adriel</span> married Saul's daughter Merab and had five sons. \([[1Sam.18.19|1Sa.18.19]]; [[2Sam.21.8|2Sa.21.8]]\)
- A man living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at [[1Sam.18.19|1Sa.18.19]]; 
- only referred to as Adriel **<big>[[H5741|עַדְרִיאֵל]]</big>**; 
- a son of Barzillai **<big>[[H1271|בַּרְזִלַּי]]</big>**; 
- husband of Merab **<big>[[H4764|מֵרָב]]</big>**.

Adriel was the son of Barzillai the Meholathite. King Saul had promised his eldest daughter Merab to David, but instead gave her in marriage to Adriel. Merab bore Adriel five sons. During David's reign, these five sons were handed over to the Gibeonites and put to death to avenge Saul's earlier massacre of the Gibeonites, with whom Israel had a treaty. \([[1Sam.18.19|1Sa.18.19]]; [[2Sam.21.8|2Sa.21.8]]\).
