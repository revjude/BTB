<small>*Male • Son of Abraham • Son of Abraham and Hagar; father of Arab tribes.*</small>

Abraham's son living at the time of the Patriarchs

<span class='tipnrhighlight'>Ishmael</span>, the son of Abraham and Hagar, was the ancestor of various Arab tribes. Though not the chosen heir, he was still blessed by God \(Gen.16; 17; 25\).
- Abraham's son living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at [[Gen.16.11|Gen.16.11]]; 
- referred to as Ishmael<[[H3458G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ָ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>אל</span>\), or Ishmaelite \(KJV= Ishmeelite\) or Ishmaelite<[[H3459]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׁ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>מ</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ע</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>אל</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\), or Ishmaelite<[[H3481G]]> \(<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ש</span>ְׂ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ר</span>ְ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>א</span>ֵ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>ל</span>ִ<span class='tipnrLangHebrew'>י</span>\);

-  father of Nebaioth<[[H5032]]>, Kedar<[[H6938]]>, Adbeel<[[H0110]]>, Mibsam<[[H4017G]]>, Mishma<[[H4927G]]>, Dumah<[[H1746G]]>, Massa<[[H4854]]>, Hadad<[[H2301]]>, Tema<[[H8485G]]>, Jetur<[[H3195G]]>, Naphish<[[H5305]]>, Kedemah<[[H6929]]> and Mahalath<[[H4258G]]>.

Ishmael was the first son of Abraham, born to him through Hagar, Sarah's Egyptian maidservant \([[Gen.16.15|Gen.16.15]]\). His birth came after Sarah, who was barren, had given Hagar to Abraham to bear a child on her behalf. God spoke to Hagar, telling her to name the child Ishmael, meaning "God hears" \([[Gen.16.11|Gen.16.11]]\). 

As he grew, Ishmael became a skilled archer \(Gen.21.20\). When Isaac was born to Sarah, she insisted that Abraham send Hagar and Ishmael away \(Gen.21.10\). God confirmed this action but promised to make Ishmael a great nation \(Gen.21.13,18\). Ishmael settled in the wilderness of Paran and married an Egyptian woman \(Gen.21.21\). 

Ishmael had twelve sons who became tribal rulers \([[Gen.17.20|Gen.17.20]]; 25.13-16\). He lived to be 137 years old \([[Gen.25.17|Gen.25.17]]\). Ishmael and Isaac together buried their father Abraham \([[Gen.25.9|Gen.25.9]]\). 

Ishmael's descendants, the Ishmaelites, are mentioned as traders who purchased Joseph from his brothers \([[Gen.37.25|Gen.37.25]]-28; 39.1\). They are also noted as skilled archers \(Isa.21.17\) and as enemies of Israel \(Psa.83.6\). One of David's warriors, Zebadiah, was the son of an Ishmaelite \([[1Chr.2.17|1Ch.2.17]]; 27.30\). 

Ishmael represents the ancestors of various Arab tribes. His story illustrates God's faithfulness to His promises, even to those not chosen for the primary covenant line.
