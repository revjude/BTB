 <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎נְפַל‎</span> **verb** ==fall== (see Biblical Hebrew); — 

**Pe\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎נ‎</span> [[Dan.2.46|Dan 2:46]] <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎נְפַ֑ל‎</span> [[Dan.4.28|Dan 4:28]] 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎נְפַ֫לוּ‎</span> [[Dan.3.23|Dan 3:23]] + [[Dan.7.20|Dan 7:20]] [[BDB#Kt|Kt]] ([[BDB#Qr|Qr]] 3 feminine plural <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎נְפַ֫לָה‎</span>, [[BDB#Kau|K]]<sup>§ 23, 2</sup>); ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎יִפֵּל‎</span> ([[BDB#Kau|K]]<sup>§ 42</sup>) [[Dan.3.6|Dan 3:6]] +; 2 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎תִּפְּלוּן‎</span> [[Dan.3.5|Dan 3:5]]; [[Dan.3.15|Dan 3:15]] ***Participle*** plural <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎נָֽפְלִין‎</span> [[Dan.3.7|Dan 3:7]] — 

**1.** ***fall***, <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎עַלאַֿנְפּוֺהִי‎</span> [[Dan.2.46|Dan 2:46]] ***fall down*** and do homage [[Dan.3.5|Dan 3:5]]; [[Dan.3.6|Dan 3:6]]; [[Dan.3.7|Dan 3:7]]; [[Dan.3.10|Dan 3:10]]; [[Dan.3.11|Dan 3:11]]; [[Dan.3.15|Dan 3:15]] <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎ל‎</span> location [[Dan.3.23|Dan 3:23]] of voice, <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎מִןשְֿׁמַיָּא‎</span> [[Dan.4.28|Dan 4:28]]. 

**2.** ***fall*** by violence [[Dan.7.20|Dan 7:20]] (with <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎קֳדָם‎</span>). 

**3.** <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎יִפֵּל לָךְ‎</span> [[Ezra.7.20|Ezra 7:20]] ***it shall fall to thee*** (thou shalt need) to give (compare Christian-Palestinian Aramaic, [[BDB#Schulth|Schulth]]<sup>Lex. 126 and ZAW xxii (1902), 163</sup>).
