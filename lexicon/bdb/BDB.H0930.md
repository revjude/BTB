 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּהֵמוֺת‎</span> **noun masculine** ==behemoth==, i.e. hippopotamus (apparently plural intensive of foregoing; according to [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[Job.40.15|Job 40:15]] compare [[BDB#De|De]] [[Isa.30.6|Isa 30:6]] from an (assumed) Egyptain ***p-ehemau, ox of the water***) [[Job.40.15|Job 40:15]] (on identity, compare further [[BDB#Bo|Bo]]<sup>Hieroz. iii. 705</sup>); probably also [[Ps.73.22|Ps 73:22]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּהֵמוֺת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָיִיתִי עִמָּ֑ךְ‎</span> ***a behemoth was I with*** (***toward***) ***thee*** (so [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#De|De]]; [[BDB#Che|Che]] ***beasts***); according to [[BDB#De|De]] Or also in <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּהֲמוֺת‎</span> [[Isa.30.6|Isa 30:6]] ***the burden of the behemoth of the south*** (supposed to be a designation of Egypt; but this unlikely, compare [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Di|Di]], read therefore) rather ***beasts of the south***, namely of Judah.
