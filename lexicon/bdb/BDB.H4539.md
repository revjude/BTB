 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָסָךְ‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==covering, screen==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[2Sam.17.19|2Sam 17:19]] + 11 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָסַךְ‎</span> [[Isa.22.8|Isa 22:8]] + 12 t.; — 

**1.** ***covering***, large cloth spread (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פרשׂ‎</span>) over well to hide persons within [[2Sam.17.19|2Sam 17:19]] figurative of protection, or of eye-screen, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְהוּדָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיְגַל אֶתמֿ‎</span> [[Isa.22.8|Isa 22:8]] ***screen***, of cloud [[Ps.105.39|Ps 105:39]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פרשׂ‎</span>). 

**2.** name given (in [[BDB#P|P]]) to each of three ***screens***, of tabernacle: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** at gate (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁעַר‎</span>) of court [[Exod.27.16|Exod 27:16]]; [[Exod.35.17|Exod 35:17]]; [[Exod.38.18|Exod 38:18]]; [[Exod.39.40|Exod 39:40]]; [[Exod.40.8|Exod 40:8]]; [[Exod.40.33|Exod 40:33]]; [[Num.3.26|Num 3:26]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּתַח‎</span>), [[Num.4.26|Num 4:26]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** at entrance (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּתַח‎</span>) of tent [[Exod.26.36|Exod 26:36]]; [[Exod.26.37|Exod 26:37]]; [[Exod.35.15|Exod 35:15]]; [[Exod.36.37|Exod 36:37]]; [[Exod.39.38|Exod 39:38]]; [[Exod.40.5|Exod 40:5]]; [[Exod.40.28|Exod 40:28]]; [[Num.3.25|Num 3:25]]; [[Num.3.31|Num 3:31]]; [[Num.4.25|Num 4:25]], 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּרֹכֶת הַמּ‎</span> (see <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פ‎</span>), dividing off the Most Holy Place within the tent, [[Exod.35.12|Exod 35:12]]; [[Exod.39.34|Exod 39:34]]; [[Exod.40.21|Exod 40:21]]; [[Num.4.5|Num 4:5]].
