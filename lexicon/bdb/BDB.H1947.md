 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺלֵלָה‎</span>] **noun feminine** ==madness== (on text see below), only plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֹלֵלוֺת‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺלֵלוֺת‎</span>, and only Ecclesiastes: [[Eccl.1.17|Eccl 1:17]] ***and I set my heart*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וָאֶתְּנָה לִבִּי‎</span>) ***to know wisdom, and to know madness and folly*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֹלֵלוֺת וְשִׂכְלוּת‎</span>), compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺלֵלוֺת‎</span> [[Eccl.2.12|Eccl 2:12]] (\|\| ***id.***); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְלָדַעַת רֶשַׁע כֶּסֶל וְהַסִּכְלוּת ה‎</span> [[Eccl.7.25|Eccl 7:25]] i.e. to know folly to be madness; possibly read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חוֺלֵלוּת‎</span>, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סִכְלוּת‎</span> &amp; following; the moral evil of it is specifically recognized in [[Eccl.9.3|Eccl 9:3]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רָע‎</span>).
