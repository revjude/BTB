 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַר‎</span> **adjective** and **substantive** ==bitter, bitterness==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Prov.27.7|Prov 27:7]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמַּר‎</span> [[Hab.1.6|Hab 1:6]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָ֑ר‎</span> [[Ps.64.4|Ps 64:4]] +; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַר‎</span> [[1Sam.22.2|1Sam 22:2]] +; feminine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָרָה‎</span> [[Gen.27.34|Gen 27:34]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָרָא‎</span> [[Ruth.1.20|Ruth 1:20]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָרַת‎</span> [[1Sam.1.10|1Sam 1:10]] masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָרִים‎</span> [[Exod.15.23|Exod 15:23]] +; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָרֵי‎</span> [[Judg.18.25|Judg 18:25]]; [[Prov.31.6|Prov 31:6]] — ***bitter:*** 

**1.** literally, of water [[Exod.15.23|Exod 15:23]] ([[BDB#J|J]]); food [[Prov.27.7|Prov 27:7]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָתוֺק‎</span>); wormwood (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַעֲנָה‎</span> [[Prov.5.4|Prov 5:4]] (in simile of end of harlot, compare [[Eccl.7.26|Eccl 7:26]] below); as substantive plural, of physical pain, result of curse <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמָרִים‎</span> [[Num.5.24|Num 5:24]]; [[Num.5.27|Num 5:27]], whence <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵי הַמָּרִים‎</span> v[[Num.5.18|Num 5:18]] (compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]) v[[Num.5.19|Num 5:19]] v[[Num.5.23|Num 5:23]] v[[Num.5.24|Num 5:24]] of harmful result of ruthless fighting [[2Sam.2.26|2Sam 2:26]] of wickedness [[Jer.2.19|Jer 2:19]]; [[Jer.4.18|Jer 4:18]] = ***injurious, hurtful*** [[Isa.5.20|Isa 5:20]] (twice in verse) (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָתוֺק‎</span>). 

**2.** figurative, 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of cry (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צְעָקָה‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זְעָקָה‎</span>) [[Gen.27.34|Gen 27:34]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[Esth.4.1|Esth 4:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִסְפֵּד מָ֑ר‎</span> [[Ezek.27.31|Ezek 27:31]] (strike out [[BDB#Co|Co]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּבָר מָ֑ר‎</span> [[Ps.64.4|Ps 64:4]] as adverb (cry, weep) ***bitterly*** [[Ezek.27.30|Ezek 27:30]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָרָה‎</span>), [[Zeph.1.14|Zeph 1:14]]; [[Isa.33.7|Isa 33:7]] (both <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַר‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of feeling, temper, especially with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶפֶשׁ‎</span>: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲנָשִׁים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָרֵי נֶפֶשׁ‎</span> [[Judg.18.25|Judg 18:25]] ***men fierce*** ([[BDB#GFM|GFM]] ***acrid***) ***of temper***, so [[2Sam.17.8|2Sam 17:8]] (like a bear robbed of whelps); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַגּוֺי הַמַּר‎</span> [[Hab.1.6|Hab 1:6]] ***the fierce nation***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרנֶֿפֶשׁ‎</span> = ***discontented*** [[1Sam.22.2|1Sam 22:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרֵי נֶפֶשׁ‎</span> = ***the bitterly wretched*** [[Job.3.20|Job 3:20]]; [[Prov.31.6|Prov 31:6]], so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָרַת נ‎</span> [[1Sam.1.10|1Sam 1:10]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יוֺם מָ֑ר‎</span> [[Amos.8.10|Amos 8:10]] as substantive abstract <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרנֶֿפֶשׁ‎</span> ***bitterness of soul*** [[Isa.38.15|Isa 38:15]]; [[Ezek.27.31|Ezek 27:31]] (strike out [[BDB#Co|Co]]), [[Job.7.11|Job 7:11]]; [[Job.10.1|Job 10:1]] = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נפשׁ מָרָה‎</span> [[Job.21.25|Job 21:25]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָרָא‎</span> [[Ruth.1.20|Ruth 1:20]] (name given to Naomi by herself); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרהַֿמָּוֶת‎</span> [[1Sam.15.32|1Sam 15:32]] ***the bitterness of death***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרמִֿמָּוֶת‎</span> [[Eccl.7.26|Eccl 7:26]] (metaphor of harlot); in [[Ezek.3.14|Ezek 3:14]] ***I went bitterly***, strike out <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַר‎</span> [[BDB#Co|Co]]. — [[Isa.38.17|Isa 38:17]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מרר‎</span>.
