 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְבָלָה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==senselessness== (as shewn in disregard of moral and religious claims), especially of disgraceful sins; also disgrace; — always absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נ‎</span>; — 

**1.** ***disgraceful folly***, especially of sins of un-chastity [[Judg.19.23|Judg 19:23]] (***wanton deed*** [[BDB#GFM|GFM]]), so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּבַר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַזּאֹת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַנּ‎</span> v[[Judg.19.24|Judg 19:24]], [[2Sam.13.12|2Sam 13:12]] often <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּיִשְׂרָאֵל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָשָׂה נ‎</span> i.e. do a thing disgraceful according to Israel's standard: [[Gen.34.7|Gen 34:7]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[Deut.22.21|Deut 22:21]] (compare [[BDB#Dr|Dr]] on meaning of word; only here of woman), [[Judg.20.26|Judg 20:26]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִמָּה‎</span>), v[[Judg.20.10|Judg 20:10]]; [[Jer.29.23|Jer 29:23]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא יֵעָשֶׂה כֵּן בְּיִשְׂרָאֵלּ‎</span> [[2Sam.13.12|2Sam 13:12]]); also of inhospitable churlishness [[1Sam.25.25|1Sam 25:25]] (see I, II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָבָל‎</span>, and especially [[Isa.32.6|Isa 32:6]]); of profane action [[Josh.7.15|Josh 7:15]] (Achan; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָשָׂה נ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּיִשְׂרָאֵל‎</span>); of senseless and irreligious language [[Isa.9.16|Isa 9:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֹּבֵר‎</span> [[Isa.32.6|Isa 32:6]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְדַבֵּר‎</span>). 

**2.** ***contumely, disgrace*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲשׂוֺת עִמָּכֶם נ‎</span> [[Job.42.8|Job 42:8]] (of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>), i.e. ***deal out to you disgrace***, expose you and punish you as <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְבָלִים‎</span> (compare [[2Sam.3.33|2Sam 3:33]], below <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָבָל‎</span>), because of your utterances about me.
