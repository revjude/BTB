$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of false gods [[Isa.41.24|Isa 41:24]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** of men, daily toil [[Job.24.5|Job 24:5]]; [[Ps.104.23|Ps 104:23]] specific act [[Ruth.2.12|Ruth 2:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָדָיו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פ‎</span> of sacrificing [[Deut.33.11|Deut 33:11]] plural of ***achievements*** [[2Sam.23.20|2Sam 23:20]] = [[1Chr.11.32|1Chr 11:32]] action as having moral quality [[Job.34.11|Job 34:11]]; [[Prov.20.11|Prov 20:11]]; [[Prov.24.12|Prov 24:12]]; [[Prov.24.29|Prov 24:29]], evil implied [[Jer.25.14|Jer 25:14]]; [[Jer.50.29|Jer 50:29]]; [[Job.36.9|Job 36:9]]; [[Ps.28.4|Ps 28:4]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּפָּיו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פ‎</span> [[Ps.9.17|Ps 9:17]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָמָס‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פ‎</span> [[Isa.59.6|Isa 59:6]] good action [[Prov.21.8|Prov 21:8]].
