 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעֹנָה‎</span> **noun feminine** id.; — 

**1.** ***den, lair*** of wild beasts: of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּפִיר‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעֹנָתוֺ‎</span> [[Amos.3.4|Amos 3:4]], so plural absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעוֺנוֺת‎</span> [[Job.38.40|Job 38:40]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סֻכָּה‎</span>), suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעוֺנֹתָם‎</span> [[Ps.104.22|Ps 104:22]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעֹנוֺת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרָיוֺת‎</span> [[Song.4.8|Song 4:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעֹנֹתָיו‎</span> [[Nah.2.13|Nah 2:13]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֹרָיו‎</span>; of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַרְיֵה‎</span>, figurative of Nineveh); of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַיָּה‎</span> in general, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּמְעוֺנֹתֶיהָ תִשְׁכֹּן‎</span> [[Job.37.8|Job 37:8]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָ֑רֶב‎</span>); so apparently figurative of men, hunted by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעוֺנוֺתֵינוּ‎</span> [[Jer.21.13|Jer 21:13]], figurative also of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> dwelling in temple, singular suffix, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעוֺנָתוֺ בְּצִיּוֺן‎</span> [[Ps.76.3|Ps 76:3]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סוּכּוֺ‎</span>). 

**2.** figurative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעֹנָה אֱלֹהֵי קֶדֶם‎</span> [[Deut.33.27|Deut 33:27]] ***the ancient God is a dwelling-place*** (for his people; compare I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָעוֺן‎</span> **3**).
