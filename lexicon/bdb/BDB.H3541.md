 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹּה‎</span> **demonstrative adverb** ==thus, here== (Biblical Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎כָּה‎</span>; [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]], [[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]]:Jerusalem, Mandean <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּא‎</span> ***here:*** in Syriac with prefixes <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhJwAWAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAnABYAAAJVjI+py+0PDwCxukkXtg1ntXFMCE4GKZrjF6CWWiae2MLR/Npnu+qX7fGdXB1gkCUjllgqpcT5hDGFUWSSCh1Sa8eu96vJfMfjmJaM1mVpK7b7DY8zCgA7"> ***hither***, <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhJgAWAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAmABYAAAJejI+pywoN04OgtnmrXTpvp3XgJ5GS8ZiHurLIhr0iGNUo69plXPMvM2v5AkFiaCQL8ZQ3ZjJ53EWjQll1RApSd0CqdnvlKIvjsjllxp7XZy7R2E7pmvO61Y7P6/e6AgA7"> ([[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]]:Jerusalem <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhOQAbAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAA5ABsAAAKEjI+py+0Po3wAsGrnxK5er0UYuJAJF1JjpqCtmTYefNCGHb8Bq/f5X+MBhz4ELmbDwVZC5Cj4PAmZzdRqR5WyUC5I9cYEL028o/aL7Y6VaOP1E11To6r50z614NvQPV4rFiYS9kcH1WLV9WMWokj0aPgIySiZyFfZ6Ii5SLkpcekZilAAADs=">) ***hence***, <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhLQAXAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAtABcAAAJujI+py+3/AIC0Hjlptg5nHEgIuJAV6W2hmXicuMIXO8rnZxtumUNwH1PNhBodcciJHBW0ZG3ZyqVe0KgqBeRZWKBdJ8uTiaVCcBhKvlaZ2Inb1Taure06Vk61621ma+vL18Qg6ETI5jQ4h7jIUAAAOw=="> ***where?*** compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵיכָה‎</span>, p. 32, and [[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>ii. 1, 252</sup>) — 

**1.** of manner, ***thus*** (so most frequently, and usually pointing to what is to follow, while <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֵּן‎</span> points commonly to what has preceded), [[Gen.15.5|Gen 15:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כה יהיה זרעך‎</span> ***thus*** shall thy seed be, [[Num.6.23|Num 6:23]]; [[1Kgs.2.30|1Kgs 2:30]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹּה דִבֶּר יוֺאָב וְכֹה עָנָנִי‎</span>, [[1Kgs.5.25|1Kgs 5:25]]; [[Isa.24.13|Isa 24:13]] (answered by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span>, as [[Jer.23.29|Jer 23:29]]); with a substantive [[Isa.20.6|Isa 20:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִנֵּה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹה מַבָּטֵנוּ‎</span>, [[Jer.9.21|Jer 9:21]] (si vera lectio), [[Jer.23.29|Jer 23:29]]; [[1Sam.27.11|1Sam 27:11]]. Mostly confined to particular phrases: thus 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** with different forms of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אמר‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כה תאמר‎</span> ***thus*** shalt thou say [[Exod.3.14|Exod 3:14]]; [[Exod.3.15|Exod 3:15]]; [[Exod.19.3|Exod 19:3]]; [[Exod.20.22|Exod 20:22]] etc.; especially <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כה אמר יי֞‎</span> thus saith (or said) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Exod.4.22|Exod 4:22]]; [[Exod.5.1|Exod 5:1]]; [[Exod.7.17|Exod 7:17]]; [[Exod.7.26|Exod 7:26]]; [[Exod.8.16|Exod 8:16]] etc.; and continually in the prophets, as [[Isa.7.7|Isa 7:7]]; [[Isa.8.11|Isa 8:11]]; [[Isa.18.4|Isa 18:4]] etc.; [[Jer.2.2|Jer 2:2]]; [[Jer.2.5|Jer 2:5]]; [[Jer.4.27|Jer 4:27]]; [[Jer.6.16|Jer 6:16]] etc.; also in the idiomatic formula <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִם כֹּה יאֹמַר‎</span> ***if he say*** (or ***used to say***) (the words spoken following) [[Gen.31.8|Gen 31:8]]; [[1Sam.14.9|1Sam 14:9]]; [[1Sam.14.10|1Sam 14:10]]; [[1Sam.20.7|1Sam 20:7]]; [[2Sam.15.26|2Sam 15:26]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עשׂה‎</span> to do ***thus***, [[Exod.5.15|Exod 5:15]]; [[Deut.7.5|Deut 7:5]] pointing back, [[Num.22.30|Num 22:30]]; [[Josh.6.14|Josh 6:14]]; [[1Sam.27.11|1Sam 27:11]] and elsewhere; especially in the phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כה יעשׂה אלהים וכה יוסיף‎</span> (with variations), [[1Sam.3.17|1Sam 3:17]] + (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יסף‎</span>), in which <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹּה‎</span> points to an imprecation understood but not expressed. With <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span> prefixed [[1Kgs.22.20|1Kgs 22:20]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ויאמר זה בְּכֹה וזה אמר בְּכֹה‎</span> and one spake ***in this wise***, and another spake ***in that wise*** (\|\| [[2Chr.18.19|2Chr 18:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָכָהכָּ֗֗֗כָה‎</span>; probably rightly). 

**2.** of place, ***here*** (rare, chiefly in [[BDB#E|E]]), [[Gen.31.37|Gen 31:37]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׂים כֹּה‎</span> place it ***here*** before my brethren, [[Num.23.15|Num 23:15]] (twice in verse); [[2Sam.18.30|2Sam 18:30]]; [[Ruth.2.8|Ruth 2:8]]. Repeated, [[Exod.2.12|Exod 2:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּפֶן כֹּה וָכֹה‎</span> and he turned ***this way*** and ***that way***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹּהכֹּ֗֗֗ה‎</span> ***on this side . . . on that side*** [[Num.11.31|Num 11:31]]. With <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַד‎</span> [[Gen.22.5|Gen 22:5]] and we will go <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדכֹּֿה‎</span> ***as far as here*** (<span class="bdbLangGreek">δεικτικῶς</span>) i.e. ***yonder.*** 

**3.** of time, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַד כֹּה‎</span> ***hitherto***, [[Exod.7.16|Exod 7:16]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדהֵֿנָּה‎</span>), [[Josh.17.14|Josh 17:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדכֹּֿה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְעַדכֹּֿה‎</span> till ***now*** and till ***then***, i.e. meanwhile, [[1Kgs.18.45|1Kgs 18:45]].
