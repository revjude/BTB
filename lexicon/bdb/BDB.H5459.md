 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְגֻלָּה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==possession, property== (on formation see [[BDB#Ba|Ba]]<sup>NB § 95 b</sup> [[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>ii. 1, 168</sup>); — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> [[Exod.19.5|Exod 19:5]] + 5 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְגֻלַּת‎</span> [[Eccl.2.8|Eccl 2:8]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְגֻלָּתוֺ‎</span> [[Ps.135.4|Ps 135:4]] — 

**1.** ***valued property, peculiar treasure***, which <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> has chosen (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בחר‎</span>) and taken to himself; always of people of Israel, first [[Exod.19.5|Exod 19:5]] ([[BDB#E|E]]; [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">λαὸς</span> <span class="bdbLangGreek">περιούσιος</span> = [[Titus.2.14|Titus 2:14]]; [[1Pet.2.9|1Pet 2:9]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">λαὸς εἰς περιποίησιν</span> = <span class="bdbLangGreek">περιποίησις</span> [[Eph.1.14|Eph 1:14]] compare [[BDB#Br|Br]]<sup>MP 102</sup>; MA 52, 235); then <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַם ס‎</span> [[Deut.7.6|Deut 7:6]]; [[Deut.14.2|Deut 14:2]]; [[Deut.26.18|Deut 26:18]] later <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> alone, [[Mal.3.17|Mal 3:17]]; [[Ps.135.4|Ps 135:4]].
