 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָ֫וֶן‎</span><sub>85</sub> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Job.5.6|Job 5:6]]</sup> trouble, sorrow, wickedness — absolute [[Num.23.21|Num 23:21]] +; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺנִי‎</span> [[Gen.35.18|Gen 35:18]] etc.; ***plural*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺנִים‎</span> [[Hos.9.4|Hos 9:4]] — 

**1.** ***trouble, sorrow*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֶּןאֿוֺנִי‎</span> ***son of my trouble*** or ***sorrow*** [[Gen.35.18|Gen 35:18]] ([[BDB#E|E]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא הִבִּיט אָוֶן בְּיַעֲקֹב‎</span> [[Num.23.21|Num 23:21]] (song of Balaam), ***he doth not behold trouble in Jacob*** (\|\| he doth not see misery — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָמָל‎</span> — in Isr); often \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָמָל‎</span> [[Ps.7.15|Ps 7:15]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִנֵּה יְחַבֶּלאָֿוֶן‎</span> ***lo he travaileth with trouble*** \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהָרָה עָמָל וְיָלַד שָׁ֑קֶר‎</span>, ***yea he hath conceived misery &amp; brought forth a lie***), probably thence [[Job.15.35|Job 15:35]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָרהֹ עָמָל וְיָלֹד אָוֶן‎</span>) = [[Isa.59.4|Isa 59:4]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָמָל וָאָוֶן‎</span> [[Ps.10.7|Ps 10:7]]; [[Ps.55.11|Ps 55:11]]; [[Ps.90.10|Ps 90:10]]; [[Job.4.8|Job 4:8]]; [[Job.5.6|Job 5:6]]; [[Isa.10.1|Isa 10:1]] (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָמָל‎</span>); also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוֺרֵעַ עַוְלָה יִקְצָראָֿוֶן‎</span> [[Prov.22.8|Prov 22:8]] ***the sower of iniquity shall reap trouble***; in this sense elsewhere only [[Deut.26.14|Deut 26:14]]; [[Prov.12.21|Prov 12:21]]; [[Jer.4.15|Jer 4:15]]; [[Hab.3.7|Hab 3:7]]; [[Amos.5.5|Amos 5:5]], plural intensive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֶחֶם אוֺנִים‎</span> ***bread of trouble, sorrow, or mourning*** [[Hos.9.4|Hos 9:4]]. 

**2.** ***idolatry*** [[Hos.12.12|Hos 12:12]]; [[Isa.41.29|Isa 41:29]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָוֶן וּתְרָפִים הַפְצַ֑ר‎</span> ***stubbornness is idolatry &amp; (the use of) teraphim*** [[1Sam.15.23|1Sam 15:23]] (poetry source); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָוֶן בֵּית‎</span> [[Hos.4.15|Hos 4:15]] (for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּית אֵל‎</span>because Bethel, house of God, is given over to idolatry) so also [[Hos.5.8|Hos 5:8]]; [[Hos.10.5|Hos 10:5]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּמוֺת א‎</span> [[Hos.10.8|Hos 10:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּקְעַת א‎</span> [[Amos.1.5|Amos 1:5]] (Baalbek); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּחוּרֵי א‎</span> read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺן‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֹן‎</span> = On, Heliopolis [[Ezek.30.17|Ezek 30:17]] — abstract for concrete = ***idols*** [[Isa.66.3|Isa 66:3]]. 

**3.** ***trouble of iniquity, wickedness***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְתֵי אָוֶן‎</span> [[Job.22.15|Job 22:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַנְשֵׁי א‎</span> [[Job.34.36|Job 34:36]] = ***men of trouble, troublers, wicked men***; compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִישׁ א‎</span> [[Prov.6.12|Prov 6:12]];  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֹּעֲלֵי אָוֶן‎</span> ***workers of trouble, evil-doers, workers of iniquity*** [[Job.31.3|Job 31:3]]; [[Job.34.8|Job 34:8]]; [[Job.34.22|Job 34:22]]; [[Ps.5.6|Ps 5:6]] + (16 t. chiefly late Psalms) [[Prov.10.29|Prov 10:29]]; [[Prov.21.15|Prov 21:15]]; [[Isa.31.2|Isa 31:2]]; [[Hos.6.8|Hos 6:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַחְשְׁבוֺת אָוֶן‎</span> ***thoughts of trouble, wicked imaginations*** [[Prov.6.18|Prov 6:18]]; [[Isa.59.7|Isa 59:7]]; [[Jer.4.14|Jer 4:14]] often of words &amp; thoughts [[Job.11.11|Job 11:11]] + (5 t.) [[Ps.36.5|Ps 36:5]] + (9 t.) [[Prov.17.4|Prov 17:4]]; [[Prov.19.28|Prov 19:28]]; [[Prov.30.20|Prov 30:20]]; [[Isa.29.20|Isa 29:20]]; [[Isa.32.6|Isa 32:6]]; [[Isa.58.9|Isa 58:9]]; [[Isa.59.6|Isa 59:6]]; [[Ezek.11.2|Ezek 11:2]]; [[Mic.2.1|Mic 2:1]]; [[Hab.1.3|Hab 1:3]]; [[Zech.10.2|Zech 10:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוּכַל אָוֶן וַעֲצָרָה‎</span> [[Isa.1.13|Isa 1:13]] ***I cannot bear iniquity with the solemn meeting*** ([[BDB#RV|RV]] &amp; most modern; [[BDB#AV|AV]] ***it is iniquity, even the solemn meeting***).
