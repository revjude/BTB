 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַפָּל‎</span>] **noun masculine**<sup>[[Job.41.15|Job 41:15]]</sup> ==refuse, hanging parts==; — 

**1.** ***fallings, refuse***, construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַפַּל בַּר‎</span> [[Amos.8.6|Amos 8:6]] ***the refuse*** of wheat. 

**2.** plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַפְּלֵי בְשָׂרוֺ‎</span> [[Job.41.15|Job 41:15]] ***the hanging*** (falling, drooping) ***parts of his flesh*** (of crocodile).
