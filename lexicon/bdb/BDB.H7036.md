 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קָלוֺן‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Prov.11.2|Prov 11:2]]</sup> ==ignominy, dishonour==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ק‎</span> [[Hos.4.7|Hos 4:7]] +; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קְלוֺן‎</span> [[Isa.22.18|Isa 22:18]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קְלוֺנֵךְ‎</span> [[Jer.13.26|Jer 13:26]] +; — 

**1.** of national ***ignominy*** [[Hos.4.7|Hos 4:7]]; [[Hos.4.18|Hos 4:18]] (dubious Line, see [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Now|Now]]), [[Jer.46.12|Jer 46:12]] (read probably <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קוֺלֵךְ‎</span>, so [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Gie|Gie]] [[BDB#Co|Co]] [[BDB#Hpt|Hpt]]), [[Hab.2.16|Hab 2:16]]; [[Ps.83.17|Ps 83:17]] of nation under figure of woman, = pudenda, [[Jer.13.26|Jer 13:26]]; [[Nah.3.5|Nah 3:5]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעְרֵךְ‎</span>). 

**2.** personal ***dishonour, disgrace***, of Shebna, as ***disgrace*** to his lord's house [[Isa.22.18|Isa 22:18]](opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּבוֺדֶ֑ךָ‎</span>); individual, [[Job.10.25|Job 10:25]]; [[Prov.3.35|Prov 3:35]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּבוֺד‎</span>), [[Prov.6.33|Prov 6:33]]; [[Prov.9.7|Prov 9:7]]; [[Prov.11.2|Prov 11:2]]; [[Prov.12.16|Prov 12:16]]; [[Prov.13.18|Prov 13:18]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְכֻבָּ֑ד‎</span>), [[Prov.18.3|Prov 18:3]]; [[Prov.22.10|Prov 22:10]].
