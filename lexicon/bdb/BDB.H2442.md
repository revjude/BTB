**1.** ***wait, tarry***, absolute [[2Kgs.9.3|2Kgs 9:3]] ***tarry not***; with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַד‎</span> till morning light [[2Kgs.7.9|2Kgs 7:9]] with accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎את איוב‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בדברים‎</span> [[Job.32.4|Job 32:4]] ***tarry for Job with words.***
