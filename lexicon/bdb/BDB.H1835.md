 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּן‎</span> **proper name, masculine** (***judge***) — 

**1.** son of Jacob and Bilhah [[Gen.30.6|Gen 30:6]]; [[Gen.35.25|Gen 35:25]]; [[Exod.1.4|Exod 1:4]]; [[Josh.19.47|Josh 19:47]]; [[Judg.18.29|Judg 18:29]]; [[1Chr.2.2|1Chr 2:2]]. 

**2.** the tribe of Dan, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַטֵּה דָן‎</span> [[Exod.31.6|Exod 31:6]]; [[Exod.35.34|Exod 35:34]]; [[Exod.38.23|Exod 38:23]]; [[Lev.24.11|Lev 24:11]]; [[Num.1.39|Num 1:39]]; [[Num.13.12|Num 13:12]]; [[Josh.21.5|Josh 21:5]]; [[Josh.21.23|Josh 21:23]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּנֵי דָן‎</span> [[Gen.46.23|Gen 46:23]]; [[Num.1.38|Num 1:38]]; [[Num.2.25|Num 2:25]]; [[Num.7.66|Num 7:66]]; [[Num.10.25|Num 10:25]]; [[Num.26.42|Num 26:42]]; [[Num.34.22|Num 34:22]]; [[Josh.19.40|Josh 19:40]]; [[Josh.19.47|Josh 19:47]] (twice in verse); [[Josh.19.48|Josh 19:48]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]) [[Judg.1.34|Judg 1:34]]; [[Judg.18.2|Judg 18:2]]; [[Judg.18.16|Judg 18:16]]; [[Judg.18.22|Judg 18:22]]; [[Judg.18.23|Judg 18:23]]; [[Judg.18.25|Judg 18:25]]; [[Judg.18.26|Judg 18:26]]; [[Judg.18.30|Judg 18:30]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַחֲנֵה דָן‎</span> [[Num.2.25|Num 2:25]]; [[Num.2.31|Num 2:31]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) — on [[Judg.13.25|Judg 13:25]]; [[Judg.18.12|Judg 18:12]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַחֲנֶה‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִשְׁפְּחֹת דָּן‎</span> [[Num.26.42|Num 26:42]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּן‎</span> alone in poetry [[Gen.49.16|Gen 49:16]]; [[Gen.49.17|Gen 49:17]]; [[Deut.33.22|Deut 33:22]] (twice in verse); [[Judg.5.17|Judg 5:17]], elsewhere [[Num.1.12|Num 1:12]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) [[Deut.27.13|Deut 27:13]]; [[1Chr.27.22|1Chr 27:22]]; [[2Chr.2.13|2Chr 2:13]]; [[Ezek.48.1|Ezek 48:1]]; [[Ezek.48.2|Ezek 48:2]]; [[Ezek.48.32|Ezek 48:32]]. 

**3.** a city on northern frontier of Israel at one of the sources of the Jordan, originally <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַיִשׁ‎</span> [[Judg.18.29|Judg 18:29]] (compare v[[Judg.18.7|Judg 18:7]]); = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֶשֶׁם‎</span> [[Josh.19.47|Josh 19:47]] captured by a colony of Danites; named Dan, and made a sacred place with rites of worship that lasted until the exile ([[Judg.18.30|Judg 18:30]]); = ***Tell el Kâdî***, [[BDB#Rob|Rob]]<sup>BR iii. 351, 358</sup> [[BDB#Bäd|Bd]]<sup>Pa1 265</sup>. This place is frequently referred to: [[Gen.14.14|Gen 14:14]]; [[Deut.34.1|Deut 34:1]]; [[1Kgs.12.29|1Kgs 12:29]]; [[1Kgs.12.30|1Kgs 12:30]]; [[1Kgs.15.20|1Kgs 15:20]]; [[2Kgs.10.29|2Kgs 10:29]]; [[2Chr.16.4|2Chr 16:4]]; [[Jer.4.15|Jer 4:15]]; [[Jer.8.16|Jer 8:16]]; [[Amos.8.14|Amos 8:14]] read also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּבְדָן‎</span> for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְכֵן‎</span> [[2Sam.20.18|2Sam 20:18]] [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#We|We]] compare [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]; and see the phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִדָּן וְעַדבְּֿאֵר שֶׁבַע‎</span> [[Judg.20.1|Judg 20:1]]; [[1Sam.3.20|1Sam 3:20]]; [[2Sam.3.10|2Sam 3:10]]; [[2Sam.17.11|2Sam 17:11]]; [[2Sam.24.2|2Sam 24:2]]; [[2Sam.24.15|2Sam 24:15]]; [[1Kgs.5.5|1Kgs 5:5]] given by Chronicles <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִבְּאֵר שֶׁבַע‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְעַדדָּֿן‎</span> [[1Chr.21.2|1Chr 21:2]]; [[2Chr.30.5|2Chr 30:5]]. ([[Ezek.27.19|Ezek 27:19]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ודן‎</span>.)


**Note.** — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּ֫נָה יַעַן‎</span> [[2Sam.24.6|2Sam 24:6]]. As no such place is known, the text is usually regarded as corrupt, and, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] being uncertain, is variously changed: (1) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּ֫נָה יַעַר‎</span> ***to Dan in the wood***, [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] after [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] ***silvestria.*** (2) (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיָּסֹבּוּ‎</span>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּ֫נָה וְעִיּוֺן‎</span> ***to Dan and Ijon*** [[BDB#Klo|Klo]] (compare [[1Kgs.15.20|1Kgs 15:20]]). (3) (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סָֽבֲבוּ‎</span>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּן וּמִדָּן‎</span> [[BDB#We|We]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]] compare [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]].
