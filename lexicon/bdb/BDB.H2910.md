 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טֻחוֺת‎</span> **noun feminine plural** ==inward parts== (as ***covered over***, concealed) — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טֻחוֺת‎</span> of seat of faithfulness, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֱמֶת חָפַצְתָּ בַּטּ‎</span> [[Ps.51.8|Ps 51:8]] ***faithfulness thou desirest in the inward parts***, i.e. in the heart (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סָתֻם‎</span>); of seat of wisdom <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָכְמָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִי שָׁת בַּטּ‎</span> [[Job.38.36|Job 38:36]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֶׂכְוִי‎</span> q. v.); from context this can hardly = heart of man (kidneys, 'reins,' [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]], Jewish interpretations, see [[BDB#Levy|NHWB]]<sup>ii, 144</sup> and others), but is rather ***cloud-layers*** (as dark, ***hidden*** spaces, see [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#VB|VB]]); their 'wisdom' appears in their obedience to natural law.
