**proper name, of a mountain 1.** mountain on border of Edom, always <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֹר הָהָר‎</span>, named as stage in Israel's journey to CanaanNum [[Ezek.20.22|Ezek 20:22]]; [[Ezek.21.4|Ezek 21:4]]; [[Ezek.33.37|Ezek 33:37]]; [[Deut.32.50|Deut 32:50]] as place of Aaron's death [[Num.20.23|Num 20:23]]; [[Num.20.25|Num 20:25]]; [[Num.20.27|Num 20:27]]; [[Num.33.38|Num 33:38]]; [[Num.33.39|Num 33:39]]; [[Num.33.41|Num 33:41]]; [[Deut.32.50|Deut 32:50]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]); = modern ***Jebel Nebi Hârûn*** approximately 50 miles south of Dead Sea, just south (southwest) of Petra, according to [[BDB#Rob|Rob]]<sup>BR ii. 125, 152</sup>, compare 519 ff. [[BDB#Bäd|Bd]]<sup>Pal 153</sup>; disputed by [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Kn|Kn]] [[BDB#Di|Di]], &amp; especially Trumbull<sup>Kadesh Barnea 128 ff.</sup>, who thinks of ***J.
