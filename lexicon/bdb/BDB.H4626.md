 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעֲרֶה‎</span>], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַ֫עַר‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==bare, naked place== (hence <span class="bdbLangGreek">Μαραθών</span>, Marathon, according to [[BDB#Lewy|Lewy]]<sup>Fremdw. 144</sup>); — 

**1.** ***bare place***, or ***space:*** construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִמַּעֲרִהגָּֿ֑בַע‎</span> [[Judg.20.33|Judg 20:33]] ***bare*** (open) ***space*** of Geba' (si vera 1.; but see II.  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעֲרָב‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּמַעַראִֿישׁ‎</span> [[1Kgs.7.36|1Kgs 7:36]] ***according to the clear space on each*** (plate, see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לוּחַ‎</span>). 

**2.** suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעְרֵח‎</span> [[Nah.3.5|Nah 3:5]] figurative of Nineveh (***pudenda*** exposed in shameful punishment; compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֶרְוָה‎</span>).
