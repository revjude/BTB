 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרְפֵּא‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרְפֵּה‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Eccl.10.4|Eccl 10:4]]</sup> ==healing, cure, health== (usually figurative); — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרְפֵּא‎</span> [[Jer.14.19|Jer 14:19]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרְפֵּה‎</span> [[Jer.8.15|Jer 8:15]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרְפֵּא‎</span> [[Prov.15.4|Prov 15:4]] — 

**1.** ***healing, cure***, of national woes, [[Jer.8.15|Jer 8:15]]; [[Jer.14.19|Jer 14:19]]b (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁלוֺם‎</span>), [[Jer.36.6|Jer 36:6]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרֻכָה‎</span>); especially <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵין לָנוּ מ‎</span> [[Jer.14.19|Jer 14:19]]a ***there is no cure for us***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַד לְאֵין מ‎</span> [[2Chr.36.16|2Chr 36:16]]. 

**2.** figurative of person, ***health, profit***, [[Prov.4.22|Prov 4:22]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַיִּים‎</span>), [[Prov.12.18|Prov 12:18]]; [[Prov.13.17|Prov 13:17]]; [[Prov.16.24|Prov 16:24]] with spiritual implication [[Mal.3.20|Mal 3:20]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵין מ‎</span> [[Prov.6.15|Prov 6:15]] = [[Prov.29.1|Prov 29:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֵב מ‎</span> [[Prov.14.30|Prov 14:30]] ***a mind of health*** (healthy, composed, mind), compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> alone = ***composure*** [[Eccl.10.4|Eccl 10:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְשׁוֺן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Eccl.15.4|Eccl 15:4]] ***healing of the tongue***, = a soothing tongue (Toy).
