 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁן‎</span> **noun feminine**<sup>[[1Sam.2.13|1Sam 2:13]]</sup> (yet **masculine** [[1Sam.14.5|1Sam 14:5]] compare [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]) ==tooth, ivory==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> absolute [[Exod.21.24|Exod 21:24]] +, construct v[[Exod.21.27|Exod 21:27]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֶׁן‎</span> [[Deut.32.24|Deut 32:24]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁנּם‎</span> [[Exod.21.27|Exod 21:27]] dual <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁנַּיִם‎</span> [[Prov.10.26|Prov 10:26]], construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁנֵּי‎</span> [[Jer.31.29|Jer 31:29]] +; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁנֵּיהֶם‎</span> [[Ps.124.6|Ps 124:6]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁנֵּימוֺ‎</span> [[Ps.58.7|Ps 58:7]], etc.; — 

**1.** ***tooth:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of man, [[Exod.21.27|Exod 21:27]] (3 t. in verse) ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[Num.11.33|Num 11:33]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[Gen.49.12|Gen 49:12]] (poem in [[BDB#J|J]]), [[Amos.4.6|Amos 4:6]]; [[Song.4.2|Song 4:2]]; [[Song.6.6|Song 6:6]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֹעָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> [[Prov.25.19|Prov 25:19]], see II.  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רעע‎</span> **2**; of ***lex talionis***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּחַת שׁ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> [[Exod.21.24|Exod 21:24]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[Lev.24.20|Lev 24:20]] ([[BDB#H|H]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁן בְּשׁ‎</span> [[Deut.19.21|Deut 19:21]] irritated by acid [[Prov.10.26|Prov 10:26]], dulled by it (in proverb of inherited guilt) [[Jer.31.29|Jer 31:29]]; [[Jer.31.30|Jer 31:30]]; [[Ezek.18.2|Ezek 18:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָרַק שׁ‎</span> [[Lam.2.16|Lam 2:16]]; [[Job.16.9|Job 16:9]]; [[Ps.35.16|Ps 35:16]]; [[Ps.112.10|Ps 112:10]], see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָרַק‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶשָּׂא בְשָׂרִי בְשִׁנָּ֑י‎</span> [[Job.13.14|Job 13:14]] = ***risk my life*** (strike out <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַלמָֿה‎</span>, dittograph; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָשִׂים בְּכַּ פִּי נַפְשִׁי‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּעוֺר שִׁנָּ֑י‎</span> [[Job.19.20|Job 19:20]], see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָלַט‎</span> **Hithpa\`el** (other conjectures see [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Bi|Bi]] [[BDB#Bu|Bu]] [[BDB#Du|Du]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of beasts : [[Deut.32.24|Deut 32:24]], crocodile [[Job.41.6|Job 41:6]], locusts [[Joel.1.6|Joel 1:6]] (teeth of lions); especially figurative of false prophets [[Mic.3.5|Mic 3:5]], of foes [[Zech.9.7|Zech 9:7]], oppressors [[Job.29.17|Job 29:17]], wicked [[Job.4.10|Job 4:10]]; [[Ps.3.8|Ps 3:8]]; [[Ps.58.7|Ps 58:7]]; [[Ps.124.6|Ps 124:6]] of their weapons [[Prov.30.14|Prov 30:14]]; [[Ps.57.5|Ps 57:5]] of Israel shattered by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** ***tooth*** of a fork, ***tine***, [[1Sam.2.13|1Sam 2:13]] (compare [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>Sm. 291</sup>); of cliff, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁן הַסֶּלַע‎</span>[[1Sam.14.4|1Sam 14:4]] (twice in verse), compare [[Job.39.28|Job 39:28]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַשֵּׁן‎</span> [[1Sam.14.5|1Sam 14:5]]. 

**2.** ***ivory***, of commerce, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קַרְנוֺת שֵׁן‎</span> [[Ezek.27.15|Ezek 27:15]] (i.e. ***tusks?***); as material, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִטּוֺת שׁ‎</span> [[Amos.6.4|Amos 6:4]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּסֵּאשֿׁ‎</span> [[1Kgs.10.18|1Kgs 10:18]] = [[2Chr.9.17|2Chr 9:17]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּית הַשּׁ‎</span> [[1Kgs.22.39|1Kgs 22:39]], compare [[Amos.3.15|Amos 3:15]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵיכְלֵי שׁ‎</span> [[Ps.45.9|Ps 45:9]], also [[Ezek.27.6|Ezek 27:6]]; [[Song.5.15|Song 5:15]]; [[Song.7.5|Song 7:5]].
