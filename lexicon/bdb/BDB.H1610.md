 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גַּף‎</span>] **noun masculine** ==body, self, height, elevation== (compare Late Hebrew <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גּוּף‎</span>) — singular suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גַּפּוֺ‎</span> [[Exod.21.3|Exod 21:3]] (twice in verse); [[Exod.21.4|Exod 21:4]] plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גַּפֵּי‎</span> [[Prov.9.3|Prov 9:3]] — 

**1.** ***body, self***, only in phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָבאֹ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎(יֵצֵא) בְּגַפּוֺ‎</span>) ***he shall come in (go out) by himself*** (with his body) [[Exod.21.3|Exod 21:3]] (twice in verse); [[Exod.21.4|Exod 21:4]] in law of slave (compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]). 

**2.** ***height, elevation*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַלגַּֿפֵּי מְרֹמֵי קָ֑רֶת‎</span> [[Prov.9.3|Prov 9:3]].
