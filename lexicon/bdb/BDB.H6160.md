 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲרָבָה‎</span><sub>61</sub> **noun feminine** ==desert-plain, steppe==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ע‎</span> [[2Sam.4.7|2Sam 4:7]] +, suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַרְבָתָהּ‎</span> [[Isa.51.3|Isa 51:3]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲרָבוֺת‎</span> [[Jer.5.6|Jer 5:6]]; [[Ps.68.5|Ps 68:5]], construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַרְבוֺת‎</span> [[2Kgs.25.5|2Kgs 25:5]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַרְבֹת‎</span> [[Jer.52.8|Jer 52:8]] — 

**1.** earliest use: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** arid steppe west of Dead Sea (in southern Judah) [[1Sam.23.24|1Sam 23:24]], also [[Ezek.47.8|Ezek 47:8]]; [[Isa.51.3|Isa 51:3]], whence name <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָם הָע‎</span> [[Josh.3.16|Josh 3:16]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]) \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָם הַמֶּלַח‎</span>), also [[2Kgs.14.25|2Kgs 14:25]]; [[Deut.4.49|Deut 4:49]], and (\|\| ***id***.) [[Deut.3.17|Deut 3:17]]; [[Josh.12.3|Josh 12:3]] ([[BDB#D|D]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַחַל הָעֲרָבָה‎</span> [[Amos.6.14|Amos 6:14]] must be east of Dead Sea, si vera lectio, but read probably <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִצְרַיִם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נ‎</span> see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַחַל‎</span>. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** Jordan-valley west of river + adjacent plain; near ford (opposite Jericho) [[2Sam.2.29|2Sam 2:29]], compare [[Josh.8.14|Josh 8:14]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]); also [[Deut.11.30|Deut 11:30]]; [[2Kgs.25.4|2Kgs 25:4]] = [[Jer.39.4|Jer 39:4]] = [[Jer.52.7|Jer 52:7]] — [[2Sam.15.28|2Sam 15:28]]; [[2Sam.17.16|2Sam 17:16]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲבָרָה‎</span> 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** Jordan-valley east of river [[2Sam.4.7|2Sam 4:7]]. 

**2.** in [[BDB#D|D]]: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** especially of east Jordan plain [[Deut.1.1|Deut 1:1]] (probably) [[Josh.12.1|Josh 12:1]]; [[Josh.12.3|Josh 12:3]], east half of Jordan-valley [[Deut.3.17|Deut 3:17]]; [[Deut.4.49|Deut 4:49]] of entire Jordan-valley (between <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּנֶּרֶת‎</span> and Dead Sea, modern ***El-Ghôr, the Depression***) [[Deut.1.7|Deut 1:7]]; [[Josh.11.2|Josh 11:2]]; [[Josh.11.16|Josh 11:16]] west Jordan plain only [[Josh.12.8|Josh 12:8]] depression south of Dead Sea (modern ***Wady el-\`Arabah***) [[Deut.2.8|Deut 2:8]]. 

**3.** in [[BDB#P|P]] always plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַרְבוֺת מוֺאָב‎</span> (east Jordan) [[Num.22.1|Num 22:1]] + 11 t. (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺאָב‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עְרֵחוֺ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ע‎</span> (West Jordan) [[Josh.4.13|Josh 4:13]]; [[Josh.5.10|Josh 5:10]], also [[2Kgs.25.5|2Kgs 25:5]] = [[Jer.39.5|Jer 39:5]] = [[Jer.52.8|Jer 52:8]]. 

**4.** apparently North Arabian desert [[Isa.40.3|Isa 40:3]]; [[Isa.41.19|Isa 41:19]]. 

**5.** in general ***the steppe*** (often \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִדְבָּר‎</span>), [[Jer.17.6|Jer 17:6]]; [[Jer.50.12|Jer 50:12]]; [[Isa.33.9|Isa 33:9]]; [[Isa.35.1|Isa 35:1]]; [[Isa.35.6|Isa 35:6]]; [[Job.24.5|Job 24:5]]; [[Job.39.6|Job 39:6]]; [[Ps.68.5|Ps 68:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זְאֵב עֲרָבוֺת‎</span> [[Jer.5.6|Jer 5:6]] (figurative of invader); compare description of Exodus <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ עֲרָבָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְשׁוּחָה‎</span> [[Jer.2.6|Jer 2:6]] in simile, of a ***plain***, of future land of Judah [[Zech.14.10|Zech 14:10]]. — See [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>[[Deut.1.1|Deut 1:1]] and references</sup> [[BDB#Buhl|Buhl]]<sup>Geogr. 111</sup>.
