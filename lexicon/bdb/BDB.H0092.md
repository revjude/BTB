 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲגֻדָּה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==band== (Mishna <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲגוּדָּה‎</span> compare [[BDB#Levy|NHWB]]). 

**1.** plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲגֻדּוֺת מוֺטָה‎</span> ***bands, thongs*** (fastening ox-bow) metaphor of fetters of slavery [[Isa.58.6|Isa 58:6]]. 

**2.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲגֻדַּת אֵזוֺב‎</span> ***bunch*** of hyssop [[Exod.12.22|Exod 12:22]]. 

**3.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> absolute ***band*** of men (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶבֶל‎</span>. English ***band***) [[2Sam.2.25|2Sam 2:25]].
