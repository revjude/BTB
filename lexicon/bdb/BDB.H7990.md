 <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שַׁלִּיט‎</span> **adjective** ==having mastery, ruling==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שׁ‎</span> [[Ezra.7.24|Ezra 7:24]] +, emphatic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אָ֯‎</span> [[Dan.2.15|Dan 2:15]] masculine plural <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שְׁלִיטִין‎</span> [[Ezra.4.20|Ezra 4:20]], <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎מִן‎</span>- [[Dan.4.23|Dan 4:23]] — 

**1.** ***having, exercising, mastery:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of God, followed by <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎ב‎</span>, [[Dan.4.14|Dan 4:14]]; [[Dan.4.22|Dan 4:22]]; [[Dan.4.29|Dan 4:29]]; [[Dan.5.21|Dan 5:21]] absolute, of heavens [[Dan.4.23|Dan 4:23]] 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of kings [[Ezra.4.20|Ezra 4:20]] (<span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎ב‎</span>). 

**2.** as noun = ***ruler*** [[Dan.2.10|Dan 2:10]], ***captain*** v[[Dan.2.15|Dan 2:15]]. 

**3.** ***having authority*** to do a thing (Infinitive), hence impersonal = ***it is authorized***, [[Ezra.7.24|Ezra 7:24]] (so often in Syriac; compare [[BDB#S-C|S-C]]<sup>A 11</sup> + <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אנת שליט למבנה‎</span>).
