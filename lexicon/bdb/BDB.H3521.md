**proper name, of a location 1.** ==city on border of Asher== [[Josh.19.27|Josh 19:27]], modern ***Kâbûl***, 4 h. (9 miles) southeast from Akko, [[BDB#Rob|Rob]]<sup>BR iii, 88</sup> [[BDB#Guérin|Guérin]]<sup>Gal, i. 422 f.</sup> 

**2.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ כָּבוּל‎</span> district in Galilee [[1Kgs.9.13|1Kgs 9:13]] containing 20 cities given by Solomon to Hiram (popular etymology possibly <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כּ‎</span> ***as*** + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֻּל‎</span> = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּל‎</span> ***not***, i.e. ***as good as nothing***, compare v[[1Kgs.9.12|1Kgs 9:12]], so [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#The|Th]]; [[BDB#Klo|Klo]] conjectures <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ארץ גָּלִיל‎</span>), [[BDB#Buhl|Buhl]]<sup>Geogr. § 116</sup>.
