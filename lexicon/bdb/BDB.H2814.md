 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָשָׁה‎</span>] **verb** ==be silent, inactive, still== (chiefly in poetry and late) (Late Hebrew ***id.***, Aramaic in derivatives) — 

**Qal** ***Imperfect*** 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תֶּחֱשֶׁה‎</span> [[Ps.28.1|Ps 28:1]]; [[Isa.64.11|Isa 64:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶחֱשֶׁה‎</span> [[Isa.62.1|Isa 62:1]]; [[Isa.65.6|Isa 65:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶחֱשׁוּ‎</span> [[Isa.62.6|Isa 62:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיֶּחֱשׁוּ‎</span> [[Ps.107.29|Ps 107:29]] — ***be silent*** [[Eccl.3.7|Eccl 3:7]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְדַבֵּר‎</span>); [[Isa.62.1|Isa 62:1]]; [[Isa.62.6|Isa 62:6]] (= neglect to speak); of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> i.e. be unresponsive [[Ps.28.1|Ps 28:1]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חרשׁ‎</span>); [[Isa.64.11|Isa 64:11]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִתְאַפַּק‎</span>); but [[Isa.65.6|Isa 65:6]] of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s keeping silence at iniquity, i.e. overlooking it (compare [[Isa.57.11|Isa 57:11]] Hiph\`il); of waves, ***be still*** [[Ps.107.29|Ps 107:29]] (subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גַּלֵּיהֶם‎</span>). **Hiph\`il** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֶחֱשֵׁיתִי‎</span> [[Ps.39.3|Ps 39:3]]; [[Isa.42.14|Isa 42:14]] ***Imperative*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֶחֱשׁוּ‎</span> [[2Kgs.2.3|2Kgs 2:3]]; [[2Kgs.2.5|2Kgs 2:5]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַחְשֶׁה‎</span> [[Isa.57.11|Isa 57:11]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַחְשִׁים‎</span> [[Judg.18.9|Judg 18:9]] + 3 t.; — 

**1.** ***exhibit silence, be silent*** [[2Kgs.2.3|2Kgs 2:3]]; [[2Kgs.2.5|2Kgs 2:5]]; [[2Kgs.7.9|2Kgs 7:9]]; [[Ps.39.3|Ps 39:3]] (followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִטּוֺב‎</span>, \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶאֱלַמְתִּי‎</span>), figurative [[Isa.42.14|Isa 42:14]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אחרישׁ‎</span>); of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s ***being silent*** at iniquity [[Isa.57.11|Isa 57:11]] (i.e. overlooking it, compare Qal [[Isa.65.5|Isa 65:5]]), possibly also is ***be silent***, opposed to ***rescue***, [[BDB#Ges|Ges]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Du|Du]]. 

**2.** ***shew inactivity*** [[Judg.18.9|Judg 18:9]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִקַּחַת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הח‎</span> [[1Kgs.22.3|1Kgs 22:3]] Gilead is ours, and we ***shew inactivity so as not to take it***. 

**3.** causat. ***make still, quiet***, followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span>, of direct object [[Neh.8.11|Neh 8:11]] (v:b <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַסּוּ‎</span> q. v.)
