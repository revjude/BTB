 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַ֫חַד‎</span><sub>45</sub> **noun [masculine]** ==unitedness==. 

**1.** as **substantive** only [[1Chr.12.17|1Chr 12:17]] (peculiar) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִהְיֶהלִּֿי עֲלֵיכֶֹם לֵבָב לֳי֑חַד‎</span> I will have a heart toward you ***for unitedness***, i.e. my heart shall be ready to become one with yours (compare v.[[1Chr.12.38|1Chr 12:38]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לב אחד‎</span>). 

**2.** elsewhere always in accusative as **adverb** ***in union, together*** (compare Arabic <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhIgAdAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAiAB0AAAJbjI+py+0PGYgLWGkmPbYrHYCfyF3SRIadWXonuaaZu4X1jecPqzd0fwI6eDPi7adCIowJoibW/LFWLxq1FctqTVAsdytLpsJHstB2rph7ynMbeE2L1zim/F4rAAA7"> in accusative with suffix ***in his solitariness = alone***) — 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***together***, of community in action, place, or time (often combined, but one usually more prominent than the others); (1) in action, [[Job.38.7|Job 38:7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּרָןיַֿחַד‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כּוֺכְבֵי אֵל‎</span> when the stars of God shouted ***together***; [[Ezra.4.3|Ezra 4:3]] we together will build (opposed to ***you***), with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִלְחַם‎</span> [[1Sam.17.10|1Sam 17:10]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִשְׁפַּט‎</span> [[Isa.43.26|Isa 43:26]] (2) in place [[1Sam.11.11|1Sam 11:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ולא נשׁארו בם שׁנים יָ֑חַד‎</span>; [[2Sam.10.15|2Sam 10:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיֵּאָֽסְפוּ יָחַ֑ד‎</span> [[Ps.2.2|Ps 2:2]]; [[Ps.31.14|Ps 31:14]]; [[Ps.88.18|Ps 88:18]]; [[Ps.1|Ps 1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֶׁבֶת יַחַד‎</span> [[Isa.50.8|Isa 50:8]], as predicate [[Mic.2.12|Mic 2:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַחַד אֲשִׂימֶנּוּ‎</span>; (3) in place and time at once.
