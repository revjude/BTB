 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶ֫בֶר‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==company, association, spell== — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָ֑בֶר‎</span> [[Deut.18.11|Deut 18:11]] + 2 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶבֶר‎</span> [[Hos.6.9|Hos 6:9]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲבָרִים‎</span> [[Ps.58.6|Ps 58:6]], suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲבָרַיִךְ‎</span> [[Isa.47.9|Isa 47:9]]; [[Isa.47.11|Isa 47:11]] — 

**1.** ***company, band*** of (bad) priests [[Hos.6.9|Hos 6:9]]. 

**2.** ***association, society*** (abstract), only in <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּית חָ֑בֶר‎</span> [[Prov.21.9|Prov 21:9]] = [[Prov.25.24|Prov 25:24]] ***house of association***, i. e. shared with a contentious woman (house in common, see [[BDB#RV|RVm]] [[BDB#VB|VB]]). 

**3.** spell : 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** as accusative of congnate meaning with verb with  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָבַר‎</span> (q. v.) [[Deut.18.11|Deut 18:11]]; [[Ps.58.6|Ps 58:6]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** alone, of Babylonian magic [[Isa.47.9|Isa 47:9]]; [[Isa.47.11|Isa 47:11]] (see [[BDB#Len|Len]] Chald. Magic [1878]; Magie u. Wahrsage-kunst 1878; Scholz<sup>G-tzendienst u. Zauberwesen, 1877. 80 ff.</sup> [[BDB#Say|Say]]<sup>TBA iii. 145 ff and Rel,. Bab 396 ff., 440 ff.</sup>).
