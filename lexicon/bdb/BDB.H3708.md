**3.** ***vexation, grief*** [[Ps.6.8|Ps 6:8]]; [[Ps.10.14|Ps 10:14]]; [[Ps.31.10|Ps 31:10]]; [[Eccl.1.18|Eccl 1:18]]; [[Eccl.2.23|Eccl 2:23]]; [[Eccl.7.3|Eccl 7:3]]; [[Eccl.11.10|Eccl 11:10]], also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כעס‎</span> [[Eccl.5.16|Eccl 5:16]] accusative [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] aI.
