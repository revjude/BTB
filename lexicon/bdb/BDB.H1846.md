**Qal** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּֽעֲכוּ‎</span> [[Isa.43.17|Isa 43:17]] ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִדְעַךְ‎</span> [[Prov.20.20|Prov 20:20]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִדְעָ֑ךְ‎</span> [[Job.18.5|Job 18:5]] + 4 t.; — ***go out, be extinguished***, of lamp, always figurative, lamp of wicked (i.e. prosperity) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺר רְשָׁעִים‎</span> [[Job.18.5|Job 18:5]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֵר ר‎</span> [[Job.18.6|Job 18:6]]; [[Job.21.17|Job 21:17]]; [[Prov.13.9|Prov 13:9]]; [[Prov.20.20|Prov 20:20]]; [[Prov.24.20|Prov 24:20]] of hostile armies [[Isa.43.17|Isa 43:17]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּב֫וּ‎</span>). **Niph\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִדְעֲכוּ‎</span> ***be made extinct, dried up***, [[Job.6.17|Job 6:17]] (of brooks). **Pu\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֹּעֲכוּ‎</span> of assailants, ***be extinguished, quenched***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּאֵשׁ קוֺצִים‎</span> [[Ps.118.12|Ps 118:12]] but read perhaps <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בערו‎</span> with [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Bae|Bae]] &amp; [[BDB#Che|Che]], see [[BDB#Che|Che]]<sup>critical note</sup>.
