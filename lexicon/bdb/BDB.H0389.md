 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ‎</span><sub>159</sub> **adverb** (perhaps from the same demonstr, root found also in <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּי‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹּה‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֵּן‎</span>). 

**1.** surely. 

**2.** with a restrictive force, emphasizing what follows: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** in contrast to what precedes, howbeit; 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** in contrast with other ideas generally, only 

**1.** asseverative, often introducing with emphasis the expression of a truth (or supposed truth) newly perceived, especially in colloquial language, ***surely, no doubt (doch wohl)***; [[Gen.26.9|Gen 26:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ הִנֵּה אִשְׁתְּךָ הִוא‎</span> ***of a surety***, lo, she is thy wife ! [[Gen.29.14|Gen 29:14]]; [[Gen.44.28|Gen 44:28]]; [[Judg.3.24|Judg 3:24]]; [[Judg.20.39|Judg 20:39]]; [[1Sam.16.6|1Sam 16:6]] ***surely*** the anointed of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> is before him ! [[1Sam.25.21|1Sam 25:21]]; [[Jer.5.4|Jer 5:4]]; [[Ps.58.12|Ps 58:12]]; [[Ps.73.1|Ps 73:1]]; [[Ps.73.13|Ps 73:13]] but also in other cases, though rarely, [[Isa.34.14|Isa 34:14]]; [[Isa.34.15|Isa 34:15]]; [[Zeph.3.7|Zeph 3:7]]; [[Ps.23.6|Ps 23:6]]; [[Ps.139.11|Ps 139:11]]; [[Ps.140.14|Ps 140:14]]; [[Job.16.7|Job 16:7]]; [[Job.18.21|Job 18:21]] &amp; rather singularly [[Exod.12.15|Exod 12:15]]; [[Exod.31.13|Exod 31:13]]; [[Lev.23.27|Lev 23:27]]; [[Lev.23.39|Lev 23:39]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]). 

**2.** restrictive: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** in contrast to what precedes, ***howbeit, yet, but :*** [[Gen.9.4|Gen 9:4]] ***howbeit***, flesh with the life thereof . . . ye shall not eat, [[Gen.20.12|Gen 20:12]]; [[Exod.21.21|Exod 21:21]]; [[Lev.21.23|Lev 21:23]]; [[Lev.27.26|Lev 27:26]]; [[Num.18.15|Num 18:15]]; [[Num.18.17|Num 18:17]]; [[2Sam.3.13|2Sam 3:13]]; [[Jer.10.24|Jer 10:24]] correct me, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ בְּמִשְׁפָּט‎</span> ***but*** with judgment, [[Job.2.6|Job 2:6]]; [[Job.13.15|Job 13:15]] sometimes with an adversative force, as [[Isa.14.15|Isa 14:15]]; [[Isa.43.24|Isa 43:24]] before an imperative (minimizing the request), [[Gen.23.13|Gen 23:13]] ***only***, if thou wilt, I pray thee, hear me ! [[Gen.27.13|Gen 27:13]]; [[Judg.10.15|Judg 10:15]]; [[1Sam.18.17|1Sam 18:17]]; [[1Kgs.17.13|1Kgs 17:13]] and elsewhere So [[1Sam.8.9|1Sam 8:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ כִּי‎</span> (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּי‎</span>; and compare <span class="bdbLangGreek">πλὴν ὅτι</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** in contrast to other ideas generally : — (<span class="bdbLangGreek">α</span>) [[Gen.7.23|Gen 7:23]]; [[Gen.18.32|Gen 18:32]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַפַּעַם‎</span> ***only*** this once (so [[Exod.10.17|Exod 10:17]] and elsewhere) [[Exod.34.15|Exod 34:15]]; [[Exod.12.16|Exod 12:16]] (note accents), [[1Sam.18.8|1Sam 18:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְעוֺד לוֺ אַךְ תַמְּלוּכָה‎</span> and there yet remains for him ***only*** the kingdom, [[1Sam.21.5|1Sam 21:5]]; [[Isa.45.14|Isa 45:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךָ בְּךָ אֵל‎</span> ***only*** in thee is God! [[Ps.62.2|Ps 62:2]] etc. [[Job.14.22|Job 14:22]]; [[Ps.37.8|Ps 37:8]] fret not thyself <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְהָרֵעַ‎</span> (which leadeth) ***only*** to do evil, [[Prov.11.24|Prov 11:24]] he that withholdeth more than is meet <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמַחְסוֺר‎</span> (tendeth) ***only*** to penury, [[Prov.14.23|Prov 14:23]]; [[Prov.21.5|Prov 21:5]]; [[Prov.22.16|Prov 22:16]] (<span class="bdbLangGreek">β</span>) attaching itself closely to the following word (usually an adjective, rarely a verb), ***only***, i.e. ***exclusively, altogether, utterly*** [[Deut.16.15|Deut 16:15]] and thou shalt be <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ שָׂמֵחַ‎</span> ***altogether*** rejoicing, [[Deut.28.29|Deut 28:29]] (compare v[[Deut.28.33|Deut 28:33]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַק‎</span>) [[Isa.16.7|Isa 16:7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ נְכָאִים‎</span> ***utterly*** stricken, [[Isa.19.11|Isa 19:11]]; [[Jer.16.19|Jer 16:19]] ***nought but*** lies, [[Jer.32.30|Jer 32:30]]; [[Hos.12.12|Hos 12:12]]; [[Job.19.13|Job 19:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ זָרוּ‎</span> are ***wholly*** estranged (with play on <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַכְזָר‎</span> ***cruel***). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** as an adverb of time (with infinitive absolute), twice: [[Gen.27.30|Gen 27:30]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ יָצאֹ יָצָא יַעֲקֹבוְ֗֗֗עֵשָׂו בָּא‎</span> ***only just*** (or ***scarcely***) had Jacob gone out, . . . and (= when) Esau came in, [[Judg.7.19|Judg 7:19]]. — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאַח‎</span> thrice : [[Gen.9.5|Gen 9:5]] ***and only*** (second limitation of v[[Gen.9.3|Gen 9:3]]); [[Num.22.20|Num 22:20]] ***but only***; [[Josh.22.19|Josh 22:19]] ***but howbeit.***


**Note**. — In some passages the affirmative and restrictive senses agree equally with the context; and authorities read the Hebrew differently. Thus ***only = nought but, altogether***, is adopted by [[BDB#Ges|Ges]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#De|De]] in [[Ps.23.6|Ps 23:6]]; [[Ps.62.10|Ps 62:10]]; [[Ps.73.1|Ps 73:1]]; [[Ps.73.13|Ps 73:13]] by [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#De|De]] in [[Ps.39.6|Ps 39:6]]; [[Ps.39.7|Ps 39:7]] ([[BDB#Che|Che]] ***surely***); by [[BDB#Ges|Ges]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#De|De]] in [[Ps.39.12|Ps 39:12]] (but [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] ***surely***); by [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] in [[Ps.73.18|Ps 73:18]] ([[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] ***surely***); by [[BDB#Ges|Ges]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#De|De]] in [[Ps.139.11|Ps 139:11]] ([[BDB#Ew|Ew]] ***doch***). [[Isa.45.14|Isa 45:14]] [[BDB#Ges|Ges]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] ***only***; but [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] ***of a truth***.
