 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כּוּשׁ‎</span> **proper name, masculine person, of a people** and **territory** (Egyptian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***Kœš,***</u></abbr> [[BDB#Steind|Steind]]<sup>BAS i, 593</sup>, Assyrian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***Kûsu,***</u></abbr> Id<sup>ib.</sup> [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>Pa 251</sup>; ***Kaši*** in Tel Amarna tablets, see [[BDB#Wkl|Wkl]]<sup>Tel Am. 39\*</sup>); — 

**1.** 1st 'son' of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָם‎</span> [[Gen.10.6|Gen 10:6]]; [[Gen.10.7|Gen 10:7]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) = [[1Chr.1.8|1Chr 1:8]]; [[1Chr.1.9|1Chr 1:9]], [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Ξους</span>, [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] ***Chus***, from whom descended according to these verses the southernmost peoples known to Hebrews. 

**2.** land and people of southern Nile-valley, or Upper Egypt, extending from Syene ([[Ezek.29.10|Ezek 29:10]]) indefinitely to the south, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Αιθιοπια</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Αιθιοπες</span>: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** the land [[Isa.11.11|Isa 11:11]]; [[Isa.18.1|Isa 18:1]]; [[Zeph.3.10|Zeph 3:10]]; [[Ezek.29.10|Ezek 29:10]]; [[Job.28.19|Job 28:19]]; [[Esth.1.1|Esth 1:1]]; [[Esth.8.9|Esth 8:9]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** the people [[Isa.20.4|Isa 20:4]]; [[Jer.46.9|Jer 46:9]]; [[Ezek.38.5|Ezek 38:5]] personified [[Ps.68.32|Ps 68:32]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** indeterminate, either land or people, or including both: [[Isa.20.3|Isa 20:3]]; [[Isa.20.5|Isa 20:5]]; [[Isa.43.3|Isa 43:3]]; [[Isa.45.14|Isa 45:14]]; [[2Kgs.19.9|2Kgs 19:9]] = [[Isa.37.9|Isa 37:9]]; [[Nah.3.9|Nah 3:9]]; [[Ezek.30.4|Ezek 30:4]]; [[Ezek.30.5|Ezek 30:5]]; [[Ezek.30.9|Ezek 30:9]]; [[Ps.87.4|Ps 87:4]] ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">λαὸς Αιθιοπων</span>). 

**3.** in [[Gen.10.8|Gen 10:8]] ([[BDB#J|J]]) = [[1Chr.1.10|1Chr 1:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כּוּשׁ‎</span> is error for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּשׁ‎</span> = Babylonian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***Kaššu,***</u></abbr> according to [[BDB#Schr|Schr]]<sup>COT</sup> on [[Gen.10.6|Gen 10:6]], [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>Pa 51 ff. 72 f.</sup> and most Assyriologists; so perhaps also [[Gen.2.13|Gen 2:13]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), yet see [[BDB#Hpt|Hpt]]<sup>Über Ld. u. Meer, 1894-5, No. 15</sup>. 



 II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כּוּשׁ‎</span> **proper name, masculine** ==a Benjamite==, [[Ps.7.1|Ps 7:1]] (title), [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Χουσει υιὁῦ Ιεμενει</span>.
