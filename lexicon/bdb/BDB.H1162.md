 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֹּ֫עַז‎</span> **proper name, masculine** (***quickness ?***) — 

**1.** kinsman of Naomi, who married Ruth [[Ruth.2.1|Ruth 2:1]]; [[Ruth.2.3|Ruth 2:3]]; [[Ruth.2.4|Ruth 2:4]]; [[Ruth.2.5|Ruth 2:5]]; [[Ruth.2.8|Ruth 2:8]]; [[Ruth.2.11|Ruth 2:11]]; [[Ruth.2.14|Ruth 2:14]]; [[Ruth.2.15|Ruth 2:15]]; [[Ruth.2.19|Ruth 2:19]]; [[Ruth.2.23|Ruth 2:23]]; [[Ruth.3.2|Ruth 3:2]]; [[Ruth.3.7|Ruth 3:7]]; [[Ruth.4.1|Ruth 4:1]] (twice in verse); [[Ruth.4.5|Ruth 4:5]]; [[Ruth.4.8|Ruth 4:8]]; [[Ruth.4.9|Ruth 4:9]]; [[Ruth.4.13|Ruth 4:13]] also v[[Ruth.4.21|Ruth 4:21]] (twice in verse); [[1Chr.2.11|1Chr 2:11]]; [[1Chr.2.12|1Chr 2:12]] ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Βοος</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Βοοζ</span>). 

**2.** name of the left hand of two pillars set up before temple (compare also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָכִין‎</span> below <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כון‎</span>) [[1Kgs.7.21|1Kgs 7:21]] = [[2Chr.3.17|2Chr 3:17]] (meaning obscure; [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] apparently reference to **1**, compare [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]] [[2Chr.3.17|2Chr 3:17]] [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] supposes name of architect or donor; [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] perhaps sons of Solomon, etc.; read possibly <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּעֹז‎</span> ***in strength***, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[2Chr.3.17|2Chr 3:17]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">ἰσχύς</span>; [[BDB#The|Th]] thinks <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יכין בעז‎</span> a sentence, one word being engraved on each pillar, ***he*** (God) ***establisheth in strength***; against him, however, [[BDB#Ke|Ke]] [[BDB#Be|Be]]; [[BDB#Öt|Öttli]] thinks an exclamation, ***in strength !*** expressing satisfaction of architect; [[BDB#Klo|Klo]] proposes for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בעז‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּעַל עֹז‎</span> (compare [[BDB#B|B]] [[1Kgs.7.21|1Kgs 7:21]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Βαλαζ</span>)).
