$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** usually attributive; of ram of Pharaoh [[Ezek.30.22|Ezek 30:22]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִשְׁבָּ֑רֶת‎</span>); especially of hand of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> in delivering Israel from Egypt, [[Exod.3.19|Exod 3:19]] (gloss ? see [[BDB#Di|Di]]), [[Exod.6.1|Exod 6:1]] (twice in verse); [[Exod.13.9|Exod 13:9]] (all [[BDB#J|J]]), [[Deut.6.21|Deut 6:21]]; [[Deut.7.8|Deut 7:8]]; [[Deut.9.26|Deut 9:26]]; [[Dan.9.15|Dan 9:15]] in wonders done by agency of Moses [[Deut.34.12|Deut 34:12]] also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּכֹחַ גָּדוֺל וּבְיָד חֲזָקָה‎</span> [[Exod.31.11|Exod 31:11]]([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]), compare [[Neh.1.10|Neh 1:10]], but oftener <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ביד חזקה וּבִזְרוֺעַ נְטוּיָה‎</span> [[Deut.4.34|Deut 4:34]]; [[Deut.5.15|Deut 5:15]]; [[Deut.7.19|Deut 7:19]]; [[Deut.11.2|Deut 11:2]]; [[Deut.26.6|Deut 26:6]]; [[Jer.32.21|Jer 32:21]]; [[Ps.136.12|Ps 136:12]] of control of enemies and deliverance from exile [[Ezek.20.33|Ezek 20:33]]; [[Ezek.20.34|Ezek 20:34]] more generally [[Deut.3.24|Deut 3:24]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גָּדְלְךָ‎</span>), [[1Kgs.8.42|1Kgs 8:42]] = [[2Chr.6.32|2Chr 6:32]] of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s opposition to Zedekiah [[Jer.21.5|Jer 21:5]] once of Edom, opposed to Israel <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בעם כבד וביד חזקה‎</span> [[Num.20.20|Num 20:20]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** of sword of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Isa.27.1|Isa 27:1]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַקָּשָׁה וְהַגְּדוֺלָה‎</span>), of wind [[Exod.10.19|Exod 10:19]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[1Kgs.19.11|1Kgs 19:11]] sound of trumpet (= ***loud***) [[Exod.19.16|Exod 19:16]] ([[BDB#E|E]]). **d** ***severe, sharp, hot***, of war [[1Sam.14.52|1Sam 14:52]] (predicate), battle [[2Sam.11.15|2Sam 11:15]], sickness [[1Kgs.17.17|1Kgs 17:17]] (predicate), famine [[1Kgs.18.2|1Kgs 18:2]].
