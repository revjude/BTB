 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֹ֫פֶל‎</span> **noun masculine** [[Job.3.6|Job 3:6]] ==darkness, gloom== (only in poetry) -<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> always absolute — 

**1.** ***darkness***,, of night [[Ps.91.6|Ps 91:6]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צָֽהֳרַיִם‎</span> deep in the earth,<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶבֶן א‎</span> [[Job.28.3|Job 28:3]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חשֶׁח‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַלְמָוֶת‎</span>); ***darkness, gloom*** of underworld [[Job.10.22|Job 10:22]] (twice in verse) (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ חשֶׁח‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵיפָפָה‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַלְמָוֶת‎</span>); personified [[Job.3.6|Job 3:6]] ***that night*** — ***let darkness take it*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִקָּחֵהוּ א‎</span>; figurative of spiritual darkness [[Isa.29.18|Isa 29:18]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חשֵׁח‎</span>); of secrecy, treachery [[Ps.11.2|Ps 11:2]]. 

**2.** especially figurative of ***calamity***, [[Job.23.17|Job 23:17]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חשׁ ך‎</span> q. v.), [[Job.30.26|Job 30:26]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺר‎</span> ).
