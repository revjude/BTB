$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אבנים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ג‎</span> raised (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הקים‎</span>) over dead body [[Josh.7.26|Josh 7:26]]; [[Josh.8.29|Josh 8:29]] compare [[2Sam.18.17|2Sam 18:17]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִצִּיב‎</span>).

$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** ***heap*** or ***pile*** made (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עשׂה‎</span>) for use in ratifying compact of Jacob with Laban [[Gen.31.46|Gen 31:46]] (twice in verse); [[Gen.31.48|Gen 31:48]]; [[Gen.31.51|Gen 31:51]]; [[Gen.38.52|Gen 38:52]]; [[Gen.31.52|Gen 31:52]] (twice in verse) compare also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גַּלְעֵד‎</span> **proper name, dual** ***heap*** of ruins [[Isa.25.2|Isa 25:2]] elsewhere plural [[Hos.12.12|Hos 12:12]]; [[2Kgs.19.25|2Kgs 19:25]] = [[Isa.37.26|Isa 37:26]]; [[Job.15.28|Job 15:28]]; [[Jer.9.10|Jer 9:10]]; [[Jer.51.37|Jer 51:37]].
