 II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָבֵשׁ‎</span> **verbal adjective** or **Participle** ==dry, dried==, so, masculine singular absolute [[Nah.1.10|Nah 1:10]] + 4 t.; feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְבֵשָׁה‎</span> [[Num.11.6|Num 11:6]] masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎(וִ)יבֵשִׁים‎</span> [[Num.6.3|Num 6:3]] feminine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְבֵשׁוֺת‎</span> [[Ezek.37.2|Ezek 37:2]]; [[Ezek.37.4|Ezek 37:4]] — 

**1.** ***dried***, literal only <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲנָבִים יְבֵשִׁיס‎</span> [[Num.6.3|Num 6:3]] ***dried grapes*** ([[BDB#P|P]]; opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַחִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ְ‎</span>); figurative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַפְשֵׁנוּ יְבֵשָׁה‎</span> [[Num.11.6|Num 11:6]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]) ***our soul*** (i.e. our appetite) ***is dried up***, namely, for want of fresh, juicy meat. 

**2.** ***dry***, of chaff, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קַשׁ‎</span> [[Nah.1.10|Nah 1:10]] in simile of Ninevites under impending judgment; compare in figure of Job, [[Job.13.25|Job 13:25]] of tree [[Ezek.17.24|Ezek 17:24]] (figurative of Davidic house; opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵץ לָ֑ךְ‎</span>), [[Ezek.21.3|Ezek 21:3]] (in prediction of Judah's devastation by Babylon, opposed to ***id.***); [[Isa.56.3|Isa 56:3]] figurative of eunuch; of the bonesin Ezekiel's vision [[Ezek.37.2|Ezek 37:2]]; [[Ezek.37.4|Ezek 37:4]].
