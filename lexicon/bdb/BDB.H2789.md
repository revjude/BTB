 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶ֫רֶשׂ‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==earthenware, earthen vessel, sherd, potsherd==, [[BDB#P|P]] and late (Late Hebrew <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶרֶס‎</span>, Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎חֲרַס‎</span>) — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Lev.6.21|Lev 6:21]] + 9 t., <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָ֑רֶשׂ‎</span> [[Num.5.17|Num 5:17]] + 4 t.; plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַרְשֵׂי‎</span> [[Isa.45.9|Isa 45:9]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲרָשֶׂיהָ‎</span> [[Ezek.23.34|Ezek 23:34]]. — 

**1.** ***earthenware:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּלִיאח‎</span> ***earthen vessel*** [[Jer.32.14|Jer 32:14]] especially [[BDB#P|P]] [[Lev.6.21|Lev 6:21]]; [[Lev.11.33|Lev 11:33]]; [[Lev.14.5|Lev 14:5]]; [[Lev.14.50|Lev 14:50]]; [[Lev.15.12|Lev 15:12]];  without <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּלִי‎</span> [[Prov.26.23|Prov 26:23]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּקְבֻּק יוֺצֵר ח‎</span> [[Jer.19.1|Jer 19:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְנִבְלֵי ח‎</span> [[Lam.4.2|Lam 4:2]] they are reckoned ***as earthen vessels***, simile of sons of Zion; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶרֶשׂ אֶתחַֿרְשֵׂי אֲדָמָה‎</span> [[Isa.45.9|Isa 45:9]] ***a potsherd*** (perhaps = ***earthen vessel***, see [[Prov.26.23|Prov 26:23]] above) ***among earthen potsherds*** (of men, over against <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> their potter); simile of dryness [[Ps.22.16|Ps 22:16]]. 

**2.** ***a fragment of earthenware, sherd*** [[Isa.30.14|Isa 30:14]]; [[Ezek.23.34|Ezek 23:34]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְהִתְגָּרֵד‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Job.2.8|Job 2:8]] ***a sherd to scrape himself***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַדּוּדֵי ח‎</span> [[Job.41.22|Job 41:22]] ***sharpest potsherds***, figurative of sharp scales on belly of crocodile.
