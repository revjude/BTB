 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סוּת‎</span>] **verb Hiph\`il** ==incite, allure, instigate== (Late Hebrew Hiph\`il, ***id.***); — 

**Hiph\`il** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֱִסיתְךָ‎</span> [[1Sam.26.19|1Sam 26:19]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲסֵיתְךָ‎</span> [[Job.36.16|Job 36:16]] 3 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵסַ֫תָּה‎</span> [[1Kgs.21.25|1Kgs 21:25]] ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:72w»§ 72w</sup> [[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>i. 460</sup>); 3 plural suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִסִּית֫וּךָ‎</span> [[Jer.38.22|Jer 38:22]] ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:72ee»§ 72ee</sup>); ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַסִּית‎</span> [[2Kgs.18.32|2Kgs 18:32]] + 2 t., but also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיָּ֫סֶת‎</span> [[2Sam.24.1|2Sam 24:1]]; [[1Chr.21.1|1Chr 21:1]], suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְִסיתְךָ‎</span> [[Deut.13.7|Deut 13:7]]; [[Job.36.18|Job 36:18]], etc.; ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַסִּית‎</span> [[Jer.43.3|Jer 43:3]]; [[2Chr.32.11|2Chr 32:11]] — 

**1. a.** ***incite*** to make a request (with accusative of person + infinitive) [[Judg.1.14|Judg 1:14]] (on text see [[BDB#GFM|GFM]]) = [[Josh.15.18|Josh 15:18]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***allure*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִמֶּנּוּ וַיְסִיתֵם‎</span> [[2Chr.18.31|2Chr 18:31]] ***and*** God ***allured them away from him*** (si vera lectio; strike out clause as gloss [[BDB#Be|Be]] [[BDB#Kit|Kit]]); so also [[Job.36.16|Job 36:16]] according to [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#Bu|Bu]] and others; ***he allureth thee*** out ***of the mouth of distress***, but [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Du|Du]] ***freedom hath seduced thee***; for meaning ***seduce, entice***, compare also v[[Job.36.18|Job 36:18]]. 

**2.** ***instigate***, in bad sense, with accusative of person, + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span> ***against***, [[1Sam.26.19|1Sam 26:19]]; [[2Sam.24.1|2Sam 24:1]]; [[Jer.43.3|Jer 43:3]]; [[Job.2.3|Job 2:3]] with accusative of person alone [[1Kgs.21.25|1Kgs 21:25]]; [[2Kgs.18.32|2Kgs 18:32]] = [[Isa.36.18|Isa 36:18]], compare [[2Chr.32.15|2Chr 32:15]]; [[Deut.13.7|Deut 13:7]]; [[Jer.38.22|Jer 38:22]] + infinitive [[1Chr.21.1|1Chr 21:1]]; [[2Chr.18.2|2Chr 18:2]]; [[2Chr.32.11|2Chr 32:11]].
