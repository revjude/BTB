 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוּלָה‎</span>] **noun [feminine]** properly ==removal==, only found in the ***stative construct*** as **preposition** and **conjunction** ==except, only, save that== — construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוּלַת‎</span> [[2Kgs.24.14|2Kgs 24:14]] and (with the obsolete case-ending <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִ֯‎</span>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוּלָתִי‎</span> [[Deut.1.36|Deut 1:36]]; [[Deut.4.12|Deut 4:12]]; [[Josh.11.13|Josh 11:13]]; [[1Kgs.3.18|1Kgs 3:18]]; [[1Kgs.12.20|1Kgs 12:20]]; [[Ps.18.32|Ps 18:32]] with suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוּלָתִי‎</span> [[Hos.13.4|Hos 13:4]]; [[Isa.45.5|Isa 45:5]]; [[Isa.45.21|Isa 45:21]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוּלָֽתְךָ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוּלָתֶ֑ךָ‎</span> [[2Sam.7.22|2Sam 7:22]] = [[1Chr.17.20|1Chr 17:20]]; [[Isa.26.13|Isa 26:13]]; [[Isa.64.3|Isa 64:3]]; [[Ruth.4.4|Ruth 4:4]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוּלָתָהּ‎</span> [[1Sam.21.10|1Sam 21:10]] — 

**1. preposition** ***except, besides***, literally ***with removal of . . .*** (synonym <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִבַּלְעֲדֵי‎</span> [[Ps.18.32|Ps 18:32]]; [[Isa.45.21|Isa 45:21]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּלְתִּי‎</span> [[Hos.13.4|Hos 13:4]] and compare [[Deut.1.36|Deut 1:36]] with [[Num.32.12|Num 32:12]]), [[Deut.1.36|Deut 1:36]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זולתי כלב‎</span> ***with the exception of*** Caleb, [[1Sam.21.10|1Sam 21:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵין אַחֶרֶת זוּלָתָהּ‎</span> there is none other ***except*** it, [[1Kgs.12.20|1Kgs 12:20]]; [[2Kgs.24.14|2Kgs 24:14]]; [[Ruth.4.4|Ruth 4:4]]: often in such phrases as, There is no God (or, Who is God?) ***except*** me (or thee), [[2Sam.7.22|2Sam 7:22]]; [[Ps.18.32|Ps 18:32]]; [[Hos.13.4|Hos 13:4]]; [[Isa.45.5|Isa 45:5]]; [[Isa.45.21|Isa 45:21]]; [[Isa.64.3|Isa 64:3]]. Always after a negative or question, except [[Isa.26.13|Isa 26:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אדנים זולתך‎</span> lords ***other than*** thou. Connected inexactly with what precedes, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> acquires the force of ***only***, [[Deut.4.12|Deut 4:12]] ye saw no form <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוּלָתִי קוֺל‎</span> ***save*** a voice = (there was) ***only*** a voice, [[1Kgs.3.18|1Kgs 3:18]]. 

**2.** conjunction [[Josh.11.13|Josh 11:13]] ***except that*** he burnt Hazor alone.
