 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁלִּיט‎</span> **adjective** ==having mastery, domineering==; — 

**1.** ***having mastery:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּרוּחַ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵין אָדָם שׁ‎</span> [[Eccl.8.8|Eccl 8:8]] elsewhere as substantive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַשּׁ‎</span> ***the ruler*** [[Eccl.10.5|Eccl 10:5]], so (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַלהָֿאָרֶץ‎</span>) [[Gen.42.6|Gen 42:6]] (probably late substitution for original word of [[BDB#E|E]]); plural as substantive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁלִּיטִים‎</span> [[Eccl.7.19|Eccl 7:19]] (specifically of Alexander's successors [[BDB#Perles|Perles]]<sup>Anal. 42</sup>). 

**2.** ***domineering, imperious***, feminine singular ([[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup> ii. 10. 201</sup>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִשָּׁה זוֺנָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁלָּ֑טֶת‎</span> [[Ezek.16.30|Ezek 16:30]] (Jerusalem personified).
