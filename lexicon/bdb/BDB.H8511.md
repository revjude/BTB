 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תָּלָא‎</span> **verb** ==hang== (\|\| form of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תלה‎</span>, [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:75rr»§ 75rr</sup>); — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** 3 plural suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּלָאוּם‎</span> [[2Sam.21.12|2Sam 21:12]] [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] the Philistines ***had hanged them*** ([[BDB#Kt|Kt]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תלום‎</span>; see [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]); ***Passive participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּלֻאִים‎</span> [[Deut.28.66|Deut 28:66]] thy life shall be ***hung up*** before thee, i.e. be in suspense, each moment uncertain. — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְעַמִּי תְלוּאִים לִמְשׁוּבָתִי‎</span> [[Hos.11.7|Hos 11:7]] ***my people is hung up to my backsliding*** is certainly corrupt ([[BDB#AV|AV]] ***bent to backsliding from me***, i.e. huge or swung toward turning from me, compare [[BDB#GASm|GASm]], but very dubious); [[BDB#Marti|Marti]] conjectures <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִלְוִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶלעֲֿצַבִּים‎</span>; Oettli [[BDB#Now|Now]]<sup>2 Harper</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִלְאָה‎</span> or <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֶלְאַ֫נִי‎</span> ***has wearied itself*** [***me***] in backsliding.
