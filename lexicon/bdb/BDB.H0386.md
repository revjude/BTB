 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵיתָן‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵתָן‎</span> **adjective** ==perennial, ever-flowing==, figurative permanent, as substantive steady flow, permanence; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵיתָן‎</span> [[Gen.49.24|Gen 49:24]] + 8 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵתָן‎</span> [[Job.33.19|Job 33:19]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵיתָנוֺ‎</span> [[Exod.14.27|Exod 14:27]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵיתָנִים‎</span> [[Job.12.19|Job 12:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵתָנִים‎</span> [[1Kgs.8.2|1Kgs 8:2]]; [[Mic.6.2|Mic 6:2]] (in form, an 'elative,' [[BDB#Ew|Ew]]<sup>§ 162 b</sup> = Arabic comparative and superlative); — 

**1.** ***ever-flowing*** (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַכְזָב‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַחַל אֵיתָן‎</span> [[Amos.5.24|Amos 5:24]] an ***ever-flowing*** wady, [[Deut.21.4|Deut 21:4]] (see [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]); as substantive in <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַהֲרוֺת אֵיתָּן‎</span> [[Ps.74.15|Ps 74:15]] ***rivers of steady flow***; as substantive also [[Exod.14.27|Exod 14:27]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), the sea returned <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְאֵיתָנוֺ‎</span> to its ***steady flow***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶרַת הָאֵיתָנִים‎</span> [[1Kgs.8.2|1Kgs 8:2]] month of ***steady flowings*** = 7th mo., Oct.-;Nov. (post-Biblical Tisri). 

**2.** figurative ***permanent, enduring*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גּוֺי אֵיתָן הוּא‎</span> [[Jer.5.15|Jer 5:15]], i.e. a nation whose numbers never dwindle or fail, ***imperishable***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְרִיב עֲצָמָיו א‎</span> [[Job.33.19|Job 33:19]] [[BDB#Kt|Kt]] the strife of his bones is ***constant***, [[Gen.49.24|Gen 49:24]] his bow abode <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּא‎</span> as an ***enduring, firm***, one, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺשָֽׁבְךָ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[Num.24.21|Num 24:21]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׂים בַּסֶּלַע‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְוֵה אֵיתָן‎</span> [[Jer.49.19|Jer 49:19]] = [[Jer.50.44|Jer 50:44]] an abode of ***permanency*** (of Edom, and Babylon, suddenly depopulated by foe, figured as lion); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎האיתנים מוסדי ארץ‎</span> [[Mic.6.2|Mic 6:2]] ye ***ever-enduring*** ones, the foundations of the earth (but read probably with [[BDB#We|We]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַאֲזִינוּ‎</span>), [[Job.12.19|Job 12:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵיתָנִים יְסַלֵּף‎</span> he subverteth them that are ***firmly seated*** (i.e. men established in hereditary offices or dignities). Very uncertain is [[Prov.13.15|Prov 13:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֶּרֶךְ בִּגְדִים אֵיתָן‎</span>, perhaps ***firm, hard, rugged*** ([[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#Now|Now]]<sup> according to</sup> [[BDB#Str|Str]]. the text is corrupt).
