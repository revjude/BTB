 <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אֵלֶּה‎</span> **demonstrative pronoun plural** ==these== (Nabataean <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אלה‎</span> ([[BDB#Ldzb|Lzb]]<sup>264</sup> [[BDB#Cooke|Cooke]]<sup>241</sup>), Cappadocian and Egyptian Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אלה‎</span> ([[BDB#Ldzb|Lzb]]<sup>Eph. i. 323</sup> [[BDB#RÉS|RÉS]]<sup>361 A 3</sup> [[BDB#S-C|S-C]]<sup>Pap. E.13, Ki 13</sup>): probably therefore not, as used to be supposed, a Hebraism, but a peculiarity of the dialects of [[Jer.10.11|Jer 10:11]] and of Ezra (in which the usually Aramaic form <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אִלֵּין‎</span> does not occur) : see further <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אִלֵּין‎</span>); — ***these***, [[Jer.10.11|Jer 10:11]]; [[Ezra.5.15|Ezra 5:15]] [[BDB#Kt|Kt]].
