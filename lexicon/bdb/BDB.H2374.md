**1.** ***seer:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of unnamed persons, synonym <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נבא(יםׅ‎</span> [[2Kgs.17.13|2Kgs 17:13]]; [[Isa.29.10|Isa 29:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֹאִים‎</span> [[Isa.30.10|Isa 30:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קֹסְמִים‎</span> [[Mic.3.7|Mic 3:7]] historical writers <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּבְרֵי הַחֹוִים‎</span> [[2Chr.33.18|2Chr 33:18]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דברי חוזים‎</span> ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]) [[2Chr.33.19|2Chr 33:19]] ([[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] apparently proper name) 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** named prophets; Gad [[2Sam.24.11|2Sam 24:11]] = [[1Chr.21.9|1Chr 21:9]]; [[1Chr.29.29|1Chr 29:29]]; [[2Chr.29.25|2Chr 29:25]] Amos [[Amos.7.12|Amos 7:12]] Iddo [[2Chr.9.29|2Chr 9:29]]; [[2Chr.12.15|2Chr 12:15]] Jehu [[2Chr.19.2|2Chr 19:2]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** the singers: Heman [[1Chr.25.5|1Chr 25:5]] Asaph [[2Chr.29.30|2Chr 29:30]] Jeduthun [[2Chr.35.15|2Chr 35:15]].
