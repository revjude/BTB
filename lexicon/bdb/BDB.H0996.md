When the space separating two distinct objects is to be indicated, this is done 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** most commonly by repeating <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בין‎</span>, as [[Gen.13.3|Gen 13:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּין בֵּיתְאֵל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּבֵין הָעָ֑י‎</span> literally ***in the interval of*** Bethel, and ***in the interval of*** \`Ai, i.e. ***between*** Bethel ***and*** \`Ai, [[Gen.16.14|Gen 16:14]]; [[Gen.17.7|Gen 17:7]]; [[Gen.31.50|Gen 31:50]]; [[Gen.31.51|Gen 31:51]] + often 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** more rarely by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּין ֗֗֗ ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span>, [[Gen.1.6|Gen 1:6]] dividing <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּין מַיִם לָמָ֑יִם‎</span> literally ***in the interval of*** waters ***with reference to*** waters, i.e. ***between*** the waters ***and*** the waters, [[Lev.20.25|Lev 20:25]]; [[Lev.27.33|Lev 27:33]]; [[Deut.17.8|Deut 17:8]] (3 t. in verse); [[2Sam.19.36|2Sam 19:36]]; [[1Kgs.3.9|1Kgs 3:9]]; [[Ezek.41.18|Ezek 41:18]]; [[Ezek.42.20|Ezek 42:20]]; [[Ezek.44.23|Ezek 44:23]] (twice in verse); [[Jonah.4.11|Jonah 4:11]]; [[Mal.3.18|Mal 3:18]] (twice in verse); [[2Chr.14.10|2Chr 14:10]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּין ֗֗֗ וּלְ‎</span> [[Joel.2.17|Joel 2:17]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּין ֗֗֗ לְבֵין‎</span> [[Isa.59.2|Isa 59:2]]. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּין‎</span> is used not only of actual locality, but also with verbs of dividing (figurative) [[Gen.1.14|Gen 1:14]]; [[Lev.10.10|Lev 10:10]], and of judging, knowing, teaching, etc., if the idea of ***distinguishing*** be involved, as [[Gen.16.5|Gen 16:5]]; [[2Sam.19.36|2Sam 19:36]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַאֵדַע בֵּין‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טוֺב לְרָע‎</span> can I discern ***between*** good and evil ? $\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶלבֵּֿין‎</span>, after a verb implying motion, ***in between, in among*** [[Ezek.31.10|Ezek 31:10]]; [[Ezek.31.14|Ezek 31:14]]. So <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶלבֵּֿינוֺת לְ‎</span> ib. [[Ezek.10.2|Ezek 10:2]].

$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַלבֵּֿין‎</span> nearly as <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶלבֵּֿין‎</span> [[Ezek.19.11|Ezek 19:11]]

[[Ezek.47.18|Ezek 47:18]] is difficult and uncertain: see Commentaries Less precisely ***from, the midst of:*** [[Num.17.2|Num 17:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִבֵּין הַשְּׂרֵפָה‎</span> ***out of the midst of*** the burning, [[Ps.104.12|Ps 104:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִבֵּין עֳפָאִים‎</span> ***from amongst*** the branches they utter their song, [[Jer.48.45|Jer 48:45]] and a flame <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִבֵּין סִיחוֺן‎</span> ***from the midst of*** Sihon (Sihon representing his people: but expression is singular; read perhaps <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִבֵּית ס‎</span>, compare [[Num.21.28|Num 21:28]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִקִּרְיַת ס‎</span>: \|\| in both passages <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵחֶשְׁבּוֺן‎</span>), [[Ezek.37.21|Ezek 37:21]].
