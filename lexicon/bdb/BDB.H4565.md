 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִסְתָּר‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==secret place, hiding place==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Ps.10.9|Ps 10:9]]; [[Hab.3.14|Hab 3:14]] usually plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִסְתָּרִים‎</span> [[Jer.13.17|Jer 13:17]] + 6 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִסְתָּרָיו‎</span> [[Jer.49.10|Jer 49:10]] — 

**1.** ***secret place(s)***, concealed from view, [[Jer.13.17|Jer 13:17]] where treasures are stored [[Isa.45.3|Isa 45:3]]. 

**2.** ***hiding-place(s):*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** for protection [[Jer.23.24|Jer 23:24]]; [[Jer.49.10|Jer 49:10]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** for perpetration of crime, especially murder: [[Ps.10.8|Ps 10:8]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַאֲרָב‎</span>), simile of lion v[[Ps.10.9|Ps 10:9]], [[Ps.17.12|Ps 17:12]], compare [[Hab.3.14|Hab 3:14]]; [[Ps.64.5|Ps 64:5]] of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> lying in wait [[Lam.3.10|Lam 3:10]] (as a lion).
