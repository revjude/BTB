$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of men [[Exod.4.11|Exod 4:11]] ([[BDB#J|J]]; opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פִּקֵּחַ‎</span>), [[2Sam.5.6|2Sam 5:6]]; [[2Sam.5.8|2Sam 5:8]] (twice in verse); [[Lev.19.14|Lev 19:14]] ([[BDB#H|H]]), [[Deut.27.18|Deut 27:18]]; [[Jer.31.8|Jer 31:8]]; [[Job.29.15|Job 29:15]] in simile [[Deut.28.29|Deut 28:29]]; [[Zeph.1.17|Zeph 1:17]]; [[Isa.59.10|Isa 59:10]], compare [[Lam.4.14|Lam 4:14]] (apposition); as adjective attribute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִישׁ ע‎</span> [[Lev.21.18|Lev 21:18]] ([[BDB#H|H]]).
