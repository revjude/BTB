 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַבּוּר‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==highest part, centre== (according to [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] ***navel***, so Late Hebrew ***id.***, and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טִיבּוּר‎</span>, Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎טִיבּוּרָא‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טִיבּוּרְיָא‎</span>) — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִנֵּה עָם יֹרְדִים מֵעִם טַבּוּר הארץ‎</span> [[Judg.9.37|Judg 9:37]] ***behold people descending from the highest part of the land***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ישְׁבֵי עַלטַֿבּוּר הארץ‎</span> [[Ezek.38.12|Ezek 38:12]] those ***dwelling upon the navel of the earth***, i.e. upon the mountainous country of Israel, central and prominent in the earth.
