 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׂגַב‎</span>] **verb** ==be (inaccessibly) high== (compare [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׂגַב‎</span> Pa\`el [from Hebrew] ***exalt***); — ***Qal*** ***Perfect*** 

**1.** 3 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָֽׂגְבָה‎</span> [[Deut.2.36|Deut 2:36]], of city, with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span>, ***be*** (too) ***high*** for capture. 

**2.** 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָֽׂגְבוּ‎</span> [[Job.5.11|Job 5:11]] of person, ***be high*** in prosperity. **Niph\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִשְׂגַּב‎</span> [[Isa.2.11|Isa 2:11]]; [[Isa.2.17|Isa 2:17]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִשְׂגָָּֽכ‎</span>[[Prov.18.10|Prov 18:10]] 3 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִשְׂגְּבָה‎</span> [[Ps.139.6|Ps 139:6]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִשְׂגָּב‎</span> [[Isa.12.4|Isa 12:4]] +; feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִשְׂגָּבָה‎</span> [[Isa.26.5|Isa 26:5]] +; — 

**1.** ***be high***, walls [[Isa.30.13|Isa 30:13]]; [[Prov.18.11|Prov 18:11]] city [[Isa.26.5|Isa 26:5]] God's knowledge [[Ps.139.6|Ps 139:6]] (unattainable). 

**2.** ***be*** (safely) ***set on high***, [[Prov.18.10|Prov 18:10]]. 

**3.** ***be exalted***, of God [[Isa.2.11|Isa 2:11]]; [[Isa.2.17|Isa 2:17]]; [[Isa.33.5|Isa 33:5]] his name [[Isa.12.4|Isa 12:4]]; [[Ps.148.13|Ps 148:13]]. **Pi\`el** ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְשַׂגֵּב‎</span> [[Isa.9.10|Isa 9:10]]; [[Ps.107.41|Ps 107:41]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְשַׂגֶּבְךָ‎</span> [[Ps.20.2|Ps 20:2]], etc.; — transitive with accusative: 

**1.** ***set*** (securely) ***on high***, [[Ps.20.2|Ps 20:2]]; [[Ps.69.30|Ps 69:30]]; [[Ps.91.14|Ps 91:14]] c, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span>, of foe [[Ps.59.2|Ps 59:2]], affliction [[Ps.107.41|Ps 107:41]]. 

**2.** ***exalt***, in effective hostility, with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל‎</span>, [[Isa.9.10|Isa 9:10]]. **Pu\`al** ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְשֻׂגָ֑ב‎</span> [[Prov.29.25|Prov 29:25]] ***be set*** (securely) ***on high***. **Hiph\`il** ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַשְׂגִּיב‎</span> [[Job.36.22|Job 36:22]] God ***acts exaltedly*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּכֹחוֺ‎</span>.
