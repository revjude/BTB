 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַס‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[1Kgs.5.27|1Kgs 5:27]]</sup> usually **collective** ==body of forced labourers, task-workers, labour-band or gang==, also (late) forced service, task-work, serfdom ( [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִסָּא‎</span>, especially in phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַסְּקֵי מִיסִּין‎</span> (implying late and [for Biblical Hebrew] false sense 'tribute,' so Late Hebrew <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעֲלֵי מָסִּים‎</span>; √ unknown, probably loan-word; follows analogue of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ע‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ע‎</span>); — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[1Kgs.5.27|1Kgs 5:27]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָמַ֑ס‎</span> [[Josh.17.13|Josh 17:13]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמַּ֑ס‎</span> [[2Sam.20.24|2Sam 20:24]] +; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִסִּים‎</span> [[Exod.1.11|Exod 1:11]] — 

**1.** ***labour-band, labour-gang***, body of task-workers for public service, formed by levy upon the people by Solomon <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַּ֫עַל הַמֶּלֶךְ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁלֹמֹה מַס מִכָּליִֿשְׂרָאֵל‎</span> [[1Kgs.5.27|1Kgs 5:27]], amounting to 30,000 men v.[[1Kgs.5.27|1Kgs 5:27]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲשֶׁר הֶעֱלָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמּ‎</span> [[1Kgs.9.15|1Kgs 9:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַּעֲלֵם שׁ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמַס עֹבֵד‎</span> v[[1Kgs.9.21|1Kgs 9:21]] ***and Solomon levied them for a toiling labour-band*** (compare [[Gen.49.15|Gen 49:15]]; [[Josh.16.10|Josh 16:10]]) = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמַס וַיַּעֲלֵם שׁ‎</span> [[2Chr.8.8|2Chr 8:8]] apparently instituted, earlier, by David, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַאֲדֹרָם עַלהַֿמַּ֑ס‎</span> [[2Sam.20.24|2Sam 20:24]], i.e. had charge of the body of labourers, so [[1Kgs.4.6|1Kgs 4:6]]; [[1Kgs.5.28|1Kgs 5:28]]; [[1Kgs.12.18|1Kgs 12:18]] = [[2Chr.10.18|2Chr 10:18]]. 

**2. a.** ***of labour-bands, slave-gangs*** of Israel in Egypt, only <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׂרֵי מִסִּים‎</span> [[Exod.1.11|Exod 1:11]] ([[BDB#J|J]]) ***gang-overseers***. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of conquered populations, subject to forced labour: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִהְיוּ לְךָ לָמַס וַעֲבָדוּךָ‎</span> [[Deut.20.11|Deut 20:11]] compare [[Judg.1.30|Judg 1:30]]; [[Judg.1.33|Judg 1:33]]; [[Judg.1.35|Judg 1:35]] (see [[BDB#GFM|GFM]]), [[Isa.31.8|Isa 31:8]]; [[Lam.1.1|Lam 1:1]] so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ויהי לְמַס‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֹבֵד‎</span> [[Josh.16.10|Josh 16:10]] (compare [[Gen.49.15|Gen 49:15]]; [[1Kgs.9.21|1Kgs 9:21]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִתְּנוּ אֶתהַֿכְּנַעֲנִי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָמַ֑ס‎</span> [[Josh.17.13|Josh 17:13]], compare (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיָּ֫שֶׂם וגו‎</span>) [[Judg.1.28|Judg 1:28]]. 

**3.** in general: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיְהִי לְמַסעֹֿבֵד‎</span> [[Gen.49.15|Gen 49:15]] ***and he*** (Issachar) became ***a slaving labour-band*** (poem in [[BDB#J|J]]; compare [[Josh.16.10|Josh 16:10]]; [[1Kgs.9.21|1Kgs 9:21]]); compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְמִיָּה תִּהְיֶה לָמַ֑ס‎</span> [[Prov.12.24|Prov 12:24]] (here apparently of individual) 

**4.** forced service, ***serfdom*** (or possibly (in late passage) ***tribute = enforced payment***, compare Late Hebrew, [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]], see above), only of Ahasuerus: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיָּשֶׂם הַמֶּלֶךְ מַס עַלהָֿאָרֶץ וגו‎</span> [[Esth.10.1|Esth 10:1]].
