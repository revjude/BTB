 <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎עַד‎</span><sub>35</sub> **preposition and conjunction** ==even to, until== (Biblical Hebrew III.  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַד‎</span>; so [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]], Syriac, Old Aramaic, Nabataean, Palmyrene, Egyptian Aramaic ([[BDB#Ldzb|Lzb]]<sup>336</sup> [[BDB#S-C|S-C]]<sup>A 5</sup> +)); — 

**1.** ***preposition:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of space, ***even to*** [[Dan.7.13|Dan 7:13]] <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎עַדכָּֿא‎</span> v[[Dan.7.28|Dan 7:28]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of amount, ***up to*** [[Ezra.7.22|Ezra 7:22]] (4 t.). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** of time, ***until*** [[Dan.2.20|Dan 2:20]]; [[Dan.6.15|Dan 6:15]]; [[Dan.6.27|Dan 6:27]]; [[Dan.7.18|Dan 7:18]] (twice in verse); [[Dan.7.26|Dan 7:26]]; [[Ezra.4.24|Ezra 4:24]]; [[Ezra.5.16|Ezra 5:16]] <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎וְעַדכְּֿעַן‎</span>; = our ***against*** [[Ezra.6.15|Ezra 6:15]] (compare Biblical Hebrew, p. 724:a top); ***to*** (the end of) = ***during*** [[Dan.6.8|Dan 6:8]] <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎עַד יוֺמִין תְּלָתִין‎</span>, v.[[Dan.6.13|Dan 6:13]]; [[Dan.7.12|Dan 7:12]]; [[Dan.7.25|Dan 7:25]]. On <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎עַדאָֿחֳרָן‎</span> [[Dan.4.5|Dan 4:5]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָחֳרָן‎</span>; and on <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎עַדדִּֿבְרַת דִּי‎</span> [[Dan.4.14|Dan 4:14]] see [ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּבְרָה‎</span>]. 

**2.** ***conjunction:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎עַדדִּֿי‎</span> ***until***, followed by perfect (of past time) [[Dan.2.34|Dan 2:34]]; [[Dan.4.30|Dan 4:30]]; [[Dan.5.21|Dan 5:21]]; [[Dan.7.4|Dan 7:4]]; [[Dan.7.9|Dan 7:9]]; [[Dan.7.11|Dan 7:11]]; [[Dan.7.22|Dan 7:22]] followed by imperfect (of future) [[Dan.2.9|Dan 2:9]]; [[Dan.4.20|Dan 4:20]]; [[Dan.4.22|Dan 4:22]] (twice in verse); [[Dan.4.29|Dan 4:29]]. — [[Dan.6.25|Dan 6:25]] <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָא מְטוֺ ֗֗֗ עַד דִּי שְׁלִטוּ וג‎</span> ***up to the time that . . . ***, i.e. ***ere that***. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎עַד‎</span> alone, [[Ezra.4.21|Ezra 4:21]]; [[Ezra.5.5|Ezra 5:5]].
