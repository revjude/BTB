 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּיֹּר‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּיּוֺר‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[1Kgs.7.38|1Kgs 7:38]]</sup> ==pot, basin== (Late Hebrew <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּיּוֺר‎</span> ***wash-basin***, so Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎כִּיּוֺרָא‎</span>) — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּיֹּר‎</span> [[1Kgs.7.30|1Kgs 7:30]] + 8 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּיּוֺר‎</span> [[1Sam.2.14|1Sam 2:14]] + 5 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּיּוֺר‎</span> [[Exod.30.18|Exod 30:18]] + 2 t.; plural absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּיֹּרוֺת‎</span> [[1Kgs.7.40|1Kgs 7:40]] (but see below), [[2Chr.4.14|2Chr 4:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּיֹּרֹת‎</span> [[1Kgs.7.43|1Kgs 7:43]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּיּוֺרִים‎</span> [[2Chr.4.6|2Chr 4:6]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּיֹּרוֺת‎</span> [[1Kgs.7.38|1Kgs 7:38]] — 

**1.** ***pot*** for cooking [[1Sam.2.14|1Sam 2:14]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דּוּד‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קַלַּחַת‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּרוּר‎</span>). 

**2.** ***fire-pot*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּיּוֺר אֵשׁ‎</span> [[Zech.12.6|Zech 12:6]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַפִּיד אֵשׁ‎</span>; figurative of chiefs of Judah). 

**3.** ***basin*** of bronze for washing, ***laver***; set on a bronze support 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** before tabernacle [[Exod.30.18|Exod 30:18]]; [[Exod.30.28|Exod 30:28]]; [[Exod.31.9|Exod 31:9]]; [[Exod.35.16|Exod 35:16]]; [[Exod.38.8|Exod 38:8]]; [[Exod.39.39|Exod 39:39]]; [[Exod.40.7|Exod 40:7]]; [[Exod.40.11|Exod 40:11]]; [[Exod.40.30|Exod 40:30]]; [[Lev.8.11|Lev 8:11]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** 10 in number, 5 at each front corner of temple [[1Kgs.7.30|1Kgs 7:30]]; [[1Kgs.7.38|1Kgs 7:38]] (3 t. in verse); [[1Kgs.7.43|1Kgs 7:43]] = [[2Chr.4.14|2Chr 4:14]]; [[2Chr.4.6|2Chr 4:6]] ([[1Kgs.7.40|1Kgs 7:40]] read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַסִּירוֺת‎</span> as v[[1Kgs.7.45|1Kgs 7:45]] and \|\| [[2Chr.4.11|2Chr 4:11]]; [[2Chr.4.16|2Chr 4:16]], so Hebrew Manuscripts [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] [[BDB#The|Th]] [[BDB#Ke|Ke]] [[BDB#Sta|Sta]] [[BDB#Klo|Klo]] and others) 

**4.** ***plat form*** or ***stage*** of bronze (probably round, bowl-like in shape) on which, according to Chronicles, Solomon stood and kneeled [[2Chr.6.13|2Chr 6:13]].
