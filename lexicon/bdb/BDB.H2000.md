**1.** ***move noisily***, transitive, drive a wagon in threshing <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גִּלְגַּל עֶגְּלָתוֺ וּפָרָשָׁיו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ה‎</span> [[Isa.28.28|Isa 28:28]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דושׁ‎</span>). 

**2.** ***confuse, discomfit***, with accusative [[Exod.14.24|Exod 14:24]]; [[Exod.23.27|Exod 23:27]]; [[Josh.10.10|Josh 10:10]]; [[Judg.4.15|Judg 4:15]]; [[1Sam.7.10|1Sam 7:10]]; [[Ps.18.15|Ps 18:15]] = [[2Sam.22.15|2Sam 22:15]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הפיץ‎</span>), [[Ps.144.6|Ps 144:6]] (\|\| ***id.***); ***discomfit, vex***, [[2Chr.15.6|2Chr 15:6]] with accusative &amp; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּכָלצָֿרָה‎</span> of instrumental; with accusative also [[Deut.2.15|Deut 2:15]] (followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַד תֻּמָּם‎</span>), [[Esth.9.24|Esth 9:24]] (followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְאַבְּדָם‎</span>); probably also [[Jer.51.34|Jer 51:34]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָכַל‎</span>), compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוּם‎</span> [[Deut.7.23|Deut 7:23]].
