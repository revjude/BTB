**1.** ***thrust, push***, subject man, followed by accusative [[Num.35.20|Num 35:20]]; [[Num.35.22|Num 35:22]] (here fatally); subject cattle, figurative of men, [[Ezek.34.21|Ezek 34:21]] (instrumental <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּכָתֵף‎</span>; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָגַח‎</span>); ***push away*** [[2Kgs.4.27|2Kgs 4:27]] (Gehazi, the Shunammite); ***thrust away*** (from oneself) figurative = ***reject***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַוַּת רְשָׁעִים‎</span> [[Prov.10.3|Prov 10:3]] (subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>).
