 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺן‎</span> **noun masculine** ==vigour, wealth== — absolute [[Hos.12.9|Hos 12:9]], suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺנִי‎</span> [[Gen.49.3|Gen 49:3]] + etc.; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺנִים‎</span> [[Ps.78.51|Ps 78:51]] +. 

**1.** ***manly vigour*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּאוֺנוֺ שָׂרָה אֶתאֱֿלֹהִים‎</span> [[Hos.12.4|Hos 12:4]] (of Jacob); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֵאשִׁית אוֺן‎</span> ***beginning of manly vigour*** [[Gen.49.3|Gen 49:3]] (of Reuben, first-born of Jacob); [[Deut.21.17|Deut 21:17]]; [[Ps.105.36|Ps 105:36]] (firstborn Egyptians), so [[Ps.78.51|Ps 78:51]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֵאשִׁית אוֺנִים‎</span> where <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> is assimilated to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִצְרַיִם‎</span>, or intensive plural
