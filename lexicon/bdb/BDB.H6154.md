 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵ֫רֶב‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Exod.12.38|Exod 12:38]]</sup> ==mixture, mixed company==; — heterogeneous body attached to a people; to Israel [[Exod.12.38|Exod 12:38]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[Neh.13.3|Neh 13:3]] to Egyptians [[Jer.25.20|Jer 25:20]] ([[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] joins to v[[Jer.25.19|Jer 25:19]], so [[BDB#Gie|Gie]]); to Chaldeans [[Jer.50.37|Jer 50:37]] in [[Jer.25.24|Jer 25:24]] strike out <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאֵת ֗֗֗ הָעֶרֶב‎</span> as doublet (so [[BDB#Gie|Gie]], compare [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]); in [[1Kgs.10.15|1Kgs 10:15]] read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲרָב‎</span> (as \|\| [[2Chr.9.14|2Chr 9:14]], so [[BDB#Benz|Benz]] [[BDB#Kit|Kit]] and others); [[Ezek.30.5|Ezek 30:5]] read probably ***id.*** ([[BDB#Co|Co]]). 

 II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵ֫רֶב‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==woof== (as ***mixed***, interwoven, with warp); — [[Lev.13.48|Lev 13:48]] + 8t. 13 (all opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁתִי‎</span> ***warp***), compare [[BDB#GFM|GFM]]<sup>PAOS 1889, clxxviii</sup>.
