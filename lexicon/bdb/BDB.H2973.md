 I. [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָאַל‎</span>] **verb** ==be foolish== (compare I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אול‎</span>) — 

**Niph\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נוֺאֲלוּ‎</span> [[Isa.19.3|Isa 19:3]]; [[Jer.5.4|Jer 5:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְנֹאָ֑לוּ‎</span> [[Jer.50.36|Jer 50:36]] 1 plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נוֺאַלְנוּ‎</span> [[Num.12.11|Num 12:11]] — ***do*** or ***act foolishly:*** — 

**1.** ***shew wicked folly*** = sin <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַטָּאת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲשֶׁר נוֺאַלְנוּ וַאֲשֶׁר חָטָאנוּ‎</span> [[Num.12.11|Num 12:11]] ([[BDB#J|J]]); in [[Jer.5.4|Jer 5:4]] an exhibition of this folly is ascribed to ignorance. 

**2.** ***become fools***, lacking insight and judgment: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נוֺאֲלוּ שְׂרֵי צֹעַן‎</span> [[Isa.19.13|Isa 19:13]] ***the princes of Zoan have become fools*** (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִשְּׁאוּ שָׂרֵי נֹף‎</span>, and, in v[[Isa.19.11|Isa 19:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֱוִילִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׂרֵי צֹעַן‎</span>, etc.); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶדֶב אֶלהַֿבַּדִּים וְנֹאָ֑לוּ‎</span> [[Jer.50.36|Jer 50:36]] ***a sword is against the praters, and they shall become fools*** — be shewn up as such (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָֽתּוּ ׃‎</span>).
