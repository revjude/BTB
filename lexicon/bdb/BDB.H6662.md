$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** of God [[Deut.32.4|Deut 32:4]]; [[Ps.119.137|Ps 119:137]]; [[Ps.129.4|Ps 129:4]]; [[Job.34.17|Job 34:17]], opposed to Pharaoh [[Exod.9.27|Exod 9:27]] ([[BDB#J|J]]); in discrimination [[Jer.12.1|Jer 12:1]]; [[Zeph.3.5|Zeph 3:5]]; [[Ps.7.10|Ps 7:10]]; [[Ps.7.12|Ps 7:12]]; [[Ps.11.7|Ps 11:7]] condemnation [[2Chr.12.6|2Chr 12:6]]; [[Dan.9.14|Dan 9:14]]; [[Lam.1.18|Lam 1:18]]; [[Ezra.9.15|Ezra 9:15]]; [[Neh.9.33|Neh 9:33]] redemption [[Isa.45.21|Isa 45:21]]; [[Ps.116.5|Ps 116:5]] keeping promises [[Neh.9.8|Neh 9:8]] in all his ways [[Ps.145.17|Ps 145:17]].
