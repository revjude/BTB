 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִכְשׁוֺל‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Jer.6.21|Jer 6:21]]</sup> ==a stumbling, means or occasion of stumbling, stumbling-block==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> absolute [[Isa.8.14|Isa 8:14]] + 3 t., <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִכְשֹׁל‎</span> [[Lev.19.14|Lev 19:14]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִכְשׁוֺל‎</span> [[1Sam.25.31|1Sam 25:31]] + 6 t. Ezekiel; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִכְשֹׁלִים‎</span> [[Jer.6.21|Jer 6:21]] + [[Ezek.21.20|Ezek 21:20]] [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] ([[BDB#Co|Co]] and others <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֻכְשָׁלִים‎</span>, see ***below***); — 

**1.** ***stumbling***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוּר מ‎</span> [[Isa.8.14|Isa 8:14]] (fig) ***a rock of stumbling*** (i.e. over which one stumbles); literal <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַרְבֵּה הַמִּכְשֹׁלִים‎</span> [[Ezek.21.20|Ezek 21:20]] ***an abundance of stumblings, falls*** (si vera lectio; [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]. <span class="bdbLangGreek">οἱ ἀσθενοῦντες,</span> apparently reading participle, compare Sm; so [[BDB#Co|Co]] [[BDB#Da|Da]] (as a possibility), i.e. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֻכְשָׁלִים‎</span> **Hoph\`al** ***Participle*** of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כשׁל‎</span> = ***those who have stumbled or been overthrown***). 

**2.** ***means, or occasion of stumbling, stumbling-block***; **a** literal [[Lev.19.14|Lev 19:14]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** figurative of misfortune, calamity Paslm [[Lev.119.16|Lev 119:16]]5; in divine judgment [[Jer.6.21|Jer 6:21]]; [[Ezek.3.20|Ezek 3:20]], as a hindrance to restoration of people [[Isa.57.14|Isa 57:14]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** in ethical sense <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲוֺנָם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Ezek.7.19|Ezek 7:19]] ***a stumbling-block of*** (i.e. occasioning) ***their iniquity***, so [[Ezek.14.3|Ezek 14:3]]; [[Ezek.14.4|Ezek 14:4]]; [[Ezek.14.7|Ezek 14:7]]; [[Ezek.18.30|Ezek 18:30]]; [[Ezek.44.12|Ezek 44:12]] (mostly with reference to idols.). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֵב‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[1Sam.25.31|1Sam 25:31]] ***a stumbling-block of heart*** i.e. ground for remorse.
