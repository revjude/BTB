 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָצַב‎</span>] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָצֵב‎</span> **verb** ==hew, hew out, cleave== (Late Hebrew ***id.***, Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎חֲצַב‎</span>; [[BDB#SI|SI]]<sup>4. 6</sup> participle plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎החצבם‎</span>; compare Phoenician <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מחצב‎</span> = Hebrew ***id.***) — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָצֵב‎</span> [[Isa.5.2|Isa 5:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָֽצְבָה‎</span> [[Prov.9.1|Prov 9:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָצַבְתִּי‎</span> [[Hos.6.5|Hos 6:5]], etc.; ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַּחְצֹב‎</span> [[2Chr.26.10|2Chr 26:10]] 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּחְצֹב‎</span> [[Deut.8.9|Deut 8:9]] ***Infinitive construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַחְצֹב‎</span> [[Jer.2.13|Jer 2:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַחְצוֺב‎</span> [[1Chr.22.2|1Chr 22:2]] ***Participle active*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֹצֵב‎</span> [[Isa.10.15|Isa 10:15]] + 4 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֹצְבִי‎</span> [[Isa.22.16|Isa 22:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֹצְבִים‎</span> [[1Chr.22.2|1Chr 22:2]] + 3 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֹצְבֵי‎</span> [[2Kgs.12.13|2Kgs 12:13]] passive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲצוּבִים‎</span> [[Deut.6.11|Deut 6:11]]; [[Neh.9.25|Neh 9:25]] — 

**1.** ***hew out, (dig)***, wine-vat <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶקֶב‎</span> [[Isa.5.2|Isa 5:2]] sepulchre (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קֶבֶר‎</span>) [[Isa.22.16|Isa 22:16]] (twice in verse); cisterns <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֹּרוֺת‎</span> [[Deut.6.11|Deut 6:11]] (twice in verse); [[2Chr.26.10|2Chr 26:10]]; [[Neh.9.25|Neh 9:25]], compare [[Jer.2.13|Jer 2:13]] of mining <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נחשׁת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מהרריה תַּת‎</span> [[Deut.8.9|Deut 8:9]] ***out of its mountains thou mayst hew out copper***. 

**2. a.** ***hew*** stone [[1Chr.22.2|1Chr 22:2]] metaphor of pillars [[Prov.9.1|Prov 9:1]] (subject wisdom); elsewhere participle, ***hewer*** of stone [[2Kgs.12.13|2Kgs 12:13]]; [[1Chr.22.2|1Chr 22:2]]; [[1Chr.22.15|1Chr 22:15]] (see [[BDB#SI|SI]]<sup>4.6 above</sup>); probably [[1Kgs.5.29|1Kgs 5:29]]; [[2Chr.2.1|2Chr 2:1]]; [[2Chr.2.17|2Chr 2:17]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּהָר‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> see [[BDB#Be|Be]]), compare [[2Chr.24.12|2Chr 24:12]]; [[Ezra.3.7|Ezra 3:7]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** apparently ***hew wood*** [[Isa.10.15|Isa 10:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בוֺ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> (i.e. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּגַרְזֶן‎</span>). 

**3.** metaph. ***hew in pieces*** [[Hos.6.5|Hos 6:5]] figurative of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> attacking people by agency of prophets (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲרַגְתִּים‎</span>); ***divide, cleave*** subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s voice [[Ps.29.7|Ps 29:7]] object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎להבות אשׁ‎</span>, i.e. the thunder of his voice sends forked lightnings (but on text compare [[BDB#Che|Che]] and critical note) **Niph\`al** ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֵחָצְבוּן‎</span> ***be cut, hewn, graven*** (words on rock) [[Job.19.24|Job 19:24]]. **Pu\`al** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֻצַּבְתֶּם‎</span> [[Isa.51.1|Isa 51:1]] figurative, ***hewn*** out of rock (of Israel's origin; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֻקַּר‎</span>). **Hiph\`il** ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַחֲצֶ֫בֶת‎</span> [[Isa.51.9|Isa 51:9]] ***hew in pieces*** = **Qal 3**, figurative of destroying <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רהב‎</span> = Egypt (compare [[Ps.89.11|Ps 89:11]]).
