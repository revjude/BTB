**2.** ***acceptance***, of persons, offering sacrifice, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לרצון לפני יהוה‎</span> [[Exod.28.38|Exod 28:38]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ל‎</span> person ***for acceptance for him before*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>; with suffix person <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לרצנוֺ‎</span> [[Lev.1.3|Lev 1:3]] abbreviated <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְרָצוֺן לָכֶם‎</span> [[Lev.22.20|Lev 22:20]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לרצנכם‎</span> [[Lev.19.5|Lev 19:5]]; [[Lev.22.19|Lev 22:19]]; [[Lev.22.29|Lev 22:29]]; [[Lev.23.11|Lev 23:11]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לצֵּון‎</span> [[Lev.22.21|Lev 22:21]]; [[Isa.56.7|Isa 56:7]]; [[Jer.6.20|Jer 6:20]]; [[Ps.19.15|Ps 19:15]] (of words; compare [[Hos.14.3|Hos 14:3]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎על רצון‎</span> [[Isa.60.7|Isa 60:7]] (read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span>, so Manuscripts, see [[BDB#Gi|Gi]], also [[BDB#Du|Du]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רצון‎</span> [[Mal.2.13|Mal 2:13]].

$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of God, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עשׂה ר‎</span> ***do his will*** [[Ezra.10.11|Ezra 10:11]]; [[Ps.40.9|Ps 40:9]]; [[Ps.103.21|Ps 103:21]]; [[Ps.143.10|Ps 143:10]].
