**1.** literally, ***maid, maid-servant***, as belonging to a mistress [[Gen.16.1|Gen 16:1]]; [[Gen.16.3|Gen 16:3]]; [[Gen.16.6|Gen 16:6]]; [[Gen.16.8|Gen 16:8]] (PJ) [[Gen.29.24|Gen 29:24]]; [[Gen.29.29|Gen 29:29]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) [[Ps.123.2|Ps 123:2]]; [[Prov.30.23|Prov 30:23]]; [[Isa.24.2|Isa 24:2]] even where concubine of master (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָמָה‎</span>) [[Gen.16.2|Gen 16:2]]; [[Gen.16.5|Gen 16:5]] (PJ) [[Gen.25.12|Gen 25:12]]; [[Gen.35.25|Gen 35:25]]; [[Gen.35.26|Gen 35:26]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) [[Gen.30.4|Gen 30:4]]; [[Gen.30.7|Gen 30:7]]; [[Gen.30.9|Gen 30:9]]; [[Gen.30.10|Gen 30:10]]; [[Gen.30.12|Gen 30:12]]; [[Gen.30.18|Gen 30:18]] (all [[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]); less often reference to master [[Gen.29.24|Gen 29:24]]; [[Gen.29.29|Gen 29:29]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[Ruth.2.13|Ruth 2:13]], of concubine [[Gen.32.23|Gen 32:23]], compare [[Gen.33.1|Gen 33:1]]; [[Gen.33.2|Gen 33:2]]; [[Gen.33.6|Gen 33:6]] (all [[BDB#J|J]]); marriageable [[Lev.19.20|Lev 19:20]] ([[BDB#H|H]], compare [[Exod.11.5|Exod 11:5]] not elsewhere in legisl.); in general, especially of menial service (never <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָמָה‎</span>) [[Exod.11.5|Exod 11:5]] ([[BDB#J|J]]) [[1Sam.25.41|1Sam 25:41]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָמָה‎</span>) [[2Sam.17.17|2Sam 17:17]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֶבֶד‎</span>) [[Gen.12.16|Gen 12:16]]; [[Gen.24.35|Gen 24:35]]([[BDB#J|J]]) [[Gen.20.14|Gen 20:14]] ([[BDB#E|E]]) [[Gen.30.43|Gen 30:43]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) [[Gen.32.6|Gen 32:6]] ([[BDB#J|J]]; collective) [[Deut.28.68|Deut 28:68]]; [[1Sam.8.16|1Sam 8:16]]; [[2Kgs.5.26|2Kgs 5:26]]; [[Isa.14.2|Isa 14:2]]; [[Jer.34.9|Jer 34:9]]; [[Jer.34.10|Jer 34:10]]; [[Jer.34.11|Jer 34:11]] (twice in verse); [[Jer.34.16|Jer 34:16]] (twice in verse); [[Joel.3.2|Joel 3:2]]; [[Eccl.2.7|Eccl 2:7]]; [[2Chr.28.10|2Chr 28:10]]; [[Esth.7.4|Esth 7:4]]. 

**2.** figurative in address, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁפְחָֽתְךָ‎</span>, etc., of speaker, in token of humility (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָמָה‎</span>) [[Ruth.2.13|Ruth 2:13]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָמָה‎</span> [[Ruth.3.9|Ruth 3:9]]), [[1Sam.1.18|1Sam 1:18]] (\|\| ***id.*** v[[1Sam.1.16|1Sam 1:16]]), [[1Sam.25.27|1Sam 25:27]], (\|\| v[[1Sam.25.24|1Sam 25:24]]f.), [[1Sam.28.21|1Sam 28:21]]; [[1Sam.28.22|1Sam 28:22]]; [[2Sam.14.6|2Sam 14:6]]; [[2Sam.14.7|2Sam 14:7]]; [[2Sam.14.12|2Sam 14:12]] (twice in verse); [[2Sam.14.15|2Sam 14:15]]; [[2Sam.14.17|2Sam 14:17]]; [[2Sam.14.19|2Sam 14:19]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָמָה‎</span> v[[2Sam.14.15|2Sam 14:15]] v[[2Sam.14.16|2Sam 14:16]]); [[2Kgs.4.2|2Kgs 4:2]]; [[2Kgs.4.16|2Kgs 4:16]] not toward God; but see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָמָה‎</span>.
