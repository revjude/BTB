<small>Aramaic form: [[H2749]]</small>
 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַרְטֹם‎</span>] **noun masculine**<sup>[[Exod.7.22|Exod 7:22]]</sup> ==engraver, writer==, only in derived sense of one possessed of occult knowledge, ***diviner, astrologer, magician*** (probably = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ ם‎</span> + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶרֶט‎</span>, see [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Ol|Ol]]<sup>§ 216, 5</sup> [[BDB#Sta|Sta]]<sup>§§ 295, 327 a</sup>) **\*\***the fact that the word is always applied to Egyptian magicians, except [[Dan.2.2|Dan 2:2]] (late), suggests Egyptian origin, but no agreement among Egyptologists: Harkavy<sup>Jas., 1870, Mars-Avril, 169</sup> proposes <span class="bdbLangGreek">χαρ</span>, ***speak*** + <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***tum***</u></abbr>, ***hidden*** = ***teller of hidden things***; Wied<sup>Samml 44</sup> thinks Hebrew word perhaps imitating an Egyptian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***cher-‰em-t***</u></abbr>, ***he who holds the book***; FC Cook<sup>'Speaker's' Comm. i. 279</sup> proposes <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***cher-tum***</u></abbr> = ***bearer of sacred words***. — only plural absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַרְטֻמִּים‎</span> [[Gen.41.24|Gen 41:24]] + 4 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַרְטֻמִּם‎</span> [[Exod.8.15|Exod 8:15]]; [[Exod.9.11|Exod 9:11]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַרְטֻמֵּי‎</span> [[Gen.41.8|Gen 41:8]] + 2 t.; — 

**1.** ***magicians*** of Egypt [[Gen.41.8|Gen 41:8]] ([[BDB#E|E]]; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲכָמִים‎</span>), v[[Gen.41.24|Gen 41:24]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[Exod.8.3|Exod 8:3]]; [[Exod.8.14|Exod 8:14]]; [[Exod.8.15|Exod 8:15]]; [[Exod.7.11|Exod 7:11]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲכָמִים‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְכַשְּׁפִים‎</span>), v[[Exod.7.22|Exod 7:22]]; [[Exod.9.11|Exod 9:11]] (twice in verse) (all [[BDB#P|P]]).
