 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְכוּשׁ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְכֻשׁ‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Gen.13.6|Gen 13:6]]</sup> ==property, goods==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְכוּשׁ‎</span> [[Num.16.32|Num 16:32]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְכֻשׁ‎</span> [[Gen.14.16|Gen 14:16]]; [[Gen.14.21|Gen 14:21]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְכוּשׁ‎</span> [[2Chr.35.7|2Chr 35:7]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְכֻשׁ‎</span> [[Gen.14.11|Gen 14:11]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְכֻשׁוֺ‎</span> v[[Gen.14.12|Gen 14:12]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְכוּשָׁם‎</span> [[Gen.12.5|Gen 12:5]] +, etc.; — ***property, goods***, Proverbs, Chronicles, Daniel, [[Gen.14|Gen 14]]: 

**1.** general term for movable possessions of all kinds (often specifically including cattle), [[Gen.12.5|Gen 12:5]]; [[Gen.15.4|Gen 15:4]]; [[Num.16.32|Num 16:32]]; [[1Chr.27.31|1Chr 27:31]]; [[1Chr.28.1|1Chr 28:1]]; [[2Chr.31.3|2Chr 31:3]]; [[2Chr.32.29|2Chr 32:29]]; [[Ezra.8.21|Ezra 8:21]]; [[Ezra.10.8|Ezra 10:8]]. 

**2.** used specifically of cattle, flocks, etc. [[Gen.13.6|Gen 13:6]]; [[Gen.31.18|Gen 31:18]]; [[Gen.36.7|Gen 36:7]]; [[Num.35.3|Num 35:3]]; [[2Chr.35.7|2Chr 35:7]]. 

**3.** of stores, utensils, etc. [[Gen.46.6|Gen 46:6]]; [[Ezra.1.4|Ezra 1:4]]; [[Ezra.1.6|Ezra 1:6]], as camp-baggage [[Dan.11.13|Dan 11:13]], especially as booty [[Gen.14.11|Gen 14:11]]; [[Gen.14.12|Gen 14:12]]; [[Gen.14.16|Gen 14:16]] (twice in verse); [[Gen.14.21|Gen 14:21]]; [[2Chr.20.25|2Chr 20:25]]; [[2Chr.21.14|2Chr 21:14]]; [[2Chr.21.17|2Chr 21:17]]; [[Dan.11.24|Dan 11:24]]; [[Dan.11.28|Dan 11:28]].
