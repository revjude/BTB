**2.** ***guilt of sin:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎היה חטא ב‎</span> ***sin*** (the guilt of sin) ***come on one*** [[Deut.15.9|Deut 15:9]]; [[Deut.21.22|Deut 21:22]]; [[Deut.23.22|Deut 23:22]]; [[Deut.23.23|Deut 23:23]]; [[Deut.24.15|Deut 24:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בחטאו מת‎</span> ***in his sin*** (guilt) ***die*** [[Num.27.3|Num 27:3]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אישׁ בחטאו‎</span> ***each for his own sin*** [[Deut.24.16|Deut 24:16]] = [[2Kgs.14.16|2Kgs 14:16]] = [[2Chr.25.4|2Chr 25:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בחטא יחמתני אמי‎</span> [[Ps.51.7|Ps 51:7]] ***in sin*** (condition of sin and guilt) ***my mother conceived me***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אם חטאיכם כשׁנים‎</span> [[Isa.1.18|Isa 1:18]] ***though your sins be like scarlet*** (in guilt); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לא כחטאינו עשׂה לנו‎</span> [[Ps.103.10|Ps 103:10]] ***not according to our sins*** (their guilt) ***hath he done to us***.
