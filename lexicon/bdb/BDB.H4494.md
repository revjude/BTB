 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָנוֺחַ‎</span> **noun masculine** ==resting-place, state, or condition of rest==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Gen.8.9|Gen 8:9]] + 4 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְנוֺחַ‎</span> [[1Chr.6.16|1Chr 6:16]] plural suffix 2 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְנוּחָ֑יְכִי‎</span> [[Ps.116.7|Ps 116:7]] ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:91»§ 91</sup> ***l***); — 

**1.** ***resting-place*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְכַף רֶגֶל‎</span> [[Gen.8.9|Gen 8:9]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), compare [[Deut.28.65|Deut 28:65]]; [[Lam.1.3|Lam 1:3]]; [[Isa.34.14|Isa 34:14]] (compare also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְנֻחָה‎</span> [[Gen.49.15|Gen 49:15]]).

**3.** with force of infinitive (compare Aramaic infinitive with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> prefix) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִמְּנוֺחַ הָאָרוֺן‎</span> [[1Chr.6.16|1Chr 6:16]] ***from the coming to rest of the ark***.
