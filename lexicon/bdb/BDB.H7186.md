**2. a.** ***severe***, of battle [[2Sam.2.17|2Sam 2:17]], wind [[Isa.27.8|Isa 27:8]], servitude (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲבֹדָה‎</span>) [[1Kgs.12.4|1Kgs 12:4]] = [[2Chr.10.4|2Chr 10:4]]; [[Deut.26.6|Deut 26:6]]; [[Isa.14.3|Isa 14:3]]; [[Exod.1.14|Exod 1:14]]; [[Exod.6.9|Exod 6:9]] (both [[BDB#P|P]]); as n.collective <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִרְאִיתָ עַמְּךָ קָשָׁה‎</span> [[Ps.60.5|Ps 60:5]] ***thou hast made thy people see severe things***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קְשֵׁה יוֺם‎</span> [[Job.30.25|Job 30:25]] i.e. one whose time (life) is hard, so [[1Sam.1.15|1Sam 1:15]] (reading <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קְשַׁת יוֺם‎</span> for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רוּחַ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ק‎</span>, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#The|Th]] [[BDB#We|We]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]] and others); ***severe, rough***, of lord (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲדֹנִים‎</span>) [[Isa.19.4|Isa 19:4]], compare [[2Sam.3.39|2Sam 3:39]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> compare [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִמֶּנּוּ‎</span>? see [[BDB#HPS|HPS]]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָלוֺח וְקָשָׁה עַלֿ‎</span> [[Judg.4.24|Judg 4:24]] the hand of the sons of Israel went on ***being more and more severe*** upon Jabin; ***fierce, relentless***, of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s sword [[Isa.27.1|Isa 27:1]] (in figurative); ***rough, rude*** [[1Sam.25.3|1Sam 25:3]] ***severe things***, of language [[Gen.42.7|Gen 42:7]]; [[Gen.42.30|Gen 42:30]] ([[BDB#E|E]]; with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּדֶּר‎</span>), [[1Sam.20.10|1Sam 20:10]]; [[1Kgs.12.13|1Kgs 12:13]] = [[2Chr.10.13|2Chr 10:13]] (all with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָנָה‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאָנֹכִי שָׁלוּחַ אֵלַיִח קָשָׁה‎</span> [[1Kgs.14.6|1Kgs 14:6]] ***I am commissioned with a severe (message) unto thee*** ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:121d»§ 121d</sup>. N.2 [[BDB#Da|Da]]<sup>Synt. §§ 75</sup> (d), 80); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָזוּת קָשָׁה‎</span> [[Isa.21.2|Isa 21:2]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***fierce, intense, vehement***, of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִנְאָה‎</span> [[Song.8.6|Song 8:6]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַזָּה‎</span>; compare [[Gen.49.9|Gen 49:9]]). 

**3.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַםקְֿשֵׁהעֹֿרֶף‎</span> ***a people stiff of neck, stubborn***, [[Exod.32.9|Exod 32:9]]; [[Exod.33.3|Exod 33:3]]; [[Exod.33.5|Exod 33:5]]; [[Exod.34.9|Exod 34:9]] (all [[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]), [[Deut.9.6|Deut 9:6]]; [[Deut.9.13|Deut 9:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָרְפְּךָ הַקָּשֶׁה‎</span> [[Deut.31.27|Deut 31:27]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דַּרְכָּם הַקָּשָׁה‎</span> [[Judg.2.19|Judg 2:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קְשֵׁי פָנִים‎</span> [[Ezek.2.4|Ezek 2:4]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִזְקֵילֵֿב‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קְשֵׁאלֵֿב‎</span> [[Ezek.3.7|Ezek 3:7]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִזְקֵימְֿצַח‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ק‎</span> alone = ***stubborn*** [[Isa.48.4|Isa 48:4]].
