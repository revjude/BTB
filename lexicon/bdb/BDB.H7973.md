**1.** ***missile, weapon***, [[Neh.4.11|Neh 4:11]] and v.[[Neh.4.17|Neh 4:17]] (where <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמַּיִם‎</span> corrupt; read probably <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּימִינוֺ‎</span> or <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּיָדוֺ‎</span>; see Ryle [[BDB#Be|BeRy]] [[BDB#Berthol|Berthol]]), [[2Chr.23.10|2Chr 23:10]] (distinguished from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲנִית‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָנֵן‎</span>); collective [[2Chr.32.5|2Chr 32:5]] and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָבַר בַּשׁ‎</span> [[Job.33.18|Job 33:18]]; [[Job.36.12|Job 36:12]] (***perish by*** or &lt; ***rush upon*** [and perish]), [[Joel.2.8|Joel 2:8]] (see <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּעַד‎</span>). 

**2.** ***sprout, shoot*** (compare [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלוּחָה‎</span>], and √ [[Ps.80.12|Ps 80:12]]; [[Jer.17.8|Jer 17:8]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלָחַיִךְ פַּרְדֵּס‎</span> [[Song.4.13|Song 4:13]] (figurative; meaning ? [[BDB#Perles|Perles]]<sup>Anal. 63</sup> proposes <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁנֵי לְחָיָיִךְ‎</span> ***thy two cheeks***); + probably [[Ezek.31.5|Ezek 31:5]] (read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּשְׁלָחָיו‎</span> [[BDB#Co|Co]] Toy and others, or <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּשִׁלְחֵי‎</span> [with v[[Ezek.31.6|Ezek 31:6]]] [[BDB#Krae|Krae]]).
