**1.** ***lodge, pass the night:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** literally, human subject, often with preposition or adverb of place, [[Gen.19.2|Gen 19:2]]; [[Gen.28.11|Gen 28:11]]; [[Judg.19.13|Judg 19:13]]; [[Judg.19.15|Judg 19:15]]; [[2Sam.17.16|2Sam 17:16]]; [[Job.24.7|Job 24:7]] + often (on [[2Sam.17.8|2Sam 17:8]] see **Hiph\`il**); with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ה‎</span> locative, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְלָן וְשָׁכַב אַ֫רְצָה‎</span> [[2Sam.12.16|2Sam 12:16]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ב‎</span> location + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ב‎</span> temporal <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּלַּיְלָה הַהוּא‎</span> [[Gen.32.14|Gen 32:14]]; [[Gen.32.22|Gen 32:22]]; [[Josh.8.9|Josh 8:9]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַלַּיְלָה‎</span> (accusative temporal), [[Num.22.8|Num 22:8]]; [[Josh.4.3|Josh 4:3]]; [[2Sam.17.16|2Sam 17:16]]; [[2Sam.19.8|2Sam 19:8]]; [[Ruth.3.13|Ruth 3:13]] of the wicked [[Ps.59.16|Ps 59:16]], according to [[BDB#Hup|Hup]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Pe|Pe]] and others; &lt; from II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לון‎</span> [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] [[BDB#Aq|Aq]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Bae|Bae]] and others, compare [[BDB#AV|AV]]; simile of temporary sojourn [[Jer.14.8|Jer 14:8]] (of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> in Israel) 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of animals : wild ox (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְאֵם‎</span>) [[Job.39.9|Job 39:9]] (followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַלאֲֿבוּסֶ֑ךָ‎</span> ), porcupine (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִפֹּד‎</span>) [[Zeph.2.14|Zeph 2:14]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** inanimate subject = ***remain all night :*** of fat of sacrifice [[Exod.23.18|Exod 23:18]] ([[BDB#E|E]]; followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדבֹּֿקֶר‎</span>), passover meal [[Exod.34.25|Exod 34:25]] ([[BDB#P|P]]; followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַבֹּקֶר‎</span>), [[Deut.16.4|Deut 16:4]] (followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַבֹּקֶר‎</span>; all these without local designation), wages of hireling [[Lev.19.13|Lev 19:13]] ([[BDB#H|H]]; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶת‎</span> = ***with*** + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדבֹּֿקֶר‎</span>); of dead body [[Deut.21.23|Deut 21:23]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל‎</span> location); dew [[Job.29.19|Job 29:19]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ב‎</span> location); bunch of myrrh [[Song.1.13|Song 1:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּין‎</span> location) 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** figurative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָלִין בֶּ֑כִי בָּעֶרֶב‎</span> [[Ps.30.6|Ps 30:6]] ***at evening weeping may come to lodge*** (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְלַבֹּקֶר רִנָּה‎</span>). 

**2.** figurative ***abide, remain***, subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַיִן‎</span> ( = ***look upon***), with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ב‎</span> [[Job.17.2|Job 17:2]] of error, [[Job.19.4|Job 19:4]] (with preposition <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵת‎</span> person), strength [[Job.41.14|Job 41:14]] (of crocodile; with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ב‎</span>); of righteousness, [[Isa.1.21|Isa 1:21]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ב‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תָּלִין נַפְשׁוֺ בְטוֺב‎</span> [[Ps.25.13|Ps 25:13]] of hearkening ear [[Prov.15.31|Prov 15:31]], with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּקֶרֶב‎</span>; of thoughts [[Jer.4.14|Jer 4:14]], with id.; absolute, of man, ***continue, endure***, [[Ps.49.13|Ps 49:13]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׂבֵעַ יָלִין‎</span> [[Prov.19.23|Prov 19:23]] ***he shall continue satisfied***. **Hiph\`il** ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָלִין‎</span> ***cause to rest, lodge***, followed by accusative, [[2Sam.17.8|2Sam 17:8]] see [[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>i. 509.</sup> **Hithpalpel** ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִתְלֹנָ֑ן‎</span> [[Job.39.28|Job 39:28]] ***dwell, abide***, of eagle; figurative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִתְלוֺנָ֑ן בְּצֵל שַׁדַּי‎</span> [[Ps.91.1|Ps 91:1]] of one trusting in <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>.
