[<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּאַר‎</span>] **verb Pi\`el** ==beautify, glorify==; — ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֵּאֲרָ֑ךְ‎</span> (object Israel) [[Isa.55.5|Isa 55:5]]; [[Isa.60.9|Isa 60:9]] with accusative of temple; ***Imperfect*** 1 singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲפָאֵר‎</span> [[Isa.60.7|Isa 60:7]] ***Infinitive construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּאֵר‎</span> v[[Isa.60.13|Isa 60:13]]; [[Ezra.7.27|Ezra 7:27]] so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּאֵר עֲנָוִים בִּישׁוּעָה‎</span> [[Ps.149.4|Ps 149:4]]. 

**Hithpa\`el 1.** ***glorify oneself***, with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל‎</span> person: = boast, ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִתְפָּאֵר‎</span> [[Judg.7.2|Judg 7:2]]; [[Isa.10.15|Isa 10:15]] as polite address to king, ***Imperative*** masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִתְפָּאֵר‎</span> [[Exod.8.5|Exod 8:5]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), ***assume the honour over me*** (to decide) ***when***, etc. 

**2.** ***get glory to oneself, be glorified, by means of*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ב‎</span>), of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּיִשְׂרָאֵל יִתְפָּאָ֑ר‎</span> [[Isa.44.23|Isa 44:23]], so 1 singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶתְפָּאָ֑ר‎</span> [[Isa.49.3|Isa 49:3]] of people [by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>], ***Infinitive construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִתְפָּאֵר‎</span> [[Isa.60.21|Isa 60:21]]; [[Isa.61.3|Isa 61:3]].
