**5.** &amp; **6**) ==king of Judah, son of Asa== [[1Kgs.15.24|1Kgs 15:24]]; [[1Kgs.22.2|1Kgs 22:2]] + 21 t. 1 Kings; [[2Kgs.1.17|2Kgs 1:17]]; [[2Kgs.3.1|2Kgs 3:1]]; [[2Kgs.3.7|2Kgs 3:7]]; [[2Kgs.3.11|2Kgs 3:11]]; [[2Kgs.3.12|2Kgs 3:12]] (twice in verse); [[2Kgs.3.14|2Kgs 3:14]]; [[2Kgs.8.16|2Kgs 8:16]] (twice in verse); [[2Kgs.12.19|2Kgs 12:19]]; [[1Chr.3.10|1Chr 3:10]]; [[2Chr.17.1|2Chr 17:1]] + 40 t. 2 Chronicles.
