**1.** adjective ***united*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּאִישׁ אֶחָד חֲבֵרִים‎</span> [[Judg.20.11|Judg 20:11]] ***knit together as one man*** ([[BDB#AV|AV]] [[BDB#RV|RV]]). 

**2. n.** ***associate, fellow:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of children (tribes) of Israel assoc. with Judah as head [[Ezek.37.16|Ezek 37:16]] id. associated with Joseph (Ehpraimitic source) as head v[[Ezek.37.16|Ezek 37:16]] v[[Ezek.37.19|Ezek 37:19]] (in all these read [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲבֵרָיו‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of like rank [[Ps.45.8|Ps 45:8]] 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** of like calling [[Song.1.7|Song 1:7]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** ***worshippers*** (associates, belonging to the society or guild) of idols [[Isa.44.11|Isa 44:11]] (see [[BDB#Che|Che]]; &gt; others ***priests***, [[BDB#Gei|Geig]]<sup>umschr. 121 493</sup> [[BDB#SS|SS]], compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶבֶר‎</span> [[Hos.6.9|Hos 6:9]] [[BDB#Du|Du]] reads <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲבָרִים‎</span> ***enchantments***). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **e.** in general [[Song.8.13|Song 8:13]]. **feminine** ==implying likeness of character== [[Isa.1.23|Isa 1:23]]; [[Prov.28.24|Prov 28:24]]; [[Ps.119.63|Ps 119:63]]. 

**3.** ***companion at a*** particular time [[Eccl.4.10|Eccl 4:10]].
