**2.** ***rule*** of heavenly bodies <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַיּוֺם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִמְשׁ‎</span> [[Gen.1.16|Gen 1:16]] the sun ***for the rule ofthe day***, compare v[[Gen.1.16|Gen 1:16]] also followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span> [[Ps.136.8|Ps 136:8]]; [[Ps.136.9|Ps 136:9]].
