$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of rain-bow cloud [[Gen.9.13|Gen 9:13]]; [[Gen.9.14|Gen 9:14]] (twice in verse); [[Gen.9.16|Gen 9:16]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), [[Ezek.1.28|Ezek 1:28]] (simile).
