 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָפְשִׁי‎</span> **adjective** ==free== (Late Hebrew ***id.***) — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Exod.21.5|Exod 21:5]] + 10 t. + [[Ps.88.6|Ps 88:6]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָפְשִׁים‎</span> [[Isa.58.6|Isa 58:6]] + 4 t. — 

**1.** ***free*** from slavery: of Hebrew bondslave (male or female) set free in 7th year [[Exod.21.2|Exod 21:2]]; [[Exod.21.5|Exod 21:5]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]), [[Deut.15.12|Deut 15:12]]; [[Deut.15.13|Deut 15:13]]; [[Deut.15.18|Deut 15:18]], compare [[Jer.34.9|Jer 34:9]]; [[Jer.34.10|Jer 34:10]]; [[Jer.34.11|Jer 34:11]]; [[Jer.34.14|Jer 34:14]]; [[Jer.34.16|Jer 34:16]] of slave (male or female) set free on account of injury done [[Exod.21.26|Exod 21:26]]; [[Exod.21.27|Exod 21:27]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵאֲדֹנָיו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֶבֶד ח‎</span> [[Job.3.19|Job 3:19]] ***a slave is free from his master*** (i.e. in <abbr title="transliteration"><u>Sh®°ôl</u></abbr>); but <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּמֵּתִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָפְשִׁי‎</span> [[Ps.88.6|Ps 88:6]] ***among the dead*** I am ***free*** (i.e. adrift, cut off from Yahweh's remembrance); more generally, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁלַּח רְצוּצִים חָפְשִׁים‎</span> [[Isa.58.6|Isa 58:6]] ***to let oppressed ones go free***. 

**2.** ***free*** from taxes, obligations, etc. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵת בֵּית אָבִיו יַעֲשֶׂה חָפְשִׁי בְּיְשְׂרָאֵל‎</span> [[1Sam.17.25|1Sam 17:25]] ***his father's house will he make free in Israel***.
