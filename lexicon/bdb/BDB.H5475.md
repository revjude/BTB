$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***divan or circle*** of familiar friends, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּחוּרִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> [[Jer.6.11|Jer 6:11]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎משׂחקים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Jer.15.17|Jer 15:17]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְתֵי סוֺדִ‎</span> [[Job.19.19|Job 19:19]] ***men of my intimate circle***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ׅ‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עמד בסוד (י‎</span> [[Jer.23.18|Jer 23:18]]; [[Jer.23.22|Jer 23:22]] ***in the intimate circle of*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בסוד אלוה‎</span> [[Job.15.8|Job 15:8]] in bad sense, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְרֵעִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> [[Ps.64.3|Ps 64:3]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***assembly, company***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְשָׁרִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> [[Ps.111.1|Ps 111:1]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵדָה‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סוֺד עמי‎</span> [[Ezek.13.9|Ezek 13:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קדשׁים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> [[Ps.89.8|Ps 89:8]] (of angels); in bad sense, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בסוד בא‎</span> [[Gen.49.6|Gen 49:6]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קהל‎</span>). 

**2.** ***counsel***, taken by those in familiar conversation: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***counsel*** itself, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מחשׁבות באין סוד‎</span> [[Prov.15.22|Prov 15:22]] ***thoughts without counsel***; of intimate friendship, [[Ps.55.15|Ps 55:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַמְתִּיק סוֺד‎</span>; in bad sense, of crafty plotting [[Ps.83.4|Ps 83:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעֲרִימוּ סוֺד‎</span>
