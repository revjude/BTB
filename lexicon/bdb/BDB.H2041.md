 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֶ֫רֶס‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==overthrow, destruction==, only [[Isa.19.18|Isa 19:18]] one shall be called <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עִיר הַהֶרֶס‎</span> ***city of destruction***, i.e. (as usually explained) with punning allusion to On-Heliopolis: it shall be called no longer <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַיר הַחֶרֶס‎</span> ***city of the sun***, but <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עיר ההרס‎</span> ***city of destruction***, city whose temples, etc., of the sun have been destroyed; but perhaps &lt; [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">πόλις ασεδεκ</span> = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עִיר הֵצֶּדֶק‎</span> ***city of righteousness***, so [[BDB#Gei|Gei]]<sup>Urschrift 79</sup> [[BDB#Brd|Brd]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] (change intentional ? compare id.); on other views see id. &amp; [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Che|Che]].
