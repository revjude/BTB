 II. [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוּק‎</span>] **verb** ==pour out, melt== (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יצק‎</span>); — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** 3 plural (according to [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] and others) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צָקוּן‎</span> [[Isa.26.16|Isa 26:16]], but see below; ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאֶבֶן יָצוּק נְחוּשָׁה‎</span> [[Job.28.2|Job 28:2]] (read perhaps <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָצוּקוּ‎</span>, see [[BDB#Bu|Bu]]) ***and stone*** (ore, ***men***) ***melt it into copper***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוּר יָצוּק עִמָּדִי פַּלְגֵישָֿׁ֑מֶן‎</span> [[Job.29.6|Job 29:6]] ***the rock used to pour out beside me streams of oil.*** — [[Isa.26.16|Isa 26:16]] (as above) must mean ***they poured out*** (uttered) a whisper (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַחַשׁ‎</span>; i. e. prayer); but form most improbable; Koppe [[BDB#Gr|Gr]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Du|Du]] and others propose <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צְקוֺן לַחַשׁ‎</span> (from assumed <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צָקוֺן‎</span>, I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוק‎</span>) = ***constraint of*** (like that of) ***magic***; &lt; [[BDB#Houb|Houb]] [[BDB#Kit|Kit]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צָֽעֲקוּ בְלַחַץ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וגו‎</span>, or [[BDB#Che|Che]]<sup>Hpt</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צָעַקְנוּ מִלַּחַץ כִּי מוּסָֽרְךָ לָנוּ‎</span>.
