 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרִיאֵל‎</span> 

**1. proper name, feminine** (probably, see [[BDB#Ew|Ew]]) ==Ariel== (***lioness of El***) name applied to Jerusalem [[Isa.29.1|Isa 29:1]]; [[Isa.29.2|Isa 29:2]] (twice in verse); [[Isa.29.7|Isa 29:7]] (so [[BDB#Ges|Ges]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] and others; Tagum [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Brd|Brd]] Or and others say ***hearth of El***, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרִאֵיל‎</span>). 

**2. proper name, masculine** ==a chief man among returning exiles== [[Ezra.8.16|Ezra 8:16]]. 

**3.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרִאֵל מוֺאָב‎</span> [[2Sam.23.20|2Sam 23:20]] = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרִיאֵל מ‎</span> [[1Chr.11.26|1Chr 11:26]] taken by [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#RV|RV]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>Sm</sup> and others as **proper name, masculine**, read ***two sons of Ariel of Moab***; [[BDB#RS|RS]]<sup>Semitic i. 469</sup> compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אראל‎</span> [[BDB#MI|MI]]<sup>12</sup>, ***altar-hearth*** (so [[BDB#Sm|Sm]] &amp; [[BDB#So|So]], and [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>Sm xci</sup>, see below <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרִיאֵיל‎</span>) and translation <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺאָב‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> ***altar-hearths of Moab.*** 

**4.** [[Ezek.43.15|Ezek 43:15]] &amp; [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] v[[Ezek.43.15|Ezek 43:15]] v[[Ezek.43.16|Ezek 43:16]] see below <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרִיאֵיל‎</span>. **\*\***Du<sup>[[Isa.29.1|Isa 29:1]]</sup> (q. v.) makes
