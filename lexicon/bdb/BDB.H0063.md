 II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָבֵל‎</span> 

**noun feminine 1.** meadow (?) [[1Sam.6.18|1Sam 6:18]] [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] but read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶבֶן‎</span> compare v[[1Sam.6.14|1Sam 6:14]] v[[1Sam.6.15|1Sam 6:15]] [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]] [[BDB#We|We]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]. 

**2. proper name, of a location** city in Northern Israel [[2Sam.20.18|2Sam 20:18]], near Beth Maacah v[[2Sam.20.14|2Sam 20:14]] = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָבֵל בֵּית מַעֲכָה‎</span> v[[2Sam.20.15|2Sam 20:15]] (so also v[[2Sam.20.14|2Sam 20:14]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#The|Th]] [[BDB#We|We]] [[BDB#Klo|Klo]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]), [[1Kgs.15.20|1Kgs 15:20]]; [[2Kgs.15.29|2Kgs 15:29]] = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַיִם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[2Chr.16.4|2Chr 16:4]] (= <abbr title="transliteration"><u>Abil el †amµ</u></abbr>, ***wheat-meadow*** northwest of Dan &amp; south of Mutulleh [[BDB#Rob|Rob]]<sup>BR III, 372</sup>). 

**3.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָבֵל הַשִּׁטִּים‎</span> **proper name, of a location** (= ***acacia-meadow***) in lowlands of Moab [[Num.33.49|Num 33:49]] = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁטִּים‎</span> [[Num.25.1|Num 25:1]]; [[Mic.6.5|Mic 6:5]] (= Tel Kefrein (?) [[BDB#Tristr|Tristr]] &amp; Merrill<sup>PESoc. 4th Statement, 89</sup>). 

**4.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָבֵל כְּרָמִים‎</span> **proper name, of a location** (= ***vineyard-meadow***) in Ammon [[Judg.11.33|Judg 11:33]] (see Euseb. <span class="bdbLangGreek">Ἀβελαμπελων</span>). 

**5.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָבֵל מְחוֺלָה‎</span> **proper name, of a location** (= ***dance-meadow***) [[Judg.7.22|Judg 7:22]]; [[1Kgs.4.12|1Kgs 4:12]] Elisha's birthplace [[1Kgs.19.16|1Kgs 19:16]] (see Euseb. <span class="bdbLangGreek">Ἀβελμαελαι</span>). 

**6.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָבֵל מִצְרַיִם‎</span> **proper name, of a location** (= ***meadow of Egypt***, i.e. fertile as Egypt ?) east of Jordan [[Gen.50.11|Gen 50:11]] (where interpreted as if <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵבֶל מ‎</span>, so [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]]; see [[BDB#Di|Di]]).
