 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶצְרוֺן‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶצְרֹן‎</span> **proper name, of a location and person** (compare Sabean <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חצֿרן‎</span> proper name, of a location or tribe [[BDB#Hal|Hal]]<sup>No. 661</sup> [[BDB#DHM|DHM]]<sup>ZMG 1883,15</sup>) — 

**1. proper name, of a location** [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Ασωρων</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Ασερων</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Εσρωμ</span>: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶצְרוֺן‎</span> [[Josh.15.3|Josh 15:3]] place in extreme south of Judah, + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַדָּ֫רָה‎</span> = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲצַראַֿדָּר‎</span> (q. v.) [[Num.34.4|Num 34:4]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קְרִיּוֺת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶצְרוֺן הִיא חָצוֺר‎</span> [[Josh.15.25|Josh 15:25]] another town of Judah in south.

$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶצְרֹן‎</span> son of Reuben [[Gen.46.9|Gen 46:9]]; [[Exod.6.14|Exod 6:14]]; [[Num.26.6|Num 26:6]] = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶצְרוֺן‎</span> [[1Chr.5.3|1Chr 5:3]].
