 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁאַף‎</span> **verb** ==gasp, pant, pant after, long for== (so [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]] [[Job.7.2|Job 7:2]]); — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְשָׁאַף‎</span> consecutive [[Job.5.5|Job 5:5]], 3 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָֽׁאֲפָה‎</span> [[Jer.2.24|Jer 2:24]], 3 plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָֽׁאֲפוּ‎</span> [[Jer.14.6|Jer 14:6]] ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִשְׁאַף‎</span> [[Job.7.2|Job 7:2]], etc.; ***Participle active*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוֺאֵף‎</span> [[Eccl.1.5|Eccl 1:5]] — 

**1.** ***gasp***, as a woman in travail, figurative of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>, [[Isa.42.14|Isa 42:14]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָשַׁם‎</span>); ***pant after, snuff up*** the wind (accusative), of wild ass [[Jer.2.24|Jer 2:24]]; [[Jer.14.6|Jer 14:6]] construction praegn.<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאֶלמְֿקוֺמוֺ שׁ‎</span> [[Eccl.1.5|Eccl 1:5]] ***unto his place he panteth*** (comes panting), of sun under figure of racer. 

**2.** ***gasp*** or ***pant*** with desire [[Ps.119.131|Ps 119:131]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָאַב‎</span>); ***pant after, be eager for***, with accusative [[Job.5.5|Job 5:5]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַמִּים‎</span> p. 855:b), [[Job.7.2|Job 7:2]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְקַוֶּה‎</span>), [[Job.36.20|Job 36:20]] (but obscure in context).
