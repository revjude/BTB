 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּדוּד‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Gen.49.19|Gen 49:19]]</sup> ==band, troop== (as ***making inroads***; others, as a ***division, detachment*** (as severed), but this usually later in Hebrew) — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ג‎</span> absolute [[1Sam.30.8|1Sam 30:8]] + 19 t.; construct [[2Chr.25.9|2Chr 25:9]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּדוּדִים‎</span> [[2Sam.4.2|2Sam 4:2]] + 2 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּדוּדֵי‎</span> [[2Kgs.6.23|2Kgs 6:23]] + 6 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּדוּדָיו‎</span> [[Job.19.12|Job 19:12]]; [[Job.25.3|Job 25:3]] — 

**1.** ***marauding band*** (making ***incursions, inroads***, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גדד‎</span>) [[1Sam.30.8|1Sam 30:8]]; [[1Sam.30.15|1Sam 30:15]] (twice in verse); [[1Sam.30.23|1Sam 30:23]] compare [[1Chr.12.22|1Chr 12:22]], also [[2Chr.22.1|2Chr 22:1]]; [[1Kgs.11.24|1Kgs 11:24]]; [[2Kgs.5.2|2Kgs 5:2]]; [[2Kgs.6.23|2Kgs 6:23]]; [[2Kgs.13.20|2Kgs 13:20]]; [[2Kgs.13.21|2Kgs 13:21]]; [[2Kgs.24.2|2Kgs 24:2]] (4 t. in verse) compare [[2Sam.22.30|2Sam 22:30]] = [[Ps.18.30|Ps 18:30]], &amp; [[Gen.49.19|Gen 49:19]] (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גַּד‎</span> [[Gen.30.11|Gen 30:11]]) [[Hos.6.9|Hos 6:9]]; [[Hos.7.1|Hos 7:1]]; [[Jer.18.22|Jer 18:22]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׂרֵי גִּדוּדִים‎</span> [[2Sam.4.2|2Sam 4:2]], compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּתְּנֵם בְּרָאשֵׁי הַגְּדוּד‎</span> [[1Chr.12.19|1Chr 12:19]] figurative of God's attacking forces, his chastisements [[Job.19.12|Job 19:12]] (compare [[Job.25.3|Job 25:3]] below) 

**2.** ***troop***, of divisions of army of Israel (late) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּדוּדֵי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צְבָא מִלְחָמָה‎</span> [[1Chr.7.4|1Chr 7:4]], compare [[2Chr.26.11|2Chr 26:11]] ***band of Israel***, i.e. troop of mercenaries hired from Israel by Amaziah [[2Chr.25.9|2Chr 25:9]]; [[2Chr.25.10|2Chr 25:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּנֵי הַגְּדוּד‎</span> i.e. ***soldiers of the band*** [[2Chr.25.13|2Chr 25:13]] of army in general <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּמֶלֶךְ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּגְּדוּד‎</span> [[Job.29.25|Job 29:25]], in simile; also [[Mic.4.14|Mic 4:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּתגְּֿדוּד‎</span> of Zion. 

**3.** ***foray, raid*** [[2Sam.3.22|2Sam 3:22]].
