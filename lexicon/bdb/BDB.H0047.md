**1.** men [[Job.24.22|Job 24:22]] (= ***violent***) [[Job.34.20|Job 34:20]]; [[1Sam.21.8|1Sam 21:8]] (read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָרָצִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[BDB#Gr|Gr]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]], compare [[1Sam.22.17|1Sam 22:17]] but [[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>Probebibel</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֹבִיל הָעֲיָרִים‎</span> see ***Id.***<sup>BN 45</sup>; [[BDB#Klo|Klo]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גִּבּוֺר‎</span>), [[Lam.1.15|Lam 1:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַבִּירֵי לֵב‎</span> ***stout of heart*** [[Isa.46.12|Isa 46:12]] (= ***obstinate***) [[Ps.76.6|Ps 76:6]].
