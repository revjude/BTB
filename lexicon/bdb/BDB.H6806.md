 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַ֫עַד‎</span>] **noun masculine**<sup>[[2Sam.6.13|2Sam 6:13]]</sup> ==step, pace==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צָ֑עַד‎</span> [[Prov.30.29|Prov 30:29]] suffix<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַעֲדוֺ‎</span> [[Jer.10.3|Jer 10:3]]; [[Prov.6.9|Prov 6:9]], etc.; plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַעֲדֵי‎</span> [[Job.18.7|Job 18:7]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צְעָדַי‎</span> [[Job.31.4|Job 31:4]], etc.; — 

**1.** literally ***step, pace***, [[2Sam.6.13|2Sam 6:13]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּרְחִיב צַעֲדִי תַּחְתֵּנִי‎</span> [[2Sam.22.37|2Sam 22:37]] = [[Ps.18.37|Ps 18:37]], i.e. givest me firm footing, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵטִיבֵי צ‎</span> [[Prov.30.29|Prov 30:29]] = ***making a good step***, stately in march; [[Lam.4.18|Lam 4:18]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָ֑כֶת‎</span>). 

**2.** ***step, steps***, in figure of course of life, fortune: singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא יֵצַר צ‎</span> [[Prov.4.12|Prov 4:12]] \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֶּרֶךְ‎</span> [[Jer.10.23|Jer 10:23]]; [[Prov.16.9|Prov 16:9]]; [[Job.14.16|Job 14:16]]; [[Job.31.4|Job 31:4]]; [[Job.34.21|Job 34:21]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַעֲדֵי אוֺנוֺ‎</span> [[Job.18.7|Job 18:7]] (compare [[Prov.4.12|Prov 4:12]] above); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִסְפַּרעָֿדַי אַגִּידֶנּוּ‎</span> [[Prov.31.37|Prov 31:37]] of harlot [[Prov.5.5|Prov 5:5]] figurative, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁאוֺל צְעָדֶיהָ יִתְמֹ֑כוּ‎</span>.
