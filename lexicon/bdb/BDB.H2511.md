 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָלָק‎</span> **adjective** ==smooth== — 

**1.** literal <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִישׁ חָלָק‎</span> [[Gen.27.11|Gen 27:11]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), ***smooth man*** (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אישׁ שָׂעִר‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָהָר הֶחָלָק‎</span> [[Josh.11.17|Josh 11:17]]; [[Josh.12.7|Josh 12:7]] ***the bald mountain*** (both [[BDB#D|D]]); southern limit of Josephus' conquest, identification dubious; (1) white cliffs 8 miles south of Dead Sea, and (2) Mt. Mâdara southwest from Pass <abbr title="transliteration"><u>es-‚afâ</u></abbr> are proposed, see [[BDB#Di|Di]].; as substantive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַלְּקֵינַֿחַל‎</span> [[Isa.57.6|Isa 57:6]] (see  I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵלֶק‎</span>, **4**) ***smooth*** (stones) of a brook-bed (ravine, wady), i.e. thou worshippest common round stones (contempt., see [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Di|Di]]). 

**2.** figurative of mouth uttering smooth words <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְחָלָק מִשֶּׁמֶן חִכָּהּ‎</span> [[Prov.5.3|Prov 5:3]] compare [[Prov.26.28|Prov 26:28]]. 

**3.** as substantive = ***flattery*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִקְסָם חָלָק‎</span> [[Ezek.12.24|Ezek 12:24]] ***divination of flattery***.
