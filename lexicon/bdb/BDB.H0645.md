 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵפוֺ‎</span> ([[Job.17.15|Job 17:15]]; [[Job.19.6|Job 19:6]]; [[Job.19.23|Job 19:23]]; [[Job.24.25|Job 24:25]]), elsewhere <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵפוֺא‎</span> (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רִבּוֺ‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רִבּוֺא‎</span> ), **enclitic particle** ==then== (probably from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פּוֺ‎</span>, a particle with a demonstrative force, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פּוֺ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֹּה‎</span> here, with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> prefixed. In the Targs. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פּוֺן‎</span> is used somewhat similarly, e.g. [[Gen.26.10|Gen 26:10]]; [[Num.11.29|Num 11:29]]; [[Isa.1.9|Isa 1:9]]; [[Isa.48.18|Isa 48:18]]; [[Isa.48.19|Isa 48:19]]), — used 

**1.** in connection with interrogatory pronouns or adverbs (like <span class="bdbLangGreek">ἄρα</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">ποτε</span> tandem): <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִי אֵפוֺא‎</span> [[Gen.27.3|Gen 27:3]] who, ***then***, . . . ? v[[Gen.27.37|Gen 27:37]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּלְכָה אֵפוֺא מָה אֶעֱשֶׂה‎</span> and for thee, ***then***, what shall I do, my son ? [[Exod.33.16|Exod 33:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּבַמֶּה יִוָּדַע אֵפוֺא‎</span> and wherein shall it be known, ***then***...? <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַיה אֵפוֺא‎</span> where, then ? [[Judg.9.38|Judg 9:38]]; [[Isa.19.12|Isa 19:12]]; [[Job.17.15|Job 17:15]]; [[Hos.13.10|Hos 13:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֱהִי מַלְכְךָ אֵפוֺא‎</span> [[Isa.22.1|Isa 22:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַהלָּֿח אֵפוֺא‎</span> what is there to thee, ***pray***. . .? 

**2.** in a command or wish : [[2Kgs.10.10|2Kgs 10:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּעוּ אֵפוֺא‎</span> ? know, ***then***; (in apodosis) [[Prov.6.3|Prov 6:3]]; [[Job.19.6|Job 19:6]]; [[Job.19.23|Job 19:23]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִיּיִֿתֵּן אֵפוֺ וְיִכָּֽתְבוּן מִלָּי֑‎</span> would, ***then***, that my words were written ! 

**3.** after <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִם‎</span> [[Gen.43.11|Gen 43:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִםכֵּֿן אֵפוֺא זאֹת עֲשׂו‎</span> if it be so, ***then***, do this, [[Job.9.24|Job 9:24]] if not ***then***, who is it ? [[Job.24.25|Job 24:25]].
