 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֹּ֫בֶר‎</span>] **noun [masculine]** ==pasture== (compare Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎דַּבְרָא‎</span>, Syriac <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhJwAZAIAAAP///wAAACH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAnABkAAAJmhI+py83hokShTgNjzXhf4FmKd2ycZIqJWp4o6FIue9Hr/LVanGMo35vYgjAgbDQi3ZRI2SlFSzVmLGjVNA0qpY+mY8tcLUNWVVjmxJafw06aDHV+k+eH8ej9tEFE+72fBCg42FMAADs=">) — suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּעֵדֶר בְּתוֺךְ הַדָּֽבְרוֺ‎</span> ***as a flock in the midst of its pasture*** [[Mic.2.12|Mic 2:12]] (article with suffix, compare [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:127»§ 127</sup>, 4 b, who attaches <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ו‎</span> as conjunction to following word); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְרָעזּ כְבָשִׂים כְּדֳבְרָם‎</span> ***and lambs shall feed as (in) their pasture*** [[Isa.5.17|Isa 5:17]]. 

 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּבֵּר‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==speaking== (so [[BDB#Ew|Ew]]<sup>§ 156</sup>) [[Jer.5.13|Jer 5:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהַדִּבֵּר אֵין בָּהֶם‎</span>; according to [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#Gf|Gf]] [[BDB#Ki|Ki]] (less probably) verb with article for relation (see [ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּבַר‎</span>] **Pi\`el**): but read rather <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַדָּבָר‎</span>, ***and the word is not in them***.
