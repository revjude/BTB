 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָכְלָה‎</span><sub>17</sub> **noun feminine** ==food, eating== (with some verbal force, compare [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>JPh xi. 217</sup>) only [[BDB#P|P]], &amp; Ezekiel; always <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְאָכְלָה‎</span>, 

**1.** especially in phrase like <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָכֶם יִהְיֶה לְאָ‎</span> [[Gen.1.29|Gen 1:29]]; [[Gen.6.21|Gen 6:21]]; [[Gen.9.3|Gen 9:3]]; [[Lev.11.39|Lev 11:39]] so [[Lev.25.6|Lev 25:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהָיְתָה שַׁבַּת הָאָרֶץ לָכֶם לְאָבְלָה‎</span> compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָתַן לְא‎</span> [[Gen.1.30|Gen 1:30]]; [[Exod.16.15|Exod 16:15]]. 

**2.** ***devouring***, by wild beasts, only figurative of ravaged people [[Ezek.29.5|Ezek 29:5]]; [[Ezek.34.5|Ezek 34:5]]; [[Ezek.34.8|Ezek 34:8]]; [[Ezek.34.10|Ezek 34:10]]; [[Ezek.39.4|Ezek 39:4]], compare [[Ezek.35.12|Ezek 35:12]]. 

**3.** ***consumimg***, in fire [[Ezek.15.4|Ezek 15:4]]; [[Ezek.15.6|Ezek 15:6]], of fire-sacrifice of children [[Ezek.23.37|Ezek 23:37]] figurative of judgment of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ezek.21.37|Ezek 21:37]] (compare also infinitive of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָכַל‎</span>).
