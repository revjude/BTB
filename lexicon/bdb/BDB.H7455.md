 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֹעַ‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==badness, evil==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ר‎</span> absolute [[Gen.41.19|Gen 41:19]] + 4 t. Jeremiah; construct [[Hos.9.15|Hos 9:15]] + 13 t.; — 

**1.** ***badness, bad quality***, of cattle [[Gen.41.19|Gen 41:19]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), figs [[Jer.24.2|Jer 24:2]]; [[Jer.24.3|Jer 24:3]]; [[Jer.24.8|Jer 24:8]]; [[Jer.29.17|Jer 29:17]]. 

**2.** ***wilfulness***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֹעַ לבב‎</span> [[1Sam.17.28|1Sam 17:28]] ([[BDB#Gerber|Gerber]]<sup>161 makes</sup> **2** and **4** [infinitive of] verb). 

**3.** ethical, ***evil, badness***, only in phrase: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֹעַ מעללים‎</span> ***evil of doings*** [[Deut.28.20|Deut 28:20]]; [[Isa.1.16|Isa 1:16]]; [[Jer.4.4|Jer 4:4]]; [[Jer.21.12|Jer 21:12]]; [[Jer.23.2|Jer 23:2]]; [[Jer.23.22|Jer 23:22]]; [[Jer.25.5|Jer 25:5]]; [[Jer.26.3|Jer 26:3]]; [[Jer.44.22|Jer 44:22]]; [[Hos.9.15|Hos 9:15]]; [[Ps.28.4|Ps 28:4]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַע מעללים‎</span> [[1Sam.25.3|1Sam 25:3]]). 

**4.** ***sadness***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּנִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ר‎</span> [[Eccl.7.3|Eccl 7:3]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֵב‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ר‎</span> [[Neh.2.3|Neh 2:3]] (compare [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רָעַע‎</span>] **2**).
