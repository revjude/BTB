 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺב‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Job.32.19|Job 32:19]]</sup> skin-bottle, necromancer, etc. — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[Lev.20.27|Lev 20:27]] + 8 t.; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֹבוֺת‎</span> [[Lev.19.31|Lev 19:31]] + 7 t. — 

**1.** ***skin-bottle***, only plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֹבוֺת חֲדָשִׁים‎</span> ***new*** (***wine-***) ***skins*** [[Job.32.19|Job 32:19]]. 

**2.** ***necromancer***, in phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺב אוֺ יִדְּעֹנִי‎</span> ***necromancer or wizard*** [[Lev.20.27|Lev 20:27]] ([[BDB#H|H]]; usually translated 'a man also or woman that hath a familiar spirit or that is a wizard' [[BDB#RV|RV]]; but better ***a man or a woman, if there should be among them, a necromancer or wizard***; no sufficient reason for exceptional use of phrase here); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְיִדְּעֹנִי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[Deut.18.11|Deut 18:11]]; [[2Chr.33.6|2Chr 33:6]] = [[2Kgs.21.6|2Kgs 21:6]] (where <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וידענים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָאֹבוֺת וְהַיִּדְּעֹנִים‎</span> [[Lev.19.31|Lev 19:31]]; [[Lev.20.6|Lev 20:6]] ([[BDB#H|H]]) [[1Sam.28.3|1Sam 28:3]]; [[1Sam.28.9|1Sam 28:9]]; [[2Kgs.23.24|2Kgs 23:24]]; [[Isa.8.19|Isa 8:19]] (where repres. as chirping &amp; muttering, in practice of their art of seeking dead for instruction, probably ventriloquism, &amp; so [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]) [[Isa.19.3|Isa 19:3]]. 

**3.** ghost, [[Isa.29.4|Isa 29:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהָיָה כְּאוֺב מֵאֶרֶץ קוֺלֵח וּמֵעָפָר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִמְרָתֵח תְּצַפְצֵף‎</span> ***and thy voice shall be as a ghost from the ground and from the dust thy speech shall chirp*** (so Ge [[BDB#MV|MV]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] and others, but chirping might be of necromancer, as [[Isa.8.19|Isa 8:19]]). 

**4.** ***necromancy*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵשֶׁת בַּעֲלַתאֿוֺב‎</span> ***a woman who was mistress of necromancy*** [[1Sam.28.7|1Sam 28:7]] (twice in verse); (&gt; [[BDB#RS|RS]]<sup>JPh xiv, 127 f makes</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺב‎</span> primarily a subterranean spirit, and significant. 

**2.** only an abbreviation of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בעלתא‎</span> etc.); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קסם בּאוֺב‎</span> ***divine by necromancy*** [[1Sam.28.8|1Sam 28:8]], which seems to be interpretation of [[1Chr.10.13|1Chr 10:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁאל בא‎</span> ***inquire by necromancy.*** (In these three examples <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺב‎</span> is usually interpreted as ghost or familiar spirit conceived as dwelling in necromancer; but this apparently not the ancient conception.)
