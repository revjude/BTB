**1.** literally ***cliff, crag*** [[Judg.6.20|Judg 6:20]]; [[Isa.2.21|Isa 2:21]]; [[Isa.7.19|Isa 7:19]]; [[Amos.6.12|Amos 6:12]]; [[Num.20.8|Num 20:8]] (twice in verse); [[Num.20.10|Num 20:10]] (twice in verse); [[Num.20.11|Num 20:11]](compare [[Neh.9.15|Neh 9:15]]; [[Ps.78.16|Ps 78:16]], and contrast the <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוּר‎</span> of [[Exod.17.6|Exod 17:6]]), [[Num.24.21|Num 24:21]]; [[Deut.32.13|Deut 32:13]] (not elsewhere Hexateuch), + 10 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁן הַסּ‎</span> [[1Sam.14.4|1Sam 14:4]] (twice in verse) ***tooth of the crag***, i.e. sharp crag, so [[Job.39.28|Job 39:28]] excavated in cliff as place of burial [[Isa.22.16|Isa 22:16]] abode of wild animals, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעֲלֵיסֿ‎</span> v[[Isa.22.1|Isa 22:1]], compare [[Ps.104.18|Ps 104:18]]; [[Prov.30.26|Prov 30:26]], of birds [[Job.39.28|Job 39:28]]; [[Song.2.14|Song 2:14]] (in figurative), compare [[Jer.48.28|Jer 48:28]], so of Edom [[Obad.1.3|Obad 1:3]] = [[Jer.49.16|Jer 49:16]] compare ([[Num.24.21|Num 24:21]] ***above*** and) [[Isa.42.11|Isa 42:11]] particular cliffs are: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵיטָם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> [[Judg.15.8|Judg 15:8]]; [[Judg.15.11|Judg 15:11]] compare v[[Judg.15.13|Judg 15:13]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎(הָ)רִמּוֺן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> [[Judg.20.45|Judg 20:45]]; [[Judg.20.47|Judg 20:47]] (twice in verse); [[Judg.21.13|Judg 21:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמַּחְלְקוֺת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> [[1Sam.23.28|1Sam 23:28]] compare v[[1Sam.23.25|1Sam 23:25]] see also  II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סֶלַע‎</span>. 

**2.** figurative, especially <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סַלְעִי‎</span> of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> (only Psalms), [[Ps.18.3|Ps 18:3]] = [[2Sam.22.2|2Sam 22:2]]; [[Ps.31.4|Ps 31:4]]; [[Ps.42.10|Ps 42:10]]; [[Ps.71.3|Ps 71:3]] of Assyrian god [[Isa.31.9|Isa 31:9]] (probably; compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוּר‎</span> [[Deut.32.31|Deut 32:31]]; [[Deut.32.37|Deut 32:37]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּבֵד‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צֵל ס‎</span> [[Isa.32.2|Isa 32:2]] (simile of protecting care); figurative of security [[Ps.40.3|Ps 40:3]] (feet on cliff), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְצָדוֺת סְלָעִים מִשְׂגַּבּוֺ‎</span> [[Isa.33.16|Isa 33:16]] symbol of obstinacy <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִזְּקוּ פְנֵיהֶם מִסּ‎</span> [[Jer.5.3|Jer 5:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צְחִיחַ ס‎</span> (***bare cliff***, literally ***glare of*** [***the***] ***cliff***), in figure of openness, flagrancy [[Ezek.24.7|Ezek 24:7]]; [[Ezek.24.8|Ezek 24:8]], of razed city [[Ezek.26.4|Ezek 26:4]]; [[Ezek.26.14|Ezek 26:14]] (only here Ezekiel); in figure of fall of Babylonian [[Jer.51.25|Jer 51:25]].
