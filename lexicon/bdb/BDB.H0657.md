 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָ֑פֶס אֶ֫פֶס‎</span> **noun masculine** properly ==ceasing==, hence 

**1.** ==end, extremity==, only in the poetic phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַפְסֵי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ‎</span> ([[Ps.59.14|Ps 59:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הארץ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span>) ***ends, extreme limits***, of the earth, used especially hyperbolically: [[Deut.33.17|Deut 33:17]]; [[1Sam.2.10|1Sam 2:10]]; [[Mic.5.3|Mic 5:3]]; [[Jer.16.19|Jer 16:19]]; [[Ps.2.8|Ps 2:8]]; [[Ps.59.14|Ps 59:14]]; [[Ps.72.8|Ps 72:8]] (= [[Zech.9.10|Zech 9:10]]); + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּלֿ‎</span> [[Isa.45.22|Isa 45:22]]; [[Isa.52.10|Isa 52:10]]b (= [[Ps.98.3|Ps 98:3]]b) [[Ps.22.28|Ps 22:28]]; [[Ps.67.8|Ps 67:8]]; [[Prov.30.4|Prov 30:4]].


**2.** Expressing ==non-existence==: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** as substantive. (mostly a rare poetic synonym of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַיִן‎</span>): [[Isa.34.12|Isa 34:12]] and all his princes <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִהְיוּ אָ֑פֶס‎</span> shall become ***nought***, ([[Isa.41.29|Isa 41:29]]; [[Isa.41.12|Isa 41:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִהְיוּ כְאַיִן וּכְאֶפֶס‎</span>; [[Isa.40.17|Isa 40:17]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵאֶפֶס וָתֹהוּ‎</span> (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּאַיִן‎</span>) as made of ***nought*** and worthlessness are they accounted by him, [[Isa.41.24|Isa 41:24]] (read<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּעָלְכָם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵאָ֑פֶס‎</span>, \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵאַיִן‎</span> see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶפַע‎</span>); [[Isa.52.4|Isa 52:4]] and Asshur oppressed him <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּאֶפֶס‎</span> ***for nought.*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** as particle of negation, prop. ***cessation of***. . ! (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵין ֗֗֗ ‎</span> ***nought of***...), very rare in prose ([[2Sam.9.3|2Sam 9:3]]), chiefly a poetic synonym of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׃אֵין‎</span> [[Isa.5.8|Isa 5:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַד אֶפֶס מָקוֺם‎</span> till there is ***an end of*** place = till there is ***no*** place (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַָד אֵין‎</span> [[Ps.40.13|Ps 40:13]]), [[Amos.6.10|Amos 6:10]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָֽיִן‎</span> [[Judg.4.20|Judg 4:20]]), [[Deut.32.36|Deut 32:36]] (hence, in prose, [[2Kgs.14.26|2Kgs 14:26]]), [[Isa.45.6|Isa 45:6]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָין‎</span> [[Isa.43.11|Isa 43:11]]) v[[Isa.43.14|Isa 43:14]]; [[Isa.46.9|Isa 46:9]]; [[Isa.54.15|Isa 54:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲנִי וְאַפְסִי עוֺד‎</span> [[Zeph.2.15|Zeph 2:15]]; [[Isa.47.8|Isa 47:8]]; [[Isa.47.10|Isa 47:10]] is probably to be rendered, 'I am, and ***there is none besides***' (so [[BDB#Ges|Ges]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] etc.), the <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> being 'paragogic' as in <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוּלָתי‎</span> etc. ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:90»§ 90</sup>, 3 a [[BDB#Ew|Ew]]<sup>§ 211 b</sup>), compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאֵין עוֺד‎</span> [[Isa.45.5|Isa 45:5]]; [[Isa.45.6|Isa 45:6]]; [[Isa.45.18|Isa 45:18]]; [[Isa.45.21|Isa 45:21]] but according to [[BDB#De|De]] the <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> is suffix of 1 singular 'I am, and ***I am nought*** besides' (i.e. and I am nought besides my all-sufficient self). — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּאֶפֶס‎</span> (like <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּאֵין‎</span>, q. v.) ***without***: [[Prov.14.28|Prov 14:28]]; [[Prov.26.20|Prov 26:20]]; [[Job.7.6|Job 7:6]]; [[Dan.8.25|Dan 8:25]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** as adverb of limitation: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***a***) ***only***: [[Num.22.35|Num 22:35]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַח‎</span> v[[Num.22.20|Num 22:20]]) [[Num.23.13|Num 23:13]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***b***) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶפֶס כִּי‎</span> ***save that, howbeit*** (qualifying a preceding statement): [[Num.13.28|Num 13:28]]; [[Deut.1.5|Deut 1:5]]; [[Judg.4.9|Judg 4:9]]; [[Amos.9.8|Amos 9:8]] (+ [[1Sam.1.5|1Sam 1:5]] [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#We|We]] [[BDB#Sta|Sta]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]). So <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶפֶס‎</span> alone [[2Sam.12.14|2Sam 12:14]] (the following <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּי‎</span> signifying ***because***).
