 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָתַר‎</span> **verb** ==dig, row== (Late Hebrew ***id., bore*** (through), so Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎חֲתַר‎</span> [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]] (rare)) — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Job.24.16|Job 24:16]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָתַרְתִּי‎</span> [[Ezek.12.7|Ezek 12:7]] ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וָאֶחְתֹּר‎</span> [[Ezek.8.8|Ezek 8:8]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַחְתְּרוּ‎</span> [[Ezek.12.12|Ezek 12:12]]; [[Amos.9.2|Amos 9:2]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַּחְתְּרוּ‎</span> [[Jonah.1.13|Jonah 1:13]] ***Imperative*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲתָרֿ‎</span> [[Ezek.8.8|Ezek 8:8]]; [[Ezek.12.5|Ezek 12:5]] — 

**1.** ***dig into*** houses, with accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּתִּים‎</span> [[Job.24.16|Job 24:16]] (of burglary); followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span> into or through a wall [[Ezek.8.8|Ezek 8:8]] (twice in verse) ([[BDB#Co|Co]] strike out on internal grounds), [[Ezek.12.5|Ezek 12:5]]; [[Ezek.12.7|Ezek 12:7]]; [[Ezek.12.12|Ezek 12:12]] metaphor followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בשׁאול‎</span> [[Amos.9.2|Amos 9:2]] ***dig into She'ôl***, i.e. as a refuge. 

**2.** ***row*** (as ***digging*** into the water) [[Jonah.1.13|Jonah 1:13]].
