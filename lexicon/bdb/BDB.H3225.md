**1.** ***right hand:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** literally of man, often opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂמאֹול‎</span> [[Gen.48.13|Gen 48:13]]; [[Gen.48.14|Gen 48:14]] (compare v[[Gen.48.18|Gen 48:18]]), [[Dan.12.7|Dan 12:7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָדַע בֵּיןיְֿמִינוֺ לִשְׂמאֹלוֺ‎</span> [[Jonah.4.11|Jonah 4:11]] ***distinguish between one's right hand and one's left***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁלח ימים‎</span> [[Judg.5.26|Judg 5:26]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יד‎</span>); as holding the lot [[Ezek.21.27|Ezek 21:27]] also of right hand as skilful [[Ps.137.5|Ps 137:5]] as receiving bribes [[Ps.26.10|Ps 26:10]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָדַיִן‎</span>); used in false swearing <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְמִינָם וְמִין‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֶׁקֶר‎</span> [[Ps.144.8|Ps 144:8]]; [[Ps.144.11|Ps 144:11]] ***their right hand is a right hand of falsehood***; oft also with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַד‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַדיִֿמִינוֺ‎</span> [[Gen.48.17|Gen 48:17]] ([[BDB#E|E]]) ***hand of his right = his right hand*** [[Judg.7.20|Judg 7:20]]; [[2Sam.20.9|2Sam 20:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִטֵּר יַדיְֿמִינוֺ‎</span> [[Judg.3.15|Judg 3:15]]; [[Judg.20.16|Judg 20:16]] ***bound as to one's right hand***, i.e. left-handed. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** figurative, of right hand as held by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Isa.41.13|Isa 41:13]]; [[Isa.45.1|Isa 45:1]] so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַדיֿמיני‎</span> [[Ps.73.23|Ps 73:23]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** ***right hand of*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>, as instrument of delivering Israel [[Exod.15.6|Exod 15:6]] (twice in verse); [[Exod.15.12|Exod 15:12]] +, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּגְכוּרוֺת יֵשַׁע יְמִינוֺ‎</span> [[Ps.20.7|Ps 20:7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁנוֺת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְמִין עֶלְיוֺן‎</span> [[Ps.77.11|Ps 77:11]] \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זְרוֺעַ‎</span> [[Ps.98.1|Ps 98:1]] as acquiring the temple-site [[Ps.78.54|Ps 78:54]] as holding his servant [[Ps.18.36|Ps 18:36]] (line omitted in \|\| [[2Sam.22.36|2Sam 22:36]]), compare [[Ps.63.9|Ps 63:9]] &amp; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּימִין צִדְקִי‎</span> [[Isa.41.10|Isa 41:10]] as finding, lighting upon (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מצא‎</span>) his enemies [[Ps.21.9|Ps 21:9]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יד‎</span>); as full of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צֶדֶק‎</span> [[Ps.48.11|Ps 48:11]] as dispensing blessings [[Ps.16.11|Ps 16:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נעימות בימינך‎</span> (compare [[Prov.3.16|Prov 3:16]] of wisdom, personified); used in divine oath is [[Ps.62.8|Ps 62:8]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זְרוֺעַ‎</span>). 

**2.** of situation on, or direction toward, ***the right:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of situation, with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִימִינָם וּמְשְּׂמאֹלָם‎</span> [[Exod.14.22|Exod 14:22]]; [[Exod.14.29|Exod 14:29]] a wall ***on their right hand and on their left hand***; similarly [[2Sam.16.6|2Sam 16:6]]; [[1Kgs.7.39|1Kgs 7:39]]; [[1Kgs.7.49|1Kgs 7:49]] = [[2Chr.3.17|2Chr 3:17]]; [[2Chr.4.6|2Chr 4:6]]; [[1Kgs.22.19|1Kgs 22:19]] (= [[2Chr.18.18|2Chr 18:18]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎על‎</span>), [[2Chr.4.7|2Chr 4:7]]; [[2Chr.4.8|2Chr 4:8]]; [[Ps.91.7|Ps 91:7]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִצִּדְּךָ‎</span>); for protection [[Ps.16.8|Ps 16:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מימין ל‎</span> [[2Kgs.23.13|2Kgs 23:13]]; [[Ezek.10.3|Ezek 10:3]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַתֵּשֶׁב לִימִינוֺ‎</span> [[1Kgs.2.19|1Kgs 2:19]], so [[Ps.110.1|Ps 110:1]]; [[Ps.45.10|Ps 45:10]]; [[Ps.109.31|Ps 109:31]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַלֿ‎</span> [[Zech.3.1|Zech 3:1]]; [[1Chr.6.24|1Chr 6:24]]; [[2Chr.18.18|2Chr 18:18]] (= [[1Kgs.22.19|1Kgs 22:19]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מן‎</span>), [[Neh.8.4|Neh 8:4]]; [[Job.30.12|Job 30:12]]; [[Ps.109.6|Ps 109:6]] compare [[Ps.110.5|Ps 110:5]] without preposition <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְמִין הָעִיר‎</span> [[2Sam.24.5|2Sam 24:5]]; [[Job.23.9|Job 23:9]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of direction toward, literal <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָטָה ימין‎</span> [[Num.20.17|Num 20:17]]; [[Num.22.26|Num 22:26]] (both [[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סוּר ימין‎</span> [[Deut.2.27|Deut 2:27]]; [[1Sam.6.12|1Sam 6:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּרַץ ימין ושׂ‎</span> [[Isa.54.3|Isa 54:3]] also (verb <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִפָּרֶד‎</span> in prev. clause) [[Gen.13.9|Gen 13:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַבֵּיט ימין‎</span> [[Ps.142.5|Ps 142:5]] and ***looking*** is implied also in [[Ezek.1.10|Ezek 1:10]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶלֿ‎</span>; opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵהַשְּׂמאֹול‎</span>); with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נטה (לָלֶכֶת) על הַיָּמִין‎</span> [[2Sam.2.19|2Sam 2:19]]; [[2Sam.2.21|2Sam 2:21]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פנה עליֿמין‎</span> [[Gen.24.49|Gen 24:49]] followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶל‎</span>, of course (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הלך‎</span>) of boundary [[Josh.17.1|Josh 17:1]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַיָמִין מֵעַל לַחוֺמָה‎</span> [[Neh.12.31|Neh 12:31]] (verb omitted, ***went) to the right above the wall***; figurative of moral deviation, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סוּר מִןהַֿדָּבָריָ֗֗֗מִין‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּשְׂמאֹול‎</span> [[Deut.17.11|Deut 17:11]], so [[Deut.5.29|Deut 5:29]]; [[Deut.17.20|Deut 17:20]]; [[Deut.28.14|Deut 28:14]]; [[Josh.1.7|Josh 1:7]]; [[Josh.23.6|Josh 23:6]]; [[2Kgs.22.2|2Kgs 22:2]] = [[2Chr.34.2|2Chr 34:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נטה ימין ושׂ‎</span> [[Prov.4.27|Prov 4:27]] also (very late) of ***the right*** as morally good, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֵב חָכָן לִימִינוֺ‎</span> (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂמאל‎</span>) [[Eccl.10.2|Eccl 10:2]] [[BDB#De|De]] Reuss [[BDB#Now|Now]].
