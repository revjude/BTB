 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּֽשְׂמִת‎</span> **proper name, feminine** (***perfume ***?) — 

**1.** Hittite woman, a wife of Esau [[Gen.26.34|Gen 26:34]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); called daughter of Ishmael, and sister of Nebaioth [[Gen.36.3|Gen 36:3]] (but due probably to R; this daughter of Ishmael is <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַחֲלַת‎</span> in [[Gen.28.9|Gen 28:9]] [[BDB#P|P]]); see also [[Gen.36.4|Gen 36:4]]; [[Gen.36.10|Gen 36:10]]; [[Gen.36.13|Gen 36:13]]; [[Gen.36.17|Gen 36:17]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]) ([[BDB#Sam|⅏]] has <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מחלת‎</span> throughout [[Gen.36|Gen 36]]). 

**2.** daughter of Solomon, wife of Ahimaaz [[1Kgs.4.15|1Kgs 4:15]].
