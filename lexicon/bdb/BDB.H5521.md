 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סֻכָּה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==thicket, booth== (properly of ***inter-woven*** boughs compare [[Neh.8.15|Neh 8:15]]); — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> [[Isa.1.8|Isa 1:8]] +; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סֻכָּת‎</span> [[Amos.9.11|Amos 9:11]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סֻכָּתוֺ‎</span> [[Job.36.29|Job 36:29]]; [[Ps.18.12|Ps 18:12]], and so read \|\| [[2Sam.22.12|2Sam 22:12]] (for [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סֻכּוֺת‎</span>; [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Hup|Hup-Now]] [[BDB#HPS|HPS]]); usually plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סֻכֹּת‎</span> [[Gen.33.17|Gen 33:17]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סֻכּוֺת‎</span> [[2Sam.11.11|2Sam 11:11]] +; — 

**1.** ***thicket***, lurking-place of lions [[Job.38.40|Job 38:40]] (compare [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סֹךְ‎</span>] 

**1.**). 

**2.** ***booth***, rude or temporary shelter, for cattle [[Gen.33.17|Gen 33:17]] ([[BDB#J|J]]; distinct from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּיִת‎</span>), but also for warriors in the field [[2Sam.11.11|2Sam 11:11]]; [[1Kgs.20.12|1Kgs 20:12]]; [[1Kgs.20.16|1Kgs 20:16]] for watchers in vineyards [[Isa.1.8|Isa 1:8]] (simile) [[Job.27.18|Job 27:18]] (simile of frailty), for man's shelter from sun [[Jonah.4.5|Jonah 4:5]], compare (figurative) [[Isa.4.6|Isa 4:6]]; [[Ps.31.21|Ps 31:21]], probably also [[Ps.27.5|Ps 27:5]] (see [ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סֹךְ‎</span>]); in poetry of fallen house (dynasty) of David [[Amos.9.11|Amos 9:11]] of clouds as (temporary) enclosure ([[BDB#AV|AV]] 'pavilion') of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> in storm [[Ps.18.12|Ps 18:12]] = [[2Sam.22.12|2Sam 22:12]]; [[Job.36.29|Job 36:29]]. 

**3.** specifically of ***booths***, made of boughs, in which people lived at harvest-feast [[Lev.23.42|Lev 23:42]] (twice in verse); [[Lev.23.43|Lev 23:43]] ([[BDB#H|H]]), [[Neh.8.14|Neh 8:14]]; [[Neh.8.15|Neh 8:15]]; [[Neh.8.16|Neh 8:16]]; [[Neh.8.17|Neh 8:17]] (twice in verse), hence called <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַג הַסֻכּוֺת‎</span> [[Deut.16.13|Deut 16:13]]; [[Deut.16.16|Deut 16:16]]; [[Deut.31.10|Deut 31:10]]; [[Lev.23.34|Lev 23:34]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), [[Zech.14.16|Zech 14:16]]; [[Zech.14.18|Zech 14:18]]; [[Zech.14.19|Zech 14:19]]; [[2Chr.8.13|2Chr 8:13]]; [[Ezra.3.4|Ezra 3:4]].
