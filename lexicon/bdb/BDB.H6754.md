 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צֶ֫לֶם‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Ezek.16.17|Ezek 16:17]]</sup> ==image== (something ***cut out***, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּסֶל‎</span>; [[BDB#Nö|Nö]] 'Schnitzbild'); — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צ‎</span> absolute [[Ps.39.7|Ps 39:7]], construct [[Gen.1.27|Gen 1:27]] +; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַלְמוֺ‎</span> v[[Gen.1.27|Gen 1:27]]; [[Gen.5.3|Gen 5:3]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַלְמֵנוּ‎</span> [[Gen.1.26|Gen 1:26]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַלְמָם‎</span> [[Ps.73.20|Ps 73:20]] plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַלְמֵי‎</span> [[1Sam.6.5|1Sam 6:5]] (twice in verse) +, suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צְלָמָיו‎</span> [[2Kgs.11.18|2Kgs 11:18]]; [[2Chr.23.17|2Chr 23:17]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַלְמֵיכֶם‎</span> [[Amos.5.26|Amos 5:26]] — 

**1.** ***images*** of tumours and mice (of gold) [[1Sam.6.5|1Sam 6:5]] (twice in verse); [[1Sam.6.11|1Sam 6:11]] especially of heathen gods [[Amos.5.26|Amos 5:26]] (text dubious; strike out [[BDB#We|We]] as gloss, compare [[BDB#GASm|GASm]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]), [[2Kgs.11.18|2Kgs 11:18]] = [[2Chr.23.17|2Chr 23:17]] (both with verb <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁבְּרוּ‎</span>), [[Ezek.7.20|Ezek 7:20]], so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זָכָר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צ‎</span> [[Ezek.16.17|Ezek 16:17]] (i.e. in male form, according to figurative of harlotry for idolatry); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַלְמֵי מַסֵכֹתָם‎</span> [[Num.33.52|Num 33:52]] ***their molten images***; of painted pictures of men [[Ezek.23.14|Ezek 23:14]]. 

**2.** ***image, likeness***, of resemblance, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּצ‎</span> (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּרָא‎</span>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָשָׂה‎</span>, of God's making man in his own image, [[Gen.1.26|Gen 1:26]](\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּדְמוּתֵנוּ‎</span>), v[[Gen.1.27|Gen 1:27]] v[[Gen.1.27|Gen 1:27]]; [[Gen.9.6|Gen 9:6]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּצ‎</span> [[Gen.5.3|Gen 5:3]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּדְמוּתוֺ‎</span>; all [[BDB#P|P]]). 

**3.** figurative = ***mere, empty, image, semblance***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּצ‎</span> [[Ps.39.7|Ps 39:7]] ***as*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ב‎</span> essentiae) ***a*** (mere) ***semblance man walks about***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַלְמָם תִּבְזֶה‎</span> [[Ps.73.20|Ps 73:20]] ***thou wilt despise their semblance***.
