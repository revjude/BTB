**Qal** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָֽרְכוּ‎</span> [[Gen.26.8|Gen 26:8]] ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַאַרְכוּ‎</span> [[Ezek.12.22|Ezek 12:22]] 3 feminine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַתֶּאֱרַכְנָה‎</span> [[Ezek.31.5|Ezek 31:5]] (strike out [[BDB#B|B]] [[BDB#Co|Co]]) — ***be long***, subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַיָמִים‎</span> (i.e. a long time passed [[Gen.26.8|Gen 26:8]] ([[BDB#J|J]]); compare Assyrian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***urriku ûmî***</u></abbr>, ***days grew long***, Creation Tablet<sup>a</sup> see [[BDB#COT|COT]] [[Gen.1.1|Gen 1:1]]); of delayed fulfillment of prophecy [[Ezek.12.22|Ezek 12:22]] subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פּאֹרֹת‎</span> [[Ezek.31.5|Ezek 31:5]] (but compare above) **Hiph\`il** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֶאֱרִיךְ‎</span> [[Prov.19.11|Prov 19:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהַאֲרַכְתָּ֫‎</span> [[Deut.22.7|Deut 22:7]], etc.; ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַאֲרִיךְ‎</span> [[Deut.17.20|Deut 17:20]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַאֲרִיכוּ‎</span> [[Deut.25.15|Deut 25:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַאֲרִכוּן‎</span> [[Exod.20.12|Exod 20:12]] compare [[Deut.5.16|Deut 5:16]]; [[Deut.6.2|Deut 6:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּאֲרִיכֻן‎</span> [[Deut.4.26|Deut 4:26]]; [[Deut.30.18|Deut 30:18]], etc.; ***Imperative*** feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַאֲרִיכִי‎</span> [[Isa.54.2|Isa 54:2]]: ***Infinitive*** construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַאֲרִיךְ‎</span> [[Num.9.19|Num 9:19]]; [[Num.9.22|Num 9:22]] ***Participle <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַאֲרִיךְ‎</span> [[Eccl.7.15|Eccl 7:15]]; [[Eccl.8.12|Eccl 8:12]]***; — 

**1.** transitive ***prolong***, 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***a***) object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָמִים‎</span> (i.e. live long) [[Deut.4.26|Deut 4:26]]; [[Deut.4.40|Deut 4:40]]; [[Deut.5.30|Deut 5:30]]; [[Deut.11.9|Deut 11:9]]; [[Deut.17.20|Deut 17:20]]; [[Deut.22.7|Deut 22:7]]; [[Deut.30.18|Deut 30:18]]; [[Deut.32.47|Deut 32:47]]; [[Josh.24.31|Josh 24:31]]= [[Judg.2.7|Judg 2:7]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַחֲרֵי‎</span> = ***survive***), [[Prov.28.16|Prov 28:16]]; [[Isa.53.10|Isa 53:10]]; [[Eccl.8.13|Eccl 8:13]] also (late) without <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ימים‎</span> [[Eccl.7.15|Eccl 7:15]]; [[Eccl.8.12|Eccl 8:12]] 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***b***) ***id.*** [[1Kgs.3.14|1Kgs 3:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהַאֲרַכְתִּי אֶתיָֿמֶיךָ‎</span> ***I will prolong thy days; (c) postpone*** anger [[Isa.48.9|Isa 48:9]] compare [[Prov.19.11|Prov 19:11]] (i.e. shew oneself slow to anger); see also [[Job.6.11|Job 6:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַאֲרִיךְ נַפְשִׁי‎</span> i.e. be patient; 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***d***) literal (but in figure) ***make long*** furrows (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ל‎</span>) [[Ps.129.3|Ps 129:3]] tentcords [[Isa.54.2|Isa 54:2]] tongue [[Isa.57.4|Isa 57:4]] (stretch out in mockery). 

**2.** intransitive ***grow long, continue long*** (i.e. ***display*** length or continuance), subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָמִים‎</span> [[Exod.20.12|Exod 20:12]] = [[Deut.5.16|Deut 5:16]]; [[Deut.6.2|Deut 6:2]]; [[Deut.25.15|Deut 25:15]] ***tarry long*** [[Num.9.19|Num 9:19]]; [[Num.9.22|Num 9:22]] (subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֶעָנָן‎</span>), ***last*** (continue) ***long***, (subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֵּן‎</span> [[Prov.28.2|Prov 28:2]] ***be long*** (literally) of staves of ark [[1Kgs.8.8|1Kgs 8:8]]; [[2Chr.5.9|2Chr 5:9]].
