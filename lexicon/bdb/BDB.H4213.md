 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִזְעָר‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==a little, a trifle, a few==. 

**1.** of time <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עוֺד מְעַט מִזְעָ֑ר‎</span> [[Isa.10.25|Isa 10:25]]; [[Isa.29.17|Isa 29:17]] ***yet a trifle, a little*** = a very little while. 

**2.** of number <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעַט מִזְעָר שְׁאָר‎</span> [[Isa.16.14|Isa 16:14]] ***a remnant, a very few*** (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לוֺא כַּ בִּיר‎</span>). In [[BDB#Isa|Isa]]<sup>3</sup> without <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מעט‎</span>: — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִזְעָֽר ׃ונשׁאר אנושׁ‎</span> [[Isa.24.6|Isa 24:6]].
