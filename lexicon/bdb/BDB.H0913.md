 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּדִיל‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==alloy, tin, dross== (originally ***that which is separated*** from precious metal; compare [[BDB#Plin|Plin]]<sup>Hist. Nat. xxiv. 16, xxxiii. 9</sup>) — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בּ‎</span> absolute [[Num.31.22|Num 31:22]] + 4 t.; Plural suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּדִילָ֑יִךְ‎</span> [[Isa.1.25|Isa 1:25]] — 

**1.** ***alloy***, [[Isa.1.25|Isa 1:25]] figurative of evil of Jerusalem, which <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> will remove (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵסִיר‎</span>; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סִגָ֑יִךְ‎</span>). 

**2.** ***tin*** (***plumbum album***) [[Num.31.22|Num 31:22]] ([[BDB#P|P]]; + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זָהָב‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֶּסֶף‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְחשֶׁת‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּרְזֶל‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֹפָ֑רֶת‎</span>); figurative of Israel [[Ezek.22.18|Ezek 22:18]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְחשֶׁת‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּרְזֶל‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עוֺפֶרֶת‎</span>); in simile v[[Ezek.22.20|Ezek 22:20]] (+ id. + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֶּסֶף‎</span>); as article of commerce brought to Tyre from Tarshish [[Ezek.27.12|Ezek 27:12]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֶּסֶף‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּרְזֶל‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עוֺפֶרֶת‎</span>). 

**3.** ***plummet***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָאֶבֶן הַבְּדִיל‎</span> (appositive) [[Zech.4.10|Zech 4:10]].
