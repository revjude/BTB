[<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֻמָּה‎</span>] **noun feminine** juxtaposition, but only in stative construct with force a **preposition** ==close by, side by side with, parallel to, agreeing with, corresponding to==; — stative with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֻמַּת‎</span>, except [[Eccl.5.15|Eccl 5:15]] always with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span>, suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְעֻמָּתוֺ‎</span>, once plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְעֻמּוֺת‎</span> [[Ezek.45.7|Ezek 45:7]] — 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***close by, side by side with:*** [[Exod.25.27|Exod 25:27]] ([[Exod.37.14|Exod 37:14]]), [[Exod.28.27|Exod 28:27]] ([[Exod.39.20|Exod 39:20]]), [[Lev.3.9|Lev 3:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְעֻמַּת הֻעָצֶה יְסִירֶנָּה‎</span> shall take it away ***close by*** the backbone; especially of what is ***parallel***, [[Ezek.42.7|Ezek 42:7]] the wall ***alongside of*** the chambers; of the contiguous portions in Ezekiel's division of the land, ***alongside of*** each other, [[Ezek.45.6|Ezek 45:6]]; [[Ezek.45.7|Ezek 45:7]]; [[Ezek.48.13|Ezek 48:13]]; [[Ezek.48.18|Ezek 48:18]] (twice in verse); [[Ezek.48.21|Ezek 48:21]] of movement in parallel lines, [[2Sam.16.13|2Sam 16:13]] Shimei was going along <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְעֻמָּתוֺ‎</span> ***parallel with*** him, v[[2Sam.16.13|2Sam 16:13]]; [[Ezek.1.20|Ezek 1:20]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהָאוֺפַנִּים יִנָָּֽשְׂאוּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְעֻמָּתָם‎</span>, v[[Ezek.1.21|Ezek 1:21]]; [[Ezek.3.13|Ezek 3:13]]; [[Ezek.10.19|Ezek 10:19]]; [[Ezek.11.22|Ezek 11:22]]; [[Ezek.3.8|Ezek 3:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָתַתִּי פָנֶיךָ חֲזָקִים לְעֻמַּת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פְּנֵיהֶם‎</span> ***by the side of*** their face (which, as antagonism is implied, = ***against***, [[BDB#RV|RV]]), v[[Ezek.3.8|Ezek 3:8]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***agreeing with, corresponding to*** (a common result of juxtaposition), [[Exod.38.18|Exod 38:18]] a screen five cubits high <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לעמת קלעי החצר‎</span> ***agreeing with*** ([[BDB#RV|RV]] ***answerable unto***) the hangings of the court (in height), [[Ezek.40.18|Ezek 40:18]] probably also [[1Chr.26.16|1Chr 26:16]]; [[Neh.12.24|Neh 12:24]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִשְׁמָר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִשְׁמָר ל‎</span> ward ***corresponding to*** ward. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** ***correspondingly to***, [[1Chr.24.31|1Chr 24:31]] they also cast lots <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְעֻמַּת אֲחֵיהֶם‎</span> ***correspondingly to*** their brethren, v<sup>b</sup> the head, ***correspondinly to*** his younger brother, [[1Chr.26.12|1Chr 26:12]] to these . . . belonged charges ***correspondingly to*** (in common with) their brethren. **\*\***[[Eccl.7.14|Eccl 7:14]] ***side by side with, as well as***. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** before a sentence ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:155n»§ 155n</sup>), [[1Chr.25.8|1Chr 25:8]] (strangely) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְעֻמַּת כַּקָּטֹן כַּגָּדוֺל‎</span> (see [[BDB#Ke|Ke]]) ***correspondingly to*** (the principle) ***of*** as the small so the great; [[Eccl.5.15|Eccl 5:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּלעֻֿמַּת שֶׁכָּא‎</span> ***quite exactly as*** he came, so shall he go (but Lambert<sup>RŠJ xxxi. 47</sup>, Rahlfs<sup>Th. Lt. -z. 1896, 587</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּלְעֻמַּת‎</span>).
