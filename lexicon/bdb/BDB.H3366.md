**1.** ***preciousness:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** = ***precious*** (***costly***) ***things*** (collective) [[Jer.20.5|Jer 20:5]] (om. [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֹסֶן וִיקָר‎</span> [[Ezek.22.25|Ezek 22:25]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּליְֿקָר‎</span> [[Job.28.10|Job 28:10]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּלִי יְקָר‎</span> [[Prov.20.15|Prov 20:15]] ***a jewel of preciousness*** = ***precious jewel*** (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זָהָב‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פְּנִינִים‎</span>). 

**2.** ***price*** [[Zech.11.13|Zech 11:13]], see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָקַר‎</span> 

**2, 3.** ***honour***, [[Ps.49.13|Ps 49:13]]; [[Ps.49.21|Ps 49:21]] elsewhere only Esther: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְקָר תִּפְאֶרֶת גְּדוּלָּתוֺ‎</span> [[Esth.1.4|Esth 1:4]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּבוֺד מַלְכוּתוֺ‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִתְּנוּ יְקָר לְ‎</span> v[[Esth.1.20|Esth 1:20]] all wives ***give honour to*** their lords; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַהנַּֿעֲשָׂה יְקָר וּגְדוּלָּה לְמָרְדְּכַי‎</span> [[Esth.6.3|Esth 6:3]], with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עשׂה לְ‎</span> also v[[Esth.6.6|Esth 6:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַיְּהוּדִים הָֽיְתָה אוֺרָה וְשִׂמְחָה וְשָׂשׂן וִיקָר‎</span> [[Esth.8.16|Esth 8:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎(הָ)אִישׁ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲשֶׁר הַמֶּלֶךְ חָפֵץ בִּיקָרוֺ‎</span> ***the man in whose honour the king delighteth*** [[Esth.6.6|Esth 6:6]]; [[Esth.6.7|Esth 6:7]]; [[Esth.6.9|Esth 6:9]] (twice in verse); [[Esth.6.11|Esth 6:11]].
