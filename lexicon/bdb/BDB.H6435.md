 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּןֿ‎</span><sub>133</sub> (always with Makkeph **\*\***except [[Gen.38.23|Gen 38:23]]; [[Gen.44.34|Gen 44:34]]; [[Deut.7.25|Deut 7:25]]; [[Deut.32.27|Deut 32:27]] [not Gi], [[Isa.27.3|Isa 27:3]]; [[Prov.25.8|Prov 25:8]]: Mandelkern<sup>Conc. under the word</sup>.) **conjunction** (averting, or deprecating), ==lest== (origin dubious: [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] [[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>ii. 334</sup> from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּנָה‎</span>, properly substantive construct in accusative ***for the aversion of***; but <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָנָה‎</span> is intransitive: [[BDB#Nö|Nö]]<sup>M. 474</sup> compares the enclitic particle <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פּוֺן‎</span> in [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]], ***then*** <span class="bdbLangGreek">ἄν</span>, 'etwa,' thinking that a particle with the meaning 'es möchte etwa,' spoken in a tone of alarm, might readily acquire a deprecatory force); — ***lest:*** 

**1.** with ***imperfect*** [[Gen.3.3|Gen 3:3]] ye shall not eat thereof . . . <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּןתְּֿמֻתוּן‎</span> ***lest*** ye die, [[Gen.11.4|Gen 11:4]] let us build a city <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּןנָֿפוּץ‎</span> ***lest*** we be scattered, [[Gen.19.15|Gen 19:15]]; [[Gen.19.17|Gen 19:17]]; [[Gen.19.19|Gen 19:19]]; [[Gen.38.23|Gen 38:23]]; [[Gen.45.1|Gen 45:1]]l [[Exod.1.10|Exod 1:10]]; [[Exod.23.29|Exod 23:29]]; [[Exod.23.33|Exod 23:33]] etc., [[2Kgs.10.23|2Kgs 10:23]] (followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֵשׁ‎</span>), [[Isa.6.10|Isa 6:10]]; [[Isa.27.3|Isa 27:3]]; [[Isa.28.22|Isa 28:22]]; [[Isa.48.5|Isa 48:5]]; [[Isa.48.7|Isa 48:7]]; [[Ps.2.12|Ps 2:12]]; [[Ps.7.3|Ps 7:3]] etc.; often after <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִשָׁמֶרלְֿךָ‎</span> ***take heed to thyself*** [[Gen.24.6|Gen 24:6]]; [[Gen.31.24|Gen 31:24]], and especially in Deuteronomy, as [[Deut.4.9|Deut 4:9]]; [[Deut.4.23|Deut 4:23]]; [[Deut.6.12|Deut 6:12]]; [[Deut.8.11|Deut 8:11]] and elsewhere; after a verb of fearing [[Gen.32.12|Gen 32:12]], and once, even, of swearing [[Judg.15.12|Judg 15:12]] deprecating pathetically, [[Gen.44.34|Gen 44:34]] how shall I go up, etc.? ***lest*** I look upon the evil that will befal my father. Once, unusually, preceding the principal clause, [[Prov.5.6|Prov 5:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֹרַח חַיִּים פֶּןתְּֿפַלֵּס‎</span> ***lest*** she should make level the path of life, her ways are unstable, etc. ([[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Be|Be]] [[BDB#Now|Now]]; see also Toy). Note especially 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** the idiom <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּי אָמַר (אָמַרְתִּי) פֶּןֿ‎</span> ***for he*** (***I***) ***said, Lest...***, implying always that some precaution has been taken to avert the dreaded contingency, [[Gen.26.9|Gen 26:9]] for I said, Lest I die on account of her (to obviate which, Isaac had called Rebecca his sister), [[Gen.31.31|Gen 31:31]]; [[Gen.38.11|Gen 38:11]]; [[Gen.42.4|Gen 42:4]]; [[Exod.13.17|Exod 13:17]]; [[Num.16.34|Num 16:34]]; [[1Sam.13.19|1Sam 13:19]]; [[1Sam.27.11|1Sam 27:11]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֵאמֹר‎</span>), [[Ps.38.17|Ps 38:17]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כי אמר‎</span> implied [[Gen.26.7|Gen 26:7]] compare, with an aposiop., [[Gen.3.22|Gen 3:22]] and now, ***lest*** he put forth his hand, etc. (implying that measures are taken to prevent this, see v[[Gen.3.23|Gen 3:23]]): compare [[Tob.8.9|Tob 8:9]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּןֿ‎</span> at the beginning of a sentence, with a dissuasive force, (Beware) ***lest:*** + [[Isa.36.18|Isa 36:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּןיַֿסִּית‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶתְכֶם חזקיהו‎</span> (beware) ***lest*** Hezekiah deceive you, [[Deut.29.17|Deut 29:17]] (twice in verse) (followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֵשׁ‎</span>), [[Jer.51.46|Jer 51:46]]; [[Job.32.13|Job 32:13]]; [[Job.36.18|Job 36:18]]. 

**2.** with ***Perfect***, the result feared being conceived as having possibly already taken place; [[2Sam.20.6|2Sam 20:6]] pursue after him, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּןמָֿצָא לוֺ ֗֗֗ וְהִצִּיל‎</span> ***lest he have found*** him fenced cities, etc. (but the tense of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎והציל‎</span> makes <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִמְצָא‎</span> probably, [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>sm</sup> [[BDB#Bu|Bu]] [[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>iii. 486</sup>), [[2Kgs.2.16|2Kgs 2:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַּשְׁלִכֵהוּ וגו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּןנְֿשָׂאוֺ רוּחַ י‎</span>.
