 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרוּמָה‎</span>] **proper name, of a location** ==home of Abimelek== ([[BDB#Thes|Thes]] Add below <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ארם‎</span>) [[Judg.9.41|Judg 9:41]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּארוּמָה‎</span>, probably = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רוּמָה‎</span> [[2Kgs.23.36|2Kgs 23:36]], compare [[BDB#Jer|Jerome]] [[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>Onomast. 146, 2nd ed. 178</sup>. It must have lain near Shechem; identified by [[BDB#MV|MV]] and others with ***El \`Ormah***, 2 hours southeast from Shechem ([[BDB#vdVelde|van de Velde]]<sup>Reisen ii. 268</sup>), but this place not otherwise known; compare [[BDB#Survey|Survey]]<sup>ii. 387</sup>.
