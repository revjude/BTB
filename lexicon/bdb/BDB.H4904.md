**1.** ***couch, bed*** (37 t.), [[2Sam.4.11|2Sam 4:11]]; [[Hos.7.14|Hos 7:14]]; [[Mic.2.1|Mic 2:1]]; [[1Kgs.1.47|1Kgs 1:47]]; [[Song.3.1|Song 3:1]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְנָפַל לְמ‎</span> [[Exod.21.18|Exod 21:18]] ([[BDB#E|E]]) ***he takes to his bed***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִדָּתָהּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Lev.15.26|Lev 15:26]] ([[BDB#P|P]]; see below I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נדד‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> as place of copulation [[Gen.49.4|Gen 49:4]] (poem in [[BDB#J|J]]), [[Isa.57.7|Isa 57:7]]; [[Isa.57.8|Isa 57:8]] (twice in verse); [[Prov.7.17|Prov 7:17]] as place of burial [[Isa.57.2|Isa 57:2]]; [[Ezek.32.25|Ezek 32:25]]; [[2Chr.16.14|2Chr 16:14]] (see <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> Inscription Tabnit<sup>8</sup> and perhaps Jewish inscription [[BDB#Cooke|Cooke]]<sup>341</sup>). 

**2.** ***act of lying:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** in general, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲדַר מ‎</span> chamber of ***lying down*** = bed-chamber, [[Exod.7.28|Exod 7:28]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[2Sam.4.7|2Sam 4:7]]; [[2Kgs.6.12|2Kgs 6:12]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּחַדְרֵי מִשְׁכָּֽבְךָ‎</span> [[Eccl.10.20|Eccl 10:20]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַצָּהֳרַיִם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎והוּא שֹׁכֵב אֵת מ‎</span> [[2Sam.4.5|2Sam 4:5]] his noon-day ***siesta***; [[2Sam.17.28|2Sam 17:28]] read perhaps <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַרְשׂת מ‎</span> couches for ***lying down*** [[BDB#Klo|Klo]] [[BDB#Bu|Bu]] [[BDB#HPS|HPS]] [[BDB#Now|Now]], compare [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** (from context) sexually: of woman <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֹדַעַת (אִישׁ לְ) מִשְׁכַּב‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זָכָת‎</span> [[Judg.21.11|Judg 21:11]]; [[Judg.21.12|Judg 21:12]]; [[Num.31.17|Num 31:17]]; [[Num.31.35|Num 31:35]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); of man <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁכב מִשְׁכְּבֵי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִשָּׁה‎</span> [[Lev.18.22|Lev 18:22]]; [[Lev.20.13|Lev 20:13]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶתזָֿכָר‎</span> sodomy; [[BDB#H|H]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֹּדִים מ‎</span> [[Ezek.23.17|Ezek 23:17]].
