 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲשִׁישָׁה‎</span> **noun feminine** (pressed) ==raisin-cake== — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[2Sam.6.19|2Sam 6:19]]; [[1Chr.16.3|1Chr 16:3]] distributed, with other viands, to people; especially as stimulating, plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲשִׁישׁוֺת‎</span> [[Song.2.5|Song 2:5]] more explicitly <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲשִׁישֵׁי עֲנָבִים‎</span> [[Hos.3.1|Hos 3:1]] used in sacrificial feasts (compare [[BDB#RS|RS]]<sup>OTJC Lect. xi. n. 7</sup>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲשִׁישֵׁי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִיר חֲרֶשֶׂת‎</span> [[Isa.16.7|Isa 16:7]], i.e. the raisin-cakes which were an article of trade at Qir-hareseth (taken by [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] and others = ***foundations***, i.e. foundations exposed by ruin, from [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָשִׁישׁ‎</span>] with such a meaning). <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵ֫שֶׁת‎</span> see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִשָּׁה‎</span> below III. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אנשׁ‎</span>.
