**1.** as **proper name, masculine**, ==son of Ham== [[Gen.9.18|Gen 9:18]]; [[Gen.9.22|Gen 9:22]]; [[Gen.9.25|Gen 9:25]]; [[Gen.9.27|Gen 9:27]]; [[Gen.10.15|Gen 10:15]] (as ancestor of Canaanites and Phoenicians; all [[BDB#J|J]]); [[Gen.10.6|Gen 10:6]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); [[1Chr.1.8|1Chr 1:8]]; [[1Chr.1.13|1Chr 1:13]] (from [[Gen.10.6|Gen 10:6]]; [[Gen.10.15|Gen 10:15]]). 

**2. a.** ==land, west of Jordan==, into which Hebrews came, and where they settled, subduing the inhabitants; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַלְכֵי כְּנַעַן‎</span> [[Judg.5.19|Judg 5:19]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מלך כ‎</span> [[Judg.4.2|Judg 4:2]]; [[Judg.4.23|Judg 4:23]]; [[Judg.4.24|Judg 4:24]] (twice in verse); hence <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַמְלְכוֺת כ‎</span> [[Ps.135.11|Ps 135:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ישְׁבֵי כ‎</span> [[Exod.15.15|Exod 15:15]] (song in [[BDB#E|E]]); compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִלְחֲמוֺת כ‎</span> [[Judg.3.1|Judg 3:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲצַבֵּי כ‎</span> [[Ps.106.38|Ps 106:38]] ***idols of Canaan***, i.e. of the former inhabitants; especially <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ (ֿ) כ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַרְצָה כ‎</span> [[Gen.44.8|Gen 44:8]]; [[Gen.46.31|Gen 46:31]]; [[Gen.47.1|Gen 47:1]]; [[Gen.47.4|Gen 47:4]]; [[Gen.47.13|Gen 47:13]]; [[Gen.47.14|Gen 47:14]]; [[Gen.47.15|Gen 47:15]]; [[Gen.50.5|Gen 50:5]]; [[Gen.50.13|Gen 50:13]] (all [[BDB#J|J]]), [[Gen.35.6|Gen 35:6]]; [[Gen.42.5|Gen 42:5]]; [[Gen.42.7|Gen 42:7]]; [[Gen.42.13|Gen 42:13]]; [[Gen.42.29|Gen 42:29]]; [[Gen.42.32|Gen 42:32]]; [[Gen.45.17|Gen 45:17]]; [[Gen.45.25|Gen 45:25]]; [[Josh.24.3|Josh 24:3]] (all [[BDB#E|E]]), [[Gen.11.31|Gen 11:31]]; [[Gen.12.5|Gen 12:5]] (twice in verse); [[Gen.13.12|Gen 13:12]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָרֵי הַכִּכָּר‎</span> v<sup>b</sup>), [[Gen.16.3|Gen 16:3]]; [[Gen.17.8|Gen 17:8]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]) + 40 t. [[BDB#P|P]], [[Judg.21.12|Judg 21:12]]; [[1Chr.16.18|1Chr 16:18]] = [[Ps.105.11|Ps 105:11]] also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָאָרֶץ כ‎</span> [[Num.34.2|Num 34:2]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּנוֺת כ‎</span> [[Gen.28.1|Gen 28:1]] ***daughters of Canaan*** = women of the land, so v[[Gen.28.6|Gen 28:6]] v[[Gen.28.8|Gen 28:8]]; [[Gen.36.2|Gen 36:2]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כנען‎</span> is personified [[Hos.12.8|Hos 12:8]] = apostate Israel; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׂפַת כ‎</span> [[Isa.19.18|Isa 19:18]] = the Hebrew language (without evil implication). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** the coast, especially Phoenicia [[Isa.23.11|Isa 23:11]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ פְּלִשְׁתִּים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כ‎</span> [[Zeph.2.5|Zeph 2:5]]. 



 II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּנָ֫עַן‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==merchant== (s) (because Canaanites, especially Phoenicians, were traders); — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּלעַֿם כְּנַעַן‎</span> [[Zeph.1.11|Zeph 1:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ כ‎</span> [[Ezek.16.29|Ezek 16:29]] (omitted by [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#B|B]] and others [[BDB#Co|Co]]), [[Ezek.17.4|Ezek 17:4]] ***a land of merchants***; compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כנעני‎</span> near the end.
