**1. a.** ***withhold, keep back, keep for oneself***, accusative, [[Gen.22.12|Gen 22:12]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), v[[Gen.22.16|Gen 22:16]]; [[Gen.39.9|Gen 39:9]] (followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִמֶּגִּי‎</span>; both [[BDB#J|J]]); absolute [[Prov.21.26|Prov 21:26]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָתַן‎</span>); ***withhold the rod*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁבט‎</span>) in discipline, [[Prov.13.24|Prov 13:24]] no object expressed [[Prov.11.24|Prov 11:24]]; [[Prov.24.11|Prov 24:11]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***keep*** one from evil, calamity, She'ôl, etc., with accusative and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> [[Gen.20.6|Gen 20:6]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[1Sam.25.39|1Sam 25:39]]; [[Ps.19.14|Ps 19:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִמָּוֶת נַפְשָׁם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Ps.78.50|Ps 78:50]] compare [[Job.33.18|Job 33:18]]; [[Isa.38.7|Isa 38:7]] (read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָשַׂכְתָּ‎</span> or <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲשׂךְ‎</span> for [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָשַׁקְתָּ‎</span>, see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חשׁק‎</span>); absolute ***hinder*** (i.e. calamities from coming) [[Isa.14.6|Isa 14:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמַטָח מֵעֲוֺנֵנוּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Ezra.9.13|Ezra 9:13]] thou hast ***kept back, downward*** (= and kept down), ***part of our iniquity***, i.e. hast not punished us according to our full desert. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** ***hold in check*** with accusative [[2Sam.18.16|2Sam 18:16]] (or ***spare*** see below); object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַגְלֵיהֶם‎</span> [[Jer.14.10|Jer 14:10]] especially of keeping silence, object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פִּי‎</span> [[Job.7.11|Job 7:11]] ***restrain my mouth***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂפתים‎</span> [[Prov.10.19|Prov 10:19]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אמרים‎</span> [[Prov.17.27|Prov 17:27]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֹק‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִפָּנַי לֹא ח‎</span> [[Job.30.10|Job 30:10]] ***from my face they have not withheld spittle*** (= they have spit in my face). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** ***refrain*** (from doing what is mentioned in the context), absolute [[Isa.54.2|Isa 54:2]]; [[Isa.58.1|Isa 58:1]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **e.** ***spare***, with accusative of person [[2Kgs.5.20|2Kgs 5:20]] ***he hath spared*** Naaman <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִקַּחַת‎</span> ***so as not to take*** (= and hath not taken anything) out of his hand; perhaps also [[2Sam.18.16|2Sam 18:16]] Joab ***spared*** the people (see above). **feminine** ***reserve*** for, with accusative + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span> [[Job.38.23|Job 38:23]]. 

**2.** (absolute) ***restrain, check*** (pain) i.e. ***assuage***, so apparently [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] [[Job.16.5|Job 16:5]] (see [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Kau|Kau]]; ***I would not restrain condolence of my lips***, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]] [[BDB#Me|Me]], reading <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לא אחשׂך‎</span> for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יחשׂך‎</span>). — [[Ezek.30.18|Ezek 30:18]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חשׁך‎</span>. **Niph\`al** ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְיוֺם אֵיד יֵחָשֶׂךְרָֿע‎</span> [[Job.21.30|Job 21:30]] ***at the day of calamity the wicked is spared*** (passive of **Qal 1 e**); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֵחָשֵׂךְ‎</span> [[Job.16.6|Job 16:6]] ***be assuaged***, of pain (pass of **Qal 2**).
