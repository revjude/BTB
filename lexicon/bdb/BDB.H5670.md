 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָבַט‎</span>] **verb denominative** ==take or give a pledge==; — 

**Qal** ***Infinitive construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַעֲבֹט‎</span> [[Deut.24.10|Deut 24:10]] ***to take possession of a thing pledged*** (with accusative of congnate meaning with verb); ***Imperfect*** 2 masculine singular ***give a pledge*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּעֲבֹט‎</span> [[Deut.15.6|Deut 15:6]] (i.e. ***borrow***). **Hiph\`il** ***Perfect*** 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהַעֲבַטְתָּ֫ גּוֺיִם רַבִּים‎</span> v[[Deut.15.6|Deut 15:6]] ***and thou shalt cause many nations to give pledges*** (i.e. lend to them); ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular suffix + ***Infinitive absolute*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַעֲבֵט תַּעֲבִיטֶנּוּ‎</span> v[[Deut.15.8|Deut 15:8]] ***thou shalt surely lend to him*** (literally as above). **Pi\`el** ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְעַבְּטוּן‎</span> [[Joel.2.7|Joel 2:7]] hardly makes sense (***lend on pledge*** = ***interchange*** ?); most read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְעַבְּתוּן‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְעַוְּתוּן‎</span> [[BDB#We|We]], compare [[BDB#Now|Now]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]] [[BDB#GASm|GASm]]; [[BDB#Gr|Gr]]. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַטוּן‎</span>.
