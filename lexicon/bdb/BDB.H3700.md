**Qal** ***Imperfect*** 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָדֶיךָ תִּכְסֹ֑ףלְמַעֲשֵׂה‎</span> [[Job.14.15|Job 14:15]] ***for the work of thy hands thou wouldest long***; 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּאַרְיֵה יִכְסוֺף לִטְרֹ֑ף‎</span> [[Ps.17.12|Ps 17:12]] ***like a lion that longeth to rend.*** **Niph\`al** ***Perfect*** 2 masculine singular + ***Infinitive absolute*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִכְסֹף נִכְסַפְתָּה לְבֵית אָבִיךָ‎</span> [[Gen.31.30|Gen 31:30]] ***thou didst long sorely for thy father's*** ***house; Perfect*** 3 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִכְסְפָה ֗֗֗ נַפְשִׁי לְחַצְרוֺת י‎</span> [[Ps.84.4|Ps 84:4]] ***my soul longeth . . . . for the courts of*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>; ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַגּוֺי לֹא נִכְסָ֑ף‎</span> [[Zeph.2.1|Zeph 2:1]] very dubious; but perhaps ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#Ke|Ke]] and others), O nation ***not turning pale***, = ***not ashamed***, compare etymology above; [[BDB#We|We]] thinks whole see corrupt.
