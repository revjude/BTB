 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּכוּנָה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==arrangement, preparation, fixed place==; — 

**1.** ***arrangement, disposition*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּכוּנָתוֺ‎</span> [[Ezek.43.11|Ezek 43:11]], i.e. the arrangement of the house (strike out [[BDB#Co|Co]] with [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] Manuscripts) 

**2.** ***preparation***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאֵין קֶצֶה לַתְּכוּנָה‎</span> [[Nah.2.10|Nah 2:10]] ***and no end to the preparation*** (i.e. things prepared, supply, store). 

**3.** ***fixed place***, i.e. dwelling-place, of God (= <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָכוֺן‎</span>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּכוּנָתוֺ‎</span> [[Job.23.3|Job 23:3]].
