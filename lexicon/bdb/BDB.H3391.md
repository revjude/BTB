**1.** ***month***, as measure of time, during, or in which [[Exod.2.2|Exod 2:2]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[Zech.11.8|Zech 11:8]]; [[Job.39.2|Job 39:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַרְחֵי שָׁ֑וְא‎</span> [[Job.7.3|Job 7:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַרְחֵי קֶדֶם‎</span> [[Job.29.2|Job 29:2]] pleonastic <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶרַח יָמִים‎</span> ***a month*** (***of***) ***days*** = ***a month of time*** [[Deut.21.13|Deut 21:13]]; [[2Kgs.15.13|2Kgs 15:13]]. 

**2.** ***calendar month***, with name <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶרַח זִו‎</span> [[1Kgs.6.37|1Kgs 6:37]] (2nd mo., compare v[[1Kgs.6.1|1Kgs 6:1]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶרַח בּוּל‎</span> v[[1Kgs.6.38|1Kgs 6:38]] (8th mo.); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶרַךְ הָאֵתָנִים‎</span> [[1Kgs.8.2|1Kgs 8:2]] (7th mo.); compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּמִסְפַּר יְרָחִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַליָֿבאֹ‎</span> [[Job.3.6|Job 3:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גֶּרֶשׁ יְרָתִים‎</span> [[Deut.33.4|Deut 33:4]] ***produce of months***, i.e. of various seasons of year. — compare synonym <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֹדֶשׁ‎</span>.
