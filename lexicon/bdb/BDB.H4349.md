 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָכוֺן‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Dan.8.11|Dan 8:11]]</sup> ==fixed or established place, foundation== (chiefly in poetry); — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Exod.15.17|Exod 15:17]] + 2 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְכוֺן‎</span> [[1Kgs.8.39|1Kgs 8:39]] + 10 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְכוֺנִי‎</span> [[Isa.18.4|Isa 18:4]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְכוֺנוֺ‎</span> [[Ezra.2.68|Ezra 2:68]] plural suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְכוֺנֶיהָ‎</span> [[Ps.104.5|Ps 104:5]] — 

**1.** ***fixed place*** of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s abode on earth [[Exod.15.17|Exod 15:17]] (song), [[1Kgs.8.13|1Kgs 8:13]] = [[2Chr.6.2|2Chr 6:2]] (poetic fragment: see [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] and [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>Intr. 182</sup>); = ***place***, or ***site*** of God's house [[Ezra.2.68|Ezra 2:68]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּלמְֿכוֺן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַרצִֿיּוֺן‎</span> [[Isa.4.5|Isa 4:5]], apparently = ***all the extent*** of Mt. Zion; redund. (si vera lectio) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהֻשְׁלַךְ מְכוֺן מִקְדָּשׁוֺ‎</span> [[Dan.8.11|Dan 8:11]] ***and the place of his sanctuary shall be thrown down*** (on difficulties of see, see [[BDB#Bev|Bev]]); of heavens <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְכוֺן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁבְתְּךָ‎</span> [[1Kgs.8.39|1Kgs 8:39]]; [[1Kgs.8.43|1Kgs 8:43]]; [[1Kgs.8.49|1Kgs 8:49]] = [[2Chr.6.30|2Chr 6:30]]; [[2Chr.6.33|2Chr 6:33]]; [[2Chr.6.39|2Chr 6:39]], compare [[Ps.33.14|Ps 33:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְכוֺנִי‎</span> alone [[Isa.18.4|Isa 18:4]] figurative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צֶדֶק וּמִשְׁפָּט מְכוֺן כִּסְאֶ֑ךָ‎</span> [[Ps.89.15|Ps 89:15]], so [[Ps.97.2|Ps 97:2]].
