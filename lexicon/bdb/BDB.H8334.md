 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁרַת‎</span>]<sub>97</sub> **verb Pi\`el** ==minister, serve== (compare Late Hebrew <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁרוּת‎</span> (temple-)***ministry***, Phoenician <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎משרת‎</span> ***ministry***, and perhaps verb <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שרת‎</span> ***minister***); — 

**Pi\`el** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁרֵת‎</span> [[Num.8.26|Num 8:26]]; [[Deut.18.7|Deut 18:7]] 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁרְתוּ‎</span> [[Num.3.6|Num 3:6]] ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיְשָׁ֫רֶת‎</span> [[Gen.39.4|Gen 39:4]], 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְשָֽׁרְתוּ‎</span> [[Num.3.31|Num 3:31]] +, etc.; ***Infinitive construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְשָׁרֵת‎</span> [[Exod.28.35|Exod 28:35]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְשָׁ֫רֶת שָׁם‎</span> [[Deut.17.12|Deut 17:12]], etc.; ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְשָׁרֵת‎</span> [[Num.11.28|Num 11:28]] + ([[2Sam.13.9|2Sam 13:9]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַשְׂרֵת‎</span>, p. 602:a), feminine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְשָׁרַת‎</span> [[1Kgs.1.15|1Kgs 1:15]], etc.; — ***serve:*** 

**1.** of ministerial service (opposed tomenial, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עבד‎</span>): 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** higher domestic service: Joseph [[Gen.39.4|Gen 39:4]]; [[Gen.40.4|Gen 40:4]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]), Elisha's chief servant [[2Kgs.4.43|2Kgs 4:43]]; [[2Kgs.6.15|2Kgs 6:15]], royal domestics [[2Sam.13.17|2Sam 13:17]]; [[2Sam.13.18|2Sam 13:18]]; [[1Kgs.10.5|1Kgs 10:5]]; [[2Chr.9.4|2Chr 9:4]]; [[Esth.2.2|Esth 2:2]]; [[Esth.6.3|Esth 6:3]], Abishag [[1Kgs.1.4|1Kgs 1:4]]; [[1Kgs.1.15|1Kgs 1:15]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of royal ***officers*** (late) [[1Chr.27.1|1Chr 27:1]]; [[1Chr.28.1|1Chr 28:1]]; [[2Chr.17.19|2Chr 17:19]]; [[2Chr.22.8|2Chr 22:8]]; [[Esth.1.10|Esth 1:10]]; [[Prov.29.12|Prov 29:12]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** Joshua as chief assistant to Moses [[Exod.24.13|Exod 24:13]]; [[Exod.33.11|Exod 33:11]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[Num.11.28|Num 11:28]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[Josh.1.1|Josh 1:1]] ([[BDB#D|D]]), Elisha to Elijah [[1Kgs.19.21|1Kgs 19:21]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** of angels to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ps.103.21|Ps 103:21]], compare [[Ps.104.4|Ps 104:4]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **e.** of kings of nations in prophets [[Isa.60.10|Isa 60:10]]. 

**2.** usually of special service in worship: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of Levitical priests, absolute [[1Kgs.8.11|1Kgs 8:11]] = [[2Chr.5.14|2Chr 5:14]] with accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Deut.10.8|Deut 10:8]]; [[Deut.17.12|Deut 17:12]]; [[Deut.21.5|Deut 21:5]]; [[Jer.33.21|Jer 33:21]] + (strangely) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְשָֽׁרְתֵי אֹתִי‎</span> v[[Jer.33.22|Jer 33:22]] also [[Isa.60.7|Isa 60:7]] of animals put figurative for those sacrificing them; of foreigners admitted to priesthood [[Isa.61.6|Isa 61:6]], compare [[Isa.56.6|Isa 56:6]] so probably Samuel conceived as priest [[1Sam.2.11|1Sam 2:11]]; [[1Sam.3.1|1Sam 3:1]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎את פני י‎</span> [[1Sam.2.18|1Sam 2:18]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פני‎</span> probably accusative, compare [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">τῷ προσώπῳ</span> v[[1Sam.2.11|1Sam 2:11]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּשֵׁם י‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span>[[Deut.18.5|Deut 18:5]]; [[Deut.18.7|Deut 18:7]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** Levites ([[BDB#P|P]], and late); with accusative of Aaronic priests [[Num.3.6|Num 3:6]]; [[Num.8.26|Num 8:26]]; [[Num.18.2|Num 18:2]], with accusative of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[1Chr.15.2|1Chr 15:2]]; [[2Chr.29.11|2Chr 29:11]] a; probably also in reference to Levitical singer [[Ps.101.6|Ps 101:6]] with accusative tabernacle [[Num.1.50|Num 1:50]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), congregation [[Num.16.9|Num 16:9]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), people [[Ezek.44.11|Ezek 44:11]] + v[[Ezek.44.12|Ezek 44:12]] (of idolatry); absolute [[1Chr.16.4|1Chr 16:4]]; [[2Chr.23.6|2Chr 23:6]]; [[2Chr.29.11|2Chr 29:11]] b [[2Chr.31.2|2Chr 31:2]]; [[Ezra.8.17|Ezra 8:17]] (participle as substantive, ***ministers***); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִפְנֵי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרוֺן בְּרִית י‎</span> [[1Chr.16.37|1Chr 16:37]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶגֶד הַכֹּהֲנִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> [[2Chr.8.14|2Chr 8:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ב‎</span> location [[1Chr.26.12|1Chr 26:12]] especially with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span>, of sacred vessels [[Num.3.31|Num 3:31]]; [[Num.4.9|Num 4:9]]; [[Num.4.12|Num 4:12]]; [[Num.4.14|Num 4:14]]; [[2Kgs.25.14|2Kgs 25:14]] = [[Jer.52.18|Jer 52:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּשִּׁיר‎</span> [[1Chr.6.17|1Chr 6:17]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** Zadokite priests (Ezekiel), absolute [[Ezek.42.14|Ezek 42:14]] + 3 t., with accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ezek.40.48|Ezek 40:48]] + 4 t., construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְשָׁרֲתֵי‎</span> before sacred place [[Ezek.45.4|Ezek 45:4]]; [[Ezek.45.5|Ezek 45:5]]; [[Ezek.46.24|Ezek 46:24]].
