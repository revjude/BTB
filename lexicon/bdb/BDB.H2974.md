**Hiph\`il** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺאִיל‎</span> [[Hos.5.11|Hos 5:11]] + 2 t.; 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺאַלְתָּ‎</span> [[1Chr.17.27|1Chr 17:27]] 1 singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺאַלְתִּי‎</span> [[Gen.18.27|Gen 18:27]]; [[Gen.18.31|Gen 18:31]] 1 plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺאַלְנוּ‎</span> [[Josh.7.7|Josh 7:7]] ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְיֹאֵל‎</span> [[Job.6.9|Job 6:9]] (jussive); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיּ֫וֺאֶל‎</span> [[Exod.2.21|Exod 2:21]] + 4 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיֹאֶל‎</span> [[1Sam.17.39|1Sam 17:39]] (but see below); ***Imperative*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺאֵל‎</span> [[2Sam.7.29|2Sam 7:29]]; [[2Kgs.5.23|2Kgs 5:23]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺאֶלנָֿא‎</span> [[Judg.19.6|Judg 19:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ה֫וֺאֶל נָא‎</span> [[2Kgs.6.3|2Kgs 6:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺאִ֫ילוּ‎</span> [[Job.6.28|Job 6:28]] — 

**1.** ***shew willingness*** to do anything, ***accept an invitation, acquiesce***, followed by infinitive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַי֫וֺאֶל משֶׁה לָשֶׁבֶת אֶתהָֿאִישׁ‎</span> [[Exod.2.21|Exod 2:21]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]) ***and Moses was willing to dwell with the man*** (and did so), = [[Judg.17.11|Judg 17:11]] (of Levite); elsewhere following by finite verb; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְלוּ הוֺאַלְנוּ וַנֵּשֶׁב‎</span> [[Josh.7.7|Josh 7:7]] ***and would that we had been willing and had stayed***; especially imperative, followed by imperative: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺאֶלנָֿא וְלִין‎</span> [[Judg.19.6|Judg 19:6]] ***be willing and spend the night*** (kindly accept my invitation) see [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:110»§ 110</sup>. 2 a, R 2; 120. 2 a, [[2Kgs.6.3|2Kgs 6:3]] and (no <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְ‎</span>) [[2Kgs.5.23|2Kgs 5:23]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺאִילוּ פְֿנוּבִֿי‎</span> [[Job.6.28|Job 6:28]] i.e. be good enough to look upon me. 

**2.** more actively, voluntarily ***undertake*** to do anything (and do it), followed by infinitive: [[Gen.18.27|Gen 18:27]] ***I have undertaken to speak*** = v[[Gen.18.31|Gen 18:31]] (both [[BDB#J|J]]); so [[1Sam.17.39|1Sam 17:39]] [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]], but read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַ֫יֵּלֶא‎</span>, ***and he laboured vainly***, for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיֹאֶל‎</span>, so [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]], [[BDB#Gei|Gei]]<sup>Urschrift, 377</sup> [[BDB#We|We]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]] (compare also v<sup>b</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא אוּכַל לָלֶכֶת בָּאֵלֶּה‎</span>); followed by finite verb <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺאִיל משֶׁה בֵּאֵר‎</span> [[Deut.1.5|Deut 1:5]] i.e. Moses ***took upon himself*** to expound the law. 

**3.** more actively still, ***be pleased, determine*** on one's own responsibility, ***resolve***, followed by infinitive: [[Josh.17.12|Josh 17:12]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]) = [[Judg.1.27|Judg 1:27]] but the Canaanites ***were determined*** (shewed a determination) to remain in this territory (= ***persisted in dwelling there***); [[Judg.1.35|Judg 1:35]] ***the Amorites persisted in dwelling*** in Har-Heres, etc.; followed by finite verb, of irreligious action, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הואיל הָלַךְ‎</span> [[Hos.5.11|Hos 5:11]] Ephraim ***persisted, he walked*** after vanity (read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוא‎</span> for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צו‎</span>, see [[BDB#Che|Che]]); elsewhere only of the divine ***good pleasure:*** followed by infinitive [[1Sam.12.22|1Sam 12:22]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> ***hath been pleased to make you a people for himself***; [[1Chr.17.27|1Chr 17:27]] followed by finite verb <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺאֵל וּבָרֵךְ‎</span> [[2Sam.7.29|2Sam 7:29]] ***be pleased and bless*** the house of thy servant; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְיֹאֵל אֱלוֺהַּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וִידַכְּאֵנִי‎</span> [[Job.6.9|Job 6:9]] ***and that Eloah would be pleased and crush me!*** (see [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:120»§ 120</sup>, 2 a [[BDB#Da|Da]]<sup>Synt. § 83 b</sup>).
