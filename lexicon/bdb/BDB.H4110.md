 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַהֲלָל‎</span>] **noun [masculine]** ==praise==; — only in <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאִישׂ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְפִי מַהֲלָלוֺ‎</span> [[Prov.27.21|Prov 27:21]] the refining pot silver, and the furnace for gold, ***and a man according to his praise***, i.e. probably according to the praise of him by others, which tests him; so [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Str|Str]] and others; perhaps however ***so let a man be to the mouth of his praise***, i.e. ***that praise him***, — testing the praise to determine its worth, [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] [[BDB#Ges|Rob-Ges]]; other views are: ***according to the measure of his boasting*** [[BDB#Ew|Ew]], i.e. is judged according to his success or failure in that of which he boasts; [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] ***according to the thing of which he boasts.***
