 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָפָר‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Lev.14.42|Lev 14:42]]</sup> ==dry earth, dust==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ע‎</span> [[Gen.2.7|Gen 2:7]] +, construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲפַר‎</span> [[Gen.13.16|Gen 13:16]] +; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲפָרֵךְ‎</span> [[Ezek.26.12|Ezek 26:12]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲפָרוֺ‎</span> [[Deut.9.21|Deut 9:21]], etc.; plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַפְרֹת‎</span> [[Job.28.6|Job 28:6]]; [[Prov.8.26|Prov 8:26]] — 

**1.** literally: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***dry, loose earth***, thrown (in malice) [[2Sam.16.13|2Sam 16:13]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲבָנִים‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַלרֿאֹשׁ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַעֲלוּ ע‎</span> [[Josh.7.6|Josh 7:6]] ([[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]; sign of grief), so [[Ezek.27.30|Ezek 27:30]]; [[Lam.2.10|Lam 2:10]], and (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זָרַק‎</span>) [[Job.2.12|Job 2:12]] loose earth (on surface of ground) [[1Kgs.18.38|1Kgs 18:38]]; [[Amos.2.7|Amos 2:7]]; [[Mic.1.10|Mic 1:10]]; [[Isa.34.7|Isa 34:7]]; [[Isa.34.9|Isa 34:9]]; [[Lev.17.13|Lev 17:13]] ([[BDB#H|H]]) [[Ezek.24.7|Ezek 24:7]] (in figurative), [[Num.5.17|Num 5:17]]; [[Job.14.19|Job 14:19]]; [[Job.38.38|Job 38:38]]; [[Job.39.14|Job 39:14]]; [[Gen.26.15|Gen 26:15]] ([[BDB#R|R]]), made into siege works [[Hab.1.10|Hab 1:10]] of sand-storm [[Deut.28.24|Deut 28:24]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָבָק‎</span>); as serpent's food, token of punishment and humiliation, [[Gen.3.14|Gen 3:14]] ([[BDB#J|J]]) [[Isa.65.25|Isa 65:25]] (compare [[Mic.7.17|Mic 7:17]] **2 e** below); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֹחֲלֵי ע‎</span> [[Deut.32.24|Deut 32:24]] (poem; compare **2 e**). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** specifically as material of human body [[Gen.2.7|Gen 2:7]]; [[Gen.3.19|Gen 3:19]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[Eccl.3.20|Eccl 3:20]], compare [[Job.4.19|Job 4:19]]; [[Job.8.19|Job 8:19]]; [[Ps.103.14|Ps 103:14]], to which it returns (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוּב אֶלֿ‎</span>) [[Gen.3.19|Gen 3:19]]; [[Job.10.9|Job 10:9]]; [[Ps.104.29|Ps 104:29]]; [[Eccl.3.20|Eccl 3:20]] so (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל‎</span>) [[Job.34.15|Job 34:15]]; [[Eccl.12.7|Eccl 12:7]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** = surface of ground [[Exod.8.12|Exod 8:12]]; [[Exod.8.13|Exod 8:13]] (twice in verse); ([[BDB#P|P]]; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָאָרַץ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ע‎</span>), [[Job.19.25|Job 19:25]]; [[Isa.25.12|Isa 25:12]]; [[Isa.26.5|Isa 26:5]] (both \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ‎</span>), surface of (whole) earth [[Job.41.25|Job 41:25]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְחִלוֺת ע‎</span> [[Isa.2.19|Isa 2:19]] (for hiding), so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ע‎</span> alone v[[Isa.2.10|Isa 2:10]], compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֹרֵי ע‎</span> [[Job.30.6|Job 30:6]] = ***soil***, [[Job.5.6|Job 5:6]]; [[Job.14.8|Job 14:8]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** ***powder*** of anything pulverized [[Deut.9.21|Deut 9:21]] (twice in verse) (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּ֑ק‎</span> \|\| [[Exod.32.20|Exod 32:20]]), [[2Kgs.23.6|2Kgs 23:6]] (twice in verse); [[2Kgs.23.12|2Kgs 23:12]]; [[2Kgs.23.15|2Kgs 23:15]], so in simile [[2Sam.22.43|2Sam 22:43]] = [[Ps.18.43|Ps 18:43]], hence = ashes v[[Ps.18.4|Ps 18:4]]; [[Num.19.17|Num 19:17]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **e.** ***debris***, of ruined city [[1Kgs.20.10|1Kgs 20:10]]; [[Ezek.26.4|Ezek 26:4]]; [[Ezek.26.12|Ezek 26:12]]; [[Neh.3.34|Neh 3:34]]; [[Neh.4.4|Neh 4:4]]; [[Ps.102.15|Ps 102:15]]. The following are late: **feminine** earth of the grave: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶשְׁכַּב‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֶע‎</span> [[Job.7.21|Job 7:21]], so (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל‎</span>) [[Job.20.11|Job 20:11]]; [[Job.21.26|Job 21:26]], compare [[Job.17.16|Job 17:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יוֺרְדֵי ע‎</span> [[Ps.22.30|Ps 22:30]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֹׁכְנֵי ע‎</span> [[Isa.26.19|Isa 26:19]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְשֵׁנֵי אַדְמַתעֿ‎</span> [[Dan.12.2|Dan 12:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲפַרמָֿוֶת‎</span> [[Ps.22.16|Ps 22:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ע‎</span> in figure [[Ps.30.10|Ps 30:10]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **g.** ***mortar*** (i.e. dried mud) for plastering houses [[Lev.14.41|Lev 14:41]]; [[Lev.14.42|Lev 14:42]]; [[Lev.14.45|Lev 14:45]] ([[BDB#P|P]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **h.** (iron-) ***ore*** [[Job.28.2|Job 28:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַפְרֹת זָהָב‎</span> v[[Job.28.6|Job 28:6]] ***gold-dust*** (but see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סַפִּיר‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **i.** material of earth [[Isa.40.12|Isa 40:12]], compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ראֹשׁ עַפְרֹת תֵּבֵל‎</span> [[Prov.8.26|Prov 8:26]] ***the first of the earth particles of the world.*** 

**2.** figurative: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of abundance [[Gen.13.16|Gen 13:16]] (twice in verse); [[Gen.28.14|Gen 28:14]] (all [[BDB#J|J]]) [[2Chr.1.9|2Chr 1:9]]; [[Job.27.16|Job 27:16]]; [[Ps.78.27|Ps 78:27]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חוֺל יַמִּים‎</span>), compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲפַר יַעֲקֹב‎</span> [[Num.23.10|Num 23:10]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]); with added idea of commonness, worthlessness [[Zeph.1.7|Zeph 1:7]]; [[Zech.9.3|Zech 9:3]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of worthlessness (alone) [[Job.22.24|Job 22:24]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** simile of the scattered, dispersed [[2Kgs.13.7|2Kgs 13:7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֶּע‎</span> [[Isa.41.2|Isa 41:2]] (so [[BDB#van d.H.|van d. H.]] [[BDB#Gi|Ginsb]]; [[BDB#Baer|Baer]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֵּע‎</span>; on meaning see [[BDB#Du|Du]] [[BDB#Che|Che]], reading <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּשִׂימֵם‎</span> for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִתֵּן‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** of self-abasement [[Gen.18.27|Gen 18:27]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵפַר‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְנִחַמְתִּי עַלעֿ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וָאֵפֶר‎</span> [[Job.42.6|Job 42:6]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פִּיהוּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִתֵּן בֶּע‎</span> [[Lam.3.29|Lam 3:29]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **e.** of humiliation (sitting or lying in dust) [[Isa.47.1|Isa 47:1]] compare [[Isa.29.4|Isa 29:4]] (twice in verse), [[Job.16.15|Job 16:15]]; [[Job.40.13|Job 40:13]]; [[Ps.7.6|Ps 7:6]]; [[Ps.44.26|Ps 44:26]]; [[Ps.119.25|Ps 119:25]], simile [[Job.30.19|Job 30:19]] licking dust [[Ps.72.9|Ps 72:9]]; [[Isa.49.23|Isa 49:23]]; [[Mic.7.17|Mic 7:17]], compare Tel Amarna <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***tikalu ipra***</u></abbr> (of enemies, see [[BDB#Wkl|Wkl]]<sup>Altor. Forsch. iii. 291</sup>; also of serpent **1 a** above); of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> raising from the dust [[1Sam.2.8|1Sam 2:8]] = [[Ps.113.7|Ps 113:7]], [[1Kgs.16.2|1Kgs 16:2]] of lifting oneself [[Isa.52.2|Isa 52:2]].
