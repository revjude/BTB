**1.** ***be precious:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** = ***highly valued, esteemed***, David's name, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּיקַר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁמוֺ מְאֹד‎</span> [[1Sam.18.30|1Sam 18:30]] of Israel, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָקַרְתָּ בְעֵינַי‎</span> [[Isa.43.4|Isa 43:4]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִכְבַּדְתָּ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲהַבְתִּיךָ‎</span>); of God's thoughts <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְלִי מַהיָָּֽֿקִרוּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֵעֶיךָ‎</span> [[Ps.139.17|Ps 139:17]] ([[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#Hup|Hup]] render ***hard, difficult:*** compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַקִּירָה‎</span> [[Dan.2.11|Dan 2:11]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** especially of life (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶפֶשׁ‎</span>), in phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָָֽקִרָה נַפְשִׁי בְּעֵינֶיךָ‎</span> [[1Sam.26.21|1Sam 26:21]], i.e. thou hast spared it; so (jussive) [[2Kgs.1.13|2Kgs 1:13]]; [[2Kgs.1.14|2Kgs 1:14]] similarly <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֵיקַר דָּמָם בְּעֵינָיו‎</span> [[Ps.72.14|Ps 72:14]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִתּוֺךְ וּמֵחָמָס יִגְאַל נַפְשָׁס‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** ***be costly***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְיֵקַר פִּדְיוֺן נַפְשָׁם‎</span> [[Ps.49.9|Ps 49:9]]. 

**2.** ***be appraised, valued***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶדֶר הַיְקָר אֲשֶׁר יָקַרְתִּי מֵעֲלֵיהֶם‎</span> [[Zech.11.13|Zech 11:13]] ***the magnificence of the price at which I was appraised (and dismissed)*** ***from them!*** **Hiph\`il** ***Imperfect*** 1 singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺקִיר אֱנוֺשׁ מִפָּ֑ז‎</span> [[Isa.13.12|Isa 13:12]] ***I will make men more rare*** (literally ***precious***) ***than fine gold. Imperative*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֹקַר‎</span> [[Prov.25.17|Prov 25:17]] ***make rare*** (i.e. ***withhold***) thy foot from the house of thy friend.
