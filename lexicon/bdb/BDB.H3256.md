 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָסַר‎</span>] **verb** ==discipline, chasten, admonish== (Talmud [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִ(י)סּוֺר‎</span> ***chastisement***; but Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎יְסַר‎</span> ***is bind ***) — 

**Qal** ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְיִסְּרֵנִי‎</span> [[Isa.8.11|Isa 8:11]] ([[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#SS|SS]] see [[BDB#Bo|Bo]]<sup>§ 1103</sup> [[BDB#Ew|Ew]]<sup>§ 249 d</sup>, but [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] [[BDB#MV|MV]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] and others **Pi\`el** Perfect); 1 singular suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאֶסְֳּרֵם‎</span> [[Hos.10.10|Hos 10:10]] ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:71»§ 71</sup>); ***Infinitive absolute*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָסֹר‎</span> [[1Chr.15.22|1Chr 15:22]] (noun [[BDB#Ke|Ke]], point as participle [[BDB#Öt|Öttli]]); ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֹסֵר‎</span> [[Prov.9.7|Prov 9:7]]; [[Ps.94.10|Ps 94:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יסורי‎</span> [[Jer.17.13|Jer 17:13]] [[BDB#Kt|Kt]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סוּר‎</span>; — 

**1.** ***admonish*** [[Isa.8.11|Isa 8:11]] (followed by suffix + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִלֶּכֶת‎</span> ***away from walking***, i. e. not to walk), [[Prov.9.7|Prov 9:7]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺכִיחַ‎</span>). 

**2.** ***instruct***, [[1Chr.15.22|1Chr 15:22]]. 

**3.** ***discipline***, of God [[Hos.10.10|Hos 10:10]]; [[Ps.94.10|Ps 94:10]]. **Niph\`al** ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִוָּ֫סֶר עֶ֫בֶד‎</span> [[Prov.29.19|Prov 29:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִוָּסֵר‎</span> [[Jer.31.18|Jer 31:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּוָּסְרוּ‎</span> [[Lev.26.23|Lev 26:23]] ***Imperative*** feminine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִוָּסְרִי‎</span> [[Jer.6.8|Jer 6:8]] masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִיָסְרוּ‎</span> [[Ps.2.10|Ps 2:10]] — (Niph\`al ***tolerate.***, [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:51»§ 51</sup>. 2), ***let oneself be corrected, admonished*** by words of man [[Ps.2.10|Ps 2:10]]; [[Prov.29.19|Prov 29:19]]); ***let oneself be chastened*** by discipline of God [[Jer.6.8|Jer 6:8]]; [[Jer.31.18|Jer 31:18]]; [[Lev.26.23|Lev 26:23]]. **Pi\`el** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִסַּר‎</span> [[1Kgs.12.11|1Kgs 12:11]] + 3 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִסְּרַנִּי‎</span> [[Ps.118.18|Ps 118:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִסְּרוֺ‎</span> [[Isa.28.26|Isa 28:26]] 3 feminine suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִסְּרַתּוּ‎</span> [[Prov.31.1|Prov 31:1]] 2 masculine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִסַּרְתָּ‎</span> [[Ps.39.12|Ps 39:12]]; [[Job.4.3|Job 4:3]], etc. + 8 t. Perfect; ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְיַסֵּר‎</span> [[Deut.8.5|Deut 8:5]] 2 masculine suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּיַסְּרֶנּוּ‎</span> [[Ps.94.12|Ps 94:12]], etc. + 5 t. Imperfect; ***Imperative*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַסֵּר‎</span> [[Prov.19.18|Prov 19:18]]; [[Prov.29.17|Prov 29:17]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַסְּרֵנִי‎</span> [[Jer.10.24|Jer 10:24]] ***Infinitive absolute*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַסֹּר‎</span> [[Ps.118.18|Ps 118:18]] ***construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַסְּרָה‎</span> [[Lev.26.18|Lev 26:18]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַסְּרֶ֑ךָּ‎</span>[[Deut.4.36|Deut 4:36]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְיַסְּרֶ֑ךָּ‎</span> [[Deut.8.5|Deut 8:5]] — 

**1.** ***discipline, correct*** (the moral nature, with more or less severity according to circumstances): 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of God, with accusative [[Hos.7.15|Hos 7:15]] (of ***training*** arms), [[Deut.4.36|Deut 4:36]] (see [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]), [[Deut.8.5|Deut 8:5]] (Israel as son), [[Isa.28.26|Isa 28:26]], happy the man <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אשׁר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תיסרנו יהּ‎</span> [[Ps.94.12|Ps 94:12]]; [[Ps.118.18|Ps 118:18]] (twice in verse) 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of man, with accusative [[Deut.8.5|Deut 8:5]]; [[Prov.19.18|Prov 19:18]]; [[Prov.29.17|Prov 29:17]] (all of a father his son), [[Job.4.3|Job 4:3]]; [[Prov.31.1|Prov 31:1]]; [[Ps.16.7|Ps 16:7]] my reins (the emotions of my own heart) ***correct, admonish*** me. 

**2.** more severely, ***chasten, chastise:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of God, followed by accusative of person, [[Jer.31.18|Jer 31:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַלבַּֿחֲמָֽתְךָ תְיַסְּרֵנִי‎</span> [[Ps.6.2|Ps 6:2]]; [[Ps.38.2|Ps 38:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּמשׁפט אל באף‎</span> [[Jer.10.24|Jer 10:24]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎למשׁפט‎</span> [[Jer.30.11|Jer 30:11]] = [[Jer.46.28|Jer 46:28]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎על‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חטאת‎</span> [[Lev.26.18|Lev 26:18]]; [[Lev.26.28|Lev 26:28]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בתוכחות על עון‎</span> [[Ps.39.12|Ps 39:12]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of man, a father his son [[Deut.21.18|Deut 21:18]] elders a man [[Deut.22.18|Deut 22:18]] (including, as perhaps [[Deut.21.18|Deut 21:18]], bodily chastisement); a king his subjects <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּשּׁוֺטִים‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּעַקְרַבִּים‎</span> [[1Kgs.12.11|1Kgs 12:11]]; [[1Kgs.12.14|1Kgs 12:14]] = [[2Chr.10.11|2Chr 10:11]]; [[2Chr.10.14|2Chr 10:14]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּיַסְּרֵךְ רָֽעָתֵךְ‎</span> [[Jer.2.19|Jer 2:19]] ***thy badness will chastise thee.*** **Nithp.** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִוַּסְּרוּ‎</span> [[Ezek.23.48|Ezek 23:48]] (for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִתְוַסְּרוּ‎</span> [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:55»§ 55</sup> R. 7); passive ***be disciplined, corrected.*** **Hiph\`il** ***Imperfect*** 1 singular suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַיְסִירֵם‎</span> ([[BDB#Ew|Ew]]<sup>§ 131 c</sup>, but error for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲיַסְּרֵם‎</span> [[BDB#Bö|Bö]]<sup>§ 437 f</sup> [[BDB#SS|SS]]), with accusative ***chasten*** [[Hos.7.12|Hos 7:12]].
