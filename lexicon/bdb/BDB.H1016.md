**proper name, of a location 1.** ==in Judah== (***house***, i.e. ***temple of Dagon***; Assyrian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***Bit-Daganna***</u></abbr> [[BDB#COT|COT]]<sup>[[Josh.15.41|Josh 15:41]], &amp; i. p. 281</sup>) [[Josh.15.41|Josh 15:41]] ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Βαγαδιηλ</span>, but [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]<sup>L</sup></lookup> <span class="bdbLangGreek">Βηθδαγων</span>) — name appears in modern ***Beit Dejân***, southeast of Jaffa, but location unsuitable, compare [[BDB#Rob|Rob]]<sup>BR ii. 232.</sup> 

**2.** in Asher [[Josh.19.27|Josh 19:27]] ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Βαιθεγενεθ</span>, but [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]<sup>L</sup></lookup> <span class="bdbLangGreek">Βηθδαγων</span>) perhaps = ***Beit Dejen***, near Akko, compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]. **\*\***Jerome<sup>opp. (ed. Vallarsl). iii. 38</sup> defines as ***domus tritici***, and says Id.<sup>ib. 174</sup>): 'sed et usque hodie grandis vicus Capherdago inter Diospolim et Jamnian demonstratur.' [Elsewhere he defines <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דגון‎</span> (Id.<sup>ib. 48</sup>) as 'piscis tristitiae'] (HPS, privately).
