 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גָּרַע‎</span>] **verb** ==diminish, restrain, withdraw== (Late Hebrew ***id.***, Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎גְּרַע‎</span> <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhMAAcAIAAAP///wAAACH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAwABwAAAJnhI+py+0Po4zh1Ilf2Dt7VnXfiHDkaYjoqK7uW8KSyF1yU6em05458FvsXL+gwqbz5Yy4IctUQwKHUWW0WA1Vn1datnPlWpDU5fam6k6ZNx2W3Q6ve233Dl6XO+uJrpTfdwaIM5hRAAA7"> ***shave head***, etc.; compare <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhJwAgAIAAAP///wAAACH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAnACAAAAJohI+py+0Po5wqWGpzzHfz4IEU0I3miabqyiaf2LovvHnu9DqaVM60DNsBEaJZxcfwIRdGJkjJcQqTtOjDqvvhSlntwfsFD6ngqZQ7PkKw1PYVPYLH0iGTmSx/34l7HjSPAjVXMVjoUAAAOw=="> ***swallow***) — 

**Qal** ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִגְרַע‎</span> [[Job.36.7|Job 36:7]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִגְרָ֑ע‎</span> [[Exod.21.10|Exod 21:10]] 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּגְרַע‎</span> [[Deut.13.1|Deut 13:1]] + 3 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶגְרַע‎</span> [[Ezek.5.11|Ezek 5:11]] (but compare ***below***), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וָאֶגְרַע‎</span> [[Ezek.16.27|Ezek 16:27]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּגְרְעוּ‎</span> [[Exod.5.8|Exod 5:8]] + 2 t.; ***Infinitive*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִגְרֹעַ‎</span> [[Eccl.3.14|Eccl 3:14]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּרוּעָה‎</span> [[Isa.15.2|Isa 15:2]] (so [[BDB#Baer|Baer]]; [[BDB#van d.H.|v. d. H.]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּדוּעָה‎</span> see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גדע‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּרֻעָה‎</span> [[Jer.48.37|Jer 48:37]] — 

**1.** ***diminish***, usually followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> [[Exod.5.8|Exod 5:8]]; [[Exod.5.19|Exod 5:19]] (the tale of bricks), [[Deut.4.2|Deut 4:2]]; [[Deut.13.1|Deut 13:1]] (in both, word of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>; opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוסיף‎</span>) compare [[Eccl.3.14|Eccl 3:14]], and also [[Jer.26.2|Jer 26:2]] (with accusative); with accusative also [[Exod.21.10|Exod 21:10]] (food etc.) compare [[Ezek.16.27|Ezek 16:27]] ***Passive participle diminished, clipped***, of beard, [[Jer.48.37|Jer 48:37]] and [[Isa.15.2|Isa 15:2]] (so [[BDB#Baer|Baer]], rightly, compare his note and see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גדע‎</span>). 

**2.** ***restrain*** [[Job.15.4|Job 15:4]] (with accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׂיחָה‎</span> meditation, devotion), [[Job.15.8|Job 15:8]] (with accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָכְמָה‎</span>, followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵלֶיךָ‎</span>, i.e. ***unto, for thyself***, monopolize; [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Da|Da]] render ***draw unto thyself***, appropriate; [[BDB#Schulth|Schult]]. compare Arabic ***swallow***, compare [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] ***saugst zu dir ein Weisheit***). 

**3.** ***withdraw*** [[Job.36.7|Job 36:7]] (object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַיִן‎</span>, ***eye***, followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span>); [[Ezek.5.11|Ezek 5:11]] absolute (si vera lectio); [[BDB#Co|Co]]. reads <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶתְגָּרֶה‎</span>, for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶגְרַע‎</span>, on internal grounds; others: ***withdraw*** (thy needs) [[BDB#Sm|Sm]] (compare [[Ezek.16.27|Ezek 16:27]]); ***withdraw*** (mine eye) [[BDB#Ges|Ges]] [[BDB#Ke|Ke]]. **Niph\`al** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְנִגְרַע‎</span> consecutive [[Lev.27.18|Lev 27:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְנִגְרְעָה‎</span> [[Num.36.3|Num 36:3]] ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִגָּרַע‎</span> [[Num.27.4|Num 27:4]]; [[Num.36.4|Num 36:4]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִגָּרֵ֑עַ‎</span> [[Num.36.3|Num 36:3]] 1 plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִגָּרַע‎</span> [[Num.9.7|Num 9:7]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִגְרָע‎</span> [[Exod.5.11|Exod 5:11]] — 

**1.** ***be withdrawn*** with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> [[Num.36.3|Num 36:3]] (twice in verse); [[Num.36.4|Num 36:4]] (inheritance, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נחלה‎</span>); [[Num.27.4|Num 27:4]] a name out of a family; [[Exod.5.11|Exod 5:11]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵעֲבֹדַתְכֶם דָּבָר‎</span>); impersonal [[Lev.27.18|Lev 27:18]] (***abatement shall be made***). 

**2.** ***be restrained*** followed by infinitive [[Num.9.7|Num 9:7]]. ***Pi\`el*** ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְגָרַע‎</span> [[Job.36.27|Job 36:27]] ***withdraw*** = ***draw up*** with accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִטְפֵימָֿ֑יִם‎</span> compare [[BDB#Di|Di]] (on other hand, [[BDB#De|De]] and others ***draw down, let down***).
