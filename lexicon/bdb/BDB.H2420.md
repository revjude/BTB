 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִידָה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==riddle, enigmatic, perplexing saying or question==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Judg.14.12|Judg 14:12]] + 6 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִידָתִי‎</span> [[Judg.14.18|Judg 14:18]]; [[Ps.49.5|Ps 49:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִידָֽתְךָ‎</span> [[Judg.14.13|Judg 14:13]] plural absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִידוֺת‎</span> [[1Kgs.10.1|1Kgs 10:1]] + 4 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִידֹת‎</span> [[Num.12.8|Num 12:8]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִידֹתָם‎</span> [[Prov.1.6|Prov 1:6]] — 

**1.** ***riddle*** = dark, obscure utterance [[Num.12.8|Num 12:8]] ([[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]; opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּהאֶֿלפֶּֿה‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרְאֶה‎</span>); of something put indirectly and needing interpretation; — an allegory <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חוד ח‎</span> [[Ezek.17.2|Ezek 17:2]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְשֹׁל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָשָׁל‎</span>); allegorical and figurative sayings [[Prov.1.6|Prov 1:6]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְלִיצָה מָשָׁל‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְרֵי חֲכָמִים‎</span> [[BDB#Di|Di]]); enigmatic sentences and comparisons, declaring fate of Chaldeans [[Hab.2.6|Hab 2:6]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְלִיצָה‎</span>; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָשָׁל‎</span>), perplexing moral problem [[Ps.49.5|Ps 49:5]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּתַח‎</span> ***open, propound***; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָשָׁל‎</span>), a lesson taught indirectly [[Ps.78.2|Ps 78:2]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִנִּי קֶדֶם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span>, with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִבִּיעַ‎</span>; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָשָׁל‎</span>). 

**2.** ***riddle, enigma***, to be guessed, in [[Judg.14|Judg 14]]: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** with vb. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חוּד‎</span> [[Judg.14.12|Judg 14:12]]; [[Judg.14.13|Judg 14:13]]; [[Judg.14.16|Judg 14:16]] ***propound a riddle***. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** with vb. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִגִּיד‎</span> ***tell*** (give answer to) ***a riddle*** [[Judg.14.14|Judg 14:14]]; [[Judg.14.15|Judg 14:15]]; [[Judg.14.17|Judg 14:17]]; [[Judg.14.19|Judg 14:19]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** with vb. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מצא‎</span> ***find out a riddle*** [[Judg.14.18|Judg 14:18]]. 

**3.** ***perplexing questions*** by which queen of Sheba put Solomon to the test (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִסָּה‎</span>) [[1Kgs.10.1|1Kgs 10:1]] = [[2Chr.9.1|2Chr 9:1]]. 

**4.** with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵבִין‎</span> skilled in ***double-dealing*** [[Dan.8.23|Dan 8:23]] ([[BDB#Bev|Bev]]).
