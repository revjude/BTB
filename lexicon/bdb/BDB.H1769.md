 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּיבֹן‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּיבוֺן‎</span> **proper name, of a location** — 

**1.** city in Moab ([[BDB#MI|MI]]<sup>21; 28</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דיבן‎</span>, compare ib.<sup>1</sup> adjective, of a people <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הדיבני‎</span>) — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּיבֹן‎</span> [[Num.21.30|Num 21:30]]; [[Num.32.3|Num 32:3]]; [[Num.32.34|Num 32:34]] (built up or at by Gad) [[Isa.15.2|Isa 15:2]] in territory of Gad, hence <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּיבֹן גָּד֑‎</span> [[Num.33.45|Num 33:45]]; [[Num.33.46|Num 33:46]] (compare on these verses &amp; [[Num.32.34|Num 32:34]] [[BDB#MI|MI]]<sup>10 f.</sup>); also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּיבוֺן‎</span> [[Josh.13.9|Josh 13:9]]; [[Josh.13.17|Josh 13:17]]; [[Jer.48.22|Jer 48:22]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ישֶׁבֶת בַּת דִּיבוֺן‎</span> [[Jer.48.18|Jer 48:18]] = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּימוֺן‎</span> [[Isa.15.9|Isa 15:9]], see also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵי דִימוֺן‎</span> v[[Isa.15.9|Isa 15:9]] = Arnon, compare [[BDB#Che|Che]] &amp; [[BDB#Hpt|Hpt]]<sup>ZA 1887, 268</sup>; — modern ***Dîbân***, north of Arnon, compare [[BDB#Seetzen|Seetzen]]<sup>:Reisen i. 409</sup> [[BDB#Tristr|Tristr]]<sup>Land of Moab 132 ff.</sup> [[BDB#Bäd|Bd]]<sup>Pal 193</sup>.


**2.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּיבֹן‎</span> [[Neh.11.25|Neh 11:25]] place in Judah, toward south = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּימוֺנָה‎</span> [[Josh.15.22|Josh 15:22]], compare [[BDB#Hpt|Hpt]]<sup>l.c</sup>.; conjectures by [[BDB#Kn|Kn]] [[BDB#Ke|Ke]] (compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]) to be ***Tell ed Dheib*** ([[BDB#vdVelde|van de Velde]]<sup>Mem. 252</sup>) called also Ehdeib ([[BDB#Rob|Rob]]<sup>BR ii. 102</sup>).
