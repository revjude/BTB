 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁבָט‎</span> **proper name, month** ==Shebat, 11th month== (post-exilic) = Feb.-March; loan-word from Babylonian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***Šabâ‰u***</u></abbr> ([[BDB#COT|COT]]<sup>[[Neh.1.1|Neh 1:1]]</sup> [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>WB</sup>), [[Zech.1.7|Zech 1:7]] (derived from <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***šabâ‰u***</u></abbr>, ***strike, kill destroy*** ([[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>Prol 38</sup>; WB), [[BDB#Lyon|Lyon]]<sup>Bib Sacr Apr. (1884), 384</sup> [[BDB#Jen|Jen]]<sup>ZA iv (1889), 273</sup> [[BDB#M-A|Muss-Arn]]<sup>JBL xi (1892) 171 f.</sup> and others, as month of ***destroying*** rain; another conjecture in [[BDB#Zim|Zim]]<sup>KAT 3. 594 n</sup>; this month called <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שבט‎</span> also in Nabataean, Palmyrene, see [[BDB#Ldzb|Lzb]] [[BDB#Cook|SAC]]<sup>111</sup>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁבִי‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁבְיָה‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁבִית‎</span> see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁבה‎</span>.
