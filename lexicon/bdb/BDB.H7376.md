 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רָטַשׁ‎</span>] **verb Pi\`el** ==dash in pieces== (compare [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְטַשׁ‎</span> cast away, reject; but possibly akin to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָטַשׁ‎</span>); — 

**Pi\`el** ***Imperfect*** 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּרַטֵּשׁ‎</span> [[2Kgs.8.12|2Kgs 8:12]] their children ***thou will dash in pieces***; 3 feminine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קְשָׁתוֺת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְעָרִים תְּרַטַּשְׁנָה‎</span> [[Isa.13.18|Isa 13:18]], but probably corrupt, [[BDB#Du|Du]] conjecture <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְעָרוֺת תְּרֻטַּשְׁנָה‎</span> compare [[BDB#Che|Che]]. **Pu\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵם עַלֿ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּנִים רֻטָּ֑שָׁה‎</span> [[Hos.14.14|Hos 14:14]] ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְרֻטְּשׁוּ‎</span> [[Isa.13.16|Isa 13:16]]; [[Nah.3.10|Nah 3:10]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְרֻטָּ֑שׁוּ‎</span> [[Hos.14.1|Hos 14:1]], all of children.
