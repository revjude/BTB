**3.** ***measured portion, extent***, stretch (of wall) [[Neh.3.11|Neh 3:11]]; [[Neh.3.19|Neh 3:19]]; [[Neh.3.20|Neh 3:20]]; [[Neh.3.21|Neh 3:21]]; [[Neh.3.24|Neh 3:24]]; [[Neh.3.27|Neh 3:27]]; [[Neh.3.30|Neh 3:30]], compare [[Ezek.45.3|Ezek 45:3]].
