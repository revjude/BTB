 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁעַטְנֵז‎</span> **noun masculine** ==mixed stuff== (perhaps of Egyptian origin; [[BDB#Kn|Kn]] derives from Coptic ***saht, woven, + nudj, false*** (Peyron<sup>Lex 224. 133</sup>), and thinks originally <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁעַטְנֹז‎</span>; [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] has <span class="bdbLangGreek">κίβδηλος</span> ***spurious***); — a kind of cloth forbidden for garments; defined [[Deut.22.11|Deut 22:11]] by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צֶמֶר וּפִשְׁתִּים יַחְדָּו‎</span>, and [[Lev.19.19|Lev 19:19]] ([[BDB#H|H]]) by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּלְאַיִם‎</span>. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁעִיר‎</span> see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׂעִיר‎</span>.
