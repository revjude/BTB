**Qal** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Deut.7.7|Deut 7:7]] + 4 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָֽשְׁקָה‎</span> [[Gen.34.8|Gen 34:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָשַׁקְתָּ‎</span> [[Isa.38.17|Isa 38:17]] (but see below); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְחָֽשַׁקְתָּ֫‎</span> [[Deut.21.11|Deut 21:11]] — ***be attached to***, only figurative = ***love***, a woman followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span> [[Gen.34.8|Gen 34:8]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) [[Deut.21.11|Deut 21:11]] elsewhere of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s; love for Israel [[Deut.7.7|Deut 7:7]]; [[Deut.10.15|Deut 10:15]], and of love to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ps.91.14|Ps 91:14]] followed by accusative of congnate meaning with verb+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span> and infinitive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כלחֵֿשֶׁק שְׁלֹמה אשׁר ח‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִבְנוֺת‎</span> [[1Kgs.9.19|1Kgs 9:19]] =[[2Chr.8.6|2Chr 8:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָשַׁקְתָּ נַפְשִׁי מִשַּׁחַת‎</span> [[Isa.38.17|Isa 38:17]] literally ***thou hast loved my soul out of the pit***, i.e. lovingly delivered it; but read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָשַׂכְתָּ‎</span> ***thou hast held back, kept***, from [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] [[BDB#Lo|Lo]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Di|Di]], or <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲשׂךְ‎</span> ***hold back*** (Imperative), so [[BDB#Du|Du]].
