 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָתִיד‎</span>, [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָתוּד‎</span>] **adjective** ==ready, prepared==; — 

**1.** absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָתִיד לַכִּידוֺר‎</span> [[Job.15.24|Job 15:24]] a king ***ready for the onset***; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲתִדִים לַיּוֺם‎</span> [[Esth.3.14|Esth 3:14]] ***ready for the day***, so [[Esth.8.13|Esth 8:13]] [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] ([[BDB#Kt|Kt]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עתודים‎</span>). 

**2.** ***ready*** = skilled <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָעֲתִידִים עֹרֵר‎</span> [[Job.3.8|Job 3:8]] ***those skilled in rousing*** Leviathan. 

**3.** ***prepared*** = impending (compare Late Hebrew = ***future***), feminine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְחָשׁ עֲתִדֹת‎</span> [[Deut.32.35|Deut 32:35]] ***the impending things are hastening*** (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קָרוֺב יוֺם אֵידָם‎</span>). 

**4.** ***prepared*** = stored up, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ועתידתיהם‎</span> [[Isa.10.13|Isa 10:13]] [[BDB#Kt|Kt]] ([[BDB#Qr|Qr]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַעֲתוּדֹתַיהֶם‎</span>, to differentiate it from above) ***and their stores*** have I plundered.
