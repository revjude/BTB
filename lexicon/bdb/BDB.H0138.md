 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎(אֲדֹיִּנָה) אֲדֹנִיָּ֫הוּ‎</span> **proper name, masculine** (***my Lord is Yahweh***, compare Phoenician <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אדנאשמן‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אדנבעל‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אדנשמש‎</span> etc., in Assyrian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***Aduniba' al***</u></abbr> [[BDB#Schr|Schr]]<sup>KB II, 172</sup>) 

**1.** fourth son of David [[1Kgs.1.8|1Kgs 1:8]] + 11 t., [[1Kgs.2.13|1Kgs 2:13]] + 5 t. (= <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲדֹנִיָּה‎</span> [[2Sam.3.4|2Sam 3:4]]; [[1Kgs.1.5|1Kgs 1:5]]; [[1Kgs.1.7|1Kgs 1:7]]; [[1Kgs.1.18|1Kgs 1:18]]; [[1Kgs.2.28|1Kgs 2:28]]; [[1Chr.3.2|1Chr 3:2]]). 

**2.** a Levite [[2Chr.17.8|2Chr 17:8]]. 

**3.** a chief of the people [[Neh.10.17|Neh 10:17]] (= <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲדֹנִיקָם‎</span> [[Neh.7.18|Neh 7:18]]; [[Ezra.2.13|Ezra 2:13]] compare [[Ezra.8.13|Ezra 8:13]]).
