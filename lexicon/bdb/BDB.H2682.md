**1.** ***grass***, as food for animals [[1Kgs.18.5|1Kgs 18:5]]; [[Job.40.15|Job 40:15]]; [[Ps.104.14|Ps 104:14]]; [[Ps.147.8|Ps 147:8]]; [[Prov.27.25|Prov 27:25]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֶּשֶׁא‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵשֶׂב‎</span>), [[Isa.15.6|Isa 15:6]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֶּשֶׁא‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶרֶק‎</span>); specifically of ***leeks*** (as still sometimes in Aramaic, see [[BDB#Löw|Löw]]<sup>pp. 226, 228</sup>) [[Num.11.5|Num 11:5]] (see [[BDB#Di|Di]]); in simile of abundant growth [[Isa.44.4|Isa 44:4]] (see [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Che|Che]]); on [[Isa.35.7|Isa 35:7]] see I.  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָצִיר‎</span> ***above*** 

**2.** as type of what is quickly perishing [[Job.8.12|Job 8:12]], hence figurative of perishing enemies <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲצִיר גַּגּוֺת‎</span> [[Isa.37.27|Isa 37:27]] = [[2Kgs.19.26|2Kgs 19:26]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֶּשֶׁא‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵשֶׂב‎</span>), [[Ps.129.6|Ps 129:6]], i.e. having no depth of root; of wicked, soon to be cut down [[Ps.37.2|Ps 37:2]] with special reference to Israel's oppressors [[Isa.40.6|Isa 40:6]] (\|\| [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎השׂרה‎</span>] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ציץ‎</span>), v[[Isa.40.7|Isa 40:7]] (\|\| ***id.***), v[[Isa.40.7|Isa 40:7]] v[[Isa.40.8|Isa 40:8]] (\|\| ***id.***), [[Isa.51.12|Isa 51:12]] in simile of frail man [[Ps.90.5|Ps 90:5]] man's days [[Ps.103.15|Ps 103:15]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ציץ השׂדה‎</span>).
