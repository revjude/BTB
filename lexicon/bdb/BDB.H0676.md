 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶצְבַּע‎</span> **noun feminine**<sup>[[Lev.14.6|Lev 14:6]]</sup> ==finger==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> absolute [[Isa.58.9|Isa 58:9]], construct [[Exod.8.15|Exod 8:15]] +; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶצְבָּעוֺ‎</span> [[Lev.4.6|Lev 4:6]] +; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶצְבָּעוֺת‎</span> [[Jer.52.21|Jer 52:21]], construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶצְבְּעֹת‎</span> [[2Sam.21.20|2Sam 21:20]] +, etc.; — 

**1.** ***finger***, especially 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***forefinger***, of priest, applying blood ([[BDB#P|P]]), [[Exod.29.12|Exod 29:12]]; [[Lev.4.6|Lev 4:6]]; [[Lev.4.17|Lev 4:17]]; [[Lev.4.25|Lev 4:25]] + 8 t.; applying oil [[Lev.14.16|Lev 14:16]] (twice in verse); [[Lev.14.27|Lev 14:27]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלַח א‎</span> of any one [[Isa.58.9|Isa 58:9]], sign of contempt. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** of God, as writing [[Exod.31.18|Exod 31:18]] ([[BDB#E|E]]) [[Deut.9.10|Deut 9:10]] = ***act*** of God [[Exod.8.15|Exod 8:15]] ([[BDB#J|J]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** plural ==of all the fingers== [[2Sam.21.20|2Sam 21:20]]a; fingers in general [[Isa.2.8|Isa 2:8]]; [[Isa.17.8|Isa 17:8]]; [[Isa.59.3|Isa 59:3]]; [[Ps.144.1|Ps 144:1]]; [[Prov.7.3|Prov 7:3]]; [[Song.5.5|Song 5:5]] of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ps.8.4|Ps 8:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֹרֶה בְא‎</span> [[Prov.6.13|Prov 6:13]] (of contemptuous gesture); as measure of thickness, [[Jer.52.21|Jer 52:21]] ***four fingers***. 

**2.** ***toes:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶצְבְּעֹת רַגְלָיו‎</span> [[2Sam.21.20|2Sam 21:20]] b, literally ***fingers of his feet***, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> \|\| [[1Chr.20.6|1Chr 20:6]] = fingers and toes.
