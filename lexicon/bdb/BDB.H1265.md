**1.** (literal) a noble tree, usually \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ארז‎</span> (except [[2Sam.6.5|2Sam 6:5]]; [[1Kgs.6.34|1Kgs 6:34]]; [[2Chr.3.5|2Chr 3:5]]; [[Ps.104.17|Ps 104:17]]; [[Isa.55.13|Isa 55:13]]; [[Hos.14.9|Hos 14:9]]; [[Nah.2.4|Nah 2:4]]); as standing and growing [[Isa.14.8|Isa 14:8]]; [[Isa.37.24|Isa 37:24]] = [[2Kgs.19.23|2Kgs 19:23]]; [[Isa.41.19|Isa 41:19]]; [[Isa.55.13|Isa 55:13]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲדַס‎</span>) [[Isa.60.13|Isa 60:13]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כבוד לבנון‎</span> = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ארז‎</span>) [[Ps.104.17|Ps 104:17]]. 

**2.** simile of luxuriance, stateliness [[Ezek.31.8|Ezek 31:8]], productiveness [[Hos.14.9|Hos 14:9]] ([[BDB#Now|Now]] thinks ***cypress***), figurative for spear-shafts [[Nah.2.4|Nah 2:4]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]]-[[BDB#St|St]], but [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פרשׁים‎</span>; figurative for mighty men [[Zech.11.2|Zech 11:2]].
