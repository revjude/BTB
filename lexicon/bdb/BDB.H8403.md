 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּבְנִית‎</span> **noun feminine** ==construction, pattern==, figure <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎֯‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תּ‎</span> absolute [[1Chr.28.19|1Chr 28:19]] construct [[Exod.25.9|Exod 25:9]] (twice in verse) + 14 t. + [[Ezek.8.10|Ezek 8:10]] (strike out [[BDB#Co|Co]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּבְנִיתוֺ‎</span> [[2Kgs.16.10|2Kgs 16:10]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַבְנִיתָם‎</span> [[Exod.25.40|Exod 25:40]] — 

**1.** apparently originally ***construction, structure***, yet only [[BDB#P|P]] &amp; late: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּבְנִית מִזְבַּה י‎</span> [[Josh.22.28|Josh 22:28]] compare [[Ps.144.12|Ps 144:12]] in simile <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵיכָל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּנוֺתֵינוּ כְזָוִיּוֺת מְחֻטָּבִים תּ‎</span> ***carved according to the construction of a palace, palace-fashion.*** 

**2.** ***pattern***, according to which anything is to be constructed ([[BDB#P|P]] &amp; late), of tabernacle [[Exod.25.9|Exod 25:9]], utensils of tabernacle v[[Exod.25.9|Exod 25:9]] v[[Exod.25.40|Exod 25:40]] an altar [[2Kgs.16.10|2Kgs 16:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּבְנִיתוֺ לְכָלֿ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעֲשֵׂהוּ‎</span> (distinguished from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּמוּת‎</span>); temple [[1Chr.28.11|1Chr 28:11]] compare v[[1Chr.28.12|1Chr 28:12]] chariot, i.e. cherubim [[1Chr.28.18|1Chr 28:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַלְאֲכוֺת הַתּ‎</span> [[1Chr.28.19|1Chr 28:19]] i.e. objects of which the patters is given. 

**3.** ***figure, image***, Hexateuch only [[BDB#D|D]], of idols in form of animals [[Deut.4.16|Deut 4:16]]; [[Deut.4.17|Deut 4:17]] (twice in verse); [[Deut.4.18|Deut 4:18]] (twice in verse); elsewhere late [[Isa.44.13|Isa 44:13]]; [[Ezek.8.10|Ezek 8:10]] (strike out [[BDB#B|B]] [[BDB#Co|Co]]) [[Ps.106.20|Ps 106:20]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָד‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תּ‎</span> [[Ezek.8.3|Ezek 8:3]]; [[Ezek.10.8|Ezek 10:8]] i.e. something like a hand.
