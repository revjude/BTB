 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָשַׁת‎</span> **verb** ==be dry, parched==; — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** 3 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְשׁוֺנָם בַּצָּמָא נָשָׁ֑תָּה‎</span> [[Isa.41.17|Isa 41:17]] (on dagesh forte affect. see [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:20i»§ 20i</sup>); also figurative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָָֽשְׁתָה גְבוּרָתָם‎</span> [[Jer.51.30|Jer 51:30]], ***their might is dry***, i.e. fails. **Niph\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 plural consecutive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְנִשְּׁתומַֿיִם מֵהַיָּ֑ם‎</span> [[Isa.19.5|Isa 19:5]] ***and water shall be dried up from the sea***; compare ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַנָָּֽתְשׁוּ מַיּם‎</span> [[Jer.18.14|Jer 18:14]], where read probably <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִנָָּֽשְׁחוּ‎</span> ([[BDB#Gf|Gf]] [[BDB#Gie|Gie]] and others).
