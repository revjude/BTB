 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָרוּם‎</span> **adjective** ==crafty, shrewd, sensible==; — 

**1.** ***crafty***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ע‎</span> as predicate, of serpent [[Gen.3.1|Gen 3:1]] plural as substantive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַחְשְׁבוֺת עֲרוּמִים‎</span> [[Job.5.12|Job 5:12]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְשׁוֺן ע‎</span> [[Job.15.5|Job 15:5]]. 

**2.** in Proverbs, in good sense (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֱוִיל‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּסִיל‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּתִי‎</span>); as attributive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָדָם עָרוּם‎</span> [[Prov.12.23|Prov 12:23]] ***a shrewd*** or ***sensible man***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ע‎</span> as substantive = ***id.***, v[[Prov.12.16|Prov 12:16]]; [[Prov.13.16|Prov 13:16]], so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָכְמַת ע‎</span> v[[Prov.13.8|Prov 13:8]] = ***prudent man*** [[Prov.14.15|Prov 14:15]]; [[Prov.22.3|Prov 22:3]]; [[Prov.27.12|Prov 27:12]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲרוּמִים‎</span> [[Prov.14.18|Prov 14:18]].
