 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְפַרְוַ֫יִם‎</span> **proper name, of a location** ==city conquered by king of Assyria==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> [[2Kgs.17.14|2Kgs 17:14]]; [[2Kgs.18.34|2Kgs 18:34]] = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְפַוָ֑יִם‎</span> [[Isa.36.19|Isa 36:19]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עִיר סְפַרְוָ֑יִם‎</span> [[2Kgs.19.13|2Kgs 19:13]] = [[Isa.37.13|Isa 37:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְפַרְוָ֑יִם‎</span> also [[2Kgs.17.31|2Kgs 17:31]]b [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] ([[BDB#Kt|Kt]]. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ספרים‎</span>); [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Σεπφαρουαιν</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Επφαρουαιμ</span>, etc., [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]<sup>L</sup></lookup> <span class="bdbLangGreek">Σεπφαρειμ</span>; — usually identification with ***Sippara*** (on a canal), between Bagdad and Babylon, a city in two parts, ***Sipp.*** of ***Samaš*** (modern ***Abbu Habba***), and ***Sipp. of Anunitu(m)*** on opposed to side of canal, hence dual <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> = ***the two Sipparas*** [[BDB#Schr|Schr]]<sup>COT [[2Kgs.17.24|2Kgs 17:24]]</sup>, compare [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>Par 209-212</sup> [[BDB#Tiele|Tiele]]<sup>Geschichte. 88</sup>; this recently disputed, in view of mention with Hamath and other northern cities ([[2Kgs.17.24|2Kgs 17:24]] and especially [[2Kgs.18.34|2Kgs 18:34]]), see [[BDB#Hal|Hal]]<sup>ZA ii. 401 ff.</sup> [[BDB#Di|Di]]<sup>[[Isa.36.19|Isa 36:19]]</sup> [[BDB#Wkl|Wkl]]<sup>Alttest, Unters. 101</sup> [[BDB#Benz|Benz]]<sup>[[2Kgs.17.24|2Kgs 17:24]]</sup> and others, who compare ***Šabara'in*** city conquered by Šalmanašarid IV ([[BDB#Wkl|Wkl]] in [[BDB#Schr|Schr]]<sup>KB ii. 276</sup>); but this by no means certain.
