 II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִדְבָּר‎</span><sub>270</sub> **noun masculine** ==wilderness== — [[Deut.32.10|Deut 32:10]] +; with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ה‎</span> locative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִדְבָּ֫רָה‎</span> [[Josh.18.12|Josh 18:12]] + 15 t.; construct [[Exod.15.22|Exod 15:22]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִדְבַּ֫רָה‎</span> [[1Kgs.19.15|1Kgs 19:15]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִדְבָּרָהּ‎</span> [[Isa.51.3|Isa 51:3]] — 

**1.** tracts of land, used for the pasturage of flocks and herds, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּֽשְׁאוּ נְאוֺת מִדְבָּר‎</span> ***the pastures of the wilderness put forth green grass*** [[Joel.2.22|Joel 2:22]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִרְעֲפוּ נ‎</span> ***the pastures of the wilderness drop*** (fertility) [[Ps.65.13|Ps 65:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָָֽבְשׁוּ נ‎</span> ***are dried up*** [[Jer.23.10|Jer 23:10]], compare [[Jer.9.9|Jer 9:9]]; [[Joel.1.19|Joel 1:19]]; [[Joel.1.20|Joel 1:20]]. 

**2.** ***uninhabited land***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִדְבָּר לֹאאָֿדָם בּוֺ‎</span> ***wilderness in which is no man*** [[Job.38.26|Job 38:26]] the abode of pelicans [[Ps.102.7|Ps 102:7]] wild asses [[Job.24.5|Job 24:5]]; [[Jer.2.24|Jer 2:24]] jackals [[Mal.1.3|Mal 1:3]] ostriches [[Lam.4.3|Lam 4:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִייִֿתְּנֵנִי בַמִּדְבָּר מְלוֺן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֹרְחִים וְֶאעֶזְבָה אֶתעַֿמִּי‎</span> ***O that I had in the wilderness a lodging place of wayfarers, that I might leave my people*** [[Jer.9.1|Jer 9:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טוֺב שֶׁבֶת בְּאֶרֶץ מִדְבָּר‎</span> ***better to dwell in a desert land***, than with a contentious woman [[Prov.21.19|Prov 21:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּאֶרֶץ מִדְבָּר וּבְתֹהוּ יְלֵל יְשִׁמֹן‎</span> ***in a desert land, and in a waste howling wilderness*** [[Deut.32.10|Deut 32:10]]. 

**3.** ***large tracts*** of such land bearing various names, in certain districts of which there might be towns and cities: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִשְׂאוּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִדְבָּר וְעָרָיו חֲצֵרִים תֵּשֵׁב קֵדָר‎</span> ***let the wilderness and its cities lift up*** (their voice), ***the villages that Kedar doth inhabit*** [[Isa.42.11|Isa 42:11]]. There were six cities in the wilderness of Judah [[Josh.15.61|Josh 15:61]]; [[Josh.15.62|Josh 15:62]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמִּדְבָּר‎</span> usually = wilderness of the wanderings [[Gen.14.6|Gen 14:6]]; [[Num.14.16|Num 14:16]]; [[Num.14.29|Num 14:29]]; [[Num.14.32|Num 14:32]]; [[Num.14.33|Num 14:33]] (twice in verse) +, or the great Arabian desert [[Judg.11.22|Judg 11:22]] +; but may also refer to any other [[Song.3.6|Song 3:6]]; [[Song.8.5|Song 8:5]]. Special tracts — (a) of the wilderness of the wandering were <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוּר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Exod.15.22|Exod 15:22]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סין‎</span>[[Exod.16.1|Exod 16:1]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סיני‎</span> [[Exod.19.1|Exod 19:1]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פארן‎</span> [[Num.13.26|Num 13:26]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צן‎</span> [[Num.20.1|Num 20:1]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קדשׁ‎</span> [[Ps.29.8|Ps 29:8]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אתם‎</span> [[Num.33.8|Num 33:8]] (b) in West Palestine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יהודה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Judg.1.16|Judg 1:16]]; [[Ps.63.1|Ps 63:1]] compare [[Josh.15.61|Josh 15:61]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מעוֺן‎</span> [[1Sam.23.24|1Sam 23:24]]; [[1Sam.23.25|1Sam 23:25]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זיף‎</span> [[1Sam.23.15|1Sam 23:15]]; [[1Sam.26.2|1Sam 26:2]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎באר שׁבע‎</span> [[Gen.21.14|Gen 21:14]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עין גדי‎</span> [[1Sam.24.2|1Sam 24:2]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תקוע‎</span> [[2Chr.20.20|2Chr 20:20]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ירואל‎</span> [[2Chr.20.16|2Chr 20:16]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גבעון‎</span> [[2Sam.2.24|2Sam 2:24]] (c) in Eastern Palestine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מואב‎</span> [[Deut.2.8|Deut 2:8]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אדום‎</span> [[2Kgs.3.8|2Kgs 3:8]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קדמות‎</span> [[Deut.2.26|Deut 2:26]]. 

**4.** figurative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְשַׂמְתִּיהָ כַמִּדְבָּר וְשַׁתִּהָ כְּאֶרֶץ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צִיָּה‎</span> ***and (lest I) make her as a wilderness and set her like a dry land*** [[Hos.2.5|Hos 2:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲמִדְבָּר הָיִיתִי לְיִשְׂרָאֵל‎</span> ***have I been a wilderness to Israel ?*** [[Jer.2.31|Jer 2:31]].
