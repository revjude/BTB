**1.** ***likeness, similitude***, of external appearance, chiefly in Ezek.: [[Ezek.1.5|Ezek 1:5]] (***likeness***, i.e. something that appeared like) so v[[Ezek.1.26|Ezek 1:26]]; [[Ezek.8.2|Ezek 8:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּמוּת כְּמַרְאֵה (אֵשׁׅ‎</span> (compare [[BDB#Co|Co]]), [[Ezek.10.1|Ezek 10:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּסֵּא‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּמַרְאֵה ד‎</span>; compare also [[Dan.10.16|Dan 10:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּדְמוּת בְּנֵי אָדָם‎</span> i.e. ***one like the sons of man; similitude, resemblance*** [[Ezek.1.5|Ezek 1:5]]; [[Ezek.1.10|Ezek 1:10]]; [[Ezek.1.16|Ezek 1:16]]; [[Ezek.1.22|Ezek 1:22]]; [[Ezek.1.26|Ezek 1:26]]; [[Ezek.10.10|Ezek 10:10]]; [[Ezek.10.21|Ezek 10:21]]; [[Ezek.10.22|Ezek 10:22]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּמוּת כְּמַרְאֵה אָדָם‎</span> [[Ezek.1.26|Ezek 1:26]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַרְאֵה ד‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּבוֺד י‎</span> v[[Ezek.1.28|Ezek 1:28]] also [[2Kgs.16.10|2Kgs 16:10]] (pattern of altar), [[2Chr.4.3|2Chr 4:3]] (images of oxen); of son in likeness of father [[Gen.5.3|Gen 5:3]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); so also of man in likeness of God [[Gen.1.26|Gen 1:26]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צֶלֶם‎</span>) [[Gen.5.1|Gen 5:1]] (both [[BDB#P|P]]); compare [[Isa.40.18|Isa 40:18]] ***what*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ד‎</span> ***will ye compare to him*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵל‎</span>) ? \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּמָּה‎</span> q. v. 

**2.** adverbially, ***in likeness of, like as*** [[Isa.13.4|Isa 13:4]] compare [[Ezek.23.15|Ezek 23:15]] &amp; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּד‎</span> [[Ps.58.5|Ps 58:5]]. — [[Ezek.1.13|Ezek 1:13]] read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּבֵינוֺת‎</span>, see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּין‎</span> and [[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#P|P]] Peters<sup>JBL 1892 40. 42</sup>. On <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּמוּת אֶחָד‎</span> [[Ezek.1.16|Ezek 1:16]]; [[Ezek.10.10|Ezek 10:10]] (apparently masculine) compare [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] &amp; [[BDB#Sm|Sm]] who translate ***the likeness of one had they all four***; [[BDB#Co|Co]] reads <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אחת‎</span>.
