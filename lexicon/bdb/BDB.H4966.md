 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָתוֺק‎</span> **adjective** ==sweet==, and **noun [masculine]** ==sweetness== (on form see [[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup> BN 30, 60</sup>, compare [[BDB#Ba|Ba]]<sup>NB 13, 36</sup>); — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Judg.14.14|Judg 14:14]] + 9 t.; feminine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְתוּקָה‎</span> [[Eccl.5.11|Eccl 5:11]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְתוּקִים‎</span> [[Ps.19.11|Ps 19:11]] — 

**1.** ***sweet***, of honey [[Judg.14.14|Judg 14:14]] (where <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> = substantive ***a sweet thing***), v[[Judg.14.18|Judg 14:18]] (both Samson's riddle), [[Prov.24.13|Prov 24:13]] so = ***sweetness***, [[Ezek.3.3|Ezek 3:3]] more generally, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּלמַֿר מ‎</span> [[Prov.27.7|Prov 27:7]] ***every bitter thing is sweet*** to one hungry; figurative [[Ps.19.11|Ps 19:11]] (God's commandments, sweeter than honey), compare [[Prov.16.24|Prov 16:24]] (simile of pleasant words) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְחִכִּי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּפִרְיוֺ מ‎</span> [[Song.2.3|Song 2:3]] (i.e his caresses); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמָ֑ר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּמ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׂמִים מַר לְמ‎</span> [[Isa.5.20|Isa 5:20]] ***making bitter into sweet and sweet into bitter*** (obscuring moral distinctions; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָאֹמְרִים לָרַע טוֺב וְלַטּוֺב רָ֑ע‎</span>). 

**2.** = ***pleasant***, sleep of the toiler [[Eccl.5.11|Eccl 5:11]] light [[Eccl.1.17|Eccl 1:17]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טוֺב לַעֵינַיִם לִרְאוֺת אֶתהַֿשֶּׁמֶשׁ‎</span>).
