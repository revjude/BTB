**Qal** ***Perfect*** 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהָדַרְתָּ֫‎</span> [[Lev.19.32|Lev 19:32]] ***Imperfect*** 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תֶּהְדַּר‎</span> [[Exod.23.3|Exod 23:3]]; [[Lev.19.15|Lev 19:15]] ***Passive participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָדוּר‎</span> [[Isa.63.1|Isa 63:1]], plural<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲדוּרִים‎</span> [[Isa.45.2|Isa 45:2]] (possibly <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הררים‎</span> [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">ὄρη</span>, compare [[BDB#Di|Di]], but see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הר‎</span>) — 

**1.** ***swell***, only passive participle plural [[Isa.45.2|Isa 45:2]] (si vera lectio) in neuter sense, of hills, ***swelling places, swells*** of land (made level before Cyrus). 

**2.** ***honour, pay honour to***, followed by accusative; in good sense <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהָדַרְתָּ פְּנֵי זָקֵן‎</span> [[Lev.19.32|Lev 19:32]] ([[BDB#H|H]]) ***and thou shalt honour the face of an old*** ***man*** (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִפְּנֵי שֵׂיבָה תָּקוּם‎</span>); in bad sense (of partiality, favouritism) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא תֶהְדַּר פְּנֵי גָדוֺל‎</span> [[Lev.19.15|Lev 19:15]] ([[BDB#H|H]]) (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא תִשָּׂא פְנֵידָֿל‎</span>); compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְדַל לֹא תֶּהְדַּר בְּרִיבוֺ‎</span> [[Exod.23.3|Exod 23:3]] ([[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]; either read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גָּדֹל‎</span> for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ודל‎</span>, — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ו‎</span> not needed at beginning of see, — so [[BDB#Kn|Kn]] [[BDB#SS|SS]] and others, yet [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">πένητα</span>; — or suppose balancing clause, as [[Lev.19.15|Lev 19:15]], to have fallen out, compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]). 

**3.** ***adorn***, only passive participle ***adorned, made splendid*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָדוֺר בִּלְבוּשׁוֺ‎</span> [[Isa.63.1|Isa 63:1]]. **Niph\`al** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶהְדָּ֑רוֺ‎</span> [[Lam.5.12|Lam 5:12]] ***were*** (not) ***honoured***, subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פְּנֵי זְקֵנִים‎</span>. **Hithpa\`el** ***Imperfect*** jussive 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּתְהַדַּר‎</span> [[Prov.25.6|Prov 25:6]] ***honour oneself***, i.e. ***claim honour***.
