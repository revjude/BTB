$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of man, only in poetry, as created by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ps.139.13|Ps 139:13]] as the most sensitive and vital part, in metaphor of one wounded by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s arrows [[Job.16.13|Job 16:13]]; [[Lam.3.13|Lam 3:13]].
