**Qal** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְטָבַל‎</span> consecutive [[Lev.4.6|Lev 4:6]] + 5 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְטָבַלְתְּ‎</span> consecutive [[Ruth.2.14|Ruth 2:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּטְבַלְתֶּם‎</span> consecutive [[Exod.12.22|Exod 12:22]] ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּטְבֹּל‎</span> [[Lev.9.9|Lev 9:9]] + 3 t.; 2 masculine singular suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּטְבְּלֵנִי‎</span> [[Job.9.31|Job 9:31]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּטְבְּלוּ‎</span> [[Gen.37.31|Gen 37:31]] — ***dip:*** 

**1.** transitive, ***dip*** a thing ***in***, with accusative of thing + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span>: in blood [[Gen.37.31|Gen 37:31]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), so especially in connection with sacrifices [[Lev.4.6|Lev 4:6]]; [[Lev.9.9|Lev 9:9]]; [[Lev.14.6|Lev 14:6]]; [[Lev.14.51|Lev 14:51]] (also in fresh water); in water, for purification [[Num.19.18|Num 19:18]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]); of dipping rod in honey [[1Sam.14.27|1Sam 14:27]], bread in vinegar [[Ruth.2.24|Ruth 2:24]], foot in oil [[Deut.33.24|Deut 33:24]] (poem); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּשַּׁחַת תִּטְבְּלֵנִי‎</span> [[Job.9.31|Job 9:31]] ***in the ditch dost thou dip me***; accusative omitted [[Exod.12.22|Exod 12:22]] ([[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]; in blood), [[2Kgs.8.15|2Kgs 8:15]] (in water); but also + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> = ***moisten with, with some of*** [[Lev.4.17|Lev 4:17]] (blood), [[Lev.14.16|Lev 14:16]] (oil). 

**2.** intransitive, ***dip (oneself)***, followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span>, [[2Kgs.5.14|2Kgs 5:14]] in Jordan (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רָחַץ בְּ‎</span> v[[2Kgs.5.10|2Kgs 5:10]] v[[2Kgs.5.12|2Kgs 5:12]]). **Niph\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִּטְבְּלוּ‎</span> [[Josh.3.15|Josh 3:15]] ***be dipped***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span> of water.
