 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁלֵו‎</span> ([[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>BN 43</sup>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁלֵרוּ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלֵרו‎</span> **adjective** ==quiet, at ease==; — 

**1.** of person ***at ease***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁלֵו‎</span> [[Job.16.12|Job 16:12]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁלֵיו‎</span> [[Job.21.23|Job 21:23]] ([[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>ii. 1.83</sup>); of nation, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלֵיו‎</span> (possibly diminutive ([[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>ii. l.144</sup>) [[Jer.49.31|Jer 49:31]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יוֺבֵ לָבֶטַח‎</span>); ***prosperous***, of city feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלֵוָה‎</span> [[Zech.7.7|Zech 7:7]] of wicked, masculine plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְשַׁלְוֵי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עוֺלָם‎</span> [[Ps.73.12|Ps 73:12]] ([[BDB#Re|Re]]<sup>H. iii. 131</sup> proposes <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִשְׁלָיוּ עֲוִילִים‎</span>). 

**2.** of land, ***quiet***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלֵשׁלֵוָה‎</span> [[1Chr.4.40|1Chr 4:40]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֹׁקֶטֶת‎</span>). 

**3.** in <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא יָדַע שָׁלֵו בְּבִטְנוֺ‎</span> [[Job.20.20|Job 20:20]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> is apparently abstract noun ***quiet, ease***, so [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#De|De]] and others read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁלְוָח‎</span> [[BDB#Siegf|Siegf]] [[BDB#Bu|Bu]], [[BDB#Du|Du]] (after [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא שָׁלֵו בְּמַטְמֹנוֺ‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁלֵו‎</span> [[Ezek.23.42|Ezek 23:42]] is corrupt, [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#Co|Co]] [[BDB#Berthol|Berthol]] (compare [[BDB#Da|Da]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁרוּ‎</span> ***sang***, Toy <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁרִים‎</span>, [[BDB#Krae|Krae]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רִים‎</span>.
