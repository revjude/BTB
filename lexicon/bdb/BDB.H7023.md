 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִיר‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Ps.62.4|Ps 62:4]]</sup> ==wall== (√ unknown); — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ק‎</span> [[Num.22.25|Num 22:25]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִר‎</span> [[Isa.22.5|Isa 22:5]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִיר‎</span> [[1Kgs.6.5|1Kgs 6:5]] +; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִירוֺת‎</span> [[1Kgs.6.16|1Kgs 6:16]] +, construct ***id.***, v[[1Kgs.6.5|1Kgs 6:5]] +; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִירֹתָיו‎</span> [[Exod.30.3|Exod 30:3]] +, etc.; — ***wall***, especially as flat surface: 

**1.**  usually of house or chamber: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** inner surface, [[Amos.5.19|Amos 5:19]]; [[1Sam.18.11|1Sam 18:11]] + 9 t., + (of temple) [[1Kgs.6.15|1Kgs 6:15]] a + v[[1Kgs.6.15|1Kgs 6:15]] b (but read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קוֺרוֺת‎</span> beams, with [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] The [[BDB#Kit|Kit]] [[BDB#Benz|Benz]]), v[[1Kgs.6.16|1Kgs 6:16]] v[[1Kgs.6.27|1Kgs 6:27]] v[[1Kgs.6.27|1Kgs 6:27]] + 6 t. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** outer surface, [[2Kgs.9.33|2Kgs 9:33]]; [[Ezek.33.30|Ezek 33:30]]; [[Isa.59.10|Isa 59:10]] (in simile); so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֶרֶם קִיר‎</span> [[Isa.25.4|Isa 25:4]] (but [[BDB#Lo|Lo]] [[BDB#Kn|Kn]] [[BDB#Gr|Gr]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Du|Du]] [[BDB#Che|Che]]<sup>Hpt</sup> and others read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קֹר‎</span>; JP Peters<sup>JBL xi (1892), 46</sup> proposes <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּדָד‎</span> [as [[Isa.28.2|Isa 28:2]]]); of temple [[1Kgs.6.5|1Kgs 6:5]] (twice in verse); [[1Kgs.6.6|1Kgs 6:6]], compare [[Ezek.41.6|Ezek 41:6]] (twice in verse). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** both inner and outer v[[Ezek.41.17|Ezek 41:17]] v[[Ezek.41.20|Ezek 41:20]] v[[Ezek.41.25|Ezek 41:25]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** indeterminate, [[Ezek.12.5|Ezek 12:5]]; [[Ezek.12.7|Ezek 12:7]]; [[Ezek.12.12|Ezek 12:12]]; [[Ezek.23.14|Ezek 23:14]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **e.** thickness specified, [[Ezek.41.5|Ezek 41:5]]; [[Ezek.41.9|Ezek 41:9]]; [[Ezek.41.12|Ezek 41:12]]; [[Ezek.41.13|Ezek 41:13]] (of Ezekiel's temple). **feminine** ==as enclosing==, [[2Kgs.4.10|2Kgs 4:10]] (of  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲלִיָּה‎</span>, q. v.); as separating [[Ezek.43.8|Ezek 43:8]]. 

**2.** ***wall*** of (temple-)court [[Ezek.8.7|Ezek 8:7]]; [[Ezek.8.8|Ezek 8:8]] (twice in verse) (in vision); of vineyard [[Num.22.25|Num 22:25]] (twice in verse) ([[BDB#J|J]]), of city [[Num.35.4|Num 35:4]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַחוֺמָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ק‎</span> [[Josh.2.15|Josh 2:15]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]) ***the*** (inner) ***surface of the wall.***
