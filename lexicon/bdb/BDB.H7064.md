 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קֵן‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Deut.22.6|Deut 22:6]]</sup> ==nest==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ק‎</span> [[Isa.10.14|Isa 10:14]] +; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קַןֿ‎</span> [[Deut.22.6|Deut 22:6]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִנּוֺ‎</span> [[Deut.32.11|Deut 32:11]]+, etc.; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִנִּים‎</span> [[Gen.6.14|Gen 6:14]] — 

**1.** ***nest***, of bird [[Deut.22.6|Deut 22:6]]; [[Job.39.27|Job 39:27]] in sim [[Isa.10.14|Isa 10:14]]; [[Prov.27.8|Prov 27:8]], ***of nestlings*** [[Deut.32.11|Deut 32:11]]; [[Isa.16.2|Isa 16:2]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קֵן מְשֻׁלָּח֑‎</span>), perhaps also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עִםקִֿנִּי‎</span> [[Job.29.18|Job 29:18]], but difficult, [[BDB#Che|Che]]<sup>JQ July, 1897, 578</sup> proposes <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זָקֵן‎</span> or <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּוִקֻנַי‎</span> (compare [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]); ***nest*** on high, of rock-dwellings [[Num.24.21|Num 24:21]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]) [[Jer.49.16|Jer 49:16]]; [[Obad.1.4|Obad 1:4]] figurative of Chaldeans' secure abode [[Hab.2.9|Hab 2:9]] temple as secure home for Israel [[Ps.84.4|Ps 84:4]] (figurative of swallow). 

**2.** ***cells***, like nests, in Noah's ark [[Gen.6.14|Gen 6:14]] ([[BDB#P|P]]; read probably <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִנִּים קִנִּים‎</span>, so [[BDB#Lag|Lag]] [[BDB#Ol|Ol]] [[BDB#Bu|Bu]]<sup>Urg.255</sup> [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Holz|Holz]] [[BDB#Gunk|Gunk]]).
