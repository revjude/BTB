**1. a.** ***terror***, [[Isa.54.14|Isa 54:14]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עשֶׁק‎</span>), [[Jer.17.17|Jer 17:17]] = object of terror, [[Jer.48.39|Jer 48:39]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׂחֹק‎</span>).
