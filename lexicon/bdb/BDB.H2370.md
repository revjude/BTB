**2.** ***see, behold:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** accusative of person [[Dan.3.25|Dan 3:25]], with <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎ל‎</span> v[[Dan.3.27|Dan 3:27]], accusative of thing [[Dan.5.5|Dan 5:5]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***see, witness*** dishonour of king (accusative) [[Ezra.4.14|Ezra 4:14]].
