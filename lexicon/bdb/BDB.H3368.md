 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָקָר‎</span> **adjective** ==precious, rare, splendid, weighty==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָקָר‎</span> [[1Sam.3.1|1Sam 3:1]] + 9 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְקַרֿ‎</span> [[Prov.17.27|Prov 17:27]] [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] ([[BDB#Kt|Kt]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וקר‎</span>; see below); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּיקַר‎</span> [[Ps.37.20|Ps 37:20]] feminine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְקָרָה‎</span> [[2Sam.12.30|2Sam 12:30]] + 14 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִקְרַת‎</span> [[Isa.28.16|Isa 28:16]] masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְקָרִים‎</span> [[Lam.4.2|Lam 4:2]] feminine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְקָדוֺת‎</span> [[1Kgs.5.31|1Kgs 5:31]] + 2 t. + [[Zech.14.6|Zech 14:6]] (see below); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְקָרֹת‎</span> [[1Kgs.7.9|1Kgs 7:9]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּיִקְּרוֺתֶ֑יךָ‎</span> [[Ps.45.10|Ps 45:10]] (so [[BDB#Baer|Baer]]; [[BDB#van d.H.|van d. H]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּיקְּ‎</span>); — 

**1.** ***precious:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***costly***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲבָנִים יְקָרוֺת‎</span> of costly building-stones [[1Kgs.5.31|1Kgs 5:31]]; [[1Kgs.7.9|1Kgs 7:9]]; [[1Kgs.7.10|1Kgs 7:10]]; [[1Kgs.7.11|1Kgs 7:11]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פִּנַּת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִקְרַת מוּסָד מוּסָּד‎</span> [[Isa.28.16|Isa 28:16]] ***a costly corner*** (-stone) of a foundation, — on construct see [[BDB#Da|Da]]<sup>Synt. § 28, R. 3, 6</sup>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺן יָקָר‎</span> ***costly wealth*** [[Prov.1.13|Prov 1:13]]; [[Prov.12.27|Prov 12:27]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺן יָקָר וְנָעִים‎</span> [[Prov.24.1|Prov 24:1]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***precious, highly valued:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶפֶשׁ יְקָרָה‎</span> [[Prov.6.26|Prov 6:26]] ***precious life***; opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוֺלֵל‎</span> [[Jer.15.19|Jer 15:19]] (of choice elements of character); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמָּֽוְתָה לַחֲסִידָיו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָקָר בְּעֵינֵי י‎</span> [[Ps.116.15|Ps 116:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְקָרָה הִיא מפניים‎</span> [[Prov.3.15|Prov 3:15]] ***she*** (wisdom) ***is more precious than rubies*** (see also **c**); ***prized***, of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חסד י‎</span> [[Ps.36.8|Ps 36:8]] = substantive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּנוֺת מְלָכִים בְּיִקְּרותֶ֑יךָ‎</span> [[Ps.45.10|Ps 45:10]] ***king's daughters are among thy precious*** ( = ***dear***) ***ones.*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶבֶן יְקָרָה‎</span> collective ***precious stones, jewels*** [[2Sam.12.30|2Sam 12:30]] = [[1Chr.20.2|1Chr 20:2]]; [[1Kgs.10.2|1Kgs 10:2]]; [[1Kgs.10.10|1Kgs 10:10]]; [[1Kgs.1.11|1Kgs 1:11]] = [[2Chr.9.1|2Chr 9:1]]; [[2Chr.9.9|2Chr 9:9]]; [[2Chr.9.10|2Chr 9:10]]; [[1Chr.29.3|1Chr 29:3]]; [[2Chr.3.6|2Chr 3:6]]; [[2Chr.32.27|2Chr 32:27]]; [[Ezek.27.22|Ezek 27:22]]; [[Ezek.28.13|Ezek 28:13]] (list of precious stones follows), [[Dan.11.38|Dan 11:38]] so also [[Lam.4.2|Lam 4:2]], read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַבְנֵי צִיּוֺן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַיְקָרִים‎</span> (for [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּנֵי וגו‎</span>), see [[BDB#VB|VB]]; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֹׁהַם יָקָר‎</span> [[Job.28.16|Job 28:16]] (see also [[Prov.3.15|Prov 3:15]] below **b**). 

**2.** ***rare***, [[1Sam.3.1|1Sam 3:1]] (compare √ Hiph\`il [[Isa.13.12|Isa 13:12]]). 

**3.** ***glorious, splendid*** (compare Aramaic), of the moon [[Job.31.26|Job 31:26]] as substantive = ***glory***, the wicked perish, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּיקַר כָּרִים‎</span> [[Ps.37.20|Ps 37:20]] ***like the glory of the pastures*** (i.e. like gay, but short-lived, flowers). 

**4.** (late and Aramaic) ***weighty, influential***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָקָר מֵחָכְמָה‎</span> [[Eccl.10.1|Eccl 10:1]] ***weightier than wisdom*** and honour is a little folly. — In [[Prov.17.27|Prov 17:27]] read [[BDB#Kt|Kt]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְקַררֿוּחַ‎</span> ***and one cool of spirit*** (see [[BDB#VB|VB]]); in [[Zech.14.6|Zech 14:6]] read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא יִהְיֶה אוֺר וְקָרוֺת וְקִפָּאוֺן‎</span> ***there shall not be light but cold and congelation*** (see ***id.*** and [[BDB#Vrss|Vrss.]]); **\*\***'***but***' is very dubious; read probably <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חוֺם וְקָרוֺת‎</span> ***there shall be neither heat nor cold***, etc. (We and others).
