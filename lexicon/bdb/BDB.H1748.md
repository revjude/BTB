 II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דּוּמָה‎</span> **proper name** 

**1. masculine** ==son of Ishmael== [[Gen.25.15|Gen 25:15]] = [[1Chr.1.30|1Chr 1:30]] compare probably ***Dumath al-Jandal*** in Northern Arabia in ***Jôf*** see [[BDB#Burckh|Burckhardt]]<sup>Travels in Syria 662f.</sup> [[BDB#Di|Di]]; also [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>Comm.</sup> on [[Isa.21.11|Isa 21:11]], Glaser<sup>Skizze ii. 392, 440.</sup> 

**2.** city in mountains of Judah [[Josh.15.52|Josh 15:52]], compare <span class="bdbLangGreek">Δοῦμα</span> in the Daroma, 17 miles from Eleutheropolis [[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>Onom. 250. 2nd ed. 258</sup>, = modern ***Daume***, southwest from Hebron [[BDB#vdVelde|van de Velde]]<sup>Memoir 308</sup> [[BDB#Guérin|Guérin]]<sup>Judée iii. 359 ff.</sup> compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]. 

**3.** name of Edom with mystic meaning [[Isa.21.11|Isa 21:11]] ***Duma*** = ***silence*** of death, desolation, [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] and others ([[BDB#JDMich|JDMich]] [[BDB#Ges|Ges]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] think **3 = 1**; [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] has <span class="bdbLangGreek">Ἰδουμαια</span>; [[BDB#Jer|Jerome]] interprets as ***part*** of Idumea; still another view below I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דּוּמָה‎</span>). 



 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דּוּמָם‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==silence== [[Hab.2.19|Hab 2:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶבֶן ד‎</span> ***stone of silence*** = ***dumb stone***; **adverb** ==in silence, silently==, [[Isa.47.5|Isa 47:5]]; [[Lam.3.26|Lam 3:26]] (? read here <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְרָמַם‎</span> from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דמם‎</span>). <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דּוּמֶּ֫שֶׂק‎</span> [[2Kgs.16.10|2Kgs 16:10]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דַּמֶּשֶׂק‎</span>.
