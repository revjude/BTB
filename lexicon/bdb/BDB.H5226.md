 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֹ֫כַח‎</span> **substantive** ==front==, always in **preposition** or **adverb** phrases, with suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִכְחוֺ‎</span> [[Exod.14.2|Exod 14:2]]; [[Ezek.46.9|Ezek 46:9]]: — 

**1.** as adverb accusative ***in front of, opposite to***, [[Exod.14.2|Exod 14:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִכְחוֺ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּחֲנוּ‎</span>, [[Exod.26.35|Exod 26:35]] the candlestick <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֹכַח הַשֻּׁלְחָן‎</span>, [[Exod.40.24|Exod 40:24]], [[Josh.18.17|Josh 18:17]] (in the \|\| [[Josh.15.7|Josh 15:7]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֹכַח לְ‎</span>: see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span> **5 c**), [[1Kgs.20.29|1Kgs 20:29]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַּחֲנוּ אֵלֶּה נֹכַח אֵלֶּה‎</span>, [[1Kgs.22.35|1Kgs 22:35]] (= [[2Chr.18.34|2Chr 18:34]]), [[Esth.5.1|Esth 5:1]] (twice in verse); [[Ezek.46.9|Ezek 46:9]] [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּי נִכְחוֺ יֵצֵא‎</span> shall go out ***in front of himself***, i.e. straightforward; compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶגְדּוֺ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶגֶד‎</span> **1 a** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***b***). Figurative [[Prov.5.21|Prov 5:21]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דַּרְכֵי אִישׁ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּי נֹכַח עֵינֵי י‎</span> ***in the sight*** of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s eyes are, etc., [[Judg.18.6|Judg 18:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דַּרְכְּכֶם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֹכַח י‎</span> ***before*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> is your way, i.e. under his eye and favourable regard. In the phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֹכַח פְּנֵי‎</span>, [[Jer.17.16|Jer 17:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָא שְׂפָתַי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֹכַח פָּנֶיךָ הָיָה‎</span> was ***before*** thy face, [[Lam.2.19|Lam 2:19]]; [[Ezek.14.3|Ezek 14:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִכְשׁוֺל עֲוֺנָם נָָֽתְנוּ נֹכַח פְּנֵיהֶם‎</span> (i.e. they contemplate it with pleasure), v[[Ezek.14.4|Ezek 14:4]] v[[Ezek.14.7|Ezek 14:7]]. 

**2.** combined with prepositions: — 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶלנֹֿכַח‎</span>, [[Num.19.4|Num 19:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פני אהל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מועד וְהִזָּה אלנֿ‎</span> and sprinkle it ***towards the front*** of, etc. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׃לְֹנכַח‎</span> 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***a***) as **adverb** [[Prov.4.25|Prov 4:25]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יביטו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עיניך לנ‎</span> let thine eyes look ***to the front*** or ***right on*** (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ועפיך‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַישִׁרוּ נֶגְדֶּ֑ךָ‎</span>); 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***b***) as **preposition** [[Gen.30.38|Gen 30:38]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְֹנכַח הַצּאֹן‎</span> ***in front of*** the flocks [[Gen.25.21|Gen 25:21]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיֶּעְתַּר ֗֗֗ לְֹנכַח אִשְׁתּוֺ‎</span> ***in fornt of***, i.e. ***on behalf of*** (compare ***pro***), his wife; 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***c***) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדנֹֿכַח‎</span>, [[Judg.19.10|Judg 19:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְבוּס‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיָּבאֹ עַדנֿ‎</span> ***as fas as in front of*** Jebus, [[Judg.20.43|Judg 20:43]]; [[Ezek.47.20|Ezek 47:20]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדנֹֿכַח לְ‎</span>).
