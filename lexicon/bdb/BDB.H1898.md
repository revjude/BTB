 II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָגָה‎</span> **verb** ==remove== ([[BDB#Thes|Thes]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יגה‎</span>) — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָגָה‎</span> [[Isa.27.8|Isa 27:8]] ([[BDB#Di|Di]] reads <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָגָהּ‎</span>); ***Infinitive absolute*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָגוֺ‎</span> [[Prov.25.4|Prov 25:4]]; [[Prov.25.5|Prov 25:5]] — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָגוֺ סִיגִים מִכָּ֑סֶף‎</span> [[Prov.25.4|Prov 25:4]] ***remove*** (literally ***a removing***) ***dross from silver***; as simile of following <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָגוֺ רָשָׁע לִפְנֵי מֶלֶךְ‎</span> [[Prov.25.5|Prov 25:5]] ***remove (the) wicked before a king***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָגָה בְּרוּחוֺ הַקָּשָׁה‎</span> [[Isa.27.8|Isa 27:8]], according to [[BDB#Di|Di]] (see above) ***he*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>) ***hath removed her*** (i.e. sent into exile; his people under figure of faithless wife) ***by his harsh wind***; so translates also [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] (***he scared her away***) [[BDB#Brd|Brd]] [[BDB#RV|RV]] [[BDB#VB|VB]]; [[BDB#De|De]] follows [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]], regards as elliptical relative clause, and renders by participle, 'sichtend (i.e. ***sifting, winnowing***) heftigen Hauches,' but conjectures <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָגֹה‎</span> (Infinitive absolute)
