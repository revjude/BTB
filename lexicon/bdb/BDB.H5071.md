**2.** ***freewill, voluntary, offering:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** including gifts used in erection and furnishing of tabernacle [[Exod.35.29|Exod 35:29]]; [[Exod.36.3|Exod 36:3]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); for first temple [[2Chr.31.14|2Chr 31:14]], second [[Ezra.1.6|Ezra 1:6]]; [[Ezra.8.28|Ezra 8:28]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** including <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עולה‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁלמים‎</span> [[Ezek.46.42|Ezek 46:42]] probably also [[Amos.4.5|Amos 4:5]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נדבות פִי‎</span> [[Ps.119.108|Ps 119:108]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** usually a <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֶבַח‎</span> for festival meals at Passover [[2Chr.35.8|2Chr 35:8]], Pentecost [[Deut.16.10|Deut 16:10]], Tabernacles [[Ezra.3.5|Ezra 3:5]] (besides the ritual offerings); distinguished from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶדֶר‎</span> votive offering [[Lev.7.16|Lev 7:16]]; [[Num.15.3|Num 15:3]]; [[Num.29.39|Num 29:39]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), [[Lev.22.18|Lev 22:18]]; [[Lev.22.21|Lev 22:21]]; [[Lev.22.23|Lev 22:23]]; [[Lev.23.38|Lev 23:38]] ([[BDB#H|H]]), [[Deut.12.6|Deut 12:6]]; [[Deut.12.17|Deut 12:17]]. — (Verbs used are <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הביא נ‎</span> [[Exod.35.29|Exod 35:29]]; [[Exod.36.3|Exod 36:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הקריב נ‎</span>[[Lev.22.18|Lev 22:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עשׂה נ‎</span> [[Num.15.3|Num 15:3]]; [[Ezek.46.12|Ezek 46:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נתן נ‎</span> [[Lev.23.38|Lev 23:38]]; [[Deut.16.10|Deut 16:10]].)
