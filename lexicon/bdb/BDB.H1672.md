 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּאַג‎</span> **verb** ==be anxious, concerned, fear== (Talmud <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּאֵיג‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּאִג‎</span> ***id.***) — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְדָאַג‎</span> consecutive [[1Sam.9.5|1Sam 9:5]]; [[1Sam.10.2|1Sam 10:2]] 2 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּאַגְתְּ‎</span> [[Isa.57.11|Isa 57:11]] ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִדְאָ֑ג‎</span> [[Jer.17.8|Jer 17:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶדְאַג‎</span> [[Ps.38.19|Ps 38:19]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֹּאֵג‎</span> [[Jer.38.19|Jer 38:19]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֹּאֲגִים‎</span> [[Jer.42.16|Jer 42:16]] —
