 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲרָדָה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==trembling, fear, anxiety== — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲרָדָה‎</span> [[Gen.27.33|Gen 27:33]] + 5 t. + [[Ezek.38.21|Ezek 38:21]] (read for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָרַי‎</span>, [[BDB#B|B]] [[BDB#Co|Co]] compare [[BDB#Dr|Dr]] [[1Sam.14.15|1Sam 14:15]]); construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶרְדַּת‎</span> [[1Sam.14.15|1Sam 14:15]]; [[Prov.29.25|Prov 29:25]] (see [[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>BN 113</sup>); plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲרָדוֺת‎</span> [[Ezek.26.16|Ezek 26:16]] — 

**1.** ***trembling, quaking*** (of terror ascribed to supernatural cause) [[1Sam.14.15|1Sam 14:15]] (twice in verse) (hence <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אלהים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> see [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַתִּרְגַּזהֿארץ‎</span>) compare [[Ezek.38.21|Ezek 38:21]] (see above); [[Dan.10.7|Dan 10:7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קול ח‎</span> [[Jer.30.5|Jer 30:5]] ***voice of trembling***; followed by genitive object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָדָם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Prov.29.25|Prov 29:25]] ***trembling before man***; opposed to pleasure [[Isa.21.4|Isa 21:4]] ***tremblings*** (plural) [[Ezek.26.16|Ezek 26:16]] under figure of garment (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ילבשׁו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span>), of effect on coast-princes of fall of Tyre. 

**2.** ***anxious care*** [[2Kgs.4.13|2Kgs 4:13]], accusative of congnate meaning with verb with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָרַד‎</span>.
