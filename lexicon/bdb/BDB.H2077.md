 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֶ֫בַח‎</span><sub>162</sub> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Judg.16.23|Judg 16:23]]</sup> ==sacrifice== **\*\***Phoenician <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זבח‎</span> of an offering of bread and oil CIS<sup>i. 165, 12; 167. 8</sup>, compare RS<sup>Semitic 205 (2nd ed. 222)</sup>. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> absolute [[Gen.31.54|Gen 31:54]] +; construct [[Exod.34.25|Exod 34:25]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זָ֑בַח‎</span> [[Lev.17.8|Lev 17:8]] + 16 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִבְחוֺ‎</span> [[Lev.7.16|Lev 7:16]]; [[Ezek.34.15|Ezek 34:15]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִבְחֲכֶם‎</span> [[Lev.19.6|Lev 19:6]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זְבָחִים‎</span> [[Gen.46.1|Gen 46:1]] +; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִבְחֵי‎</span> [[Lev.17.5|Lev 17:5]] +; suffix 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִבְחֵיהֶם‎</span> [[Lev.17.5|Lev 17:5]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זְבָחֵימוֺ‎</span> [[Deut.32.38|Deut 32:38]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִבְחֹתָם‎</span> [[Hos.4.19|Hos 4:19]] etc.; — the common and most ancient sacrifice, whose essential rite was eating the flesh of the victim at a feast in which the god of the clan shared by receiving the blood and fat pieces. In the older literature it is distinguished from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִנְחָה‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֹלָה‎</span>, in the later literature from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַטָּאת‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָשָׁם‎</span>. **I.** ==general name for all sacrifices eaten at feasts==: — 

**1.** of the God of Israel [[Gen.46.1|Gen 46:1]]; [[Exod.10.25|Exod 10:25]]; [[Exod.18.12|Exod 18:12]] ([[BDB#E|E]]) [[Hos.3.4|Hos 3:4]]; [[Hos.6.6|Hos 6:6]]; [[Hos.9.4|Hos 9:4]]; [[Amos.5.25|Amos 5:25]]; [[Isa.1.11|Isa 1:11]]; [[Isa.19.21|Isa 19:21]]; [[Deut.12.27|Deut 12:27]]; [[Deut.18.3|Deut 18:3]]; [[1Sam.2.13|1Sam 2:13]]; [[1Sam.2.29|1Sam 2:29]]; [[1Sam.3.14|1Sam 3:14]]; [[1Sam.6.15|1Sam 6:15]]; [[1Sam.9.12|1Sam 9:12]]; [[1Sam.9.13|1Sam 9:13]]; [[1Sam.15.22|1Sam 15:22]] (twice in verse); [[1Sam.16.3|1Sam 16:3]]; [[1Sam.16.5|1Sam 16:5]] (twice in verse); [[2Sam.15.12|2Sam 15:12]]; [[1Kgs.8.62|1Kgs 8:62]]; [[1Kgs.12.27|1Kgs 12:27]]; [[2Kgs.5.17|2Kgs 5:17]]; [[2Kgs.16.15|2Kgs 16:15]]; [[Ps.40.7|Ps 40:7]]; [[Ps.50.8|Ps 50:8]]; [[Ps.51.18|Ps 51:18]]; [[Ps.51.19|Ps 51:19]]; [[Prov.15.8|Prov 15:8]]; [[Prov.21.3|Prov 21:3]]; [[Prov.21.27|Prov 21:27]]; [[Isa.43.23|Isa 43:23]]; [[Isa.43.24|Isa 43:24]]; [[Isa.56.7|Isa 56:7]]; [[Jer.6.20|Jer 6:20]]; [[Jer.7.21|Jer 7:21]]; [[Jer.7.22|Jer 7:22]]; [[Jer.17.26|Jer 17:26]]; [[Jer.33.18|Jer 33:18]]; [[Ezek.40.42|Ezek 40:42]]; [[Ezek.44.11|Ezek 44:11]]; [[1Chr.29.21|1Chr 29:21]] (twice in verse); [[2Chr.7.1|2Chr 7:1]]; [[2Chr.7.4|2Chr 7:4]]; [[2Chr.7.5|2Chr 7:5]]; [[2Chr.7.12|2Chr 7:12]]; [[Neh.12.43|Neh 12:43]]; [[Dan.9.27|Dan 9:27]]; [[Eccl.4.17|Eccl 4:17]]. They should be <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִבְחֵי צֶדֶק‎</span> ***sacrifices of righteousness*** (offered in righteousness by the righteous) [[Deut.33.19|Deut 33:19]]; [[Ps.4.6|Ps 4:6]]; [[Ps.51.21|Ps 51:21]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִבְחֵי הַבְהָבַי‎</span> ***sacrifices of gifts to me*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>) [[Hos.8.13|Hos 8:13]] the temple is <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּית זָבַ֑ח‎</span> [[2Chr.7.12|2Chr 7:12]] they should not be <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רִיב‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> ***sacrifices of strife***, where strife prevails [[Prov.17.1|Prov 17:1]]. 

**2.** of other deities [[Exod.34.15|Exod 34:15]]; [[Num.25.2|Num 25:2]]; [[Deut.32.38|Deut 32:38]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]) [[Judg.16.23|Judg 16:23]]; [[2Kgs.10.19|2Kgs 10:19]]; [[2Kgs.10.24|2Kgs 10:24]]; [[Hos.4.19|Hos 4:19]]; [[Isa.57.7|Isa 57:7]]; [[Ezek.20.28|Ezek 20:28]] these are <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִבְחֵי מֵתִים‎</span> ***sacrifices to dead things*** (lifeless gods, opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> as living), [[Ps.106.28|Ps 106:28]]. **II.** ==there are several kinds of== <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זְבָחִים‎</span> which are gradually distinguished: 

**1.** ***the covenant sacrifice***, between Jacob and Laban [[Gen.31.54|Gen 31:54]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), with God [[Ps.50.5|Ps 50:5]] (compare [[Exod.24.5|Exod 24:5]]; [[1Sam.11.15|1Sam 11:15]]). 

**2.** ***the passover*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֶבַח חַג‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַפֶּסַח‎</span> [[Exod.34.25|Exod 34:25]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זבח פסח‎</span> [[Exod.12.27|Exod 12:27]] ([[BDB#J|J]]); and probably also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זבחי‎</span> [[Exod.23.18|Exod 23:18]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]) = [[Exod.34.25|Exod 34:25]] (***id.***) 

**3.** ***annual sacrifice*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֶבַח הַיָּמִים‎</span> [[1Sam.1.21|1Sam 1:21]]; [[1Sam.2.19|1Sam 2:19]]; [[1Sam.20.6|1Sam 20:6]], compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִשְׁפָּחָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> sacrifice of the clan [[1Sam.20.29|1Sam 20:29]]. 

**4.** ***thank offering*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎(הַ)תּוֺדָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> [[Lev.7.12|Lev 7:12]]; [[Lev.22.29|Lev 22:29]]; [[Ps.107.22|Ps 107:22]]; [[Ps.116.17|Ps 116:17]], which is given as <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קָרְבָּנוֺ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִבְחוֺ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַזָּבַח‎</span> [[Lev.7.16|Lev 7:16]]; [[Lev.7.17|Lev 7:17]] and as a variety of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלָמִים‎</span> [[Lev.7.13|Lev 7:13]]; [[Lev.7.15|Lev 7:15]] it is implied in <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִבְחֵי תְרוּעָה‎</span> [[Ps.27.6|Ps 27:6]] yet <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֶבַח‎</span> is generic with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תּוֺדָה‎</span> [[2Chr.29.31|2Chr 29:31]] (twice in verse). 

**5.** in ritual of [[BDB#H|H]] &amp; [[BDB#P|P]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זבחים‎</span> are defined by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁלמים‎</span> (q. v.) [[Lev.3.1|Lev 3:1]] + 39 t. Leviticus, Numbers, [[1Sam.10.8|1Sam 10:8]]; [[1Kgs.8.63|1Kgs 8:63]]; [[2Chr.30.22|2Chr 30:22]]; [[2Chr.33.16|2Chr 33:16]]; [[Prov.7.14|Prov 7:14]], but sometimes (rarely) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> alone is used in this sense, with or without suffix, where the meaning is plain from the context: [[Lev.17.5|Lev 17:5]]; [[Lev.17.7|Lev 17:7]]; [[Lev.17.8|Lev 17:8]]; [[Lev.19.6|Lev 19:6]]; [[Lev.23.37|Lev 23:37]] ([[BDB#H|H]]) [[Num.15.3|Num 15:3]]; [[Num.15.5|Num 15:5]]; [[Num.15.8|Num 15:8]]; [[Josh.22.26|Josh 22:26]]; [[Josh.22.29|Josh 22:29]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]). These ritual offerings are of three varieties <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תּוֺדָה‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶדֶר‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְדָבָה‎</span> [[Lev.7.12|Lev 7:12]]; [[Lev.7.16|Lev 7:16]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); in the several lists <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זבחים‎</span> are distinguished from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תודות‎</span> [[Amos.2.31|Amos 2:31]], from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נדרים‎</span> [[Num.15.8|Num 15:8]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) [[Deut.12.6|Deut 12:6]]; [[Deut.12.11|Deut 12:11]]; [[Jonah.1.16|Jonah 1:16]], from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נדבות‎</span> [[Amos.4.4|Amos 4:4]]f.; [[Deut.12.6|Deut 12:6]] and from the more comprehensive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁלמים‎</span> [[Num.15.8|Num 15:8]]; [[Josh.22.27|Josh 22:27]] ([[BDB#P|P]]). So also the sacrifice at the institution of the covenant at Horeb [[Exod.24.5|Exod 24:5]] ([[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]) and the sacrifice at the installation of Saul [[1Sam.11.5|1Sam 11:5]] are defined as <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זבחים שׁלמים‎</span>. Thus <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> seems not only to be used for all these special forms but also to include other festal sacrifices not defined in the codes of law. The ritual was the same for the entire class. They were all sacrifices for feasts in which the flesh of the victim was eaten by the offerers, except so far as the officiating priests had certain choice pieces and the blood and fat pieces went to the altar for God. The sacrifice at the institution of the covenant at Horeb, the Passover victim, and the ram of consecration, were special, in that there was an application of a portion of the blood to the persons and things which were to be consecrated, [[Exod.12.22|Exod 12:22]]; [[Exod.24.8|Exod 24:8]]; [[Lev.8.23|Lev 8:23]]. 

**6.** the slaughter of hostile nations is also a <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֶבַח‎</span> offered by God Himself, in which the vultures devour the flesh of the victims [[Isa.34.6|Isa 34:6]]; [[Jer.46.10|Jer 46:10]]; [[Ezek.39.17|Ezek 39:17]] (twice in verse); [[Ezek.39.19|Ezek 39:19]]; [[Zeph.1.7|Zeph 1:7]]; [[Zeph.1.8|Zeph 1:8]]. 

**7.** the verb used with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֶבַח‎</span> is ordinarily <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זָבַח‎</span> but others also are found e.g. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָשָׂה‎</span> [[Num.6.17|Num 6:17]]; [[Num.15.3|Num 15:3]]; [[Num.15.5|Num 15:5]]; [[Num.15.8|Num 15:8]]; [[Josh.22.23|Josh 22:23]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) [[1Kgs.12.27|1Kgs 12:27]]; [[2Kgs.5.17|2Kgs 5:17]]; [[2Kgs.10.24|2Kgs 10:24]]; [[Jer.33.18|Jer 33:18]] (compare Sabean <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עסי דֿבח‎</span> [[BDB#Hal|Hal]]<sup>148</sup> [[BDB#DHM|DHM]]<sup>ZMG 1888, 374</sup>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵבִיא‎</span> [[Amos.4.4|Amos 4:4]]; [[Deut.12.6|Deut 12:6]]; [[Deut.12.11|Deut 12:11]]; [[Jer.17.26|Jer 17:26]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִגִּישׁ‎</span> [[Amos.5.25|Amos 5:25]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִקְרִיב‎</span> [[Lev.7.11|Lev 7:11]]; [[Lev.7.16|Lev 7:16]]; [[Lev.22.21|Lev 22:21]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָבַד‎</span> [[Isa.19.21|Isa 19:21]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָתַן‎</span> [[Eccl.4.17|Eccl 4:17]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעֲלָה עֹלָה אוֺ זֶבַח‎</span> [[Lev.17.8|Lev 17:8]] ([[BDB#H|H]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּשֵּׁל ז‎</span> [[Ezek.46.24|Ezek 46:24]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁחַט אֶתהָֿעוֺלָה וְהַזָּבַ֑ח‎</span> [[Ezek.40.42|Ezek 40:42]] compare [[Ezek.44.11|Ezek 44:11]].
