 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִקּוּד‎</span>] **noun [masculine]** perhaps ==what is crumbled or easily crumbles, crumb== (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְקֻדָּה‎</span>); — only plural 

**1.** ***crumbs:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹּל לֶחֶם צֵידָם יָבֵ֖שׁ הָיָה נִקֻּדִים‎</span> [[Josh.9.5|Josh 9:5]] ***all the bread of their provision was dry*** (and) ***become crumbs*** ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">βεβρωμένος [οι]</span>; [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]] ***in frusta comminuti***), compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָבֵשׁ וְהָיָה נ‎</span> v[[Josh.9.12|Josh 9:12]] (see [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Benn|Benn]]). 

**2.** apparently a kind of (hard) biscuit or cake [[1Kgs.14.3|1Kgs 14:3]] (brought as present; + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֶחֶם‎</span>, etc.).
