**1.** ***root*** of people under figure of tree, involving firmness, permanence, [[Amos.2.9|Amos 2:9]]; [[Hos.9.16|Hos 9:16]]; [[Hos.14.6|Hos 14:6]]; [[Isa.14.30|Isa 14:30]]; [[2Kgs.19.30|2Kgs 19:30]] = [[Isa.37.31|Isa 37:31]] — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁרְשָׁם‎</span> [[Judg.5.14|Judg 5:14]] is probably corrupt, see [[BDB#GFM|GFM]]; — so of person (compare Phoenician, Eshmunazar<sup>11</sup> [[BDB#Cooke|GACooke]]<sup>pp. 30, 36</sup>) [[Isa.5.24|Isa 5:24]]; [[Mal.3.19|Mal 3:19]]; [[Job.8.17|Job 8:17]]; [[Job.18.16|Job 18:16]]; [[Job.29.19|Job 29:19]]; [[Prov.12.3|Prov 12:3]]; [[Prov.12.12|Prov 12:12]] = stock, family [[Isa.11.1|Isa 11:1]]; [[Isa.11.10|Isa 11:10]]; [[Dan.11.7|Dan 11:7]], compare (of serpent) [[Isa.14.29|Isa 14:29]] = source or cause [[Deut.29.17|Deut 29:17]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּבָר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> [[Job.19.28|Job 19:28]]. 

**2.** literally ***root*** of tree or shrub [[Jer.17.8|Jer 17:8]] (simile), [[Ezek.31.7|Ezek 31:7]] (metaphor of people). [[Isa.53.2|Isa 53:2]] (simile), [[Job.14.8|Job 14:8]]; [[Job.30.4|Job 30:4]] of vine (metaphor of people) [[Ezek.17.6|Ezek 17:6]]; [[Ezek.17.7|Ezek 17:7]]; [[Ezek.17.9|Ezek 17:9]] (twice in verse); [[Ps.80.10|Ps 80:10]] (accusative of congnate meaning with verb with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁרשׁ‎</span> **Hiph\`il**). 

**3.** ***root***, figurative=lowest stratum, of mountain [[Job.28.9|Job 28:9]], of sea [[Job.36.30|Job 36:30]]= bottom (text strange and dubious: [[BDB#Du|Du]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רָאשֵׁי הָרִים‎</span>, with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵרוֺ‎</span> in v<sup>a</sup>); of feet, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁרְשֵׁי רַגְלַי‎</span> [[Job.13.27|Job 13:27]], i.e. soles [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Da|Da]] and others (elsewhere <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּף‎</span>), [[BDB#Bu|Bu]] place of treading, ***footholds***; [[BDB#Du|Du]] (arbitrarily) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁרְשִׁי‎</span>, strike out <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רגלי‎</span>.
