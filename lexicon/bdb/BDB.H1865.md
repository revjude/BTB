 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּרוֺר‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==a flowing; free run, liberty==; — 

**1.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָרדְּֿרוֺר‎</span> [[Exod.30.23|Exod 30:23]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) i.e. ***myrrh of flowing***, fine-flowing myrrh compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֹר‎</span> &amp; [[BDB#Di|Di]]. 

**2.** with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קרא‎</span> ***proclaim liberty***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּקְרָאתֶם דְּרוֺר בָּאָרֶץ לְכָליֿשְׁבֶ֑יהָ‎</span> [[Lev.25.10|Lev 25:10]] ([[BDB#H|H]]) in Jubilee year, compare [[Isa.61.1|Isa 61:1]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פְּקַחקֿוֺחַ‎</span>); [[Jer.34.8|Jer 34:8]]; [[Jer.34.15|Jer 34:15]]; [[Jer.34.17|Jer 34:17]] all reference to liberty of Sabbatical year; v[[Jer.34.17|Jer 34:17]] (iron.) ***lo ! I proclaim to you liberty unto the sword, unto pestilence &amp; unto famine***, i.e. liberty to be destroyed by sword, pestilence and famine, (a judgment of Yahweh); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁנַתהַֿדּ‎</span> [[Ezek.46.17|Ezek 46:17]] i.e. year of Jubilee (cf [[Lev.25.10|Lev 25:10]]).
