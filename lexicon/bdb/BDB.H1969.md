 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִין‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Lev.19.36|Lev 19:36]]</sup> ==hin==, a liquid measure ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">εἴν</span> (<span class="bdbLangGreek">Ἴν</span>; [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]<sup>L</sup></lookup> <span class="bdbLangGreek">Ἴν</span>); but <span class="bdbLangGreek">χοῦς</span> [[Lev.19.36|Lev 19:36]] Greek <span class="bdbLangGreek">ἴυιον</span>, Egyptian measure, = ***sextarius***, accusative to Böckh<sup>Metrol. Unters. 244</sup> compare Brandis<sup>Münz- Mass- &amp; Gewichtswesen 29, 30</sup>; the Hebrew hin (6.06 litres) was, however, much larger than the Egyptian hin (0.456 litre), see [[BDB#Hultsch|Hultsch]]<sup>Metrol., Tab. xxi. p. 714, &amp; pp. 368, 450 ff.</sup>; see further [[BDB#Ri|Ri]]<sup>HWB</sup> [[BDB#Smith|Smith]]<sup>Dict.Bib.</sup> [[BDB#Mey|EMey]]<sup>Geschichte. i. 228</sup>) — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִין‎</span> [[Exod.30.24|Exod 30:24]] + 4 t. [[Ezek.45.46|Ezek 45:46]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַהִין‎</span> [[Exod.29.40|Exod 29:40]] (twice in verse) + 14 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִין‎</span> [[Lev.19.36|Lev 19:36]] — 

**1.** a measure, used of quantity of water for drinking [[Ezek.4.11|Ezek 4:11]] usually in connection with offerings: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of oil used with meal-offering [[Exod.29.40|Exod 29:40]]; [[Num.15.4|Num 15:4]]; [[Num.15.6|Num 15:6]]; [[Num.15.9|Num 15:9]]; [[Num.28.5|Num 28:5]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]), [[Ezek.45.24|Ezek 45:24]]; [[Ezek.46.5|Ezek 46:5]]; [[Ezek.46.6|Ezek 46:6]]; [[Ezek.46.11|Ezek 46:11]]; [[Ezek.46.14|Ezek 46:14]] — usually 1/4 hin to 1/10 ephah (of meal-offering); [[Num.15.6|Num 15:6]] 1/3; v[[Num.15.9|Num 15:9]] 1/2; in Ezekiel a (whole) hin, except [[Ezek.46.14|Ezek 46:14]] 1/3 hin to 1/6. ephah. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** used in mixing the anointing oil [[Exod.30.24|Exod 30:24]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** of wine as drink-offering [[Lev.23.13|Lev 23:13]] ([[BDB#H|H]]); elsewhere [[BDB#P|P]], [[Exod.29.40|Exod 29:40]]; [[Num.15.5|Num 15:5]]; [[Num.28.14|Num 28:14]] (all 1/4 hin) [[Num.15.7|Num 15:7]]; [[Num.28.14|Num 28:14]] (both 1/3 hin), [[Num.15.10|Num 15:10]]; [[Num.28.14|Num 28:14]] (both 1/2 hin; all with 1/10 to 3/10 ephah in case of meal-offering). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** once of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁכָר‎</span> as drink-offering [[Num.28.7|Num 28:7]] ([[BDB#P|P]]; 1/4 hin; compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]). 

**2.** vessel with capacity of hin [[Lev.19.36|Lev 19:36]] ([[BDB#H|H]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִיןצֶֿדֶק‎</span> ***a just hin*** (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מאֹזְנֵי צ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַבְנֵיצֿ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵיפַת צ‎</span>). — The hin = 1/6 bath according to [[BDB#Jos|Joseph.]]<sup>Ant. «JOSEPHUS:Antiquities 3:8»iii. 8</sup>, 3; «JOSEPHUS:Antiquities 3:9»9, 4, = 6.06 litres ([[BDB#Hultsch|Hultsch]]<sup>l.c</sup>.), see II.  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּת‎</span>. On order of words in above passages, note that <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִין‎</span> (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְבִיעִת ה‎</span> etc.) is usually appositive of liquid measured, &amp; either precedes it [[Exod.29.40|Exod 29:40]]; [[Num.15.4|Num 15:4]]; [[Num.28.14|Num 28:14]], or, more often, follows it [[Exod.30.24|Exod 30:24]] + 10 t.; [[Num.28.7|Num 28:7]] they are in different clauses; in [[Exod.29.40|Exod 29:40]] we find <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּשֶׁמֶןרֶ֗֗֗בַע הַהִין‎</span>, compare [[Num.15.6|Num 15:6]]; [[Num.15.9|Num 15:9]]; [[Num.28.5|Num 28:5]] — [[Num.28.14|Num 28:14]] is peculiar: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְנִסְכֵּיהֶם חֲצִי הַהִין יִהְיֶה לַפָּר וּשְׁלִישִׁת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַהִין לָאַיִל וּרְבִיעִת הַהִין לַכֶּבֶשׂ יָ֑יִן‎</span> (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יין‎</span> only at end).
