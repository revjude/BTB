 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דּוּב‎</span>] **verb** ==pine away== (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דאב‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דאבון‎</span>) — 

**Hiph\`il** ***Participle*** feminine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְדִיבֹת נפשׁ‎</span> [[Lev.26.16|Lev 26:16]] (diseases) ***causing to pine away the soul*** (life), compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]; in [[1Sam.2.33|1Sam 2:33]] read probably ***Infinitive*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְהָדִיב את נפשׁך‎</span> (for [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַאֲדִיב‎</span>) so [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]; transitive to ***cause thy*** (al. his) ***soul to pine away*** (compare [[BDB#VB|VB]]).
