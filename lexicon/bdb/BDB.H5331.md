 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֵ֫צַח‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Lam.3.18|Lam 3:18]]</sup> ==eminence, enduring, everlastingness, perpetuity== (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לנצח‎</span> [[Eccl.40.14|Eccl 40:14]] as 4 below); — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֵ֫צַח‎</span> [[1Sam.15.29|1Sam 15:29]] + 3 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶ֫צַח‎</span> [[Amos.1.11|Amos 1:11]] + 36 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִצְחִי‎</span> [[Lam.3.18|Lam 3:18]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְצָחִים‎</span> 34:10; — 

**1.** ***eminence:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נצח ישׂראל‎</span> ***the Eminence of Israel*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>) [[1Sam.15.29|1Sam 15:29]] ([[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>Sam. 98</sup> ***glory of Israel***); attribute of God [[1Chr.29.11|1Chr 29:11]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוד‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תפארת‎</span> et al.). 

**2.** ***enduring of life***, and so ***life*** itself as enduring: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָבַד נִצְחִי‎</span> [[Lam.3.18|Lam 3:18]] ***my endurance doth vanish*** ([[BDB#De|De]] sap of life, see II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נ‎</span>). 

**3.** ***endurance in time:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎משׁאות נ‎</span> [[Ps.74.3|Ps 74:3]] ***perpetual desolations***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְדַבֵּר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לנ‎</span> [[Prov.21.28|Prov 21:28]] ***speaketh continually*** (?; see Toy); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עדנֿ‎</span> [[Ps.49.20|Ps 49:20]] ***unto the end*** (a long duration), so [[Job.34.36|Job 34:36]].
