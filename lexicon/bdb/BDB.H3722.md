**1.** ***cover over, pacify, propitiate***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲכַפָּרָה פָנָיו בַּמִּנְחָה‎</span> [[Gen.32.21|Gen 32:21]] ***let me cover his face by the present*** (so that he does not see the offence, i.e. ***pacify***, him; [[BDB#E|E]]; [[BDB#RS|RS]]<sup>OTJC, 2d ed., 381</sup> 'wipe clean the face,' blackened by displeasure, as the Arabs say 'whiten the face'); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא תוּכְלִי כַּפְּרָהּוְתִפֹּל עָלַיִךְ הֹוָה‎</span> [[Isa.47.11|Isa 47:11]] ***and disaster will fall upon thee, thou wilt not be able to propitiate it*** (by payment of a <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹּפֶר‎</span>, see [[Isa.43.3|Isa 43:3]]); pacify the wrath of a king [[Prov.16.14|Prov 16:14]] (e.g. by a gift). 

**2.** ***cover over, atone for sin***, without sacrifice : 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** man as subject, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּמָּה אֲכַפֵּר‎</span> [[2Sam.21.3|2Sam 21:3]], ***with what shall I cover over*** (namely, the bloodguiltiness of the house of Saul, says David The answer is by a death penalty of seven sons of the guilty house); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חטאתכםבְּעַד‎</span> [[Exod.32.30|Exod 32:30]] ***on behalf of your sins*** ([[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]; Moses, by intercession); with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎על‎</span> of persons [[Num.17.11|Num 17:11]]; [[Num.17.12|Num 17:12]] ([[BDB#P|P]]; by incense), [[Num.25.13|Num 25:13]] ([[BDB#P|P]] when Phinehas slays the ringleaders). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** with God as subject, with accusative of person, ***cover***, i.e. treat as covered, view propitiously, Yahweh's land [[Deut.32.43|Deut 32:43]] (song); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span> person [[Deut.21.8|Deut 21:8]] (bloodguiltiness flows away in the stream), [[Ezek.16.63|Ezek 16:63]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּעַד‎</span> of person [[2Chr.30.18|2Chr 30:18]] with accusative of ***the sin*** [[Ps.65.4|Ps 65:4]]; [[Ps.78.38|Ps 78:38]], probably also [[Dan.9.24|Dan 9:24]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ׅ‎</span> \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎( חטאתח֯תם‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל‎</span> of sin, [[Ps.79.9|Ps 79:9]]; [[Jer.18.23|Jer 18:23]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ׅ‎</span> \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎( מחה‎</span>. It is conceived that God in his sovereignty may himself provide an atonement or covering for men and their sins which could not be provided by men.
