 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִלּוּל‎</span>] **noun [masculine]** (Late Hebrew ***id.***) only plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִלּוּלִים‎</span> ==rejoicing, praise==: 

**1.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וִיַּעֲשׂוּ הִלּוּלִים‎</span> [[Judg.9.27|Judg 9:27]] i.e. a vintage-rejoicing, merry-making, connected with thanksgiving <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיָּבאֹוּ בֵּית אֱלֹהֵיהֶם‎</span> etc. (i.e. god baal-Berith, see v[[Judg.9.4|Judg 9:4]]). 

**2.** of praise to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פקדֶשׁ ה‎</span> [[Lev.19.24|Lev 19:24]] ([[BDB#H|H]]) ***holiness of praise***, i.e. a consecrated thing in token of thanksgiving for fruit, offered in 4th year (compare Late Hebrew).
