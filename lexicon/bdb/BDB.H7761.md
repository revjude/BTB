 [<span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שׂוּם‎</span>], <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שׂים‎</span> **verb** ==set, make== (see Biblical Hebrew; for Persian influence on usage compare [[BDB#Scheft|Scheft]]<sup>65</sup>); — 

**Pe\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שָׂם‎</span> [[Ezra.5.3|Ezra 5:3]] +, suffix <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שָׂמֵהּ‎</span> [[Ezra.5.14|Ezra 5:14]] 2 masculine singular, <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שַׂ֫מְתָּ‎</span> [[Dan.3.10|Dan 3:10]] 1 singular <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שָׂ֫מֶת טְעֵם‎</span> [[Ezra.6.12|Ezra 6:12]] 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שָׂמוּ‎</span> [[Dan.3.12|Dan 3:12]] ***Imperative*** masculine plural <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שִׂ֫ימוּ‎</span> [[Ezra.4.21|Ezra 4:21]] — 

**1.** ***make***, accusative <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎טְעֵם‎</span> = ***make decree*** [[Dan.3.10|Dan 3:10]]; [[Ezra.6.1|Ezra 6:1]]; [[Ezra.6.12|Ezra 6:12]] + verb finite v[[Ezra.6.8|Ezra 6:8]], + infinitive [[Ezra.4.21|Ezra 4:21]]; [[Ezra.5.13|Ezra 5:13]] and (with <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎ל‎</span> person) v[[Ezra.5.3|Ezra 5:3]] v[[Ezra.5.9|Ezra 5:9]]. 

**2.** ***make, appoint***, one's name [[Dan.5.12|Dan 5:12]] (2 accusative), one to office [[Ezra.5.14|Ezra 5:14]] (2 accusative). 

**3.** ***set, fix***, accusative <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎בָּל‎</span> ***mind***, [[Dan.6.15|Dan 6:15]] (c. infinitive); accusative <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎טְעֵם‎</span> [[Dan.3.12|Dan 3:12]]; [[Dan.6.14|Dan 6:14]] ***pay*** due regard (<span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎טְעֵם‎</span>) to (<span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎עַל‎</span> person). **Pe\`il** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎מִנִּי שִׂים טְעֵם‎</span> [[Ezra.4.19|Ezra 4:19]], ***from me a decree is made***, so [[Ezra.5.17|Ezra 5:17]]; [[Ezra.6.8|Ezra 6:8]]; [[Ezra.6.11|Ezra 6:11]]; [[Ezra.7.13|Ezra 7:13]]; [[Ezra.7.21|Ezra 7:21]]; [[Dan.3.29|Dan 3:29]]; [[Dan.4.3|Dan 4:3]]; [[Dan.6.27|Dan 6:27]] literal 3 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שֻׂמַת‎</span> (read probably <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎שִׂימַת‎</span> [[BDB#Kau|K]]<sup>§ 45, Beisp. 1.</sup> ***d)***) [[Dan.6.18|Dan 6:18]] (of stone, <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎עַל‎</span> local). **Hithpe\`el** ***be made: Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎יִתְּשָׂם‎</span> [[Ezra.4.24|Ezra 4:24]] (of decree, <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎סטְעֵס‎</span> with <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎מִן‎</span> agent.); 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎יִתְּשָׂמוּן‎</span> [[Dan.2.5|Dan 2:5]] subject of thing + noun predicate ***be made into; Participle*** [[Ezra.5.8|Ezra 5:8]] literally ***be set, laid***, of wood, <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎ב‎</span> of wall.
