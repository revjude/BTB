 [<span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אֲכַל‎</span>] **verb** ==eat, devour== (Biblical Hebrew); — 

**Pe\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אֲכַ֫לוּ‎</span> [[Dan.3.8|Dan 3:8]]; [[Dan.6.25|Dan 6:25]] ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎יֵאכֻל‎</span> [[Dan.4.30|Dan 4:30]] 3 feminine singular<span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎תֵּאכֻל‎</span> [[Dan.7.23|Dan 7:23]] ***Imperative*** feminine singular <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אֲכֻּ֫לִי‎</span> v[[Dan.7.5|Dan 7:5]] ***Participle*** feminine singular <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אָֽכְלָה‎</span>, v[[Dan.7.7|Dan 7:7]] v[[Dan.7.9|Dan 7:9]] — 

**1.** ***eat***, of beast, [[Dan.4.30|Dan 4:30]] (accusative of thing). 

**2.** ***devour***, of beast in vision, [[Dan.7.5|Dan 7:5]] (accusative of thing), absolute v[[Dan.7.7|Dan 7:7]] v[[Dan.7.19|Dan 7:19]] hence figurative of kingdom, ***devour, devastate***, v[[Dan.7.23|Dan 7:23]] (accusative of earth). 

**3.** in phrase <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎ק־רְצֵיהוֺן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎א‎</span> [[Dan.3.8|Dan 3:8]] ***eat their pieces***, i.e. slander them, compare [[Dan.6.25|Dan 6:25]] (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קְרַץ‎</span>).
