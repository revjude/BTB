 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּלָיָ֫הוּ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּלָיָה‎</span> **proper name, masculine** (***Yah(u) hath drawn***) — 

**1.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּלָיָהוּ‎</span>, 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** a priest, contemporary of David, [[1Chr.24.18|1Chr 24:18]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** a prince of Judah, contemporary of Jeremiah [[Jer.36.12|Jer 36:12]]; [[Jer.36.25|Jer 36:25]]. 

**2.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּלָיָה‎</span>, 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** head of a family contemporary with Zerubbabel [[Ezra.2.60|Ezra 2:60]] = [[Neh.7.62|Neh 7:62]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** descendant of Zerubbabel. [[1Chr.3.24|1Chr 3:24]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** father of contemporary of Nehemiah [[Neh.6.10|Neh 6:10]].
