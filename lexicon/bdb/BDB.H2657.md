 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶפְצִיבָֿהּ‎</span> **proper name, feminine** (***my delight is in her***; compare Phoenician <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חפצבעל‎</span>) — 

**1.** mother of king Manasseh [[2Kgs.21.1|2Kgs 21:1]]. 

**2.** figurative name of Zion [[Isa.62.4|Isa 62:4]], here explained by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּי חפץ יהוה בָּךְ‎</span> ***for Yahweh delighteth in thee***.
