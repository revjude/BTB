 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁלָל‎</span><sub>75</sub> **noun masculine**<sup>[[1Sam.30.16|1Sam 30:16]]</sup> ==prey, spoil, plunder, booty==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> [[Gen.49.27|Gen 49:27]] +; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלַל‎</span> [[Judg.5.30|Judg 5:30]] +, suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלָלֵךְ‎</span> [[Zech.14.1|Zech 14:1]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלַלְכֶם‎</span> [[Isa.33.4|Isa 33:4]], etc.; — 

**1.** ***prey***, of Benjamin as wolf [[Gen.49.27|Gen 49:27]] (poem in [[BDB#J|J]]; with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִלֵּק‎</span>). 

**2.** ***booty, spoil*** of war, of all kinds (often \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּז‎</span>): including garments, gold and silver [[Josh.7.21|Josh 7:21]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), ornaments [[Judg.8.24|Judg 8:24]]; [[Judg.8.25|Judg 8:25]], fabrics and women [[Judg.5.30|Judg 5:30]] a, b, flocks and herds [[1Sam.30.20|1Sam 30:20]]; [[Jer.49.32|Jer 49:32]]; [[2Chr.15.11|2Chr 15:11]], + persons [[Deut.20.14|Deut 20:14]] (twice in verse), garments, food and drink [[2Chr.28.15|2Chr 28:15]], etc. (58 t.; among these) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלַל הֶעָרִים‎</span> [[Deut.2.25|Deut 2:25]]; [[2Sam.12.30|2Sam 12:30]]; [[1Chr.20.2|1Chr 20:2]] and (+ cattle in addition) [[Deut.3.7|Deut 3:7]]; [[Josh.8.27|Josh 8:27]]; [[Josh.11.14|Josh 11:14]] (both [[BDB#R|R]]<sup>D</sup>), compare (also of city) [[Isa.8.4|Isa 8:4]]; [[Deut.13.17|Deut 13:17]] (twice in verse); [[Zech.14.1|Zech 14:1]] and (+ cattle) [[Josh.8.2|Josh 8:2]] ([[BDB#R|R]]<sup>D</sup>), but including person and cattle [[Deut.20.14|Deut 20:14]], compare v[[Deut.20.14|Deut 20:14]] figurative of entire nation [[Jer.50.10|Jer 50:10]]; [[Ezek.7.21|Ezek 7:21]]; [[Zech.2.13|Zech 2:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> in symbolic proper name <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַהֵר שָׁלָל וגו‎</span> [[Isa.8.1|Isa 8:1]]; [[Isa.8.3|Isa 8:3]], **see** above p.555:a; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> in metaphor of future majesty of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֶבֶד י‎</span> [[Isa.53.12|Isa 53:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהֳיְתָהאלּוֺ נַפְשׁוֺ לְשׁ‎</span> i.e. life shall be spared, [[Jer.21.9|Jer 21:9]]; [[Jer.38.2|Jer 38:2]]; [[Jer.39.18|Jer 39:18]], compare [[Jer.45.5|Jer 45:5]]. 

**3.** private ***plunder*** [[Isa.10.2|Isa 10:2]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַלְמָנוֺת שְׁלָלָם‎</span>), [[Prov.1.13|Prov 1:13]] and (perhaps in current saying) [[Prov.16.19|Prov 16:19]]. 

**4.** = ***gain*** [[Prov.31.11|Prov 31:11]]. — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְצַוִּארֵי שׁ‎</span> [[Judg.5.30|Judg 5:30]] c is clearly wrong; meaning dubious (see especially [[BDB#GFM|GFM]]); plausible conjectures are <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְצַוָּארַי שׁ‎</span> ***for my neck as spoil***, Reuss<sup>Br</sup> and others, and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְצַוְּארֵי שֵׁגָל‎</span> ***for the neck of the queen*** [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Be|Be]] [[BDB#Kit|Kit]] and others (but <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁגָל‎</span> elsewhere late); [[BDB#Now|Now]] strike out <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁלָל‎</span> and reads <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְצַוָּארָיו‎</span>.
