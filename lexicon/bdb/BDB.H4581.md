**2. a.** ==figurative, of God== (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>) as ***refuge*** [[Nah.1.7|Nah 1:7]]; [[Ps.27.1|Ps 27:1]]; [[Ps.31.5|Ps 31:5]]; [[Ps.37.39|Ps 37:39]]; [[Ps.52.9|Ps 52:9]]; [[Isa.25.4|Isa 25:4]] (twice in verse); [[Joel.4.16|Joel 4:16]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֻזִּי וּמָעֻזִּי וּמְנוּסִי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Jer.16.19|Jer 16:19]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְשׁוּעוֺת מְשִׁיחוֺ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Ps.28.8|Ps 28:8]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָזֿ‎</span>); so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוּר מ‎</span> [[Isa.17.10|Isa 17:10]]; [[Ps.31.3|Ps 31:3]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֱלֹהֵי מ‎</span> [[Ps.43.2|Ps 43:2]] also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַחֲזֵק בְּמָעֻזִּי‎</span> [[Isa.27.5|Isa 27:5]] ***let him lay hold of my refuge*** (me as refuge); cf . <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִיא מָעֻזְּכֶם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶדְוַת י‎</span> [[Neh.8.10|Neh 8:10]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַתֹּם דֶּרֶךְ י‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Prov.10.29|Prov 10:29]].
