 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זָדוֺן‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Prov.11.2|Prov 11:2]]</sup> ==insolence, presumptuousness== (on formation compare [[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>BN 204, Anm</sup>.), absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> [[Deut.17.12|Deut 17:12]] + 7 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זְדוֺן‎</span> [[Jer.49.16|Jer 49:16]]; [[Obad.1.3|Obad 1:3]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זְדֹנְךָ‎</span> [[1Sam.17.28|1Sam 17:28]] — 

**1.** ***insolence, presumption*** toward men; affirmed of David by Eliab [[1Sam.17.28|1Sam 17:28]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֹעַ לְבָבֶ֑ךָ‎</span>); usually 

**2.** of presumptuous godlessness (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוּד‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֵד‎</span>), exhibited in disobedience to priest or judge [[Deut.17.12|Deut 17:12]] in false prediction [[Deut.18.22|Deut 18:22]] in general [[Prov.11.2|Prov 11:2]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צְנוּעִים‎</span>), [[Prov.13.10|Prov 13:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֶבְרַת ז‎</span> [[Prov.21.24|Prov 21:24]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֵד‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִבֶּ֑ךָ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span>) [[Jer.49.16|Jer 49:16]] ( = [[Obad.1.3|Obad 1:3]]) ***presumption of thine heart***; of Babylon personified under figure of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> [[Jer.50.31|Jer 50:31]]; [[Jer.50.32|Jer 50:32]] dubious is <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּרַח הַזּ‎</span> [[Ezek.7.10|Ezek 7:10]] ***insolence hath blossomed*** (preceded by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צָץ הַמַּטֶּה‎</span>) i.e. either of Israel as demanding punishment, or of Babylon as instrument of punishment.
