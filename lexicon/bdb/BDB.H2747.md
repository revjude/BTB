 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶ֫רֶט‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==graving-tool, stylus== — 

**1.** ***graving-tool***, with which Aaron fashioned (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיָּצַר‎</span>) the molten calf [[Exod.32.4|Exod 32:4]] ([[BDB#E|E]]). 

**2.** ***stylus***, for writing on tablet (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גִּלָּיוֺן‎</span>): <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּתֹב עָלָיו בְּחֶרֶט אֱנוֺשׁ‎</span> [[Isa.8.1|Isa 8:1]] ***write on it with a man's*** (i.e. an ordinary) ***stylus*** = in common characters, intelligible to all (see [[BDB#Benz|Benz]]<sup>Archaeology 290</sup>).
