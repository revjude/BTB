 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂוֺכֹה‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂוֺכוֺ‎</span> **proper name, of a location** ==in Judah:== 

**1.** in Shephelah, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂוֺכֹה‎</span> [[Josh.15.35|Josh 15:35]]; [[1Sam.17.1|1Sam 17:1]] (twice in verse) (from [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Σοκχωθ</span> [[BDB#HPS|HPS]] (after [[BDB#We|We]], compare [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]) conjectures <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂכות‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂוֺכוֺ‎</span> [[2Chr.11.7|2Chr 11:7]]; [[2Chr.28.18|2Chr 28:18]], perhaps also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂכֹה‎</span> [[1Kgs.4.10|1Kgs 4:10]] ([[BDB#Buhl|Buhl]] [[BDB#Benz|Benz]] [[BDB#SS|SS]] ['perhaps']; [[BDB#The|Th]] [[BDB#Kit|Kit]] below **2**); modern <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***Eš-Šuw¢ke***</u></abbr>, south-southeast from Beth Shemesh, in <abbr title="transliteration"><u>Wady-es-San‰</u></abbr> [[BDB#Rob|Rob]]<sup>BR 1. 494</sup> [[BDB#Buhl|Buhl]]<sup>Geogr. 194</sup>; <span class="bdbLangGreek">Σωχω, Σοκχωθ</span>, etc. 

**2.** in hill-country, [[Josh.15.48|Josh 15:48]] ([[BDB#Kt|Kt]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂוכה‎</span>, [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂוכוֺ‎</span>), probably also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂוֺכוֺ‎</span> [[1Chr.4.18|1Chr 4:18]] ('son' of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶבֶר‎</span>); modern <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***Šuwêke***</u></abbr>, 4 h. south of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶבְרֵוֺן‎</span> [[BDB#Rob|Rob]]<sup>BR ii. 16. 21</sup> [[BDB#Buhl|Buhl]]<sup>Geogr. 164</sup>; <span class="bdbLangGreek">Σωχω</span>(<span class="bdbLangGreek">ν</span>).
