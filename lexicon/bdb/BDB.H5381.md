**Hiph\`il** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהִשִּׂיג‎</span> consecutive [[Lev.25.6|Lev 25:6]], suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהִשִּׂיגוֺ‎</span> consecutive [[Deut.19.6|Deut 19:6]] 2 masculine singular suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהִשַּׂגְתָּם‎</span> consecutive [[Gen.44.4|Gen 44:4]], etc.; ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַשִּׂיג‎</span> [[Lev.26.5|Lev 26:5]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַּשֵּׂג‎</span> [[Gen.31.25|Gen 31:25]], suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַּשִּׂגֵם‎</span> [[Gen.44.26|Gen 44:26]], etc.; ***Infinitive absolute*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַשֵּׂג‎</span> [[1Sam.30.8|1Sam 30:8]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַשִּׂיג‎</span> [[1Sam.14.26|1Sam 14:26]], suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַשִּׂיגֵ֫הוּ‎</span> [[Job.41.18|Job 41:18]] feminine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַשֶּׂגֶת‎</span> [[Lev.14.21|Lev 14:21]]; [[1Chr.21.12|1Chr 21:12]] (but read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאִם הֱיוֺת‎</span> as \|\| [[2Sam.24.13|2Sam 24:13]] [[BDB#We|We]]<sup>Sm</sup> [[BDB#Kit|Kit]]); — 

**1.** ***overtake***, with accusative, often after <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רָדַף‎</span>: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** literal [[Gen.31.25|Gen 31:25]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[Gen.44.4|Gen 44:4]]; [[Gen.44.6|Gen 44:6]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[Exod.14.9|Exod 14:9]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), [[Exod.15.9|Exod 15:9]] (song), [[Deut.19.6|Deut 19:6]]; [[Josh.2.5|Josh 2:5]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]), [[1Sam.30.8|1Sam 30:8]] (3 t. in verse); [[2Sam.15.14|2Sam 15:14]]; [[2Kgs.25.5|2Kgs 25:5]] = [[Jer.39.5|Jer 39:5]] = [[Jer.52.8|Jer 52:8]], [[Hos.2.9|Hos 2:9]], compare [[Ps.7.6|Ps 7:6]]; [[Ps.18.38|Ps 18:38]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וָאַשְׁמִידֵם‎</span> in \|\| [[2Sam.22.38|2Sam 22:38]]), [[Lam.1.3|Lam 1:3]] (metaphor). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** figurative of battle [[Hos.10.9|Hos 10:9]], sword [[Jer.42.16|Jer 42:16]] on [[1Chr.21.12|1Chr 21:12]] see above; of blessings [[Deut.28.2|Deut 28:2]], curses v[[Deut.28.15|Deut 28:15]] v[[Deut.28.45|Deut 28:45]], predictions [[Zech.1.6|Zech 1:6]], justice [[Isa.59.9|Isa 59:9]], wrath of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ps.69.25|Ps 69:25]], iniquities [[Ps.40.13|Ps 40:13]], calamities [[Job.27.20|Job 27:20]]. 

**2. a.** ***reach, attain to***, [[Job.41.18|Job 41:18]] (if one) ***reach it*** (crocodile) with the sword (2 accusative); figurative paths of life [[Prov.2.19|Prov 2:19]], joy, etc., [[Isa.35.10|Isa 35:10]] also [[Isa.51.11|Isa 51:11]] ([[BDB#Che|Che]]<sup>Hpt</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַשִּׂיגוּם‎</span>, for [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַשִּׂיגוּן‎</span>; [[BDB#Perles|Perles]]<sup>Analekten 64</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִשְׂגּוּן‎</span>, from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂגה‎</span>); of time [[Gen.47.9|Gen 47:9]] my days ***have not reached*** the days ... of my fathers, compare [[Lev.26.5|Lev 26:5]] (twice in verse). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***cause to reach, bring, put***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַשִּׂיג יָדוֺ אֶלפִּֿיו‎</span> [[1Sam.14.26|1Sam 14:26]] no one ***put his hand to his mouth***, but read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵשִׁיב‎</span> [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]] [[BDB#Klo|Klo]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]] [[BDB#Bu|Bu]] [[BDB#Kit|Kit]] [[BDB#HPS|HPS]] Löhr.
