**1.** ***doing wrong, committing a trespass or offence*** [[2Chr.24.18|2Chr 24:18]]; [[2Chr.28.10|2Chr 28:10]]; [[2Chr.28.13|2Chr 28:13]] (3 t. in verse) (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חטאת‎</span> v[[2Chr.28.13|2Chr 28:13]]) [[2Chr.32.23|2Chr 32:23]]; [[Ezra.9.6|Ezra 9:6]]; [[Ezra.9.7|Ezra 9:7]]; [[Ezra.9.13|Ezra 9:13]]; [[Ezra.9.15|Ezra 9:15]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָוֺן‎</span> v[[Ezra.9.13|Ezra 9:13]]) [[Ps.69.6|Ps 69:6]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִוֶּלָת‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִכֹּל אֲשֶׁר יַעֲשֶׂה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְאַשְׁמָה בָהּ‎</span> ***of all that one may do wrong therein*** [[Lev.5.26|Lev 5:26]] ([[BDB#P|P]]; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַחֲטֹא בָהֵנָּה‎</span> v.[[Lev.6.22|Lev 6:22]]). 

**2.** ***becoming guilty*** [[1Chr.21.3|1Chr 21:3]]; [[Ezra.10.10|Ezra 10:10]]; [[Ezra.10.19|Ezra 10:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶחֱטָא לְאַשְׁמַת הָעָם‎</span> ***sin to the becoming guilty of the people*** (so that the people incur guilt) [[Lev.4.3|Lev 4:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַשְׁמַת שֹׁמְרוֺן‎</span> ***guilt of Samaria*** (their idols) [[Amos.8.14|Amos 8:14]]. 

**3.** ***bringing a trespass-offering***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּיוֺם אַשְׁמָתוֺ‎</span> ***in the day of his bringing his trespass-offering*** [[Lev.5.24|Lev 5:24]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲוֺן אַשְׁמָה‎</span> ***iniquity requiring a trespass-offering*** [[Lev.22.16|Lev 22:16]].
