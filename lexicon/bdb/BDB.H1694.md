 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֶּ֫בֶק‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Isa.41.7|Isa 41:7]]</sup> ==joining, soldering, appendage== — 

**1.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דּ‎</span> ***joining, soldering*** [[Isa.41.7|Isa 41:7]]. 

**2.** plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַדְּבָקִים‎</span> ***appendages*** of breastplate (?) [[1Kgs.22.34|1Kgs 22:34]] = [[2Chr.18.33|2Chr 18:33]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ובין הַשִּׁרְיָן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּין הַדּ‎</span>. (The [[BDB#Be|Be]] and others compare [[BDB#VB|VB]]: the jointed ***attachment*** or ***appendage*** to the rigid breast-armour, which covered the abdomen; &gt; [[BDB#Klo|Klo]] proposes appendage of helmet; [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] suggests armpits, literally ***joints*** (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַדְבְּקֵי יָד‎</span> [[Jer.38.12|Jer 38:12]] [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]]); [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] the soft muscles etc. ***connecting*** the chest with the bottom of the back.)
