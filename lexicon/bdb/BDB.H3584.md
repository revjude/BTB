Pr</sup>.; + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְרָצֹחַ וְגָנֹב וְנָאֹ֑ף‎</span>), compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא תִגְנֹ֑בוּ וְלֹאֿ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְכַחֲשׁוּ וְלֹא תְשַׁקְּרוּ אִישׁ בַּעֲמִיתוֺ‎</span> [[Lev.19.11|Lev 19:11]] ([[BDB#H|H]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמַעַן כ‎</span> [[Zech.13.4|Zech 13:4]] ***in order to deceive***; followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span> person [[1Kgs.13.18|1Kgs 13:18]]; [[Job.31.28|Job 31:28]] followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְ‎</span> person and of thing [[Lev.5.21|Lev 5:21]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְ‎</span> of thing v.[[Lev.6.22|Lev 6:22]]. 

**2.** ***act deceptively*** against (i.e. seem to acknowledge, but not really do so), followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְ‎</span> person [[Josh.24.27|Josh 24:27]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[Jer.5.12|Jer 5:12]]; [[Isa.59.13|Isa 59:13]]; [[Job.8.18|Job 8:18]], apparently also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּהּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְתִירוֺשׁ יכ‎</span> [[Hos.9.2|Hos 9:2]] ***and new wine shall deny her*** (Israel; i.e. refuse to acknowledge her as its mistress, not yield itself to her); absolute [[Prov.30.9|Prov 30:9]].
