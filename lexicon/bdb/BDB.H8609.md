 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תָּפַר‎</span>] **verb** ==sew together== (Late Hebrew ***id.***; Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎תְּפַר‎</span>); — 

**Qal** ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּתְפְרוּ‎</span> [[Gen.3.7|Gen 3:7]] ([[BDB#J|J]]) ***they sewed together*** fig-leaves (accusative); ***Perfect*** 1 singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תָּפַ֫רְתִּי‎</span> [[Job.16.15|Job 16:15]], with accusative + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲלֵי‎</span>, ***sackcloth I have sewed upon*** my skin; ***Infinitive construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵת לִתְפּוֺר‎</span> [[Eccl.3.6|Eccl 3:6]] ***a time to sew together*** (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קָרַע‎</span>). **Pi\`el** ***Participle*** as substantive feminine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְתַפְּרוֺת‎</span> [[Ezek.13.18|Ezek 13:18]] ***those who keep sewing***, with accusative + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל‎</span>.
