 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַתַּנְיָ֫הוּ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַתַּנְיָה‎</span> **proper name, masculine** (compare [[BDB#Gray|Gray]]<sup>Prop. N. 294, No. 82</sup>); — [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Μαθθας</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Μανθανιας</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Ματθανιας</span>, etc.; — 

**1.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַתַּנְיָה‎</span>, last king of Judah, name changed to Zedekiah [[2Kgs.24.17|2Kgs 24:17]]. 

**2.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַתַּנְיָ֫הוּ‎</span>, Levite name: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** son of Heman [[1Chr.25.4|1Chr 25:4]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** Asaphite [[1Chr.25.16|1Chr 25:16]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** ***id.***, [[2Chr.29.13|2Chr 29:13]] (Hezekiah's time). 

**3.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַתַּנְיָה‎</span>, Levite name: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** Asaphite [[1Chr.9.15|1Chr 9:15]]; [[Neh.11.17|Neh 11:17]]; [[Neh.11.22|Neh 11:22]]; [[Neh.12.8|Neh 12:8]]; [[Neh.12.35|Neh 12:35]], perhaps = **2 b, c** (but much confusion and uncertainty; e.g. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> is of Hezekiah's time [[2Chr.29.13|2Chr 29:13]], and 4th generation before Nehemiah, [[Neh.11.12|Neh 11:12]]; [[Neh.12.35|Neh 12:35]] while contemporary of Zerubbabel [[Neh.12.8|Neh 12:8]] and of Nehemiah [[Neh.11.17|Neh 11:17]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** other Levites: (1) [[2Chr.20.14|2Chr 20:14]], (2) [[Neh.12.25|Neh 12:25]], (3) [[Neh.13.13|Neh 13:13]]. 

**4.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַתַּנְיָה‎</span>, name of several who took strange wives: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** [[Ezra.10.26|Ezra 10:26]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** v[[Ezra.10.27|Ezra 10:27]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** v[[Ezra.10.30|Ezra 10:30]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** v[[Ezra.10.37|Ezra 10:37]].
