$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** outside of thigh, where sword was worn, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׂים חֶרֶב עַליְֿרֵכוֺ‎</span> [[Exod.32.27|Exod 32:27]] ([[BDB#E|E]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַחְגֹּר אֹתָהּ עַליֶֿרֶךְ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְמִינוֺ‎</span> [[Judg.3.16|Judg 3:16]] ***and he girded it upon his right thigh***, v[[Judg.3.21|Judg 3:21]]; [[Ps.45.4|Ps 45:4]]; [[Song.3.8|Song 3:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּף הַיָּרֵךְ‎</span> [[Gen.32.33|Gen 32:33]] ***hollow of the thigh***, [[Gen.32.26|Gen 32:26]] (twice in verse); [[Gen.32.33|Gen 32:33]] (all [[BDB#J|J]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַמּוּקֵי יְרֵכַיִךְ‎</span> [[Song.7.2|Song 7:2]] ***the roundings of thy thighs***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִמָּתְנַיִם וְעַדיְֿרֵכַיִם‎</span> [[Exod.28.42|Exod 28:42]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֶּטֶן‎</span> [[Num.5.21|Num 5:21]]; [[Num.5.22|Num 5:22]]; [[Num.5.27|Num 5:27]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צֹלֵעַ עַליְֿרֵכוֺ‎</span> [[Gen.32.32|Gen 32:32]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), ***limping upon his thigh***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סָפַק עַליָֿרֵח‎</span> [[Jer.31.19|Jer 31:19]] and (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶלֿ‎</span>) [[Ezek.21.17|Ezek 21:17]], ***smite upon, thigh***, in token of consternation; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַּךְ אוֺתָם שׁוֺק עַלֿ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָרֵךְ‎</span> [[Judg.15.8|Judg 15:8]] ***and he smote them, hip upon thigh***, a great slaughter. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***thigh*** = ***loins***, as seat of procreative power ([[BDB#RS|RS]]<sup>K. 34</sup>; Semitic i, 360 (380)) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֹצְאֵי ירך‎</span> ***those proceeding from the loins of*** any one [[Gen.46.26|Gen 46:26]]; [[Exod.1.5|Exod 1:5]] (both [[BDB#P|P]]), [[Judg.8.30|Judg 8:30]] hence <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׂים יַד תַּחַת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ירך‎</span> ***place the hand under thigh***, in taking oath [[Gen.24.2|Gen 24:2]]; [[Gen.24.9|Gen 24:9]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[Gen.47.29|Gen 47:29]].
