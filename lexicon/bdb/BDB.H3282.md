 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן‎</span><sub>96</sub> properly **substantive**, ==purpose, intention==, but always used as **preposition** or **conjunction**, ==on account of, because== (for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעֲנֶה‎</span>, of the form <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִצְהָר‎</span> etc., [[BDB#Sta|Sta]]<sup>§ 259</sup> [[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>ii. 403</sup>; compare <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhIgAdAPcAAAAAAAEBAQICAgMDAwQEBAUFBQYGBgcHBwgICAkJCQoKCgsLCwwMDA0NDQ4ODg8PDxAQEBERERISEhMTExQUFBUVFRYWFhcXFxgYGBkZGRoaGhsbGxwcHB0dHR4eHh8fHyAgICEhISIiIiMjIyQkJCUlJSYmJicnJygoKCkpKSoqKisrKywsLC0tLS4uLi8vLzAwMDExMTIyMjMzMzQ0NDU1NTY2Njc3Nzg4ODk5OTo6Ojs7Ozw8PD09PT4+Pj8/P0BAQEFBQUJCQkNDQ0REREVFRUZGRkdHR0hISElJSUpKSktLS0xMTE1NTU5OTk9PT1BQUFFRUVJSUlNTU1RUVFVVVVZWVldXV1hYWFlZWVpaWltbW1xcXF1dXV5eXl9fX2BgYGFhYWJiYmNjY2RkZGVlZWZmZmdnZ2hoaGlpaWpqamtra2xsbG1tbW5ubm9vb3BwcHFxcXJycnNzc3R0dHV1dXZ2dnd3d3h4eHl5eXp6ent7e3x8fH19fX5+fn9/f4CAgIGBgYKCgoODg4SEhIWFhYaGhoeHh4iIiImJiYqKiouLi4yMjI2NjY6Ojo+Pj5CQkJGRkZKSkpOTk5SUlJWVlZaWlpeXl5iYmJmZmZqampubm5ycnJ2dnZ6enp+fn6CgoKGhoaKioqOjo6SkpKWlpaampqenp6ioqKmpqaqqqqurq6ysrK2tra6urq+vr7CwsLGxsbKysrOzs7S0tLW1tba2tre3t7i4uLm5ubq6uru7u7y8vL29vb6+vr+/v8DAwMHBwcLCwsPDw8TExMXFxcbGxsfHx8jIyMnJycrKysvLy8zMzM3Nzc7Ozs/Pz9DQ0NHR0dLS0tPT09TU1NXV1dbW1tfX19jY2NnZ2dra2tvb29zc3N3d3d7e3t/f3+Dg4OHh4eLi4uPj4+Tk5OXl5ebm5ufn5+jo6Onp6erq6uvr6+zs7O3t7e7u7u/v7/Dw8PHx8fLy8vPz8/T09PX19fb29vf39/j4+Pn5+fr6+vv7+/z8/P39/f7+/v///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAiAB0ABwjbAP/xG0iwoMGDCA0KTMiwocOHECNKnEixIsSFFgn+27hRG8eNCTE6/GcPAIBpHN8VE1lQIEeG/0ya5CgTpEKXLFtuDPeRDACNOXOG/Pjy40GhCP9FAODro8l3Ni3+0wGAozYAS6sivXmU6EycI4kStfcB3cYBMmc2FMsRAwC0aePK2cpvIzMASDb6mtkCgFuTGORg4Bk16T85Wv8B+vqRGdu1/5AkjlmTrVGY/z4AILwxD+PCL9eWNOm5cmigIgvX3RiXM92HH9HZu5xRY+3buHPrlvj6du+KHAMCADs="> ***mean, intend***): — 

**1.** as **preposition**, rarely with a substantive, [[Ezek.5.9|Ezek 5:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן תּוֺעֲבוֺתֵיכֶם‎</span>, [[Hag.1.9|Hag 1:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן מָה‎</span>, v[[Hag.1.9|Hag 1:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן בֵּיתִי‎</span>; with a participle [[Ezek.36.13|Ezek 36:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יען אמרים‎</span> ***because of men saying*** to you (but read probably<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָמְרָם‎</span>, [[BDB#Co|Co]] Toy [[BDB#Berthol|Berthol]]); frequently with infinitive with, [[1Kgs.21.20|1Kgs 21:20]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן הִתְמַכֶּרְךָ‎</span> ***on account of*** thy having sold thyself, [[Amos.5.11|Amos 5:11]]; [[Isa.30.12|Isa 30:12]]; [[Isa.37.29|Isa 37:29]] (= [[2Kgs.19.28|2Kgs 19:28]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעֵן הִתְרַגֶּזְךָ אֵלַי‎</span>, [[Jer.5.14|Jer 5:14]]; [[Jer.7.13|Jer 7:13]]; [[Jer.23.38|Jer 23:38]]; [[Jer.48.7|Jer 48:7]], and often. (c. 18 t.) in Ezek., as [[Jer.5.7|Jer 5:7]] (read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן הַמְרֹתְכֶם‎</span>), [[Jer.13.8|Jer 13:8]]; [[Jer.13.22|Jer 13:22]]; [[Jer.25.3|Jer 25:3]]; [[Jer.25.6|Jer 25:6]]; [[Jer.25.8|Jer 25:8]]; [[Jer.25.12|Jer 25:12]].


**2.** As **conjunction**: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן אֲשֶׁר‎</span> (32 t.), usually with perfect, as [[Gen.22.16|Gen 22:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יען אשׁר עשׂית זאת‎</span> ***because that*** thou hast done this, [[Deut.1.36|Deut 1:36]] ([[Josh.14.14|Josh 14:14]]), [[Judg.2.20|Judg 2:20]]; [[1Sam.30.22|1Sam 30:22]]; [[1Kgs.3.11|1Kgs 3:11]]; [[1Kgs.8.18|1Kgs 8:18]]; [[Jer.19.4|Jer 19:4]]; [[Jer.25.8|Jer 25:8]]; [[Jer.29.23|Jer 29:23]] (not Isaiah) +; with imperfect (in frequent. sense) [[Ezek.44.12|Ezek 44:12]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַען כִּי‎</span>, [[Num.11.20|Num 11:20]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן כִּי מְאַסְתֶּם אֶתיֿ‎</span>, [[1Kgs.13.21|1Kgs 13:21]]; [[1Kgs.21.29|1Kgs 21:29]]; [[Isa.3.16|Isa 3:16]]; [[Isa.7.5|Isa 7:5]]; [[Isa.8.6|Isa 8:6]]; [[Isa.29.13|Isa 29:13]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן‎</span> alone (23 t.), with perfect [[Num.20.12|Num 20:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן לֹא הֶאֱמַנְתֶּם בִּי‎</span>, [[1Sam.15.23|1Sam 15:23]]; [[1Kgs.14.13|1Kgs 14:13]]; [[1Kgs.20.42|1Kgs 20:42]]; [[2Kgs.22.19|2Kgs 22:19]]; [[Hos.8.1|Hos 8:1]]; [[Isa.61.1|Isa 61:1]]; [[Isa.65.12|Isa 65:12]]; [[Isa.66.4|Isa 66:4]] with imperfect (frequently) [[Ezek.34.21|Ezek 34:21]] of the future [[Ezek.12.12|Ezek 12:12]] ***because*** he shall not see, etc., (but [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Co|Co]] [[BDB#Kau|Kau]] [[BDB#Berthol|Berthol]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמַעַן אשׁר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לא יֵרָאֶה לָעַיִן‎</span>): usually the verb follows immediately, but Ezek. sometimes puts the object first for emphasis, [[Ezek.5.11|Ezek 5:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן אֶתמִֿקְדָּשִׁי טִמֵּאתָ‎</span>, [[Ezek.20.16|Ezek 20:16]]; [[Ezek.20.24|Ezek 20:24]]; [[Ezek.36.6|Ezek 36:6]], compare [[Ezek.34.21|Ezek 34:21]].


**3.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן וּבְיַעַן‎</span>, q.d. ***because and by the cause*** (that), followed by perhaps, [[Lev.26.43|Lev 26:43]] ([[BDB#H|H]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יען וביען בְּמִשְׁפָּטַי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָאָ֔סוּ‎</span>, [[Ezek.13.10|Ezek 13:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַן בְּיַעַז‎</span> followed by infinitive, [[Ezek.36.3|Ezek 36:3]]. (In all, reckoning <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יען (ו)ביען‎</span> once each, 93 t., of which 20 are in 1-2 Kings, 11 in Jeremiah, 38 in Ezekiel; and only 9 in Genesis—2Samuel.)
