**1. a.** ***blow, stripe***, literally of scourging, chastising [[Deut.25.3|Deut 25:3]]; [[Prov.20.30|Prov 20:30]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַבֻּרוֺת פֶּצַע‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַכַּת חֶרֶב‎</span> [[Esth.9.5|Esth 9:5]] (as accusative of congnate meaning with verb). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***beating, scourging***, figurative of defeat of Midian [[Isa.10.26|Isa 10:26]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עוֺרֵר שׁוֺט‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** often ***wound*** [[1Kgs.22.35|1Kgs 22:35]]; [[2Kgs.8.29|2Kgs 8:29]]; [[2Kgs.9.15|2Kgs 9:15]] = [[2Chr.22.6|2Chr 22:6]] (read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִןהַֿמּ‎</span>), [[Zech.13.6|Zech 13:6]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּין יָדֶיךָ‎</span>); symbolic of violence [[Jer.6.7|Jer 6:7]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֳלִי‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּצַע וְחַבּוּרָה וּמַכָּה טְרִיָּה‎</span> [[Isa.1.6|Isa 1:6]] (of Judah, under figure of maltreated man), compare [[Mic.1.9|Mic 1:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲנוּשָׁה‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span>), [[Nah.3.19|Nah 3:19]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֶׁבֶר‎</span>; of Nineveh), especially in Jeremiah (of Judah): [[Jer.10.19|Jer 10:19]]; [[Jer.14.17|Jer 14:17]]; [[Jer.30.12|Jer 30:12]] (all + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַחֲלָה‎</span> ***severe***; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֶׁבֶר‎</span>), [[Jer.15.18|Jer 15:18]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲנוּשָׁה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span>), [[Jer.19.8|Jer 19:8]]; [[Jer.47.17|Jer 47:17]]; [[Jer.50.13|Jer 50:13]], as accusative of congnate meaning with verb [[Jer.30.14|Jer 30:14]], with verb <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רָפָא‎</span> ***heal*** v[[Jer.30.17|Jer 30:17]] also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַחַץ מ‎</span> [[Isa.30.26|Isa 30:26]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֶׁבֶר‎</span>); of wounds made by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s arrows [[Ps.64.8|Ps 64:8]]. 

**2.** = ***slaughter*** [[1Sam.4.10|1Sam 4:10]]; [[1Sam.14.30|1Sam 14:30]], usually as accusative of congnate meaning with verb with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִכָּה‎</span> [[Josh.10.20|Josh 10:20]]; [[1Sam.6.19|1Sam 6:19]]; [[1Sam.14.14|1Sam 14:14]]; [[1Sam.19.8|1Sam 19:8]]; [[1Sam.23.5|1Sam 23:5]]; [[1Kgs.20.21|1Kgs 20:21]]; [[2Chr.13.17|2Chr 13:17]]. 

**3.** = ***defeat***, as accusative of congnate meaning with verb with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִכָּה‎</span> [[Josh.10.10|Josh 10:10]]; [[Josh.11.33|Josh 11:33]]; [[Josh.15.8|Josh 15:8]]; [[2Chr.28.5|2Chr 28:5]] = ***conquest*** (of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>) [[Isa.14.6|Isa 14:6]]. 

**4.** ***plague***, especially disease, as punishment (of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>), [[1Sam.4.8|1Sam 4:8]]; [[Deut.28.59|Deut 28:59]] (3 t. in verse) (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֳלִי‎</span>), v[[Deut.28.61|Deut 28:61]] (\|\| ***id.***), [[Deut.29.21|Deut 29:21]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּחֲלֻאִים‎</span>), [[Lev.26.21|Lev 26:21]] ([[BDB#H|H]]), as accusative of congnate meaning with verb [[Num.11.33|Num 11:33]]. — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַכּוֺת‎</span> [[2Chr.2.9|2Chr 2:9]] read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַכֹּלֶת‎</span> (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אכל‎</span>) as \|\| [[1Kgs.5.25|1Kgs 5:25]], so [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] [[BDB#Be|Be]] [[BDB#Öt|Öttli]] and [[BDB#The|Th]]<sup>[[1Kgs.5.25|1Kgs 5:25]]</sup>.
