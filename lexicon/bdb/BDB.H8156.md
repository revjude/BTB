**Qal** ***Participle*** (with accusative of congnate meaning with verb <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֶׁ֫סַע‎</span>): masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְשֹׁסַע‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֶׁסַע פַּרְסָה‎</span> [[Lev.11.7|Lev 11:7]] ([[BDB#P|P]]; on <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֹׁ‎</span> compare [[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>BN 84</sup>) ***cleaving the cleft of the hoof***, i.e. having cloven hoof, so feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֹׁסַ֫עַת‎</span> v[[Lev.11.3|Lev 11:3]], compare [[Deut.14.6|Deut 14:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֶׁסַע אֵינֶנָּה שֹׁסַ֫עַת‎</span> [[Lev.11.26|Lev 11:26]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); ***passive*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַפַּרְסָה הַשְּׁסוּעָה‎</span> [[Deut.14.7|Deut 14:7]]. **Pi\`el** ***tear in two***, a lion, kid, ***Imperfect, Infinitive construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיְשַׁסְּעֵהוּ כְּשַׁסַּע‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַגְּדִי‎</span> [[Judg.14.6|Judg 14:6]] a bird (by, or at, its wings), ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular consecutive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְשִׁסַּע אֹתוֺ‎</span> [[Lev.1.17|Lev 1:17]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); ***imperfect*** 3 masculine singular (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶתאֲֿנָשָׁיו בַּדְּבָרִים‎</span>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיְשַׁסַּע‎</span> [[1Sam.24.8|1Sam 24:8]] gives too violent a meaning; probably corrupt for word = ***restrain***, or the like [[BDB#Dr|Dr]] [[BDB#HPS|HPS]], compare [[BDB#Bu|Bu]] [[BDB#Now|Now]] (hesitantly).
