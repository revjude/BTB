 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעֲכָה‎</span> **proper name, masculine, feminine and of a people** 

**1. masculine** [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Μ</span>(<span class="bdbLangGreek">ο</span>)<span class="bdbLangGreek">ωχα</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Μα</span>(<span class="bdbLangGreek">α</span>)<span class="bdbLangGreek">χα</span>: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** son of Nahor by his concubine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רְאוּמָה‎</span> [[Gen.22.22|Gen 22:22]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), ancestor of people see 

**3. b.** ==father of== <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָנָן‎</span>, a hero of David [[1Chr.11.43|1Chr 11:43]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** Simeonite name [[1Chr.27.16|1Chr 27:16]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** father of Achish [[1Kgs.2.39|1Kgs 2:39]] (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָעוֺךְ‎</span> above). 

**2. feminine** [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Μ</span>(<span class="bdbLangGreek">ο</span>)<span class="bdbLangGreek">ωχα</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Μααχα,</span> etc.: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** Geshurite princess, wife of David, mother of Absalom, [[2Sam.3.3|2Sam 3:3]] = [[1Chr.3.2|1Chr 3:2]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** daughter of Absalom, wife of Rehoboam, mother of Abijam, [[1Kgs.15.2|1Kgs 15:2]]; [[2Chr.11.20|2Chr 11:20]]; [[2Chr.11.21|2Chr 11:21]]; [[2Chr.11.22|2Chr 11:22]] read also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[2Chr.13.2|2Chr 13:2]] (so [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]]) for [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִיכָיָה‎</span>, where called daughter of Uriel of Gibeah (on this difficulty see [[BDB#Be|Be]] [[BDB#Öt|Öttli]], and compare **c**). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** mother of Asa, and called also daughter of Absalom (as **b**) [[1Kgs.15.10|1Kgs 15:10]], compare v[[1Kgs.15.13|1Kgs 15:13]] = [[2Chr.15.16|2Chr 15:16]], (on this difficulty see [[BDB#The|Th]] [[BDB#Kmp|Kmp]]: in [[BDB#Kau|Kau]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** concubine of Caleb according to [[1Chr.2.48|1Chr 2:48]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **e.** wife of Machir [[1Chr.7.16|1Chr 7:16]], compare v[[1Chr.7.15|1Chr 7:15]] (corrupt, see [[BDB#Be|Be]]). **feminine** ==wife of== (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יעיאל‎</span>), father of Gibeon, [[1Chr.8.29|1Chr 8:29]]; [[1Chr.9.35|1Chr 9:35]]. 

**3. of a people**, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Μοοχα</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Μωχα</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Μα</span>(<span class="bdbLangGreek">α</span>)<span class="bdbLangGreek">χα</span>; dwelling west of Bashan, near ***Soba***, and ***Geshur*** (compare especially adjective, of a people below), [[2Sam.10.6|2Sam 10:6]]; [[2Sam.10.8|2Sam 10:8]] = [[1Chr.19.7|1Chr 19:7]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרַם מ‎</span> v[[1Chr.19.6|1Chr 19:6]] (compare [[Gen.22.24|Gen 22:24]] above); = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעֲכָת‎</span> [[Josh.13.13|Josh 13:13]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּשׁוּר‎</span>), [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">ὀ Μαχατει</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">Μαχαθι</span>. — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָבֵל בֵּית מַעֲכָה‎</span> see below <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּיִת‎</span>, p. 112 above, and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָבֵל‎</span>, p. 5.
