 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַךְ‎</span> **adjective** ==tender, delicate, soft==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ר‎</span> absolute [[Gen.18.7|Gen 18:7]] +, construct [[Deut.20.8|Deut 20:8]]; [[2Chr.13.7|2Chr 13:7]] feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַכָּה‎</span> [[Deut.28.56|Deut 28:56]] +; masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֵבִּים‎</span> [[Gen.33.13|Gen 33:13]] feminine plural absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַכּוֺת‎</span> [[Gen.29.17|Gen 29:17]]; [[Job.40.27|Job 40:27]] — 

**1.** ***tender*** of flesh [[Gen.18.7|Gen 18:7]] ([[BDB#J|J]]; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֶּןבֶּֿקָר‎</span>); ***tender, delicate***, especially in body, of children [[Gen.33.13|Gen 33:13]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), compare (implying weakness of undeveloped character) [[2Sam.3.39|2Sam 3:39]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַעַר וָרָ֑ךְ‎</span> [[1Chr.22.5|1Chr 22:5]]; [[1Chr.29.1|1Chr 29:1]], (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְיָתִיד‎</span>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֵךְ‎</span> [[Prov.4.3|Prov 4:3]] ('of tenderage,' Toy, q. v.); of man ( + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָנֹג‎</span>; i.e. delicately nurtured) [[Deut.28.54|Deut 28:54]], so of woman v[[Deut.28.56|Deut 28:56]] ( + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲנֻגָּה‎</span>), so, figurative of Babylonian, [[Isa.47.1|Isa 47:1]] ( + ***id.***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַךְ‎</span> as substantive =***tender*** (twing) [[Ezek.17.22|Ezek 17:22]] (in figurative); ***delicate, weak***, of eyes [[Gen.29.17|Gen 29:17]] ([[BDB#E|E]]). 

**2.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֵךְ (ה)לֵבָב‎</span> ***weak of heart***, timid, [[Deut.20.8|Deut 20:8]] ( + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָרֵא‎</span>), [[2Chr.13.7|2Chr 13:7]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַעַד‎</span>). 

**3.** ***soft***, of words, absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַכּוֺת‎</span> [[Job.40.27|Job 40:27]] = substantive, mild, gentle words (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּחֲנוּנִים‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעֲנֶה רַךְ‎</span> [[Prov.15.1|Prov 15:1]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּבַר עֶצֶב‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָשׁוֺן רַכָּה‎</span> [[Prov.25.15|Prov 25:15]].
