 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֶ֫שֶׁת‎</span> **noun feminine**<sup>[[Exod.27.5|Exod 27:5]]</sup> ==net==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֶשֶׁת‎</span> [[Hos.5.1|Hos 5:1]] + 11 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רָ֑שֶׁת‎</span> [[Prov.1.17|Prov 1:17]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֶשֶׁת‎</span> [[Exod.27.4|Exod 27:4]]; [[Exod.38.4|Exod 38:4]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רִשְׁתִּי‎</span> [[Hos.7.12|Hos 7:12]] + 2 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רִשְׁתּוֺ‎</span> [[Ps.10.9|Ps 10:9]]; [[Ps.35.8|Ps 35:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רִשְׁתָּם‎</span> [[Ezek.19.8|Ezek 19:8]]; [[Ps.35.7|Ps 35:7]] — 

**1.** ***net:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** for catching (birds, etc.) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִנָּם מְזֹרָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָרָ֑שֶׁת‎</span> [[Prov.1.17|Prov 1:17]] ***to no purpose is the net spread*** in the eyes of any bird; elsewhere figurative (1) of judgment of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>, all with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פרשׁ‎</span>: [[Hos.7.12|Hos 7:12]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּעוֺף הַשָּׁמַיִם אוֺרִידֵם‎</span>), [[Ezek.12.13|Ezek 12:13]]; [[Ezek.17.20|Ezek 17:20]] (both \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְצוּדָתִי‎</span>), [[Ezek.32.3|Ezek 32:3]] (of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> catching Pharaoh, under figure of sea-monster; \|\| II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵרֶם‎</span>); (2) of nations capturing king of Israel under figure of lion [[Ezek.19.8|Ezek 19:8]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פרשׁ‎</span>; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁחְתָּם‎</span>); (3) of priests and rulers (as entangling people in sin) [[Hos.5.1|Hos 5:1]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פַּח‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** spread for feet of man (figurative): (1) by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Lam.1.13|Lam 1:13]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּרַשׁ‎</span>); (2) by wicked (= ***plot***) [[Prov.29.5|Prov 29:5]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פרשׁ‎</span>), [[Ps.140.6|Ps 140:6]] (c. ***id.***; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טָמַן פַּךְ‎</span>), [[Ps.10.9|Ps 10:9]]; [[Ps.25.15|Ps 25:15]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֻׁלַּח בְּדֶשֶׁת בְּרַגְלָיו‎</span> [[Job.18.8|Job 18:8]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׂבָכָה‎</span>); elsewhere with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טמן‎</span> [[Ps.9.16|Ps 9:16]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁחַת‎</span>), [[Ps.31.5|Ps 31:5]]; [[Ps.35.7|Ps 35:7]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁחַת רִשְׁתָּם‎</span>), v[[Ps.31.8|Ps 31:8]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵכִין‎</span> [[Ps.57.7|Ps 57:7]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁיחָה‎</span>).
