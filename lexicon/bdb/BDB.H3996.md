**1.** ***entrance***, i.e. place or way of entrance, into a city [[Judg.1.24|Judg 1:24]]; [[Judg.1.25|Judg 1:25]]; [[1Chr.4.39|1Chr 4:39]]; [[Prov.8.3|Prov 8:3]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פְּתָחִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span>); into buildings [[2Kgs.11.16|2Kgs 11:16]]; [[2Kgs.16.18|2Kgs 16:18]]; [[1Chr.9.19|1Chr 9:19]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֹׁמְרֵי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמָּבוֺא‎</span>), [[2Chr.23.13|2Chr 23:13]]; [[2Chr.23.15|2Chr 23:15]]; [[Jer.38.14|Jer 38:14]]; [[Ezek.42.9|Ezek 42:9]]; [[Ezek.46.19|Ezek 46:19]] see also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְבוֺאֹת יָם‎</span> ***entrance of (the) sea, gate of the sea***, of situation of Tyre [[Ezek.27.3|Ezek 27:3]]. 

**2.** ***entering***, act of entrance, by violence, in storm of city [[Ezek.26.10|Ezek 26:10]] ***coming in*** or together, making a crowd, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּמְבוֺא עָ֑ם‎</span> [[Ezek.33.31|Ezek 33:31]] simile of eagerness to hear Yahweh's word; in phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶתמֿוֺצָאֲךָ וְאֶתמֿבואך‎</span> [[2Sam.3.25|2Sam 3:25]] ***thy going out and thy coming in, mode of entering temple***, or ***the people who enter*** [[Ezek.44.5|Ezek 44:5]] particularly of ***sunset*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְבוֺא הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ‎</span> [[Ps.104.19|Ps 104:19]] = ***west*** [[Deut.11.30|Deut 11:30]]; [[Josh.1.4|Josh 1:4]]; [[Josh.23.4|Josh 23:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַיָּם הַגָּדוֺל מְבוֺא‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ‎</span> = Mediterranean, [[Zech.8.7|Zech 8:7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ מְבוֺא הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ‎</span> (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ מִזְרָח‎</span>), [[Mal.1.11|Mal 1:11]]; [[Ps.50.1|Ps 50:1]]; [[Ps.113.3|Ps 113:3]] ***sunset*** = ***west***, opposed to sunrise = east; in phrase ***from east to west***, i.e. over the whole earth, everywhere on earth.
