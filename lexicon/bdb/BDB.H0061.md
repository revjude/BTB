 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲבָל‎</span> **adverb** 

**1.** in older Hebrew with an asseverative force, verily, of a truth [[Gen.42.21|Gen 42:21]]; [[2Sam.14.5|2Sam 14:5]]; [[1Kgs.1.43|1Kgs 1:43]]; [[2Kgs.4.14|2Kgs 4:14]], with a slight adversative force, nay, but [[Gen.17.19|Gen 17:19]] ([[BDB#P|P]]). 

**2.** in late Hebrew as a decided adversative, howbeit, but [[Dan.10.7|Dan 10:7]]; [[Dan.10.21|Dan 10:21]]; [[Ezra.10.13|Ezra 10:13]]; [[2Chr.1.4|2Chr 1:4]]; [[2Chr.19.3|2Chr 19:3]]; [[2Chr.33.17|2Chr 33:17]] (compare Arabic <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhFwAcAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAXABwAAAJDjI+pi+DAYnAGymXnZTTvRH3iKD1XWD0ehk5r+Y4t6dJNLM719+psjwMBebPiT1XUIVUKJXHl+x1MTejSp1zSkDZbAQA7"> ***of a truth***, sometimes, from the context, ***nay rather*** Qor 2:82; 2:94; 2:110; 2:129; 2:149; 2:165; 2:261; 3:143; 4:52 etc.)
