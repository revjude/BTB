 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵ֫לֶק‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Josh.18.5|Josh 18:5]]</sup> ==portion, tract, territory== — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> absolute [[Gen.31.14|Gen 31:14]] +; construct [[Gen.14.24|Gen 14:24]] +; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶלְקִי‎</span> [[Ps.16.5|Ps 16:5]] + 6 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶלְקְךָ‎</span> [[Num.18.20|Num 18:20]]; [[Eccl.9.9|Eccl 9:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶלְקֶ֑ךָ‎</span> [[Ps.50.18|Ps 50:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶלְקֵךְ‎</span> [[Isa.57.6|Isa 57:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶלְקוֺ‎</span> [[Hab.1.16|Hab 1:16]] + 5 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶלְקָם‎</span> [[Gen.14.24|Gen 14:24]] + 3 t.; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲלָקִים‎</span> [[Josh.18.5|Josh 18:5]] + 5 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶלְקֵיהֶם‎</span> [[Hos.5.7|Hos 5:7]] — 

**1.** ***portion, share:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of booty [[Gen.14.24|Gen 14:24]] (twice in verse); [[Num.31.36|Num 31:36]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) [[1Sam.30.24|1Sam 30:24]] (twice in verse). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of food [[Lev.6.10|Lev 6:10]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יאכלו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כּח‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Deut.18.8|Deut 18:8]] = food [[Hab.1.16|Hab 1:16]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַאֲכָל‎</span>); so (of unrestricted charity) [[Eccl.11.2|Eccl 11:2]] see [[BDB#Now|Now]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span>, ***share*** or ***interest in*** [[Eccl.9.6|Eccl 9:6]], with idea of ***obligation to*** [[Gen.31.14|Gen 31:14]] ([[BDB#E|E]]; in father's house), [[2Sam.20.1|2Sam 20:1]] = [[1Kgs.12.16|1Kgs 12:16]] = [[2Chr.10.16|2Chr 10:16]] (in royal line; all \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַחֲלָה‎</span>); of ***right*** (***privilege***) ***in*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ביהוה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Josh.22.25|Josh 22:25]]; [[Josh.22.27|Josh 22:27]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); [[Neh.2.20|Neh 2:20]] (in Jerusalem; + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צְדָקָה וְזִכָּרוֺן‎</span>), 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** proper ***share*** or ***part*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַעֲנֶה חֶלְקִי‎</span> [[Job.32.17|Job 32:17]] i.e. I will do my part in replying. Especially 

**2.** ***portion, tract***, of land: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** as distributive at conquest [[Josh.19.9|Josh 19:9]], seven ***portions*** [[Josh.18.5|Josh 18:5]]; [[Josh.18.6|Josh 18:6]]; [[Josh.18.9|Josh 18:9]] (all [[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]) v[[Josh.18.7|Josh 18:7]] ([[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]; + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נחלה‎</span> in \|\| clause), [[Josh.15.13|Josh 15:13]]; [[Josh.19.9|Josh 19:9]] (both [[BDB#P|P]]); none for tribe of Levi [[Deut.10.9|Deut 10:9]]; [[Deut.12.12|Deut 12:12]]; [[Deut.14.27|Deut 14:27]]; [[Deut.14.29|Deut 14:29]]; [[Deut.18.1|Deut 18:1]]; [[Josh.18.7|Josh 18:7]] (all [[BDB#D|D]]; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נחלה‎</span>), [[Josh.14.4|Josh 14:4]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), none for priests [[Num.18.20|Num 18:20]] ([[BDB#P|P]]; \|\| verb <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נחל‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of land possessed by people [[Hos.5.7|Hos 5:7]]; [[Mic.2.4|Mic 2:4]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** portion to be assigned after exile [[Isa.61.7|Isa 61:7]]; [[Ezek.45.7|Ezek 45:7]]; [[Ezek.48.8|Ezek 48:8]]; [[Ezek.48.21|Ezek 48:21]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶלְקוֺ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַלאַֿדְמַת הַקֹּדֶשׁ‎</span> [[Zech.2.16|Zech 2:16]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** specifically of ***town-land***, district about a town, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִזְרְעֵאל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[2Kgs.9.10|2Kgs 9:10]]; [[2Kgs.9.36|2Kgs 9:36]] + [[1Kgs.21.23|1Kgs 21:23]] ([[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵל‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִזְ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַשָּׂדֶה בְּח‎</span> v[[1Kgs.21.37|1Kgs 21:37]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **e.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַחֵלֶק‎</span> [[Amos.7.4|Amos 7:4]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּהוֺם‎</span>) apparently of ***land*** of Israel 

**3.** hence ***portion***, i.e. (acquired) ***possession:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of people as <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s possession [[Deut.32.9|Deut 32:9]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶבֶל נחלתו‎</span>); but especially 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> as possession of his servants: of priests, as enjoying perquisites of altar [[Num.18.20|Num 18:20]] ([[BDB#P|P]]; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נחלה‎</span>), in spiritual sense [[Ps.73.26|Ps 73:26]]; [[Ps.119.57|Ps 119:57]]; [[Lam.3.24|Lam 3:24]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְנָת חֶלְקִי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ps.16.5|Ps 16:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּאֶרֶץ הַחַיִּים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Ps.142.6|Ps 142:6]] hence <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יעקב‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Jer.10.16|Jer 10:16]] = [[Jer.51.19|Jer 51:19]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁבֶט נַחֲלָתוֺ‎</span> in \|\| clause) 

**4.** chosen ***portion***, habitual mode of life <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עִםמְֿנָאֲפִים ח‎</span> [[Ps.50.18|Ps 50:18]] ***with adulterers is thy chosen life*** (companionship, shewing character); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּחַלְּקֵינַֿחַל חֶלְקֵךְ‎</span> [[Isa.57.6|Isa 57:6]] ***among smooth*** (things i.e. stones) ***of a brook-bed is thy chosen life*** (of idolatrous habit; see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָלָק‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶלְקָם בַּחַיִּים‎</span> [[Ps.17.14|Ps 17:14]]. 

**5.** ***portion, award***, from God <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֱלוֺהַּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Job.31.2|Job 31:2]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נחלה‎</span>); specifically of punishment of wicked [[Isa.17.14|Isa 17:14]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גּוֺרָל‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עִםאֵֿל‎</span> [[Job.27.13|Job 27:13]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵאֱלֹהִים‎</span> [[Job.20.29|Job 20:29]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נחלה‎</span>); = ***profit, reward*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶלְקִי מִכָּלעֲֿמָלִי‎</span> [[Eccl.2.10|Eccl 2:10]], compare v[[Eccl.2.21|Eccl 2:21]]; [[Eccl.3.22|Eccl 3:22]]; [[Eccl.5.17|Eccl 5:17]]; [[Eccl.5.18|Eccl 5:18]]; [[Eccl.9.9|Eccl 9:9]]. — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְחֵלָק‎</span> [[Job.17.5|Job 17:5]] is dubious: ***a share*** of feast, or of booty, [[BDB#Kmp|Kmp]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]]; ***a prey, spoil***, [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Da|Da]]; [[BDB#AV|AV]] ***flattery*** does not suit context. 



 III. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵ֫לֶק‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==smoothness, seductiveness== of speech, only singular construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּחֵלֶק שְׁפָתֶיהָ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּדִּיחֶ֑נּוּ‎</span> [[Prov.7.21|Prov 7:21]] ***with the seductiveness of her lips she impelleth him*** (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִטַּתּוּ בְּרֹב לִקְחָהּ‎</span>). — I. and II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵלֶק‎</span> see below I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חלק‎</span>.
