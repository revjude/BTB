 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַ֫עַר‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[2Sam.18.8|2Sam 18:8]]</sup> ==wood, forest, thicket== ([[BDB#MI|MI]]<sup>21</sup> plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎היערן‎</span> (= proper name, of a location ?); Phoenician <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יער‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יר‎</span>, compare [[BDB#DHM|DHM]] in [[BDB#MV|MV]]<sup></sup> (10) 983; Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎יַעֲרָא‎</span>, <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhJwAVAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAnABUAAAJWjI+py63gGjTzgGpvXLcjn4Db12GBFprjiaaPOrbut3ISzOCxnvKO/BPVWLMXcFc0HoNDitDCMfmcwCcxYyWVWFuStgQOi4+ysRkijiabuSnb+I7LVwUAOw=="> ***wood, thicket***; Assyrian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***âru***</u></abbr> [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>HWB 230</sup>, compare Jäger<sup>BAS i, 476</sup>) — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ַ֫ יעַר‎</span> [[Isa.7.2|Isa 7:2]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָ֑עַר‎</span> [[Mic.3.12|Mic 3:12]] +; with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ה‎</span> locative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעֲרָה‎</span> [[Josh.17.15|Josh 17:15]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ַ֫ יעַר‎</span> [[2Sam.18.6|2Sam 18:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעְרָהּ‎</span> [[Jer.46.23|Jer 46:23]], etc.; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְעָרִים‎</span> [[Ezra.2.25|Ezra 2:25]] + 4 t. (including [[Ezek.34.25|Ezek 34:25]] [[BDB#Qr|Qr]], so [[BDB#Co|Co]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְעָרוֺת‎</span> [[Ps.29.9|Ps 29:9]] — 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***wood, forest, wooded height***, with trees to be felled [[Josh.17.15|Josh 17:15]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עלה‎</span> ***go up*** to), v[[Josh.17.18|Josh 17:18]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[Deut.19.5|Deut 19:5]] (not elsewhere Hexateuch); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵץ מִיַּעַר כְּרָתוֺ‎</span> [[Jer.10.13|Jer 10:13]] ***as wood out of a forest he hath cut it***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יחטבו מןהֿיערים‎</span> [[Ezek.39.10|Ezek 39:10]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עצים מןהֿשׂדה‎</span> in \|\| clause); as producing trees <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יער צוֺמֵחַ עֵצִים‎</span> [[Eccl.2.6|Eccl 2:6]] in designation of Solomon's palace <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּית יַעַר הַלְּבָנוֺן‎</span> [[1Kgs.7.2|1Kgs 7:2]] (on structure of this house see [[BDB#Sta|Sta]]<sup>Salomos Bauten, ZAW 1883, 150</sup>), [[1Kgs.10.17|1Kgs 10:17]]; [[1Kgs.10.21|1Kgs 10:21]] = [[2Chr.9.16|2Chr 9:16]]; [[2Chr.9.20|2Chr 9:20]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּית הַיָּ֫עַר‎</span> [[Isa.22.8|Isa 22:8]] figurative of foes to be cut down and destroyed <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִקַּף סִבְכֵי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎היער‎</span> [[Isa.10.34|Isa 10:34]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּֽרְתוּ יַעְרָהּ‎</span> [[Jer.46.23|Jer 46:23]] so [[Isa.32.19|Isa 32:19]] and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יער הַבָּצִו֯ר‎</span> [[Zech.11.2|Zech 11:2]] (read [[BDB#Kt|Kt]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַבָּצוּר‎</span> ***inaccessible forest***). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** as hiding-place for fugitive [[1Sam.22.5|1Sam 22:5]] lurking-place of wild beasts [[Amos.3.4|Amos 3:4]]; [[Mic.5.7|Mic 5:7]]; [[2Kgs.2.24|2Kgs 2:24]]; [[Jer.5.6|Jer 5:6]]; [[Jer.12.8|Jer 12:8]]; [[Isa.56.9|Isa 56:9]]; [[Ps.50.10|Ps 50:10]]; [[Ps.80.14|Ps 80:14]]; [[Ps.104.20|Ps 104:20]], compare [[Ezek.34.25|Ezek 34:25]] (read [[BDB#Qr|Qr]], see above) 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** stripped by voice of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ps.29.9|Ps 29:9]] devoured by fire [[Isa.9.17|Isa 9:17]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סִבְכֵי הַיּ‎</span>; figurative of the people); so in metaphor or simile of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s judgements [[Isa.10.18|Isa 10:18]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּבוֺד יַעְרוֺ‎</span>, figurative of Assyr.), [[Jer.21.14|Jer 21:14]]; [[Ezek.21.2|Ezek 21:2]]; [[Ezek.21.3|Ezek 21:3]] (see below) [[Ps.83.15|Ps 83:15]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּרְמֶל‎</span> (***garden-land***) [[Isa.29.17|Isa 29:17]]; [[Isa.32.15|Isa 32:15]] but also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַר כַּרְמִלּוֺ‎</span> ***his garden woodland*** [[2Kgs.19.23|2Kgs 19:23]] = [[Isa.37.24|Isa 37:24]], and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַר בְּתוֺךְ כַּרְמֶל‎</span> [[Mic.7.14|Mic 7:14]] (secluded and fertile abode for flock, figurative of people, see [[BDB#Che|Che]]; [[BDB#Hi|Hi]]-[[BDB#St|St]] thinks of ***sacred grove***). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **e.** = ***thicket***, especially as symbol of desolation [[Hos.2.14|Hos 2:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּמוֺת יער‎</span> ***thicket-covered heights*** (overgrown with bushes and trees) [[Mic.3.12|Mic 3:12]] = [[Jer.26.18|Jer 26:18]] also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּיַּעַר בּעֲרַב‎</span> [[Isa.21.13|Isa 21:13]] ***in the thicket*** (or ***bushes***, so [[BDB#VB|VB]]) ***in Arabia*** must ye lodge, caravans of Dedanites. **feminine** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲצֵי (ה)יער‎</span> ***trees of the forest*** [[Isa.7.2|Isa 7:2]] (in sim), [[Isa.10.19|Isa 10:19]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעְרוֺ שְׁאָר עֵץ‎</span>, figurative), [[Isa.44.14|Isa 44:14]]; [[Ezek.15.2|Ezek 15:2]], also v[[Ezek.15.6|Ezek 15:6]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵץ היער‎</span>), [[Song.2.3|Song 2:3]] (in simile); figurative, as singing before <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ps.96.12|Ps 96:12]] = [[1Chr.16.33|1Chr 16:33]]; [[Isa.44.23|Isa 44:23]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יער וְכָלעֵֿץ בּוֺ‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **g.** particular forests are: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יער אפרים‎</span> [[2Sam.18.6|2Sam 18:6]], see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶפְרַיִם‎</span> above; compare v[[2Sam.18.18|2Sam 18:18]] v[[2Sam.18.17|2Sam 18:17]] (see also [[Josh.17.15|Josh 17:15]]; [[Josh.17.18|Josh 17:18]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַר חָ֑רֶת‎</span> [[1Sam.22.5|1Sam 22:5]] (see above); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַר לְבָנוֺן‎</span> (see above); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַר הַשָּׂדֶה נֶגֶב‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעַר הַנָּגֶב‎</span> [[Ezek.21.2|Ezek 21:2]]; [[Ezek.21.3|Ezek 21:3]] (see above) figurative of Judah (land and people); see also  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְעָרִים‎</span> below — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׂדֵיָֿ֑ יעַר‎</span> [[Ps.132.6|Ps 132:6]] is a proper name, of a location (but [[BDB#Bae|Bae]] thinks appellative 'auf waldigem Gefilcle'), see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִרְיַת יְעָרִים‎</span>.
