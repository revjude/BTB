 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָטַר‎</span>] **verb** ==surround== (Late Hebrew ***id.***, and derivatives; Phoenician <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עטרת‎</span> ***wreath***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עטר‎</span> Pi\`el denominative ***crown*** [[BDB#Ldzb|Lzb]]<sup>339</sup>; Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎עֲטַר‎</span>; Assyrian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***etêru***</u></abbr>, ***spare***, ***rescue***); — 

**Qal** ***Imperfect*** 2 masculine singular suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּצִּנָּה רָצוֺן תַּעְטְרֶנּוּ‎</span> [[Ps.5.13|Ps 5:13]] ***as with a shield, with favour will thou surround him; Participle*** plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֹטְרִים אֶלדָּֿוִד‎</span> [[1Sam.23.26|1Sam 23:26]] Saul and his men ***were surrounding*** (closing in) ***upon David.***
