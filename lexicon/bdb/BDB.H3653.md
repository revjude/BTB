 III. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֵּן‎</span> **noun masculine** ==base, pedestal, office== (Late Hebrew <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּנָּה‎</span>; Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎כַּנְתָּא‎</span>, <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhGwAXAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAbABcAAAJLjI+py+0OwFOR1mmiRHqf7mUXF4pacqJl0FnlKjbjA381RlrLPcdkqtLZQMKf7ah6hZa9lqkGfIKmUxf1GmVVsNTdDecDM77iMqMAADs=">) — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כ‎</span> [[Isa.33.23|Isa 33:23]]; [[1Kgs.7.31|1Kgs 7:31]] (but see below); suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּנּוֺ‎</span> [[Exod.30.18|Exod 30:18]] + 11 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּנֶּ֑ךָ‎</span> [[Gen.40.13|Gen 40:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּנִּי‎</span> [[Gen.41.13|Gen 41:13]] — 

**1.** literally ***base, pedestal***, [[1Kgs.7.31|1Kgs 7:31]] (like) the work of ***a pedestal*** ([[BDB#The|Th]] [[BDB#VB|VB]]), according to [[BDB#Sta|Sta]]<sup>ZAW iii, 1883, 161, 162</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעֲשֵׂהכֵֿן‎</span> is in wrong place, being originally part of a gloss to v[[1Kgs.7.35|1Kgs 7:35]] — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כ‎</span> in v[[1Kgs.7.29|1Kgs 7:29]] = ***thus***, or is text error; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֵּןתָּֿרְנָם‎</span> [[Isa.33.23|Isa 33:23]] ***the base*** (***support***or ***socket***) ***of their mast*** (so [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] and most); especially of base of laver of tabernacle [[Exod.30.18|Exod 30:18]]; [[Exod.30.28|Exod 30:28]]; [[Exod.31.9|Exod 31:9]]; [[Exod.35.16|Exod 35:16]]; [[Exod.38.8|Exod 38:8]]; [[Exod.39.39|Exod 39:39]]; [[Exod.40.11|Exod 40:11]]; [[Lev.8.11|Lev 8:11]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]). 

**2.** ***office, place*** [[Gen.40.13|Gen 40:13]]; [[Gen.41.13|Gen 41:13]] (both [[BDB#E|E]]); hence (late) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַלכַּֿנּוֺ‎</span> ***in his place*** [[Dan.11.20|Dan 11:20]]; [[Dan.11.21|Dan 11:21]]; [[Dan.11.38|Dan 11:38]] (i.e. in his stead, as his successor, compare German ***an seiner Stelle***); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל‎</span> omitted v[[Dan.11.7|Dan 11:7]].
