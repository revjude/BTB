**1.** ***cleave, cleave open***, followed by accusative, [[Judg.15.19|Judg 15:19]] ***God cleft open the hollow*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמֵּכְתֵּשׁ‎</span>), and water came out, compare [[Isa.48.21|Isa 48:21]] (object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוּר‎</span>), also [[Ps.74.15|Ps 74:15]] ***bring forth by cleaving***, object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעְיָן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָנָחַ֑ל‎</span>, all three of divine operation; ***cleave*** or ***rip open*** pregnant women [[Amos.1.13|Amos 1:13]] of a broken staff, tearing the shoulder [[Ezek.29.7|Ezek 29:7]] (but read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּף‎</span> ***hand*** for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּתֵף‎</span>, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] [[BDB#Sm|Sm]] (?)[[BDB#Co|Co]].); ***cleave*** wood [[Eccl.10.9|Eccl 10:9]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַסִּיעַ אֲבָנִים‎</span>); of ploughing (furrowing) the earth [[Ps.141.7|Ps 141:7]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֹּלֵחֵ‎</span>; in simile); especially of ***dividing*** the sea, [[Exod.14.16|Exod 14:16]] ([[BDB#P|P]]) [[Neh.9.11|Neh 9:11]]; [[Ps.78.13|Ps 78:13]], compare [[Isa.63.12|Isa 63:12]], object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַיִם‎</span>; — in all these subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> except [[Exod.14.16|Exod 14:16]] where he commands Moses; of ***hatching out*** (a brood, but no object expressed) [[Isa.34.15|Isa 34:15]], subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִפּוֺו‎</span> ***arrow-snake.***

**1.** ***be cleft, rent open***, subject the ground, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָֽאֲדָמָה‎</span> [[Num.16.31|Num 16:31]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָאָרֶץ‎</span> [[1Kgs.1.40|1Kgs 1:40]] (hyperb.); mountain [[Zech.14.4|Zech 14:4]] ***burst open***, of men hurled from rock [[2Chr.25.12|2Chr 25:12]] of cloud beneath its weight of water [[Job.26.8|Job 26:8]] hyperb. of belly full of words seeking a vent, [[Job.32.19|Job 32:19]] ***like new wine-skins it will burst open***; so of the water-receptacles (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מעינות‎</span>) of the great deep, at the flood [[Gen.7.11|Gen 7:11]] of the water-masses themselves, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תהומות‎</span> [[Prov.3.20|Prov 3:20]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מים ונהלים‎</span> [[Isa.35.6|Isa 35:6]] also of the Red Sea, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִבָּקְעוּ המים‎</span> [[Exod.14.21|Exod 14:21]] of light breaking forth [[Isa.58.8|Isa 58:8]] (figurative); of serpent's egg ***hatching out*** as a viper [[Isa.59.5|Isa 59:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַזּוּרֶה תִּבָּקַע אֶפְעֶה‎</span>.
