 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵ֫רֶם‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Lev.27.28|Lev 27:28]]</sup> 

**1.** ==devoted thing==. 

**2.** ==devotion, ban==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> absolute [[Josh.6.17|Josh 6:17]] + 24 t. (most read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶרֶם‎</span> [[Zech.14.11|Zech 14:11]], [[BDB#Baer|Baer]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵרֶם‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶ֑רֶם‎</span> [[Josh.7.1|Josh 7:1]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶרְמִי‎</span> [[1Kgs.20.42|1Kgs 20:42]]; [[Isa.34.5|Isa 34:5]] — ***thing devoted*** to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>: 

**1.** thing hostile to theocracy, and therefore (in the strictest application) to be either destroyed, or, in the case of certain objects (e.g. silver and gold, vessels of brass and iron [[Josh.6.19|Josh 6:19]]; [[Josh.6.24|Josh 6:24]]), set apart to sacred uses; especially 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of a Canaan. city, as Jericho, including all inhabitants (except Rahab's family) and spoil [[Josh.6.17|Josh 6:17]]; [[Josh.6.18|Josh 6:18]] (twice in verse); [[Josh.7.1|Josh 7:1]]; [[Josh.7.11|Josh 7:11]]; [[Josh.7.15|Josh 7:15]] Achan by taking <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִןהַֿחֵרֶם‎</span> made (camp of) Israel <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Josh.7.12|Josh 7:12]] compare [[Josh.6.18|Josh 6:18]], and became himself <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span>, and was stoned and, with his family and possessions, including the spoil, was burnt [[Josh.7.1|Josh 7:1]]; [[Josh.7.11|Josh 7:11]]; [[Josh.7.12|Josh 7:12]]; [[Josh.7.13|Josh 7:13]] (twice in verse) (all [[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]] except [[Josh.7.1|Josh 7:1]] [[BDB#P|P]]; compare v[[Josh.7.15|Josh 7:15]] v[[Josh.7.24|Josh 7:24]] v[[Josh.7.25|Josh 7:25]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָעַל מַעַל בַּח‎</span> ***committed unfaithfulness in the*** matter of the ***devoted thing*** is term for the sin [[Josh.7.1|Josh 7:1]]; [[Josh.22.20|Josh 22:20]] (both [[BDB#P|P]]), compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָעַל בַּח‎</span> [[1Chr.2.7|1Chr 2:7]] Saul and Israel spared Agag king of Amalek and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֵאשִׁית הַח‎</span> i.e. of the spoil (sheep and oxen), which should have been utterly destroyed [[1Sam.15.21|1Sam 15:21]] (compare v[[1Sam.15.3|1Sam 15:3]] v[[1Sam.15.8|1Sam 15:8]] Saul rejected by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> for this v[[1Sam.15.11|1Sam 15:11]] v[[1Sam.15.26|1Sam 15:26]]); so an idolatrous city in Israel should become <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span>, with all its contents, and be utterly destroyed [[Deut.13.17|Deut 13:17]] (compare v.[[Deut.13.13|Deut 13:13]] v.[[Deut.13.15|Deut 13:15]] v.[[Deut.13.16|Deut 13:16]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of individuals, one having relic of Canaanit. god in his house should become <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span>, the relic being <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Deut.7.26|Deut 7:26]] (twice in verse); every human being who became <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> should be killed [[Lev.27.29|Lev 27:29]] (twice in verse) ([[BDB#P|P]]; see [[BDB#Di|Di]]). 

**2.** apparently (so [[BDB#Di|Di]]) anything ***devoted*** to sanctuary under specially stringent conditions [[Lev.27.28|Lev 27:28]] (see [[BDB#Di|Di]]; and compare [[Ezra.10.8|Ezra 10:8]]); a field consecrated to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> becomes under certain conditions <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּשְׂדֵה הַח‎</span> [[Lev.27.21|Lev 27:21]] every <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> is <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קֹדֶשׁ קָֽדָשִׁים ליהוה‎</span> v[[Lev.27.28|Lev 27:28]], and no <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> that a manmay devote, whether man, beast or field, may be sold or redeemed v[[Lev.27.28|Lev 27:28]] every such <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> (as in case of metals and metal objects [[Josh.6.17|Josh 6:17]]; [[Josh.6.19|Josh 6:19]]) went to Aaron and his sons [[Num.18.14|Num 18:14]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), to Zadokite priests [[Ezek.44.29|Ezek 44:29]]. 

**3.** ***devotion, ban***, involving destruction; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִישׁ חֶרְמִי‎</span> [[1Kgs.20.42|1Kgs 20:42]] ***man under my*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s) ***ban*** (of Benhadad); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַם חֶרְמִי‎</span> [[Isa.34.5|Isa 34:5]] (of Edom); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נתן לַח‎</span> [[Isa.43.28|Isa 43:28]] figurative of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s giving over Judah to Chaldaea; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> not to be in future [[Zech.14.11|Zech 14:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּןאָֿבוֺא וְהִכֵּיתִי אֶתהָֿאָרֶץ ח‎</span> [[Mal.3.24|Mal 3:24]] ***smite the land with a ban***, i.e. utterly destroy it.
