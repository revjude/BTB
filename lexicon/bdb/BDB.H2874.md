 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טֶ֫בַח‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Isa.34.6|Isa 34:6]]</sup> ==slaughtering, slaughter== — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ט‎</span> [[Gen.43.16|Gen 43:16]] + 6 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טָ֑בַח‎</span> [[Ezek.21.20|Ezek 21:20]] + 3 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טִבְחָהּ‎</span> [[Prov.9.2|Prov 9:2]] — 

**1.** ***slaughtering, slaughter***, of animals for food; as accusative of congnate meaning with verb after  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טָבַח‎</span> (q. v.) [[Gen.43.16|Gen 43:16]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[Prov.9.2|Prov 9:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יוּבָ֑ל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּשֶּׂה לַטּ‎</span> [[Isa.53.7|Isa 53:7]], simile of suffering servant of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>; also [[Prov.7.22|Prov 7:22]] simile of fatuousness of one following a strange woman, thoughtless of consequences; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֵרְדוּ לַטּ‎</span> [[Jer.50.27|Jer 50:27]] metaphor of Babylonian leaders, under figure of bullocks; so probably [[Jer.48.15|Jer 48:15]], and perhaps <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּכְרָ֑עוּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַטּ‎</span> [[Isa.65.12|Isa 65:12]]. 

**2.** in poetry figurative ***slaughter***, to which the nations are given over by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Isa.34.2|Isa 34:2]], specifically of Edom v[[Isa.34.6|Isa 34:6]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֶבַח‎</span>; see <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> **6**); of Israel [[Ezek.21.20|Ezek 21:20]], as accusative of congnate meaning with verb after <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טָבַח‎</span> v.[[Ezek.21.15|Ezek 21:15]] of Ammon v.[[Ezek.21.33|Ezek 21:33]].
