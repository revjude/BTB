 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּרַר‎</span>] **verb** ==purify, select== (compare Assyrian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***barâru***</u></abbr>, ***be shining***, in derivatives [[BDB#Zim|Zim]]<sup>BP 46. 73</sup> Belser<sup>BAS ii. 154</sup>; Arabic <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhGgAjAIAAAP///wAAACH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAaACMAAAJKhI+py+0cYngvUjdNvktuDoaIJQKWV5oeKa5sCsdy/MLSrOJ5+Gl9xbrxXDXKbfK7FEvFJSTpVAhTKGoVRExKNyfOynZlanVkXAEAOw=="> ***pious, kind, true***; <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhFQAbAIAAAP///wAAACH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAVABsAAAI5hI+pGt0LQTgzuTVdlRdrGiFPSJbmiZpjyrHUlq4s7MFdqGU09PSnjNoBGcKdxUi6qZTJT3DoiqIKADs="> ***he was pious, good, virtuous, honest***) — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּרוֺתִי‎</span> [[Ezek.20.38|Ezek 20:38]] ***Infinitive*** suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְבָרָם‎</span> [[Eccl.3.18|Eccl 3:18]], [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:67»§ 67</sup> R, 3; compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָבוּר‎</span> [[Eccl.9.1|Eccl 9:1]] (si vera lectio) as metaplastic form, but on text see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בּוּר‎</span> above; ***Participle*** passive masculine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּרוּר‎</span> [[Job.33.3|Job 33:3]] + 4 t.; feminine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּרוּרָה‎</span> [[Neh.5.18|Neh 5:18]]; [[Zeph.3.9|Zeph 3:9]] — 

**1.** ***purge out, purify:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּבָרוֺתִי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִכֶּם הַמֹּרְדִים‎</span> ***and I will purge out from among you the rebels*** [[Ezek.20.38|Ezek 20:38]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׂפָה בְּרוּרָה‎</span> ***a purified lip*** [[Zeph.3.9|Zeph 3:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּרוּר מִלֵּל֑וּ‎</span> ***utter in a pure, sincere manner*** [[Job.33.3|Job 33:3]]. 

**2.** ***choose, select***, only ***Participle*** and in Chronicler: chosen, valiant men [[1Chr.7.40|1Chr 7:40]] porters [[1Chr.9.22|1Chr 9:22]] musicians [[1Chr.16.41|1Chr 16:41]] sheep [[Neh.5.18|Neh 5:18]]. 

**3.** ***cleanse, make shining, polish***, passive participle <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵץ בָּרוּר‎</span> ***polished arrow*** [[Isa.49.2|Isa 49:2]] (compare [[BDB#De|De]] &amp; [[Jer.51.11|Jer 51:11]] below) 

**4.** ***test, prove*** [[Eccl.3.18|Eccl 3:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְבָרָם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָאֱלֹהִים‎</span> ***that God may prove them*** ([[BDB#RV|RV]]); — on [[Eccl.9.1|Eccl 9:1]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בּוּר‎</span>. **Niph\`al** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִבָּרוּ‎</span> [[Isa.52.11|Isa 52:11]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָבָר‎</span> [[2Sam.22.27|2Sam 22:27]] (= [[Ps.18.27|Ps 18:27]]); — ***purify oneself:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ceremonially, the bearers of the sacred vessels [[Isa.52.11|Isa 52:11]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** morally [[2Sam.22.27|2Sam 22:27]] = [[Ps.18.27|Ps 18:27]]. **Pi\`el** ***Infinitive*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְבָרֵר‎</span> [[Dan.11.35|Dan 11:35]] ***purify*** (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִצְרוֺף‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַלְבֵּן‎</span>). 

**Hiph\`il 1.** ***Infinitive*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְהָבַר‎</span> [[Jer.4.11|Jer 4:11]] ***purify, cleanse.*** 

**2.** ***Imperative*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָבֵרוּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַחִצִּים‎</span> [[Jer.51.11|Jer 51:11]] ***polish arrows*** (see **3** above). 

**Hithpa\`el 1.** ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִתְבָּרֲרוּ‎</span> [[Dan.12.10|Dan 12:10]] ***purify oneself.*** 

**2.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּתְבָּרַר‎</span> [[Ps.18.27|Ps 18:27]] = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּתָּבָר‎</span> [[2Sam.22.27|2Sam 22:27]] ***shew oneself pure, just, kind.***
