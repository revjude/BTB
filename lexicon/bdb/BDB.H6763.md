**1.** ***rib*** of man [[Gen.2.21|Gen 2:21]]; [[Gen.2.22|Gen 2:22]] ([[BDB#J|J]]). 

**2.** ***rib*** of hill, i.e. ridge, or terrace [[2Sam.16.13|2Sam 16:13]]. 

**3.** ***side-chambers or cells*** (enclosing temple like ribs) [[1Kgs.6.5|1Kgs 6:5]]; [[1Kgs.6.6|1Kgs 6:6]] (read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַצּ‎</span> for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎היצוע‎</span>, see [ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָצִיעַ‎</span>]), [[1Kgs.7.3|1Kgs 7:3]], so of Ezekiel's temple [[Ezek.41.5|Ezek 41:5]] + 10 t. 41 (on text see [[BDB#Co|Co]] Toy [[BDB#Krae|Krae]]). 

**4.** ***ribs*** of cedar and fir, i.e. ***planks, boards*** (plural), of temple wall [[1Kgs.6.15|1Kgs 6:15]]; [[1Kgs.6.16|1Kgs 6:16]] floor v[[1Kgs.6.15|1Kgs 6:15]]. 

**5.** ***leaves*** of door v[[1Kgs.6.34|1Kgs 6:34]]. 

**6.** (in [[BDB#P|P]]) ***side***, of ark (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרוֺן‎</span>) [[Exod.25.12|Exod 25:12]] (twice in verse); [[Exod.25.14|Exod 25:14]] = [[Exod.37.3|Exod 37:3]] (twice in verse); [[Exod.37.5|Exod 37:5]] of tabernacle (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִשְׁכָּן‎</span>) [[Exod.26.20|Exod 26:20]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֵּאָה‎</span> v[[Exod.26.18|Exod 26:18]]), v[[Exod.26.26|Exod 26:26]] v[[Exod.26.27|Exod 26:27]] v[[Exod.26.27|Exod 26:27]] = [[Exod.36.25|Exod 36:25]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פ‎</span> v[[Exod.36.23|Exod 36:23]]), v[[Exod.36.31|Exod 36:31]] v[[Exod.36.32|Exod 36:32]], [[Exod.26.35|Exod 26:35]] (twice in verse); of altar [[Exod.27.7|Exod 27:7]] = [[Exod.38.7|Exod 38:7]], [[Exod.30.4|Exod 30:4]] = [[Exod.37.27|Exod 37:27]]. — [[Jer.20.10|Jer 20:10]]; [[Job.18.12|Job 18:12]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צֶלַע‎</span> below II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צלע‎</span>.
