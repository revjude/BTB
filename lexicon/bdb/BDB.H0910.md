 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּדָד‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==isolation, separation==: [[Isa.27.10|Isa 27:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עִיר בְּצוּרָה בָּדָד‎</span> the fenced city is ***isolation***, i.e. is solitary (substantive for adjective : [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>§ 189. 2</sup>); more usually as adverb accusative, to signify ***alone***, [[Deut.32.12|Deut 32:12]] especially with verbs of dwelling, [[Lev.13.46|Lev 13:46]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּדָד יֵשֵׁב‎</span> he shall dwell ***alone*** (literally ***in isolation***), [[Jer.15.17|Jer 15:17]]; [[Lam.1.1|Lam 1:1]]; [[Lam.3.28|Lam 3:28]]: figurative of freedom from attack, security [[Deut.33.28|Deut 33:28]] (of Israel), [[Jer.49.31|Jer 49:31]] (Kedar); so<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְבָדָד‎</span> [[Num.23.9|Num 23:9]] (Israel) [[Mic.7.14|Mic 7:14]]; [[Ps.4.9|Ps 4:9]] for thou <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְבָדָד לָבֶטַח תּוֺשִׁיבֵנִי‎</span> makest me dwell ***solitarily***, in safety (see [[Deut.33.28|Deut 33:28]]).
