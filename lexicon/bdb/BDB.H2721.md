**1.** ***dryness*** [[Judg.6.37|Judg 6:37]]; [[Judg.6.39|Judg 6:39]]; [[Judg.6.40|Judg 6:40]] (all of absence of dew); [[Jer.50.38|Jer 50:38]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָבֵשׁ‎</span>); so read also [[Zech.11.17|Zech 11:17]] (for [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶרֶב‎</span>, see v<sup>b and</sup> [[BDB#Sta|Sta]]<sup>ZAW i. 1881, 29</sup>), ***a drying up upon his arm*** (i.e. a ***withering***) ***and upon his right eye***.
