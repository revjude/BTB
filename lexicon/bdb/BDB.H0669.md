 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶפְרַ֫יִם‎</span> **proper name, masculine** ==Ephraim== ([[Gen.41.52|Gen 41:52]] connected with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִפְרָה‎</span>, Hiph\`il of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פרה‎</span>, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פרא‎</span> Hiph\`il [[Hos.13.15|Hos 13:15]]).
