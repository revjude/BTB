 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָצוֺר‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָצֹר‎</span> **proper name, of a location** ([[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>BN 47 f.</sup>) — [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Ασωρ</span>, etc.; 

**1.** a royal city of northern Canaan [[Josh.11.1|Josh 11:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָבִין מֶלֶךְחֿ‎</span>, compare [[Josh.12.19|Josh 12:19]], and [[Judg.4.2|Judg 4:2]]; [[Judg.4.17|Judg 4:17]]; [[1Sam.12.9|1Sam 12:9]]; [[Josh.11.10|Josh 11:10]] (twice in verse); [[Josh.11.11|Josh 11:11]]; [[Josh.11.13|Josh 11:13]]; [[Josh.19.36|Josh 19:36]]; [[2Kgs.15.29|2Kgs 15:29]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָצֹר‎</span> [[1Kgs.9.15|1Kgs 9:15]]. 

**2.** places in the Negeb: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** [[Josh.15.23|Josh 15:23]]. **b. c.** [[Josh.15.25|Josh 15:25]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וחָצוֺר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲדַתָּה וּקְרִיּוֺת חֶצְרוֺן הִיא חָצוֺר‎</span>. 

**3.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָצוֺר‎</span> [[Neh.11.33|Neh 11:33]] a Benjamite town, probably = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּעַל חָצוֺר‎</span> q. v. 

**4.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַמְלְכוֺת חָצוֺר אֲשֶׂר הִכָּה נְבוּכַדְרֶאצַּו֯ר‎</span> [[Jer.49.28|Jer 49:28]], compare v[[Jer.49.30|Jer 49:30]] v[[Jer.49.33|Jer 49:33]], an Arabian locality, see Winer<sup>BW</sup> ***Hazor*** [[BDB#Gf|Gf]] [[BDB#Gie|Gie]]. — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵין ח‎</span> [[Josh.19.37|Josh 19:37]], see below <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַיִן‎</span>.
