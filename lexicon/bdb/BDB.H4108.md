 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַהֲלָךְ‎</span>] **noun masculine**<sup>[[Neh.2.6|Neh 2:6]]</sup> ***walk, journey, going*** (late); construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַהֲלַךְ‎</span> [[Ezek.42.4|Ezek 42:4]] + 2 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַהֲלָֽכְךָ‎</span> [[Neh.2.6|Neh 2:6]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַהְלְכִים‎</span> [[Zech.3.7|Zech 3:7]] — 

**1.** ***walk*** 'of 10 cubits' breadth before the chambers' in Ezekiel's temple [[Ezek.42.4|Ezek 42:4]] (compare [[BDB#Da|Da]]). 

**2.** ***journey*** from Babylonian to Jerusalem [[Neh.2.6|Neh 2:6]] of dimension of Nineveh, ***a journey of three days*** [[Jonah.3.3|Jonah 3:3]] i.e. diameter or length, compare v[[Jonah.3.4|Jonah 3:4]] (according to [[BDB#Herod|Herodot]]<sup>see 58 one day's journey</sup> = 150 stadia, = length of Nineveh according to [[BDB#Diod|Diod]]<sup>ii.3</sup>). 

**3.** plural ***goings***, i.e. ***free access*** ([[BDB#VB|VB]]) [[Zech.3.7|Zech 3:7]].
