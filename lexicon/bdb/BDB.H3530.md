 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּבְרָה‎</span>] **noun feminine** apparently ==distance==, only in the phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּבְרַת (ה)ארץ‎</span> a ***distance of land*** or ***length of way***, [[Gen.35.16|Gen 35:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָכוֺא אֶפְרָתָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיְהִי עוֺד כ‎</span>, [[Gen.48.7|Gen 48:7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָבאֹ אֶפְרָתָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּעוֺד כ‎</span>, [[2Kgs.5.19|2Kgs 5:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָ֑רֶץ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיֵּלֶךְ מֵאִתּוֺ כ‎</span>. The distance meant is uncertain, but from [[2Kgs.5.19|2Kgs 5:19]] it would seem to have been a short one; see further [[BDB#Di|Di]]<sup>[[Gen.35.16|Gen 35:16]] and</sup> [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] (Assyrian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***kibrâti***</u></abbr> is a (widely extended) ***territory, quarter of the world***, etc., [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>HWB 315</sup>; according to [[BDB#Hoffm|Hoffm.]]<sup>GGAbh. xxxvi (1890), 23-25</sup> perhaps the distance that one can see).
