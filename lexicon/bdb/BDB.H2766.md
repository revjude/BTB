 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָרִם‎</span> **proper name, masculine** (***consecrated***; compare Sabean proper name, masculine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חרם‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יחרמאל‎</span> [[BDB#Hal|Hal]]<sup>411. 504</sup> [[BDB#DHM|DHM]]<sup>l.c</sup>.) —
