 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶ֫תֶר‎</span><sub>94</sub> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Neh.2.16|Neh 2:16]]</sup> ==remainder, excellence, excess==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶ֫תֶר‎</span> [[Neh.2.16|Neh 2:16]] + 2 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָ֑תֶר‎</span> [[Prov.17.7|Prov 17:7]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶ֫תֶר‎</span> [[Gen.49.3|Gen 49:3]] + 84 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶ֫תֶר‎</span> [[Isa.56.12|Isa 56:12]] (see [[BDB#Baer|Baer's]] note); suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִתְרוֺ‎</span> [[Isa.44.19|Isa 44:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִתְרָם‎</span> [[Exod.23.11|Exod 23:11]] + 2 t. (not [[Job.4.21|Job 4:21]], q. v. below II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר‎</span>); — 

**1.** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***remainder, remnant*** (with implied inferiority in number or quality), of crops, etc. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִתְרָם‎</span> [[Exod.23.11|Exod 23:11]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]), ***their remnant***, i.e. what they (the enemy) leave, so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר הַגָּזָם‎</span> [[Joel.1.4|Joel 1:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר הָאַרְבֶּה‎</span> v[[Joel.1.4|Joel 1:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר הַיֶּלֶק‎</span> v[[Joel.1.4|Joel 1:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר הַפְּלֵטָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַנִּשְׁאֶרֶת‎</span> [[Exod.10.5|Exod 10:5]] ([[BDB#J|J]]); of vessels, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר הַכֵּלִים הַנּוֺתָרִים‎</span> [[Jer.27.19|Jer 27:19]] of a tree [[Isa.44.19|Isa 44:19]] (= <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁאֵרִית‎</span> v[[Isa.44.17|Isa 44:17]]); elsewhere of person [[Deut.3.11|Deut 3:11]] = [[Josh.12.4|Josh 12:4]]; [[Josh.3.12|Josh 3:12]] (both [[BDB#D|D]]), [[Deut.28.54|Deut 28:54]]; [[Josh.23.12|Josh 23:12]] ([[BDB#D|D]]), [[2Sam.21.2|2Sam 21:2]]; [[2Kgs.25.11|2Kgs 25:11]] = [[Jer.52.15|Jer 52:15]]; [[Ezek.34.18|Ezek 34:18]] of a rescued remnant of Israel (Judah) [[Mic.5.2|Mic 5:2]]; [[Zeph.2.9|Zeph 2:9]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁאֵרִית‎</span>), [[Zech.14.2|Zech 14:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר הַקָּדֵשׁ אֲשֶׁר נִשְׁאַר‎</span> [[1Kgs.22.47|1Kgs 22:47]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר הָעָם הַנִּשְׁאָרִים‎</span> [[Jer.36.9|Jer 36:9]] (twice in verse); [[2Kgs.25.11|2Kgs 25:11]] = [[Jer.52.15|Jer 52:15]] . 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***remainder, rest, other part*** of people, elders, etc., [[2Sam.10.10|2Sam 10:10]] = [[1Chr.19.11|1Chr 19:11]]; [[2Sam.12.28|2Sam 12:28]]; [[1Kgs.12.23|1Kgs 12:23]]; [[Jer.29.1|Jer 29:1]]; [[Hab.2.8|Hab 2:8]]; [[Ezek.48.23|Ezek 48:23]] sometimes including a majority [[Judg.7.6|Judg 7:6]]; [[1Sam.13.2|1Sam 13:2]] of common people (opposed to rulers) [[Neh.2.16|Neh 2:16]]; [[Neh.4.8|Neh 4:8]]; [[Neh.4.13|Neh 4:13]] compare [[Neh.6.1|Neh 6:1]] of land [[Deut.3.13|Deut 3:13]]; [[Josh.13.27|Josh 13:27]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); of other inanimate things [[Lev.14.17|Lev 14:17]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), [[Num.31.32|Num 31:32]] (***id.***), of years [[Isa.38.10|Isa 38:10]] elsewhere in phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶחֶר דִּבְרֵי‎</span> ***the rest of the affairs of***, in summaries of reigns of kings of Israel and Judah [[1Kgs.11.41|1Kgs 11:41]] + 41 t. Kings Chronicles; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר כָּלדִּֿבְרֵי‎</span> [[1Kgs.15.23|1Kgs 15:23]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר דְּבָרָיו‎</span> [[2Chr.28.26|2Chr 28:26]]. 

**2. a.** ***excess***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׂפַת יָ֑תֶר‎</span> [[Prov.17.7|Prov 17:7]] ***a lip of excess*** = arrogant speech (see [[BDB#VB|VB]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** in adverb phrases, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל יֶתֶר‎</span> [[Ps.31.24|Ps 31:24]] ***on the basis of abundance = abundantly***, as adverb accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גָּדוֺל יֶתֶר מְאֹד‎</span> [[Isa.56.12|Isa 56:12]] great, ***in abundance***, exceedingly, [[Dan.8.9|Dan 8:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַתִּגְדַּליֶֿתֶר‎</span> and grew great ***in excess, exceedingly*** (compare <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhKQAYAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAApABgAAAJajI+py+3PgAwT2iYzuFzp3YVHJYpkaWgPiI5ZS10f+kIf29Xrpof46AH1OD9Kj6QK/m5Mlo6ZSkabyduUisM+rVcoQjWLnBJSF+yMThvL6i+3TX7Dv7HiPF0AADs="> ***much***, <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhRwAbAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAABHABsAAAKYjI+py+0Po5y0JoCB3Srz7HEiiFUaKHblQ36GliIhu24wHAcojee+PmP0fr4dMfiqxVrHIGl4QAqZv1bJeFE2rtoiVvqSrMARMJU8zSbN0HUXuHsjv7inLtp9WvVy/nnmhwUXyDdFiMdEOKQItOZwiAiXZTfZZvP2OIJJxKJpyVm5WSYKOkgKgVZKmZJaCiroGtsaS1srUgAAOw=="> ***more than***). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** specifically ***what is over and above*** immediate necessities, ***abundance, affluence*** [[Job.22.20|Job 22:20]]; [[Ps.17.14|Ps 17:14]]. 

**3.** ***superiority, excellency*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר שְׂאֵת וְיֶתֶר עָ֫ז‎</span> [[Gen.49.3|Gen 49:3]]. — On [[Job.4.1|Job 4:1]] see  II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֶתֶר‎</span>.
