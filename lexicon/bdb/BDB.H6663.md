**1.** ***have a just cause, be in the right***, [[Job.9.15|Job 9:15]]; [[Job.9.20|Job 9:20]]; [[Job.13.18|Job 13:18]]; [[Job.34.5|Job 34:5]] in complaint [[Job.33.12|Job 33:12]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> person [[Gen.38.26|Gen 38:26]] ([[BDB#J|J]]; of Tamar).

**3.** ***be just:*** of God, in his government, in charging with sin [[Ps.51.6|Ps 51:6]] of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִשְׁפְּטֵייֿ‎</span> [[Ps.19.10|Ps 19:10]]. 

**4.** ***be just, righteous***, in conduct and character: of men [[Job.10.15|Job 10:15]]; [[Job.15.14|Job 15:14]]; [[Job.22.3|Job 22:3]]; [[Job.35.7|Job 35:7]], with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> compare, [[Job.4.17|Job 4:17]] (more than God; [[BDB#Bev|Bev]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]] [[BDB#Marti|Marti]]<sup>Comm.</sup> ***be justified***, its cause vindicated). **Pi\`el** ***Perfect*** 3 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צִדְּקָה‎</span> [[Jer.3.11|Jer 3:11]] ***Imperfect*** 2 feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּצַדְּקִי‎</span> [[Ezek.16.51|Ezek 16:51]] ***Infinitive construct*** suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַדְּקֶ֑ךָּ‎</span> [[Job.33.32|Job 33:32]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַדֶּקְתֵּךְ‎</span> ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:52p»§ 52p</sup>) [[Ezek.16.52|Ezek 16:52]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַדְּקוֺ‎</span> [[Job.32.2|Job 32:2]] — ***justify:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל צַדְּקוֺ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַפְשׁוֺ מֵאֱלֹהִים‎</span> [[Job.32.2|Job 32:2]] ***because he justified himself rather than God*** (compare **Qal 1**); [[Job.27.5|Job 27:5]] ***Infinitive construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַצְדִּיק‎</span> [[1Kgs.8.32|1Kgs 8:32]] = [[2Chr.6.23|2Chr 6:23]] ***Imperative*** masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַצְדִּיקוּ‎</span> [[Ps.82.3|Ps 82:3]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַצְדִּיק‎</span> [[Prov.17.15|Prov 17:15]], etc.; — 

**1.** ***do justice***, in administering law [[2Sam.15.4|2Sam 15:4]]; [[Ps.82.3|Ps 82:3]]. 

**2.** ***declare righteous, justify***, with accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎(ה)צַדִּיק‎</span> [[Deut.25.1|Deut 25:1]]; [[1Kgs.8.32|1Kgs 8:32]] = [[2Chr.6.23|2Chr 6:23]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רָשָׁע‎</span> [[Exod.23.7|Exod 23:7]] ([[BDB#E|E]]) [[Isa.5.23|Isa 5:23]]; [[Prov.17.15|Prov 17:15]] ***Justify*** accusers, by recognizing charge as just [[Job.27.5|Job 27:5]]. 

**3.** ***justify, vindicate the cause of, save***, with accusative, [[Isa.50.8|Isa 50:8]] (of God), with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span> of object [[Isa.53.11|Isa 53:11]] (of servant of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>). 

**4.** ***make righteous, turn to righteousness***, [[Dan.12.3|Dan 12:3]], compare Aboth [[Dan.5.26|Dan 5:26]]; [[Dan.5.27|Dan 5:27]] [[BDB#Bev|Bev]]. **Hithpa\`el** ***Imperfect*** 1 plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִצְטַדָּ֑ק‎</span> [[Gen.44.16|Gen 44:16]] ([[BDB#J|J]]) how ***shall we justify ourselves***, clear ourselves from suspicion?
