 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֹן‎</span> ([[Gen.41.45|Gen 41:45]]; [[Gen.46.20|Gen 46:20]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺן‎</span> ([[Gen.41.50|Gen 41:50]]; [[Ezek.30.17|Ezek 30:17]]) **proper name, of a location** ==On== (Egypt ***An*** compare [[BDB#Eb|Eb]]<sup>GS 75</sup> [[BDB#Wied|Wied]]<sup>Samm 146</sup>; perhaps = Assyrian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***Unu***</u></abbr> [[BDB#Steind|Steind]]<sup>BAS i.610</sup>, contr. [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>Pa 318</sup>) city in lower Egypt, probably on border of land of Goshen, residence of 'Potiphera, priest of On,' father-in-law of Joseph [[Gen.41.45|Gen 41:45]]; [[Gen.41.50|Gen 41:50]]; [[Gen.46.20|Gen 46:20]] also [[Ezek.30.17|Ezek 30:17]] ( where [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָוֶן‎</span>); it was celebrated for worship of sun-god Ra, &amp; hence called also ***sun-city***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּית שֶׁמֶשׁ‎</span> [[Jer.43.13|Jer 43:13]], [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">ἡλίου πόλις</span>; modern ***Matariye***, on east bank of Nile, approximately 7 miles east of north from Cairo &amp; 18 from Memphis; — compare [[BDB#Eb|Eb]]<sup>GS 75, 507 f.</sup> &amp; map [[BDB#Wied|Wied]]<sup>l.c</sup>.
