 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲלִיכָה‎</span>] **noun feminine** ==a going, way, travelling-company==, suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲלִיכָתָם‎</span> [[Nah.2.6|Nah 2:6]] [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הלוכתם‎</span> [[BDB#Kt|Kt]]); elsewhere only plural, construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲלִיכוֺת‎</span> [[Ps.68.25|Ps 68:25]], so [[Prov.31.27|Prov 31:27]] [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הילכות‎</span> [[BDB#Kt|Kt]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲלִכוֺת‎</span> [[Hab.3.6|Hab 3:6]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲלִיכֹת‎</span> [[Job.6.19|Job 6:19]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲלִיכוֺתֶיךָ‎</span> [[Ps.68.25|Ps 68:25]] — 

**1.** ***going, walk***, 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** [[Nah.2.6|Nah 2:6]] ***they shall stumble in their going***; elsewhere plural ***goings:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** [[Ps.68.25|Ps 68:25]] (twice in verse) of ***going, marching, progress of God into the sanctuary*** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּקֹּדֶשׁ‎</span>; so [[BDB#Hup|Hup]] [[BDB#Pe|Pe]] [[BDB#Che|Che]]; [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#De|De]] ***in holiness*** reference either to solemn processions of worshippers (e.g. [[BDB#De|De]]) or, perhaps better, to the theophanic progress of God himself (e.g. [[BDB#Che|Che]]); also 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** of goings = ***doings*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוֺפִיָּה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּיתָהּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ה‎</span> [[Prov.31.27|Prov 31:27]] ***watching the doings of her household***; of God [[Hab.3.6|Hab 3:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲלִכוֺת עוֺלָם לוֺ‎</span> ***ways of eternity*** (al ***of old***) ***are his.*** 

**2.** ***travelling-company, caravan*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲלִיכֹת שְׁבָא‎</span> [[Job.6.19|Job 6:19]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָרְהוֺת תֵּמָא‎</span>).
