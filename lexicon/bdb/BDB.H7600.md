 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁאֲנָן‎</span> **adjective** ==at ease, secure== ([[BDB#Ba|Ba]]<sup>NB § 143a</sup> [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:84b»§ 84 b k</sup>); — absolute<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> [[Isa.33.20|Isa 33:20]] plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁאֲנִּים‎</span> [[Amos.6.1|Amos 6:1]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁאֲנַנּוֺת‎</span> [[Isa.32.9|Isa 32:9]]; [[Isa.32.11|Isa 32:11]]; [[Isa.32.18|Isa 32:18]] — 

**1.** ***at ease, secure:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָוֶה שׁ‎</span> [[Isa.33.20|Isa 33:20]] ,***secure habitation*** (of Jerusalem), compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְנוּחוֺת שׁ‎</span> [[Isa.32.18|Isa 32:18]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִבְטָחִים‎</span>). 

**2.** as substantive, ***one at ease***, free from misfortune, [[Job.12.5|Job 12:5]]. 

**3.** ***at ease***, with collateral idea of ***careless, wanton, arrogant***, [[Amos.6.1|Amos 6:1]]; [[Isa.32.9|Isa 32:9]]; [[Isa.32.11|Isa 32:11]]; [[Zech.1.15|Zech 1:15]]; [[Ps.123.4|Ps 123:4]]. 

**4.** as substantive abstract = ***arrogance:*** suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁאֲנַנְךָ‎</span>, [[2Kgs.19.28|2Kgs 19:28]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִתְרַגֶּזְךָ‎</span>) = [[Isa.37.29|Isa 37:29]], but &lt; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁאוֺנְךָ‎</span> ***thine uproar***, [[BDB#Bu|Bu]]<sup>ZAW xii (1892), 36</sup> [[BDB#Gr|Gr]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Marti|Marti]] [[BDB#Kit|Kit]] (perhaps), [[BDB#Bur|Bur]]. compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁלאנן‎</span>.
