 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶ֫גֶד‎</span><sub>151</sub> **substantive** ==what is conspicuous or in front==, always as **adverb** or **preposition** ==in front of, in sight of, opposite to==, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶ֑גֶד‎</span>, construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶגֶד‎</span>, suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶגְדִּי‎</span> [[Ps.38.11|Ps 38:11]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶגְדְּךָ‎</span> [[Ps.38.10|Ps 38:10]] +, etc.; with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ה‎</span> ***locative*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶ֫גְדָּה‎</span> [[Ps.116.14|Ps 116:14]]; [[Ps.116.18|Ps 116:18]] — 

**1.** as adverb accusative ***in front of***, 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** locally (rather stronger and distincter than <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִפְנֵי‎</span>), 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***a***) [[Gen.31.32|Gen 31:32]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶגֶד אַחֵינוּ הַכֶּרנָֿא‎</span> ***in front of*** our brethren own it, now, v[[Gen.31.37|Gen 31:37]]; [[Gen.47.15|Gen 47:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ולמה נמות‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נגדך‎</span> why should we die ***in thy sight ?*** [[Exod.19.2|Exod 19:2]] Israel encamped <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נגד ההר‎</span> ***in front of*** the mountain, [[Exod.34.10|Exod 34:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נגד כלעֿמך אעשׂה נִפְלָאֹת‎</span>, ***before, in sight of, all thy people*** (compare [[Ps.78.12|Ps 78:12]]), [[Josh.3.16|Josh 3:16]] the people passed over <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נגד ירחו‎</span> ***in front of*** J., [[1Kgs.20.27|1Kgs 20:27]], etc.; [[Neh.3.23|Neh 3:23]] ***in front of*** their house, v[[Neh.3.29|Neh 3:29]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נגד (כלׅ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ישׂראל‎</span> [[Deut.31.11|Deut 31:11]]; [[Josh.8.35|Josh 8:35]] = [[1Sam.15.30|1Sam 15:30]]; [[1Sam.16.6|1Sam 16:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְשִׁיחוֺ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַךְ נֶגֶד י‎</span>, so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶגְדִּי‎</span> [[Ps.89.37|Ps 89:37]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶגְדּוֺ‎</span> [[Ps.18.13|Ps 18:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נגד‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎השׁמשׁ‎</span> [[Num.25.4|Num 25:4]]; [[2Sam.12.12|2Sam 12:12]]; [[Ps.22.26|Ps 22:26]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יראיו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נ‎</span>, [[Ps.52.11|Ps 52:11]] (so in poetry <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶגְדָּה לְ‎</span> [[Ps.116.14|Ps 116:14]]; [[Ps.116.18|Ps 116:18]]); hostilely, [[Job.10.17|Job 10:17]] with collateral idea of ***to the mortification of***, [[Ps.23.5|Ps 23:5]]; [[Ps.31.20|Ps 31:20]], compare [[Joel.1.16|Joel 1:16]] with collateral idea of being ***open*** or ***known to***, [[Prov.15.11|Prov 15:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁאול ואבדון נגד י‎</span>, [[Job.26.6|Job 26:6]]; [[Isa.59.12|Isa 59:12]] so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נֶגֶד פָּנַי הָיוּ‎</span> [[Hos.7.2|Hos 7:2]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***b***) with reflexive suffix, and verb of motion, to go ***in front of oneself***, i.e. ***straightforward***, [[Josh.6.5|Josh 6:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ועלו העם אישׁ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נגדו‎</span> each ***straightforward***, v[[Josh.6.20|Josh 6:20]]; [[Amos.4.3|Amos 4:3]]; [[Jer.31.29|Jer 31:29]]; [[Neh.12.37|Neh 12:37]], compare [[Prov.4.25|Prov 4:25]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** mentally, = vividly present to, [[Ps.38.18|Ps 38:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ומכאובי נגדי תמיד‎</span>, [[Ps.44.16|Ps 44:16]]; [[Ps.51.5|Ps 51:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וחטאתי נגדי תמיד‎</span>, [[Ps.109.15|Ps 109:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נ‎</span> (remembered by): nearly = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּעֵינָיו‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּאַיִן נֶגְדּוֺ‎</span> [[Isa.40.17|Isa 40:17]]; [[Ps.39.6|Ps 39:6]] so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְנ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פְּנֵיהֶם נְבוֺנִים‎</span> [[Isa.5.21|Isa 5:21]]. But [[Exod.10.10|Exod 10:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כי רעה נגד פניכם‎</span> ***before your face*** is = in your view, or purpose (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לא אשׁית לְנֶגֶד עֵינַי דברבֿליעל‎</span> [[Ps.101.3|Ps 101:3]]).


**2.** With prepositions: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּנֶגֶד‎</span> ***according to what is in front of*** = ***corresponding to***, [[Gen.2.18|Gen 2:18]] I will make him <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵזֶר כְּנֶגְדּוֺ‎</span> a help ***corresponding to*** him i.e. equal and adequate to himself, v[[Gen.2.20|Gen 2:20]] among the animals there was no <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵזֶר כְּנֶגְדּוֺ‎</span>. **\*\***Note that in Late Hebrew <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מכנגד‎</span> = ***in front of***, Ber 4:5; 9:5; Ab 2:10, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מכנגד פני‎</span> Git 7:7 and elsewhere. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְנֶגֶד‎</span> ***in front of, before***, [[Gen.33.12|Gen 33:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְֵאלְכָה לְנֶנְדֶּ֑ךָ‎</span>, [[Num.22.32|Num 22:32]]; [[2Kgs.1.13|2Kgs 1:13]] fell on his knees <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לנגד אליהו‎</span>, [[Isa.1.7|Isa 1:7]] your ground <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לנגדכם זרים אֹכלים אותהּ‎</span>; with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָמַד‎</span> [[Josh.5.13|Josh 5:13]]; [[Dan.8.15|Dan 8:15]]; [[Dan.10.16|Dan 10:16]], in a hostile sense v[[Dan.10.13|Dan 10:13]], compare [[Prov.21.30|Prov 21:30]] ***in the sight*** or ***presence of***, [[Hab.1.3|Hab 1:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְשֹׁד וְחָמָס לְנֶגְדִּי‎</span>, [[Ps.39.2|Ps 39:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בעוד רשׁע לנגדי‎</span>, [[Ps.50.8|Ps 50:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ועולֹתיך לנגדי תמיד‎</span>, [[Neh.3.37|Neh 3:37]] ***opposite to*** (probably of opposite choirs), [[Neh.12.9|Neh 12:9]]; [[Neh.12.24|Neh 12:24]] = ***parallel to***, [[1Chr.5.11|1Chr 5:11]] = ***over, for***, a business (peculiar) [[Neh.11.22|Neh 11:22]] figurative of what is visible morally, [[Ps.18.23|Ps 18:23]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כי כל משׁפטיו לנגדי‎</span> (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא אָסִיר מֶנִּי‎</span>), with ***to set*** or ***place*** [[Ps.16.8|Ps 16:8]]; [[Ps.54.5|Ps 54:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לא שׂמו אלהים לנגדם‎</span> (compare [[Ps.86.14|Ps 86:14]],), [[Ps.90.8|Ps 90:8]] often in the phrase <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְנֶגֶד עֵינֵי ֗֗֗ ‎</span>, both literal, as [[Job.4.16|Job 4:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּמוּנָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵינָ֑י‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לנ‎</span>, [[Ps.5.6|Ps 5:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עיניך‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לא יתיצבו הוללים לנ‎</span> (compare [[Ps.101.7|Ps 101:7]]); and in a moral sense, [[Ps.18.25|Ps 18:25]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עיניו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּבֹר ידי לנ‎</span>, [[Ps.26.3|Ps 26:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עיני‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כי חסדך לנ‎</span> i.e. is ever present to my mind, [[Ps.36.2|Ps 36:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עיניו‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אין פחד אלהים לנ‎</span> i.e. he has no eye to discern God's awe-inspiring judgments (compare [[Ps.10.5|Ps 10:5]]); $\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***a***) ***from the front of, away from***, [[Song.6.5|Song 6:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָסֵבִּי עֵינַיִךְ מִנֶּגְדִּי‎</span>, [[1Sam.26.20|1Sam 26:20]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אליֿפֹל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פני י‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דמי ארצה מנ‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מנגד עיני‎</span> ***from before the eyes of***, with verbs of removing [[Isa.1.16|Isa 1:16]], cutting off [[Ps.31.23|Ps 31:23]]; [[Jonah.2.5|Jonah 2:5]], hiding [[Amos.9.3|Amos 9:3]]; [[Jer.16.17|Jer 16:17]].
