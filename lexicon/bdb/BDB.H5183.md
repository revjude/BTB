**1.** ***quietness, quiet attitude*** [[Isa.30.15|Isa 30:15]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַשְׁקֵט‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִשְׁמָעִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּבְרֵי חֲכָמִים בְּנ‎</span> [[Eccl.9.17|Eccl 9:17]] opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָמָל וּרְעוּת רוּחַ‎</span> [[Eccl.4.6|Eccl 4:6]] opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רָגַז‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׂחַק‎</span> [[Prov.29.9|Prov 29:9]] of comfort of one's table, i.e. its satisfying plenty, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֻׁלְחָֽנְךָ מָלֵא דָ֑שֶׁן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְנ‎</span> [[Job.36.16|Job 36:16]] ***the comfort of thy table, which*** (i.e. the table) ***was full of fat*** ([[BDB#Di|Di]], compare [[BDB#Bu|Bu]]; &gt; ***that which is set on thy table shall be***, etc. [[BDB#De|De]] and others, making <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נ‎</span> subject of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָלֵא‎</span>, and so masculine).
