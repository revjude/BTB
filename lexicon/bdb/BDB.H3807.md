 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּתַת‎</span>] **verb** ==beat, crush by beating== (Late Hebrew ***id.***; Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎כְּתַת‎</span>); — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** 1 singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְכַתּוֺתִ֫י‎</span> [[Ps.89.24|Ps 89:24]] ***Imperfect*** 1 singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וָאֶבֹּת‎</span> [[Deut.9.21|Deut 9:21]] ***Imperative*** masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹּ֫תּוּ‎</span> [[Joel.4.10|Joel 4:10]] ***Passive participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּתוּת‎</span> [[Isa.30.14|Isa 30:14]]; [[Lev.22.24|Lev 22:24]] — 

**1.** ***beat or crush fine***, of a potter's vessel [[Isa.30.14|Isa 30:14]], the golden calf [[Deut.9.21|Deut 9:21]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טָחוֺן הֵיטֵב‎</span>); a sacrificial vicitim [[Lev.22.24|Lev 22:24]] (i.e. its testicles; + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָעוּךְ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָתוּק‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּרוּת‎</span>); figurative of enemies [[Ps.89.24|Ps 89:24]]. 

**2.** ***beat, hammer*** (ploughshares into swords, compare **Pi\`el 2**) [[Joel.4.10|Joel 4:10]]. **Pi\`el** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּתַּת‎</span> [[2Kgs.18.4|2Kgs 18:4]]; [[2Chr.34.7|2Chr 34:7]] 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְכִּתְּתוּ‎</span> consecutive [[Isa.2.4|Isa 2:4]] + 2 t.; — as 

**Qal 1.** ***beat*** or ***crush fine*** [[2Kgs.18.4|2Kgs 18:4]]; [[2Chr.34.7|2Chr 34:7]] (of images); figurative of devastating the land [[Zech.11.6|Zech 11:6]]. 

**2.** ***beat, hammer*** (swords into ploughshares, compare **Qal 2**) [[Isa.2.4|Isa 2:4]] = [[Mic.4.3|Mic 4:3]]. **Pu\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְכֻתְּתוּ‎</span> [[2Chr.15.6|2Chr 15:6]] ***and they were beaten in pieces***, one nation against another. **Hiph\`il** ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַּכְּתוּ‎</span> [[Deut.1.44|Deut 1:44]] ***beat in pieces*** an enemy, suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיַּכּוּם וַיַּכְּתוּם‎</span> [[Num.14.45|Num 14:45]]. **Hoph\`al** ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּשְׁאִיָּה יֻכַּתשָֿׁ֑עַר‎</span> [[Isa.24.12|Isa 24:12]] ***and to ruins is the gate crushed***; 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֻכַּתּוּ‎</span> [[Mic.1.7|Mic 1:7]] (of idol-images); figurative of warriors [[Jer.46.5|Jer 46:5]] of frail man [[Job.4.20|Job 4:20]].
