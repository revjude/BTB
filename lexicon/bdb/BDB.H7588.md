 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁאוֺן‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Hos.10.14|Hos 10:14]]</sup> ==roar== (of waters, etc.), ==din, crash, uproar==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> [[Hos.10.14|Hos 10:14]] +; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁאוֺן‎</span> [[Isa.13.4|Isa 13:4]] +; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁאוֺנָהּ‎</span> [[Isa.5.14|Isa 5:14]] — 

**1.** ***roar*** of water (waves), [[Isa.17.12|Isa 17:12]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָמָה‎</span>), simile of noise of invading host, so v[[Isa.17.13|Isa 17:13]], but strike out [[BDB#Du|Du]] [[BDB#Che|Che]]<sup>Hpt</sup> [[BDB#Marti|Marti]], as doublet; ***roar*** of this host v[[Isa.17.12|Isa 17:12]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָמוֺן‎</span>), compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁאוֺן קוֺלָם‎</span> [[Jer.51.55|Jer 51:55]] of waves also [[Ps.65.8|Ps 65:8]] (twice in verse); of ***din***, or ***crash*** of battle [[Amos.2.2|Amos 2:2]]; [[Hos.10.14|Hos 10:14]]; [[Ps.74.23|Ps 74:23]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּנֵי שׁ‎</span> [[Jer.48.45|Jer 48:45]] (i. e. warriors); of battle in which <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> beats down his foes [[Jer.25.31|Jer 25:31]], compare [[Isa.66.6|Isa 66:6]] of gathering hosts [[Isa.13.4|Isa 13:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זָרִים‎</span> [[Isa.25.3|Isa 25:3]] ([[BDB#Du|Du]] [[BDB#Marti|Marti]], plausibly, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּאוֺן זְדִים‎</span>, as [[Isa.13.11|Isa 13:11]]); read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> also probably (for  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׁאֲנָן‎</span>, q. v.) [[2Kgs.19.28|2Kgs 19:28]] = [[Isa.37.29|Isa 37:29]] in mocking appell. of Pharaoh (Necho), [[Jer.46.17|Jer 46:17]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פּ‎</span> ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִרְאוּ שֵׁם‎</span> call ye the name of Phoenician a ***crash***. 

**2.** ***uproar*** of revellers [[Isa.5.14|Isa 5:14]]; [[Isa.24.8|Isa 24:8]]. — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בּוֺר שׁ‎</span> [[Ps.40.3|Ps 40:3]], ***pit of roaring*** (of waters? figurative, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעֲמַקֵּימַֿיִם‎</span> [[Ps.69.3|Ps 69:3]], where also \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָוִן‎</span>); [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] and others ***of destruction*** (see √ **2**), but against usage of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁאוֺן‎</span>; &gt; [[BDB#Du|Du]] proposes <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בּוֺר שָׁוְא‎</span> (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָפַת שָׁוְא‎</span> [[Isa.30.28|Isa 30:28]]).
