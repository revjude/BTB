 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרָם‎</span><sub>149</sub> **proper name, masculine** ==Aram== (Assyrian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***Aramu,***</u></abbr> etc., see [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>Pa 257</sup>; [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] and others propose √ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ארם‎</span> = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רום‎</span> but compare [[BDB#Nö|Nö]] as below) — 

**1.** 5th son of Shem [[Gen.10.22|Gen 10:22]]; [[Gen.10.23|Gen 10:23]]; [[1Chr.1.17|1Chr 1:17]]. 

**2.** grandson of Nahor [[Gen.22.21|Gen 22:21]]. 

**3.** [[1Chr.2.23|1Chr 2:23]]. 

**4.** a descendant of Asher [[1Chr.7.34|1Chr 7:34]]. — Elsewhere only of AramÊan people &amp; land (= **1** above), **feminine**<sup>[[2Sam.8.5|2Sam 8:5]]</sup> **masculine**<sup>[[2Sam.10.14|2Sam 10:14]]</sup>. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** people, singular collective = ***the Aramoeans***, a leading branch of the Shemitic stock inhabiting Mesopotamia &amp; northern Syria, in many tribes &amp; settlements; [[2Sam.8.5|2Sam 8:5]] (twice in verse); [[2Sam.8.6|2Sam 8:6]] + [[1Kgs.20.20|1Kgs 20:20]]; [[1Kgs.20.21|1Kgs 20:21]] + [[1Chr.19.10|1Chr 19:10]]; [[1Chr.19.12|1Chr 19:12]] + (64 t. Samuel Kings Chronicles) [[Amos.9.7|Amos 9:7]]; [[Isa.7.2|Isa 7:2]]; [[Isa.7.4|Isa 7:4]]; [[Isa.7.5|Isa 7:5]]; [[Isa.7.8|Isa 7:8]]; [[Isa.9.11|Isa 9:11]]; [[Isa.17.3|Isa 17:3]];  so [[Ezek.16.57|Ezek 16:57]]; [[Ezek.27.16|Ezek 27:16]], but [[BDB#Co|Co]] in both <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אדום‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַם אֲרָם‎</span> [[Amos.1.5|Amos 1:5]] of particular divisions of Aram, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בית רְחוֺב‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[2Sam.10.6|2Sam 10:6]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוֺבָא‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[2Sam.10.6|2Sam 10:6]]; [[2Sam.10.8|2Sam 10:8]]; [[Ps.60.2|Ps 60:2]] (title), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דַּמֶּשֶׂק‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[2Sam.8.5|2Sam 8:5]] compare [[1Chr.18.5|1Chr 18:5]], even <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַהֲרַיִם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[Ps.60.2|Ps 60:2]] (title); (note that Assyrian never gives name ***Aramu*** to people west of Euphrates, but ***Chatti*** instead, with other particular names, [[BDB#COT|COT]] [[Gen.10.22|Gen 10:22]], also [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>l.c</sup>.); on [[2Sam.8.12|2Sam 8:12]]; [[2Sam.8.13|2Sam 8:13]]; [[1Chr.18.11|1Chr 18:11]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֱדוֺם‎</span>. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** less often clearly of land, Aram [[Num.23.7|Num 23:7]]; [[2Sam.15.8|2Sam 15:8]] + [[2Chr.20.2|2Chr 20:2]] (read however here <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אדום‎</span> [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] Add and others), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׂדֵה א‎</span> [[Hos.12.13|Hos 12:13]] also of particular divisions of the territory <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נַהֲרַיִם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> ***'Mesopotamia,'*** i.e. probably land between Euphrates &amp; Chaboras, so [[BDB#Di|Di]] after [[BDB#Kiep|Kiep]], [[Gen.24.10|Gen 24:10]]; [[Deut.23.5|Deut 23:5]]; [[Judg.3.8|Judg 3:8]] (compare [[Ps.60.2|Ps 60:2]] above); compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פַּדַּן א‎</span> ***Paddan-Aram*** [[Gen.25.20|Gen 25:20]]; [[Gen.31.18|Gen 31:18]]; [[Gen.33.18|Gen 33:18]]; [[Gen.35.9|Gen 35:9]]; [[Gen.35.26|Gen 35:26]]; [[Gen.46.15|Gen 46:15]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פַּדֶּ֫נָה א‎</span> [[Gen.28.2|Gen 28:2]]; [[Gen.28.5|Gen 28:5]]; [[Gen.28.6|Gen 28:6]]; [[Gen.28.7|Gen 28:7]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פדן‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דַּמֶּשֶׂק‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[2Sam.8.6|2Sam 8:6]] compare [[1Chr.18.6|1Chr 18:6]].
