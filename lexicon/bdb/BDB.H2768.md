 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶרְמוֺן‎</span> **proper name, of a mountain** ==Hermon== (***sacred*** mountain, compare Sabean <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מחרם‎</span>, etc., ***temple***, Arabic <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhJQAfAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAlAB8AAAJkjI+pywsPmpwHRorTvblX+HnYg5Ca+aGZam2tGLCcYcH0bFNqrvMd7msANcHY0Aji7VKvSfI2ap5q0FvrSmU+Udguris86TjLEKzsyKHFtnVKfXzDJU+1I6v81n3uotEPGChYAAA7"> ***interior of mosque***, <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhKwAfAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAArAB8AAAJwjI+py+0PIwCxyjmtXplunjFYWGGgJ5pnQpHG+AWji6KHqs1KaL/9afshYEPhDhdLKpfMj6t5gVqQLIjuQTwab9eUD+iodbljWUdcnGV96jbN7b7wnmrzlvmUhu/6YT/6t8fXRxVINohXZviy2JhQAAA7"> ***asylum***, [[BDB#Wetzst|Wetzst]]<sup>ZKW</sup> see 1884, 115 [[BDB#RS|RS]]<sup>Semitic i, 93; 2nd ed., 94</sup>) — the highest peak of Anti-Lebanon range, usually snow-capped, commanding southern Syria &amp; northern Palestine; it is called <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׂיאֹן‎</span> by the author of [[Deut.4.48|Deut 4:48]] by the Amorites <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׂנִיר‎</span>, by the Sidonians <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׂרְיֹן‎</span> [[Deut.3.9|Deut 3:9]]. It has three peaks; and the names <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חרמון‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂניר‎</span>, distinguished in [[1Chr.5.23|1Chr 5:23]]; [[Song.4.8|Song 4:8]], may refer to two of these peaks; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶרְמוֺנִים‎</span> [[Ps.42.7|Ps 42:7]] probably refers to these different peaks (see [[BDB#Rob|Rob]]<sup>BR iii. 357</sup> [[BDB#Bäd|Bäd]]<sup>Pal 301</sup>). It is a northern boundary <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עד הר ח‎</span> [[Deut.3.8|Deut 3:8]]; [[Josh.12.1|Josh 12:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הר חרמוֺן‎</span> ***mount Hermon*** is used also [[Josh.11.17|Josh 11:17]]; [[Josh.12.5|Josh 12:5]]; [[Josh.13.5|Josh 13:5]]; [[Josh.13.11|Josh 13:11]]; [[1Chr.5.23|1Chr 5:23]] but <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חרמון‎</span> [[Josh.11.3|Josh 11:3]]; [[Ps.89.13|Ps 89:13]]; [[Ps.133.3|Ps 133:3]]; [[Song.4.8|Song 4:8]]. see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּעַל חֶרְמוֺן‎</span> [[Judg.3.3|Judg 3:3]]; [[1Chr.5.23|1Chr 5:23]] (see [[BDB#Wetzst|Wetzst]]<sup>ib. 115</sup>). <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶרְמוֺנִים‎</span> see foregoing.
