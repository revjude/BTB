 II. [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוּר‎</span>] **verb** ==behold, regard== (especially Job); — 

**Qal** ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָשׁוּר‎</span> [[Jer.5.26|Jer 5:26]], suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְשׁוּרֶנּוּ‎</span> [[Job.34.29|Job 34:29]], etc.; ***Imperative*** masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוּר‎</span> [[Job.35.5|Job 35:5]] — 

**1.** ***behold***, with accusative of person [[Num.23.9|Num 23:9]]; [[Num.24.17|Num 24:17]] (poems in [[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]], \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רָאָה‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּשׁוּרֵנִי‎</span>, subject eye, [[Job.24.15|Job 24:15]], and (= I shall not exist) [[Job.7.8|Job 7:8]], compare [[Job.20.9|Job 20:9]] (subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְקוֺמוֺ‎</span>), [[Job.17.15|Job 17:15]] God object [[Job.34.29|Job 34:29]]; [[Job.35.14|Job 35:14]] ***look, gaze***, absolute, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> local [[Song.4.8|Song 4:8]]. 

**2.** ***regard*** with watchful care (subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>) [[Hos.14.9|Hos 14:9]] so, = ***notice***, [[Job.35.13|Job 35:13]] ***regard, observe***, accusative of thing, God's word [[Job.33.14|Job 33:14]] ([[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Da|Da]] and others), so, (reading 2 masculine singular), [[BDB#Siegf|Siegf]] [[BDB#Bu|Bu]]; [[BDB#Du|Du]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְשִׁיבֶנּוּ‎</span> ***he*** (God) ***retracts it not.*** 

**3.** ***watch*** stealthily, ***lie in wait***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּנָמֵר עַלדֶּֿרֶךְ אָשׁוּר‎</span> [[Hos.13.7|Hos 13:7]] (subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>; [[BDB#Meinh|Meinh]] [[BDB#Marti|Marti]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶשְׁקֹד‎</span>; [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] [[BDB#We|We]] [[BDB#Now|Now]] and others <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַשּׁוּר‎</span>), [[Jer.5.26|Jer 5:26]] (subject wicked). — [[Job.33.27|Job 33:27]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁיר‎</span>.
