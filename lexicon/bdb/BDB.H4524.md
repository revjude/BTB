 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵסַב‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==that which surrounds, or is round==; — 

**1. a.** plural ***surrounding places***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְסִבֵּי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְרוּשׁ‎</span> [[2Kgs.23.5|2Kgs 23:5]] ***places round about Jerusalem***. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** as adverb <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵסַב‎</span> ***round about*** [[1Kgs.6.29|1Kgs 6:29]] feminine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְסִבּוֺת‎</span> ***on all sides, in all directions***, [[Job.37.12|Job 37:12]] (of cloud [[BDB#Di|Di]] &lt; of lightning [[BDB#Bu|Bu]] [[BDB#Du|Du]]).
