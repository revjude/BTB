**1.**) = ***Besit-Rima***, 13 miles east-northeast of Lydda ([[BDB#GASm|GASm]]<sup>Geogr. 254</sup> [[BDB#Buhl|Buhl]]<sup>Geogr. 170</sup>); or ***Râm-allah***, 3 miles southwest of Bethel ([[BDB#Ew|Ew]]<sup>Hist. ii. 421</sup> and others) [compare also <span class="bdbLangGreek">Ἀριμαθαμα</span>; [[Matt.27.57|Matt 27:57]] +].
