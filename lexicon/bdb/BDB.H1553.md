 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּלִילוֺת‎</span> **proper name, of a location** (strictly plural of foregoing; probably = ***circles*** of stones, [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] ***tumuli***, compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]) 

**1.** [[Josh.18.17|Josh 18:17]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַגִּלְגָּל‎</span> [[Josh.15.7|Josh 15:7]]), place on border between Benjamin &amp; Judah. 

**2.** locality described as follows: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַיַּרְדֵּן אֲשֶׁר בְּאֶרֶץ כְּנָ֑עַן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶלגֿ‎</span> [[Josh.22.10|Josh 22:10]] ***unto Geliloth (= the circles) of Jordan which is in the land of Canaan***; ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Γαλγαλα</span>, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]<sup>L</sup></lookup> <span class="bdbLangGreek">Γαλιλωθ</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶלמֿוּל אֶרֶץ כְּנַעַן אֶלגְּֿלִילוֺת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַיַּרְדֵּן אֶלעֵֿבֶר בְּנֵי יִשְׂרָאֵל‎</span> v[[Josh.22.11|Josh 22:11]] ***in front of the land of Canaan, in Geliloth of Jordan, beyond the sons of Israel*** ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Γαλααδ</span>, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]<sup>L</sup></lookup> omitted) see [[BDB#Di|Di]]; yet compare [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>Intr. 106 n.</sup> Others render <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ג‎</span> here ***circuit of Jordan*** = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּכַּר הַיַּרְדֵּן‎</span>; ***districts*** [[BDB#VB|VB]], compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּלִילָה‎</span>.
