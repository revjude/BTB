 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּשְׁרוֺן‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==skill, success==; — 

**1.** ***skill***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּחָכְמָה וּבְדַעַת וּבְכִשְׁר֑וֺן‎</span> [[Eccl.2.21|Eccl 2:21]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּלכִּֿשְׁרוֺן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמַּעֲשֶׂה‎</span> [[Eccl.4.4|Eccl 4:4]] ***all skill of work*** = skilful work. 

**2.** ***success, profit***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּמַהכִּֿשְׁרוֺן לִבְעָלָיהָ‎</span> [[Eccl.5.10|Eccl 5:10]] ***and what profit have the owners of it*** ?
