 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָא‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Hos.6.3|Hos 6:3]]</sup> ==place or act of going forth, issue, export, souce, spring==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Job.28.1|Job 28:1]]; [[Ps.75.7|Ps 75:7]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Num.30.13|Num 30:13]] + 9 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֹצָא‎</span> [[Job.38.27|Job 38:27]]; [[Dan.9.25|Dan 9:25]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָאֲךָ‎</span> [[2Sam.3.25|2Sam 3:25]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֹצָאוֺ‎</span> [[Hos.6.3|Hos 6:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָאוֺ‎</span> [[Ps.19.7|Ps 19:7]] plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָאֵי‎</span> [[Isa.41.18|Isa 41:18]] + 5 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָאָיו‎</span> [[Ezek.43.11|Ezek 43:11]]<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָאֵיהֶם‎</span> [[Num.33.2|Num 33:2]] (twice in verse); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָאֵיהֶן‎</span> [[Ezek.42.11|Ezek 42:11]] — 

**1.** ***a going forth:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** the act, of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Hos.6.3|Hos 6:3]] of the sun = ***rising*** [[Ps.19.7|Ps 19:7]] (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יצא‎</span> **1 f**); of man [[2Sam.3.25|2Sam 3:25]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָבוֺא‎</span> [[BDB#Kt|Kt]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּבָר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Dan.9.25|Dan 9:25]] ***going forth of a command.*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** concrete, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָאֵי גּוֺלָה‎</span> [[Ezek.12.4|Ezek 12:4]] ***goings forth of*** exile, i.e. ***those going forth into*** exile (in simile). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** ***way out, exit:*** concrete, of chambers in temple [[Ezek.42.11|Ezek 42:11]]; [[Ezek.43.11|Ezek 43:11]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺבָאָיו‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָאֵי הַמִּקְדָּשׁ‎</span> [[Ezek.44.5|Ezek 44:5]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְבוֺא הַבַּיִת‎</span>). 

**2.** ***that which goes forth:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***utteranc*** of mouth or lips (especially of solemn or formal speech), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָא פִייֿ‎</span> [[Deut.8.3|Deut 8:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׂפָתַי מ‎</span> [[Jer.17.16|Jer 17:16]]; [[Ps.89.35|Ps 89:35]] so [[Deut.23.24|Deut 23:24]]; [[Num.30.13|Num 30:13]] ([[BDB#P|P]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***export*** of horses [[1Kgs.10.23|1Kgs 10:23]] = [[2Chr.1.16|2Chr 1:16]]. 

**3.** ***place of going forth:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***source*** or ***spring*** of water [[2Kgs.2.21|2Kgs 2:21]]; [[Isa.58.11|Isa 58:11]] (in simile) [[Ps.107.33|Ps 107:33]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְהָרוֺת‎</span>), v[[Ps.107.35|Ps 107:35]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲגַםמֿים‎</span>) = [[Isa.41.18|Isa 41:18]] (\|\| ***id.***); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָא מֵימֵי גִיחוֺן הָעֶלְיוֺן‎</span> [[2Chr.32.30|2Chr 32:30]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***place of departure*** of Israel on marchNum [[2Chr.33.2|2Chr 33:2]] (twice in verse) ([[BDB#P|P]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָא‎</span> = east (place of sun's going forth) [[Ps.75.7|Ps 75:7]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעֲרָב‎</span>); zeugmatically, [[Ps.65.9|Ps 65:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺצָאֵי בֹּקֶר וָעֶרֶב‎</span> i.e. ***the east and the west*** (compare the ***two Orients***, Qor 43:37). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** place whence silver comes = ***mine*** [[Job.28.1|Job 28:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּלְהַצְמִיחַ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֹצָא דֶשֶׁא‎</span> [[Job.38.27|Job 38:27]] and to cause the ***growing-place*** (others, ***the growth***) of young grass to sprout.
