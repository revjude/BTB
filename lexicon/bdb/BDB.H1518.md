**Qal** ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָגִיחַ‎</span> [[Job.40.23|Job 40:23]], masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַתָּ֫גַח‎</span> [[Ezek.32.2|Ezek 32:2]] ***Imperative*** feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גֹּ֫חִי‎</span> [[Mic.4.10|Mic 4:10]] ***Infinitive*** suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎(בְּ)גִיחו‎</span> [[Job.38.8|Job 38:8]] ***Participle*** suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גֹּחִ֫י‎</span> [[Ps.22.10|Ps 22:10]] (but compare below); — 

**1.** intransitive ***burst forth***, of dashing river (a very Jordan) [[Job.40.23|Job 40:23]] of sea figurative as babe from womb [[Job.38.8|Job 38:8]], compare 

**2.** transitive 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***draw forth*** from womb (subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>) [[Ps.22.10|Ps 22:10]] (where read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גָּחִ֫י‎</span> participle from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גּוּחַ‎</span>, or regard <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גֹּחִי‎</span> as metaplastic, as if from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ג֗֗֗חח‎</span>? see [[BDB#De|De]] &amp; [[BDB#Che|Che]]<sup>critical note</sup>) compare [[Ps.71.6|Ps 71:6]] also 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***thrust forth, bring forth***, rather '***break forth*** (with that which is to be born),' figurative of travail, applied to Jerusalem in distress [[Mic.4.10|Mic 4:10]] (compare [[BDB#Sta|Sta]]<sup>§ 599 b</sup> [[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>i. 505</sup>); burst forth with rivers (figurative of Pharaoh under image of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּנִּים‎</span>) [[Ezek.32.2|Ezek 32:2]], but for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בנהרותיך‎</span> (***rivers***) read probably <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּנְחִרוֺתֶיךָ‎</span> [[Job.41.12|Job 41:12]] (***nostrils***), i.e. snort with thy nostrils, so [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Co|Co]]; on figurative then compare <ref ref="Job 41:11-13" b="18" cBegin="41" vBegin="11" cEnd="41" vEnd="13" onclick="bcv(18,41,11)">Job 41:11-13</ref>.
