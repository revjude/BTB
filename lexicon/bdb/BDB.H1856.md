**Qal** ***Perfect*** 3 ***plural*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּקָ֑רוּ‎</span> [[Zech.12.10|Zech 12:10]] ***suffix*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּדְקָרֻנִי‎</span> consecutive [[1Sam.31.4|1Sam 31:4]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּדְקָרֻהוּ‎</span> consecutive [[Zech.13.3|Zech 13:3]] ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּדְקֹר‎</span> [[Num.25.8|Num 25:8]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּדְקְרֵהוּ‎</span> [[Judg.9.54|Judg 9:54]] — ***pierce, run through*** (always with accusative): as retributive act, Israelite and Midianite woman [[Num.25.8|Num 25:8]] false prophet [[Zech.13.3|Zech 13:3]] but also as speedy death [[Judg.9.54|Judg 9:54]] (Abimelech); [[1Sam.31.4|1Sam 31:4]] a (Saul) = [[1Chr.10.4|1Chr 10:4]], also [[1Sam.31.4|1Sam 31:4]]b (but strike out [[BDB#Be|Be]] [[BDB#We|We]] after [[1Chr.10.4|1Chr 10:4]] &amp; so [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]<sup>L</sup></lookup> [[1Sam.31.4|1Sam 31:4]] b), see also [[Zech.12.10|Zech 12:10]] (compare [[John.19.37|John 19:37]]). **Niph\`al** ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִדָּקֵר‎</span> ***be pierced through***, slain [[Isa.13.15|Isa 13:15]] (in conquest of Babylonian by Medes). **Pu\`al** ***Participle*** plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְדֻקָּרִים‎</span> ***pierced, riddled***, (i.e. desperately wounded) warriors, [[Jer.37.10|Jer 37:10]], slain [[Jer.51.4|Jer 51:4]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חללים‎</span>); by hunger [[Lam.4.9|Lam 4:9]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַלְלֵי רָעָב‎</span>).
