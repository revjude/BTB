[<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִיץ‎</span>] **verb Hiph\`il** ==awake== (\|\| form of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יקץ‎</span>; Late Hebrew in Hiph\`il (rare)); — ***Perfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵקִיץ‎</span> [[2Kgs.4.31|2Kgs 4:31]]; [[Ezek.7.6|Ezek 7:6]] 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַהֲקיצ֫וֺתָ‎</span> consecutive [[Prov.6.22|Prov 6:22]], etc.; ***Imperfect*** 1 singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָקִיץ‎</span> [[Prov.23.35|Prov 23:35]] 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָקִ֫יצוּ‎</span> [[Jer.51.39|Jer 51:39]] +; ***Imperative*** masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָקִ֫יצָה‎</span> [[Hab.2.19|Hab 2:19]] +; masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָקִ֫יצוּ‎</span> [[Isa.26.19|Isa 26:19]]; [[Joel.1.5|Joel 1:5]] ***Infinitive construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָקִיץ‎</span> [[Ps.17.15|Ps 17:15]]; [[Ps.73.20|Ps 73:20]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵקִיץ‎</span> [[1Sam.26.12|1Sam 26:12]] — ***shew signs of waking, awake:*** 

**1.** from sleep, 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** [[1Sam.26.12|1Sam 26:12]]; [[Isa.29.8|Isa 29:8]] (twice in verse); [[Ps.3.6|Ps 3:6]]; [[Ps.73.20|Ps 73:20]]; [[Ps.139.18|Ps 139:18]]; [[Prov.6.22|Prov 6:22]] (doubtful line, see Toy). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ps.44.24|Ps 44:24]] ***awake*** [from sleep] to activity, so [[Ps.35.23|Ps 35:23]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ל‎</span> of thing), [[Ps.59.6|Ps 59:6]] (c. infinitive purpose). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** from ecstatic sleep of prophets [[Jer.31.26|Jer 31:26]]. 

**2.** from sleep of death, with negative [[2Kgs.4.31|2Kgs 4:31]]; [[Jer.51.39|Jer 51:39]]; [[Jer.51.57|Jer 51:57]]; [[Job.14.12|Job 14:12]] of resurrection [[Isa.26.19|Isa 26:19]]; [[Dan.12.2|Dan 12:2]] 

**3.** from stupor (of drunkenness) [[Joel.1.5|Joel 1:5]]; [[Prov.23.35|Prov 23:35]]. 

**4.** of inanimate thing [[Hab.2.19|Hab 2:19]] compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּא הַקֵּץ הֵקִיץ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵלָ֑יִח‎</span> [[Ezek.7.6|Ezek 7:6]] ***the end cometh, it hath awaked unto thee*** ([[BDB#Co|Co]] strike out <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵקִיץ‎</span>, but the word-play favours it). — [[Ps.17.15|Ps 17:15]] is put by [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] [[BDB#Ol|Ol]] [[BDB#Hup|Hup]] [[BDB#Du|Du]] and others below **1 a**; [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] below **2**; we below **1 b**; [[BDB#Calv|Calv]] [[BDB#Now|Now]]<sup></sup> from night of distress and helplessness, and so [[BDB#Bae|Bae]] (psalmist representing Israel).
