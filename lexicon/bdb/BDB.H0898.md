 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּגַד‎</span>] **verb** ==act or deal treacherously== — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּֽגְדָה‎</span> [[Jer.3.20|Jer 3:20]] + 14 t.; ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִבְגֹּד‎</span> [[Mal.2.15|Mal 2:15]] + 5 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִבְגַּד‎</span> [[Mal.2.10|Mal 2:10]] ***Infinitive absolute*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּגוֺד‎</span> [[Isa.48.8|Isa 48:8]]; [[Jer.5.11|Jer 5:11]] ***construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּגֹד‎</span> [[Isa.33.1|Isa 33:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּגְדוֺ‎</span> [[Exod.21.8|Exod 21:8]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֹּגֵד‎</span> [[Prov.22.12|Prov 22:12]] + 11 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בּוֺגֵד‎</span> [[Isa.33.1|Isa 33:1]] + 10 t.; — ***act*** or ***deal treacherously, faithlessly, deceitfully***, in the marriage relation, in matters of property or right, in covenants, in word and in general conduct. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***absolute*** [[1Sam.14.33|1Sam 14:33]]; [[Job.6.15|Job 6:15]]; [[Ps.78.57|Ps 78:57]]; [[Isa.24.16|Isa 24:16]]; [[Isa.33.1|Isa 33:1]] (twice in verse); [[Isa.48.8|Isa 48:8]]; [[Mal.2.11|Mal 2:11]]. compare phrases <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אחי בגדו כמו נחל‎</span> ***my brethren have dealt deceitfully as a brook*** [[Job.6.15|Job 6:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֹּגְדִים בָּנָ֑דוּ וּבֶגֶד בּוֺגְדִים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּגָ֑דוּ‎</span> ***the treacherous have dealt treacherously, yea in treachery have the treacherous dealt treacherously*** [[Isa.24.16|Isa 24:16]] (striking alliteration); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֹּגְדֵי אָוֶן‎</span> ***treacherous in wickedness*** [[Ps.59.6|Ps 59:6]].
