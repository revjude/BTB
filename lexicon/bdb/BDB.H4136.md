 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמוֺא֯ל‎</span> [[Neh.12.38|Neh 12:38]] read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַשְּׂמאֹל‎</span>, see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׂמאל‎</span>. 

 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוּל‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺל‎</span> ( [[Deut.1.1|Deut 1:1]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוֺאל‎</span> ( [[Neh.12.38|Neh 12:38]]) **substantive** and **preposition** ==front, in front of== (derivatives dubious; according to [[BDB#Ol|Ol]]<sup>§ 223 c</sup> [[BDB#Sta|Sta]]<sup>§ 378 a, for</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְאוֺל‎</span> from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוּל‎</span> ***to be in front:*** see further [[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>ii. 300 f.</sup> [[BDB#Hpt|Hpt]]<sup>BAS i. 172</sup>): — 

**1. substantive** only [[1Kgs.7.5|1Kgs 7:5]] (si vera 1.: see [[BDB#Sta|Sta]]<sup>ZAW 1883, 151</sup>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּמוּל מֶחֱזָה אֶלמֶֿחֱזָה‎</span> and the ***front*** of (one) window was towards (the other) window, and [[Neh.12.38|Neh 12:38]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמוֺאל‎</span> = ***in the opposite direction*** (but read probably <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִשְׂמאוֺל‎</span>, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַיָּמִין‎</span> v[[Neh.12.31|Neh 12:31]]). 

**2.** as **preposition**, [[Deut.1.1|Deut 1:1]]; [[Deut.2.19|Deut 2:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוּל בני עמון‎</span> ***in front of*** the Ammonites, [[Deut.3.29|Deut 3:29]] (= [[Deut.4.46|Deut 4:46]]; [[Deut.34.6|Deut 34:6]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוּל בית פעור‎</span>, [[Deut.11.30|Deut 11:30]]; [[Josh.18.18|Josh 18:18]]; [[Josh.19.46|Josh 19:46]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוּל יָפוֺ‎</span> [[1Sam.14.5|1Sam 14:5]] (twice in verse); figurative [[Exod.18.19|Exod 18:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֱיֵה אַתָּה לעם מול האלהים‎</span> ***in front of*** God, i.e. representing God to them. With prefixes: — 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אלמֿול‎</span> ***towards the front of***, [[1Sam.17.30|1Sam 17:30]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵאֶצְלוֺ אֶלמֿוּל אַחֵר וַיִּסֹב‎</span> ***to the front of*** another; ***on the front of***, [[Exod.34.3|Exod 34:3]] (pregnantly) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאַליִֿרְעוּ אלמֿול ההר‎</span>, Joel [[Exod.8.33|Exod 8:33]] (twice in verse); [[Exod.9.1|Exod 9:1]]; [[Exod.22.11|Exod 22:11]] (see [[BDB#WAW|WAW]]<sup>JPhoenician xiii. 117 ff.</sup>); specifically <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מוּל פְּנֵי אֶלֿ‎</span> ***towards*** or ***on the forefront of***, [[Exod.26.9|Exod 26:9]]; [[Exod.28.25|Exod 28:25]] (\|\| [[Exod.39.18|Exod 39:18]]), v[[Exod.39.37|Exod 39:37]]; [[Lev.8.9|Lev 8:9]]; [[Num.8.2|Num 8:2]]; [[Num.8.3|Num 8:3]]; [[2Sam.11.15|2Sam 11:15]] set ye Uriah <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אלמֿול פני המלחמה‎</span>. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִמּוּל‎</span>: — 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***a***) ***from the front of***, [[Mic.2.8|Mic 2:8]] (text dubious: read probably <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵעַל‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***b***) of position, ***off*** (see <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מן‎</span> **1 c**) ***the front of***, [[Lev.5.8|Lev 5:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִמּוּל עָרְפּוֺ‎</span> ***off the front of*** its neck, i.e. close in front of it, [[Num.22.5|Num 22:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎והוא יושׁב מִמֻּלִי‎</span> ***close in front of*** me, [[2Sam.5.23|2Sam 5:23]] ([[1Chr.14.14|1Chr 14:14]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ממול בכאים ובאת להם‎</span> come to them ***off the front of*** the mulberry-trees, [[1Kgs.7.39|1Kgs 7:39]] ([[2Chr.4.10|2Chr 4:10]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִמּוּל נֶגֶב‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִמּוּל פָּנָיו‎</span> ***on the forefront of***, [[Exod.28.27|Exod 28:27]] (\|\| [[Exod.39.20|Exod 39:20]]).
