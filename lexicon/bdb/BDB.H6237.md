 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָשַׂר‎</span>] **verb denominative** ==take the tenth of, tithe==; — 

**Qal** ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעְשׂר‎</span> [[1Sam.8.15|1Sam 8:15]]; [[1Sam.8.17|1Sam 8:17]] (with accusative of thing tithed). **Pi\`el** ***Imperfect*** + ***Infinitive absolute give a tenth of***, with accusative of thing: 2 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַשֵּׂר תְּעַשֵּׂר‎</span> [[Deut.14.22|Deut 14:22]] 1 singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַשֵּׂר אֲעַשְּׂרֶנּוּ לָ֑ח‎</span> [[Gen.28.22|Gen 28:22]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); ***Participle taking the tenth***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמְעַשְּׂרִים‎</span> [[Neh.10.38|Neh 10:38]]. **Hiph\`il** (so usually explained) ***Infinitive*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַעְשֵׂר‎</span> [[Deut.26.12|Deut 26:12]] with accusative of congnate meaning with verb i.e. ***taking, reckoning, tithe***, but Infinitive construct with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ֵ֯‎</span> improbable, &lt; read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְעַשֵּׂר‎</span> **Pi\`el**; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּעְשֵׂר‎</span> [[Neh.10.39|Neh 10:39]] absolute, i.e. ***receiving tithes***, but read probably <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּעְשׂר‎</span> **Qal** (on anomalous points, see [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:53k»§ 53k</sup> [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>[[Deut.26.12|Deut 26:12]]</sup>).
