 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֵ֫כֶר‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Ps.9.7|Ps 9:7]]</sup> ==remembrance, memorial== (compare [[BDB#Hpt|Hpt]]<sup>Hebraica. 1885, 230</sup>) — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> absolute [[Ps.111.4|Ps 111:4]] construct [[Deut.25.19|Deut 25:19]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זֶכֶר‎</span> construct [[Exod.17.14|Exod 17:14]] [[BDB#van d.H.|van d. H]]; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִכְרִי‎</span> [[Exod.3.15|Exod 3:15]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִכְרְךָ‎</span> [[Isa.26.8|Isa 26:8]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִכְרֶ֑ךָ‎</span> [[Ps.6.6|Ps 6:6]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִכְרוֺ‎</span> [[Hos.12.6|Hos 12:6]] +, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זִכְרָם‎</span> [[Deut.32.26|Deut 32:26]] +: — 

**1.** ***remembrance, memory:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of persons or people, blotted out by their destruction, [[Exod.17.14|Exod 17:14]] ([[BDB#E|E]]; with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מחה‎</span>) = [[Deut.25.19|Deut 25:19]]; [[Deut.32.26|Deut 32:26]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎השׁבית‎</span>) [[Ps.34.17|Ps 34:17]]; [[Ps.109.15|Ps 109:15]] (both <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הכרית‎</span>), [[Ps.9.7|Ps 9:7]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אבד‎</span>), [[Job.18.17|Job 18:17]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אבד‎</span>; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁם‎</span>), [[Isa.26.14|Isa 26:14]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אבּד‎</span>); compare on other hand <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עוֺלָם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> as portion of righteous. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ps.6.6|Ps 6:6]]; [[Ps.102.13|Ps 102:13]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַתָּה‎</span> = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יהוה‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s character and works [[Ps.30.5|Ps 30:5]]; [[Ps.97.12|Ps 97:12]]; [[Ps.111.4|Ps 111:4]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** ***remembrance*** of particular days, i.e. their observance [[Esth.9.28|Esth 9:28]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַפּוּרִים יְמֵי‎</span>). 

**2.** ***memorial***, by which one is remembered: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** nearly = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁם‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַדִּיק לִבְרָכָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> [[Prov.10.7|Prov 10:7]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁם‎</span>), compare also [[Eccl.9.5|Eccl 9:5]] (with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נשׁכח‎</span>); especially of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Exod.3.15|Exod 3:15]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[Hos.12.6|Hos 12:6]]; [[Isa.26.8|Isa 26:8]]; [[Ps.135.13|Ps 135:13]] (all \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁם‎</span>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** = renown (of Israel) [[Hos.14.8|Hos 14:8]] (compare [[BDB#VB|VB]]).
