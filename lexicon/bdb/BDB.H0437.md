 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַלּוֺן‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Gen.35.8|Gen 35:8]]</sup> ==oak== ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">βάλανος</span>, <span class="bdbLangGreek">δρῦς</span>, etc.) — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> absolute [[Gen.35.8|Gen 35:8]] +; construct ***ib.***; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַלּוֺנִים‎</span> [[Amos.2.9|Amos 2:9]]; [[Ezek.27.6|Ezek 27:6]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַלּוֺנֵי‎</span> [[Isa.2.13|Isa 2:13]]; [[Zech.11.12|Zech 11:12]] — as marking grave of Deborah, Rebekah's nurse [[Gen.35.8|Gen 35:8]] ([[BDB#E|E]]); whence called ***oak of weeping***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּכוּת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> ***ib.*** (see [[BDB#Di|Di]] ***on the passage***, &amp; below <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵלוֺן‎</span> p. 18); elsewhere only in prophets; as marking illicit shrines [[Hos.4.13|Hos 4:13]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִבְנֶה‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵלָה‎</span>); as felled [[Isa.6.13|Isa 6:13]] (in simile; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵלָה‎</span>); as furnishing material for making idols [[Isa.44.14|Isa 44:14]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶז‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּרְזָה‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֹרֶן‎</span>); as simile for strength (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חסן‎</span>) [[Amos.2.9|Amos 2:9]] also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַלּוֺנֵי הַבָּשָׁן‎</span> [[Isa.2.13|Isa 2:13]] (as lofty &amp; majestic), compare [[Ezek.27.6|Ezek 27:6]] (as strong, for making oars); [[Zech.11.2|Zech 11:2]], metaphor of prominent men.
