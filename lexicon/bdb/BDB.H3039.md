 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָדִיד‎</span>] **adjective** (poetry) ==beloved== (Late Hebrew ***id.***, Aramaic <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhKAAaAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAoABoAAAJljI+py+0PIQCxukltxYhrw02JB4oXZqJmuQYko2buyx5tE9vyeH/dzgP6Nr2hpGg8CZNKpoXmPOmEvSUypZruUL8qNpabgblB1thzJv+6FDDbrbgtF1AYMsorR+7HuR7/wAf4UQAAOw==">) — construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְדִיד‎</span> [[Deut.33.12|Deut 33:12]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִידִידִי‎</span> [[Isa.5.1|Isa 5:1]] (twice in verse); [[Jer.11.15|Jer 11:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִידִידוֺ‎</span> [[Ps.127.2|Ps 127:2]] plural suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְדִידֶיךָ‎</span> [[Ps.60.7|Ps 60:7]]; [[Ps.108.7|Ps 108:7]] feminine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְדִידֹת‎</span> [[Ps.45.1|Ps 45:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְדִידוֺת‎</span> [[Ps.84.2|Ps 84:2]] — 

**1.** ***my beloved***, used by prophets of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> under figure of husbandman [[Isa.5.1|Isa 5:1]] (twice in verse); so ***my*** (thy, his) ***beloved*** [[Jer.11.15|Jer 11:15]]; [[Ps.60.7|Ps 60:7]] = [[Ps.108.7|Ps 108:7]]; [[Ps.2|Ps 2]] ***beloved***, of Benjamin as beloved by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Deut.33.12|Deut 33:12]]. 

**2.** ***lovely***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַהיְּֿדִידוֺת מִשְׁכְּנוֺתֶיךָ‎</span> [[Ps.84.2|Ps 84:2]] ***how lovely are thy habitations !*** 

**3.** feminine plural as abstract substantive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁיר יְדִידֹת‎</span> [[Ps.45.1|Ps 45:1]] = ***a song of love***.
