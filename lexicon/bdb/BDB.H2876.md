<small>Aramaic form: **<big>[[H2877|טַבָּח]]</big>**</small>
 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַבָּח‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[1Sam.9.23|1Sam 9:23]]</sup> 

**1.** ==cook==, 

**2.** ==guardsman==; — 

**1.** ***cook*** (who also killed the animal for food and served it) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַבָּח‎</span> absolute [[1Sam.9.23|1Sam 9:23]]; [[1Sam.9.24|1Sam 9:24]] . 

**2.** elsewhere only plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַבָּחִים‎</span> ***guardsmen, bodyguard*** (originally royal ***slaughterers***; see [[BDB#RS|RS]]<sup>OTJC 426</sup> (262); Semitic i. 1st ed., 396); always in the following combinations: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שַׂר הַטּ‎</span> ***captain of*** Pharaoh's ***bodyguard*** [[Gen.37.36|Gen 37:36]]; [[Gen.39.1|Gen 39:1]] (both [[BDB#J|J]]), [[Gen.40.3|Gen 40:3]]; [[Gen.40.4|Gen 40:4]]; [[Gen.41.10|Gen 41:10]]; [[Gen.41.12|Gen 41:12]] (all [[BDB#E|E]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַבטֿ‎</span> ***chief of*** Nebuchadrezzar's ***bodyguard*** [[2Kgs.25.8|2Kgs 25:8]]; [[2Kgs.25.10|2Kgs 25:10]]; [[2Kgs.25.11|2Kgs 25:11]]; [[2Kgs.25.12|2Kgs 25:12]]; [[2Kgs.25.15|2Kgs 25:15]]; [[2Kgs.25.18|2Kgs 25:18]]; [[2Kgs.25.20|2Kgs 25:20]]; [[Jer.39.9|Jer 39:9]] + 16 t. Jeremiah (hence Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎רַב טַבָּחַיָּא‎</span> [[Dan.2.14|Dan 2:14]]).
