 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָשַׂף‎</span> **verb** ==strip off, strip, make bare== — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָשַׂף‎</span> [[Isa.52.10|Isa 52:10]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲשָׂפָהּ‎</span> [[Joel.1.7|Joel 1:7]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָשַׂפְתִּי‎</span> [[Jer.13.26|Jer 13:26]]; [[Jer.49.10|Jer 49:10]] ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיֶּחֱשׂף‎</span> [[Ps.29.9|Ps 29:9]] ***Imperative*** feminine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶשְׂפִּיֿ‎</span> [[Isa.47.2|Isa 47:2]] ***Infinitive absolute*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָשׂף‎</span> [[Joel.1.7|Joel 1:7]] ***construct*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַחְשׂף‎</span> [[Isa.30.14|Isa 30:14]]; [[Hag.2.16|Hag 2:16]] ***Passive participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲשׂוּפָה‎</span> [[Ezek.4.7|Ezek 4:7]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲשׂוּפַי‎</span> [[Isa.20.4|Isa 20:4]] (plural construct ? compare ***below***). — 

**1.** ***strip off***, expose oneself by removing (object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֹׁבֶל‎</span>, flowing skirt, train) [[Isa.47.2|Isa 47:2]] of Babylon, personified as queen (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גַּלִּי שׁוֺק‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוּלַיִךְ עַלפָּֿנַיִךְ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Jer.13.26|Jer 13:26]]. 

**2.** ***strip, lay bare***, figurative of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Isa.52.10|Isa 52:10]] object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זְרוֺעַ קדשׁו‎</span>; of prophet, [[Ezek.4.7|Ezek 4:7]] (but [[BDB#Co|Co]] strike out see as interpolation); object person [[Jer.49.10|Jer 49:10]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גִּלֵּיתִי‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָשׂף חשׂפהּ‎</span> [[Joel.1.7|Joel 1:7]] of locusts stripping fig-tree; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲשׂוּפַי שֵׁת‎</span> [[Isa.20.4|Isa 20:4]] (participle either singular collective, with formative ending <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַ֯‎</span>, [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>Se i. 19</sup> (compare 69), BN 192; or plural construct, see [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:87»§ 87</sup>, 1 c.); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְעָרוֺת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Ps.29.9|Ps 29:9]] of voice of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> (i.e. a storm) stripping forests. 

**3.** ***draw*** (water) [[Isa.30.14|Isa 30:14]] (wine) [[Hag.2.16|Hag 2:16]] properly ***take from the surface, skim***.
