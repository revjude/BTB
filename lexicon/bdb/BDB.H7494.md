 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַ֫עַשׁ‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Jer.10.22|Jer 10:22]]</sup> ==quaking, shaking==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ר‎</span> absolute [[1Kgs.19.11|1Kgs 19:11]] +, construct [[Nah.3.2|Nah 3:2]]; [[Job.41.21|Job 41:21]] — 

**1.** specifically ***earthquake***, [[Amos.1.1|Amos 1:1]]; [[Zech.14.5|Zech 14:5]]; [[1Kgs.19.11|1Kgs 19:11]] (twice in verse); [[1Kgs.19.12|1Kgs 19:12]] figurative of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s judgement [[Isa.29.6|Isa 29:6]]; [[Ezek.38.19|Ezek 38:19]] compare (in vision) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קוֺל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גָּרוֺל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ר‎</span> [[Ezek.3.12|Ezek 3:12]]; [[Ezek.3.13|Ezek 3:13]] probably also [[Ezek.37.7|Ezek 37:7]] ([[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] [[BDB#Co|Co]] [[BDB#Krae|Krae]]; &gt; ***rustling, rattling***, [[BDB#Hi|Hi]]-[[BDB#Sm|Sm]] Toy); hence hyperbole of shaking of earth by tramping of warriors [[Isa.9.4|Isa 9:4]], by war-chariots [[Jer.47.3|Jer 47:3]], compare [[Jer.10.22|Jer 10:22]], or wheels [[Nah.3.2|Nah 3:2]]. 

**2.** ***quaking, trembling***, of person [[Ezek.12.18|Ezek 12:18]]. 

**3.** ***shaking, quivering***, of horse eager to start [[Job.39.24|Job 39:24]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֹגֶז‎</span>), of dart [[Job.41.21|Job 41:21]] (al. ***rushing sound***).
