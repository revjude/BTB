 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַכְשֵׁלָה‎</span> 

**noun feminine 1.** ==overthrown mass==. 

**2.** ==stumbling-block==; — 

**1.** figurative of kingdom [[Isa.3.6|Isa 3:6]]. 

**2.** plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמַּכְשֵׁלוֺת‎</span> [[Zeph.1.3|Zeph 1:3]] ***stumbling-blocks***=idols (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִכְשׁוֺל‎</span> **2 c**), but rather dubious; [[BDB#We|We]] thinks a gloss. [[BDB#Schw|Schw]]<sup>ZAW x. (1890), 169</sup> reads <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְכִשַּׁלְתִּי‎</span> ***and I will cause*** the wicked to ***stumble.***
