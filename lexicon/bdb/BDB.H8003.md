 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁלֵם‎</span> **adjective** ==complete, safe, at peace==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> [[Gen.15.16|Gen 15:16]] +; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלֵמִים‎</span> [[Gen.34.21|Gen 34:21]]; [[Nah.1.12|Nah 1:12]] feminine <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלֵמָה‎</span> [[Deut.25.15|Deut 25:15]] +; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁלֵמוֺת‎</span> [[Deut.27.6|Deut 27:6]]; [[Josh.8.31|Josh 8:31]] — 

**1.** ***complete:*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***full, perfect:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָבָן שְׁלֵמָה‎</span> ***full weight*** [[Deut.25.15|Deut 25:15]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵיפָה שׁ‎</span> v[[Deut.25.15|Deut 25:15]]; [[Prov.11.1|Prov 11:1]] of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֶוֺן‎</span> [[Gen.15.16|Gen 15:16]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]); number of captives [[Amos.1.6|Amos 1:6]]; [[Amos.1.9|Amos 1:9]] of army [[Nah.1.12|Nah 1:12]] (text corrupt; [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎משֵׁל מַיִם‎</span>, but ?; see Commentaries); of reward [[Ruth.2.12|Ruth 2:12]] of stones, ***whole*** (in natural condition, ***unhewn***) [[Deut.27.6|Deut 27:6]]; [[Josh.8.31|Josh 8:31]] (compare law [[Exod.20.25|Exod 20:25]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***finished:*** stones for temple [[1Kgs.6.7|1Kgs 6:7]] temple [[2Chr.8.16|2Chr 8:16]]. 

**2.** ***safe, unharmed***, of person [[Gen.33.18|Gen 33:18]] ([[BDB#P|P]]; Samaritan <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁלוֺם‎</span>, compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בשׁלום‎</span> [[Gen.28.21|Gen 28:21]] not proper name, of a location as [[BDB#Vrss|Vrss]]). 

**3.** ***in covt. of peace***, friendship, with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵת‎</span> [[Gen.34.21|Gen 34:21]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֵבָב שָׁלֵם עִם י‎</span> ***a mind at peace with*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>, keeping covt. relation, hence ***complete, perfect***, [[1Kgs.8.61|1Kgs 8:61]]; [[1Kgs.11.4|1Kgs 11:4]]; [[1Kgs.15.3|1Kgs 15:3]]; [[1Kgs.15.14|1Kgs 15:14]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶל‎</span> [[2Chr.16.9|2Chr 16:9]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶל‎</span> omitted [[2Kgs.20.3|2Kgs 20:3]] = [[Isa.38.3|Isa 38:3]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֵב‎</span>), [[1Chr.12.38|1Chr 12:38]]; [[2Chr.15.17|2Chr 15:17]]; [[2Chr.19.9|2Chr 19:9]]; [[2Chr.25.2|2Chr 25:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֵב‎</span> [[1Chr.28.9|1Chr 28:9]]; [[1Chr.29.9|1Chr 29:9]].
