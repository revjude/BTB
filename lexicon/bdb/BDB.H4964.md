 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֶ֫תֶג‎</span> **noun masculine** ==bridle== (Aramaic ([[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִתְגָּא‎</span>; Late Hebrew <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֶתֶג‎</span> of secondary accent <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ָֽ‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִתֵּג‎</span> Pi\`el denominative ***put on a bridle***); — 

**1.** literally ***bridle***, for animals; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֶתֶג‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַחֲמוֺר‎</span> [[Prov.26.3|Prov 26:3]] a whip for the horse, ***a bridle for the ass***, etc.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וָרֶסֶן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּמ‎</span> [[Ps.32.9|Ps 32:9]] ***with bridle and halter*** (on text of verse compare [[BDB#Che|Che]]); of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s ***birdle*** for Sennach. (under figure of beast), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּמִתְגִּי בִּשְׂפָתֶיךָ‎</span> [[2Kgs.19.28|2Kgs 19:28]] and I will put (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְשַׂמְתִּי‎</span>) my hook in thy nose ***and my bridle in thy lips*** = [[Isa.37.29|Isa 37:29]] (see [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>Is. ed. 2, 220</sup>). 

**2.** figurative = ***control, authority***, only in <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָאַמָּה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[2Sam.8.1|2Sam 8:1]]***authority of the mother city***, according to [[BDB#MV|MV]] [[BDB#Buhl|Buhl]], of especially [[BDB#We|We]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]; [[BDB#Bu|Bu]] omit as corrupt (see I.  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַמָּה‎</span>, above); see, further, [[BDB#HPS|HPS]].
