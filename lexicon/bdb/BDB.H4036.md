 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סָבִיב‎</span><sub>336</sub> **substantive**, used mostly as **adverb** and **preposition**, ==circuit, round about==: — construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְבִיב‎</span> [[Amos.3.11|Amos 3:11]] plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְבִיבֵי‎</span> [[Jer.32.44|Jer 32:44]]; [[Jer.33.13|Jer 33:13]], suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְבִיבֶיךָ‎</span> etc. (10 t.), much oftener <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְבִיבוֺת‎</span> [[Exod.7.24|Exod 7:24]] + 22 t., <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְבִיבוֺתַי‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תֶיךָ‎</span>-, etc., [[Deut.17.14|Deut 17:14]], etc. (48 t.): — 

**1.** in singular — 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** as **substantive** [[1Chr.11.8|1Chr 11:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מןהֿמלוא וְעַדהַֿסָּבִיב‎</span> and to ***the parts round about***. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** as **adverb accusative** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סָבִיב‎</span> (in) ***a circuit***, i.e. ***round about***, [[Gen.23.17|Gen 23:17]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּכָלגְּֿבֻלוֺ סָבִיב‎</span> [[Exod.19.12|Exod 19:12]]; [[Exod.25.11|Exod 25:11]]; [[Exod.25.24|Exod 25:24]]; [[Exod.25.25|Exod 25:25]]; [[Lev.1.5|Lev 1:5]]; [[Lev.1.11|Lev 1:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל הַמִּזְבֵּחַ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סָבִיב‎</span>, [[Judg.20.29|Judg 20:29]]; [[1Kgs.3.1|1Kgs 3:1]]; [[1Kgs.5.11|1Kgs 5:11]]; [[Ps.3.7|Ps 3:7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׁתוּ עָלָ֑י‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲשֶׁר ס‎</span>, [[Ps.12.9|Ps 12:9]]; [[Ps.34.8|Ps 34:8]] + often; sometimes doubled, for the sake of emphasis, [[2Chr.4.3|2Chr 4:3]]; [[Ezek.8.10|Ezek 8:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ס‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַל הַקִּיר ס‎</span>, [[Ezek.37.2|Ezek 37:2]]; [[Ezek.40.5|Ezek 40:5]] and often in Ezek 40-43. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** as **preposition**: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***a***) Psalms, [[Amos.3.11|Amos 3:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צַר וּסְבִיב הָאָרֶץ‎</span> ***distress***, and that ***in the circuit of*** (= ***round about***) the land (but read probably with [[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְסֹבֵב‎</span> ***will encircle***). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***b***) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סָבִיב לְ‎</span> [[Exod.16.13|Exod 16:13]]; [[Exod.40.33|Exod 40:33]]; [[Num.1.50|Num 1:50]]; [[Num.1.53|Num 1:53]]; [[Num.2.2|Num 2:2]]; [[Judg.7.21|Judg 7:21]]; [[1Kgs.6.5|1Kgs 6:5]]; [[1Kgs.18.32|1Kgs 18:32]]; [[1Kgs.18.35|1Kgs 18:35]]; [[Ezek.41.10|Ezek 41:10]]; [[Ezek.14.16|Ezek 14:16]]; [[Job.19.12|Job 19:12]]; [[Ps.34.8|Ps 34:8]]; [[Ps.78.28|Ps 78:28]]; [[Ps.125.2|Ps 125:2]] (twice in verse) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סָבִיב לְעַמּוֺ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>, [[Ps.128.3|Ps 128:3]]; [[Song.3.7|Song 3:7]]; [[Nah.3.8|Nah 3:8]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***c***) strangely, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סָבִיב אֵת‎</span> [[1Kgs.6.5|1Kgs 6:5]] (omitted by [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]]), [[Ezek.43.17|Ezek 43:17]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִסָּבִיב‎</span><sub>42</sub>, ***from round about, from every side***, [[Ezek.16.33|Ezek 16:33]]; [[Ezek.16.37|Ezek 16:37]]; [[Ezek.23.22|Ezek 23:22]]; [[Ezek.37.21|Ezek 37:21]], but usually (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> **1 c**) = ***on every side***, [[Jer.4.17|Jer 4:17]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָיוּ עָלֶיהָ מִסָּבִיב‎</span>, [[Isa.42.25|Isa 42:25]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַתְּלַהֲטֵהוּ מ‎</span>, [[Joel.4.11|Joel 4:11]]; [[Joel.4.12|Joel 4:12]] Psalms <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָגוֺר מִסָּבִיב‎</span> terror ***on every side !*** [[Jer.6.25|Jer 6:25]]; [[Jer.20.3|Jer 20:3]]; [[Jer.20.10|Jer 20:10]]; [[Jer.46.5|Jer 46:5]]; [[Jer.49.29|Jer 49:29]]; [[Ps.31.14|Ps 31:14]], compare [[Lam.2.22|Lam 2:22]] especially in the Deuteronomic phrases <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺיְבִים מ‎</span>, or <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵנִיחַ מ‎</span> (sometimes in combination), as [[Deut.12.10|Deut 12:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎והניח לכם מכלאֿויביכם מ‎</span>, [[Deut.25.19|Deut 25:19]]; [[Josh.21.42|Josh 21:42]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָהֶם מ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיָּנַח י‎</span>, [[Josh.23.1|Josh 23:1]]; [[Judg.2.14|Judg 2:14]]; [[Judg.8.34|Judg 8:34]]; [[1Sam.12.11|1Sam 12:11]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִסָּבִיב לְ‎</span> ***from round about***, [[Num.16.24|Num 16:24]]; [[Num.16.27|Num 16:27]]. — On [[1Sam.14.21|1Sam 14:21]]; [[2Sam.24.6|2Sam 24:6]]; [[Job.10.8|Job 10:8]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סָבַב‎</span>. 

**2.** In ***plur.:*** — 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְבִיבִים‎</span>: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***a***) in masculine sense, ***those round about***, [[Ps.76.12|Ps 76:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּלסְֿבִיבָיו יֹבִילוּ שַׁי‎</span>, [[Ps.89.8|Ps 89:8]]; [[Jer.48.17|Jer 48:17]]; [[Jer.48.39|Jer 48:39]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***b***) in neuter sense, ***the parts round about***, [[Jer.49.5|Jer 49:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִכָּלסְֿבִיבָ֑יִךְ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּסְבִיבֵי ירושׁלם‎</span> [[Jer.32.44|Jer 32:44]]; [[Jer.33.13|Jer 33:13]]; [[Jer.21.14|Jer 21:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאָֽכְלָה כָּלסְֿבִיבֶיהָ‎</span>, [[Jer.46.14|Jer 46:14]] with the force of a **perp.**, [[Ps.50.3|Ps 50:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וסביביו נשׂערה מאד‎</span>, [[Ps.97.2|Ps 97:2]]; [[Lam.1.17|Lam 1:17]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְבִיבוֺת‎</span> 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***a***) as **substantive** (<span class="bdbLangGreek">α</span>) ***circuits***, [[Eccl.1.6|Eccl 1:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ועל סביבותיו שָׁב הָרוּחַ‎</span>; (<span class="bdbLangGreek">β</span>) ***the parts round about***, [[Num.22.4|Num 22:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ילחכו הקהל אתכֿלסְֿבִיבֹתֵינוּ‎</span>, [[Jer.17.26|Jer 17:26]]; [[Jer.50.32|Jer 50:32]] (compare [[Jer.21.14|Jer 21:14]] above); with reference to their inhabitants, [[Ps.44.19|Ps 44:19]] = [[Ps.79.4|Ps 79:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לעג וקלס לסב‎</span>, [[Ezek.16.57|Ezek 16:57]]; [[Ezek.28.24|Ezek 28:24]]; [[Dan.9.16|Dan 9:16]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ (***b***) with the force a **preposition** [[Exod.7.24|Exod 7:24]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְבִיבֹת הַיְאֹר‎</span> literally (in) ***the circuits of*** the Nile = ***round about*** the Nile, [[Num.11.24|Num 11:24]]; [[Num.11.31|Num 11:31]]; [[Num.11.32|Num 11:32]]; [[Num.35.2|Num 35:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְבִיבֹתֵיהֶם‎</span>, [[Judg.7.18|Judg 7:18]]; [[1Sam.26.5|1Sam 26:5]]; [[1Sam.26.7|1Sam 26:7]]; [[2Kgs.6.17|2Kgs 6:17]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סביבֹת אלישׁע‎</span>, [[Ps.18.12|Ps 18:12]]; [[Ps.27.6|Ps 27:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎על אֹיְבַי סביבותי‎</span>, etc.; often idiomatic preceded by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אשׁר‎</span>, as [[Gen.33.5|Gen 33:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הערים אשׁר סביבותיהם‎</span> [[Gen.41.48|Gen 41:48]]; [[Lev.25.44|Lev 25:44]]; [[Num.16.34|Num 16:34]]; [[Deut.6.14|Deut 6:14]]; [[Deut.13.8|Deut 13:8]]; [[Deut.17.14|Deut 17:14]]; [[Deut.21.2|Deut 21:2]] +. In the same sense <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִסְּבִיבוֺתָם‎</span> [[Ezek.28.26|Ezek 28:26]].
