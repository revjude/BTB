**1. a.** ***pass on quickly*** [[1Sam.10.3|1Sam 10:3]] elsewhere only in poetry, ***move*** or ***sweep on***, of a flood [[Isa.8.8|Isa 8:8]], or wind [[Isa.21.1|Isa 21:1]]; [[Job.4.15|Job 4:15]]; [[Hab.1.11|Hab 1:11]], of God [[Job.9.11|Job 9:11]]; [[Job.11.10|Job 11:10]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***pass away*** (***vanish***) [[Job.9.26|Job 9:26]] (of days); [[Song.2.11|Song 2:11]] (of rain \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עבר‎</span>), of the heavens [[Ps.102.27|Ps 102:27]], of idols [[Isa.2.18|Isa 2:18]], (but perhaps gloss [[BDB#JBL|JBL]]<sup>ix. 1890, 86</sup>). 

**2.** of grass, ***come on anew***, i. e. ***sprout again*** (compare **Hiph\`il 2**) [[Ps.90.5|Ps 90:5]]; [[Ps.90.6|Ps 90:6]], so [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] [[BDB#AV|AV]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Bae|Bae]] and others; less suitably in context, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] ***pass away***, above

$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***pass through***, i.e. pierce, followed by accusative [[Judg.5.26|Judg 5:26]]; [[Job.20.24|Job 20:24]].

**1.** ***change*** (transitive) garments [[Gen.35.2|Gen 35:2]] ([[BDB#E|E]]) [[Ps.102.27|Ps 102:27]], wages [[Gen.31.7|Gen 31:7]]; [[Gen.31.41|Gen 31:41]] (both [[BDB#E|E]]); no object expressed [[Lev.27.10|Lev 27:10]] ([[BDB#H|H]]); ***substitute*** i. e. ***cause to succeed*** [[Isa.9.9|Isa 9:9]] ***change*** for better, ***renew***, object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹּחַ‎</span> [[Isa.40.31|Isa 40:31]]; [[Isa.41.1|Isa 41:1]].
