 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּ֫לֶת‎</span>] **substantive** (from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בָּלָה‎</span>, of the form <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֶּלֶת‎</span> [[BDB#Ol|Ol]]<sup>§ 146 b</sup>) properly ***failure***, hence used as **particle of negation**, ==not, except== (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּלִי‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶפֶס‎</span>), twice with suffix (see below), elsewhere always <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּלְתִּי‎</span> (with binding vowel <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִ֯‎</span>, as mark of construct state: [[BDB#Sta|Sta]]<sup>§ 343</sup> [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:90»§ 90</sup>, 3), (Phoenician <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בלת‎</span> ***only:*** Tabnith-Inscription<sup>5</sup>) — 

**1. adverb** not, with an adjective [[1Sam.20.26|1Sam 20:26]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּלְתִּי טָהוֺר‎</span> ***not*** clean, with a substantive [[Isa.14.6|Isa 14:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַכַּת בִּלְתִּי סָרָה‎</span> a stroke of ***non***-cessation, i.e. a never-ceasing stroke, with a finite verb (si vera lectio) [[Ezek.13.3|Ezek 13:3]] ([[BDB#RV|RVm]]: but see [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>§ 41 Obs</sup>.). 

**2.** after a preceding negation, ***not*** = except (synonym <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זוּלָתִי‎</span>), [[Gen.21.26|Gen 21:26]] I have not heard <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּלְתִּי הַיּוֺם‎</span> ***except*** to-day, [[Exod.22.19|Exod 22:19]] he that sacrificeth <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּלְתִּי לי‎</span> ***except*** unto <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>, [[Num.32.12|Num 32:12]]; [[Josh.11.19|Josh 11:19]]: so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּלְתִּי אִם‎</span> [[Gen.47.18|Gen 47:18]]; [[Judg.7.14|Judg 7:14]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּי אִם‎</span> [[Gen.28.17|Gen 28:17]]; [[Neh.2.2|Neh 2:2]]). With suffix (attached to the ground-form <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּלֶת‎</span>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּלְתִּי‎</span> ***except*** me [[Hos.13.4|Hos 13:4]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּלְתֶּ֑ךָ‎</span> ***expect*** thee [[1Sam.2.2|1Sam 2:2]]. 

**3. conjunction** (likewise after a negative, expressed or implied) [[Gen.43.3|Gen 43:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּלְתִּי אֲחִיכֶם אִתְּכֶם‎</span> ***except*** your brother (be) with you, v[[Gen.43.5|Gen 43:5]]; [[Num.11.6|Num 11:6]] our soul is dry, there is nothing at all; ***save that*** our eyes are toward the manna, [[Isa.10.4|Isa 10:4]] (and where will ye leave your glory ?) ***save that*** they bow down under the prisoners, and fall under the slain! i.e. (iron.) their only refuge will be among the corpses of a battle-field. So <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּלְתִּי אִם‎</span> [[Amos.3.3|Amos 3:3]]; [[Amos.3.4|Amos 3:4]]. — [[Dan.11.18|Dan 11:18]], where no negative precedes, it is difficult to extract a sense consistent with the general usage of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּלְתִּי‎</span>: [[BDB#Ges|Ges]] ***besides that*** his reproach he will return unto him, [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] ***only, nothing but***, [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] ***certainly***, Drechsler (on [[Isa.10.4|Isa 10:4]]) ***nay, even*** (compare [[BDB#RV|RV]]).


**4.** With prepositions 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְבִלְתִּי‎</span><sub>86</sub> ***so as not***..., ***in order not***... negation of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span> followed by infinitive), usually followed by infinitive construct, as [[Gen.4.15|Gen 4:15]] gave a sign to Cain <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְבִלְתִּי הַכּוֺתאֹֿתוֺ‎</span> ***in order*** that any finding him should ***not*** smite him, [[Gen.19.21|Gen 19:21]]; [[Gen.38.9|Gen 38:9]]; [[Exod.8.18|Exod 8:18]]; [[Exod.8.25|Exod 8:25]]; [[Exod.9.17|Exod 9:17]]; [[Lev.18.30|Lev 18:30]]; [[Lev.20.4|Lev 20:4]]; [[Lev.26.15|Lev 26:15]]; [[Deut.8.11|Deut 8:11]]; [[Deut.17.12|Deut 17:12]] the man that doeth presumptuously <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְבִלְתִּי שְׁמֹעַ‎</span> ***so as not*** to hearken etc. (compare [[Jer.16.12|Jer 16:12]]; [[Jer.17.23|Jer 17:23]]; [[Jer.18.10|Jer 18:10]]; [[Jer.42.13|Jer 42:13]]; [[Dan.9.11|Dan 9:11]]) v[[Dan.9.20|Dan 9:20]]; [[Judg.2.23|Judg 2:23]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְבִלְתִּי הוֺעִיל‎</span> ***in order not to*** profit (the result represented forcibly as the design; compare below <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמַעַן‎</span>) [[Isa.44.10|Isa 44:10]]; [[Jer.7.8|Jer 7:8]] after verbs of commanding [[Gen.3.11|Gen 3:11]] which I commanded thee <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְבִלְתִּי אֲכָלמִֿמֶּנּוּ‎</span> ***not to*** eat thereof, [[2Kgs.17.15|2Kgs 17:15]]; [[Jer.35.8|Jer 35:8]]f.; [[Jer.35.14|Jer 35:14]]; [[Ruth.2.9|Ruth 2:9]], swearing [[Deut.4.21|Deut 4:21]]; [[Josh.5.6|Josh 5:6]]; [[Judg.21.7|Judg 21:7]]; [[Ezek.20.15|Ezek 20:15]], agreeing [[2Kgs.12.9|2Kgs 12:9]], interceding [[Jer.36.25|Jer 36:25]]. Once <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְבִלְתִּי לְ‎</span> [[2Kgs.23.10|2Kgs 23:10]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְמַעַן לְ‎</span> [[Ezek.21.20|Ezek 21:20]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּעֲבוּר לְ‎</span> [[1Chr.19.3|1Chr 19:3]]). Twice as conjunction with the imperfect, [[Exod.20.20|Exod 20:20]]; [[2Sam.14.14|2Sam 14:14]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּעֲבוּר‎</span>, &amp; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> [[Deut.33.11|Deut 33:11]]). In [[Jer.23.14|Jer 23:14]]; [[Jer.27.18|Jer 27:18]] followed by perfect, which is inconsistent with the nature of a final conjunction: read either <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָשׁוּבוּ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָבאֹוּ‎</span>, or <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוּב‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בוֺא‎</span> (compare [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>§ 41 Obs</sup>.). On [[Ezek.13.3|Ezek 13:3]] see above 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִבִּלְתִּי‎</span> ***an account of not***... (negation of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> followed by infinitive): followed by infinitive [[Num.14.16|Num 14:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִבִּלְתִּי יְכֹלֶת י‎</span>; a verbal noun [[Ezek.16.28|Ezek 16:28]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִבִּלְתִּי שָׂבְעָתֵךְ‎</span>. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדבִּֿלְתִּי‎</span> ***until not***..., followed by a perf. ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]]; [[BDB#RS|RS]]<sup>JPh xvi. 72</sup>), or an infinitive ([[BDB#Ew|Ew]]<sup>§ 238 d</sup>), in the phrase (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָהֶם‎</span>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדבִּֿלְתִּי הִשְׁאִירלֿוֺ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שָׂרִיד‎</span> ***until*** one left him (them) ***not*** a remnant, [[Num.21.35|Num 21:35]]; [[Deut.3.3|Deut 3:3]]; [[Josh.8.22|Josh 8:22]]; [[Josh.10.33|Josh 10:33]]; [[Josh.11.8|Josh 11:8]]; [[2Kgs.10.11|2Kgs 10:11]]. — [[Job.14.12|Job 14:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדבִּֿלְתִּי שָׁמַיִם‎</span> ***till*** there be ***no*** heaven (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדבְּֿלִי‎</span> [[Ps.72.7|Ps 72:7]]).
