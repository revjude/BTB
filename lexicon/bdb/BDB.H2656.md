 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵ֫פֶץ‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Prov.3.15|Prov 3:15]]</sup> ==delight, pleasure==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Isa.54.12|Isa 54:12]] + 20 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶפְצְךָ‎</span> [[Isa.58.13|Isa 58:13]] etc. + 13 t. suffixes; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲפָצִים‎</span> [[Prov.8.11|Prov 8:11]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲפָצֶיךָ‎</span> [[Prov.3.15|Prov 3:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲפָצֶ֖ךָ‎</span> [[Isa.58.13|Isa 58:13]] — 

**1.** ***delight*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אבני ח‎</span> [[Isa.54.12|Isa 54:12]] ***delightful stones***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ ח‎</span> [[Mal.3.12|Mal 3:12]] ***delightsome land***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּבְּרֵי ח‎</span> [[Eccl.12.10|Eccl 12:10]] so perhaps also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּגְדֵי ח‎</span> [[Ezek.27.20|Ezek 27:20]] ***garments of delight***, i.e. of beauty and luxury ([[BDB#Gr|Gr]]; [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֹפֶשׁ‎</span> q. v.); with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּ‎</span> of persons [[Ps.16.3|Ps 16:3]]; [[Eccl.5.3|Eccl 5:3]]; [[Mal.1.10|Mal 1:10]] of things [[1Sam.15.22|1Sam 15:22]]; [[1Sam.18.25|1Sam 18:25]]; [[Ps.1.2|Ps 1:2]]; [[Job.21.21|Job 21:21]]; [[Eccl.12.1|Eccl 12:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּלִי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵין חֵפֶץ בּוֺ‎</span> ***vessel wherein is no pleasure*** [[Jer.22.28|Jer 22:28]]; [[Jer.48.38|Jer 48:38]]; [[Hos.8.8|Hos 8:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַחֵפֶץ לְשַׁדַּי כִּי‎</span> [[Job.22.3|Job 22:3]] ***is it a pleasure to Shadday that ?*** 

**2.** ***desire, longing*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דלים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מנע מח‎</span> [[Job.31.16|Job 31:16]] ***withhold the poor from*** (***their***) ***desire***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְחוֺז חֶפְצָם‎</span> ***the city of their desire*** [[Ps.107.30|Ps 107:30]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כל חפץ‎</span> ***all*** (***one's***) ***desire*** [[2Sam.23.5|2Sam 23:5]]; [[1Kgs.5.22|1Kgs 5:22]]; [[1Kgs.5.23|1Kgs 5:23]]; [[1Kgs.5.24|1Kgs 5:24]]; [[1Kgs.9.11|1Kgs 9:11]]; [[1Kgs.10.13|1Kgs 10:13]] = [[2Chr.9.12|2Chr 9:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כל חפצים‎</span> ***all things to be desired*** [[Prov.3.15|Prov 3:15]]; [[Prov.8.11|Prov 8:11]]. 

**3.** ***the good pleasure, will, purpose***, of Yahweh [[Isa.44.28|Isa 44:28]]; [[Isa.46.10|Isa 46:10]]; [[Isa.48.14|Isa 48:14]]. 

**4.** ***that in*** which one takes delight, his ***business*** (late), or ***matter*** (very late, compare in Mishna = ***thing***) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּיָדוֺ יִצְלַח‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵפֶץ י‎</span> ***the good pleasure*** (***cause, business***) ***of Yahweh will prosper in his hands*** [[Isa.53.10|Isa 53:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מצא ח‎</span> [[Isa.58.3|Isa 58:3]]; [[Isa.58.13|Isa 58:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲשׂוֺת חֲפָצֶךָ‎</span> ***doing thy affairs*** [[Isa.58.13|Isa 58:13]] (see [[BDB#De|De]] on the passage); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּחֵפֶץ כַּפֶּיהָ‎</span> ***in the business of her hands*** [[Prov.31.13|Prov 31:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֵתּ לְכָלחֵֿפֶץ‎</span> ***time for every matter, affair*** [[Eccl.3.1|Eccl 3:1]]; [[Eccl.3.17|Eccl 3:17]]; [[Eccl.8.6|Eccl 8:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַלֿ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּתְּמַהּ עַלהַֿחֵפֶץ‎</span> ***marvel not at the matter*** [[Eccl.5.7|Eccl 5:7]].
