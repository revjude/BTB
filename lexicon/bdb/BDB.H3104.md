 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יוֺבֵל‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֹבֵל‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Num.36.4|Num 36:4]]</sup> ==ram, ram's horn, cornet== (Phoenician <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יבל‎</span> ***ram***; compare [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[Lev.25.10|Lev 25:10]] [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>Pr 124</sup>) — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יוֺבֵל‎</span> [[Josh.6.5|Josh 6:5]] + 7 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֹבֵל‎</span> [[Exod.19.13|Exod 19:13]] + 13 t.; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יוֺבְלִים‎</span> [[Josh.6.4|Josh 6:4]] + 2 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֹבְלִים‎</span> [[Josh.6.13|Josh 6:13]] — 

**1.** ***ram***, only in combination: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּמְשֹׁךְ בְּקֶרֶן הַיּ‎</span> [[Josh.6.5|Josh 6:5]] ***the ram's horn***, as wind-instrument; so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוֺפְרוֺת(ה)י(ו)בלים‎</span> [[Josh.6.4|Josh 6:4]]; [[Josh.6.6|Josh 6:6]]; [[Josh.6.8|Josh 6:8]]; [[Josh.6.13|Josh 6:13]] ***rams' horns*** (see [[BDB#Benz|Benz]]<sup>Archäol. 276</sup>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֹבֵל‎</span> alone, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּמְשֹׁךְ הַיֹּבֵל‎</span> [[Exod.19.13|Exod 19:13]] ***at the sounding of the ram*** ('s horn). 

**2.** designation of 50th year, marked by blowing of cornets, [[BDB#AV|AV]] 'jubile' (so Late Hebrew <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יוֺבֵל‎</span>, [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יובֵילָא‎</span>, as loan-word); originally no doubt <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁנַת הַיּוֺבֵל‎</span> ***year of the ram*** ('s horn), as [[Lev.25.13|Lev 25:13]]; [[Lev.25.28|Lev 25:28]]; [[Lev.25.40|Lev 25:40]]; [[Lev.25.50|Lev 25:50]]; [[Lev.25.52|Lev 25:52]]; [[Lev.25.54|Lev 25:54]] (all [[BDB#H|H]]), [[Lev.27.17|Lev 27:17]]; [[Lev.27.18|Lev 27:18]]; [[Lev.27.23|Lev 27:23]]; [[Lev.27.24|Lev 27:24]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]), but then, without <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁנת‎</span>, as <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יוֺבֵל הִוא תִּהְיֶה לָכֶם‎</span> [[Lev.25.10|Lev 25:10]] ***a ram*** ('s horn blowing) ***shall it be to you***; so v[[Lev.25.11|Lev 25:11]] v[[Lev.25.12|Lev 25:12]] v[[Lev.25.15|Lev 25:15]] v[[Lev.25.28|Lev 25:28]] v[[Lev.25.30|Lev 25:30]] v[[Lev.25.31|Lev 25:31]] v[[Lev.25.33|Lev 25:33]] (all [[BDB#H|H]]), [[Lev.27.18|Lev 27:18]]; [[Num.36.4|Num 36:4]] (both [[BDB#P|P]]).
