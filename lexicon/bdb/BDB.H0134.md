**1.** ***pedestals*** of fine gold, on which pillars of marble were set [[Song.5.15|Song 5:15]]. 

**2.** ***pedestals*** of the earth on which its pillars were placed [[Job.38.6|Job 38:6]] (\|\| corner-stone).
