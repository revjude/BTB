**Qal** ***Perfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּן‎</span> [[Jer.22.16|Jer 22:16]] etc.; ***Imperfect*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָדִין‎</span> [[Gen.49.16|Gen 49:16]] + 9 t., <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָדוֺן‎</span> [[Gen.6.3|Gen 6:3]] compare below, etc.; ***Imperative*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּין‎</span> [[Prov.31.9|Prov 31:9]]; [[Job.35.14|Job 35:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּינוּ‎</span> [[Jer.21.12|Jer 21:12]] ***Infinitive*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָדִין‎</span> [[Ps.50.4|Ps 50:4]] + 2 t.; ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּן‎</span> [[Gen.15.14|Gen 15:14]]; [[Jer.30.13|Jer 30:13]] — 

**1.** ***act as judge, minister judgment***, of God <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ידין עמוֺ‎</span> [[Deut.32.36|Deut 32:36]] (poetry = [[Ps.135.14|Ps 135:14]]) [[Ps.50.4|Ps 50:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דין עמים‎</span> [[Ps.7.9|Ps 7:9]]; [[Ps.96.10|Ps 96:10]]; [[Isa.3.13|Isa 3:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָדִין‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְאֻמִּים בְּמֵישָׁרִים‎</span> [[Ps.9.9|Ps 9:9]] Messianic king [[Ps.72.2|Ps 72:2]]. 

**2.** ***plead the cause***, 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of men, usually with accusative of congnate meaning with verb, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵיןדָּֿן דִּינֵךְ‎</span> ***there is none to plead thy cause*** [[Jer.30.13|Jer 30:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּין יָתוֺם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ד‎</span> ***plead cause of orphan*** [[Jer.5.28|Jer 5:28]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דין עני ואביון‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ד‎</span> [[Jer.22.16|Jer 22:16]] also, accusative of congnate meaning with verb omitted, [[Prov.31.9|Prov 31:9]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of God, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּנַנִּי אֱלֹהִים‎</span> ***God has pled my cause*** [[Gen.30.6|Gen 30:6]] ([[BDB#E|E]]). 

**3.** ***execute judgment, vindicate***, in battle against enemies, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דָּן יָדִין עַמּוֺ‎</span> ***Dan will judge*** (vindicate as a warrior) ***his people*** (see [[BDB#Di|Di]]) [[Gen.49.16|Gen 49:16]] (poetry); the Messianic king <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בגוים‎</span> [[Ps.110.6|Ps 110:6]] God, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּגְבוּרָֽתְךָ תְדִינֵנִי‎</span> ***in thy might judge me*** (vindicate me) [[Ps.54.3|Ps 54:3]]. 

**4.** ***execute judgment, requite***, of man, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דִּינוּ לַבֹּקֶר מִשְׁפָּט‎</span> ***execute judgment every morning*** [[Jer.21.12|Jer 21:12]] of God, upon Egypt [[Gen.15.14|Gen 15:14]] ([[BDB#E|E]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עמים‎</span> by means of powers of nature [[Job.36.31|Job 36:31]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אפסי ארץ‎</span> [[1Sam.2.10|1Sam 2:10]] (poetry) 

**5.** ***govern***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַתָּה תָּדִין אֶתבֵּֿיתִי‎</span> ***thou shalt govern my house*** [[Zech.3.7|Zech 3:7]]. 

**6.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹאיֿוּכַל לָדִין עִם‎</span> ***he cannot contend with*** one mightier than he [[Eccl.6.10|Eccl 6:10]]. **Niph\`al** ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיְהִי כָלהָֿעָם נָדוֺן בְּכָלשִֿׁבְטֵי יִשְׂרָאֵל‎</span> ***and all the people were at strife throughout all the tribes of Israel*** [[2Sam.19.10|2Sam 19:10]] (reciprocal, compare [[BDB#Dr|Dr]].)
