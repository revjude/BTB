 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טֹּ֫הַר‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==purity, purifying== — 

**1.** ***purity***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּעֶצֶם הַשָּׁמַיִם לָטֹּ֫הַר‎</span> [[Exod.24.10|Exod 24:10]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]), ***as the body of heavens for purity.*** 

**2.** suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְמֵי טָהֳרָהּ‎</span> [[Lev.12.4|Lev 12:4]]; [[Lev.12.6|Lev 12:6]] ***the days of her purifying*** (menstruation). 



 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טֳהָר‎</span>] (see [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:10»§ 10</sup>. 2. B), or [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טְהָר‎</span>] ([[BDB#Ol|Ol]]<sup>§ 173 b</sup> [[BDB#Bö|Bö]]<sup>§ 386</sup>), **noun [masculine]** ==clearness, lustre== (dubious word; below <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טֹהַר‎</span> [[BDB#Hup|Hup]] [[BDB#Pe|Pe]] [[BDB#SS|SS]]; so [[BDB#Thes|Thes]], reading <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִטָּהֳרוֺ‎</span>) — only <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִשְׁבַּתָּ מִטֳּהָרוֺ‎</span> [[Ps.89.45|Ps 89:45]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Hup|Hup]] [[BDB#Pe|Pe]] [[BDB#Sch|Sch]] ***thou hast made*** (him) ***to cease from his lustre***; [[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] ***made his lustre to cease***, so [[BDB#AE|AE]] [[BDB#Ki|Ki]] and others reading noun <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִטְהָרוֺ‎</span> or <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִטְּהָרוֺ‎</span> (dagesh forte according to [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:20»§ 20</sup>. 2. 2 b), and [[BDB#Schr|Schr]]<sup>SK 1868, 642</sup> who reads <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִשְׁבַּתָּה טְהָרוֺ‎</span>; [[BDB#Gr|Gr]] proposes <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עטרתוֺ‎</span> ***his crown***; [[BDB#Bae|Bae]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַטֶּה מִיָּדוֺ‎</span> ***sceptre from his hand.***
