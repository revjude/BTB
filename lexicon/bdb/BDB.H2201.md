 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זְעָקָה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==cry, outcry==, absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> [[Jer.18.22|Jer 18:22]] + 5 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זַעֲקַת‎</span> [[Gen.18.20|Gen 18:20]] + 3 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זַעֲקָתִי‎</span> [[Job.16.18|Job 16:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זַעֲקָתָם‎</span> [[Isa.15.5|Isa 15:5]] + 3 t.; — 

**1.** ***outcry***, against, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זַעֲקַת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְדֹם וגו‎</span> [[Gen.18.20|Gen 18:20]] ([[BDB#J|J]]). 

**2.** ***cry of distress***, concerning something <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זַעֲקַתשֶֿׁבֶר‎</span> [[Isa.15.5|Isa 15:5]] (object of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְעֹעֵ֑רוּ‎</span>; compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּבְכִי‎</span> in prev. clause); absolute v[[Isa.15.8|Isa 15:8]] (subject of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִקִּיפָה‎</span>, \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְלָלָה‎</span>); [[Jer.18.22|Jer 18:22]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּשָּׁמַע ז‎</span>), [[Jer.20.16|Jer 20:16]]; [[Jer.48.4|Jer 48:4]]; [[Jer.50.46|Jer 50:46]]; [[Neh.5.6|Neh 5:6]]; [[Neh.9.9|Neh 9:9]] (all with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁמע‎</span>), [[Jer.48.34|Jer 48:34]] (jointed with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נתן קול‎</span>), [[Job.16.18|Job 16:18]]; [[Esth.9.31|Esth 9:31]] (lamentation), compare [[Esth.4.1|Esth 4:1]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּדוֺלָה וּמָרָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span>, accusative of congnate meaning with verb with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זעק‎</span>); specifically <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זעקתדָּֿ֑ל‎</span> [[Prov.21.13|Prov 21:13]] ***cry of poor***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קול ז‎</span> [[Isa.65.19|Isa 65:19]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קול בכי‎</span>), [[Jer.51.54|Jer 51:54]]; [[Ezek.27.28|Ezek 27:28]].
