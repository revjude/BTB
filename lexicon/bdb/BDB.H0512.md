 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶלְקוֺשִׁי‎</span> **adjective, of a people** with article Nahum the Elkoshite [[Nah.1.1|Nah 1:1]] (perhaps = ***from Elkosh***, but locality unknown; identified by [[BDB#Jer|Jer]]<sup>Comm.</sup> with a village ***Elcesi*** in Galilee; compare also Capernaum (= <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּפַר נַחוּם‎</span> ?); others (improbably) with an Elkosh on east bank of Tigris, near Mosul). **\*\***On etymology, and site of place, see Da<sup>Nahum, etc., 9 ff.</sup> GASm<sup>Twelve Proph. ii. 79f</sup>.
