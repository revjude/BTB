Conversely [[Joel.2.9|Joel 2:9]] ***in through*** the windows they come (the locusts) as a thief. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** idiomatic with verbs of ***shutting***, especially <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סָגַר בְּעַד‎</span> to shut ***behind*** or ***upon*** — whether oneself, [[Judg.9.51|Judg 9:51]] and they entered the tower <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּסְגְּרוּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּעֲדָם‎</span> and shut (the doors) ***upon*** themselves (that is, from the inside), [[2Kgs.4.4|2Kgs 4:4]];
