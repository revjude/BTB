 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זָכַךְ‎</span>] **verb** ==be bright, clean, pure== (kindred with foregoing. Only Lamentations, Job) — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** only 3 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎זַכּ֫וּ‎</span>; — 

**1.** ***be right, shining***, figurative of splendour of nobles <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נְזִירִים מִשֶּׁלֶג‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ז‎</span> [[Lam.4.7|Lam 4:7]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵחָלָב צַחוּ‎</span>). 

**2.** ***be clean, pure*** in God's sight, of heavens [[Job.15.15|Job 15:15]], of stars [[Job.25.5|Job 25:5]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַאֲהִיל‎</span> of moon; compare also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִזְכֶּה‎</span> [[Job.15.14|Job 15:14]]; [[Job.25.4|Job 25:4]]). **Hiph\`il** ***cleanse***, only ***Perfect*** 1 singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַהֲזִכּוֺתִי בְּבֹר כַּפָּֽי׃‎</span> [[Job.9.30|Job 9:30]], figurative of making morally spotless (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִתְרָחַצְתִּי‎</span>).
