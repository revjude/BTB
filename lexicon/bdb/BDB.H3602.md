 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּ֫כָה‎</span><sub>37</sub> **adverb** ==thus== (Late Hebrew abbreviated to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּךְ‎</span>, with prepositions <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֵּין כָּךְ‎</span> ***meanwhile***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְפִיכָךְ‎</span> ***therefore***; probably either from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָה‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּ‎</span>, literally ***as thus***, [[BDB#Ol|Ol]]<sup>§ 223 h</sup>; or from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּה כָה‎</span> [[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>ii. 1, 253</sup>), somewhat more emphatic than <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹּה‎</span>, usually prefixed to word which it qualifies: — [[Exod.12.11|Exod 12:11]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וככה תאכלו אתו‎</span> and ***thus*** shall ye eat it, [[Exod.29.35|Exod 29:35]]; [[Num.8.26|Num 8:26]]; [[Num.11.15|Num 11:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ואם ככה את עשׂה לי‎</span>, [[Num.15.11|Num 15:11]]; [[Num.15.12|Num 15:12]]; [[Num.15.13|Num 15:13]]; [[Deut.25.9|Deut 25:9]]; [[Deut.29.23|Deut 29:23]] (compare [[1Kgs.9.8|1Kgs 9:8]]; [[Jer.22.8|Jer 22:8]]); so often with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עשׂה‎</span>: [[1Sam.19.17|1Sam 19:17]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎למה ככה רמיתני‎</span> why hast thou ***thus*** deceived me ? [[2Sam.17.21|2Sam 17:21]]; [[1Kgs.1.6|1Kgs 1:6]]; [[1Kgs.1.48|1Kgs 1:48]]; [[Jer.13.9|Jer 13:9]]; [[Jer.19.11|Jer 19:11]]; [[Jer.28.11|Jer 28:11]]; [[Jer.51.64|Jer 51:64]] once with an adjective [[2Sam.13.4|2Sam 13:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מדוע אתה כָכָה דל‎</span> why art thou ***thus*** lean ? [[Eccl.11.5|Eccl 11:5]] answering to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כַּאֲשֶׁר‎</span>; [[Ps.144.15|Ps 144:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎העם שֶׁכָּכָה לוֺ‎</span> the people to which it is ***thus.*** Once [[2Chr.18.19|2Chr 18:19]] repeated, ***in this way . . . in that way*** (\|\| [[1Kgs.22.20|1Kgs 22:20]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּכֹהבְּ֗֗֗כֹה‎</span>). compare the synonym <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּזֹאת‎</span>. With a preposition once, in late Hebrew, [[Esth.9.26|Esth 9:26]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַלכָּֿ֫כָה‎</span> concerning such a matter. With the interrogative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אי‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵיכָכָה‎</span> ***how*** ? (p. 32).
