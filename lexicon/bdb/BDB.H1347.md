 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גָּאוֺן‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Hos.5.5|Hos 5:5]]</sup> ==exaltation== — [[Job.40.10|Job 40:10]] + 5 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּאוֺן‎</span> [[Lev.26.19|Lev 26:19]] + 31 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּאוֺנְךָ‎</span> etc. [[Exod.15.7|Exod 15:7]] + 9 t.; plural suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּאוֺנָ֑יִךְ‎</span> [[Ezek.16.56|Ezek 16:56]] — 

**1.** ***exaltation, majesty, excellence***, 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of nations, their wealth, power, magnificence of buildings, e.g. Egypt [[Ezek.32.12|Ezek 32:12]], Chaldeans [[Isa.13.11|Isa 13:11]]; [[Isa.13.19|Isa 13:19]]; [[Isa.14.11|Isa 14:11]], Philistines [[Zech.9.6|Zech 9:6]], Assyria [[Zech.10.11|Zech 10:11]], Jacob [[Ps.47.5|Ps 47:5]]; [[Amos.6.8|Amos 6:8]]; [[Amos.8.7|Amos 8:7]]; [[Nah.2.3|Nah 2:3]], Israel [[Hos.5.5|Hos 5:5]]; [[Hos.7.10|Hos 7:10]] (probably appellation of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>), [[Nah.2.3|Nah 2:3]], Judah [[Jer.13.9|Jer 13:9]], Jerusalem v[[Jer.13.9|Jer 13:9]]; [[Ezek.16.56|Ezek 16:56]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּאוֺן עֻזָּהּ‎</span> ***pride of her strength*** [[Ezek.30.6|Ezek 30:6]]; [[Ezek.30.18|Ezek 30:18]]; [[Ezek.33.28|Ezek 33:28]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גאון עזים‎</span> [[Ezek.7.24|Ezek 7:24]] (but [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#Co|Co]] read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עזם‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גאון עזכם‎</span> [[Lev.26.19|Lev 26:19]]; [[Ezek.24.21|Ezek 24:21]] the fruit of land of Judah will become <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לגאון ולתפארת‎</span> ***majestic and beautiful*** [[Isa.4.2|Isa 4:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גאון כל צבי‎</span> ***the majesty of all the splendour*** (of Tyre) [[Isa.23.9|Isa 23:9]] Zion is to become <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גאון עולם‎</span> ***an everlasting excellency*** [[Isa.60.15|Isa 60:15]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of God [[Exod.15.7|Exod 15:7]]; [[Isa.24.14|Isa 24:14]]; [[Mic.5.3|Mic 5:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲדַר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּאֹנוֺ‎</span> [[Isa.2.10|Isa 2:10]]; [[Isa.2.19|Isa 2:19]]; [[Isa.2.21|Isa 2:21]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַרְעֵם בְּקוֺל גְּאוֺנוֺ‎</span> [[Job.37.4|Job 37:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲדֵה נָא‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גָּאוֺן וָגֹבַהּ‎</span> [[Job.40.10|Job 40:10]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גאון הירדן‎</span> ***majesty of Jordan***, referring to the green and shady banks, clothed with willows, tamarisks, and cane, in which the lions made their covert [[Jer.49.19|Jer 49:19]]; [[Jer.50.44|Jer 50:44]]; [[Zech.11.3|Zech 11:3]], and therefore dangerous [[Jer.12.5|Jer 12:5]] ([[BDB#Ew|Ew]] thinks of the ***swelling*** of its agitated waters); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גְּאוֺן גַּלֶּיךָ‎</span> ***majesty of thy waves*** [[Job.38.11|Job 38:11]]. 

**2.** ***pride*** (bad sense) [[Job.35.12|Job 35:12]]; [[Ps.59.13|Ps 59:13]]; [[Prov.8.13|Prov 8:13]]; [[Prov.16.18|Prov 16:18]]; [[Ezek.7.20|Ezek 7:20]]; [[Ezek.16.49|Ezek 16:49]]; [[Zeph.2.10|Zeph 2:10]] of Moab [[Isa.16.6|Isa 16:6]] (twice in verse) = [[Jer.48.29|Jer 48:29]] (twice in verse).
