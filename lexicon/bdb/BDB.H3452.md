$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** with article, almost proper name [[Num.21.20|Num 21:20]] (commanded by Pisgah), [[Num.23.28|Num 23:28]] (by Pe\`or; both [[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]), frequented by David as an outlaw [[1Sam.23.19|1Sam 23:19]]; [[1Sam.23.24|1Sam 23:24]]; [[1Sam.26.1|1Sam 26:1]]; [[1Sam.26.3|1Sam 26:3]].
