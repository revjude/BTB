 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֵיקָם‎</span> **adverb** ==emptily, vainly==; — 

**1.** ***in empty condition, empty*** = with empty hands; with verb of sending [[Gen.31.42|Gen 31:42]] ([[BDB#E|E]]), [[Deut.15.13|Deut 15:13]]; [[1Sam.6.3|1Sam 6:3]]; [[Job.22.9|Job 22:9]] of going forth [[Exod.3.21|Exod 3:21]], returning [[Ruth.1.21|Ruth 1:21]]; [[Ruth.3.17|Ruth 3:17]] = without an offering, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא יֵרָאוּ פָנַי ד‎</span> [[Exod.23.15|Exod 23:15]] ([[BDB#E|E]]) = [[Exod.34.20|Exod 34:20]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), more fully [[Deut.16.16|Deut 16:16]] (compare [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָנֶה‎</span>] II. 

**2.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ראה‎</span> **Niph\`al 1 b**). 

**2.** ***in vain, without effect :*** with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוּב‎</span> [[2Sam.1.22|2Sam 1:22]] (poem), [[Isa.55.11|Isa 55:11]]; [[Jer.14.3|Jer 14:3]] (but see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֵיק‎</span> **1**), [[Jer.50.9|Jer 50:9]] probably also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוֺרֲרִי ר‎</span> [[Ps.7.5|Ps 7:5]] ***those who were my adversaries in vain*** (without success); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַבּוֺגְדִים ר‎</span> [[Ps.25.3|Ps 25:3]] that ***deal treacherously in vain*** &lt;in both most ***without cause***).
