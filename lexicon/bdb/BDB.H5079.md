 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִדָּה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==impurity== (as ***abhorrent, shunned)***; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נ‎</span> [[Ezek.18.6|Ezek 18:6]] +; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִדַּת‎</span> [[Lev.12.2|Lev 12:2]] +; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִדָּתָהּ‎</span> v[[Lev.12.5|Lev 12:5]] +; — ***impurity*** (especially [[BDB#P|P]] and Ezekiel): 

**1.** of ceremonial impurity, as union with brother's wife, [[Lev.20.21|Lev 20:21]] especially of menstruation [[Ezek.18.6|Ezek 18:6]] (read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִשָּׁה בְּנִדָּתָהּ‎</span> for [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִדָּה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> [[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]] [[BDB#Co|Co]]), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טְמֵאַת הַנּ‎</span> [[Ezek.22.10|Ezek 22:10]], compare [[Ezek.36.17|Ezek 36:17]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִדַּת דְּוֺתָהּ‎</span> [[Lev.12.2|Lev 12:2]], compare v[[Lev.12.5|Lev 12:5]]; [[Lev.15.19|Lev 15:19]]; [[Lev.15.20|Lev 15:20]]; [[Lev.15.24|Lev 15:24]]; [[Lev.15.25|Lev 15:25]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֶתנִֿדּ‎</span>), (3 t. in verse), v[[Lev.15.26|Lev 15:26]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִשְׁכַּב נִדּ‎</span>), v[[Lev.15.26|Lev 15:26]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טֻמְאַת נִדּ‎</span>), v[[Lev.15.33|Lev 15:33]]; [[Lev.18.19|Lev 18:19]] of defilement contracted by contact with a corpse, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵי (הַ)נִּדָּה‎</span> ***water of impurity*** (i.e. to remove it, compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]) [[Num.19.9|Num 19:9]]; [[Num.19.13|Num 19:13]]; [[Num.19.20|Num 19:20]]; [[Num.19.21|Num 19:21]] (twice in verse); [[Num.31.23|Num 31:23]]. 

**2.** figurative (with allusion to cerem. usage), ***impure thing***, [[Ezek.7.19|Ezek 7:19]]; [[Ezek.7.20|Ezek 7:20]] (of gold), [[Lam.1.17|Lam 1:17]] (of Jerusalem); of idolatry, immorality, etc., [[2Chr.29.5|2Chr 29:5]]; [[Ezra.9.11|Ezra 9:11]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֶרֶץ נִדָּה‎</span>), v[[Ezra.9.11|Ezra 9:11]], [[Zech.13.1|Zech 13:1]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְחַטַּאת וּלְנִדָּה‎</span>).
