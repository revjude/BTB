 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֶאָח‎</span> **interjection** (onomatopoetic) expressing joy, ==Aha!== always introduced by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָמַר‎</span>, [[Isa.44.16|Isa 44:16]] he says, ***Aha***, I am warm, I have seen the fire. Of satisfaction over the misfortune of an enemy or rival, [[Ps.35.21|Ps 35:21]]; [[Ps.35.25|Ps 35:25]] let them not say in their heart <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֶאָח נַפְשֵׁנוּ‎</span> ***Aha***, so would we have it ! (***lit*** our desire !) [[Ps.40.16|Ps 40:16]] (= [[Ps.70.4|Ps 70:4]]), [[Ezek.25.3|Ezek 25:3]]; [[Ezek.26.2|Ezek 26:2]]; [[Ezek.36.2|Ezek 36:2]]. Metaph., of the neighing of a war horse in the battle, [[Job.39.25|Job 39:25]].
