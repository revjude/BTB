 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָדֹם‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָדֵם‎</span>] **verb** ==be red== (on formation compare [[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>BN 83</sup> compare 120) — 

**Qal** ***Perfect*** 3 plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָֽדְמוּ‎</span> ***ruddy***, of Nazirites [[Lam.4.7|Lam 4:7]] **Pu\`al** ***Participle reddened, dyed red***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְאָדָּם‎</span> [[Nah.2.4|Nah 2:4]] (of shield), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְאָדָּמִים‎</span> of rams' skins [[Exod.25.5|Exod 25:5]]; [[Exod.26.14|Exod 26:14]]; [[Exod.35.7|Exod 35:7]]; [[Exod.35.23|Exod 35:23]]; [[Exod.36.19|Exod 36:19]]; [[Exod.39.34|Exod 39:34]] (all [[BDB#P|P]]). **Hithpa\`el** ***Imperfect redden, grow*** or ***look red***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִתְאַדָּ֑ם‎</span> [[Prov.23.31|Prov 23:31]] (of wine); **Hiph\`il** ***Imperfect emit (show) redness*** (compare [[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>BN 120</sup>) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַאְדִּימוּ כַתּוֺלָע‎</span> [[Isa.1.18|Isa 1:18]] (of sins) i.e. be glaring, flagrant (compare also v[[Isa.1.15|Isa 1:15]]).
