 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּנוּפָה‎</span><sub>30</sub> **noun feminine** ==a swinging, waving, wave-offering, offering==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ת‎</span> [[Isa.30.32|Isa 30:32]] +; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּנוּפַת‎</span> [[Isa.19.16|Isa 19:16]]; [[Exod.35.22|Exod 35:22]] plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּנוּפֹת‎</span> [[Num.18.11|Num 18:11]] — 

**1.** ***a swinging, brandishing***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַד י‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ת‎</span> [[Isa.19.16|Isa 19:16]] ***the brandishing of***<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s ***hand*** (in hostility); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִלְהֲמוֺת ת‎</span> [[Isa.30.32|Isa 30:32]] ***battles of brandishing*** (brandished weapons). 

**2. a.** ***waving, wave-offering***, technical term in [[BDB#P|P]] ([[BDB#H|H]]), originally of priest's share of sacrifice (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נוף‎</span> **Hiph\`il 4**), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְךָ נְתַתִּים‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִשׂ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְכָּלתְּֿנוּפֹת בְּנֵ‎</span> [[Num.18.11|Num 18:11]] often as 2nd accusative after <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵנִיף‎</span> [[Exod.29.25|Exod 29:25]] + 5 t.; even of Levites [[Num.8.11|Num 8:11]]; [[Num.8.13|Num 8:13]]; [[Num.8.15|Num 8:15]]; [[Num.8.20|Num 8:20]] (on all see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נוף‎</span> **Hiph\`il 4**); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲזֵה הַתְּ‎</span> [[Exod.29.27|Exod 29:27]] ***the wave-breast***, so [[Lev.7.34|Lev 7:34]]; [[Lev.10.14|Lev 10:14]]; [[Lev.10.15|Lev 10:15]]; [[Num.6.20|Num 6:20]] (all + <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוֺק הַתְּרוּמָה‎</span>) [[Num.18.18|Num 18:18]] (+ <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוֺק הַיָּמִין‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֹמֶר הַתְּ‎</span> [[Lev.23.15|Lev 23:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָחֶם הַתְּ‎</span> v[[Lev.23.17|Lev 23:17]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ת‎</span> alone v[[Lev.23.20|Lev 23:20]] (all [[BDB#H|H]]); less accurately [[Exod.29.24|Exod 29:24]]; [[Lev.8.27|Lev 8:27]]; [[Lev.14.12|Lev 14:12]]; [[Lev.14.21|Lev 14:21]]; [[Lev.14.24|Lev 14:24]] (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נוף‎</span><sup>l.c.</sup>). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***offering***, of gold and brass for tabernacle, [[Exod.35.22|Exod 35:22]]; [[Exod.38.24|Exod 38:24]]; [[Exod.38.29|Exod 38:29]]. — On <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ת‎</span> see [[BDB#Di|Di]]<sup>[[Lev.7.30|Lev 7:30]]</sup> [[BDB#Benz|Benz]]<sup>Archaeology 459 f.</sup> and especially [[BDB#Now|Now]]<sup>Archaeology ii. 239 f</sup>; also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּרוּמָה‎</span>.
