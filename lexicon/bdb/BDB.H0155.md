 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַדֶּ֫רֶת‎</span> **noun feminine** ==glory, cloak== — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> absolute [[Josh.7.24|Josh 7:24]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַדָּ֑רֶת‎</span> [[Ezek.17.8|Ezek 17:8]] construct [[Gen.25.25|Gen 25:25]] + 4 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַדַּרְתּוֺ‎</span> [[1Kgs.19.13|1Kgs 19:13]] + 3 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַדַּרתָּם‎</span> [[Zech.11.3|Zech 11:3]] — 

**1.** ***glory, magnificence***, of vine [[Ezek.17.8|Ezek 17:8]] (so [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] [[BDB#MV|MV]], but &lt; ***adjective f.*** from  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַדִּיר‎</span> q. v.), of shepherds [[Zech.11.3|Zech 11:3]] (or ***sub*** **2**). 

**2.** ***mantle, cloak*** (wide garment) of hair <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׂעָר‎</span> [[Gen.25.25|Gen 25:25]]; [[Zech.13.4|Zech 13:4]] (as prophetic mantle, so perhaps [[Zech.11.3|Zech 11:3]] of shepherds = false prophets) compare of Elijah [[1Kgs.19.13|1Kgs 19:13]]; [[1Kgs.19.19|1Kgs 19:19]]; [[2Kgs.2.8|2Kgs 2:8]]; [[2Kgs.2.13|2Kgs 2:13]]; [[2Kgs.2.14|2Kgs 2:14]] but <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַדֶּרֶת שִׁנְעָר טוֺבָה‎</span> (fine mantle of Shinar = Babylonian mantle — doubtless costly) [[Josh.7.21|Josh 7:21]]; [[Josh.7.24|Josh 7:24]] ([[BDB#J|J]]) &amp; (late) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎א‎</span> alone [[Jonah.3.6|Jonah 3:6]].
