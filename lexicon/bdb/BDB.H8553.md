**1.** place in hill-country of Judah [[Gen.38.12|Gen 38:12]]; [[Gen.38.13|Gen 38:13]]; [[Gen.38.14|Gen 38:14]]. probably = [[Josh.15.57|Josh 15:57]]. Site unknown (compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]<sup>[[Gen.38.12|Gen 38:12]]</sup> [[BDB#Rob|Rob]]<sup>BR ii. 17 n</sup>.). 

**2.** place on border of Judah [[Josh.15.10|Josh 15:10]]; [[Josh.19.43|Josh 19:43]] (assigned to Dan), [[Judg.14.1|Judg 14:1]] (twice in verse); [[Judg.14.2|Judg 14:2]]; [[Judg.14.5|Judg 14:5]] (twice in verse); [[2Chr.28.18|2Chr 28:18]]. Modern ***Tibneh***, approximately 15 miles south of west from Jerusalem compare [[BDB#Rob|Rob]]<sup>BR ii.17</sup> [[BDB#Buhl|Buhl]]<sup>Geogr. 196</sup>.
