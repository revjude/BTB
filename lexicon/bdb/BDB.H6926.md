 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִדְמָה‎</span>] **noun feminine** ==front, East==; — only construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִדְמַת‎</span> as preposition: 

**1.** ***in front of, over against*** (compare [[BDB#Nö|Nö]]<sup>ZMG xxxiii (1879), 532</sup>), [[Gen.2.14|Gen 2:14]] (i.e. from standpoint of writer; really, on geographic grounds, = west of; yet see [[BDB#Di|Di]]<sup>ed. 6</sup>), [[Gen.4.16|Gen 4:16]] (both [[BDB#J|J]]), 

**2.** ***on the east of***, [[Ezek.39.11|Ezek 39:11]]; [[1Sam.13.5|1Sam 13:5]] may be either.
