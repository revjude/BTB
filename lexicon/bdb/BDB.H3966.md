 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְאֹד‎</span> **noun masculine** ==muchness, force, abundance, exceedingly== (compare Assyrian <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***mu'du***</u></abbr>, ***abundance***, [[BDB#Hom|Hom]]<sup>ZMG 1878, 711</sup> ('treasures ***ana mu'di, in abundance'***) [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>HWB 399</sup>): — 

**1.** ***force, might***, [[Deut.6.5|Deut 6:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וּבְכָלמְֿאֹדֶ֑ךָ‎</span> and with all thy ***might***; hence [[2Kgs.23.25|2Kgs 23:25]]. 

**2.** in different idioms (298 t.) to express the idea of ***exceedingly, greatly, very*** (whether of magnitude or degree): 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** (so mostly) as adverb accusative, qualifying both adjectives and obs., [[Gen.1.31|Gen 1:31]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טוֺב מְאֹד‎</span> good ***exceedingly***, [[Gen.4.5|Gen 4:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּחַד לְקַיִן מְאֹד‎</span> and Cain was wroth ***exceedingly***, [[Gen.7.18|Gen 7:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְאֹד‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּרְבּוּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמַּיִם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּגְבְּרוּ‎</span>, [[Gen.12.14|Gen 12:14]]; [[Gen.13.2|Gen 13:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּמִּקְנֶה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְאֹד‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּבֵד‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְאַבְרָם‎</span>, v[[Gen.13.13|Gen 13:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מאד‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַטָּאִים לי‎</span>, [[Gen.18.20|Gen 18:20]]; [[Gen.19.3|Gen 19:3]]; [[Gen.19.9|Gen 19:9]]; [[Gen.21.11|Gen 21:11]]; [[Gen.24.16|Gen 24:16]]; [[Gen.24.35|Gen 24:35]], + often, [[Ps.46.2|Ps 46:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִמְצָא מְאֹד עֶזְרָה בְצָרוֺת‎</span> as a help in troubles, to be found (= present) ***exceedingly***; with an infinitive [[Isa.47.9|Isa 47:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּעָצְמַת חֲבָרַיִח מְאֹד‎</span> despite of thy spells being ***very*** numerous, [[Josh.9.13|Josh 9:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵרֹב הַדֶּרֶךְ מְאֹד‎</span>, [[1Kgs.7.47|1Kgs 7:47]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַרְבֵּה מְאֹד‎</span> [[Gen.15.1|Gen 15:1]]; [[Gen.41.49|Gen 41:49]]; [[Deut.3.5|Deut 3:5]] +, for which in the later language is found <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָרֹב מְאֹד‎</span> [[Zech.14.14|Zech 14:14]]; [[2Chr.4.18|2Chr 4:18]]; [[2Chr.9.9|2Chr 9:9]] (in [[1Kgs.10.10|1Kgs 10:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַרְבֵּה מְאֹד‎</span>); [[2Chr.24.24|2Chr 24:24]]; [[2Chr.30.13|2Chr 30:13]], and even <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְהַרְבֵּה מְאֹד‎</span> [[2Chr.11.12|2Chr 11:12]]; [[2Chr.16.8|2Chr 16:8]]. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְאֹד‎</span> is not usually separated from the word it qualifies by more than one or two words (as [[Gen.20.8|Gen 20:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎האנשׁים מאד וייראו‎</span>, [[Num.11.10|Num 11:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מאד‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ויחר אף י‎</span>); see, however, [[Deut.30.14|Deut 30:14]]; [[Judg.12.2|Judg 12:2]]; [[2Sam.12.5|2Sam 12:5]]; [[1Kgs.11.19|1Kgs 11:19]]; [[Ps.31.12|Ps 31:12]]. It precedes its verb, [[Ps.47.10|Ps 47:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְאֹד נַעֲלָה‎</span> (hence [[Ps.97.9|Ps 97:9]]), [[Ps.92.6|Ps 92:6]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדמְֿאֹד‎</span> ***up to abundance, to a great degree, exceedingly***, [[Gen.27.33|Gen 27:33]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲדָדָה גְּדֹלָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדמֿ‎</span>, v[[Gen.27.34|Gen 27:34]]; [[1Sam.11.15|1Sam 11:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וישׂמח ֗֗֗ עד מ‎</span>, [[1Sam.25.36|1Sam 25:36]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהוּא‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁכֹּר עַדמֿ‎</span>, [[2Sam.2.17|2Sam 2:17]]; [[1Kgs.1.4|1Kgs 1:4]]; [[Isa.64.8|Isa 64:8]] (compare [[Lam.5.22|Lam 5:22]]), v[[Lam.5.11|Lam 5:11]]; [[Ps.38.7|Ps 38:7]]; [[Ps.38.9|Ps 38:9]]; [[Ps.119.8|Ps 119:8]]; [[Ps.119.43|Ps 119:43]]; [[Ps.119.51|Ps 119:51]]; [[Ps.119.107|Ps 119:107]]; [[Dan.8.8|Dan 8:8]]; [[Dan.11.25|Dan 11:25]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדלִֿמְאֹד‎</span> (see <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַד לְ‎</span>) [[2Chr.16.14|2Chr 16:14]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** duplicated ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:113k»§ 113k</sup>), [[Gen.7.19|Gen 7:19]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהַמַּיִם גָָּֽבְרוּ מ‎</span>, [[Gen.30.43|Gen 30:43]]; [[Num.14.7|Num 14:7]]; [[1Kgs.7.47|1Kgs 7:47]]; [[2Kgs.10.4|2Kgs 10:4]]; [[Ezek.37.10|Ezek 37:10]].
