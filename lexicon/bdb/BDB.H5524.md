Rawlinson) proposes ***Zîr-bânit***, or ***Zarpanituv***, wife of ***Marduk*** (compare [[BDB#Hpt|Hpt]]<sup>AR, May 1886</sup> [[BDB#M-A|Muss-Arn]]<sup>JBL xi (1892), 167</sup>), rejecting ***Sakkut-binûtu*** = Marduk [[BDB#Dl|Dl]]<sup>Par 215</sup>; [[BDB#Jen|Jen]]<sup>ZA iv. 352</sup> makes <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּנוֺת‎</span> = <abbr title="transliteration"><u>***banîtu***</u></abbr>, epithet of Ištar; all doubtful.
