Internat. 1895, 23 ff.</sup> with ***Khirbet Sanbariye*** on the river <abbr title="transliteration"><u>„âƒbâni</u></abbr>, southwest of Hermon, compare [[BDB#Buhl|Buhl]]<sup>Geogr. 67, 238</sup>, but this apparently too far southwest; [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Σεβραμ</span>, [[BDB#A|A]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">Σεφραμ</span>. A city ***Šabara'in*** is named in Babylonian Chronicle<sup>i. 28</sup> ([[BDB#Schr|Schr]]<sup>KB ii. 276</sup>); but location not given.
