 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעַט‎</span><sub>101</sub> **substantive** ==a little, fewness, a few==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> absolute and construct, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעָ֑ט‎</span> [[Isa.10.7|Isa 10:7]] +, plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעַטִּים‎</span> [[Ps.109.8|Ps 109:8]]; [[Eccl.5.1|Eccl 5:1]] —

$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** as substantive with following genitive, [[Gen.18.4|Gen 18:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעַט מַיִם‎</span> ***a little of water***, [[Gen.24.17|Gen 24:17]]; [[Gen.24.43|Gen 24:43]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעַט אֹכֶל‎</span> [[Gen.43.2|Gen 43:2]]; [[Gen.44.25|Gen 44:25]]; [[1Sam.14.43|1Sam 14:43]]; [[1Sam.17.28|1Sam 17:28]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעַט הַצּאֹן הָהֵ֫נָּה‎</span> literally ***the fewness of*** those sheep, [[Prov.6.10|Prov 6:10]] +; as genitive to a previous substantive, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְתֵי מְעָ֑ט‎</span> men ***of fewness*** [[Deut.26.5|Deut 26:5]]; [[Deut.28.62|Deut 28:62]] in apposition, [[Isa.10.7|Isa 10:7]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְהַכְרִית גּוֺיִם לֹא מְעָ֑ט‎</span>, [[Ezra.9.8|Ezra 9:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִחְיָה מְעַט‎</span>, [[Neh.2.12|Neh 2:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַאֲנָשִׁים מְעַט עִמִּי‎</span>, [[Eccl.9.4|Eccl 9:4]] either genitive or appositive, [[Eccl.10.1|Eccl 10:1]]; [[Dan.11.34|Dan 11:34]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יֵעָֽזְרוּ עֵזֶר מְעָ֑ט‎</span> with a help ***of smallness.*** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** twice declined as an adjective , [[Ps.109.8|Ps 109:8]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִהְיוּ יָמָיו מְעַטִּים‎</span>, [[Eccl.5.1|Eccl 5:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עלכֿן יהיו דבריך‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעַטִּים‎</span>. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** as adverb. accusative, of place, [[2Sam.16.1|2Sam 16:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְדָוִד עֹבֵר מְעַט‎</span>; of time, [[Job.10.20|Job 10:20]] b; of degree, [[2Kgs.10.18|2Kgs 10:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַחְאָב עָבַד אֶתהַֿבַּעַל מְעָ֑ט‎</span>, [[Ezek.11.16|Ezek 11:16]] and have been to them for a sanctuary ***but little***, [[Zech.1.15|Zech 1:15]] repeated <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעַט מְעַט‎</span> ***by little, by little*** = gradually, [[Exod.23.30|Exod 23:30]]; [[Deut.7.22|Deut 7:22]].

**2.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּמְעַט‎</span> ***like a little:*** hence 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***within a little, almost*** (compare <span class="bdbLangGreek">ὀλίγου</span>), [[Gen.26.10|Gen 26:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּמְעַט שָׁכַב ֗֗֗ ‎</span> ***almost*** had lain ..., [[Ps.73.2|Ps 73:2]] [[BDB#Qr|Qr]], [[Ps.94.17|Ps 94:17]]; [[Ps.119.87|Ps 119:87]]; [[Prov.5.14|Prov 5:14]] (all followed by perfect); [[Ezek.16.47|Ezek 16:47]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּמְעַט קָט וַתַּשְׁחִתִי מֵהֶן‎</span> ***like a little***, and thou hadst done more corruptly than they; [[Song.3.4|Song 3:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּמְעַט שֶׁעָבַרְתִּי מֵהֶם‎</span> = ***hardly*** had I passed. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** with imperfect, ***just***, [[2Sam.19.37|2Sam 19:37]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כמעט יעבר עבדך‎</span> ***just*** would thy servant pass over; ***lightly, easily, quickly*** (compare <span class="bdbLangGreek">δἰ ὀλίγου</span>), [[Ps.2.12|Ps 2:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּי יִבְעַר כִּמְעַט אַפּוֺ‎</span>, [[Ps.81.15|Ps 81:15]]; [[Job.32.22|Job 32:22]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** = ***shortly***, [[2Chr.12.7|2Chr 12:7]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **d.** = ***little worth***, [[Prov.10.20|Prov 10:20]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כמעט ולב רשׁעים‎</span>. ***e.*** pleonastic for <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעַט‎</span>, [[Isa.1.9|Isa 1:9]] (accents) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּמְעָ֑ט שָׂרִיר‎</span> a ***little*** remnant, [[Ps.105.12|Ps 105:12]] (= [[1Chr.16.19|1Chr 16:19]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּמְעַט וְגָרִים בָּהּ‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּמְעַט רֶגַע‎</span> ***like the littleness of*** a moment = ***for a little*** moment, [[Isa.26.20|Isa 26:20]]; [[Ezra.9.8|Ezra 9:8]].
