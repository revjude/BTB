**1.** ==brier, bramble==. 

**2.** ==hook, ring, fetter==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> absolute [[Hos.9.6|Hos 9:6]] + 8 t; plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲוָחִים‎</span> [[1Sam.13.6|1Sam 13:6]] (but see below), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חוֺחִים‎</span> [[Song.2.2|Song 2:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֹחִים‎</span> [[2Chr.33.11|2Chr 33:11]] — 

**1. a.** ***brier, bramble*** [[2Kgs.14.9|2Kgs 14:9]] (twice in verse) = [[2Chr.25.18|2Chr 25:18]] (twice in verse) (allegory of Jehoash); collective, sign of desolation [[Hos.9.6|Hos 9:6]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִמּוֺשׁ‎</span>), [[Isa.34.13|Isa 34:13]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִמּוֺשׁ‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סִירִים‎</span>), [[Job.31.40|Job 31:40]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חִטִּים‎</span>); in simile of fool's parable <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָלָה בְּיַדשִֿׁכּוֺר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Prov.26.9|Prov 26:9]] ***a brier cometh into the hand of a drunken man*** ([[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Now|Now]] [[BDB#Str|Str]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּשׁוֺשַׁנָּה בֵּין הַחוֺחִים‎</span>; [[Song.2.2|Song 2:2]] ***briers*** = ***thickets*** as hiding-places [[1Sam.13.6|1Sam 13:6]] (but [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#We|We]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חוֺרִים‎</span> ***holes***, compare [[1Sam.14.11|1Sam 14:11]] see below III. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חרר‎</span>; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעָרוֺת‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎סְלָעִים‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צְרִחִים‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֹּרוֺת‎</span>; [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] here <span class="bdbLangGreek">ἐν τ. μάνδραις</span>; [[1Sam.14.11|1Sam 14:11]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">ἐκ τ. τρωγλῶν</span>. 

**2.** late, 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** ***hook*** or ***ring***, in jaw of crocodile with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּקֹּב‎</span> [[Job.40.26|Job 40:26]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲתָשִׂים אַגְמֹן בְּאַפּוֺ‎</span>); of captive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַיִּלְכְּדוּ אֶתמְֿנַשֶּׁה בַּחֹחִים‎</span> [[2Chr.33.11|2Chr 33:11]] (compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַח‎</span>).
