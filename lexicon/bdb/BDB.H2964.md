 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טֶ֫רֶף‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Nah.3.1|Nah 3:1]]</sup> ==prey, food; leaf==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טֶרֶף‎</span> [[Gen.49.9|Gen 49:9]] +; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טָ֑רֶף‎</span> [[Job.4.11|Job 4:11]] +; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַרְפֵךְ‎</span> [[Nah.2.14|Nah 2:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַרְפּוֺ‎</span> [[Isa.31.4|Isa 31:4]] plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַרְפֵּי‎</span> [[Ezek.17.9|Ezek 17:9]] — 

**1.** ***prey*** of lion [[Amos.3.4|Amos 3:4]]; [[Job.4.11|Job 4:11]]; [[Job.38.39|Job 38:39]]; [[Ps.104.21|Ps 104:21]] metaphor of Judah's conquests [[Gen.49.9|Gen 49:9]], Israel like lion [[Num.23.24|Num 23:24]] (both poems in [[BDB#J|J]] [[BDB#E|E]]); of Assyrians [[Isa.5.29|Isa 5:29]] of Nineveh and its king [[Nah.2.13|Nah 2:13]]; [[Nah.2.14|Nah 2:14]]; [[Nah.3.1|Nah 3:1]] Israel's princes (as young lion) [[Ezek.19.3|Ezek 19:3]]; [[Ezek.19.6|Ezek 19:6]] simile of false prophet (like lion) [[Ezek.22.25|Ezek 22:25]] of princes of Judah v[[Ezek.22.27|Ezek 22:27]] simile of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s descending to battle, like lion [[Isa.31.4|Isa 31:4]] compare [[Ps.76.5|Ps 76:5]] coming down from ***mountains of prey*** (the lion's lair), but read perhaps <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַד‎</span>, compare [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Bi|Bi]] [[BDB#Che|Che]]<sup>critical note</sup>.; figurative of spoil of wicked [[Job.29.17|Job 29:17]], compare [[Ps.124.6|Ps 124:6]]. 

**2.** ***food***, of outcasts, under figure of wild ass <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֶּרֶא‎</span> [[Job.24.5|Job 24:5]] of human food (late): for those who fear God [[Ps.111.5|Ps 111:5]] for household [[Prov.31.15|Prov 31:15]] in <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s house [[Mal.3.10|Mal 3:10]]. 

**3.** ***leaf***, (compare [[Gen.8.11|Gen 8:11]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַרְפֵּי צִמְחָהּ‎</span> [[Ezek.17.9|Ezek 17:9]] metaphor of Judah.
