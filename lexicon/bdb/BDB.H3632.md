 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּלִיל‎</span> **adjective** and **substantive** ==entire, whole, holocaust==, construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּלִיל‎</span>, feminine construction <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּלִילַת‎</span>: — 

**1. adjective** [[Ezek.16.14|Ezek 16:14]] (of Jerusalem) the report <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּיָפְיֵךְ כִּי כָּלִיל הוּא‎</span>, [[Ezek.28.12|Ezek 28:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּלִיל יָֹֽפִי׃‎</span> (of the king of Tyre) ***entire, perfect*** in beauty; [[Ezek.27.3|Ezek 27:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲנִי כְּלִילַת יָֹֽפִי׃‎</span> (of Tyre), [[Lam.2.15|Lam 2:15]] (of Jerusalem) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲזֹאת הָעִיר שֶׁיּאֹמְרוּ כְּלִילַת יֹ֔פִי‎</span>. 

**2. substantive a.** ***entirety, whole***, [[Exod.28.31|Exod 28:31]] thou shalt make the robe of the ephod <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּלִיל תְּכֵלֶת‎</span> a ***whole*** of purple (i.e. wholly purple); so [[Exod.39.22|Exod 39:22]]; [[Num.4.6|Num 4:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֶּגֶד כְּלִיל תְּכֵלֶת‎</span>; [[Judg.20.40|Judg 20:40]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎והנה עלה כְּלִילהָֿעִיר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎השׁמימה‎</span> the ***whole*** of the city (perhaps with allusion to meaning **b**); as adverb accusative, [[Isa.2.18|Isa 2:18]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהָאֱלִילִים כָּלִיל יַחֲלֹף‎</span> will pass away ***in entirety, wholly***. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** as a sacrificial term, ***entire-*** or ***whole***-offering, ***holocaust***, of a sacrifice consumed ***wholly*** on the altar (compare Phoenician <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כלל‎</span> [[BDB#CIS|CIS]]<sup>I. i, 165, 167</sup>; [[BDB#RS|RS]]<sup>Rel. Semitic 237</sup>), usually a descriptive synonym of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עוֺלָה‎</span>: [[Deut.33.10|Deut 33:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָשִׂימוּ קְטוֺרָה בְּאַפֶּ֔ךָ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְכָלִיל עַל מִזְכְֶּחן׃‎</span>, [[Ps.51.21|Ps 51:21]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אז תחפץ זבחי צדק עוֺלָה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְכָלִיל‎</span>, in app. [[1Sam.7.9|1Sam 7:9]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ויעלה עוֺלָה כָּלִיל ליהוה‎</span>; of the priests' <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִנְחָה‎</span>, [[Lev.6.15|Lev 6:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ליהוה כָּלִיל תָּקְטָֽר‎</span>, v.[[Lev.6.16|Lev 6:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְכָלמִֿנְחַת כֹּהֵן כָּלִיל תִּהְיֶה לֹא תֵאָכֵל‎</span>; fig, [[Deut.13.17|Deut 13:17]] (of idolatrous city) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ושׂרפת באשׁ אתהֿעיר ואתכֿל שׁללהּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אלהיך‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּלִיל לי‎</span> and thou shalt burn the city and all its spoil as ***a whole-offering to*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>.
