 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פְּנִ֫ימָה‎</span> **adverb** (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ה‎</span> locative) 

**1.** ==towards the (in-)side==. 

**2.** ==within== (literally ***faceward***, from point of view of one entering by opposite door); — of a building, usually temple: 

**1.** after verb of motion [[Lev.10.18|Lev 10:18]]; [[2Chr.29.18|2Chr 29:18]], and (after <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הִגִּיד‎</span>) [[2Kgs.7.11|2Kgs 7:11]], also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִפ‎</span> [[Ezek.41.3|Ezek 41:3]]; [[2Chr.29.16|2Chr 29:16]]. 

**2.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פ‎</span> [[1Kgs.6.18|1Kgs 6:18]], also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִפ‎</span> v[[1Kgs.6.30|1Kgs 6:30]], so read also v[[1Kgs.6.29|1Kgs 6:29]] (for [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִלִּפְנִים‎</span>, see [[BDB#Kmp|Kmp]]<sup>Kau</sup> [[BDB#Kit|Kit]] [[BDB#Benz|Benz]]), [[Ezek.40.16|Ezek 40:16]] (twice in verse), also [[Ps.45.14|Ps 45:14]] [[BDB#MT|ᵑ0]], i.e. within the house, but [[BDB#Krochm|Krochm]] [[BDB#Gr|Gr]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] and others (plausibly) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פְּנִינִים‎</span>, q. v.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִפּ‎</span> [[1Kgs.6.19|1Kgs 6:19]]; [[1Kgs.6.21|1Kgs 6:21]].= [[2Chr.3.4|2Chr 3:4]].
