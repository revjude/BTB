 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תָּם‎</span> **adjective** ==complete== — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ת‎</span> [[Gen.25.27|Gen 25:27]] + 10 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּמָּתִי‎</span> [[Song.5.2|Song 5:2]]; [[Song.6.9|Song 6:9]] (late, except [[Gen.25.27|Gen 25:27]]); — 

**1.** ***complete, perfect:*** in beauty [[Song.5.2|Song 5:2]]; [[Song.6.9|Song 6:9]] of physical strength [[Ps.73.4|Ps 73:4]] (read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לָ֑מוֺ תָּם‎</span> [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>Sam. xxxi</sup> [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Bae|Bae]] and others). 

**2.** ***sound, wholesome:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אישׁ תָּם‎</span>, of Jacob as tent-dweller, opposed to Esau, hunter, [[Gen.25.27|Gen 25:27]] ([[BDB#J|J]]; [[BDB#De|De]] ***leutseliger Mann***, [[BDB#Di|Di]] ***ruhig, still***, [[BDB#AV|AV]] [[BDB#RV|RV]] ***plain man***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ת‎</span> here possibly redactional, = **3**). 

**3.** ***complete, morally innocent, having integrity:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִישׁ תָּם‎</span> [[Job.1.8|Job 1:8]]; [[Job.2.3|Job 2:3]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ת‎</span> alone [[Job.1.1|Job 1:1]]; [[Job.8.20|Job 8:20]]; [[Job.9.20|Job 9:20]]; [[Job.9.21|Job 9:21]]; [[Job.9.22|Job 9:22]]; [[Prov.29.10|Prov 29:10]]; [[Ps.37.37|Ps 37:37]]; [[Ps.64.5|Ps 64:5]]. — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּמִּים‎</span> see [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תָּאם‎</span>]. p. 1060f
