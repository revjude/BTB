 I. [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צֹ֫הַר‎</span>] **[noun masculine]** only plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צָהֳרַ֫יִם‎</span> ==midday, noon==, [[Deut.28.29|Deut 28:29]]+, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צָהֳרָ֑יִם‎</span> [[Gen.43.16|Gen 43:16]] +(when sun ***mounts*** its highest; on form as expanded plural (not dual) see [[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:88c»§ 88c</sup> and references); — usually 

**1.** ***noon*** as a specific time of day, [[1Kgs.18.29|1Kgs 18:29]] especially <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּצּ‎</span> ***at noon*** [[Gen.43.16|Gen 43:16]]; [[Gen.43.25|Gen 43:25]]; [[Amos.8.9|Amos 8:9]]; [[1Kgs.18.27|1Kgs 18:27]]; [[1Kgs.20.16|1Kgs 20:16]]; [[Song.1.7|Song 1:7]] (resting-time for flock), [[Jer.6.4|Jer 6:4]] (opposed to evening); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּעֵת צ‎</span> [[Jer.20.16|Jer 20:16]] (distinguished from morning) as time of supposed security [[Jer.15.8|Jer 15:8]]; [[Zeph.2.4|Zeph 2:4]] also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵהַבֹּקֶר וְעַד הַצּ‎</span> [[1Kgs.18.26|1Kgs 18:26]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַדהַֿצּ‎</span> [[2Kgs.4.20|2Kgs 4:20]] without <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ב‎</span>, as adverb, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צ‎</span> as time of prayer [[Ps.55.18|Ps 55:18]] (+<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֹּקֶר‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֶרֶב‎</span>); as time of wasting <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קֶטֶב‎</span> [[Ps.91.6|Ps 91:6]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִשְׁכַּב הַצּ‎</span> [[2Sam.4.5|2Sam 4:5]] ***noonday repose.*** 

**2.** ***noon***, as bright, simile of happiness, blessing, [[Isa.58.10|Isa 58:10]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲפֵלָה‎</span>), [[Ps.37.6|Ps 37:6]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּאוֺר‎</span>); compare [[Job.11.17|Job 11:17]] ([[BDB#Ges|Ges]]<sup>«GKC:133e»§ 133e</sup>); see also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּתוֺךְ הַצּ‎</span> [[Isa.16.3|Isa 16:3]] (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צִלֵּךְ‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בַּצּ‎</span> [[Deut.28.29|Deut 28:29]]; [[Isa.59.10|Isa 59:10]]; [[Job.5.14|Job 5:14]]. 



 II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צֹ֫הַר‎</span> **noun feminine** probably ==roof== (compare Arabic, Assyrian, Tel Amarna ***back***; &gt; [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] and others ***light***, window); — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תַּעֲשֶׂה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לַתֵּבָה‎</span> [[Gen.6.16|Gen 6:16]].
