 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָעוֺן‎</span> **noun [masculine]** ==dwelling, habitation==; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Ps.71.3|Ps 71:3]] +, construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעוֺן‎</span> [[Jer.25.30|Jer 25:30]] +, suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעוֺנֶ֑ךָ‎</span> [[Ps.91.9|Ps 91:9]], etc.; — 

**1.** ***lair*** of jackals [[Jer.9.10|Jer 9:10]]; [[Jer.10.22|Jer 10:22]]; [[Jer.49.33|Jer 49:33]]; [[Jer.51.37|Jer 51:37]] (all of desolated cities); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲרָיוֺת‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Nah.2.12|Nah 2:12]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִרְעֶה‎</span>; figurative of Nineveh). 

**2.** ***dwelling*** of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>: 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** in heaven <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קָדְשְׁךָ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Deut.26.15|Deut 26:15]] compare [[Jer.25.30|Jer 25:30]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מָרוֺם‎</span>), [[Zech.2.17|Zech 2:17]]; [[Ps.68.6|Ps 68:6]]; [[2Chr.30.27|2Chr 30:27]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** in temple <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּיתֶ֑ךָ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[Ps.26.8|Ps 26:8]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעוֺנוֺ‎</span> [[2Chr.36.15|2Chr 36:15]] (only here alone). 

**3.** figurative of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> as abode of his people, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎צוּר מ‎</span> [[Ps.71.3|Ps 71:3]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> alone [[Ps.90.1|Ps 90:1]]; [[Ps.91.9|Ps 91:9]]. — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מ‎</span> [[1Sam.2.29|1Sam 2:29]]; [[1Sam.2.32|1Sam 2:32]] is unintelligible (see Commentaries), possibly read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעוֺיֵן‎</span> (***eyeing*** [enviously], see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עין‎</span> denominative) [[BDB#Klo|Klo]] [[BDB#HPS|HPS]], compare [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Bu|Bu]]. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְעוֺנָהּ‎</span> [[Zeph.3.7|Zeph 3:7]] is difficult in context, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#We|We]] [[BDB#Now|Now]] read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵעֵינֶיה‎</span> (compare [[Josh.9.23|Josh 9:23]]; [[2Sam.3.29|2Sam 3:29]]).
