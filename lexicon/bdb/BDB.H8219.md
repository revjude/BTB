 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁפֵלָה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==lowland==; — always absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁ‎</span> with article, except suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שְׁפֵלָתֹה‎</span> [[Josh.11.16|Josh 11:16]] — ***lowland:*** 

**1.** usually technical term of strip west of Judaean mountains (on exact limits see [[BDB#GASm|GASm]]<sup>Geogr. 201 ff.; Expos. Dec. 1896, 404 f.</sup> [[BDB#Buhl|Buhl]]<sup>Geogr. 104</sup> [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>Hast. DB iii. 892 f.</sup>): almost always distinguished from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָהָר‎</span> and <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַנֶּגֶב‎</span>, sometimes from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָעֲרָבָה‎</span>, in [[Deut.1.7|Deut 1:7]]; [[Josh.9.1|Josh 9:1]] ([[BDB#R|R]]<sup>D</sup>), also from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חוֺף הַיָּם‎</span>, compare [[Josh.10.40|Josh 10:40]]; [[Josh.12.8|Josh 12:8]] (both [[BDB#R|R]]<sup>D</sup>), [[Judg.1.9|Judg 1:9]] (perhaps [[BDB#R|R]]<sup>D</sup>, compare [[BDB#Bu|Bu]] [[BDB#GFM|GFM]]), [[Josh.15.33|Josh 15:33]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), [[Jer.17.26|Jer 17:26]]; [[Jer.32.44|Jer 32:44]]; [[Jer.33.13|Jer 33:13]]; [[Obad.1.19|Obad 1:19]]; [[Zech.7.7|Zech 7:7]]; [[2Chr.26.10|2Chr 26:10]] (distinguished from <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַמִּישׁוֺר‎</span>, table-land east of Jordan); region of sycomores [[1Kgs.10.27|1Kgs 10:27]] = [[2Chr.1.15|2Chr 1:15]]; [[2Chr.9.27|2Chr 9:27]]; [[1Chr.27.28|1Chr 27:28]].
