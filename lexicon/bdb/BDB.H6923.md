**1. a.** ***meet, confront*** (with hostility) with accusative of person [[2Sam.22.6|2Sam 22:6]] (twice in verse).= [[Ps.18.6|Ps 18:6]]; [[Ps.18.9|Ps 18:9]]; [[Job.30.27|Job 30:27]], with accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּנָיו‎</span> [[Ps.17.13|Ps 17:13]] of hostile approach to city (active) [[2Kgs.19.32|2Kgs 19:32]] = [[Isa.37.33|Isa 37:33]] (+ adverb accusative). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** ***come to meet*** one (accusative) as friend, with (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ב‎</span>) something [[Isa.21.14|Isa 21:14]]; [[Deut.23.5|Deut 23:5]]; [[Neh.13.2|Neh 13:2]], so, as worshippers (accusative of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>), [[Mic.6.6|Mic 6:6]] (twice in verse); with accusative of person + of thing [[Ps.21.4|Ps 21:4]], so [[Ps.59.11|Ps 59:11]] [[BDB#Kt|Kt]] [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] (accusative of person alone [[BDB#Qr|Qr]]), accusative of person alone [[Ps.79.8|Ps 79:8]] accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Ps.88.14|Ps 88:14]], accusative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּנָיו‎</span> (of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>) [[Ps.95.2|Ps 95:2]], compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶסֶד וֶאֱמֶת יְקַדְּמוּ פָנֶיךָ‎</span> [[Ps.89.15|Ps 89:15]] (namely, to join themselves to thee). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** ***meet, receive***, accusative of person, [[Job.3.12|Job 3:12]].
