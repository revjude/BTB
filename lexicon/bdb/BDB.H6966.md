**3.** ***arise*** (out of inaction), and build [[Ezra.5.2|Ezra 5:2]], devour [[Dan.7.5|Dan 7:5]]b. 

**4.** ***stand***, literal, <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎לָקֳבֵל‎</span> person [[Dan.2.31|Dan 2:31]], compare [[Dan.3.3|Dan 3:3]], <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎קֳדָם‎</span> person [[Dan.7.10|Dan 7:10]] participle as noun, ***those standing*** (there) v[[Dan.7.16|Dan 7:16]].

**1.** ***set up***, literal image [[Dan.3.1|Dan 3:1]]; [[Dan.3.2|Dan 3:2]]; [[Dan.3.3|Dan 3:3]] (twice in verse); [[Dan.3.5|Dan 3:5]]; [[Dan.3.7|Dan 3:7]]; [[Dan.3.12|Dan 3:12]]; [[Dan.3.14|Dan 3:14]]; [[Dan.3.18|Dan 3:18]] figurative kings [[Dan.2.21|Dan 2:21]], kingdom v[[Dan.2.44|Dan 2:44]]. 

**2.** ***lift up*** one side [[Dan.7.5|Dan 7:5]] a ([[BDB#Dr|Dr]]).
