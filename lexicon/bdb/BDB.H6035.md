 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָנָי‎</span> (?; [[BDB#Lag|Lag]]<sup>BN 48</sup>) **noun masculine** ==poor, afflicted, humble, meek==; — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָנָו‎</span> [[Num.12.3|Num 12:3]] ([[BDB#Kt|Kt]]; [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָנָיו‎</span> is to ensure the <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ָ֯‎</span> according to [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#Kö|Kö]]<sup>ii. 1. 76</sup>); elsewhere plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲנָוִים‎</span> [[Isa.29.19|Isa 29:19]] + 11 t. + [[BDB#Kt|Kt]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲנָוִים‎</span> [[Isa.32.7|Isa 32:7]]; [[Ps.9.19|Ps 9:19]] ([[BDB#Qr|Qr]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲנִיִּים‎</span>); [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲנָוִים‎</span> [[Ps.9.13|Ps 9:13]]; [[Ps.10.12|Ps 10:12]]; [[Prov.3.34|Prov 3:34]]; [[Prov.14.21|Prov 14:21]]; [[Prov.16.19|Prov 16:19]] ([[BDB#Kt|Kt]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עניים‎</span>); construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עַנְוֵי‎</span> [[Zeph.2.3|Zeph 2:3]] + 2 t., + [[BDB#Kt|Kt]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ענוי‎</span> ([[BDB#Qr|Qr]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲנִיֵּי‎</span>) [[Amos.8.4|Amos 8:4]] (so [[Job.24.4|Job 24:4]] [[BDB#van d.H.|van d. H.]]; but <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֲנִיֵּי‎</span> [[BDB#Kt|Kt]] and [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] [[BDB#Baer|Baer]] [[BDB#Gi|Ginsb]]) — these forms shew confusion with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָנִי‎</span>, which is perhaps only another form of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָנָו‎</span> (otherwise Rahlfs<sup>op. cit. 62 f.</sup> [[BDB#Dr|Dr]]<sup>'Poor' in</sup> Hastings<sup>DB</sup>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָנָו‎</span> = ***humble, meek***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָנִי‎</span> = passive ***humbled, afflicted***); — 

**1.** ***poor, needy***, [[Prov.14.21|Prov 14:21]] ([[BDB#Qr|Qr]]). 

**2.** ***poor and weak.*** oppressed by rich and powerful [[Amos.2.7|Amos 2:7]]; [[Isa.29.19|Isa 29:19]]; [[Isa.32.7|Isa 32:7]] ([[BDB#Kt|Kt]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ענוי (ה)ארץ‎</span> [[Ps.76.10|Ps 76:10]]; [[Isa.11.4|Isa 11:4]]; [[Zeph.2.3|Zeph 2:3]]; [[Amos.8.4|Amos 8:4]] ([[BDB#Kt|Kt]]), [[Job.24.4|Job 24:4]]. 

**3.** ***poor, weak and afflicted*** Israel (usually rendered ***meek***) [[Ps.10.17|Ps 10:17]]; [[Ps.22.27|Ps 22:27]]; [[Ps.25.9|Ps 25:9]] (twice in verse); [[Ps.34.3|Ps 34:3]]; [[Ps.37.11|Ps 37:11]]; [[Ps.69.33|Ps 69:33]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָנִי‎</span> **3**, also in all these Psalms), [[Ps.147.6|Ps 147:6]]; [[Ps.149.4|Ps 149:4]]; [[Isa.61.1|Isa 61:1]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נשׁברי לב‎</span>, compare [[Isa.66.2|Isa 66:2]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָנִי‎</span> **3**), [[Ps.9.19|Ps 9:19]] ([[BDB#Kt|Kt]]) v[[Ps.9.13|Ps 9:13]]; [[Ps.10.12|Ps 10:12]] ([[BDB#Qr|Qr]]). 

**4.** ***humble, lowly, meek*** [[Num.12.3|Num 12:3]] (Moses); [[Prov.3.34|Prov 3:34]]; [[Prov.16.19|Prov 16:19]] (both [[BDB#Qr|Qr]]).
