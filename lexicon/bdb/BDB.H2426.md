 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵל‎</span>, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵיל‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Obad.1.20|Obad 1:20]]</sup> ==rampart, fortress== (perhaps originally ***surrounding wall***, compare [[BDB#SabDenkm|SabDenkm]]<sup>91 n. 2</sup>) — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵיל‎</span> [[Nah.3.8|Nah 3:8]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵל‎</span> [[Lam.2.8|Lam 2:8]] + 3 t.;(+ [[Ps.10.10|Ps 10:10]] [[BDB#Qr|Qr]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חלכה‎</span>); construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵל‎</span> [[1Kgs.21.23|1Kgs 21:23]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵילֵךְ‎</span> [[Ps.122.7|Ps 122:7]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵילָהּ‎</span> [[Zech.9.4|Zech 9:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵילָה‎</span> = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵילָהּ‎</span> [[Ps.48.14|Ps 48:14]], [[BDB#Ol|Ol]]<sup>§ 40 approximately 75g. 96 e</sup> [[BDB#Sta|Sta]]<sup>§ 347 c</sup>; — 

**1.** ***rampart*** (defined by Jews as <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בֶּן‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חוֺמָה‎</span>, i.e. a little wall, compare [[BDB#Ki|Ki]] under the word), of an outer fortification [[2Sam.20.15|2Sam 20:15]] (others, by metonymy, of space between outer and inner fortification, including moat, see [[BDB#Dr|Dr]] [[BDB#Kit|Kit]]); [[Isa.26.1|Isa 26:1]]; [[Lam.2.8|Lam 2:8]] (both \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חוֺמוֺת‎</span>); in general of defences, or sea-power of Tyre <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְהִכָּה בַיָּם חֵילָהּ‎</span> [[Zech.9.4|Zech 9:4]] (see [[BDB#Sta|Sta]]<sup>ZAW i.1881, 15</sup>), of No-Amon (Thebes) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲשֶׁר חֵיל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָם מִיָּם חוֺמָתָהּ‎</span> [[Nah.3.8|Nah 3:8]] of Zion [[Ps.122.7|Ps 122:7]], also [[Ps.48.14|Ps 48:14]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חילה‎</span> = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵילָהּ‎</span>, compare above; both \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ארמנותיך‎</span>). — For <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵל‎</span> [[1Kgs.21.23|1Kgs 21:23]] read probably  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵלֶק‎</span>, q. v. 

**2.** ***fortress*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גָּלֻת הַחֵל הַזֶּה‎</span> [[Obad.1.20|Obad 1:20]] ***the exiles of this fortress*** ([[BDB#Hi|Hi]]-[[BDB#St|St]]; Or, [[BDB#AV|AV]] [[BDB#RV|RV]] ***this army***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֵל‎</span> = <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חַיִל‎</span>).
