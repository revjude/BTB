 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּמְנָע‎</span> **proper name, masculine and feminine** ==Edomite name.== [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">θαμνα</span>: — 

**1.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּמְנָע‎</span> **proper name, masculine** 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** a chief of Edom [[Gen.36.40|Gen 36:40]] = [[1Chr.1.51|1Chr 1:51]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** son of Eliphaz [[1Chr.1.36|1Chr 1:36]] (for Korah [[Gen.36.15|Gen 36:15]]; [[Gen.36.16|Gen 36:16]]).
