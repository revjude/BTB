**2.** ***do good to, deal well*** with, a person usually with preposition: with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְ‎</span>, [[Exod.1.20|Exod 1:20]]; [[Josh.24.20|Josh 24:20]] (both [[BDB#E|E]]), [[Gen.12.16|Gen 12:16]]; [[Num.10.29|Num 10:29]]; [[Num.10.32|Num 10:32]] (all [[BDB#J|J]]), [[Judg.17.13|Judg 17:13]]; [[1Sam.25.31|1Sam 25:31]]; [[Ps.49.19|Ps 49:19]]; [[Ps.125.4|Ps 125:4]] with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עִם‎</span>, [[Gen.32.10|Gen 32:10]]; [[Gen.32.13|Gen 32:13]] (twice in verse); [[Num.10.32|Num 10:32]] (all [[BDB#J|J]]), [[Mic.2.7|Mic 2:7]] with accusative [[Deut.8.16|Deut 8:16]]; [[Deut.28.63|Deut 28:63]]; [[Deut.30.5|Deut 30:5]]; [[1Sam.2.32|1Sam 2:32]]; [[Jer.18.10|Jer 18:10]]; [[Jer.32.40|Jer 32:40]]; [[Jer.32.41|Jer 32:41]]; [[Zech.8.15|Zech 8:15]]; [[Job.24.21|Job 24:21]]; [[Ps.51.20|Ps 51:20]] absolute [[Ezek.36.11|Ezek 36:11]] opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵרַע‎</span> (in prov. phrase, cannot ***do good or ill***, = cannot do anything at all; test of deity) [[Zeph.1.12|Zeph 1:12]]; [[Isa.41.23|Isa 41:23]]; [[Jer.10.5|Jer 10:5]].

**4.** ***make a thing good, right, beautiful:*** with accusative of thing: the head [[2Kgs.9.30|2Kgs 9:30]] ***dress, trim:*** a lamp [[Exod.30.7|Exod 30:7]] ([[BDB#P|P]]); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַצֵּבוֺת‎</span> [[Hos.10.1|Hos 10:1]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גֵּהָה‎</span> [[Prov.17.22|Prov 17:22]] a glad heart ***maketh a good cure***; followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִן‎</span> comparative, object <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שֵׁם‎</span> [[1Kgs.1.47|1Kgs 1:47]] ( = ***make it more glorious then***); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֶסֶד‎</span> [[Ruth.3.10|Ruth 3:10]] followed by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דֶּרֶךְ‎</span> = ***course of life or action*** [[Jer.2.33|Jer 2:33]] (in order to win love); ***amend*** one's ways and doings [[Jer.7.3|Jer 7:3]]; [[Jer.18.11|Jer 18:11]]; [[Jer.26.13|Jer 26:13]]; [[Jer.35.15|Jer 35:15]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֵיטֵיב תֵּיטִיבוּ אתדֿ‎</span> [[Jer.7.5|Jer 7:5]].

$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** of men, [[Gen.4.7|Gen 4:7]] (twice in verse) ([[BDB#J|J]]), [[Lev.5.4|Lev 5:4]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), [[Ps.36.4|Ps 36:4]]; [[Isa.1.17|Isa 1:17]]; [[Jer.4.22|Jer 4:22]]; [[Jer.13.23|Jer 13:23]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** of God, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טוֺב מֵטִיב‎</span> ***good and doing good*** [[Ps.119.68|Ps 119:68]] so infinitive absolute as adverb <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַהֵיטֵב חָרָה לך‎</span> [[Jonah.4.4|Jonah 4:4]]; [[Jonah.4.9|Jonah 4:9]] ***art thou rightly angry ? followed by*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לי‎</span> v[[Jonah.4.9|Jonah 4:9]].
