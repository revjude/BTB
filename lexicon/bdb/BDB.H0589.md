Appended to a verb, it expresses, in early Hebrew, a real emphasis, as [[Judg.8.23|Judg 8:23]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא אֶמְשֹׁל אֲנִי בָּכֶם‎</span> ***I*** will not rule over you, [[2Sam.12.28|2Sam 12:28]] lest ***I*** take the city, [[2Sam.17.15|2Sam 17:15]] thus and thus did Ahitophel counsel, and thus and thus <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יָעַצְתִּי אָ֫נִי‎</span> did ***I*** counsel; but in later Hebrew It is sometimes pelonastc, [[Eccl.2.11|Eccl 2:11]]; [[Eccl.2.15|Eccl 2:15]]; [[Eccl.2.18|Eccl 2:18]]; [[Eccl.2.20|Eccl 2:20]] +. In response to a question, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אָ֫נִי‎</span> alone = ***I am, It is I***, [[Gen.27.24|Gen 27:24]]; [[Judg.13.11|Judg 13:11]]; [[1Kgs.18.8|1Kgs 18:8]] +.
