 I. [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּמַר‎</span>] **verb Niph\`al** ==grow warm and tender, be or grow hot== (Late Hebrew ***id.*** Pi\`el ***heat*** fruit in the ground, making it ripe, over-ripe, tender; Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎כְּמַר‎</span> ***id.***, and more generally ***make warm*** (one's flesh, or food); compare ***kemr, fermentation***, etc., in modern Syria [[BDB#Wetzst|Wetzst]]<sup>ZPV xiv (1891), 6</sup>) — 

**Niph\`al** ***Perfect*** 3 plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִכְמְרוּ‎</span> [[Gen.43.30|Gen 43:30]] + 2 t., <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִכְמָ֑רוּ‎</span> [[Lam.5.10|Lam 5:10]] — 

**1.** ***grow warm*** and ***tender***, figurative, subject <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רחמים‎</span>; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַחֲמָיו אֶלאָֿחִיונ‎</span> [[Gen.43.30|Gen 43:30]] ([[BDB#J|J]]), so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַחֲמֶיהָ עַלבְּֿנָהּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נ‎</span> [[1Kgs.3.26|1Kgs 3:26]] (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רַחֲמָ֑י‎</span> [[BDB#We|We]]) <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נִחוּמָ֑י‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַחַד נ‎</span> [[Hos.11.8|Hos 11:8]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עָלַי לִבִּינֶהְפַּךְ‎</span>). 

**2.** ***be*** or ***grow hot***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עוֺרֵנוּ כְתַנּוּר נ‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִפְּנֵי וגו‎</span> [[Lam.5.10|Lam 5:10]] ***our skin has become hot like a furnace***, because of the famine.
