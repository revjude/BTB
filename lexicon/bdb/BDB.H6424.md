 [<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פָּלַס‎</span>] **verb Pi\`el denominative** ==weigh, make level== (Phoenician <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פלס‎</span> Pi\`el ***make level (?)*** in proper names); — ***Imperfect*** 3 masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יְפַלֵּס‎</span> [[Ps.78.20|Ps 78:20]] 2 masculine plural <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תְּפַלֵּס֑וּן‎</span> [[Ps.58.3|Ps 58:3]] (see below), etc.; ***Imperative*** masculine singular <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פַּלֵּס‎</span> [[Prov.4.26|Prov 4:26]] ***Participle*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְפַלֵּס‎</span> [[Prov.5.11|Prov 5:11]] — 

**1.** ***weign out*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲמַס יְדֵיכֶם תּפי‎</span> [[Ps.58.3|Ps 58:3]] (figurative; but read perhaps <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תָמָס יְדֵיכֶם תְּפַלֵּסְנָה‎</span> ***violence do your hands weigh out***, so [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Syr|ᵑ6]] [[BDB#Tg|ᵑ7]] [[BDB#Bae|Bae]] [[BDB#Kau|Kau]] [[BDB#We|We]]). 

**2.** ***make level***, smooth (compare [[BDB#Gerber|Gerber]]<sup>32</sup>), with accusative of path (figurative), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מַעְגָּל‎</span> [[Prov.4.26|Prov 4:26]]; [[Prov.5.21|Prov 5:21]]; [[Isa.26.7|Isa 26:7]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נָתִּיב‎</span> [[Ps.78.50|Ps 78:50]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֹרַח‎</span> [[Prov.5.6|Prov 5:6]].
