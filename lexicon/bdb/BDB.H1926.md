 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָדָר‎</span> **noun masculine** [[Ps.149.9|Ps 149:9]] ==ornament, splendour, honour== (chiefly in poetry) — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ה‎</span> [[Deut.33.17|Deut 33:17]] + 12 t.; construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲדַר‎</span> [[Isa.2.10|Isa 2:10]] + 7 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲדָרִי‎</span> [[Mic.2.9|Mic 2:9]] (but compare below) [[Ezek.16.14|Ezek 16:14]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲדָֽרְךָ‎</span> [[Ps.45.5|Ps 45:5]]; [[Ps.90.16|Ps 90:16]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲדָרֶ֑ךָ‎</span> [[Ps.45.4|Ps 45:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲדָרֵךְ‎</span> [[Ezek.27.10|Ezek 27:10]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲדָרָהּ‎</span> [[Isa.5.14|Isa 5:14]]; [[Lam.1.6|Lam 1:6]] plural construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַדְרֵי‎</span> [[Ps.110.3|Ps 110:3]] (but read probably <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הדדי‎</span> so [[BDB#Symm|Symm]] [[BDB#Jer|Jerome]] editions; O1 [[BDB#Hup|Hup]] [[BDB#Gr|Gr]] [[BDB#Bi|Bi]] [[BDB#Che|Che]]); — 

**1.** ***ornament*** [[Prov.20.29|Prov 20:29]] (grey hair, for old men; \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תִּפְאֶרֶת‎</span>), [[Ezek.16.14|Ezek 16:14]] (figurative of ornaments of Jerusalem as bride of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פְּרִי עֵץ הָדָר‎</span> [[Lev.23.40|Lev 23:40]] ([[BDB#H|H]]) i.e. ***fruit of goodly*** (ornamental, beautiful) ***trees***, so [[BDB#AV|AV]] [[BDB#RV|RV]] &amp; most, or ***goodly tree-fruit***, [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] <span class="bdbLangGreek">καρπόν ξύλου</span> <span class="bdbLangGreek">ὡραῖον</span>, so [[BDB#Di|Di]]; — here would come also <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בהדרי קדשׁ‎</span> [[Ps.110.3|Ps 110:3]], reference to sacred, festal garments, according to [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] and others compare [[BDB#RV|RVm]]; but read probably ***on the holy mountains***, see above 

**2.** ***splendour, majesty*** [[Deut.33.17|Deut 33:17]] of Ephraim under figure of a noble bull (compare [[BDB#Di|Di]]); of Jerusalem [[Isa.5.14|Isa 5:14]] compare [[Lam.1.6|Lam 1:6]] of Carmel [[Isa.35.2|Isa 35:2]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּבוֺד‎</span>); of ***majesty &amp; dignity*** conferred by <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> on man [[Ps.8.6|Ps 8:6]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כָּבוֺד‎</span>), on king [[Ps.21.6|Ps 21:6]] (\|\| ***id.***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺד‎</span>), compare [[Ps.45.4|Ps 45:4]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺד‎</span>) &amp; v[[Ps.45.5|Ps 45:5]] (but text perhaps erroneous [[BDB#Che|Che]]<sup>critical note</sup>.); denied of suffering servant of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[Isa.53.2|Isa 53:2]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תֹּאַר‎</span>); of ***dignity*** of good &amp; capable woman (<span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֵשֶׁת חַיִל‎</span>) [[Prov.31.25|Prov 31:25]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎עֹז‎</span>); of ***splendour*** due to warlike equipment [[Ezek.27.10|Ezek 27:10]] especially of ***majesty*** of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span> [[1Chr.16.27|1Chr 16:27]] = [[Ps.96.6|Ps 96:6]], compare [[Job.40.10|Job 40:10]]; [[Ps.111.3|Ps 111:3]] (all \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הוֺד‎</span>); also [[Ps.104.1|Ps 104:1]] (\|\| ***id.***, under figure of garment); further [[Ps.29.4|Ps 29:4]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כֹּחַ‎</span>), [[Ps.90.16|Ps 90:16]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎פֹּעַל‎</span>); in combination <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲדַר כְּבוֺד הוֺדְךָ‎</span> [[Ps.145.5|Ps 145:5]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּבוֺד ה‎</span> v[[Ps.145.12|Ps 145:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֲדַר גְּאֹנוֺ‎</span> [[Isa.2.10|Isa 2:10]]; [[Isa.2.19|Isa 2:19]]; [[Isa.2.21|Isa 2:21]]. 

**3.** ***honour, glory*** [[Ps.149.9|Ps 149:9]] for saints of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>; compare [[Mic.2.9|Mic 2:9]] of <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>'s glory as possession of his people, lost by exile &amp; slavery: but perhaps text error; [[BDB#Hoffm|Hoffm]]<sup>ZAW 1882, 103</sup> proposes <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הַדְּרוֺר‎</span>, from their children ye take ***freedom*** (yet <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֵעַל‎</span> then hardly suitable).
