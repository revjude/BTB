 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָזוּת‎</span> **noun feminine** ==vision, conspicuousness== (on formation compare Lg<sup>BN 202</sup>) — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Isa.21.2|Isa 21:2]]; [[Dan.8.5|Dan 8:5]]; [[Dan.8.8|Dan 8:8]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָזוּת‎</span> [[Isa.29.11|Isa 29:11]] suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָזוּתְכֶם‎</span> [[Isa.28.18|Isa 28:18]] — 

**1.** ***vision, oracle of a prophet*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָזוּת קָשָׁה הֻגַּדלִֿי‎</span> [[Isa.21.2|Isa 21:2]] ***a hard vision is declared to me***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וַתְּהִי לָכֶם חָזוּת הַּכֹּל‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְּדִבְרֵי הַסֵּפֶר הֶחָתוּם‎</span> [[Isa.29.11|Isa 29:11]] ***the vision*** (prophecy) ***of the whole is become to you as the words of a sealed writing***; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָזוּתְכֶם אֶתשְֿׁאוֺל לאֹ תָקוּם‎</span> [[Isa.28.18|Isa 28:18]] ***your agreement with Sheol will not stand***, so [[BDB#Thes|Thes]] [[BDB#MV|MV]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] and others after [[BDB#Vrss|Vrss]], but [[BDB#Ew|Ew]]<sup>§ 156 e</sup> renders ***oracle***, and [[BDB#Siegf|Siegf]]. ***Hellseherei***, see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֹזֶה‎</span> 

**2. 2.** ***conspicuousness in appearance:*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קֶרֶן הָזוּת‎</span> ***a conspicuous horn*** [[Dan.8.5|Dan 8:5]] = the great horn v[[Dan.8.8|Dan 8:8]] v[[Dan.8.21|Dan 8:21]] so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חָזוּת אַרְבַּע‎</span> [[Dan.8.8|Dan 8:8]], but &lt; read <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אֲחֵרוֺת‎</span> ***others***, as [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Gr|Gr]] [[BDB#Bev|Bev]].
