 II. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּבִיר‎</span> 

**1. proper name, masculine** ==king of Eglon== [[Josh.10.3|Josh 10:3]]. 

**2. proper name, of a location a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּבִ֫רָה‎</span> with <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ָ  ה‎</span> locative, northern border Judah [[Josh.15.7|Josh 15:7]] (***westward*** [[BDB#Hup|Hup]] [[Ps.28.2|Ps 28:2]]). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִדְבִר‎</span> town of the Gadites [[Josh.13.26|Josh 13:26]], perhaps ***Lôdebâr*** [[2Sam.9.4|2Sam 9:4]] see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֹא דְבָר‎</span> [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#MV|MV]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּבִיר‎</span> [[Judg.1.11|Judg 1:11]] (twice in verse); [[1Chr.6.43|1Chr 6:43]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּבִרָה‎</span> [[Josh.10.38|Josh 10:38]]; [[Josh.10.39|Josh 10:39]], elsewhere <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎דְּבִר‎</span> [[Josh.15.15|Josh 15:15]] +, a royal city of Canaanites anciently called <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִרְיַת סֵפֶר‎</span> [[Josh.15.15|Josh 15:15]]; [[Judg.1.11|Judg 1:11]], <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִרְיַת סַנָּה‎</span> [[Josh.15.49|Josh 15:49]] on the mountains of Judah, in region of Hebron [[Josh.11.21|Josh 11:21]]; [[Josh.15.49|Josh 15:49]], assigned to the Aaronite priests [[Josh.21.15|Josh 21:15]] as a city of refuge [[1Chr.6.43|1Chr 6:43]] modern ***Dhoherîye***, 5 hours southwest from Hebron, according to [[BDB#Kn|Kn]], so [[BDB#Survey|Survey]]<sup>iii. 402</sup>; other conjectures in [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[Josh.10.39|Josh 10:39]] [[BDB#Ri|Ri]]<sup>HWB 265</sup>.
