 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲצֹצְרָה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==clarion== (Late Hebrew <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲצוֺצֶרֶת‎</span>, Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎חֲצוֺצַרְתָּצ‎</span>) — mostly [[BDB#P|P]] and late; — absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span> [[Hos.5.8|Hos 5:8]] plural absolute <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲצֹצְרוֺת‎</span> [[Num.10.8|Num 10:8]] + 22 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲצֹצְרֹת‎</span> [[Num.10.9|Num 10:9]]; [[Num.10.10|Num 10:10]] construct ***id.*** [[Num.31.6|Num 31:6]]; [[2Chr.13.12|2Chr 13:12]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲצוֺצְרֹת‎</span> [[Num.10.2|Num 10:2]] ***clarion:*** 

**1.** as secular instr. [[Hos.5.8|Hos 5:8]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוֺפָר‎</span>) [[2Kgs.11.14|2Kgs 11:14]] (twice in verse) = [[2Chr.23.13|2Chr 23:13]] (twice in verse). 

**2.** as sacred instr. [[2Kgs.12.14|2Kgs 12:14]], especially for use by priests (only [[BDB#P|P]], [[Ps.98|Ps 98]] and Chronicles). 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **a.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תקע בח‎</span> (of blowing a single long blast) [[Num.10.3|Num 10:3]]; [[Num.10.4|Num 10:4]]; [[Num.10.7|Num 10:7]]; [[Num.10.8|Num 10:8]], to gather congregation or <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎נשׂיא‎</span> together, and, on festivals, over sacrifice, 'to be remembered before <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>,' v[[Num.10.10|Num 10:10]]. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **b.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎תקע תרועה בח‎</span>, or <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הריע בח‎</span> (of sounding alarm, — a series of quick blasts) for camps to move [[Num.10.5|Num 10:5]]; [[Num.10.6|Num 10:6]], also in battle, v[[Num.10.9|Num 10:9]], 'to be remembered before <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>;' so [[Num.31.6|Num 31:6]]; [[2Chr.13.12|2Chr 13:12]] (compare v[[2Chr.13.14|2Chr 13:14]]), both <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎התרועה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ח‎</span>. 
$\quad$ $\quad$ **c.** especially in Chr's descriptions of ceremonies at festivals, to express rejoicing: [[1Chr.13.8|1Chr 13:8]]; [[1Chr.15.28|1Chr 15:28]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קוֺל שׁוֺפָר‎</span>), [[1Chr.16.6|1Chr 16:6]]; [[1Chr.16.42|1Chr 16:42]]; [[2Chr.15.14|2Chr 15:14]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוֺפָר‎</span>), [[2Chr.20.28|2Chr 20:28]]; [[2Chr.29.26|2Chr 29:26]]; [[2Chr.29.27|2Chr 29:27]]; [[Ezra.3.10|Ezra 3:10]]; [[Neh.12.35|Neh 12:35]]; [[Neh.12.41|Neh 12:41]]; [[Ps.98.6|Ps 98:6]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קוֺל שׁוֺפָר‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הָרִים קוֺל בח‎</span> [[2Chr.5.13|2Chr 5:13]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מחצצרים בח‎</span> [[1Chr.15.24|1Chr 15:24]]; [[2Chr.5.12|2Chr 5:12]]; [[2Chr.5.13|2Chr 5:13]]; [[2Chr.13.14|2Chr 13:14]] in [[2Chr.29.28|2Chr 29:28]] this participle agrees with noun in ***sense***, and is masculine; ***and the clarions*** (= players on the clarions) ***sounded***. — The <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲצֹצְרָה‎</span>, or (sacred) clarion, was a long, straight, slender metal tube, with flaring end, see [[BDB#Benz|Benz]]<sup>Archäol. 277</sup>; distinguished thus from the <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוֺפָר‎</span> which was originally a ram's horn, and probably always retained the horn-shape; the <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוֺפָר‎</span> is mentioned constantly in the earlier literature, and was used by watchmen, warriors, etc., as well as priests (see [[BDB#Benz|Benz]]<sup>ib. 276 and</sup> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁוֺפָר‎</span>).
