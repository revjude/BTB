**1.** ***back***, of man (figurative of Israel) [[Ps.129.3|Ps 129:3]] apparently of cherubim [[Ezek.10.12|Ezek 10:12]], but [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#Sm|Sm]] emend v. so that <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גּ‎</span> refers to wheels, ***rim*** see **6** below 

**2.** ***mound***, for illicit worship [[Ezek.16.24|Ezek 16:24]]; [[Ezek.16.31|Ezek 16:31]]; [[Ezek.16.39|Ezek 16:39]] (all \|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רמה‎</span>); [[BDB#Sept|ᵐ5]] [[BDB#Vg|ᵑ9]] ***lupanar, brothel***, after analogy of ***fornix***, but this without sufficient proof, &amp; needless. 

**3.** ***boss***, or convex projection, of shield [[Job.15.26|Job 15:26]] (figurative) ***he runneth against him... with the stout bosses of his shields*** (i.e. wicked against <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎י‎</span>); so <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhJwAYAIAAAP///wAAACH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAnABgAAAJWhI+pCxEMY3Sy2kavVu7tf3ggKGZjlZmnISLp6qqs18FYO9/yRuEz7WPIaIvUToh7GJW9I7IDjUqhmuaUqQtOkjlbN8byfmNOXrlcrYmL1HVI6o7LbQUAOw=="> in Arabic in similar phrase; compare also Arabic <img class="bdbSeriacGlyph" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhKAAdAIAAAP///wAAACH5BAAAAAAALAAAAAAoAB0AAAJphI+py+0PIwzB1UepzSlr7DHfB3jkUnHNeJXnpoqhpMTrS+f6zvf+D5S0gENUsYerDV8mWaQ1M5hwydpBc7w2pbZUNzrdusJRLZmstK24pXbQSsx25O7nRK5GVsfxvVQv9qZUJ1homFMAADs=">, ***shield***; also French ***bouclier*** from ***boucle***. 

**4.** ***bulwarks, breastworks***, figurative for arguments <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לְגַבֵּיחֹֿמֶר גַּבֵּיכֶם‎</span> [[Job.13.12|Job 13:12]] ***breastworks of clay are your breastworks***. 

**5.** ***brow***, only <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גַּבֹּת עֵינָיו‎</span> [[Lev.14.9|Lev 14:9]] ***his eyebrows***. 

**6.** ***rim*** of wheel, ***felloe*** [[1Kgs.7.33|1Kgs 7:33]]; [[Ezek.1.18|Ezek 1:18]] (twice in verse); so perhaps [[Ezek.10.12|Ezek 10:12]] see **1** above — [[Ezek.43.13|Ezek 43:13]] ***elevation***, i.e. basement of altar, [[BDB#Da|Da]] after MT., but see <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎גֹּבַהּ‎</span>, compare above
