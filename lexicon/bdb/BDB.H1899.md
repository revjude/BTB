 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎הֶ֫גֶה‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Job.37.2|Job 37:2]]</sup> ==a rumbling, growling, moaning==: — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ה‎</span> absolute [[Ezek.2.10|Ezek 2:10]] + 2 t.; — 

**1.** ***a rumbling, growling sound*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מִפִּיו יֵצֵא‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְה‎</span> [[Job.37.2|Job 37:2]] of thunder, as sound going forth from God's mouth. 

**2.** ***a moaning*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וָהִי‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎קִינִים וָה‎</span> [[Ezek.2.10|Ezek 2:10]] ***lamentations and moaning and woe***.


**3.** ***a sigh*** or ***moan***, as transient, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כִּלִּינוּ שָׁנֵינוּ‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎כְמוֺהֶֿ֑גֶה‎</span> [[Ps.90.9|Ps 90:9]] ***we bring our years to an end as a sign***, i.e. ***a fleeting sound*** (compare [[BDB#RV|RVm]] [[BDB#VB|VB]]).
