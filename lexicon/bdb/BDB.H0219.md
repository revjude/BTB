 I. <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺרָה‎</span> **noun feminine** ==light== (late, Mishna ***id.***, compare Aramaic <span class="bdbLangAramaic">‎אוּרְתָּא‎</span> ***evening-light, moon-light, star-light***, etc.) 

**1.** ***light*** (opposed to <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎חֲשֵׁיכָה‎</span>) [[Ps.139.12|Ps 139:12]]. 

**2.** ***light*** of joy &amp; happiness [[Esth.8.16|Esth 8:16]] plural intensive <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺרוֺת‎</span> ***light*** of life [[Isa.26.19|Isa 26:19]] (light that quickens dead bodies as dew the plants [[BDB#Ew|Ew]] [[BDB#Hi|Hi]] [[BDB#De|De]] [[BDB#Che|Che]] [[BDB#Di|Di]] [[BDB#RV|RVm]]; see [[BDB#Br|Br]]<sup>MP 303</sup> compare <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אוֺר חַיִּים‎</span>; but [[BDB#Ki|Ki]] [[BDB#Ges|Ges]] [[BDB#MV|MV]] [[BDB#Bö|Bö]] [[BDB#RV|RV]] translate ***herbs***).
