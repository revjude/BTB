 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁלּוּחִים‎</span> **noun [masculine] plural** ==sending away, parting gift==; — 

**1.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אַחר שִׁלוּחֶיהָ‎</span> [[Exod.18.2|Exod 18:2]] ([[BDB#J|J]]) ***after she had been sent away.*** 

**2.** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁלֻּחִים‎</span> [[1Kgs.9.16|1Kgs 9:16]] he gave (city) as ***parting gift*** (i.e. dowry) to his daughter; compare figurative <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שִׁלּוּחִים‎</span> [[Mic.1.14|Mic 1:14]] thou shalt give ***a pearting gift*** (= say farewell to, lose) Morešeth of Gath.
