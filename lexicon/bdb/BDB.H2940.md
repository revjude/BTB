 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַ֫עַם‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Jer.48.11|Jer 48:11]]</sup> ==taste, judegement== — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ט‎</span> absolute [[Job.6.6|Job 6:6]]; [[Ps.119.66|Ps 119:66]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טָ֑עַם‎</span> [[Prov.11.22|Prov 11:22]]; [[Prov.26.16|Prov 26:16]] construct <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַעַם‎</span> [[Num.11.8|Num 11:8]] + 2 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַעְמוֺ‎</span> [[Exod.16.31|Exod 16:31]] + 4 t.; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַעְמֵךְ‎</span> [[1Sam.25.33|1Sam 25:33]] — 

**1.** ***taste*** of manna [[Exod.16.31|Exod 16:31]] ([[BDB#P|P]]), [[Num.11.8|Num 11:8]] ([[BDB#J|J]][[BDB#E|E]]), compare v[[Num.11.8|Num 11:8]] in juice of purslain [[Job.6.6|Job 6:6]] of Moab under figure of wine [[Jer.48.11|Jer 48:11]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎רֵיחַ‎</span>). 

**2.** figurative ***judgement, discretion, discernment***, [[1Sam.25.33|1Sam 25:33]]; [[Prov.11.22|Prov 11:22]]; [[Job.12.20|Job 12:20]], so <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טוּב טַעַם וָדַעַת לַמְּדֵנִי‎</span> [[Ps.119.66|Ps 119:66]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מְשִׁיבֵי ט‎</span> [[Prov.26.16|Prov 26:16]] ***those answering discretion***, i.e. discreetly: <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎שׁנה‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טעם‎</span> ***change, disguise one's judgement, sense*** [[1Sam.21.13|1Sam 21:13]]; [[Ps.34.1|Ps 34:1]], of David feigning madness. 

**3.** (late Aramaism, compare [[Dan.3.10|Dan 3:10]] etc.) ***decision, decree*** <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טַעַם מֶלָךְ וּגְּדֹלָיו‎</span> [[Jonah.3.7|Jonah 3:7]].
