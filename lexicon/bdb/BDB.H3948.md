 <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֶ֫קַח‎</span> **noun masculine**<sup>[[Prov.4.2|Prov 4:2]]</sup> ==learning, teaching== ( concrete; probably ***what is received*** ); — <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ל‎</span> absolute [[Isa.29.24|Isa 29:24]] + 5 t.; suffix <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִקְחִי‎</span> [[Deut.32.3|Deut 32:3]]; [[Job.11.4|Job 11:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִקְחָהּ‎</span> [[Prov.7.21|Prov 7:21]] — 

**1.** ***instruction***, subjective, as personal acquirement; <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יִלְמְדוּ לֶ֑קַח‎</span> [[Isa.29.24|Isa 29:24]], they ***shall learn instruction*** (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בִּינָה‎</span>); <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְיוֺסֶף לֶ֑קַח‎</span> [[Prov.1.5|Prov 1:5]] a wise man will hear ***and will increase*** (his) ***learning***, so [[Prov.9.9|Prov 9:9]]. 

**2.** ***teaching***, object, as thing taught, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎יַעֲרֹף כַּמָּטָר‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לִקְחִי‎</span> [[Deut.32.2|Deut 32:2]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎אִמְרָתִי‎</span>), [[Job.11.4|Job 11:4]] <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎טוֺב נָתַתִּי לָכֶם‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎ל‎</span> [[Prov.4.2|Prov 4:2]] also = ***teaching-power, persuasiveness***, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מֶתֶק שְׂפָתַיִם יוֺסִיף ל‎</span> [[Prov.16.21|Prov 16:21]] ***sweetness of lips increaseth persuasiveness*** (see  <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎מתק‎</span>), <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎וְעַל שְׂפָתָיו יוֺסִיף‎</span> <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎לֶקַח‎</span> v[[Prov.16.23|Prov 16:23]] in bad sense, of seductive words of adulteress, <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎׳‎</span><span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּרֹב ל‎</span> [[Prov.7.21|Prov 7:21]] (\|\| <span class="bdbLangHebrew">‎בְּחֵלֶק שְׂפָתֶיהָ‎</span>).
