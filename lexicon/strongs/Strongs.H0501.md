**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֶלְעָשָׂה‎</span></big>** **ʼElʻâsâh** <small>*(el-aw-saw')*</small> ==**Eleasah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H0410|‎אֵל‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H6213|‎עָשָׂה‎]]</big>**; God has made</small>

$\quad$ Elasah, Eleasah.
- § Elasah or Eleasah = *"God has made"*
- ***Elasah***, the name of four Israelites

**1)** a descendant of Judah, son of Helez
**2)** a priest of Pashur's line with a foreign wife during Ezra's time
**3)** Shaphan's son, Zedekiah's servant
**4)** a descendant of Benjamin, son of Rapha, father of Azel
