**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פּוּחַ‎</span></big>** **pûwach** <small>*(poo'akh)*</small> ==**to breathe**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ blow (upon), break, puff, bring into a snare, speak, utter.
- ***to puff***, i.e. ***blow with the breath*** or ***air***
-  hence, ***to fan*** (as a breeze), ***to utter***, ***to kindle*** (a fire), ***to scoff***

**1)** to breathe, blow
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to breathe
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cause to exhale or breathe
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to puff, snort
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to excite, inflame
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to puff, pant for it
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** to breathe out, utter
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b6)** to blow, blast
