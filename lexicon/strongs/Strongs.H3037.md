<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יַדּוּעַ‎</span></big> **Yaddûwaʻ** <small>*(yad-doo'-ah)*</small> ==**Jaddua**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎יָדַע‎</span><sup><small>[[H3045]]</small></sup>; knowing</small>

$\quad$ Jaddua.
- § Jaddua = *"knowing"*
- ***Jaddua***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** one of the chiefs of the people who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
**2)** son and successor in the high priesthood of Jonathan, Jeshua, or Johanan; the last of the high priests mentioned in the OT, probably living in the time of Alexander the Great
