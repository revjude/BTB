**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָטַר‎</span></big>** **qâṭar** <small>*(kaw-tar')*</small> ==**to offer: burn**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (identical with through the idea of fumigation in a close place and perhaps thus driving out the occupants)</small>

$\quad$ burn (incense, sacrifice) (upon), (altar for) incense, kindle, offer (incense, a sacrifice).
- ***to smoke***, i.e. ***turn into fragrance by fire*** (especially as an act of worship)

v
**1)** to sacrifice, burn incense, burn sacrifices, make sacrifices smoke
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to make sacrifices smoke
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to sacrifice
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) to smoke a sacrifice
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to make sacrifices smoke
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to cause incense to smoke, offer incense
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to make smoke upon
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to be made to smoke
n m
**2)** incense
n f
**3)** incense-altar
