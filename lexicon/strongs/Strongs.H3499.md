<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יֶתֶר‎</span></big> **yether** <small>*(yeh'-ther)*</small> ==**remainder**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup>

$\quad$ [phrase] abundant, cord, exceeding, excellancy(-ent), what they leave, that hath left, plentifully, remnant, residue, rest, string, with.
- properly, ***an overhanging***, i.e. (by implication) ***an excess***, ***superiority***, ***remainder***
-  also ***a small rope*** (as hanging free)

**1)** remainder, excess, rest, remnant, excellence
$\quad$ **1a)** remainder, remnant
$\quad$ **1b)** remainder, rest, other part
$\quad$ **1c)** excess
$\quad$ **1d)** abundantly (adv)
$\quad$ **1e)** abundance, affluence
$\quad$ **1f)** superiority, excellency
