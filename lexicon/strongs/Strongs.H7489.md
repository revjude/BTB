<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָעַע‎</span></big> **râʻaʻ** <small>*(raw-ah')*</small> ==**be evil**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ afflict, associate selves (by mistake for <span class="ashebrew">‎רָעָה‎</span><sup><small>[[H7462]]</small></sup>), break (down, in pieces), [phrase] displease, (be, bring, do) evil (doer, entreat, man), show self friendly (by mistake for <span class="ashebrew">‎רָעָה‎</span><sup><small>[[H7462]]</small></sup>), do harm, (do) hurt, (behave self, deal) ill, [idiom] indeed, do mischief, punish, still, vex, (do) wicked (doer, -ly), be (deal, do) worse.
- properly, ***to spoil*** (literally, by breaking to pieces)
-  figuratively, ***to make*** (or be) ***good for******nothing***, i.e. ***bad*** (physically, socially or morally)

**1)** to be bad, be evil
$\quad$ **1a)**(Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be displeasing
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be sad
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be injurious, be evil
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to be wicked, be evil (ethically)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to do an injury or hurt
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to do evil or wickedly
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** mischief (participle)
**2)** to break, shatter
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a1)** to break
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a2)** broken (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a3)** to be broken
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hithpolel) to be broken, be broken in pieces, be broken asunder
