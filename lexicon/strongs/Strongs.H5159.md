**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נַחֲלָה‎</span></big>** **nachălâh** <small>*(nakh-al-aw')*</small> ==**inheritance**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5157|‎נָחַל‎]]</big>** (in its usual sense)</small>

$\quad$ heritage, to inherit, inheritance, possession. Compare **<big>[[H5158|‎נַחַל‎]]</big>**.
- properly, ***something inherited***, i.e. (abstractly) ***occupancy***, or (concretely) ***an heirloom***
-  generally ***an estate***, ***patrimony*** or ***portion***

**1)** possession, property, inheritance, heritage
$\quad$ **1a)** property
$\quad$ **1b)** portion, share
$\quad$ **1c)** inheritance, portion
