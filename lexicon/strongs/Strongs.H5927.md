<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָלָה‎</span></big> **ʻâlâh** <small>*(aw-law')*</small> ==**to ascend: rise**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ arise (up), (cause to) ascend up, at once, break (the day) (up), bring (up), (cause to) burn, carry up, cast up, [phrase] shew, climb (up), (cause to, make to) come (up), cut off, dawn, depart, exalt, excel, fall, fetch up, get up, (make to) go (away, up); grow (over) increase, lay, leap, levy, lift (self) up, light, (make) up, [idiom] mention, mount up, offer, make to pay, [phrase] perfect, prefer, put (on), raise, recover, restore, (make to) rise (up), scale, set (up), shoot forth (up), (begin to) spring (up), stir up, take away (up), work.
- ***to ascend***, ***in***transitively (be high) or ***actively*** (mount)
-  used in a great variety of senses, primary and secondary, literal and figurative

**1)** to go up, ascend, climb
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to go up, ascend
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to meet, visit, follow, depart, withdraw, retreat
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to go up, come up (of animals)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to spring up, grow, shoot forth (of vegetation)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to go up, go up over, rise (of natural phenomenon)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to come up (before God)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7)** to go up, go up over, extend (of boundary)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a8)** to excel, be superior to
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be taken up, be brought up, be taken away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to take oneself away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be exalted
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to bring up, cause to ascend or climb, cause to go up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to bring up, bring against, take away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to bring up, draw up, train
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to cause to ascend
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c5)** to rouse, stir up (mentally)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c6)** to offer, bring up (of gifts)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c7)** to exalt
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c8)** to cause to ascend, offer
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to be carried away, be led up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to be taken up into, be inserted in
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to be offered
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to lift oneself
