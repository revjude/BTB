**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ὑστερέω‎</span></big>** **hysteréō** <small>*(hoos-ter-eh'-o)*</small> ==**to lack**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G5306|‎ὕστερος‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ come behind (short), be destitute, fail, lack, suffer need, (be in) want, be the worse.
- to be later, i.e. (by implication) to be inferior
-  generally, to fall short (be deficient)
