**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּרַץ‎</span></big>** **pârats** <small>*(paw-rats')*</small> ==**to break through**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] abroad, (make a) breach, break (away, down, -er, forth, in, up), burst out, come (spread) abroad, compel, disperse, grow, increase, open, press, scatter, urge.
- ***to break out*** (in many applications, direct and indirect, literal and figurative)

**1)** to break through or down or over, burst, breach
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to break or burst out (from womb or enclosure)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to break through or down, make a breach in
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to break into
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to break open
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to break up, break in pieces
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to break out (violently) upon
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7)** to break over (limits), increase
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a8)** to use violence
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a9)** to burst open 1a10) to spread, distribute
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be broken through
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be broken down
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael) to break away
