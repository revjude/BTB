**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תָּמַךְ‎</span></big>** **tâmak** <small>*(taw-mak')*</small> ==**to grasp**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (take, up-) hold (up), maintain, retain, stay (up).
- ***to sustain***
-  by implication, ***to obtain***, ***keep fast***
-  figuratively, ***to help***, ***follow close***

**1)** to grasp, hold, support, attain, lay hold of, hold fast
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to grasp, lay hold of, attain
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to hold up, support
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to hold, keep
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to take hold of each other
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be seized, be held
