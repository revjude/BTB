**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עֲשָׂיָה‎</span></big>** **ʻĂsâyâh** <small>*(aw-saw-yaw')*</small> ==**Asaiah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H6213|‎עָשָׂה‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3050|‎יָהּ‎]]</big>**; Jah has made</small>

$\quad$ Asaiah.
- § Asaiah or Asahiah = *"made by Jehovah"*
- ***Asajah***, the name of three or four Israelites

**1)** a prince of the tribe of Simeon in the time of king Hezekiah of Judah
**2)** a servant of king Josiah of Judah
**3)** a Merarite Levite, chief of his family, in the time of David
