<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נוּד‎</span></big> **nûwd** <small>*(nood)*</small> ==**to wander**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bemoan, flee, get, mourn, make to move, take pity, remove, shake, skip for joy, be sorry, vagabond, way, wandering.
- ***to nod***, i.e. ***waver***
-  figuratively, ***to wander***, ***flee***, ***disappear***
-  also (from shaking the head in sympathy), ***to console***, ***deplore***, or (from tossing the head in scorn) ***taunt***

**1)** to shake, waver, wander, move to and fro, flutter, show grief, have compassion on
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to move to and fro, wander (aimlessly), take flight
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to flutter
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to waver, wave, shake
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to show grief
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4a)** to lament, condole, show sympathy
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cause to wander (aimlessly)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to make a wagging, wag (with the head)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpolel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to move oneself to and fro, sway, totter
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to shake oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to bemoan oneself
