<big><span class="ashebrew">‎לוּץ‎</span></big> **lûwts** <small>*(loots)*</small> ==**to mock**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ ambassador, have in derision, interpreter, make a mock, mocker, scorn(-er, -ful), teacher.
- properly, ***to make mouths at***, i.e. ***to scoff***
-  hence (from the effort to pronounce a foreign language) ***to interpret***, or (generally) ***intercede***

**1)** to scorn, make mouths at, talk arrogantly
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to boast
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to scorn
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to mock, deride
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to interpret (language)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2a)** interpreter (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2b)** ambassador (fig.)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpalpel) to be inflated, scoff, act as a scorner, show oneself a mocker
