**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נְבֵלָה‎</span></big>** **nᵉbêlâh** <small>*(neb-ay-law')*</small> ==**carcass**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5034|‎נָבֵל‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ (dead) body, (dead) carcase, dead of itself, which died, (beast) that (which) dieth of itself.
- ***a flabby thing***, i.e. ***a carcase*** or ***carrion*** (human or bestial, often collectively)
-  figuratively, ***an idol***

**1)** carcass, corpse
$\quad$ **1a)** of humans, idols, animals
