<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מִפְקָד‎</span></big> **miphqâd** <small>*(mif-kawd')*</small> ==**Muster (Gate)**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎פָּקַד‎</span><sup><small>[[H6485]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ appointed place, commandment, number.
- ***an appointment***, i.e. ***mandate***
-  concretely, ***a designated spot***
-  specifically, ***a census***

**1)** muster, appointment, appointed place
$\quad$ **1a)** muster (of people)
$\quad$ **1b)** appointment
$\quad$ **1c)** appointed place
**2)** (TWOT) number
