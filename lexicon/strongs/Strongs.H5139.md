<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָזִיר‎</span></big> **nâzîyr** <small>*(naw-zeer')*</small> ==**Nazirite**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--TG|N:N--TG]]</small></sup> 
<small>or <span class="ashebrew">נָזִר</span> **nâzir** <small>*(naw-zeer')*</small>; from <span class="ashebrew">‎נָזַר‎</span><sup><small>[[H5144]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ Nazarite (by a false alliteration with Nazareth), separate(-d), vine undressed.
- ***separate***, i.e. ***consecrated*** (as prince, a Nazirite)
-  hence (figuratively from the latter) ***an unpruned vine*** (like an unshorn Nazirite)

**1)** consecrated or devoted one, Nazarite
$\quad$ **1a)** consecrated one
$\quad$ **1b)** devotee, Nazarite
$\quad$ **1c)** untrimmed (vine)
