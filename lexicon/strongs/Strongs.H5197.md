**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָטַף‎</span></big>** **nâṭaph** <small>*(naw-taf')*</small> ==**to drip/prophesy**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ drop(-ping), prophesy(-et).
- ***to ooze***, i.e. ***distil gradually***
-  by implication, ***to fall in drops***
-  figuratively, ***to speak by inspiration***

**1)** to drop, drip, distil, prophesy, preach, discourse
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to drop, drip
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to drip
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to drop (prophecy)
