$\quad$ (all) along, apace, behave (self), come, (on) continually, be conversant, depart, [phrase] be eased, enter, exercise (self), [phrase] follow, forth, forward, get, go (about, abroad, along, away, forward, on, out, up and down), [phrase] greater, grow, be wont to haunt, lead, march, [idiom] more and more, move (self), needs, on, pass (away), be at the point, quite, run (along), [phrase] send, speedily, spread, still, surely, [phrase] tale-bearer, [phrase] travel(-ler), walk (abroad, on, to and fro, up and down, to places), wander, wax, (way-) faring man, [idiom] be weak, whirl.
- ***to walk*** (in a great variety of applications, literally and figuratively)

**1)** to go, walk, come
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to go, walk, come, depart, proceed, move, go away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to die, live, manner of life (fig.)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to walk
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to walk (fig.)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to traverse
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to walk about
$\quad$ **1d)** (Niphal) to lead, bring, lead away, carry, cause to walk
