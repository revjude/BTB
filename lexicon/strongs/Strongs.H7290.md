<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (be fast a-, be in a deep, cast into a dead, that) sleep(-er, -eth).

**1)** (Niphal) to be asleep, be unconscious, be in heavy sleep, fall into heavy sleep, be fast asleep
