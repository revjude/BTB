<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָלָה‎</span></big> **qâlâh** <small>*(kaw-law')*</small> ==**to dishonor**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ base, contemn, despise, lightly esteem, set light, seem vile.
- ***to be light*** (as implied in rapid motion), but figuratively, only (be (causatively, hold) ***in contempt***)

**1)** to disgrace, dishonour, be lightly esteemed, be dishonoured, be despised
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to be dishonoured, be despised, be lightly esteemed
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to treat with contempt, dishonour, treat shamefully
