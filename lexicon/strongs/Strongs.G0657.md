**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἀποτάσσομαι‎</span></big>** **apotássomai** <small>*(ap-ot-as'-som-ahee)*</small> ==**to leave**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>middle voice from **<big>[[G0575|‎ἀπό‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G5021|‎τάσσω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ bid farewell, forsake, take leave, send away.
- literally, to say adieu (by departing or dismissing)
-  figuratively, to renounce
