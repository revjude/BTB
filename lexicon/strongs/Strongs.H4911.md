**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָשַׁל‎</span></big>** **mâshal** <small>*(maw-shal')*</small> ==**to liken**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>denominative from **<big>[[H4912|‎מָשָׁל‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ be(-come) like, compare, use (as a) proverb, speak (in proverbs), utter.
- ***to liken***, i.e. (transitively) ***to use*** figurative ***language*** (an allegory, adage, song or the like)
-  ***in***transitively, ***to resemble***

**1)** to represent, liken, be like
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to liken, be like, be similar
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to compare
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to become like
**2)** to speak in a proverb, use a proverb, speak in parables, speak in sentences of poetry
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to use a proverb, speak a parable or proverb
$\quad$ **2b)** (Piel) to make a parable
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b1)** maker of parables (participle)
