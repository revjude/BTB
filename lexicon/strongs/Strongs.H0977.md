<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּחַר‎</span></big> **bâchar** <small>*(baw-khar')*</small> ==**to choose**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ acceptable, appoint, choose (choice), excellent, join, be rather, require.
- properly, ***to try***, i.e. (by implication) ***select***

**1)** to choose, elect, decide for
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to choose
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be chosen
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be chosen, selected
