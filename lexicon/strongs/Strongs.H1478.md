<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּוַע‎</span></big> **gâvaʻ** <small>*(gaw-vah')*</small> ==**to die**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ die, be dead, give up the ghost, perish.
- ***to breathe out***, i.e. (by implication) ***expire***

**1)** to expire, die, perish, give up the ghost, yield up the ghost, be dead, be ready to die
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to expire, die, be about to die
