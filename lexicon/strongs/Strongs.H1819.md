<big><span class="ashebrew">‎דָּמָה‎</span></big> **dâmâh** <small>*(daw-maw')*</small> ==**to resemble**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ compare, devise, (be) like(-n), mean, think, use similitudes.
- ***to compare***
-  by implication, ***to resemble***, ***liken***, ***consider***

**1)** to be like, resemble
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be like, resemble
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to liken, compare
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to imagine, think
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to make oneself like
$\quad$ **1d)** (Niphal)
