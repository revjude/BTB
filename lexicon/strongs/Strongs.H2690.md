**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָצַר‎</span></big>** **châtsar** <small>*(khaw-tsar')*</small> ==**to blow**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; properly, to surround with a stockade, and thus separate from the open country; but used only in the reduplicated form **<big><span class="ashebrew">חֲצֹצֵר</span></big>** **chătsôtsêr** <small>*(khast-o-tsare')*</small>; or (2 Chronicles 5:12) **<big><span class="ashebrew">חֲצֹרֵר</span></big>** **chătsôrêr** <small>*(khatso-rare')*</small>; as dem. from **<big>[[H2689|‎חֲצֹצְרָה‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ blow, sound, trumpeter.
- ***to trumpet***, i.e. ***blow on that instrument***

**1)** to sound a trumpet
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel) players on clarions (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) sound with clarions (participle)
