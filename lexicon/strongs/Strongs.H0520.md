<small>prolonged from <span class="ashebrew">‎אֵם‎</span><sup><small>[[H0517]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ cubit, [phrase] hundred (by exchange for <span class="ashebrew">‎מֵאָה‎</span><sup><small>[[H3967]]</small></sup>), measure, post.
- properly, ***a mother*** (i.e. unit of measure, or the fore-arm (below the elbow), i.e. ***a cubit***
-  also ***a door-base*** (as a bond of the entrance)

**1)** cubit-a measure of distance (the forearm), roughly 18 in (. 5m). There are several cubits used in the OT, the cubit of a man or common cubit (De 3:11), the legal cubit or cubit of the sanctuary (Eze 40:5) plus others. See a Bible Dictionary for a complete treatment.
