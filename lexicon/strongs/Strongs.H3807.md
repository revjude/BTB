<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּתַת‎</span></big> **kâthath** <small>*(kaw-thath')*</small> ==**to crush**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ beat (down, to pieces), break in pieces, crushed, destroy, discomfit, smite, stamp. l
- ***to bruise*** or ***violently strike***

**1)** to beat, crush by beating, crush to pieces, crush fine
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to beat or crush fine
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to beat, hammer
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to beat or crush fine
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to beat, hammer
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be beaten
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to beat in pieces, shatter
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hophal) to be beaten, be crushed
