$\quad$ pond, pool, standing (water).
- ***a marsh***
-  hence ***a rush*** (as growing in swamps)
-  hence ***a stockade of reeds***

**1)** pool, troubled pool
$\quad$ **1a)** troubled or muddy (gloomy) pools, marshes
$\quad$ **1b)** any pool, pond
$\quad$ **1c)** swamp reeds, reeds, rush(es)
