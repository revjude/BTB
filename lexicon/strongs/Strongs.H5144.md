<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָזַר‎</span></big> **nâzar** <small>*(naw-zar')*</small> ==**to dedicate**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ consecrate, separate(-ing, self).
- ***to hold aloof***, i.e. (intransitivey) ***abstain*** (from food and drink, from impurity, and even from divine worship (i.e. apostatize))
-  specifically, ***to set apart*** (to sacred purposes), i.e. ***devote***

**1)** to dedicate, consecrate, separate
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to dedicate oneself, devote oneself
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to keep sacredly separate
**2)**(Hiphil) to be a Nazarite, live as a Nazarite
