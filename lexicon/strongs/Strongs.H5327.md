<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָצָה‎</span></big> **nâtsâh** <small>*(naw-tsaw')*</small> ==**to struggle**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be laid waste, runinous, strive (together).
- properly, ***to go forth***, i.e. (by implication) ***to be expelled***, and (consequently) ***desolate***
-  causatively, ***to lay waste***
-  also (specifically), ***to quarrel***

**1)** (Qal) to fly
**2)** to struggle
$\quad$ **2a)** (Niphal) to struggle
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hiphil) to struggle
**3)** to strip off, make desolate, fall in ruins
$\quad$ **3a)** (Qal) to fall in ruins
$\quad$ **3b)** (Niphal) desolated, ruined heaps (participle)
