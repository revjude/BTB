**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ῥίπτω‎</span></big>** **rhíptō** <small>*(hrip'-to)*</small> ==**to throw/lay down**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary verb (perhaps rather akin to the base of **<big>[[G4474|‎ῥαπίζω‎]]</big>**, through the idea of sudden motion)</small>

$\quad$ cast (down, out), scatter abroad, throw.
- to fling (properly, with a quick toss, thus differing from **<big>[[G0906|‎βάλλω‎]]</big>**, which denotes a deliberate hurl
-  and from <greek BETA="TEI/NW" unicode="τείνω" translit="teínō"/> (see in **<big>[[G1614|‎ἐκτείνω‎]]</big>**), which indicates an extended projection)
-  by qualification, to deposit (as if a load)
-  by extension, to disperse
