<big><span class="asgreek">‎ῥίπτω‎</span></big> **rhíptō** <small>*(hrip'-to)*</small> ==**to throw/lay down**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary verb (perhaps rather akin to the base of <span class="asgreek">ῥαπίζω</span><sup><small>[[G4474]]</small></sup>, through the idea of sudden motion)</small>

$\quad$ cast (down, out), scatter abroad, throw.
- to fling (properly, with a quick toss, thus differing from <span class="asgreek">βάλλω</span><sup><small>[[G0906]]</small></sup>, which denotes a deliberate hurl
-  and from <greek BETA="TEI/NW" unicode="τείνω" translit="teínō"/> (see in <span class="asgreek">ἐκτείνω</span><sup><small>[[G1614]]</small></sup>), which indicates an extended projection)
-  by qualification, to deposit (as if a load)
-  by extension, to disperse
