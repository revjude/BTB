**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אוּץ‎</span></big>** **ʼûwts** <small>*(oots)*</small> ==**to hasten**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (make) haste(-n, -y), labor, be narrow.
- ***to press***
-  (by implication) ***to be close***, ***hurry***, ***withdraw***

**1)** to press, be pressed, make haste, urge, be narrow
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to press, hasten
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be pressed, confined, narrow
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to hasten, make haste
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** be narrow
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to urge, insist
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to hasten
