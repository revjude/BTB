$\quad$ Ishmaiah.
- § Ishmaiah = *"Jehovah will hear"*
- ***Jishmajah***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** a Gibeonite and one of David's mighty warriors
**2)** a son of Obadiah and the ruler of the tribe of Zebulun in the time of king David
