**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גּוּר‎</span></big>** **gûwr** <small>*(goor)*</small> ==**to sojourn**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ abide, assemble, be afraid, dwell, fear, gather (together), inhabitant, remain, sojourn, stand in awe, (be) stranger, [idiom] surely.
- properly, ***to turn aside from the road*** (for a lodging or any other purpose), i.e. ***sojourn*** (as a guest)
-  also ***to shrink***, ***fear*** (as in a strange place)
-  also ***to gather for******hostility*** (as afraid)

**1)** to sojourn, abide, dwell in, dwell with, remain, inhabit, be a stranger, be continuing, surely
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to sojourn, dwell for a time
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to abide, stay, temporarily dwell
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hithpolel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to seek hospitality with
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to assemble oneself
**2)** to stir up trouble, strife, quarrel, gather together
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a1)** to stir up strife
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a2)** to quarrel
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hithpolel) to excite oneself
**3)** to dread, fear, stand in awe, be afraid
$\quad$ **3a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **3a1)** to fear, be afraid
$\quad$ $\quad$ **3a2)** to be in awe, stand in awe
