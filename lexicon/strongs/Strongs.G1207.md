<big><span class="asgreek">‎δευτερόπρωτος‎</span></big> **deuteróprōtos** <small>*(dyoo-ter-op'-ro-tos)*</small> ==**second-first**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:A|G:A]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="asgreek">δεύτερος</span><sup><small>[[G1208]]</small></sup> and <span class="asgreek">πρῶτος</span><sup><small>[[G4413]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ second … after the first.
- second-first, i.e. (specially) a designation of the Sabbath immediately after the Paschal week (being the second after Passover day, and the first of the seven Sabbaths intervening before Pentecost)
