**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἐπισκιάζω‎</span></big>** **episkiázō** <small>*(ep-ee-skee-ad'-zo)*</small> ==**to overshadow**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G1909|‎ἐπί‎]]</big>** and a derivative of **<big>[[G4639|‎σκιά‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ overshadow.
- to cast a shade upon, i.e. (by analogy) to envelop in a haze of brilliancy
-  figuratively, to invest with preternatural influence
