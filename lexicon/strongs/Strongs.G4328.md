**<big><span class="asgreek">‎προσδοκάω‎</span></big>** **prosdokáō** <small>*(pros-dok-ah'-o)*</small> ==**to look for**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4314|‎πρός‎]]</big>** and dokeuo (to watch)</small>

$\quad$ (be in) expect(-ation), look (for), when looked, tarry, wait for.
- to anticipate (in thought, hope or fear)
-  by implication, to await
