<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָרַד‎</span></big> **chârad** <small>*(khaw-rad')*</small> ==**to tremble**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be (make) afraid, be careful, discomfit, fray (away), quake, tremble.
- ***to shudder with terror***
-  hence, ***to fear***
-  also ***to hasten*** (with anxiety)

**1)** to tremble, quake, move about, be afraid, be startled, be terrified
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to tremble, quake (of a mountain)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to tremble (of people)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be anxiously careful
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to go or come trembling (with prep)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cause to tremble
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to drive in terror, rout (an army)
