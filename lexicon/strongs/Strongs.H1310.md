<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּשַׁל‎</span></big> **bâshal** <small>*(baw-shal')*</small> ==**to boil**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bake, boil, bring forth, roast, seethe, sod (be sodden).
- properly, ***to boil up***
-  hence, to be done in ***cooking***
-  figuratively ***to ripen***

**1)** to boil, cook, bake, roast, ripen, grow ripe
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to boil, cook
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to grow ripe, ripen
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to boil
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to cook
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to be boiled
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to be sodden
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to ripen
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** ripen, brought to ripeness
