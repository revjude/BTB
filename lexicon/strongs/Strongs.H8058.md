**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁמַט‎</span></big>** **shâmaṭ** <small>*(shaw-mat')*</small> ==**to release**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ discontinue, overthrow, release, let rest, shake, stumble, throw down.
- ***to fling down***
-  incipiently ***to jostle***
-  figuratively, ***to let alone***, ***desist***, ***remit***

**1)** to release, let drop or loose or rest or fall
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to let drop or fall
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be made to fall down, be thrown down
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to cause to let drop
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to release, let drop
