<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָדִיב‎</span></big> **nâdîyb** <small>*(naw-deeb')*</small> ==**noble: willing**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N|H:N]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎נָדַב‎</span><sup><small>[[H5068]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ free, liberal (things), noble, prince, willing (hearted).
- properly, ***voluntary***, i.e. ***generous***
-  hence, ***magnanimous***
-  as noun, ***a grandee*** (sometimes a tyrant)

adj
**1)** inclined, willing, noble, generous
$\quad$ **1a)** incited, inclined, willing
$\quad$ **1b)** noble, princely (in rank)
$\quad$ **1c)** noble (in mind and character)
n m
**2)** noble one
