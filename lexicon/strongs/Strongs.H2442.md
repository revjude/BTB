**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָכָה‎</span></big>** **châkâh** <small>*(khaw-kaw')*</small> ==**to wait**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (apparently akin to **<big>[[H2707|‎חָקָה‎]]</big>** through the idea of piercing)</small>

$\quad$ long, tarry, wait.
- properly, ***to adhere to***
-  hence, ***to await***

**1)** to wait, wait for, await
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to wait for
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to wait, tarry
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to wait (in ambush)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to wait for, long for
