**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁבַח‎</span></big>** **shâbach** <small>*(shaw-bakh')*</small> ==**to soothe**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ commend, glory, keep in, praise, still, triumph.
- properly, ***to address in a******loud tone***, i.e. (specifically) ***loud***
-  figuratively, ***to pacify*** (as if by words)

**1)** to soothe, still, stroke
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel) to soothe, still
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) stilling (participle)
**2)** to laud, praise, commend
$\quad$ **2a)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a1)** to laud, praise (God)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a2)** to commend, congratulate (the dead)
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hithpael) to boast
