**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֲדֹנָי‎</span></big>** **ʼĂdônây** <small>*(ad-o-noy')*</small> ==**Lord**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>an emphatic form of **<big>[[H0113|‎אָדוֹן‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ (my) Lord.
- ***the Lord*** (used as a proper name of God only)

**1)** my lord, lord
$\quad$ **1a)** of men
$\quad$ **1b)** of God
**2)** Lord-title, spoken in place of Yahweh in Jewish display of reverence
