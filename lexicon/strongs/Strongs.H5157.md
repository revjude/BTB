**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָחַל‎</span></big>** **nâchal** <small>*(naw-khal')*</small> ==**to inherit**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ divide, have (inheritance), take as a heritage, (cause to, give to, make to) inherit, (distribute for, divide (for, for an, by), give for, have, leave for, take (for)) inheritance, (have in, cause to, be made to) possess(-ion).
- ***to inherit*** (as a (figurative) mode of descent), or (generally) ***to occupy***
-  causatively, ***to bequeath***, or (generally) ***distribute***, ***instate***

**1)** to get as a possession, acquire, inherit, possess
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to take possession, inherit
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to have or get as a possession or property (fig.)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to divide the land for a possession
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to acquire (testimonies) (fig.)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to divide for a possession
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to possess oneself of
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to give as a possession
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to cause to inherit, give as an inheritance
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hophal) to be allotted, be made to possess
