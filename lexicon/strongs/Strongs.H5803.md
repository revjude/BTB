**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עַזְגָּד‎</span></big>** **ʻAzgâd** <small>*(az-gawd')*</small> ==**Azgad**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup>

$\quad$ Azgad.
- § Azgad = *"Gad is mighty"*
- ***Azgad***, an Israelite

**1)** ancestor of a family of laymen who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
**2)** a man who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
