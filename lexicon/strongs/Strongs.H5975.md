<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָמַד‎</span></big> **ʻâmad** <small>*(aw-mad')*</small> ==**to stand: stand**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ abide (behind), appoint, arise, cease, confirm, continue, dwell, be employed, endure, establish, leave, make, ordain, be (over), place, (be) present (self), raise up, remain, repair, [phrase] serve, set (forth, over, -tle, up), (make to, make to be at a, with-) stand (by, fast, firm, still, up), (be at a) stay (up), tarry.
- ***to stand***, ***in various relations*** (literal and figurative, intransitive and transitive)

**1)** to stand, remain, endure, take one's stand
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to stand, take one's stand, be in a standing attitude, stand forth, take a stand, present oneself, attend upon, be or become servant of
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to stand still, stop (moving or doing), cease
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to tarry, delay, remain, continue, abide, endure, persist, be steadfast
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to make a stand, hold one's ground
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to stand upright, remain standing, stand up, rise, be erect, be upright
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to arise, appear, come on the scene, stand forth, appear, rise up or against
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7)** to stand with, take one's stand, be appointed, grow flat, grow insipid
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to station, set
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to cause to stand firm, maintain
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to cause to stand up, cause to set up, erect
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to present (one) before (king)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** to appoint, ordain, establish
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hophal) to be presented, be caused to stand, be stood before
