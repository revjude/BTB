<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָשַׁב‎</span></big> **châshab** <small>*(khaw-shab')*</small> ==**to devise: design**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (make) account (of), conceive, consider, count, cunning (man, work, workman), devise, esteem, find out, forecast, hold, imagine, impute, invent, be like, mean, purpose, reckon(-ing be made), regard, think.
- properly, ***to plait*** or ***interpenetrate***, i.e. (literally) ***to weave*** or (generally) ***to fabricate***
-  figuratively, ***to plot*** or ***contrive*** (usually in a malicious sense)
-  hence (from the mental effort) ***to think***, ***regard***, ***value***, ***compute***

**1)** to think, plan, esteem, calculate, invent, make a judgment, imagine, count
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to think, account
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to plan, devise, mean
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to charge, impute, reckon
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to esteem, value, regard
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to invent
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be accounted, be thought, be esteemed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be computed, be reckoned
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be imputed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to think upon, consider, be mindful of
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to think to do, devise, plan
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to count, reckon
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael) to be considered
