**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֵיפָה‎</span></big>** **ʼêyphâh** <small>*(ay-faw')*</small> ==**ephah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>or (shortened) **<big><span class="ashebrew">אֵפָה</span></big>** **ʼêphâh** <small>*(ay-faw')*</small> ; of Egyptian derivation</small>

$\quad$ ephah, (divers) measure(-s).
- ***an ephah*** or measure for grain
-  hence, a ***measure*** in general

**1)** ephah
$\quad$ **1a)** a dry measure of quantity, equal to 3 seahs, 10 omers; the same as the liquid measure bath; (about 9 imperial gallons (40 l), rabbinical writings give sizes of one-half this amount)
$\quad$ **1b)** the receptacle for measuring or holding that amount
