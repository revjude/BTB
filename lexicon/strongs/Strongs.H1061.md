<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בִּכּוּר‎</span></big> **bikkûwr** <small>*(bik-koor')*</small> ==**firstfruit**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎בָּכַר‎</span><sup><small>[[H1069]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ first fruit (-ripe (figuratively)), hasty fruit.
- ***the first-fruits of the crop***

**1)** first-fruits
$\quad$ **1a)** the first of the crops and fruit that ripened, was gathered, and offered to God according to the ritual of Pentecost
$\quad$ **1b)** the bread made of the new grain offered at Pentecost
$\quad$ **1c)** the day of the first-fruits (Pentecost)
