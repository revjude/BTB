$\quad$ decision, diligent, (fine) gold, pointed things, sharp, threshing instrument, wall.
- properly, ***incised*** or (active) ***incisive***
-  hence (as noun masculine or feminine) ***a trench*** (as dug), ***gold*** (as mined), ***a threshing-sledge*** (having sharp teeth)
-  (figuratively) ***determination***
-  also ***eager***
