**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָצַב‎</span></big>** **yâtsab** <small>*(yaw-tsab')*</small> ==**to stand**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ present selves, remaining, resort, set (selves), (be able to, can, with-) stand (fast, forth, -ing, still, up).
- ***to place*** (any thing so as to stay)
-  reflexively, ***to station***, ***offer***, ***continue***

**1)** to place, set, stand, set or station oneself, present oneself
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hithpael) to station oneself, take one's stand, stand, present oneself, stand with someone
