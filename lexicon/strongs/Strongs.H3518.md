<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּבָה‎</span></big> **kâbâh** <small>*(kaw-baw')*</small> ==**to quench**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ go (put) out, quench.
- ***to expire*** or (causatively) ***to extinguish*** (fire, light, anger)

**1)** to quench, put out, be put out, be quenched, be extinguished
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be quenched, be extinguished
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to quench, extinguish
