<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ kill, offer, (do) sacrifice, slay.
- ***to slaughter an animal*** (usually in sacrifice)

**1)** to slaughter, kill, sacrifice, slaughter for sacrifice
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to slaughter for sacrifice
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to slaughter for eating
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to slaughter in divine judgment
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to sacrifice, offer sacrifice
