**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּקַק‎</span></big>** **bâqaq** <small>*(baw-kah')*</small> ==**be luxuriant**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (make) empty (out), fail, [idiom] utterly, make void.
- ***to pour out***, i.e. ***to empty***, figuratively, ***to depopulate***
-  by analogy, ***to spread out*** (as a fruitful vine)

**1)** to empty
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to empty, lay waste
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to make void (fig.)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be emptied
$\quad$ **1c)** (Polel) to empty out, devastate
