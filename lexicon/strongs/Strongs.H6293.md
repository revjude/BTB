**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּגַע‎</span></big>** **pâgaʻ** <small>*(paw-gah')*</small> ==**to fall on**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ come (betwixt), cause to entreat, fall (upon), make intercession, intercessor, intreat, lay, light (upon), meet (together), pray, reach, run.
- ***to impinge***, ***by accident*** or ***violence***, or (figuratively) ***by importunity***

**1)** to encounter, meet, reach, entreat, make intercession
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to meet, light upon, join
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to meet (of kindness)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to encounter, fall upon (of hostility)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to encounter, entreat (of request)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to strike, touch (of boundary)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cause to light upon
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to cause to entreat
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to make entreaty, interpose
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to make attack
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** to reach the mark
