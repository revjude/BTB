<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָשַׁךְ‎</span></big> **châshak** <small>*(khaw-shak')*</small> ==**to darken**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be black, be (make) dark, darken, cause darkness, be dim, hide.
- ***to be dark*** (as withholding light)
-  transitively, ***to darken***

**1)** to be or become dark, grow dim, be darkened, be black, be hidden
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be or grow dark
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to have a dark colour
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to grow dim
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to make dark, cause to be dark
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to hide, conceal
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to obscure, confuse (fig.)
