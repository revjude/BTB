<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁלַט‎</span></big> **shâlaṭ** <small>*(shaw-lat')*</small> ==**to domineer**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (bear, have) rule, have dominion, give (have) power.
- ***to dominate***, i.e. ***govern***
-  by implication, ***to permit***

**1)** to domineer, exercise power over, dominate, have mastery, be master, lord it over
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to domineer, lord it over, become master
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to give power of
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to get mastery of
