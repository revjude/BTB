**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָדָה‎</span></big>** **yâdâh** <small>*(yaw-daw')*</small> ==**to give thanks**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; used only as denominative from **<big>[[H3027|‎יָד‎]]</big>**; literally, to use (i.e. hold out) the hand</small>

$\quad$ cast (out), (make) confess(-ion), praise, shoot, (give) thank(-ful, -s, -sgiving).
- physically, ***to throw*** (a stone, an arrow) ***at*** or ***away***
-  especially ***to revere*** or ***worship*** (with extended hands)
-  intensively, ***to bemoan*** (by wringing the hands)

**1)** to throw, shoot, cast
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to shoot (arrows)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to cast, cast down, throw down
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to give thanks, laud, praise
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to confess, confess (the name of God)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to confess (sin)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to give thanks
