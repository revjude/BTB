**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎זָלַל‎</span></big>** **zâlal** <small>*(zaw-lal')*</small> ==**to shake**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (compare **<big>[[H2107|‎זוּל‎]]</big>**)</small>

$\quad$ blow down, glutton, riotous (eater), vile.
- ***to shake*** (as in the wind), i.e. ***to quake***
-  figuratively, ***to be loose morally***, ***worthless*** or ***prodigal***

**1)** to be worthless, be vile, be insignificant, be light
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be worthless, be insignificant
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to make light of, squander, be lavish with
**2)** to shake, tremble, quake
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to shake, quake
