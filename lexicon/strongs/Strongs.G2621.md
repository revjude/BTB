**<big><span class="asgreek">‎κατάκειμαι‎</span></big>** **katákeimai** <small>*(kat-ak'-i-mahee)*</small> ==**to recline**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G2596|‎κατά‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2749|‎κεῖμαι‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ keep, lie, sit at meat (down).
- to lie down, i.e. (by implication) be sick
-  specially, to recline at a meal
