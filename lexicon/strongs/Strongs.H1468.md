<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גּוּז‎</span></big> **gûwz** <small>*(gooz)*</small> ==**to cut off**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (compare <span class="ashebrew">‎גַּזָז‎</span><sup><small>[[H1494]]</small></sup>)</small>

$\quad$ bring, cut off.
- properly, ***to shear off***
-  but used only in the (figuratively) sense of ***passing rapidly***

**1)** to pass over, pass away
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to pass away (of life)
**2)** (TWOT) to bring, cut off
