**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ὑπολαμβάνω‎</span></big>** **hypolambánō** <small>*(hoop-ol-am-ban'-o)*</small> ==**to take up/suppose**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G5259|‎ὑπό‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2983|‎λαμβάνω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ answer, receive, suppose.
- to take from below, i.e. carry upward
-  figuratively, to take up, i.e. continue a discourse or topic
-  mentally, to assume (presume)
