**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֲבִירָם‎</span></big>** **ʼĂbîyrâm** <small>*(ab-ee-rawm')*</small> ==**Abiram**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H0001|‎אָב‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H7311|‎רוּם‎]]</big>**; father of height (i.e. lofty)</small>

$\quad$ Abiram.
- § Abiram = *"my father is exalted" or "(the) Exalted One is (my) father"*
- ***Abiram***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** a Reubenite, son of Eliab in exodus
**2)** son of Hiel the Bethelite who worked to rebuild Jericho
