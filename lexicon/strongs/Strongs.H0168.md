**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֹהֶל‎</span></big>** **ʼôhel** <small>*(o'-hel)*</small> ==**tent**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H0166|‎אָהַל‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ covering, (dwelling) (place), home, tabernacle, tent.
- ***a tent*** (as clearly conspicuous from a distance)

**1)** tent
$\quad$ **1a)** nomad's tent, and thus symbolic of wilderness life, transience
$\quad$ **1b)** dwelling, home, habitation
$\quad$ **1c)** the sacred tent of Jehovah (the tabernacle)
