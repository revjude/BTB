**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נְדָבָה‎</span></big>** **nᵉdâbâh** <small>*(ned-aw-baw')*</small> ==**voluntariness**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup>
