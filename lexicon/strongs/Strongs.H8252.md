<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁקַט‎</span></big> **shâqaṭ** <small>*(shaw-kat')*</small> ==**to quiet**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ appease, idleness, (at, be at, be in, give) quiet(-ness), (be at, be in, give, have, take) rest, settle, be still.
- ***to repose*** (usually figurative)

**1)** to be quiet, be tranquil, be at peace, be quiet, rest, lie still, be undisturbed
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be quiet, be undisturbed
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** to be at peace (of land)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be quiet, be inactive
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to show quietness
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1a)** quietness, display of quietness (subst)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to quiet, be quiet
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to cause quietness, pacify, allay
