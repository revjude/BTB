<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָרָה‎</span></big> **qârâh** <small>*(kaw-raw')*</small> ==**to meet**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ appoint, lay (make) beams, befall, bring, come (to pass unto), floor, (hap) was, happen (unto), meet, send good speed.
- ***to light upon*** (chiefly by accident)
-  causatively, ***to bring about***
-  specifically, ***to impose timbers*** (for roof or floor)

**1)** to encounter, meet, befall, happen, come to meet
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to encounter, meet
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to befall
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to encounter, meet (without pre-arrangement)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to chance to be present
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to come to meet
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to cause to meet, appoint
**2)** to build with beams
$\quad$ **2a)** (Piel) to lay the beams of, furnish with beams
