**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גּוֹרָל‎</span></big>** **gôwrâl** <small>*(go-rawl')*</small> ==**allotted**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>or (shortened) **<big><span class="ashebrew">גֹּרָל</span></big>** **gôrâl** <small>*(go-ral')*</small>; from an unused root meaning to be rough (as stone)</small>

$\quad$ lot.
- properly, ***a pebble***, i.e. ***a lot*** (small stones being used for that purpose)
-  figuratively, ***a portion*** or ***destiny*** (as if determined by lot)

**1)** lot
$\quad$ **1a)** lot-pebbles used for systematically making decisions
**2)** portion
$\quad$ **2a)** lot, portion (thing assigned by casting lots)
$\quad$ **2b)** recompense, retribution
