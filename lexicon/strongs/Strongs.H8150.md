**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁנַן‎</span></big>** **shânan** <small>*(shaw-nan')*</small> ==**to sharpen**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ prick, sharp(-en), teach diligently, whet.
- ***to point*** (transitive or intransitive)
-  intensively, ***to pierce***
-  figuratively, ***to inculcate***

**1)** to sharpen, whet
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to whet, sharpen
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to sharpen, teach (incisively)
$\quad$ **1c)**(Hithpolel) to be pierced
