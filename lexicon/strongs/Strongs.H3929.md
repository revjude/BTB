<big><span class="ashebrew">‎לֶמֶךְ‎</span></big> **Lemek** <small>*(leh'-mek)*</small> ==**Lamech**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root of uncertain meaning</small>

$\quad$ Lamech.
- § Lamech = *"powerful"*
- ***Lemek***, the name of two antediluvian patriarchs

**1)** the 5th lineal descendant from Cain, husband of Adah and Zillah, father of sons, Jabal, Jubal, and Tubal-cain, and daughter, Naamah
**2)** father of Noah
