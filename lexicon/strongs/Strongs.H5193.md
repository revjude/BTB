<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָטַע‎</span></big> **nâṭaʻ** <small>*(naw-tah')*</small> ==**to plant**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ fastened, plant(-er).
- properly, ***to strike in***, i.e. ***fix***
-  specifically, ***to plant*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to plant, fasten, fix, establish
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to plant
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to plant, establish (fig.)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be planted
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be established (fig.)
