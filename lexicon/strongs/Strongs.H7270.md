- ***to walk along***
-  but only in specifically, applications, ***to reconnoiter***, ***to be a******tale-bearer*** (i.e. slander)
-  ***to lead about***

**1)** to go on foot, spy out, foot it, go about, walk along, move the feet
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be a tale-bearer, slander, go about
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to slander
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to go about as explorer, spy
$\quad$ **1c)** (Tiphel) to teach to walk
