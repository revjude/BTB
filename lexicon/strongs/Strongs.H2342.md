<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חוּל‎</span></big> **chûwl** <small>*(khool)*</small> ==**to twist: tremble**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>or <span class="ashebrew">חִיל</span> **chîyl** <small>*(kheel)*</small>; a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bear, (make to) bring forth, (make to) calve, dance, drive away, fall grievously (with pain), fear, form, great, grieve, (be) grievous, hope, look, make, be in pain, be much (sore) pained, rest, shake, shapen, (be) sorrow(-ful), stay, tarry, travail (with pain), tremble, trust, wait carefully (patiently), be wounded.
- properly, ***to twist*** or ***whirl*** (in a circular or spiral manner), i.e. (specifically) ***to dance***, ***to writhe in pain*** (especially of parturition) or ***fear***
-  figuratively, ***to wait***, ***to pervert***

**1)** to twist, whirl, dance, writhe, fear, tremble, travail, be in anguish, be pained
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to dance
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to twist, writhe
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to whirl, whirl about
$\quad$ **1b)** (Polel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to dance
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to writhe (in travail with), bear, bring forth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to wait anxiously
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pulal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to be made to writhe, be made to bear
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to be brought forth
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to be born
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpolel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** whirling (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e2)** writhing, suffering torture (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e3)** to wait longingly
$\quad$ **1f)** (Hithpalpel) to be distressed
