**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּלָה‎</span></big>** **kâlâh** <small>*(kaw-law')*</small> ==**to end: finish**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ accomplish, cease, consume (away), determine, destroy (utterly), be (when... were) done, (be an) end (of), expire, (cause to) fail, faint, finish, fulfil, [idiom] fully, [idiom] have, leave (off), long, bring to pass, wholly reap, make clean riddance, spend, quite take away, waste.
- ***to end***, whether intransitive (to cease, be finished, perish) or transitived (to complete, prepare, consume)

**1)** to accomplish, cease, consume, determine, end, fail, finish, be complete, be accomplished, be ended, be at an end, be finished, be spent
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be complete, be at an end
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be completed, be finished
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be accomplished, be fulfilled
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to be determined, be plotted (bad sense)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to be spent, be used up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to waste away, be exhausted, fail
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7)** to come to an end, vanish, perish, be destroyed
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to complete, bring to an end, finish
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to complete (a period of time)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to finish (doing a thing)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to make an end, end
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** to accomplish, fulfil, bring to pass
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b6)** to accomplish, determine (in thought)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b7)** to put an end to, cause to cease
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b8)** to cause to fail, exhaust, use up, spend
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b9)** to destroy, exterminate
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be finished, be ended, be completed
