<big><span class="ashebrew">‎דּוּשׁ‎</span></big> **dûwsh** <small>*(doosh)*</small> ==**to tread**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>or <span class="ashebrew">דּוֹשׁ</span> **dôwsh** <small>*(dosh)*</small>; or <span class="ashebrew">דִּישׁ</span> **dîysh** <small>*(deesh)*</small>; a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ break, tear, thresh, tread out (down), at grass (Jeremiah 50:11, by mistake for <span class="ashebrew">‎דֶּשֶׁא‎</span><sup><small>[[H1877]]</small></sup>).
- ***to trample*** or ***thresh***

**1)** to tread out, thresh
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to tread on, trample on, thresh
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be trampled down
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hophal) to be threshed
