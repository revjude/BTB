**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ὀρθοτομέω‎</span></big>** **orthotoméō** <small>*(or-thot-om-eh'-o)*</small> ==**to cut straight**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from a compound of **<big>[[G3717|‎ὀρθός‎]]</big>** and the base of **<big>[[G5114|‎τομώτερος‎]]</big>**, to make a straight cut, i.e. (figuratively) to dissect (expound) correctly (the divine message)</small>

$\quad$ rightly divide.
