<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שׁוּר‎</span></big> **shûwr** <small>*(shoor)*</small> ==**to see**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (identical with through the idea of going round for inspection)</small>

$\quad$ behold, lay wait, look, observe, perceive, regard, see.
- ***to spy out***, i.e. (generally) ***survey***, (for evil) ***lurk for***, (for good) ***care for***

**1)** to see, behold, observe, regard
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to behold, observe
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to regard
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to watch, watch (stealthily), lie in wait
