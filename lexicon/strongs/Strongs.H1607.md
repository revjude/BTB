**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּעַשׁ‎</span></big>** **gâʻash** <small>*(gaw-ash')*</small> ==**to shake**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ move, shake, toss, trouble.
- ***to agitate violently***

**1)** to shake, quake
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to shake
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) to be shaken up, convulsed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to shake back and forth, toss or reel to and fro
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpoel) to reel to and fro
