<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שַׁמּוּעַ‎</span></big> **Shammûwaʻ** <small>*(sham-moo'-ah)*</small> ==**Shammua**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁמֵם‎</span><sup><small>[[H8074]]</small></sup>; renowned</small>

$\quad$ Shammua, Shammuah.
- § Shammua = *"renowned"*
- ***Shammua***, the name of four Israelites

**1)** son of Zaccur and the spy from the tribe of Reuben
**2)** son of David by his wife Bathsheba
$\quad$ **2a)** also 'Shammuah' and 'Shimea'
**3)** aLevite, son of Galal and father of Abda who lived in Jerusalem after the return from exile in the time of Nehemiah
$\quad$ **3a)** also 'Shemaiah'
**4)** a priest, head of the family of Bilgah in the time of Joiakim after the return from exile
