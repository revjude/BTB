<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מוּג‎</span></big> **mûwg** <small>*(moog)*</small> ==**to melt**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ consume, dissolve, (be) faint(-hearted), melt (away), make soft.
- ***to melt***, i.e. literally (to soften, flow down, disappear), or figuratively (to fear, faint)

**1)** to melt, cause to melt
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to melt, faint
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to cause to melt
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to melt away
$\quad$ **1c)** (Polel) to soften, dissolve, dissipate
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpolel) to melt, flow
