$\quad$ shekel.
- probably ***a weight***
-  used as ***a commercial standard***

**1)** shekel
$\quad$ **1a)** the chief unit of weight or measure
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** gold-1/10000 of a talent and equal to 220 grains
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** silver-1/3000 of a talent and equal to 132 grains
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** copper-1/1500 of a talent and equal to 528 grains
