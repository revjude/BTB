<big><span class="ashebrew">‎טָרַף‎</span></big> **ṭâraph** <small>*(taw-raf')*</small> ==**to tear**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ catch, [idiom] without doubt, feed, ravin, rend in pieces, [idiom] surely, tear (in pieces).
- ***to pluck off*** or ***pull to pieces***
-  causatively ***to supply with food*** (as in morsels)

**1)** to tear, rend, pluck
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to tear, rend
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be torn in pieces
$\quad$ **1c)** (Poal) to be torn in pieces
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to provide food
