**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁכַן‎</span></big>** **shâkan** <small>*(shaw-kan')*</small> ==**to dwell**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (apparently akin (by transmission) to **<big>[[H7901|‎שָׁכַב‎]]</big>** through the idea of lodging; compare **<big>[[H5531|‎סִכְלוּת‎]]</big>**, **<big>[[H7925|‎שָׁכַם‎]]</big>**)</small>

$\quad$ abide, continue, (cause to, make to) dwell(-er), have habitation, inhabit, lay, place, (cause to) remain, rest, set (up).
- ***to reside*** or ***permanently stay*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to settle down, abide, dwell, tabernacle, reside
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to settle down to abide
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to abide, dwell, reside
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to make settle down, establish
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to make or cause to dwell
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to lay, place, set, establish, settle, fix
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to cause to dwell or abide
