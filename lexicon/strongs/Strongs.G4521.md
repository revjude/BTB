<big><span class="asgreek">‎σάββατον‎</span></big> **sábbaton** <small>*(sab'-bat-on)*</small> ==**Sabbath**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-N|G:N-N]]</small></sup> 
<small>of Hebrew origin (<span class="ashebrew">‎שַׁבָּת‎</span><sup><small>[[H7676]]</small></sup>)</small>

$\quad$ sabbath (day), week.
- the Sabbath (i.e. Shabbath), or day of weekly repose from secular avocations (also the observance or institution itself)
-  by extension, a se'nnight, i.e. the interval between two Sabbaths
-  likewise the plural in all the above applications
