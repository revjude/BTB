<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָצַק‎</span></big> **yâtsaq** <small>*(yaw-tsak')*</small> ==**to pour: pour**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cast, cleave fast, be (as) firm, grow, be hard, lay out, molten, overflow, pour (out), run out, set down, stedfast.
- properly, ***to pour out*** (transitive or intransitive)
-  by implication, ***to melt*** or ***cast as metal***
-  by extension, ***to place firmly***, ***to stiffen*** or ***grow hard***

**1)** to pour, flow, cast, pour out
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to pour, pour out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to cast
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to flow
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to pour, pour out
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to be poured
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** cast, molten (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** being firmly established (participle)
