<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָאַל‎</span></big> **yâʼal** <small>*(yaw-al')*</small> ==**be foolish**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ dote, be (become, do) foolish(-ly).
- properly, ***to be slack***, i.e. (figuratively) ***to be foolish***

**1)** to be foolish, become fools, act foolishly, show wicked folly
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to show wicked folly
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to become fools
