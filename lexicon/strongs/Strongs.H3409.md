**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָרֵךְ‎</span></big>** **yârêk** <small>*(yaw-rake')*</small> ==**thigh**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root meaning to be soft</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] body, loins, shaft, side, thigh.
- ***the thigh*** (from its fleshy softness)
-  by euphemistically ***the generative parts***
-  figuratively, ***a shank***, ***flank***, ***side***

**1)** thigh, side, loin, base
$\quad$ **1a)** thigh
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** outside of thigh (where sword was worn)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** loins (as the seat of procreative power)
$\quad$ **1b)** side (flank) (of object)
$\quad$ **1c)** base
