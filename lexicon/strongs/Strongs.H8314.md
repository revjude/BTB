**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׂרָף‎</span></big>** **sârâph** <small>*(saw-rawf')*</small> ==**serpent**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H8313|‎שָׂרַף‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ fiery (serpent), seraph.
- ***burning***, i.e. (figuratively) ***poisonous*** (serpent)
-  specifically, ***a saraph*** or ***symbolical creature*** (from their copper color)

**1)** serpent, fiery serpent
$\quad$ **1a)** poisonous serpent (fiery from burning effect of poison)
**2)** seraph, seraphim
$\quad$ **2a)** majestic beings with 6 wings, human hands or voices in attendance upon God
