**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָשַׂג‎</span></big>** **nâsag** <small>*(naw-sag')*</small> ==**to overtake**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ ability, be able, attain (unto), (be able to, can) get, lay at, put, reach, remove, wax rich, [idiom] surely, (over-) take (hold of, on, upon).
- ***to reach*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to reach, overtake, take hold upon
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to overtake
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to reach, attain to, cause to reach
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be able to secure, reach, have enough
