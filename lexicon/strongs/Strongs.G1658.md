**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἐλεύθερος‎</span></big>** **eleútheros** <small>*(el-yoo'-ther-os)*</small> ==**free/freedom**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:A|G:A]]</small></sup> 
<small>probably from the alternate of **<big>[[G2064|‎ἔρχομαι‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ free (man, woman), at liberty.
- unrestrained (to go at pleasure), i.e. (as a citizen) not a slave (whether freeborn or manumitted), or (genitive case) exempt (from obligation or liability)
