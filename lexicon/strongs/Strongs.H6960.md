<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָוָה‎</span></big> **qâvâh** <small>*(kaw-vaw')*</small> ==**to await**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ gather (together), look, patiently, tarry, wait (for, on, upon).
- ***to bind together*** (perhaps by twisting), i.e. ***collect***
-  (figuratively) ***to expect***

**1)** to wait, look for, hope, expect
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) waiting (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to wait or look eagerly for
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to lie in wait for
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to wait for, linger for
**2)** to collect, bind together
$\quad$ **2a)** (Niphal) to be collected
