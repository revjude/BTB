**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָבַשׁ‎</span></big>** **châbash** <small>*(khaw-bash')*</small> ==**to saddle/tie**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bind (up), gird about, govern, healer, put, saddle, wrap about.
- ***to wrap firmly*** (especially a turban, compress, or saddle)
-  figuratively, ***to stop***, ***to rule***

**1)** to tie, bind, bind on, bind up, saddle, restrain, bandage, govern
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to bind, bind on
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to bind up
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to bind, restrain
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be bound up
