$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to test (and prove true)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** smelter, refiner, goldsmith (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be refined
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to be a refiner
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** refiner (participle)
