<big><span class="ashebrew">‎סָבַל‎</span></big> **çâbal** <small>*(saw-bal')*</small> ==**to bear**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bear, be a burden, carry, strong to labour.
- ***to carry*** (literally or figuratively), or (reflexively) ***be burdensome***
-  specifically, ***to be gravid***

**1)** to bear, bear a load, drag oneself along
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to bear (a load)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) laden (participle)
$\quad$ **1c)**(Hithpael) to make oneself a burden, drag oneself along
