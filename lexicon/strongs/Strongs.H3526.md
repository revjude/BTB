**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּבַס‎</span></big>** **kâbaç** <small>*(kaw-bas')*</small> ==**Washer's**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ fuller, wash(-ing).
- ***to trample***
-  hence, ***to wash*** (properly, by stamping with the feet), ***whether*** literal (including the fulling process) or figurative

**1)** to wash (by treading), be washed, perform the work of a fuller
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) washer, fuller, treader (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to wash (garments, person)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be washed
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hothpael) to be washed out
