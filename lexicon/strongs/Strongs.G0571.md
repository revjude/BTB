<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἄπιστος‎</span></big> **ápistos** <small>*(ap'-is-tos)*</small> ==**unbelieving**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:A|G:A]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="asgreek">Α</span><sup><small>[[G0001]]</small></sup> (as a negative particle) and <span class="asgreek">πιστός</span><sup><small>[[G4103]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ that believeth not, faithless, incredible thing, infidel, unbeliever(-ing).
- (actively) disbelieving, i.e. without Christian faith (specially, a heathen)
-  (passively) untrustworthy (person), or incredible (thing)
