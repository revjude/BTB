<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁפַךְ‎</span></big> **shâphak** <small>*(shaw-fak')*</small> ==**to pour: pour**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cast (up), gush out, pour (out), shed(-der, out), slip.
- ***to spill forth*** (blood, a libation, liquid metal
-  or even a solid, i.e. to mound up)
-  also (figuratively) ***to expend*** (life, soul, complaint, money, etc.)
-  intensively, ***to sprawl out***

**1)** to pour, pour out, spill
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to pour, pour out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to shed (blood)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to pour out (anger or heart) (fig)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be poured out, be shed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be poured out, be shed
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to be poured out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to pour out oneself
