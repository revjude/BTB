**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מֶשֶׁךְ‎</span></big>** **Meshek** <small>*(meh'-shek)*</small> ==**Meshech**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>the same in form as **<big>[[H4901|‎מֶשֶׁךְ‎]]</big>**, but probably of foreign derivation</small>

$\quad$ Mesech, Meshech.
- § Mesech or Meshech = *"drawing out"*
- ***Meshek***, a son of Japheth, and the people descended from him

**1)** son of Japheth, grandson of Noah, and progenitor of peoples to the north of Israel
$\quad$ **1a)** descendants of Mesech often mentioned in connection with Tubal, Magog, and other northern nations including the Moschi, a people on the borders of Colchis and Armenia
