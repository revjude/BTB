<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּסַח‎</span></big> **pâçach** <small>*(paw-sakh')*</small> ==**to pass**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ halt, become lame, leap, pass over.
- ***to hop***, i.e. (figuratively) ***skip over*** (or spare)
-  by implication, ***to hesitate***
-  also (literally) ***to limp***, ***to dance***

**1)** to pass over, spring over
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to pass over
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to skip, pass over
**2)** to limp
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to limp
$\quad$ **2b)** (Niphal) to be lame
$\quad$ **2c)** (Piel) to limp
