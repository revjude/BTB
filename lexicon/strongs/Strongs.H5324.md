<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָצַב‎</span></big> **nâtsab** <small>*(naw-tsab')*</small> ==**to stand**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ appointed, deputy, erect, establish, [idiom] Huzzah (by mistake for a proper name), lay, officer, pillar, present, rear up, set (over, up), settle, sharpen, establish, (make to) stand(-ing, still, up, upright), best state.
- ***to station***, in various applications (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to stand, take one's stand, stand upright, be set (over), establish
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to station oneself, take one's stand
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to stand, be stationed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to stand, take an upright position
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to be stationed, be appointed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** deputy, prefect, appointed, deputed (subst)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to stand firm
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to station, set
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to set up, erect
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to cause to stand erect
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to fix, establish
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hophal) to be fixed, be determined, be stationary
