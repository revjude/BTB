<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קוּץ‎</span></big> **qûwts** <small>*(koots)*</small> ==**to loathe**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (identical with through the idea of severing oneself from (compare <span class="ashebrew">‎קוּט‎</span><sup><small>[[H6962]]</small></sup>))</small>

$\quad$ abhor, be distressed, be grieved, loathe, vex, be weary.
- ***to be*** (causatively, make) ***disgusted*** or ***anxious***

**1)** to be grieved, loathe, abhor, feel a loathing or abhorrence or sickening dread
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to feel a loathing at, abhor
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to feel a sickening dread
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cause sickening dread
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to cause loathing
