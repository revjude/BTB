<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁקַד‎</span></big> **shâqad** <small>*(shaw-kad')*</small> ==**to watch**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ hasten, remain, wake, watch (for).
- ***to be alert***, i.e. ***sleepless***
-  hence ***to be on the lookout*** (whether for good or ill)

**1)** to wake, watch, awake, be alert
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to keep watch of, be wakeful over
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be wakeful, wake (as mourner or sufferer)
