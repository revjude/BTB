<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עֲמָשַׂי‎</span></big> **ʻĂmâsay** <small>*(am-aw-sah'-ee)*</small> ==**Amasai**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎עָמַס‎</span><sup><small>[[H6006]]</small></sup>; burdensome</small>

$\quad$ Amasai.
- § Amasai = *"burdensome"*
- ***Amasai***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** a warrior and chief of the captains of Judah and Benjamin who joined David at Ziklag
**2)** a Kohathite Levite, father of Mahath and ancestor of Samuel
**3)** a priest who blew the trumpet before the ark
**4)** another Kohathite Levite in the reign of king Hezekiah of Judah
