<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָזַק‎</span></big> **châzaq** <small>*(khaw-zak')*</small> ==**to strengthen: strengthen**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ aid, amend, [idiom] calker, catch, cleave, confirm, be constant, constrain, continue, be of good (take) courage(-ous, -ly), encourage (self), be established, fasten, force, fortify, make hard, harden, help, (lay) hold (fast), lean, maintain, play the man, mend, become (wax) mighty, prevail, be recovered, repair, retain, seize, be (wax) sore, strengthen (self), be stout, be (make, shew, wax) strong(-er), be sure, take (hold), be urgent, behave self valiantly, withstand.
- ***to fasten upon***
-  hence, ***to seize***, ***be strong*** (figuratively, courageous, causatively strengthen, cure, help, repair, fortify), ***obstinate***
-  ***to bind***, ***restrain***, ***conquer***

**1)** to strengthen, prevail, harden, be strong, become strong, be courageous, be firm, grow firm, be resolute, be sore
