<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שֵׁבֶט‎</span></big> **shêbeṭ** <small>*(shay'-bet)*</small> ==**tribe: staff**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root probably meaning to branch off</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] correction, dart, rod, sceptre, staff, tribe.
- ***a scion***, i.e. (literally) ***a stick*** (for punishing, writing, fighting, ruling, walking, etc.) or (figuratively) ***a clan***

**1)** rod, staff, branch, offshoot, club, sceptre, tribe
$\quad$ **1a)** rod, staff
$\quad$ **1b)** shaft (of spear, dart)
$\quad$ **1c)** club (of shepherd's implement)
$\quad$ **1d)** truncheon, sceptre (mark of authority)
$\quad$ **1e)** clan, tribe
