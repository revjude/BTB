<big><span class="ashebrew">‎סָפָה‎</span></big> **çâphâh** <small>*(saw-faw')*</small> ==**to snatch**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ add, augment, consume, destroy, heap, join, perish, put.
- properly, ***to scrape*** (literally, to shave
-  but usually figuratively) ***together*** (i.e. to accumulate or increase) or ***away*** (i.e. to scatter, remove, or ruin
-  intransitively, to perish)

**1)** to sweep or snatch away, catch up, destroy, consume
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be snatched away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to sweep or snatch away
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be swept away, be destroyed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be caught up, be captured
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to catch up, gather
