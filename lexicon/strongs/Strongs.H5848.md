**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָטַף‎</span></big>** **ʻâṭaph** <small>*(aw-taf')*</small> ==**to turn aside**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cover (over), fail, faint, feebler, hide self, be overwhelmed, swoon.
- ***to shroud***, i.e. ***clothe*** (whether transitive or reflex.)
-  hence (from the idea of darkness) ***to languish***

**1)** to turn aside
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to turn aside, turn (in order to cover)
**2)** to envelop oneself
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to envelop oneself, cover
**3)** to be feeble, be faint, grow weak
$\quad$ **3a)** (Qal) to be feeble, be faint
$\quad$ **3b)** (Niphal) to faint
$\quad$ **3c)** (Hiphil) to show feebleness
$\quad$ **3d)** (Hithpael) to faint, faint away
