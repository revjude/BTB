**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁמַד‎</span></big>** **shâmad** <small>*(shaw-mad')*</small> ==**to destroy**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ destory(-uction), bring to nought, overthrow, perish, pluck down, [idiom] utterly.
- ***to desolate***

**1)** to destroy, exterminate, be destroyed, be exterminated
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be annihilated, be exterminated
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be destroyed, be devastated
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to annihilate, exterminate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to destroy
