**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּרַע‎</span></big>** **gâraʻ** <small>*(gaw-rah')*</small> ==**to dimish**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ abate, clip, (di-) minish, do (take) away, keep back, restrain, make small, withdraw.
- ***to scrape off***
-  by implication, ***to shave***, ***remove***, ***lessen***, ***withhold***

**1)** to diminish, restrain, withdraw, abate, keep back, do away, take from, clip
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to diminish
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to restrain
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to withdraw
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be withdrawn
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be restrained
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to withdraw, draw up
