**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָנֶה‎</span></big>** **mâneh** <small>*(maw-neh')*</small> ==**mina**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H4487|‎מָנָה‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ maneh, pound.
- properly, ***a fixed weight*** or ***measured amount***, i.e. (techn.) ***a maneh*** or ***mina***

**1)** maneh, mina, pound
$\quad$ **1a)** 60 shekels and 1/50 talent (of silver)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** 1/60 talent in early Babylonian standard
$\quad$ **1b)** 100 shekels and 1/100 talent (of gold)
