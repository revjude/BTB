<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָגַע‎</span></big> **nâgaʻ** <small>*(naw-gah')*</small> ==**to touch**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ beat, ([idiom] be able to) bring (down), cast, come (nigh), draw near (nigh), get up, happen, join, near, plague, reach (up), smite, strike, touch.
- properly, ***to touch***, i.e. ***lay the hand upon*** (for any purpose
-  euphemistically, to lie with a woman)
-  by implication, ***to reach*** (figuratively, to arrive, acquire)
-  ***violently***, ***to strike*** (punish, defeat, destroy, etc.)

**1)** to touch, reach, strike
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to touch
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to strike
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to reach, extend to
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to be stricken
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4a)** stricken (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be stricken, be defeated
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to strike
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be stricken (by disease)
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hiphil) to cause to touch, reach, approach, arrive
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** to cause to touch, apply
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e2)** to reach, extend, attain, arrive, come
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e3)** to approach (of time)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e4)** to befall (of fate)
