$\quad$ (in) all (manner, (ye)), altogether, any (manner), enough, every (one, place, thing), howsoever, as many as, (no-) thing, ought, whatsoever, (the) whole, whoso(-ever).

$\quad$ **1b)** any, each, every, anything
$\quad$ **1c)** totality, everything
