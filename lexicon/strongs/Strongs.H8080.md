<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁמַן‎</span></big> **shâman** <small>*(shaw-man')*</small> ==**to grow fat**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ become (make, wax) fat.
- ***to shine***, i.e. (by analogy) ***be*** (causatively, make) ***oily*** or ***gross***

**1)** to be or become fat, grow fat
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) fat (subst)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to make fat, show fatness
