**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָרַב‎</span></big>** **qârab** <small>*(kaw-rab')*</small> ==**to present: come**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (cause to) approach, (cause to) bring (forth, near), (cause to) come (near, nigh), (cause to) draw near (nigh), go (near), be at hand, join, be near, offer, present, produce, make ready, stand, take.
- ***to approach*** (causatively, bring near) for whatever purpose

**1)** to come near, approach, enter into, draw near
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to approach, draw near
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be brought near
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to cause to approach, bring near, cause to draw near
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to bring near, bring, present
