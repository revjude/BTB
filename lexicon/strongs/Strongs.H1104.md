<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּלַע‎</span></big> **bâlaʻ** <small>*(baw-lah')*</small> ==**to swallow up**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cover, destroy, devour, eat up, be at end, spend up, swallow down (up).
- ***to make away with*** (specifically by swallowing)
-  generally, ***to destroy***

**1)** to swallow down, swallow up, engulf, eat up
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to swallow down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to swallow up, engulf
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be swallowed up
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to swallow
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to swallow up, engulf
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** squandering (fig.)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be swallowed up
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to be ended
