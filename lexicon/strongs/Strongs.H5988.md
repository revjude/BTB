**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עַמִּיאֵל‎</span></big>** **ʻAmmîyʼêl** <small>*(am-mee-ale')*</small> ==**Ammiel**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5971|‎עַם‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H0410|‎אֵל‎]]</big>**; people of God</small>

$\quad$ Ammiel.
- § Ammiel = *"my kinsman is God"*
- ***Ammiel***, the name of three or four Israelites

**1)** the spy from the tribe of Dan who perished in the plague for his evil report
**2)** father of Machir of Lo-debar
**3)** father of Bathsheba; also 'Eliam'
**4)** the 6th son of Obed-edom and doorkeeper of the temple
