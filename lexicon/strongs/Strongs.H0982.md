<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּטַח‎</span></big> **bâṭach** <small>*(baw-takh')*</small> ==**to trust**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup>

- figuratively, ***to trust***, ***be confident*** or ***sure***

**1)** to trust

$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to trust, trust in
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to have confidence, be confident
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be bold
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to be secure
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cause to trust, make secure
