<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָחַז‎</span></big> **ʼâchaz** <small>*(aw-khaz')*</small> ==**to grasp**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [phrase] be affrighted, bar, (catch, lay, take) hold (back), come upon, fasten, handle, portion, (get, have or take) possess(-ion).
- ***to seize*** (often with the accessory idea of holding in possession)

**1)** grasp, take hold, seize, take possession
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to grasp, take hold of
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be caught, grasped, be settled
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to enclose, overlay
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) fastened
