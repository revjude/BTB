**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עַד‎</span></big>** **ʻad** <small>*(ad)*</small> ==**perpetuity**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5710|‎עָדָה‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ eternity, ever(-lasting, -more), old, perpetually, [phrase] world without end.
- properly, ***a*** (peremptory) ***terminus***, i.e. (by implication) ***duration***, in the sense of ***advance*** or ***perpetuity*** (substantially as a noun, either with or without a preposition)

**1)** perpetuity, for ever, continuing future
$\quad$ **1a)** ancient (of past time)
$\quad$ **1b)** for ever (of future time)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** of continuous existence
$\quad$ **1c)** for ever (of God's existence)
