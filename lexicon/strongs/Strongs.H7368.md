<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָחַק‎</span></big> **râchaq** <small>*(raw-khak')*</small> ==**to remove**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (a-, be, cast, drive, get, go, keep (self), put, remove, be too, (wander), withdraw) far (away, off), loose, [idiom] refrain, very, (be) a good way (off).
- ***to widen*** (in any direction), i.e. (intransitively) ***recede*** or (transitively) ***remove*** (literally or figuratively, of place or relation)

v
**1)** to be or become far, be or become distant, be removed, go far away
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be far, be distant
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to send far away, extend
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to make or exhibit distance, be gone far
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to remove, put far away
**2)** (Niphal) loose
v inf (as adv)
**3)** at a distance
