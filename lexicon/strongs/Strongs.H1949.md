<big><span class="ashebrew">‎הוּם‎</span></big> **hûwm** <small>*(hoom)*</small> ==**to make noise**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (compare <span class="ashebrew">‎הָמַם‎</span><sup><small>[[H2000]]</small></sup>)</small>

$\quad$ destroy, move, make a noise, put, ring again.
- ***to make an uproar***, or ***agitate greatly***

**1)** to distract, ring again, make a (great) noise, murmur, roar, discomfit, be moved
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to discomfit
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be in a stir
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to murmur
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to show disquietude
