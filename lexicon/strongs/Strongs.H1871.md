$\quad$ dram.
- ***a 'drachma'***, or ***coin***

**1)** daric, drachma, dram, unit of measure
$\quad$ **1a)** a gold coin current in Palestine in the period after the return from Babylon; 128 grains (8.32 grams) of gold worth about $120 dollars and 128 grains of silver worth about $2.40
