<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָרֵא‎</span></big> **yârêʼ** <small>*(yaw-ray')*</small> ==**to fear**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ affright, be (make) afraid, dread(-ful), (put in) fear(-ful, -fully, -ing), (be had in) reverence(-end), [idiom] see, terrible (act, -ness, thing).
- ***to fear***
-  morally, ***to revere***
-  ***caus***. ***to frighten***

**1)** to fear, revere, be afraid
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to fear, be afraid
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to stand in awe of, be awed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to fear, reverence, honour, respect
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be fearful, be dreadful, be feared
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to cause astonishment and awe, be held in awe
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to inspire reverence or godly fear or awe
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to make afraid, terrify
**2)** (TWOT) to shoot, pour
