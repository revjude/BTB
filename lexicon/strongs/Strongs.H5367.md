**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָקַשׁ‎</span></big>** **nâqash** <small>*(naw-kash')*</small> ==**to snare**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ catch (lay a) snare.
- ***to entrap*** (with a noose), literally or figuratively

**1)** to knock, strike, bring down
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to strike down
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be thrust after, be impelled
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to strike at, take aim at
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael) to be struck
**2)** (CLBL) to ensnare
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to ensnare
$\quad$ **2b)** (Niphal) to be ensnared
$\quad$ **2c)** (Piel) to lay snares
$\quad$ **2d)** (Hithpael) to lay snares
