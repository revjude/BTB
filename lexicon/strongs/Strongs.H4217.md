**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מִזְרָח‎</span></big>** **mizrâch** <small>*(miz-rawkh')*</small> ==**East (Gate)**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H2224|‎זָרַח‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ east (side, -ward), (sun-) rising (of the sun).
- ***sunrise***, i.e. ***the east***

**1)** place of sunrise, east
$\quad$ **1a)** sunrise, east (with 'sun')
$\quad$ **1b)** the east (without 'sun')
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to or toward the place of sunrise
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to the east, eastward
