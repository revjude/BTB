<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּתַח‎</span></big> **pâthach** <small>*(paw-thakh')*</small> ==**to open**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ appear, break forth, draw (out), let go free, (en-) grave(-n), loose (self), (be, be set) open(-ing), put off, ungird, unstop, have vent.
- ***to open wide*** (literally or figuratively)
-  specifically, ***to loosen***, ***begin***, ***plough***, ***carve***

**1)** to open
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to open
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be opened, be let loose, be thrown open
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to free
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to loosen
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to open, open oneself
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael) to loose oneself
**2)** to carve, engrave
$\quad$ **2a)** (Piel) to engrave
$\quad$ **2b)** (Pual) to be engraved
