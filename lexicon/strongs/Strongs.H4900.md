<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָשַׁךְ‎</span></big> **mâshak** <small>*(maw-shak')*</small> ==**to draw**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ draw (along, out), continue, defer, extend, forbear, [idiom] give, handle, make (pro-, sound) long, [idiom] sow, scatter, stretch out.
- ***to draw***, used in a great variety of applications (including to sow, to sound, to prolong, to develop, to march, to remove, to delay, to be tall, etc.)

**1)** to draw, drag, seize
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to draw (and lift out), drag along, lead along, drag or lead off, draw down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to draw (the bow)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to proceed, march
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to draw out or give (a sound)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to draw out, prolong, continue
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to trail (seed in sowing)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7)** to cheer, draw, attract, gratify
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be drawn out
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to be drawn out, be postponed, be deferred
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to be tall
