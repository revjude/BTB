**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עַשְׁתֹּרֶת‎</span></big>** **ʻAshtôreth** <small>*(ash-to'reth)*</small> ==**Ashtoreth**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-F-T|N:N-F-T]]</small></sup> 
<small>probably for **<big>[[H6251|‎עַשְׁתְּרָה‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ Ashtoreth.
- § Ashtoreth = *"star"*
- ***Ashtoreth***, the Phoenician goddess of love (and increase)

**1)** the principal female deity of the Phoenicians worshipped in war and fertility
$\quad$ **1a)** also 'Ishtar' of Assyria and 'Astarte' by the Greeks and Romans
