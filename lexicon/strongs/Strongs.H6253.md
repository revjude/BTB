$\quad$ Ashtoreth.
- § Ashtoreth = *"star"*
- ***Ashtoreth***, the Phoenician goddess of love (and increase)

**1)** the principal female deity of the Phoenicians worshipped in war and fertility
$\quad$ **1a)** also 'Ishtar' of Assyria and 'Astarte' by the Greeks and Romans
