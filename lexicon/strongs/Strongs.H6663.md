<big><span class="ashebrew">‎צָדַק‎</span></big> **tsâdaq** <small>*(tsaw-dak')*</small> ==**to justify**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cleanse, clear self, (be, do) just(-ice, -ify, -ify self), (be turn to) righteous(-ness).
- ***to be*** (causatively, make) ***right*** (in a moral or forensic sense)

**1)** to be just, be righteous
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to have a just cause, be in the right
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be justified
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be just (of God)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to be just, be righteous (in conduct and character)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be put or made right, be justified
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) justify, make to appear righteous, make someone righteous
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to do or bring justice (in administering law)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to declare righteous, justify
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to justify, vindicate the cause of, save
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d4)** to make righteous, turn to righteousness
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to justify oneself
