<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּרַס‎</span></big> **pâraç** <small>*(paw-ras')*</small> ==**to divide**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ deal, divide, have hoofs, part, tear.
- ***to break in pieces***, i.e. (usually without violence) ***to split***, ***distribute***

**1)** to divide, break in two
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to break, break up
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to be divided (of hoofs)
