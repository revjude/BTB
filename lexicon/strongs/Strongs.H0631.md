<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָסַר‎</span></big> **ʼâçar** <small>*(aw-sar')*</small> ==**to bind**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bind, fast, gird, harness, hold, keep, make ready, order, prepare, prison(-er), put in bonds, set in array, tie.
- ***to yoke*** or ***hitch***
-  by analogy, ***to fasten*** in any sense, ***to join battle***

**1)** to tie, bind, imprison
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to tie, bind
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to tie, harness
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to bind (with cords)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to gird (rare and late)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to begin the battle, make the attack
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** of obligation of oath (figurative)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be imprisoned, bound
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be taken prisoner
