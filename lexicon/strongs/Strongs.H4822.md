<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מְרֵמוֹת‎</span></big> **Mᵉrêmôwth** <small>*(mer-ay-mohth')*</small> ==**Meremoth**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>plural from <span class="ashebrew">‎רוּם‎</span><sup><small>[[H7311]]</small></sup>; heights</small>

$\quad$ Meremoth.
- § Meremoth = *"elevations"*
- ***Meremoth***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** a priest, son of Uriah of the family of Koz active in rebuilding the wall of Jerusalem and in the 7th course of temple service in the time of Ezra and Nehemiah
**2)** a priest in the time of Zerubbabel
**3)** a returning exile of the family of Bani who had a foreign wife in the time of Ezra
