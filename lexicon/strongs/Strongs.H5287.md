<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָעַר‎</span></big> **nâʻar** <small>*(naw-ar')*</small> ==**to shake**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (probably identical with <span class="ashebrew">‎נָעַר‎</span><sup><small>[[H5286]]</small></sup>, through the idea of the rustling of mane, which usually accompanies the lion's roar)</small>

$\quad$ shake (off, out, self), overthrow, toss up and down.
- ***to tumble about***

**1)** to shake, shake out or off
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to shake out, show emptiness
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be shaken
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to shake oneself
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to shake off or out
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael) to shake oneself
