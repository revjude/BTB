**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἀποκρίνομαι‎</span></big>** **apokrínomai** <small>*(ap-ok-ree'-nom-ahee)*</small> ==**to answer**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G0575|‎ἀπό‎]]</big>** and <greek BETA="KRI/NW" unicode="κρίνω" translit="krínō"/></small>

$\quad$ answer.
- to conclude for oneself, i.e. (by implication) to respond
-  by Hebraism (compare **<big>[[H6030|‎עָנָה‎]]</big>**) to begin to speak (where an address is expected)
