$\quad$ alone, apart, bar, besides, branch, by self, of each alike, except, only, part, staff, strength.
- properly, ***separation***
-  by implication, ***a part of the body***, ***branch of a******tree***, ***bar for******carrying***
-  figuratively, ***chief of*** a city
-  especially (with prepositional prefix) as an adverb, apart, only, besides

**1)** alone, by itself, besides, a part, separation, being alone
$\quad$ **1a)** separation, alone, by itself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** only (adv)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** apart from, besides (prep)
$\quad$ **1b)** part
$\quad$ **1c)** parts (eg limbs, shoots), bars
