<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָרַשׁ‎</span></big> **yârash** <small>*(yaw-rash')*</small> ==**to possess: possess**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>or <span class="ashebrew">יָרֵשׁ</span> **yârêsh** <small>*(yaw-raysh')*</small>; a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cast out, consume, destroy, disinherit, dispossess, drive(-ing) out, enjoy, expel, [idiom] without fail, (give to, leave for) inherit(-ance, -or) [phrase] magistrate, be (make) poor, come to poverty, (give to, make to) possess, get (have) in (take) possession, seize upon, succeed, [idiom] utterly.
- ***to occupy*** (by driving out previous tenants, and possessing in their place)
-  by implication, ***to seize***, ***to rob***, ***to inherit***
-  also ***to expel***, ***to impoverish***, ***to ruin***

**1)** to seize, dispossess, take possession off, inherit, disinherit, occupy, impoverish, be an heir
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to take possession of
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to inherit
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to impoverish, come to poverty, be poor
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be dispossessed, be impoverished, come to poverty
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to devour
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to cause to possess or inherit
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to cause others to possess or inherit
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to impoverish
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d4)** to dispossess
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d5)** to destroy, bring to ruin, disinherit
