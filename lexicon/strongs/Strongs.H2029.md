<big><span class="ashebrew">‎הָרָה‎</span></big> **hârâh** <small>*(haw-raw')*</small> ==**to conceive**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ been, be with child, conceive, progenitor.
- ***to be*** (or become) ***pregnant***, ***conceive*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to conceive, become pregnant, bear, be with child, be conceived, progenitor
$\quad$ **1a)**(Qal) to conceive, become pregnant
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) to be conceived
$\quad$ **1c)** (Poel) to conceive, contrive, devise
