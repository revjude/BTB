**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָמֵץ‎</span></big>** **châmêts** <small>*(khaw-mates')*</small> ==**to leaven**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cruel (man), dyed, be grieved, leavened.
- ***to be pungent***
-  i.e. ***in taste*** (sour, i.e. literally fermented, or figuratively, harsh), ***in color*** (dazzling)

**1)** to be leavened, be sour
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be leavened
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to taste something leavened
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to be embittered, grieved
**2)** to be cruel, oppress, be ruthless
**3)** to be red
