<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מַחֲנֶה‎</span></big> **machăneh** <small>*(makh-an-eh')*</small> ==**camp**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎חָנָה‎</span><sup><small>[[H2583]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ army, band, battle, camp, company, drove, host, tents.
- ***an encampment*** (of travellers or troops)
-  hence, ***an army***, whether literal (of soldiers) or figurative (of dancers, angels, cattle, locusts, stars
-  or even the sacred courts)

**1)** encampment, camp
$\quad$ **1a)** camp, place of encampment
$\quad$ **1b)** camp of armed host, army camp
$\quad$ **1c)** those who encamp, company, body of people
