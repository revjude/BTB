**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מוּת‎</span></big>** **mûwth** <small>*(mooth)*</small> ==**to die**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] at all, [idiom] crying, (be) dead (body, man, one), (put to, worthy of) death, destroy(-er), (cause to, be like to, must) die, kill, necro(-mancer), [idiom] must needs, slay, [idiom] surely, [idiom] very suddenly, [idiom] in (no) wise.
- ***to die*** (literally or figuratively)
-  causatively, ***to kill***

**1)** to die, kill, have one executed
$\quad$ **1a)**(Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to die
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to die (as penalty), be put to death
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to die, perish (of a nation)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to die prematurely (by neglect of wise moral conduct)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Polel) to kill, put to death, dispatch
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to kill, put to death
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to be killed, be put to death
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1a)** to die prematurely
