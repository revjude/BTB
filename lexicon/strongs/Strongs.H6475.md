**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּצָה‎</span></big>** **pâtsâh** <small>*(paw-tsaw')*</small> ==**to open**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ deliver, gape, open, rid, utter.
- ***to rend***, i.e. ***open*** (especially the mouth)

**1)** to part, open, separate, set free
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to open (mouth), utter
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to snatch away, set free
