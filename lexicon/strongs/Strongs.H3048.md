**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יְדַעְיָה‎</span></big>** **Yᵉdaʻyâh** <small>*(yed-ah-yaw')*</small> ==**Jedaiah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H3045|‎יָדַע‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3050|‎יָהּ‎]]</big>**; Jah has known</small>

$\quad$ Jedaiah.
- § Jedaiah = *"Jehovah has known"*
- ***Jedajah***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** a priest in Jerusalem
**2)** a man who returned with Zerubbabel
**3)** a ruler of the priests in the days of Jeshua
**4)** a post exilic priest
**5)** one honoured by Jehovah, perhaps one of the above
