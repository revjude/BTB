<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עוּר‎</span></big> **ʻûwr** <small>*(oor)*</small> ==**to rouse**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (rather identical with through the idea of opening the eyes)</small>

$\quad$ (a-) wake(-n, up), lift up (self), [idiom] master, raise (up), stir up (self).
- ***to wake*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to rouse oneself, awake, awaken, incite
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to rouse oneself, awake
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be roused
$\quad$ **1c)** (Polel) to stir up, rouse, incite
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpolel) to be excited, be triumphant
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** to rouse, stir up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e2)** to act in an aroused manner, awake
