<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁכֹל‎</span></big> **shâkôl** <small>*(shaw-kole')*</small> ==**be bereaved**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bereave (of children), barren, cast calf (fruit, young), be (make) childless, deprive, destroy, [idiom] expect, lose children, miscarry, rob of children, spoil.
- properly, ***to miscarry***, i.e. ***suffer abortion***
-  by analogy, ***to bereave*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to be bereaved, make childless, miscarry
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be bereaved
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to make childless
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to cause barrenness, show barrenness or abortion
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to miscarry
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) miscarrying (participle)
