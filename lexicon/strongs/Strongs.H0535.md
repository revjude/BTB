<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָמַל‎</span></big> **ʼâmal** <small>*(aw-mal')*</small> ==**to weaken**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ languish, be weak, wax feeble.
- ***to droop***
-  by implication ***to be sick***, ***to mourn***

**1)** to be weak, to droop, to languish, to be exhausted
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) pass participle (of the heart)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be weak
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to droop
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pulal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be or grow feeble
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to languish
