**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎צָעַד‎</span></big>** **tsâʻad** <small>*(tsaw-ad')*</small> ==**to march**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bring, go, march (through), run over.
- ***to pace***, i.e. ***step regularly***
-  (upward) ***to mount***
-  (along) ***to march***
-  (down and causatively) ***to hurl***

**1)** to step, march, stride
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to step, march
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to cause to march
