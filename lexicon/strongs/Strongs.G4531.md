**<big><span class="asgreek">‎σαλεύω‎</span></big>** **saleúō** <small>*(sal-yoo'-o)*</small> ==**to shake**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4535|‎σάλος‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ move, shake (together), which can(-not) be shaken, stir up.
- to waver, i.e. agitate, rock, topple or (by implication) destroy
-  figuratively, to disturb, incite
