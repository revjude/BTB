<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָנַן‎</span></big> **ʻânan** <small>*(aw-nan')*</small> ==**to cloud**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; to cover; used only as a denominative from <span class="ashebrew">‎עָנָן‎</span><sup><small>[[H6051]]</small></sup>, to cloud over</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] bring, enchanter, Meonemin, observe(-r of) times, soothsayer, sorcerer.
- figuratively, ***to act covertly***, i.e. ***practise magic***

**1)** (Piel) to make appear, produce, bring (clouds)
**2)** (Poel) to practise soothsaying, conjure
$\quad$ **2a)** to observe times, practice soothsaying or spiritism or magic or augury or witchcraft
$\quad$ **2b)** soothsayer, enchanter, sorceress, diviner, fortuneteller, barbarian, Meonenim (participle)
