<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֵל‎</span></big> **ʼêl** <small>*(ale)*</small> ==**God**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>shortened from <span class="ashebrew">‎אַיִל‎</span><sup><small>[[H0352]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ God (god), [idiom] goodly, [idiom] great, idol, might(-y one), power, strong. Compare names in '-el.'
- ***strength***
-  ***as adjective***, ***mighty***
-  especially ***the Almighty*** (but used also of any deity)

**1)** god, god-like one, mighty one
$\quad$ **1a)** mighty men, men of rank, mighty heroes
$\quad$ **1b)** angels
$\quad$ **1c)** god, false god, (demons, imaginations)
$\quad$ **1d)** God, the one true God, Jehovah
**2)** mighty things in nature
**3)** strength, power
