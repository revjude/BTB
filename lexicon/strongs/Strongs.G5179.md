<big><span class="asgreek">‎τύπος‎</span></big> **týpos** <small>*(too'-pos)*</small> ==**mark/example**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-M|G:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="asgreek">τύπτω</span><sup><small>[[G5180]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ en-(ex-)ample, fashion, figure, form, manner, pattern, print.
- a die (as struck), i.e. (by implication) a stamp or scar
-  by analogy, a shape, i.e. a statue, (figuratively) style or resemblance
-  specially, a sampler ("type"), i.e. a model (for imitation) or instance (for warning)
