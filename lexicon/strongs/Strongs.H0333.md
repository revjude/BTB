$\quad$ Ater.
- § Ater = *"binder"*
- ***Ater***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** an exiled descendant of Hezekiah
**2)** an exiled Levite porter (perhaps same as 1)
**3)** a chief Israelite who sealed Nehemiah's covenant
