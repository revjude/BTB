<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֲשֵׁרָה‎</span></big> **ʼăshêrâh** <small>*(ash-ay-raw')*</small> ==**Asherah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-F-T|N:N-F-T]]</small></sup> 
<small>or <span class="ashebrew">אֲשֵׁירָה</span> **ʼăshêyrâh** <small>*(ash-ayraw')*</small>; from <span class="ashebrew">‎אָשַׁר‎</span><sup><small>[[H0833]]</small></sup>; happy</small>

$\quad$ grove. Compare <span class="ashebrew">‎עַשְׁתֹּרֶת‎</span><sup><small>[[H6253]]</small></sup>.
- § Ashera(h) = *"groves (for idol worship)"*
- ***Asherah*** (or Astarte) a Phoenician goddess
-  also an image of the same

**1)** a Babylonian (Astarte)-Canaanite goddess (of fortune and happiness), the supposed consort of Baal, her images
$\quad$ **1a)** the goddess, goddesses
$\quad$ **1b)** her images
$\quad$ **1c)** sacred trees or poles set up near an altar
