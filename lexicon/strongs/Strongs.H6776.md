<small>a yoke or team (i.e. pair)</small>

$\quad$ acre, couple, [idiom] together, two (donkeys), yoke (of oxen).
- hence, ***an acre*** (i.e. day's task for a yoke of cattle to plough)

**1)** couple, pair, team, yoke
$\quad$ **1a)** couple, pair, span (usually of animals)
$\quad$ **1b)** acre
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** the amount of land a span of oxen could plow in one day
