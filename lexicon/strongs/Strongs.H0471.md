<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֶלְיָקִים‎</span></big> **ʼElyâqîym** <small>*(el-yaw-keem')*</small> ==**Eliakim**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎אֵל‎</span><sup><small>[[H0410]]</small></sup> and <span class="ashebrew">‎קוּם‎</span><sup><small>[[H6965]]</small></sup>; God of raising</small>

$\quad$ Eliakim.
- § Eliakim = *"God raises" or "God sets up"*
- ***Eljakim***, the name of four Israelites

**1)** the son of Hilkiah, master of Hezekiah's household
**2)** Josiah's son, enthroned by Pharaoh
**3)** a priest who assisted Nehemiah
