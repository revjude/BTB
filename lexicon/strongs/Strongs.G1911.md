**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἐπιβάλλω‎</span></big>** **epibállō** <small>*(ep-ee-bal'-lo)*</small> ==**to put on/seize**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G1909|‎ἐπί‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G0906|‎βάλλω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ beat into, cast (up-)on, fall, lay (on), put (unto), stretch forth, think on.
- to throw upon (literal or figurative, transitive or reflexive
-  usually with more or less force)
-  specially (with **<big>[[G1438|‎ἑαυτοῦ‎]]</big>** implied) to reflect
-  impersonally, to belong to
