<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָצָר‎</span></big> **ʻâtsâr** <small>*(aw-tsar')*</small> ==**to restrain**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] be able, close up, detain, fast, keep (self close, still), prevail, recover, refrain, [idiom] reign, restrain, retain, shut (up), slack, stay, stop, withhold (self).
- ***to inclose***
-  by analogy, ***to hold back***
-  also ***to maintain***, ***rule***, ***assemble***

**1)** to restrain, retain, close up, shut, withhold, refrain, stay, detain
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to restrain, halt, stop
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to retain
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be restrained, be stayed, be under restraint
