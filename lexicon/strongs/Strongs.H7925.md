**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁכַם‎</span></big>** **shâkam** <small>*(shaw-kam')*</small> ==**to rise**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; properly, to incline (the shoulder to a burden); but used only as denominative from **<big>[[H7926|‎שְׁכֶם‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ (arise, be up, get (oneself) up, rise up) early (betimes), morning.
- literally, ***to load up*** (on the back of man or beast), i.e. ***to start early in the morning***

**1)** to rise or start early
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to rise early, make an early start
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** early (as adverb)
