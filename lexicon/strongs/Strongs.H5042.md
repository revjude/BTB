<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָבַע‎</span></big> **nâbaʻ** <small>*(naw-bah')*</small> ==**to bubble**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ belch out, flowing, pour out, send forth, utter (abundantly).
- ***to gush forth***
-  figuratively, ***to utter*** (good or bad words)
-  specifically, ***to emit*** (a foul odor)

**1)** to flow, pour out, pour, gush forth, spring, bubble up, ferment
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to flow
$\quad$ **1b)**(Hiphil) to pour out, emit, cause to bubble, belch forth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to pour out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to cause to bubble, ferment
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to pour forth, emit, belch forth
