<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תָּפַשׂ‎</span></big> **tâphas** <small>*(taw-fas')*</small> ==**to capture**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ catch, handle, (lay, take) hold (on, over), stop, [idiom] surely, surprise, take.
- ***to manipulate***, i.e. ***seize***
-  ***chiefly to capture***, ***wield***, specifically, ***to overlay***
-  figuratively, ***to use unwarrantably***

**1)** to catch, handle, lay hold, take hold of, seize, wield
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to lay hold of, seize, arrest, catch
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to grasp (in order to) wield, wield, use skilfully
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be seized, be arrested, be caught, be taken, captured
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to catch, grasp (with the hands)
