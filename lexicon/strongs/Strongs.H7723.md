**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁוְא‎</span></big>** **shâvᵉʼ** <small>*(shawv)*</small> ==**vanity: false**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">שַׁו</span></big>** **shav** <small>*(shav)*</small>; from the same as **<big>[[H7722|‎שׁוֹא‎]]</big>** in the sense of desolating</small>

$\quad$ false(-ly), lie, lying, vain, vanity.
- ***evil*** (as destructive), literally (ruin) or morally (especially guile)
-  figuratively ***idolatry*** (as false, subjective), ***uselessness*** (as deceptive, objective
-  also adverbially, in vain)

**1)** emptiness, vanity, falsehood
$\quad$ **1a)** emptiness, nothingness, vanity
$\quad$ **1b)** emptiness of speech, lying
$\quad$ **1c)** worthlessness (of conduct)
