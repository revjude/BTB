<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּנָה‎</span></big> **kânâh** <small>*(kaw-naw')*</small> ==**to flatter**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ give flattering titles, surname (himself).
- ***to address by an additional name***
-  hence, ***to eulogize***

**1)** to title, surname, be surnamed, give an epithet or cognomen, give a flattering title
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel) to call by name, give a title, betitle
