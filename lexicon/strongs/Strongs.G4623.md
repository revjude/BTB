<big><span class="asgreek">‎σιωπάω‎</span></big> **siōpáō** <small>*(see-o-pah'-o)*</small> ==**be quiet**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <greek BETA="SIWPH/" unicode="σιωπή" translit="siōpḗ"/> (silence, i.e. a hush</small>

$\quad$ dumb, (hold) peace.
- properly, muteness, i.e. involuntary stillness, or inability to speak
-  and thus differing from <span class="asgreek">σιγή</span><sup><small>[[G4602]]</small></sup>, which is rather a voluntary refusal or indisposition to speak, although the terms are often used synonymously)
-  to be dumb (but not deaf also, like 2974 properly)
-  figuratively, to be calm (as quiet water)
