<big><span class="asaramaic">‎נְפַק‎</span></big> **nᵉphaq** <small>*(nef-ak')*</small> ==**to go out**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#A:V|A:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>(Aramaic) a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ come (go, take) forth (out).
- ***to issue***
-  causatively, ***to bring out***

**1)** to go or come out, bring or come forth
$\quad$ **1a)** (P'al) to go out or forth
$\quad$ **1b)** (Aphel) to bring forth
