<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּרַק‎</span></big> **pâraq** <small>*(paw-rak')*</small> ==**to tear**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ break (off), deliver, redeem, rend (in pieces), tear in pieces.
- ***to break off*** or ***crunch***
-  figuratively, ***to deliver***

**1)** to tear apart or away, tear off, break away
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to tear away, snatch, rescue
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to tear off
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to tear off from oneself, be broken off, be broken in pieces
