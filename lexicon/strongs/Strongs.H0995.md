<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בִּין‎</span></big> **bîyn** <small>*(bene)*</small> ==**to understand**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ attend, consider, be cunning, diligently, direct, discern, eloquent, feel, inform, instruct, have intelligence, know, look well to, mark, perceive, be prudent, regard, (can) skill(-full), teach, think, (cause, make to, get, give, have) understand(-ing), view, (deal) wise(-ly, man).
- ***to separate mentally*** (or distinguish), i.e.(generally) ***understand***

**1)** to discern, understand, consider
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to perceive, discern
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to understand, know (with the mind)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to observe, mark, give heed to, distinguish, consider
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to have discernment, insight, understanding
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be discerning, intelligent, discreet, have understanding
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to understand
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to cause to understand, give understanding, teach
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpolel) to show oneself discerning or attentive, consider diligently
$\quad$ **1e)** (Polel) to teach, instruct
**2)** (TWOT) prudent, regard
