<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎כֶּרֶם‎</span><sup><small>[[H3754]]</small></sup>; gardener</small>

$\quad$ Carmi.
- § Carmi = *"my vineyard"*
- ***Karmi***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** the 4th son of Reuben and progenitor of the Carmites
**2)** a Judaite, father of Achan, the 'troubler of Israel' in the time of Joshua
