**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּגַד‎</span></big>** **bâgad** <small>*(baw-gad')*</small> ==**to act treacherously**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ deal deceitfully (treacherously, unfaithfully), offend, transgress(-or), (depart), treacherous (dealer, -ly, man), unfaithful(-ly, man), [idiom] very.
- ***to cover*** (with a garment)
-  figuratively, ***to act covertly***
-  by implication, ***to pillage***

**1)** to act treacherously, deceitfully, deal treacherously
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to act or deal treacherously, faithlessly, deceitfully, offend
