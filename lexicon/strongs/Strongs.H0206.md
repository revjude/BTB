- § Aven = *"vanity"*
- ***Aven***, the contemptuous synonym of three places, one in Coele-Syria, one in Egypt (On), and one in Palestine (Bethel)

**1)** a name used contemptuously for the following places of idolatrous worship
$\quad$ **1a)** a city in Egypt, possibly On (Eze 30:17)
$\quad$ **1b)** Bethel with its calf worship (Ho 10:8)
$\quad$ **1c)** A town or region in Syria (Am 1:5)
