**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ψύχω‎</span></big>** **psýchō** <small>*(psoo'-kho)*</small> ==**to cool**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary verb</small>

$\quad$ wax cold.
- to breathe (voluntarily but gently, thus differing on the one hand from **<big>[[G4154|‎πνέω‎]]</big>**, which denotes properly a forcible respiration
-  and on the other from the base of **<big>[[G0109|‎ἀήρ‎]]</big>**, which refers properly to an inanimate breeze), i.e. (by implication, of reduction of temperature by  evaporation) to chill (figuratively)
