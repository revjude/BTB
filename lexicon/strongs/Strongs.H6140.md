<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָקַשׁ‎</span></big> **ʻâqash** <small>*(aw-kash')*</small> ==**to twist**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ make crooked, (prove, that is) perverse(-rt).
- ***to knot*** or ***distort***
-  figuratively, ***to pervert*** (act or declare perverse)

**1)** to be perverse, twist, pervert, make crooked, prove perverse, declare perverse
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to be crooked
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to twist, distort, pervert, make crooked
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to declare crooked
$\quad$ **1d)** (Qal) perverse
