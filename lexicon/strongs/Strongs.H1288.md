<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָרַךְ‎</span></big> **bârak** <small>*(baw-rak')*</small> ==**to bless**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] abundantly, [idiom] altogether, [idiom] at all, blaspheme, bless, congratulate, curse, [idiom] greatly, [idiom] indeed, kneel (down), praise, salute, [idiom] still, thank.
- ***to kneel***
-  by implication ***to bless God*** (as an act of adoration), and (vice-versa) ***man*** (as a benefit)
-  also (by euphemism) ***to curse*** (God or the king, as treason)

**1)** to bless, kneel
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to kneel
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to bless
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be blessed, bless oneself
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to bless
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be blessed, be adored
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hiphil) to cause to kneel
$\quad$ **1f)** (Hithpael) to bless oneself
**2)** (TWOT) to praise, salute, curse
