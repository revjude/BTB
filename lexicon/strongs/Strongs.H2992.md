<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָבַם‎</span></big> **yâbam** <small>*(yaw-bam')*</small> ==**be brother-in-law**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root of doubtful meaning; used only as a denominative from <span class="ashebrew">‎יָבָם‎</span><sup><small>[[H2993]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ perform the duty of a husband's brother, marry.
- ***to marry a*** (deceased) ***brother's widow***

**1)** (Piel) to perform levirate marriage, perform the duty of a brother-in-law
$\quad$ **1a)** the duty due to a brother who died childless - to marry his widow and have a son for his name
