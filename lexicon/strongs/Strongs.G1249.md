**<big><span class="asgreek">‎διάκονος‎</span></big>** **diákonos** <small>*(dee-ak'-on-os)*</small> ==**servant**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-M/F|G:N-M/F]]</small></sup> 
<small>probably from an obsolete <greek BETA="DIA/KW" unicode="διάκω" translit="diákō"/> (to run on errands; compare **<big>[[G1377|‎διώκω‎]]</big>**)</small>

$\quad$ deacon, minister, servant.
- an attendant, i.e. (genitive case) a waiter (at table or in other menial duties)
-  specially, a Christian teacher and pastor (technically, a deacon or deaconess)
