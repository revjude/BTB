**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׂרַר‎</span></big>** **sârar** <small>*(saw-rar')*</small> ==**to rule**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] altogether, make self a prince, (bear) rule.
- ***to have*** (transitively, exercise
-  reflexively, get) ***dominion***

**1)** to be or act as prince, rule, contend, have power, prevail over, reign, govern
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to rule over, govern
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hithpael) to lord it over
