**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָרַשׁ‎</span></big>** **chârash** <small>*(khaw-rash')*</small> ==**to plow/plot**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] altogether, cease, conceal, be deaf, devise, ear, graven, imagine, leave off speaking, hold peace, plow(-er, man), be quiet, rest, practise secretly, keep silence, be silent, speak not a word, be still, hold tongue, worker.
- ***to scratch***, i.e. (by implication) ***to engrave***, ***plough***
-  hence (from the use of tools) ***to fabricate*** (of any material)
-  figuratively, ***to devise*** (in a bad sense)
-  hence (from the idea of secrecy) ***to be silent***, ***to let alone***
-  hence (by implication) ***to be deaf*** (as an accompaniment of dumbness)

**1)** to cut in, plough, engrave, devise
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to cut in, engrave
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to plough
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to devise
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be ploughed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to plot evil
**2)** to be silent, be dumb, be speechless, be deaf
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a1)** to be silent
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a2)** to be deaf
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b1)** to be silent, keep quiet
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b2)** to make silent
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b3)** to be deaf, show deafness
$\quad$ **2c)** (Hithpael) to remain silent
