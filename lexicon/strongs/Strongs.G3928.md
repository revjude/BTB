**<big><span class="asgreek">‎παρέρχομαι‎</span></big>** **parérchomai** <small>*(par-er'-khom-ahee)*</small> ==**to pass by**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G3844|‎παρά‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2064|‎ἔρχομαι‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ come (forth), go, pass (away, by, over), past, transgress.
- to come near or aside, i.e. to approach (arrive), go by (or away), (figuratively) perish or neglect, (causative) avert
