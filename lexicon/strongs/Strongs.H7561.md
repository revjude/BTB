<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָשַׁע‎</span></big> **râshaʻ** <small>*(raw-shah')*</small> ==**be wicked**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ condemn, make trouble, vex, be (commit, deal, depart, do) wicked(-ly, -ness).
- ***to be*** (causatively, do or declare) ***wrong***
-  by implication, ***to disturb***, ***violate***

**1)** to be wicked, act wickedly
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be wicked, act wickedly
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be guilty, be condemned
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to condemn as guilty (in civil relations)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to condemn as guilty (in ethical or religious relations)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to act wickedly (in ethics and religion)
