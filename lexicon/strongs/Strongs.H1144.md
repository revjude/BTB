**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בִּנְיָמִין‎</span></big>** **Binyâmîyn** <small>*(bin-yaw-mene')*</small> ==**Benjamin**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H1121|‎בֵּן‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3225|‎יָמִין‎]]</big>**; son of (the) right hand</small>

$\quad$ Benjamin.
- § Benjamin = *"son of the right hand"*
- ***Binjamin***, youngest son of Jacob
-  also the tribe descended from him, and its territory

**1)** Jacob's and Rachel's youngest son, Joseph's full brother
**2)** son of Bilhan, great-grandson of Benjamin
**3)** a Benjamite, one of the sons of Harim, in the time of Ezra who had taken a strange wife
**4)** the tribe descended from Benjamin, the son of Jacob
