<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֵפוֹד‎</span></big> **ʼêphôwd** <small>*(ay-fode')*</small> ==**ephod**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>rarely <span class="ashebrew">אֵפֹד</span> **ʼêphôd** <small>*(ay-fode')*</small>; probably of foreign derivation</small>

$\quad$ ephod.
- ***a girdle***
-  specifically ***the ephod*** or ***highpriest's shoulder-piece***
-  also generally, ***an image***

**1)** ephod
$\quad$ **1a)** priestly garment, shoulder-cape or mantle, outer garment
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** worn by an ordinary priest and made of white stuff
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** worn by the high priest-more costly, woven of gold, blue, purple, scarlet, and linen threads provided with shoulder-pieces and a breast piece of like material, ornamented with gems and gold
