**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מַשְׁקֶה‎</span></big>** **mashqeh** <small>*(mash-keh')*</small> ==**cupbearer**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H8248|‎שָׁקָה‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ butler(-ship), cupbearer, drink(-ing), fat pasture, watered.
- properly, ***causing to drink***, i.e. ***a butler***
-  by implication (intransitively), ***drink*** (itself)
-  figuratively, ***a well-watered region***

**1)** irrigation, drink
$\quad$ **1a)** irrigation
$\quad$ **1b)** drink, drinking vessels
$\quad$ **1c)** butlership (office of butler)
**2)** butler, cup-bearer
