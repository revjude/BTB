<big><span class="asgreek">‎εἴδω‎</span></big> **eídō** <small>*(i'-do)*</small> ==**to perceive: understand**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary verb</small>

$\quad$ be aware, behold, X can (+ not tell), consider, (have) know(-ledge), look (on), perceive, see, be sure, tell, understand, wish, wot.
- used only in certain past tenses, the others being borrowed from the equivalent <span class="asgreek">ὀπτάνομαι</span><sup><small>[[G3700]]</small></sup> and <span class="asgreek">ὁράω</span><sup><small>[[G3708]]</small></sup>
-  properly, to see (literally or figuratively)
-  by implication, (in the perfect tense only) to know
