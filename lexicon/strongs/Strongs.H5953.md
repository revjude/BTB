**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָלַל‎</span></big>** **ʻâlal** <small>*(aw-lal')*</small> ==**to abuse**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ abuse, affect, [idiom] child, defile, do, glean, mock, practise, thoroughly, work (wonderfully).
- ***to effect thoroughly***
-  specifically, ***to glean*** (also figuratively)
-  by implication (in a bad sense) ***to overdo***, i.e. ***maltreat***, ***be saucy to***, ***pain***, ***impose*** (also literal)

**1)** to act severely, deal with severely, make a fool of someone
$\quad$ **1a)** (Poel) to act severely
$\quad$ **1b)** (Poal) to be severely dealt with
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to busy oneself, divert oneself, deal wantonly, deal ruthlessly, abuse (by thrusting through)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpoel) to practise practices, thrust forth (in wickedness)
**2)** (Poel) to glean
**3)** (Poel) to act or play the child
**4)** (Poel) to insert, thrust, thrust in, thrust upon
