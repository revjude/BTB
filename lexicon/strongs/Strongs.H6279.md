**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָתַר‎</span></big>** **ʻâthar** <small>*(aw-thar')*</small> ==**to pray**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (rather denominative from **<big>[[H6281|‎עֶתֶר‎]]</big>**)</small>

$\quad$ intreat, (make) pray(-er).
- ***to burn incense in worship***, i.e. ***intercede*** (reciprocally, listen to prayer)

**1)** to pray, entreat, supplicate
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to pray, entreat
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be supplicated, be entreated
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to make supplication, plead
