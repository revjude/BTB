<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אוֹר‎</span></big> **ʼôwr** <small>*(ore)*</small> ==**to light**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] break of day, glorious, kindle, (be, en-, give, show) light (-en, -ened), set on fire, shine.
- ***to be*** (causative, make) ***luminous*** (literally and metaphorically)

**1)** to be or become light, shine
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to become light (day)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to shine (of the sun)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to become bright
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be illuminated
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to become lighted up
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to give light, shine (of sun, moon, and stars)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to illumine, light up, cause to shine, shine
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to kindle, light (candle, wood)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** lighten (of the eyes, his law, etc)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c5)** to make shine (of the face)
