<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כֹּפֶר‎</span></big> **kôpher** <small>*(ko'-fer)*</small> ==**ransom**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎כָּפַר‎</span><sup><small>[[H3722]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ bribe, camphire, pitch, ransom, satisfaction, sum of money, village.
- properly, ***a cover***, i.e. (literally) ***a village*** (as covered in)
-  (specifically) ***bitumen*** (as used for coating), and ***the henna plant*** (as used for dyeing)
-  figuratively, ***a redemption-price***

**1)** price of a life, ransom, bribe
**2)** asphalt, pitch (as a covering)
**3)** the henna plant, name of a plant (henna?)
**4)** village
