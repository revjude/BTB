$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to make clean, make pure, keep clean, keep pure
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to cleanse
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to make yourself clean, purify oneself
