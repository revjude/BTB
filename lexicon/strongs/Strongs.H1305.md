<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּרַר‎</span></big> **bârar** <small>*(baw-rar')*</small> ==**to purify**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ make bright, choice, chosen, cleanse (be clean), clearly, polished, (shew self) pure(-ify), purge (out).
- ***to clarify*** (i.e. brighten), ***examine***, ***select***

**1)** to purify, select, polish, choose, purge, cleanse or make bright, test or prove
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to purge, purge out, purify
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to choose, select
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to cleanse, make shining, polish
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to test, prove
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to purify oneself
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to purify
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to purify
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to polish arrows
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** to purify oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e2)** to show oneself pure, just, kind
