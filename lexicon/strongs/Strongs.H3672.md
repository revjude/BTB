<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כִּנְּרוֹת‎</span></big> **Kinnᵉrôwth** <small>*(kin-ner-oth')*</small> ==**(Sea of) Chinnereth**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--L|N:N--L]]</small></sup> 
<small>or <span class="ashebrew">כִּנֶּרֶת</span> **Kinnereth** <small>*(kinneh'-reth)*</small>; respectively plural and singular feminine from the same as <span class="ashebrew">‎כִּנּוֹר‎</span><sup><small>[[H3658]]</small></sup>; perhaps harp-shaped</small>

$\quad$ Chinnereth, Chinneroth, Cinneroth.
- § Chinneroth or Cinneroth or Chinnereth = *"harps"*
- ***Kinneroth*** or ***Kinnereth***, a place in Palestine

**1)** the early name of the Sea of Galilee
**2)** a town and district in Naphtali near the Sea of Galilee
