**<big><span class="asgreek">‎συμφέρω‎</span></big>** **symphérō** <small>*(soom-fer'-o)*</small> ==**be profitable**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4862|‎σύν‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G5342|‎φέρω‎]]</big>** (including its alternate)</small>

$\quad$ be better for, bring together, be expedient (for), be good, (be) profit(-able for).
- to bear together (contribute), i.e. (literally) to collect, or (figuratively) to conduce
-  especially (neuter participle as a noun) advantage
