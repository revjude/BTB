<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּלָה‎</span></big> **bâlâh** <small>*(baw-law')*</small> ==**to become old**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ consume, enjoy long, become (make, wax) old, spend, waste.
- ***to fail***
-  by implication ***to wear out***, ***decay*** (causatively, consume, spend)

**1)** to wear out, become old
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to wear out
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to wear out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to wear out by use, use up completely
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to enjoy, use to the full
