**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָרַר‎</span></big>** **chârar** <small>*(khaw-rar')*</small> ==**to scorch**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be angry, burn, dry, kindle.
- ***to glow***, i.e. literally (to melt, burn, dry up) or figuratively (to show or incite passion)

**1)** to burn, be hot, be scorched, be charred
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be hot, be scorched
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to burn, be burned
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be scorched, be burned
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to burn
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be dry, be angry
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pilpel) to cause to burn
