<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָגַז‎</span></big> **râgaz** <small>*(raw-gaz')*</small> ==**to tremble**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be afraid, stand in awe, disquiet, fall out, fret, move, provoke, quake, rage, shake, tremble, trouble, be wroth.
- ***to quiver*** (with any violent emotion, especially anger or fear)

**1)** tremble, quake, rage, quiver, be agitated, be excited, be perturbed
$\quad$ **1a)**(Qal) to quake, be disquieted, be excited, be perturbed
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to cause to quake, disquiet, enrage, disturb
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to excite oneself
