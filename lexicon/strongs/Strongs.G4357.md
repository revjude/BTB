**<big><span class="asgreek">‎προσμένω‎</span></big>** **prosménō** <small>*(pros-men'-o)*</small> ==**to remain/persist**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4314|‎πρός‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G3306|‎μένω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ abide still, be with, cleave unto, continue in (with).
- to stay further, i.e. remain in a place, with a person
-  figuratively, to adhere to, persevere in
