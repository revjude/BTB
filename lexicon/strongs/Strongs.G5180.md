<big><span class="asgreek">‎τύπτω‎</span></big> **týptō** <small>*(toop'-to)*</small> ==**to strike**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary verb (in a strengthened form)</small>

$\quad$ beat, smite, strike, wound.
- to "thump", i.e. cudgel or pummel (properly, with a stick or bastinado), but in any case by repeated blows
-  thus differing from <span class="asgreek">παίω</span><sup><small>[[G3817]]</small></sup> and <span class="asgreek">πατάσσω</span><sup><small>[[G3960]]</small></sup>, which denote a (usually single) blow with the hand or any instrument, or <span class="asgreek">πλήσσω</span><sup><small>[[G4141]]</small></sup> with the fist (or a hammer), or <span class="asgreek">ῥαπίζω</span><sup><small>[[G4474]]</small></sup> with the palm
-  as well as from <span class="asgreek">τυγχάνω</span><sup><small>[[G5177]]</small></sup>, an accidental collision)
-  by implication, to punish
-  figuratively, to offend (the conscience)
