<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁאַף‎</span></big> **shâʼaph** <small>*(shaw-af')*</small> ==**to long for**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ desire (earnestly), devour, haste, pant, snuff up, swallow up.
- ***to inhale eagerly***
-  figuratively, ***to cover***
-  by implication, ***to be angry***
-  also ***to hasten***

**1)** to gasp, pant, pant after, long for, breathe heavily
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to gasp, pant after, snuff up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to gasp or pant (with desire), pant after, be eager for
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to thirst for one's blood (from actions of animals) (fig)
**2)** to crush, trample, trample upon
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to trample upon, crush
