<big><span class="ashebrew">‎דּוֹד‎</span></big> **dôwd** <small>*(dode)*</small> ==**beloved**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>or (shortened) <span class="ashebrew">דֹּד</span> **dôd** <small>*(dode)*</small>; from an unused root meaning properly, to boil, i.e.</small>

$\quad$ (well-) beloved, father's brother, love, uncle.
- (figuratively) ***to love***
-  by implication, ***a love-token***, ***lover***, ***friend***
-  specifically ***an uncle***

**1)** beloved, love, uncle
$\quad$ **1a)** loved one, beloved
$\quad$ **1b)** uncle
$\quad$ **1c)** love (pl. abstract)
