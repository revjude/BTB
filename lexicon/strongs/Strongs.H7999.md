**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁלַם‎</span></big>** **shâlam** <small>*(shaw-lam')*</small> ==**to complete**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ make amends, (make an) end, finish, full, give again, make good, (re-) pay (again), (make) (to) (be at) peace(-able), that is perfect, perform, (make) prosper(-ous), recompense, render, requite, make restitution, restore, reward, [idiom] surely.
- ***to be safe*** (in mind, body or estate)
-  figuratively, ***to be*** (causatively, make) ***completed***
-  by implication, ***to be friendly***
-  by extension, ***to reciprocate*** (in various applications)

**1)** to be in a covenant of peace, be at peace
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be at peace
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** peaceful one (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) one in covenant of peace (participle)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to make peace with
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to cause to be at peace
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to live in peace
**2)** to be complete, be sound
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a1)** to be complete, be finished, be ended
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a2)** to be sound, be uninjured
$\quad$ **2b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b1)** to complete, finish
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b2)** to make safe
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b3)** to make whole or good, restore, make compensation
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b4)** to make good, pay
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b5)** to requite, recompense, reward
$\quad$ **2c)** (Pual)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2c1)** to be performed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2c2)** to be repaid, be requited
$\quad$ **2d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2d1)** to complete, perform
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2d2)** to make an end of
