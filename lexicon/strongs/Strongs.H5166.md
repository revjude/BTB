<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נְחֶמְיָה‎</span></big> **Nᵉchemyâh** <small>*(nekh-em-yaw')*</small> ==**Nehemiah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎נָחַם‎</span><sup><small>[[H5162]]</small></sup> and <span class="ashebrew">‎יָהּ‎</span><sup><small>[[H3050]]</small></sup>; consolation of Jah</small>

$\quad$ Nehemiah.
- § Nehemiah = *"Jehovah comforts"*
- ***Nechemjah***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** the son of Hachaliah, cupbearer to king Artaxerxes, who became governor of Judah after the return from exile
**2)** one of the 12 heads of the people who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
**3)** son of Azbuk and ruler of the half part of Beth-zur, who helped to repair the wall of Jerusalem
