**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּוָה‎</span></big>** **kâvâh** <small>*(kaw-vaw')*</small> ==**to burn**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ burn.
- properly, ***to prick*** or ***penetrate***
-  hence, ***to blister*** (as smarting or eating into)

**1)** to burn, scorch, brand
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to be burned, be scorched
