<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁמַר‎</span></big> **shâmar** <small>*(shaw-mar')*</small> ==**to keep: obey**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ beward, be circumspect, take heed (to self), keep(-er, self), mark, look narrowly, observe, preserve, regard, reserve, save (self), sure, (that lay) wait (for), watch(-man).
- properly, ***to hedge about*** (as with thorns), i.e. ***guard***
-  generally, ***to protect***, ***attend to***, etc.

**1)** to keep, guard, observe, give heed
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to keep, have charge of
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to keep, guard, keep watch and ward, protect, save life
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2a)** watch, watchman (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to watch for, wait for
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to watch, observe
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to keep, retain, treasure up (in memory)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to keep (within bounds), restrain
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7)** to observe, celebrate, keep (sabbath or covenant or commands), perform (vow)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a8)** to keep, preserve, protect
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a9)** to keep, reserve
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be on one's guard, take heed, take care, beware
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to keep oneself, refrain, abstain
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be kept, be guarded
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to keep, pay heed
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael) to keep oneself from
