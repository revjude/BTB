**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אִישׁ‎</span></big>** **ʼîysh** <small>*(eesh)*</small> ==**man**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>contracted for **<big>[[H0582|‎אֱנוֹשׁ‎]]</big>** (or perhaps rather from an unused root meaning to be extant)</small>

$\quad$ also, another, any (man), a certain, [phrase] champion, consent, each, every (one), fellow, (foot-, husband-) man, (good-, great, mighty) man, he, high (degree), him (that is), husband, man(-kind), [phrase] none, one, people, person, [phrase] steward, what (man) soever, whoso(-ever), worthy. Compare **<big>[[H0802|‎אִשָּׁה‎]]</big>**.
- ***a man as an individual*** or ***a male person***
-  often used as an adjunct to a more definite term (and in such cases frequently not expressed in translation)

**1)** man
$\quad$ **1a)** man, male (in contrast to woman, female)
$\quad$ **1b)** husband
$\quad$ **1c)** human being, person (in contrast to God)
$\quad$ **1d)** servant
$\quad$ **1e)** mankind
$\quad$ **1f)** champion
$\quad$ **1g)** great man
**2)** whosoever
**3)** each (adjective)
