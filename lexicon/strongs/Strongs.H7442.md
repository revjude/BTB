<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָנַן‎</span></big> **rânan** <small>*(raw-nan')*</small> ==**to overcome**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ aloud for joy, cry out, be joyful (greatly, make to) rejoice, (cause to) shout (for joy), (cause to) sing (aloud, for joy, out), triumph.
- properly, ***to creak*** (or emit a stridulous sound), i.e. ***to shout*** (usually for joy)

**1)** to overcome
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hithpolel) to be overcome
**2)** to cry out, shout for joy, give a ringing cry
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a1)** to give a ringing cry (in joy, exaltation, distress)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a2)** to cry aloud (in summons, exhortation of wisdom)
$\quad$ **2b)** (Piel) to give a ringing cry (in joy, exultation, praise
$\quad$ **2c)** (Pual) ringing cry, singing out (passive)
$\quad$ **2d)** (Hiphil) to cause to ring or sing out (for joy)
$\quad$ **2e)** (Hithpolel) rejoicing (participle)
