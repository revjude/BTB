<big><span class="ashebrew">‎הָמָה‎</span></big> **hâmâh** <small>*(haw-maw')*</small> ==**to roar**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (compare <span class="ashebrew">‎הוּם‎</span><sup><small>[[H1949]]</small></sup>)</small>

$\quad$ clamorous, concourse, cry aloud, be disquieted, loud, mourn, be moved, make a noise, rage, roar, sound, be troubled, make in tumult, tumultuous, be in an uproar.
- ***to make a loud sound*** (like English 'hum')
-  by implication, to be in great ***commotion*** or ***tumult***, ***to rage***, ***war***, ***moan***, ***clamor***

**1)** to murmur, growl, roar, cry aloud, mourn, rage, sound, make noise, tumult, be clamorous, be disquieted, be loud, be moved, be troubled, be in an uproar
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to growl
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to murmur (fig. of a soul in prayer)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to roar
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to be in a stir, be in a commotion
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to be boisterous, be turbulent
