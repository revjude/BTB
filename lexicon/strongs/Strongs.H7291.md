<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָדַף‎</span></big> **râdaph** <small>*(raw-daf')*</small> ==**to pursue**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ chase, put to flight, follow (after, on), hunt, (be under) persecute(-ion, -or), pursue(-r).
- ***to run after*** (usually with hostile intent
-  figuratively (of time) ***gone by***)

**1)** to be behind, follow after, pursue, persecute, run after
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to pursue, put to flight, chase, dog, attend closely upon
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to persecute, harass (fig)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to follow after, aim to secure (fig)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to run after (a bribe) (fig)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be pursued
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** one pursued (participle)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to pursue ardently, aim eagerly to secure, pursue
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be pursued, be chased away
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hiphil) to pursue, chase
