$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to plead a cause
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to execute judgment, requite, vindicate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to govern
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to contend, strive
