<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נוּעַ‎</span></big> **nûwaʻ** <small>*(noo'-ah)*</small> ==**to shake**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ continually, fugitive, [idiom] make, to (go) up and down, be gone away, (be) move(-able, -d), be promoted, reel, remove, scatter, set, shake, sift, stagger, to and fro, be vagabond, wag, (make) wander (up and down).
- ***to waver***, in a great variety of applications, literally and figuratively (as subjoined)

**1)** to quiver, totter, shake, reel, stagger, wander, move, sift, make move, wave, waver, tremble
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to wave, quiver, vibrate, swing, stagger, tremble, be unstable
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to totter, go tottering
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2a)** vagabond (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be tossed about or around
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to toss about
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to shake, cause to totter
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to shake, disturb
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to cause to wander
