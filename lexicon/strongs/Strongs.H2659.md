<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָפֵר‎</span></big> **châphêr** <small>*(khaw-fare')*</small> ==**be ashamed**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (perhaps rather the same as <span class="ashebrew">‎חָפַר‎</span><sup><small>[[H2658]]</small></sup> through the idea of detection)</small>

$\quad$ be ashamed, be confounded, be brought to confusion (unto shame), come (be put to) shame, bring reproach.
- ***to blush***
-  figuratively, ***to be ashamed***, ***disappointed***
-  causatively, ***to shame***, ***reproach***

**1)** to be ashamed, be confounded, be abashed, feel abashed
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be ashamed, be abashed
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to display shame, demonstrate shame, cause embarrassment
