**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎טוּחַ‎</span></big>** **ṭûwach** <small>*(too'-akh)*</small> ==**to overspread**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ daub, overlay, plaister, smut.
- ***to smear***, especially ***with lime***

**1)** to spread over, overlay, plaster, cover over, coat, besmear
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to overspread, coat, overlay
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be coated, be plastered
**2)** (Qal) to be besmeared
