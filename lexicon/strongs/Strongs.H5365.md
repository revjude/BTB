<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָקַר‎</span></big> **nâqar** <small>*(naw-kar')*</small> ==**to dig**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ dig, pick out, pierce, put (thrust) out.
- ***to bore*** (penetrate, quarry)

**1)** to bore, pick, dig, pick out
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to bore, pick, dig
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to bore out
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be dug out
