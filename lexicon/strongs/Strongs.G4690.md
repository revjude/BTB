**<big><span class="asgreek">‎σπέρμα‎</span></big>** **spérma** <small>*(sper'-mah)*</small> ==**seed: offspring**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-N|G:N-N]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4687|‎σπείρω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ issue, seed.
- something sown, i.e. seed (including the male "sperm")
-  by implication, offspring
-  specially, a remnant (figuratively, as if kept over for planting)
