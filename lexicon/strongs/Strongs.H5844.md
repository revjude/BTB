<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָטָה‎</span></big> **ʻâṭâh** <small>*(aw-taw')*</small> ==**to enwrap**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ array self, be clad, (put a) cover (-ing, self), fill, put on, [idiom] surely, turn aside.
- ***to wrap***, i.e. ***cover***, ***veil***, ***cloth***, or ***roll***

**1)** to cover, enwrap, wrap oneself, envelop oneself
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to wrap, envelop oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to cover (the beard in mourning)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** mourner, one who covers (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to cover, enwrap, envelop
**2)** to grasp
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to grasp
