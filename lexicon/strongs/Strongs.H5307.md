<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָפַל‎</span></big> **nâphal** <small>*(naw-fal')*</small> ==**to fall: fall**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be accepted, cast (down, self, (lots), out), cease, die, divide (by lot), (let) fail, (cause to, let, make, ready to) fall (away, down, -en, -ing), fell(-ing), fugitive, have (inheritance), inferior, be judged (by mistake for <span class="ashebrew">‎פָּלַל‎</span><sup><small>[[H6419]]</small></sup>), lay (along), (cause to) lie down, light (down), be ([idiom] hast) lost, lying, overthrow, overwhelm, perish, present(-ed, -ing), (make to) rot, slay, smite out, [idiom] surely, throw down.
- ***to fall***, in a great variety of applications (intransitive or causative, literal or figurative)

**1)** to fall, lie, be cast down, fail
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to fall
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to fall (of violent death)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to fall prostrate, prostrate oneself before
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to fall upon, attack, desert, fall away to,  go away to, fall into the hand of
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to fall short, fail, fall out, turn out, result
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to settle, waste away, be offered, be inferior to
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7)** to lie, lie prostrate
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cause to fall, fell, throw down, knock out, lay prostrate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to overthrow
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to make the lot fall, assign by lot, apportion by lot
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to let drop, cause to fail (fig.)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** to cause to fall
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to throw or prostrate oneself, throw oneself upon
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to lie prostrate, prostrate oneself
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pilel) to fall
