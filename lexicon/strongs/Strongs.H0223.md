<small>or (prolonged) <span class="ashebrew">אוּרִיָּהוּ</span> **ʼÛwrîyâhûw** <small>*(oo-ree-yaw'-hoo)*</small> ; from <span class="ashebrew">‎אוּר‎</span><sup><small>[[H0217]]</small></sup> and <span class="ashebrew">‎יָהּ‎</span><sup><small>[[H3050]]</small></sup>; flame of Jah</small>

$\quad$ Uriah, Urijah.
- § Uriah or Urijah = *"Jehovah (Yahweh) is my light (flame)"*
- ***Urijah***, the name of one Hittite and five Israelites

**1)** Hittite husband of Bathsheba
**2)** a priest who built king Ahaz' heathen altar
**3)** a priest who rebuilt Jerusalem's wall
**4)** a prophet slain by Jehoiakim
