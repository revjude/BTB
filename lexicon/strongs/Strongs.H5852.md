<small>or <span class="ashebrew">עֲטָרֹת</span> **ʻĂṭârôth** <small>*(at-awroth')*</small>; plural of <span class="ashebrew">‎עֲטָרָה‎</span><sup><small>[[H5850]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ Ataroth.
- § Ataroth = *"crowns"*
- ***Ataroth***, the name (thus simply) of two places in Palestine

**1)** a town east of the Jordan in Gilead, taken and built by the tribe of Gad
**2)** a place on the boundary of Ephraim and Manasseh
**3)** a place between Ephraim and Benjamin
