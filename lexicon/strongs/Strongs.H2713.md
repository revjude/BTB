**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָקַר‎</span></big>** **châqar** <small>*(khaw-kar')*</small> ==**to search**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ find out, (make) search (out), seek (out), sound, try.
- properly, ***to penetrate***
-  hence, ***to examine intimately***

**1)** to search, search for, search out, examine, investigate
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to search (for)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to search through, explore
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to examine thoroughly
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be searched out, be found out, be ascertained, be examined
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to search out, seek out
