<big><span class="ashebrew">‎לָקַט‎</span></big> **lâqaṭ** <small>*(law-kat')*</small> ==**to gather**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ gather (up), glean.
- properly, ***to pick up***, i.e. (generally) ***to gather***
-  specifically, ***to glean***

**1)** to pick up, gather, glean, gather up
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to pick up, gather
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to glean
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to gather, gather up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to collect (money)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to glean
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be picked up
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael) to collect oneself
