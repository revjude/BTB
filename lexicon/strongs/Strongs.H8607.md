**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תִּפְסַח‎</span></big>** **Tiphçach** <small>*(tif-sakh')*</small> ==**Tiphsah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--L|N:N--L]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H6452|‎פָּסַח‎]]</big>**; ford</small>

$\quad$ Tipsah.
- § Tiphsah = *"cross over"*
- ***Tiphsach***, a place in Mesopotamia

**1)** a place at the northeast limit of Solomon's empire; located on the Euphrates river
**2)** a place in the northern kingdom of Israel which king Menahem attacked and where he ripped open all the pregnant women
$\quad$ **2a)** might be the same as 1
