$\quad$ Elnathan.
- § Elnathan = *"God has given"*
- ***Elnathan***, the name of four Israelites

**1)** king Jehoiachin's maternal grandfather
**2)** three chief men in Ezra's time
**3)** son of Achbor, a military commander under Jehoiakim
