<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בּוּשׁ‎</span></big> **bûwsh** <small>*(boosh)*</small> ==**be ashamed**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (be, make, bring to, cause, put to, with, a-) shamed(-d), be (put to) confounded(-fusion), become dry, delay, be long.
- properly, ***to pale***, i.e. by implication ***to be ashamed***
-  also (by implication) ***to be disappointed*** or ***delayed***

**1)** to put to shame, be ashamed, be disconcerted, be disappointed
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to feel shame
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be ashamed, disconcerted, disappointed (by reason of)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to delay (in shame)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to put to shame
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to act shamefully
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to be ashamed
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpolel) to be ashamed before one another
