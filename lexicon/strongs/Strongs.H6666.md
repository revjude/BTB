$\quad$ justice, moderately, right(-eous) (act, -ly, -ness).
- ***rightness*** (abstractly), ***subjectively*** (rectitude), ***objectively*** (justice), morally (virtue) or figuratively (prosperity)

**1)** justice, righteousness

$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** of judge, ruler, king
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** of law
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** of Davidic king Messiah
$\quad$ **1b)** righteousness (of God's attribute)
$\quad$ **1c)** righteousness (in a case or cause)
$\quad$ **1d)** righteousness, truthfulness
$\quad$ **1e)** righteousness (as ethically right)
$\quad$ **1f)** righteousness (as vindicated), justification, salvation
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f1)** of God
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f2)** prosperity (of people)
$\quad$ **1g)** righteous acts
