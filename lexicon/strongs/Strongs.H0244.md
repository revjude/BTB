$\quad$ Ozni, Oznites.
- § Ozni or Oznite = *"my ear (hearing)"*
- ***Ozni***, an Israelite
-  also an Oznite (collectively), his descendant

**1)** son of Gad
**2)** pertaining to Ozni and his descendants
