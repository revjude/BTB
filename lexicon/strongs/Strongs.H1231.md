**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בֻּקִּי‎</span></big>** **Buqqîy** <small>*(book-kee')*</small> ==**Bukki**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H1238|‎בָּקַק‎]]</big>**; wasteful</small>

$\quad$ Bukki.
- § Bukki = *"wasting"*
- ***Bukki***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** son of Abishua and father of Uzzi, fifth from Aaron in the line of the high priests
**2)** son of Jogli, prince of the tribe of Dan, one of the ten men chosen to apportion the land of Canaan between the tribes
