**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָגַף‎</span></big>** **nâgaph** <small>*(naw-gaf')*</small> ==**to strike**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ beat, dash, hurt, plague, slay, smite (down), strike, stumble, [idiom] surely, put to the worse.
- ***to push***, ***gore***, ***defeat***, ***stub*** (the toe), ***inflict*** (a disease)

**1)** to strike, smite
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to strike, smite
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be stricken, be smitten
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to stumble
