<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָצַר‎</span></big> **yâtsar** <small>*(yaw-tsar')*</small> ==**be distressed**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be distressed, be narrow, be straitened (in straits), be vexed.
- ***to press*** (intransitive), i.e. ***be narrow***
-  figuratively, ***be in distress***

**1)** to bind, be distressed, be in distress, be cramped, be narrow, be scant, be in straits, make narrow, cause distress, beseige
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to suffer distress
