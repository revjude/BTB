**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָדָה‎</span></big>** **ʻâdâh** <small>*(aw-daw')*</small> ==**to advance**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ adorn, deck (self), pass by, take away.
- ***to advance***, i.e. ***pass on*** or ***continue***
-  causatively, ***to remove***
-  specifically, ***to bedeck*** (i.e. bring an ornament upon)

**1)** to pass on, advance, go on, pass by, remove
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to pass on, put on
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to remove
