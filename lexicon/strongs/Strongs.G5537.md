**<big><span class="asgreek">‎χρηματίζω‎</span></big>** **chrēmatízō** <small>*(khray-mat-id'-zo)*</small> ==**to announce**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G5536|‎χρῆμα‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ be called, be admonished (warned) of God, reveal, speak.
- to utter an oracle (compare the original sense of **<big>[[G5530|‎χράομαι‎]]</big>**), i.e. divinely intimate
-  by implication, (compare the secular sense of **<big>[[G5532|‎χρεία‎]]</big>**) to constitute a firm for business, i.e. (generally) bear as a title
