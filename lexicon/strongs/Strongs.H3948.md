**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎לֶקַח‎</span></big>** **leqach** <small>*(leh'-kakh)*</small> ==**teaching**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H3947|‎לָקַח‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ doctrine, learning, fair speech.
- properly, ***something received***, i.e. (mentally) ***instruction*** (whether on the part of the teacher or hearer)
-  also (in an active and sinister sense) ***inveiglement***

**1)** learning, teaching, insight
$\quad$ **1a)** instruction (obj)
$\quad$ **1b)** teaching (thing taught)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** teaching-power
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** persuasiveness
