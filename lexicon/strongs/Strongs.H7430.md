**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָמַשׂ‎</span></big>** **râmas** <small>*(raw-mas')*</small> ==**to creep**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ creep, move.
- properly, ***to glide swiftly***, i.e. ***to crawl*** or ***move with short steps***
-  by analogy ***to swarm***

**1)** to creep, move lightly, move about, walk on all fours
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to creep, teem (of all creeping things)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to creep (of animals)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to move lightly, glide about (of water animals)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to move about (of all land animals generally)
