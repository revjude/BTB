**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֵיד‎</span></big>** **ʼêyd** <small>*(ade)*</small> ==**calamity**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from the same as **<big>[[H0181|‎אוּד‎]]</big>** (in the sense of bending down)</small>

$\quad$ calamity, destruction.
- ***oppression***
-  by implication ***misfortune***, ***ruin***

**1)** distress, burden, calamity
$\quad$ **1a)** burden (of the righteous)
$\quad$ **1b)** calamity (of nation)
$\quad$ **1c)** disaster (of wicked)
$\quad$ **1d)** day of calamity
