- ***oppression***
-  by implication ***misfortune***, ***ruin***

**1)** distress, burden, calamity
$\quad$ **1a)** burden (of the righteous)
$\quad$ **1b)** calamity (of nation)
$\quad$ **1c)** disaster (of wicked)
$\quad$ **1d)** day of calamity
