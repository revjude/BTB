<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּרָה‎</span></big> **gârâh** <small>*(gaw-raw')*</small> ==**to stir up**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ contend, meddle, stir up, strive.
- properly, ***to grate***, i.e. (figuratively) ***to anger***

**1)** to cause strife, stir up, contend, meddle, strive, be stirred up
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel) to stir up strife, excite strife
$\quad$ **1b)**(Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to excite oneself against, engage in strife
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to excite oneself (against foe), wage war
