**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כֹּה‎</span></big>** **kôh** <small>*(ko)*</small> ==**thus**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:Adv|H:Adv]]</small></sup> 
<small>from the prefix k and **<big>[[H1931|‎הוּא‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ also, here, + hitherto, like, on the other side, so (and much), such, on that manner, (on) this (manner, side, way, way and that way), + mean while, yonder.
- properly, ***like this***, i.e. by implication, (of manner) ***thus*** (or so)
-  also (of place) ***here*** (or hither)
-  or (of time) ***now***

**1)** thus, here, in this manner
$\quad$ **1a)** thus, so
$\quad$ **1b)** here, here and there
$\quad$ **1c)** until now, until now...until then, meanwhile
