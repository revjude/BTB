<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָעַשׁ‎</span></big> **râʻash** <small>*(raw-ash)*</small> ==**to shake**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ make afraid, (re-) move, quake, (make to) shake, (make to) tremble.
- ***to undulate*** (as the earth, the sky, etc.
-  also a field of grain), particularly ***through fear***
-  specifically, ***to spring*** (as a locust)

**1)** to quake, shake
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to quake, shake
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be made to quake
$\quad$ **1c)**(Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to cause to quake
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to cause to spring or leap (of horse)
