<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ assemble, fight, perform, muster, wait upon, war.
- ***to mass*** (an army or servants)

**1)** to go forth, wage war, fight, serve
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to wage war, fight, go forth to war
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to serve (at sacred tent)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to cause to go forth, muster
