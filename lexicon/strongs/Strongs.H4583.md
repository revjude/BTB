**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָעוֹן‎</span></big>** **mâʻôwn** <small>*(maw-ohn')*</small> ==**habitation**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">מָעִין</span></big>** **mâʻîyn** <small>*(maw-een')*</small>; (1 Chronicles 4:41), from the same as **<big>[[H5772|‎עוֹנָה‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ den, dwelling((-) place), habitation.
- ***an abode***, ***of God*** (the Tabernacle or the Temple), ***men*** (their home) or ***animals*** (their lair)
-  hence, ***a retreat*** (asylum)

**1)** dwelling, habitation, refuge
$\quad$ **1a)** lair, refuge (of jackals)
$\quad$ **1b)** dwelling
