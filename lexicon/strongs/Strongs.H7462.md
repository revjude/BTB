<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָעָה‎</span></big> **râʻâh** <small>*(raw-aw')*</small> ==**House of Shepherds**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--L|N:N--L]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] break, companion, keep company with, devour, eat up, evil entreat, feed, use as a friend, make friendship with, herdman, keep (sheep) (-er), pastor, [phrase] shearing house, shepherd, wander, waste.
- ***to tend a flock***
-  i.e. ***pasture it***
-  ***in***transitively, ***to graze*** (literally or figuratively)
-  generally ***to rule***
-  by extension, ***to associate with*** (as a friend)

**1)** to pasture, tend, graze, feed
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to tend, pasture
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** to shepherd
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1b)** of ruler, teacher (fig)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1c)** of people as flock (fig)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1d)** shepherd, herdsman (subst)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to  feed, graze
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2a)** of cows, sheep etc (literal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2b)** of idolater, Israel as flock (fig)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) shepherd, shepherdess
**2)** to associate with, be a friend of (meaning probable)
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to associate with
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hithpael) to be companions
**3)** (Piel) to be a special friend
