$\quad$ prophesy(-ing), make self a prophet.

**1)** to prophesy

$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to prophesy
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** under influence of divine spirit
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1b)** of false prophets
$\quad$ **1b)**(Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to prophesy
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1a)** under influence of divine spirit
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1b)** of false prophets
