**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּלַח‎</span></big>** **gâlach** <small>*(gaw-lakh')*</small> ==**to shave**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ poll, shave (off).
- properly, ***to be bald***, i.e. (causatively) ***to shave***
-  figuratively ***to lay waste***

**1)** to poll, shave, shave off, be bald
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to shave
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to shave off
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** (fig. of devastation)
$\quad$ **1b)**(Pual) to be shaven
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to shave oneself
