**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎דָּרַךְ‎</span></big>** **dârak** <small>*(daw-rak')*</small> ==**to tread**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ archer, bend, come, draw, go (over), guide, lead (forth), thresh, tread (down), walk.
- ***to tread***
-  by implication, ***to walk***
-  also ***to string a******bow*** (by treading on it in bending)

**1)** to tread, bend, lead, march
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to tread, march, march forth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to tread on, tread upon
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to tread (a press)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to tread (bend) a bow
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** archer, bow-benders (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to tread, tread down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to tread (bend with the foot) a bow
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to cause to go, lead, march, tread
