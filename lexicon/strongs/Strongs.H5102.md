<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָהַר‎</span></big> **nâhar** <small>*(naw-har')*</small> ==**to flow**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ flow (together), be lightened.
- ***to sparkle***, i.e. (figuratively) ***be cheerful***
-  hence (from the sheen of a running stream) ***to flow***, i.e. (figuratively) ***assemble***

**1)** to shine, beam, light, burn
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to beam, be radiant
**2)** to flow, stream
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to flow, stream
