<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ dismiss, free, let (shoot) out, slip away.
- ***to cleave*** or ***burst through***, i.e. (causatively) ***to emit***, whether literal or figurative (gape)

**1)** to separate, set free, remove, open, escape, burst through
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to remove (oneself), escape
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to set free, let out
