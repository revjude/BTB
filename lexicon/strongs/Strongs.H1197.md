<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּעַר‎</span></big> **bâʻar** <small>*(baw-ar')*</small> ==**to burn: burn**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; also as denominative from <span class="ashebrew">‎בַּעַר‎</span><sup><small>[[H1198]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ be brutish, bring (put, take) away, burn, (cause to) eat (up), feed, heat, kindle, set (on fire), waste.
- ***to kindle***, i.e. ***consume*** (by fire or by eating)
- ***  to be***(-come) ***brutish***

**1)** to burn, consume, kindle, be kindled
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to begin to burn, be kindled, start burning
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to burn, be burning
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to burn, consume
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** Jehovah's wrath, human wrath (fig.)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to kindle, burn
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to consume, remove (of guilt) (fig.)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to kindle
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to burn up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to consume (destroy)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to burn
v denom
**2)** to be stupid, brutish, barbarous
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to be stupid, dull-hearted, unreceptive
$\quad$ **2b)** (Niphal) to be stupid, dull-hearted
$\quad$ **2c)** (Piel) to feed, graze
$\quad$ **2d)** (Hiphil) to cause to be grazed over
