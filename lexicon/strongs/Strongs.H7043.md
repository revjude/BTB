<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָלַל‎</span></big> **qâlal** <small>*(kaw-lal')*</small> ==**to lighten**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ abate, make bright, bring into contempt, (ac-) curse, despise, (be) ease(-y, -ier), (be a, make, make somewhat, move, seem a, set) light(-en, -er, -ly, -ly afflict, -ly esteem, thing), [idiom] slight(-ly), be swift(-er), (be, be more, make, re-) vile, whet.
- ***to be*** (causatively, make) ***light***, literally (swift, small, sharp, etc.) or figuratively (easy, trifling, vile, etc.)

**1)** to be slight, be swift, be trifling, be of little account, be light
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be slight, be abated (of water)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be swift
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be trifling, be of little account
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be swift, show oneself swift
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to appear trifling, be too trifling, be insignificant
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be lightly esteemed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to make despicable
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to curse
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be cursed
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** to make light, lighten
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e2)** to treat with contempt, bring contempt or dishonour
$\quad$ **1f)** (Pilpel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f1)** to shake
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f2)** to whet
$\quad$ **1g)** (Hithpalpel) to shake oneself, be moved to and fro
