**<big><span class="asgreek">‎παρίστημι‎</span></big>** **parístēmi** <small>*(par-is'-tay-mee)*</small> ==**to stand by**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G3844|‎παρά‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2476|‎ἵστημι‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ assist, bring before, command, commend, give presently, present, prove, provide, shew, stand (before, by, here, up, with), yield.
- to stand beside, i.e. (transitively) to exhibit, proffer, (specially), recommend, (figuratively) substantiate
-  or (intransitively) to be at hand (or ready), aid
