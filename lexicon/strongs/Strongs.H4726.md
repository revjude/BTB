<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָקוֹר‎</span></big> **mâqôwr** <small>*(maw-kore')*</small> ==**fountain**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup>

$\quad$ fountain, issue, spring, well(-spring).
- properly, ***something dug***, i.e. ***a*** (general) ***source*** (of water, even when naturally flowing
-  also of tears, blood (by euphemism, of the female pudenda)
-  figuratively, ***of happiness***, ***wisdom***, ***progeny)***

**1)** spring, fountain
$\quad$ **1a)** spring
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** of source of life, joy, purification (fig.)
$\quad$ **1b)** of the eye (fig.)
$\quad$ **1c)** source (of menstruous blood)
$\quad$ **1d)** flow (of blood after child birth)
