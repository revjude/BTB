<big><span class="ashebrew">‎זָנָה‎</span></big> **zânâh** <small>*(zaw-naw')*</small> ==**to fornicate**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (highly-fed and therefore wanton)</small>

$\quad$ (cause to) commit fornication, [idiom] continually, [idiom] great, (be an, play the) harlot, (cause to be, play the) whore, (commit, fall to) whoredom, (cause to) go a-whoring, whorish.
- ***to commit adultery*** (usually of the female, and less often of simple fornication, rarely of involuntary ravishment)
-  figuratively, ***to commit idolatry*** (the Jewish people being regarded as the spouse of Jehovah)

**1)** to commit fornication, be a harlot, play the harlot
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be a harlot, act as a harlot, commit fornication
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to commit adultery
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be a cult prostitute
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to be unfaithful (to God) (fig.)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) to play the harlot
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to cause to commit adultery
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to force into prostitution
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to commit fornication
