**<big><span class="asgreek">‎πράσσω‎</span></big>** **prássō** <small>*(pras'-so)*</small> ==**to do/require**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary verb</small>

$\quad$ commit, deeds, do, exact, keep, require, use arts.
- to "practise", i.e. perform repeatedly or habitually (thus differing from **<big>[[G4160|‎ποιέω‎]]</big>**, which properly refers to a single act)
-  by implication, to execute, accomplish, etc.
-  specially, to collect (dues), fare (personally)
