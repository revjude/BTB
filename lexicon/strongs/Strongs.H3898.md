<big><span class="ashebrew">‎לָחַם‎</span></big> **lâcham** <small>*(law-kham')*</small> ==**to fight**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ devour, eat, [idiom] ever, fight(-ing), overcome, prevail, (make) war(-ring).
- ***to feed on***
-  figuratively, ***to consume***
-  by implication, ***to battle*** (as destruction)

**1)** to fight, do battle, make war
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to fight, do battle
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to engage in battle, wage war
**2)** (Qal) to eat, use as food
