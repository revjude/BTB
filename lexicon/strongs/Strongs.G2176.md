<big><span class="asgreek">‎εὐώνυμος‎</span></big> **euṓnymos** <small>*(yoo-o'-noo-mos)*</small> ==**left/south**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:A|G:A]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="asgreek">εὖ</span><sup><small>[[G2095]]</small></sup> and <span class="asgreek">ὄνομα</span><sup><small>[[G3686]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ (on the) left.
- properly, well-named (good-omened), i.e. the left (which was the lucky side among the pagan Greeks)
-  neuter as adverbial, at the left hand
