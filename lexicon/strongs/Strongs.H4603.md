<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָעַל‎</span></big> **mâʻal** <small>*(maw-al')*</small> ==**be unfaithful**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ transgress, (commit, do a) trespass(-ing).
- properly, ***to cover up***
-  used only figuratively, ***to act covertly***, i.e. ***treacherously***

**1)** to act unfaithfully, act treacherously, transgress, commit a trespass
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to act unfaithfully or treacherously
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** against man
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** against God
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** against devoted thing
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** against husband
