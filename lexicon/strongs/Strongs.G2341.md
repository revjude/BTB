**<big><span class="asgreek">‎θηριομαχέω‎</span></big>** **thēriomachéō** <small>*(thay-ree-om-akh-eh'-o)*</small> ==**to fight beasts**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from a compound of **<big>[[G2342|‎θηρίον‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G3164|‎μάχομαι‎]]</big>**</small>

- to be a beast-fighter (in the gladiatorial show), i.e. (figuratively) to encounter (furious men)
