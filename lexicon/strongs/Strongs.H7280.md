**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָגַע‎</span></big>** **râgaʻ** <small>*(raw-gah')*</small> ==**to disturb**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ break, divide, find ease, be a moment, (cause, give, make to) rest, make suddenly.
- properly, ***to toss violently and suddenly*** (the sea with waves, the skin with boils)
-  figuratively (in a favorable manner) ***to settle***, i.e. ***quiet***
-  specifically, ***to wink*** (from the motion of the eye-lids)

**1)** to act in an instant, stir up, disturb
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to stir up, disturb
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to make a twinkling
**2)** to rest or repose, be at rest or repose, settle, quiet, give rest
$\quad$ **2a)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a1)** to cause to rest (of sword, of the sea)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a2)** to wink (with prep)
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b1)** to give rest to
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b2)** to rest, repose
**3)** to harden
