<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָקַר‎</span></big> **ʻâqar** <small>*(aw-kar')*</small> ==**to uproot**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ dig down, hough, pluck up, root up.
- ***to pluck up*** (especially by the roots)
-  specifically, ***to hamstring***
-  figuratively, ***to exterminate***

**1)** to pluck up, root up
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to pluck up, root up
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be plucked up
**2)** to cut, hamstring
$\quad$ **2a)** (Piel) to cut, hamstring
