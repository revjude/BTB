<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָשַׁת‎</span></big> **ʻâshath** <small>*(aw-shath')*</small> ==**to gleam**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ shine, think.
- probably ***to be sleek***, i.e. ***glossy***
-  hence (through the idea of polishing) ***to excogitate*** (as if forming in the mind)

**1)** (Qal) to be smooth, be shiny, gleam
**2)** (Hithpael) to think
