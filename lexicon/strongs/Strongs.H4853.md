**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מַשָּׂא‎</span></big>** **massâʼ** <small>*(mas-saw')*</small> ==**burden**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5375|‎נָשָׂא‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ burden, carry away, prophecy, [idiom] they set, song, tribute.
- § Massa = *"burden"*
- ***a burden***
-  specifically, ***tribute***, or (abstractly) ***porterage***
-  figuratively, ***an utterance***, ***chiefly a******doom***, especially ***singing***
-  ***mental***, ***desire***

n m
**1)** load, bearing, tribute, burden, lifting
$\quad$ **1a)** load, burden
$\quad$ **1b)** lifting, uplifting, that to which the soul lifts itself up
$\quad$ **1c)** bearing, carrying
$\quad$ **1d)** tribute, that which is carried or brought or borne
**2)** utterance, oracle, burden
n pr m
**3)** (BDB) a son of Ishmael
