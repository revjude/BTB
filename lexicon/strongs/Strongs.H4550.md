**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מַסַּע‎</span></big>** **maççaʻ** <small>*(mas-sah')*</small> ==**journey**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup>

$\quad$ journey(-ing).
- ***a departure*** (from striking the tents), i.e. ***march*** (not necessarily a single day's travel)
-  by implication, ***a station*** (or point of departure)

**1)** a pulling up (of stakes), breaking camp, setting out, journey
$\quad$ **1a)** pulling up, breaking camp
$\quad$ **1b)** setting out
$\quad$ **1c)** station, stage, journey
