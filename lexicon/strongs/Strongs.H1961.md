<big><span class="ashebrew">‎הָיָה‎</span></big> **hâyâh** <small>*(haw-yaw)*</small> ==**to be**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root (compare <span class="ashebrew">‎הָוָא‎</span><sup><small>[[H1933]]</small></sup>)</small>

$\quad$ beacon, [idiom] altogether, be(-come), accomplished, committed, like), break, cause, come (to pass), do, faint, fall, [phrase] follow, happen, [idiom] have, last, pertain, quit (one-) self, require, [idiom] use.
- ***to exist***, i.e. ***be*** or ***become***, ***come to pass*** (always emphatic, and not a mere copula or auxiliary)

**1)** to be, become, come to pass, exist, happen, fall out
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** ——-
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** to happen, fall out, occur, take place, come about, come to pass
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1b)** to come about, come to pass
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to come into being, become
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2a)** to arise, appear, come
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2b)** to become
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2b1)** to become
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2b2)** to become like
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2b3)** to be instituted, be established
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3a)** to exist, be in existence
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3b)** to abide, remain, continue (with word of place or time)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3c)** to stand, lie, be in, be at, be situated (with word of locality)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3d)** to accompany, be with
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to occur, come to pass, be done, be brought about
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be done, be finished, be gone
