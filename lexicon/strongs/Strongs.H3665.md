<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּנַע‎</span></big> **kânaʻ** <small>*(kaw-nah')*</small> ==**be humble**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bring down (low), into subjection, under, humble (self), subdue.
- properly, ***to bend the knee***
-  hence, ***to humiliate***, ***vanquish***

**1)** to be humble, be humbled, be subdued, be brought down, be low, be under, be brought into subjection
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to humble oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be humbled, be subdued
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to humble
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to subdue
