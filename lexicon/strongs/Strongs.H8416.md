<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תְּהִלָּה‎</span></big> **tᵉhillâh** <small>*(teh-hil-law')*</small> ==**praise**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎הָלַל‎</span><sup><small>[[H1984]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ praise.
- ***laudation***
-  specifically (concretely) ***a hymn***

**1)** praise, song or hymn of praise
$\quad$ **1a)** praise, adoration, thanksgiving (paid to God)
$\quad$ **1b)** act of general or public praise
$\quad$ **1c)** praise-song (as title)
$\quad$ **1d)** praise (demanded by qualities or deeds or attributes of God)
$\quad$ **1e)** renown, fame, glory
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** of Damascus, God
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e2)** object of praise, possessor of renown (fig)
