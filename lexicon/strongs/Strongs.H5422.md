$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to pull down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to break down, break off
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be pulled or broken down
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to tear down
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be torn down
