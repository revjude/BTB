<big><span class="ashebrew">‎צָעָה‎</span></big> **tsâʻâh** <small>*(tsaw-aw')*</small> ==**to march**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ captive exile, travelling, (cause to) wander(-er).
- ***to tip over*** (for the purpose of spilling or pouring out), i.e. (figuratively) ***depopulate***
-  by implication, ***to imprison*** or ***conquer***
-  (reflexive) ***to lie down*** (for coitus)

**1)** to stoop, bend, incline
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to stoop, bend
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to incline, tip
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to tip over
