<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָתַר‎</span></big> **yâthar** <small>*(yaw-thar')*</small> ==**to remain**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ excel, leave (a remnant), left behind, too much, make plenteous, preserve, (be, let) remain(-der, -ing, -nant), reserve, residue, rest.
- ***to jut over*** or ***exceed***
-  by implication, ***to excel***
-  (intransitively) ***to remain*** or ***be left***
-  causatively, ***to leave***, ***cause to abound***, ***preserve***

**1)** to be left over, remain, remain over, leave
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) remainder (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be left over, remain over, be left behind
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to leave over, leave
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to save over, preserve alive
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to excel, show pre-eminence
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to show excess, have more than enough, have an excess
