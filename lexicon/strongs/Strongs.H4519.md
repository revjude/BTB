<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מְנַשֶּׁה‎</span></big> **Mᵉnashsheh** <small>*(men-ash-sheh')*</small> ==**Manasseh**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎נָשָׁה‎</span><sup><small>[[H5382]]</small></sup>; causing to forget</small>

$\quad$ Manasseh.
- § Manasseh = *"causing to forget"*
- ***Menashsheh***, a grandson of Jacob, also the tribe descended from him, and its territory

**1)** the eldest son of Joseph and progenitor of the tribe of Manasseh
$\quad$ **1a)** the tribe descended from Manasseh
$\quad$ **1b)** the territory occupied by the tribe of Manasseh
**2)** son of king Hezekiah of Judah and himself king of Judah; he was the immediate and direct cause for the exile
**3)** a descendant of Pahath-moab who put away a foreign wife in the time of Ezra
**4)** a descendant of Hashum who put away a foreign wife in the time of Ezra
