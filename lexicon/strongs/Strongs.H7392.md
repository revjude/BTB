<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָכַב‎</span></big> **râkab** <small>*(raw-kab')*</small> ==**to ride**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bring (on (horse-) back), carry, get (oneself) up, on (horse-) back, put, (cause to, make to) ride (in a chariot, on, -r), set.
- ***to ride*** (on an animal or in a vehicle)
-  causatively, ***to place upon*** (for riding or generally), ***to despatch***

**1)** to mount and ride, ride
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to mount, mount and sit or ride
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to ride, be riding
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** rider (subst)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cause to ride, cause to (mount and) ride
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to cause to draw (plough, etc)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to cause to ride upon (fig)
