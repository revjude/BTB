<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יֹאשִׁיָּה‎</span></big> **Yôʼshîyâh** <small>*(yo-shee-yaw')*</small> ==**Josiah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>or <span class="ashebrew">יֹאשִׁיָּהוּ</span> **Yôʼshîyâhûw** <small>*(yoshe-yaw'-hoo)*</small>; from the same root as <span class="ashebrew">‎אֲשׁוּיָה‎</span><sup><small>[[H0803]]</small></sup> and <span class="ashebrew">‎יָהּ‎</span><sup><small>[[H3050]]</small></sup>; founded of Jah</small>

$\quad$ Josiah.
- § Josiah = *"whom Jehovah heals"*
- ***Joshijah***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** son of Amon by Jedidah who succeeded his father to the throne of Judah and reigned for 31 years; his reign is noteworthy for the great revivals back to the worship of Jehovah which he led
**2)** a returned exile and son of Zephaniah at whose house took place the solemn and symbolical crowning of Joshua the high priest in the time of Zechariah the prophet
