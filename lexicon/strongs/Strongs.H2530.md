<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָמַד‎</span></big> **châmad** <small>*(khaw-mad')*</small> ==**to desire**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ beauty, greatly beloved, covet, delectable thing, ([idiom] great) delight, desire, goodly, lust, (be) pleasant (thing), precious (thing).
- ***to delight in***

v
**1)** to desire, covet, take pleasure in, delight in
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to desire
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be desirable
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to delight greatly, desire greatly
n f
**2)** desirableness, preciousness
