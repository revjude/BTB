<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָרַד‎</span></big> **yârad** <small>*(yaw-rad')*</small> ==**to go down**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] abundantly, bring down, carry down, cast down, (cause to) come(-ing) down, fall (down), get down, go(-ing) down(-ward), hang down, [idiom] indeed, let down, light (down), put down (off), (cause to, let) run down, sink, subdue, take down.
- ***to descend*** (literally, to go downwards
-  or conventionally to a lower region, as the shore, a boundary, the enemy, etc.
-  or figuratively, to fall)
-  causatively, ***to bring down*** (in all the above applications)

**1)** to go down, descend, decline, march down, sink down
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to go or come down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to sink
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be prostrated
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to come down (of revelation)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to bring down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to send down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to take down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to lay prostrate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** to let down
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to be brought down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to be taken down
