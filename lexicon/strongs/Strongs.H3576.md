<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּזַב‎</span></big> **kâzab** <small>*(kaw-zab')*</small> ==**to lie**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ fail, (be found a, make a) liar, lie, lying, be in vain.
- ***to lie*** (i.e. deceive), literally or figuratively

**1)** to lie, tell a lie, be a liar, be found a liar, be in vain, fail
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) liar (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be proven to be lying
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to lie, tell a lie, tell a lie with, deceive
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to disappoint, fail
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to make a liar, prove to be a liar
