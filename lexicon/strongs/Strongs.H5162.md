**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָחַם‎</span></big>** **nâcham** <small>*(naw-kham')*</small> ==**to be sorry: comfort**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ comfort (self), ease (one's self), repent(-er,-ing, self).
- properly, ***to sigh***, i.e. ***breathe strongly***
-  by implication, ***to be sorry***, i.e. (in a favorable sense) ***to pity***, ***console*** or (reflexively) ***rue***
-  or (unfavorably) ***to avenge*** (oneself)

**1)** to be sorry, console oneself, repent, regret, comfort, be comforted
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be sorry, be moved to pity, have compassion
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be sorry, rue, suffer grief, repent
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to comfort oneself, be comforted
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to comfort oneself, ease oneself
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to comfort, console
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be comforted, be consoled
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to be sorry, have compassion
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to rue, repent of
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to comfort oneself, be comforted
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d4)** to ease oneself
