<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָלַם‎</span></big> **châlam** <small>*(khaw-lam')*</small> ==**be healthy**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (cause to) dream(-er), be in good liking, recover.
- properly, ***to bind firmly***, i.e. (by implication) ***to be*** (causatively to make) ***plump***
-  also (through the figurative sense of dumbness) ***to dream***

**1)** to dream
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to dream (ordinary)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to dream (prophetic)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to dream (of false prophets)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to dream
**2)** to be healthy, be strong
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to be healthy
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hiphil) to restore to health
