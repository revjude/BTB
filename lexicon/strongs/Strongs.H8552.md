**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תָּמַם‎</span></big>** **tâmam** <small>*(taw-mam')*</small> ==**to finish**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ accomplish, cease, be clean (pass-) ed, consume, have done, (come to an, have an, make an) end, fail, come to the full, be all gone, [idiom] be all here, be (make) perfect, be spent, sum, be (shew self) upright, be wasted, whole.
- ***to complete***, in a good or a bad sense, literal, or figurative, transitive or intransitive

**1)** to be complete, be finished, be at an end
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be finished, be completed
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** completely, wholly, entirely (as auxiliary with verb)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be finished, come to an end, cease
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be complete (of number)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to be consumed, be exhausted, be spent
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to be finished, be consumed, be destroyed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to be complete, be sound, be unimpaired, be upright (ethically)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7)** to complete, finish
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a8)** to be completely crossed over
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be consumed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to finish, complete, perfect
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to finish, cease doing, leave off doing
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to complete, sum up, make whole
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to destroy (uncleanness)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c5)** to make sound
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael) to deal in integrity, act uprightly
