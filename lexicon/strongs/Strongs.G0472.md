**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἀντέχομαι‎</span></big>** **antéchomai** <small>*(an-tekh'-om-ahee)*</small> ==**to cling to**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G0473|‎ἀντί‎]]</big>** and the middle voice of **<big>[[G2192|‎ἔχω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ hold fast, hold to, support.
- to hold oneself opposite to, i.e. (by implication) adhere to
-  by extension to care for
