<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מוֹצָא‎</span></big> **môwtsâʼ** <small>*(mo-tsaw')*</small> ==**exit**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>or <span class="ashebrew">מֹצָא</span> **môtsâʼ** <small>*(mo-tsaw')*</small>; from <span class="ashebrew">‎יָצָא‎</span><sup><small>[[H3318]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ brought out, bud, that which came out, east, going forth, goings out, that which (thing that) is gone out, outgoing, proceeded out, spring, vein, (water-) course (springs).
- ***a going forth***, i.e. (the act) ***an egress***, or (the place) ***an exit***
-  hence, ***a source*** or ***product***
-  specifically, ***dawn***, ***the rising of the sun*** (the East), ***exportation***, ***utterance***, ***a gate***, ***a fountain***, ***a mine***, ***a meadow*** (as producing grass)

**1)** act or place of going out or forth, issue, export, source, spring
$\quad$ **1a)** a going forth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** rising (sun), going forth of a command
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** goings forth, those going forth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** way out, exit
$\quad$ **1b)** that which goes forth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** utterance
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** export
$\quad$ **1c)** place of going forth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** source or spring (of water)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** place of departure
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** east (of sun)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** mine (of silver)
