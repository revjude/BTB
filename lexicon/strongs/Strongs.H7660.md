<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ embroider, set.
- ***to interweave*** (colored) ***threads in squares***
-  by implication (of reticulation) ***to inchase gems in gold***

**1)** to weave in checkered or plaited work or pattern (meaning probable)
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel) to weave, plait
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) inwoven, set (participle)
