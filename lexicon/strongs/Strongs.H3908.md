<big><span class="ashebrew">‎לַחַשׁ‎</span></big> **lachash** <small>*(lakh'-ash)*</small> ==**charm**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎לָחַשׁ‎</span><sup><small>[[H3907]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ charmed, earring, enchantment, orator, prayer.
- properly, ***a whisper***, i.e. by implication, (in a good sense) ***a private prayer***, (in a bad one) ***an incantation***
-  concretely, ***an amulet***

**1)** whispering, charming
$\quad$ **1a)** serpent-charming
$\quad$ **1b)** charms, amulets (worn by women)
$\quad$ **1c)** whisper (of prayer)
