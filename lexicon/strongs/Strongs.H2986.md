**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָבַל‎</span></big>** **yâbal** <small>*(yaw-bal')*</small> ==**to conduct**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bring (forth), carry, lead (forth).
- properly, ***to flow***
-  causatively, ***to bring*** (especially with pomp)

**1)** to bring, lead, carry, conduct, bear along
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to bear along, bring
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to carry away, lead away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to lead, conduct
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be borne along
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be borne (to the grave)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be brought, be led, be conducted
