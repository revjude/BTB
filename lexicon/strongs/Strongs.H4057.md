<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מִדְבָּר‎</span></big> **midbâr** <small>*(mid-bawr')*</small> ==**mouth**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎דָבַר‎</span><sup><small>[[H1696]]</small></sup> in the sense of driving</small>

$\quad$ desert, south, speech, wilderness.
- ***a pasture*** (i.e. open field, whither cattle are driven)
-  by implication, ***a desert***
-  also ***speech*** (including its organs)

**1)** wilderness
$\quad$ **1a)** pasture
$\quad$ **1b)** uninhabited land, wilderness
$\quad$ **1c)** large tracts of wilderness (around cities)
$\quad$ **1d)** wilderness (fig.)
**2)** mouth
$\quad$ **2a)** mouth (as organ of speech)
