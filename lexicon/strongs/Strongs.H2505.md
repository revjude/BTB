<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָלַק‎</span></big> **châlaq** <small>*(khaw-lak')*</small> ==**to divide**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ deal, distribute, divide, flatter, give, (have, im-) part(-ner), take away a portion, receive, separate self, (be) smooth(-er).
- ***to be smooth*** (figuratively)
-  by implication (as smooth stones were used for lots) ***to apportion*** or ***separate***

**1)** to divide, share, plunder, allot, apportion, assign
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to divide, apportion
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to assign, distribute
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to assign, impart
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to share
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to divide up, plunder
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to divide oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be divided
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to assign, distribute
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to divide, apportion
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to assign, distribute
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to scatter
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be divided
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hiphil) to receive a portion or part
$\quad$ **1f)** (Hithpael) to divide among themselves
**2)** to be smooth, slippery, deceitful
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to be smooth, slippery
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b1)** to be smooth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b2)** to flatter
