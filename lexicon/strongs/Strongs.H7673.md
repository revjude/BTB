<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁבַת‎</span></big> **shâbath** <small>*(shaw-bath')*</small> ==**to cease**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (cause to, let, make to) cease, celebrate, cause (make) to fail, keep (sabbath), suffer to be lacking, leave, put away (down), (make to) rest, rid, still, take away.
- ***to repose***, i.e. ***desist from exertion***
-  used in many implied relations (causative, figurative or specific)

**1)** to cease, desist, rest
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to cease
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to rest, desist (from labour)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to cease
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to cause to cease, put an end to
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to exterminate, destroy
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to cause to desist from
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to remove
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c5)** to cause to fail
**2)** (Qal) to keep or observe the sabbath
