<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָנַף‎</span></big> **ʼânaph** <small>*(aw-naf')*</small> ==**be angry**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be angry (displeased).
- ***to breathe hard***, i.e. ***be enraged***

**1)** to be angry, to be displeased, to breathe hard
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be angry (of God)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hithpael) to be angry (always of God)
