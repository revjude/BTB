<big><span class="ashebrew">‎צָלַח‎</span></big> **tsâlach** <small>*(tsaw-lakh')*</small> ==**to rush**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>or <span class="ashebrew">צָלֵחַ</span> **tsâlêach** <small>*(tsaw-lay'-akh)*</small>; a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ break out, come (mightily), go over, be good, be meet, be profitable, (cause to, effect, make to, send) prosper(-ity, -ous, -ously).
- ***to push forward***, in various senses (literal or figurative, transitive or intransitive)

**1)** (Qal) to rush
**2)** to advance, prosper, make progress, succeed, be profitable
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to prosper
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b1)** to make prosperous, bring to successful issue, cause to prosper
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b2)** to show or experience prosperity, prosper
