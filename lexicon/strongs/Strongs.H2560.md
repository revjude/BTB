**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָמַר‎</span></big>** **châmar** <small>*(khaw-mar')*</small> ==**to aggitate**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root; also  as denominative (from **<big>[[H2564|‎חֵמָר‎]]</big>**)</small>

$\quad$ daub, befoul, be red, trouble.
- properly, ***to boil up***
-  hence, ***to ferment*** (with scum)
-  ***to glow*** (with redness)
-  ***to smear with pitch***

**1)** to boil, foam, foam up, ferment
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to boil, foam up
$\quad$ **1b)** (Poalal) to be troubled, be in turmoil
**2)**(Poalal) to be reddened
**3)** (Qal) to daub, seal up, cover or smear with asphalt
