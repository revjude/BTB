<big><span class="ashebrew">‎זוּר‎</span></big> **zûwr** <small>*(zoor)*</small> ==**be a stranger**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (come from) another (man, place), fanner, go away, (e-) strange(-r, thing, woman).
- ***to turn aside*** (especially for lodging)
-  hence ***to be a******foreigner***, ***strange***, ***profane***
-  specifically (active participle) ***to commit adultery***

**1)** to be strange, be a stranger
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to become estranged
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** strange, another, stranger, foreigner, an enemy (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** loathsome (of breath) (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** strange woman, prostitute, harlot (meton)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be estranged
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hophal) to be a stranger, be one alienated
