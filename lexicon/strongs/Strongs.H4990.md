**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מִתְרְדָת‎</span></big>** **Mithrᵉdâth** <small>*(mith-red-awth')*</small> ==**Mithredath**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>of Persian origin</small>

$\quad$ Mithredath.
- § Mithredath = *"given by Mithra"*
- ***Mithredath***, the name of two Persians

**1)** treasurer of king Cyrus of Persia
**2)** a Persian officer stationed at Samaria in the time of Artaxerxes
