<big><span class="asgreek">‎ψυχή‎</span></big> **psychḗ** <small>*(psoo-khay')*</small> ==**soul**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-F|G:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="asgreek">ψύχω</span><sup><small>[[G5594]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ heart (+ -ily), life, mind, soul, + us, + you.
- breath, i.e. (by implication) spirit, abstractly or concretely (the animal sentient principle only
-  thus distinguished on the one hand from <span class="asgreek">πνεῦμα</span><sup><small>[[G4151]]</small></sup>, which is the rational and immortal soul
-  and on the other from <span class="asgreek">ζωή</span><sup><small>[[G2222]]</small></sup>, which is mere vitality, even of plants: these terms thus exactly correspond respectively to the Hebrew <span class="ashebrew">‎נֶפֶשׁ‎</span><sup><small>[[H5315]]</small></sup>, <span class="ashebrew">‎רוּחַ‎</span><sup><small>[[H7307]]</small></sup> and <span class="ashebrew">‎חַי‎</span><sup><small>[[H2416]]</small></sup>)
