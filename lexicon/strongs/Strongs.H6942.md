<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָדַשׁ‎</span></big> **qâdash** <small>*(kaw-dash')*</small> ==**to consecrate: consecate**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ appoint, bid, consecrate, dedicate, defile, hallow, (be, keep) holy(-er, place), keep, prepare, proclaim, purify, sanctify(-ied one, self), [idiom] wholly.
- ***to be*** (causatively, make, pronounce or observe as) ***clean*** (ceremonially or morally)

**1)** to consecrate, sanctify, prepare, dedicate, be hallowed, be holy, be sanctified, be separate
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be set apart, be consecrated
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be hallowed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** consecrated, tabooed
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to show oneself sacred or majestic
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be honoured, be treated as sacred
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be holy
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to set apart as sacred, consecrate, dedicate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to observe as holy, keep sacred
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to honour as sacred, hallow
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to consecrate
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to be consecrated
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** consecrated, dedicated
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** to set apart, devote, consecrate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e2)** to regard or treat as sacred or hallow
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e3)** to consecrate
$\quad$ **1f)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f1)** to keep oneself apart or separate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f2)** to cause Himself to be hallowed (of God)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f3)** to be observed as holy
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f4)** to consecrate oneself
