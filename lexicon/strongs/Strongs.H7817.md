**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁחַח‎</span></big>** **shâchach** <small>*(shaw-khakh')*</small> ==**to bow**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bend, bow (down), bring (cast) down, couch, humble self, be (bring) low, stoop.
- ***to sink*** or ***depress*** (reflexive or causative)

**1)** to bow, crouch, bow down, be bowed down
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be bowed down, be prostrated, be humbled
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to bow (in homage)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to bow (of mourner)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to crouch (of wild beast in lair)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be prostrated, be humbled, be reduced, be weakened, proceed humbly, be bowed down
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to prostrate, lay low, bow down
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpolel) to be cast down, be despairing
