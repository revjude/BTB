<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֶרֶץ‎</span></big> **ʼerets** <small>*(eh'-rets)*</small> ==**land: country/planet**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root probably meaning to be firm</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] common, country, earth, field, ground, land, [idiom] natins, way, [phrase] wilderness, world.
- ***the earth*** (at large, or partitively a land)

**1)** land, earth
$\quad$ **1a)** earth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** whole earth (as opposed to a part)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** earth (as opposed to heaven)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** earth (inhabitants)
$\quad$ **1b)** land
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** country, territory
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** district, region
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** tribal territory
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** piece of ground
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** land of Canaan, Israel
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b6)** inhabitants of land
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b7)** Sheol, land without return, (under) world
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b8)** city (-state)
$\quad$ **1c)** ground, surface of the earth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** ground
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** soil
$\quad$ **1d)** (in phrases)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** people of the land
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** space or distance of country (in measurements of distance)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** level or plain country
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d4)** land of the living
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d5)** end(s) of the earth
$\quad$ **1e)** (almost wholly late in usage)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** lands, countries
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1a)** often in contrast to Canaan
