<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָשָׁה‎</span></big> **nâshâh** <small>*(naw-shaw')*</small> ==**to forget**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ forget, deprive, exact.
- ***to forget***
-  figuratively, ***to neglect***
-  causatively, ***to remit***, ***remove***

**1)** to forget, deprive
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to forget
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be forgotten
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to cause to forget
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to cause to forget, allow to be forgotten
