<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָפַשׁ‎</span></big> **nâphash** <small>*(naw-fash')*</small> ==**be refreshed**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (be) refresh selves (-ed).
- ***to breathe***
-  passively, to be breathed upon , i.e. (figuratively) ***refreshed*** (as if by a current of air)

**1)** (Niphal) to take breath, refresh oneself
