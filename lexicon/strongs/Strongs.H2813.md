<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎חֶשְׁבּוֹן‎</span><sup><small>[[H2808]]</small></sup> and <span class="ashebrew">‎יָהּ‎</span><sup><small>[[H3050]]</small></sup>; thought of Jah</small>

$\quad$ Hashabniah.
- § Hashabniah = *"whom Jehovah regards"*
- ***Chashabnejah***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** the father of Hattush, repairer of the wall
**2)** a Levite who was among those who officiated at the great fast under Ezra and Nehemiah when the covenant was sealed
