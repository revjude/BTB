**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָבַר‎</span></big>** **châbar** <small>*(khaw-bar')*</small> ==**to unite**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ charm(-er), be compact, couple (together), have fellowship with, heap up, join (self, together), league.
- ***to join*** (literally or figuratively)
-  specifically (by means of spells) ***to fascinate***

**1)** to unite, join, bind together, be joined, be coupled, be in league, heap up, have fellowship with, be compact, be a charmer
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to unite, be joined
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to tie magic charms, charm
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to unite with, make an ally of
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to unite, join, ally
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to be allied with, be united
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to be joined together
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to join together, pile up (words)
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to join oneself to, make an alliance, league together
