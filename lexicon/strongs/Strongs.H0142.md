**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָדַר‎</span></big>** **ʼâdar** <small>*(aw-dar')*</small> ==**be glorious**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (become) glorious, honourable.
- ***to expand***, i.e. ***be great*** or (figuratively) ***magnificent***

**1)** to be great, be majestic, wide, noble (poetic)
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) majestic, glorious (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) make glorious
