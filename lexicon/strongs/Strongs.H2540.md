$\quad$ Hammon.
- § Hammon = *"warm springs"*
- ***Chammon***, the name of two places in Palestine

**1)** a town in Asher, apparently not far from Zidon-rabbah
**2)** a town in Naphtali allotted to the Levites; also called 'Hammath' and 'Hammoth-dor'
