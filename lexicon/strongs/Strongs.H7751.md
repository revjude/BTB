<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שׁוּט‎</span></big> **shûwṭ** <small>*(shoot)*</small> ==**to rove**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ go (about, through, to and fro), mariner, rower, run to and fro.
- properly, ***to push forth***
-  (but used only figuratively) ***to lash***, i.e. (the sea with oars) ***to row***
-  by implication, ***to travel***

**1)** to go, go or rove about, go to and fro
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to go or rove about
$\quad$ **1b)** (Polel) to go to and fro, go eagerly or quickly to and fro
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpolel) to run to and fro
**2)** to row
