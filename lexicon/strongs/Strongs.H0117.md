<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אַדִּיר‎</span></big> **ʼaddîyr** <small>*(ad-deer')*</small> ==**great**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:A|H:A]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎אָדַר‎</span><sup><small>[[H0142]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ excellent, famous, gallant, glorious, goodly, lordly, mighty(-ier one), noble, principal, worthy.
- ***wide*** or (generally) ***large***
-  figuratively, ***powerful***

**1)** great, majestic
$\quad$ **1a)** of waters of sea
$\quad$ **1b)** of a tree
$\quad$ **1c)** of kings, nations, gods
**2)** great one, majestic one
$\quad$ **2a)** of nobles, chieftains, servants
