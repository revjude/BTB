<big><span class="asgreek">‎πυνθάνομαι‎</span></big> **pynthánomai** <small>*(poon-than'-om-ahee)*</small> ==**to inquire**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>middle voice prolonged from a primary <greek BETA="PU/QW" unicode="πύθω" translit="pýthō"/> (which occurs only as an alternate in certain tenses)</small>

$\quad$ ask, demand, enquire, understand.
- to question, i.e. ascertain by inquiry (as a matter of information merely
-  and thus differing from <span class="asgreek">ἐρωτάω</span><sup><small>[[G2065]]</small></sup>, which properly means a request as a favor
-  and from <span class="asgreek">αἰτέω</span><sup><small>[[G0154]]</small></sup>, which is strictly a demand for something due
-  as well as from <span class="asgreek">ζητέω</span><sup><small>[[G2212]]</small></sup>, which implies a search for something hidden
-  and from <span class="asgreek">δέομαι</span><sup><small>[[G1189]]</small></sup>, which involves the idea of urgent need)
-  by implication, to learn (by casual intelligence)
