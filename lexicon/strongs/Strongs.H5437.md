<big><span class="ashebrew">‎סָבַב‎</span></big> **çâbab** <small>*(saw-bab')*</small> ==**to turn: turn**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bring, cast, fetch, lead, make, walk, [idiom] whirl, [idiom] round about, be about on every side, apply, avoid, beset (about), besiege, bring again, carry (about), change, cause to come about, [idiom] circuit, (fetch a) compass (about, round), drive, environ, [idiom] on every side, beset (close, come, compass, go, stand) round about, inclose, remove, return, set, sit down, turn (self) (about, aside, away, back).
- ***to revolve***, ***surround***, or ***border***
-  used in various applications, literally and figuratively

**1)** to turn, turn about or around or aside or back or towards, go about or around, surround, encircle, change direction
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to turn, turn about, be brought round, change
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to march or walk around, go partly around, circle about, skirt, make a round, make a circuit, go about to, surround, encompass
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to turn oneself, close round, turn round
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be turned over to
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to turn about, change, transform
$\quad$ **1d)** (Poel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to encompass, surround
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to come about, assemble round
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to march, go about
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d4)** to enclose, envelop
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** to turn, cause to turn, turn back, reverse, bring over, turn into, bring round
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e2)** to cause to go around, surround, encompass
$\quad$ **1f)** (Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f1)** to be turned
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f2)** to be surrounded
