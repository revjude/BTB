**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שִׁמְעָא‎</span></big>** **Shimʻâʼ** <small>*(shim-aw')*</small> ==**Shimea**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>for **<big>[[H8093|‎שִׁמְעָה‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ Shimea, Shimei, Shamma.
- § Shimea = *"fame"*
- ***Shima***, the name of four Israelites

**1)** son of David by Bathsheba
**2)** brother of David, the 3rd son of Jesse
$\quad$ **2a)** also 'Shammah', 'Shimma',  and 'Shimeah'
**3)** a Gershonite Levite, father of Berachiah, and grandfather of Asaph
**4)** a Merarite Levite, son of Uzzah, and father of Haggiah
