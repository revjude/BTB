**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁלַךְ‎</span></big>** **shâlak** <small>*(shaw-lak)*</small> ==**to throw**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ adventure, cast (away, down, forth, off, out), hurl, pluck, throw.
- ***to throw out***, ***down*** or ***away*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to throw, cast, hurl, fling
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to throw, cast, throw away, cast off, shed, cast down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to cast (lots) (fig)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be thrown, be cast
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be cast forth or out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be cast down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to be cast (metaph)
