**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בֹּעַז‎</span></big>** **Bôʻaz** <small>*(bo'-az)*</small> ==**Boaz**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root of uncertain meaning</small>

$\quad$ Boaz.
- § Boaz = *"fleetness"*
- ***Boaz***, the ancestor of David
-  also the name of a pillar in front of the temple

**1)** ancestor of David, kinsman-redeemer to Ruth, daughter-in-law of Naomi
**2)** name of the left of two brazen pillars, 18 cubits high, erected in the porch of Solomon's temple
