**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שְׁבַנְיָה‎</span></big>** **Shᵉbanyâh** <small>*(sheb-an-yaw')*</small> ==**Shebaniah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">שְׁבַנְיָהוּ</span></big>** **Shᵉbanyâhûw** <small>*(sheb-an-yaw'-hoo)*</small>; from the same as **<big>[[H7644|‎שֶׁבְנָא‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3050|‎יָהּ‎]]</big>**; Jah has grown (i.e. prospered)</small>

$\quad$ Shebaniah.
- § Shebaniah = *"increased by Jehovah"*
- ***Shebanjah***, the name of three or four Israelites

**1)** a Levite who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
**2)** a priest who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
**3)** a 2nd Levite who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
**4)** a priest appointed by David to blow with the trumpets before the ark of God
