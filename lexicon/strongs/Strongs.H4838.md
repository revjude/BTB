<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָרַק‎</span></big> **mâraq** <small>*(maw-rak')*</small> ==**to polish**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bright, furbish, scour.
- ***to polish***
-  by implication, ***to sharpen***
-  also ***to rinse***

**1)** to scour, polish
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to scour, polish
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) to be scoured
