<big><span class="ashebrew">‎הָפַךְ‎</span></big> **hâphak** <small>*(haw-fak')*</small> ==**to overturn**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] become, change, come, be converted, give, make (a bed), overthrow (-turn), perverse, retire, tumble, turn (again, aside, back, to the contrary, every way).
- ***to turn about*** or ***over***
-  by implication, ***to change***, ***overturn***, ***return***, ***pervert***

**1)** to turn, overthrow, overturn
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to overturn, overthrow
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to turn, turn about, turn over, turn around
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to change, transform
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to turn oneself, turn, turn back
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to change oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be perverse
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to be turned, be turned over, be changed, be turned against
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** to be reversed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b6)** to be overturned, be overthrown
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b7)** to be upturned
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to transform oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to turn this way and that, turn every way
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to turn on someone
