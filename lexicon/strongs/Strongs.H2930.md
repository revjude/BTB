**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎טָמֵא‎</span></big>** **ṭâmêʼ** <small>*(taw-may')*</small> ==**to defile**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ defile (self), pollute (self), be (make, make self, pronounce) unclean, [idiom] utterly.
- ***to be foul***, especially in a ceremial or moral sense (contaminated)

**1)** to be unclean, become unclean, become impure
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be or become unclean
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** sexually
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** religiously
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** ceremonially
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to defile oneself, be defiled
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1a)** sexually
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1b)** by idolatry
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1c)** ceremonially
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be regarded as unclean
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to defile
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1a)** sexually
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1b)** religiously
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1c)** ceremonially
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to pronounce unclean, declare unclean (ceremonially)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to profane (God's name)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be defiled
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to be unclean
$\quad$ **1f)** (Hothpael) to be defiled
