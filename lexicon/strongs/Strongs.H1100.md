**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בְּלִיַּעַל‎</span></big>** **bᵉlîyaʻal** <small>*(bel-e-yah'-al)*</small> ==**Belial**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H1097|‎בְּלִי‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3276|‎יַעַל‎]]</big>**;(often in connection with **<big>[[H0376|‎אִישׁ‎]]</big>**, **<big>[[H0802|‎אִשָּׁה‎]]</big>**, **<big>[[H1121|‎בֵּן‎]]</big>**, etc.)</small>

$\quad$ Belial, evil, naughty, ungodly (men), wicked.
- ***without profit***, ***worthlessness***
-  by extension, ***destruction***, ***wickedness***

**1)** worthlessness
$\quad$ **1a)** worthless, good for nothing, unprofitable, base fellow
$\quad$ **1b)** wicked
$\quad$ **1c)** ruin, destruction (construct)
