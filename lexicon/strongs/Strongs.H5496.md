<big><span class="ashebrew">‎סוּת‎</span></big> **çûwth** <small>*(sooth)*</small> ==**to incite**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>perhaps denominative from <span class="ashebrew">‎שַׁיִת‎</span><sup><small>[[H7898]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ entice, move, persuade, provoke, remove, set on, stir up, take away.
- properly, ***to prick***, i.e. (figuratively) ***stimulate***
-  by implication, ***to seduce***

**1)** to incite, allure, instigate, entice
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to incite (to a request)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to allure, lure
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to instigate (bad sense)
