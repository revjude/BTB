**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רוּק‎</span></big>** **rûwq** <small>*(rook)*</small> ==**to empty**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] arm, cast out, draw (out), (make) empty, pour forth (out).
- ***to pour out*** (literally or figuratively), i.e. ***empty***

**1)** to make empty, empty out
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to empty, keep empty or hungry
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to pour out or down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to empty out
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hophal) to be emptied out
