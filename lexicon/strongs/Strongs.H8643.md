**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תְּרוּעָה‎</span></big>** **tᵉrûwʻâh** <small>*(ter-oo-aw')*</small> ==**shout**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H7321|‎רוּעַ‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ alarm, blow(-ing) (of, the) (trumpets), joy, jubile, loud noise, rejoicing, shout(-ing), (high, joyful) sound(-ing).
- ***clamor***, i.e. ***acclamation of joy*** or ***a battle-cry***
-  especially ***clangor******of trumpets***, ***as an alarum***

**1)** alarm, signal, sound of tempest, shout, shout or blast of war or alarm or joy
$\quad$ **1a)** alarm of war, war-cry, battle-cry
$\quad$ **1b)** blast (for march)
$\quad$ **1c)** shout of joy (with religious impulse)
$\quad$ **1d)** shout of joy (in general)
