<big><span class="ashebrew">‎לָפַת‎</span></big> **lâphath** <small>*(law-fath')*</small> ==**to twist**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ take hold, turn aside (self).
- properly, ***to bend***, i.e. (by implication) ***to clasp***
-  also (reflexively) ***to turn around*** or ***aside***

**1)** to twist, grasp, turn, grasp with a twisting motion
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to grasp
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to twist, weave, wind
