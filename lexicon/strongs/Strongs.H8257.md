**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁקַע‎</span></big>** **shâqaʻ** <small>*(shaw-kah')*</small> ==**to sink**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>(abbreviated Amos 8:8); a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ make deep, let down, drown, quench, sink.
- ***to subside***
-  by implication, ***to be overflowed***, ***cease***
-  causatively, ***to abate***, ***subdue***

**1)** to sink, sink down, subside
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to sink
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to sink, collapse
$\quad$ **1c)**(Hiphil) to sink down, cause to settle
