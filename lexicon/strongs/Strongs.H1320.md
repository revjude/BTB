<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּשָׂר‎</span></big> **bâsâr** <small>*(baw-sawr')*</small> ==**flesh**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎בָּשַׂר‎</span><sup><small>[[H1319]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ body, (fat, lean) flesh(-ed), kin, (man-) kind, [phrase] nakedness, self, skin.
- ***flesh*** (from its freshness)
-  by extension, ***body***, ***person***
-  also (by euphemistically) ***the pudenda of a******man***

**1)** flesh
$\quad$ **1a)** of the body
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** of humans
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** of animals
$\quad$ **1b)** the body itself
$\quad$ **1c)** male organ of generation (euphemism)
$\quad$ **1d)** kindred, blood-relations
$\quad$ **1e)** flesh as frail or erring (man against God)
$\quad$ **1f)** all living things
$\quad$ **1g)** animals
$\quad$ **1h)** mankind
