<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָפַק‎</span></big> **ʼâphaq** <small>*(aw-fak')*</small> ==**to refrain**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ force (oneself), restrain.
- ***to contain***, i.e. (reflex.) ***abstain***

**1)** to hold, be strong, restrain, hold back
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to restrain oneself, refrain
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to force, compel oneself
