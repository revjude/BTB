<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָבַץ‎</span></big> **râbats** <small>*(raw-bats')*</small> ==**to stretch**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ crouch (down), fall down, make a fold, lay, (cause to, make to) lie (down), make to rest, sit.
- ***to crouch*** (on all four legs folded, like a recumbent animal)
-  ***be implication***, ***to recline***, ***repose***, ***brood***, ***lurk***, ***imbed***

**1)** to stretch oneself out, lie down, lie stretched out
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to lie down, lie
$\quad$ **1b)**(Hiphil) to cause to lie down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** laying (stones)
