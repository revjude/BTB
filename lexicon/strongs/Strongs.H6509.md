**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּרָה‎</span></big>** **pârâh** <small>*(paw-raw')*</small> ==**be fruitful**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bear, bring forth (fruit), (be, cause to be, make) fruitful, grow, increase.
- ***to bear fruit*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to bear fruit, be fruitful, branch off
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to bear fruit, be fruitful
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cause to bear fruit
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to make fruitful
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to show fruitfulness, bear fruit
