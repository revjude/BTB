<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תָּעָה‎</span></big> **tâʻâh** <small>*(taw-aw')*</small> ==**to go astray**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (cause to) go astray, deceive, dissemble, (cause to, make to) err, pant, seduce, (make to) stagger, (cause to) wander, be out of the way.
- ***to vacillate***, i.e. ***reel*** or ***stray*** (literally or figuratively)
-  also causative of both

**1)** to err, wander, go astray, stagger
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to err
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to wander about (physically)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** of intoxication
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** of sin (ethically)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** wandering (of the mind)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be made to wander about, be made to stagger (drunkard)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be led astray (ethically)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to cause to wander
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to cause to wander about (physically)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to cause to wander (of intoxication)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to cause to err, mislead (mentally and morally)
