<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָגַד‎</span></big> **nâgad** <small>*(naw-gad')*</small> ==**to tell**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bewray, [idiom] certainly, certify, declare(-ing), denounce, expound, [idiom] fully, messenger, plainly, profess, rehearse, report, shew (forth), speak, [idiom] surely, tell, utter.
- properly, ***to front***, i.e. ***stand boldly out opposite***
-  by implication (causatively), ***to manifest***
-  figuratively, ***to announce*** (always by word of mouth to one present)
-  specifically, ***to expose***, ***predict***, ***explain***, ***praise***

**1)** to be conspicuous, tell, make known
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil) to tell, declare
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to tell, announce, report
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to declare, make known, expound
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to inform of
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to publish, declare, proclaim
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to avow, acknowledge, confess
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5a)** messenger (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hophal) to be told, be announced, be reported
