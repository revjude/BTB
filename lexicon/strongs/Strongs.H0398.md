<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָכַל‎</span></big> **ʼâkal** <small>*(aw-kal')*</small> ==**to eat**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] at all, burn up, consume, devour(-er, up), dine, eat(-er, up), feed (with), food, [idiom] freely, [idiom] in...wise(-deed, plenty), (lay) meat, [idiom] quite.
- ***to eat*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to eat, devour, burn up, feed
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to eat (human subject)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to eat, devour (of beasts and birds)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to devour, consume (of fire)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to devour, slay (of sword)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to devour, consume, destroy (inanimate subjects - ie, pestilence, drought)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to devour (of oppression)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be eaten (by men)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be devoured, consumed (of fire)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be wasted, destroyed (of flesh)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to cause to eat, feed with
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to cause to devour
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to feed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to cause to eat
$\quad$ **1e)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** consume
