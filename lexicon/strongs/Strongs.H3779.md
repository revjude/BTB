**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כַּשְׂדַּי‎</span></big>** **Kasday** <small>*(kas-dah'-ee)*</small> ==**Chaldean**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--LG|N:N--LG]]</small></sup> 
<small>(Aramaic) corresponding to **<big>[[H3778|‎כַּשְׂדִּי‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ Chaldean.
- § Chaldean = *"clod breakers"*
- ***a Chaldaean*** or inhabitant of Chaldaea
-  by implication, a Magian or professional astrologer

**1)** the inhabitants of Chaldea, living on the lower Euphrates and Tigris
**2)** those persons considered the wisest in the land (by extension)
