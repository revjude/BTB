**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָקָה‎</span></big>** **nâqâh** <small>*(naw-kaw')*</small> ==**to clear**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ acquit [idiom] at all, [idiom] altogether, be blameless, cleanse, (be) clear(-ing), cut off, be desolate, be free, be (hold) guiltless, be (hold) innocent, [idiom] by no means, be quit, be (leave) unpunished, [idiom] utterly, [idiom] wholly.
- ***to be*** (or make) ***clean*** (literally or figuratively)
-  by implication (in an adverse sense) ***to be bare***, i.e. ***extirpated***

**1)** to be empty, be clear, be pure, be free, be innocent, be desolate, be cut off
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be empty, be clean, be pure
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be cleaned out, be purged out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be clean, be free from guilt, be innocent
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be free, be exempt from punishment
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to be free, be exempt from obligation
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to hold innocent, acquit
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to leave unpunished
