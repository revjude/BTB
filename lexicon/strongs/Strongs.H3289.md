<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָעַץ‎</span></big> **yâʻats** <small>*(yaw-ats')*</small> ==**to advise**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ advertise, take advise, advise (well), consult, (give, take) counsel(-lor), determine, devise, guide, purpose.
- ***to advise***
-  reflexively, ***to deliberate*** or ***resolve***

**1)** to advise, consult, give counsel, counsel, purpose, devise, plan
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to advise, counsel, give counsel, consult
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** counsellor (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to consult together, exchange counsel, deliberate, counsel together
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to conspire
