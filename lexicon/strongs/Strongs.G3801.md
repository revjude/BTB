**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ὁ ὢν καί ὁ ἦν καί ὁ ἐρχόμενος‎</span></big>** **ho ṑn kaí ho ēn kaí ho erchómenos** <small>*(ho own kahee ho ane kahee ho er-khom'-en-os)*</small> ==**was, is, will be**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:T + G:V + G:CONJ + G:T + G:V + G:CONJ + G:T + G:V|G:T + G:V + G:CONJ + G:T + G:V + G:CONJ + G:T + G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a phrase combining **<big>[[G3588|‎ὁ‎]]</big>** with the present participle and imperfect of **<big>[[G1510|‎εἰμί‎]]</big>** and the present participle of **<big>[[G2064|‎ἔρχομαι‎]]</big>** by means of **<big>[[G2532|‎καί‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ which art (is, was), and (which) wast (is, was), and art (is) to come (shalt be).
- the one being and the one that was and the one coming, i.e. the Eternal, as a divine epithet of Christ
