-  also (abstractly) ***equity*** or (figuratively) ***prosperity***

**1)** justice, rightness, righteousness
$\quad$ **1a)** what is right or just or normal, rightness, justness (of weights and measures)
$\quad$ **1b)** righteousness (in government)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** of judges, rulers, kings
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** of law
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** of Davidic king, Messiah
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** of Jerusalem as seat of just government
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** of God's attribute
$\quad$ **1c)** righteousness, justice (in case or cause)
$\quad$ **1d)** rightness (in speech)
$\quad$ **1e)** righteousness (as ethically right)
$\quad$ **1f)** righteousness (as vindicated), justification (in controversy), deliverance, victory, prosperity
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f1)** of God as covenant-keeping in redemption
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f2)** in name of Messianic king
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f3)** of people enjoying salvation
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1f4)** of Cyrus
