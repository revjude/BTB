<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָזַל‎</span></big> **ʼâzal** <small>*(aw-zal')*</small> ==**be gone**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ fail, gad about, go to and fro (but in Ezekiel 27:19 the word is rendered by many 'from Uzal,' by others 'yarn'), be gone (spent).
- ***to go away***, hence, ***to disappear***

**1)** to go, to go away, to go about
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to go away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to go about
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to be used up, be exhausted, be gone, evaporated
$\quad$ **1b)** (Pual) to go to and fro
