**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּלָה‎</span></big>** **gâlâh** <small>*(gaw-law')*</small> ==**Heglam**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [phrase] advertise, appear, bewray, bring, (carry, lead, go) captive (into captivity), depart, disclose, discover, exile, be gone, open, [idiom] plainly, publish, remove, reveal, [idiom] shamelessly, shew, [idiom] surely, tell, uncover.
- ***to denude*** (especially in a disgraceful sense)
-  by implication, ***to exile*** (captives being usually stripped)
-  figuratively, ***to reveal***

**1)** to uncover, remove
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to uncover
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to remove, depart
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to go into exile
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** (reflexive)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1a)** to uncover oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1b)** to discover or show oneself
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1c)** to reveal himself (of God)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** (passive)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2a)** to be uncovered
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2b)** to be disclosed, be discovered
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2c)** to be revealed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be removed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to uncover (nakedness)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1a)** nakedness
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1b)** general
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to disclose, discover, lay bare
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to make known, show, reveal
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be uncovered
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hiphil) to carry away into exile, take into exile
$\quad$ **1f)** (Hophal) to be taken into exile
$\quad$ **1g)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1g1)** to be uncovered
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1g2)** to reveal oneself
