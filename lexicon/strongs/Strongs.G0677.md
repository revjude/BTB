**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἀπρόσκοπος‎</span></big>** **apróskopos** <small>*(ap-ros'-kop-os)*</small> ==**not giving offence**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:A|G:A]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G0001|‎Α‎]]</big>** (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of **<big>[[G4350|‎προσκόπτω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ none (void of, without) offence.
- actively, inoffensive, i.e. not leading into sin
-  passively, faultless, i.e. not led into sin
