<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גִּיל‎</span></big> **gîyl** <small>*(gheel)*</small> ==**to rejoice**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>or (by permutation) <span class="ashebrew">גּוּל</span> **gûwl** <small>*(gool)*</small>; a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be glad, joy, be joyful, rejoice.
- properly, ***to spin round*** (under the influence of any violent emotion), i.e. usually ***rejoice***, or (as cringing) ***fear***

**1)** to rejoice, exult, be glad
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to rejoice
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to tremble (from fear)
