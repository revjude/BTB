$\quad$ Araunah.
- ***Aravnah*** (or Arnijah or Ornah), a Jebusite

Araunah = "I shall shout for joy" = "make ye to shine" (2 Sa 24:16) or "joyful shouting of Jah" (2Sa 24:18)
**1)** a Jebusite who sold David the site for an altar
