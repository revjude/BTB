<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָשָׁה‎</span></big> **qâshâh** <small>*(kaw-shaw')*</small> ==**to harden**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be cruel, be fiercer, make grievous, be ((ask a), be in, have, seem, would) hard(-en, (labour), -ly, thing), be sore, (be, make) stiff(-en, (-necked)).
- properly, ***to be dense***, i.e. ***tough*** or ***severe*** (in various applications)

**1)** to be hard, be severe, be fierce, be harsh
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be hard, be difficult
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be hard, be severe
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be ill-treated
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be hard pressed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to have severe labour (of women)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to make difficult, make difficulty
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to make severe, make burdensome
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to make hard, make stiff, make stubborn
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3a)** of obstinacy (fig)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d4)** to show stubbornness
