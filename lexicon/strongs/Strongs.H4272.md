<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָחַץ‎</span></big> **mâchats** <small>*(maw-khats')*</small> ==**to wound**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ dip, pierce (through), smite (through), strike through, wound.
- ***to dash asunder***
-  by implication, ***to crush***, ***smash*** or ***violently plunge***
-  figuratively, ***to subdue*** or ***destroy***

**1)** to smite through, shatter, wound severely
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to shatter
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** shattering (participle)
