$\quad$ manna.
- literally ***a whatness*** (so to speak), i.e. ***manna*** (so called from the question about it)

**1)** manna
$\quad$ **1a)** the bread from Heaven that fed the Israelites for 40 years of wilderness wanderings
$\quad$ **1b)** means 'What is it?'
