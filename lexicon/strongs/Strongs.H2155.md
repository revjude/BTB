**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎זִמָּה‎</span></big>** **Zimmâh** <small>*(zim-maw')*</small> ==**Zimmah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>the same as **<big>[[H2154|‎זִמָּה‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ Zimmah.
- § Zimmah = *"plotter"*
- ***Zimmah***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** a Gershonite Levite, son of Jahath
**2)** another Gershonite, son of Shimei; perhaps the same as 1 above
**3)** father or ancestor of Joah, a Gershonite in the reign of Hezekiah
