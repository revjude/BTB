<big><span class="asgreek">‎συνέχω‎</span></big> **synéchō** <small>*(soon-ekh'-o)*</small> ==**to hold/oppress**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="asgreek">σύν</span><sup><small>[[G4862]]</small></sup> and <span class="asgreek">ἔχω</span><sup><small>[[G2192]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ constrain, hold, keep in, press, lie sick of, stop, be in a strait, straiten, be taken with, throng.
- to hold together, i.e. to compress (the ears, with a crowd or siege) or arrest (a prisoner)
-  figuratively, to compel, perplex, afflict, preoccupy
