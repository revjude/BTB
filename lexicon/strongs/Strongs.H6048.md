$\quad$ Anammelech.
- § Anammelech = *"image of the king"*
- ***Anammelek***, an Assyrian deity

**1)** an Assyrian false god introduced to Israel during the monarchy; worshipped with rites resembling those of Molech; companion god of 'Adrammelech'
