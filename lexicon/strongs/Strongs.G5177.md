**<big><span class="asgreek">‎τυγχάνω‎</span></big>** **tynchánō** <small>*(toong-khan'-o)*</small> ==**to obtain/happen**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>probably for an obsolete <greek BETA="TU/XW" unicode="τύχω" translit="týchō"/> (for which the middle voice of another alternate <greek BETA="TEU/XW" unicode="τεύχω" translit="teúchō"/> (to make ready or bring to pass) is used in certain tenses</small>

$\quad$ be, chance, enjoy, little, obtain, X refresh…self, + special.
- akin to the base of **<big>[[G5088|‎τίκτω‎]]</big>** through the idea of effecting
-  properly, to affect
-  or (specially), to hit or light upon (as a mark to be reached), i.e. (transitively) to attain or secure an object or end, or (intransitively) to happen (as if meeting with)
-  but in the latter application only impersonal (with **<big>[[G1487|‎εἰ‎]]</big>**), i.e. perchance
-  or (present participle) as adjective, usual (as if commonly met with, with **<big>[[G3756|‎οὐ‎]]</big>**, extraordinary), neuter (as adverb) perhaps
-  or (with another verb) as adverb, by accident (as it were)
