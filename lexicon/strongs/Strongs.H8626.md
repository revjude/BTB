**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תָּקַן‎</span></big>** **tâqan** <small>*(taw-kan')*</small> ==**be straight**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ set in order, make straight.
- ***to equalize***, i.e. ***straighten*** (intransitive or transitive)
-  figuratively, ***to compose***

**1)** to equalise, make straight, become straight
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to become straight
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to make straight, put straight, arrange in order, set in order
