<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָתַךְ‎</span></big> **nâthak** <small>*(naw-thak')*</small> ==**to pour**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ drop, gather (together), melt, pour (forth, out).
- ***to flow forth*** (literally or figuratively)
-  by implication, ***to liquify***

**1)** to pour out or forth, drop (or rain), be poured, be poured out, be melted, be molten
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to pour forth
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be poured, be poured out
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to pour out, melt
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to be melted
