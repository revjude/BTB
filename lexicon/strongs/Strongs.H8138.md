<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁנָה‎</span></big> **shânâh** <small>*(shaw-naw')*</small> ==**to change**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ do (speak, strike) again, alter, double, (be given to) change, disguise, (be) diverse, pervert, prefer, repeat, return, do the second time.
- ***to fold***, i.e. ***duplicate*** (literally or figuratively)
-  by implication, ***to transmute*** (transitive or intransitive)

**1)** to repeat, do again, change, alter
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to change
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be repeated
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to change, alter
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hithpael) to disguise oneself
