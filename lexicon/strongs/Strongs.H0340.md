**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָיַב‎</span></big>** **ʼâyab** <small>*(aw-yab')*</small> ==**be hostile**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be an enemy.
- ***to hate*** (as one of an opposite tribe or party)
-  hence to be hostile

**1)** to be hostile to, to be an enemy to
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be hostile to
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to treat as an enemy
