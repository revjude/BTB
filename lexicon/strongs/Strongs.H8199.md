<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁפַט‎</span></big> **shâphaṭ** <small>*(shaw-fat')*</small> ==**to judge**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [phrase] avenge, [idiom] that condemn, contend, defend, execute (judgment), (be a) judge(-ment), [idiom] needs, plead, reason, rule.
- ***to judge***, i.e. ***pronounce sentence*** (for or against)
-  by implication, ***to vindicate*** or ***punish***
-  by extenssion, ***to govern***
-  passively, ***to litigate*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to judge, govern, vindicate, punish
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to act as law-giver or judge or governor (of God, man)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** to rule, govern, judge
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to decide controversy (of God, man)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to execute judgment
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3a)** discriminating (of man)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3b)** vindicating
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3c)** condemning and punishing
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3d)** at theophanic advent for final judgment
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to enter into controversy, plead, have controversy together
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be judged
$\quad$ **1c)** (Poel) judge, opponent-at-law (participle)
