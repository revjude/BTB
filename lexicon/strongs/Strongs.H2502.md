<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָלַץ‎</span></big> **châlats** <small>*(khaw-lats')*</small> ==**to rescue**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ arm (self), (go, ready) armed ([idiom] man, soldier), deliver, draw out, make fat, loose, (ready) prepared, put off, take away, withdraw self.
- ***to pull off***
-  hence (intensively) ***to strip***, (reflex.) ***to depart***
-  by implication, ***to deliver***, ***equip*** (for fight)
-  ***present***, ***strengthen***

**1)** to remove, draw out, draw off, take off, withdraw, equip (for war), arm for war, rescue, be rescued
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) equipped (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be equipped
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to go equipped
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be armed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to make strong, brace up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to invigorate
**2)** to draw off or out, withdraw
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a1)** to draw, draw off
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a2)** to withdraw
$\quad$ **2b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b1)** to be delivered
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2b2)** to be saved
$\quad$ **2c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2c1)** to pull out, tear out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2c2)** to rescue, deliver, set free
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2c3)** to take away, plunder
