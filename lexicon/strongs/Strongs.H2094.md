**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎זָהַר‎</span></big>** **zâhar** <small>*(zaw-har')*</small> ==**to shine**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ admonish, shine, teach, (give) warn(-ing).
- ***to gleam***
-  figuratively, ***to enlighten*** (by caution)

**1)** to admonish, warn, teach, shine, send out light, be light, be shining
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to be taught, be admonished
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to teach, warn
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to shine, send out light (fig.)
