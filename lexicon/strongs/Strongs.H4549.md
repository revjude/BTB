**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָסַס‎</span></big>** **mâçaç** <small>*(maw-sas')*</small> ==**to melt**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ discourage, faint, be loosed, melt (away), refuse, [idiom] utterly.
- ***to liquefy***
-  figuratively, ***to waste*** (with disease), ***to faint*** (with fatigue, fear or grief)

**1)** to dissolve, melt
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to waste away
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to melt, vanish, drop off, melt away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to faint, grow fearful (fig.)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** wasted, worthless (participle)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to cause to melt
