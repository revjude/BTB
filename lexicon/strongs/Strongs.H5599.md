**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎סָפִיחַ‎</span></big>** **çâphîyach** <small>*(saw-fee'-akh)*</small> ==**outpouring**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5596|‎סָפַח‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ (such) things as (which) grow (of themselves), which groweth of its own accord (itself).
- ***something*** (spontaneously) ***falling off***, i.e. ***a self-sown crop***
-  figuratively, ***a freshet***

**1)** outpouring
**2)** growth from spilled kernels, after-growth, volunteer plants
