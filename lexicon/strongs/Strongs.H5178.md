<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נְחֹשֶׁת‎</span></big> **nᵉchôsheth** <small>*(nekh-o'-sheth)*</small> ==**bronze**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>for <span class="ashebrew">‎נְחוּשָׁה‎</span><sup><small>[[H5154]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ brasen, brass, chain, copper, fetter (of brass), filthiness, steel.
- ***copper***, hence, ***something made of that metal***, i.e. ***coin***, ***a fetter***
-  figuratively, ***base*** (as compared with gold or silver)

**1)** copper, bronze
$\quad$ **1a)** copper (ore), bronze (as copper alloy)
$\quad$ **1b)** fetters (of copper or bronze)
$\quad$ **1c)** copper (as value)
**2)** lust, harlotry
$\quad$ **2a)** meaning dubious
