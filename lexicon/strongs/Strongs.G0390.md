**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἀναστρέφω‎</span></big>** **anastréphō** <small>*(an-as-tref'-o)*</small> ==**to live/return**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G0303|‎ἀνά‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G4762|‎στρέφω‎]]</big>**</small>
