<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎טוֹב‎</span><sup><small>[[H2895]]</small></sup> and <span class="ashebrew">‎אֵל‎</span><sup><small>[[H0410]]</small></sup>; pleasing (to) God</small>

$\quad$ Tabeal, Tabeel.
- § Tabeel = *"God is good"*
- ***Tabeel***, the name of a Syrian and of a Persian

**1)** a man whose son was either in the army of Pekah or the army of Rezin and whom Pekah and Rezin proposed to make king of Israel
**2)** an officer of the Persian government in Samaria in the reign of Artaxerxes
