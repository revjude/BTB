**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֲחִימַעַץ‎</span></big>** **ʼĂchîymaʻats** <small>*(akh-ee-mah'-ats)*</small> ==**Ahimaaz**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H0251|‎אָח‎]]</big>** and the equivalent of **<big>[[H4619|‎מַעַץ‎]]</big>**; brother of anger</small>

$\quad$ Ahimaaz.
- § Ahimaaz = *"my brother is anger (wrath)"*
- ***Achimaats***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** father-in-law of Saul
**2)** Zadok's son who aided David during Absalom's rebellion
**3)** a supply officer of Solomon
