**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּדָה‎</span></big>** **pâdâh** <small>*(paw-daw')*</small> ==**to ransom**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] at all, deliver, [idiom] by any means, ransom, (that are to be, let be) redeem(-ed), rescue, [idiom] surely.
- ***to sever***, i.e. ***ransom***
-  ***gener***. ***to release***, ***preserve***

**1)** to ransom, redeem, rescue, deliver
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to ransom
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be ransomed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to allow one to be ransomed
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) redeemed
