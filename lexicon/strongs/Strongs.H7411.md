<big><span class="ashebrew">‎רָמָה‎</span></big> **râmâh** <small>*(raw-maw')*</small> ==**to shoot**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ beguile, betray, (bow-) man, carry, deceive, throw.
- ***to hurl***
-  specifically, ***to shoot***
-  figuratively, ***to delude*** or ***betray*** (as if causing to fall)

**1)** to cast, shoot, hurl
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to throw
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** bow-shooters, bowmen (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to throw down
**2)** to beguile, deceive, mislead, deal treacherously
$\quad$ **2a)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a1)** to beguile, deceive, mislead, trick
$\quad$ $\quad$ **2a2)** to deal treacherously with, betray
