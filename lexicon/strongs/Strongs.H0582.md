**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֱנוֹשׁ‎</span></big>** **ʼĕnôwsh** <small>*(en-oshe')*</small> ==**human**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H0605|‎אָנַשׁ‎]]</big>**; properly, a mortal (and thus differing from the more dignified **<big>[[H0120|‎אָדָם‎]]</big>**); hence,</small>

$\quad$ another, [idiom] (blood-) thirsty, certain, chap(-man); divers, fellow, [idiom] in the flower of their age, husband, (certain, mortal) man, people, person, servant, some ([idiom] of them), [phrase] stranger, those, [phrase] their trade. It is often unexpressed in the English versions, especially when used in apposition with another word. Compare **<big>[[H0376|‎אִישׁ‎]]</big>**.
- ***a man*** in general (singly or collectively)

**1)** man, mortal man, person, mankind
$\quad$ **1a)** of an individual
$\quad$ **1b)** men (collective)
$\quad$ **1c)** man, mankind
