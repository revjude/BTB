**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בְּעַד‎</span></big>** **bᵉʻad** <small>*(beh-ad')*</small> ==**about/through/for**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:A|H:A]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5704|‎עַד‎]]</big>** with prepositional prefix</small>

$\quad$ about, at by (means of), for, over, through, up (-on), within.
- ***in up to*** or ***over against***
-  generally ***at***, ***beside***, ***among***, ***behind***, ***for***, etc.

**1)** behind, through, round about, on behalf of, away from, about
$\quad$ **1a)** through (of action)
$\quad$ **1b)** behind (with verbs of shutting)
$\quad$ **1c)** about (with verbs of fencing)
$\quad$ **1d)** on behalf of (metaph. especially with Hithpael)
