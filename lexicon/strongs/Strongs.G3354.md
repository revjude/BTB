**<big><span class="asgreek">‎μετρέω‎</span></big>** **metréō** <small>*(met-reh'-o)*</small> ==**to measure**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G3358|‎μέτρον‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ figuratively, to estimate:--measure, mete.
- to measure (i.e. ascertain in size by a fixed standard)
-  by implication, to admeasure (i.e. allot by rule)
