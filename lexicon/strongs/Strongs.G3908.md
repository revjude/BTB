<big><span class="asgreek">‎παρατίθημι‎</span></big> **paratíthēmi** <small>*(par-at-ith'-ay-mee)*</small> ==**to set before**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="asgreek">παρά</span><sup><small>[[G3844]]</small></sup> and <span class="asgreek">τίθημι</span><sup><small>[[G5087]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ allege, commend, commit (the keeping of), put forth, set before.
- to place alongside, i.e. present (food, truth)
-  by implication, to deposit (as a trust or for protection)
