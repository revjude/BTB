<big><span class="asgreek">‎πονηρός‎</span></big> **ponērós** <small>*(pon-ay-ros')*</small> ==**evil/bad**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:A|G:A]]</small></sup> 
<small>from a derivative of <span class="asgreek">πόνος</span><sup><small>[[G4192]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ bad, evil, grievous, harm, lewd, malicious, wicked(-ness).
- hurtful, i.e. evil (properly, in effect or influence, and thus differing from <span class="asgreek">κακός</span><sup><small>[[G2556]]</small></sup>, which refers rather to essential character, as well as from <span class="asgreek">σαπρός</span><sup><small>[[G4550]]</small></sup>, which indicates degeneracy from original virtue)
-  figuratively, calamitous
-  also (passively) ill, i.e. diseased
-  but especially (morally) culpable, i.e. derelict, vicious, facinorous
-  neuter (singular) mischief, malice, or (plural) guilt
-  masculine (singular) the devil, or (plural) sinners
