**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָתַשׁ‎</span></big>** **nâthash** <small>*(naw-thash')*</small> ==**to uproot**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ destroy, forsake, pluck (out, up, by the roots), pull up, root out (up), [idiom] utterly. s
- ***to tear away***

**1)** to pull up, expel, root out, pluck up
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to pull or pluck up
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be plucked up
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hophal) to be plucked up
