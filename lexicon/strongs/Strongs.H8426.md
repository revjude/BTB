<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תּוֹדָה‎</span></big> **tôwdâh** <small>*(to-daw')*</small> ==**thanksgiving**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-F|H:N-F]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎יָדָה‎</span><sup><small>[[H3034]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ confession, (sacrifice of) praise, thanks(-giving, offering).
- properly, ***an extension of the hand***, i.e. (by implication) ***avowal***, or (usually) ***adoration***
-  specifically, ***a choir of worshippers***

**1)** confession, praise, thanksgiving
$\quad$ **1a)** give praise to God
$\quad$ **1b)** thanksgiving in songs of liturgical worship, hymn of praise
$\quad$ **1c)** thanksgiving choir or procession or line or company
$\quad$ **1d)** thank-offering, sacrifice of thanksgiving
$\quad$ **1e)** confession
