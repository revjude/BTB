<big><span class="asgreek">‎χρόνος‎</span></big> **chrónos** <small>*(khron'-os)*</small> ==**time**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-M|G:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>of uncertain derivation</small>

$\quad$ + years old, season, space, (X often-)time(-s), (a) while.
- a space of time (in general, and thus properly distinguished from <span class="asgreek">καιρός</span><sup><small>[[G2540]]</small></sup>, which designates a fixed or special occasion
-  and from <span class="asgreek">αἰών</span><sup><small>[[G0165]]</small></sup>, which denotes a particular period) or interval
-  by extension, an individual opportunity
-  by implication, delay
