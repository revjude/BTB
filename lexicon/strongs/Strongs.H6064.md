<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָנַשׁ‎</span></big> **ʻânash** <small>*(aw-nash')*</small> ==**to fine**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ amerce, condemn, punish, [idiom] surely.
- properly, ***to urge***
-  by implication, ***to inflict a******penalty***, specifically, ***to fine***

**1)** to fine, amerce, punish, condemn, mulct
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to fine, punish
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be fined, be punished, be mulcted
