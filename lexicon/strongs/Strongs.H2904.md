**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎טוּל‎</span></big>** **ṭûwl** <small>*(tool)*</small> ==**to cast**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ carry away, (utterly) cast (down, forth, out), send out.
- ***to pitch over*** or ***reel***
-  hence (transitively) ***to cast down*** or ***out***

**1)** to hurl, cast
$\quad$ **1a)** (Pilpel) to throw away, carry away, hurl
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to throw, cast, cast out
$\quad$ **1c)**(Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to be hurled, hurled down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to be cast, be thrown, be cast out, be thrown down
