<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָעַד‎</span></big> **yâʻad** <small>*(yaw-ad')*</small> ==**to appoint**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ agree,(maxke an) appoint(-ment, a time), assemble (selves), betroth, gather (selves, together), meet (together), set (a time).
- ***to fix upon*** (by agreement or appointment)
-  by implication, ***to meet*** (at a stated time), ***to summon*** (to trial), ***to direct*** (in a certain quarter or position), ***to engage*** (for marriage)

**1)** to fix, appoint, assemble, meet, set, betroth
$\quad$ **1a)**(Qal) to appoint, assign, designate
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to meet
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to meet by appointment
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to gather, assemble by appointment
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to cause to meet
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) to be set, be placed before, be fixed
