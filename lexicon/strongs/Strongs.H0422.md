**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָלָה‎</span></big>** **ʼâlâh** <small>*(aw-law')*</small> ==**to swear**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ adjure, curse, swear.
- properly, ***to adjure***, i.e. (usually in a bad sense) ***imprecate***

**1)** to swear, curse
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to swear, take an oath (before God)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to curse
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to put under oath, adjure
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to put under a curse
