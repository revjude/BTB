**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָרַץ‎</span></big>** **chârats** <small>*(khaw-rats')*</small> ==**to decide**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bestir self, decide, decree, determine, maim, move.
- properly, ***to point sharply***, i.e. (literally) ***to wound***
-  figuratively, ***to be alert***, ***to decide***

**1)** to cut, sharpen, decide, decree, determine, maim, move, be decisive, be mutilated
$\quad$ **1a)**(Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to cut, mutilate
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to sharpen
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to decide
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be decisive
