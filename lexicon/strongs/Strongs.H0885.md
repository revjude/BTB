**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בְּאֵרֹת בְּנֵי־יַעֲקַן‎</span></big>** **Bᵉʼêrôth Bᵉnêy-Yaʻăqan** <small>*(be-ay-roth' be-nay' yah-a-can')*</small> ==**Beeroth-bene-jaakan**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N--L|N:N--L]]</small></sup> 
<small>from the feminine plural of **<big>[[H0875|‎בְּאֵר‎]]</big>**, and the plural contraction of **<big>[[H1121|‎בֵּן‎]]</big>**, and **<big>[[H3292|‎יַעֲקָן‎]]</big>**; wells of (the) sons of Jaakan</small>

$\quad$ Beeroth of the children of Jaakan.
- § Beeroth Bene-Jaakan or Beeroth = *"wells" or "wells of the sons of Jaakan"*
- ***Beeroth-Bene-Jaakan***, a place in the Desert

**1)** an encampment for Israel in the wilderness probably in the country of the Horites
