<small>a primitive root (identical with through the idea of vibration)</small>

$\quad$ quiver, tingle.
- ***to tinkle***, i.e. ***rattle together*** (as the ears in reddening with shame, or the teeth in chattering with fear)

**1)** to tingle, quiver
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to tingle (of ears)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to quiver (of fear)
