<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּהַן‎</span></big> **kâhan** <small>*(kaw-han')*</small> ==**to minister**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root, apparently meaning to mediate in religious services; but used only as denominative from <span class="ashebrew">‎כֹּהֵן‎</span><sup><small>[[H3548]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ deck, be (do the office of a, execute the, minister in the) priest('s office).
- ***to officiate as a priest***
-  figuratively, ***to put on regalia***

**1)** to act as a priest, minister in a priest's office
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to minister as a priest, serve as a priest
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be or become a priest
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to play the priest
