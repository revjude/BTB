<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מִשְׁפָּט‎</span></big> **mishpâṭ** <small>*(mish-pawt')*</small> ==**justice: judgement**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁפַט‎</span><sup><small>[[H8199]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ [phrase] adversary, ceremony, charge, [idiom] crime, custom, desert, determination, discretion, disposing, due, fashion, form, to be judged, judgment, just(-ice, -ly), (manner of) law(-ful), manner, measure, (due) order, ordinance, right, sentence, usest, [idiom] worthy, [phrase] wrong.
- properly, ***a verdict*** (favorable or unfavorable) pronounced judicially, especially ***a sentence*** or ***formal decree*** (human or (participant's) ***divine law***, individual or collective), including the act, the place, the suit, the crime, and the penalty
-  abstractly, ***justice***, including a participant's ***right*** or ***privilege*** (statutory or customary), or even a ***style***

**1)** judgment, justice, ordinance

$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** act of deciding a case

$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** process, procedure, litigation (before judges)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** case, cause (presented for judgment)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** sentence, decision (of judgment)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** execution (of judgment)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7)** time (of judgment)
$\quad$ **1b)** justice, right, rectitude (attributes of God or man)
$\quad$ **1c)** ordinance
$\quad$ **1d)** decision (in law)
