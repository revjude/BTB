<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נוּחַ‎</span></big> **nûwach** <small>*(noo'-akh)*</small> ==**to rest**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ cease, be confederate, lay, let down, (be) quiet, remain, (cause to, be at, give, have, make to) rest, set down. Compare <span class="ashebrew">‎יָנִים‎</span><sup><small>[[H3241]]</small></sup>.
- ***to rest***, i.e. ***settle down***
-  used in a great variety of applications, literal and figurative, intransitive, transitive and causative (to dwell, stay, let fall, place, let alone, withdraw, give comfort, etc.)

**1)** to rest
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to rest, settle down and remain
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to repose, have rest, be quiet
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiph)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to cause to rest, give rest to, make quiet
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to cause to rest, cause to alight, set down
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to lay or set down, deposit, let lie, place
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to let remain, leave
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** to leave, depart from
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b6)** to abandon
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b7)** to permit
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hoph)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to obtain rest, be granted rest
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to be left, be placed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** open space (subst)
