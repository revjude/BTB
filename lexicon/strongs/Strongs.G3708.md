**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ὁράω‎</span></big>** **horáō** <small>*(hor-ah'-o)*</small> ==**to see: see**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>properly, to stare at (compare **<big>[[G3700|‎ὀπτάνομαι‎]]</big>**), i.e. (by implication) to discern clearly (physically or mentally)</small>

$\quad$ behold, perceive, see, take heed.
- by extension, to attend to
-  by Hebraism, to experience
-  passively, to appear
