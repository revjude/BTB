<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָשֵׁן‎</span></big> **yâshên** <small>*(yaw-shane')*</small> ==**to sleep**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ old (store), remain long, (make to) sleep.
- properly, ***to be slack*** or ***languid***, i.e. (by implication) ***sleep*** (figuratively, to die)
-  also ***to grow old***, ***stale*** or ***inveterate***

**1)** to sleep, be asleep
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to sleep, go to sleep, be asleep
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to remain a long time, be stored up
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be festering (of leprosy)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to cause to sleep, entice to sleep
