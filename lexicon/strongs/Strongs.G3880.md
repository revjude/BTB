**<big><span class="asgreek">‎παραλαμβάνω‎</span></big>** **paralambánō** <small>*(par-al-am-ban'-o)*</small> ==**to take**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G3844|‎παρά‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2983|‎λαμβάνω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ receive, take (unto, with).
- to receive near, i.e. associate with oneself (in any familiar or intimate act or relation)
-  by analogy, to assume an office
-  figuratively, to learn
