<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּחַד‎</span></big> **kâchad** <small>*(kaw-khad')*</small> ==**to hide**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ conceal, cut down (off), desolate, hide.
- ***to secrete***, ***by act*** or ***word***
-  hence (intensively) ***to destroy***

**1)** to hide, conceal, cut off, cut down, make desolate, kick
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be hidden
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be effaced, be destroyed, be cut off
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to cover, hide
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to hide
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to efface, annihilate
