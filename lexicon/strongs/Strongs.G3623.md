<big><span class="asgreek">‎οἰκονόμος‎</span></big> **oikonómos** <small>*(oy-kon-om'-os)*</small> ==**manager**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-M|G:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="asgreek">οἶκος</span><sup><small>[[G3624]]</small></sup> and the base of <span class="asgreek">νόμος</span><sup><small>[[G3551]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ chamberlain, governor, steward.
- a house-distributor (i.e. manager), or overseer, i.e. an employee in that capacity
-  by extension, a fiscal agent (treasurer)
-  figuratively, a preacher (of the Gospel)
