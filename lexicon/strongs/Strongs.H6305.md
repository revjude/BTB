**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פְּדָיָה‎</span></big>** **Pᵉdâyâh** <small>*(ped-aw-yaw')*</small> ==**Pedaiah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">פְּדָיָהוּ</span></big>** **Pᵉdâyâhûw** <small>*(ped-awyaw'-hoo)*</small>; from **<big>[[H6299|‎פָּדָה‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3050|‎יָהּ‎]]</big>**; Jah has ransomed</small>

$\quad$ Pedaiah.
- § Pedaiah = *"Jehovah has ransomed"*
- ***Pedajah***, the name of six Israelites

**1)** father of Zebudah, the wife of king Josiah and the mother of king Jehoiakim both of Judah
**2)** father of Zerubbabel and brother of Shealtiel who is usually called the father of Zerubbabel probably because of a lack of an heir from Shealtiel who was in the direct line of succession
