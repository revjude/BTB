**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פֶּלֶת‎</span></big>** **Peleth** <small>*(peh'-leth)*</small> ==**Peleth**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root meaning to flee; swiftness</small>

$\quad$ Peleth.
- § Peleth = *"swiftness"*
- ***Peleth***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** a Reubenite, father of On who rebelled with Dathan and Abiram in the days of the wilderness wanderings
**2)** son of Jonathan and a descendant of Jerahmeel of Judah
