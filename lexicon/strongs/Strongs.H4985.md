<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָתַק‎</span></big> **mâthaq** <small>*(maw-thak')*</small> ==**be sweet**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be (made, [idiom] take) sweet.
- ***to suck***, by implication, ***to relish***, or (intransitively) ***be sweet***

**1)** to be or become sweet or pleasing
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be or become sweet
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be or become pleasant
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to suck
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to give sweet taste
