$\quad$ Zabbai.
- § Zabbai = *"pure"*
- ***Zabbai*** (or Zaccai), an Israelite

**1)** a descendant of Bebai who took a foreign wife in the days of Ezra
**2)** father of Baruch, who assisted Nehemiah in rebuilding the city wall
