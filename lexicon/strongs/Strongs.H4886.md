<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מָשַׁח‎</span></big> **mâshach** <small>*(maw-shakh')*</small> ==**to anoint**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ anoint, paint.
- ***to rub with oil***, i.e. ***to anoint***
-  by implication, ***to consecrate***
-  also ***to paint***

**1)** to smear, anoint, spread a liquid
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to smear
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to anoint (as consecration)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to anoint, consecrate
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be anointed
