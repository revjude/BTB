$\quad$ sack(-cloth, -clothes).
- properly, ***a mesh*** (as allowing a liquid to run through), i.e. ***coarse loose cloth*** or ***sacking*** (used in mourning and for bagging)
-  hence, ***a bag*** (for grain, etc.)

**1)** mesh, sackcloth, sack, sacking
$\quad$ **1a)** sack (for grain)
$\quad$ **1b)** sackcloth
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** worn in mourning or humiliation
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** same material spread out to lie on
