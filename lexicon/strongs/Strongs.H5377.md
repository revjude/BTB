<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָשָׁא‎</span></big> **nâshâʼ** <small>*(naw-shaw')*</small> ==**to deceive**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ beguile, deceive, [idiom] greatly, [idiom] utterly.
- ***to lead astray***, i.e. (mentally) ***to delude***, or (morally) ***to seduce***

**1)** to beguile, deceive
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to be beguiled
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to beguile, deceive
$\quad$ **1c)** (Qal) utterly (infinitive)
