<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָמַם‎</span></big> **ʻâmam** <small>*(aw-mam')*</small> ==**to darken**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ become dim, hide.
- ***to associate***
-  by implication, ***to overshadow*** (by huddling together)

**1)** to dim, darken, grow dark
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to dim, eclipse, be held dark
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hophal) to be dimmed, grow dark
