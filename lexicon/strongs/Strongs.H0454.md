**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֶלְיְהוֹעֵינַי‎</span></big>** **ʼElyᵉhôwʻêynay** <small>*(el-ye-ho-ay-nah'ee)*</small> ==**Eliehoenai**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>or (shortened) **<big><span class="ashebrew">אֶלְיוֹעֵינַי</span></big>** **ʼElyôwʻêynay** <small>*(el-yo-ay-nah'ee)*</small>; from **<big>[[H0413|‎אֵל‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3068|‎יְהֹוָה‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H5869|‎עַיִן‎]]</big>**; towards Jehovah (are) my eyes</small>

$\quad$ Elihoenai, Elionai.
- § Elioenai or Elihoenai = *"unto Jehovah are my eyes"*
- ***Eljehoenai*** or ***Eljoenai***, the name of seven Israelites

**1)** a Korahite temple doorkeeper, son of Meshelemiah
**2)**  two men with foreign wife during the exile; one a priest
**3)** a son of Neariah
**4)** a Simeonite
**5)** a Benjamite, son of Becher
