<big><span class="ashebrew">‎גָּאַל‎</span></big> **gâʼal** <small>*(gaw-al')*</small> ==**to defile**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root, (rather identified with <span class="ashebrew">‎גָּאַל‎</span><sup><small>[[H1350]]</small></sup>, through the idea of freeing, i.e. repudiating)</small>

$\quad$ defile, pollute, stain.
- ***to soil*** or (figuratively) ***desecrate***

**1)** to defile, pollute, desecrate
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to be defiled, be polluted
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to pollute, desecrate
$\quad$ **1c)**(Pual) to be desecrated (of removal from priesthood)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to pollute, stain
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to defile oneself
