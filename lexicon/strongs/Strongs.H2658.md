<small>a primitive root</small>

- properly, ***to pry into***
-  by implication, ***to delve***, ***to explore***

**1)** to dig, search for

$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to dig
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to search, search out, explore
