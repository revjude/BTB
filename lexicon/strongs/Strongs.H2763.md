<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָרַם‎</span></big> **châram** <small>*(khaw-ram')*</small> ==**to devote/destroy**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ make accursed, consecrate, (utterly) destroy, devote, forfeit, have a flat nose, utterly (slay, make away).
- ***to seclude***
-  specifically (by a ban) ***to devote to religious uses*** (especially destruction)
-  physical and reflexive, ***to be blunt as to the nose***

**1)** to ban, devote, destroy utterly, completely destroy, dedicate for destruction, exterminate
$\quad$ **1a)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to prohibit (for common use), ban
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to consecrate, devote, dedicate for destruction
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to exterminate, completely destroy
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be put under the ban, be devoted to destruction
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be devoted, be forfeited
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be completely destroyed
**2)** to split, slit, mutilate (a part of the body)
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to mutilate
$\quad$ **2b)** (Hiphil) to divide
