**<big><span class="asgreek">‎φρέαρ‎</span></big>** **phréar** <small>*(freh'-ar)*</small> ==**well/abyss**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-N|G:N-N]]</small></sup> 
<small>of uncertain derivation</small>

$\quad$ well, pit.
- a hole in the ground (dug for obtaining or holding water or other purposes), i.e. a cistern or well
-  figuratively, an abyss (as a prison)
