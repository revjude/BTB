<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָבַל‎</span></big> **châbal** <small>*(khaw-bal')*</small> ==**to pledge**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] at all, band, bring forth, (deal) corrupt(-ly), destroy, offend, lay to (take a) pledge, spoil, travail, [idiom] very, withhold.
- ***to wind tightly*** (as a rope), i.e. ***to bind***
-  specifically, by a ***pledge***
-  figuratively, ***to pervert***, ***destroy***
-  also ***to writhe in pain*** (especially of parturition)

**1)** to bind
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to bind
**2)** to take a pledge, lay to pledge
$\quad$ **2a)** (Qal) to hold by a pledge, take in pledge, hold in pledge
$\quad$ **2b)** (Niphal) to give a pledge, become pledged
**3)** to destroy, spoil, deal corruptly, offend
$\quad$ **3a)** (Qal) to spoil, corrupt, offend
$\quad$ **3b)** (Niphal) to be ruined
$\quad$ **3c)** (Piel) to destroy, ruin
$\quad$ **3d)** (Pual) to be ruined, be broken
**4)** to bring forth, travail
$\quad$ **4a)** (Piel) to writhe, twist, travail
