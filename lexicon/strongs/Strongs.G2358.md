<big><span class="asgreek">‎θριαμβεύω‎</span></big> **thriambeúō** <small>*(three-am-byoo'-o)*</small> ==**to triumph**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from a prolonged compound of the base of <span class="asgreek">θροέω</span><sup><small>[[G2360]]</small></sup></small>

$\quad$ (cause) to triumph (over).
- and a derivative of <span class="asgreek">ἅπτομαι</span><sup><small>[[G0680]]</small></sup> (meaning a noisy iambus, sung in honor of Bacchus)
-  to make an acclamatory procession, i.e. (figuratively) to conquer or (by Hebraism) to give victory
