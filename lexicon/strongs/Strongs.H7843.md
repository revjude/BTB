**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎שָׁחַת‎</span></big>** **shâchath** <small>*(shaw-khath')*</small> ==**to ruin**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ batter, cast off, corrupt(-er, thing), destroy(-er, -uction), lose, mar, perish, spill, spoiler, [idiom] utterly, waste(-r).
- ***to decay***, i.e. (causatively) ***ruin*** (literally or figuratively)

**1)** to destroy, corrupt, go to ruin, decay
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to be marred, be spoiled, be corrupted, be corrupt, be injured, be ruined, be rotted
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to spoil, ruin
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to pervert, corrupt, deal corruptly (morally)
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to spoil, ruin, destroy
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to pervert, corrupt (morally)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** destroyer (participle)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hophal) spoiled, ruined (participle)
