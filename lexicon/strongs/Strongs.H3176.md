<big><span class="ashebrew">‎יָחַל‎</span></big> **yâchal** <small>*(yaw-chal')*</small> ==**to wait: wait**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (cause to, have, make to) hope, be pained, stay, tarry, trust, wait.
- ***to wait***
-  by implication, ***to be patient***, ***hope***

**1)** to wait, hope, expect
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to wait
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to wait, await, tarry
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to wait for, hope for
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to wait, tarry, wait for, hope for
