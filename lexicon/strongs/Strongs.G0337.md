**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ἀναιρέω‎</span></big>** **anairéō** <small>*(an-ahee-reh'-o)*</small> ==**to kill**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G0303|‎ἀνά‎]]</big>** and (the active of) **<big>[[G0138|‎αἱρέομαι‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ put to death, kill, slay, take away, take up.
- to take up, i.e. adopt
-  by implication, to take away (violently), i.e. abolish, murder
