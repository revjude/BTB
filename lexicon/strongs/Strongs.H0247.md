**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָזַר‎</span></big>** **ʼâzar** <small>*(aw-zar')*</small> ==**to gird**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bind (compass) about, gird (up, with).
- ***to belt***

**1)** gird, encompass, equip, clothe
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to gird, gird on (metaphorical of strength)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) be girded
$\quad$ **1c)**(Piel) hold close, clasp
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphpael) gird oneself (for war)
