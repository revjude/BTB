**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎מֵעֶה‎</span></big>** **mêʻeh** <small>*(may-aw')*</small> ==**belly**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M|H:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root probably meaning to be soft</small>

$\quad$ belly, bowels, [idiom] heart, womb.
- used only in plural ***the intestines***, or (collectively) ***the abdomen***, figuratively, ***sympathy***
-  by implication, ***a vest***
-  by extension ***the stomach***, ***the uterus*** (or of men, the seat of generation), ***the heart*** (figuratively)

**1)** internal organs, inward parts, bowels, intestines, belly
$\quad$ **1a)** inward parts
$\quad$ **1b)** digestive organs
$\quad$ **1c)** organs of procreation, womb
$\quad$ **1d)** place of emotions or distress or love (fig.)
$\quad$ **1e)** external belly
