**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אֲבִינָדָב‎</span></big>** **ʼĂbîynâdâb** <small>*(ab-ee-naw-dawb')*</small> ==**Abinadab**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H0001|‎אָב‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H5068|‎נָדַב‎]]</big>**; father of generosity (i.e. liberal)</small>

$\quad$ Abinadab.
- § Abinadab = *"my father is noble" or "my father is willing"*
- ***Abinadab***, the name of four Israelites

**1)** a man of Gibeah who sheltered the ark
**2)** second son of Jesse, David's older brother
**3)** a son of Saul, slain with him by the Philistines
