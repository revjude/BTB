<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎אָב‎</span><sup><small>[[H0001]]</small></sup> and <span class="ashebrew">‎נָדַב‎</span><sup><small>[[H5068]]</small></sup>; father of generosity (i.e. liberal)</small>

$\quad$ Abinadab.
- § Abinadab = *"my father is noble" or "my father is willing"*
- ***Abinadab***, the name of four Israelites

**1)** a man of Gibeah who sheltered the ark
**2)** second son of Jesse, David's older brother
**3)** a son of Saul, slain with him by the Philistines
