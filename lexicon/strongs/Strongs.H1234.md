<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּקַע‎</span></big> **bâqaʻ** <small>*(baw-kah')*</small> ==**to break up/open**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ make a breach, break forth (into, out, in pieces, through, up), be ready to burst, cleave (asunder), cut out, divide, hatch, rend (asunder), rip up, tear, win.
- ***to cleave***
-  generally, ***to rend***, ***break***, ***rip*** or ***open***

**1)** to split, cleave, break open, divide, break through, rip up, break up, tear
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to cleave, cleave open
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to break through, break into
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be cleft, be rent open, be split open
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be broken into
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to cleave, cut to pieces, rend open
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to break through, break down
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to be ripped open, be torn open
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to be rent
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to be broken into
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** to break into
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e2)** to break through
$\quad$ **1f)** (Hophal) to be broken into
$\quad$ **1g)** (Hithpael) to burst (themselves) open, cleave asunder
