<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תַּעָב‎</span></big> **taʻâb** <small>*(taw-ab')*</small> ==**to abhor**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (make to be) abhor(-red), (be, commit more, do) abominable(-y), [idiom] utterly.
- ***to loathe***, i.e. (morally) ***detest***

**1)** to abhor, be abominable, do abominably
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal) to be abhorred, be detested
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** in the ritual sense
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** in the ethical sense
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to loathe, abhor, regard as an abomination
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1a)** in the ritual sense
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1b)** in the ethical sense
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to cause to be an abomination
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hiphil) to make abominable, do abominably
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** in the ritual sense
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** in the ethical sense
