<big><span class="ashebrew">‎זָמַר‎</span></big> **zâmar** <small>*(zaw-mar')*</small> ==**to sing**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup>

$\quad$ give praise, sing forth praises, psalms.

-  hence to ***celebrate*** in song and music

**1)** to sing, sing praise, make music

$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to make music, sing
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to play a musical instrument
