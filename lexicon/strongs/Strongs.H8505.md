<big><span class="ashebrew">‎תָּכַן‎</span></big> **tâkan** <small>*(taw-kan')*</small> ==**to measure**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bear up, direct, be (un-)equal, mete, ponder, tell, weigh.
- ***to balance***, i.e. ***measure out*** (by weight or dimension)
-  figuratively, ***arrange***, ***equalize***, through the idea of ***levelling*** (ment. estimate, test)

**1)** to regulate, measure, estimate, ponder, balance, make even, level, weigh, be equal, be weighed out, test, prove
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) estimate (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be estimated
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be made even or right, be adjusted to the standard
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel) to weigh out, mete out
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to be weighed out, be measured out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** measured out (participle)
