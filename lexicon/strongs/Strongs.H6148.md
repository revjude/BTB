**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָרַב‎</span></big>** **ʻârab** <small>*(aw-rab')*</small> ==**to pledge**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ engage, (inter-) meddle (with), mingle (self), mortgage, occupy, give pledges, be(-come, put in) surety, undertake.
- ***to braid***, i.e. ***intermix***
-  ***technically***, ***to traffic*** (as if by barter)
-  also or ***give to be security*** (as a kind of exchange)

**1)** to pledge, exchange, mortgage, engage, occupy, undertake for, give pledges, be or become surety, take on pledge, give in pledge
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to take on pledge, go surety for
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to give in pledge
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to exchange
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to pledge
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to exchange pledges
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to have fellowship with, share
