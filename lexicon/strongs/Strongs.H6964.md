**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קוֹלָיָה‎</span></big>** **Qôwlâyâh** <small>*(ko-law-yaw')*</small> ==**Kolaiah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H6963|‎קוֹל‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[H3050|‎יָהּ‎]]</big>**; voice of Jah</small>

$\quad$ Kolaiah.
- § Kolaiah = *"voice of Jehovah"*
- ***Kolajah***, the name of two Israelites

**1)** father of the false prophet Ahab
**2)** a Benjamite whose descendants settled in Jerusalem after the return from captivity
**3)** a Levite in the time of Ezra
