<small>or **<big><span class="ashebrew">עֲרֹעֵר</span></big>** **ʻĂrôʻêr** <small>*(ar-o-ayr')*</small>; or **<big><span class="ashebrew">עַרְעוֹר</span></big>** **ʻArʻôwr** <small>*(ar-ore')*</small>; the same as **<big>[[H6176|‎עֲרוֹעֵר‎]]</big>**; nudity of situation</small>

$\quad$ Aroer.
- § Aroer = *"ruins"*
- ***Aroer***, the name of three places in or near Palestine

**1)** a city on the north bank of the river Arnon, the southern point of the territory of Sihon the king of the Amorites and later of Reuben; modern 'Arair'
**2)** a city in Ammon near the Jabbok belonging to Gad
**3)** a town in southern Judah
