**<big><span class="asgreek">‎κατασκευάζω‎</span></big>** **kataskeuázō** <small>*(kat-ask-yoo-ad'-zo)*</small> ==**to prepare**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G2596|‎κατά‎]]</big>** and a derivative of **<big>[[G4632|‎σκεῦος‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ build, make, ordain, prepare.
- to prepare thoroughly (properly, by external equipment
-  whereas **<big>[[G2090|‎ἑτοιμάζω‎]]</big>** refers rather to internal fitness)
-  by implication, to construct, create
