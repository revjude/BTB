<small>dual of an unused singular <span class="ashebrew">שָׁמֶה</span> **shâmeh** <small>*(shaw-meh')*</small>; from an unused root meaning to be lofty</small>

$\quad$ air, [idiom] astrologer, heaven(-s).
- ***the sky*** (as aloft
-  the dual perhaps alluding to the visible arch in which the clouds move, as well as to the higher ether where the celestial bodies revolve)

**1)** heaven, heavens, sky
$\quad$ **1a)** visible heavens, sky
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** as abode of the stars
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** as the visible universe, the sky, atmosphere, etc
$\quad$ **1b)** Heaven (as the abode of God)
