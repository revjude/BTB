<big><span class="ashebrew">‎אָבִיב‎</span></big> **ʼâbîyb** <small>*(aw-beeb')*</small> ==**Abib**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:N-M / N:N--T|H:N-M / N:N--T]]</small></sup> 
<small>from an unused root (meaning to be tender)</small>

$\quad$ Abib, ear, green ears of corn (not maize).
- ***green***, i.e. ***a young ear of grain***
-  hence, the name of the month Abib or Nisan

**1)** fresh, young barley ears, barley
**2)** month of ear-forming, of greening of crop, of growing green Abib, month of exodus and passover (March or April)
