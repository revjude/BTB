<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חוּשׁ‎</span></big> **chûwsh** <small>*(koosh)*</small> ==**to hasten**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (make) haste(-n), ready.
- ***to hurry***
-  figuratively, ***to be eager with excitement*** or ***enjoyment***

**1)** to haste, make haste, hurry
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to make haste
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to show haste, act quickly, hasten, come quickly
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to enjoy, be excited
