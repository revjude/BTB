**<big><span class="asgreek">‎προσκόπτω‎</span></big>** **proskóptō** <small>*(pros-kop'-to)*</small> ==**to strike**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G4314|‎πρός‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G2875|‎κόπτω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ beat upon, dash, stumble (at).
- to strike at, i.e. surge against (as water)
-  specially, to stub on, i.e. trip up (literally or figuratively)
