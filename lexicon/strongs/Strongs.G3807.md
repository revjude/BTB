**<big><span class="asgreek">‎παιδαγωγός‎</span></big>** **paidagōgós** <small>*(pahee-dag-o-gos')*</small> ==**guardian**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:N-M|G:N-M]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G3816|‎παῖς‎]]</big>** and a reduplicated form of **<big>[[G0071|‎ἄγω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$  instructor, schoolmaster.
- a boy-leader, i.e. a servant whose office it was to take the children to school
-  (by implication, (figuratively) a tutor ("pædagogue"))
