**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎כָּלַם‎</span></big>** **kâlam** <small>*(kaw-lawm')*</small> ==**be humiliated**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ be (make) ashamed, blush, be confounded, be put to confusion, hurt, reproach, (do, put to) shame.
- properly, ***to wound***
-  but only figuratively, ***to taunt*** or ***insult***

**1)** to insult, shame, humiliate, blush, be ashamed, be put to shame, be reproached, be put to confusion, be humiliated
$\quad$ **1a)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be humiliated, be ashamed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to be put to shame, be dishonoured, be confounded
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to put to shame, insult, humiliate, cause shame to
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to exhibit shame
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hophal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to be insulted, be humiliated
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to be put to shame, be dishonoured, be confounded
