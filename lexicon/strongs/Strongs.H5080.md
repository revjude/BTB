<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָדַח‎</span></big> **nâdach** <small>*(naw-dakh')*</small> ==**to banish**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ banish, bring, cast down (out), chase, compel, draw away, drive (away, out, quite), fetch a stroke, force, go away, outcast, thrust away (out), withdraw.
- ***to push off***
-  used in a great variety of applications, literally and figuratively (to expel, mislead, strike, inflict, etc.)

**1)** to impel, thrust, drive away, banish
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to impel
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to thrust away, banish
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be impelled
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be thrust out, be banished
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2a)** banished, outcast (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be driven away
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** to be thrust away, thrust aside
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be thrust into
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** thrust (participle)
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d1)** to thrust, move, impel
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d2)** to thrust out, banish
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1d3)** to thrust away, thrust aside
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hophal) to be chased, be hunted
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1e1)** chased, hunted (participle)
