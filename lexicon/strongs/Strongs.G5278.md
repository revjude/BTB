**<big><span class="asgreek">‎ὑπομένω‎</span></big>** **hypoménō** <small>*(hoop-om-en'-o)*</small> ==**to remain/endure**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[G5259|‎ὑπό‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G3306|‎μένω‎]]</big>**</small>

$\quad$ abide, endure, (take) patient(-ly), suffer, tarry behind.
- to stay under (behind), i.e. remain
-  figuratively, to undergo, i.e. bear (trials), have fortitude, persevere
