<big><span class="ashebrew">‎דָּרַשׁ‎</span></big> **dârash** <small>*(daw-rash')*</small> ==**to seek**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ ask, [idiom] at all, care for, [idiom] diligently, inquire, make inquisition, (necro-) mancer, question, require, search, seek (for, out), [idiom] surely.
- properly, ***to tread*** or ***frequent***
-  usually ***to follow*** (for pursuit or search)
-  by implication, ***to seek*** or ***ask***
-  specifically ***to worship***

**1)** to resort to, seek, seek with care, enquire, require
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to resort to, frequent (a place), (tread a place)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to consult, enquire of, seek
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2a)** of God
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2b)** of heathen gods, necromancers
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to seek deity in prayer and worship
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3a)** God
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3b)** heathen deities
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to seek (with a demand), demand, require
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to investigate, enquire
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a6)** to ask for, require, demand
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a7)** to practice, study, follow, seek with application
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a8)** to seek with care, care for
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to allow oneself to be enquired of, consulted (only of God)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be sought, be sought out
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** to be required (of blood)
