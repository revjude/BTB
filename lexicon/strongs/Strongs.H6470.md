<big><span class="ashebrew">‎פָּעַם‎</span></big> **pâʻam** <small>*(paw-am')*</small> ==**to trouble**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ move, trouble.
- ***to tap***, i.e. ***beat regularly***
-  hence (generally) ***to impel*** or ***agitate***

**1)** to thrust, impel, push, beat persistently
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to impel
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal) to be beaten, be disturbed
$\quad$ **1c)** (Hithpael) to be disturbed
