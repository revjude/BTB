<big><span class="ashebrew">‎חָכַם‎</span></big> **châkam** <small>*(khaw-kam')*</small> ==**be wise**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root,</small>

$\quad$ [idiom] exceeding, teach wisdom, be (make self, shew self) wise, deal (never so) wisely, make wiser.
- ***to be wise*** (in mind, word or act)

**1)** to be wise
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be or become wise, act wisely
$\quad$ **1b)** (Piel) to make wise, teach wisdom, instruct
$\quad$ **1c)** (Pual) to be made wise
$\quad$ **1d)** (Hiphil) to make wise
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to show oneself wise, deceive, show one's wisdom
