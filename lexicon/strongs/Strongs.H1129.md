<big><span class="ashebrew">‎בָּנָה‎</span></big> **bânâh** <small>*(baw-naw')*</small> ==**to build**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ (begin to) build(-er), obtain children, make, repair, set (up), [idiom] surely.
- ***to build*** (literally and figuratively)

**1)** to build, rebuild, establish, cause to continue
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to build, rebuild
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to build a house (ie, establish a family)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be built
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be rebuilt
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b3)** established (of restored exiles) (fig.)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b4)** established (made permanent)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b5)** to be built up (of childless wife becoming the mother of a family through the children of a concubine)
