$\quad$ Eliezer.
- § Eliezer = *"God is help"*
- ***Eliezer***, the name of a Damascene and of ten Israelites

**1)** Abraham's Damascene servant
**2)** a son of Moses
**3)** a Benjamite
**4)** a priest who helped move the ark
**5)** a Reubenite
**6)** a prophet who spoke to Jehoshaphat
**7)** a Levite chief
**8)** son of Harim
**9)** priest with foreign wife
