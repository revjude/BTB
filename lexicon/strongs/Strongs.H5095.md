**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎נָהַל‎</span></big>** **nâhal** <small>*(naw-hal')*</small> ==**to guide**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ carry, feed, guide, lead (gently, on).
- properly, ***to run with a******sparkle***, i.e. ***flow***
-  hence (transitively), ***to conduct***, and (by inference) ***to protect***, ***sustain***

**1)** to lead, give rest, lead with care, guide to a watering place or station, cause to rest, bring to a station or place of rest, guide, refresh
$\quad$ **1a)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to lead to a watering-place or station and cause to rest there
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to lead or bring to a station or goal
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a3)** to lead, guide
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a4)** to give rest to
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a5)** to refresh (with food)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hithpael)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to lead on
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to journey by stations or stages
