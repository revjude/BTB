<big><span class="asgreek">‎λέγω‎</span></big> **légō** <small>*(leg'-o)*</small> ==**to say**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary verb</small>

$\quad$ ask, bid, boast, call, describe, give out, name, put forth, say(-ing, on), shew, speak, tell, utter.
- properly, to "lay" forth, i.e. (figuratively) relate (in words (usually of systematic or set discourse
-  whereas <span class="asgreek">ἔπω</span><sup><small>[[G2036]]</small></sup> and <span class="asgreek">φημί</span><sup><small>[[G5346]]</small></sup> generally refer to an individual expression or speech respectively
-  while <span class="asgreek">ῥέω</span><sup><small>[[G4483]]</small></sup> is properly to break silence merely, and <span class="asgreek">λαλέω</span><sup><small>[[G2980]]</small></sup> means an extended or random harangue))
-  by implication, to mean
