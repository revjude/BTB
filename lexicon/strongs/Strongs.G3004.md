**<big><span class="asgreek">‎λέγω‎</span></big>** **légō** <small>*(leg'-o)*</small> ==**to say**==  <sup><small>[[TEGMC#G:V|G:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primary verb</small>

$\quad$ ask, bid, boast, call, describe, give out, name, put forth, say(-ing, on), shew, speak, tell, utter.
- properly, to "lay" forth, i.e. (figuratively) relate (in words (usually of systematic or set discourse
-  whereas **<big>[[G2036|‎ἔπω‎]]</big>** and **<big>[[G5346|‎φημί‎]]</big>** generally refer to an individual expression or speech respectively
-  while **<big>[[G4483|‎ῥέω‎]]</big>** is properly to break silence merely, and **<big>[[G2980|‎λαλέω‎]]</big>** means an extended or random harangue))
-  by implication, to mean
