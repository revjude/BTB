**<big><span class="ashebrew">‎עָדָה‎</span></big>** **ʻÂdâh** <small>*(aw-daw')*</small> ==**Adah**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-F-P|N:N-F-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from **<big>[[H5710|‎עָדָה‎]]</big>**; ornament</small>

$\quad$ Adah.
- § Adah = *"ornament"*
- ***Adah***, the name of two women

**1)** the 1st of the 2 wives of Lamech and mother of Jabal and Jubal
**2)** a Hittitess, 1 of the 3 wives of Esau and mother of Eliphaz
$\quad$ **2a)** also called 'Bashemath'
