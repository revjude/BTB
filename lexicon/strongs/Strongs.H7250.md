<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ let gender, lie down.
- ***to squat*** or ***lie out flat***, i.e. (specifically) ***in copulation***

**1)** to lie stretched out, lie down
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to lie down
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** for repose
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1b)** for copulation (of woman with beast)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to cause to lay down (of cattle breeding)
