<big><span class="ashebrew">‎קָפָא‎</span></big> **qâphâʼ** <small>*(kaw-faw')*</small> ==**to congeal**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ congeal, curdle, dark, settle.
- ***to shrink***, i.e. ***thicken*** (as unracked wine, curdled milk, clouded sky, frozen water)

v
**1)** to thicken, condense, congeal, settle, become dense
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to be condensed
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** thickening (participle)
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to cause to curdle
n m
**2)** congelation
