<small>or <span class="ashebrew">מָלָא</span> **mâlâʼ** <small>*(mawlaw')*</small>; (Esther 7:5), a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ accomplish, confirm, [phrase] consecrate, be at an end, be expired, be fenced, fill, fulfil, (be, become, [idiom] draw, give in, go) full(-ly, -ly set, tale), (over-) flow, fulness, furnish, gather (selves, together), presume, replenish, satisfy, set, space, take a (hand-) full, [phrase] have wholly.
- ***to fill*** or (intransitively) ***be full of***, in a wide application (literally and figuratively)

**1)** to fill, be full
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1)** to be full
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1a)** fulness, abundance (participle)
$\quad$ $\quad$ $\quad$ **1a1b)** to be full, be accomplished, be ended
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1a2)** to consecrate, fill the hand
$\quad$ **1b)** (Niphal)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b1)** to be filled, be armed, be satisfied
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1b2)** to be accomplished, be ended
$\quad$ **1c)** (Piel)
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c1)** to fill
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c2)** to satisfy
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c3)** to fulfil, accomplish, complete
$\quad$ $\quad$ **1c4)** to confirm
$\quad$ **1d)** (Pual) to be filled
$\quad$ **1e)** (Hithpael) to mass themselves against
