<big><span class="ashebrew">‎דּוֹדוֹ‎</span></big> **Dôwdôw** <small>*(do-do')*</small> ==**Dodo**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#N:N-M-P|N:N-M-P]]</small></sup> 
<small>from <span class="ashebrew">‎דּוֹד‎</span><sup><small>[[H1730]]</small></sup>; loving</small>

$\quad$ Dodo.
- § Dodo = *"his beloved"*
- ***Dodo***, the name of three Israelites

**1)** a man of the tribe of Issachar
**2)** a man of Bethlehem, father of Elhanan who was one of David's 30 captains
**3)** an Ahohite, father of Eleazar, the second of the 3 mighty men who were over the 30
