<big><span class="ashebrew">‎זָנַח‎</span></big> **zânach** <small>*(zaw-nakh')*</small> ==**to reject**==  <sup><small>[[TEHMC#H:V|H:V]]</small></sup> 
<small>a primitive root meaning to push aside, i.e.</small>

$\quad$ cast away (off), remove far away (off).
- ***reject***, ***forsake***, ***fail***

**1)** to cast off, reject, spurn
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to reject
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to forcefully reject someone
**2)** to stink, emit stench, become odious
$\quad$ **2a)** (Hiphil) stink (perfect)
