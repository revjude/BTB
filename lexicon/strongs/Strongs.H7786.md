<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ make princes, have power, reign. See <span class="ashebrew">‎סוּר‎</span><sup><small>[[H5493]]</small></sup>.

$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to rule over, govern
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to make rulers, play the prince
