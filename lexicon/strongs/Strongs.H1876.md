<small>a primitive root</small>

$\quad$ bring forth, spring.
- ***to sprout***

**1)** to sprout, shoot, grow green
$\quad$ **1a)** (Qal) to sprout, grow green
$\quad$ **1b)** (Hiphil) to cause to sprout, cause to shoot forth
