Greek of: <small>[[H3120H]]</small> 


1. <b>==Ἑλλάς== </b>, a city of Thessaly, founded by Hellen, [<a href="javascript:void(0)" title="Iliad by Homer">8th/7th c.BC</a>] 


2. <b>that part of Thessaly in which the Myrmidons dwelt </b>, also called Phthiotis, [<a href="javascript:void(0)" title="Homer">8th/7th c.BC</a>] 


3. <b>Northern Greece </b>, as opposite to Peloponnesus, [<a href="javascript:void(0)" title="Odyssey by Homer">8th/7th c.BC</a>] 


4. later, the name for <b>Greece </b>, from the South to Epirus and Thessaly inclusively, [<a href="javascript:void(0)" title="Hesiod">8th/7th c.BC</a>, <a href="javascript:void(0)" title="Herdotus Historicus">Refs 5th c.BC</a>], etc. 


5. as <i>adjective</i> with a fem. <i>Substantive</i> <b>Hellenic, Greek </b>, [<a href="javascript:void(0)" title="Herdotus Historicus">Refs 5th c.BC</a>], etc.  (Middle Liddel)
