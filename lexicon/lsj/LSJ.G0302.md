$\quad$ ****<b>\_\_A</b> In Simple Sentences, and in the Apodosis of Compound Sentences; here <span class='asgreek'>==ἄν==</span> belongs to the Verb, and denotes that the assertion made by the Verb is dependent on a condition, expressed or implied: thus <span class='asgreek'>ἦλθεν</span> <b>he came,</b> <span class='asgreek'>ἦλθεν</span> <span class='asgreek'>==ἄν==</span> <b>he would have come</b> (under conditions, which may or may not be defined), and so <b>he might have come;</b> <span class='asgreek'>ἔλθοι</span> <b>may he come,</b> <span class='asgreek'>ἔλθοι</span> <span class='asgreek'>==ἄν==</span> <b>he would come</b> (under certain conditions), and so <b>he might come.</b>
