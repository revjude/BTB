<b> <span class='asgreek'>Ξ</span> <span class='asgreek'>ξ</span></b>, 


 <b><span class='asgreek'>ξεῖ</span></b> (which see), <span class='asgreek'>τό</span>, <i>indeclinable</i>, fifteenth (later fourteenth) letter of most of the Eastern Greek alphabets, including the Ionic (later general Gr.) alphabet; not used in Western Greek alphabets or in the native Attic alphabet; in form and serial position [<a href="javascript:void(0)" title="(“IG” 14.2420)">Refs</a>] it corresponds to the Semitic <b>samech</b> (a form of <b>s</b>), but has acquired the new value <b>ks</b>; as numeral <span class='asgreek'>ξ᾽</span> = 60, but <span class='asgreek'>ξ᾽</span> = 60,000.
