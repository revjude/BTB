
**FARTHING**

→ (This word is used to translate two different words in the Greek  text)

- Assarion, a Roman coin worth, in American money, only a few cents;  in English money, about three farthings [[Matt.10.29|Mt 10:29]]; [[Luke.12.6|Lu 12:6]]

→ And kodrantes, worth, in American money, about two-fifths of  a cent; in English money, less than a farthing [[Matt.5.26|Mt 5:26]]; [[Mark.12.42|Mr 12:42]]
