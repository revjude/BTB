
**SHECHEM**

1. Also called SICHEM and SYCHEM, a district in the central part  of the land of Canaan

- Abraham lives in [[Gen.12.6|Ge 12:6]]

- The flocks and herds of Jacob kept in [[Gen.37.12|Ge 37:12]]-[[Gen.37.14|14]]

- Joseph buried in [[Josh.24.32|Jos 24:32]]

- Joshua assembled the tribes of Israel at, with all their elders,  chiefs, and judges, and presented them before the Lord [[Josh.24.1|Jos 24:1]]-[[Josh.24.28|28]]
