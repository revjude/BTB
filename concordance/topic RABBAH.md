- Captured by David [[2Sam.11.1|2Sa 11:1]]; [[2Sam.12.26|12:26]]-[[2Sam.12.31|31]]; [[1Chr.20.1|1Ch 20:1]]-[[1Chr.20.3|3]]

- Possessed again by the Ammonites; prophesied against [[Jer.49.2|Jer 49:2]],[[Jer.49.3|3]]; [[Ezek.21.20|Eze 21:20]]; [[Ezek.25.5|25:5]]; [[Amos.1.14|Am 1:14]]

2. A city in the territory of the tribe of Judah [[Josh.15.60|Jos  15:60]]
