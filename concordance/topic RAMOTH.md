
**RAMOTH**

1. An Israelite at the time of Ezra

- Had taken a non-Israelite wife [[Ezra.10.29|Ezr 10:29]]

2. Called RAMATH OF THE SOUTH

- A place probably in the south of the tribe of Simeon  [[Josh.19.8|Jos 19:8]]; [[1Sam.30.27|1Sa 30:27]]

3. A city of the territory of the tribe of Issachar, allotted  to the Levites [[1Chr.6.73|1Ch 6:73]]

4. Ramoth in Gilead
