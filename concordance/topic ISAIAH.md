
**ISAIAH**

→ Also called ESAIAS

→ Son of Amos [[Isa.1.1|Isa 1:1]]

→ Prophesies in the days of Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah,  kings of Judah [[Isa.1.1|Isa 1:1]]; [[Isa.6.1|6:1]]; [[Isa.7.1|7:1]],[[Isa.7.3|3]]; [[Isa.14.27|14:27]]; [[Isa.20.1|20:1]]; [[Isa.36.1|36:1]]; [[Isa.38.1|38:1]]; [[Isa.39.1|39:1]]

→ Prophecy at the time of the invasion by Tartan, of Assyria [[Isa.20.1|Isa 20:1]]

→ Symbolically wears sackcloth, and walks barefoot as a sign to  Israel [[Isa.20.2|Isa 20:2]],[[Isa.20.3|3]]

→ Comforts and encourages Hezekiah and the people during the siege  of Jerusalem by Rab-shakeh [[2Kgs.18|2Ki 18]]; [[2Kgs.19|19]]; [[Isa.37.6|Isa 37:6]],[[Isa.37.7|7]]

→ Comforts Hezekiah in his affliction [[2Kgs.20.1|2Ki 20:1]]-[[2Kgs.20.11|11]]; [[Isa.38|Isa  38]]

→ Performs the miracle of the returning shadow to confirm Hezekiah's  faith [[2Kgs.20.8|2Ki 20:8]]-[[2Kgs.20.11|11]]

→ Reproves Hezekiah's folly in exhibiting his resources to the  commissioners from Babylon [[2Kgs.20.12|2Ki 20:12]]-[[2Kgs.20.19|19]]; [[Isa.39|Isa 39]]

→ Is the chronicler of the times of Uzziah and Hezekiah [[2Chr.26.22|2Ch 26:22]]; [[2Chr.32.32|32:32]]
