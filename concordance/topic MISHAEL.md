- One of three Hebrew young men trained with Daniel at the court  of Babylon [[Dan.1.6|Da 1:6]],[[Dan.1.7|7]],[[Dan.1.11|11]]-[[Dan.1.20|20]]

- Assists Daniel in interpreting Nebuchadnezzars dream [[Dan.2.17|Da 2:17]]-[[Dan.2.23|23]]
