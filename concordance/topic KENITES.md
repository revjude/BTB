
**KENITES**

1. A Canaanite tribe whose country was given to Abraham [[Gen.15.19|Ge 15:19]]; [[Num.24.21|Nu 24:21]]-[[Num.24.23|23]]

2. The descendants of Jethro, a Midianite, the father-in-law  of Moses

- Join the Israelites and live at Jericho [[Judg.1.16|Jud 1:16]]; [[Judg.4.11|4:11]]; [[1Chr.2.55|1Ch 2:55]]

- Later in the wilderness of Judah [[Judg.1.16|Jud 1:16]],[[Judg.1.17|17]]

- Jael, one of the, betrays and kills Sisera [[Judg.4.17|Jud 4:17]]-[[Judg.4.21|21]]
