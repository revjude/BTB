**Jeremiah 4:10** 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then said I, Ah, Lord GOD! surely thou hast greatly deceived this people and Jerusalem, saying, Ye shall have peace; whereas the sword reacheth unto the soul.
