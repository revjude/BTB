**Proverbs 27:1** 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Boast not thyself of to morrow; for thou knowest not what a day may bring forth.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Do not boast about tomorrow, for you do not know what a day may bring.
