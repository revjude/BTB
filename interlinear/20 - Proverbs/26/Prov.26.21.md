**Proverbs 26:21** 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[As] coals [are] to burning coals, and wood to fire; so [is] a contentious man to kindle strife.

Like charcoal for embers and wood for fire, so is a quarrelsome man for kindling strife.
