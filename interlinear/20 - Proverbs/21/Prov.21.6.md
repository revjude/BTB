**Proverbs 21:6** 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The getting of treasures by a lying tongue [is] a vanity tossed to and fro of them that seek death.

Making a fortune by a lying tongue is a vanishing mist, a deadly pursuit.
