**Isaiah 28:21** 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For the LORD shall rise up as [in] mount Perazim, he shall be wroth as [in] the valley of Gibeon, that he may do his work, his strange work; and bring to pass his act, his strange act.
