**Isaiah 51:9** 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Awake, awake, put on strength, O arm of the LORD; awake, as in the ancient days, in the generations of old. [Art] thou not it that hath cut Rahab, [and] wounded the dragon?
