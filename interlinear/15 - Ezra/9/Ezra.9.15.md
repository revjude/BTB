**Ezra 9:15** 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
O LORD God of Israel, thou [art] righteous: for we remain yet escaped, as [it is] this day: behold, we [are] before thee in our trespasses: for we cannot stand before thee because of this.
