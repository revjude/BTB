**Psalms 62:0** (62:1)
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician, to Jeduthun, A Psalm of David. Truly my soul waiteth upon God: from him [cometh] my salvation.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
