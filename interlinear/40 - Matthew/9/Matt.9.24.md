**Matthew 9:24
**<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He said unto them, Give place: for the maid is not dead, but sleepeth. And they laughed him to scorn.

“Go away,” He told them. “The girl is not dead, but asleep.” And they laughed at Him.
