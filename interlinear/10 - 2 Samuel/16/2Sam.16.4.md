**2 Samuel 16:4** 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then said the king to Ziba, Behold, thine [are] all that [pertained] unto Mephibosheth. And Ziba said, I humbly beseech thee [that] I may find grace in thy sight, my lord, O king.
