[[Gen.11|Genesis 11:10 - Genesis 11:26]]
*Genealogy from Shem to Abram*

[[Gen.11.10|10]] These \[are\] the generations of Shem: Shem \[was\] an hundred years old, and begat Arphaxad two years after the flood:

10: 

[[Gen.11.11|11]] And Shem lived after he begat Arphaxad five hundred years, and begat sons and daughters.

11: 

[[Gen.11.12|12]] And Arphaxad lived five and thirty years, and begat Salah:

12: 

[[Gen.11.13|13]] And Arphaxad lived after he begat Salah four hundred and three years, and begat sons and daughters.

13: 

[[Gen.11.14|14]] And Salah lived thirty years, and begat Eber:

14: 

[[Gen.11.15|15]] And Salah lived after he begat Eber four hundred and three years, and begat sons and daughters.

15: 

[[Gen.11.16|16]] And Eber lived four and thirty years, and begat Peleg:

16: 

[[Gen.11.17|17]] And Eber lived after he begat Peleg four hundred and thirty years, and begat sons and daughters.

17: 

[[Gen.11.18|18]] And Peleg lived thirty years, and begat Reu:

18: 

[[Gen.11.19|19]] And Peleg lived after he begat Reu two hundred and nine years, and begat sons and daughters.

19: 

[[Gen.11.20|20]] And Reu lived two and thirty years, and begat Serug:

20: 

[[Gen.11.21|21]] And Reu lived after he begat Serug two hundred and seven years, and begat sons and daughters.

21: 

[[Gen.11.22|22]] And Serug lived thirty years, and begat Nahor:

22: 

[[Gen.11.23|23]] And Serug lived after he begat Nahor two hundred years, and begat sons and daughters.

23: 

[[Gen.11.24|24]] And Nahor lived nine and twenty years, and begat Terah:

24: 

[[Gen.11.25|25]] And Nahor lived after he begat Terah an hundred and nineteen years, and begat sons and daughters.

25: 

[[Gen.11.26|26]] And Terah lived seventy years, and begat Abram, Nahor, and Haran.

26:
