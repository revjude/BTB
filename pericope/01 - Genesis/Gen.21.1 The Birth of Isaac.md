*The Birth of Isaac*

[[Gen.21.1|1]] And the LORD visited Sarah as he had said, and the LORD did unto Sarah as he had spoken.

1: 

[[Gen.21.2|2]] For Sarah conceived, and bare Abraham a son in his old age, at the set time of which God had spoken to him.

2: 

[[Gen.21.3|3]] And Abraham called the name of his son that was born unto him, whom Sarah bare to him, Isaac.

3: 

[[Gen.21.4|4]] And Abraham circumcised his son Isaac being eight days old, as God had commanded him.

4: 

[[Gen.21.5|5]] And Abraham was an hundred years old, when his son Isaac was born unto him.

6:

7:
