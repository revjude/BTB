[[Gen.17|Genesis 17:9 - Genesis 17:27]]
*The Covenant of Circumcision*

[[Gen.17.9|9]] And God said unto Abraham, Thou shalt keep my covenant therefore, thou, and thy seed after thee in their generations.

9: 

[[Gen.17.10|10]] This \[is\] my covenant, which ye shall keep, between me and you and thy seed after thee; Every man child among you shall be circumcised.

10: 

[[Gen.17.11|11]] And ye shall circumcise the flesh of your foreskin; and it shall be a token of the covenant betwixt me and you.

11: 

[[Gen.17.12|12]] And he that is eight days old shall be circumcised among you, every man child in your generations, he that is born in the house, or bought with money of any stranger, which \[is\] not of thy seed.

12: 

[[Gen.17.13|13]] He that is born in thy house, and he that is bought with thy money, must needs be circumcised: and my covenant shall be in your flesh for an everlasting covenant.

13: 

[[Gen.17.14|14]] And the uncircumcised man child whose flesh of his foreskin is not circumcised, that soul shall be cut off from his people; he hath broken my covenant.

14: 

[[Gen.17.15|15]] And God said unto Abraham, As for Sarai thy wife, thou shalt not call her name Sarai, but Sarah \[shall\] her name \[be\].

15: 

[[Gen.17.16|16]] And I will bless her, and give thee a son also of her: yea, I will bless her, and she shall be \[a mother\] of nations; kings of people shall be of her.

16: 

[[Gen.17.17|17]] Then Abraham fell upon his face, and laughed, and said in his heart, Shall \[a child\] be born unto him that is an hundred years old? and shall Sarah, that is ninety years old, bear?

17: 

[[Gen.17.18|18]] And Abraham said unto God, O that Ishmael might live before thee!

18: 

[[Gen.17.19|19]] And God said, Sarah thy wife shall bear thee a son indeed; and thou shalt call his name Isaac: and I will establish my covenant with him for an everlasting covenant, \[and\] with his seed after him.

19: 

[[Gen.17.20|20]] And as for Ishmael, I have heard thee: Behold, I have blessed him, and will make him fruitful, and will multiply him exceedingly; twelve princes shall he beget, and I will make him a great nation.

20: 

[[Gen.17.21|21]] But my covenant will I establish with Isaac, which Sarah shall bear unto thee at this set time in the next year.

21: 

[[Gen.17.22|22]] And he left off talking with him, and God went up from Abraham.
