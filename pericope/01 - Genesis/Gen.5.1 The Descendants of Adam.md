[[Gen.5|Genesis 5:1 - Genesis 5:17]]
*The Descendants of Adam*

[[Gen.5.1|1]] This \[is\] the book of the generations of Adam. In the day that God created man, in the likeness of God made he him;

1: 

[[Gen.5.2|2]] Male and female created he them; and blessed them, and called their name Adam, in the day when they were created.

2: 

[[Gen.5.3|3]] And Adam lived an hundred and thirty years, and begat \[a son\] in his own likeness, after his image; and called his name Seth:

3: 

[[Gen.5.4|4]] And the days of Adam after he had begotten Seth were eight hundred years: and he begat sons and daughters:

4: 

[[Gen.5.5|5]] And all the days that Adam lived were nine hundred and thirty years: and he died.

5: 

[[Gen.5.6|6]] And Seth lived an hundred and five years, and begat Enos:

6: 

[[Gen.5.7|7]] And Seth lived after he begat Enos eight hundred and seven years, and begat sons and daughters:

7: 

[[Gen.5.8|8]] And all the days of Seth were nine hundred and twelve years: and he died.

8: 

[[Gen.5.9|9]] And Enos lived ninety years, and begat Cainan:

9: 

[[Gen.5.10|10]] And Enos lived after he begat Cainan eight hundred and fifteen years, and begat sons and daughters:

10: 

[[Gen.5.11|11]] And all the days of Enos were nine hundred and five years: and he died.

11: 

[[Gen.5.12|12]] And Cainan lived seventy years, and begat Mahalaleel:

12: 

[[Gen.5.13|13]] And Cainan lived after he begat Mahalaleel eight hundred and forty years, and begat sons and daughters:

13: 

[[Gen.5.14|14]] And all the days of Cainan were nine hundred and ten years: and he died.

14: 

[[Gen.5.15|15]] And Mahalaleel lived sixty and five years, and begat Jared:

15: 

[[Gen.5.16|16]] And Mahalaleel lived after he begat Jared eight hundred and thirty years, and begat sons and daughters:

16: 

[[Gen.5.17|17]] And all the days of Mahalaleel were eight hundred ninety and five years: and he died.

17:
