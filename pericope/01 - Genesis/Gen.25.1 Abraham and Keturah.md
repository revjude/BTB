[[Gen.25|Genesis 25:1 - Genesis 25:6]]
*Abraham and Keturah*

[[Gen.25.1|1]] Then again Abraham took a wife, and her name \[was\] Keturah.

1: 

[[Gen.25.2|2]] And she bare him Zimran, and Jokshan, and Medan, and Midian, and Ishbak, and Shuah.

2: 

[[Gen.25.3|3]] And Jokshan begat Sheba, and Dedan. And the sons of Dedan were Asshurim, and Letushim, and Leummim.

3: 

[[Gen.25.4|4]] And the sons of Midian; Ephah, and Epher, and Hanoch, and Abida, and Eldaah. All these \[were\] the children of Keturah.

4: 

[[Gen.25.5|5]] And Abraham gave all that he had unto Isaac.

5: 

[[Gen.25.6|6]] But unto the sons of the concubines, which Abraham had, Abraham gave gifts, and sent them away from Isaac his son, while he yet lived, eastward, unto the east country.
