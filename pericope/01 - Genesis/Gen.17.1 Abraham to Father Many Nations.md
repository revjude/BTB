*Abraham to Father Many Nations*

[[Gen.17.1|1]] And when Abram was ninety years old and nine, the LORD appeared to Abram, and said unto him, I \[am\] the Almighty God; walk before me, and be thou perfect.

1: 

[[Gen.17.2|2]] And I will make my covenant between me and thee, and will multiply thee exceedingly.

2: 

[[Gen.17.3|3]] And Abram fell on his face: and God talked with him, saying,

3:

4: 

[[Gen.17.5|5]] Neither shall thy name any more be called Abram, but thy name shall be Abraham; for a father of many nations have I made thee.

5:

6:

7: 

[[Gen.17.8|8]] And I will give unto thee, and to thy seed after thee, the land wherein thou art a stranger, all the land of Canaan, for an everlasting possession; and I will be their God.
