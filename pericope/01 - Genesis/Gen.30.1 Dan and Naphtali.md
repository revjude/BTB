*Dan and Naphtali*

[[Gen.30.1|1]] And when Rachel saw that she bare Jacob no children, Rachel envied her sister; and said unto Jacob, Give me children, or else I die.

1: 

[[Gen.30.2|2]] And Jacob’s anger was kindled against Rachel: and he said, \[Am\] I in God’s stead, who hath withheld from thee the fruit of the womb?

2: 

[[Gen.30.3|3]] And she said, Behold my maid Bilhah, go in unto her; and she shall bear upon my knees, that I may also have children by her.

3: 

[[Gen.30.4|4]] And she gave him Bilhah her handmaid to wife: and Jacob went in unto her.

4: 

[[Gen.30.5|5]] And Bilhah conceived, and bare Jacob a son.

5: 

[[Gen.30.6|6]] And Rachel said, God hath judged me, and hath also heard my voice, and hath given me a son: therefore called she his name Dan.

6: 

[[Gen.30.7|7]] And Bilhah Rachel’s maid conceived again, and bare Jacob a second son.

7: 

[[Gen.30.8|8]] And Rachel said, With great wrestlings have I wrestled with my sister, and I have prevailed: and she called his name Naphtali.
