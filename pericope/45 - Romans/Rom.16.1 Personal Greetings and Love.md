[[Rom.16|Romans 16:1 - Romans 16:16]]
*Personal Greetings and Love*

[[Rom.16.1|1]] I commend unto you Phebe our sister, which is a servant of the church which is at Cenchrea:

1: 

[[Rom.16.2|2]] That ye receive her in the Lord, as becometh saints, and that ye assist her in whatsoever business she hath need of you: for she hath been a succourer of many, and of myself also.

2: 

[[Rom.16.3|3]] Greet Priscilla and Aquila my helpers in Christ Jesus:

3: 

[[Rom.16.4|4]] Who have for my life laid down their own necks: unto whom not only I give thanks, but also all the churches of the Gentiles.

4: 

[[Rom.16.5|5]] Likewise \[greet\] the church that is in their house. Salute my wellbeloved Epaenetus, who is the firstfruits of Achaia unto Christ.

5: 

[[Rom.16.6|6]] Greet Mary, who bestowed much labour on us.

6: 

[[Rom.16.7|7]] Salute Andronicus and Junia, my kinsmen, and my fellowprisoners, who are of note among the apostles, who also were in Christ before me.

7: 

[[Rom.16.8|8]] Greet Amplias my beloved in the Lord.

8: 

[[Rom.16.9|9]] Salute Urbane, our helper in Christ, and Stachys my beloved.

9: 

[[Rom.16.10|10]] Salute Apelles approved in Christ. Salute them which are of Aristobulusâ€™ \[household\].

10: 

[[Rom.16.11|11]] Salute Herodion my kinsman. Greet them that be of the \[household\] of Narcissus, which are in the Lord.

11: 

[[Rom.16.12|12]] Salute Tryphena and Tryphosa, who labour in the Lord. Salute the beloved Persis, which laboured much in the Lord.

12: 

[[Rom.16.13|13]] Salute Rufus chosen in the Lord, and his mother and mine.

13: 

[[Rom.16.14|14]] Salute Asyncritus, Phlegon, Hermas, Patrobas, Hermes, and the brethren which are with them.

14: 

[[Rom.16.15|15]] Salute Philologus, and Julia, Nereus, and his sister, and Olympas, and all the saints which are with them.

15: 

[[Rom.16.16|16]] Salute one another with an holy kiss. The churches of Christ salute you.
