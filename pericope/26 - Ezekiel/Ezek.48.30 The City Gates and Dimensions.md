*The City Gates and Dimensions*

[[Ezek.48.30|30]] And these \[are\] the goings out of the city on the north side, four thousand and five hundred measures.

30: 

[[Ezek.48.31|31]] And the gates of the city \[shall be\] after the names of the tribes of Israel: three gates northward; one gate of Reuben, one gate of Judah, one gate of Levi.

31: 

[[Ezek.48.32|32]] And at the east side four thousand and five hundred: and three gates; and one gate of Joseph, one gate of Benjamin, one gate of Dan.

32: 

[[Ezek.48.33|33]] And at the south side four thousand and five hundred measures: and three gates; one gate of Simeon, one gate of Issachar, one gate of Zebulun.

33: 

[[Ezek.48.34|34]] At the west side four thousand and five hundred, \[with\] their three gates; one gate of Gad, one gate of Asher, one gate of Naphtali.

34:
