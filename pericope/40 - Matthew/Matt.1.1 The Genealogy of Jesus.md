[[Matt.1|Matthew 1:1 - Matthew 1:17]]
*The Genealogy of Jesus*

[[Matt.1.1|1]] The book of the generation of Jesus Christ, the son of David, the son of Abraham.

1: 

[[Matt.1.2|2]] Abraham begat Isaac; and Isaac begat Jacob; and Jacob begat Judas and his brethren;

2: 

[[Matt.1.3|3]] And Judas begat Phares and Zara of Thamar; and Phares begat Esrom; and Esrom begat Aram;

3: 

[[Matt.1.4|4]] And Aram begat Aminadab; and Aminadab begat Naasson; and Naasson begat Salmon;

4: 

[[Matt.1.5|5]] And Salmon begat Booz of Rachab; and Booz begat Obed of Ruth; and Obed begat Jesse;

5: 

[[Matt.1.6|6]] And Jesse begat David the king; and David the king begat Solomon of her \[that had been the wife\] of Urias;

6: 

[[Matt.1.7|7]] And Solomon begat Roboam; and Roboam begat Abia; and Abia begat Asa;

7: 

[[Matt.1.8|8]] And Asa begat Josaphat; and Josaphat begat Joram; and Joram begat Ozias;

8: 

[[Matt.1.9|9]] And Ozias begat Joatham; and Joatham begat Achaz; and Achaz begat Ezekias;

9: 

[[Matt.1.10|10]] And Ezekias begat Manasses; and Manasses begat Amon; and Amon begat Josias;

10: 

[[Matt.1.11|11]] And Josias begat Jechonias and his brethren, about the time they were carried away to Babylon:

11: 

[[Matt.1.12|12]] And after they were brought to Babylon, Jechonias begat Salathiel; and Salathiel begat Zorobabel;

12: 

[[Matt.1.13|13]] And Zorobabel begat Abiud; and Abiud begat Eliakim; and Eliakim begat Azor;

13: 

[[Matt.1.14|14]] And Azor begat Sadoc; and Sadoc begat Achim; and Achim begat Eliud;

14: 

[[Matt.1.15|15]] And Eliud begat Eleazar; and Eleazar begat Matthan; and Matthan begat Jacob;

15: 

[[Matt.1.16|16]] And Jacob begat Joseph the husband of Mary, of whom was born Jesus, who is called Christ.

16: 

[[Matt.1.17|17]] So all the generations from Abraham to David \[are\] fourteen generations; and from David until the carrying away into Babylon \[are\] fourteen generations; and from the carrying away into Babylon unto Christ \[are\] fourteen generations.

17:
