[[Num.34|Numbers 34:16 - Numbers 34:29]]
*Leaders to Divide the Land*

[[Num.34.16|16]] And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,

16: 

[[Num.34.17|17]] These \[are\] the names of the men which shall divide the land unto you: Eleazar the priest, and Joshua the son of Nun.

17: 

[[Num.34.18|18]] And ye shall take one prince of every tribe, to divide the land by inheritance.

18: 

[[Num.34.19|19]] And the names of the men \[are\] these: Of the tribe of Judah, Caleb the son of Jephunneh.

19: 

[[Num.34.20|20]] And of the tribe of the children of Simeon, Shemuel the son of Ammihud.

20: 

[[Num.34.21|21]] Of the tribe of Benjamin, Elidad the son of Chislon.

21: 

[[Num.34.22|22]] And the prince of the tribe of the children of Dan, Bukki the son of Jogli.

22: 

[[Num.34.23|23]] The prince of the children of Joseph, for the tribe of the children of Manasseh, Hanniel the son of Ephod.

23: 

[[Num.34.24|24]] And the prince of the tribe of the children of Ephraim, Kemuel the son of Shiphtan.

24: 

[[Num.34.25|25]] And the prince of the tribe of the children of Zebulun, Elizaphan the son of Parnach.

25: 

[[Num.34.26|26]] And the prince of the tribe of the children of Issachar, Paltiel the son of Azzan.

26: 

[[Num.34.27|27]] And the prince of the tribe of the children of Asher, Ahihud the son of Shelomi.

27: 

[[Num.34.28|28]] And the prince of the tribe of the children of Naphtali, Pedahel the son of Ammihud.

28: 

[[Num.34.29|29]] These \[are they\] whom the LORD commanded to divide the inheritance unto the children of Israel in the land of Canaan.

29:
