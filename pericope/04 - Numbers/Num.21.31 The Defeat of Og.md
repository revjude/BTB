[[Num.21|Numbers 21:31 - Numbers 21:35]]
*The Defeat of Og*

[[Num.21.31|31]] Thus Israel dwelt in the land of the Amorites.

31: 

[[Num.21.32|32]] And Moses sent to spy out Jaazer, and they took the villages thereof, and drove out the Amorites that \[were\] there.

32: 

[[Num.21.33|33]] And they turned and went up by the way of Bashan: and Og the king of Bashan went out against them, he, and all his people, to the battle at Edrei.

33: 

[[Num.21.34|34]] And the LORD said unto Moses, Fear him not: for I have delivered him into thy hand, and all his people, and his land; and thou shalt do to him as thou didst unto Sihon king of the Amorites, which dwelt at Heshbon.

34: 

[[Num.21.35|35]] So they smote him, and his sons, and all his people, until there was none left him alive: and they possessed his land.
