*Moses Separates the People*

[[Num.16.23|23]] And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,

23: 

[[Num.16.24|24]] Speak unto the congregation, saying, Get you up from about the tabernacle of Korah, Dathan, and Abiram.

24: 

[[Num.16.25|25]] And Moses rose up and went unto Dathan and Abiram; and the elders of Israel followed him.

25: 

[[Num.16.26|26]] And he spake unto the congregation, saying, Depart, I pray you, from the tents of these wicked men, and touch nothing of theirs, lest ye be consumed in all their sins.

26:
