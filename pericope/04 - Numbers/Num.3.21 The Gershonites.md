[[Num.3|Numbers 3:21 - Numbers 3:26]]
*The Gershonites*

[[Num.3.21|21]] Of Gershon \[was\] the family of the Libnites, and the family of the Shimites: these \[are\] the families of the Gershonites.

21: 

[[Num.3.22|22]] Those that were numbered of them, according to the number of all the males, from a month old and upward, \[even\] those that were numbered of them \[were\] seven thousand and five hundred.

22: 

[[Num.3.23|23]] The families of the Gershonites shall pitch behind the tabernacle westward.

23: 

[[Num.3.24|24]] And the chief of the house of the father of the Gershonites \[shall be\] Eliasaph the son of Lael.

24: 

[[Num.3.25|25]] And the charge of the sons of Gershon in the tabernacle of the congregation \[shall be\] the tabernacle, and the tent, the covering thereof, and the hanging for the door of the tabernacle of the congregation,

25: 

[[Num.3.26|26]] And the hangings of the court, and the curtain for the door of the court, which \[is\] by the tabernacle, and by the altar round about, and the cords of it for all the service thereof.
