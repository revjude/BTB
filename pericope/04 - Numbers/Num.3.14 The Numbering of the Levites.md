[[Num.3|Numbers 3:14 - Numbers 3:20]]
*The Numbering of the Levites*

[[Num.3.14|14]] And the LORD spake unto Moses in the wilderness of Sinai, saying,

14: 

[[Num.3.15|15]] Number the children of Levi after the house of their fathers, by their families: every male from a month old and upward shalt thou number them.

15: 

[[Num.3.16|16]] And Moses numbered them according to the word of the LORD, as he was commanded.

16: 

[[Num.3.17|17]] And these were the sons of Levi by their names; Gershon, and Kohath, and Merari.

17: 

[[Num.3.18|18]] And these \[are\] the names of the sons of Gershon by their families; Libni, and Shimei.

18: 

[[Num.3.19|19]] And the sons of Kohath by their families; Amram, and Izehar, Hebron, and Uzziel.

19: 

[[Num.3.20|20]] And the sons of Merari by their families; Mahli, and Mushi. These \[are\] the families of the Levites according to the house of their fathers.
