[[Num.3|Numbers 3:27 - Numbers 3:32]]
*The Kohathites*

[[Num.3.27|27]] And of Kohath \[was\] the family of the Amramites, and the family of the Izeharites, and the family of the Hebronites, and the family of the Uzzielites: these \[are\] the families of the Kohathites.

27: 

[[Num.3.28|28]] In the number of all the males, from a month old and upward, \[were\] eight thousand and six hundred, keeping the charge of the sanctuary.

28: 

[[Num.3.29|29]] The families of the sons of Kohath shall pitch on the side of the tabernacle southward.

29: 

[[Num.3.30|30]] And the chief of the house of the father of the families of the Kohathites \[shall be\] Elizaphan the son of Uzziel.

30: 

[[Num.3.31|31]] And their charge \[shall be\] the ark, and the table, and the candlestick, and the altars, and the vessels of the sanctuary wherewith they minister, and the hanging, and all the service thereof.

31: 

[[Num.3.32|32]] And Eleazar the son of Aaron the priest \[shall be\] chief over the chief of the Levites, \[and have\] the oversight of them that keep the charge of the sanctuary.
