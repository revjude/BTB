[[Num.3|Numbers 3:33 - Numbers 3:37]]
*The Merarites*

[[Num.3.33|33]] Of Merari \[was\] the family of the Mahlites, and the family of the Mushites: these \[are\] the families of Merari.

33: 

[[Num.3.34|34]] And those that were numbered of them, according to the number of all the males, from a month old and upward, \[were\] six thousand and two hundred.

34: 

[[Num.3.35|35]] And the chief of the house of the father of the families of Merari \[was\] Zuriel the son of Abihail: \[these\] shall pitch on the side of the tabernacle northward.

35: 

[[Num.3.36|36]] And \[under\] the custody and charge of the sons of Merari \[shall be\] the boards of the tabernacle, and the bars thereof, and the pillars thereof, and the sockets thereof, and all the vessels thereof, and all that serveth thereto,

36: 

[[Num.3.37|37]] And the pillars of the court round about, and their sockets, and their pins, and their cords.
