[[Num.26|Numbers 26:5 - Numbers 26:11]]
*The Tribe of Reuben*

[[Num.26.5|5]] Reuben, the eldest son of Israel: the children of Reuben; Hanoch, \[of whom cometh\] the family of the Hanochites: of Pallu, the family of the Palluites:

5: 

[[Num.26.6|6]] Of Hezron, the family of the Hezronites: of Carmi, the family of the Carmites.

6: 

[[Num.26.7|7]] These \[are\] the families of the Reubenites: and they that were numbered of them were forty and three thousand and seven hundred and thirty.

7: 

[[Num.26.8|8]] And the sons of Pallu; Eliab.

8: 

[[Num.26.9|9]] And the sons of Eliab; Nemuel, and Dathan, and Abiram. This \[is that\] Dathan and Abiram, \[which were\] famous in the congregation, who strove against Moses and against Aaron in the company of Korah, when they strove against the LORD:

9: 

[[Num.26.10|10]] And the earth opened her mouth, and swallowed them up together with Korah, when that company died, what time the fire devoured two hundred and fifty men: and they became a sign.

10: 

[[Num.26.11|11]] Notwithstanding the children of Korah died not.

11:
