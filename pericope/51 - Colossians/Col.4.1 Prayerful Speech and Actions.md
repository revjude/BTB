[[Col.4|Colossians 4:1 - Colossians 4:6]]
*Prayerful Speech and Actions*

[[Col.4.1|1]] Masters, give unto \[your\] servants that which is just and equal; knowing that ye also have a Master in heaven.

1: 

[[Col.4.2|2]] Continue in prayer, and watch in the same with thanksgiving;

2: 

[[Col.4.3|3]] Withal praying also for us, that God would open unto us a door of utterance, to speak the mystery of Christ, for which I am also in bonds:

3: 

[[Col.4.4|4]] That I may make it manifest, as I ought to speak.

4: 

[[Col.4.5|5]] Walk in wisdom toward them that are without, redeeming the time.

5: 

[[Col.4.6|6]] Let your speech \[be\] alway with grace, seasoned with salt, that ye may know how ye ought to answer every man.
