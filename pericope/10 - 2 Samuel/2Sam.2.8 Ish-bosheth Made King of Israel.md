*Ish-bosheth Made King of Israel*

[[2Sam.2.8|8]] But Abner the son of Ner, captain of Saul’s host, took Ishbosheth the son of Saul, and brought him over to Mahanaim;

8: 

[[2Sam.2.9|9]] And made him king over Gilead, and over the Ashurites, and over Jezreel, and over Ephraim, and over Benjamin, and over all Israel.

9:

10:
