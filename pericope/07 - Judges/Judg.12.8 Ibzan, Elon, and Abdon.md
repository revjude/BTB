[[Judg.12|Judges 12:8 - Judges 12:15]]
*Ibzan, Elon, and Abdon*

[[Judg.12.8|8]] And after him Ibzan of Bethlehem judged Israel.

8: 

[[Judg.12.9|9]] And he had thirty sons, and thirty daughters, \[whom\] he sent abroad, and took in thirty daughters from abroad for his sons. And he judged Israel seven years.

9: 

[[Judg.12.10|10]] Then died Ibzan, and was buried at Bethlehem.

10: 

[[Judg.12.11|11]] And after him Elon, a Zebulonite, judged Israel; and he judged Israel ten years.

11: 

[[Judg.12.12|12]] And Elon the Zebulonite died, and was buried in Aijalon in the country of Zebulun.

12: 

[[Judg.12.13|13]] And after him Abdon the son of Hillel, a Pirathonite, judged Israel.

13: 

[[Judg.12.14|14]] And he had forty sons and thirty nephews, that rode on threescore and ten ass colts: and he judged Israel eight years.

14: 

[[Judg.12.15|15]] And Abdon the son of Hillel the Pirathonite died, and was buried in Pirathon in the land of Ephraim, in the mount of the Amalekites.
