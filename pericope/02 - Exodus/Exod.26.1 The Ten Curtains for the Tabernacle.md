[[Exod.26|Exodus 26:1 - Exodus 26:6]]
*The Ten Curtains for the Tabernacle*

[[Exod.26.1|1]] Moreover thou shalt make the tabernacle \[with\] ten curtains \[of\] fine twined linen, and blue, and purple, and scarlet: \[with\] cherubims of cunning work shalt thou make them.

1: 

[[Exod.26.2|2]] The length of one curtain \[shall be\] eight and twenty cubits, and the breadth of one curtain four cubits: and every one of the curtains shall have one measure.

2: 

[[Exod.26.3|3]] The five curtains shall be coupled together one to another; and \[other\] five curtains \[shall be\] coupled one to another.

3: 

[[Exod.26.4|4]] And thou shalt make loops of blue upon the edge of the one curtain from the selvedge in the coupling; and likewise shalt thou make in the uttermost edge of \[another\] curtain, in the coupling of the second.

4: 

[[Exod.26.5|5]] Fifty loops shalt thou make in the one curtain, and fifty loops shalt thou make in the edge of the curtain that \[is\] in the coupling of the second; that the loops may take hold one of another.

5: 

[[Exod.26.6|6]] And thou shalt make fifty taches of gold, and couple the curtains together with the taches: and it shall be one tabernacle.

6:
