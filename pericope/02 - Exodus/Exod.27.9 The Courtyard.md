[[Exod.27|Exodus 27:9 - Exodus 27:19]]
*The Courtyard*

[[Exod.27.9|9]] And thou shalt make the court of the tabernacle: for the south side southward \[there shall be\] hangings for the court \[of\] fine twined linen of an hundred cubits long for one side:

9: 

[[Exod.27.10|10]] And the twenty pillars thereof and their twenty sockets \[shall be of\] brass; the hooks of the pillars and their fillets \[shall be of\] silver.

10: 

[[Exod.27.11|11]] And likewise for the north side in length \[there shall be\] hangings of an hundred \[cubits\] long, and his twenty pillars and their twenty sockets \[of\] brass; the hooks of the pillars and their fillets \[of\] silver.

11: 

[[Exod.27.12|12]] And \[for\] the breadth of the court on the west side \[shall be\] hangings of fifty cubits: their pillars ten, and their sockets ten.

12: 

[[Exod.27.13|13]] And the breadth of the court on the east side eastward \[shall be\] fifty cubits.

13: 

[[Exod.27.14|14]] The hangings of one side \[of the gate shall be\] fifteen cubits: their pillars three, and their sockets three.

14: 

[[Exod.27.15|15]] And on the other side \[shall be\] hangings fifteen \[cubits\]: their pillars three, and their sockets three.

15: 

[[Exod.27.16|16]] And for the gate of the court \[shall be\] an hanging of twenty cubits, \[of\] blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine twined linen, wrought with needlework: \[and\] their pillars \[shall be\] four, and their sockets four.

16: 

[[Exod.27.17|17]] All the pillars round about the court \[shall be\] filleted with silver; their hooks \[shall be of\] silver, and their sockets \[of\] brass.

17: 

[[Exod.27.18|18]] The length of the court \[shall be\] an hundred cubits, and the breadth fifty every where, and the height five cubits \[of\] fine twined linen, and their sockets \[of\] brass.

18: 

[[Exod.27.19|19]] All the vessels of the tabernacle in all the service thereof, and all the pins thereof, and all the pins of the court, \[shall be of\] brass.

19:
