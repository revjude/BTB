[[Exod.25|Exodus 25:1 - Exodus 25:9]]
*Offerings for the Tabernacle*

[[Exod.25.1|1]] And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,

1: 

[[Exod.25.2|2]] Speak unto the children of Israel, that they bring me an offering: of every man that giveth it willingly with his heart ye shall take my offering.

2: 

[[Exod.25.3|3]] And this \[is\] the offering which ye shall take of them; gold, and silver, and brass,

3: 

[[Exod.25.4|4]] And blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine linen, and goats’ \[hair\],

4: 

[[Exod.25.5|5]] And rams’ skins dyed red, and badgers’ skins, and shittim wood,

5: 

[[Exod.25.6|6]] Oil for the light, spices for anointing oil, and for sweet incense,

6: 

[[Exod.25.7|7]] Onyx stones, and stones to be set in the ephod, and in the breastplate.

7: 

[[Exod.25.8|8]] And let them make me a sanctuary; that I may dwell among them.

8: 

[[Exod.25.9|9]] According to all that I shew thee, \[after\] the pattern of the tabernacle, and the pattern of all the instruments thereof, even so shall ye make \[it\].
