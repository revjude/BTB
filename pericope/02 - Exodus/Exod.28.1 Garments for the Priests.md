[[Exod.28|Exodus 28:1 - Exodus 28:5]]
*Garments for the Priests*

[[Exod.28.1|1]] And take thou unto thee Aaron thy brother, and his sons with him, from among the children of Israel, that he may minister unto me in the priest’s office, \[even\] Aaron, Nadab and Abihu, Eleazar and Ithamar, Aaron’s sons.

1: 

[[Exod.28.2|2]] And thou shalt make holy garments for Aaron thy brother for glory and for beauty.

2: 

[[Exod.28.3|3]] And thou shalt speak unto all \[that are\] wise hearted, whom I have filled with the spirit of wisdom, that they may make Aaron’s garments to consecrate him, that he may minister unto me in the priest’s office.

3: 

[[Exod.28.4|4]] And these \[are\] the garments which they shall make; a breastplate, and an ephod, and a robe, and a broidered coat, a mitre, and a girdle: and they shall make holy garments for Aaron thy brother, and his sons, that he may minister unto me in the priest’s office.

4: 

[[Exod.28.5|5]] And they shall take gold, and blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine linen.
