[[Exod.38|Exodus 38:1 - Exodus 38:7]]
*The Bronze Altar*

[[Exod.38.1|1]] And he made the altar of burnt offering \[of\] shittim wood: five cubits \[was\] the length thereof, and five cubits the breadth thereof; \[it was\] foursquare; and three cubits the height thereof.

1: 

[[Exod.38.2|2]] And he made the horns thereof on the four corners of it; the horns thereof were of the same: and he overlaid it with brass.

2: 

[[Exod.38.3|3]] And he made all the vessels of the altar, the pots, and the shovels, and the basons, \[and\] the fleshhooks, and the firepans: all the vessels thereof made he \[of\] brass.

3: 

[[Exod.38.4|4]] And he made for the altar a brasen grate of network under the compass thereof beneath unto the midst of it.

4: 

[[Exod.38.5|5]] And he cast four rings for the four ends of the grate of brass, \[to be\] places for the staves.

5: 

[[Exod.38.6|6]] And he made the staves \[of\] shittim wood, and overlaid them with brass.

6: 

[[Exod.38.7|7]] And he put the staves into the rings on the sides of the altar, to bear it withal; he made the altar hollow with boards.
