*Laws of Social Responsibility*

16: 

[[Exod.22.17|17]] If her father utterly refuse to give her unto him, he shall pay money according to the dowry of virgins.

17: 

[[Exod.22.18|18]] Thou shalt not suffer a witch to live.

18: 

[[Exod.22.19|19]] Whosoever lieth with a beast shall surely be put to death.

19: 

[[Exod.22.20|20]] He that sacrificeth unto \[any\] god, save unto the LORD only, he shall be utterly destroyed.

20: 

[[Exod.22.21|21]] Thou shalt neither vex a stranger, nor oppress him: for ye were strangers in the land of Egypt.

21: 

[[Exod.22.22|22]] Ye shall not afflict any widow, or fatherless child.

[[Exod.22.23|23]] If thou afflict them in any wise, and they cry at all unto me, I will surely hear their cry;

23: 

[[Exod.22.24|24]] And my wrath shall wax hot, and I will kill you with the sword; and your wives shall be widows, and your children fatherless.

24: 

[[Exod.22.25|25]] If thou lend money to \[any of\] my people \[that is\] poor by thee, thou shalt not be to him as an usurer, neither shalt thou lay upon him usury.
