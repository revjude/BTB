*Paul Ministers in Corinth*

[[Acts.18.1|1]] After these things Paul departed from Athens, and came to Corinth;

1: 

[[Acts.18.2|2]] And found a certain Jew named Aquila, born in Pontus, lately come from Italy, with his wife Priscilla; (because that Claudius had commanded all Jews to depart from Rome:) and came unto them.

2: 

[[Acts.18.3|3]] And because he was of the same craft, he abode with them, and wrought: for by their occupation they were tentmakers.

4:
