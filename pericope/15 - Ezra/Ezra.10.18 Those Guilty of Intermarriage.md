[[Ezra.10|Ezra 10:18 - Ezra 10:44]]
*Those Guilty of Intermarriage*

[[Ezra.10.18|18]] And among the sons of the priests there were found that had taken strange wives: \[namely\], of the sons of Jeshua the son of Jozadak, and his brethren; Maaseiah, and Eliezer, and Jarib, and Gedaliah.

18: 

[[Ezra.10.19|19]] And they gave their hands that they would put away their wives; and \[being\] guilty, \[they offered\] a ram of the flock for their trespass.

19: 

[[Ezra.10.20|20]] And of the sons of Immer; Hanani, and Zebadiah.

20: 

[[Ezra.10.21|21]] And of the sons of Harim; Maaseiah, and Elijah, and Shemaiah, and Jehiel, and Uzziah.

21: 

[[Ezra.10.22|22]] And of the sons of Pashur; Elioenai, Maaseiah, Ishmael, Nethaneel, Jozabad, and Elasah.

22: 

[[Ezra.10.23|23]] Also of the Levites; Jozabad, and Shimei, and Kelaiah, (the same \[is\] Kelita,) Pethahiah, Judah, and Eliezer.

23: 

[[Ezra.10.24|24]] Of the singers also; Eliashib: and of the porters; Shallum, and Telem, and Uri.

24: 

[[Ezra.10.25|25]] Moreover of Israel: of the sons of Parosh; Ramiah, and Jeziah, and Malchiah, and Miamin, and Eleazar, and Malchijah, and Benaiah.

25: 

[[Ezra.10.26|26]] And of the sons of Elam; Mattaniah, Zechariah, and Jehiel, and Abdi, and Jeremoth, and Eliah.

26: 

[[Ezra.10.27|27]] And of the sons of Zattu; Elioenai, Eliashib, Mattaniah, and Jeremoth, and Zabad, and Aziza.

27: 

[[Ezra.10.28|28]] Of the sons also of Bebai; Jehohanan, Hananiah, Zabbai, \[and\] Athlai.

28: 

[[Ezra.10.29|29]] And of the sons of Bani; Meshullam, Malluch, and Adaiah, Jashub, and Sheal, and Ramoth.

29: 

[[Ezra.10.30|30]] And of the sons of Pahathmoab; Adna, and Chelal, Benaiah, Maaseiah, Mattaniah, Bezaleel, and Binnui, and Manasseh.

30: 

[[Ezra.10.31|31]] And \[of\] the sons of Harim; Eliezer, Ishijah, Malchiah, Shemaiah, Shimeon,

31: 

[[Ezra.10.32|32]] Benjamin, Malluch, \[and\] Shemariah.

32: 

[[Ezra.10.33|33]] Of the sons of Hashum; Mattenai, Mattathah, Zabad, Eliphelet, Jeremai, Manasseh, \[and\] Shimei.

33: 

[[Ezra.10.34|34]] Of the sons of Bani; Maadai, Amram, and Uel,

34: 

[[Ezra.10.35|35]] Benaiah, Bedeiah, Chelluh,

35: 

[[Ezra.10.36|36]] Vaniah, Meremoth, Eliashib,

36: 

[[Ezra.10.37|37]] Mattaniah, Mattenai, and Jaasau,

37: 

[[Ezra.10.38|38]] And Bani, and Binnui, Shimei,

38: 

[[Ezra.10.39|39]] And Shelemiah, and Nathan, and Adaiah,

39: 

[[Ezra.10.40|40]] Machnadebai, Shashai, Sharai,

40: 

[[Ezra.10.41|41]] Azareel, and Shelemiah, Shemariah,

41: 

[[Ezra.10.42|42]] Shallum, Amariah, \[and\] Joseph.

42: 

[[Ezra.10.43|43]] Of the sons of Nebo; Jeiel, Mattithiah, Zabad, Zebina, Jadau, and Joel, Benaiah.

43: 

[[Ezra.10.44|44]] All these had taken strange wives: and \[some\] of them had wives by whom they had children.
