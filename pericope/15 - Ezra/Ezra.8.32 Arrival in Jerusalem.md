[[Ezra.8|Ezra 8:32 - Ezra 8:36]]
*Arrival in Jerusalem*

[[Ezra.8.32|32]] And we came to Jerusalem, and abode there three days.

32: 

[[Ezra.8.33|33]] Now on the fourth day was the silver and the gold and the vessels weighed in the house of our God by the hand of Meremoth the son of Uriah the priest; and with him \[was\] Eleazar the son of Phinehas; and with them \[was\] Jozabad the son of Jeshua, and Noadiah the son of Binnui, Levites;

33: 

[[Ezra.8.34|34]] By number \[and\] by weight of every one: and all the weight was written at that time.

34: 

[[Ezra.8.35|35]] \[Also\] the children of those that had been carried away, which were come out of the captivity, offered burnt offerings unto the God of Israel, twelve bullocks for all Israel, ninety and six rams, seventy and seven lambs, twelve he goats \[for\] a sin offering: all \[this was\] a burnt offering unto the LORD.

35: 

[[Ezra.8.36|36]] And they delivered the king’s commissions unto the king’s lieutenants, and to the governors on this side the river: and they furthered the people, and the house of God.
