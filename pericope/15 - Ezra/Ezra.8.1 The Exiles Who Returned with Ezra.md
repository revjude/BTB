[[Ezra.8|Ezra 8:1 - Ezra 8:14]]
*The Exiles Who Returned with Ezra*

[[Ezra.8.1|1]] These \[are\] now the chief of their fathers, and \[this is\] the genealogy of them that went up with me from Babylon, in the reign of Artaxerxes the king.

1: 

[[Ezra.8.2|2]] Of the sons of Phinehas; Gershom: of the sons of Ithamar; Daniel: of the sons of David; Hattush.

2: 

[[Ezra.8.3|3]] Of the sons of Shechaniah, of the sons of Pharosh; Zechariah: and with him were reckoned by genealogy of the males an hundred and fifty.

3: 

[[Ezra.8.4|4]] Of the sons of Pahathmoab; Elihoenai the son of Zerahiah, and with him two hundred males.

4: 

[[Ezra.8.5|5]] Of the sons of Shechaniah; the son of Jahaziel, and with him three hundred males.

5: 

[[Ezra.8.6|6]] Of the sons also of Adin; Ebed the son of Jonathan, and with him fifty males.

6: 

[[Ezra.8.7|7]] And of the sons of Elam; Jeshaiah the son of Athaliah, and with him seventy males.

7: 

[[Ezra.8.8|8]] And of the sons of Shephatiah; Zebadiah the son of Michael, and with him fourscore males.

8: 

[[Ezra.8.9|9]] Of the sons of Joab; Obadiah the son of Jehiel, and with him two hundred and eighteen males.

9: 

[[Ezra.8.10|10]] And of the sons of Shelomith; the son of Josiphiah, and with him an hundred and threescore males.

10: 

[[Ezra.8.11|11]] And of the sons of Bebai; Zechariah the son of Bebai, and with him twenty and eight males.

11: 

[[Ezra.8.12|12]] And of the sons of Azgad; Johanan the son of Hakkatan, and with him an hundred and ten males.

12: 

[[Ezra.8.13|13]] And of the last sons of Adonikam, whose names \[are\] these, Eliphelet, Jeiel, and Shemaiah, and with them threescore males.

13: 

[[Ezra.8.14|14]] Of the sons also of Bigvai; Uthai, and Zabbud, and with them seventy males.
