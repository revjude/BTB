17: 

[[Ezra.4.18|18]] The letter which ye sent unto us hath been plainly read before me.

[[Ezra.4.20|20]] There have been mighty kings also over Jerusalem, which have ruled over all \[countries\] beyond the river; and toll, tribute, and custom, was paid unto them.

20:

21: 

[[Ezra.4.22|22]] Take heed now that ye fail not to do this: why should damage grow to the hurt of the kings?
