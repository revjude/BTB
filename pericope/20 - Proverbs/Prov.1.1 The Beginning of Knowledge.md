[[Prov.1|Proverbs 1:1 - Proverbs 1:7]]
*The Beginning of Knowledge*

[[Prov.1.1|1]] The proverbs of Solomon the son of David, king of Israel;

1: 

[[Prov.1.2|2]] To know wisdom and instruction; to perceive the words of understanding;

2: 

[[Prov.1.3|3]] To receive the instruction of wisdom, justice, and judgment, and equity;

3: 

[[Prov.1.4|4]] To give subtilty to the simple, to the young man knowledge and discretion.

4: 

[[Prov.1.5|5]] A wise \[man\] will hear, and will increase learning; and a man of understanding shall attain unto wise counsels:

5: 

[[Prov.1.6|6]] To understand a proverb, and the interpretation; the words of the wise, and their dark sayings.

6: 

[[Prov.1.7|7]] The fear of the LORD \[is\] the beginning of knowledge: \[but\] fools despise wisdom and instruction.
