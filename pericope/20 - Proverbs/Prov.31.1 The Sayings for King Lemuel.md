[[Prov.31|Proverbs 31:1 - Proverbs 31:9]]
*The Sayings for King Lemuel*

[[Prov.31.1|1]] The words of king Lemuel, the prophecy that his mother taught him.

1: 

[[Prov.31.2|2]] What, my son? and what, the son of my womb? and what, the son of my vows?

2: 

[[Prov.31.3|3]] Give not thy strength unto women, nor thy ways to that which destroyeth kings.

3: 

[[Prov.31.4|4]] \[It is\] not for kings, O Lemuel, \[it is\] not for kings to drink wine; nor for princes strong drink:

4: 

[[Prov.31.5|5]] Lest they drink, and forget the law, and pervert the judgment of any of the afflicted.

5: 

[[Prov.31.6|6]] Give strong drink unto him that is ready to perish, and wine unto those that be of heavy hearts.

6: 

[[Prov.31.7|7]] Let him drink, and forget his poverty, and remember his misery no more.

7: 

[[Prov.31.8|8]] Open thy mouth for the dumb in the cause of all such as are appointed to destruction.

8: 

[[Prov.31.9|9]] Open thy mouth, judge righteously, and plead the cause of the poor and needy.
