[[Heb.10|Hebrews 10:19 - Hebrews 10:39]]
*A Call to Persevere*

[[Heb.10.19|19]] Having therefore, brethren, boldness to enter into the holiest by the blood of Jesus,

19: 

[[Heb.10.20|20]] By a new and living way, which he hath consecrated for us, through the veil, that is to say, his flesh;

20: 

[[Heb.10.21|21]] And \[having\] an high priest over the house of God;

21: 

[[Heb.10.22|22]] Let us draw near with a true heart in full assurance of faith, having our hearts sprinkled from an evil conscience, and our bodies washed with pure water.

22: 

[[Heb.10.23|23]] Let us hold fast the profession of \[our\] faith without wavering; (for he \[is\] faithful that promised;)

23: 

[[Heb.10.24|24]] And let us consider one another to provoke unto love and to good works:

24: 

[[Heb.10.25|25]] Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some \[is\]; but exhorting \[one another\]: and so much the more, as ye see the day approaching.

25: 

[[Heb.10.26|26]] For if we sin wilfully after that we have received the knowledge of the truth, there remaineth no more sacrifice for sins,

26: 

[[Heb.10.27|27]] But a certain fearful looking for of judgment and fiery indignation, which shall devour the adversaries.

27: 

[[Heb.10.28|28]] He that despised Mosesâ€™ law died without mercy under two or three witnesses:

28: 

[[Heb.10.29|29]] Of how much sorer punishment, suppose ye, shall he be thought worthy, who hath trodden under foot the Son of God, and hath counted the blood of the covenant, wherewith he was sanctified, an unholy thing, and hath done despite unto the Spirit of grace?

29: 

[[Heb.10.30|30]] For we know him that hath said, Vengeance \[belongeth\] unto me, I will recompense, saith the Lord. And again, The Lord shall judge his people.

30: 

[[Heb.10.31|31]] \[It is\] a fearful thing to fall into the hands of the living God.

31: 

[[Heb.10.32|32]] But call to remembrance the former days, in which, after ye were illuminated, ye endured a great fight of afflictions;

32: 

[[Heb.10.33|33]] Partly, whilst ye were made a gazingstock both by reproaches and afflictions; and partly, whilst ye became companions of them that were so used.

33: 

[[Heb.10.34|34]] For ye had compassion of me in my bonds, and took joyfully the spoiling of your goods, knowing in yourselves that ye have in heaven a better and an enduring substance.

34: 

[[Heb.10.35|35]] Cast not away therefore your confidence, which hath great recompence of reward.

35: 

[[Heb.10.36|36]] For ye have need of patience, that, after ye have done the will of God, ye might receive the promise.

36: 

[[Heb.10.37|37]] For yet a little while, and he that shall come will come, and will not tarry.

37: 

[[Heb.10.38|38]] Now the just shall live by faith: but if \[any man\] draw back, my soul shall have no pleasure in him.

38: 

[[Heb.10.39|39]] But we are not of them who draw back unto perdition; but of them that believe to the saving of the soul.

39:
