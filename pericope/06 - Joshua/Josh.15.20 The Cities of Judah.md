[[Josh.15|Joshua 15:20 - Joshua 15:63]]
*The Cities of Judah*

[[Josh.15.20|20]] This \[is\] the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Judah according to their families.

20: 

[[Josh.15.21|21]] And the uttermost cities of the tribe of the children of Judah toward the coast of Edom southward were Kabzeel, and Eder, and Jagur,

21: 

[[Josh.15.22|22]] And Kinah, and Dimonah, and Adadah,

22: 

[[Josh.15.23|23]] And Kedesh, and Hazor, and Ithnan,

23: 

[[Josh.15.24|24]] Ziph, and Telem, and Bealoth,

24: 

[[Josh.15.25|25]] And Hazor, Hadattah, and Kerioth, \[and\] Hezron, which \[is\] Hazor,

25: 

[[Josh.15.26|26]] Amam, and Shema, and Moladah,

26: 

[[Josh.15.27|27]] And Hazargaddah, and Heshmon, and Bethpalet,

27: 

[[Josh.15.28|28]] And Hazarshual, and Beersheba, and Bizjothjah,

28: 

[[Josh.15.29|29]] Baalah, and Iim, and Azem,

29: 

[[Josh.15.30|30]] And Eltolad, and Chesil, and Hormah,

30: 

[[Josh.15.31|31]] And Ziklag, and Madmannah, and Sansannah,

31: 

[[Josh.15.32|32]] And Lebaoth, and Shilhim, and Ain, and Rimmon: all the cities \[are\] twenty and nine, with their villages:

32: 

[[Josh.15.33|33]] \[And\] in the valley, Eshtaol, and Zoreah, and Ashnah,

33: 

[[Josh.15.34|34]] And Zanoah, and Engannim, Tappuah, and Enam,

34: 

[[Josh.15.35|35]] Jarmuth, and Adullam, Socoh, and Azekah,

35: 

[[Josh.15.36|36]] And Sharaim, and Adithaim, and Gederah, and Gederothaim; fourteen cities with their villages:

36: 

[[Josh.15.37|37]] Zenan, and Hadashah, and Migdalgad,

37: 

[[Josh.15.38|38]] And Dilean, and Mizpeh, and Joktheel,

38: 

[[Josh.15.39|39]] Lachish, and Bozkath, and Eglon,

39: 

[[Josh.15.40|40]] And Cabbon, and Lahmam, and Kithlish,

40: 

[[Josh.15.41|41]] And Gederoth, Bethdagon, and Naamah, and Makkedah; sixteen cities with their villages:

41: 

[[Josh.15.42|42]] Libnah, and Ether, and Ashan,

42: 

[[Josh.15.43|43]] And Jiphtah, and Ashnah, and Nezib,

43: 

[[Josh.15.44|44]] And Keilah, and Achzib, and Mareshah; nine cities with their villages:

44: 

[[Josh.15.45|45]] Ekron, with her towns and her villages:

45: 

[[Josh.15.46|46]] From Ekron even unto the sea, all that \[lay\] near Ashdod, with their villages:

46: 

[[Josh.15.47|47]] Ashdod with her towns and her villages, Gaza with her towns and her villages, unto the river of Egypt, and the great sea, and the border \[thereof\]:

47: 

[[Josh.15.48|48]] And in the mountains, Shamir, and Jattir, and Socoh,

48: 

[[Josh.15.49|49]] And Dannah, and Kirjathsannah, which \[is\] Debir,

49: 

[[Josh.15.50|50]] And Anab, and Eshtemoh, and Anim,

50: 

[[Josh.15.51|51]] And Goshen, and Holon, and Giloh; eleven cities with their villages:

51: 

[[Josh.15.52|52]] Arab, and Dumah, and Eshean,

52: 

[[Josh.15.53|53]] And Janum, and Bethtappuah, and Aphekah,

53: 

[[Josh.15.54|54]] And Humtah, and Kirjatharba, which \[is\] Hebron, and Zior; nine cities with their villages:

54: 

[[Josh.15.55|55]] Maon, Carmel, and Ziph, and Juttah,

55: 

[[Josh.15.56|56]] And Jezreel, and Jokdeam, and Zanoah,

56: 

[[Josh.15.57|57]] Cain, Gibeah, and Timnah; ten cities with their villages:

57: 

[[Josh.15.58|58]] Halhul, Bethzur, and Gedor,

58: 

[[Josh.15.59|59]] And Maarath, and Bethanoth, and Eltekon; six cities with their villages:

59: 

[[Josh.15.60|60]] Kirjathbaal, which \[is\] Kirjathjearim, and Rabbah; two cities with their villages:

60: 

[[Josh.15.61|61]] In the wilderness, Betharabah, Middin, and Secacah,

61: 

[[Josh.15.62|62]] And Nibshan, and the city of Salt, and Engedi; six cities with their villages.

62: 

[[Josh.15.63|63]] As for the Jebusites the inhabitants of Jerusalem, the children of Judah could not drive them out: but the Jebusites dwell with the children of Judah at Jerusalem unto this day.

63:
