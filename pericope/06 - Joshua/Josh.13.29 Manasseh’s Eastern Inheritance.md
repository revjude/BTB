*Manasseh’s Eastern Inheritance*

[[Josh.13.29|29]] And Moses gave \[inheritance\] unto the half tribe of Manasseh: and \[this\] was \[the possession\] of the half tribe of the children of Manasseh by their families.

29: 

[[Josh.13.30|30]] And their coast was from Mahanaim, all Bashan, all the kingdom of Og king of Bashan, and all the towns of Jair, which \[are\] in Bashan, threescore cities:

30: 

[[Josh.13.31|31]] And half Gilead, and Ashtaroth, and Edrei, cities of the kingdom of Og in Bashan, \[were pertaining\] unto the children of Machir the son of Manasseh, \[even\] to the one half of the children of Machir by their families.

31: 

[[Josh.13.32|32]] These \[are the countries\] which Moses did distribute for inheritance in the plains of Moab, on the other side Jordan, by Jericho, eastward.

32:
