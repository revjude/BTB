[[Lev.24|Leviticus 24:5 - Leviticus 24:9]]
*The Showbread*

[[Lev.24.5|5]] And thou shalt take fine flour, and bake twelve cakes thereof: two tenth deals shall be in one cake.

5: 

[[Lev.24.6|6]] And thou shalt set them in two rows, six on a row, upon the pure table before the LORD.

6: 

[[Lev.24.7|7]] And thou shalt put pure frankincense upon \[each\] row, that it may be on the bread for a memorial, \[even\] an offering made by fire unto the LORD.

7: 

[[Lev.24.8|8]] Every sabbath he shall set it in order before the LORD continually, \[being taken\] from the children of Israel by an everlasting covenant.

8: 

[[Lev.24.9|9]] And it shall be Aaron’s and his sons’; and they shall eat it in the holy place: for it \[is\] most holy unto him of the offerings of the LORD made by fire by a perpetual statute.
