*The Deliverance of Israel*

[[Obad.1.15|15]] For the day of the LORD \[is\] near upon all the heathen: as thou hast done, it shall be done unto thee: thy reward shall return upon thine own head.

15: 

[[Obad.1.16|16]] For as ye have drunk upon my holy mountain, \[so\] shall all the heathen drink continually, yea, they shall drink, and they shall swallow down, and they shall be as though they had not been.

16:

17:

18:
