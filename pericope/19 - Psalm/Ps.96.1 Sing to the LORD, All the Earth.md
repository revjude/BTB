[[Ps.96|Psalm 96:1 - Psalm 96:13]]
*Sing to the LORD, All the Earth*

[[Ps.96.1|1]] O sing unto the LORD a new song: sing unto the LORD, all the earth.

1: 

[[Ps.96.2|2]] Sing unto the LORD, bless his name; shew forth his salvation from day to day.

2: 

[[Ps.96.3|3]] Declare his glory among the heathen, his wonders among all people.

3: 

[[Ps.96.4|4]] For the LORD \[is\] great, and greatly to be praised: he \[is\] to be feared above all gods.

4: 

[[Ps.96.5|5]] For all the gods of the nations \[are\] idols: but the LORD made the heavens.

5: 

[[Ps.96.6|6]] Honour and majesty \[are\] before him: strength and beauty \[are\] in his sanctuary.

6: 

[[Ps.96.7|7]] Give unto the LORD, O ye kindreds of the people, give unto the LORD glory and strength.

7: 

[[Ps.96.8|8]] Give unto the LORD the glory \[due unto\] his name: bring an offering, and come into his courts.

8: 

[[Ps.96.9|9]] O worship the LORD in the beauty of holiness: fear before him, all the earth.

9: 

[[Ps.96.10|10]] Say among the heathen \[that\] the LORD reigneth: the world also shall be established that it shall not be moved: he shall judge the people righteously.

10: 

[[Ps.96.11|11]] Let the heavens rejoice, and let the earth be glad; let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof.

11: 

[[Ps.96.12|12]] Let the field be joyful, and all that \[is\] therein: then shall all the trees of the wood rejoice

12: 

[[Ps.96.13|13]] Before the LORD: for he cometh, for he cometh to judge the earth: he shall judge the world with righteousness, and the people with his truth.

13:
