[[Ps.97|Psalm 97:1 - Psalm 97:12]]
*Let the Earth Rejoice*

[[Ps.97.1|1]] The LORD reigneth; let the earth rejoice; let the multitude of isles be glad \[thereof\].

1: 

[[Ps.97.2|2]] Clouds and darkness \[are\] round about him: righteousness and judgment \[are\] the habitation of his throne.

2: 

[[Ps.97.3|3]] A fire goeth before him, and burneth up his enemies round about.

3: 

[[Ps.97.4|4]] His lightnings enlightened the world: the earth saw, and trembled.

4: 

[[Ps.97.5|5]] The hills melted like wax at the presence of the LORD, at the presence of the Lord of the whole earth.

5: 

[[Ps.97.6|6]] The heavens declare his righteousness, and all the people see his glory.

6: 

[[Ps.97.7|7]] Confounded be all they that serve graven images, that boast themselves of idols: worship him, all \[ye\] gods.

7: 

[[Ps.97.8|8]] Zion heard, and was glad; and the daughters of Judah rejoiced because of thy judgments, O LORD.

8: 

[[Ps.97.9|9]] For thou, LORD, \[art\] high above all the earth: thou art exalted far above all gods.

9: 

[[Ps.97.10|10]] Ye that love the LORD, hate evil: he preserveth the souls of his saints; he delivereth them out of the hand of the wicked.

10: 

[[Ps.97.11|11]] Light is sown for the righteous, and gladness for the upright in heart.

11: 

[[Ps.97.12|12]] Rejoice in the LORD, ye righteous; and give thanks at the remembrance of his holiness.

12:
