[[Ps.98|Psalm 98:1 - Psalm 98:9]]
*Sing to the LORD a New Song*

[[Ps.98.0|0]] A Psalm.

0: 

[[Ps.98.1|1]] O sing unto the LORD a new song; for he hath done marvellous things: his right hand, and his holy arm, hath gotten him the victory.

1: 

[[Ps.98.2|2]] The LORD hath made known his salvation: his righteousness hath he openly shewed in the sight of the heathen.

2: 

[[Ps.98.3|3]] He hath remembered his mercy and his truth toward the house of Israel: all the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.

3: 

[[Ps.98.4|4]] Make a joyful noise unto the LORD, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise.

4: 

[[Ps.98.5|5]] Sing unto the LORD with the harp; with the harp, and the voice of a psalm.

5: 

[[Ps.98.6|6]] With trumpets and sound of cornet make a joyful noise before the LORD, the King.

6: 

[[Ps.98.7|7]] Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof; the world, and they that dwell therein.

7: 

[[Ps.98.8|8]] Let the floods clap \[their\] hands: let the hills be joyful together

8: 

[[Ps.98.9|9]] Before the LORD; for he cometh to judge the earth: with righteousness shall he judge the world, and the people with equity.
