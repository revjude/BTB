[[Ps.136|Psalm 136:1 - Psalm 136:26]]
*His Loving Devotion Endures Forever*

[[Ps.136.1|1]] O give thanks unto the LORD; for \[he is\] good: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

1: 

[[Ps.136.2|2]] O give thanks unto the God of gods: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

2: 

[[Ps.136.3|3]] O give thanks to the Lord of lords: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

3: 

[[Ps.136.4|4]] To him who alone doeth great wonders: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

4: 

[[Ps.136.5|5]] To him that by wisdom made the heavens: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

5: 

[[Ps.136.6|6]] To him that stretched out the earth above the waters: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

6: 

[[Ps.136.7|7]] To him that made great lights: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

7: 

[[Ps.136.8|8]] The sun to rule by day: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

8: 

[[Ps.136.9|9]] The moon and stars to rule by night: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

9: 

[[Ps.136.10|10]] To him that smote Egypt in their firstborn: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

10: 

[[Ps.136.11|11]] And brought out Israel from among them: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

11: 

[[Ps.136.12|12]] With a strong hand, and with a stretched out arm: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

12: 

[[Ps.136.13|13]] To him which divided the Red sea into parts: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

13: 

[[Ps.136.14|14]] And made Israel to pass through the midst of it: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

14: 

[[Ps.136.15|15]] But overthrew Pharaoh and his host in the Red sea: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

15: 

[[Ps.136.16|16]] To him which led his people through the wilderness: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

16: 

[[Ps.136.17|17]] To him which smote great kings: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

17: 

[[Ps.136.18|18]] And slew famous kings: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

18: 

[[Ps.136.19|19]] Sihon king of the Amorites: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

19: 

[[Ps.136.20|20]] And Og the king of Bashan: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

20: 

[[Ps.136.21|21]] And gave their land for an heritage: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

21: 

[[Ps.136.22|22]] \[Even\] an heritage unto Israel his servant: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

22: 

[[Ps.136.23|23]] Who remembered us in our low estate: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever:

23: 

[[Ps.136.24|24]] And hath redeemed us from our enemies: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

24: 

[[Ps.136.25|25]] Who giveth food to all flesh: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

25: 

[[Ps.136.26|26]] O give thanks unto the God of heaven: for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

26:
