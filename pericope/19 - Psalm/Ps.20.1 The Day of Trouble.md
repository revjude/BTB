[[Ps.20|Psalm 20:1 - Psalm 20:9]]
*The Day of Trouble*

[[Ps.20.0|0]] To the chief Musician, A Psalm of David.

0: 

[[Ps.20.1|1]] The LORD hear thee in the day of trouble; the name of the God of Jacob defend thee;

1: 

[[Ps.20.2|2]] Send thee help from the sanctuary, and strengthen thee out of Zion;

2: 

[[Ps.20.3|3]] Remember all thy offerings, and accept thy burnt sacrifice; Selah.

3: 

[[Ps.20.4|4]] Grant thee according to thine own heart, and fulfil all thy counsel.

4: 

[[Ps.20.5|5]] We will rejoice in thy salvation, and in the name of our God we will set up \[our\] banners: the LORD fulfil all thy petitions.

5: 

[[Ps.20.6|6]] Now know I that the LORD saveth his anointed; he will hear him from his holy heaven with the saving strength of his right hand.

6: 

[[Ps.20.7|7]] Some \[trust\] in chariots, and some in horses: but we will remember the name of the LORD our God.

7: 

[[Ps.20.8|8]] They are brought down and fallen: but we are risen, and stand upright.

8: 

[[Ps.20.9|9]] Save, LORD: let the king hear us when we call.
