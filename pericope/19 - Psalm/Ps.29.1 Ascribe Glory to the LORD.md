[[Ps.29|Psalm 29:1 - Psalm 29:11]]
*Ascribe Glory to the LORD*

[[Ps.29.0|0]] A Psalm of David.

0: 

[[Ps.29.1|1]] Give unto the LORD, O ye mighty, give unto the LORD glory and strength.

1: 

[[Ps.29.2|2]] Give unto the LORD the glory due unto his name; worship the LORD in the beauty of holiness.

2: 

[[Ps.29.3|3]] The voice of the LORD \[is\] upon the waters: the God of glory thundereth: the LORD \[is\] upon many waters.

3: 

[[Ps.29.4|4]] The voice of the LORD \[is\] powerful; the voice of the LORD \[is\] full of majesty.

4: 

[[Ps.29.5|5]] The voice of the LORD breaketh the cedars; yea, the LORD breaketh the cedars of Lebanon.

5: 

[[Ps.29.6|6]] He maketh them also to skip like a calf; Lebanon and Sirion like a young unicorn.

6: 

[[Ps.29.7|7]] The voice of the LORD divideth the flames of fire.

7: 

[[Ps.29.8|8]] The voice of the LORD shaketh the wilderness; the LORD shaketh the wilderness of Kadesh.

8: 

[[Ps.29.9|9]] The voice of the LORD maketh the hinds to calve, and discovereth the forests: and in his temple doth every one speak of \[his\] glory.

9: 

[[Ps.29.10|10]] The LORD sitteth upon the flood; yea, the LORD sitteth King for ever.

10: 

[[Ps.29.11|11]] The LORD will give strength unto his people; the LORD will bless his people with peace.
