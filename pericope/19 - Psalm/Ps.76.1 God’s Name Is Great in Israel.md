[[Ps.76|Psalm 76:1 - Psalm 76:12]]
*God’s Name Is Great in Israel*

[[Ps.76.0|0]] To the chief Musician on Neginoth, A Psalm \[or\] Song of Asaph.

0: 

[[Ps.76.1|1]] In Judah \[is\] God known: his name \[is\] great in Israel.

1: 

[[Ps.76.2|2]] In Salem also is his tabernacle, and his dwelling place in Zion.

2: 

[[Ps.76.3|3]] There brake he the arrows of the bow, the shield, and the sword, and the battle. Selah.

3: 

[[Ps.76.4|4]] Thou \[art\] more glorious \[and\] excellent than the mountains of prey.

4: 

[[Ps.76.5|5]] The stouthearted are spoiled, they have slept their sleep: and none of the men of might have found their hands.

5: 

[[Ps.76.6|6]] At thy rebuke, O God of Jacob, both the chariot and horse are cast into a dead sleep.

6: 

[[Ps.76.7|7]] Thou, \[even\] thou, \[art\] to be feared: and who may stand in thy sight when once thou art angry?

7: 

[[Ps.76.8|8]] Thou didst cause judgment to be heard from heaven; the earth feared, and was still,

8: 

[[Ps.76.9|9]] When God arose to judgment, to save all the meek of the earth. Selah.

9: 

[[Ps.76.10|10]] Surely the wrath of man shall praise thee: the remainder of wrath shalt thou restrain.

10: 

[[Ps.76.11|11]] Vow, and pay unto the LORD your God: let all that be round about him bring presents unto him that ought to be feared.

11: 

[[Ps.76.12|12]] He shall cut off the spirit of princes: \[he is\] terrible to the kings of the earth.

12:
