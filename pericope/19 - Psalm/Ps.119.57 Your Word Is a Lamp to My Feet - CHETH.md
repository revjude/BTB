*Your Word Is a Lamp to My Feet - CHETH*

57: 

[[Ps.119.58|58]] I intreated thy favour with \[my\] whole heart: be merciful unto me according to thy word.

58: 

[[Ps.119.59|59]] I thought on my ways, and turned my feet unto thy testimonies.

59: 

[[Ps.119.60|60]] I made haste, and delayed not to keep thy commandments.

60: 

[[Ps.119.61|61]] The bands of the wicked have robbed me: \[but\] I have not forgotten thy law.

61: 

[[Ps.119.62|62]] At midnight I will rise to give thanks unto thee because of thy righteous judgments.

62: 

[[Ps.119.63|63]] I \[am\] a companion of all \[them\] that fear thee, and of them that keep thy precepts.

63:
