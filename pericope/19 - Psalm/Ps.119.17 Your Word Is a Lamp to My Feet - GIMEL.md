*Your Word Is a Lamp to My Feet - GIMEL*

17: 

[[Ps.119.18|18]] Open thou mine eyes, that I may behold wondrous things out of thy law.

18: 

[[Ps.119.19|19]] I \[am\] a stranger in the earth: hide not thy commandments from me.

19: 

[[Ps.119.20|20]] My soul breaketh for the longing \[that it hath\] unto thy judgments at all times.

20: 

[[Ps.119.21|21]] Thou hast rebuked the proud \[that are\] cursed, which do err from thy commandments.

21: 

[[Ps.119.22|22]] Remove from me reproach and contempt; for I have kept thy testimonies.

22: 

[[Ps.119.23|23]] Princes also did sit \[and\] speak against me: \[but\] thy servant did meditate in thy statutes.

23:
