*Give Praise, O Servants of the LORD*

[[Ps.135.1|1]] Praise ye the LORD. Praise ye the name of the LORD; praise \[him\], O ye servants of the LORD.

1: 

[[Ps.135.2|2]] Ye that stand in the house of the LORD, in the courts of the house of our God,

2:

3:

4: 

[[Ps.135.5|5]] For I know that the LORD \[is\] great, and \[that\] our Lord \[is\] above all gods.

5:

8:

[[Ps.135.10|10]] Who smote great nations, and slew mighty kings;

[[Ps.135.12|12]] And gave their land \[for\] an heritage, an heritage unto Israel his people.

12: 

[[Ps.135.13|13]] Thy name, O LORD, \[endureth\] for ever; \[and\] thy memorial, O LORD, throughout all generations.

13:
