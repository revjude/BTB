[[Ps.148|Psalm 148:1 - Psalm 148:14]]
*Praise the LORD from the Heavens*

[[Ps.148.1|1]] Praise ye the LORD. Praise ye the LORD from the heavens: praise him in the heights.

1: 

[[Ps.148.2|2]] Praise ye him, all his angels: praise ye him, all his hosts.

2: 

[[Ps.148.3|3]] Praise ye him, sun and moon: praise him, all ye stars of light.

3: 

[[Ps.148.4|4]] Praise him, ye heavens of heavens, and ye waters that \[be\] above the heavens.

4: 

[[Ps.148.5|5]] Let them praise the name of the LORD: for he commanded, and they were created.

5: 

[[Ps.148.6|6]] He hath also stablished them for ever and ever: he hath made a decree which shall not pass.

6: 

[[Ps.148.7|7]] Praise the LORD from the earth, ye dragons, and all deeps:

7: 

[[Ps.148.8|8]] Fire, and hail; snow, and vapour; stormy wind fulfilling his word:

8: 

[[Ps.148.9|9]] Mountains, and all hills; fruitful trees, and all cedars:

9: 

[[Ps.148.10|10]] Beasts, and all cattle; creeping things, and flying fowl:

10: 

[[Ps.148.11|11]] Kings of the earth, and all people; princes, and all judges of the earth:

11: 

[[Ps.148.12|12]] Both young men, and maidens; old men, and children:

12: 

[[Ps.148.13|13]] Let them praise the name of the LORD: for his name alone is excellent; his glory \[is\] above the earth and heaven.

13: 

[[Ps.148.14|14]] He also exalteth the horn of his people, the praise of all his saints; \[even\] of the children of Israel, a people near unto him. Praise ye the LORD.

14:
