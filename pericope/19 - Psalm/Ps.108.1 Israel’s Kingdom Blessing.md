[[Ps.108|Psalm 108:1 - Psalm 108:13]]
*Israel’s Kingdom Blessing*

[[Ps.108.0|0]] A Song \[or\] Psalm of David.

0: 

[[Ps.108.1|1]] O God, my heart is fixed; I will sing and give praise, even with my glory.

1: 

[[Ps.108.2|2]] Awake, psaltery and harp: I \[myself\] will awake early.

2: 

[[Ps.108.3|3]] I will praise thee, O LORD, among the people: and I will sing praises unto thee among the nations.

3: 

[[Ps.108.4|4]] For thy mercy \[is\] great above the heavens: and thy truth \[reacheth\] unto the clouds.

4: 

[[Ps.108.5|5]] Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: and thy glory above all the earth;

5: 

[[Ps.108.6|6]] That thy beloved may be delivered: save \[with\] thy right hand, and answer me.

6: 

[[Ps.108.7|7]] God hath spoken in his holiness; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.

7: 

[[Ps.108.8|8]] Gilead \[is\] mine; Manasseh \[is\] mine; Ephraim also \[is\] the strength of mine head; Judah \[is\] my lawgiver;

8: 

[[Ps.108.9|9]] Moab \[is\] my washpot; over Edom will I cast out my shoe; over Philistia will I triumph.

9: 

[[Ps.108.10|10]] Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into Edom?

10: 

[[Ps.108.11|11]] \[Wilt\] not \[thou\], O God, \[who\] hast cast us off? and wilt not thou, O God, go forth with our hosts?

11: 

[[Ps.108.12|12]] Give us help from trouble: for vain \[is\] the help of man.

12: 

[[Ps.108.13|13]] Through God we shall do valiantly: for he \[it is that\] shall tread down our enemies.

13:
