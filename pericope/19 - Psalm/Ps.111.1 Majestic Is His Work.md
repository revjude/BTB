[[Ps.111|Psalm 111:1 - Psalm 111:10]]
*Majestic Is His Work*

[[Ps.111.1|1]] Praise ye the LORD. I will praise the LORD with \[my\] whole heart, in the assembly of the upright, and \[in\] the congregation.

1: 

[[Ps.111.2|2]] The works of the LORD \[are\] great, sought out of all them that have pleasure therein.

2: 

[[Ps.111.3|3]] His work \[is\] honourable and glorious: and his righteousness endureth for ever.

3: 

[[Ps.111.4|4]] He hath made his wonderful works to be remembered: the LORD \[is\] gracious and full of compassion.

4: 

[[Ps.111.5|5]] He hath given meat unto them that fear him: he will ever be mindful of his covenant.

5: 

[[Ps.111.6|6]] He hath shewed his people the power of his works, that he may give them the heritage of the heathen.

6: 

[[Ps.111.7|7]] The works of his hands \[are\] verity and judgment; all his commandments \[are\] sure.

7: 

[[Ps.111.8|8]] They stand fast for ever and ever, \[and are\] done in truth and uprightness.

8: 

[[Ps.111.9|9]] He sent redemption unto his people: he hath commanded his covenant for ever: holy and reverend \[is\] his name.

9: 

[[Ps.111.10|10]] The fear of the LORD \[is\] the beginning of wisdom: a good understanding have all they that do \[his commandments\]: his praise endureth for ever.
