[[Ps.150|Psalm 150:1 - Psalm 150:6]]
*Let Everything That Has Breath Praise the LORD*

[[Ps.150.1|1]] Praise ye the LORD. Praise God in his sanctuary: praise him in the firmament of his power.

1: 

[[Ps.150.2|2]] Praise him for his mighty acts: praise him according to his excellent greatness.

2: 

[[Ps.150.3|3]] Praise him with the sound of the trumpet: praise him with the psaltery and harp.

3: 

[[Ps.150.4|4]] Praise him with the timbrel and dance: praise him with stringed instruments and organs.

4: 

[[Ps.150.5|5]] Praise him upon the loud cymbals: praise him upon the high sounding cymbals.

5: 

[[Ps.150.6|6]] Let every thing that hath breath praise the LORD. Praise ye the LORD.

6:
