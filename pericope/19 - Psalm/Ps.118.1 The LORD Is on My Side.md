*The LORD Is on My Side*

[[Ps.118.1|1]] O give thanks unto the LORD; for \[he is\] good: because his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.

1: 

[[Ps.118.2|2]] Let Israel now say, that his mercy \[endureth\] for ever.
