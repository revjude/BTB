[[Ps.149|Psalm 149:1 - Psalm 149:9]]
*Sing to the LORD a New Song*

[[Ps.149.1|1]] Praise ye the LORD. Sing unto the LORD a new song, \[and\] his praise in the congregation of saints.

1: 

[[Ps.149.2|2]] Let Israel rejoice in him that made him: let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.

2: 

[[Ps.149.3|3]] Let them praise his name in the dance: let them sing praises unto him with the timbrel and harp.

3: 

[[Ps.149.4|4]] For the LORD taketh pleasure in his people: he will beautify the meek with salvation.

4: 

[[Ps.149.5|5]] Let the saints be joyful in glory: let them sing aloud upon their beds.

5: 

[[Ps.149.6|6]] \[Let\] the high \[praises\] of God \[be\] in their mouth, and a twoedged sword in their hand;

6: 

[[Ps.149.7|7]] To execute vengeance upon the heathen, \[and\] punishments upon the people;

7: 

[[Ps.149.8|8]] To bind their kings with chains, and their nobles with fetters of iron;

8: 

[[Ps.149.9|9]] To execute upon them the judgment written: this honour have all his saints. Praise ye the LORD.
