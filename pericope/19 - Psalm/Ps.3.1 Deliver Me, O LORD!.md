*Deliver Me, O LORD!*

[[Ps.3.0|0]] A Psalm of David, when he fled from Absalom his son.

0: 

[[Ps.3.1|1]] LORD, how are they increased that trouble me! many \[are\] they that rise up against me.

1: 

[[Ps.3.2|2]] Many \[there be\] which say of my soul, \[There is\] no help for him in God. Selah.

3: 

[[Ps.3.4|4]] I cried unto the LORD with my voice, and he heard me out of his holy hill. Selah.

4:

5:

6:

7: 

[[Ps.3.8|8]] Salvation \[belongeth\] unto the LORD: thy blessing \[is\] upon thy people. Selah.
