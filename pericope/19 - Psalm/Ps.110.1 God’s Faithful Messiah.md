[[Ps.110|Psalm 110:1 - Psalm 110:7]]
*God’s Faithful Messiah*

[[Ps.110.0|0]] A Psalm of David.

0: 

[[Ps.110.1|1]] The LORD said unto my Lord, Sit thou at my right hand, until I make thine enemies thy footstool.

1: 

[[Ps.110.2|2]] The LORD shall send the rod of thy strength out of Zion: rule thou in the midst of thine enemies.

2: 

[[Ps.110.3|3]] Thy people \[shall be\] willing in the day of thy power, in the beauties of holiness from the womb of the morning: thou hast the dew of thy youth.

3: 

[[Ps.110.4|4]] The LORD hath sworn, and will not repent, Thou \[art\] a priest for ever after the order of Melchizedek.

4: 

[[Ps.110.5|5]] The Lord at thy right hand shall strike through kings in the day of his wrath.

5: 

[[Ps.110.6|6]] He shall judge among the heathen, he shall fill \[the places\] with the dead bodies; he shall wound the heads over many countries.

6: 

[[Ps.110.7|7]] He shall drink of the brook in the way: therefore shall he lift up the head.
