*The Earth Is the LORD’s*

[[Ps.24.0|0]] A Psalm of David.

0: 

[[Ps.24.1|1]] The earth \[is\] the LORD’S, and the fulness thereof; the world, and they that dwell therein.

1: 

[[Ps.24.2|2]] For he hath founded it upon the seas, and established it upon the floods.

3:

4: 

[[Ps.24.5|5]] He shall receive the blessing from the LORD, and righteousness from the God of his salvation.

5:
