[[Ps.48|Psalm 48:1 - Psalm 48:14]]
*Broken Bondage*

[[Ps.48.0|0]] A Song \[and\] Psalm for the sons of Korah.

0: 

[[Ps.48.1|1]] Great \[is\] the LORD, and greatly to be praised in the city of our God, \[in\] the mountain of his holiness.

1: 

[[Ps.48.2|2]] Beautiful for situation, the joy of the whole earth, \[is\] mount Zion, \[on\] the sides of the north, the city of the great King.

2: 

[[Ps.48.3|3]] God is known in her palaces for a refuge.

3: 

[[Ps.48.4|4]] For, lo, the kings were assembled, they passed by together.

4: 

[[Ps.48.5|5]] They saw \[it, and\] so they marvelled; they were troubled, \[and\] hasted away.

5: 

[[Ps.48.6|6]] Fear took hold upon them there, \[and\] pain, as of a woman in travail.

6: 

[[Ps.48.7|7]] Thou breakest the ships of Tarshish with an east wind.

7: 

[[Ps.48.8|8]] As we have heard, so have we seen in the city of the LORD of hosts, in the city of our God: God will establish it for ever. Selah.

8: 

[[Ps.48.9|9]] We have thought of thy lovingkindness, O God, in the midst of thy temple.

9: 

[[Ps.48.10|10]] According to thy name, O God, so \[is\] thy praise unto the ends of the earth: thy right hand is full of righteousness.

10: 

[[Ps.48.11|11]] Let mount Zion rejoice, let the daughters of Judah be glad, because of thy judgments.

11: 

[[Ps.48.12|12]] Walk about Zion, and go round about her: tell the towers thereof.

12: 

[[Ps.48.13|13]] Mark ye well her bulwarks, consider her palaces; that ye may tell \[it\] to the generation following.

13: 

[[Ps.48.14|14]] For this God \[is\] our God for ever and ever: he will be our guide \[even\] unto death.
