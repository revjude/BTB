*Residents Outside Jerusalem*

[[Neh.11.20|20]] And the residue of Israel, of the priests, \[and\] the Levites, \[were\] in all the cities of Judah, every one in his inheritance.

20: 

[[Neh.11.21|21]] But the Nethinims dwelt in Ophel: and Ziha and Gispa \[were\] over the Nethinims.

21: 

[[Neh.11.22|22]] The overseer also of the Levites at Jerusalem \[was\] Uzzi the son of Bani, the son of Hashabiah, the son of Mattaniah, the son of Micha. Of the sons of Asaph, the singers \[were\] over the business of the house of God.

22: 

[[Neh.11.23|23]] For \[it was\] the king’s commandment concerning them, that a certain portion should be for the singers, due for every day.

23: 

[[Neh.11.24|24]] And Pethahiah the son of Meshezabeel, of the children of Zerah the son of Judah, \[was\] at the king’s hand in all matters concerning the people.

24: 

[[Neh.11.25|25]] And for the villages, with their fields, \[some\] of the children of Judah dwelt at Kirjatharba, and \[in\] the villages thereof, and at Dibon, and \[in\] the villages thereof, and at Jekabzeel, and \[in\] the villages thereof,

25: 

[[Neh.11.26|26]] And at Jeshua, and at Moladah, and at Bethphelet,

26: 

[[Neh.11.27|27]] And at Hazarshual, and at Beersheba, and \[in\] the villages thereof,

27: 

[[Neh.11.28|28]] And at Ziklag, and at Mekonah, and in the villages thereof,

28: 

[[Neh.11.29|29]] And at Enrimmon, and at Zareah, and at Jarmuth,

29: 

[[Neh.11.30|30]] Zanoah, Adullam, and \[in\] their villages, at Lachish, and the fields thereof, at Azekah, and \[in\] the villages thereof. And they dwelt from Beersheba unto the valley of Hinnom.

30: 

[[Neh.11.31|31]] The children also of Benjamin from Geba \[dwelt\] at Michmash, and Aija, and Bethel, and \[in\] their villages,

[[Neh.11.32|32]] \[And\] at Anathoth, Nob, Ananiah,

34: 

[[Neh.11.35|35]] Lod, and Ono, the valley of craftsmen.
