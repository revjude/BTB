[[Neh.12|Nehemiah 12:1 - Nehemiah 12:26]]
*The Priests and Levites Who Returned*

[[Neh.12.1|1]] Now these \[are\] the priests and the Levites that went up with Zerubbabel the son of Shealtiel, and Jeshua: Seraiah, Jeremiah, Ezra,

1: 

[[Neh.12.2|2]] Amariah, Malluch, Hattush,

2: 

[[Neh.12.3|3]] Shechaniah, Rehum, Meremoth,

3: 

[[Neh.12.4|4]] Iddo, Ginnetho, Abijah,

4: 

[[Neh.12.5|5]] Miamin, Maadiah, Bilgah,

5: 

[[Neh.12.6|6]] Shemaiah, and Joiarib, Jedaiah,

6: 

[[Neh.12.7|7]] Sallu, Amok, Hilkiah, Jedaiah. These \[were\] the chief of the priests and of their brethren in the days of Jeshua.

7: 

[[Neh.12.8|8]] Moreover the Levites: Jeshua, Binnui, Kadmiel, Sherebiah, Judah, \[and\] Mattaniah, \[which was\] over the thanksgiving, he and his brethren.

8: 

[[Neh.12.9|9]] Also Bakbukiah and Unni, their brethren, \[were\] over against them in the watches.

9: 

[[Neh.12.10|10]] And Jeshua begat Joiakim, Joiakim also begat Eliashib, and Eliashib begat Joiada,

10: 

[[Neh.12.11|11]] And Joiada begat Jonathan, and Jonathan begat Jaddua.

11: 

[[Neh.12.12|12]] And in the days of Joiakim were priests, the chief of the fathers: of Seraiah, Meraiah; of Jeremiah, Hananiah;

12: 

[[Neh.12.13|13]] Of Ezra, Meshullam; of Amariah, Jehohanan;

13: 

[[Neh.12.14|14]] Of Melicu, Jonathan; of Shebaniah, Joseph;

14: 

[[Neh.12.15|15]] Of Harim, Adna; of Meraioth, Helkai;

15: 

[[Neh.12.16|16]] Of Iddo, Zechariah; of Ginnethon, Meshullam;

16: 

[[Neh.12.17|17]] Of Abijah, Zichri; of Miniamin, of Moadiah, Piltai;

17: 

[[Neh.12.18|18]] Of Bilgah, Shammua; of Shemaiah, Jehonathan;

18: 

[[Neh.12.19|19]] And of Joiarib, Mattenai; of Jedaiah, Uzzi;

19: 

[[Neh.12.20|20]] Of Sallai, Kallai; of Amok, Eber;

20: 

[[Neh.12.21|21]] Of Hilkiah, Hashabiah; of Jedaiah, Nethaneel.

21: 

[[Neh.12.22|22]] The Levites in the days of Eliashib, Joiada, and Johanan, and Jaddua, \[were\] recorded chief of the fathers: also the priests, to the reign of Darius the Persian.

22: 

[[Neh.12.23|23]] The sons of Levi, the chief of the fathers, \[were\] written in the book of the chronicles, even until the days of Johanan the son of Eliashib.

23: 

[[Neh.12.24|24]] And the chief of the Levites: Hashabiah, Sherebiah, and Jeshua the son of Kadmiel, with their brethren over against them, to praise \[and\] to give thanks, according to the commandment of David the man of God, ward over against ward.

24: 

[[Neh.12.25|25]] Mattaniah, and Bakbukiah, Obadiah, Meshullam, Talmon, Akkub, \[were\] porters keeping the ward at the thresholds of the gates.

25: 

[[Neh.12.26|26]] These \[were\] in the days of Joiakim the son of Jeshua, the son of Jozadak, and in the days of Nehemiah the governor, and of Ezra the priest, the scribe.

26:
