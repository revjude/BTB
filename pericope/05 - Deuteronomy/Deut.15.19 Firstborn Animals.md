*Firstborn Animals*

[[Deut.15.19|19]] All the firstling males that come of thy herd and of thy flock thou shalt sanctify unto the LORD thy God: thou shalt do no work with the firstling of thy bullock, nor shear the firstling of thy sheep.

19: 

[[Deut.15.20|20]] Thou shalt eat \[it\] before the LORD thy God year by year in the place which the LORD shall choose, thou and thy household.

20: 

[[Deut.15.21|21]] And if there be \[any\] blemish therein, \[as if it be\] lame, or blind, \[or have\] any ill blemish, thou shalt not sacrifice it unto the LORD thy God.

21:
