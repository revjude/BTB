[[1Chr.23|1 Chronicles 23:1 - 1 Chronicles 23:6]]
*The Divisions of the Levites*

[[1Chr.23.1|1]] So when David was old and full of days, he made Solomon his son king over Israel.

1: 

[[1Chr.23.2|2]] And he gathered together all the princes of Israel, with the priests and the Levites.

2: 

[[1Chr.23.3|3]] Now the Levites were numbered from the age of thirty years and upward: and their number by their polls, man by man, was thirty and eight thousand.

3: 

[[1Chr.23.4|4]] Of which, twenty and four thousand \[were\] to set forward the work of the house of the LORD; and six thousand \[were\] officers and judges:

4: 

[[1Chr.23.5|5]] Moreover four thousand \[were\] porters; and four thousand praised the LORD with the instruments which I made, \[said David\], to praise \[therewith\].

5: 

[[1Chr.23.6|6]] And David divided them into courses among the sons of Levi, \[namely\], Gershon, Kohath, and Merari.

6:
