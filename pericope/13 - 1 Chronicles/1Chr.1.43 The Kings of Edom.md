*The Kings of Edom*

[[1Chr.1.43|43]] Now these \[are\] the kings that reigned in the land of Edom before \[any\] king reigned over the children of Israel; Bela the son of Beor: and the name of his city \[was\] Dinhabah.

43: 

[[1Chr.1.44|44]] And when Bela was dead, Jobab the son of Zerah of Bozrah reigned in his stead.

44: 

[[1Chr.1.45|45]] And when Jobab was dead, Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his stead.

45: 

[[1Chr.1.46|46]] And when Husham was dead, Hadad the son of Bedad, which smote Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in his stead: and the name of his city \[was\] Avith.

46: 

[[1Chr.1.47|47]] And when Hadad was dead, Samlah of Masrekah reigned in his stead.

47: 

[[1Chr.1.48|48]] And when Samlah was dead, Shaul of Rehoboth by the river reigned in his stead.

48: 

[[1Chr.1.49|49]] And when Shaul was dead, Baalhanan the son of Achbor reigned in his stead.
