*The Leaders of the Twelve Tribes*

[[1Chr.27.16|16]] Furthermore over the tribes of Israel: the ruler of the Reubenites \[was\] Eliezer the son of Zichri: of the Simeonites, Shephatiah the son of Maachah:

16: 

[[1Chr.27.17|17]] Of the Levites, Hashabiah the son of Kemuel: of the Aaronites, Zadok:

17: 

[[1Chr.27.18|18]] Of Judah, Elihu, \[one\] of the brethren of David: of Issachar, Omri the son of Michael:

18: 

[[1Chr.27.19|19]] Of Zebulun, Ishmaiah the son of Obadiah: of Naphtali, Jerimoth the son of Azriel:

19: 

[[1Chr.27.20|20]] Of the children of Ephraim, Hoshea the son of Azaziah: of the half tribe of Manasseh, Joel the son of Pedaiah:

20: 

[[1Chr.27.21|21]] Of the half \[tribe\] of Manasseh in Gilead, Iddo the son of Zechariah: of Benjamin, Jaasiel the son of Abner:

21: 

[[1Chr.27.22|22]] Of Dan, Azareel the son of Jeroham. These \[were\] the princes of the tribes of Israel.

22: 

[[1Chr.27.23|23]] But David took not the number of them from twenty years old and under: because the LORD had said he would increase Israel like to the stars of the heavens.

23: 

[[1Chr.27.24|24]] Joab the son of Zeruiah began to number, but he finished not, because there fell wrath for it against Israel; neither was the number put in the account of the chronicles of king David.
