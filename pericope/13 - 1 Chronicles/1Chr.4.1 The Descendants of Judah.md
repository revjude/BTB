*The Descendants of Judah*

[[1Chr.4.1|1]] The sons of Judah; Pharez, Hezron, and Carmi, and Hur, and Shobal.

1: 

[[1Chr.4.2|2]] And Reaiah the son of Shobal begat Jahath; and Jahath begat Ahumai, and Lahad. These \[are\] the families of the Zorathites.

2: 

[[1Chr.4.3|3]] And these \[were of\] the father of Etam; Jezreel, and Ishma, and Idbash: and the name of their sister \[was\] Hazelelponi:

3: 

[[1Chr.4.4|4]] And Penuel the father of Gedor, and Ezer the father of Hushah. These \[are\] the sons of Hur, the firstborn of Ephratah, the father of Bethlehem.

4: 

[[1Chr.4.5|5]] And Ashur the father of Tekoa had two wives, Helah and Naarah.

5: 

[[1Chr.4.6|6]] And Naarah bare him Ahuzam, and Hepher, and Temeni, and Haahashtari. These \[were\] the sons of Naarah.

6: 

[[1Chr.4.7|7]] And the sons of Helah \[were\], Zereth, and Jezoar, and Ethnan.

7: 

[[1Chr.4.8|8]] And Coz begat Anub, and Zobebah, and the families of Aharhel the son of Harum.
