*The Kohathites*

[[1Chr.23.12|12]] The sons of Kohath; Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel, four.

12: 

[[1Chr.23.13|13]] The sons of Amram; Aaron and Moses: and Aaron was separated, that he should sanctify the most holy things, he and his sons for ever, to burn incense before the LORD, to minister unto him, and to bless in his name for ever.

13: 

[[1Chr.23.14|14]] Now \[concerning\] Moses the man of God, his sons were named of the tribe of Levi.

14: 

[[1Chr.23.15|15]] The sons of Moses \[were\], Gershom, and Eliezer.

15: 

[[1Chr.23.16|16]] Of the sons of Gershom, Shebuel \[was\] the chief.

16: 

[[1Chr.23.17|17]] And the sons of Eliezer \[were\], Rehabiah the chief. And Eliezer had none other sons; but the sons of Rehabiah were very many.

17: 

[[1Chr.23.18|18]] Of the sons of Izhar; Shelomith the chief.

18: 

[[1Chr.23.19|19]] Of the sons of Hebron; Jeriah the first, Amariah the second, Jahaziel the third, and Jekameam the fourth.

19: 

[[1Chr.23.20|20]] Of the sons of Uzziel; Michah the first, and Jesiah the second.
