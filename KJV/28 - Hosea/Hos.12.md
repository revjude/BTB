<big><big>[[Hos.12.1 A Reproof of Ephraim, Judah, and Jacob|⥃]]</big></big>***A Reproof of Ephraim, Judah, and Jacob***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ephraim feedeth<small><small>[^2]</small></small> on wind, and followeth after the east wind: he daily<small><small>[^3]</small></small> increaseth lies and desolation; and they<small><small>[^4]</small></small> do make a covenant with the Assyrians, and oil is carried into Egypt. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath also a controversy<small><small>[^5]</small></small> with Judah, and will<small><small>[^6]</small></small> punish<small><small>[^7]</small></small> Jacob according to his ways; according to his doings<small><small>[^8]</small></small> will he recompense him. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]He took<small><small>[^9]</small></small> his brother by the heel in the womb, and by his strength he had<small><small>[^10]</small></small> power with God: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yea, he had power over the angel<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, and prevailed: he wept, and made<small><small>[^12]</small></small> supplication unto him: he found<small><small>[^13]</small></small> him \[in\] Beth–el, and there he spake<small><small>[^14]</small></small> with us; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Even<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of hosts; the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is<small><small>[^16]</small></small>\] his memorial. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore turn<small><small>[^17]</small></small> thou to thy God: keep<small><small>[^18]</small></small> mercy and judgment, and wait<small><small>[^19]</small></small> on thy God continually. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[He is\] a merchant<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, the balances<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of deceit \[are\] in his hand: he loveth<small><small>[^22]</small></small> to oppress<small><small>[^23]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Ephraim said, Yet<small><small>[^24]</small></small> I am become rich, I have<small><small>[^25]</small></small> found me out substance: \[in\] all<small><small>[^26]</small></small> my labours they<small><small>[^27]</small></small> shall find none iniquity in me that<small><small>[^28]</small></small> \[were\] sin. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I \[that<small><small>[^29]</small></small> am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> thy God from the land of Egypt will yet<small><small>[^30]</small></small> make thee to dwell in tabernacles, as in<small><small>[^31]</small></small> the days of the solemn feast. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have also<small><small>[^32]</small></small> spoken by the prophets, and I have multiplied<small><small>[^33]</small></small> visions, and used<small><small>[^34]</small></small> similitudes, by the ministry<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of the prophets. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Is there\] iniquity<small><small>[^36]</small></small> \[in\] Gilead? surely<small><small>[^37]</small></small> they are vanity: they sacrifice<small><small>[^38]</small></small> bullocks in Gilgal; yea, their altars<small><small>[^39]</small></small> \[are\] as heaps in the furrows of the fields. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jacob<small><small>[^40]</small></small> fled into the country of Syria, and Israel<small><small>[^41]</small></small> served<small><small>[^42]</small></small> for a wife, and for a wife he kept \[sheep\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And by a prophet the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> brought Israel out of Egypt, and by a prophet was he preserved.<small><small>[^43]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.12.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ephraim provoked<small><small>[^44]</small></small> \[him\] to anger most bitterly<small><small>[^45]</small></small>: therefore<small><small>[^46]</small></small> shall he leave his blood<small><small>[^47]</small></small> upon him, and his<small><small>[^48]</small></small> reproach shall his Lord return unto him. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Hos.11|<< Hosea 11]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Hos.13|Hosea 13 >>]]</td></tr></table>
