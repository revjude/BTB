***God’s Case against His People*** ^bsb1975

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, ye children of Israel: for<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath a controversy with the inhabitants of the land, because \[there is\] no truth<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, nor mercy, nor knowledge<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of God in the land.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By swearing<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, and lying, and killing, and stealing, and committing adultery, they break out, and blood<small><small>[^8]</small></small> toucheth<small><small>[^9]</small></small> blood. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore shall the land<small><small>[^10]</small></small> mourn, and every one that dwelleth therein shall languish, with the beasts<small><small>[^11]</small></small> of the field, and with the fowls of heaven; yea, the fishes of the sea also shall be taken away. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yet let<small><small>[^12]</small></small> no man strive, nor reprove another: for thy people \[are\] as<small><small>[^13]</small></small> they that strive with the priest. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore shalt thou fall in the day, and the prophet<small><small>[^14]</small></small> also shall fall with thee in the night, and I will destroy<small><small>[^15]</small></small> thy<small><small>[^16]</small></small> mother. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My people<small><small>[^17]</small></small> are destroyed<small><small>[^18]</small></small> for<small><small>[^19]</small></small> lack of knowledge: because<small><small>[^20]</small></small> thou hast rejected knowledge, I will also reject<small><small>[^21]</small></small> thee, that thou shalt be no priest to me: seeing<small><small>[^22]</small></small> thou hast forgotten the law of thy God, I will also<small><small>[^23]</small></small> forget thy children. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As they were<small><small>[^24]</small></small> increased, so they sinned against me: \[therefore<small><small>[^25]</small></small>\] will I change their glory into shame. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They eat<small><small>[^26]</small></small> up the sin of my people, and they set their heart on their iniquity<small><small>[^27]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there shall be, like people<small><small>[^28]</small></small>, like priest: and I will punish<small><small>[^29]</small></small> them for their ways, and reward them<small><small>[^30]</small></small> their doings. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they shall eat<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, and not have enough: they shall commit<small><small>[^32]</small></small> whoredom, and shall not increase: because they have left<small><small>[^33]</small></small> off to take heed to the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whoredom and wine and new wine take<small><small>[^34]</small></small> away the heart. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My people ask<small><small>[^35]</small></small> counsel at their stocks, and their staff declareth unto them: for<small><small>[^36]</small></small> the spirit of whoredoms hath caused \[them\] to err, and they have gone<small><small>[^37]</small></small> a whoring from under their God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They sacrifice<small><small>[^38]</small></small> upon the tops of the mountains, and burn incense upon the hills, under oaks and poplars and elms, because the shadow thereof \[is\] good: therefore<small><small>[^39]</small></small> your daughters shall commit whoredom, and your spouses shall commit adultery. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.4.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will not<small><small>[^40]</small></small> punish<small><small>[^41]</small></small> your daughters when they commit whoredom, nor your spouses when they commit adultery: for<small><small>[^42]</small></small> themselves are separated with whores, and they<small><small>[^43]</small></small> sacrifice with harlots: therefore<small><small>[^44]</small></small> the people \[that\] doth not understand shall fall<small><small>[^45]</small></small>.
