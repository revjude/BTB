<big><big>[[Hos.9.1 Israel’s Punishment|⥃]]</big></big>***Israel’s Punishment***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Rejoice<small><small>[^2]</small></small> not, O Israel, for joy, as<small><small>[^3]</small></small> \[other\] people: for thou hast gone<small><small>[^4]</small></small> a whoring from thy God, thou hast loved<small><small>[^5]</small></small> a reward upon<small><small>[^6]</small></small> every cornfloor. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The floor<small><small>[^7]</small></small> and the winepress<small><small>[^8]</small></small> shall not feed them, and the new wine shall fail in her. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They shall not<small><small>[^9]</small></small> dwell in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s<small><small>[^10]</small></small></span> land; but<small><small>[^11]</small></small> Ephraim shall return to Egypt, and they shall eat unclean \[things\] in Assyria<small><small>[^12]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They shall not<small><small>[^13]</small></small> offer wine \[offerings\] to the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, neither<small><small>[^14]</small></small> shall they be pleas<small><small>[^15]</small></small>ing unto him: their sacrifices \[shall be\] unto them as the bread of mourners; all that eat thereof shall be polluted: for their bread<small><small>[^16]</small></small> for their soul shall not come into the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What<small><small>[^17]</small></small> will ye do in<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the solemn day, and in the day of the feast of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, lo, they<small><small>[^19]</small></small> are gone because of destruction<small><small>[^20]</small></small>: Egypt<small><small>[^21]</small></small> shall gather them up, Memphis shall bury them: the pleasant \[places\] for their silver, nettles<small><small>[^22]</small></small> shall possess them: thorns \[shall be\] in their tabernacles. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The days of visitation<small><small>[^23]</small></small> are come, the days of recompence are come; Israel<small><small>[^24]</small></small> shall know \[it\]: the prophet<small><small>[^25]</small></small> \[is\] a fool, the spiritual man<small><small>[^26]</small></small> \[is\] mad<small><small>[^27]</small></small>, for the multitude<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of thine iniquity, and the great hatred. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The watchman<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of Ephraim \[was\] with<small><small>[^30]</small></small> my God: \[but<small><small>[^31]</small></small>\] the prophet \[is\] a snare of a fowler in all his ways, \[and\] hatred in the<small><small>[^32]</small></small> house of his God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They have deeply<small><small>[^33]</small></small> corrupted \[themselves\], as in the days of Gibeah<small><small>[^34]</small></small>: \[therefore<small><small>[^35]</small></small>\] he will remember their iniquity, he will visit their sins. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I found<small><small>[^36]</small></small> Israel like grapes<small><small>[^37]</small></small> in the wilderness; I saw your fathers as the firstripe in the fig tree at her first time: \[but<small><small>[^38]</small></small>\] they went to Baal–peor, and separated<small><small>[^39]</small></small> themselves unto \[that\] shame; and \[their<small><small>[^40]</small></small>\] abominations were according as they loved. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[As for\] Ephraim, their<small><small>[^41]</small></small> glory shall fly away like a bird, from the birth<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, and from the womb<small><small>[^43]</small></small>, and from the conception. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Though they bring up their children, yet<small><small>[^44]</small></small> will I bereave them, \[that there shall\] not<small><small>[^45]</small></small> \[be\] a man \[left\]: yea, woe<small><small>[^46]</small></small> also to them when I depart from them! <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ephraim, as<small><small>[^47]</small></small> I saw Tyrus, \[is\] planted in a pleasant place: but Ephraim shall<small><small>[^48]</small></small> bring forth his children to the murderer. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Give them, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: what<small><small>[^49]</small></small> wilt thou give? give them a miscarrying womb<small><small>[^50]</small></small> and dry breasts. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All their wickedness \[is\] in<small><small>[^51]</small></small> Gilgal: for there I hated<small><small>[^52]</small></small> them: for the wickedness of their doings I will drive<small><small>[^53]</small></small> them out of mine house, I will love them no more: all<small><small>[^54]</small></small> their princes \[are\] revolters. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ephraim is smitten, their root<small><small>[^55]</small></small> is dried up, they shall bear no fruit: yea, though they bring forth, yet will I slay \[even\] the beloved \[fruit<small><small>[^56]</small></small>\] of their womb. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.9.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My God<small><small>[^57]</small></small> will cast them away, because<small><small>[^58]</small></small> they did not hearken unto him: and<small><small>[^59]</small></small> they shall be wanderers
