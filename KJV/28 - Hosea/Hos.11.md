***Out of Egypt I Called My Son***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.2|Matthew 2:13–15]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1982

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.11.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When Israel<small><small>[^2]</small></small> \[was\] a child, then I loved him, and called<small><small>[^3]</small></small> my son out of Egypt. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.11.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[As\] they called<small><small>[^4]</small></small> them, so they went from them: they sacrificed<small><small>[^5]</small></small> unto Baalim, and burned<small><small>[^6]</small></small> incense to graven images. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.11.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I taught<small><small>[^7]</small></small> Ephraim also to go, taking them by their arms; but they knew not that I healed<small><small>[^8]</small></small> them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.11.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I drew<small><small>[^9]</small></small> them with cords of a<small><small>[^10]</small></small> man, with bands of love: and I was<small><small>[^11]</small></small> to them as they that take off<small><small>[^12]</small></small> the yoke on their jaws, and I laid<small><small>[^13]</small></small> meat unto them. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.11.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]He shall not<small><small>[^14]</small></small> return into the land of Egypt, but<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the Assyrian shall be his king, because<small><small>[^16]</small></small> they refused to return. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.11.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sword<small><small>[^17]</small></small> shall abide on his cities, and shall consume<small><small>[^18]</small></small> his branches, and devour \[them\], because<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of their own counsels. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Hos.11.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And my people are bent<small><small>[^20]</small></small> to backsliding from me: though they called them to the most High, none at all would exalt \[him<small><small>[^21]</small></small>\].

***God’s Love for Israel*** ^bsb1983
