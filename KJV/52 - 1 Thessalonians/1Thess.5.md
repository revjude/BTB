<big><big>[[1Thess.5.1 The Day of the Lord|⥃]]</big></big>***The Day of the Lord***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Zeph.1|Zephaniah 1:7–18]] <big>†</big> [[Mal.4|Malachi 4:1–6]] <big>†</big> [[2Pet.3|2 Peter 3:8–13]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But of the times<small><small>[^2]</small></small> and the seasons, brethren, ye<small><small>[^3]</small></small> have no need that I write unto you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For yourselves know<small><small>[^4]</small></small> perfectly that the day<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of the Lord so cometh as a thief in the night. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For when they shall say, Peace<small><small>[^6]</small></small> and safety; then<small><small>[^7]</small></small> sudden destruction cometh upon them, as<small><small>[^8]</small></small> travail upon a woman with child; and they<small><small>[^9]</small></small> shall not escape. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But ye, brethren, are<small><small>[^10]</small></small> not in darkness, that that day should overtake<small><small>[^11]</small></small> you as a thief. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye are all the children of light, and the children of the day: we are not of the night, nor of darkness.<small><small>[^12]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore let us not<small><small>[^13]</small></small> sleep, as \[do\] others; but let us watch<small><small>[^14]</small></small> and be sober<small><small>[^15]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they that sleep<small><small>[^16]</small></small> sleep in the night; and they<small><small>[^17]</small></small> that be drunken are drunken in the night. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But let us, who<small><small>[^18]</small></small> are of the day, be sober, putting on the breastplate<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of faith and love; and for an helmet, the hope<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of salvation. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For God hath not<small><small>[^21]</small></small> appointed us to wrath, but to obtain<small><small>[^22]</small></small> salvation by our Lord Jesus Christ, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who died<small><small>[^23]</small></small> for us, that, whether<small><small>[^24]</small></small> we wake or sleep, we should live together with him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore<small><small>[^25]</small></small> comfort<small><small>[^26]</small></small> yourselves together, and edify<small><small>[^27]</small></small> one another, even<small><small>[^28]</small></small> as also ye do.

<big><big>[[1Thess.5.12 Christian Living|⥃]]</big></big>***Christian Living***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And we beseech you, brethren, to know<small><small>[^29]</small></small> them which labour<small><small>[^30]</small></small> among you, and are<small><small>[^31]</small></small> over you in the Lord, and admonish<small><small>[^32]</small></small> you; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And to esteem<small><small>[^33]</small></small> them very highly in love for their work’s sake. \[And\] be<small><small>[^34]</small></small> at peace among yourselves. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now we exhort<small><small>[^35]</small></small> you<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, brethren, warn<small><small>[^37]</small></small> them that<small><small>[^38]</small></small> are unruly<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, comfort<small><small>[^40]</small></small> the feebleminded, support<small><small>[^41]</small></small> the weak, be<small><small>[^42]</small></small> patient toward all \[men\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> See<small><small>[^43]</small></small> that none<small><small>[^44]</small></small> render evil for evil unto any \[man\]; but ever<small><small>[^45]</small></small> follow that which is good, both among yourselves, and<small><small>[^46]</small></small> to all \[men\].  <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Rejoice<small><small>[^47]</small></small> evermore. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Pray<small><small>[^48]</small></small> without ceasing. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In every<small><small>[^49]</small></small> thing give thanks: for<small><small>[^50]</small></small> this is the will of God in Christ Jesus concerning you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Quench<small><small>[^51]</small></small> not the Spirit<small><small>[^52]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Despise not prophesyings.<small><small>[^53]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Prove<small><small>[^54]</small></small> all things; hold<small><small>[^55]</small></small> fast that which is good. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Abstain from all appearance of evil.<small><small>[^56]</small></small>

<big><big>[[1Thess.5.23 Final Blessings and Instructions|⥃]]</big></big>***Final Blessings and Instructions***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the very God<small><small>[^57]</small></small> of peace sanctify<small><small>[^58]</small></small> you wholly; and \[I pray God\] your<small><small>[^59]</small></small> whole spirit and soul and body be preserved<small><small>[^60]</small></small> blameless unto the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Faithful<small><small>[^61]</small></small> \[is\] he that calleth<small><small>[^62]</small></small> you, who<small><small>[^63]</small></small> also will do \[it\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Brethren, pray for us.<small><small>[^64]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Greet all the brethren with an holy kiss.<small><small>[^65]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I charge<small><small>[^66]</small></small> you by the Lord that<small><small>[^67]</small></small> this epistle be read unto all the holy<small><small>[^68]</small></small> brethren. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Thess.5.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ \[be\] with you.
