<big><big>[[John.7.1 Jesus Teaches at the Feast|⥃]]</big></big>***Jesus Teaches at the Feast***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> After these things Jesus walked<small><small>[^2]</small></small> in Galilee: for he would not walk in Jewry<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, because<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the Jews sought to kill him.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now the Jews’ feast of tabernacles was at hand.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> His brethren<small><small>[^7]</small></small> therefore said unto him, Depart<small><small>[^8]</small></small> hence, and go into Judea, that thy disciples also may see the works that thou doest. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For \[there<small><small>[^9]</small></small> is\] no man \[that\] doeth any thing in secret, and he himself seeketh to be known openly. If thou do these things, shew<small><small>[^10]</small></small> thyself to the world. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For neither did his brethren believe in him.<small><small>[^11]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Jesus said unto them, <span style="color:red;">My time<small><small>[^12]</small></small> is not yet come: but your time is alway ready.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">The world<small><small>[^13]</small></small> cannot hate you; but<small><small>[^14]</small></small> me it hateth, because<small><small>[^15]</small></small> I testify of it, that the works thereof are evil.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Go ye up unto this feast: I go not<small><small>[^16]</small></small> up yet unto this feast; for my time is not yet full come.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When he had said these words unto them, he abode \[still\] in Galilee. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But when his brethren were gone up, then<small><small>[^17]</small></small> went he also up unto the feast, not<small><small>[^18]</small></small> openly, but as it were in secret. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the Jews<small><small>[^19]</small></small> sought him at the feast, and said, Where is he? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there was much murmuring<small><small>[^20]</small></small> among the people concerning him: for some<small><small>[^21]</small></small> said, He is a<small><small>[^22]</small></small> good man: others said, Nay; but he deceiveth<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Howbeit no man spake<small><small>[^24]</small></small> openly of him for fear of the Jews. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now about the midst<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of the feast Jesus went up into the temple<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, and taught. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Jews marvelled<small><small>[^27]</small></small>, saying, How<small><small>[^28]</small></small> knoweth this man letters<small><small>[^29]</small></small>, having never learned? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Jesus answered them, and said, <span style="color:red;">My<small><small>[^30]</small></small> doctrine is not mine, but<small><small>[^31]</small></small> his that sent me.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">If any man will do his will, he shall know of the doctrine, whether it be of God, or \[whether\] I speak of myself.</span><small><small>[^32]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">He that speaketh<small><small>[^33]</small></small> of himself seeketh his own glory: but he that seeketh his glory<small><small>[^34]</small></small> that sent him, the same is true, and no unrighteousness is in him.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Did not<small><small>[^35]</small></small> Moses give you the law, and \[yet<small><small>[^36]</small></small>\] none of you keepeth the law? Why<small><small>[^37]</small></small> go ye about to kill me?</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The people answered and said, Thou<small><small>[^38]</small></small> hast a devil: who goeth about to kill thee? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Jesus answered and said unto them, <span style="color:red;">I have<small><small>[^39]</small></small> done one work, and ye all marvel.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Moses therefore gave unto you circumcision<small><small>[^40]</small></small>; (not because it is of Moses, but of the fathers;) and ye on the sabbath day circumcise a man.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">If a man on the sabbath day receive circumcision, that the law of Moses should not be broken<small><small>[^41]</small></small>; are ye angry at me, because I have made<small><small>[^42]</small></small> a man every whit whole on the sabbath day?</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Judge not according to the appearance, but judge righteous judgment.</span><small><small>[^43]</small></small>

Do<small><small>[^47]</small></small> the rulers know indeed that this is the very Christ? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Howbeit we know<small><small>[^48]</small></small> this man whence he is: but when Christ cometh, no man<small><small>[^49]</small></small> knoweth whence he is. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then cried Jesus in the temple as he taught, saying, <span style="color:red;">Ye both<small><small>[^50]</small></small> know me, and ye know whence I am: and I<small><small>[^51]</small></small> am not come of myself, but he that sent me is true<small><small>[^52]</small></small>, whom<small><small>[^53]</small></small> ye know not.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">But I<small><small>[^54]</small></small> know him: for<small><small>[^55]</small></small> I am from him, and he hath sent me.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then they<small><small>[^56]</small></small> sought to take him: but<small><small>[^57]</small></small> no man laid hands on him, because his hour was not yet come. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.31|31]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And many of the people believed<small><small>[^58]</small></small> on him, and said, When<small><small>[^59]</small></small> Christ cometh, will he do more miracles than these which this \[man\] hath done? 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.32|32]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The Pharisees heard<small><small>[^60]</small></small> that the people murmured such things concerning him; and the Pharisees and the chief priests sent<small><small>[^61]</small></small> officers to take him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.33|33]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said Jesus unto them, <span style="color:red;">Yet<small><small>[^62]</small></small> a little while am I with you, and \[then\] I go unto him that sent me.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.34|34]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Ye shall seek me, and shall not find \[me\]: and where I am, \[thither\] ye cannot come.</span><small><small>[^63]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.35|35]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said the Jews among themselves, Whither will he go, that we shall not find him? will he go unto the dispersed<small><small>[^64]</small></small> among the Gentiles<small><small>[^65]</small></small>, and teach<small><small>[^66]</small></small> the Gentiles? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.36|36]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What \[manner<small><small>[^67]</small></small> of\] saying is this that he said, Ye shall<small><small>[^68]</small></small> seek me, and shall not find \[me\]: and where I am, \[thither\] ye cannot come?

<big><big>[[John.7.37 Living Water|⥃]]</big></big>***Living Water***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.37|37]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the last<small><small>[^69]</small></small> day, that great \[day\] of the feast, Jesus stood and cried<small><small>[^70]</small></small>, saying, <span style="color:red;">If<small><small>[^71]</small></small> any man thirst, let<small><small>[^72]</small></small> him come unto me, and drink<small><small>[^73]</small></small>.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.38|38]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">He that<small><small>[^74]</small></small> believeth on me, as the scripture hath said, out<small><small>[^75]</small></small> of his belly shall flow rivers of living water.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.7.39|39]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> (But this spake<small><small>[^76]</small></small> he of<small><small>[^77]</small></small> the Spirit, which they that believe on him should receive: for<small><small>[^78]</small></small> the Holy Ghost was not yet \[given\]; because that Jesus was not yet glorified<small><small>[^79]</small></small>.)
