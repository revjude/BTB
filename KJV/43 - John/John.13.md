<big><big>[[John.13.1 Jesus Washes His Disciples’ Feet|⥃]]</big></big>***Jesus Washes His Disciples’ Feet***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now before the feast<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of the passover, when Jesus knew<small><small>[^3]</small></small> that his hour was come that he should depart<small><small>[^4]</small></small> out of this world unto the Father, having<small><small>[^5]</small></small> loved his own which were in the world, he loved them unto<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the end. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And supper<small><small>[^7]</small></small> being ended, the devil<small><small>[^8]</small></small> having now put<small><small>[^9]</small></small> into the heart of Judas Iscariot, Simon’s \[son\], to betray him; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Jesus knowing<small><small>[^10]</small></small> that the Father had given all things into his hands, and that<small><small>[^11]</small></small> he was come from God, and went to God; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He riseth from supper, and laid aside<small><small>[^12]</small></small> his garments; and took a towel, and girded himself. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> After that he poureth<small><small>[^13]</small></small> water into a bason, and began to wash<small><small>[^14]</small></small> the disciples’ feet<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, and to wipe \[them\] with the towel wherewith he was girded. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then cometh he to Simon Peter<small><small>[^16]</small></small>: and Peter saith unto him, Lord<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, dost thou wash my feet? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Jesus answered and said unto him, <span style="color:red;">What<small><small>[^18]</small></small> I do thou knowest not now; but thou shalt know hereafter.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Peter saith unto him, Thou shalt<small><small>[^19]</small></small> never wash my feet. Jesus answered him, <span style="color:red;">If<small><small>[^20]</small></small> I wash thee not, thou hast no part with me.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Simon Peter saith unto him, Lord, not<small><small>[^21]</small></small> my feet only, but also \[my\] hands and \[my\] head. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Jesus saith to him, <span style="color:red;">He<small><small>[^22]</small></small> that is washed needeth<small><small>[^23]</small></small> not save to wash \[his\] feet, but<small><small>[^24]</small></small> is clean every whit: and ye<small><small>[^25]</small></small> are clean, but not all.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he knew who should betray him; therefore said he, Ye are not all clean.<small><small>[^26]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So after he had washed their feet, and had taken his garments, and was set down again, he said unto them, <span style="color:red;">Know<small><small>[^27]</small></small> ye what I have done to you?</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Ye call<small><small>[^28]</small></small> me Master and<small><small>[^29]</small></small> Lord: and ye say well; for \[so\] I am.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">If I then<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, \[your\] Lord and Master, have washed your feet; ye also<small><small>[^31]</small></small> ought to wash one another’s feet.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">For I have given<small><small>[^32]</small></small> you an example, that ye should do as I have done to you.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Verily<small><small>[^33]</small></small>, verily, I say unto you, The servant<small><small>[^34]</small></small> is not greater than his lord; neither he that is sent greater than he that sent him.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">If ye know these things, happy<small><small>[^35]</small></small> are ye if ye do them.</span>

<big><big>[[John.13.18 Jesus Predicts His Betrayal|⥃]]</big></big>***Jesus Predicts His Betrayal***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Ps.41|Psalms 41:1–13]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">I speak not of you all: I know<small><small>[^36]</small></small> whom I have chosen: but<small><small>[^37]</small></small> that the scripture may be fulfilled, He that eateth bread with me hath lifted up his heel against me.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Now<small><small>[^38]</small></small> I tell<small><small>[^39]</small></small> you before it come, that, when it is come to pass, ye may believe that I<small><small>[^40]</small></small> am \[he\].</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Verily, verily, I say unto you, He<small><small>[^41]</small></small> that receiveth whomsoever I send receiveth me; and he that receiveth me receiveth him that sent me.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When Jesus had thus said, he was<small><small>[^42]</small></small> troubled in spirit, and testified, and said, <span style="color:red;">Verily, verily, I say unto you, that one<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of you shall betray me.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the disciples looked<small><small>[^44]</small></small> one on another, doubting of whom he spake. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now there was leaning<small><small>[^45]</small></small> on Jesus’ bosom one of his disciples, whom<small><small>[^46]</small></small> Jesus loved. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Simon Peter therefore beckoned<small><small>[^47]</small></small> to him, that he should ask who it should be of whom he spake. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He then lying on Jesus’ breast saith unto him, Lord, who<small><small>[^48]</small></small> is it? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Jesus answered, <span style="color:red;">He it is<small><small>[^49]</small></small>, to whom I shall give a sop<small><small>[^50]</small></small>, when I have dipped \[it\].</span> And when he had dipped the sop, he gave \[it\] to Judas Iscariot<small><small>[^51]</small></small>, \[the son\] of Simon. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And after the sop Satan<small><small>[^52]</small></small> entered into him. Then said Jesus unto him, <span style="color:red;">That<small><small>[^53]</small></small> thou doest, do quickly.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now no man at the table knew for what intent he spake this unto him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For some \[of them\] thought, because Judas had the bag, that<small><small>[^54]</small></small> Jesus had said unto him, Buy \[those things\] that we have need of against the feast; or, that he should give something to the poor. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He then having received the sop went<small><small>[^55]</small></small> immediately out: and it<small><small>[^56]</small></small> was night.

<big><big>[[John.13.31 Love One Another|⥃]]</big></big>***Love One Another***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Rom.12|Romans 12:9–13]] <big>†</big> [[1John.3|1 John 3:11–24]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.31|31]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore, when he was gone out, Jesus said, <span style="color:red;">Now<small><small>[^57]</small></small> is the Son of man glorified, and God<small><small>[^58]</small></small> is glorified in him.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.32|32]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">If God be glorified in him, God shall<small><small>[^59]</small></small> also glorify him in himself, and<small><small>[^60]</small></small> shall straightway glorify him.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.33|33]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Little<small><small>[^61]</small></small> children, ye<small><small>[^62]</small></small>t<small><small>[^63]</small></small> a little while I am with you. Ye shall seek me: and as I said unto the Jews, Whither I go, ye cannot come; so now I say to you.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.34|34]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">A new<small><small>[^64]</small></small> commandment I give unto you, That ye love<small><small>[^65]</small></small> one another; as I have loved you, that ye also love one another.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.13.35|35]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">By this shall all \[men\] know that ye are my disciples, if ye have love one to another.</span><small><small>[^66]</small></small>
