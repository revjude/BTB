<big><big>[[John.2.1 The Wedding at Cana|⥃]]</big></big>***The Wedding at Cana***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the third<small><small>[^2]</small></small> day there was a marriage<small><small>[^3]</small></small> in Cana<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of Galilee; and the mother of Jesus was there:<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And both<small><small>[^6]</small></small> Jesus was called, and his<small><small>[^7]</small></small> disciples, to the marriage<small><small>[^8]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when they wanted<small><small>[^9]</small></small> wine, the mother of Jesus saith unto him, They have<small><small>[^10]</small></small> no wine. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Jesus saith unto her, <span style="color:red;">Woman<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, what<small><small>[^12]</small></small> have I to do with thee? mine<small><small>[^13]</small></small> hour is not yet come.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> His mother saith unto the servants, Whatsoever<small><small>[^14]</small></small> he saith unto you, do \[it\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there were set there six waterpots of stone, after<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the manner of the purifying of the Jews, containing two or three firkins apiece. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Jesus saith unto them, <span style="color:red;">Fill<small><small>[^16]</small></small> the waterpots with water.</span> And they filled them up to the brim. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he saith unto them, <span style="color:red;">Draw<small><small>[^17]</small></small> out now, and bear unto the governor<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of the feast.</span> And they bare \[it\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When the ruler of the feast had tasted the water that<small><small>[^19]</small></small> was made wine, and knew not whence it was: (but<small><small>[^20]</small></small> the servants which drew the water knew;) the governor of the feast called the bridegroom, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And saith unto him, Every man at the beginning doth set forth good wine; and when<small><small>[^21]</small></small> men have well drunk, then that which is worse: \[but<small><small>[^22]</small></small>\] thou hast kept the good wine until now. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This beginning<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of miracles did<small><small>[^24]</small></small> Jesus in Cana of Galilee, and manifested<small><small>[^25]</small></small> forth his glory; and his<small><small>[^26]</small></small> disciples believed on him.

<big><big>[[John.2.12 Jesus Cleanses the Temple|⥃]]</big></big>***Jesus Cleanses the Temple***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.21|Matthew 21:12–17]] <big>†</big> [[Mark.11|Mark 11:15–19]] <big>†</big> [[Luke.19|Luke 19:45–48]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]After this he went down to Capernaum<small><small>[^27]</small></small>, he, and his mother, and his brethren<small><small>[^28]</small></small>, and his disciples: and they continued there not many days. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Jews’ passover<small><small>[^29]</small></small> was at hand, and Jesus went up to Jerusalem, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And found in the temple those that sold oxen and sheep and doves, and the changers of money sitting:<small><small>[^30]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when he had made a scourge of small cords, he drove<small><small>[^31]</small></small> them all out of the temple, and the sheep, and the oxen; and poured out the changers’ money, and overthrew the tables; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And said unto them that sold doves, <span style="color:red;">Take these things hence; make<small><small>[^32]</small></small> not my<small><small>[^33]</small></small> Father’s house an house of merchandise.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And his disciples remembered that it was written, The zeal<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of thine house hath eaten me up. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then answered the Jews and said unto him, What<small><small>[^35]</small></small> sign shewest thou unto us, seeing<small><small>[^36]</small></small> that thou doest these things? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Jesus answered and said unto them, <span style="color:red;">Destroy<small><small>[^37]</small></small> this temple, and in<small><small>[^38]</small></small> three days I will<small><small>[^39]</small></small> raise it up.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said the Jews, Forty and six years was this temple in building, and wilt thou rear it up in three days? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But he<small><small>[^40]</small></small> spake of the temple<small><small>[^41]</small></small> of his body. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When therefore he was risen from the dead, his<small><small>[^42]</small></small> disciples remembered that he had said this unto them; and they<small><small>[^43]</small></small> believed the scripture, and the word which Jesus had said. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now when he was in Jerusalem at the passover, in the feast \[day\], many<small><small>[^44]</small></small> believed in his name, when they saw the miracles which he did. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Jesus did<small><small>[^45]</small></small> not commit himself unto them, because<small><small>[^46]</small></small> he knew all \[men\], <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[John.2.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And needed not that any should testify of man: for he knew what was in man. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[John.1|<< John 1]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[John.3|John 3 >>]]</td></tr></table>
