<big><big>[[Judg.13.1 The Birth of Samson|⥃]]</big></big>***The Birth of Samson***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Num.6|Numbers 6:1–21]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the children of Israel did<small><small>[^2]</small></small> evil again in the sight<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; and the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> delivered<small><small>[^4]</small></small> them into the<small><small>[^5]</small></small> hand of the Philistines forty years. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And there was a certain man of Zorah<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, of the family of the Danites, whose name \[was\] Manoah; and his wife \[was\] barren<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, and bare not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the angel<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> appeared unto the woman, and said unto her, Behold now, thou \[art\] barren, and bearest not: but thou<small><small>[^9]</small></small> shalt conceive, and bear a son. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now therefore beware, I pray thee, and drink not<small><small>[^10]</small></small> wine nor strong drink, and eat not<small><small>[^11]</small></small> any unclean \[thing\]: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, lo, thou shalt conceive, and bear a son; and no razor<small><small>[^12]</small></small> shall come on his head: for the child shall be a Nazarite unto God from the womb: and he shall begin<small><small>[^13]</small></small> to deliver Israel out of the hand of the Philistines. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Then the woman came and told her husband, saying, A man<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of God came unto me, and his countenance \[was<small><small>[^15]</small></small>\] like the countenance of an angel of God, very terrible<small><small>[^16]</small></small>: but I asked<small><small>[^17]</small></small> him not whence he \[was\], neither told he me his name<small><small>[^18]</small></small>:<small><small>[^19]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But he said unto me, Behold, thou shalt conceive, and bear a son; and now drink no wine nor strong drink, neither eat any unclean \[thing\]: for the child shall be a Nazarite to God from the womb to the day of his death. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Manoah intreated the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and said, O my Lord, let the man of God which thou didst send come again unto us, and teach us<small><small>[^20]</small></small> what we shall do unto the child that shall be born. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God hearkened<small><small>[^21]</small></small> to the voice of Manoah; and the angel of God came again unto the woman as she sat in the field: but Manoah her husband \[was\] not with her. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the woman made haste, and ran, and shewed her husband, and said unto him, Behold<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, the man hath appeared unto me, that came unto me the \[other\] day<small><small>[^23]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Manoah arose, and went after his wife, and came to the man, and said unto him, \[Art\] thou the man that spakest unto the woman? And he said, I \[am\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Manoah said, Now let thy words come to pass. How shall we order the child<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, and \[how\] shall we do unto him<small><small>[^25]</small></small>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the angel of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Manoah, Of all that I said unto the woman let her beware. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> She may not eat of any \[thing\] that cometh of the vine, neither<small><small>[^26]</small></small> let her drink wine or strong drink, nor eat any unclean \[thing\]: all that I<small><small>[^27]</small></small> commanded her let her observe. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Manoah said unto the angel of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, I pray thee, let us<small><small>[^28]</small></small> detain thee, until<small><small>[^29]</small></small> we shall have made ready a kid for thee<small><small>[^30]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the angel of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Manoah, Though thou detain me, I will not<small><small>[^31]</small></small> eat of thy bread: and if<small><small>[^32]</small></small> thou wilt offer a burnt offering, thou must offer it unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. For Manoah knew not that he \[was\] an angel of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Manoah said unto the angel of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, What \[is\] thy name, that when thy sayings come to pass we may do thee honour? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the angel of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto him, Why askest<small><small>[^33]</small></small> thou thus after my name, seeing it \[is\] secret<small><small>[^34]</small></small>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Manoah took<small><small>[^35]</small></small> a kid with a meat offering, and offered \[it\] upon a rock unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and \[the angel\] did wondrously<small><small>[^36]</small></small>; and Manoah and his wife looked on. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For it came to pass, when the flame<small><small>[^37]</small></small> went up toward heaven from off the altar, that the angel of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> ascended in the flame of the altar. And Manoah and his wife looked on \[it\], and fell on<small><small>[^38]</small></small> their faces to the ground. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the angel of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> did no more appear to Manoah and to his wife. Then Manoah knew<small><small>[^39]</small></small> that he \[was\] an angel of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Manoah said unto his wife, We shall<small><small>[^40]</small></small> surely die, because we have<small><small>[^41]</small></small> seen God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But his wife<small><small>[^42]</small></small> said unto him, If the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> were pleased to kill us, he would not<small><small>[^43]</small></small> have received a burnt offering and a meat offering at our hands, neither would he have shewed<small><small>[^44]</small></small> us all these \[things\], nor would as at this time have told us \[such things\] as these. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the woman bare a son, and called his name Samson<small><small>[^45]</small></small>: and the child<small><small>[^46]</small></small> grew, and the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> blessed him.<small><small>[^47]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.13.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Spirit<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> began to move him at times in the camp of Dan<small><small>[^49]</small></small> between<small><small>[^50]</small></small> Zorah and Eshtaol. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Judg.12|<< Judges 12]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Judg.14|Judges 14 >>]]</td></tr></table>
