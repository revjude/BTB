***Gideon Defeats Zebah and Zalmunna*** ^bsb618

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the men<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of Ephraim said unto him, Why<small><small>[^3]</small></small> hast thou served us thus, that thou calledst us not, when thou wentest to fight with the Midianites? And they did chide with him sharply. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto them, What<small><small>[^4]</small></small> have I done now in comparison of you? \[Is\] not the<small><small>[^5]</small></small> gleaning of the grapes of Ephraim better than the vintage of Abi–ezer?<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> God<small><small>[^7]</small></small> hath delivered into your hands the princes of Midian, Oreb and Zeeb: and what was I able to do in comparison of you? Then<small><small>[^8]</small></small> their anger<small><small>[^9]</small></small> was abated toward him, when he had said that. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Gideon came to Jordan, \[and\] passed over, he, and the three hundred men that \[were\] with him, faint<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, yet pursuing \[them\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto the men of Succoth<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, Give, I pray you, loaves<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of bread unto the people that follow me; for they \[be\] faint, and I am pursuing after Zebah and Zalmunna, kings of Midian. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And the princes of Succoth said, \[Are\] the hands of Zebah and Zalmunna now in thine hand, that we should give bread unto thine army?<small><small>[^13]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Gideon said, Therefore when the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath delivered Zebah and Zalmunna into mine hand, then I will tear<small><small>[^14]</small></small> your flesh with the thorns of the wilderness and with briers. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And he went up thence to Penuel, and spake unto them likewise: and the men of Penuel answered him as the men of Succoth had answered \[him\].<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he spake also unto the men of Penuel, saying, When I come<small><small>[^16]</small></small> again in peace, I will break<small><small>[^17]</small></small> down this tower. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Now Zebah and Zalmunna \[were\] in Karkor<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, and their hosts with them, about fifteen thousand \[men\], all that were left of all the hosts of the children<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of the east: for there fell an hundred<small><small>[^20]</small></small> and twenty thousand men that drew sword. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Gideon went up by the way of them that dwelt in tents on the east of Nobah<small><small>[^21]</small></small> and Jogbehah, and smote the host: for the host was secure<small><small>[^22]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when Zebah and Zalmunna fled, he pursued after them, and took<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the two kings of Midian, Zebah and Zalmunna, and discomfited<small><small>[^24]</small></small> all the host. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Gideon the son of Joash returned from battle before<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the sun \[was up\], <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And caught<small><small>[^26]</small></small> a young man of the men of Succoth, and enquired of him: and he described<small><small>[^27]</small></small> unto him the princes of Succoth, and the elders thereof, \[even\] threescore and seventeen men. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he came unto the men of Succoth, and said, Behold Zebah and Zalmunna, with whom ye did upbraid<small><small>[^28]</small></small> me, saying, \[Are\] the hands of Zebah and Zalmunna now in thine hand, that we should give bread unto thy men \[that are\] weary? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he took the elders<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of the city, and thorns<small><small>[^30]</small></small> of the wilderness and briers, and with them he taught<small><small>[^31]</small></small> the men of Succoth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he beat down the tower of Penuel, and slew the men of the city.<small><small>[^32]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said he unto Zebah and Zalmunna, What manner of men \[were they\] whom ye slew at Tabor<small><small>[^33]</small></small>? And Gideon arose, and slew<small><small>[^38]</small></small> Zebah and Zalmunna, and took away the ornaments<small><small>[^39]</small></small> that \[were\] on their camels’ necks.

***Gideon’s Ephod*** ^bsb619

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the men of Israel said unto Gideon, Rule thou<small><small>[^40]</small></small> over us, both thou, and thy son, and thy son’s son also: for thou hast delivered us from the hand of Midian. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Judg.8.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Gideon said unto them, I will<small><small>[^41]</small></small> not rule over you, neither shall my son rule over you: the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall rule over you.<small><small>[^42]</small></small>
