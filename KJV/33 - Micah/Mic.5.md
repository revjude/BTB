<big><big>[[Mic.5.1 A Ruler from Bethlehem|⥃]]</big></big>***A Ruler from Bethlehem***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.2|Matthew 2:1–12]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now gather<small><small>[^2]</small></small> thyself in troops, O daughter of troops: he hath<small><small>[^3]</small></small> laid siege against us: they<small><small>[^4]</small></small> shall smite the judge<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of Israel with a rod upon the cheek. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But thou<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, Beth–lehem Ephratah<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, \[though\] thou be little among<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the thousands<small><small>[^9]</small></small> of Judah, \[yet<small><small>[^10]</small></small>\] out of thee shall he come forth unto me \[that is<small><small>[^11]</small></small>\] to be ruler in Israel; whose<small><small>[^12]</small></small> goings forth \[have been\] from of old, from everlasting<small><small>[^13]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore<small><small>[^14]</small></small> will he give<small><small>[^15]</small></small> them up, until the time \[that\] she<small><small>[^16]</small></small> which travaileth hath brought forth: then<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the remnant of his<small><small>[^18]</small></small> brethren shall return unto the children of Israel. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And he shall stand<small><small>[^19]</small></small> and feed<small><small>[^20]</small></small> in the strength of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, in the majesty<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^22]</small></small></span> his God; and they shall abide<small><small>[^23]</small></small>: for now shall he be great<small><small>[^24]</small></small> unto the ends of the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And this<small><small>[^25]</small></small> \[man\] shall be the peace, when the<small><small>[^26]</small></small> Assyrian shall come into our land: and when he shall tread in our palaces, then<small><small>[^27]</small></small> shall we raise against him seven<small><small>[^28]</small></small> shepherds, and eight principal men<small><small>[^29]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they<small><small>[^30]</small></small> shall waste<small><small>[^31]</small></small> the land of Assyria with the sword, and the land of Nimrod<small><small>[^32]</small></small> in the entrances thereof<small><small>[^33]</small></small>: thus shall he deliver \[us\] from the Assyrian<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, when he cometh into our land, and when he treadeth within our borders.

<big><big>[[Mic.5.7 The Remnant of Jacob|⥃]]</big></big>***The Remnant of Jacob***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Mic.2|Micah 2:12–13]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the remnant<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of Jacob shall be in the midst of many people as a dew<small><small>[^36]</small></small> from the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, as the showers upon the grass, that tarrieth<small><small>[^37]</small></small> not for man, nor waiteth for the sons of men. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And the remnant of Jacob shall be among the Gentiles in the midst of many people as a lion<small><small>[^38]</small></small> among the beasts of the forest, as a young lion among the flocks of sheep<small><small>[^39]</small></small>: who, if he go through, both treadeth down, and teareth in pieces, and none<small><small>[^40]</small></small> can deliver. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thine hand<small><small>[^41]</small></small> shall be lifted up upon thine adversaries, and all thine enemies shall be cut off. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass in that day, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, that I<small><small>[^42]</small></small> will cut<small><small>[^43]</small></small> off thy horses out of the midst of thee, and I will destroy thy chariots: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will cut off the cities of thy land, and throw<small><small>[^44]</small></small> down all thy strong holds: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will cut off witchcrafts out of thine hand; and thou shalt have no \[more\] soothsayers:<small><small>[^45]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thy graven<small><small>[^46]</small></small> images also will I cut off, and thy standing images<small><small>[^47]</small></small> out of the midst of thee; and thou shalt no<small><small>[^48]</small></small> more worship the work of thine hands. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will pluck up thy groves out of the midst of thee: so will I destroy thy cities<small><small>[^49]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.5.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will execute vengeance in anger and fury upon the heathen, such as they have not heard. <small><small>[^50]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Mic.4|<< Micah 4]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Mic.6|Micah 6 >>]]</td></tr></table>
