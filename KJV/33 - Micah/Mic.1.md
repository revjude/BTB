<big><big>[[Mic.1.1 Judgment to Come|⥃]]</big></big>***Judgment to Come***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Isa.7|Isaiah 7:17–25]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.1.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> that came to Micah<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the Morasthite in the days of Jotham<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, Ahaz, \[and\] Hezekiah, kings of Judah, which<small><small>[^4]</small></small> he saw concerning<small><small>[^5]</small></small> Samaria and Jerusalem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.1.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear, all ye people<small><small>[^6]</small></small>; hearken<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, O earth, and all that therein is<small><small>[^8]</small></small>: and let<small><small>[^9]</small></small> the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span> be witness against you, the Lord from<small><small>[^10]</small></small> his holy temple. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.1.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, behold, the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> cometh<small><small>[^11]</small></small> forth out of his place<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, and will come down, and tread<small><small>[^13]</small></small> upon the high<small><small>[^14]</small></small> places of the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.1.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the mountains<small><small>[^15]</small></small> shall be molten under him, and the valleys<small><small>[^16]</small></small> shall be cleft, as wax<small><small>[^17]</small></small> before the fire, \[and\] as the waters \[that are\] poured down a steep place<small><small>[^18]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.1.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the transgression of Jacob<small><small>[^19]</small></small> \[is\] all this, and for the sins of the house of Israel.
