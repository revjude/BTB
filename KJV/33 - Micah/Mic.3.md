<big><big>[[Mic.3.1 Rulers and Prophets Condemned|⥃]]</big></big>***Rulers and Prophets Condemned***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I said, Hear<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, I pray you, O heads of Jacob, and ye princes of the house of Israel; \[Is it<small><small>[^3]</small></small>\] not for you to know judgment?<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who hate<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the good, and love<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the evil; who pluck<small><small>[^7]</small></small> off their skin from off them, and their flesh from off their bones; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who also eat<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the flesh of my people, and flay their skin from off them; and they break their bones, and chop<small><small>[^9]</small></small> them in pieces, as for the pot, and as flesh within the caldron. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then<small><small>[^10]</small></small> shall they cry<small><small>[^11]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, but he will not hear them: he will even<small><small>[^12]</small></small> hide his face from them at that time, as<small><small>[^13]</small></small> they have behaved themselves ill in their doings. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> concerning<small><small>[^14]</small></small> the prophets that make my people err, that bite<small><small>[^15]</small></small> with their teeth, and cry, Peace; and he that putteth not into their mouths, they even prepare war against him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore night<small><small>[^16]</small></small> \[shall be\] unto you, that ye shall not have a vision<small><small>[^17]</small></small>; and it shall be dark unto you, that ye shall not divine<small><small>[^18]</small></small>; and the sun<small><small>[^19]</small></small> shall go down over the prophets, and the day shall be dark over them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then shall the seers<small><small>[^20]</small></small> be ashamed, and the diviners confounded: yea, they shall all cover<small><small>[^21]</small></small> their lips<small><small>[^22]</small></small>; for \[there is\] no<small><small>[^23]</small></small> answer of God. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But truly I am<small><small>[^24]</small></small> full of power by the spirit of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and of judgment, and of might, to declare<small><small>[^25]</small></small> unto Jacob his transgression, and to Israel his sin. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear this, I pray<small><small>[^26]</small></small> you, ye heads of the house of Jacob, and princes of the house of Israel, that<small><small>[^27]</small></small> abhor judgment, and pervert all equity. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They build up Zion<small><small>[^28]</small></small> with blood<small><small>[^29]</small></small>, and Jerusalem with iniquity. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The heads<small><small>[^30]</small></small> thereof judge for reward, and the priests<small><small>[^31]</small></small> thereof teach for hire, and the prophets<small><small>[^32]</small></small> thereof divine for money: yet<small><small>[^33]</small></small> will they lean upon the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and say<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, \[Is\] not the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> among us? none evil can come<small><small>[^35]</small></small> upon us. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mic.3.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore shall Zion<small><small>[^36]</small></small> for your sake be plowed \[as\] a field, and Jerusalem shall become heaps, and the mountain<small><small>[^37]</small></small> of the house as the high places of the forest. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Mic.2|<< Micah 2]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Mic.4|Micah 4 >>]]</td></tr></table>
