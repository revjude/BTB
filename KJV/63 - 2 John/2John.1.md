***Walking in the Truth***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[John.8|John 8:30–47]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb3015

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2John.1.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I rejoiced<small><small>[^13]</small></small> greatly that I found of thy children walking<small><small>[^14]</small></small> in truth, as we have received a commandment from the Father. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2John.1.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And now I beseech thee, lady, not<small><small>[^15]</small></small> as though I wrote a new commandment unto thee, but that which we had from the beginning, that we<small><small>[^16]</small></small> love one another. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2John.1.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And this is love<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, that we walk after his commandments. This is the<small><small>[^18]</small></small> commandment, That, as ye have heard from the beginning, ye should walk in it.

***Beware of Deceivers*** ^bsb3016

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2John.1.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For many<small><small>[^19]</small></small> deceivers are entered into the world, who<small><small>[^20]</small></small> confess not that Jesus Christ is come in the flesh. This is<small><small>[^21]</small></small> a deceiver and an antichrist. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2John.1.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Look<small><small>[^22]</small></small> to yourselves, that we lose<small><small>[^23]</small></small> not those things which we have wrought<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, but that we receive<small><small>[^25]</small></small> a full reward. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2John.1.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whosoever transgresseth, and abideth not<small><small>[^26]</small></small> in the doctrine<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of Christ, hath not<small><small>[^28]</small></small> God. He that<small><small>[^29]</small></small> abideth in the doctrine of Christ, he hath<small><small>[^30]</small></small> both the Father and the Son. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2John.1.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If there come<small><small>[^31]</small></small> any unto you, and bring not this doctrine, receive him not into \[your\] house, neither<small><small>[^32]</small></small> bid him God speed: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2John.1.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he that biddeth him God speed is partaker<small><small>[^33]</small></small> of his evil deeds.
