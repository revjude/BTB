***Zephaniah Prophesies Judgment on Judah***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.13|Matthew 13:36–43]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2046

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The word<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> which came unto Zephaniah the son of Cushi, the son of Gedaliah, the son of Amariah, the son of Hizkiah, in the days<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of Josiah the son of Amon, king of Judah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will<small><small>[^4]</small></small> utterly consume all \[things\] from off the land<small><small>[^5]</small></small>, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will consume man<small><small>[^6]</small></small> and beast; I will consume the fowls of the heaven, and the fishes of the sea, and the stumblingblocks<small><small>[^7]</small></small> with the wicked; and I<small><small>[^8]</small></small> will cut off man from off the land, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will also stretch<small><small>[^9]</small></small> out mine hand upon Judah, and upon all the inhabitants of Jerusalem; and I will cut off the remnant<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of Baal from this place, \[and\] the name of the Chemarims<small><small>[^11]</small></small> with the priests; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And them that worship<small><small>[^12]</small></small> the host of heaven upon the housetops; and them<small><small>[^13]</small></small> that worship \[and\] that<small><small>[^14]</small></small> swear by the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^15]</small></small></span>, and that swear by<small><small>[^16]</small></small> Malcham<small><small>[^17]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And them that are turned<small><small>[^18]</small></small> back from the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; and \[those<small><small>[^19]</small></small>\] that have not sought the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, nor enquired for him.

all they that bear silver are cut off. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass at that time, \[that\] I<small><small>[^38]</small></small> will search Jerusalem with candles, and punish the men<small><small>[^39]</small></small> that are settled<small><small>[^40]</small></small> on their lees: that say in their heart, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^41]</small></small></span> will not do good, neither will he do evil. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore their goods<small><small>[^42]</small></small> shall become a booty, and their houses a desolation: they shall also build<small><small>[^43]</small></small> houses, but not inhabit \[them\]; and they shall plant vineyards, but not drink the wine thereof. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The great<small><small>[^44]</small></small> day of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] near, \[it is<small><small>[^45]</small></small>\] near, and hasteth greatly, \[even<small><small>[^46]</small></small>\] the voice of the day of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: the mighty<small><small>[^47]</small></small> man shall cry there bitterly. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That day \[is<small><small>[^48]</small></small>\] a day of wrath, a day of trouble and distress, a day of wasteness and desolation, a day of darkness<small><small>[^49]</small></small> and gloominess, a day of clouds and thick darkness, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> A day<small><small>[^50]</small></small> of the trumpet and alarm against the fenced cities, and against<small><small>[^51]</small></small> the high towers. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will bring distress upon men, that they shall<small><small>[^52]</small></small> walk like blind men, because<small><small>[^53]</small></small> they have sinned against the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and their blood<small><small>[^54]</small></small> shall be poured out as dust, and their flesh as the dung. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.1.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither their silver<small><small>[^55]</small></small> nor their gold shall be able to deliver them in the day<small><small>[^56]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span> wrath; but<small><small>[^57]</small></small> the whole land shall be devoured by the fire<small><small>[^58]</small></small> of his jealousy: for he shall<small><small>[^59]</small></small> make even a speedy riddance of all them that dwell in the land. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Hab.3|<< Habakkuk 3]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Zeph.2|Zephaniah 2 >>]]</td></tr></table>
