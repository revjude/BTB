<big><big>[[Zeph.2.1 A Call to Repentance|⥃]]</big></big>***A Call to Repentance***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Joel.1|Joel 1:13–20]] <big>†</big> [[Amos.5|Amos 5:4–15]] <big>†</big> [[Luke.13|Luke 13:1–5]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Gather yourselves together, yea, gather together<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, O nation<small><small>[^3]</small></small> not desired<small><small>[^4]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Before the decree<small><small>[^5]</small></small> bring forth, \[before\] the day pas<small><small>[^6]</small></small>s as the chaff, before the fierce<small><small>[^7]</small></small> anger of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> come upon you, before the day of<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span> anger come upon you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Seek ye<small><small>[^9]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, all<small><small>[^10]</small></small> ye meek of the earth, which have wrought his judgment; seek righteousness<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, seek meekness: it may<small><small>[^12]</small></small> be ye shall be hid<small><small>[^13]</small></small> in the day of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span> anger.

<big><big>[[Zeph.2.4 Judgment on the Philistines|⥃]]</big></big>***Judgment on the Philistines***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Jer.47|Jeremiah 47:1–7]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For Gaza<small><small>[^14]</small></small> shall be forsaken, and Ashkelon a desolat<small><small>[^15]</small></small>ion: they shall drive out Ashdod at the noon day, and Ekron shall be rooted up. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.2.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Woe unto the inhabitants of the sea coast, the nation of the Cherethites<small><small>[^16]</small></small>! the word<small><small>[^17]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] against you; O Canaan<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, the land of the Philistines, I will even destroy thee, that there shall be no inhabitant. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.2.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sea<small><small>[^19]</small></small> coast shall be dwellings \[and\] cottages for shepherds, and folds for flocks. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.2.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the coast<small><small>[^20]</small></small> shall be for the remnant<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of the house of Judah; they shall feed thereupon: in the houses of Ashkelon shall they lie down in the evening: for<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> their God shall visit<small><small>[^23]</small></small> them, and turn<small><small>[^24]</small></small> away their captivity.

<big><big>[[Zeph.2.8 Judgment on Moab and Ammon|⥃]]</big></big>***Judgment on Moab and Ammon***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Isa.16|Isaiah 16:1–14]] <big>†</big> [[Jer.48|Jeremiah 48:1–47]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.2.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have heard<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the reproach of Moab, and the revilings<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of the children of Ammon, whereby they have reproached my people, and magnified \[themselves\] against their border. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.2.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore \[as\] I<small><small>[^27]</small></small> live, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, the God of Israel, Surely<small><small>[^28]</small></small> Moab shall be as Sodom, and the children of Ammon as Gomorrah<small><small>[^29]</small></small>, \[even\] the breeding of nettles, and saltpits, and a perpetual desolation: the residue<small><small>[^30]</small></small> of my people shall spoil them, and the remnant of my people shall possess them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.2.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This shall they have for<small><small>[^31]</small></small> their pride, because they have reproached and magnified<small><small>[^32]</small></small> \[themselves\] against the people of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.2.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[will be\] terrible unto them: for<small><small>[^33]</small></small> he will famish<small><small>[^34]</small></small> all the gods of the earth; and \[men<small><small>[^35]</small></small>\] shall worship him, every one from his place, \[even\] all the isles<small><small>[^36]</small></small> of the heathen.
