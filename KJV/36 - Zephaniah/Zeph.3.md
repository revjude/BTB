<big><big>[[Zeph.3.1 Judgment on Jerusalem|⥃]]</big></big>***Judgment on Jerusalem***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Woe to her that is filthy<small><small>[^2]</small></small> and polluted, to the<small><small>[^3]</small></small> oppressing city! <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> She obeyed<small><small>[^4]</small></small> not the voice; she received<small><small>[^5]</small></small> not correction<small><small>[^6]</small></small>; she trusted<small><small>[^7]</small></small> not in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; she drew<small><small>[^8]</small></small> not near to her God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Her princes<small><small>[^9]</small></small> within her \[are\] roaring lions; her judges \[are\] evening<small><small>[^10]</small></small> wolves; they gnaw not the bones till the morrow. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Her prophets \[are\] light<small><small>[^11]</small></small> \[and\] treacherous persons: her priests<small><small>[^12]</small></small> have polluted the sanctuary, they have done violence to the law. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The just<small><small>[^13]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] in<small><small>[^14]</small></small> the midst thereof; he will<small><small>[^15]</small></small> not do iniquity: every morning<small><small>[^16]</small></small> doth he bring<small><small>[^17]</small></small> his judgment to light, he faileth not; but<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the unjust knoweth no shame.

<big><big>[[Zeph.3.6 Purification of the Nations|⥃]]</big></big>***Purification of the Nations***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have cut<small><small>[^19]</small></small> off the nations: their towers<small><small>[^20]</small></small> are desolate; I made their streets waste, that none passeth by: their cities are destroyed, so that there is no man, that there is none inhabitant. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I said, Surely<small><small>[^21]</small></small> thou wilt fear me, thou wilt receive instruction; so<small><small>[^22]</small></small> their dwelling should not be cut off, howsoever<small><small>[^23]</small></small> I punished them: but they<small><small>[^24]</small></small> rose early, \[and\] corrupted<small><small>[^25]</small></small> all their doings. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore wait<small><small>[^26]</small></small> ye upon me, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, until the day that I rise<small><small>[^27]</small></small> up to the prey: for my determination \[is\] to gather<small><small>[^28]</small></small> the nations, that I may assemble the kingdoms, to pour upon them mine indignation, \[even\] all my fierce anger: for all<small><small>[^29]</small></small> the earth shall be devoured with the fire of my jealousy.

<big><big>[[Zeph.3.9 A Faithful Remnant|⥃]]</big></big>***A Faithful Remnant***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For then will<small><small>[^30]</small></small> I turn to the people a pure language<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, that<small><small>[^32]</small></small> they may all call upon the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, to serve him with one consent<small><small>[^33]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> From beyond the rivers of Ethiopia my suppliants, \[even\] the daughter of my dispersed, shall bring mine offering.<small><small>[^34]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In that day shalt thou<small><small>[^35]</small></small> not be ashamed for all thy doings, wherein thou hast transgressed against me: for then I will take away out of the midst of thee them that rejoice<small><small>[^36]</small></small> in thy pride, and thou shalt no more be haughty because of my holy<small><small>[^37]</small></small> mountain. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will also leave<small><small>[^38]</small></small> in the midst of thee an afflicted and<small><small>[^39]</small></small> poor people, and they shall trust in the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zeph.3.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The remnant<small><small>[^40]</small></small> of Israel shall not<small><small>[^41]</small></small> do iniquity, nor<small><small>[^42]</small></small> speak lies; neither shall a deceitful tongue be found in their mouth: for they<small><small>[^43]</small></small> shall feed and<small><small>[^44]</small></small> lie down, and none shall make \[them\] afraid.
