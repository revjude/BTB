<big><big>[[Rev.9.1 The Fifth Trumpet|⥃]]</big></big>***The Fifth Trumpet***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the fifth<small><small>[^2]</small></small> angel sounded, and I saw a star<small><small>[^3]</small></small> fall from heaven unto the earth: and to him<small><small>[^4]</small></small> was given the key of the bottomless<small><small>[^5]</small></small> pit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he opened the bottomless pit; and there<small><small>[^6]</small></small> arose a smoke out of the pit, as the smoke of a great furnace; and the sun<small><small>[^7]</small></small> and the air were darkened by reason of the smoke of the pit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there came out of the smoke locusts<small><small>[^8]</small></small> upon the earth: and unto them was<small><small>[^9]</small></small> given power, as the scorpions of the earth have power. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it was commanded them that they<small><small>[^10]</small></small> should not hurt<small><small>[^11]</small></small> the grass of the earth, neither any green thing, neither any tree; but<small><small>[^12]</small></small> only those men which<small><small>[^13]</small></small> have not the seal of God in their foreheads. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And to them it was<small><small>[^14]</small></small> given that they should not<small><small>[^15]</small></small> kill them, but that they should be<small><small>[^16]</small></small> tormented five<small><small>[^17]</small></small> months: and their<small><small>[^18]</small></small> torment \[was\] as the torment of a scorpion, when he striketh a man. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And in those days shall men<small><small>[^19]</small></small> seek death, and shall not find it; and shall desire to die, and death shall flee from them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the shapes<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of the locusts \[were\] like unto horses prepared unto battle; and on their heads \[were\] as it were crowns like gold, and their faces<small><small>[^21]</small></small> \[were\] as the faces of men. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they had hair<small><small>[^22]</small></small> as the hair of women, and their<small><small>[^23]</small></small> teeth were as \[the teeth\] of lions. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they had<small><small>[^24]</small></small> breastplates, as it were breastplates of iron; and the<small><small>[^25]</small></small> sound of their wings \[was\] as the sound of chariots of many horses running to battle. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they had tails<small><small>[^26]</small></small> like unto scorpions, and there were stings in their tails: and their power \[was\] to hurt men five months. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they had<small><small>[^27]</small></small> a king over them, \[which is\] the angel<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of the bottomless pit, whose name in the Hebrew tongue \[is\] Abaddon<small><small>[^29]</small></small>, but in the Greek tongue hath \[his\] name Apollyon. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> One woe<small><small>[^30]</small></small> is past; \[and\], behold, there come two<small><small>[^31]</small></small> woes more hereafter.

<big><big>[[Rev.9.13 The Sixth Trumpet|⥃]]</big></big>***The Sixth Trumpet***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sixth<small><small>[^32]</small></small> angel sounded, and I heard a voice<small><small>[^33]</small></small> from the four horns of the golden altar which is before God, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Saying to the<small><small>[^34]</small></small> sixth angel which had the trumpet, Loose<small><small>[^35]</small></small> the four angels which are bound in the great<small><small>[^36]</small></small> river Euphrates. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the four angels were loosed, which were prepared for<small><small>[^37]</small></small> an hour<small><small>[^38]</small></small>, and a day, and a month, and a year, for to<small><small>[^39]</small></small> slay the third part of men. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the number<small><small>[^40]</small></small> of the army of the horsemen<small><small>[^41]</small></small> \[were\] two hundred thousand thousand: and I heard<small><small>[^42]</small></small> the number of them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thus I saw the horses in the vision, and them that sat on them, having<small><small>[^43]</small></small> breastplates of fire, and of jacinth<small><small>[^44]</small></small>, and brimstone<small><small>[^45]</small></small>: and the heads of the horses \[were\] as the<small><small>[^46]</small></small> heads of lions; and out of their mouths issued fire and smoke and brimstone. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By these three was the third<small><small>[^47]</small></small> part of men killed, by the fire, and by the smoke, and by the brimstone, which issued out of their mouths. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For their power is in their mouth, and in their tails<small><small>[^48]</small></small>: for their tails \[were\] like unto serpents, and had heads, and with them they do hurt. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the<small><small>[^49]</small></small> rest of the men which were not killed by these plagues yet<small><small>[^50]</small></small> repented not of the works of their hands, that they should not worship<small><small>[^51]</small></small> devils, and idols<small><small>[^52]</small></small> of gold, and silver, and brass, and stone, and of wood: which neither can see, nor hear, nor walk: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.9.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither repented they of their murders<small><small>[^53]</small></small>, nor of their sorceries<small><small>[^54]</small></small>, nor of their fornication<small><small>[^55]</small></small>, nor of their thefts. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Rev.8|<< Revelation 8]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Rev.10|Revelation 10 >>]]</td></tr></table>
