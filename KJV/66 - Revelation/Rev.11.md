<big><big>[[Rev.11.1 The Two Witnesses|⥃]]</big></big>***The Two Witnesses***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there was given me a reed<small><small>[^2]</small></small> like unto a rod: and the<small><small>[^3]</small></small> angel stood, saying, Rise<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, and measure the temple of God, and the altar, and them that worship therein. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the court<small><small>[^5]</small></small> which is without the temple leave out<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, and measure it not; for it is<small><small>[^7]</small></small> given unto the Gentiles: and the<small><small>[^8]</small></small> holy city shall they tread<small><small>[^9]</small></small> under foot forty<small><small>[^10]</small></small> \[and\] two months. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will give \[power<small><small>[^11]</small></small>\] unto my two<small><small>[^12]</small></small> witnesses<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, and they shall<small><small>[^14]</small></small> prophesy a thousand<small><small>[^15]</small></small> two hundred \[and\] threescore days, clothed<small><small>[^16]</small></small> in sackcloth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> These are the two olive<small><small>[^17]</small></small> trees, and the two candlesticks<small><small>[^18]</small></small> standing<small><small>[^19]</small></small> before the God<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if any man will hurt them, fire<small><small>[^21]</small></small> proceedeth out of their mouth, and devoureth their enemies: and if any man will hurt them, he must in this manner be killed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> These have power<small><small>[^22]</small></small> to shut heaven, that it rain not in the days of their prophecy: and have power over<small><small>[^23]</small></small> waters to turn them to blood, and to smite the earth with all plagues, as often as they will.

<big><big>[[Rev.11.7 The Witnesses Killed and Raised|⥃]]</big></big>***The Witnesses Killed and Raised***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when<small><small>[^24]</small></small> they shall have finished their testimony, the beast<small><small>[^25]</small></small> that ascendeth out<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of the bottomless pit shall make war against them, and shall overcome them, and kill them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And their dead<small><small>[^27]</small></small> bodies \[shall lie\] in the street of the great<small><small>[^28]</small></small> city, which spiritually is called Sodom<small><small>[^29]</small></small> and Egypt<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, where also our Lord<small><small>[^31]</small></small> was crucified. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they of the people<small><small>[^32]</small></small> and kindreds and tongues and nations shall see their dead bodies three<small><small>[^33]</small></small> days and an half, and shall not<small><small>[^34]</small></small> suffer their dead bodies to be put in graves. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they that dwell<small><small>[^35]</small></small> upon the earth shall rejoice<small><small>[^36]</small></small> over them, and make<small><small>[^37]</small></small> merry, and shall send gifts one to another; because these<small><small>[^38]</small></small> two prophets tormented them that dwelt on the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And after three<small><small>[^39]</small></small> days and an half the Spirit<small><small>[^40]</small></small> of life from God entered into them, and they stood upon their feet; and great<small><small>[^41]</small></small> fear fell upon them which saw them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they heard a great voice from heaven saying unto them, Come<small><small>[^42]</small></small> up hither. And they<small><small>[^43]</small></small> ascended up to heaven in<small><small>[^44]</small></small> a cloud; and their<small><small>[^45]</small></small> enemies beheld them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the same hour was there<small><small>[^46]</small></small> a great earthquake, and the tenth<small><small>[^47]</small></small> part of the city fell, and in the earthquake were slain of men<small><small>[^48]</small></small> seven thousand: and the remnant<small><small>[^49]</small></small> were affrighted, and gave<small><small>[^50]</small></small> glory to the God of heaven. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The second woe is past; \[and\], behold, the third woe cometh quickly.<small><small>[^51]</small></small>

<big><big>[[Rev.11.15 The Seventh Trumpet|⥃]]</big></big>***The Seventh Trumpet***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.11.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the seventh<small><small>[^52]</small></small> angel sounded; and there<small><small>[^53]</small></small> were great voices in heaven, saying, The kingdoms<small><small>[^54]</small></small> of this world are become \[the kingdoms\] of our Lord, and of his Christ;
