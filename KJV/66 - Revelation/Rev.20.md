<big><big>[[Rev.20.1 Satan Bound|⥃]]</big></big>***Satan Bound***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I saw<small><small>[^2]</small></small> an angel come down from heaven, having<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the key of the bottomless pit and a great<small><small>[^4]</small></small> chain in his hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he laid<small><small>[^5]</small></small> hold on the dragon<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, that old serpent, which is the Devil, and Satan, and bound him a thousand years, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And cast<small><small>[^7]</small></small> him into the bottomless pit, and shut him up, and set<small><small>[^8]</small></small> a seal upon him, that he should deceive<small><small>[^9]</small></small> the nations no more, till the thousand<small><small>[^10]</small></small> years should be fulfilled: and after<small><small>[^11]</small></small> that he must be loosed a little season. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I saw thrones<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, and they sat upon them, and judgment was given unto them: and \[I saw\] the souls<small><small>[^13]</small></small> of them that were beheaded<small><small>[^14]</small></small> for the witness<small><small>[^15]</small></small> of Jesus, and for the word of God, and which<small><small>[^16]</small></small> had not worshipped the beast, neither his image, neither had received \[his\] mark upon their foreheads, or in their hands; and they<small><small>[^17]</small></small> lived and reigned with Christ a thousand years. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the rest<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of the dead lived not again until the thousand years were finished. This<small><small>[^19]</small></small> \[is\] the first resurrection. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Blessed<small><small>[^20]</small></small> and holy \[is\] he that hath part in the first resurrection: on such the second<small><small>[^21]</small></small> death hath no power, but they shall be priests<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of God and of Christ, and shall<small><small>[^23]</small></small> reign with him a thousand years.

<big><big>[[Rev.20.7 Satan Cast into the Lake of Fire|⥃]]</big></big>***Satan Cast into the Lake of Fire***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when the thousand years are expired, Satan shall be loosed out of his prison,<small><small>[^24]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And shall go out to deceive<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the nations which are in the four quarters of the earth, Gog<small><small>[^26]</small></small> and Magog, to gather<small><small>[^27]</small></small> them together to battle: the number<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of whom \[is\] as the sand of the sea. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they went<small><small>[^29]</small></small> up on the breadth of the earth, and compassed<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the camp<small><small>[^31]</small></small> of the saints about, and the beloved city: and fire<small><small>[^32]</small></small> came down from God out of heaven, and devoured them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the devil<small><small>[^33]</small></small> that deceived them was cast into the lake<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of fire and brimstone, where the beast and the false prophet \[are\], and shall be tormented<small><small>[^35]</small></small> day and night for ever and ever.

<big><big>[[Rev.20.11 Judgment before the Great White Throne|⥃]]</big></big>***Judgment before the Great White Throne***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I saw<small><small>[^36]</small></small> a great white throne, and him that sat on it, from<small><small>[^37]</small></small> whose face the earth and the heaven fled away; and there<small><small>[^38]</small></small> was found no place for them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I saw<small><small>[^39]</small></small> the dead, small<small><small>[^40]</small></small> and great, stand<small><small>[^41]</small></small> before God; and the books<small><small>[^42]</small></small> were opened: and another<small><small>[^43]</small></small> book was opened, which is \[the book\] of life: and the dead were judged out of those things which were written in the books, according<small><small>[^44]</small></small> to their works. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sea<small><small>[^45]</small></small> gave up the dead which were in it; and death<small><small>[^46]</small></small> and hell<small><small>[^47]</small></small> delivered up the dead which were in them: and they<small><small>[^48]</small></small> were judged every man according to their works. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And death<small><small>[^49]</small></small> and hell were cast into the lake of fire. This<small><small>[^50]</small></small> is the second death. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.20.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And whosoever<small><small>[^51]</small></small> was not found written in the book of life was cast<small><small>[^52]</small></small> into the lake of fire. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Rev.19|<< Revelation 19]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Rev.21|Revelation 21 >>]]</td></tr></table>
