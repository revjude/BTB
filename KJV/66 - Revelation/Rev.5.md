***The Lamb Takes the Scroll*** ^bsb3038

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.5.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I saw in the right hand of him that sat<small><small>[^2]</small></small> on the throne a class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.5.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he came and took the book out<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of the right hand of him that sat upon the throne. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.5.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when he had taken the book, the four<small><small>[^20]</small></small> beasts and four \[and\] twenty elders fell down before the Lamb, having<small><small>[^21]</small></small> every one of them harps, and golden<small><small>[^22]</small></small> vials full of class="endversemarker">]</span> And they sung<small><small>[^25]</small></small> a new song, saying, Thou art<small><small>[^26]</small></small> worthy to take the book, and to open the seals thereof: for<small><small>[^27]</small></small> thou wast slain, and hast<small><small>[^28]</small></small> redeemed us to God by thy blood out<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of every kindred, and tongue, and people, and nation; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.5.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And hast made us unto our God kings<small><small>[^30]</small></small> and priests: and we<small><small>[^31]</small></small> shall reign on the earth.
