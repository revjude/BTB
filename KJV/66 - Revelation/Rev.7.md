<big><big>[[Rev.7.1 144,000 Sealed|⥃]]</big></big>***144,000 Sealed***

Of the tribe of Gad \[were\] sealed twelve thousand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.7.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Of the tribe of Aser<small><small>[^18]</small></small> \[were\] sealed twelve thousand. Of the tribe of Manasses \[were\] sealed twelve thousand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.7.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Of the tribe of Simeon<small><small>[^19]</small></small> \[were\] sealed twelve thousand. Of the tribe of Issachar<small><small>[^21]</small></small> \[were\] sealed twelve thousand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.7.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Of the tribe of Zabulon \[were\] sealed twelve thousand. Of the tribe of Joseph \[were\] sealed twelve thousand. Of the tribe of Benjamin \[were\] sealed twelve thousand.

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.7.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> After this I beheld, and, lo, a great<small><small>[^22]</small></small> multitude, which no man<small><small>[^23]</small></small> could number, of all<small><small>[^24]</small></small> nations, and kindreds, and people, and tongues, stood<small><small>[^25]</small></small> before the throne, and before the Lamb, clothed<small><small>[^26]</small></small> with white robes, and palms<small><small>[^27]</small></small> in their hands; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.7.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And cried<small><small>[^28]</small></small> with a loud voice, saying, Salvation<small><small>[^29]</small></small> to our God which sitteth<small><small>[^30]</small></small> upon the throne, and unto<small><small>[^31]</small></small> the Lamb. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.7.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all<small><small>[^32]</small></small> the angels stood round about the throne, and \[about\] the elders and the four beasts, and fell<small><small>[^33]</small></small> before the throne on their faces, and worshipped<small><small>[^34]</small></small> God, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.7.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Saying, Amen<small><small>[^35]</small></small>: Blessing<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, and glory, and wisdom, and thanksgiving<small><small>[^37]</small></small>, and honour, and power, and might, \[be\] unto our God for ever and ever.
