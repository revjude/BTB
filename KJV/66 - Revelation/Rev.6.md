<big><big>[[Rev.6.1 The First Seal：The White Horse|⥃]]</big></big>***The First Seal: The White Horse***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I saw when<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the Lamb opened one of the seals, and I heard, as it were the noise<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of thunder, one<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of the four beasts saying, Come and see. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I saw, and behold a white<small><small>[^5]</small></small> horse: and he that<small><small>[^6]</small></small> sat on him had a bow; and a<small><small>[^7]</small></small> crown was given unto him: and he went<small><small>[^8]</small></small> forth conquering, and to conquer.

<big><big>[[Rev.6.3 The Second Seal：War|⥃]]</big></big>***The Second Seal: War***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when he had opened the second seal, I heard the second beast say, Come and see.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there went out another horse<small><small>[^10]</small></small> \[that was\] red: and \[power<small><small>[^11]</small></small>\] was given to him that sat thereon to take peace from the earth, and that they should kill one another: and there<small><small>[^12]</small></small> was given unto him a great sword.

<big><big>[[Rev.6.5 The Third Seal：Famine|⥃]]</big></big>***The Third Seal: Famine***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when he had<small><small>[^13]</small></small> opened the third seal, I heard the third beast say, Come and see. And I beheld, and lo a black<small><small>[^14]</small></small> horse; and he that sat on him had<small><small>[^15]</small></small> a pair of balances in his hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I heard a voice in the midst of the four beasts say, A measure<small><small>[^16]</small></small> of wheat for a penny, and three measures of barley for a penny; and \[see<small><small>[^17]</small></small>\] thou hurt not the oil and the wine.

<big><big>[[Rev.6.7 The Fourth Seal：Death|⥃]]</big></big>***The Fourth Seal: Death***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when he had opened the fourth seal, I heard the voice of the fourth beast say, Come and see.<small><small>[^18]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I looked, and behold a pale<small><small>[^19]</small></small> horse: and his name that sat on him was Death<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and Hell followed with him. And power was given unto them<small><small>[^21]</small></small> over<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the fourth part of the earth, to kill<small><small>[^23]</small></small> with sword, and with hunger, and with death, and with the beasts of the earth.

<big><big>[[Rev.6.9 The Fifth Seal：The Martyrs|⥃]]</big></big>***The Fifth Seal: The Martyrs***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when he had opened the fifth seal, I saw<small><small>[^24]</small></small> under the altar the souls<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of them that were slain<small><small>[^26]</small></small> for the word of God, and for the testimony which they held: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they cried<small><small>[^27]</small></small> with a loud voice, saying, How<small><small>[^28]</small></small> long, O Lord, holy<small><small>[^29]</small></small> and true, dost<small><small>[^30]</small></small> thou not judge and avenge<small><small>[^31]</small></small> our blood on them that dwell on the earth? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And white<small><small>[^32]</small></small> robes were given unto every one of them; and it was said unto them, that they<small><small>[^33]</small></small> should rest yet for a little season, until<small><small>[^34]</small></small> their fellowservants also and their brethren, that should be killed as they \[were\], should be fulfilled.

<big><big>[[Rev.6.12 The Sixth Seal：Terror|⥃]]</big></big>***The Sixth Seal: Terror***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I beheld when he had opened the sixth seal, and, lo, there<small><small>[^35]</small></small> was a great earthquake; and the sun<small><small>[^36]</small></small> became black as sackcloth of hair, and the moon became as blood; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the stars<small><small>[^37]</small></small> of heaven fell unto the earth, even as a fig tree casteth her untimely figs<small><small>[^38]</small></small>, when she is shaken of a<small><small>[^39]</small></small> mighty wind. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the heaven<small><small>[^40]</small></small> departed as a scroll when it is rolled together; and every<small><small>[^41]</small></small> mountain and island were moved out of their places. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the kings<small><small>[^42]</small></small> of the earth, and the great men, and the rich men, and the chief captains, and the mighty men, and every bondman, and every free man, hid<small><small>[^43]</small></small> themselves in the dens and in the rocks of the mountains; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And said to the mountains and rocks, Fall<small><small>[^44]</small></small> on us, and hide us from the face<small><small>[^45]</small></small> of him that sitteth on the throne, and from<small><small>[^46]</small></small> the wrath of the Lamb: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.6.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the great<small><small>[^47]</small></small> day of his wrath is come; and who<small><small>[^48]</small></small> shall be able to stand?
