***The First Six Bowls of Wrath*** ^bsb3067

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I heard<small><small>[^2]</small></small> a great voice out of the temple saying to the seven<small><small>[^3]</small></small> angels, Go your ways, and pour<small><small>[^4]</small></small> out the vials of the wrath of God upon the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the first went, and poured out his vial upon the earth<small><small>[^5]</small></small>; and there fell a noisome<small><small>[^6]</small></small> and grievous sore upon the men which had<small><small>[^7]</small></small> the mark of the beast, and \[upon\] them which worshipped his image. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the second angel poured out his vial upon<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the sea; and it became<small><small>[^9]</small></small> as the blood of a dead \[man\]: and every<small><small>[^10]</small></small> living soul died in the sea. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the third angel poured out his vial upon<small><small>[^11]</small></small> the rivers and fountains of waters; and they<small><small>[^12]</small></small> became blood. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I heard the angel<small><small>[^13]</small></small> of the waters say, Thou art<small><small>[^14]</small></small> righteous, O Lord, which art<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, and wast, and shalt be, because thou hast judged thus. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they have<small><small>[^16]</small></small> shed the blood of saints and prophets, and thou hast given them blood to drink; for they are<small><small>[^17]</small></small> worthy. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I heard another out<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of the altar say, Even<small><small>[^19]</small></small> so, Lord God Almighty, true<small><small>[^20]</small></small> and righteous \[are\] thy judgments. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the fourth angel poured out his vial upon<small><small>[^21]</small></small> the sun; and power<small><small>[^22]</small></small> was given unto him to scorch men with fire. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And men were scorched<small><small>[^23]</small></small> with great heat, and blasphemed<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the name of God, which hath power over these plagues: and they<small><small>[^25]</small></small> repented not to give<small><small>[^26]</small></small> him glory. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the fifth angel poured out his vial upon<small><small>[^27]</small></small> the seat of the beast; and his kingdom was full<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of darkness; and they<small><small>[^29]</small></small> gnawed their tongues for pain, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And blasphemed<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the God<small><small>[^31]</small></small> of heaven because<small><small>[^32]</small></small> of their pains and their sores, and repented not<small><small>[^33]</small></small> of their deeds. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sixth angel poured out his vial upon<small><small>[^34]</small></small> the great river Euphrates; and the water<small><small>[^35]</small></small> thereof was dried up, that the<small><small>[^36]</small></small> way of the kings of the east might be prepared. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I saw three<small><small>[^37]</small></small> unclean spirits like<small><small>[^38]</small></small> frogs \[come\] out of<small><small>[^39]</small></small> the mouth of the dragon, and out of the mouth of the beast, and out of the mouth of the false<small><small>[^40]</small></small> prophet. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they are the spirits<small><small>[^41]</small></small> of devils, working<small><small>[^42]</small></small> miracles, \[which<small><small>[^43]</small></small>\] go forth unto the kings of the earth and of the whole<small><small>[^44]</small></small> world, to gather<small><small>[^45]</small></small> them to the battle of that great day of God Almighty<small><small>[^46]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, I come<small><small>[^47]</small></small> as a thief. Blessed<small><small>[^48]</small></small> \[is\] he that watcheth, and keepeth his garments, lest<small><small>[^49]</small></small> he walk naked, and they see his shame. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.16.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he<small><small>[^50]</small></small> gathered them together into a place called in the Hebrew<small><small>[^51]</small></small> tongue Armageddon<small><small>[^52]</small></small>.

***The Seventh Bowl of Wrath*** ^bsb3068
