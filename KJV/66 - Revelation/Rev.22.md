***The River of Life*** ^bsb3084

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shewed me a pure<small><small>[^2]</small></small> river of water<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of life, clear<small><small>[^4]</small></small> as crystal, proceeding<small><small>[^5]</small></small> out of the throne of God and of the Lamb. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the midst<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of the street of it, and on either side of the river, \[was there\] the tree of life<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, which bare twelve \[manner of\] fruits, \[and\] yielded her fruit every month: and the leaves of the tree \[were\] for the healing<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of the nations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there<small><small>[^9]</small></small> shall be no more curse: but<small><small>[^10]</small></small> the throne of God and of the Lamb shall be in it; and his<small><small>[^11]</small></small> servants shall serve him: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they<small><small>[^12]</small></small> shall see his face; and his<small><small>[^13]</small></small> name \[shall be\] in their foreheads. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there shall be no night<small><small>[^14]</small></small> there; and they<small><small>[^15]</small></small> need no candle, neither light of the sun; for the Lord God giveth them light: and they shall reign for ever and ever.

***Jesus Is Coming*** ^bsb3085

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto me, These<small><small>[^16]</small></small> sayings \[are\] faithful and true: and the Lord God of the holy<small><small>[^17]</small></small> prophets sent<small><small>[^18]</small></small> his angel to shew unto his servants the things which<small><small>[^19]</small></small> must shortly be done. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Behold, I come<small><small>[^20]</small></small> quickly:</span> blessed<small><small>[^21]</small></small> \[is\] he that keepeth the sayings of the prophecy of this book. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I John saw these things, and heard \[them\]. And when I had heard and seen, I fell<small><small>[^22]</small></small> down to worship before the feet of the angel which shewed me these things. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then saith he unto me, See<small><small>[^23]</small></small> \[thou do it\] not: for I am thy fellowservant, and of thy brethren the prophets, and of them which keep the sayings of this book: worship God<small><small>[^24]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he saith<small><small>[^25]</small></small> unto me, Seal<small><small>[^26]</small></small> not the sayings of the prophecy of this book: for<small><small>[^27]</small></small> the time is at hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He that is unjust<small><small>[^28]</small></small>, let him be unjust still: and he which is filthy, let him be filthy still: and he that<small><small>[^29]</small></small> is righteous, let him be righteous still: and he that is holy, let him be holy still. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">And, behold, I come<small><small>[^30]</small></small> quickly; and my<small><small>[^31]</small></small> reward \[is\] with me, to give<small><small>[^32]</small></small> every man according as his work shall be.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">I am Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end, the first and the last.</span><small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Blessed<small><small>[^34]</small></small> \[are\] they that do his commandments, that they may have<small><small>[^35]</small></small> right to the<small><small>[^36]</small></small> tree of life, and may<small><small>[^37]</small></small> enter in through the gates into the city. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For without<small><small>[^38]</small></small> \[are\] dogs<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, and sorcerers<small><small>[^40]</small></small>, and whoremongers<small><small>[^41]</small></small>, and murderers, and idolaters, and whosoever<small><small>[^42]</small></small> loveth and maketh a lie. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">I Jesus<small><small>[^43]</small></small> have sent mine angel to testify<small><small>[^44]</small></small> unto you these things in the churches. I am<small><small>[^45]</small></small> the root and the offspring of David, \[and\] the bright<small><small>[^46]</small></small> and morning star.</span><small><small>[^47]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Spirit<small><small>[^48]</small></small> and the bride<small><small>[^49]</small></small> say, Come<small><small>[^50]</small></small>. And let him that heareth<small><small>[^51]</small></small> say, Come. And let him that is athirst<small><small>[^52]</small></small> come. And whosoever will, let him take<small><small>[^53]</small></small> the water of life freely<small><small>[^54]</small></small>.

***Nothing May Be Added or Removed*** ^bsb3086

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I testify<small><small>[^55]</small></small> unto every man that heareth<small><small>[^56]</small></small> the words of the prophecy of this book, If<small><small>[^57]</small></small> any man shall add unto these things, God<small><small>[^58]</small></small> shall add unto him the plagues that are written in this book: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if any man shall take<small><small>[^59]</small></small> away from the words of the book of this prophecy, God<small><small>[^60]</small></small> shall take away his part out of the book of life<small><small>[^61]</small></small>, and out<small><small>[^62]</small></small> of the holy city, and \[from<small><small>[^63]</small></small>\] the things which are written in this book. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He which<small><small>[^64]</small></small> testifieth these things saith, <span style="color:red;">Surely<small><small>[^65]</small></small> I come quickly.</span> Amen<small><small>[^66]</small></small>. Even so, come, Lord Jesus. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.22.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ \[be\] with you all.
