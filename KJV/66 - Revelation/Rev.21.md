And he said unto me, Write<small><small>[^19]</small></small>: for these<small><small>[^20]</small></small> words are true and faithful. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto me, It is<small><small>[^21]</small></small> done. I am<small><small>[^22]</small></small> Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end. I will<small><small>[^23]</small></small> give unto him that is athirst of the fountain<small><small>[^24]</small></small> of the water of life freely<small><small>[^25]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He that overcometh<small><small>[^26]</small></small> shall inherit<small><small>[^27]</small></small> all things<small><small>[^28]</small></small>; and I<small><small>[^29]</small></small> will be his God, and he shall be my son. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the fearful<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, and unbelieving, and the<small><small>[^31]</small></small> abominable, and murderers, and whoremongers, and sorcerers, and idolaters<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, and all<small><small>[^33]</small></small> liars, shall have their part in the lake<small><small>[^34]</small></small> which burneth with fire and brimstone: which is<small><small>[^35]</small></small> the second death.

***The New Jerusalem*** ^bsb3083

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there came unto me one of the seven angels which<small><small>[^36]</small></small> had the seven vials full of the seven last plagues, and talked with me, saying, Come hither, I will shew thee the bride, the Lamb’s<small><small>[^37]</small></small> wife. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he carried<small><small>[^38]</small></small> me away in the spirit to a great and high mountain, and shewed me that<small><small>[^39]</small></small> great city, the holy Jerusalem, descending out of heaven from God, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Having the glory<small><small>[^40]</small></small> of God: and her<small><small>[^41]</small></small> light \[was\] like unto a stone most precious, even like a jasper stone, clear<small><small>[^42]</small></small> as crystal; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And had a wall<small><small>[^43]</small></small> great and high, \[and\] had twelve gates<small><small>[^44]</small></small>, and at the gates twelve angels<small><small>[^45]</small></small>, and names<small><small>[^46]</small></small> written thereon, which are \[the names\] of the twelve tribes of the children of Israel: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> On the east three gates; class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The fifth, sardonyx; the sixth, sardius; the seventh, chrysolite; the eighth, beryl; the ninth, a topaz; the tenth, a chrysoprasus; the eleventh, a jacinth; the twelfth, an amethyst. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Lamb<small><small>[^64]</small></small> are the temple of it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the city<small><small>[^65]</small></small> had no need of the sun, neither of the moon, to shine in it: for<small><small>[^66]</small></small> the glory of God did lighten it, and the Lamb<small><small>[^67]</small></small> \[is\] the light thereof. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the nations<small><small>[^68]</small></small> of them which are saved shall walk<small><small>[^69]</small></small> in the light of it: and the kings<small><small>[^70]</small></small> of the earth do bring their glory and honour into it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the gates<small><small>[^71]</small></small> of it shall not be shut at all by day: for<small><small>[^72]</small></small> there shall be no night there. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall bring the glory<small><small>[^73]</small></small> and honour of the nations into it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.21.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there<small><small>[^74]</small></small> shall in no wise enter into it any thing that defileth, neither \[whatsoever\] worketh<small><small>[^75]</small></small> abomination, or
