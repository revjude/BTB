***The Woman and the Dragon*** ^bsb3056

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.12.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there<small><small>[^2]</small></small> appeared a great wonder<small><small>[^3]</small></small> in heaven; a woman<small><small>[^4]</small></small> clothed<small><small>[^5]</small></small> with the sun, and the<small><small>[^6]</small></small> moon under her feet, and upon her head a crown<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of twelve stars: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.12.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And she being with child cried, travailing<small><small>[^8]</small></small> in birth, and pained to be delivered. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.12.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there appeared another wonder<small><small>[^9]</small></small> in heaven; ***The War in Heaven*** ^bsb3057

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.12.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there was war<small><small>[^23]</small></small> in heaven: Michael<small><small>[^24]</small></small> and his<small><small>[^25]</small></small> angels fought against the dragon<small><small>[^26]</small></small>; and they loved not<small><small>[^44]</small></small> their lives unto the death. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rev.12.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore rejoice<small><small>[^45]</small></small>, \[ye\] heavens, and ye that dwell in them. Woe<small><small>[^46]</small></small> to the inhabiters of the earth and of the sea! for the devil is come down unto you, having great wrath, because<small><small>[^47]</small></small> he knoweth that he hath but a short time.
