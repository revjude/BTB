<big><big>[[Mark.16.1 The Resurrection|⥃]]</big></big>***The Resurrection***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.28|Matthew 28:1–10]] <big>†</big> [[Luke.24|Luke 24:1–12]] <big>†</big> [[John.20|John 20:1–9]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the sabbath was past, Mary Magdalene<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, and Mary the \[mother\] of James, and Salome, had bought sweet<small><small>[^4]</small></small> spices, that they might come and anoint him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And very early in the morning the first \[day\] of the week, they came unto the sepulchre at the rising of the sun.<small><small>[^5]</small></small><small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they said among themselves, Who<small><small>[^7]</small></small> shall roll us away the stone from the door of the sepulchre? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when they looked, they saw<small><small>[^8]</small></small> that the stone was rolled away: for it was very great. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And entering<small><small>[^9]</small></small> into the sepulchre, they saw a young<small><small>[^10]</small></small> man sitting on the right side, clothed in a long white garment; and they<small><small>[^11]</small></small> were affrighted. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he saith unto them, Be not<small><small>[^12]</small></small> affrighted: Ye seek<small><small>[^13]</small></small> Jesus<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of Nazareth, which was crucified: he is risen<small><small>[^15]</small></small>; he is not here: behold the place where they laid him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But go your way, tell<small><small>[^16]</small></small> his disciples and Peter that he goeth before you into Galilee: there<small><small>[^17]</small></small> shall ye see him, as he said unto you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they went<small><small>[^18]</small></small> out quickly, and fled from the sepulchre; for they trembled<small><small>[^19]</small></small> and were amazed: neither<small><small>[^20]</small></small> said they any thing to any \[man\]; for they were afraid.

<big><big>[[Mark.16.9 Jesus Appears to Mary Magdalene|⥃]]</big></big>***Jesus Appears to Mary Magdalene***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[John.20|John 20:10–18]] ]</sup></small>

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Afterward he appeared<small><small>[^29]</small></small> unto the eleven as they sat at meat<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, and upbraided<small><small>[^31]</small></small> them with their unbelief<small><small>[^32]</small></small> and hardness of heart, because they believed not them which had seen him after he was risen. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto them, <span style="color:red;">Go<small><small>[^33]</small></small> ye into<small><small>[^34]</small></small> all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">He that believeth and<small><small>[^35]</small></small> is<small><small>[^36]</small></small> baptized shall be saved; but<small><small>[^37]</small></small> he that believeth not shall be damned.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">And these<small><small>[^38]</small></small> signs shall follow them that believe; In<small><small>[^39]</small></small> my name shall they<small><small>[^40]</small></small> cast out devils; they shall speak with new tongues;</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mark.16.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">They shall take<small><small>[^41]</small></small> up serpents; and if<small><small>[^42]</small></small> they drink any deadly thing, it shall not hurt them; they shall lay<small><small>[^43]</small></small> hands on the sick, and they shall recover.</span>
