fir<small><small>[^23]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.5.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My servants shall bring \[them\] down from class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.5.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> gave Solomon wisdom, as he promised him<small><small>[^30]</small></small>: and there was peace between Hiram and Solomon; and they two<small><small>[^31]</small></small> made a league together.
