***Naboth’s Vineyard*** ^bsb876

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass after<small><small>[^2]</small></small> these things, \[that\] Naboth the Jezreel<small><small>[^3]</small></small>ite had a vineyard, which \[was\] in Jezreel, hard by the palace of Ahab king of Samaria.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Ahab spake unto Naboth, saying, Give me<small><small>[^5]</small></small> thy vineyard, that I may have it for a garden of herbs<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, because it \[is\] near unto my house: and I will give thee for it a better vineyard than it; \[or\], if it seem good to thee<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, I will give thee the worth of it in money. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Naboth said to Ahab, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^8]</small></small></span> forbid it me, that I should give<small><small>[^9]</small></small> the inheritance of my fathers unto thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Ahab came into his house heavy<small><small>[^10]</small></small> and displeased because of the word which Naboth the Jezreelite had spoken to him: for he had said, I will not<small><small>[^11]</small></small> give thee the inheritance of my fathers. And he laid him<small><small>[^12]</small></small> down upon his bed, and turned away his face, and would eat no bread. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Jezebel<small><small>[^13]</small></small> his wife came to him, and said unto him, Why is thy spirit<small><small>[^14]</small></small> so sad, that thou eatest no bread? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto her, Because<small><small>[^15]</small></small> I spake unto Naboth the Jezreelite, and said unto him, Give me thy vineyard for money; or else, if it please thee, I will give thee \[another\] vineyard for it: and he answered, I will not give<small><small>[^16]</small></small> thee my vineyard. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jezebel his wife said unto him, Dost thou now<small><small>[^17]</small></small> govern the kingdom of Israel? arise, \[and\] eat bread, and let thine heart be merry: I will give thee<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the vineyard of Naboth the Jezreelite.

***Jezebel’s Plot*** ^bsb877

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So she wrote<small><small>[^19]</small></small> letters in Ahab’s name, and sealed \[them\] with his seal, and sent the letters unto the elders<small><small>[^20]</small></small> and to the nobles<small><small>[^21]</small></small> that \[were\] in his city, dwelling with Naboth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And she wrote in the letters, saying, Proclaim a fast<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, and set Naboth on high among<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the people: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And set two men<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, sons of Belial<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, before him, to bear witness against him, saying, Thou didst blaspheme<small><small>[^26]</small></small> God and the king. And \[then\] carry him out, and stone him, that he may die. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the men of his city, \[even\] the elders and the nobles who were the inhabitants in his city, did as Jezebel<small><small>[^27]</small></small> had sent unto them, \[and\] as it \[was\] written in the letters which she had sent unto them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They proclaimed a fast, and set Naboth on high among the people.<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there came in two men, children of Belial, and sat before him: and the men of Belial<small><small>[^29]</small></small> witnessed against him, \[even\] against Naboth, in the presence of the people, saying, Naboth did blaspheme God<small><small>[^30]</small></small> and the king<small><small>[^31]</small></small>. Then they carried him<small><small>[^32]</small></small> forth out of the city, and stoned him with stones, that he died. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then they sent to Jezebel, saying, Naboth is stoned<small><small>[^33]</small></small>, and is dead. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And it came to pass, when Jezebel heard that Naboth was stoned, and was dead, that Jezebel said to Ahab, Arise<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, take possession of the vineyard of Naboth the Jezreelite, which he refused to give thee for money: for Naboth is not alive, but dead. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when Ahab heard that Naboth was dead, that Ahab rose up<small><small>[^35]</small></small> to go down to the vineyard of Naboth the Jezreelite, to take possession of it.

***Elijah Denounces Ahab and Jezebel*** ^bsb878

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came to Elijah the Tishbite, saying,<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Arise, go down to meet Ahab king of Israel, which \[is\] in Samaria<small><small>[^37]</small></small>: behold, \[he is\] in the vineyard of Naboth, whither he is gone down to possess it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thou shalt speak unto him, saying, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, Hast thou killed<small><small>[^38]</small></small>, and also taken possession? And thou shalt speak unto him, saying, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, In the place<small><small>[^39]</small></small> where dogs licked the blood of Naboth shall dogs lick thy blood, even thine. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Ahab said to Elijah, Hast thou found me<small><small>[^40]</small></small>, O mine enemy? And he answered, I have found \[thee\]: because thou hast sold<small><small>[^41]</small></small> thyself to work<small><small>[^42]</small></small> evil in the sight of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold<small><small>[^43]</small></small>, I will bring evil upon thee, and will take away thy posterity, and will cut off from Ahab him that pisseth<small><small>[^44]</small></small> against the wall, and him that is shut up<small><small>[^45]</small></small> and left in Israel, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And will make thine<small><small>[^46]</small></small> house like the house of Jeroboam the son of Nebat, and like the house of Baasha the son of Ahijah, for the provocation wherewith thou hast provoked \[me\] to anger, and made Israel to sin<small><small>[^47]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And of Jezebel<small><small>[^48]</small></small> also spake the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, saying, The dogs<small><small>[^49]</small></small> shall eat Jezebel by the wall<small><small>[^50]</small></small> of Jezreel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.21.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Him that dieth<small><small>[^51]</small></small> of Ahab in the city the dogs shall eat; and him that dieth in the field shall the fowls of the air eat.
