***Solomon’s Twelve Officers*** ^bsb805

***Solomon’s Prosperity*** ^bsb806

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.4.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Judah and Israel \[were\] many, as the sand<small><small>[^47]</small></small> which \[is\] by the sea in multitude, eating<small><small>[^48]</small></small> and drinking, and making merry. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.4.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Solomon<small><small>[^49]</small></small> reigned over all kingdoms from the river unto the land of the Philistines, and unto the border of Egypt: they brought<small><small>[^50]</small></small> presents, and served Solomon all the days of his life. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.4.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Solomon’s provision<small><small>[^51]</small></small> for one day was thirty measures<small><small>[^52]</small></small> of fine flour, and threescore measures of meal, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.4.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ten fat<small><small>[^53]</small></small> oxen, and twenty oxen out of the pastures, and an hundred sheep, beside harts<small><small>[^54]</small></small>, and roebucks<small><small>[^55]</small></small>, and fallowdeer<small><small>[^56]</small></small>, and fatted fowl. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.4.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he had dominion over all \[the region\] on this side the river, from Tiphsah even to Azzah<small><small>[^57]</small></small>, over all the kings<small><small>[^58]</small></small> on this side the river: and he had peace<small><small>[^59]</small></small> on all sides round about him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.4.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Judah and Israel dwelt safely<small><small>[^60]</small></small>, every man<small><small>[^61]</small></small> under his vine and under his fig tree, from Dan<small><small>[^62]</small></small> even to Beer–sheba, all the days of Solomon. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.4.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Solomon had forty thousand<small><small>[^63]</small></small> stalls of horses for his chariots, and twelve thousand horsemen. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.4.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And those officers<small><small>[^64]</small></small> provided victual for king Solomon, and for all that came unto king Solomon’s table, every man in his month: they lacked nothing. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Kgs.4.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Barley also and straw for the horses and dromedaries<small><small>[^65]</small></small> brought they unto the place where \[the officers\] were, every man according to his charge.
