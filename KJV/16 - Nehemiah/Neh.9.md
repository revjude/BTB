 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.9.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now<small><small>[^2]</small></small> in the twenty<small><small>[^3]</small></small> and fourth day of this month<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the children<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of Israel were assembled with fasting, and with sackclothes, and earth<small><small>[^6]</small></small> upon them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.9.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the seed<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of Israel separated themselves from all strangers<small><small>[^8]</small></small>, and stood and confessed<small><small>[^9]</small></small> their sins, and the iniquities of their fathers. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.9.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they stood<small><small>[^10]</small></small> up in their place, and read in the book of the law of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> their God \[one\] fourth<small><small>[^11]</small></small> part of the day; and \[another\] fourth part they confessed, and worshipped the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> their God.

<span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.9.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And foundest<small><small>[^27]</small></small> his heart faithful before thee, and madest<small><small>[^28]</small></small> a covenant with him to give the land of the Canaanites<small><small>[^29]</small></small>, the Hittites, the Amorites, and the Perizzites, and the Jebusites, and the Girgashites, to give \[it\], \[I say\], to his seed, and hast performed<small><small>[^30]</small></small> thy words; for thou \[art\] righteous<small><small>[^31]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.9.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And didst see<small><small>[^32]</small></small> the affliction of our fathers in Egypt, and heardest<small><small>[^33]</small></small> their cry by the Red sea; So didst<small><small>[^35]</small></small> thou get thee a name, as \[it is\] this day.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.9.11|11]]**<span persecutors<small><small>[^38]</small></small> thou threwest into the deeps, as a stone<small><small>[^39]</small></small> into the mighty waters. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.9.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover thou leddest<small><small>[^40]</small></small> them in the day by a cloudy pillar; and in the night
