***The Work Ridiculed*** ^bsb1200

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But it came to pass, that when Sanballat<small><small>[^2]</small></small> heard that we builded the wall, he was wroth, and took great indignation, and mocked<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the Jews. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he spake before his brethren and the army<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of Samaria, and said, What do these feeble<small><small>[^5]</small></small> Jews? will they fortify themselves<small><small>[^6]</small></small>? will they sacrifice<small><small>[^7]</small></small>? will they make an end in a day? will they revive<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the stones out of the heaps of the rubbish which are burned? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now Tobiah<small><small>[^9]</small></small> the Ammonite \[was\] by him, and he said, Even that which they build, if a fox go up, he shall even break down their stone wall. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, O our God; for we are despised<small><small>[^11]</small></small>: and turn<small><small>[^12]</small></small> their reproach upon their own head, and give them for a prey in the land of captivity: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And cover not<small><small>[^13]</small></small> their iniquity, and let not their sin<small><small>[^14]</small></small> be blotted out from before thee: for they have provoked \[thee\] to anger before the builders<small><small>[^15]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So built we the wall; and all the wall<small><small>[^16]</small></small> was joined together unto the half thereof: for the people<small><small>[^17]</small></small> had a mind<small><small>[^18]</small></small> to work.

***Discouragement Overcome*** ^bsb1201

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Nevertheless<small><small>[^27]</small></small> we made our prayer unto our God, and set a watch<small><small>[^28]</small></small> against them day and night, because of them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Judah said, The strength<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of the bearers<small><small>[^30]</small></small> of burdens is decayed, and \[there is\] much rubbish; so that we are not able to build the wall. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And our adversaries said, They shall not<small><small>[^31]</small></small> know, neither see, till we come in the midst among them, and slay them, and cause the work to cease. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, that when the Jews which dwelt by them came, they said unto us ten times<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, From all places<small><small>[^33]</small></small> whence ye shall return<small><small>[^34]</small></small> unto us \[they will be upon you\].

and the rulers \[were\] behind all the house of Judah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They which builded on the wall, and they that bare burdens<small><small>[^46]</small></small>, with those that laded, \[every one<small><small>[^47]</small></small>\] with one<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of his hands wrought in the work, and with the other \[hand\] held a weapon. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Neh.4.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the builders, every one had his sword girded by his side<small><small>[^49]</small></small>, and \[so\] builded. And he that sounded<small><small>[^50]</small></small> the trumpet \[was\] by me.
