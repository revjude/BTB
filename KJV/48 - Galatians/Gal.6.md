***Carry One Another’s Burdens*** ^bsb2822

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Brethren, if<small><small>[^2]</small></small> a man be overtaken<small><small>[^3]</small></small> in a fault, ye which are spiritual<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, restore<small><small>[^5]</small></small> such an one in the<small><small>[^6]</small></small> spirit of meekness; considering<small><small>[^7]</small></small> thyself, lest thou also be tempted. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Bear<small><small>[^8]</small></small> ye one another’s burdens, and so fulfil the law<small><small>[^9]</small></small> of Christ. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For if<small><small>[^10]</small></small> a man think himself to be something, when<small><small>[^11]</small></small> he is nothing, he deceiveth<small><small>[^12]</small></small> himself. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But let every man prove<small><small>[^13]</small></small> his own work, and then shall he have rejoicing<small><small>[^14]</small></small> in himself alone, and not<small><small>[^15]</small></small> in another. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For every man shall bear his own burden.<small><small>[^16]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let him that is taught in the word communicate unto him that teacheth in all good things.<small><small>[^17]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be not<small><small>[^18]</small></small> deceived; God<small><small>[^19]</small></small> is not mocked: for<small><small>[^20]</small></small> whatsoever a man soweth, that shall he also reap. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he that soweth to his<small><small>[^21]</small></small> flesh shall of the flesh reap<small><small>[^22]</small></small> corruption; but he that soweth to the<small><small>[^23]</small></small> Spirit shall of the Spirit<small><small>[^24]</small></small> reap life everlasting. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And let us<small><small>[^25]</small></small> not be weary in well<small><small>[^26]</small></small> doing: for<small><small>[^27]</small></small> in due season we shall reap, if<small><small>[^28]</small></small> we faint not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As we have therefore opportunity<small><small>[^29]</small></small>, let us do good<small><small>[^30]</small></small> unto all \[men\], especially<small><small>[^31]</small></small> unto them who are of the household of faith.

***Final Warnings and Blessings*** ^bsb2823

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye see how large a letter I have written<small><small>[^32]</small></small> unto you with mine own hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As many as desire<small><small>[^33]</small></small> to make a fair shew in the flesh, they constrain<small><small>[^34]</small></small> you to be circumcised; only lest<small><small>[^35]</small></small> they should suffer persecution for the cross of Christ. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For neither they themselves who are circumcised keep<small><small>[^36]</small></small> the law; but desire to have you circumcised, that they may<small><small>[^37]</small></small> glory in your flesh. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But God<small><small>[^38]</small></small> forbid that I<small><small>[^39]</small></small> should glory, save<small><small>[^40]</small></small> in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, by whom<small><small>[^41]</small></small> the world<small><small>[^42]</small></small> is crucified unto me, and I unto the world. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For in<small><small>[^43]</small></small> Christ Jesus neither<small><small>[^44]</small></small> circumcision availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision, but<small><small>[^45]</small></small> a new creature. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And as many as walk<small><small>[^46]</small></small> according to this rule, peace<small><small>[^47]</small></small> \[be\] on them, and mercy, and upon the Israel<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> From henceforth let<small><small>[^49]</small></small> no man trouble me: for I bear<small><small>[^50]</small></small> in my body the marks of the Lord Jesus. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.6.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Brethren, the grace<small><small>[^51]</small></small> of our Lord Jesus Christ \[be\] with your spirit.
