***Faith and Belief***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Jas.2|James 2:14–26]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2813

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.3.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For as many<small><small>[^24]</small></small> as are of the works of the law are under<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the curse: for it is written, Cursed<small><small>[^26]</small></small> \[is\] every one that continueth not in all things which are written in the book of the law to do them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.3.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But that<small><small>[^27]</small></small> no man is justified by the law in the sight of God, \[it is\] evident: for, The just<small><small>[^28]</small></small> shall live by faith. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.3.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the law<small><small>[^29]</small></small> is not of faith: but, The man<small><small>[^30]</small></small> that doeth them shall live in them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.3.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Christ hath redeemed<small><small>[^31]</small></small> us from the curse of the law, being<small><small>[^32]</small></small> made a curse for<small><small>[^33]</small></small> us: for it is written, Cursed<small><small>[^34]</small></small> \[is\] every one that hangeth on a tree: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.3.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That the blessing<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of Abraham might come on the Gentiles through<small><small>[^36]</small></small> Jesus Christ; that we might<small><small>[^37]</small></small> receive the promise of the Spirit through faith.

***The Purpose of the Law***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Rom.7|Romans 7:1–6]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2815

God for<small><small>[^59]</small></small>bid<small><small>[^60]</small></small>: for if there had been a law given which could have given life, verily righteousness<small><small>[^61]</small></small> should have been by the law. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.3.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the scripture hath concluded<small><small>[^62]</small></small> all under sin, that<small><small>[^63]</small></small> the promise by faith of Jesus Christ might be given to<small><small>[^64]</small></small> them that believe. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.3.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But before faith came<small><small>[^65]</small></small>, we were kept under<small><small>[^66]</small></small> the law, shut up unto the faith<small><small>[^67]</small></small> which should afterwards be revealed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.3.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore the law<small><small>[^68]</small></small> was our schoolmaster \[to bring us\] unto Christ, that we might be justified<small><small>[^69]</small></small> by faith. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.3.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But after that faith<small><small>[^70]</small></small> is come, we<small><small>[^71]</small></small> are no longer under a schoolmaster.
