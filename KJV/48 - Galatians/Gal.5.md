<span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.5.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This persuasion \[cometh\] not of him<small><small>[^20]</small></small> that calleth you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.5.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> A little leaven leaveneth the whole lump.<small><small>[^21]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.5.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have confidence<small><small>[^22]</small></small> in you through the Lord, that ye will be none otherwise minded: but<small><small>[^23]</small></small> he that troubleth you shall bear<small><small>[^24]</small></small> his judgment, whosoever<small><small>[^25]</small></small> he be. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gal.5.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I, brethren, if<small><small>[^26]</small></small> I yet preach circumcision, why<small><small>[^27]</small></small> do I yet suffer persecution?
