***The Glory of the LORD Returns to the Temple*** ^bsb1919

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Afterward he brought me to the gate, \[even\] the gate that<small><small>[^2]</small></small> looketh toward the east: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And, behold, the glory<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of the God of Israel came<small><small>[^4]</small></small> from the way of the east: and his voice<small><small>[^5]</small></small> \[was\] like a noise of many waters: and the earth<small><small>[^6]</small></small> shined with his glory. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[it was\] according to the appearance<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of the vision which I saw, \[even\] according to the vision that I saw when I came to destroy the city<small><small>[^8]</small></small>: and the visions \[were\] like the vision that I saw by the river<small><small>[^9]</small></small> Chebar; and I fell upon my face. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the glory<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came<small><small>[^11]</small></small> into the house by the way of the gate whose prospect \[is\] toward the east. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So the spirit<small><small>[^12]</small></small> took me up, and brought<small><small>[^13]</small></small> me into the inner court; and, behold, the glory<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> filled the house. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I heard<small><small>[^15]</small></small> \[him\] speaking unto me out of the house; and the man<small><small>[^16]</small></small> stood by me. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto me, Son of man, the place of my throne<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, and the place<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of the soles of my feet, where I<small><small>[^19]</small></small> will dwell in the midst of the children of Israel for ever, and my holy name, shall the house of Israel no more<small><small>[^20]</small></small> defile, \[neither\] they, nor their kings, by their whoredom, nor by the carcases<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of their kings in their high places. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In their setting<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of their threshold by my thresholds, and their post by my posts, and the wall between me and them<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, they have even defiled my holy name by their abominations that they have committed: wherefore I have consumed them in mine anger. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now let<small><small>[^24]</small></small> them put away their whoredom, and the carcases<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of their kings, far from me, and I<small><small>[^26]</small></small> will dwell in the midst of them for ever. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thou son of man, shew the house to the house of Israel, that they<small><small>[^27]</small></small> may be ashamed of their iniquities: and let them measure the pattern<small><small>[^28]</small></small>.<small><small>[^29]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if they be ashamed of all that they have done, shew them the form of the house, and the fashion thereof, and the goings out thereof, and the comings in thereof, and all the forms thereof, and all the ordinances<small><small>[^30]</small></small> thereof, and all the forms thereof, and all the laws thereof: and write \[it\] in their sight, that they may keep the whole form thereof, and all the ordinances thereof, and do<small><small>[^31]</small></small> them.<small><small>[^32]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This \[is\] the law of the house; Upon<small><small>[^33]</small></small> the top of the mountain the whole limit thereof round about \[shall be\] most holy. Behold, this \[is\] the law of the house.

***The Altar of Sacrifice*** ^bsb1920

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And these \[are\] the measures<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of the altar after the cubits: The cubit \[is\] a cubit and an hand breadth; even the bottom<small><small>[^35]</small></small> \[shall be\] a cubit, and the breadth a cubit, and the border thereof by the edge<small><small>[^36]</small></small> thereof round about \[shall be\] a span: and this \[shall be\] the higher place of the altar.<small><small>[^37]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And from the bottom \[upon\] the ground \[even\] to the lower settle<small><small>[^38]</small></small> \[shall be\] two cubits, and the breadth one cubit; and from the lesser settle \[even\] to the greater settle \[shall be\] four cubits, and the breadth \[one\] cubit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So the altar<small><small>[^39]</small></small> \[shall be\] four cubits; and from the altar<small><small>[^40]</small></small> and upward \[shall be\] four horns<small><small>[^41]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the altar \[shall be\] twelve \[cubits<small><small>[^42]</small></small>\] long, twelve broad, square<small><small>[^43]</small></small> in the four squares thereof. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the settle \[shall be\] fourteen \[cubits\] long and fourteen broad in the four squares thereof; and the border<small><small>[^44]</small></small> about it \[shall be\] half a cubit; and the bottom thereof \[shall be\] a cubit about; and his stairs<small><small>[^45]</small></small> shall look toward<small><small>[^46]</small></small> the east.

and they<small><small>[^56]</small></small> shall cleanse the altar, as they did cleanse \[it\] with the bullock. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When thou hast made an end of cleansing \[it\], thou shalt offer a young bullock without blemish, and a ram out of the flock without blemish. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thou shalt offer them before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and the priests shall cast salt<small><small>[^57]</small></small> upon them, and they shall offer them up \[for\] a burnt offering unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Seven days shalt thou prepare every day a goat \[for\] a sin offering: they shall also prepare a young bullock, and a ram out of the flock, without blemish.<small><small>[^58]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Seven days shall they purge the altar and purify it; and they shall<small><small>[^59]</small></small> consecrate themselves<small><small>[^60]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.43.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when these days are expired, it shall be, \[that\] upon<small><small>[^61]</small></small> the eighth day, and \[so\] forward, the priests shall make<small><small>[^62]</small></small> your burnt offerings upon the altar, and your peace offerings<small><small>[^63]</small></small>;
