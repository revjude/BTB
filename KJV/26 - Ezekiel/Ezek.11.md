<big><big>[[Ezek.11.1 Evil in High Places|⥃]]</big></big>***Evil in High Places***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover the spirit<small><small>[^2]</small></small> lifted me up, and brought me unto the east<small><small>[^3]</small></small> gate of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span> house, which looketh eastward: and behold<small><small>[^4]</small></small> at the door of the gate five and twenty men; among whom I saw Jaazaniah<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the son of Azur, and Pelatiah<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the son of Benaiah, princes of the people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said he unto me, Son of man, these \[are\] the men that devise mischief, and give wicked counsel in this city:<small><small>[^7]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which say, \[It is\] not<small><small>[^8]</small></small> near; let us build houses: this \[city<small><small>[^9]</small></small> is\] the caldron, and we \[be\] the flesh. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Therefore prophesy against them, prophesy, O son of man.<small><small>[^10]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Spirit<small><small>[^11]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> fell upon me, and said unto me, Speak<small><small>[^12]</small></small>; Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; Thus have<small><small>[^13]</small></small> ye said, O house of Israel: for<small><small>[^14]</small></small> I know the things that come into your mind, \[every one of\] them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye have multiplied your slain in this city, and ye have filled the streets thereof with the slain.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; Your<small><small>[^16]</small></small> slain whom ye have laid in the midst of it, they \[are\] the flesh, and this \[city is\] the caldron: but<small><small>[^17]</small></small> I will bring you forth out of the midst of it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye have feared the sword; and I will bring a sword upon you, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>.<small><small>[^18]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will bring you out of the midst thereof, and deliver<small><small>[^19]</small></small> you into the hands of strangers, and will<small><small>[^20]</small></small> execute judgments among you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye shall fall<small><small>[^21]</small></small> by the sword; I will judge you in<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the border of Israel; and ye<small><small>[^23]</small></small> shall know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This \[city\] shall not be your caldron, neither shall ye be the flesh in the midst thereof; \[but\] I will judge you in the border of Israel:<small><small>[^24]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And ye shall know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: for ye have not walked in my statutes, neither executed my judgments, but<small><small>[^25]</small></small> have done after the manners of the heathen that \[are\] round about you.<small><small>[^26]</small></small>

<big><big>[[Ezek.11.13 A Promise of Restoration|⥃]]</big></big>***A Promise of Restoration***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Jer.32|Jeremiah 32:36–44]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And it<small><small>[^27]</small></small> came to pass, when<small><small>[^28]</small></small> I prophesied, that Pelatiah the son of Benaiah died. Then<small><small>[^29]</small></small> fell I down upon my face, and cried with a loud voice, and said, Ah<small><small>[^30]</small></small> Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>! wilt thou make a full end of the remnant of Israel? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Again the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, thy brethren<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, \[even\] thy brethren, the men of thy kindred, and all the house of Israel wholly, \[are\] they unto whom the inhabitants of Jerusalem have said, Get<small><small>[^32]</small></small> you far from the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: unto<small><small>[^33]</small></small> us is this land given in possession. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore say, Thus saith<small><small>[^34]</small></small> the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; Although I have cast them far off among the heathen, and although I have scattered them among the countries, yet will I be to them as a<small><small>[^35]</small></small> little sanctuary in the countries where they shall come. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore say, Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; I will even gather you from the people, and assemble you out of the countries where ye have been scattered, and I will give you the land of Israel.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall come thither, and they shall take away all the detestable things thereof and all the abominations thereof from thence.<small><small>[^37]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will give<small><small>[^38]</small></small> them one heart, and I will put<small><small>[^39]</small></small> a new spirit within you; and I will take<small><small>[^40]</small></small> the stony heart out of their flesh, and will give them an heart of flesh: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That they may<small><small>[^41]</small></small> walk in my statutes, and keep mine ordinances, and do them: and they<small><small>[^42]</small></small> shall be my people, and I will be their God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.11.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But \[as for them\] whose<small><small>[^43]</small></small> heart walketh after the heart of their detestable<small><small>[^44]</small></small> things and their abominations, I will<small><small>[^45]</small></small> recompense their way upon their own heads, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>.
