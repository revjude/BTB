***A Prophecy against Israel’s Shepherds*** ^bsb1894

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, prophesy against the shepherds<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of Israel, prophesy, and say unto them, Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span> unto the shepherds; Woe<small><small>[^3]</small></small> \[be\] to the shepherds of Israel that do feed<small><small>[^4]</small></small> themselves! should not the shepherds feed the flocks? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye eat<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the fat, and ye clothe you with the wool, ye kill<small><small>[^6]</small></small> them that are fed: \[but\] ye feed not the flock. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The diseased<small><small>[^7]</small></small> have ye not strengthened, neither have ye healed that which was sick, neither have ye bound up \[that which was\] broken, neither have ye brought again that which was driven away, neither have ye sought<small><small>[^8]</small></small> that which was lost; but with<small><small>[^9]</small></small> force and with cruelty have ye ruled them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they were<small><small>[^10]</small></small> scattered, because \[there is\] no shepherd<small><small>[^11]</small></small>: and they became<small><small>[^12]</small></small> meat to all the beasts of the field, when they were scattered. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My sheep wandered<small><small>[^13]</small></small> through all the mountains, and upon every high hill: yea, my flock<small><small>[^14]</small></small> was scattered upon all the face of the earth, and none<small><small>[^15]</small></small> did search or seek \[after them\]. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore, ye shepherds, hear the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>;<small><small>[^16]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[As\] I live, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>, surely because my flock became a prey<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, and my flock became meat to every beast of the field, because \[there was\] no shepherd, neither did my shepherds search for my flock, but the shepherds<small><small>[^18]</small></small> fed themselves, and fed not my flock; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore, O ye shepherds, hear the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; Behold, I \[am<small><small>[^19]</small></small>\] against the shepherds; and I will<small><small>[^20]</small></small> require my flock at their hand, and cause<small><small>[^21]</small></small> them to cease from feeding the flock; neither shall<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the shepherds feed themselves any more; for I will<small><small>[^23]</small></small> deliver my flock from their mouth, that they may not be meat for them.

***The Good Shepherd***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Ps.23|Psalms 23:1–6]] <big>†</big> [[John.10|John 10:1–21]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1895

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]For thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; Behold, I<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, \[even\] I, will both search<small><small>[^25]</small></small> my sheep, and seek them out. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As a shepherd seeketh out<small><small>[^26]</small></small> his flock in the day that he is among his sheep \[that are\] scattered; so will I seek out my sheep, and will deliver them out of all places where they have been scattered in the cloudy<small><small>[^27]</small></small> and dark day. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will bring<small><small>[^28]</small></small> them out from the people, and gather them from the countries, and will bring them to their own land, and feed<small><small>[^29]</small></small> them upon the mountains of Israel by the rivers, and in all the inhabited places of the country. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will feed them<small><small>[^30]</small></small> in a good pasture, and upon the high mountains of Israel shall their fold be: there shall<small><small>[^31]</small></small> they lie in a good fold, and \[in\] a fat pasture shall they feed upon the mountains of Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will feed my flock, and I will cause them to lie down, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>.<small><small>[^32]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will seek that<small><small>[^33]</small></small> which was lost, and bring again that which was driven away, and will bind up \[that which was\] broken, and will strengthen that which was sick: but I<small><small>[^34]</small></small> will destroy the fat and the strong; I will feed<small><small>[^35]</small></small> them with judgment. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[as for\] you, O my flock, thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; Behold, I judge<small><small>[^36]</small></small> between cattle and cattle<small><small>[^37]</small></small>, between the rams and the he goats<small><small>[^38]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Seemeth it\] a small<small><small>[^39]</small></small> thing unto you to have<small><small>[^40]</small></small> eaten up the good pasture, but ye must tread down with your feet the residue of your pastures? and to have drunk of the deep waters, but ye must foul the residue with your feet? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[as for\] my flock, they eat that which ye have trodden with your feet; and they drink that which ye have fouled with your feet. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Therefore thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span> unto them; Behold<small><small>[^41]</small></small>, I, \[even\] I, will judge between the fat cattle and between the lean cattle. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because ye have<small><small>[^42]</small></small> thrust with side and with shoulder, and pushed<small><small>[^43]</small></small> all the diseased with your horns, till ye have scattered them abroad; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore will I<small><small>[^44]</small></small> save my flock, and they shall no more be a prey; and I<small><small>[^45]</small></small> will judge between cattle and cattle. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will<small><small>[^46]</small></small> set up one shepherd over them, and he shall feed them, \[even\] my servant<small><small>[^47]</small></small> David; he shall feed them, and he shall be their shepherd. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will be their God, and my servant David a prince<small><small>[^48]</small></small> among them; I the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> have spoken \[it\].<small><small>[^49]</small></small>

***The Covenant of Peace*** ^bsb1896

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will make<small><small>[^50]</small></small> with them a covenant of peace, and will<small><small>[^51]</small></small> cause the evil beasts to cease out of the land: and they<small><small>[^52]</small></small> shall dwell safely in the wilderness, and sleep in the woods. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will make them<small><small>[^53]</small></small> and the places round about my hill<small><small>[^54]</small></small> a blessing; and I will cause<small><small>[^55]</small></small> the shower<small><small>[^56]</small></small> to come down in his season; there shall be showers of blessing. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the tree<small><small>[^57]</small></small> of the field shall yield her fruit, and the earth shall yield her increase, and they shall be safe in their land, and shall know that<small><small>[^58]</small></small> I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, when I<small><small>[^59]</small></small> have broken the bands of their yoke, and delivered them out of the hand of those that served<small><small>[^60]</small></small> themselves of them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall<small><small>[^61]</small></small> no more be a prey to the heathen, neither<small><small>[^62]</small></small> shall the beast of the land devour them; but they shall dwell safely, and none shall make \[them\] afraid. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will<small><small>[^63]</small></small> raise up for them a plant of renown<small><small>[^64]</small></small>, and they shall be no more consumed<small><small>[^65]</small></small> with hunger in the land, neither<small><small>[^66]</small></small> bear the shame of the heathen any more. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus shall they know that I the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> their God \[am\] with them, and \[that\] they, \[even\] the house of Israel, \[are\] my people, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>.<small><small>[^67]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.34.31|31]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And ye my<small><small>[^68]</small></small> flock, the flock of my pasture, \[are\] men, \[and\] I<small><small>[^69]</small></small> \[am\] your God, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ezek.33|<< Ezekiel 33]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ezek.35|Ezekiel 35 >>]]</td></tr></table>
