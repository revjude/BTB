 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover he said unto me, Son of man, eat<small><small>[^2]</small></small> that thou findest; eat this roll, and go<small><small>[^3]</small></small> speak unto the house of Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So I opened my mouth, and he caused me to eat that roll.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto me, Son of man, cause thy belly to eat, and fill<small><small>[^5]</small></small> thy bowels with this roll that I give thee. $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And he said unto me, Son of man, go, get thee unto the house of Israel, and speak with my words unto them.<small><small>[^8]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thou<small><small>[^9]</small></small> \[art\] not sent to a people of a strange speech and of an hard language<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, \[but\] to the house of Israel; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Not to many people of a strange speech and of an hard language<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, whose words thou canst not understand. whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the spirit<small><small>[^22]</small></small> took me up, and I heard behind me a voice<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of a great rushing, \[saying\], Blessed<small><small>[^24]</small></small> \[be\] the glory<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> from his place. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[I heard\] also the noise<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of the wings of the living creatures that touched<small><small>[^27]</small></small> one another, and the noise<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of the wheels over against them, and a noise of a great rushing. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So the spirit<small><small>[^29]</small></small> lifted me up, and took me away, and I went in bitterness, in the heat of my spirit<small><small>[^30]</small></small>; ***A Watchman for Israel*** ^bsb1830

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass at the end of seven days, that the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, I have<small><small>[^34]</small></small> made thee a watchman<small><small>[^35]</small></small> unto the house of Israel: therefore hear<small><small>[^36]</small></small> the word at my mouth, and give them warning from me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When I say<small><small>[^37]</small></small> unto the wicked, Thou shalt surely die; but his<small><small>[^49]</small></small> blood will I require at thine hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.3.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Nevertheless if thou<small><small>[^50]</small></small> warn the righteous \[man\], that the righteous sin not, and he doth not sin, he shall<small><small>[^51]</small></small> surely live, because he is warned; also<small><small>[^52]</small></small> thou hast delivered thy soul.
