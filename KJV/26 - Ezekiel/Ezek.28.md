***A Prophecy against the Ruler of Tyre*** ^bsb1879

***A Lament for the King of Tyre*** ^bsb1880

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.28.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.28.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, take up<small><small>[^26]</small></small> a lamentation upon the king of Tyrus, and say unto him, Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; ***A Prophecy against Sidon*** ^bsb1881

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.28.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Again the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.28.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, set<small><small>[^56]</small></small> thy face against Zidon<small><small>[^57]</small></small>, and prophesy against it, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.28.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And say, Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; Behold, I \[am\] against<small><small>[^58]</small></small> thee, O Zidon; and I will<small><small>[^59]</small></small> be glorified in the midst of thee: and they shall know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, when I shall have executed judgments in her, and shall be<small><small>[^60]</small></small> sanctified in her. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.28.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I will send<small><small>[^61]</small></small> into her pestilence, and blood into her streets; and the wounded shall be judged in the midst of her by the sword upon her on every side; and they shall<small><small>[^62]</small></small> know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.28.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And there shall be no more a pricking<small><small>[^63]</small></small> brier unto the house of Israel, nor \[any\] grieving thorn of all \[that are\] round about them, that despised them; and they<small><small>[^64]</small></small> shall know that I \[am\] the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>.
