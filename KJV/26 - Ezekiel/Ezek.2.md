<big><big>[[Ezek.2.1 Ezekiel’s Call|⥃]]</big></big>***Ezekiel’s Call***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto me, Son<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of man, stand<small><small>[^3]</small></small> upon thy feet, and I will speak unto thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the spirit entered into me when he spake unto me, and set me upon my feet, that I heard him that spake unto me.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto me, Son of man, I send<small><small>[^5]</small></small> thee to the children of Israel, to a rebellious nation<small><small>[^6]</small></small> that hath rebelled<small><small>[^7]</small></small> against me: they and their fathers have transgressed against me, \[even\] unto this very day. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For \[they<small><small>[^8]</small></small> are\] impudent<small><small>[^9]</small></small> children and stiffhearted. I do send thee unto them; and thou shalt say unto them, Thus<small><small>[^10]</small></small> saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.2.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they, whether<small><small>[^11]</small></small> they will hear, or whether they will forbear, (for they \[are\] a rebellious house,) yet<small><small>[^12]</small></small> shall know that there hath been a prophet among them. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.2.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thou, son of man, be not<small><small>[^13]</small></small> afraid of them, neither be afraid of their words, though briers<small><small>[^14]</small></small> and thorns \[be\] with thee, and thou dost dwell among scorpions<small><small>[^15]</small></small>: be not afraid of their words, nor be dismayed at their looks, though they<small><small>[^16]</small></small> \[be\] a rebellious house. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.2.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thou<small><small>[^17]</small></small> shalt speak my words unto them, whether<small><small>[^18]</small></small> they will hear, or whether they will forbear: for they \[are\] most rebellious<small><small>[^19]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.2.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But thou, son of man, hear what I say unto thee; Be<small><small>[^20]</small></small> not thou rebellious like that rebellious house: open<small><small>[^21]</small></small> thy mouth, and eat that I give thee.
