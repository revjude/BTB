<big><big>[[Ezek.32.1 A Lament for Pharaoh King of Egypt|⥃]]</big></big>***A Lament for Pharaoh King of Egypt***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass in the twelfth<small><small>[^2]</small></small> year, in the twelfth month, in the first \[day\] of the month, \[that\] the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying,<small><small>[^3]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, take up<small><small>[^4]</small></small> a lamentation for Pharaoh king of Egypt, and say unto him, Thou art like<small><small>[^5]</small></small> a young lion of the nations, and thou \[art\] as<small><small>[^6]</small></small> a whale<small><small>[^7]</small></small> in the seas: and thou camest forth with thy rivers, and troubledst<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the waters with thy feet, and fouledst their rivers. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; I will therefore spread out my net over thee with a company of many people; and they shall bring thee up in my net.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then will I leave thee upon the land, I will cast thee forth upon the open field, and will cause all the fowls of the heaven to remain upon thee, and I will fill the beasts of the whole earth with thee.<small><small>[^10]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I<small><small>[^11]</small></small> will lay thy flesh upon the mountains, and fill the valleys with thy height. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will also water<small><small>[^12]</small></small> with thy blood the land<small><small>[^13]</small></small> wherein thou swimmest<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, \[even\] to the mountains; and the rivers shall be full of thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when I shall put thee out<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, I will cover the heaven<small><small>[^16]</small></small>, and make<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the stars thereof dark; I will cover the sun<small><small>[^18]</small></small> with a cloud, and the moon<small><small>[^19]</small></small> shall not give her light. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All the bright lights of heaven<small><small>[^20]</small></small> will I make dark<small><small>[^21]</small></small> over thee, and set darkness upon<small><small>[^22]</small></small> thy land, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will also vex<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the hearts of many people, when<small><small>[^24]</small></small> I shall bring thy destruction among the nations, into the countries which thou hast not known. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yea, I will make many people amazed<small><small>[^25]</small></small> at thee, and their kings shall be horribly afraid for thee, when I shall brandish my sword<small><small>[^26]</small></small> before them; and they<small><small>[^27]</small></small> shall tremble at \[every\] moment, every man for his own life, in the day of thy fall. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]For thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; The sword<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of the king of Babylon shall come upon thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By the swords of the mighty will I cause thy multitude to fall, the terrible<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of the nations, all of them: and they shall<small><small>[^30]</small></small> spoil the pomp of Egypt, and all the multitude thereof shall be destroyed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will destroy<small><small>[^31]</small></small> also all the beasts thereof from beside the great waters; neither<small><small>[^32]</small></small> shall the foot of man trouble them any more, nor the hoofs of beasts trouble them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then will I make their waters deep, and cause<small><small>[^33]</small></small> their rivers to run like oil, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When I shall make the land of Egypt desolate, and the country shall be destitute of that whereof<small><small>[^34]</small></small> it was full, when I shall smite all them that dwell therein, then<small><small>[^35]</small></small> shall they know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.32.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This \[is\] the lamentation wherewith they shall lament her: the daughters of the nations shall lament her: they shall lament for her, \[even\] for Egypt, and for all her multitude, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>.<small><small>[^36]</small></small>
