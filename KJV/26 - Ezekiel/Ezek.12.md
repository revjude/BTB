***Signs of the Coming Captivity*** ^bsb1845

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> also came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, thou<small><small>[^2]</small></small> dwellest in the midst of a rebellious house, which<small><small>[^3]</small></small> have eyes to see, and see not; they have ears to hear, and hear not: for<small><small>[^4]</small></small> they \[are\] a rebellious house. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore, thou son of man, prepare<small><small>[^5]</small></small> thee stuff<small><small>[^6]</small></small> for removing, and remove by day in their sight; and thou shalt remove from thy place to another place in their sight: it may<small><small>[^7]</small></small> be they will consider, though they \[be\] a rebellious house. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then shalt thou bring forth thy stuff by day in their sight, as stuff for removing: and thou shalt go forth at even<small><small>[^8]</small></small> in their sight, as they that go forth into<small><small>[^9]</small></small> captivity. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Dig thou<small><small>[^10]</small></small> through the wall in their sight, and carry out thereby. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In their sight shalt thou bear \[it\] upon \[thy\] shoulders, \[and\] carry \[it\] forth in the twilight: thou shalt<small><small>[^11]</small></small> cover<small><small>[^12]</small></small> thy face, that thou see not the ground: for I<small><small>[^13]</small></small> have set thee \[for\] a sign unto the house of Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I did so<small><small>[^14]</small></small> as I was commanded: I brought<small><small>[^15]</small></small> forth my stuff by day, as stuff for captivity, and in the even I digged<small><small>[^16]</small></small> through the wall with mine hand; I brought \[it\] forth in the twilight, \[and\] I bare \[it\] upon \[my\] shoulder in their sight. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And in the morning came the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, hath not the house of Israel, the rebellious<small><small>[^17]</small></small> house, said unto thee, What<small><small>[^18]</small></small> doest thou? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Say thou unto them, Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; This<small><small>[^19]</small></small> burden \[concerneth\] the prince<small><small>[^20]</small></small> in Jerusalem, and all the house of Israel that \[are\] among them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Say, I \[am<small><small>[^21]</small></small>\] your sign: like as I have done, so shall it be done unto them: they shall remove \[and\] go<small><small>[^22]</small></small> into captivity. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the prince that \[is\] among them shall bear upon \[his\] shoulder in the twilight, and shall go forth: they shall dig through the wall to carry out thereby: he shall cover his face, that he see not the ground with \[his\] eyes.<small><small>[^23]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My net<small><small>[^24]</small></small> also will I spread upon him, and he shall be taken in my snare: and I<small><small>[^25]</small></small> will bring him to Babylon \[to\] the land of the Chaldeans; yet shall he not see it, though he shall die there. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will scatter<small><small>[^26]</small></small> toward every wind all that \[are\] about him to help him, and all his bands; and I will draw<small><small>[^27]</small></small> out the sword after them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, when I shall scatter them among the nations, and disperse them in the countries.<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will<small><small>[^29]</small></small> leave a few men<small><small>[^30]</small></small> of them from the sword, from the famine, and from the pestilence; that they<small><small>[^31]</small></small> may declare all their abominations among the heathen whither they come; and they<small><small>[^32]</small></small> shall know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came to me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, eat thy bread with quaking, and drink thy water with trembling and with carefulness;<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And say unto the people of the land, Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span> of the inhabitants of Jerusalem, \[and\] of the land of Israel; They shall eat their bread with carefulness<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, and drink their water with astonishment, that her<small><small>[^35]</small></small> land may be desolate from all that is therein<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, because<small><small>[^37]</small></small> of the violence of all them that dwell therein. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the cities that are inhabited shall be laid waste, and the land shall be desolate; and ye shall know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^38]</small></small>

***The Presumptuous Proverb*** ^bsb1846

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, what<small><small>[^39]</small></small> \[is\] that proverb \[that\] ye have in the land of Israel, saying, The days<small><small>[^40]</small></small> are prolonged, and every vision faileth? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Tell them therefore, Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; I will<small><small>[^41]</small></small> make this proverb to cease, and they shall no more use it as a proverb in Israel; but say unto them, The days<small><small>[^42]</small></small> are at hand, and the effect of every vision. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For there shall be no more any vain vision nor flattering divination within the house of Israel.<small><small>[^43]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: I will<small><small>[^44]</small></small> speak, and the word that I shall speak shall come to pass; it shall be no more prolonged: for in your<small><small>[^45]</small></small> days, O rebellious<small><small>[^46]</small></small> house, will I say the word, and will perform it, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Again the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came to me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, behold, \[they of\] the house of Israel say, The vision that he seeth \[is\] for<small><small>[^47]</small></small> many days \[to come\], and he prophesieth of the times \[that are\] far off. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.12.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore say unto them, Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; There shall<small><small>[^48]</small></small> none of my words be prolonged any more, but the word which I have spoken shall be done, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ezek.11|<< Ezekiel 11]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ezek.13|Ezekiel 13 >>]]</td></tr></table>
