<big><big>[[Ezek.15.1 Jerusalem the Useless Vine|⥃]]</big></big>***Jerusalem the Useless Vine***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.15.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.15.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, What<small><small>[^2]</small></small> is the vine tree more than any tree, \[or than\] a branch which is among<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the trees of the forest? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.15.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Shall wood be taken thereof to do any work? or will \[men\] take a pin of it to hang any vessel thereon?<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.15.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, it is cast<small><small>[^5]</small></small> into the fire<small><small>[^6]</small></small> for fuel; the fire devoureth both the ends of it, and the midst of it is burned. Is it meet<small><small>[^7]</small></small> for \[any\] work? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.15.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, when it was whole, it was meet<small><small>[^8]</small></small> for no work: how much less shall it be meet yet for \[any\] work, when the fire hath devoured it, and it is burned? 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.15.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Therefore thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; As the vine tree among the trees of the forest, which I have given to the fire for fuel, so will I give the inhabitants of Jerusalem.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.15.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will set<small><small>[^10]</small></small> my face against them; they shall<small><small>[^11]</small></small> go out from \[one\] fire, and \[another\] fire shall devour them; and ye shall<small><small>[^12]</small></small> know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, when I set my face against them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.15.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will<small><small>[^13]</small></small> make the land desolate, because they have committed a trespass<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ezek.14|<< Ezekiel 14]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ezek.16|Ezekiel 16 >>]]</td></tr></table>
