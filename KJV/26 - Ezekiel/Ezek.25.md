<big><big>[[Ezek.25.1 A Prophecy against Ammon|⥃]]</big></big>***A Prophecy against Ammon***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came again unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Son of man, set thy face<small><small>[^2]</small></small> against the Ammonites<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, and prophesy against them; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And say unto the Ammonites, Hear the word of the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; Because thou saidst<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, Aha, against my sanctuary, when it was profaned; and against the land of Israel, when it was desolate; and against the house of Judah, when they went into captivity; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, therefore I will deliver thee to the men<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of the east<small><small>[^6]</small></small> for a possession, and they shall set their palaces in thee, and make their dwellings in thee: they shall eat<small><small>[^7]</small></small> thy fruit, and they shall drink thy milk. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will make Rabbah<small><small>[^8]</small></small> a stable<small><small>[^9]</small></small> for camels, and the Ammonites a couchingplace for flocks: and ye<small><small>[^10]</small></small> shall know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; Because thou hast<small><small>[^11]</small></small> clapped \[thine\] hands<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, and stamped<small><small>[^13]</small></small> with the feet<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, and rejoiced<small><small>[^15]</small></small> in heart<small><small>[^16]</small></small> with all thy despite against the land of Israel; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, therefore I will stretch<small><small>[^17]</small></small> out mine hand upon thee, and will<small><small>[^18]</small></small> deliver thee for a spoil<small><small>[^19]</small></small> to the heathen; and I will cut thee off from the people, and I will cause thee to perish out of the countries: I will destroy thee; and thou shalt know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.

<big><big>[[Ezek.25.8 A Prophecy against Moab|⥃]]</big></big>***A Prophecy against Moab***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; Because that Moab<small><small>[^20]</small></small> and Seir<small><small>[^21]</small></small> do say, Behold, the house<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of Judah \[is\] like unto all the heathen; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore, behold, I will open the side<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of Moab from the cities, from his cities \[which are\] on his frontiers, the glory of the country, Beth–jeshimoth<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, Baal–meon<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, and Kiriathaim, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Unto the men<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of the east with the Ammonites<small><small>[^27]</small></small>, and will give them in possession, that the Ammonites may<small><small>[^28]</small></small> not be remembered among the nations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will<small><small>[^29]</small></small> execute judgments upon<small><small>[^30]</small></small> Moab; and they<small><small>[^31]</small></small> shall know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.

<big><big>[[Ezek.25.12 A Prophecy against Edom|⥃]]</big></big>***A Prophecy against Edom***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; Because<small><small>[^32]</small></small> that Edom hath dealt against the house of Judah by taking vengeance<small><small>[^33]</small></small>, and hath greatly offended, and revenged himself upon them; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>; I will also<small><small>[^34]</small></small> stretch out mine hand upon Edom, and will<small><small>[^35]</small></small> cut off man and beast from it; and I will make it desolate from Teman<small><small>[^36]</small></small>; and they of Dedan shall fall by the sword<small><small>[^37]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezek.25.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will lay my vengeance upon Edom by the hand<small><small>[^38]</small></small> of my people Israel: and they shall do in Edom according to mine anger and according to my fury; and they shall know<small><small>[^39]</small></small> my vengeance, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>.
