<big><big>[[2Cor.10.1 Paul’s Apostolic Authority|⥃]]</big></big>***Paul’s Apostolic Authority***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now I Paul<small><small>[^2]</small></small> myself beseech<small><small>[^3]</small></small> you by<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the meekness and gentleness of Christ, who in presence<small><small>[^5]</small></small> \[am\] base<small><small>[^6]</small></small> among you, but being absent am bold<small><small>[^7]</small></small> toward you: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I beseech \[you\], that I<small><small>[^8]</small></small> may not be bold when I am present with that confidence, wherewith I think<small><small>[^9]</small></small> to be bold against some, which think of us as if we walked<small><small>[^10]</small></small> according to the flesh. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For though we walk<small><small>[^11]</small></small> in the flesh, we do<small><small>[^12]</small></small> not war after the flesh: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> (For the weapons<small><small>[^13]</small></small> of our<small><small>[^14]</small></small> warfare \[are\] not carnal, but mighty<small><small>[^15]</small></small> through God<small><small>[^16]</small></small> to the<small><small>[^17]</small></small> pulling down of strong holds;) <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Casting down<small><small>[^18]</small></small> imaginations<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, and every<small><small>[^20]</small></small> high thing that exalteth itself against the knowledge of God, and bringing<small><small>[^21]</small></small> into captivity every thought<small><small>[^22]</small></small> to the obedience<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of Christ; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And havin<small><small>[^24]</small></small>g in a readiness to revenge all disobedience, when<small><small>[^25]</small></small> your obedience is fulfilled. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Do ye look<small><small>[^26]</small></small> on things after the outward appearance? If<small><small>[^27]</small></small> any man trust to himself that he is Christ’s, let him of himself think this again, that, as he \[is\] Christ’s, even<small><small>[^28]</small></small> so \[are\] we Christ’s. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For though<small><small>[^29]</small></small> I should boast somewhat more of our authority, which the Lord hath given us for edification, and not for your destruction, I should not<small><small>[^30]</small></small> be ashamed: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That I may not seem as if I would terrify<small><small>[^31]</small></small> you by letters. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For \[his\] letters, say they<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, \[are\] weighty and powerful; but<small><small>[^33]</small></small> \[his\] bodily presence \[is\] weak, and \[his<small><small>[^34]</small></small>\] speech contemptible. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let such<small><small>[^35]</small></small> an one think this, that, such as we are in word by letters when we are absent, such \[will we be\] also in deed when we are present. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For we dare not<small><small>[^36]</small></small> make ourselves of the number, or compare ourselves with some that commend themselves: but they measuring themselves by themselves, and comparing themselves among themselves, are not wise<small><small>[^37]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But we will not<small><small>[^38]</small></small> boast of things without \[our\] measure, but according<small><small>[^39]</small></small> to the measure of the rule<small><small>[^40]</small></small> which God hath distributed to us, a measure to reach even unto you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For we stretch not<small><small>[^41]</small></small> ourselves beyond \[our measure\], as though we reached not unto you: for we are come as far as to you also in \[preaching\] the gospel<small><small>[^42]</small></small> of Christ: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Not boasting<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of things without \[our\] measure, \[that is\], of other men’s labours; but having hope, when your faith is increased, that we shall be enlarged by you<small><small>[^44]</small></small> according to our rule abundantly, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To preach<small><small>[^45]</small></small> the gospel in the \[regions\] beyond you, \[and\] not to boast in another man’s line<small><small>[^46]</small></small> of things made ready to our hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But he that glorieth, let him glory in the Lord.<small><small>[^47]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.10.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For not<small><small>[^48]</small></small> he that commendeth himself is approved<small><small>[^49]</small></small>, but<small><small>[^50]</small></small> whom the Lord commendeth. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[2Cor.9|<< 2 Corinthians 9]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[2Cor.11|2 Corinthians 11 >>]]</td></tr></table>
