<big><big>[[2Cor.8.1 Generosity Commended|⥃]]</big></big>***Generosity Commended***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Phil.4|Philippians 4:10–20]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover, brethren, we<small><small>[^2]</small></small> do you to wit of the grace<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of God bestowed on the churches<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of Macedonia; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> How that in<small><small>[^5]</small></small> a great trial of affliction the abundance<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of their joy and their deep<small><small>[^7]</small></small> poverty abounded unto the riches<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of their liberality<small><small>[^9]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For to<small><small>[^10]</small></small> \[their\] power, I bear<small><small>[^11]</small></small> record, yea, and beyond<small><small>[^12]</small></small> \[their\] power \[they were\] willing of themselves; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praying us with much intreaty that<small><small>[^13]</small></small> we would receive the gift, and \[take upon us\] the fellowship of the ministering<small><small>[^14]</small></small> to the saints. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[this they did\], not as we hoped, but first<small><small>[^15]</small></small> gave their own selves to the Lord, and unto<small><small>[^16]</small></small> us by the will of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Insomuch that we<small><small>[^17]</small></small> desired Titus, that as he had begun, so he would also finish in you the same grace<small><small>[^18]</small></small> also. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore, as<small><small>[^19]</small></small> ye abound in every \[thing\], \[in\] faith<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and utterance, and knowledge<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, and \[in\] all diligence, and \[in\] your<small><small>[^22]</small></small> love to us, \[see<small><small>[^23]</small></small>\] that ye abound in this<small><small>[^24]</small></small> grace also. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I speak<small><small>[^25]</small></small> not by commandment, but by occasion<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of the forwardness of others, and to prove<small><small>[^27]</small></small> the sincerity of your love. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For ye know the grace<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of our Lord Jesus Christ, that, though<small><small>[^29]</small></small> he was rich, yet for<small><small>[^30]</small></small> your sakes he became<small><small>[^31]</small></small> poor, that ye<small><small>[^32]</small></small> through his poverty might be rich. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And herein I give<small><small>[^33]</small></small> \[my\] advice: for this is expedient<small><small>[^34]</small></small> for you, who have begun before, not only to do, but also to be<small><small>[^35]</small></small> forward<small><small>[^36]</small></small> a year ago. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now therefore perform the doing \[of it\]; that as \[there was\] a readiness to will, so \[there may be\] a performance also out of that which ye have. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For if<small><small>[^37]</small></small> there be first a willing mind, \[it is\] accepted according to that a man hath, \[and\] not according to that he hath not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For \[I mean\] not<small><small>[^38]</small></small> that other men be eased, and ye burdened: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But by an equality, \[that\] now at this time your abundance \[may be a supply\] for their want, that their abundance also may be \[a supply\] for your want: that there may be equality: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As it is written, He that \[had gathered\] much had nothing over; and he that \[had gathered\] little had no lack.<small><small>[^39]</small></small>

<big><big>[[2Cor.8.16 Titus Commended|⥃]]</big></big>***Titus Commended***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Titus.1|Titus 1:1–4]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But thanks<small><small>[^40]</small></small> \[be\] to God, which put the same earnest<small><small>[^41]</small></small> care into the heart of Titus for you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For indeed he accepted<small><small>[^42]</small></small> the exhortation; but<small><small>[^43]</small></small> being more forward, of his own accord he went unto you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And we have sent with him the brother<small><small>[^44]</small></small>, whose praise \[is\] in the gospel throughout<small><small>[^45]</small></small> all the churches; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And not \[that\] only, but<small><small>[^46]</small></small> who was also chosen of the churches to travel with us with this grace<small><small>[^47]</small></small>, which is administered by us to the<small><small>[^48]</small></small> glory of the same Lord, and \[declaration of\] your ready mind: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Avoiding this, that<small><small>[^49]</small></small> no man should blame us in this abundance which is administered by us: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Providing for<small><small>[^50]</small></small> honest things, not<small><small>[^51]</small></small> only in the sight of the Lord, but also in the sight of men. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And we have sent with them our brother, whom<small><small>[^52]</small></small> we have oftentimes proved diligent in many things, but now much more diligent, upon the great confidence which \[I have<small><small>[^53]</small></small>\] in you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whether \[any do enquire\] of Titus<small><small>[^54]</small></small>, \[he is\] my<small><small>[^55]</small></small> partner and fellowhelper<small><small>[^56]</small></small> concerning you: or our brethren \[be enquired of\], \[they are\] the messengers<small><small>[^57]</small></small> of the churches, \[and\] the glory of Christ. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.8.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore shew ye to them, and before the churches, the proof of your love, and of our boasting on your behalf. <small><small>[^58]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td
