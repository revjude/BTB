***Examine Yourselves*** ^bsb2806

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This \[is\] the third<small><small>[^2]</small></small> \[time\] I am coming to you. In<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the mouth of two or three witnesses shall every word be established. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I told<small><small>[^4]</small></small> you before, and foretell you, as if I were present, the second time; and being<small><small>[^5]</small></small> absent now I write to them which heretofore<small><small>[^6]</small></small> have sinned, and to all other, that, if I come again, I will not spare: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Since ye seek<small><small>[^7]</small></small> a proof of Christ<small><small>[^8]</small></small> speaking in me, which<small><small>[^9]</small></small> to you-ward is not weak, but is mighty in you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For though he was<small><small>[^10]</small></small> crucified through weakness, yet<small><small>[^11]</small></small> he liveth by the power of God. For we also<small><small>[^12]</small></small> are weak in him<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, but<small><small>[^14]</small></small> we shall live with him by the power of God toward you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Examine<small><small>[^15]</small></small> yourselves, whether ye be in the faith<small><small>[^16]</small></small>; prove your own selves. Know<small><small>[^17]</small></small> ye not your own selves, how that Jesus Christ<small><small>[^18]</small></small> is in you, except ye be reprobates<small><small>[^19]</small></small>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I trust that ye shall know that we are not reprobates.<small><small>[^20]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now I pray<small><small>[^21]</small></small> to God that ye do no evil; not that we should appear approved<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, but that ye should do that which is honest<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, though we be as reprobates<small><small>[^24]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For we can do nothing against the truth, but for the truth.<small><small>[^25]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For we are glad, when<small><small>[^26]</small></small> we are weak, and ye are strong: and this also we wish, \[even<small><small>[^27]</small></small>\] your perfection. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore I write<small><small>[^28]</small></small> these things being absent, lest<small><small>[^29]</small></small> being present I should use sharpness, according<small><small>[^30]</small></small> to the power which the Lord hath given me to edification, and not to destruction.

***Benediction and Farewell*** ^bsb2807

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Finally, brethren, farewell<small><small>[^31]</small></small>. Be perfect<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, be of good<small><small>[^33]</small></small> comfort, be of one<small><small>[^34]</small></small> mind, live<small><small>[^35]</small></small> in peace; and the God<small><small>[^36]</small></small> of love and peace shall be with<small><small>[^37]</small></small> you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Greet one another with an holy kiss.<small><small>[^38]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All the saints salute you.<small><small>[^39]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Cor.13.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The grace<small><small>[^40]</small></small> of the Lord Jesus Christ, and the love<small><small>[^41]</small></small> of God, and the communion<small><small>[^42]</small></small> of the Holy Ghost, \[be\] with you all.
