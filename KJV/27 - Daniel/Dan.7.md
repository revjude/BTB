***Daniel’s Vision of the Four Beasts***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Rev.13|Revelation 13:1–10]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1959

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the first year of Belshazzar<small><small>[^2]</small></small> king of Babylon Daniel<small><small>[^3]</small></small> had<small><small>[^4]</small></small> a dream and visions<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of his head upon his bed: then he wrote<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the dream, \[and\] told the sum of the matters<small><small>[^7]</small></small>.<small><small>[^8]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Daniel spake and said, I saw in my vision by night, and, behold, the four<small><small>[^9]</small></small> winds of the heaven strove upon the great<small><small>[^10]</small></small> sea. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And four<small><small>[^11]</small></small> great beasts<small><small>[^12]</small></small> came up from the sea, diverse one from another. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The first \[was\] like<small><small>[^13]</small></small> a lion, and had eagle’s wings: I beheld till the wings<small><small>[^14]</small></small> thereof were plucked, and it<small><small>[^15]</small></small> was lifted<small><small>[^16]</small></small> up from the earth, and made stand upon the feet as a man, and a<small><small>[^17]</small></small> man’s heart was given to it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And behold another<small><small>[^18]</small></small> beast, a second, like to a bear, and it raised up itself on one side<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, and \[it had\] three ribs<small><small>[^20]</small></small> in the mouth of it between the teeth of it: and they said thus unto it, Arise<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, devour much flesh. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> After this I beheld, and lo<small><small>[^22]</small></small> another<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, like a leopard, which had upon the back of it four wings<small><small>[^24]</small></small> of a fowl; the beast had also four heads<small><small>[^25]</small></small>; and dominion was given to it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> After this I it devoured and brake in pieces, and stamped the residue with the feet of it: and it \[was\] diverse from all the beasts that \[were\] before it; and it had ten<small><small>[^28]</small></small> horns. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I considered the horns, and, behold, there came up among them another<small><small>[^29]</small></small> little horn, before whom there were three of the first horns plucked up by the roots: and, behold, in this horn \[were\] eyes like<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the eyes of man, and a mouth<small><small>[^31]</small></small> speaking great things.

***Daniel’s Vision of the Ancient of Days*** ^bsb1960

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I beheld till<small><small>[^32]</small></small> the thrones were cast down, and the Ancient<small><small>[^33]</small></small> of days did sit, whose<small><small>[^34]</small></small> garment \[was\] white as snow, and the hair of his head like the pure wool: his throne<small><small>[^35]</small></small> \[was like\] the fiery flame, \[and\] his wheels<small><small>[^36]</small></small> \[as\] burning fire. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> A fiery<small><small>[^37]</small></small> stream issued and came forth from before him: thousand thousands<small><small>[^38]</small></small> ministered unto him, and ten thousand times ten thousand stood before him: the judgment<small><small>[^39]</small></small> was set, and the books were opened. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I beheld then because of the voice<small><small>[^40]</small></small> of the great words which the horn spake: I beheld \[even<small><small>[^41]</small></small>\] till the beast was slain, and his body destroyed, and given to the burning flame. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As concerning the rest<small><small>[^42]</small></small> of the beasts, they had their dominion taken away: yet their lives were prolonged<small><small>[^43]</small></small> for a season and time.

***Daniel’s Vision of the Son of Man*** ^bsb1961

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I saw in the night visions, and, behold, \[one\] like<small><small>[^44]</small></small> the Son of man came with the clouds of heaven, and came to the Ancient<small><small>[^45]</small></small> of days, and they<small><small>[^46]</small></small> brought him near before him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there was given<small><small>[^47]</small></small> him dominion, and glory, and a kingdom, that all<small><small>[^48]</small></small> people, nations, and languages, should serve him: his dominion \[is\] an everlasting<small><small>[^49]</small></small> dominion, which shall not pass away, and his kingdom \[that\] which shall not be destroyed.

and he shall be diverse from the first, and he shall subdue three kings. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall speak<small><small>[^79]</small></small> \[great\] words against the most High, and shall wear out<small><small>[^80]</small></small> the saints of the most High, and think<small><small>[^81]</small></small> to change times and laws: and they shall be given into his hand until a time<small><small>[^82]</small></small> and times and the dividing of time. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the judgment shall sit, and they shall take away his dominion, to consume and to destroy \[it\] unto the end.<small><small>[^83]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the kingdom and<small><small>[^84]</small></small> dominion, and the greatness of the kingdom under the whole heaven, shall be given to the people of the saints of the most High, whose kingdom<small><small>[^85]</small></small> \[is\] an everlasting kingdom, and all<small><small>[^86]</small></small> dominions<small><small>[^87]</small></small> shall serve and obey him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.7.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hitherto \[is\] the end<small><small>[^88]</small></small> of the matter.
