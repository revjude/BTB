<big><big>[[Dan.9.1 Daniel’s Prayer for His People|⥃]]</big></big>***Daniel’s Prayer for His People***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the first year of Darius<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the son of Ahasuerus<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, of the seed of the Medes, which<small><small>[^4]</small></small> was made king over the realm of the Chaldeans;<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the first year of his reign I Daniel understood<small><small>[^6]</small></small> by books the number of the years, whereof the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came to Jeremiah<small><small>[^7]</small></small> the prophet, that he would accomplish seventy years in the desolations<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of Jerusalem. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And I set<small><small>[^9]</small></small> my face unto the Lord God, to seek by prayer and supplications, with<small><small>[^10]</small></small> fasting, and sackcloth, and ashes: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I prayed unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> my God, and made<small><small>[^11]</small></small> my confession, and said, O Lord, the great<small><small>[^12]</small></small> and dreadful God, keeping the covenant and mercy to them that love him, and to them that keep his commandments; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> We have sinned<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, and have committed iniquity, and have done wickedly, and have rebelled, even by departing<small><small>[^14]</small></small> from thy precepts and from thy judgments: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither have we<small><small>[^15]</small></small> hearkened unto thy servants the prophets, which spake in thy name to our kings<small><small>[^16]</small></small>, our princes, and our fathers, and to all the people of the land. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O Lord, righteousness<small><small>[^17]</small></small> \[belongeth\] unto thee<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, but unto us<small><small>[^19]</small></small> confusion of faces, as at this day; to the men of Judah, and to the inhabitants of Jerusalem, and unto all Israel, \[that are\] near<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and \[that are\] far off, through all the countries whither<small><small>[^21]</small></small> thou hast driven them, because of their trespass that they have trespassed against thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O Lord, to us<small><small>[^22]</small></small> \[belongeth\] confusion of face, to our kings, to our princes, and to our fathers, because<small><small>[^23]</small></small> we have sinned against thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To the Lord<small><small>[^24]</small></small> our God \[belong\] mercies and forgivenesses, though<small><small>[^25]</small></small> we have rebelled against him; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither have we obeyed the voice of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> our God, to walk in his laws, which<small><small>[^26]</small></small> he set before us by his servants the prophets. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yea, all<small><small>[^27]</small></small> Israel have transgressed thy law, even by departing, that they might not obey thy voice; therefore the curse<small><small>[^28]</small></small> is poured upon us, and the oath that \[is\] written in the law of Moses the servant of God, because we have sinned against him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he hath confirmed<small><small>[^29]</small></small> his words, which he spake against us, and against our judges<small><small>[^30]</small></small> that judged us, by bringing upon us a great evil: for under<small><small>[^31]</small></small> the whole heaven hath not been done as hath been done upon Jerusalem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As \[it is<small><small>[^32]</small></small>\] written in the law of Moses, all this evil is come upon us: yet made we not our prayer before<small><small>[^33]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> our God, that we<small><small>[^34]</small></small> might turn from our iniquities, and understand thy truth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore hath the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> watched<small><small>[^35]</small></small> upon the evil, and brought it upon us: for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^36]</small></small></span> our God \[is\] righteous in all his works which he doeth: for<small><small>[^37]</small></small> we obeyed not his voice. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And now, O Lord our God, that hast<small><small>[^38]</small></small> brought thy people forth out of the land of Egypt with a mighty hand, and hast<small><small>[^39]</small></small> gotten thee renown<small><small>[^40]</small></small>, as at this day; we have sinned<small><small>[^41]</small></small>, we have done wickedly. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]O Lord, according<small><small>[^42]</small></small> to all thy righteousness, I beseech thee, let thine anger and thy fury be turned away from thy city Jerusalem, thy holy<small><small>[^43]</small></small> mountain: because for our sins, and for the<small><small>[^44]</small></small> iniquities of our fathers, Jerusalem<small><small>[^45]</small></small> and thy people \[are become\] a reproach to all \[that are\] about us. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now therefore, O our God, hear the prayer of thy servant, and his supplications, and cause<small><small>[^46]</small></small> thy face to shine upon thy sanctuary<small><small>[^47]</small></small> that is desolate, for<small><small>[^48]</small></small> the Lord’s sake. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O my God, incline<small><small>[^49]</small></small> thine ear, and hear; open thine eyes, and behold<small><small>[^50]</small></small> our desolations, and the city which is called by thy name<small><small>[^51]</small></small>: for we<small><small>[^52]</small></small> do not present<small><small>[^53]</small></small> our supplications before thee for our righteousnesses, but for thy great mercies. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O Lord, hear; O Lord, forgive<small><small>[^54]</small></small>; O Lord, hearken and do; defer<small><small>[^55]</small></small> not, for thine<small><small>[^56]</small></small> own sake, O my God: for thy<small><small>[^57]</small></small> city and thy people are called by thy name.

<span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yea, whiles I \[was\] speaking in prayer, even the man<small><small>[^61]</small></small> Gabriel, whom I had seen in the vision at the beginning, being caused to fly<small><small>[^62]</small></small> swiftly<small><small>[^63]</small></small>, touched<small><small>[^64]</small></small> me about the time<small><small>[^65]</small></small> of the evening oblation. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he informed<small><small>[^66]</small></small> \[me\], and talked with me, and said, O Daniel, I am now come forth to give thee skill and understanding<small><small>[^67]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> At the beginning<small><small>[^68]</small></small> of thy supplications the commandment<small><small>[^69]</small></small> came for<small><small>[^70]</small></small>th, and I am come to shew \[thee\]; for thou \[art\] greatly beloved<small><small>[^71]</small></small>: therefore understand<small><small>[^72]</small></small> the matter, and consider the vision. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Seventy weeks<small><small>[^73]</small></small> are determined upon thy people and upon thy holy city, to finish<small><small>[^74]</small></small> the transgression, and to<small><small>[^75]</small></small> make an end of<small><small>[^76]</small></small> sins, and to make<small><small>[^77]</small></small> reconciliation for iniquity, and to bring<small><small>[^78]</small></small> in everlasting righteousness, and to seal up<small><small>[^79]</small></small> the vision and prophecy<small><small>[^80]</small></small>, and to anoint<small><small>[^81]</small></small> the most<small><small>[^82]</small></small> Holy. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Know therefore and understand<small><small>[^83]</small></small>, \[that\] from<small><small>[^84]</small></small> the going forth of the commandment to restore and to build Jerusalem<small><small>[^85]</small></small> unto the Messiah<small><small>[^86]</small></small> the Prince<small><small>[^87]</small></small> \[shall be\] seven weeks<small><small>[^88]</small></small>, and threescore and two weeks: the street shall be built again<small><small>[^89]</small></small>, and the wall<small><small>[^90]</small></small>, even in troublous times<small><small>[^91]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And after threescore and two weeks shall Messiah<small><small>[^92]</small></small> be cut off, but not<small><small>[^93]</small></small> for himself: and the people<small><small>[^94]</small></small> of the prince<small><small>[^95]</small></small> that shall come shall destroy the city and the sanctuary; and the end<small><small>[^96]</small></small> thereof \[shall be\] with<small><small>[^97]</small></small> a flood, and unto the end of the war desolations are determined<small><small>[^98]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Dan.9.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall confirm<small><small>[^99]</small></small> the covenant<small><small>[^100]</small></small> with many for one week: and in the midst of the week he shall cause<small><small>[^101]</small></small> the sacrifice and the oblation to cease, and for the overspreading of abominations he shall make \[it\] desolate<small><small>[^102]</small></small>, even until the consummation, and that determined<small><small>[^103]</small></small> shall be poured upon the desolate<small><small>[^104]</small></small>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Dan.8|<< Daniel 8]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Dan.10|Daniel 10 >>]]</td></tr></table>
