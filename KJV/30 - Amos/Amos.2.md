***Judgment on Moab, Judah, and Israel*** ^bsb2000

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; For three<small><small>[^2]</small></small> transgressions of Moab<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, and for four, I will not turn away \[the punishment\] thereof; because<small><small>[^4]</small></small> he burned the bones of the king of Edom into lime: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will send a fire upon Moab, and it shall devour the palaces of Kerioth<small><small>[^5]</small></small>: and Moab shall die with tumult<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, with shouting, \[and\] with the sound of the trumpet: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will cut off the judge from the midst thereof, and will slay all the princes thereof with him, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^7]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; For<small><small>[^8]</small></small> three transgressions of Judah<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, and for four, I will not turn away \[the punishment\] thereof; because<small><small>[^10]</small></small> they have despised the law of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and have not kept his commandments, and their<small><small>[^11]</small></small> lies caused them to err, after<small><small>[^12]</small></small> the which their fathers have walked: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.2.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will<small><small>[^13]</small></small> send a fire upon Judah, and it shall devour the palaces of Jerusalem.

saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.2.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But ye gave the Nazarites wine to drink; and commanded the prophets, saying, Prophesy not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.2.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, I am pressed<small><small>[^37]</small></small> under you, as a cart is pressed \[that is\] full of sheaves. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.2.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore the flight<small><small>[^38]</small></small> shall perish from the swift, and the strong shall not strengthen his force, neither shall the mighty deliver himself<small><small>[^39]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.2.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither<small><small>[^40]</small></small> shall he stand that handleth the bow; and \[he that is\] swift of foot shall not deliver \[himself<small><small>[^41]</small></small>\]: neither shall he that rideth the horse deliver himself. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.2.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[he that is\] courageous<small><small>[^42]</small></small> among the mighty shall flee<small><small>[^43]</small></small> away naked in that day, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Amos.1|<< Amos 1]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Amos.3|Amos 3 >>]]</td></tr></table>
