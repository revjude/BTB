***The Destruction of Israel*** ^bsb2011

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.9.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I saw<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the Lord standing upon<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the altar: and he said, Smite<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the lintel<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of the door, that the posts may shake: and cut them<small><small>[^6]</small></small> in the head, all of them; and I will slay the last of them with the sword: he that fleeth of them shall not flee<small><small>[^7]</small></small> away, and he that escapeth of them shall not be delivered. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.9.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Though<small><small>[^8]</small></small> they dig<small><small>[^9]</small></small> into hell, thence shall mine hand take them; and though they be hid from my sight in the bottom of the sea, thence will I command the serpent<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, and he shall bite them: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.9.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And though they go<small><small>[^14]</small></small> into captivity before their enemies, thence will I command the sword, and it shall slay them: and I will set<small><small>[^15]</small></small> mine eyes upon them for evil, and not for good. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.9.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span> of hosts \[is\] he that toucheth<small><small>[^16]</small></small> the land, and it shall melt, and all<small><small>[^17]</small></small> that dwell therein shall mourn: and it shall rise<small><small>[^18]</small></small> up wholly like a flood; and shall be drowned, as \[by\] the flood of Egypt. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.9.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[It is\] he that buildeth<small><small>[^19]</small></small> his stories<small><small>[^20]</small></small> in the heaven, and hath founded his troop<small><small>[^21]</small></small> in the earth; Have not<small><small>[^25]</small></small> I brought up Israel out of the land of Egypt? and the Philistines<small><small>[^26]</small></small> from Caphtor, and the Syrians<small><small>[^27]</small></small> from Kir? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.9.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, the eyes<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span> \[are\] upon the sinful kingdom, and I<small><small>[^29]</small></small> will destroy it from off the face of the earth; saving<small><small>[^30]</small></small> that I will not utterly destroy the house of Jacob, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.9.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, lo, I will command, and I<small><small>[^31]</small></small> will sift<small><small>[^32]</small></small> the house of Israel among all nations, like as \[corn\] is sifted in a sieve, yet shall not the least grain<small><small>[^33]</small></small> fall upon the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.9.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All the sinners<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of my people shall die by the sword, which say, The evil<small><small>[^35]</small></small> shall not overtake nor prevent us.
