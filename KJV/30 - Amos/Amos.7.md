 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus hath the<small><small>[^2]</small></small> Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span> shewed unto me; and, behold, he formed grasshoppers<small><small>[^3]</small></small> in the beginning of the shooting up of the latter growth; and, lo, \[it was\] the latter growth after the king’s mowings<small><small>[^4]</small></small>.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, \[that\] when<small><small>[^6]</small></small> they had made an end of eating the grass of the land, then I said, O Lord<small><small>[^7]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">God</span>, forgive, I beseech thee: by whom shall Jacob arise<small><small>[^8]</small></small>? for<small><small>[^9]</small></small> he \[is\] small. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> repented for this: It shall not be, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^10]</small></small>

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thus he shewed me: and, behold, the Lord stood upon a wall<small><small>[^16]</small></small> \[made\] by a plumbline, with a plumbline in his hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto me, Amos<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, what seest thou? And I said, A plumbline. Then said the Lord, Behold, I will set a plumbline<small><small>[^18]</small></small> in the midst of my people Israel: I will not<small><small>[^19]</small></small> again pass by them any more:<small><small>[^20]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the high<small><small>[^21]</small></small> places of Isaac shall be desolate, and the sanctuaries of Israel shall be laid waste; and I will<small><small>[^22]</small></small> rise against the house of Jeroboam with the sword.

***Amaziah Accuses Amos*** ^bsb2009

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Amaziah the priest<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of Beth–el sent to Jeroboam king of Israel, saying, Amos hath<small><small>[^24]</small></small> conspired against thee in the midst of the house of Israel: the land is not<small><small>[^25]</small></small> able to bear all his words. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus<small><small>[^26]</small></small> Amos saith, Jeroboam<small><small>[^27]</small></small> shall die by the sword, and Israel<small><small>[^28]</small></small> shall surely be led away captive out of their own land. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Also Amaziah said unto Amos, O thou<small><small>[^29]</small></small> seer, go<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, flee thee away into the land of Judah, and there eat<small><small>[^31]</small></small> bread, and prophesy there: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But prophesy<small><small>[^32]</small></small> not again any more at Beth–el: for<small><small>[^33]</small></small> it \[is\] the king’s chapel<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, and it \[is\] the king’s court.<small><small>[^35]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Then answered Amos, and said to Amaziah, I \[was\] no prophet, neither<small><small>[^36]</small></small> \[was\] I a prophet’s son; but I \[was\] an herdman<small><small>[^37]</small></small>, and a gatherer<small><small>[^38]</small></small> of sycomore fruit:<small><small>[^39]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> took<small><small>[^40]</small></small> me as I followed<small><small>[^41]</small></small> the flock, and the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto me, Go<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, prophesy unto my people Israel. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Now therefore hear<small><small>[^43]</small></small> thou the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: Thou sayest, Prophesy<small><small>[^44]</small></small> not against Israel, and drop<small><small>[^45]</small></small> not \[thy word\] against the house of Isaac. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.7.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; Thy wife<small><small>[^46]</small></small> shall be an harlot in the city, and thy sons and thy daughters shall fall by the sword, and thy land<small><small>[^47]</small></small> shall be divided by line; and thou shalt die<small><small>[^48]</small></small> in a polluted land: and Israel<small><small>[^49]</small></small> shall surely go into captivity forth of his land. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Amos.6|<< Amos 6]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Amos.8|Amos 8 >>]]</td></tr></table>
