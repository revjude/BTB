<big><big>[[Amos.3.1 Witnesses against Israel|⥃]]</big></big>***Witnesses against Israel***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear<small><small>[^2]</small></small> this word that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath spoken against<small><small>[^3]</small></small> you, O children of Israel, against the whole family which<small><small>[^4]</small></small> I brought up from the land of Egypt, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> You only<small><small>[^5]</small></small> have I known of all<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the families of the earth: therefore<small><small>[^7]</small></small> I will punish<small><small>[^8]</small></small> you for all your iniquities. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Can two walk together, except they be agreed?<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Will a lion<small><small>[^10]</small></small> roar in the forest, when he hath no prey?
