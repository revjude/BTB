<big><big>[[Amos.1.1 Judgment on Israel’s Neighbors|⥃]]</big></big>***Judgment on Israel’s Neighbors***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Jer.12|Jeremiah 12:14–17]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The words<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of Amos, who<small><small>[^3]</small></small> was among the herdmen of Tekoa<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, which he saw<small><small>[^5]</small></small> concerning Israel in the<small><small>[^6]</small></small> days of Uzziah king of Judah, and in<small><small>[^7]</small></small> the days of Jeroboam the son of Joash king of Israel, two years before the earthquake<small><small>[^8]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^9]</small></small></span> will roar from Zion, and utter his voice from Jerusalem; and the habitations<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of the shepherds shall mourn, and the top of Carmel<small><small>[^11]</small></small> shall wither. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; For<small><small>[^12]</small></small> three transgressions of Damascus<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, and for four<small><small>[^14]</small></small><small><small>[^15]</small></small>, I will not turn away \[the punishment\] thereof; because<small><small>[^16]</small></small> they have threshed Gilead with threshing instruments of iron: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will<small><small>[^17]</small></small> send a fire into the house of Hazael<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, which shall devour the palaces of Ben–hadad<small><small>[^19]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will break<small><small>[^20]</small></small> also the bar of Damascus, and cut off the inhabitant from the plain of Aven<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, and him that holdeth the sceptre from the house of Eden<small><small>[^22]</small></small>: and the people<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of Syria shall go into captivity unto Kir, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; For three<small><small>[^24]</small></small> transgressions of Gaza<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, and for four, I will not turn away \[the punishment\] thereof; because they carried<small><small>[^26]</small></small> away captive the whole captivity, to deliver \[them\] up to Edom<small><small>[^27]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will<small><small>[^28]</small></small> send a fire<small><small>[^29]</small></small> on the wall of Gaza, which shall devour the palaces thereof: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will cut<small><small>[^30]</small></small> off the inhabitant from Ashdod, and him that holdeth the sceptre from Ashkelon, and I will turn<small><small>[^31]</small></small> mine hand against Ekron: and the<small><small>[^32]</small></small> remnant of the Philistines shall perish, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; For three transgressions of Tyrus<small><small>[^33]</small></small>, and for four, I will not turn away \[the punishment\] thereof; because<small><small>[^34]</small></small> they delivered up the whole captivity to Edom, and remembered not the brotherly covenant<small><small>[^35]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will send a fire on the wall of Tyrus, which shall devour the palaces thereof.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; For three transgressions of Edom<small><small>[^37]</small></small>, and for four, I will not turn away \[the punishment\] thereof; because<small><small>[^38]</small></small> he did pursue his brother with the sword, and did cast off all pity<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, and his anger did tear perpetually, and he kept<small><small>[^40]</small></small> his wrath for ever: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will send a fire upon Teman<small><small>[^41]</small></small>, which shall devour the palaces of Bozrah<small><small>[^42]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; For three transgressions of the children<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of Ammon, and for<small><small>[^44]</small></small> four, I will not turn away \[the punishment\] thereof; because<small><small>[^45]</small></small> they have ripped up the women with child<small><small>[^46]</small></small> of Gilead, that they might enlarge their border: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will kindle a fire in the wall of Rabbah<small><small>[^47]</small></small>, and it shall devour the palaces thereof, with shouting<small><small>[^48]</small></small> in the day of battle, with a<small><small>[^49]</small></small> tempest in the day of the whirlwind: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.1.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And their king shall go into captivity, he and his princes together, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <small><small>[^50]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Joel.3|<< Joel 3]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Amos.2|Amos 2 >>]]</td></tr></table>
