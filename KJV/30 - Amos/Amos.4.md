***Punishment Brings No Repentance*** ^bsb2002

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear this word, ye kine<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of Bashan, that \[are\] in the mountain<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of Samaria, which oppress<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the poor, which crush<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the needy, which say to their masters, Bring<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, and let us drink. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span> hath sworn<small><small>[^7]</small></small> by his holiness, that, lo, the days shall come upon you, that he will<small><small>[^8]</small></small> take you away with hooks, and your posterity with fishhooks.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And ye shall go<small><small>[^10]</small></small> out at the breaches, every \[cow at that which is\] before her; and ye shall cast \[them\] into the palace<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Come<small><small>[^12]</small></small> to Beth–el, and transgress; at Gilgal<small><small>[^13]</small></small> multiply transgression; and bring<small><small>[^14]</small></small> your sacrifices every morning, \[and\] your<small><small>[^15]</small></small> tithes after three years<small><small>[^16]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And offer a sacrifice<small><small>[^17]</small></small> of thanksgiving with leaven, and proclaim<small><small>[^18]</small></small> \[and\] publish the free offerings: for<small><small>[^19]</small></small> this liketh you<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, O ye children of Israel, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I also have given you cleanness<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of teeth in all your cities, and want<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of bread in all your places: yet<small><small>[^23]</small></small> have ye not returned unto me, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And also I have<small><small>[^24]</small></small> withholden the rain from you, when<small><small>[^25]</small></small> \[there were\] yet three months to the harvest: and I<small><small>[^26]</small></small> caused it to rain upon one city, and caused it not to rain upon another city: one piece was rained upon, and the<small><small>[^27]</small></small> piece whereupon it rained not withered. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So two<small><small>[^28]</small></small> \[or\] three cities wandered unto one city, to drink water; but<small><small>[^29]</small></small> they were not satisfied: yet<small><small>[^30]</small></small> have ye not returned unto me, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have smitten you with<small><small>[^31]</small></small> blasting and mildew: when<small><small>[^32]</small></small> your gardens and your vineyards and your fig trees and your olive trees increased, the palmerworm<small><small>[^33]</small></small> devoured \[them\]: yet<small><small>[^34]</small></small> have ye not returned unto me, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have sent among you the pestilence<small><small>[^35]</small></small> after the manner<small><small>[^36]</small></small> of Egypt: your young<small><small>[^37]</small></small> men have I slain with the sword, and have taken away your horses<small><small>[^38]</small></small>; and I have made the stink<small><small>[^39]</small></small> of your camps to come up unto your nostrils: yet<small><small>[^40]</small></small> have ye not returned unto me, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have overthrown \[some\] of you, as God<small><small>[^41]</small></small> overthrew Sodom and Gomorrah, and ye were as a<small><small>[^42]</small></small> firebrand plucked out of the burning: yet<small><small>[^43]</small></small> have ye not returned unto me, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore thus<small><small>[^44]</small></small> will I do unto thee, O Israel: \[and\] because I will do this unto thee, prepare<small><small>[^45]</small></small> to meet thy God, O Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.4.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, lo, he that<small><small>[^46]</small></small> formeth the mountains, and createth<small><small>[^47]</small></small> the wind<small><small>[^48]</small></small>, and declareth<small><small>[^49]</small></small> unto man what \[is\] his thought, that maketh<small><small>[^50]</small></small> the morning darkness, and treadeth<small><small>[^51]</small></small> upon the high places of the earth, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^52]</small></small></span>, The God of hosts, \[is\] his name. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Amos.3|<< Amos 3]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Amos.5|Amos 5 >>]]</td></tr></table>
