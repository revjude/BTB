<big><big>[[Amos.8.1 The Basket of Summer Fruit|⥃]]</big></big>***The Basket of Summer Fruit***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus hath the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span> shewed unto me: and behold a basket of summer fruit.<small><small>[^2]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Amos<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, what seest thou? And I said, A basket<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of summer fruit. Then said the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> unto me, The end<small><small>[^5]</small></small> is come upon my people of Israel; I will not<small><small>[^6]</small></small> again pass by them any more. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the songs<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of the temple shall be howlings<small><small>[^8]</small></small> in that day, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>: \[there shall be\] many<small><small>[^9]</small></small> dead bodies in every place; they shall<small><small>[^10]</small></small> cast \[them\] forth with silence<small><small>[^11]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear<small><small>[^12]</small></small> this, O ye that swallow<small><small>[^13]</small></small> up the needy, even to make the poor of the land to fail, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Saying, When<small><small>[^14]</small></small> will the new moon<small><small>[^15]</small></small> be gone<small><small>[^16]</small></small>, that we may sell corn? and the<small><small>[^17]</small></small> sabbath, that we may set forth<small><small>[^18]</small></small> wheat, making<small><small>[^19]</small></small> the ephah small, and the shekel great, and falsifying the balances by deceit<small><small>[^20]</small></small>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That we may buy the poor for silver, and the needy for a pair of shoes; \[yea\], and sell the refuse of the wheat?<small><small>[^21]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath sworn<small><small>[^22]</small></small> by the excellency of Jacob, Surely I will<small><small>[^23]</small></small> never forget any of their works. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Shall not the land<small><small>[^24]</small></small> tremble for this, and every one<small><small>[^25]</small></small> mourn that dwelleth therein? and it shall rise<small><small>[^26]</small></small> up wholly as a flood; and it shall be cast out and drowned, as \[by\] the flood of Egypt. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass in that day, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>, that I<small><small>[^27]</small></small> will cause the sun to go down at noon, and I<small><small>[^28]</small></small> will darken the earth in the clear day: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will turn<small><small>[^29]</small></small> your feasts into mourning, and all your songs into lamentation; and I will bring up sackcloth<small><small>[^30]</small></small> upon all loins, and baldness upon every head; and I will make it as the<small><small>[^31]</small></small> mourning of an only \[son\], and the end thereof as a bitter<small><small>[^32]</small></small> day. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, the days come, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>, that I will send a famine in the land, not a famine of bread, nor a thirst for water, but<small><small>[^33]</small></small> of hearing the words of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall wander from sea to sea, and from the north even to the east, they shall run<small><small>[^34]</small></small> to and fro to seek the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and shall not find \[it\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In that day shall the fair virgins and young men faint for thirst.<small><small>[^35]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Amos.8.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They that swear<small><small>[^36]</small></small> by the sin<small><small>[^37]</small></small> of Samaria, and say, Thy god, O Dan, liveth; and, The manner<small><small>[^38]</small></small> of Beer–sheba<small><small>[^39]</small></small> liveth; even they shall fall<small><small>[^40]</small></small>, and never rise up again. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Amos.7|<< Amos 7]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Amos.9|Amos 9 >>]]</td></tr></table>
