<big><big>[[Acts.11.1 Peter’s Report at Jerusalem|⥃]]</big></big>***Peter’s Report at Jerusalem***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the apostles<small><small>[^2]</small></small> and brethren that were in Judea heard that the Gentiles<small><small>[^3]</small></small> had also received the word of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when Peter was come up to Jerusalem, they<small><small>[^4]</small></small> that were of the circumcision contended with him, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Saying, Thou wentest in to men uncircumcised, and didst eat with them.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Peter rehearsed \[the matter\] from the beginning, and expounded \[it\] by order unto them, saying,<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I was<small><small>[^7]</small></small> in the city of Joppa praying: and in a<small><small>[^8]</small></small> trance I saw a vision, A certain vessel descend, as it had been a great sheet, let down from heaven by four corners; and it<small><small>[^9]</small></small> came even to me: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Upon the which when I had fastened<small><small>[^10]</small></small> mine eyes, I considered, and saw fourfooted beasts of the earth, and wild beasts, and creeping things, and fowls of the air. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I heard a voice saying unto me, Arise, Peter; slay and eat. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I said, Not so, Lord: for nothing common<small><small>[^11]</small></small> or unclean<small><small>[^12]</small></small> hath at any time entered into my mouth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the voice answered me again from heaven, What<small><small>[^13]</small></small> God hath cleansed, \[that\] call not thou common. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And this was done three<small><small>[^14]</small></small> times: and all were drawn up again into heaven. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And, behold, immediately there were three men already come unto the house where I was, sent from Cesarea unto me.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the spirit<small><small>[^16]</small></small> bade me go with them, nothing<small><small>[^17]</small></small> doubting. Moreover these<small><small>[^18]</small></small> six brethren accompanied me, and we entered into the man’s house: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shewed<small><small>[^19]</small></small> us how he had seen an angel in his house, which stood and said unto him, Send men to Joppa<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and call for Simon, whose surname is Peter; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who shall tell thee words<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, whereby thou and all<small><small>[^22]</small></small> thy house shall be saved. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And as I<small><small>[^23]</small></small> began to speak, the Holy Ghost<small><small>[^24]</small></small> fell on them, as on<small><small>[^25]</small></small> us at the beginning. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then remembered<small><small>[^26]</small></small> I the word of the Lord, how<small><small>[^27]</small></small> that he said, <span style="color:red;">John indeed baptized with water; but<small><small>[^28]</small></small> ye shall be baptized with the Holy Ghost.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Forasmuch then as God<small><small>[^29]</small></small> gave them the like gift as \[he did\] unto us, who believed on the Lord Jesus Christ; what<small><small>[^30]</small></small> was I, that I could withstand God? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When they heard these things, they held<small><small>[^31]</small></small> their peace, and glorified<small><small>[^32]</small></small> God, saying, Then hath<small><small>[^33]</small></small> God also to the Gentiles granted<small><small>[^34]</small></small> repentance unto life.

<big><big>[[Acts.11.19 The Church at Antioch|⥃]]</big></big>***The Church at Antioch***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now they<small><small>[^35]</small></small> which were scattered abroad upon the persecution that arose about Stephen travelled as far as Phenice<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, and Cyprus<small><small>[^37]</small></small>, and Antioch<small><small>[^38]</small></small>, preaching the word to none<small><small>[^39]</small></small> but unto the Jews only. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And some of them were men of Cyprus and Cyrene<small><small>[^40]</small></small>, which, when they were come to Antioch, spake unto the Grecians<small><small>[^41]</small></small>, preaching<small><small>[^42]</small></small> the Lord Jesus. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the hand<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of the Lord was with them: and a<small><small>[^44]</small></small> great number believed, and turned<small><small>[^45]</small></small> unto the Lord. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then tidings<small><small>[^46]</small></small> of these things came unto the ears of the church which was in Jerusalem: and they<small><small>[^47]</small></small> sent forth Barnabas, that he should go as far as Antioch.<small><small>[^48]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who, when he came, and had seen<small><small>[^49]</small></small> the grace of God, was glad, and exhorted<small><small>[^50]</small></small> them all, that with purpose<small><small>[^51]</small></small> of heart they would cleave<small><small>[^52]</small></small> unto the Lord. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he was<small><small>[^53]</small></small> a good man, and full<small><small>[^54]</small></small> of the Holy Ghost and of faith: and much<small><small>[^55]</small></small> people was added unto the Lord. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then departed Barnabas to Tarsus<small><small>[^56]</small></small>, for to seek Saul: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when he had found him, he brought him unto Antioch. And it came to pass, that<small><small>[^57]</small></small> a whole year they assembled themselves with the church<small><small>[^58]</small></small>, and taught<small><small>[^59]</small></small> much people. And the disciples were<small><small>[^60]</small></small> called Christians first in Antioch. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And in these days came prophets<small><small>[^61]</small></small> from Jerusalem unto Antioch. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there stood up one of them named Agabus<small><small>[^62]</small></small>, and signified by the spirit that there should be great<small><small>[^63]</small></small> dearth throughout all the world: which came to pass in the days of Claudius<small><small>[^64]</small></small> Cesar. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the disciples, every<small><small>[^65]</small></small> man according to his ability, determined to send<small><small>[^66]</small></small> relief unto the brethren which dwelt in Judea: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.11.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which also they did, and sent it to the<small><small>[^67]</small></small> elders by<small><small>[^68]</small></small> the hands of Barnabas and Saul. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Acts.10|<< Acts 10]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Acts.12|Acts 12 >>]]</td></tr></table>
