***A Lame Man Walks*** ^bsb2588

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now Peter<small><small>[^2]</small></small> and John went<small><small>[^3]</small></small> up together into the temple at the hour<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of prayer, \[being\] the ninth \[hour\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And a certain man lame<small><small>[^5]</small></small> from his mother’s womb was carried, whom<small><small>[^6]</small></small> they laid daily at the gate of the temple which<small><small>[^7]</small></small> is called Beautiful, to ask<small><small>[^8]</small></small> alms of them that entered into the temple; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who seeing Peter and John about to go into the temple asked an alms. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Peter, fastening<small><small>[^9]</small></small> his eyes upon him with John, said, Look<small><small>[^10]</small></small> on us. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.3.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he gave heed unto them, expecting to receive something of them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.3.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Peter said, Silver<small><small>[^11]</small></small> and gold have I none;
