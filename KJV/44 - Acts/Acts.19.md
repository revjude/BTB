<big><big>[[Acts.19.1 The Holy Spirit Received at Ephesus|⥃]]</big></big>***The Holy Spirit Received at Ephesus***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Acts.10|Acts 10:44–48]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, that<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, while Apollos was at Corinth, Paul<small><small>[^3]</small></small> having passed through the upper coasts came<small><small>[^4]</small></small> to Ephesus: and finding certain disciples, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He said unto them, Have ye<small><small>[^5]</small></small> received the Holy Ghost since ye believed? And they said unto him, We have<small><small>[^6]</small></small> not so much as heard whether there be any Holy Ghost. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto them, Unto what<small><small>[^7]</small></small> then were ye baptized? And they said, Unto John’s<small><small>[^8]</small></small> baptism. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said Paul, John<small><small>[^9]</small></small> verily baptized with the baptism of repentance, saying unto the people, that they should believe on him which should come after him, that is, on Christ Jesus. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When they<small><small>[^10]</small></small> heard \[this\], they were baptized in the name of the Lord Jesus. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when Paul had laid<small><small>[^11]</small></small> \[his\] hands upon them, the Holy Ghost<small><small>[^12]</small></small> came on them; and they spake with tongues, and prophesied<small><small>[^13]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all the men were about twelve.

<big><big>[[Acts.19.8 Paul Ministers in Ephesus|⥃]]</big></big>***Paul Ministers in Ephesus***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Eph.1|Ephesians 1:1–2]] <big>†</big> [[Rev.2|Revelation 2:1–7]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he went<small><small>[^14]</small></small> into the synagogue, and spake boldly for the space of three months, disputing<small><small>[^15]</small></small> and persuading the things concerning the kingdom of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But when divers<small><small>[^16]</small></small> were hardened, and believed not, but spake<small><small>[^17]</small></small> evil of that way before the multitude, he departed<small><small>[^18]</small></small> from them, and separated the disciples, disputing daily<small><small>[^19]</small></small> in the school of one Tyrannus.<small><small>[^20]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And this<small><small>[^21]</small></small> continued by the space of two years; so that all they which dwelt in Asia<small><small>[^22]</small></small> heard the word of the Lord Jesus, both<small><small>[^23]</small></small> Jews and Greeks. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God wrought special miracles by the hands of Paul:<small><small>[^24]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So that from his body were brought unto the sick handkerchiefs or aprons, and the diseases departed from them, and the evil spirits went out of them.<small><small>[^25]</small></small>

<big><big>[[Acts.19.13 Seven Sons of Sceva|⥃]]</big></big>***Seven Sons of Sceva***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then certain of the vagabond<small><small>[^26]</small></small> Jews, exorcists<small><small>[^27]</small></small>, took<small><small>[^28]</small></small> upon them to call over them which had evil spirits the name of the Lord Jesus, saying, We adjure<small><small>[^29]</small></small> you by Jesus whom Paul preacheth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there were seven sons of \[one\] Sceva, a Jew, \[and\] chief of the priests, which did so. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the evil spirit answered and said, Jesus I know, and Paul I know; but who are ye?<small><small>[^30]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the man in whom the evil spirit was leaped on them, and overcame them, and prevailed against them, so that they fled out of that house naked and wounded.<small><small>[^31]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And this was known to all<small><small>[^32]</small></small> the Jews and Greeks also dwelling at Ephesus; and fear<small><small>[^33]</small></small> fell on them all, and the name<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of the Lord Jesus was magnified. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And many that believed came, and confessed<small><small>[^35]</small></small>, and shewed their deeds. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Many of them also which used<small><small>[^36]</small></small> curious<small><small>[^37]</small></small> arts brought their books together, and burned<small><small>[^38]</small></small> them before all \[men\]: and they counted the price of them, and found \[it\] fifty<small><small>[^39]</small></small> thousand \[pieces\] of silver. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Acts.19.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So mightily grew the word of God and prevailed.<small><small>[^40]</small></small>
