***The Vision of the Four Chariots*** ^bsb2070

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I turned<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, and lifted up mine eyes, and looked, and, behold, there came four<small><small>[^3]</small></small> chariots out from between two mountains; and the<small><small>[^4]</small></small> mountains \[were\] mountains of brass. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the first chariot \[were\] red<small><small>[^5]</small></small> horses; and in the second chariot black<small><small>[^6]</small></small> horses; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And in the third chariot white<small><small>[^7]</small></small> horses; and in the fourth chariot grisled<small><small>[^8]</small></small> and bay<small><small>[^9]</small></small> horses. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then I answered and said unto<small><small>[^10]</small></small> the angel that talked with me, What \[are\] these, my lord? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the angel answered and said unto me, These<small><small>[^11]</small></small> \[are\] the four spirits<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of the heavens, which go<small><small>[^13]</small></small> forth from standing before the Lord<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of all the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The black horses which \[are\] therein go forth into the north<small><small>[^15]</small></small> country; and the white go forth after<small><small>[^16]</small></small> them; and the grisled go forth toward<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the south country. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the bay<small><small>[^18]</small></small> went forth, and sought to go that they might walk to and fro through the earth: and he said, Get you hence, walk to and fro through the earth. So they walked to and fro through the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then cried he upon me, and spake unto me, saying, Behold, these that go toward the north country have quieted<small><small>[^19]</small></small> my spirit in the north country.

***The Crown and the Temple*** ^bsb2071

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying,<small><small>[^20]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Take of \[them of\] the captivity, \[even\] of Heldai, of Tobijah, and of Jedaiah, which<small><small>[^21]</small></small> are come from Babylon, and come thou the same day, and go into the house of Josiah the son<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of Zephaniah; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then take silver and gold, and make<small><small>[^23]</small></small> crowns, and set \[them\] upon the head of Joshua<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the son of Josedech, the high priest; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And speak unto him, saying, Thus speaketh the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, saying, Behold the man whose<small><small>[^25]</small></small> name \[is\] The BRANCH; and he shall grow up out of his place<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, and he shall build<small><small>[^27]</small></small> the temple of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>:<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Even he shall build the temple of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; and he shall bear<small><small>[^29]</small></small> the glory, and shall sit and rule upon his throne; and he shall be a priest<small><small>[^30]</small></small> upon his throne: and the<small><small>[^31]</small></small> counsel of peace shall be between them both. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the crowns shall be to Helem<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, and to Tobijah, and to Jedaiah, and to Hen the son of Zephaniah, for a memorial<small><small>[^33]</small></small> in the temple of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.6.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they<small><small>[^34]</small></small> \[that are\] far off shall come and build in the temple of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and ye<small><small>[^35]</small></small> shall know that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts hath sent me unto you. And \[this<small><small>[^36]</small></small>\] shall come to pass, if ye will diligently obey the voice of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Zech.5|<< Zechariah 5]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Zech.7|Zechariah 7 >>]]</td></tr></table>
