<big><big>[[Zech.14.1 The Destroyers of Jerusalem Destroyed|⥃]]</big></big>***The Destroyers of Jerusalem Destroyed***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, the day of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> cometh, and thy spoil shall be divided in the midst of thee.<small><small>[^2]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I will gather<small><small>[^3]</small></small> all nations against Jerusalem to battle; and the city<small><small>[^4]</small></small> shall be taken, and the houses<small><small>[^5]</small></small> rifled, and the women ravished; and half of the city shall go forth into captivity, and the residue<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of the people shall not<small><small>[^7]</small></small> be cut off from the city. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then shall the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> go forth, and fight against those nations, as<small><small>[^8]</small></small> when he fought in the day of battle.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And his feet<small><small>[^10]</small></small> shall stand in that day upon the mount of Olives, which \[is\] before Jerusalem on the east, and the mount of Olives shall cleave<small><small>[^11]</small></small> in the midst thereof toward the east and toward the west, \[and there shall be\] a very<small><small>[^12]</small></small> great valley; and half of the<small><small>[^13]</small></small> mountain shall remove toward the north, and half of it toward the south. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And ye shall flee \[to\] the valley of the mountains<small><small>[^14]</small></small>; for the valley of the mountains shall reach unto Azal: yea, ye shall flee, like as ye fled<small><small>[^15]</small></small> from before the earthquake in the days of Uzziah king of Judah: and the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^16]</small></small></span> my God shall come, \[and\] all<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the saints with thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass in that day, \[that\] the<small><small>[^18]</small></small> light shall not<small><small>[^19]</small></small> be clear<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, \[nor\] dark<small><small>[^21]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But it shall be one day<small><small>[^22]</small></small> which<small><small>[^23]</small></small> shall be known to the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, not day, nor night: but it shall come to pass, \[that<small><small>[^24]</small></small>\] at evening time it shall be light. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall be in that day, \[that\] living<small><small>[^25]</small></small> waters shall go out from Jerusalem; half of them toward the former<small><small>[^26]</small></small> sea, and half of them toward the hinder sea: in summer<small><small>[^27]</small></small> and in winter shall it be. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^28]</small></small></span> shall be king over all the earth: in that day shall there be one <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^29]</small></small></span>, and<small><small>[^30]</small></small> his name one. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All the land<small><small>[^31]</small></small> shall be turned<small><small>[^32]</small></small> as a plain from Geba<small><small>[^33]</small></small> to Rimmon<small><small>[^34]</small></small> south of Jerusalem: and it shall be lifted up, and inhabited<small><small>[^35]</small></small> in her place, from Benjamin’s<small><small>[^36]</small></small> gate unto the place of the first gate, unto the corner gate, and \[from\] the tower of Hananeel unto the king’s winepresses. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[men\] shall dwell in it, and there<small><small>[^37]</small></small> shall be no more utter destruction; but Jerusalem shall be safely inhabited<small><small>[^38]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And this shall be the plague wherewith<small><small>[^39]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will smite all the people that have fought against Jerusalem; Their flesh<small><small>[^40]</small></small> shall consume away while they stand upon their feet, and their eyes shall consume away in their holes, and their tongue shall consume away in their mouth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass in that day, \[that\] a great<small><small>[^41]</small></small> tumult from the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall be among them; and they shall lay hold every one on the hand of his neighbour, and his hand shall rise up against the hand of his neighbour. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Judah also shall<small><small>[^42]</small></small> fight at<small><small>[^43]</small></small> Jerusalem; and the<small><small>[^44]</small></small> wealth of all the heathen round about shall be gathered together, gold, and silver, and apparel, in great abundance. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.14.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And so shall be the plague of the horse, of the mule, of the camel, and of the ass, and of all the beasts that shall be in these tents, as this plague.<small><small>[^45]</small></small>
