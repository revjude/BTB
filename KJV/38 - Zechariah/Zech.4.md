***The Vision of the Lampstand and Olive Trees*** ^bsb2067

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the angel<small><small>[^2]</small></small> that talked with me came again, and waked<small><small>[^3]</small></small> me, as a man that is wakened out of his sleep, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And said unto me, What<small><small>[^4]</small></small> seest thou? And I said, I have looked, and behold a candlestick<small><small>[^5]</small></small> all \[of\] gold, with a bowl<small><small>[^6]</small></small> upon the top of it, and his seven<small><small>[^7]</small></small> lamps thereon, and seven pipes to the seven lamps<small><small>[^8]</small></small>, which \[are\] upon the top thereof: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And two olive trees by it, one upon the right \[side\] of the bowl, and the other upon the left \[side\] thereof.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So I answered and spake to the angel that talked with me, saying, What<small><small>[^10]</small></small> \[are\] these, my lord? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the angel that talked with me answered and said unto me, Knowest<small><small>[^11]</small></small> thou not what these be? And I said, No<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, my lord. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then he answered and spake unto me, saying, This \[is\] the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> unto Zerubbabel, saying, Not<small><small>[^13]</small></small> by might<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, nor by power, but by my spirit, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who \[art\] thou, O great<small><small>[^15]</small></small> mountain? before Zerubbabel \[thou shalt become\] a plain: and he shall bring forth the headstone<small><small>[^16]</small></small> \[thereof with\] shoutings<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, \[crying\], Grace<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, grace unto it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The hands of Zerubbabel have<small><small>[^19]</small></small> laid the foundation of this house; his hands<small><small>[^20]</small></small> shall also finish it; and<small><small>[^21]</small></small> thou shalt know that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts hath sent me unto you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For who hath despised<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the day of small things? for they<small><small>[^23]</small></small> shall rejoice, and shall<small><small>[^24]</small></small> see the plummet<small><small>[^25]</small></small> in the hand of Zerubbabel \[with\] those<small><small>[^26]</small></small> seven; they \[are<small><small>[^27]</small></small>\] the eyes of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, which run to and fro through the whole earth. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then answered I, and said unto him, What<small><small>[^28]</small></small> \[are\] these two olive trees upon the right \[side\] of the candlestick and upon the left \[side\] thereof? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I answered again, and said unto him, What \[be<small><small>[^29]</small></small> these\] two olive branches which through<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the two golden pipes empty<small><small>[^31]</small></small> the golden<small><small>[^32]</small></small> \[oil\] out of themselves? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he answered me and said, Knowest<small><small>[^33]</small></small> thou not what these \[be\]? And I said, No, my lord. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.4.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said he, These<small><small>[^34]</small></small> \[are\] the two anointed ones<small><small>[^35]</small></small>, that<small><small>[^36]</small></small> stand by the Lord<small><small>[^37]</small></small> of the whole earth. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Zech.3|<< Zechariah 3]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Zech.5|Zechariah 5 >>]]</td></tr></table>
