and they shall live<small><small>[^38]</small></small> with their children, and turn again. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.10.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will bring them again also out of the<small><small>[^39]</small></small> land of Egypt, and gather them out of Assyria; and I will bring them into<small><small>[^40]</small></small> the land of Gilead and Lebanon; and \[place<small><small>[^41]</small></small>\] shall not be found for them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.10.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall<small><small>[^42]</small></small> pass through the sea with affliction, and shall smite<small><small>[^43]</small></small> the waves in the sea, and all the deeps of the river shall dry up: and the pride<small><small>[^44]</small></small> of Assyria shall be brought down, and the sceptre<small><small>[^45]</small></small> of Egypt shall depart away. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.10.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will<small><small>[^46]</small></small> strengthen them in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>;
