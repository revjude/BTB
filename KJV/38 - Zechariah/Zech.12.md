<big><big>[[Zech.12.1 The Coming Deliverance of Jerusalem|⥃]]</big></big>***The Coming Deliverance of Jerusalem***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The burden<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> for<small><small>[^3]</small></small> Israel, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, which<small><small>[^4]</small></small> stretcheth forth the heavens, and layeth the foundation of the earth, and formeth<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the spirit of man within him.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, I will make Jerusalem a cup<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of trembling<small><small>[^8]</small></small> unto all the people round about, when they<small><small>[^9]</small></small> shall be in the siege both against Judah \[and\] against Jerusalem. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And in that<small><small>[^10]</small></small> day will I make Jerusalem a burdensome<small><small>[^11]</small></small> stone for all people: all that burden themselves with it shall be cut in pieces, though<small><small>[^12]</small></small> all the people of the earth be gathered together against it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In that day<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, I will smite<small><small>[^14]</small></small> every horse with astonishment, and his rider with madness: and I will open<small><small>[^15]</small></small> mine eyes upon the house of Judah, and will smite every horse of the people with blindness. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the governors<small><small>[^16]</small></small> of Judah shall say in their heart, The inhabitants<small><small>[^17]</small></small> of Jerusalem \[shall be\] my strength in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts their God. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]In that day will I make the governors of Judah like an hearth<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of fire among the wood, and like a torch of fire in a sheaf; and they<small><small>[^19]</small></small> shall devour all the people round about, on the right<small><small>[^20]</small></small> hand and on the left: and Jerusalem shall<small><small>[^21]</small></small> be inhabited again in her own place, \[even\] in Jerusalem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> also shall save<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the tents of Judah first, that the glory of the house of David and the glory of the inhabitants of Jerusalem do<small><small>[^23]</small></small> not magnify \[themselves\] against Judah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In that day shall the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> defend<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the<small><small>[^25]</small></small> inhabitants of Jerusalem; and he that is feeble<small><small>[^26]</small></small> among them at that day shall be as David; and the house<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of David \[shall be\] as God, as the<small><small>[^28]</small></small> angel of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> before them. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass in that day, \[that\] I will<small><small>[^29]</small></small> seek to destroy all the nations that come against Jerusalem.

<big><big>[[Zech.12.10 Mourning the One They Pierced|⥃]]</big></big>***Mourning the One They Pierced***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[John.19|John 19:31–37]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will pour<small><small>[^30]</small></small> upon the house<small><small>[^31]</small></small> of David, and upon the inhabitants of Jerusalem, the spirit<small><small>[^32]</small></small> of grace and of supplications<small><small>[^33]</small></small>: and they shall look<small><small>[^34]</small></small> upon me whom they have pierced, and they shall mourn<small><small>[^35]</small></small> for him, as one mourneth for \[his\] only \[son\], and shall be in bitterness for him, as one that is in bitterness for \[his\] firstborn. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In that day shall there be a great mourning in Jerusalem, as<small><small>[^36]</small></small> the mourning of Hadadrimmon in the valley of Megiddon. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the land<small><small>[^37]</small></small> shall mourn, every family apart<small><small>[^38]</small></small>; the family of the house of David apart<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, and their<small><small>[^40]</small></small> wives apart; the family of the house of Nathan<small><small>[^41]</small></small> apart, and their wives apart; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.12.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All the families that remain, every family apart, and<small><small>[^44]</small></small> their wives apart. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Zech.11|<< Zechariah 11]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Zech.13|Zechariah 13 >>]]</td></tr></table>
