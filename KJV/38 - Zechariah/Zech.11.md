***The Doomed Flock*** ^bsb2078

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Open thy doors, O Lebanon<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, that<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the fire may devour thy cedars. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Howl<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, fir tree; for the cedar is fallen; because the mighty<small><small>[^5]</small></small> are spoiled: howl, O ye oaks of Bashan; for<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the forest of the vintage<small><small>[^7]</small></small> is come down. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]\[There is\] a voice<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of the howling of the shepherds; for their<small><small>[^9]</small></small> glory is spoiled: a voice<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of the roaring of young lions; for the pride<small><small>[^11]</small></small> of Jordan is spoiled. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^12]</small></small></span> my God; Feed<small><small>[^13]</small></small> the flock of the slaughter; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whose possessors<small><small>[^14]</small></small> slay them, and hold<small><small>[^15]</small></small> themselves not guilty: and they that sell<small><small>[^16]</small></small> them say, Blessed<small><small>[^17]</small></small> \[be\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; for I am rich: and their<small><small>[^18]</small></small> own shepherds pity them not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I will no<small><small>[^19]</small></small> more pity the inhabitants of the land, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: but, lo, I will deliver<small><small>[^20]</small></small> the men every one into his neighbour’s hand, and into the<small><small>[^21]</small></small> hand of his king: and they shall<small><small>[^22]</small></small> smite the land, and out<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of their hand I will not deliver \[them\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will<small><small>[^24]</small></small> feed the flock of slaughter, \[even\] you, O poor<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of the flock. And I took unto me two staves<small><small>[^26]</small></small>; the one<small><small>[^27]</small></small> I called Beauty, and the other I called Bands<small><small>[^28]</small></small>; and I fed the flock. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Three shepherds also I cut off in<small><small>[^29]</small></small> one month; and my<small><small>[^30]</small></small> soul lothed them<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, and their soul also abhorred me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said I, I will<small><small>[^32]</small></small> not feed you: that that dieth<small><small>[^33]</small></small>, let it die; and that that is to be cut off, let it be cut off; and let<small><small>[^34]</small></small> the rest eat every one the flesh of another<small><small>[^35]</small></small>.

***Thirty Pieces of Silver***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.27|Matthew 27:3–10]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2079

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And I took my staff, \[even\] Beauty<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, and cut it asunder, that<small><small>[^37]</small></small> I might break my covenant which I had made with all the people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it was broken in that day: and so<small><small>[^38]</small></small> the poor of the flock that waited<small><small>[^39]</small></small> upon me knew<small><small>[^40]</small></small> that it \[was\] the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I said unto them, If ye think good<small><small>[^41]</small></small>, give<small><small>[^42]</small></small> \[me\] my price; and if not, forbear. So<small><small>[^43]</small></small> they weighed for my price thirty \[pieces\] of silver. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto me, Cast<small><small>[^44]</small></small> it unto the potter: a goodly<small><small>[^45]</small></small> price that I was prised at of them. And I took the thirty \[pieces\] of silver, and cast them to the potter in the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.11.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then I cut<small><small>[^46]</small></small> asunder mine other staff, \[even\] Bands<small><small>[^47]</small></small>, that I might break the brotherhood between Judah and Israel.
