 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Again the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts came \[to me\], saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts; I was jealous<small><small>[^2]</small></small> for Zion with great jealousy, and I was jealous for her with great fury. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; I am<small><small>[^3]</small></small> returned unto Zion, and will dwell<small><small>[^4]</small></small> in the midst of Jerusalem: and Jerusalem shall be called a city<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of truth; and the mountain<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts the holy<small><small>[^7]</small></small> mountain. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts; There<small><small>[^8]</small></small> shall yet old men and old women dwell in the streets of Jerusalem, and every man with his staff in his hand for very age<small><small>[^9]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the streets of the city shall be full of boys and girls playing<small><small>[^10]</small></small> in the streets thereof. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts; If it be marvellous<small><small>[^11]</small></small> in the eyes of the remnant of this people in these days, should<small><small>[^12]</small></small> it also be marvellous in mine eyes? saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus sai<small><small>[^13]</small></small>th the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts; Behold, I will save my people from the east country, and from the west country<small><small>[^14]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will bring them, and they shall dwell<small><small>[^15]</small></small> in the midst of Jerusalem: and they shall be my<small><small>[^16]</small></small> people, and I will be their God, in truth<small><small>[^17]</small></small> and in righteousness. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts; Let<small><small>[^18]</small></small> your hands be strong, ye that hear in these days these words by the mouth of the prophets<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, which \[were\] in the day \[that\] the foundation of the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts was laid, that the temple might be built. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For before<small><small>[^20]</small></small> these days there was no hire for man<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, nor any hire for beast; As<small><small>[^34]</small></small> I thought to punish you, when your fathers provoked me to wrath, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, and I repented<small><small>[^35]</small></small> not: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So again have<small><small>[^36]</small></small> I thought in these days to do well unto Jerusalem and to the house of Judah: fear<small><small>[^37]</small></small> ye not. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]These \[are<small><small>[^38]</small></small>\] the things that ye shall do; Speak<small><small>[^39]</small></small> ye every man the truth to his neighbour; execute the judgment of truth and peace<small><small>[^40]</small></small> in your gates: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And let<small><small>[^41]</small></small> none of you imagine evil in your hearts against his neighbour; and love<small><small>[^42]</small></small> no false oath: for all these \[are things<small><small>[^43]</small></small>\] that I hate, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.8.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts; The fast of the fourth<small><small>[^44]</small></small> \[month\], and the fast of the fifth<small><small>[^45]</small></small>, and the fast of the seventh<small><small>[^46]</small></small>, and the fast of the tenth<small><small>[^47]</small></small>, shall be to the house of Judah joy<small><small>[^48]</small></small> and gladness, and cheerful feasts<small><small>[^49]</small></small>;
