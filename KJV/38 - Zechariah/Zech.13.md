***An End to Idolatry*** ^bsb2082

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.13.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In that<small><small>[^2]</small></small> day there shall be a fountain<small><small>[^3]</small></small> opened to the house<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of David and to the inhabitants of Jerusalem for sin and for uncleanness<small><small>[^5]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.13.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And it shall come to pass in that day, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, \[that\] I will cut<small><small>[^6]</small></small> off the names of the idols out of the land, and they shall no more be remembered: and also I will cause<small><small>[^7]</small></small> the prophets and the unclean<small><small>[^8]</small></small> spirit to pass out of the land. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.13.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass, \[that\] when any shall yet prophesy, then his father and his<small><small>[^9]</small></small> mother that begat him shall say unto him, Thou shalt not live; for thou speakest lies in the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and his father and his mother that begat him shall thrust him through when he prophesieth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.13.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass in that day, \[that\] the prophets<small><small>[^10]</small></small> shall be ashamed every one of his vision, when he hath prophesied; neither shall they wear<small><small>[^11]</small></small> a rough garment to deceive<small><small>[^12]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.13.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But he shall say, I \[am\] no<small><small>[^13]</small></small> prophet, I \[am\] an husbandman; for man taught me to keep cattle from my youth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Zech.13.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[one\] shall say unto him, What<small><small>[^14]</small></small> \[are\] these wounds in thine hands? Then he shall answer, \[Those\] with which I was<small><small>[^15]</small></small> wounded \[in\] the house of my friends.
