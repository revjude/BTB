$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.22.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The same<small><small>[^49]</small></small> day came to him the Sadducees<small><small>[^50]</small></small>, which<small><small>[^51]</small></small> say that there is no resurrection, and asked him, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.22.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Saying, Master<small><small>[^52]</small></small>, Moses<small><small>[^53]</small></small> said, If a man die, having no children, his brother shall marry his wife, and raise up seed unto his brother. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.22.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now there were with us seven brethren: and the first, when he had married a wife, deceased, and, having no issue, left his wife unto his brother:<small><small>[^54]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.22.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Likewise the second also, and the third, unto the seventh<small><small>[^55]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.22.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And last of all the woman died also. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.22.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore in the resurrection whose wife shall she be of the seven?
