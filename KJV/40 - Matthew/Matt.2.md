***The Pilgrimage of the Magi***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Mic.5|Micah 5:1–6]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2096

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now when Jesus<small><small>[^2]</small></small> was born in Beth–lehem<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of Judea in the days of Herod<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the king, behold, there came wise men from<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the east to Jerusalem with him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when he had gathered all the chief<small><small>[^11]</small></small> priests and people Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.2.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Herod, when he had privily called the wise men, enquired of them diligently what time also. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.2.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When they had heard the king, they departed; and, lo, the star<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, When they saw the star, they rejoiced<small><small>[^22]</small></small> with exceeding great joy. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.2.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when they were come into the house, they saw<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the young child with Mary his mother, and fell down, and worshipped<small><small>[^24]</small></small> him: and when they had opened their treasures, they presented<small><small>[^25]</small></small> unto him gifts; gold, and frankincense<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, and myrrh. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.2.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And being warned<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of God in a dream that they should not return to Herod, they departed<small><small>[^28]</small></small> into their own country another way.

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.2.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when they were departed, behold, the angel<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of the Lord appeareth to Joseph in a dream, saying, Arise<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, and take the young child and his mother, and flee into Egypt, and be thou there until<small><small>[^31]</small></small> I bring thee word: for<small><small>[^32]</small></small> Herod will seek the young child to destroy him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.2.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When he arose, he took the young child and his mother by night, and departed into Egypt:<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.2.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And was there until<small><small>[^34]</small></small> the death of Herod: that<small><small>[^35]</small></small> it might be fulfilled which was spoken of the Lord by the prophet, saying, Out<small><small>[^36]</small></small> of Egypt have I called my son.

***Weeping and Great Mourning***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Jer.31|Jeremiah 31:1–25]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2098
