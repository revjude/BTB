<big><big>[[Matt.3.1 The Mission of John the Baptist|⥃]]</big></big>***The Mission of John the Baptist***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Isa.40|Isaiah 40:1–5]] <big>†</big> [[Mark.1|Mark 1:1–8]] <big>†</big> [[Luke.3|Luke 3:1–20]] <big>†</big> [[John.1|John 1:19–28]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In those<small><small>[^2]</small></small> days came John<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the Baptist, preaching<small><small>[^4]</small></small> in the wilderness<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of Judea, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And saying, Repent<small><small>[^6]</small></small> ye: for<small><small>[^7]</small></small> the kingdom of heaven is at hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For this is he that was spoken of by<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the prophet Esaias, saying, The voice of one crying in the wilderness, Prepare<small><small>[^9]</small></small> ye the way of the Lord, make his paths straight. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the same John had his raiment<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of camel’s hair, and a leathern girdle about his loins; and his<small><small>[^11]</small></small> meat was locusts and wild<small><small>[^12]</small></small> honey. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.3.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then went out to him Jerusalem, and all Judea, and all the region round about Jordan,<small><small>[^13]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Matt.3.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And were<small><small>[^14]</small></small> baptized of him in Jordan, confessing<small><small>[^15]</small></small> their sins.
