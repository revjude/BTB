***Corruption on the Earth***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.24|Matthew 24:36–51]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb21

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.6.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when men began to multiply<small><small>[^2]</small></small> on the face of the earth, and daughters were born unto them,<small><small>[^3]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.6.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That the sons<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of God saw<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the daughters of men that they<small><small>[^6]</small></small> \[were\] fair; and they<small><small>[^7]</small></small> took them wives of all which they chose. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.6.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said, My<small><small>[^8]</small></small> spirit shall not always strive with man, for that he also \[is<small><small>[^9]</small></small>\] flesh: yet his days shall be an hundred and twenty years. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.6.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> There were giants<small><small>[^10]</small></small> in the earth in those days; and also after<small><small>[^11]</small></small> that, when the sons of God came in unto the daughters of men, and they bare \[children\] to them, the same \[became\] mighty men which \[were\] of old, men of<small><small>[^12]</small></small> renown.

***Noah’s Favor with God*** ^bsb22

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.6.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Noah found grace in the eyes of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^21]</small></small>

and it shall be for food for thee, and for them.<small><small>[^55]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.6.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus did Noah; according to all that God commanded him, so did he. <small><small>[^56]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Gen.5|<< Genesis 5]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Gen.7|Genesis 7 >>]]</td></tr></table>
