<big><big>[[Gen.48.1 Jacob Blesses Ephraim and Manasseh|⥃]]</big></big>***Jacob Blesses Ephraim and Manasseh***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass after these things, that \[one\] told Joseph, Behold, thy father<small><small>[^2]</small></small> \[is\] sick: and he took with him his two sons<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, Manasseh and Ephraim. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[one\] told Jacob, and said, Behold, thy son Joseph cometh unto thee: and Israel strengthened<small><small>[^4]</small></small> himself, and sat upon the bed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jacob said unto Joseph, God<small><small>[^5]</small></small> Almighty appeared<small><small>[^6]</small></small> unto me at Luz<small><small>[^7]</small></small> in the land of Canaan, and blessed me, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And said unto me, Behold, I<small><small>[^8]</small></small> will make thee fruitful, and multiply thee, and I will make of thee a multitude of people; and will give<small><small>[^9]</small></small> this land to thy seed after thee \[for\] an everlasting<small><small>[^10]</small></small> possession. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And now thy two sons<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, Ephraim and Manasseh, which were born unto thee in the land of Egypt before I came unto thee into Egypt, \[are\] mine<small><small>[^12]</small></small>; as Reuben<small><small>[^13]</small></small> and Simeon, they shall be mine. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thy issue, which thou begettest after them, shall be thine, \[and\] shall be called<small><small>[^14]</small></small> after the name of their brethren in their inheritance. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And as for me, when I came from Padan<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, Rachel<small><small>[^16]</small></small> died by me in the land of Canaan in the way, when yet \[there was\] but a little way to come unto Ephrath: and I buried her there in the way of Ephrath; the same \[is\] Beth–lehem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Israel beheld Joseph’s sons, and said, Who \[are\] these? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joseph said unto his father, They \[are\] my sons<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, whom God hath given me in this \[place\]. And he said, Bring them, I pray thee, unto me, and I will bless them<small><small>[^18]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now the eyes<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of Israel were dim<small><small>[^20]</small></small> for age, \[so that\] he could not see. And he brought them near unto him; and he kissed<small><small>[^21]</small></small> them, and embraced them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Israel said unto Joseph, I had not<small><small>[^22]</small></small> thought to see thy face: and, lo, God<small><small>[^23]</small></small> hath shewed me also thy seed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joseph brought them out from between his knees, and he bowed himself<small><small>[^24]</small></small> with his face to the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joseph took them both, Ephraim in his right hand toward Israel’s left hand, and Manasseh in his left hand toward Israel’s right hand, and brought \[them\] near unto him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Israel stretched out his right hand<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, and laid<small><small>[^26]</small></small> \[it\] upon Ephraim’s head, who \[was\] the younger, and his left hand upon Manasseh’s head, guiding<small><small>[^27]</small></small> his hands wittingly; for Manasseh \[was\] the firstborn<small><small>[^28]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And he blessed<small><small>[^29]</small></small> Joseph, and said, God, before whom my fathers Abraham and Isaac did walk<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, the God which fed me<small><small>[^31]</small></small> all my life long unto this day, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The Angel<small><small>[^32]</small></small> which redeemed<small><small>[^33]</small></small> me from all evil, bless the lads; and let my name<small><small>[^34]</small></small> be named on them, and the name of my fathers Abraham and Isaac; and let them grow into<small><small>[^35]</small></small> a multitude<small><small>[^36]</small></small> in the midst of the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when Joseph saw that his father laid his<small><small>[^37]</small></small> right hand upon the head of Ephraim, it displeased him<small><small>[^38]</small></small>: and he held up his father’s hand, to remove it from Ephraim’s head unto Manasseh’s head. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joseph said unto his father, Not so<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, my father: for this<small><small>[^40]</small></small> \[is\] the firstborn; put thy right hand upon his head. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And his father refused, and said, I know \[it<small><small>[^41]</small></small>\], my son, I know \[it\]: he also shall become<small><small>[^42]</small></small> a people, and he also shall be great: but truly his younger brother shall be greater than he, and his seed shall become a multitude<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of nations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he blessed them that day, saying, In thee shall Israel bless<small><small>[^44]</small></small>, saying, God make thee as Ephraim and as Manasseh: and he set<small><small>[^45]</small></small> Ephraim before Manasseh. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Israel said unto Joseph, Behold<small><small>[^46]</small></small>, I die: but God<small><small>[^47]</small></small> shall be with you, and bring you again unto the land<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of your fathers. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.48.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover I have given<small><small>[^49]</small></small> to thee one portion above thy brethren, which I took out of the hand of the Amorite<small><small>[^50]</small></small> with my sword and with my bow. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Gen.47|<< Genesis 47]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Gen.49|Genesis 49 >>]]</td></tr></table>
