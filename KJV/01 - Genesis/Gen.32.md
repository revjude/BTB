<big><big>[[Gen.32.1 Jacob Prepares to Meet Esau|⥃]]</big></big>***Jacob Prepares to Meet Esau***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jacob went on his way, and the angels<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of God met him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when Jacob saw them, he said, This \[is\] God’s<small><small>[^3]</small></small> host: and he called the name<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of that place Mahanaim<small><small>[^5]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jacob sent<small><small>[^6]</small></small> messengers before him to Esau his brother unto the land<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of Seir<small><small>[^8]</small></small>, the country<small><small>[^9]</small></small> of Edom<small><small>[^10]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he commanded them, saying, Thus shall ye speak unto my lord<small><small>[^11]</small></small> Esau; Thy servant<small><small>[^12]</small></small> Jacob saith thus, I have sojourned with Laban, and stayed there until now: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I have oxen<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, and asses, flocks, and menservants, and womenservants: and I have sent to tell my lord, that I may find<small><small>[^14]</small></small> grace in thy sight. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And the messengers returned to Jacob, saying, We came to thy brother Esau, and also he cometh to meet thee, and four<small><small>[^15]</small></small> hundred men with him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Jacob was greatly<small><small>[^16]</small></small> afraid and distressed<small><small>[^17]</small></small>: and he<small><small>[^18]</small></small> divided the people that \[was\] with him, and the flocks, and herds, and the camels, into two bands; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And said, If Esau come to the one company, and smite it, then the other company which is left shall escape.<small><small>[^19]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jacob<small><small>[^20]</small></small> said, O God<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of my father Abraham, and God of my father Isaac, the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^22]</small></small></span> which saidst unto me, Return unto thy country, and to thy kindred, and I will deal well with thee: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I am not worthy of the least of all<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the mercies<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, and of all the truth<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, which thou hast shewed unto thy servant; for with my staff<small><small>[^26]</small></small> I passed over this Jordan; and now I am become two bands<small><small>[^27]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Deliver<small><small>[^28]</small></small> me, I pray thee, from the hand of my brother, from the hand of Esau: for I fear him, lest he will come and smite me, \[and\] the mother<small><small>[^29]</small></small> with<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the children. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thou<small><small>[^31]</small></small> saidst, I will<small><small>[^32]</small></small> surely do thee good, and make thy seed as the sand of the sea, which cannot be numbered for multitude. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he lodged there that same night; and took of that which<small><small>[^33]</small></small> came to his hand<small><small>[^34]</small></small> a present<small><small>[^35]</small></small> for Esau his brother; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Two hundred she goats, and twenty he goats, two hundred ewes, and twenty rams,<small><small>[^36]</small></small><small><small>[^37]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thirty milch camels with their colts, forty kine, and ten bulls, twenty she asses, and ten foals. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he delivered \[them\] into the hand of his servants, every drove by themselves; and said unto his servants, Pass over before me, and put a space<small><small>[^38]</small></small> betwixt drove and drove. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he commanded the foremost, saying, When Esau my brother meeteth thee, and asketh thee, saying, Whose \[art<small><small>[^39]</small></small>\] thou? and whither goest thou? and whose \[are\] these before thee? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then thou shalt say, \[They be\] thy servant Jacob’s; it \[is\] a present sent unto my lord Esau: and, behold, also he \[is\] behind us.<small><small>[^40]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And so commanded he the second, and the third, and all that followed the droves, saying, On this manner shall ye speak unto Esau, when ye find him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And say ye moreover, Behold, thy servant Jacob \[is\] behind us. For he said, I will appease<small><small>[^41]</small></small> him with the present that goeth before me, and afterward I will see his face; peradventure<small><small>[^42]</small></small> he will accept of me<small><small>[^43]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.32.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So went the present over before him: and himself lodged that night in the company.
