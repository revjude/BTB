***The Covenant of Circumcision*** ^bsb51

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.17.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And God said unto Abraham, Thou shalt keep my covenant therefore, thou, and thy seed after thee in their generations.<small><small>[^24]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.17.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This \[is\] my covenant, which ye shall keep, between me and you and thy seed after thee; Every<small><small>[^25]</small></small> man child among you shall be circumcised. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.17.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And ye shall circumcise the flesh<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of your foreskin; and it shall be a token<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of the covenant betwixt me and you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.17.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he that is eight days old<small><small>[^28]</small></small> shall be circumcised among you, every man child in your generations, he that is born<small><small>[^29]</small></small> in the house, or bought with money of any stranger, which \[is\] not of thy seed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.17.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He that is born<small><small>[^30]</small></small> in thy house, and he that is bought<small><small>[^31]</small></small> with thy money, must needs be circumcised: and my covenant shall be in your flesh for an everlasting covenant. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.17.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the uncircumcised man child whose flesh of his foreskin is not circumcised, that soul shall be cut<small><small>[^32]</small></small> off from his people; he hath broken<small><small>[^33]</small></small> my covenant.
