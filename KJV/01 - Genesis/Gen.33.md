***Jacob Meets Esau*** ^bsb99

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jacob lifted up his eyes, and looked, and, behold, Esau came<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, and with him four hundred men. And he<small><small>[^3]</small></small> divided the children unto Leah, and unto Rachel, and unto the two handmaids. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he put the handmaids and their children foremost, and Leah and her children after, and Rachel<small><small>[^4]</small></small> and Joseph hindermost. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he passed<small><small>[^5]</small></small> over before them, and bowed<small><small>[^6]</small></small> himself to the ground seven times<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, until he came near to his brother. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Esau ran to meet him, and embraced<small><small>[^8]</small></small> him, and fell on<small><small>[^9]</small></small> his neck, and kissed him: and they wept. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he lifted up his eyes, and saw the women and the children; and said, Who \[are\] those with<small><small>[^10]</small></small> thee? And he said, The children<small><small>[^11]</small></small> which God hath graciously given thy servant. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the handmaidens came near, they and their children, and they bowed themselves. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Leah also with her children came near, and bowed themselves: and after came Joseph near and Rachel, and they bowed themselves. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, What \[meanest\] thou by all this drove<small><small>[^12]</small></small> which I met? And he said, \[These are\] to find<small><small>[^13]</small></small> grace in the sight of my lord. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Esau said, I have enough<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, my brother<small><small>[^15]</small></small>; keep that thou hast unto thyself<small><small>[^16]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jacob said, Nay, I pray thee, if now<small><small>[^17]</small></small> I have found grace in thy sight, then receive<small><small>[^18]</small></small> my present at my hand: for therefore I have seen<small><small>[^19]</small></small> thy face, as though I had seen the face of God, and thou wast pleased with me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Take, I pray thee, my blessing<small><small>[^20]</small></small> that is brought to thee; because God hath dealt graciously with me, and because<small><small>[^21]</small></small> I have enough<small><small>[^22]</small></small>. And he urged him<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, and he took \[it\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Let us take our journey, and let us go, and I will go before thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto him, My lord knoweth that the children<small><small>[^24]</small></small> \[are\] tender, and the flocks and herds with young \[are\] with me: and if men should overdrive them one day, all the flock will die. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let my lord, I pray thee, pass over before his servant: and I will lead on softly, according as<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the cattle that goeth before me and the children be able<small><small>[^26]</small></small> to endure, until I come unto my lord unto Seir<small><small>[^27]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Esau said, Let me now leave<small><small>[^28]</small></small> with thee \[some\] of the folk that \[are\] with me. And he said, What needeth it? let me find grace<small><small>[^29]</small></small> in the sight of my lord. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Esau returned that day on his way unto Seir. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.33.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jacob journeyed to Succoth<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, and built him an house, and made booths for his cattle: therefore the name of the place is called Succoth<small><small>[^31]</small></small>.
