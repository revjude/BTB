***Jacob Returns to Bethel*** ^bsb103

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.35.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God said<small><small>[^2]</small></small> unto Jacob, Arise, go up to Beth–el<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, and dwell there: and make there an altar unto God, that appeared unto thee when thou<small><small>[^4]</small></small> fleddest from the face of Esau thy brother. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.35.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Jacob said unto his<small><small>[^5]</small></small> household, and to all that \[were\] with him, Put away the strange<small><small>[^6]</small></small> gods that \[are\] among you, and be clean<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, and change your garments: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.35.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And let us arise, and go up to Beth–el; and I will make there an altar unto God, who answered<small><small>[^8]</small></small> me in the day of my distress, and was with<small><small>[^9]</small></small> me in the way which I went. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.35.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they gave unto Jacob all the strange gods which \[were\] in their hand, and \[all their\] earrings<small><small>[^10]</small></small> which \[were\] in their ears; and Jacob hid them<small><small>[^11]</small></small> under the oak<small><small>[^12]</small></small> which \[was\] by Shechem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.35.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they journeyed: and the terror of God was upon the cities that \[were\] round about them, and they did not pursue after the sons of Jacob.<small><small>[^13]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.35.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Jacob came to Luz<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, which \[is\] in the land of Canaan, that \[is\], Beth–el, he and all the people that \[were\] with him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.35.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he built<small><small>[^15]</small></small> there an altar, and called the place El–beth–el<small><small>[^16]</small></small>: because there God appeared unto him, when he fled from the face of his brother. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.35.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Deborah Rebekah’s<small><small>[^17]</small></small> nurse died, and she was buried beneath Beth–el under an oak<small><small>[^18]</small></small>: and the name of it was called Allon–bachuth<small><small>[^19]</small></small>.
