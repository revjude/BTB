***Abram and Lot Part Ways*** ^bsb41

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abram went up out of Egypt, he, and his wife, and all that he had, and Lot with him, into the south<small><small>[^2]</small></small>.<small><small>[^3]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abram \[was\] very rich in cattle, in silver, and in gold.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he went on his journeys from<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the south even to Beth–el, unto the place where his tent had been at the beginning, between Beth–el and Hai<small><small>[^6]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Unto<small><small>[^7]</small></small> the place of the altar, which he had made there at the first: and there Abram called<small><small>[^8]</small></small> on the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Lot also, which went with Abram, had flocks, and herds, and tents<small><small>[^9]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the land was not able to bear them, that they might dwell together: for their substance was great, so that they could not dwell together.<small><small>[^10]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there was a strife<small><small>[^11]</small></small> between the herdmen of Abram’s cattle and the herdmen of Lot’s cattle: and the Canaanite<small><small>[^12]</small></small> and the Perizzite dwelled<small><small>[^13]</small></small> then in the land. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abram said unto Lot, Let<small><small>[^14]</small></small> there be no strife, I pray thee, between me and thee, and between my herdmen and thy herdmen; for we \[be\] brethren<small><small>[^15]</small></small><small><small>[^16]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Is\] not<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the whole land before thee? separate thyself, I pray thee, from me: if \[thou wilt<small><small>[^18]</small></small> take\] the left hand, then I will go to the right; or if \[thou depart\] to the right hand, then I will go to the left.

***Lot Proceeds toward Sodom*** ^bsb42

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Lot lifted up his eyes, and beheld<small><small>[^19]</small></small> all the plain<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of Jordan, that it \[was\] well watered every where, before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> destroyed Sodom and Gomorrah, \[even\] as the garden<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, like the land of Egypt, as thou comest unto Zoar<small><small>[^22]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Lot chose<small><small>[^23]</small></small> him all the plain of Jordan; and Lot journeyed east: and they<small><small>[^24]</small></small> separated themselves the one from the other.<small><small>[^25]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Abram dwelled in the land of Canaan, and Lot dwelled<small><small>[^26]</small></small> in the cities of the plain, and pitched<small><small>[^27]</small></small> \[his\] tent toward Sodom. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the<small><small>[^28]</small></small> men of Sodom \[were\] wicked and sinners before<small><small>[^29]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> exceedingly.

***God Renews the Promise to Abram*** ^bsb43

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Abram, after that Lot was<small><small>[^30]</small></small> separated from him, Lift<small><small>[^31]</small></small> up now thine eyes, and look from the place where thou art northward<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, and southward, and eastward, and westward: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For all the land which thou seest, to thee will I give it, and to thy seed for ever.<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will make thy seed as the dust of the earth: so that if a man can number the dust of the earth, \[then\] shall thy seed also be numbered.<small><small>[^34]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Arise, walk through the land in the length of it and in the breadth of it; for I will give it unto thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.13.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Abram removed \[his\] tent, and came and dwelt in the plain<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of Mamre<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, which \[is\] in Hebron<small><small>[^37]</small></small>, and built there an altar<small><small>[^38]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Gen.12|<< Genesis 12]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Gen.14|Genesis 14 >>]]</td></tr></table>
