***Joseph’s Dreams*** ^bsb110

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jacob dwelt in the land wherein his father was a stranger<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, in the land of Canaan.<small><small>[^3]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> These \[are\] the generations<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of Jacob. Joseph, \[being\] seventeen years old, was feeding the flock with his brethren; and the lad \[was\] with the sons of Bilhah, and with the sons of Zilpah, his father’s wives<small><small>[^5]</small></small>: and Joseph brought unto his father their evil report<small><small>[^6]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now Israel loved<small><small>[^7]</small></small> Joseph more than all his children, because he \[was\] the son<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of his old age: and he made him a coat<small><small>[^9]</small></small> of \[many\] colours<small><small>[^10]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when his brethren saw that their father loved him more than all his brethren, they hated him<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, and could not speak peaceably unto him<small><small>[^12]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joseph dreamed<small><small>[^13]</small></small> a dream, and he told \[it\] his brethren: and they<small><small>[^14]</small></small> hated him yet the more. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto them, Hear<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, I pray you, this dream which I have dreamed: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, behold, we \[were\] binding sheaves in the field, and, lo, my sheaf arose, and also stood upright; and, behold, your sheaves<small><small>[^16]</small></small> stood round about, and made obeisance<small><small>[^17]</small></small> to my sheaf. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And his brethren said to him, Shalt thou indeed reign over us<small><small>[^18]</small></small>? or shalt thou indeed have dominion over us? And they hated him yet the more for his dreams, and for his words. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And he dreamed yet another dream<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, and told it his brethren, and said, Behold, I have dreamed a dream more; and, behold, the sun<small><small>[^20]</small></small> and the moon and the eleven stars<small><small>[^21]</small></small> made obeisance to me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he told \[it\] to his father, and to his brethren: and his father rebuked him, and said unto him, What \[is\] this dream that thou hast dreamed? $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Reuben returned unto the pit; and, behold, Joseph \[was\] not in the pit; and he rent<small><small>[^64]</small></small> his clothes. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.37.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he returned unto his brethren, and said, The child \[is\] not; and I, whither shall I go?<small><small>[^65]</small></small>
