 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.16.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now Sarai Abram’s wife bare<small><small>[^2]</small></small> him no children: and she had an handmaid, an Egyptian<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, whose name<small><small>[^4]</small></small> \[was\] Hagar.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.16.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Sarai said unto Abram, Behold now, the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^6]</small></small></span> hath restrained me from bearing: I pray thee, go in unto my maid; it may be that I may obtain children<small><small>[^7]</small></small> by her.
