***The Covenant of the Rainbow*** ^bsb29

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God blessed<small><small>[^2]</small></small> Noah and his sons, and said unto them, Be<small><small>[^3]</small></small> fruitful, and multiply, and replenish the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the fear of you and the dread of you shall be upon every beast of the earth, and upon every fowl of the air, upon all that moveth \[upon\] the earth, and upon all the fishes of the sea; into your hand are they delivered.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Every<small><small>[^5]</small></small> moving thing that liveth shall be meat for you; even<small><small>[^6]</small></small> as the green herb have I given you all things. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But flesh with the life<small><small>[^7]</small></small> thereof, \[which is\] the blood thereof, shall ye not eat. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And surely your blood of your lives will I require; at the hand of every<small><small>[^8]</small></small> beast will I require it, and at<small><small>[^9]</small></small> the hand of man; at the hand of every man’s brother<small><small>[^10]</small></small> will I require the life of man. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whoso sheddeth man’s blood, by<small><small>[^11]</small></small> man shall his blood be shed: for in<small><small>[^12]</small></small> the image of God made he man. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And you, be ye fruitful, and multiply; bring forth abundantly in the earth, and multiply therein.<small><small>[^13]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God spake unto Noah, and to his sons with him, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I, behold, I establish my covenant with you, and with your seed after you;<small><small>[^14]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And with every living creature that \[is\] with you, of the fowl, of the cattle, and of every beast of the earth with you; from all that go out of the ark, to every beast of the earth.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I<small><small>[^16]</small></small> will establish my covenant with you; neither shall all<small><small>[^17]</small></small> flesh be cut off any more by the waters of a flood; neither shall there any more be a flood to destroy the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God said, This \[is\] the token of the covenant which I make between me and you and every living creature that \[is\] with you, for perpetual generations:<small><small>[^18]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I do set my bow in the cloud, and it shall be for a token of a covenant between me and the earth.<small><small>[^19]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass, when I bring a cloud over the earth, that the bow shall be seen in the cloud: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will remember<small><small>[^20]</small></small> my covenant, which \[is\] between me and you and every living creature of all flesh; and the waters<small><small>[^21]</small></small> shall no more become a flood to destroy all flesh. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the bow shall be in the cloud; and I will look upon it, that I may remember the everlasting<small><small>[^22]</small></small> covenant between God and every living creature of all flesh that \[is\] upon the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God said unto Noah, This \[is\] the token of the covenant, which I have established between me and all flesh that \[is\] upon the earth.

***Noah’s Shame and Canaan’s Curse*** ^bsb30

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sons of Noah, that went forth of the ark, were Shem<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, and Ham<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, and Japheth: and Ham \[is\] the father of Canaan<small><small>[^25]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> These<small><small>[^26]</small></small> \[are\] the three sons of Noah: and of<small><small>[^27]</small></small> them was the whole earth overspread. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Noah began \[to be\] an husbandman<small><small>[^28]</small></small>, and he planted<small><small>[^29]</small></small> a vineyard: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he drank of the wine, and was<small><small>[^30]</small></small> drunken; and he<small><small>[^31]</small></small> was uncovered within his tent. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Ham<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, the father of Canaan, saw the nakedness of his father, and told<small><small>[^33]</small></small> his two brethren without. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Shem and Japheth took a garment, and laid \[it\] upon both their shoulders, and went backward, and covered the nakedness of their father; and their faces \[were\] backward, and they saw not their father’s nakedness.<small><small>[^34]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Noah awoke from his wine, and knew what his younger son had done unto him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Cursed<small><small>[^35]</small></small> \[be\] Canaan; a servant<small><small>[^36]</small></small> of servants shall he be unto his brethren.

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Blessed<small><small>[^37]</small></small> \[be\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^38]</small></small></span> God of Shem<small><small>[^39]</small></small>; and Canaan shall be his servant<small><small>[^40]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.9.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> God shall enlarge<small><small>[^41]</small></small> Japheth<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, and he shall dwell<small><small>[^43]</small></small><small><small>[^44]</small></small> in the tents of Shem; and Canaan shall be his servant.
