<big><big>[[Gen.5.1 The Descendants of Adam|⥃]]</big></big>***The Descendants of Adam***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[1Chr.1|1 Chronicles 1:1–3]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This \[is\] the book<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of the generations of Adam. $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Adam lived an hundred<small><small>[^6]</small></small> and thirty years, and begat \[a son\] in his<small><small>[^7]</small></small> own likeness, after his image; and called<small><small>[^8]</small></small> his name Seth:<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the<small><small>[^10]</small></small> days of Adam after he had begotten Seth were eight hundred years: and he<small><small>[^11]</small></small> begat sons and daughters: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all the days that Adam lived were nine<small><small>[^12]</small></small> hundred and thirty years: and he died<small><small>[^13]</small></small>.<small><small>[^14]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Seth lived an hundred and five years, and begat<small><small>[^15]</small></small> Enos:<small><small>[^16]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Seth lived after he begat Enos eight hundred and seven years, and begat sons and daughters: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all the days of Seth were nine hundred and twelve years: and he died.<small><small>[^17]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Enos lived ninety years, and begat Cainan<small><small>[^18]</small></small>:<small><small>[^19]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Enos lived after he begat<small><small>[^20]</small></small> Cainan eight hundred and fifteen years, and begat sons and daughters: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all the days of Enos were nine hundred and five years: and he died<small><small>[^21]</small></small>.<small><small>[^22]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Cainan lived seventy years, and begat Mahalaleel<small><small>[^23]</small></small>:<small><small>[^24]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Cainan lived after he begat Mahalaleel eight hundred and forty years, and begat<small><small>[^25]</small></small> sons and daughters: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all the days of Cainan were nine hundred and ten years: and he died.<small><small>[^26]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Mahalaleel lived sixty and five years, and begat Jared<small><small>[^27]</small></small>:<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Mahalaleel lived after he begat Jared eight hundred and thirty years, and begat<small><small>[^29]</small></small> sons and daughters: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.5.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all the days of Mahalaleel were eight hundred ninety and five years: and he died<small><small>[^30]</small></small>.<small><small>[^31]</small></small>
