<big><big>[[Gen.39.1 Joseph and Potiphar’s Wife|⥃]]</big></big>***Joseph and Potiphar’s Wife***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joseph<small><small>[^2]</small></small> was brought down to Egypt; and Potiphar, an officer of Pharaoh, captain of the guard, an Egyptian, bought him of the hands of the Ishmeelites<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, which had brought him down thither.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^5]</small></small></span> was with Joseph, and he was a prosperous man; and he was in the house<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of his master the Egyptian. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And his master saw that<small><small>[^7]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[was\] with him, and that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> made all that he did to prosper<small><small>[^8]</small></small> in his hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joseph<small><small>[^9]</small></small> found grace in his sight, and he served him: and he made him overseer<small><small>[^10]</small></small> over his house, and all \[that\] he had he put into his hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass from the time \[that\] he had made him overseer in his house, and over all that he had, that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> blessed the Egyptian’s house for Joseph’s<small><small>[^11]</small></small> sake; and the blessing of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> was upon all that he had in the house, and in the field. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he left<small><small>[^12]</small></small> all that he had in Joseph’s hand; and he knew not ought he had, save<small><small>[^13]</small></small> the bread which he did eat. And Joseph was \[a\] goodly \[person<small><small>[^14]</small></small>\], and well favoured. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass after these things, that his master’s wife cast<small><small>[^15]</small></small> her eyes upon Joseph; and she said, Lie<small><small>[^16]</small></small> with me.<small><small>[^17]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But he refused<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, and said unto his master’s wife, Behold, my master<small><small>[^19]</small></small> wotteth not what \[is\] with me in the house, and he hath committed all that he hath to my hand; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[There is\] none<small><small>[^20]</small></small> greater in this house than I; neither hath he kept back any thing from me but thee, because thou \[art\] his wife: how then<small><small>[^21]</small></small> can I do this great wickedness, and sin<small><small>[^22]</small></small> against God? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, as she spake<small><small>[^23]</small></small> to Joseph day by day, that he hearkened not unto her, to lie by her, \[or\] to be<small><small>[^24]</small></small> with her. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass about this time, that \[Joseph\] went into the house to do his business; and \[there was\] none of the men<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of the house there within. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And she caught<small><small>[^26]</small></small> him by his garment, saying, Lie with me: and he left<small><small>[^27]</small></small> his garment in her hand, and fled, and got him out.

<big><big>[[Gen.39.13 Joseph Falsely Imprisoned|⥃]]</big></big>***Joseph Falsely Imprisoned***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when she saw that he had left his garment in her hand, and was fled forth, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That she called unto the men of her house, and spake unto them, saying, See, he hath<small><small>[^28]</small></small> brought in an Hebrew<small><small>[^29]</small></small> unto us to mock us; he came<small><small>[^30]</small></small> in unto me to lie with me, and I cried with a loud<small><small>[^31]</small></small> voice: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when he heard that I lifted up my voice and cried, that he left his garment with me, and fled, and got him out. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And she laid up his garment by her, until his lord came home.<small><small>[^32]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And she spake unto him according to these words, saying, The Hebrew servant, which thou hast brought unto us, came in unto me to mock me:<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, as I lifted up my voice and cried, that he left his garment with me, and fled out. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when his master heard<small><small>[^34]</small></small> the words of his wife, which she spake unto him, saying, After this manner did thy servant to me; that his wrath<small><small>[^35]</small></small> was kindled. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.39.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joseph’s master took him, and put him into the prison<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, a place where the king’s<small><small>[^37]</small></small> prisoners \[were\] bound: and he was there in the prison.
