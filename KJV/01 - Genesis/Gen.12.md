<big><big>[[Gen.12.1 The Call of Abram|⥃]]</big></big>***The Call of Abram***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Gen.26|Genesis 26:1–5]] <big>†</big> [[Acts.7|Acts 7:1–8]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> had<small><small>[^2]</small></small> said unto Abram, Get<small><small>[^3]</small></small> thee out of thy country, and from thy kindred, and from thy father’s house, unto a land that I will shew thee: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will make of thee a great nation, and I will bless thee, and make thy name great; and thou shalt<small><small>[^4]</small></small> be a blessing:<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I<small><small>[^6]</small></small> will bless them that bless thee, and curse him that curseth thee: and in thee<small><small>[^7]</small></small> shall all families of the earth be blessed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Abram departed, as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> had spoken unto him; and Lot<small><small>[^8]</small></small> went with him: and Abram \[was\] seventy and five years old when he departed out<small><small>[^9]</small></small> of Haran. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abram took Sarai his wife, and Lot his brother’s son, and all their substance that they had gathered, and the souls<small><small>[^10]</small></small> that they had gotten in<small><small>[^11]</small></small> Haran; and they went forth to go into the land of Canaan; and into<small><small>[^12]</small></small> the land of Canaan<small><small>[^13]</small></small> they came. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abram passed<small><small>[^14]</small></small> through the land unto the place of Sichem<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, unto the plain<small><small>[^16]</small></small> of Moreh<small><small>[^17]</small></small>. And the Canaanite<small><small>[^18]</small></small> \[was\] then in the land. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> appeared<small><small>[^19]</small></small> unto Abram, and said, Unto thy<small><small>[^20]</small></small> seed will I give this land: and there builded<small><small>[^21]</small></small> he an altar unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, who appeared unto him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he removed from thence unto a mountain on the east of Beth–el<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, and pitched his tent, \[having\] Beth–el on the west, and Hai<small><small>[^23]</small></small> on the east: and there he builded an altar unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and called<small><small>[^24]</small></small> upon the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abram journeyed, going on still<small><small>[^25]</small></small> toward the south.

<big><big>[[Gen.12.10 Abram and Sarai in Egypt|⥃]]</big></big>***Abram and Sarai in Egypt***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there was a<small><small>[^26]</small></small> famine in the land: and Abram went<small><small>[^27]</small></small> down into Egypt to sojourn there; for the famine \[was\] grievous in the land.<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when he was come near to enter into Egypt, that he said unto Sarai his wife, Behold now, I know that thou \[art\] a fair<small><small>[^29]</small></small> woman to look upon: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore it shall come to pass, when the Egyptians shall see thee, that they shall say, This \[is\] his wife: and they will kill<small><small>[^30]</small></small> me, but they will save thee alive. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Say<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, I pray thee, thou<small><small>[^32]</small></small> \[art\] my sister: that it may be well with me for thy sake; and<small><small>[^33]</small></small> my soul shall live because of thee. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, that, when Abram was come into Egypt, the Egyptians beheld<small><small>[^34]</small></small> the woman that she \[was\] very fair. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The princes<small><small>[^35]</small></small> also of Pharaoh saw her, and commended her before Pharaoh: and the woman was taken<small><small>[^36]</small></small> into Pharaoh’s house. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he<small><small>[^37]</small></small> entreated Abram well for her sake: and he had<small><small>[^38]</small></small> sheep, and oxen, and he asses, and menservants, and maidservants, and she asses, and camels. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> plagued Pharaoh and his house with great plagues because of Sarai Abram’s wife.<small><small>[^39]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Pharaoh called Abram, and said, What \[is\] this \[that\] thou hast done unto me? why didst thou not tell me that she \[was\] thy wife?<small><small>[^40]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Why saidst thou, She \[is\] my sister? so I might have taken her to me to wife: now therefore behold thy wife, take \[her\], and go thy way. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.12.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Pharaoh commanded \[his\] men concerning him: and they sent him away, and his wife, and all that he had. <small><small>[^41]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Gen.11|<< Genesis 11]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Gen.13|Genesis 13 >>]]</td></tr></table>
