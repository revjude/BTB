<big><big>[[Gen.49.1 Jacob Blesses His Sons|⥃]]</big></big>***Jacob Blesses His Sons***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jacob called unto his sons, and said, Gather<small><small>[^2]</small></small> yourselves together, that I may tell you \[that\] which shall befall you in the last days<small><small>[^3]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Gather yourselves together, and hear, ye sons of Jacob; and hearken<small><small>[^4]</small></small> unto Israel your father. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Reuben, thou \[art\] my firstborn<small><small>[^5]</small></small>, my might<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, and the beginning of my strength, the excellency of dignity, and the excellency of power: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Unstable<small><small>[^7]</small></small> as water, thou shalt not excel<small><small>[^8]</small></small>; because<small><small>[^9]</small></small> thou wentest up to thy father’s bed; then defiledst thou \[it\]: he went up to my couch<small><small>[^10]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Simeon<small><small>[^11]</small></small> and Levi \[are\] brethren; instruments<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of cruelty \[are in\] their habitations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O my soul<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, come<small><small>[^14]</small></small> not thou into their secret<small><small>[^15]</small></small>; unto their<small><small>[^16]</small></small> assembly, mine honour<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, be not thou united: for in their anger they slew a man<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, and in their selfwill they digged down a wall<small><small>[^19]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Cursed<small><small>[^20]</small></small> \[be\] their anger, for \[it was\] fierce; and their wrath, for it was cruel: I will divide<small><small>[^21]</small></small> them in Jacob, and scatter them in Israel. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Judah, thou \[art he\] whom thy brethren shall praise<small><small>[^22]</small></small>: thy hand<small><small>[^23]</small></small> \[shall be\] in the neck<small><small>[^24]</small></small> of thine enemies; thy father’s<small><small>[^25]</small></small> children shall bow down before thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Judah \[is\] a lion’s<small><small>[^26]</small></small> whelp: from the prey, my son, thou art gone up: he stooped<small><small>[^27]</small></small> down, he couched as a lion, and as an old lion; who shall rouse him up? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The sceptre<small><small>[^28]</small></small> shall not depart from Judah, nor a lawgiver<small><small>[^29]</small></small> from between<small><small>[^30]</small></small> his feet, until<small><small>[^31]</small></small> Shiloh come; and unto him \[shall\] the gathering<small><small>[^32]</small></small> of the people \[be\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Binding his foal<small><small>[^33]</small></small> unto the vine, and his ass’s colt unto the choice vine; he washed<small><small>[^34]</small></small> his garments in wine, and his clothes in the blood of grapes: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> His eyes \[shall be\] red with wine, and his teeth white with milk.<small><small>[^35]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Zebulun shall dwell at the haven of the sea; and he \[shall be\] for an haven of ships; and his border \[shall be\] unto Zidon.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Issachar \[is\] a strong ass couching down between two burdens:<small><small>[^37]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he saw that rest<small><small>[^38]</small></small> \[was\] good, and the land that \[it was\] pleasant; and bowed<small><small>[^39]</small></small> his shoulder to bear, and became a servant unto tribute. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Dan shall judge his people, as one of the tribes of Israel.<small><small>[^40]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Dan shall be<small><small>[^41]</small></small> a serpent by the way, an adder<small><small>[^42]</small></small> in the path, that biteth the horse heels, so that his rider shall fall backward. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have waited for thy salvation, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^43]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Gad, a troop shall overcome him: but he shall overcome at the last.<small><small>[^44]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Out of Asher his bread \[shall be\] fat, and he shall yield royal dainties.<small><small>[^45]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Naphtali \[is\] a hind let loose: he giveth goodly words.<small><small>[^46]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Joseph \[is\] a fruitful<small><small>[^47]</small></small> bough, \[even\] a fruitful bough by a well; \[whose\] branches<small><small>[^48]</small></small> run over the wall: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The archers have sorely grieved him, and shot \[at him\], and hated him:<small><small>[^49]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But his bow<small><small>[^50]</small></small> abode in strength, and the arms of his hands were made<small><small>[^51]</small></small> strong by the hands of the mighty<small><small>[^52]</small></small> \[God\] of Jacob; (from thence \[is\] the shepherd<small><small>[^53]</small></small>, the stone<small><small>[^54]</small></small> of Israel:) <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Even\] by the God<small><small>[^55]</small></small> of thy father, who shall help thee; and by the Almighty<small><small>[^56]</small></small>, who shall bless thee with blessings<small><small>[^57]</small></small> of heaven above, blessings of the deep that lieth under, blessings of the breasts, and of the womb: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The blessings of thy father have prevailed<small><small>[^58]</small></small> above the blessings of my progenitors unto the utmost bound of the everlasting hills<small><small>[^59]</small></small>: they shall<small><small>[^60]</small></small> be on the head of Joseph, and on the crown of the head of him that was separate<small><small>[^61]</small></small> from his brethren. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Benjamin shall ravin<small><small>[^62]</small></small> \[as\] a wolf<small><small>[^63]</small></small>: in the morning he shall devour the prey, and at night<small><small>[^64]</small></small> he shall divide the spoil. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.49.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All these \[are\] the twelve<small><small>[^65]</small></small> tribes of Israel: and this \[is it\] that their father spake unto them, and blessed them; every one<small><small>[^66]</small></small> according to his blessing he blessed them.
