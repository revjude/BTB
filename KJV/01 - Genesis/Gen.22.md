***The Offering of Isaac***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[John.3|John 3:1–21]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb63

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass after these things, that God<small><small>[^2]</small></small> did tempt<small><small>[^3]</small></small> Abraham, and said unto him, Abraham: and he said, Behold, \[here\] I \[am<small><small>[^4]</small></small>\].<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Take<small><small>[^6]</small></small> now thy son, thine only \[son\] Isaac, whom thou lovest, and get thee into the land of Moriah<small><small>[^7]</small></small>; and offer<small><small>[^8]</small></small> him there for a burnt offering upon one of the mountains which I will tell thee of. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abraham rose up early in the morning, and saddled his ass, and took two of his young men with him, and Isaac his son, and clave the wood for the burnt offering, and rose up, and went unto the place of which God had told him.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then on the third<small><small>[^10]</small></small> day Abraham lifted up his eyes, and saw<small><small>[^11]</small></small> the place afar off. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abraham said unto his young men, Abide<small><small>[^12]</small></small> ye here with the ass; and I and the lad will go yonder and worship, and come<small><small>[^13]</small></small> again to you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abraham took the wood of the burnt offering, and laid \[it<small><small>[^14]</small></small>\] upon Isaac his son; and he took the fire in his hand, and a knife; and they went both of them together. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Isaac spake unto Abraham his father, and said, My father<small><small>[^15]</small></small>: and he said, Here \[am\] I<small><small>[^16]</small></small>, my son. And he said, Behold the fire and the wood: but<small><small>[^17]</small></small> where \[is\] the lamb<small><small>[^18]</small></small> for a burnt offering? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abraham said, My son, God will provide himself a lamb for a burnt offering: so they went both of them together.<small><small>[^19]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they came to the place<small><small>[^20]</small></small> which God had told him of; and Abraham built<small><small>[^21]</small></small> an altar there, and laid the wood in order, and bound<small><small>[^22]</small></small> Isaac his son, and laid him on the altar upon the wood. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abraham stretched forth his hand, and took the knife to slay his son.<small><small>[^23]</small></small>

***The LORD Provides the Sacrifice*** ^bsb64

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the angel<small><small>[^24]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> called unto him out of heaven, and said, Abraham<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, Abraham: and he said, Here \[am\] I. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Lay<small><small>[^26]</small></small> not thine hand upon the lad, neither do thou any thing unto him: for now<small><small>[^27]</small></small> I know that thou fearest God, seeing<small><small>[^28]</small></small> thou hast not withheld thy son, thine only \[son\] from me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abraham lifted up his eyes, and looked, and behold behind<small><small>[^29]</small></small> \[him\] a ram caught in a thicket by his horns: and Abraham went and took the ram, and offered him up for a burnt offering in the<small><small>[^30]</small></small> stead of his son. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abraham called<small><small>[^31]</small></small> the name of that place Jehovah–jireh: as it is said \[to\] this day, In<small><small>[^32]</small></small> the mount of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> it shall be seen<small><small>[^33]</small></small>.<small><small>[^34]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the angel of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> called unto Abraham out of heaven the second time,<small><small>[^35]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And said, By myself have I sworn, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, for because thou hast done this thing, and hast not withheld thy son, thine only \[son\]:<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That in blessing<small><small>[^37]</small></small> I will bless thee, and in multiplying I will multiply<small><small>[^38]</small></small> thy seed as the stars of the heaven, and as the sand which \[is\] upon the sea shore<small><small>[^39]</small></small>; and thy seed<small><small>[^40]</small></small> shall possess the gate of his enemies; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And in<small><small>[^41]</small></small> thy seed shall all the nations of the earth be blessed; because thou hast obeyed<small><small>[^42]</small></small> my voice. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.22.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Abraham<small><small>[^43]</small></small> returned unto his young men, and they rose up and went together to Beer–sheba; and Abraham dwelt at Beer–sheba.<small><small>[^44]</small></small>
