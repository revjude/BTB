and they<small><small>[^23]</small></small> sewed fig leaves together, and made themselves aprons<small><small>[^24]</small></small>.

<big><big>[[Gen.3.8 God Arraigns Adam and Eve|⥃]]</big></big>***God Arraigns Adam and Eve***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.3.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they<small><small>[^25]</small></small> heard the voice of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God walking in the garden in the cool of the day<small><small>[^26]</small></small>: and Adam and his wife hid<small><small>[^27]</small></small> themselves from the presence of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God amongst the trees of the garden. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.3.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God called unto Adam, and said unto him, Where \[art\] thou?<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.3.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, I heard thy voice in the garden, and I was<small><small>[^29]</small></small> afraid, because<small><small>[^30]</small></small> I \[was\] naked; and I hid myself. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.3.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Who told thee that thou \[wast\] naked?
