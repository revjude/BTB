<big><big>[[Gen.21.1 The Birth of Isaac|⥃]]</big></big>***The Birth of Isaac***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.21.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> visited<small><small>[^2]</small></small> Sarah as<small><small>[^3]</small></small> he had said, and the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> did unto Sarah as he had spoken. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.21.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For Sarah conceived<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, and bare Abraham a son in his old age, at the set<small><small>[^5]</small></small> time of which God had spoken to him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.21.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abraham called the name of his son that was born unto him, whom Sarah bare to him, Isaac.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.21.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abraham circumcised his son Isaac being eight days old, as God had commanded him.<small><small>[^7]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.21.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Abraham was an hundred years old, when his son Isaac was born unto him.<small><small>[^8]</small></small>

And she sat over against \[him\], and lift<small><small>[^36]</small></small> up her voice, and wept. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.21.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God heard<small><small>[^37]</small></small> the voice of the lad; and the angel<small><small>[^38]</small></small> of God called to Hagar out of heaven, and said unto her, What<small><small>[^39]</small></small> aileth thee, Hagar? fear<small><small>[^40]</small></small> not; for God hath heard the voice of the lad where he \[is\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.21.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Arise, lift up the lad, and hold him in thine hand; for I will<small><small>[^41]</small></small> make him a great nation. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.21.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God opened her eyes, and she saw a well of water; and she went, and filled the bottle with water, and gave the lad drink.<small><small>[^42]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.21.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God<small><small>[^43]</small></small> was with the lad; and he grew, and dwelt in the wilderness, and became an archer<small><small>[^44]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Gen.21.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he dwelt in the<small><small>[^45]</small></small> wilderness of Paran: and his mother took him a wife<small><small>[^46]</small></small> out of the land of Egypt.
