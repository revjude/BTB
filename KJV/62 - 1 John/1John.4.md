***Testing the Spirits*** ^bsb3008

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Beloved, believe not<small><small>[^2]</small></small> every spirit, but try<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the spirits whether they are of God: because many<small><small>[^4]</small></small> false prophets are gone out into the world. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hereby know ye the Spirit of God: Every<small><small>[^5]</small></small> spirit that confesseth that Jesus Christ is come<small><small>[^6]</small></small> in the flesh is of God: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And every spirit that confesseth not that Jesus Christ is come in the flesh is not of God: and this<small><small>[^7]</small></small> is that \[spirit\] of antichrist, whereof ye have heard that it should come; and even now already is it in the world. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye are<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of God, little children, and have<small><small>[^9]</small></small> overcome them: because greater<small><small>[^10]</small></small> is he that is in you, than<small><small>[^11]</small></small> he that is in the world. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They are<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of the world: therefore speak they of the world, and<small><small>[^13]</small></small> the world heareth them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> We are<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of God: he that knoweth<small><small>[^15]</small></small> God heareth us; he that is not of God heareth not us. Hereby<small><small>[^16]</small></small> know we the spirit of truth<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, and<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the spirit of error.

***Love Comes from God*** ^bsb3009

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Beloved, let<small><small>[^19]</small></small> us love one another: for love is<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of God; and every<small><small>[^21]</small></small> one that loveth is born of God, and knoweth<small><small>[^22]</small></small> God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He that loveth not knoweth<small><small>[^23]</small></small> not God; for God is<small><small>[^24]</small></small> love. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In this was<small><small>[^25]</small></small> manifested the love of God toward us, because that God sent<small><small>[^26]</small></small> his only<small><small>[^27]</small></small> begotten Son into the world, that we<small><small>[^28]</small></small> might live through him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Herein<small><small>[^29]</small></small> is love, not<small><small>[^30]</small></small> that we loved God, but that he loved us, and sent<small><small>[^31]</small></small> his Son \[to be\] the propitiation for our sins. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Beloved, if God so loved us, we ought also to love one another.<small><small>[^32]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> No man hath seen<small><small>[^33]</small></small> God at any time. If we love one<small><small>[^34]</small></small> another, God dwelleth in us, and his<small><small>[^35]</small></small> love is perfected in us. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hereby know we that we dwell in him, and he in us, because he hath given us of his Spirit.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And we have<small><small>[^37]</small></small> seen and do testify that the Father<small><small>[^38]</small></small> sent the Son \[to be\] the Saviour<small><small>[^39]</small></small> of the world. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whosoever shall confess<small><small>[^40]</small></small> that Jesus is the Son of God, God dwelleth<small><small>[^41]</small></small> in him, and he in God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And we<small><small>[^42]</small></small> have known and believed the love that God hath to us. God is love<small><small>[^43]</small></small>; and he<small><small>[^44]</small></small> that dwelleth in love dwelleth in God, and God in him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Herein is our love<small><small>[^45]</small></small> made<small><small>[^46]</small></small> perfect, that we may<small><small>[^47]</small></small> have boldness in the day<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of judgment: because as<small><small>[^49]</small></small> he is, so are we in this world. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> There is no<small><small>[^50]</small></small> fear in love; but perfect love casteth out fear: because fear hath<small><small>[^51]</small></small> torment. He that<small><small>[^52]</small></small> feareth is not made perfect in love. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> We love him, because he first loved us.<small><small>[^53]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If a man<small><small>[^54]</small></small> say, I love God, and hateth his brother, he is a liar: for he that loveth not<small><small>[^55]</small></small> his brother whom he hath seen, how can he love God whom he hath not seen? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.4.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And this commandment have we from him, That he who loveth God love his brother also. <small><small>[^56]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[1John.3|<< 1 John 3]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[1John.5|1 John 5 >>]]</td></tr></table>
