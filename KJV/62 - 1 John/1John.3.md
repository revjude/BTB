***Children of God*** ^bsb3006

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, what<small><small>[^2]</small></small> manner of love the Father hath bestowed upon us, that<small><small>[^3]</small></small> we should be called the sons of God: therefore the world<small><small>[^4]</small></small> knoweth us not, because it knew him not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1John.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Beloved, now are we the<small><small>[^5]</small></small> sons of God, and it<small><small>[^6]</small></small> doth not yet appear what<small><small>[^7]</small></small> we shall be: but we know that, when<small><small>[^8]</small></small> he shall appear, we shall be like him;
