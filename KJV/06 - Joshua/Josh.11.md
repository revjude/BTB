***Conquest of the Northern Cities*** ^bsb564

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when Jabin<small><small>[^2]</small></small> king of Hazor had heard \[those things\], that he sent<small><small>[^3]</small></small> to Jobab king of Madon<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, and to the king of Shimron<small><small>[^5]</small></small>, and to the king of Achshaph<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And to the kings that \[were\] on the north<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of the mountains, and of the plains south of Chinneroth<small><small>[^8]</small></small>, and in the valley, and in the borders of Dor<small><small>[^9]</small></small> on the west,<small><small>[^10]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[And to\] the Canaanite on the east and on the west, and \[to\] the Amorite, and the Hittite, and the Perizzite, and the Jebusite<small><small>[^11]</small></small> in the mountains, and \[to\] the Hivite<small><small>[^12]</small></small> under Hermon<small><small>[^13]</small></small> in the land<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of Mizpeh. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they went out, they and all their hosts with them, much people, even as the sand<small><small>[^15]</small></small> that \[is\] upon the sea shore in multitude, with horses and chariots very many. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when all these<small><small>[^16]</small></small> kings were met together<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, they came and pitched together at the waters<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of Merom, to fight against Israel. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Joshua, Be not<small><small>[^19]</small></small> afraid because of them: for to morrow<small><small>[^20]</small></small> about this time will I deliver them up all slain before Israel: thou shalt hough their horses<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, and burn their chariots with fire. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Joshua came, and all the people of war with him, against them by the waters of Merom suddenly<small><small>[^22]</small></small>; and they fell upon them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> delivered them into the hand of Israel, who smote them, and chased them unto great Zidon<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, and unto Misrephoth–maim, and unto the valley of Mizpeh eastward; and they smote them, until they left them none remaining.<small><small>[^24]</small></small><small><small>[^25]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joshua did unto them as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> bade him: he houghed their horses, and burnt their chariots with fire.<small><small>[^26]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joshua at that time turned back, and took Hazor<small><small>[^27]</small></small>, and smote the king thereof with the sword: for Hazor beforetime was the head of all those kingdoms. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they smote all the souls that \[were\] therein with the edge of the sword, utterly destroying \[them\]: there was not any left to breathe<small><small>[^28]</small></small>: and he burnt Hazor with fire. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all the<small><small>[^29]</small></small> cities of those kings, and all the kings of them, did Joshua take, and smote them with the edge of the sword, \[and\] he utterly destroyed them, as Moses<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the servant of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> commanded. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But \[as for\] the cities that stood still in their<small><small>[^31]</small></small> strength, Israel burned none of them, save Hazor only; \[that\] did Joshua burn. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all the spoil<small><small>[^32]</small></small> of these cities, and the cattle, the children of Israel took for a prey unto themselves; but every man they smote with the edge of the sword, until they had destroyed them, neither<small><small>[^33]</small></small> left they any to breathe. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> commanded Moses his servant, so did Moses<small><small>[^34]</small></small> command Joshua, and so did Joshua<small><small>[^35]</small></small>; he left nothing<small><small>[^36]</small></small> undone of all that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> commanded Moses.<small><small>[^37]</small></small>

***Joshua Takes the Whole Land*** ^bsb565

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Joshua took all that land<small><small>[^38]</small></small>, the hills<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, and all the south country, and all the land<small><small>[^40]</small></small> of Goshen, and the valley, and the plain, and the mountain<small><small>[^41]</small></small> of Israel, and the valley of the same; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Even\] from the mount Halak<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, that goeth<small><small>[^43]</small></small> up to Seir<small><small>[^44]</small></small>, even unto Baal–gad in the valley of Lebanon under mount Hermon: and all their<small><small>[^45]</small></small> kings he took, and smote them, and slew them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Joshua made war a long time<small><small>[^46]</small></small> with all those kings. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> There was not a city that made peace with the children of Israel, save the Hivites<small><small>[^47]</small></small> the inhabitants of Gibeon: all \[other\] they took in battle. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For it was<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> to harden their hearts, that they should come against Israel in battle, that he might destroy them utterly, \[and\] that they might have no favour, but that he might destroy them, as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> commanded Moses.<small><small>[^49]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And at that time came Joshua, and cut off the Anakims<small><small>[^50]</small></small> from the mountains, from Hebron, from Debir, from Anab, and from all the mountains of Judah, and from all the mountains of Israel: Joshua destroyed<small><small>[^51]</small></small> them utterly with their cities. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> There was none of the Anakims left in the land of the children of Israel: only in Gaza<small><small>[^52]</small></small>, in Gath, and in Ashdod<small><small>[^53]</small></small>, there remained. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.11.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Joshua took the whole land, according to all<small><small>[^54]</small></small> that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Moses; and Joshua gave it for an inheritance unto Israel according to their<small><small>[^55]</small></small> divisions by their tribes.
