***Joshua’s Charge to Leaders*** ^bsb593

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass a long time after that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> had given rest unto Israel from all their enemies round about, that Joshua waxed old<small><small>[^2]</small></small> \[and\] stricken in age<small><small>[^3]</small></small>.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joshua called for all Israel<small><small>[^5]</small></small>, \[and\] for their elders<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, and for their heads, and for their judges, and for their officers, and said unto them, I am old \[and\] stricken in age: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And ye<small><small>[^7]</small></small> have seen all that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God hath done unto all these nations because of you; for the<small><small>[^8]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God \[is\] he that hath fought for you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, I have divided unto you by lot these nations that remain, to be an inheritance for your tribes, from Jordan, with all the nations that I have cut off, even unto the great sea westward<small><small>[^10]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God, he shall<small><small>[^11]</small></small> expel them from before you, and drive them from out of your sight; and ye shall possess their land, as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God hath promised unto you.<small><small>[^12]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be ye therefore very<small><small>[^13]</small></small> courageous to keep and to do all that is written in the book of the law of Moses, that ye<small><small>[^14]</small></small> turn not aside therefrom \[to\] the right hand or \[to\] the left; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That ye come<small><small>[^15]</small></small> not among these nations, these that remain among you; neither<small><small>[^16]</small></small> make mention of the name of their gods, nor cause to swear<small><small>[^17]</small></small> \[by them\], neither serve them, nor bow yourselves unto them: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But cleave unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God, as ye have done unto this day.<small><small>[^18]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath driven out from before you great nations and strong: but \[as for\] you, no man<small><small>[^19]</small></small> hath been able to stand before you unto this day.<small><small>[^20]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> One man<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of you shall chase a thousand: for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^22]</small></small></span> your God, he \[it is\] that fighteth for you, as he hath promised you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Take good<small><small>[^23]</small></small> heed therefore unto yourselves<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, that ye love<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Else if ye do in any wise go back<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, and cleave<small><small>[^27]</small></small> unto the remnant of these nations, \[even\] these that remain among you, and shall make<small><small>[^28]</small></small> marriages with them, and go in unto them, and they to you: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Know for a certainty that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God will no<small><small>[^29]</small></small> more drive out \[any of\] these nations from before you; but they shall be snares<small><small>[^30]</small></small> and traps unto you, and scourges in your sides, and thorns in your eyes, until ye perish<small><small>[^31]</small></small> from off this good land which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God hath given you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And, behold, this day I \[am\] going<small><small>[^32]</small></small> the way of all the earth: and ye know in all your hearts and in all your souls, that not one thing<small><small>[^33]</small></small> hath failed of all the good things which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God spake concerning you; all are come to pass unto you, \[and\] not one thing hath failed thereof. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore it shall come to pass, \[that\] as all good things are come upon you, which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God promised you; so shall<small><small>[^34]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> bring upon you all evil things, until he have destroyed you from off this good land which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God hath given you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.23.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When ye have transgressed the covenant of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God, which he commanded you, and have gone and served other gods, and bowed yourselves to them; then shall<small><small>[^35]</small></small> the anger of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> be kindled against you, and ye shall perish<small><small>[^36]</small></small> quickly from off the good land which he hath given unto you. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;"
