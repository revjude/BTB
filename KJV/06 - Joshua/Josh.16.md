***Ephraim’s Inheritance*** ^bsb578

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.16.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the lot of the children of Joseph fell<small><small>[^2]</small></small> from Jordan by Jericho, unto the water<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of Jericho on the east, to the wilderness that goeth up from Jericho throughout mount Beth–el, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.16.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And goeth out from Beth–el<small><small>[^4]</small></small> to Luz, and passeth along unto the borders of Archi<small><small>[^5]</small></small> to Ataroth, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.16.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And goeth down westward to the coast of Japhleti, unto the coast of Beth–horon<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the nether, and to Gezer<small><small>[^7]</small></small>: and the goings out thereof are at the sea<small><small>[^8]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.16.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So the children of Joseph, Manasseh and Ephraim, took their inheritance.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.16.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the border of the children of Ephraim according to their families was \[thus\]: even the border of their inheritance on the east side was Ataroth–addar<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, unto Beth–horon the upper; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.16.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the border went out toward the sea to Michmethah<small><small>[^11]</small></small> on the north side; and the border went about eastward unto Taanath–shiloh<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, and passed by it on the east to Janohah<small><small>[^13]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.16.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it went down from Janohah to Ataroth<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, and to Naarath, and came to Jericho<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, and went out at Jordan. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Josh.16.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The border went out from Tappuah<small><small>[^16]</small></small> westward unto the river Kanah<small><small>[^17]</small></small>; and the goings out thereof were at the sea<small><small>[^18]</small></small>.
