***Christ’s Eternal Priesthood*** ^bsb2942

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.8.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now of the things which we have spoken \[this is\] the sum<small><small>[^2]</small></small>: We have<small><small>[^3]</small></small> such an high priest, who<small><small>[^4]</small></small> is set on the right hand of the throne of the Majesty<small><small>[^5]</small></small> in the heavens; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.8.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> A minister<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of the sanctuary<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, and of the true<small><small>[^8]</small></small> tabernacle, which<small><small>[^9]</small></small> the Lord pitched, and not man. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.8.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For every high priest is ordained to offer gifts and sacrifices: wherefore \[it is\] of necessity that this man have<small><small>[^10]</small></small> somewhat also to offer.<small><small>[^11]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.8.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For if he were on earth, he should<small><small>[^12]</small></small> not be a priest, seeing that there are priests<small><small>[^13]</small></small> that offer gifts<small><small>[^14]</small></small> according to the law: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.8.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who serve unto<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the example<small><small>[^16]</small></small> and shadow of heavenly things, as Moses was admonished of God when he was about to make the tabernacle: for, See<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, saith he, \[that\] thou make all things according to the pattern<small><small>[^18]</small></small> shewed to thee in the mount.
