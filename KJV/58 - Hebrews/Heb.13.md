<big><big>[[Heb.13.1 Brotherly Love|⥃]]</big></big>***Brotherly Love***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let brotherly love continue.<small><small>[^2]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be not<small><small>[^3]</small></small> forgetful to entertain strangers: for thereby some<small><small>[^4]</small></small> have entertained angels unawares. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Remember them that<small><small>[^5]</small></small> are in bonds, as bound with them; \[and\] them which suffer<small><small>[^6]</small></small> adversity, as being yourselves also in the body. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Marriage<small><small>[^7]</small></small> \[is\] honourable in all, and the bed<small><small>[^8]</small></small> undefiled: but whoremongers and adulterers God<small><small>[^9]</small></small> will judge.

<big><big>[[Heb.13.5 Christ’s Unchanging Nature|⥃]]</big></big>***Christ’s Unchanging Nature***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Let your\] conversation<small><small>[^10]</small></small> \[be\] without covetousness; \[and be<small><small>[^11]</small></small>\] content with such things as ye have: for he hath said, I will<small><small>[^12]</small></small> never leave thee, nor forsake thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So that we may boldly<small><small>[^13]</small></small> say, The Lord<small><small>[^14]</small></small> \[is\] my helper, and I will<small><small>[^15]</small></small> not fear what man shall do unto me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Remember them which<small><small>[^16]</small></small> have the rule<small><small>[^17]</small></small> over you, who have spoken unto you the word<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of God: whose<small><small>[^19]</small></small> faith follow, considering<small><small>[^20]</small></small> the end<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of \[their\] conversation. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Jesus Christ the same yesterday, and to day, and for ever.<small><small>[^22]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be not carried<small><small>[^23]</small></small> about with divers and strange doctrines. For \[it is<small><small>[^24]</small></small>\] a good thing that the heart be established with grace; not with<small><small>[^25]</small></small> meats, which have not profited them that have been occupied therein. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> We have an altar<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, whereof they have no right to eat which serve<small><small>[^27]</small></small> the tabernacle. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the bodies<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of those beasts, whose blood is brought into the sanctuary by the high priest for sin, are burned without the camp. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore Jesus also, that he might sanctify<small><small>[^29]</small></small> the people with his own blood, suffered<small><small>[^30]</small></small> without the gate. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let us go forth therefore unto him without the camp, bearing his reproach.<small><small>[^31]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For here have we no continuing city, but we seek one to come.<small><small>[^32]</small></small>

<big><big>[[Heb.13.15 Sacrifice, Obedience, and Prayer|⥃]]</big></big>***Sacrifice, Obedience, and Prayer***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By him<small><small>[^33]</small></small> therefore let us offer the sacrifice<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of praise to God continually, that is, the fruit<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of \[our\] lips giving thanks to<small><small>[^36]</small></small> his name. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But to do<small><small>[^37]</small></small> good and to communicate<small><small>[^38]</small></small> forget not: for with such<small><small>[^39]</small></small> sacrifices God is well pleased. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Obey<small><small>[^40]</small></small> them that have the rule over<small><small>[^41]</small></small> you, and submit<small><small>[^42]</small></small> yourselves: for they watch<small><small>[^43]</small></small> for your souls, as they that must give account<small><small>[^44]</small></small>, that they may do it with joy<small><small>[^45]</small></small>, and not with grief<small><small>[^46]</small></small>: for that \[is\] unprofitable for you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Pray<small><small>[^47]</small></small> for us: for we trust we have<small><small>[^48]</small></small> a good conscience, in all<small><small>[^49]</small></small> things willing to live honestly. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I beseech \[you\] the rather to do this, that I<small><small>[^50]</small></small> may be restored to you the sooner.

<big><big>[[Heb.13.20 Benediction and Farewell|⥃]]</big></big>***Benediction and Farewell***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now the God<small><small>[^51]</small></small> of peace, that brought<small><small>[^52]</small></small> again from the dead our Lord Jesus, that great<small><small>[^53]</small></small> shepherd of the sheep, through the blood<small><small>[^54]</small></small> of the everlasting<small><small>[^55]</small></small> covenant<small><small>[^56]</small></small>, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Make<small><small>[^57]</small></small> you perfect in every<small><small>[^58]</small></small> good work to do<small><small>[^59]</small></small> his will, working<small><small>[^60]</small></small> in you that which is wellpleasing in his sight, through<small><small>[^61]</small></small> Jesus Christ; to whom<small><small>[^62]</small></small> \[be\] glory for ever and ever. Amen<small><small>[^63]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I beseech you, brethren, suffer<small><small>[^64]</small></small> the word of exhortation: for<small><small>[^65]</small></small> I have written a letter unto you in few words. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Know ye that \[our\] brother<small><small>[^66]</small></small> Timothy is set<small><small>[^67]</small></small> at liberty; with whom, if he come shortly, I will<small><small>[^68]</small></small> see you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Salute<small><small>[^69]</small></small> all them that have the rule<small><small>[^70]</small></small> over you, and all<small><small>[^71]</small></small> the saints. They<small><small>[^72]</small></small> of Italy<small><small>[^73]</small></small> salute you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.13.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Grace \[be\] with you all.
