 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore<small><small>[^2]</small></small> we ought to give the more<small><small>[^3]</small></small> earnest heed to the things which we have heard, lest at any time we should<small><small>[^4]</small></small> let \[them\] slip<small><small>[^5]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For if the word spoken<small><small>[^6]</small></small> by angels was stedfast, and every<small><small>[^7]</small></small> transgression and disobedience received a just recompence of reward;<small><small>[^8]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> How<small><small>[^9]</small></small> shall we escape, if we neglect so<small><small>[^10]</small></small> great salvation; which at the first began<small><small>[^11]</small></small> to be spoken by the Lord, and was<small><small>[^12]</small></small> confirmed unto us by them that heard \[him\];  <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Heb.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> God<small><small>[^13]</small></small> also bearing \[them\] witness, both with signs and wonders, and with divers miracles, and gifts<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of the Holy Ghost, according<small><small>[^15]</small></small> to his own will?
