<big><big>[[1Tim.2.1 A Call to Prayer|⥃]]</big></big>***A Call to Prayer***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I exhort<small><small>[^2]</small></small> therefore, that, first<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of all, supplications<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, prayers, intercessions, \[and<small><small>[^5]</small></small>\] giving of thanks, be made for all men<small><small>[^6]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For kings<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, and \[for\] all<small><small>[^8]</small></small> that are in authority<small><small>[^9]</small></small>; that<small><small>[^10]</small></small> we may lead a quiet and peaceable life in all godliness<small><small>[^11]</small></small> and honesty. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For this<small><small>[^12]</small></small> \[is\] good and acceptable in the sight of God<small><small>[^13]</small></small> our Saviour; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who will<small><small>[^14]</small></small> have all men to be saved, and<small><small>[^15]</small></small> to come unto the knowledge<small><small>[^16]</small></small> of the truth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.2.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For \[there is\] one God<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, and<small><small>[^18]</small></small> one mediator between God and men, the man<small><small>[^19]</small></small> Christ Jesus; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.2.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who gave<small><small>[^20]</small></small> himself a ransom for all, to be testified<small><small>[^21]</small></small> in<small><small>[^22]</small></small> due time. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.2.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whereunto I am<small><small>[^23]</small></small> ordained a preacher<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, and an apostle, (I speak<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the truth in Christ, \[and\] lie not;) a teacher<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of the Gentiles in faith<small><small>[^27]</small></small> and verity<small><small>[^28]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.2.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will<small><small>[^29]</small></small> therefore that men pray<small><small>[^30]</small></small> every where, lifting<small><small>[^31]</small></small> up holy hands, without<small><small>[^32]</small></small> wrath and<small><small>[^33]</small></small> doubting.

<big><big>[[1Tim.2.9 Instructions to Women|⥃]]</big></big>***Instructions to Women***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.2.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In like manner also, that<small><small>[^34]</small></small> women adorn themselves in modest apparel, with shamefacedness<small><small>[^35]</small></small> and sobriety;
