***Serving with Honor***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Eph.6|Ephesians 6:5–9]] <big>†</big> [[Col.3|Colossians 3:22–25]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2899

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let as many servants<small><small>[^2]</small></small> as are under the yoke count<small><small>[^3]</small></small> their own masters worthy of all honour, that the<small><small>[^4]</small></small> name of God and \[his\] doctrine be not blasphemed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they that have believing<small><small>[^5]</small></small> masters, let<small><small>[^6]</small></small> them not despise \[them\], because they are brethren<small><small>[^7]</small></small>; but rather do \[them\] service, because they are<small><small>[^8]</small></small> faithful<small><small>[^9]</small></small> and beloved, partakers<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of the benefit. These<small><small>[^11]</small></small> things teach and exhort.

***Reject False Doctrines*** ^bsb2900

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If any<small><small>[^12]</small></small> man teach otherwise, and consent not to wholesome<small><small>[^13]</small></small> words, \[even\] the words<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of our Lord Jesus Christ, and to the doctrine<small><small>[^15]</small></small> which is according to godliness; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He<small><small>[^16]</small></small> is proud<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, knowing nothing, but doting<small><small>[^18]</small></small> about<small><small>[^19]</small></small> questions and strifes of words<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, whereof cometh envy, strife, railings, evil surmisings, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Perverse<small><small>[^21]</small></small> disputings of men<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of corrupt minds, and destitute of the truth, supposing<small><small>[^23]</small></small> that gain is godliness: from<small><small>[^24]</small></small> such withdraw thyself.

***Godliness with Contentment*** ^bsb2901

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But godliness<small><small>[^25]</small></small> with contentment<small><small>[^26]</small></small> is great gain. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For we brought<small><small>[^27]</small></small> nothing into \[this\] world, \[and it is\] certain<small><small>[^28]</small></small> we can carry nothing out. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And having food and raiment let us be therewith content.<small><small>[^29]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But they<small><small>[^30]</small></small> that will be rich fall into temptation and a snare<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, and \[into\] many<small><small>[^32]</small></small> foolish and hurtful lusts, which<small><small>[^33]</small></small> drown men in destruction and perdition. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the love<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of money is the root of all evil: which while some coveted<small><small>[^35]</small></small> after, they have erred<small><small>[^36]</small></small> from the faith, and pierced<small><small>[^37]</small></small> themselves through with many sorrows.

***Fight the Good Fight*** ^bsb2902

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But<small><small>[^38]</small></small> thou, O man<small><small>[^39]</small></small> of God, flee<small><small>[^40]</small></small> these things; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That thou keep<small><small>[^51]</small></small> \[this\] commandment without<small><small>[^52]</small></small> spot, unrebukeable<small><small>[^53]</small></small>, until<small><small>[^54]</small></small> the appearing of our Lord Jesus Christ: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which in his times he shall shew, \[who<small><small>[^55]</small></small> is\] the blessed and only Potentate, the King<small><small>[^56]</small></small> of kings, and Lord of lords; Amen.

***A Charge to the Rich***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Prov.23|Proverbs 23:1–5]] <big>†</big> [[Jas.5|James 5:1–6]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2903

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Charge<small><small>[^61]</small></small> them that are rich<small><small>[^62]</small></small> in this world, that they<small><small>[^63]</small></small> be not highminded, nor trust<small><small>[^64]</small></small> in uncertain riches<small><small>[^65]</small></small>, but<small><small>[^66]</small></small> in the living<small><small>[^67]</small></small> God, who<small><small>[^68]</small></small> giveth us richly<small><small>[^69]</small></small> all things to enjoy; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That they do<small><small>[^70]</small></small> good, that they be rich<small><small>[^71]</small></small> in good works, ready<small><small>[^72]</small></small> to distribute, willing to communicate<small><small>[^73]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.6.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Laying<small><small>[^74]</small></small> up in store for themselves a good foundation<small><small>[^75]</small></small> against the time<small><small>[^76]</small></small> to come, that they may lay<small><small>[^77]</small></small> hold on eternal life.
