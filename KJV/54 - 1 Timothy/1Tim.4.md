<big><big>[[1Tim.4.1 A Warning against Apostasy|⥃]]</big></big>***A Warning against Apostasy***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now the Spirit<small><small>[^2]</small></small> speaketh expressly<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, that in the latter<small><small>[^4]</small></small> times some shall depart<small><small>[^5]</small></small> from the faith, giving heed to seducing<small><small>[^6]</small></small> spirits, and doctrines<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of devils; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Speaking lies<small><small>[^8]</small></small> in hypocrisy; having their<small><small>[^9]</small></small> conscience seared with a hot iron; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Forbidding<small><small>[^10]</small></small> to marry, \[and commanding\] to abstain<small><small>[^11]</small></small> from meats, which<small><small>[^12]</small></small> God hath created to be received with<small><small>[^13]</small></small> thanksgiving of them which believe<small><small>[^14]</small></small> and know the truth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For every<small><small>[^15]</small></small> creature of God \[is\] good, and<small><small>[^16]</small></small> nothing to be refused, if it be received with thanksgiving: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For it<small><small>[^17]</small></small> is sanctified by the<small><small>[^18]</small></small> word of God and prayer.

<big><big>[[1Tim.4.6 A Good Servant of Jesus Christ|⥃]]</big></big>***A Good Servant of Jesus Christ***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If thou put<small><small>[^19]</small></small> the brethren in remembrance of these things, thou shalt be a good<small><small>[^20]</small></small> minister of Jesus Christ, nourished<small><small>[^21]</small></small> up in the words of faith and of good doctrine<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, whereunto thou hast<small><small>[^23]</small></small> attained. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But refuse<small><small>[^24]</small></small> profane and old wives’ fables, and exercise<small><small>[^25]</small></small> thyself \[rather\] unto godliness. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For bodily<small><small>[^26]</small></small> exercise profiteth little<small><small>[^27]</small></small>: but godliness<small><small>[^28]</small></small> is profitable unto all things, having<small><small>[^29]</small></small> promise of the life that now is, and of that which is to come. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This \[is\] a faithful saying and worthy of all acceptation.<small><small>[^30]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For therefore<small><small>[^31]</small></small> we both labour and suffer reproach, because<small><small>[^32]</small></small> we trust in the living<small><small>[^33]</small></small> God, who is the Saviour<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of all men, specially<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of those that believe. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> These things command and teach.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let no<small><small>[^37]</small></small> man despise thy youth; but be thou<small><small>[^38]</small></small> an example of the believers, in word<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, in conversation, in charity, in spirit, in faith, in purity. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Till I come<small><small>[^40]</small></small>, give attendance to reading<small><small>[^41]</small></small>, to exhortation<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, to doctrine<small><small>[^43]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neglect<small><small>[^44]</small></small> not the gift that is in thee, which<small><small>[^45]</small></small> was given thee by prophecy, with<small><small>[^46]</small></small> the laying on of the hands of the presbytery. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Meditate<small><small>[^47]</small></small> upon these things; give<small><small>[^48]</small></small> thyself wholly to them; that<small><small>[^49]</small></small> thy profiting may appear to all<small><small>[^50]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Tim.4.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Take<small><small>[^51]</small></small> heed unto thyself, and unto the<small><small>[^52]</small></small> doctrine; continue<small><small>[^53]</small></small> in them: for in doing this thou shalt<small><small>[^54]</small></small> both save thyself, and them<small><small>[^55]</small></small> that hear thee. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td
