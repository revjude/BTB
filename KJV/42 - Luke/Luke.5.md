 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.5.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass on a certain day, as he was teaching, that there<small><small>[^35]</small></small> were Pharisees and doctors of the law sitting by, which were come out of every town of Galilee, and Judea, and Jerusalem<small><small>[^36]</small></small>: and the power<small><small>[^37]</small></small> of the Lord was \[present\] to heal them.
