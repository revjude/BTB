<big><big>[[Luke.17.1 Temptations and Trespasses|⥃]]</big></big>***Temptations and Trespasses***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.18|Matthew 18:6–9]] <big>†</big> [[Mark.9|Mark 9:42–48]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said he unto the disciples, <span style="color:red;">It is<small><small>[^2]</small></small> impossible but that offences will come: but woe \[unto him\], through whom they come!</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">It were better<small><small>[^3]</small></small> for him that a millstone<small><small>[^4]</small></small> were hanged about his neck, and he cast into the sea, than that he should offend one of these little ones.</span> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Take heed<small><small>[^5]</small></small> to yourselves: If<small><small>[^6]</small></small> thy brother trespass against thee, rebuke<small><small>[^7]</small></small> him; and if he repent, forgive him.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">And if<small><small>[^8]</small></small> he trespass against thee seven times in a day, and seven times in a day turn again to thee, saying, I repent<small><small>[^9]</small></small>; thou shalt forgive him.</span>

<big><big>[[Luke.17.5 The Power of Faith|⥃]]</big></big>***The Power of Faith***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.17|Matthew 17:19–20]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the apostles said unto the Lord, Increase<small><small>[^10]</small></small> our faith. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Lord said, <span style="color:red;">If<small><small>[^11]</small></small> ye had faith as<small><small>[^12]</small></small> a grain of mustard seed, ye might say unto this sycamine tree, Be thou plucked up by the root, and be thou planted in the sea; and it should obey you.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">But which of you, having a servant plowing or feeding cattle, will say unto him by and by, when he is come from the field, Go and sit down to meat?</span><small><small>[^13]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">And will not rather say unto him, Make<small><small>[^14]</small></small> ready wherewith I may sup, and gird<small><small>[^15]</small></small> thyself, and serve me, till I have eaten and drunken; and afterward thou shalt eat and drink?</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">Doth he thank that servant because he did the things that were commanded him? I trow not.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">So likewise ye, when ye shall have done all those things which are commanded you, say, We are unprofitable servants: we have done that which was our duty to do.</span><small><small>[^16]</small></small>

<big><big>[[Luke.17.11 The Ten Lepers|⥃]]</big></big>***The Ten Lepers***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Kgs.5|2 Kings 5:1–14]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, as he went to Jerusalem, that he passed through the midst of Samaria and Galilee.<small><small>[^17]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And as he entered into a certain village, there met him ten men that were lepers, which<small><small>[^18]</small></small> stood afar off: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they lifted up \[their\] voices, and said, Jesus, Master, have<small><small>[^19]</small></small> mercy on us. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when he saw \[them\], he said unto them, <span style="color:red;">Go<small><small>[^20]</small></small> shew yourselves unto the priests.</span> And it came to pas<small><small>[^21]</small></small>s, that, as they went, they were cleansed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And one of them, when he saw that he was healed, turned back, and with a loud voice glorified God,<small><small>[^22]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And fell<small><small>[^23]</small></small> down on \[his\] face at his feet, giving him thanks: and he<small><small>[^24]</small></small> was a Samaritan. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Jesus answering said, <span style="color:red;">Were there not ten cleansed? but<small><small>[^25]</small></small> where \[are\] the nine?</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">There are not found that returned to give<small><small>[^26]</small></small> glory to God, save<small><small>[^27]</small></small> this stranger.</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.17.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto him, <span style="color:red;">Arise, go thy way: thy faith<small><small>[^28]</small></small> hath made thee whole.</span>
