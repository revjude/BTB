<big><big>[[Luke.10.13 Woe to the Unrepentant|⥃]]</big></big>***Woe to the Unrepentant***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.11|Matthew 11:20–24]] ]</sup></small>

<big><big>[[Luke.10.17 The Joyful Return|⥃]]</big></big>***The Joyful Return***

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.10.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he turned him unto \[his\] disciples, and said privately, <span style="color:red;">Blessed<small><small>[^54]</small></small> \[are\] the eyes which see the things that ye see:</span> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Luke.10.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span style="color:red;">For I tell you, that many<small><small>[^55]</small></small> prophets and kings have desired to see those things which ye see, and have not seen \[them\]; and to hear those things which ye hear, and have not heard \[them\].</span>
