***The Greatest Commandment***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.22|Matthew 22:34–40]] <big>†</big> [[Mark.12|Mark 12:28–34]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb474

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.6.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now these \[are\] the commandments<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, the statutes, and the judgments, which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God commanded to teach you, that ye might do \[them\] in the land whither ye go to possess it<small><small>[^3]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.6.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That thou mightest fear<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> thy God, to keep all his statutes and his commandments, which I command thee, thou, and thy son<small><small>[^5]</small></small>, and thy son’s son, all the days of thy life; and that thy days<small><small>[^6]</small></small> may be prolonged. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.6.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Hear therefore, O Israel, and observe<small><small>[^7]</small></small> to do \[it\]; that it may be well with thee, and that ye may<small><small>[^8]</small></small> increase mightily, as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of thy fathers hath promised thee, in the these words, which I command thee this day, shall be<small><small>[^14]</small></small> in thine heart: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.6.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span>
