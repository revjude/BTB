***The Death of Moses*** ^bsb545

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.34.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses went up from the plains of Moab unto the mountain<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of Nebo, to the top of Pisgah<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, that \[is\] over against Jericho. And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shewed him all the land of Gilead, unto Dan<small><small>[^4]</small></small>,<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.34.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all Naphtali, and the land of Ephraim, and Manasseh, and all the land of Judah, unto<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the utmost sea, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.34.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the south, and the plain of the valley of Jericho, the city of palm<small><small>[^7]</small></small> trees, unto Zoar<small><small>[^8]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.34.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto him, This \[is\] the land<small><small>[^9]</small></small> which I sware unto Abraham, unto Isaac, and unto Jacob, saying, I will give it unto thy seed: I have caused<small><small>[^10]</small></small> thee to see \[it\] with thine eyes, but thou shalt not go over thither. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.34.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Moses<small><small>[^11]</small></small> the servant of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> died there<small><small>[^12]</small></small> in the land of Moab, according to the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.34.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he buried him<small><small>[^13]</small></small> in a valley in the land of Moab, over against Beth–peor: but no man knoweth of his sepulchre unto this day. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.34.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Moses \[was\] an hundred<small><small>[^14]</small></small> and twenty years old when he died: his eye<small><small>[^15]</small></small> was not dim, nor his natural force abated<small><small>[^16]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.34.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And the children of Israel wept for Moses<small><small>[^17]</small></small> in the plains of Moab thirty days: so the days of weeping \[and\] mourning for Moses were ended. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Deut.34.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joshua the son of Nun was full of the spirit<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of wisdom; for Moses<small><small>[^19]</small></small> had laid his hands upon him: and the children<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of Israel hearkened unto him, and did as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> commanded Moses.
