***Partakers of the Divine Nature*** ^bsb2993

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.1.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> According as his<small><small>[^11]</small></small> divine power hath given unto us Jesus Christ. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.1.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore I will not<small><small>[^47]</small></small> be negligent to put you always in
