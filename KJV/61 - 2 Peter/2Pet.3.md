***The Coming Judgment***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Gen.7|Genesis 7:1–24]] <big>†</big> [[Jude.1|Jude 1:17–23]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2996

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This second epistle<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, beloved, I now write unto you; in \[both\] which I stir<small><small>[^3]</small></small> up your pure<small><small>[^4]</small></small> minds by way<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of remembrance: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That ye may<small><small>[^6]</small></small> be mindful of the words which were spoken before by the holy prophets, and of<small><small>[^7]</small></small> the commandment of us the apostles of the Lord and Saviour: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Knowing this first, that there<small><small>[^8]</small></small> shall come in the last days scoffers<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, walking<small><small>[^10]</small></small> after their own lusts, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And saying, Where<small><small>[^11]</small></small> is the promise of his coming? for since the fathers fell asleep, all things continue as \[they were\] from the beginning<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of the creation. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For this they willingly<small><small>[^13]</small></small> are ignorant of, that by the word<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of God the heavens were of old, and the earth standing<small><small>[^15]</small></small> out of the water and in the water: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whereby the world that then was, being overflowed with water, perished:<small><small>[^16]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the heavens<small><small>[^17]</small></small> and the earth, which are now, by the same word are kept in store, reserved unto fire against<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the day of judgment and perdition<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of ungodly men.

***The Day of the Lord***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Zeph.1|Zephaniah 1:7–18]] <big>†</big> [[Mal.4|Malachi 4:1–6]] <big>†</big> [[1Thess.5|1 Thessalonians 5:1–11]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2997

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But, beloved, be not<small><small>[^20]</small></small> ignorant of this one thing, that one<small><small>[^21]</small></small> day \[is\] with the Lord as a thousand years, and a thousand years as one day. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The Lord is not<small><small>[^22]</small></small> slack concerning his promise, as some men count slackness; but is<small><small>[^23]</small></small> longsuffering to us-ward, not willing<small><small>[^24]</small></small> that any should perish, but that<small><small>[^25]</small></small> all should come to repentance. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the day<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of the Lord will come as a<small><small>[^27]</small></small> thief in the night; in the which<small><small>[^28]</small></small> the heavens shall pass away with a great noise, and the elements<small><small>[^29]</small></small> shall melt<small><small>[^30]</small></small> with fervent heat, the earth<small><small>[^31]</small></small> also and the works that are therein shall be burned up. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Seeing\] then \[that\] all these<small><small>[^32]</small></small> things shall be dissolved, what<small><small>[^33]</small></small> manner \[of persons\] ought ye to be in \[all<small><small>[^34]</small></small>\] holy conversation and godliness<small><small>[^35]</small></small>, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Looking<small><small>[^36]</small></small> for and hasting unto the coming<small><small>[^37]</small></small> of the day of God, wherein the heavens<small><small>[^38]</small></small> being on fire shall be dissolved, and the elements shall melt<small><small>[^39]</small></small> with fervent heat? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Nevertheless we, according<small><small>[^40]</small></small> to his promise, look for new heavens and a new earth, wherein dwelleth righteousness.

***Final Exhortations*** ^bsb2998

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore, beloved, seeing<small><small>[^41]</small></small> that ye look for such things, be diligent<small><small>[^42]</small></small> that ye may be found of him in peace<small><small>[^43]</small></small>, without spot, and blameless. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And account<small><small>[^44]</small></small> \[that\] the longsuffering of our Lord \[is\] salvation; even as our beloved<small><small>[^45]</small></small> brother Paul also according<small><small>[^46]</small></small> to the wisdom given unto him hath written unto you; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As also in all<small><small>[^47]</small></small> \[his\] epistles, speaking<small><small>[^48]</small></small> in them of these things; in which are some things hard<small><small>[^49]</small></small> to be understood, which they that are unlearned and unstable<small><small>[^50]</small></small> wrest<small><small>[^51]</small></small>, as \[they do\] also the other<small><small>[^52]</small></small> scriptures, unto their own<small><small>[^53]</small></small> destruction. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye therefore, beloved, seeing<small><small>[^54]</small></small> ye know \[these things\] before, beware<small><small>[^55]</small></small> lest ye also, being<small><small>[^56]</small></small> led away with the error of the wicked, fall from<small><small>[^57]</small></small> your own stedfastness. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Pet.3.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But grow<small><small>[^58]</small></small> in grace, and \[in\] the knowledge<small><small>[^59]</small></small> of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ. To him<small><small>[^60]</small></small> \[be\] glory both now and for ever.
