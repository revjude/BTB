***Preach the Word*** ^bsb2912

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I charge<small><small>[^2]</small></small> \[thee\] therefore before God, and the Lord Jesus Christ, who<small><small>[^3]</small></small> shall judge the quick and the dead at<small><small>[^4]</small></small> his appearing and his kingdom<small><small>[^5]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Preach<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the word; be<small><small>[^7]</small></small> in<small><small>[^8]</small></small>stant in season, out of season; reprove<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, rebuke, exhort<small><small>[^10]</small></small> with all<small><small>[^11]</small></small> longsuffering and doctrine. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the time<small><small>[^12]</small></small> will come when they will<small><small>[^13]</small></small> not endure sound<small><small>[^14]</small></small> doctrine; but<small><small>[^15]</small></small> after their own lusts shall they heap to themselves teachers, having<small><small>[^16]</small></small> itching ears; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.4.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall turn<small><small>[^17]</small></small> away \[their\] ears from the truth, and shall be turned unto<small><small>[^18]</small></small> fables. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.4.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But watch<small><small>[^19]</small></small> thou in all things, endure<small><small>[^20]</small></small> afflictions, do the work of an<small><small>[^21]</small></small> evangelist, make full proof of<small><small>[^22]</small></small> thy ministry. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.4.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I am<small><small>[^23]</small></small> now ready to be offered, and<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the time of my departure is at hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.4.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have fought a good fight, I have finished<small><small>[^25]</small></small> \[my\] course, I have kept<small><small>[^26]</small></small> the faith:<small><small>[^27]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.4.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Henceforth there<small><small>[^28]</small></small> is laid up for me a crown<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of righteousness, which the Lord, the righteous<small><small>[^30]</small></small> judge, shall give me at that<small><small>[^31]</small></small> day: and not to me only, but unto all them also that love<small><small>[^32]</small></small> his appearing.

***The Lord Remains Faithful*** ^bsb2914

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.4.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> At my first answer<small><small>[^53]</small></small> no<small><small>[^54]</small></small> man stood with me, but all \[men\] forsook me: \[I pray<small><small>[^55]</small></small> God\] that it may not be laid to their charge. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.4.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Notwithstanding the Lord<small><small>[^56]</small></small> stood with me, and strengthened<small><small>[^57]</small></small> me; that by<small><small>[^58]</small></small> me the preaching might be fully known, and \[that\] all the Gentiles might hear: and I<small><small>[^59]</small></small> was delivered out of the mouth of the lion. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.4.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Lord shall deliver<small><small>[^60]</small></small> me from every evil work, and will<small><small>[^61]</small></small> preserve \[me\] unto his heavenly kingdom: to whom<small><small>[^62]</small></small> \[be\] glory for ever and ever. Amen.
