***Paul’s Greeting to Timothy***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[1Tim.1|1 Timothy 1:1–2]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2905

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.1.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Paul, an<small><small>[^2]</small></small> apostle of Jesus Christ by the will of God, according to the promise<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of life which is in Christ Jesus, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.1.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To Timothy<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, \[my\] dearly beloved son: Grace<small><small>[^5]</small></small>, mercy, \[and\] peace, from God the Father and Christ Jesus our Lord.

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Tim.1.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I thank<small><small>[^6]</small></small> God, whom<small><small>[^7]</small></small> I serve from \[my\] forefathers with<small><small>[^8]</small></small> pure conscience, that<small><small>[^9]</small></small> without ceasing I have remembrance of thee in my prayers night<small><small>[^10]</small></small> and day;
