***The Rights of an Apostle***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Deut.18|Deuteronomy 18:1–8]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2759

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Am I not an<small><small>[^2]</small></small> apostle? am I not free<small><small>[^3]</small></small>? have<small><small>[^4]</small></small> I not seen Jesus Christ our Lord? are<small><small>[^5]</small></small> not ye my work in the Lord? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If I be not an apostle unto others, yet doubtless I am to you: for<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the seal of mine apostleship are ye in the Lord. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Mine answer<small><small>[^7]</small></small> to them<small><small>[^8]</small></small> that do examine me is this, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Have we<small><small>[^9]</small></small> not power to eat and to drink? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Have we not power to lead<small><small>[^10]</small></small> about a sister<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, a wife<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, as well as other apostles, and \[as\] the brethren<small><small>[^13]</small></small> of the Lord, and Cephas<small><small>[^14]</small></small>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Or I only and Barnabas<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, have<small><small>[^16]</small></small> not we power to forbear working? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who goeth<small><small>[^17]</small></small> a warfare any time at his own charges? who planteth<small><small>[^18]</small></small> a vineyard, and eateth not of the fruit thereof? or<small><small>[^19]</small></small> who feedeth a flock, and eateth not of the milk<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of the flock? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Say I these things as<small><small>[^21]</small></small> a man? or<small><small>[^22]</small></small> saith not the law the same also? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For it is written in the law of Moses, Thou<small><small>[^23]</small></small> shalt not muzzle the mouth of the ox that treadeth out the corn. Doth<small><small>[^24]</small></small> God take care for oxen? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Or saith he \[it\] altogether for our sakes? For<small><small>[^25]</small></small> our sakes, no doubt, \[this\] is written: that he that ploweth<small><small>[^26]</small></small> should plow in hope; and that he that thresheth in hope should be partaker of his hope. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If we have sown<small><small>[^27]</small></small> unto you spiritual things, \[is it\] a great<small><small>[^28]</small></small> thing if we shall reap your carnal things? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If others<small><small>[^29]</small></small> be partakers of \[this\] power over you, \[are\] not<small><small>[^30]</small></small> we rather? Nevertheless<small><small>[^31]</small></small> we have not used this power; but<small><small>[^32]</small></small> suffer all things, lest we should hinder<small><small>[^33]</small></small> the gospel of Christ. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Do ye not know that they<small><small>[^34]</small></small> which minister about holy things live<small><small>[^35]</small></small> \[of the things\] of the temple? and they which wait at the altar are partakers with the altar? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Even so hath the Lord ordained<small><small>[^36]</small></small> that they which preach the gospel should live of the gospel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I have<small><small>[^37]</small></small> used none of these things: neither<small><small>[^38]</small></small> have I written these things, that it should be so done unto me: for<small><small>[^39]</small></small> \[it were\] better for me to die, than that any man should make my glorying void. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For though I preach the gospel, I have<small><small>[^40]</small></small> nothing to glory of: for<small><small>[^41]</small></small> necessity is laid upon me; yea, woe<small><small>[^42]</small></small> is unto me, if I preach not the gospel! <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For if I<small><small>[^43]</small></small> do this thing willingly, I have<small><small>[^44]</small></small> a reward: but if against<small><small>[^45]</small></small> my will, a dispensation<small><small>[^46]</small></small> \[of the gospel\] is committed unto me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What is my reward then? \[Verily\] that, when<small><small>[^47]</small></small> I preach the gospel, I may make the gospel of Christ without charge, that I<small><small>[^48]</small></small> abuse not my power in the gospel.

***Paul the Servant to All*** ^bsb2760

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For though I be<small><small>[^49]</small></small> free from all \[men\], yet have I made<small><small>[^50]</small></small> myself servant unto all, that<small><small>[^51]</small></small> I might gain the more. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And unto<small><small>[^52]</small></small> the Jews I became as a Jew, that I might gain the Jews; to them that are under<small><small>[^53]</small></small> the law, as under the law, that I might gain them that are under the law; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To them<small><small>[^54]</small></small> that are without law, as without law, (being not<small><small>[^55]</small></small> without law to God, but under the law to Christ,) that I might gain them that are without law. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To the weak<small><small>[^56]</small></small> became I as weak, that I might gain the weak: I am<small><small>[^57]</small></small> made all things to all \[men\], that I might by<small><small>[^58]</small></small> all means save some. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And this I do for<small><small>[^59]</small></small> the gospel’s sake, that<small><small>[^60]</small></small> I might be partaker thereof with \[you\].

Now they \[do it\] to obtain a corruptible crown; but<small><small>[^66]</small></small> we an incorruptible. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I therefore so run, not<small><small>[^67]</small></small> as uncertainly; so<small><small>[^68]</small></small> fight I, not as one that beateth the air: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.9.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I keep<small><small>[^69]</small></small> under my body, and<small><small>[^70]</small></small> bring \[it\] into subjection: lest<small><small>[^71]</small></small> that by any means, when I have preached to others, I myself should be a castaway<small><small>[^72]</small></small>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[1Cor.8|<< 1 Corinthians 8]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[1Cor.10|1 Corinthians 10 >>]]</td></tr></table>
