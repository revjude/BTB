***Paul’s Message by the Spirit’s Power*** ^bsb2743

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I, brethren, when<small><small>[^2]</small></small> I came to you, came not with<small><small>[^3]</small></small> excellency of speech or of wisdom, declaring unto you the testimony<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I determined not<small><small>[^5]</small></small> to know any thing among you, save Jesus Christ, and him crucified. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I was with you in weakness, and in fear, and in much trembling.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And my speech<small><small>[^7]</small></small> and my preaching \[was\] not<small><small>[^8]</small></small> with enticing<small><small>[^9]</small></small> words of man’s wisdom, but<small><small>[^10]</small></small> in demonstration of the Spirit and of power: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.2.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That your faith should not stand<small><small>[^11]</small></small> in the wisdom of men, but<small><small>[^12]</small></small> in the power of God.
