<big><big>[[1Cor.8.1 Food Sacrificed to Idols|⥃]]</big></big>***Food Sacrificed to Idols***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Ezek.14|Ezekiel 14:1–11]] <big>†</big> [[Rom.14|Romans 14:13–23]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now as touching<small><small>[^2]</small></small> things offered unto idols, we know that we all have knowledge<small><small>[^3]</small></small>. Knowledge puffeth up, but<small><small>[^4]</small></small> charity edifieth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if<small><small>[^5]</small></small> any man think that he knoweth any thing, he knoweth nothing yet as he ought to know. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But if any man love<small><small>[^6]</small></small> God, the same is<small><small>[^7]</small></small> known of him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As concerning therefore the eating of those things that are offered in sacrifice unto idols, we know<small><small>[^8]</small></small> that an idol \[is\] nothing in the world, and that \[there is<small><small>[^9]</small></small>\] none other God but one. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For though there be that<small><small>[^10]</small></small> are called gods, whether in heaven or in earth, (as there be gods many, and lords many,) <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But to us \[there is but\] one God<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, the Father, of whom<small><small>[^12]</small></small> \[are\] all things, and we<small><small>[^13]</small></small> in him<small><small>[^14]</small></small>; and one<small><small>[^15]</small></small> Lord Jesus Christ, by whom \[are\] all things, and we by<small><small>[^16]</small></small> him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Howbeit \[there<small><small>[^17]</small></small> is\] not in every man that knowledge: for some with<small><small>[^18]</small></small> conscience of the idol unto this hour eat \[it\] as a thing offered unto an idol; and their conscience being weak is defiled. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But meat<small><small>[^19]</small></small> commendeth us not to God: for neither, if we eat, are we the better<small><small>[^20]</small></small>; neither, if we eat not, are we the worse<small><small>[^21]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But take<small><small>[^22]</small></small> heed lest by any means this liberty<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of yours become a stumblingblock<small><small>[^24]</small></small> to them that are weak<small><small>[^25]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For if any man see thee which hast<small><small>[^26]</small></small> knowledge sit<small><small>[^27]</small></small> at meat in the idol’s temple, shall not<small><small>[^28]</small></small> the conscience of him which is weak be emboldened<small><small>[^29]</small></small> to eat those things which are offered to idols; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And through thy knowledge shall<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the weak brother perish, for whom Christ died? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But when<small><small>[^31]</small></small> ye sin so against the brethren, and wound their weak conscience, ye sin against<small><small>[^32]</small></small> Christ. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.8.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore, if meat<small><small>[^33]</small></small> make my brother to offend, I will eat no flesh while the world standeth, lest I make my brother to offend. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[1Cor.7|<< 1 Corinthians 7]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[1Cor.9|1 Corinthians 9 >>]]</td></tr></table>
