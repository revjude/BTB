***The Collection for the Saints***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Cor.9|2 Corinthians 9:1–15]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2778

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now concerning<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the collection for the saints<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, as I have given order to the churches<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of Galatia, even so do ye. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Upon the first<small><small>[^5]</small></small> \[day\] of the week let every one of you lay by him in store, as \[God<small><small>[^6]</small></small>\] hath prospered him, that<small><small>[^7]</small></small> there be no gatherings when I come. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when<small><small>[^8]</small></small> I come, whomsoever<small><small>[^9]</small></small> ye shall approve by \[your\] letters, them will I send to bring your liberality<small><small>[^10]</small></small> unto Jerusalem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if it be meet that I go also, they shall go with me.<small><small>[^11]</small></small>

***Paul’s Travel Plans***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Rom.15|Romans 15:23–33]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2779

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now I will come unto you, when<small><small>[^12]</small></small> I shall pass through Macedonia: for I do pass through Macedonia. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it may be that I will abide, yea, and winter<small><small>[^13]</small></small> with you, that ye<small><small>[^14]</small></small> may bring me on my journey whithersoever I go. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I will not see you now by the way; but I trust to tarry a while with you, if<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the Lord permit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will tarry at<small><small>[^16]</small></small> Ephesus until Pentecost<small><small>[^17]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For a great<small><small>[^18]</small></small> door<small><small>[^19]</small></small> and effectual is opened unto me, and \[there<small><small>[^20]</small></small> are\] many adversaries.

***Timothy and Apollos***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Phil.2|Philippians 2:19–30]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2780

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now if<small><small>[^21]</small></small> Timotheus come, see that he may be with you without<small><small>[^22]</small></small> fear: for<small><small>[^23]</small></small> he worketh the work of the Lord, as I also \[do\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let no<small><small>[^24]</small></small> man therefore despise him: but<small><small>[^25]</small></small> conduct him forth in peace, that he may come unto me: for I look for him with the brethren. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As touching \[our<small><small>[^26]</small></small>\] brother Apollos, I greatly desired him to come unto you with the brethren: but his will was not at all to come at this time; but he will come when<small><small>[^27]</small></small> he shall have convenient time.

***Concluding Exhortations*** ^bsb2781

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Watch<small><small>[^28]</small></small> ye, stand<small><small>[^29]</small></small> fast in the faith, quit<small><small>[^30]</small></small> you like men, be<small><small>[^31]</small></small> strong. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let all your things be done with charity.<small><small>[^32]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I beseech you, brethren, (ye know the house<small><small>[^33]</small></small> of Stephanas, that it is the firstfruits<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of Achaia, and \[that\] they have addicted themselves to the<small><small>[^35]</small></small> ministry of the saints,) <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That ye<small><small>[^36]</small></small> submit yourselves unto such, and to every one that helpeth<small><small>[^37]</small></small> with \[us\], and laboureth<small><small>[^38]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I am glad of the coming of Stephanas<small><small>[^39]</small></small> and Fortunatus and Achaicus: for<small><small>[^40]</small></small> that which was lacking on your part they have supplied. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they<small><small>[^41]</small></small> have refreshed my spirit and yours: therefore<small><small>[^42]</small></small> acknowledge ye them that are such.

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The churches<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of Asia salute you. Aquila<small><small>[^44]</small></small> and Priscilla salute you much in the Lord, with the church<small><small>[^45]</small></small> that is in their house. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All the brethren<small><small>[^46]</small></small> greet you. Greet<small><small>[^47]</small></small> ye one another with an holy kiss. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The salutation<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of \[me\] Paul with mine own hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If any man love<small><small>[^49]</small></small> not the Lord Jesus Christ, let him be Anathema<small><small>[^50]</small></small> Maran-atha. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ \[be\] with you.<small><small>[^51]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.16.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My love<small><small>[^52]</small></small> \[be\] with you all in Christ Jesus.
