***Lawsuits among Believers*** ^bsb2752

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Dare any of you, having<small><small>[^2]</small></small> a matter against another, go<small><small>[^3]</small></small> to law before the unjust, and not before the saints<small><small>[^4]</small></small>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Do ye not know that the saints<small><small>[^5]</small></small> shall judge the world? and if the world shall be judged by you, are ye unworthy to judge the smallest<small><small>[^6]</small></small> matters? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Know ye not that we shall judge<small><small>[^7]</small></small> angels? how much more things that pertain<small><small>[^8]</small></small> to this life? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If then ye<small><small>[^9]</small></small> have judgments of things pertaining to this life, set them to judge who<small><small>[^10]</small></small> are least<small><small>[^11]</small></small> esteemed in the church. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I speak to your<small><small>[^12]</small></small> shame. Is it<small><small>[^13]</small></small> so, that there is not a wise man among you? no, not one that shall be able to judge between his brethren? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But brother<small><small>[^14]</small></small> goeth to law with brother, and that before the unbelievers. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now there<small><small>[^15]</small></small>fore there is utterly a fault among you, because ye go to law one with another. Why<small><small>[^16]</small></small> do ye not rather take wrong? why do ye not rather \[suffer yourselves to\] be defrauded? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Nay, ye do wrong, and defraud, and that \[your\] brethren.<small><small>[^17]</small></small>

***Members of Christ*** ^bsb2753

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Know<small><small>[^18]</small></small> ye not that the unrighteous<small><small>[^19]</small></small> shall not inherit<small><small>[^20]</small></small> the kingdom of God? Be not deceived: neither fornicators<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, nor idolaters, nor adulterers, nor effeminate, nor abusers<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of themselves with mankind, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Nor thieves<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, nor covetous<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, nor drunkards, nor revilers, nor extortioners, shall inherit the kingdom of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And such<small><small>[^25]</small></small> were some of you: but ye are washed<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, but ye are sanctified<small><small>[^27]</small></small>, but ye are justified<small><small>[^28]</small></small> in the name of the Lord Jesus, and by the Spirit of our God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All things are lawful<small><small>[^29]</small></small> unto me, but all things are not<small><small>[^30]</small></small> expedient: all things are lawful for me, but I<small><small>[^31]</small></small> will not be brought under the power of any. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Meats for<small><small>[^32]</small></small> the belly, and the belly for meats: but God<small><small>[^33]</small></small> shall destroy both it and them. Now the body \[is\] not for fornication, but for<small><small>[^34]</small></small> the Lord; and the Lord for the body. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God<small><small>[^35]</small></small> hath both raised up the Lord, and will also raise up us by<small><small>[^36]</small></small> his own power. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Know ye not that your<small><small>[^37]</small></small> bodies are the members of Christ? shall I then take the members of Christ, and make \[them\] the members of an harlot? God<small><small>[^38]</small></small> forbid. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What? know ye not that he which is joined to an harlot<small><small>[^39]</small></small> is one body? for<small><small>[^40]</small></small> two, saith he, shall be one flesh. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.6.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But he that is joined unto the Lord is one spirit.<small><small>[^41]</small></small>
