***Spiritual Gifts*** ^bsb2767

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now concerning spiritual<small><small>[^2]</small></small> \[gifts\], brethren, I would not<small><small>[^3]</small></small> have you ignorant. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye know that<small><small>[^4]</small></small> ye were Gentiles, carried away unto these dumb<small><small>[^5]</small></small> idols, even<small><small>[^6]</small></small> as ye were led. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore I give you to understand, that no man<small><small>[^7]</small></small> speaking by the Spirit of God calleth Jesus accursed<small><small>[^8]</small></small>: and \[that\] no man<small><small>[^9]</small></small> can say that Jesus is the Lord, but by the Holy Ghost. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now there<small><small>[^10]</small></small> are diversities of gifts, but the same Spirit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there are differences of administrations<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, but<small><small>[^12]</small></small> the same Lord. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there are diversities of operations, but it is the same God which worketh<small><small>[^13]</small></small> all<small><small>[^14]</small></small> in all. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the manifestation of the Spirit is given to every man to profit withal.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For to one is given<small><small>[^16]</small></small> by the Spirit the word of wisdom; to another the word of knowledge by the same Spirit; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To another faith<small><small>[^17]</small></small> by the same Spirit; to another the gifts<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of healing by the same Spirit; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To another the working<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of miracles; to another prophecy<small><small>[^20]</small></small>; to another discerning<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of spirits; to another \[divers<small><small>[^22]</small></small>\] kinds of tongues; to another the<small><small>[^23]</small></small> interpretation of tongues: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But all<small><small>[^24]</small></small> these worketh that one and the selfsame Spirit, dividing to every man severally as<small><small>[^25]</small></small> he will.

***The Body of Christ*** ^bsb2768

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For as<small><small>[^26]</small></small> the body is one, and hath many members, and all the members of that one body, being many, are one body: so<small><small>[^27]</small></small> also \[is\] Christ. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For by<small><small>[^28]</small></small> one Spirit are we all baptized into one body, whether \[we be\] Jews<small><small>[^29]</small></small> or Gentiles<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, whether \[we be\] bond<small><small>[^31]</small></small> or free; and have been all made to drink<small><small>[^32]</small></small> into one Spirit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the body is not one member, but many.<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If the foot shall say, Because I am not the hand, I am not of the body; is it therefore not of the body?<small><small>[^34]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if the ear shall say, Because I am not the eye, I am not of the body; is it<small><small>[^35]</small></small> therefore not of the body? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If the whole body \[were\] an eye, where \[were\] the hearing? If the whole \[were\] hearing, where \[were\] the smelling?<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But now hath<small><small>[^37]</small></small> God set the members every one of them in the body, as it<small><small>[^38]</small></small> hath pleased him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if they were all one member, where \[were\] the body?<small><small>[^39]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But now \[are they\] many members, yet but one body. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the eye cannot say unto the hand, I have no need of thee: nor again the head to the feet, I have no need of you.<small><small>[^40]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Nay, much more those members of the body, which seem to be more feeble, are necessary:<small><small>[^41]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And those \[members\] of the body, which we think to be less honourable, upon these we bestow<small><small>[^42]</small></small> more abundant honour; and our uncomely \[parts\] have more abundant comeliness. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For our comely \[parts\] have no need: but God hath tempered the body together, having given more abundant honour to that \[part\] which lacked:<small><small>[^43]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That there<small><small>[^44]</small></small> should be no schism<small><small>[^45]</small></small> in the body; but \[that\] the members should have the same<small><small>[^46]</small></small> care one for another. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Cor.12.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And whether one member suffer, all the members suffer with it; or one member be honoured, all the members rejoice with it.<small><small>[^47]</small></small>

***The Greater Gifts*** ^bsb2769
