<big><big>[[Num.12.1 The Complaint of Miriam and Aaron|⥃]]</big></big>***The Complaint of Miriam and Aaron***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Miriam<small><small>[^2]</small></small> and Aaron spake against Moses because of the Ethiopian<small><small>[^3]</small></small> woman whom he had married<small><small>[^4]</small></small>: for he had married an Ethiopian woman. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they said, Hath the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^5]</small></small></span> indeed spoken only by Moses? hath he not<small><small>[^6]</small></small> spoken also by us? And the<small><small>[^7]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> heard \[it\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> (Now the man Moses \[was\] very<small><small>[^8]</small></small> meek, above<small><small>[^9]</small></small> all the men which \[were\] upon the face of the earth.) <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^10]</small></small></span> spake suddenly unto Moses, and unto Aaron, and unto Miriam, Come out<small><small>[^11]</small></small> ye three unto the tabernacle of the congregation. And they three came out. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came down in the pillar of the cloud, and stood \[in\] the door of the tabernacle, and called Aaron and Miriam: and they both came forth.<small><small>[^12]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Hear now my words: If there be a prophet<small><small>[^13]</small></small> among you, \[I\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will make myself known unto him in a vision<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, \[and\] will speak unto him in a dream<small><small>[^15]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My servant<small><small>[^16]</small></small> Moses \[is\] not so, who \[is\] faithful<small><small>[^17]</small></small> in all mine house. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> With him will I speak mouth<small><small>[^18]</small></small> to mouth, even apparently, and not in dark speeches<small><small>[^19]</small></small>; and the similitude<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall he behold: wherefore then were ye<small><small>[^21]</small></small> not afraid to speak against my servant Moses? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the anger of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> was kindled against them; and he departed.<small><small>[^22]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the cloud<small><small>[^23]</small></small> departed from off the tabernacle; and, behold<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, Miriam \[became\] leprous<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, \[white\] as snow: and Aaron looked upon Miriam, and, behold, \[she was\] leprous. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Aaron said unto Moses, Alas, my lord, I beseech thee<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, lay not<small><small>[^27]</small></small> the sin upon us, wherein we have done foolishly, and wherein we have sinned. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let her not be as one dead<small><small>[^28]</small></small>, of whom<small><small>[^29]</small></small> the flesh is half consumed when he cometh out of his mother’s womb. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses cried unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, saying, Heal her now, O God, I beseech thee.<small><small>[^30]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Moses, If her father had but spit<small><small>[^31]</small></small> in her face, should she not be ashamed seven days? let her be<small><small>[^32]</small></small> shut out from the camp seven days, and after that let her be received in \[again\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Miriam was shut out<small><small>[^33]</small></small> from the camp seven days: and the<small><small>[^34]</small></small> people journeyed not till Miriam<small><small>[^35]</small></small> was brought in \[again\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.12.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And afterward<small><small>[^36]</small></small> the people removed from Hazeroth<small><small>[^37]</small></small>, and pitched in the wilderness<small><small>[^38]</small></small> of Paran. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Num.11|<< Numbers 11]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Num.13|Numbers 13 >>]]</td></tr></table>
