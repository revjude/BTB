<big><big>[[Num.9.1 The Second Passover|⥃]]</big></big>***The Second Passover***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Exod.12|Exodus 12:1–13]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses in the wilderness of Sinai, in the first month<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of the second year after they were come out of the land of Egypt, saying,<small><small>[^3]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let the children of Israel also keep<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the passover at his appointed<small><small>[^5]</small></small> season. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the fourteenth<small><small>[^6]</small></small> day of this month, at even<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, ye shall keep it in his appointed season: according to all the rites<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of it, and according to all the ceremonies thereof, shall ye keep it.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses spake unto the children of Israel, that they should keep the passover. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they kept<small><small>[^10]</small></small> the passover on the fourteenth day of the first month at even in the wilderness of Sinai: according<small><small>[^11]</small></small> to all that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> commanded Moses, so did the children of Israel. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And there were certain men, who were defiled<small><small>[^12]</small></small> by the dead body of a man, that they could not keep the passover on that day: and they came<small><small>[^13]</small></small> before Moses and before Aaron on that day: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And those men said unto him, We \[are\] defiled by the dead body of a man: wherefore are we kept back, that we may not offer<small><small>[^14]</small></small> an offering of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> in his appointed season among the children of Israel? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses said unto them, Stand<small><small>[^15]</small></small> still, and I will<small><small>[^16]</small></small> hear what the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will command concerning you. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Speak unto the children of Israel, saying, If any man of you or of your posterity shall be unclean<small><small>[^17]</small></small> by reason of a dead body, or \[be\] in a journey afar off, yet he shall keep<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the passover unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The fourteenth<small><small>[^19]</small></small> day of the second month at even they shall keep it, \[and\] eat it<small><small>[^20]</small></small> with unleavened bread and bitter \[herbs\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They shall leave<small><small>[^21]</small></small> none of it unto the morning, nor break any bone<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of it: according<small><small>[^23]</small></small> to all the ordinances of the passover they shall keep it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the man that \[is\] clean, and is not in a journey, and forbeareth<small><small>[^24]</small></small> to keep the passover, even the same soul shall be cut off from among his people: because<small><small>[^25]</small></small> he brought not the offering of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> in his appointed season, that man shall bear his sin<small><small>[^26]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if a stranger shall sojourn among you, and will keep the passover unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; according to the ordinance of the passover, and according to the manner thereof, so shall he do: ye shall have one ordinance, both for the stranger, and for him that was born in the land.<small><small>[^27]</small></small>

<big><big>[[Num.9.15 The Cloud above the Tabernacle|⥃]]</big></big>***The Cloud above the Tabernacle***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Exod.40|Exodus 40:34–38]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And on the day<small><small>[^28]</small></small> that the tabernacle was reared up the cloud<small><small>[^29]</small></small> covered the tabernacle, \[namely\], the tent of the testimony: and at even<small><small>[^30]</small></small> there was upon the tabernacle as it were the appearance of fire, until the morning. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So it was alway: the cloud covered it \[by day\], and the appearance of fire by night.<small><small>[^31]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when the cloud<small><small>[^32]</small></small> was taken up from the tabernacle, then after that the children of Israel journeyed: and in the<small><small>[^33]</small></small> place where the cloud abode, there the children of Israel pitched their tents. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> At the commandment of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> the children of Israel journeyed, and at the<small><small>[^34]</small></small> commandment of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> they pitched: as long as<small><small>[^35]</small></small> the cloud abode upon the tabernacle they rested in their tents. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when the cloud tarried long<small><small>[^36]</small></small> upon the tabernacle many days, then the children of Israel kept the<small><small>[^37]</small></small> charge of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and journeyed not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[so\] it was, when the cloud was a few days upon the tabernacle; according to the commandment of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> they abode in their tents, and according to the commandment of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> they journeyed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[so\] it was, when the cloud abode<small><small>[^38]</small></small> from even unto the morning, and \[that\] the cloud was taken up in the morning, then they journeyed: whether \[it was\] by day or by night that the cloud was taken up, they journeyed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Or \[whether it were\] two days, or a month, or a year, that the cloud tarried upon the tabernacle, remaining thereon, the children of Israel abode<small><small>[^39]</small></small> in their tents, and journeyed not: but when it was taken up, they journeyed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.9.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> At the commandment of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> they rested in the tents, and at the commandment of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> they journeyed: they kept<small><small>[^40]</small></small> the charge of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, at the commandment of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> by the hand of Moses. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Num.8|<< Numbers 8]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Num.10|Numbers 10 >>]]</td></tr></table>
