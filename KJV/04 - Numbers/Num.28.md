***The Daily Offerings***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Exod.29|Exodus 29:38–44]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb435

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Command the children of Israel, and say unto them, My offering, \[and\] my bread<small><small>[^2]</small></small> for my sacrifices made by fire, \[for\] a sweet savour unto me<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, shall ye observe to offer unto me in their due season<small><small>[^4]</small></small>.<small><small>[^5]</small></small><small><small>[^6]</small></small><small><small>[^7]</small></small><small><small>[^8]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thou shalt say unto them, This \[is\] the offering made by fire which ye shall offer unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; two lambs<small><small>[^9]</small></small> of the first year without spot day by day<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, \[for\] a continual burnt offering. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The one lamb shalt thou offer in the morning, and the other<small><small>[^11]</small></small> lamb shalt thou offer at even<small><small>[^12]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And a tenth \[part\] of an ephah of flour for a meat offering, mingled with the fourth \[part\] of an hin of beaten oil.<small><small>[^13]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[It is\] a continual<small><small>[^14]</small></small> burnt offering, which was ordained<small><small>[^15]</small></small> in mount Sinai for a sweet savour, a sacrifice made by fire unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the drink offering thereof \[shall be\] the fourth \[part\] of an hin for the one lamb: in the holy<small><small>[^16]</small></small> \[place\] shalt thou cause the strong wine to be poured<small><small>[^17]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[for\] a drink offering. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the other lamb shalt thou offer at even: as the meat offering of the morning, and as the drink offering thereof, thou shalt offer \[it\], a sacrifice made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.

***The Sabbath Offerings*** ^bsb436

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And on the sabbath day two lambs of the first year without spot, and two tenth deals of flour \[for\] a meat offering, mingled with oil, and the drink offering thereof:<small><small>[^18]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[This is\] the burnt<small><small>[^19]</small></small> offering of every sabbath, beside the continual<small><small>[^20]</small></small> burnt offering, and his drink offering.

***The Monthly Offerings*** ^bsb437

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And in the beginnings<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of your months ye shall offer a burnt offering unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; two young<small><small>[^22]</small></small> bullocks, and one ram, seven lambs of the first year without spot; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And three tenth deals of flour \[for\] a meat offering, mingled with oil, for one bullock; and two tenth deals of flour \[for\] a meat offering, mingled with oil, for one ram;<small><small>[^23]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And a several tenth deal of flour mingled with oil \[for\] a meat offering unto one lamb; \[for\] a burnt<small><small>[^24]</small></small> offering of a sweet savour, a sacrifice made by fire unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And their drink offerings shall be half an hin of wine unto a bullock, and the third \[part\] of an hin unto a ram, and a fourth \[part\] of an hin unto a lamb: this \[is\] the burnt offering of every month throughout the months of the year. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And one kid<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of the goats for a sin offering unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall be offered, beside<small><small>[^26]</small></small> the continual burnt offering, and his drink offering.

***Passover and the Feast of Unleavened Bread***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Exod.12|Exodus 12:14–28]] <big>†</big> [[Lev.23|Leviticus 23:4–8]] <big>†</big> [[Deut.16|Deuteronomy 16:1–8]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb438

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And in the fourteenth day of the first month \[is\] the passover of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^27]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And in the fifteenth day of this month \[is\] the feast: seven days shall unleavened bread be eaten.<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the first day \[shall be\] an holy convocation; ye shall do no manner of servile work \[therein\]:<small><small>[^29]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But ye shall offer a sacrifice made by fire \[for\] a burnt offering unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; two young<small><small>[^30]</small></small> bullocks, and one ram, and seven lambs of the first year: they shall<small><small>[^31]</small></small> be unto you without blemish: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And their meat offering \[shall be of\] flour mingled with oil: three tenth deals shall ye offer for a bullock, and two tenth deals for a ram; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> A several tenth deal shalt thou offer for every lamb, throughout the seven lambs: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And one goat \[for\] a sin offering, to make an atonement for you.<small><small>[^32]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye shall offer these beside the burnt offering in the morning, which \[is\] for a continual burnt offering.<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> After this manner ye shall offer daily, throughout the seven days, the meat of the sacrifice made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: it shall be offered beside the continual burnt offering, and his drink offering. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Num.28.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And on the seventh<small><small>[^34]</small></small> day ye shall have an holy convocation; ye shall do<small><small>[^35]</small></small> no servile work.
