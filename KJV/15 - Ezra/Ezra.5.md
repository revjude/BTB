<big><big>[[Ezra.5.1 Temple Rebuilding Resumes|⥃]]</big></big>***Temple Rebuilding Resumes***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Hag.1|Haggai 1:1–11]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the prophets, Haggai<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the prophet, and Zechariah<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the son of Iddo<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, prophesied unto the Jews that \[were\] in Judah and Jerusalem in the name<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of the God of Israel, \[even\] unto them.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then rose up<small><small>[^7]</small></small> Zerubbabel the son of Shealtiel, and Jeshua<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the son of Jozadak, and began to build the house of God which \[is\] at Jerusalem: and with them \[were\] the prophets<small><small>[^9]</small></small> of God helping them. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> At the same time came to them Tatnai<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, governor on this side the river, and Shethar–boznai, and their companions, and said thus unto them, Who hath commanded you<small><small>[^11]</small></small> to build this house, and to make up this wall? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said we unto them after this manner, What are<small><small>[^12]</small></small> the names of the men that make this building<small><small>[^13]</small></small>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the eye<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of their God was upon the elders of the Jews, that they<small><small>[^15]</small></small> could not cause them to cease, till the matter came to Darius: and then they returned<small><small>[^16]</small></small> answer by letter concerning this \[matter\].

<big><big>[[Ezra.5.6 Tattenai’s Letter to Darius|⥃]]</big></big>***Tattenai’s Letter to Darius***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]The copy<small><small>[^17]</small></small> of the letter that Tatnai, governor on this side the river, and Shethar–boznai, and his companions the Apharsachites<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, which \[were\] on this side the river, sent unto Darius the king:<small><small>[^19]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They sent a letter unto him, wherein<small><small>[^20]</small></small> was written thus; Unto Darius the king, all peace<small><small>[^21]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be it known unto the king, that we went into the province<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of Judea, to the house of the great God<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, which is builded with great stones<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, and timber is laid in the walls, and this work goeth fast on, and prospereth in their hands. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then asked we those elders, \[and\] said unto them thus, Who commanded<small><small>[^25]</small></small> you to build this house, and to make up these walls? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> We asked<small><small>[^26]</small></small> their names also, to certify thee, that we might write the names of the men that \[were\] the chief of them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thus they returned us answer, saying, We are<small><small>[^27]</small></small> the servants of the God of heaven and earth, and build the house that was builded these many years ago, which a great<small><small>[^28]</small></small> king of Israel builded and set up. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But after<small><small>[^29]</small></small> that our fathers had provoked the God of heaven unto wrath, he gave<small><small>[^30]</small></small> them into the hand<small><small>[^31]</small></small> of Nebuchadnezzar the king of Babylon, the Chaldean, who destroyed this house, and carried the people away into Babylon.<small><small>[^32]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But in the first year of Cyrus the king of Babylon \[the same\] king Cyrus made a decree to build this house of God.<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the vessels<small><small>[^34]</small></small> also of gold and silver of the house of God, which Nebuchadnezzar took out of the temple that \[was\] in Jerusalem, and brought them into the temple of Babylon, those did Cyrus the king<small><small>[^35]</small></small> take out of the temple of Babylon, and they were delivered unto \[one\], whose name \[was\] Sheshbazzar<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, whom<small><small>[^37]</small></small> he had made governor<small><small>[^38]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And said unto him, Take these vessels, go, carry them into the temple that \[is\] in Jerusalem, and let the house<small><small>[^39]</small></small> of God be builded in his place. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then came the same Sheshbazzar<small><small>[^40]</small></small>, \[and\] laid<small><small>[^41]</small></small> the foundation of the house of God which \[is\] in Jerusalem: and since that time even until now hath it been in building, and \[yet\] it is not finished<small><small>[^42]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.5.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now therefore, if \[it seem\] good to the king, let there be<small><small>[^43]</small></small> search made in the king’s treasure house, which \[is\] there at Babylon, whether it be \[so\], that a decree<small><small>[^44]</small></small> was made of Cyrus
