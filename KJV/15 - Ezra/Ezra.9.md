<big><big>[[Ezra.9.1 Intermarriage with Neighboring Peoples|⥃]]</big></big>***Intermarriage with Neighboring Peoples***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Neh.13|Nehemiah 13:23–31]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.9.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now when these things were done, the princes<small><small>[^2]</small></small> came to me, saying, The people of Israel, and the priests, and the Levites, have not separated<small><small>[^3]</small></small> themselves from the people of the lands, \[doing\] according<small><small>[^4]</small></small> to their abominations, \[even\] of the Canaanites<small><small>[^5]</small></small>, the Hittites, the Perizzites, the Jebusites, the Ammonites<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, the Moabites<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, the Egyptians, and the Amorites. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.9.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they have taken of their<small><small>[^8]</small></small> daughters for themselves, and for their sons: so that the holy seed<small><small>[^9]</small></small> have mingled<small><small>[^10]</small></small> themselves with the people of \[those\] lands: yea, the hand<small><small>[^11]</small></small> of the princes and rulers hath been chief in this trespass. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.9.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when I heard this thing, I rent<small><small>[^12]</small></small> my garment and my mantle, and plucked off<small><small>[^13]</small></small> the hair of my head and of my beard, and sat<small><small>[^14]</small></small> down astonied. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.9.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then were assembled unto me every one that trembled<small><small>[^15]</small></small> at the words of the God of Israel, because of the transgression of those that had been carried away; and I sat astonied until<small><small>[^16]</small></small> the evening sacrifice.

reviving<small><small>[^35]</small></small> in our bondage. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.9.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For we \[were\] bondmen<small><small>[^36]</small></small>; yet our God<small><small>[^37]</small></small> hath not forsaken us in our bondage, but hath extended mercy unto us in the sight<small><small>[^38]</small></small> of the kings of Persia, to give us a reviving, to set up<small><small>[^39]</small></small> the house of our God, and to class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.9.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And now, O our God, what shall we say<small><small>[^42]</small></small> after this? for we have forsaken thy commandments, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.9.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which thou hast commanded by thy servants<small><small>[^43]</small></small> the with their abominations, which have filled it from one end to another<small><small>[^46]</small></small> with their uncleanness. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.9.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now therefore give not<small><small>[^47]</small></small> your daughters unto their sons, neither take their daughters unto your sons, nor seek their peace<small><small>[^48]</small></small> or their wealth for ever: that ye may<small><small>[^49]</small></small> be strong, and eat<small><small>[^50]</small></small> the good of the land, and leave \[it<small><small>[^51]</small></small>\] for an inheritance to your children for ever. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.9.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And after all<small><small>[^52]</small></small> that is come upon us for our evil deeds, and for our great trespass, seeing that thou our God hast punished<small><small>[^53]</small></small> us less<small><small>[^54]</small></small> than our iniquities \[deserve\], and hast given us<small><small>[^55]</small></small> \[such\] deliverance as this; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.9.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Should we again<small><small>[^56]</small></small> break thy commandments, and join in<small><small>[^57]</small></small> affinity with the people of these abominations? wouldest not thou<small><small>[^58]</small></small> be angry with us till thou hadst consumed \[us\], so that \[there should be\] no remnant<small><small>[^59]</small></small> nor escaping? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ezra.9.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O <span
