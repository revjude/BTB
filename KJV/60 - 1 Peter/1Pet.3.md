<big><big>[[1Pet.3.1 Wives and Husbands|⥃]]</big></big>***Wives and Husbands***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Song.1|Song 1:1–17]] <big>†</big> [[Eph.5|Ephesians 5:22–33]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Likewise, ye<small><small>[^2]</small></small> wives, \[be\] in subjection to your own husbands; that, if any obey<small><small>[^3]</small></small> not the word, they<small><small>[^4]</small></small> also may without the word be won<small><small>[^5]</small></small> by the conversation of the wives; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> While they behold<small><small>[^6]</small></small> your chaste conversation \[coupled\] with<small><small>[^7]</small></small> fear. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whose adorning let<small><small>[^8]</small></small> it not be that<small><small>[^9]</small></small> outward \[adorning\] of plaiting the hair, and of wearing of gold, or of putting on of apparel; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But \[let it be\] the hidden<small><small>[^10]</small></small> man of the heart, in that which is not<small><small>[^11]</small></small> corruptible, \[even the ornament\] of a meek<small><small>[^12]</small></small> and quiet<small><small>[^13]</small></small> spirit, which is in<small><small>[^14]</small></small> the sight of God of great price. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For after this manner in the old time the holy<small><small>[^15]</small></small> women also, who<small><small>[^16]</small></small> trusted in God, adorned<small><small>[^17]</small></small> themselves, being in subjection unto their own husbands: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Even as Sara<small><small>[^18]</small></small> obeyed Abraham, calling him lord: whose daughters<small><small>[^19]</small></small> ye are, as long as ye do well, and<small><small>[^20]</small></small> are not afraid with any amazement. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Likewise, ye<small><small>[^21]</small></small> husbands, dwell with \[them\] according to knowledge, giving<small><small>[^22]</small></small> honour unto the wife, as unto the weaker vessel, and as being heirs<small><small>[^23]</small></small> together of the grace of life; that<small><small>[^24]</small></small> your prayers be not hindered.

<big><big>[[1Pet.3.8 Turning from Evil|⥃]]</big></big>***Turning from Evil***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Finally, \[be<small><small>[^25]</small></small> ye\] all of one mind, having<small><small>[^26]</small></small> compassion one of another, love as brethren<small><small>[^27]</small></small>, \[be\] pitiful<small><small>[^28]</small></small>, \[be\] courteous<small><small>[^29]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Not rendering<small><small>[^30]</small></small> evil for evil, or railing for railing: but contrariwise blessing; knowing that ye are thereunto called<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, that<small><small>[^32]</small></small> ye should inherit a blessing. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he<small><small>[^33]</small></small> that will love<small><small>[^34]</small></small> life, and see<small><small>[^35]</small></small> good days, let him refrain<small><small>[^36]</small></small> his tongue from evil, and his lips that they speak<small><small>[^37]</small></small> no guile: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let him eschew<small><small>[^38]</small></small> evil, and do<small><small>[^39]</small></small> good; let him seek<small><small>[^40]</small></small> peace, and ensue it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the eyes<small><small>[^41]</small></small> of the Lord \[are\] over the righteous, and his ears<small><small>[^42]</small></small> \[are open\] unto their prayers: but<small><small>[^43]</small></small> the face of the Lord \[is\] against<small><small>[^44]</small></small> them that do evil. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And who<small><small>[^45]</small></small> \[is\] he that will harm you, if ye be followers<small><small>[^46]</small></small> of that which is good?

<big><big>[[1Pet.3.14 Suffering for Righteousness|⥃]]</big></big>***Suffering for Righteousness***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But and if<small><small>[^47]</small></small> ye suffer for righteousness’ sake, happy \[are ye\]: and be<small><small>[^48]</small></small> not afraid of their terror, neither be troubled; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But sanctify<small><small>[^49]</small></small> the Lord God in your hearts: and \[be<small><small>[^50]</small></small>\] ready always to \[give\] an answer to every man that asketh you a reason<small><small>[^51]</small></small> of the hope<small><small>[^52]</small></small> that is in you with<small><small>[^53]</small></small> meekness and fear<small><small>[^54]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Having a good<small><small>[^55]</small></small> conscience; that, whereas<small><small>[^56]</small></small> they speak evil of you, as of evildoers, they may be ashamed that falsely<small><small>[^57]</small></small> accuse your good<small><small>[^58]</small></small> conversation in Christ. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For \[it is\] better, if<small><small>[^59]</small></small> the will of God be so, that ye suffer<small><small>[^60]</small></small> for well doing, than for evil doing. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For Christ<small><small>[^61]</small></small> also hath once suffered for sins, the just<small><small>[^62]</small></small> for the unjust, that<small><small>[^63]</small></small> he might bring us to God, being<small><small>[^64]</small></small> put to death in the flesh, but<small><small>[^65]</small></small> quickened by the Spirit: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By which<small><small>[^66]</small></small> also he went and preached unto the spirits in<small><small>[^67]</small></small> prison; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which sometime<small><small>[^68]</small></small> were disobedient, when once the longsuffering<small><small>[^69]</small></small> of God waited in the days<small><small>[^70]</small></small> of Noah, while<small><small>[^71]</small></small> the ark was a preparing, wherein<small><small>[^72]</small></small> few, that is, eight souls were saved by<small><small>[^73]</small></small> water. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The like<small><small>[^74]</small></small> figure whereunto \[even\] baptism<small><small>[^75]</small></small> doth also now save us (not the putting<small><small>[^76]</small></small> away of the filth of the flesh, but the answer<small><small>[^77]</small></small> of a good conscience toward God,) by<small><small>[^78]</small></small> the resurrection of Jesus Christ: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.3.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who is gone<small><small>[^79]</small></small> into heaven, and is on<small><small>[^80]</small></small> the right hand of God;
