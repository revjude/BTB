<big><big>[[1Pet.4.1 Living for God’s Glory|⥃]]</big></big>***Living for God’s Glory***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[1Cor.10|1 Corinthians 10:23–33]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Forasmuch then as Christ<small><small>[^2]</small></small> hath suffered for us in the flesh, arm<small><small>[^3]</small></small> yourselves likewise with the same mind: for<small><small>[^4]</small></small> he that hath suffered in the flesh hath ceased<small><small>[^5]</small></small> from sin; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That he no<small><small>[^6]</small></small> longer should live the rest of \[his\] time in the flesh to the lusts<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of men, but to the will<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the time<small><small>[^9]</small></small> past of \[our\] life may suffice us to have<small><small>[^10]</small></small> wrought the will of the Gentiles, when we walked in lasciviousness<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, lusts, excess<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of wine, revellings<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, banquetings, and<small><small>[^14]</small></small> abominable idolatries: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.4.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherein they think it strange that ye run not with \[them\] to the same excess<small><small>[^15]</small></small> of riot, speaking<small><small>[^16]</small></small> evil of \[you\]: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.4.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who shall<small><small>[^17]</small></small> give account to him that<small><small>[^18]</small></small> is ready to judge the quick and the dead. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.4.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For for this cause was the gospel preached also to them<small><small>[^19]</small></small> that are dead, that they<small><small>[^20]</small></small> might be judged according to men in the flesh, but<small><small>[^21]</small></small> live according to God in the spirit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.4.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the end<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of all things is at hand: be ye<small><small>[^23]</small></small> therefore sober, and<small><small>[^24]</small></small> watch unto prayer. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.4.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And above<small><small>[^25]</small></small> all things have fervent<small><small>[^26]</small></small> charity among yourselves: for<small><small>[^27]</small></small> charity shall<small><small>[^28]</small></small> cover the multitude of sins. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.4.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Use hospitality<small><small>[^29]</small></small> one to another without<small><small>[^30]</small></small> grudging. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.4.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As every<small><small>[^31]</small></small> man hath received the gift, \[even so\] minister<small><small>[^32]</small></small> the same one to another, as good<small><small>[^33]</small></small> stewards of the manifold<small><small>[^34]</small></small> grace of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.4.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If any<small><small>[^35]</small></small> man speak, \[let him speak\] as the<small><small>[^36]</small></small> oracles of God; if<small><small>[^37]</small></small> any man minister, \[let him do it\] as of the ability<small><small>[^38]</small></small> which God giveth: that<small><small>[^39]</small></small> God in all things may be glorified through<small><small>[^40]</small></small> Jesus Christ, to whom<small><small>[^41]</small></small> be praise and dominion<small><small>[^42]</small></small> for ever and ever. Amen.
