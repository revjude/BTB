<big><big>[[1Pet.5.1 Instructions to Elders|⥃]]</big></big>***Instructions to Elders***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[1Tim.3|1 Timothy 3:1–7]] <big>†</big> [[Titus.1|Titus 1:5–9]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The elders<small><small>[^2]</small></small> which are among you I exhort, who<small><small>[^3]</small></small> am also<small><small>[^4]</small></small> an elder, and a<small><small>[^5]</small></small> witness of the sufferings of Christ, and also a partaker<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of the glory that shall be revealed: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Feed<small><small>[^7]</small></small> the flock<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of God which is among you<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, taking<small><small>[^10]</small></small> the oversight \[thereof\], not by<small><small>[^11]</small></small> constraint, but willingly; not for<small><small>[^12]</small></small> filthy lucre, but of<small><small>[^13]</small></small> a ready mind; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither as<small><small>[^14]</small></small> being lords over<small><small>[^15]</small></small> \[God’s\] heritage<small><small>[^16]</small></small>, but<small><small>[^17]</small></small> being ensamples to the flock. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when the<small><small>[^18]</small></small> chief Shepherd shall appear<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, ye shall receive a crown<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of glory that fadeth not away.

<big><big>[[1Pet.5.5 Cast Your Cares on Him|⥃]]</big></big>***Cast Your Cares on Him***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Likewise, ye<small><small>[^21]</small></small> younger, submit yourselves unto the elder. Yea, all<small><small>[^22]</small></small> \[of you\] be subject one to another, and be clothed<small><small>[^23]</small></small> with humility: for God<small><small>[^24]</small></small> resisteth the proud, and giveth<small><small>[^25]</small></small> grace to the humble. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Humble<small><small>[^26]</small></small> yourselves the<small><small>[^27]</small></small>refore under the mighty hand of God, that<small><small>[^28]</small></small> he may exalt you in<small><small>[^29]</small></small> due time: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Casting<small><small>[^30]</small></small> all your care upon him; for<small><small>[^31]</small></small> he careth for you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be sober<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, be vigilant; because your<small><small>[^33]</small></small> adversary the devil<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, as<small><small>[^35]</small></small> a roaring lion, walketh<small><small>[^36]</small></small> about, seeking whom he may devour<small><small>[^37]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whom resist<small><small>[^38]</small></small> stedfast<small><small>[^39]</small></small> in the faith, knowing that the same<small><small>[^40]</small></small> afflictions are accomplished in your brethren that are in the world.

<big><big>[[1Pet.5.10 Benediction and Farewell|⥃]]</big></big>***Benediction and Farewell***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the God<small><small>[^41]</small></small> of all grace, who<small><small>[^42]</small></small> hath called us unto his eternal<small><small>[^43]</small></small> glory by Christ Jesus, after<small><small>[^44]</small></small> that ye have suffered a while, make<small><small>[^45]</small></small> you perfect, stablish<small><small>[^46]</small></small>, strengthen<small><small>[^47]</small></small>, settle<small><small>[^48]</small></small> \[you\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To him \[be\] glory and dominion for ever and ever. Amen.<small><small>[^49]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By Silvanus<small><small>[^50]</small></small>, a faithful<small><small>[^51]</small></small> brother unto you, as I suppose, I have<small><small>[^52]</small></small> written briefly, exhorting<small><small>[^53]</small></small>, and testifying<small><small>[^54]</small></small> that this is the true<small><small>[^55]</small></small> grace of God wherein<small><small>[^56]</small></small> ye stand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The \[church that<small><small>[^57]</small></small> is\] at Babylon, elected<small><small>[^58]</small></small> together with \[you\], saluteth you; and \[so doth\] Marcus<small><small>[^59]</small></small> my son. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Pet.5.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Greet ye one another with a<small><small>[^60]</small></small> kiss of charity. Peace<small><small>[^61]</small></small> \[be\] with you all that are in<small><small>[^62]</small></small> Christ Jesus.
