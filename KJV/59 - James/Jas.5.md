***A Warning to the Rich***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Prov.23|Proverbs 23:1–5]] <big>†</big> [[1Tim.6|1 Timothy 6:17–19]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2973

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Go<small><small>[^2]</small></small> to now, \[ye<small><small>[^3]</small></small>\] rich men, weep<small><small>[^4]</small></small> and howl for your miseries that shall come upon \[you\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Your riches<small><small>[^5]</small></small> are corrupted, and your garments<small><small>[^6]</small></small> are motheaten. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Your gold and silver is cankered<small><small>[^7]</small></small>; and the rust of them shall be a witness<small><small>[^8]</small></small> against you, and shall<small><small>[^9]</small></small> eat your flesh as it were fire. Ye have<small><small>[^10]</small></small> heaped treasure together for the last<small><small>[^11]</small></small> days. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, the hire<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of the labourers who have reaped down your fields, which is of you kept back by fraud, crieth: and the cries<small><small>[^13]</small></small> of them which have reaped are entered into the ears of the Lord<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of sabaoth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye have lived<small><small>[^15]</small></small> in pleasure on the earth, and been<small><small>[^16]</small></small> wanton; ye have nourished your hearts, as in<small><small>[^17]</small></small> a day of slaughter. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye have<small><small>[^18]</small></small> condemned \[and\] killed the just; \[and\] he<small><small>[^19]</small></small> doth not resist you.

***Patience in Suffering***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Job.1|Job 1:1–5]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb2974

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be patient<small><small>[^20]</small></small> therefore, brethren, unto<small><small>[^21]</small></small> the coming of the Lord. Behold, the husbandman waiteth for the precious fruit of the earth, and hath long patience for it, until<small><small>[^22]</small></small> he receive the early and latter rain. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be ye also<small><small>[^23]</small></small> patient; stablish<small><small>[^24]</small></small> your hearts: for<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the coming of the Lord draweth nigh. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Grudge not<small><small>[^26]</small></small> one against another, brethren, lest<small><small>[^27]</small></small> ye be condemned: behold, the judge standeth before the door.<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Take, my brethren, the prophets, who<small><small>[^29]</small></small> have spoken in the name of the Lord, for<small><small>[^30]</small></small> an example of suffering affliction, and of patience. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, we count<small><small>[^31]</small></small> them happy which endure. Ye<small><small>[^32]</small></small> have heard of the patience of Job, and have<small><small>[^33]</small></small> seen the end of the Lord; that the Lord is<small><small>[^34]</small></small> very pitiful, and of tender mercy. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But above<small><small>[^35]</small></small> all things, my brethren, swear not<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, neither by heaven, neither by the earth, neither by any other oath: but<small><small>[^37]</small></small> let your yea be yea; and \[your\] nay, nay; lest<small><small>[^38]</small></small> ye fall into condemnation.

***The Prayer of Faith*** ^bsb2975

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Is any among<small><small>[^39]</small></small> you afflicted? let him pray. Is any merry<small><small>[^40]</small></small>? let him sing<small><small>[^41]</small></small> psalms. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Is any sick among you? let him call for<small><small>[^42]</small></small> the elders of the church; and let them pray<small><small>[^43]</small></small> over him, anointing<small><small>[^44]</small></small> him with oil in the name of the Lord: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the prayer<small><small>[^45]</small></small> of faith shall save the sick, and the Lord shall raise him up; and if he<small><small>[^46]</small></small> have committed sins, they shall be forgiven him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Confess<small><small>[^47]</small></small> \[your\] faults one to another, and pray<small><small>[^48]</small></small> one for another, that<small><small>[^49]</small></small> ye may be healed. The effectual<small><small>[^50]</small></small> fervent prayer of a righteous<small><small>[^51]</small></small> man availeth much. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Elias<small><small>[^52]</small></small> was a man subject<small><small>[^53]</small></small> to like passions as we are, and he<small><small>[^54]</small></small> prayed earnestly<small><small>[^55]</small></small> that it might not rain: and it rained not<small><small>[^56]</small></small> on the earth by the space of three years and six months. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jas.5.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he prayed again, and the heaven gave rain, and the earth brought forth her fruit.<small><small>[^57]</small></small>
