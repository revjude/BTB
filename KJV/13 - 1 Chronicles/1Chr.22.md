***Preparations for the Temple*** ^bsb1040

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then David said, This \[is\] the house<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God, and this \[is\] the altar<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of the burnt offering for Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And David commanded to gather together the strangers<small><small>[^4]</small></small> that \[were\] in the land of Israel; and he set masons<small><small>[^5]</small></small> to hew wrought stones to build the house of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And David prepared iron<small><small>[^6]</small></small> in abundance for the nails for the doors of the gates, and for the joinings; and brass in abundance without weight<small><small>[^7]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Also cedar trees<small><small>[^8]</small></small> in abundance: for the Zidonians and they of Tyre brought much cedar wood to David. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And David said, Solomon<small><small>[^9]</small></small> my son \[is\] young and tender, and the house \[that is\] to be builded for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[must be\] exceeding<small><small>[^10]</small></small> magnifical, of fame and of glory throughout all countries: I will \[therefore\] now make preparation for it. So David prepared<small><small>[^11]</small></small> abundantly before his death.

***Solomon Anointed to Build the Temple*** ^bsb1041

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then he called for Solomon his son, and charged him<small><small>[^12]</small></small> to build an house for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And David said to Solomon, My son, as for me, it was in<small><small>[^13]</small></small> my mind to build an house unto the name<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> my God: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came to me, saying, Thou hast shed<small><small>[^15]</small></small> blood abundantly, and hast made great wars: thou shalt not<small><small>[^16]</small></small> build an house unto my name, because thou hast shed much blood upon the earth in my sight. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, a son<small><small>[^17]</small></small> shall be born to thee, who shall be a man of rest; and I will give<small><small>[^18]</small></small> him rest from all his enemies round about: for his name shall be Solomon<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, and I will give peace<small><small>[^20]</small></small> and quietness unto Israel in his days. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He shall build<small><small>[^21]</small></small> an house for my name; and prosper thou, and build the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> thy God, as he hath said of thee.<small><small>[^24]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Only the<small><small>[^25]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> give thee wisdom and understanding, and give thee charge concerning Israel, that thou mayest<small><small>[^26]</small></small> keep the law of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> thy God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then shalt<small><small>[^27]</small></small> thou prosper, if thou takest heed to fulfil<small><small>[^28]</small></small> the statutes and judgments which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> charged Moses with concerning Israel: be strong<small><small>[^29]</small></small>, and of good courage; dread not, nor be dismayed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now, behold, in my trouble<small><small>[^30]</small></small> I have prepared for the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> an hundred thousand<small><small>[^31]</small></small> talents of gold, and a thousand thousand talents of silver<small><small>[^32]</small></small>; and of brass and iron without weight<small><small>[^33]</small></small>; for it is in abundance: timber also and stone have I prepared; and thou mayest add thereto. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover \[there are\] workmen with thee in abundance, hewers and workers of stone and timber<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, and all manner<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of cunning men for every manner of work. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.22.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Of the gold<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, the silver, and the brass, and the iron, \[there is\] no number. Arise<small><small>[^37]</small></small> \[therefore\], and be doing, and the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> be with thee.<small><small>[^38]</small></small>
