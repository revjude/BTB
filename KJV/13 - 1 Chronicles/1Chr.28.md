<big><big>[[1Chr.28.1 David Commissions Solomon|⥃]]</big></big>***David Commissions Solomon***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And David assembled<small><small>[^2]</small></small> all the princes<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of Israel, the princes of the tribes, and the captains of the companies<small><small>[^4]</small></small> that ministered to the king by course, and the captains over the thousands, and captains over the hundreds, and the stewards<small><small>[^5]</small></small> over all the substance<small><small>[^6]</small></small> and possession of the king, and of his sons<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, with the officers, and with the mighty men<small><small>[^8]</small></small>, and with all the valiant men, unto Jerusalem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then David the king stood up<small><small>[^9]</small></small> upon his feet, and said, Hear me, my brethren<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, and my people: \[As for me\], I \[had\] in mine heart<small><small>[^11]</small></small> to build an house of rest<small><small>[^12]</small></small> for the ark of the covenant of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and for the footstool<small><small>[^13]</small></small> of our God, and had made ready<small><small>[^14]</small></small> for the building: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But God said unto me, Thou shalt<small><small>[^15]</small></small> not build an house for my name, because thou \[hast been\] a man of war, and hast shed blood<small><small>[^16]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Howbeit the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel chose me<small><small>[^17]</small></small> before all the house of my father to be king over Israel for ever: for he hath chosen Judah<small><small>[^18]</small></small> \[to be\] the ruler; and of the house of Judah<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, the house of my father<small><small>[^20]</small></small>; and among the sons<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of my father he liked me to make \[me\] king over all Israel: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And of all my sons<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, (for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath given me many sons,) he hath chosen<small><small>[^23]</small></small> Solomon my son to sit<small><small>[^24]</small></small> upon the throne of the kingdom of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> over Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto me, Solomon thy son, he shall<small><small>[^25]</small></small> build my house and my courts: for I have<small><small>[^26]</small></small> chosen him \[to be\] my son, and I will be his father. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover<small><small>[^27]</small></small> I will establish his kingdom for ever, if<small><small>[^28]</small></small> he be constant<small><small>[^29]</small></small> to do my commandments and my judgments, as at this day<small><small>[^30]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now therefore in the sight<small><small>[^31]</small></small> of all Israel the congregation of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and in the audience<small><small>[^32]</small></small> of our God, keep<small><small>[^33]</small></small> and seek for all the commandments of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God: that ye may<small><small>[^34]</small></small> possess this good land, and leave \[it<small><small>[^35]</small></small>\] for an inheritance for your children after you for ever. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And thou, Solomon my son, know thou<small><small>[^36]</small></small> the God<small><small>[^37]</small></small> of thy father, and serve him<small><small>[^38]</small></small> with a perfect heart and with a willing mind<small><small>[^39]</small></small>: for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^40]</small></small></span> searcheth all hearts, and understandeth all the imaginations<small><small>[^41]</small></small> of the thoughts: if thou seek<small><small>[^42]</small></small> him, he will be found of thee; but if thou forsake<small><small>[^43]</small></small> him, he will cast thee off for ever. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Take heed now<small><small>[^44]</small></small>; for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath chosen thee to build an house for the sanctuary: be strong, and do \[it\].

\[silver also\] for all instruments of silver by weight, for all instruments of every kind of service: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Even the weight for the candlesticks<small><small>[^60]</small></small> of gold, and for their lamps of gold, by weight for every candlestick, and for the lamps thereof: and for the candlesticks of silver by weight, \[both\] for the candlestick, and \[also\] for the lamps thereof, according to the use of every candlestick. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And by weight \[he gave\] gold for the tables of shewbread<small><small>[^61]</small></small>, for every table; and \[likewise\] silver for the tables of silver: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Also pure gold<small><small>[^62]</small></small> for the fleshhooks, and the bowls<small><small>[^63]</small></small>, and the cups: and for the golden basons \[he gave gold\] by weight for every bason; and \[likewise silver\] by weight for every bason of silver: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.28.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And for the altar<small><small>[^64]</small></small> of incense refined gold by weight;
