***God’s Covenant with David***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Sam.7|2 Samuel 7:1–17]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1028

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now it came to pass, as David<small><small>[^2]</small></small> sat in his house, that David said to Nathan<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the prophet, Lo, I dwell<small><small>[^4]</small></small> in an house of cedars, but the ark<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of the covenant of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[remaineth\] under curtains<small><small>[^6]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Nathan said unto David, Do all<small><small>[^7]</small></small> that \[is\] in thine heart; for God<small><small>[^8]</small></small> \[is\] with thee. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And it came to pass the same night, that the word<small><small>[^9]</small></small> of God came to Nathan, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Go and tell<small><small>[^10]</small></small> David my servant, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, Thou shalt not<small><small>[^11]</small></small> build me an house to dwell in: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I have not dwelt<small><small>[^12]</small></small> in an house since the day that I brought up Israel unto this day; but have gone<small><small>[^13]</small></small> from tent to tent<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, and from \[one\] tabernacle \[to another\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wheresoever I have walked<small><small>[^15]</small></small> with all Israel, spake I a word to any of the judges<small><small>[^16]</small></small> of Israel, whom I commanded to feed<small><small>[^17]</small></small> my people, saying, Why have ye not built me an house of cedars? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now therefore thus shalt thou say unto my servant David, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, I took thee<small><small>[^18]</small></small> from the sheepcote, \[even\] from following<small><small>[^19]</small></small> the sheep, that thou shouldest be ruler<small><small>[^20]</small></small> over my people Israel: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I have been<small><small>[^21]</small></small> with thee whithersoever thou hast walked, and have cut off<small><small>[^22]</small></small> all thine enemies from before thee, and have made thee<small><small>[^23]</small></small> a name like the name of the great men that \[are\] in the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Also I will<small><small>[^24]</small></small> ordain a place for my people Israel, and will plant<small><small>[^25]</small></small> them, and they shall dwell in their place, and shall be<small><small>[^26]</small></small> moved no more; neither shall the children<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of wickedness waste<small><small>[^28]</small></small> them any more, as at the<small><small>[^29]</small></small> beginning, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And since<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the time that I commanded judges \[to be\] over my people Israel. Moreover<small><small>[^31]</small></small> I will subdue all thine enemies. Furthermore I tell thee that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^32]</small></small></span> will build thee an house. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And it shall come to pass, when thy<small><small>[^33]</small></small> days be expired that thou must go \[to be<small><small>[^34]</small></small>\] with thy fathers, that I will raise<small><small>[^35]</small></small> up thy seed after thee, which shall be of thy sons; and I will establish his kingdom. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He shall<small><small>[^36]</small></small> build me an house, and I will<small><small>[^37]</small></small> stablish his throne for ever. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will be<small><small>[^38]</small></small> his father, and he shall be my son<small><small>[^39]</small></small>: and I will not<small><small>[^40]</small></small> take my mercy away from him, as I took<small><small>[^41]</small></small> \[it\] from \[him\] that was before thee: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will settle him in mine<small><small>[^42]</small></small> house and in my kingdom for ever: and his throne shall be established for evermore. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.17.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> According<small><small>[^43]</small></small> to all these words, and according to all this vision, so did Nathan speak unto David.
