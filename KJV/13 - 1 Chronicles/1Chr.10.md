 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.10.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now the Philistines fought<small><small>[^2]</small></small> against Israel; and the men of Israel fled from before the Philistines, and fell down slain<small><small>[^3]</small></small> in mount<small><small>[^4]</small></small> Gilboa.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.10.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Philistines followed hard after Saul, and after his sons; and the Philistines slew Jonathan<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, and Abinadab<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, and Malchi–shua, the sons<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of Saul. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.10.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the battle went<small><small>[^9]</small></small> sore against Saul, and the archers<small><small>[^10]</small></small> hit<small><small>[^11]</small></small> him, and he was<small><small>[^12]</small></small> wounded of the archers. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.10.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said Saul to his armourbearer, Draw<small><small>[^13]</small></small> thy sword, and thrust me through therewith; lest these uncircumcised<small><small>[^14]</small></small> come and abuse<small><small>[^15]</small></small> me. <big><big>[[1Chr.10.7 The Philistines Possess the Towns|⥃]]</big></big>***The Philistines Possess the Towns***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[1Sam.31|1 Samuel 31:7–10]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.10.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when all the men of Israel that \[were\] in the valley saw that they fled, and that Saul and his sons were dead, then they<small><small>[^20]</small></small> forsook their cities, and fled: and the Philistines came and dwelt in them. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.10.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass on the morrow, when the Philistines came to strip<small><small>[^21]</small></small> the slain, that they found Saul and his sons fallen in mount Gilboa. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.10.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when they had stripped him, they took<small><small>[^22]</small></small> his head, and his armour, and sent into the land of the Philistines round about, to carry tidings<small><small>[^23]</small></small> unto their idols, and to the people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.10.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they put his armour in the house of their gods<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, and fastened his head in the temple<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of Dagon.
