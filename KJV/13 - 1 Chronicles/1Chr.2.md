$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]The sons of Judah; Er, and<small><small>[^11]</small></small> Onan, and Shelah: \[which\] three were born unto him of the daughter of Shua the Canaanitess. And the sons of Shammai; Nadab, and Abishur. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.2.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the name of the wife of Abishur \[was\] Abihail, and she bare him Ahban, and Molid. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.2.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sons of Nadab<small><small>[^57]</small></small>; Seled, and Appaim: but Seled died without children. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.2.31|31]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sons of Appaim; Ishi. $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Chr.2.34|34]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Now Sheshan had no sons<small><small>[^62]</small></small>, but daughters<small><small>[^63]</small></small>.
