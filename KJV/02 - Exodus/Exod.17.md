<big><big>[[Exod.17.1 Water from the Rock|⥃]]</big></big>***Water from the Rock***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Num.20|Numbers 20:1–13]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all the congregation of the children of Israel journeyed from the wilderness of Sin<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, after their journeys, according to the commandment of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and pitched in Rephidim<small><small>[^3]</small></small>: and \[there was\] no water for the people to drink. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore the people<small><small>[^4]</small></small> did chide with Moses, and said, Give us<small><small>[^5]</small></small> water that we may drink. And Moses said unto them, Why chide ye with me? wherefore<small><small>[^6]</small></small> do ye tempt the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the people thirsted there for water; and the people murmured against Moses, and said, Wherefore \[is\] this \[that\] thou hast<small><small>[^7]</small></small> brought us up out of Egypt, to kill us and our children and our cattle with thirst? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses cried<small><small>[^8]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, saying, What shall I do unto this people? they be almost<small><small>[^9]</small></small> ready to stone me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Moses, Go on<small><small>[^10]</small></small> before the people, and take with thee of the elders of Israel; and thy rod<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, wherewith thou smotest the river, take in thine hand, and go. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, I will<small><small>[^12]</small></small> stand before thee there upon the rock<small><small>[^13]</small></small> in Horeb<small><small>[^14]</small></small>; and thou<small><small>[^15]</small></small> shalt smite the rock, and there shall come water out of it, that the people<small><small>[^16]</small></small> may drink. And Moses did so in the sight of the elders of Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he called the name of the place Massah<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, and Meribah<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, because of the chiding<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of the children of Israel, and because they tempted<small><small>[^20]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, saying, Is the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^21]</small></small></span> among us, or not?

<big><big>[[Exod.17.8 The Defeat of the Amalekites|⥃]]</big></big>***The Defeat of the Amalekites***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then came Amalek, and fought with Israel in Rephidim.<small><small>[^22]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses said unto Joshua<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, Choose<small><small>[^24]</small></small> us out men, and go out, fight with Amalek: to morrow I will stand on the top of the hill with the rod<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of God in mine hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Joshua<small><small>[^26]</small></small> did as Moses had said to him, and fought with Amalek: and Moses<small><small>[^27]</small></small>, Aaron, and Hur<small><small>[^28]</small></small> went up to the top of the hill. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when Moses held up his hand, that Israel prevailed: and when he let down his hand, Amalek prevailed.<small><small>[^29]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Moses’ hands<small><small>[^30]</small></small> \[were\] heavy; and they took a stone, and put \[it\] under him, and he sat thereon; and Aaron and Hur stayed up his hands<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, the one on the one side, and the other on the other side; and his hands were steady until the going down of the sun. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Joshua discomfited Amalek and his people with the edge of the sword.<small><small>[^32]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Moses, Write this \[for\] a memorial<small><small>[^33]</small></small> in a book, and rehearse \[it\] in the ears of Joshua: for I will<small><small>[^34]</small></small> utterly put out the remembrance<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of Amalek from under heaven. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses built an altar, and called the name of it JEHOVAH–nissi<small><small>[^36]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.17.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he said, Because<small><small>[^37]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath sworn \[that\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[will have\] war<small><small>[^38]</small></small> with Amalek from generation to generation. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Exod.16|<< Exodus 16]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Exod.18|Exodus 18 >>]]</td></tr></table>
