<big><big>[[Exod.7.1 God Commands Moses and Aaron|⥃]]</big></big>***God Commands Moses and Aaron***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Moses, See<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, I have made thee a god<small><small>[^3]</small></small> to Pharaoh: and Aaron thy brother shall be thy prophet. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt speak all that I command thee: and Aaron thy brother shall speak unto Pharaoh, that he send the children of Israel out of his land.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I<small><small>[^5]</small></small> will harden Pharaoh’s heart, and multiply<small><small>[^6]</small></small> my signs and my wonders in the land of Egypt. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Pharaoh shall not hearken unto you, that I<small><small>[^7]</small></small> may lay my hand upon Egypt, and bring forth mine armies<small><small>[^8]</small></small>, \[and\] my people the children of Israel, out of the land of Egypt by great<small><small>[^9]</small></small> judgments. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Egyptians<small><small>[^10]</small></small> shall know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, when I stretch<small><small>[^11]</small></small> forth mine hand upon Egypt, and bring out the children of Israel from among them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses and Aaron did as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> commanded them, so did they.<small><small>[^12]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses \[was\] fourscore years old, and Aaron fourscore and three years old, when they spake unto Pharaoh.<small><small>[^13]</small></small>

<big><big>[[Exod.7.8 Aaron’s Staff|⥃]]</big></big>***Aaron’s Staff***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses and unto Aaron, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When Pharaoh shall speak unto you, saying, Shew<small><small>[^14]</small></small> a miracle for you: then thou shalt say unto Aaron, Take<small><small>[^15]</small></small> thy rod, and cast \[it\] before Pharaoh, \[and\] it shall become a serpent<small><small>[^16]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Moses and Aaron went in unto Pharaoh, and they did so as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^17]</small></small></span> had commanded: and Aaron cast down his rod before Pharaoh, and before his servants, and it became<small><small>[^18]</small></small> a serpent. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Pharaoh also called the wise men<small><small>[^19]</small></small> and the sorcerers<small><small>[^20]</small></small>: now the magicians of Egypt, they also<small><small>[^21]</small></small> did in like manner with their enchantments<small><small>[^22]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they cast down every man his rod, and they became serpents: but Aaron’s<small><small>[^23]</small></small> rod swallowed up their rods. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he hardened Pharaoh’s heart, that he hearkened not unto them; as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> had said.<small><small>[^24]</small></small>

<big><big>[[Exod.7.14 The First Plague：Blood|⥃]]</big></big>***The First Plague: Blood***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Moses, Pharaoh’s<small><small>[^25]</small></small> heart \[is\] hardened, he refuseth<small><small>[^26]</small></small> to let the people go. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Get thee unto Pharaoh in the morning; lo, he goeth<small><small>[^27]</small></small> out unto the water; and thou shalt stand by the river’s brink against he come; and the rod<small><small>[^28]</small></small> which was turned to a serpent shalt thou take in thine hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thou shalt say unto him, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^29]</small></small></span> God of the Hebrews hath sent me unto thee, saying, Let my<small><small>[^30]</small></small> people go, that they may serve<small><small>[^31]</small></small> me in the wilderness: and, behold, hitherto thou wouldest not hear. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, In this thou shalt<small><small>[^32]</small></small> know that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: behold, I will smite with the rod that \[is\] in mine hand upon the waters which \[are\] in the river, and they<small><small>[^33]</small></small> shall be turned to blood. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the fish<small><small>[^34]</small></small> that \[is\] in the river shall die, and the river shall stink; and the Egyptians<small><small>[^35]</small></small> shall lothe<small><small>[^36]</small></small> to drink of the water of the river. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, Say unto Aaron, Take thy rod, and stretch<small><small>[^37]</small></small> out thine hand upon the waters of Egypt, upon their streams, upon their rivers, and upon their ponds, and upon all their pools<small><small>[^38]</small></small> of water, that they may become blood; and \[that\] there may be blood throughout all the land of Egypt, both in \[vessels of\] wood, and in \[vessels of\] stone. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses and Aaron did so, as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> commanded; and he lifted<small><small>[^39]</small></small> up the rod, and smote the waters that \[were\] in the river, in the sight of Pharaoh, and in the sight of his servants; and all the waters<small><small>[^40]</small></small> that \[were\] in the river were turned to blood. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the fish that \[was\] in the river died; and the river stank, and the Egyptians could not drink of the water of the river; and there was blood throughout all the land of Egypt.<small><small>[^41]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the magicians<small><small>[^42]</small></small> of Egypt did so with their enchantments: and Pharaoh’s<small><small>[^43]</small></small> heart was hardened, neither did he hearken unto them; as the<small><small>[^44]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> had said. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Pharaoh turned and went into his house, neither<small><small>[^45]</small></small> did he set his heart to this also. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all the Egyptians digged round about the river for water to drink; for they<small><small>[^46]</small></small> could not drink of the water of the river. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.7.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And seven days were fulfilled, after that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> had smitten the river. <small><small>[^47]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Exod.6|<< Exodus 6]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Exod.8|Exodus 8 >>]]</td></tr></table>
