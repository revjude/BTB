<big><big>[[Exod.13.1 The Dedication of the Firstborn|⥃]]</big></big>***The Dedication of the Firstborn***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Deut.15|Deuteronomy 15:19–23]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Sanctify<small><small>[^2]</small></small> unto me all the firstborn, whatsoever openeth the womb among the children of Israel, \[both\] of man and of beast: it \[is\] mine. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Moses said unto the people, Remember<small><small>[^3]</small></small> this day, in which ye came out from Egypt, out of the<small><small>[^4]</small></small> house of bondage<small><small>[^5]</small></small>; for by strength<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of hand the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> brought you out from this \[place\]: there<small><small>[^7]</small></small> shall no leavened bread be eaten. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This day came ye out in the month Abib<small><small>[^8]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And it shall be when the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall bring<small><small>[^9]</small></small> thee into the land of the Canaanites, and the Hittites, and the Amorites, and the Hivites, and the Jebusites, which he sware<small><small>[^10]</small></small> unto thy fathers to give thee, a land<small><small>[^11]</small></small> flowing with milk and honey, that thou shalt keep<small><small>[^12]</small></small> this service in this month. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Seven days thou shalt eat unleavened bread, and in the seventh day \[shall be\] a feast to the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^13]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Unleavened bread shall be eaten seven days; and there shall no leavened bread be seen with thee, neither shall there be leaven seen with thee in all thy quarters.<small><small>[^14]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And thou shalt shew thy son in that day, saying, \[This is done\] because of that \[which\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> did unto me when I came forth out of Egypt.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall be for a sign<small><small>[^16]</small></small> unto thee upon thine hand, and for a memorial between thine eyes, that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span> law may be<small><small>[^17]</small></small> in thy mouth: for with a strong hand<small><small>[^18]</small></small> hath the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> brought thee out of Egypt. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt therefore keep this ordinance in his season from year to year.<small><small>[^19]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall be when the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall bring thee into the land of the Canaanites, as he sware<small><small>[^20]</small></small> unto thee and to thy fathers, and shall give it thee, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That thou shalt<small><small>[^21]</small></small> set apart<small><small>[^22]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> all that openeth<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the matrix, and every firstling that cometh of a beast which thou hast; the males \[shall be\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And every firstling of an ass<small><small>[^24]</small></small> thou shalt redeem with a lamb<small><small>[^25]</small></small>; and if thou wilt not redeem it, then thou shalt break his neck: and all the firstborn of man among thy children shalt thou<small><small>[^26]</small></small> redeem. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And it shall be when thy son<small><small>[^27]</small></small> asketh thee in time to come<small><small>[^28]</small></small>, saying, What \[is\] this? that thou shalt say unto him, By strength<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of hand the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> brought us out from Egypt, from the house of bondage: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when Pharaoh would hardly let us go, that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> slew<small><small>[^30]</small></small> all the firstborn in the land of Egypt, both the firstborn of man, and the firstborn of beast: therefore I<small><small>[^31]</small></small> sacrifice to the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> all that openeth the matrix, being males; but all the firstborn of my children I redeem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall be for a token<small><small>[^32]</small></small> upon thine hand, and for frontlets<small><small>[^33]</small></small> between thine eyes: for by<small><small>[^34]</small></small> strength of hand the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> brought us forth out of Egypt.

<big><big>[[Exod.13.17 The Pillars of Cloud and Fire|⥃]]</big></big>***The Pillars of Cloud and Fire***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when Pharaoh had let the people go, that God led them not \[through\] the way of the land of the Philistines, although that \[was\] near; for God said, Lest peradventure the people repent<small><small>[^35]</small></small> when they see war, and they return<small><small>[^36]</small></small> to Egypt: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But God led the<small><small>[^37]</small></small> people about, \[through\] the way of the wilderness of the Red sea: and the children of Israel went up harnessed<small><small>[^38]</small></small> out of the land of Egypt. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses took the bones of Joseph with him: for he had<small><small>[^39]</small></small> straitly sworn the children of Israel, saying, God<small><small>[^40]</small></small> will surely visit you; and ye shall carry up my bones away hence with you. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And they took their journey from Succoth, and encamped in Etham, in the edge of the wilderness.<small><small>[^41]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> went before them by day in a pillar of a cloud, to lead them the way; and by night in a pillar of fire, to give them light; to go by day and night:<small><small>[^42]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.13.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He took<small><small>[^43]</small></small> not away the pillar of the cloud by day, nor the pillar of fire<small><small>[^44]</small></small> by night, \[from\] before the people. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Exod.12|<< Exodus 12]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Exod.14|Exodus 14 >>]]</td></tr></table>
