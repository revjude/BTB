<big><big>[[Exod.24.1 The Covenant Sealed|⥃]]</big></big>***The Covenant Sealed***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto Moses, Come up<small><small>[^2]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, thou, and Aaron, Nadab<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, and Abihu, and seventy<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of the elders of Israel; and worship ye afar off. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses alone shall come near the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: but they shall not come nigh; neither shall the people go up with him.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Moses came and told the people all the words of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and all the judgments<small><small>[^6]</small></small>: and all the people answered with one voice, and said, All the words<small><small>[^7]</small></small> which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath said will we do. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses wrote<small><small>[^8]</small></small> all the words of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and rose up early in the morning, and builded<small><small>[^9]</small></small> an altar under the hill, and twelve pillars<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, according<small><small>[^11]</small></small> to the twelve tribes of Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he sent young men<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of the children of Israel, which offered burnt offerings<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, and sacrificed peace offerings<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of oxen unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses took half of the blood, and put \[it\] in basons; and half of the blood he<small><small>[^15]</small></small> sprinkled on the altar<small><small>[^16]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he took the book<small><small>[^17]</small></small> of the covenant, and read<small><small>[^18]</small></small> in the audience of the people: and they said, All that<small><small>[^19]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath said will we do, and be obedient. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses took the blood, and sprinkled<small><small>[^20]</small></small> \[it\] on the people, and said, Behold<small><small>[^21]</small></small> the blood of the covenant, which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath made with you concerning all these words. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then went up Moses, and Aaron, Nadab, and Abihu, and seventy of the elders of Israel:<small><small>[^22]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they saw<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the God of Israel: and \[there was\] under his feet as it were a paved work of a sapphire stone<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, and as it were the body of heaven in \[his\] clearness<small><small>[^25]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And upon the nobles<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of the children of Israel he laid not<small><small>[^27]</small></small> his hand: also they saw<small><small>[^28]</small></small> God, and did eat<small><small>[^29]</small></small> and drink.

<big><big>[[Exod.24.12 Moses on the Mountain|⥃]]</big></big>***Moses on the Mountain***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Moses, Come up<small><small>[^30]</small></small> to me into the mount, and be there: and I will give thee tables<small><small>[^31]</small></small> of stone, and a law, and commandments which I have written; that thou<small><small>[^32]</small></small> mayest teach them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses rose up, and his minister<small><small>[^33]</small></small> Joshua: and Moses went up<small><small>[^34]</small></small> into the mount of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto the elders, Tarry ye<small><small>[^35]</small></small> here for us, until we come again unto you: and, behold, Aaron and Hur<small><small>[^36]</small></small> \[are\] with you: if any man<small><small>[^37]</small></small> have any matters to do, let him come unto them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses went up into the mount, and a cloud<small><small>[^38]</small></small> covered the mount. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the glory<small><small>[^39]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> abode upon mount Sinai, and the cloud covered it six days: and the seventh day<small><small>[^40]</small></small> he called unto Moses out of the midst of the cloud. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sight of the glory of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[was\] like devouring fire<small><small>[^41]</small></small> on the top of the mount in the eyes of the children of Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.24.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses went into<small><small>[^42]</small></small> the midst of the cloud, and gat him up into the mount: and Moses was in the mount forty days<small><small>[^43]</small></small> and forty nights.
