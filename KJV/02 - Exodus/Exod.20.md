<big><big>[[Exod.20.1 The Ten Commandments|⥃]]</big></big>***The Ten Commandments***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Deut.5|Deuteronomy 5:6–21]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God spake all these words, saying,<small><small>[^2]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^3]</small></small></span> thy God, which have brought<small><small>[^4]</small></small> thee out of the<small><small>[^5]</small></small> land of Egypt, out of the house of bondage<small><small>[^6]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt have no other gods before me.<small><small>[^7]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt not make unto thee any graven image, or any likeness \[of any thing\] that \[is\] in heaven above, or that \[is\] in the earth beneath, or that \[is\] in the water under the earth:<small><small>[^8]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt not bow down<small><small>[^9]</small></small> thyself to them, nor serve them: for I<small><small>[^10]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> thy God \[am\] a jealous God, visiting<small><small>[^11]</small></small> the iniquity of the fathers upon the children unto the third and fourth \[generation\] of them<small><small>[^12]</small></small> that hate me; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And shewing<small><small>[^13]</small></small> mercy unto thousands of them that love me<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, and keep my commandments. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt not take<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> thy God in vain; for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will not hold him guiltless<small><small>[^16]</small></small> that taketh his name in vain. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Remember the sabbath day, to keep it holy.<small><small>[^17]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Six days shalt thou labour, and do all thy work:<small><small>[^18]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the seventh<small><small>[^19]</small></small> day \[is\] the sabbath of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> thy God: \[in it\] thou shalt<small><small>[^20]</small></small> not do any work, thou, nor thy son, nor thy daughter, thy manservant<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, nor thy maidservant, nor thy cattle, nor thy stranger<small><small>[^22]</small></small> that \[is\] within thy gates: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For \[in\] six days the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that in them \[is\], and rested the seventh day: wherefore the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> blessed the sabbath day, and hallowed it.<small><small>[^23]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Honour<small><small>[^24]</small></small> thy father and thy mother: that thy<small><small>[^25]</small></small> days may be long upon the land which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> thy God giveth thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt not kill.<small><small>[^26]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt not commit adultery.<small><small>[^27]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt not steal.<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt not bear false witness against thy neighbour.<small><small>[^29]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt not covet thy neighbour’s house<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, thou shalt not covet thy neighbour’s wife<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, nor his manservant, nor his maidservant, nor his ox, nor his ass, nor any thing that \[is\] thy neighbour’s<small><small>[^32]</small></small>.

<big><big>[[Exod.20.18 Moses Comforts the People|⥃]]</big></big>***Moses Comforts the People***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Deut.5|Deuteronomy 5:22–33]] <big>†</big> [[Heb.12|Hebrews 12:18–29]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all<small><small>[^33]</small></small> the people saw the thunderings, and the lightnings, and the noise of the trumpet, and the mountain smoking: and when the people saw \[it\], they removed<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, and stood afar off. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they said unto Moses, Speak thou<small><small>[^35]</small></small> with us, and we will hear: but let not<small><small>[^36]</small></small> God speak with us, lest we die. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses said unto the people, Fear not<small><small>[^37]</small></small>: for God is come to prove<small><small>[^38]</small></small> you, and that his fear<small><small>[^39]</small></small> may be before your faces, that ye sin not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Exod.20.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the people<small><small>[^40]</small></small> stood afar off, and Moses drew near unto the thick<small><small>[^41]</small></small> darkness where God \[was\].
