<big><big>[[2Chr.15.1 The Prophecy of Azariah|⥃]]</big></big>***The Prophecy of Azariah***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Spirit<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of God came upon Azariah the son of Oded: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he went out to meet Asa<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, and said unto him, Hear ye me<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, Asa, and all Judah and Benjamin; The <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^5]</small></small></span> \[is\] with you, while ye be with him; and if ye seek him<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, he will be found of you; but if ye forsake<small><small>[^7]</small></small> him, he will forsake you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now for a long<small><small>[^8]</small></small> season Israel \[hath been\] without the true God<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, and without a teaching<small><small>[^10]</small></small> priest, and without law<small><small>[^11]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But when they in their trouble<small><small>[^12]</small></small> did turn unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel, and sought him, he was found of them<small><small>[^13]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And in those times \[there was\] no peace<small><small>[^14]</small></small> to him that went out, nor to him that came in, but great vexations<small><small>[^15]</small></small> \[were\] upon all the inhabitants of the countries. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And nation<small><small>[^16]</small></small> was destroyed<small><small>[^17]</small></small> of nation, and city of city: for God<small><small>[^18]</small></small> did vex them with all adversity. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be ye strong<small><small>[^19]</small></small> therefore, and let not your hands be weak: for your work<small><small>[^20]</small></small> shall be rewarded.

<big><big>[[2Chr.15.8 Asa’s Reforms|⥃]]</big></big>***Asa’s Reforms***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[1Kgs.15|1 Kings 15:9–15]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when Asa heard these words, and the prophecy of Oded<small><small>[^21]</small></small> the prophet, he took courage<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, and put away the abominable idols<small><small>[^23]</small></small> out of all the land of Judah and Benjamin, and out of the cities<small><small>[^24]</small></small> which he had taken from mount Ephraim, and renewed the altar of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^25]</small></small></span>, that \[was\] before the porch of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he gathered all Judah and Benjamin, and the strangers<small><small>[^26]</small></small> with them out of Ephraim and Manasseh, and out of Simeon: for they fell<small><small>[^27]</small></small> to him out of Israel in abundance, when they saw<small><small>[^28]</small></small> that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> his God \[was\] with him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So they gathered themselves together at Jerusalem in the third month<small><small>[^29]</small></small>, in the fifteenth year of the reign of Asa. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they offered<small><small>[^30]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> the same time<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, of the spoil \[which\] they had brought, seven hundred<small><small>[^32]</small></small> oxen and seven thousand sheep. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they entered<small><small>[^33]</small></small> into a covenant to seek<small><small>[^34]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of their fathers with all their heart and with all their soul; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That whosoever<small><small>[^35]</small></small> would not seek the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel should be put to death, whether small<small><small>[^36]</small></small> or great, whether man or woman. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they sware<small><small>[^37]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> with a loud voice, and with shouting, and with trumpets<small><small>[^38]</small></small>, and with cornets. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all Judah rejoiced<small><small>[^39]</small></small> at the oath: for they had sworn<small><small>[^40]</small></small> with all their heart, and sought him<small><small>[^41]</small></small> with their whole desire; and he was<small><small>[^42]</small></small> found of them: and the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^43]</small></small></span> gave them rest round about. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And also \[concerning\] Maachah<small><small>[^44]</small></small> the mother<small><small>[^45]</small></small> of Asa the king, he removed<small><small>[^46]</small></small> her from \[being\] queen, because she had made an idol<small><small>[^47]</small></small> in a grove: and Asa cut down<small><small>[^48]</small></small> her idol, and stamped \[it\], and burnt \[it\] at the brook Kidron. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.15.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the high places<small><small>[^49]</small></small> were not taken away out of Israel: nevertheless the heart of Asa<small><small>[^50]</small></small> was perfect all his days.
