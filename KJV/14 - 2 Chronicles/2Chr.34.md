***Josiah Reigns in Judah***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Kgs.22|2 Kings 22:1–2]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1153

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.34.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Josiah<small><small>[^2]</small></small> \[was\] eight years<small><small>[^3]</small></small> old when he began to reign, and he reigned in Jerusalem one and thirty years.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.34.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he did \[that which was\] right in the sight<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and walked in the ways of David his father, and declined<small><small>[^6]</small></small> \[neither\] to the right hand, nor to the left.
