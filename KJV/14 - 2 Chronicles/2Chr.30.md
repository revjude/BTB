***Hezekiah Celebrates the Passover*** ^bsb1141

passover<small><small>[^55]</small></small> otherwise than it was written. But Hezekiah prayed<small><small>[^56]</small></small> for them, saying, The good<small><small>[^57]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> pardon every one <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.30.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[That\] prepareth<small><small>[^58]</small></small> his heart to seek God, the <span present<small><small>[^61]</small></small> at Jerusalem kept the feast<small><small>[^62]</small></small> of unleavened bread seven days with great gladness<small><small>[^63]</small></small>: and the Levites and the priests<small><small>[^64]</small></small> praised the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> day by day, \[singing\] with loud instruments<small><small>[^65]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.30.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Hezekiah spake comfortably unto all<small><small>[^66]</small></small> the Levites that taught<small><small>[^67]</small></small> the good<small><small>[^68]</small></small> knowledge of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and they did eat throughout the feast seven days, offering peace offerings, and making<small><small>[^69]</small></small> confession to the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of their fathers. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.30.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the whole assembly took counsel<small><small>[^70]</small></small> to keep<small><small>[^71]</small></small> other seven days: and they kept \[other\] seven days with gladness. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.30.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For Hezekiah king of Judah did give<small><small>[^72]</small></small> to the congregation a thousand bullocks and seven thousand sheep; and the princes gave to the class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.30.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And all the congregation of Judah, with the priests and the Levites, and all the congregation that came out class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.30.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So there was great joy in Jerusalem: for since the time<small><small>[^76]</small></small> of Solomon the son of David king of Israel \[there was\] not the like in Jerusalem.
