***Shemaiah’s Prophecy***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[1Kgs.12|1 Kings 12:20–24]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1087

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when Rehoboam<small><small>[^2]</small></small> was come to Jerusalem, he gathered of the house of Judah and Benjamin an hundred<small><small>[^3]</small></small> and fourscore thousand chosen \[men\], which were warriors, to fight against Israel, that he might bring the kingdom again to Rehoboam. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came to Shemaiah<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the man<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of God, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Speak unto Rehoboam the son of Solomon, king of Judah, and to all Israel<small><small>[^6]</small></small> in Judah and Benjamin, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, Ye shall not go up, nor fight against<small><small>[^7]</small></small> your brethren: return<small><small>[^8]</small></small> every man to his house: for this thing<small><small>[^9]</small></small> is done of me. And they obeyed<small><small>[^10]</small></small> the words of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and returned from going against Jeroboam.

***Rehoboam Fortifies Judah*** ^bsb1088

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Rehoboam dwelt in Jerusalem, and built<small><small>[^11]</small></small> cities for defence in Judah.<small><small>[^12]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He built even Beth–lehem<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, and Etam<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, and Tekoa<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Beth–zur<small><small>[^16]</small></small>, and Shoco<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, and Adullam<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Gath<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, and Mareshah<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and Ziph<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Adoraim, and Lachish<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, and Azekah, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Zorah<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, and Aijalon, and Hebron<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, which \[are\] in Judah and in Benjamin fenced cities. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he fortified<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the strong holds, and put captains<small><small>[^26]</small></small> in them, and store of victual, and of oil and wine. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And in every several city \[he put\] shields<small><small>[^27]</small></small> and spears, and made them exceeding strong, having Judah<small><small>[^28]</small></small> and Benjamin on his side.

***Jeroboam Forsakes the Priests and Levites*** ^bsb1089

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the priests and the Levites that \[were\] in all Israel resorted to him<small><small>[^29]</small></small> out of all their coasts.<small><small>[^30]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the Levites left their suburbs<small><small>[^31]</small></small> and their possession<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, and came to Judah and Jerusalem: for Jeroboam<small><small>[^33]</small></small> and his sons had cast them off from executing the priest’s office unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he ordained him priests for the high places, and for the devils<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, and for the calves<small><small>[^35]</small></small> which he had made. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And after<small><small>[^36]</small></small> them out of all the tribes of Israel such as set<small><small>[^37]</small></small> their hearts to seek the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel came to Jerusalem, to sacrifice<small><small>[^38]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of their fathers. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So they strengthened<small><small>[^39]</small></small> the kingdom of Judah, and made Rehoboam the son of Solomon strong, three years<small><small>[^40]</small></small>: for three years they walked in the way of David and Solomon.<small><small>[^41]</small></small>

***Rehoboam’s Family*** ^bsb1090

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Rehoboam took him Mahalath the daughter<small><small>[^42]</small></small> of Jerimoth the son of David to wife, \[and\] Abihail the daughter of Eliab<small><small>[^43]</small></small> the son of Jesse;<small><small>[^44]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which bare him children; Jeush, and Shamariah, and Zaham. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And after her he took Maachah<small><small>[^45]</small></small> the daughter of Absalom<small><small>[^46]</small></small>; which bare him Abijah<small><small>[^47]</small></small>, and Attai, and Ziza, and Shelomith. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Rehoboam loved Maachah the daughter of Absalom above all his wives and his concubines: (for he took eighteen wives<small><small>[^48]</small></small>, and threescore concubines; and begat twenty and eight sons, and threescore daughters.) <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Rehoboam made Abijah<small><small>[^49]</small></small> the son of Maachah the chief, \[to be\] ruler among his brethren: for \[he thought\] to make him king. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Chr.11.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he dealt<small><small>[^50]</small></small> wisely, and dispersed<small><small>[^51]</small></small> of all his children throughout all the countries of Judah and Benjamin, unto every fenced city<small><small>[^52]</small></small>: and he gave them victual in abundance.
