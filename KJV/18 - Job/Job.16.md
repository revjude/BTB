<big><big>[[Job.16.1 Job Decries His Comforters|⥃]]</big></big>***Job Decries His Comforters***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Job answered and said, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have heard<small><small>[^2]</small></small> many such things: miserable<small><small>[^3]</small></small> comforters \[are\] ye all. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Shall vain words<small><small>[^4]</small></small> have an end? or what you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[But\] I would<small><small>[^9]</small></small> strengthen you with my mouth, and the moving of my lips should asswage \[your grief\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Though I speak, my grief<small><small>[^10]</small></small> is not asswaged: and \[though\] I forbear, what am I eased<small><small>[^11]</small></small>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But now he hath<small><small>[^12]</small></small> made me weary: thou hast made<small><small>[^13]</small></small> desolate all my company. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.8|8]]**<span \[me<small><small>[^17]</small></small>\] in his wrath, who hateth me: he gnasheth<small><small>[^18]</small></small> upon me with his teeth; mine<small><small>[^19]</small></small> enemy sharpeneth his eyes upon me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They have gaped<small><small>[^20]</small></small> upon me with their mouth; they have smitten<small><small>[^21]</small></small> me upon the cheek reproachfully; they have gathered<small><small>[^22]</small></small> themselves together against me. <span and turned<small><small>[^25]</small></small> me over into the hands of the wicked. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I was at ease<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, but he hath broken me<small><small>[^27]</small></small> asunder: he hath also taken \[me\] by my neck<small><small>[^28]</small></small>, and shaken<small><small>[^29]</small></small> me to pieces, and set me up<small><small>[^30]</small></small> for his mark. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> His archers<small><small>[^31]</small></small> compass me round about, he cleaveth<small><small>[^32]</small></small> my reins asunder, and doth<small><small>[^33]</small></small> not spare; he poureth<small><small>[^34]</small></small> out my gall upon the ground. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He breaketh<small><small>[^35]</small></small> me with breach upon breach, he runneth<small><small>[^36]</small></small> upon me like a giant. <span horn<small><small>[^38]</small></small> in the dust. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My face<small><small>[^39]</small></small> is foul with weeping, and on my eyelids<small><small>[^40]</small></small> \[is\] the shadow of death; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Not for<small><small>[^41]</small></small> \[any\] injustice in mine hands: also my prayer<small><small>[^42]</small></small> \[is\] pure. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O earth<small><small>[^43]</small></small>, cover not<small><small>[^44]</small></small> thou my blood, and let my cry<small><small>[^45]</small></small> have no place. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Also now, behold, my witness<small><small>[^46]</small></small> \[is\] in heaven, and my record \[is\] on high<small><small>[^47]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My friends scorn me<small><small>[^48]</small></small>: \[but\] mine eye poureth<small><small>[^49]</small></small> out \[tears\] unto God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O that one might plead<small><small>[^50]</small></small> for a man with God, as a man \[pleadeth\] for his neighbour<small><small>[^51]</small></small>! <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.16.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When a few years<small><small>[^52]</small></small> are come, then I shall go the way
