<span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.11.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If iniquity<small><small>[^28]</small></small> \[be\] in thine hand, put it far<small><small>[^29]</small></small> away, and let not<small><small>[^30]</small></small> wickedness dwell in thy tabernacles. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.11.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For then shalt thou lift up<small><small>[^31]</small></small> thy face without spot;
