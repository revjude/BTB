<big><big>[[Job.30.1 Job’s Honor Turned to Contempt|⥃]]</big></big>***Job’s Honor Turned to Contempt***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.30.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But now \[they that are<small><small>[^2]</small></small>\] younger than I<small><small>[^3]</small></small> have me in derision, whose<small><small>[^4]</small></small> fathers I would have disdained to have set with the dogs of my flock. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.30.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yea, whereto \[might\] the strength of their hands \[profit\] me, in whom old age was perished? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.30.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For want and famine \[they were\] solitary<small><small>[^5]</small></small>; under the nettles<small><small>[^15]</small></small> they were gathered together. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.30.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[They were\] children<small><small>[^16]</small></small> of fools<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, yea, children of base men<small><small>[^18]</small></small>: they were viler<small><small>[^19]</small></small> than the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.30.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And now am I<small><small>[^20]</small></small> their song, yea, I am their byword. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.30.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They abhor me<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, they flee far<small><small>[^22]</small></small> from me, and spare not to spit in my face<small><small>[^23]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.30.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because he hath loosed<small><small>[^24]</small></small> my cord, and afflicted me, they have also let loose<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the bridle before me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.30.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Upon \[my\] right \[hand\] rise<small><small>[^26]</small></small> the youth; hand<small><small>[^42]</small></small> thou opposest thyself against me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.30.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou liftest class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.30.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I know \[that\] thou wilt bring me \[to\] death, and \[to\] the house<small><small>[^46]</small></small> appointed for all living. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.30.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Howbeit he will not stretch out \[his\] hand to the
