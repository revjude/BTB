 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.21.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Job answered and said, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.21.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear<small><small>[^2]</small></small> diligently my speech, and let this be<small><small>[^3]</small></small> your consolations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.21.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Suffer me that I may<small><small>[^4]</small></small> speak; and after that I have spoken, mock on<small><small>[^5]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.21.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As for me, \[is\] my complaint<small><small>[^6]</small></small> to man? and where \[are\] the dwelling places<small><small>[^56]</small></small> of the wicked? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.21.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Have ye not asked them that go by<small><small>[^57]</small></small> the way? and do ye not know their tokens, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.21.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That the wicked<small><small>[^58]</small></small> is reserved to the day<small><small>[^59]</small></small> of destruction? they shall be brought forth to the day of wrath<small><small>[^60]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.21.31|31]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who shall declare<small><small>[^61]</small></small> his way to his face? and who shall repay<small><small>[^62]</small></small> him \[what\] he hath done? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.21.32|32]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yet shall he be<small><small>[^63]</small></small> brought to the grave<small><small>[^64]</small></small>, and shall remain in the tomb<small><small>[^65]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.21.33|33]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The clods of the valley shall be sweet<small><small>[^66]</small></small> unto him, and every man<small><small>[^67]</small></small> shall draw after him, as \[there are\] innumerable before him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.21.34|34]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> How then comfort<small><small>[^68]</small></small> ye me in vain, seeing<small><small>[^69]</small></small> in your answers there remaineth falsehood<small><small>[^70]</small></small>?
