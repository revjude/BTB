<big><big>[[Job.2.1 Job Loses His Health|⥃]]</big></big>***Job Loses His Health***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Again<small><small>[^2]</small></small> there was a day when the sons of God came to present themselves before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and Satan came also among them to present himself before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Satan, From whence<small><small>[^3]</small></small> comest thou? And Satan answered the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and said, From going<small><small>[^4]</small></small> to and fro in the earth, and from walking up and down in it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Satan, Hast thou<small><small>[^5]</small></small> considered my servant Job, that \[there is\] none like him in the earth, a perfect and an upright<small><small>[^6]</small></small> man, one that feareth God, and escheweth evil? and still he holdeth<small><small>[^7]</small></small> fast his integrity, although thou movedst<small><small>[^8]</small></small> me against him, to destroy him<small><small>[^9]</small></small> without<small><small>[^10]</small></small> cause. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Satan answered the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and said, Skin for skin, yea, all that<small><small>[^11]</small></small> a man hath will he give for his life. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.2.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But put forth<small><small>[^12]</small></small> thine hand now, and touch his bone and his flesh, and he will curse<small><small>[^13]</small></small> thee to thy face. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.2.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Satan, Behold<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, he \[is\] in thine hand; but<small><small>[^15]</small></small> save<small><small>[^16]</small></small> his life<small><small>[^17]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.2.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So went<small><small>[^18]</small></small> Satan forth from the presence of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and smote Job with sore boils<small><small>[^19]</small></small> from the sole<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of his foot unto his crown. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.2.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he took him<small><small>[^21]</small></small> a potsherd to scrape himself withal; and he sat<small><small>[^22]</small></small> down among the ashes. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.2.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Then said his wife<small><small>[^23]</small></small> unto him, Dost thou still retain<small><small>[^24]</small></small> thine integrity? curse God<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, and die. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.2.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But he said unto her, Thou speakest<small><small>[^26]</small></small> as one<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of the foolish women speaketh. What? shall we receive<small><small>[^28]</small></small> good at the hand of God, and shall we not receive evil? In all this<small><small>[^29]</small></small> did not Job sin with his lips.
