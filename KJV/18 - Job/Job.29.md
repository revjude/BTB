<big><big>[[Job.29.1 Job’s Former Blessings|⥃]]</big></big>***Job’s Former Blessings***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover Job continued<small><small>[^2]</small></small> his parable, and said, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Oh that I were as \[in\] months<small><small>[^3]</small></small> past, as \[in\] the days \[when\] God<small><small>[^4]</small></small> preserved me; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When his candle<small><small>[^5]</small></small> class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When the Almighty<small><small>[^8]</small></small> \[was\] yet with me, \[when\] my class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When I went out to the gate through the city, \[when\] I prepared my seat in the street!<small><small>[^12]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The young men<small><small>[^13]</small></small> saw me, and hid themselves: and the aged<small><small>[^14]</small></small> arose, \[and\] stood up. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The princes refrained<small><small>[^15]</small></small> talking, and laid<small><small>[^16]</small></small> \[their\] hand on their mouth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The nobles held their peace<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, and their tongue cleaved to the roof of their mouth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When the ear<small><small>[^18]</small></small> heard \[me\], then it blessed me; and when the eye saw \[me\], it gave witness to me: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because I delivered<small><small>[^19]</small></small> the poor that cried, and the fatherless<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and \[him that had\] none to help him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The blessing<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of him that was ready<small><small>[^22]</small></small> to perish came upon me: and I caused<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the widow’s heart to sing<small><small>[^24]</small></small> for joy. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I put<small><small>[^25]</small></small> on righteousness, and it clothed me: my judgment \[was\] as a robe and a diadem<small><small>[^26]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I was eyes<small><small>[^27]</small></small> to the blind, and feet \[was\] I to the lame. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I \[was\] a father<small><small>[^28]</small></small> to the poor: and the cause<small><small>[^29]</small></small> \[which\] I knew not I searched out. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I brake<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the jaws<small><small>[^31]</small></small> of the wicked, and plucked<small><small>[^32]</small></small> the spoil out of his teeth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then I said, I shall die<small><small>[^33]</small></small> in my nest, and I shall multiply<small><small>[^34]</small></small> \[my\] days as the sand<small><small>[^35]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My root<small><small>[^36]</small></small> \[was\] spread out<small><small>[^37]</small></small> by the waters, and the dew lay all night upon my branch. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My glory<small><small>[^38]</small></small> \[was\] fresh<small><small>[^39]</small></small> in me, and my bow<small><small>[^40]</small></small> was renewed<small><small>[^41]</small></small> in my hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Unto me \[men\] gave ear<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, and waited, and kept silence at my counsel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> After my<small><small>[^43]</small></small> words they spake not again; and my speech<small><small>[^44]</small></small> dropped upon them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they waited for me as for the rain<small><small>[^45]</small></small>; and they opened their mouth wide \[as\] for the latter rain<small><small>[^46]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[If\] I laughed on them, they believed<small><small>[^47]</small></small> \[it\] not; and the light<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of my countenance they cast not down. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Job.29.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I chose out<small><small>[^49]</small></small> their way, and sat chief, and dwelt<small><small>[^50]</small></small> as a king in the army, as one \[that<small><small>[^51]</small></small>\] comforteth the
