Though I have afflicted thee, I will<small><small>[^39]</small></small> afflict thee no more. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Nah.1.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For now will I<small><small>[^40]</small></small> break his yoke from off thee, and will burst<small><small>[^41]</small></small> thy bonds in sunder. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Nah.1.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath given<small><small>[^42]</small></small> a commandment concerning thee, \[that<small><small>[^43]</small></small>\] no more of thy name be sown: out<small><small>[^44]</small></small> of the house of thy gods will I cut off the graven image and the molten image: I will make<small><small>[^45]</small></small> thy grave; for<small><small>[^46]</small></small> thou art vile. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Nah.1.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold upon<small><small>[^47]</small></small> the mountains the feet of him that bringeth good tidings, that publisheth peace!
