***A Father’s Instruction*** ^bsb1450

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear, ye<small><small>[^2]</small></small> children, the instruction of a father, and attend<small><small>[^3]</small></small> to know understanding. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I give you good<small><small>[^4]</small></small> doctrine, forsake<small><small>[^5]</small></small> ye not my law. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I was my father’s son, tender and only \[beloved\] in the sight of my mother.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He<small><small>[^7]</small></small> taught me also, and said unto me, Let<small><small>[^8]</small></small> thine heart retain my words: keep<small><small>[^9]</small></small> my commandments, and live. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Get wisdom<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, get understanding: forget \[it\] not; neither<small><small>[^11]</small></small> decline from the words of my mouth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Forsake her not, and she shall preserve thee: love<small><small>[^12]</small></small> her, and she shall keep thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wisdom \[is<small><small>[^13]</small></small>\] the principal thing; \[therefore\] get wisdom: and with<small><small>[^14]</small></small> all thy getting get understanding<small><small>[^15]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Exalt her, and she shall promote thee: she shall bring thee to honour, when thou dost embrace her.<small><small>[^16]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> She shall give<small><small>[^17]</small></small> to thine head an ornament of grace: a crown<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of glory shall she deliver to thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear, O my<small><small>[^19]</small></small> son, and receive my sayings; and the<small><small>[^20]</small></small> years of thy life shall be many. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have taught<small><small>[^21]</small></small> thee in the way of wisdom; I have led<small><small>[^22]</small></small> thee in right paths. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When thou goest<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, thy steps shall not be straitened; and when thou runnest, thou shalt<small><small>[^24]</small></small> not stumble. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Take<small><small>[^25]</small></small> fast hold of instruction; let<small><small>[^26]</small></small> \[her\] not go: keep her; for she<small><small>[^27]</small></small> \[is\] thy life. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Enter not into the path of the wicked, and go not in the way of evil \[men\].<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Avoid it, pass not by it, turn from it, and pass away.<small><small>[^29]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they sleep not, except they have done mischief; and their sleep is taken away, unless they cause \[some\] to fall.<small><small>[^30]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they eat the bread of wickedness, and drink the wine of violence.<small><small>[^31]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the path of the just \[is\] as the shining light, that shineth more and more unto the perfect day.<small><small>[^32]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The way of the wicked \[is\] as darkness: they know not at what they stumble.<small><small>[^33]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My son, attend to my words; incline thine ear unto my sayings.<small><small>[^34]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let them not depart<small><small>[^35]</small></small> from thine eyes; keep them in the<small><small>[^36]</small></small> midst of thine heart. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.4.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they \[are\] life<small><small>[^37]</small></small> unto those that find them, and health<small><small>[^38]</small></small> to all their flesh.
