***The Excellence of Wisdom*** ^bsb1455

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Doth not wisdom cry? and understanding put forth her voice?<small><small>[^2]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> She standeth in the top of high places, by the way in the places of the paths. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> She crieth at the gates, at the entry of the city, at the coming in at the doors.<small><small>[^3]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Unto you, O men, I call; and my voice \[is\] to the sons of man.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O ye simple, understand wisdom: and, ye fools, be ye of an understanding heart.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear; for<small><small>[^6]</small></small> I will speak of excellent things; and the opening<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of my lips \[shall be\] right things. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For my mouth<small><small>[^8]</small></small> shall speak truth; and wickedness \[is\] an abomination to<small><small>[^9]</small></small> my lips. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All<small><small>[^10]</small></small> the words of my mouth \[are\] in righteousness; \[there<small><small>[^11]</small></small> is\] nothing froward<small><small>[^12]</small></small> or perverse in them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They \[are\] all plain to him that understandeth, and right to them that find knowledge.<small><small>[^13]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Receive my instruction, and not silver; and knowledge rather than choice gold.<small><small>[^14]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For wisdom<small><small>[^15]</small></small> \[is\] better than rubies; and all the things that may be desired are not to be compared to it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I wisdom<small><small>[^16]</small></small> dwell with prudence<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, and find out knowledge<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of witty inventions. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The fear<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] to hate evil: pride<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and arrogancy, and the evil way, and the froward<small><small>[^21]</small></small> mouth, do I hate. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Counsel<small><small>[^22]</small></small> \[is\] mine, and sound<small><small>[^23]</small></small> wisdom: I \[am\] understanding; I have<small><small>[^24]</small></small> strength. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By<small><small>[^25]</small></small> me kings reign, and princes decree<small><small>[^26]</small></small> justice. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By me princes rule, and nobles, \[even\] all the judges of the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I love<small><small>[^27]</small></small> them that love me; and those<small><small>[^28]</small></small> that seek me early shall find me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Riches and honour<small><small>[^29]</small></small> \[are\] with me; \[yea\], durable<small><small>[^30]</small></small> riches and righteousness<small><small>[^31]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My fruit \[is\] better than gold, yea, than fine gold; and my revenue than choice silver.<small><small>[^32]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I lead<small><small>[^33]</small></small> in the<small><small>[^34]</small></small> way of righteousness, in the midst of the paths of judgment: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That I may cause those that love me to inherit<small><small>[^35]</small></small> substance; and I will fill<small><small>[^36]</small></small> their treasures. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> possessed me in the beginning of his way, before his works of old.<small><small>[^37]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I was set up from everlasting, from the beginning, or ever the earth was.<small><small>[^38]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When \[there were\] no depths, I was<small><small>[^39]</small></small> brought forth; when \[there were\] no fountains abounding with water. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Before the mountains were settled, before the hills was I brought forth:<small><small>[^40]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> While as yet<small><small>[^41]</small></small> he had not made the earth, nor the fields<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, nor the highest part<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of the dust of the world. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When he prepared<small><small>[^44]</small></small> the heavens, I \[was\] there: when he set a compass<small><small>[^45]</small></small> upon the face of the depth: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When he established the clouds above: when he strengthened the fountains of the deep: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When he gave<small><small>[^46]</small></small> to the sea his decree, that the waters should not pass his commandment: when he appointed<small><small>[^47]</small></small> the foundations of the earth: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then I was by him, \[as\] one<small><small>[^48]</small></small> brought up \[with him\]: and I was daily<small><small>[^49]</small></small> \[his\] delight, rejoicing always before him; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.31|31]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Rejoicing in the habitable part of his earth; and my<small><small>[^50]</small></small> delights \[were\] with the sons of men. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.32|32]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now therefor<small><small>[^51]</small></small>e hearken unto me, O ye children: for blessed \[are they that\] keep my ways. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.33|33]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear<small><small>[^52]</small></small> instruction, and be wise, and refuse<small><small>[^53]</small></small> it not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.34|34]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Blessed \[is\] the man that heareth me, watching<small><small>[^54]</small></small> daily at my gates, waiting at the posts of my doors. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.35|35]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For whoso<small><small>[^55]</small></small> findeth me findeth life, and shall obtain<small><small>[^56]</small></small> favour<small><small>[^57]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Prov.8.36|36]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But he<small><small>[^58]</small></small> that sinneth against me wrongeth his own soul: all<small><small>[^59]</small></small> they that hate me love death. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Prov.7|<< Proverbs 7]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Prov.9|Proverbs 9 >>]]</td></tr></table>
