***The LORD Judges the Nations*** ^bsb1997

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, behold, in those<small><small>[^2]</small></small> days, and in that time, when<small><small>[^3]</small></small> I shall bring again the captivity of Judah and Jerusalem, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will also<small><small>[^4]</small></small> gather all nations, and will bring them down into the valley<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of Jehoshaphat, and will plead<small><small>[^6]</small></small> with them there for my people and \[for\] my heritage Israel, whom they have scattered among the nations, and parted<small><small>[^7]</small></small> my land. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they have cast lots for my people; and have given a boy for an harlot, and sold a girl for wine, that they might drink.<small><small>[^8]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yea, and what<small><small>[^9]</small></small> have ye to do with me, O Tyre<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, and Zidon, and all the coasts of Palestine? will ye<small><small>[^11]</small></small> render me a recompence? and if ye recompense me, swiftly<small><small>[^12]</small></small> \[and\] speedily will I return your recompence upon your own head; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because ye<small><small>[^13]</small></small> have taken my silver and my gold, and have carried into<small><small>[^14]</small></small> your temples my goodly pleasant<small><small>[^15]</small></small> things: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The children also of Judah and the children of Jerusalem have ye<small><small>[^16]</small></small> sold unto the Grecians<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, that ye might remove them far from their border. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, I will<small><small>[^18]</small></small> raise them out of the place whither ye have sold them, and will<small><small>[^19]</small></small> return your recompence upon your own head: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will<small><small>[^20]</small></small> sell your sons<small><small>[^21]</small></small> and your daughters into the hand of the children of Judah, and they shall sell them to the Sabeans<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, to a people far off<small><small>[^23]</small></small>: for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath spoken \[it\]. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Proclaim<small><small>[^24]</small></small> ye this among the Gentiles; Prepare<small><small>[^25]</small></small> war, wake<small><small>[^26]</small></small> up the mighty men, let all the men of war draw near; let them come up: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Beat your plowshares<small><small>[^27]</small></small> into swords, and your pruninghooks<small><small>[^28]</small></small> into spears: let<small><small>[^29]</small></small> the weak say, I \[am\] strong. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Assemble<small><small>[^30]</small></small> yourselves, and come, all ye heathen, and gather yourselves together round about: thither cause<small><small>[^31]</small></small> thy mighty ones to come down, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let the heathen be wakened, and come up to the valley<small><small>[^32]</small></small> of Jehoshaphat: for<small><small>[^33]</small></small> there will I sit to judge all the heathen round about. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Put ye in the sickle<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, for the harvest<small><small>[^35]</small></small> is ripe: come, get you down; for the press<small><small>[^36]</small></small> is full, the fats overflow; for their<small><small>[^37]</small></small> wickedness \[is\] great. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Multitudes, multitudes<small><small>[^38]</small></small> in the valley of decision<small><small>[^39]</small></small>: for<small><small>[^40]</small></small> the day of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] near in the valley of decision. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The sun and the moon shall be darkened, and the stars shall withdraw their shining.<small><small>[^41]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Joel.3.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> also shall roar<small><small>[^42]</small></small> out of Zion, and utter his voice from Jerusalem; and the heavens<small><small>[^43]</small></small> and the earth shall shake: but the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[will be\] the hope<small><small>[^44]</small></small> of his people, and the strength<small><small>[^45]</small></small> of the children of Israel.
