 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Col.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If ye then be risen<small><small>[^2]</small></small> with Christ, seek<small><small>[^3]</small></small> those things which are above, where<small><small>[^4]</small></small> Christ sitteth on the right hand of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Col.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Set<small><small>[^5]</small></small> your affection<small><small>[^6]</small></small> on things above, not<small><small>[^7]</small></small> on things on the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Col.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For ye are<small><small>[^8]</small></small> dead, and your<small><small>[^9]</small></small> life is hid<small><small>[^10]</small></small> with Christ in God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Col.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When Christ, \[who is\] our<small><small>[^11]</small></small> life, shall appear<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, then shall ye<small><small>[^13]</small></small> also appear with him in glory. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Col.3.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Mortify<small><small>[^14]</small></small> therefore your members<small><small>[^15]</small></small> which are upon the earth;
