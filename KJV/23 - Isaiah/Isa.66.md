***Heaven Is My Throne*** ^bsb1692

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, The heaven<small><small>[^2]</small></small> \[is\] my throne, and the earth \[is\] my footstool: where \[is\] the house<small><small>[^3]</small></small> that ye build unto me? and where \[is\] the place of my rest? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For all those<small><small>[^4]</small></small> \[things\] hath mine hand made, and all those \[things\] have been, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: but to this<small><small>[^5]</small></small> \[man\] will I look, \[even\] to \[him that is\] poor and of a contrite spirit, and trembleth<small><small>[^6]</small></small> at my word. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He that killeth<small><small>[^7]</small></small> an ox \[is as if\] he slew a man; he that sacrificeth a lamb<small><small>[^8]</small></small>, \[as if\] he cut<small><small>[^9]</small></small> off a dog’s neck; he that offereth an oblation, \[as if he offered<small><small>[^10]</small></small>\] swine’s blood; he that burneth<small><small>[^11]</small></small> incense, \[as if\] he blessed an idol. Yea, they have<small><small>[^12]</small></small> chosen their own ways, and their soul delighteth in their abominations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I also will choose<small><small>[^13]</small></small> their delusions<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, and will bring<small><small>[^15]</small></small> their fears upon them; because when I called<small><small>[^16]</small></small>, none did answer; when I spake, they did<small><small>[^17]</small></small> not hear: but they did evil before mine eyes, and chose \[that\] in which I delighted not. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, ye that<small><small>[^18]</small></small> tremble at his word; Your<small><small>[^19]</small></small> brethren that hated you, that cast you out for my name’s sake, said, Let<small><small>[^20]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> be glorified: but<small><small>[^21]</small></small> he shall appear to your joy, and they shall be ashamed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> A voice of noise from the city, a voice from the temple, a voice of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^22]</small></small></span> that rendereth recompence to his enemies.

***Rejoice with Jerusalem*** ^bsb1693

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Before she travailed, she brought forth; before her pain came, she was delivered of a man child.<small><small>[^23]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who hath heard<small><small>[^24]</small></small> such a thing? who hath seen such things? Shall the earth be made to bring forth in one day? \[or\] shall a nation<small><small>[^25]</small></small> be born at once? for as soon as Zion travailed, she brought forth her children. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Shall I bring to<small><small>[^26]</small></small> the birth, and not cause to bring forth<small><small>[^27]</small></small>? saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: shall I cause to bring forth, and shut \[the womb\]? saith thy God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Rejoice ye<small><small>[^28]</small></small> with Jerusalem, and be glad with her, all ye that love<small><small>[^29]</small></small> her: rejoice for joy with her, all ye that mourn<small><small>[^30]</small></small> for her: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That ye may suck<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, and be satisfied with the breasts of her consolations; that ye may milk out, and be delighted with the abundance<small><small>[^32]</small></small> of her glory. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, Behold, I will<small><small>[^33]</small></small> extend peace to her like a river, and the glory<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of the Gentiles like a flowing stream: then<small><small>[^35]</small></small> shall ye suck, ye shall<small><small>[^36]</small></small> be borne upon \[her\] sides, and be dandled upon \[her\] knees. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As one<small><small>[^37]</small></small> whom his mother comforteth, so will I comfort you; and ye shall<small><small>[^38]</small></small> be comforted in Jerusalem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when ye see \[this\], your heart<small><small>[^39]</small></small> shall rejoice, and your bones<small><small>[^40]</small></small> shall flourish like an herb: and the hand<small><small>[^41]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall be known toward his servants, and \[his\] indignation toward his enemies.

***Final Judgments against the Wicked*** ^bsb1694

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, behold, the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^42]</small></small></span> will come with fire, and with his<small><small>[^43]</small></small> chariots like a whirlwind, to render his anger with fury, and his rebuke with flames of fire. <span sanctify<small><small>[^45]</small></small> themselves, and purify themselves in the gardens behind one \[tree\] in the midst<small><small>[^46]</small></small>, eating swine’s flesh, and the abomination, and the mouse, shall be consumed together, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I \[know<small><small>[^47]</small></small>\] their works and their thoughts<small><small>[^48]</small></small>: it shall come, that I<small><small>[^49]</small></small> will gather all nations and tongues; and they shall come, and see<small><small>[^50]</small></small> my glory. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will set<small><small>[^51]</small></small> a sign among them, and I will send<small><small>[^52]</small></small> those that escape of them unto the nations, \[to\] Tarshish<small><small>[^53]</small></small>, Pul, and Lud, that draw the bow, \[to\] Tubal<small><small>[^54]</small></small>, and Javan, \[to\] the isles<small><small>[^55]</small></small> afar off, that have<small><small>[^56]</small></small> not heard my fame, neither have seen my glory; and they shall declare my glory among the Gentiles. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall bring all<small><small>[^57]</small></small> your brethren \[for\] an offering<small><small>[^58]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> out of all nations upon horses<small><small>[^59]</small></small>, and in chariots, and in litters<small><small>[^60]</small></small>, and upon mules, and upon swift beasts, to my holy<small><small>[^61]</small></small> mountain Jerusalem, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, as the children of Israel bring an offering in a clean vessel into the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will also take of them for priests \[and\] for Levites, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^62]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For as the new<small><small>[^63]</small></small> heavens and the new earth, which I will make, shall remain before me, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, so shall<small><small>[^64]</small></small> your seed and your name remain. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass, \[that\] from<small><small>[^65]</small></small> one new<small><small>[^66]</small></small> moon to another, and from one sabbath to another, shall all<small><small>[^67]</small></small> flesh come to worship before me, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.66.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall go forth, and look<small><small>[^68]</small></small> upon the carcases of the men that have transgressed against me: for their worm<small><small>[^69]</small></small> shall not die, neither shall their fire<small><small>[^70]</small></small> be quenched; and they<small><small>[^71]</small></small> shall be an abhorring unto
