and with his mighty<small><small>[^44]</small></small> wind shall he shake<small><small>[^45]</small></small> his hand over the river, and shall smite<small><small>[^46]</small></small> it in the seven streams, and make \[men\] go over dryshod<small><small>[^47]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.11.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there shall<small><small>[^48]</small></small> be an highway for the remnant of his people, which shall be left, from Assyria;
