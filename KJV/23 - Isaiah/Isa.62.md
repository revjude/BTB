<big><big>[[Isa.62.1 Zion’s Salvation and New Name|⥃]]</big></big>***Zion’s Salvation and New Name***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For Zion’s<small><small>[^2]</small></small> sake will I not hold my peace, and for Jerusalem’s sake I will not rest, until the righteousness<small><small>[^3]</small></small> thereof go forth as brightness, and the salvation thereof as a lamp \[that\] burneth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Gentiles<small><small>[^4]</small></small> shall see thy righteousness, and all kings<small><small>[^5]</small></small> thy glory: and thou shalt<small><small>[^6]</small></small> be called by a new name, which the mouth of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall name. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt also be a crown of glory in the hand of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and a royal diadem in the hand of thy God.<small><small>[^7]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt no<small><small>[^8]</small></small> more be termed Forsaken; neither shall thy land any more be termed Desolate: but thou shalt be called Hephzi–bah<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, and thy land Beulah<small><small>[^10]</small></small>: for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> delighteth in thee, and thy land shall be married. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]For \[as\] a young man marrieth a virgin, \[so\] shall thy sons<small><small>[^11]</small></small> marry thee: and \[as\] the bridegroom rejoiceth<small><small>[^12]</small></small> over the bride, \[so\] shall thy God rejoice over thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have set watchmen<small><small>[^13]</small></small> upon thy walls, O Jerusalem, \[which<small><small>[^14]</small></small>\] shall never hold their peace day nor night: ye that make mention of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^15]</small></small></span>, keep<small><small>[^16]</small></small> not silence, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And give him no rest<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, till he establish, and till he make<small><small>[^18]</small></small> Jerusalem a praise in the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath sworn<small><small>[^19]</small></small> by his right hand, and by the arm of his strength, Surely I will no more give<small><small>[^20]</small></small> thy corn \[to be\] meat for thine enemies; and the sons of the stranger shall not drink thy wine, for the which thou hast laboured: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But they that have gathered it shall eat<small><small>[^21]</small></small> it, and praise the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; and they that have brought it together shall drink it in the courts of my holiness. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Go through<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, go through the gates; prepare ye the way of the people; cast up, cast up the highway; gather out the stones; lift up<small><small>[^23]</small></small> a standard for the people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^24]</small></small></span> hath proclaimed unto the end of the world, Say<small><small>[^25]</small></small> ye to the daughter of Zion, Behold, thy salvation cometh; behold, his reward<small><small>[^26]</small></small> \[is\] with him, and his work<small><small>[^27]</small></small> before him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.62.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall call them, The holy<small><small>[^28]</small></small> people, The redeemed<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and thou shalt be called, Sought out<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, A city not<small><small>[^31]</small></small> forsaken. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Isa.61|<< Isaiah 61]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Isa.63|Isaiah 63 >>]]</td></tr></table>
