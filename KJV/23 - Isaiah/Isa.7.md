***A Message to Ahaz*** ^bsb1586

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass in the days<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of Ahaz the son of Jotham, the son of Uzziah, king of Judah, \[that\] Rezin<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the king of Syria, and Pekah the son of Remaliah, king of Israel, went up toward Jerusalem to war against it, but could<small><small>[^4]</small></small> not prevail against it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it was told the house<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of David, saying, Syria is confederate with<small><small>[^6]</small></small> Ephraim. And his heart<small><small>[^7]</small></small> was moved, and the heart of his people, as the trees of the wood are moved with the wind. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> unto Isaiah, Go forth<small><small>[^8]</small></small> now to meet Ahaz, thou, and Shear–jashub<small><small>[^9]</small></small> thy son, at the end<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of the conduit of the upper pool in the highway<small><small>[^11]</small></small> of the fuller’s field; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And say unto him, Take heed<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, and be quiet; fear not<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, neither be fainthearted<small><small>[^14]</small></small> for the two tails<small><small>[^15]</small></small> of these smoking firebrands, for the fierce anger of Rezin with Syria, and of the son of Remaliah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because Syria<small><small>[^16]</small></small>, Ephraim, and the son of Remaliah, have taken evil counsel against thee, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let us go up against Judah, and vex<small><small>[^17]</small></small> it, and let us make a breach therein for us, and set a king in the midst of it, \[even\] the son of Tabeal: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>, It shall not stand, neither shall it come to pass.<small><small>[^18]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the head<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of Syria \[is\] Damascus, and the head of Damascus \[is\] Rezin; and within<small><small>[^20]</small></small> threescore and five years shall Ephraim be broken, that it be not a people<small><small>[^21]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the head<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of Ephraim \[is\] Samaria, and the head of Samaria \[is\] Remaliah’s son. If ye<small><small>[^23]</small></small> will not believe, surely ye shall not be established.

***The Sign of Immanuel***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.1|Matthew 1:18–25]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1587

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake again unto Ahaz, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ask thee a sign<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> thy God; ask it either in the depth<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, or in the height above. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Ahaz said, I will not ask<small><small>[^27]</small></small>, neither<small><small>[^28]</small></small> will I tempt<small><small>[^29]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Hear ye now, O house<small><small>[^30]</small></small> of David; \[Is it\] a small<small><small>[^31]</small></small> thing for you to weary<small><small>[^32]</small></small> men, but will ye<small><small>[^33]</small></small> weary my God also? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore the Lord himself shall give you a sign; Behold<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, a virgin shall conceive, and bear a son, and shall call<small><small>[^35]</small></small><small><small>[^36]</small></small> his name Immanuel<small><small>[^37]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Butter<small><small>[^38]</small></small> and honey shall he eat, that he may know<small><small>[^39]</small></small> to refuse the evil, and choose the good. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.7.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For before<small><small>[^40]</small></small> the child shall know to refuse the evil, and choose the good, the land<small><small>[^41]</small></small> that thou abhorrest shall be forsaken of both her kings.
