<big><big>[[Isa.61.1 The Year of the LORD’s Favor|⥃]]</big></big>***The Year of the LORD’s Favor***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Luke.4|Luke 4:16–30]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.61.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The Spirit<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span> \[is\] upon me; because the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath anointed<small><small>[^3]</small></small> me to preach<small><small>[^4]</small></small> good tidings unto the meek; he hath sent me to bind<small><small>[^5]</small></small> up the brokenhearted, to proclaim<small><small>[^6]</small></small> liberty to the captives, and the opening of the prison to \[them that are\] bound; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.61.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To proclaim the acceptable<small><small>[^7]</small></small> year of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the day of vengeance of our God; to comfort<small><small>[^9]</small></small> all that mourn; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.61.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To appoint unto them that mourn in Zion, to give unto them beauty<small><small>[^10]</small></small> for ashes, the oil<small><small>[^11]</small></small> of joy for mourning, the garment<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of praise for the spirit of heaviness; that they might be called<small><small>[^13]</small></small> trees of righteousness, the planting of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, that he<small><small>[^14]</small></small> might be glorified. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.61.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall build the old wastes, they shall raise up the former desolations, and they shall repair the waste cities, the desolations of many generations.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.61.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And strangers shall stand and feed your flocks, and the sons of the alien \[shall be\] your plowmen and your vinedressers.<small><small>[^16]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.61.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But ye shall be named<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the Priests of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: \[men\] shall call you the Ministers of our God: ye shall eat<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the riches of the Gentiles, and in their glory shall ye boast yourselves. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.61.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]For your shame<small><small>[^19]</small></small> \[ye shall have\] double; and \[for\] confusion they shall rejoice in their portion: therefore in their land they shall possess the double: everlasting<small><small>[^20]</small></small> joy shall be unto them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.61.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^21]</small></small></span> love judgment, I hate<small><small>[^22]</small></small> robbery for burnt offering; and I will direct<small><small>[^23]</small></small> their work in truth, and I will make<small><small>[^24]</small></small> an everlasting covenant with them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.61.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And their seed<small><small>[^25]</small></small> shall be known among the Gentiles, and their offspring among the people: all that see them shall acknowledge them, that they \[are<small><small>[^26]</small></small>\] the seed \[which\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath blessed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.61.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will greatly<small><small>[^27]</small></small> rejoice in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, my soul shall be joyful in my God; for<small><small>[^28]</small></small> he hath clothed me with the garments of salvation, he hath covered me with the robe of righteousness, as a<small><small>[^29]</small></small> bridegroom decketh \[himself<small><small>[^30]</small></small>\] with ornaments, and as a bride adorneth \[herself\] with her<small><small>[^31]</small></small> jewels. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.61.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For as the earth<small><small>[^32]</small></small> bringeth forth her bud, and as the garden causeth the things that are so<small><small>[^33]</small></small>wn in it to spring forth; so the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span> will cause righteousness and praise<small><small>[^34]</small></small> to spring forth before all the nations. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Isa.60|<< Isaiah 60]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Isa.62|Isaiah 62 >>]]</td></tr></table>
