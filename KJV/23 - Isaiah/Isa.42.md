***Here Is My Servant***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.12|Matthew 12:15–21]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1651

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold my servant<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, whom I<small><small>[^3]</small></small> uphold; mine elect<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, \[in whom\] my soul<small><small>[^5]</small></small> delighteth; I have<small><small>[^6]</small></small> put my spirit upon him: he shall<small><small>[^7]</small></small> bring forth judgment to the Gentiles. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He shall not cry, nor lift up, nor cause his voice to be heard in the street.<small><small>[^8]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> A bruised<small><small>[^9]</small></small> reed shall he not break, and the smoking<small><small>[^10]</small></small> flax shall he not quench<small><small>[^11]</small></small>: he shall<small><small>[^12]</small></small> bring forth judgment unto truth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He shall not<small><small>[^13]</small></small> fail nor be discouraged<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, till he have set judgment in the earth: and the isles<small><small>[^15]</small></small> shall wait for his law. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith God the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, he that created<small><small>[^16]</small></small> the heavens, and stretched them out; he that spread<small><small>[^17]</small></small> forth the earth, and that which cometh out of it; he that giveth<small><small>[^18]</small></small> breath unto the people upon it, and spirit to them that walk therein: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> have called<small><small>[^19]</small></small> thee in righteousness, and will hold<small><small>[^20]</small></small> thine hand, and will keep thee, and give<small><small>[^21]</small></small> thee for a covenant of the people, for a light<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of the Gentiles; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To open<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the blind eyes, to bring<small><small>[^24]</small></small> out the prisoners from the prison, \[and\] them that sit in darkness out of the prison house. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: that \[is<small><small>[^25]</small></small>\] my name: and my glory<small><small>[^26]</small></small> will I not give to another, neither my praise to graven images. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, the former<small><small>[^27]</small></small> things are come to pass, and new things<small><small>[^28]</small></small> do I declare: before they spring forth I tell you of them.

***A New Song of Praise***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Ps.98|Psalms 98:1–9]] <big>†</big> [[Ps.149|Psalms 149:1–9]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1652

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Sing<small><small>[^29]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> a new song, \[and\] his praise from the end of the earth, ye that go<small><small>[^30]</small></small> down to the sea, and all that is therein<small><small>[^31]</small></small>; he shall prevail<small><small>[^40]</small></small> against his enemies. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have long time<small><small>[^41]</small></small> holden my peace; I have been still, \[and\] refrained myself: \[now\] will I cry like a travailing woman; I will destroy and devour<small><small>[^42]</small></small> at once. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will make waste mountains and hills, and dry up all their herbs; and I will make the rivers islands, and I will dry up the pools.<small><small>[^43]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will bring<small><small>[^44]</small></small> the blind by a way \[that\] they knew not; I will lead<small><small>[^45]</small></small> them in paths \[that\] they have not known: I will make darkness light before them, and crooked<small><small>[^46]</small></small> things straight<small><small>[^47]</small></small>. These things will I do unto them, and not<small><small>[^48]</small></small> forsake them. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.42.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]They shall be turned back, they shall be greatly<small><small>[^49]</small></small> ashamed, that trust in graven images, that say to<small><small>[^50]</small></small> the molten images, Ye \[are\] our gods.
