***The Song of the Vineyard***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Luke.13|Luke 13:6–9]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1583

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.5.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now<small><small>[^2]</small></small> will I sing to my wellbeloved<small><small>[^3]</small></small> a song of my beloved touching<small><small>[^4]</small></small> his vineyard.
