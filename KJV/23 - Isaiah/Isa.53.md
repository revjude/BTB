<big><big>[[Isa.53.1 The Suffering Servant|⥃]]</big></big>***The Suffering Servant***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Acts.8|Acts 8:26–40]] <big>†</big> [[1Pet.2|1 Peter 2:21–25]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who<small><small>[^2]</small></small> hath believed our report<small><small>[^3]</small></small>? and to whom is the<small><small>[^4]</small></small> arm of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> revealed<small><small>[^5]</small></small>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he shall grow<small><small>[^6]</small></small> up before him as a tender plant, and as a root out of a dry ground: he hath no<small><small>[^7]</small></small> form nor comeliness; and when we shall see him, \[there is\] no beauty that we should desire him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He is despised<small><small>[^8]</small></small> and rejected of men; a man<small><small>[^9]</small></small> of sorrows, and acquainted with grief: and we hid as it were \[our\] faces from him<small><small>[^10]</small></small>; he was despised, and we esteemed him not. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Surely he hath<small><small>[^11]</small></small> borne our griefs, and carried our sorrows: yet<small><small>[^12]</small></small> we did esteem him stricken, smitten of God, and afflicted. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But he \[was<small><small>[^13]</small></small>\] wounded<small><small>[^14]</small></small> for our transgressions, \[he was\] bruised<small><small>[^15]</small></small> for our iniquities: the chastisement<small><small>[^16]</small></small> of our peace \[was\] upon him; and with his stripes<small><small>[^17]</small></small> we are healed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All we<small><small>[^18]</small></small> like sheep have gone astray; we have turned every one to his own<small><small>[^19]</small></small> way; and the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath laid on him the iniquity of us all<small><small>[^20]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He was oppressed, and he was afflicted, yet<small><small>[^21]</small></small> he opened not his mouth: he is<small><small>[^22]</small></small> brought as a lamb to the slaughter, and as a sheep before her shearers is dumb, so he openeth not his mouth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He was taken from prison and from judgment: and who<small><small>[^23]</small></small> shall declare his generation? for he was cut off<small><small>[^24]</small></small> out of the land of the living: for the transgression of my people was he stricken<small><small>[^25]</small></small>.<small><small>[^26]</small></small>

<big><big>[[Isa.53.9 A Grave Assigned|⥃]]</big></big>***A Grave Assigned***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Matt.27|Matthew 27:57–61]] <big>†</big> [[Mark.15|Mark 15:42–47]] <big>†</big> [[Luke.23|Luke 23:50–56]] <big>†</big> [[John.19|John 19:38–42]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he made<small><small>[^27]</small></small> his grave with the wicked, and with the rich in his death<small><small>[^28]</small></small>; because he had done no violence, neither \[was any\] deceit<small><small>[^29]</small></small> in his mouth. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yet it pleased<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> to bruise him; he hath<small><small>[^31]</small></small> put \[him\] to grief: when thou shalt make his soul<small><small>[^32]</small></small> an offering for sin, he shall see<small><small>[^33]</small></small> \[his\] seed, he shall prolong<small><small>[^34]</small></small> \[his\] days, and the pleasure<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall prosper in his hand. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He shall see<small><small>[^36]</small></small> of the travail of his soul, \[and\] shall be satisfied: by his<small><small>[^37]</small></small> knowledge shall my righteous servant justify<small><small>[^38]</small></small> many; for he shall bear<small><small>[^39]</small></small> their iniquities. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.53.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore will I<small><small>[^40]</small></small> divide him \[a portion\] with the great, and he shall divide the spoil with the strong; because he hath poured<small><small>[^41]</small></small> out his soul unto death: and he was<small><small>[^42]</small></small> numbered with the transgressors; and he bare<small><small>[^43]</small></small> the sin of many, and made<small><small>[^44]</small></small> intercession for the transgressors. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Isa.52|<< Isaiah 52]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Isa.54|Isaiah 54 >>]]</td></tr></table>
