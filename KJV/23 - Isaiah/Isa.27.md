<big><big>[[Isa.27.1 The LORD’s Vineyard|⥃]]</big></big>***The LORD’s Vineyard***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[John.15|John 15:1–8]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.27.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In that day<small><small>[^1]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> with his<small><small>[^2]</small></small> sore and great and strong sword shall punish leviathan<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the piercing<small><small>[^4]</small></small> serpent, even leviathan that crooked<small><small>[^5]</small></small> serpent; and he shall slay the dragon<small><small>[^6]</small></small> that \[is\] in the sea<small><small>[^7]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.27.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In that day sing<small><small>[^8]</small></small> ye unto her, A vineyard<small><small>[^9]</small></small> of red wine. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.27.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I the<small><small>[^10]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> do keep<small><small>[^11]</small></small> it; I will water<small><small>[^12]</small></small> it every moment: lest \[any\] hurt it, I will keep it night and day. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.27.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Fury<small><small>[^13]</small></small> \[is\] not in me: who would<small><small>[^14]</small></small> set the briers \[and\] thorns against me in battle? I would go through<small><small>[^15]</small></small> them, I would burn them together. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.27.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Or let him<small><small>[^16]</small></small> take hold of my strength, \[that\] he may make peace with me; \[and\] he<small><small>[^17]</small></small> shall make peace with me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.27.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He shall cause them that come of Jacob to take root: Israel shall blossom and bud, and fill the face of the world with fruit.<small><small>[^18]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.27.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hath he smitten<small><small>[^19]</small></small> him, as he smote<small><small>[^20]</small></small> those that smote him? \[or\] is he slain according to the slaughter of them that are slain by him? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.27.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In measure<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, when it shooteth forth<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, thou wilt<small><small>[^23]</small></small> debate with it: he stayeth<small><small>[^24]</small></small> his rough<small><small>[^25]</small></small> wind in the day of the east wind. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.27.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By this therefore<small><small>[^26]</small></small> shall the iniquity of Jacob be purged; and this \[is\] all the fruit to take away his sin; when<small><small>[^27]</small></small> he maketh all the stones of the altar as chalkstones that are beaten in sunder, the groves<small><small>[^28]</small></small> and images<small><small>[^29]</small></small> shall not stand up. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.27.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yet the defenced<small><small>[^30]</small></small> city \[shall be\] desolate, \[and\] the habitation forsaken, and left like a wilderness: there shall the<small><small>[^31]</small></small> calf feed, and there shall he lie down, and consume the branches thereof. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.27.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When the boughs<small><small>[^32]</small></small> thereof are withered, they shall be broken off: the women come, \[and\] set them on fire: for it \[is<small><small>[^33]</small></small>\] a people of no understanding: therefore<small><small>[^34]</small></small> he that made them will not have mercy on them, and he that formed them will shew them no favour.
