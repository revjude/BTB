<big><big>[[Isa.32.1 A Righteous King|⥃]]</big></big>***A Righteous King***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.32.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, a king<small><small>[^2]</small></small> shall reign in righteousness, and princes<small><small>[^3]</small></small> shall rule in judgment. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.32.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And a man<small><small>[^4]</small></small> shall be as an hiding<small><small>[^5]</small></small> place from the wind, and a covert from the tempest; as rivers<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of water in a dry place, as the shadow of a great<small><small>[^7]</small></small> rock<small><small>[^8]</small></small> in a weary land. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.32.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the eyes of them that see shall not be dim, and the ears of them that hear shall hearken.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.32.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The heart<small><small>[^10]</small></small> also of the rash<small><small>[^11]</small></small> shall understand knowledge, and the tongue<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of the stammerers shall be ready to speak plainly<small><small>[^13]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.32.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The vile<small><small>[^14]</small></small> person shall be no more called liberal, nor<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the churl said \[to be\] bountiful. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.32.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the vile<small><small>[^16]</small></small> person will speak villany, and his heart<small><small>[^17]</small></small> will work iniquity, to practise hypocrisy, and to utter error against the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, to make empty<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the soul of the hungry, and he will cause the drink of the thirsty to fail. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.32.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The instruments<small><small>[^19]</small></small> also of the churl \[are\] evil: he deviseth<small><small>[^20]</small></small> wicked devices to destroy the poor with lying<small><small>[^21]</small></small> words, even when the needy speaketh right<small><small>[^22]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.32.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the liberal<small><small>[^23]</small></small> deviseth liberal things; and by liberal things shall he stand<small><small>[^24]</small></small>.

yea, upon<small><small>[^35]</small></small> all the houses of joy \[in\] the<small><small>[^36]</small></small> joyous city: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.32.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because the palaces<small><small>[^37]</small></small> shall be forsaken; and the effect of righteousness quietness<small><small>[^44]</small></small> and assurance for ever. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.32.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And my people shall dwell in a peaceable habitation, and in sure dwellings, and in quiet resting places;<small><small>[^45]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.32.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When it shall<small><small>[^46]</small></small> hail, coming down on the<small><small>[^47]</small></small> forest;
