<big><big>[[Isa.35.1 The Glory of Zion|⥃]]</big></big>***The Glory of Zion***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.35.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The wilderness<small><small>[^2]</small></small> and the solitary place shall be<small><small>[^3]</small></small> glad for them; and the desert<small><small>[^4]</small></small> shall rejoice, and blossom as the rose. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.35.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> It shall blossom abundantly, and rejoice<small><small>[^5]</small></small> even with joy and singing: the glory<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of Lebanon shall be given unto it, the excellency<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of Carmel and Sharon, they shall<small><small>[^8]</small></small> see the glory of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, \[and\] the excellency of our God. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.35.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Strengthen ye the weak hands, and confirm the feeble knees.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.35.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Say to them \[that are\] of a fearful<small><small>[^10]</small></small> heart, Be strong<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, fear not<small><small>[^12]</small></small>: behold<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, your God will come \[with\] vengeance, \[even\] God \[with\] a recompence; he will come and save you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.35.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the eyes<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of the blind shall be opened, and the ears<small><small>[^15]</small></small> of the deaf shall be unstopped. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.35.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then shall the lame<small><small>[^16]</small></small> \[man\] leap as an hart, and the tongue<small><small>[^17]</small></small> of the dumb sing: for<small><small>[^18]</small></small> in the wilderness shall waters break out, and streams in the desert. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.35.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the parched<small><small>[^19]</small></small> ground shall become a pool, and the thirsty land springs of water: in the<small><small>[^20]</small></small> habitation of dragons, where each lay, \[shall be\] grass with reeds<small><small>[^21]</small></small> and rushes. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.35.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And an highway<small><small>[^22]</small></small> shall be there, and a way, and it shall be called The way<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of holiness; the unclean<small><small>[^24]</small></small> shall not pass over it; but it \[shall be\] for those<small><small>[^25]</small></small>: the wayfaring<small><small>[^26]</small></small> men, though fools, shall not err \[therein\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.35.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> No lion<small><small>[^27]</small></small> shall be there, nor \[any\] ravenous beast shall go up thereon, it shall not be found there; but<small><small>[^28]</small></small> the redeemed shall walk \[there\]: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.35.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the ransomed<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall return, and come<small><small>[^30]</small></small> to Zion with songs and everlasting joy upon their heads: they shall obtain joy and gladness, and sorrow<small><small>[^31]</small></small> and sighing shall flee away. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Isa.34|<< Isaiah 34]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Isa.36|Isaiah 36 >>]]</td></tr></table>
