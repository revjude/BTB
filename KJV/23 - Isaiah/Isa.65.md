<big><big>[[Isa.65.1 Judgments and Promises|⥃]]</big></big>***Judgments and Promises***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Rom.10|Romans 10:1–21]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I am sought<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of \[them that\] asked not \[for me\]; I am found of \[them that\] sought me not: I said, Behold<small><small>[^3]</small></small> me, behold me, unto<small><small>[^4]</small></small> a nation \[that\] was not called by my name. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have spread<small><small>[^5]</small></small> out my hands all the day unto a rebellious<small><small>[^6]</small></small> people, which<small><small>[^7]</small></small> walketh in a way \[that was\] not good, after<small><small>[^8]</small></small> their own thoughts; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> A people<small><small>[^9]</small></small> that provoketh me to anger continually to my face<small><small>[^10]</small></small>; that sacrificeth<small><small>[^11]</small></small> in gardens, and burneth incense upon altars<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of brick; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which remain<small><small>[^13]</small></small> among the graves, and lodge in the monuments, which eat<small><small>[^14]</small></small> swine’s flesh, and broth<small><small>[^15]</small></small> of abominable \[things is in\] their vessels; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which say, Stand<small><small>[^16]</small></small> by thyself, come not near to me; for I am holier than thou. These<small><small>[^17]</small></small> \[are\] a smoke in my nose<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, a fire<small><small>[^19]</small></small> that burneth all the day. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, \[it is<small><small>[^20]</small></small>\] written before me: I will<small><small>[^21]</small></small> not keep silence, but<small><small>[^22]</small></small> will recompense, even recompense into their bosom, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Your iniquities<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, and the iniquities of your fathers together, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, which have burned<small><small>[^24]</small></small> incense upon the mountains, and blasphemed<small><small>[^25]</small></small> me upon the hills: therefore<small><small>[^26]</small></small> will I measure their former work into their bosom. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, As the new wine is found in the cluster, and \[one\] saith, Destroy it not; for a blessing \[is\] in it: so will I do for my servants’ sakes, that I may not destroy them all.<small><small>[^27]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will<small><small>[^28]</small></small> bring forth a seed out of Jacob, and out of Judah an inheritor of my mountains: and mine elect<small><small>[^29]</small></small> shall inherit it, and my servants shall dwell there. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Sharon<small><small>[^30]</small></small> shall be a fold of flocks, and the<small><small>[^31]</small></small> valley of Achor a place for the herds to lie down in, for my people that have sought me. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But ye \[are\] they that<small><small>[^32]</small></small> forsake the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, that forget my holy<small><small>[^33]</small></small> mountain, that prepare<small><small>[^34]</small></small> a table for that troop<small><small>[^35]</small></small>, and that furnish the drink offering unto that number<small><small>[^36]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore will I<small><small>[^37]</small></small> number you to the sword, and ye shall all bow down to the slaughter: because<small><small>[^38]</small></small> when I called, ye did not answer; when I spake, ye did not hear; but did evil<small><small>[^39]</small></small> before mine eyes, and did<small><small>[^40]</small></small> choose \[that\] wherein I delighted not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>, Behold, my servants shall eat<small><small>[^41]</small></small>, but ye shall be hungry: behold, my servants shall drink, but ye shall be thirsty: behold, my servants shall rejoice<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, but ye shall be ashamed: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, my servants<small><small>[^43]</small></small> shall sing for joy of heart, but ye shall<small><small>[^44]</small></small> cry for sorrow of heart, and shall howl for vexation<small><small>[^45]</small></small> of spirit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And ye shall<small><small>[^46]</small></small> leave your name for a curse unto my chosen<small><small>[^47]</small></small>: for the Lord<small><small>[^48]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">God</span> shall slay thee, and call his servants<small><small>[^49]</small></small> by another name: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That he who<small><small>[^50]</small></small> blesseth himself in the earth shall bless himself in the God<small><small>[^51]</small></small> of truth; and he that<small><small>[^52]</small></small> sweareth in the earth shall swear by the God of truth; because<small><small>[^53]</small></small> the former troubles are forgotten, and because they are hid from mine eyes.

<big><big>[[Isa.65.17 A New Heaven and a New Earth|⥃]]</big></big>***A New Heaven and a New Earth***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Rev.21|Revelation 21:1–8]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, behold, I create<small><small>[^54]</small></small> new heavens and a new earth: and the former<small><small>[^55]</small></small> shall not be remembered, nor come into mind<small><small>[^56]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But be ye glad and rejoice for ever \[in that\] which I create: for, behold, I create Jerusalem a rejoicing, and her people a joy.<small><small>[^57]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will<small><small>[^58]</small></small> rejoice in Jerusalem, and joy in my people: and the voice of weeping<small><small>[^59]</small></small> shall be no more heard in her, nor the voice of crying. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> There shall<small><small>[^60]</small></small> be no more thence an infant of days, nor an old man that hath not filled his days: for the child shall die an hundred years old; but<small><small>[^61]</small></small> the sinner \[being\] an hundred years old shall be accursed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall build houses, and inhabit \[them\]; and they shall plant vineyards, and eat the fruit of them.<small><small>[^62]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They shall not build, and another inhabit; they shall not plant, and another eat: for as<small><small>[^63]</small></small> the days of a tree \[are\] the days of my people, and mine elect shall long enjoy<small><small>[^64]</small></small> the work of their hands. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They shall<small><small>[^65]</small></small> not labour in vain, nor bring for<small><small>[^66]</small></small>th for trouble; for they \[are\] the seed of the blessed of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and their offspring with them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass, that before they call, I will answer; and while they are yet speaking, I will hear.<small><small>[^67]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.65.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The wolf<small><small>[^68]</small></small> and the lamb shall feed together, and the lion shall eat straw like the bullock: and dust<small><small>[^69]</small></small> \[shall be\] the serpent’s meat.
