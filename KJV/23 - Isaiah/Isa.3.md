***Judgment on Jerusalem and Judah*** ^bsb1580

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, behold<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, the Lord<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, the $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.3.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The shew<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of their countenance doth witness against them; and they declare<small><small>[^22]</small></small> their sin as Sodom, they hide \[it\] not. $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.3.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]\[As for\] my people, children<small><small>[^29]</small></small> \[are\] their oppressors, and women rule over them.
