<big><big>[[Isa.2.1 The Mountain of the House of the LORD|⥃]]</big></big>***The Mountain of the House of the LORD***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Mic.4|Micah 4:1–5]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The word that Isaiah the son of Amoz saw<small><small>[^2]</small></small> concerning Judah and Jerusalem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall<small><small>[^3]</small></small> come to pass in the last<small><small>[^4]</small></small> days, \[that\] the mountain<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span> house shall be established<small><small>[^6]</small></small> in the top of the mountains, and shall be exalted above the hills; and all<small><small>[^7]</small></small> nations shall flow unto it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And many people shall go and say, Come ye<small><small>[^8]</small></small>, and let us go up to the mountain of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, to the house of the God of Jacob; and he will teach<small><small>[^9]</small></small> us of his ways, and we will walk in his paths: for out<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of Zion shall go forth the law, and the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> from Jerusalem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he<small><small>[^11]</small></small> shall judge among the nations, and shall rebuke many people: and they<small><small>[^12]</small></small> shall beat their swords into plowshares, and their spears into pruninghooks<small><small>[^13]</small></small>: nation shall not lift up sword against nation, neither<small><small>[^14]</small></small> shall they learn war any more.
