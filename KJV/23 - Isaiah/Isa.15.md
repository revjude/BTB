<big><big>[[Isa.15.1 The Burden against Moab|⥃]]</big></big>***The Burden against Moab***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Jer.48|Jeremiah 48:1–47]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.15.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The burden<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of Moab<small><small>[^3]</small></small>. Because in the<small><small>[^4]</small></small> night Ar<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of Moab is laid waste, \[and\] brought to silence<small><small>[^6]</small></small>; because in the night Kir<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of Moab is laid waste, \[and\] brought to silence; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.15.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He is gone<small><small>[^8]</small></small> up to Bajith, and to Dibon, the high places, to weep: Moab<small><small>[^9]</small></small> shall howl over Nebo<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, and over Medeba<small><small>[^11]</small></small>: on all<small><small>[^12]</small></small> their heads \[shall be\] baldness, \[and\] every beard cut off. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.15.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In their streets<small><small>[^13]</small></small> they shall gird themselves with sackcloth: on the<small><small>[^14]</small></small> tops of their houses, and in their streets, every one shall howl, weeping abundantly<small><small>[^15]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.15.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Heshbon<small><small>[^16]</small></small> shall cry, and Elealeh: their voice shall be heard \[even\] unto Jahaz<small><small>[^17]</small></small>: therefore the armed soldiers of Moab shall cry out; his<small><small>[^18]</small></small> life shall be grievous unto him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.15.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My heart<small><small>[^19]</small></small> shall cry out for Moab; his fugitives<small><small>[^20]</small></small> \[shall flee\] unto Zoar<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, an heifer of three<small><small>[^22]</small></small> years old: for by the mounting<small><small>[^23]</small></small> up of Luhith with<small><small>[^24]</small></small> weeping shall they go it up; for in the way of Horonaim they shall raise up a cry of destruction<small><small>[^25]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.15.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the waters of Nimrim<small><small>[^26]</small></small> shall be desolate<small><small>[^27]</small></small>: for the hay is withered away, the grass<small><small>[^28]</small></small> faileth, there is no green thing. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.15.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore the abundance<small><small>[^29]</small></small> they have gotten, and that which they have laid up, shall they carry away to the<small><small>[^30]</small></small> brook of the willows<small><small>[^31]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.15.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the cry<small><small>[^32]</small></small> is gone round about the borders of Moab; the howling thereof unto Eglaim<small><small>[^33]</small></small>, and the howling thereof unto Beer–elim. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.15.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the waters of Dimon<small><small>[^34]</small></small> shall be full of blood: for I will bring more<small><small>[^35]</small></small> upon Dimon, lions<small><small>[^36]</small></small> upon him<small><small>[^37]</small></small> that escapeth of Moab, and upon the remnant of the land. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Isa.14|<< Isaiah 14]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Isa.16|Isaiah 16 >>]]</td></tr></table>
