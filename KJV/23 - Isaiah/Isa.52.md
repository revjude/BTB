<big><big>[[Isa.52.1 Deliverance for Jerusalem|⥃]]</big></big>***Deliverance for Jerusalem***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Awake<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, awake; put on thy strength, O Zion; put on thy beautiful<small><small>[^3]</small></small> garments, O Jerusalem, the holy<small><small>[^4]</small></small> city: for henceforth there shall<small><small>[^5]</small></small> no more come into thee the uncircumcised and the unclean. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Shake<small><small>[^6]</small></small> thyself from the dust; arise<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, \[and\] sit down, O Jerusalem: loose<small><small>[^8]</small></small> thyself from the bands of thy neck, O captive daughter of Zion. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, Ye have sold yourselves for nought; and ye shall be redeemed without money.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>, My people<small><small>[^10]</small></small> went down aforetime into Egypt to sojourn there; and the Assyrian<small><small>[^11]</small></small> oppressed them without<small><small>[^12]</small></small> cause. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now therefore, what<small><small>[^13]</small></small> have I here, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, that my people<small><small>[^14]</small></small> is taken away for nought? they that rule over them make<small><small>[^15]</small></small> them to howl, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; and my name<small><small>[^16]</small></small> continually every day \[is\] blasphemed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore my people<small><small>[^17]</small></small> shall know my name: therefore \[they shall know\] in that day that I \[am\] he<small><small>[^18]</small></small> that doth speak: behold, \[it is\] I. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> How beautiful<small><small>[^19]</small></small> upon the mountains are the feet of him that bringeth good tidings, that publisheth<small><small>[^20]</small></small> peace; that bringeth good tidings of good, that publisheth salvation; that saith unto Zion, Thy God<small><small>[^21]</small></small> reigneth! <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thy<small><small>[^22]</small></small> watchmen shall lift<small><small>[^23]</small></small> up the voice; with<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the voice together shall they sing: for they shall see<small><small>[^25]</small></small> eye to eye, when the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall bring again Zion. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Break<small><small>[^26]</small></small> forth into joy, sing together, ye waste<small><small>[^27]</small></small> places of Jerusalem: for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath comforted his people, he hath redeemed Jerusalem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath made<small><small>[^28]</small></small> bare his holy arm in the eyes of all<small><small>[^29]</small></small> the nations; and all the ends of the earth shall see the salvation of our God. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Depart ye<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, depart ye, go ye out from thence, touch<small><small>[^31]</small></small> no unclean \[thing\]; go ye out of the midst of her; be<small><small>[^32]</small></small> ye clean, that bear the vessels of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.52.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For ye shall<small><small>[^33]</small></small> not go out with haste, nor go by flight: for<small><small>[^34]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will go before you; and the God<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of Israel \[will be\] your rereward<small><small>[^36]</small></small>.
