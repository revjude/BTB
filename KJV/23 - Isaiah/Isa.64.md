<big><big>[[Isa.64.1 A Prayer for God’s Power|⥃]]</big></big>***A Prayer for God’s Power***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Oh that<small><small>[^2]</small></small> thou wouldest rend the heavens, that thou wouldest come<small><small>[^3]</small></small> down, that the<small><small>[^4]</small></small> mountains might flow down at thy presence, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As \[when\] the melting fire<small><small>[^5]</small></small> burneth, the fire causeth the waters to boil, to make<small><small>[^6]</small></small> thy name known to thine adversaries, \[that\] the nations<small><small>[^7]</small></small> may tremble at thy presence! <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When thou didst<small><small>[^8]</small></small> terrible things \[which\] we looked not for, thou camest down, the mountains<small><small>[^9]</small></small> flowed down at thy presence. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For since the beginning of the world \[men\] have not<small><small>[^10]</small></small> heard, nor perceived by the ear, neither hath the eye seen<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, O God, beside thee, \[what\] he hath prepared for him that waiteth<small><small>[^12]</small></small> for him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou meetest<small><small>[^13]</small></small> him that rejoiceth<small><small>[^14]</small></small> and worketh righteousness, \[those that<small><small>[^15]</small></small>\] remember thee in thy ways: behold, thou art wroth<small><small>[^16]</small></small>; for we have sinned: in those<small><small>[^17]</small></small> is continuance, and we shall be saved. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But we are all<small><small>[^18]</small></small> as an unclean \[thing\], and all our<small><small>[^19]</small></small> righteousnesses \[are\] as filthy rags; and we all<small><small>[^20]</small></small> do fade as a leaf; and our iniquities<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, like the wind, have taken us away. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[there is<small><small>[^22]</small></small>\] none that calleth upon thy name, that stirreth up himself to take<small><small>[^23]</small></small> hold of thee: for thou hast hid<small><small>[^24]</small></small> thy face from us, and hast consumed<small><small>[^25]</small></small> us, because<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of our iniquities. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But now, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, thou \[art<small><small>[^27]</small></small>\] our father; we \[are\] the clay<small><small>[^28]</small></small>, and thou our potter; and we all \[are<small><small>[^29]</small></small>\] the work of thy hand. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Be not wroth<small><small>[^30]</small></small> very sore, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, neither remember<small><small>[^31]</small></small> iniquity for ever: behold, see, we beseech thee, we \[are<small><small>[^32]</small></small>\] all thy people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thy holy cities are a wilderness, Zion is a wilderness, Jerusalem a desolation.<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Our holy<small><small>[^34]</small></small> and our beautiful house, where<small><small>[^35]</small></small> our fathers praised thee, is burned up with fire: and all our<small><small>[^36]</small></small> pleasant things are laid waste. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Isa.64.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wilt thou refrain thyself for these \[things\], O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>? wilt thou hold thy peace, and afflict us very sore? <small><small>[^37]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Isa.63|<< Isaiah 63]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Isa.65|Isaiah 65 >>]]</td></tr></table>
