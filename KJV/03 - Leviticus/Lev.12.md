***Purification after Childbirth*** ^bsb294

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.12.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.12.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Speak unto the children of Israel, saying, If a woman<small><small>[^2]</small></small> have conceived seed, and born a man child: then she shall be unclean seven days; according<small><small>[^3]</small></small> to the days of the separation for her infirmity shall she be unclean. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.12.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And in the eighth day the flesh of his foreskin shall be circumcised.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.12.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And she shall then continue in the blood of her purifying three and thirty days; she shall touch no hallowed thing, nor come into the sanctuary, until the days of her purifying be fulfilled.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.12.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But if she bear a maid child, then she shall be unclean two weeks, as in her separation: and she shall continue in the blood of her purifying threescore and six days.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.12.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when the days of her purifying are fulfilled, for a son, or for a daughter, she shall bring a lamb<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of the first year<small><small>[^8]</small></small> for a burnt offering, and a young pigeon, or a turtledove, for a sin offering, unto the door of the tabernacle of the congregation, unto the priest: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.12.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who shall offer it before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and make<small><small>[^9]</small></small> an atonement for her; and she shall be cleansed<small><small>[^10]</small></small> from the issue of her blood. This \[is\] the law for her that hath born a male<small><small>[^11]</small></small> or a female. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.12.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if she be not able to bring a lamb<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, then she shall bring two turtles, or two young pigeons; the one for the burnt offering, and the other for a sin offering: and the priest shall make an atonement<small><small>[^13]</small></small> for her, and she shall be clean. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Lev.11|<< Leviticus 11]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Lev.13|Leviticus 13 >>]]</td></tr></table>
