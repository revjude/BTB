***Laws for Peace Offerings***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Lev.7|Leviticus 7:11–21]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb274

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if his oblation \[be\] a sacrifice<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of peace offering, if he offer \[it\] of the herd; whether \[it be\] a male or female, he shall offer it without<small><small>[^3]</small></small> blemish before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall lay<small><small>[^4]</small></small> his hand upon the head of his offering, and kill it<small><small>[^5]</small></small> \[at\] the door of the tabernacle of the congregation: and Aaron’s sons the priests shall sprinkle the blood upon the altar round about. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall offer of the sacrifice of the peace offering an offering made by fire unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; the fat<small><small>[^6]</small></small> that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that \[is\] upon the inwards, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the two kidneys, and the fat that \[is\] on them, which \[is\] by the flanks, and the caul above the liver, with the kidneys<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, it shall he take away. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Aaron’s<small><small>[^8]</small></small> sons shall burn it on the altar upon the burnt<small><small>[^9]</small></small> sacrifice, which \[is\] upon the wood that \[is\] on the fire: \[it is\] an offering made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if his offering for a sacrifice<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of peace offering unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[be\] of<small><small>[^11]</small></small> the flock; male<small><small>[^12]</small></small> or female, he shall<small><small>[^13]</small></small> offer it without blemish. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If he offer a lamb for his offering, then shall he offer it<small><small>[^14]</small></small> before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall<small><small>[^15]</small></small> lay his hand upon the head of his offering, and kill it<small><small>[^16]</small></small> before the tabernacle of the congregation: and Aaron’s sons shall sprinkle<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the blood thereof round about upon the altar. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall offer of the sacrifice of the peace offering an offering made by fire unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; the fat<small><small>[^18]</small></small> thereof, \[and\] the whole rump<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, it shall he take off hard by the backbone; and the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that \[is\] upon the inwards, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the two kidneys, and the fat that \[is\] upon them, which \[is\] by the flanks, and the caul<small><small>[^20]</small></small> above the liver, with the kidneys, it shall he take away. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the priest shall burn<small><small>[^21]</small></small> it upon the altar: \[it is\] the food<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of the offering made by fire unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And if his offering \[be\] a goat<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, then he shall offer it before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall lay his hand<small><small>[^24]</small></small> upon the head of it, and kill it before the tabernacle of the congregation: and the sons of Aaron shall sprinkle<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the blood thereof upon the altar round about. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall offer thereof his offering, \[even\] an offering made by fire unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; the fat that covereth<small><small>[^26]</small></small> the inwards, and all the fat that \[is\] upon the inwards, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the two kidneys, and the fat that \[is\] upon them, which \[is\] by the flanks, and the caul above<small><small>[^27]</small></small> the liver, with the kidneys, it shall he take away. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the priest shall burn them upon the altar: \[it is\] the food<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of the offering made by fire for a sweet savour: all the fat<small><small>[^29]</small></small> \[is\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.3.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[It shall be\] a perpetual<small><small>[^30]</small></small> statute for your generations throughout all your dwellings, that ye eat neither<small><small>[^31]</small></small> fat nor blood<small><small>[^32]</small></small>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Lev.2|<< Leviticus 2]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Lev.4|Leviticus 4 >>]]</td></tr></table>
