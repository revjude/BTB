<big><big>[[Lev.19.1 Commandments for Holiness|⥃]]</big></big>***Commandments for Holiness***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Speak unto all the congregation of the children of Israel, and say unto them, Ye shall<small><small>[^2]</small></small> be holy: for I the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God \[am\] holy. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Ye shall fear<small><small>[^3]</small></small> every man his mother, and his father, and keep<small><small>[^4]</small></small> my sabbaths: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Turn ye not unto<small><small>[^5]</small></small> idols, nor make to yourselves molten gods<small><small>[^6]</small></small>: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God.<small><small>[^7]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And if ye offer a sacrifice<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of peace offerings unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, ye shall<small><small>[^9]</small></small> offer it at your own will<small><small>[^10]</small></small>.<small><small>[^11]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> It shall be eaten the same day ye offer it, and on the morrow: and if ought remain until the third day, it shall be burnt in the fire.<small><small>[^12]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if it be eaten at all on the third day, it \[is\] abominable<small><small>[^13]</small></small>; it shall<small><small>[^14]</small></small> not be accepted. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore \[every one\] that eateth it shall bear his iniquity, because he hath profaned the hallowed thing of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and that soul shall be cut off from among his people.

<big><big>[[Lev.19.9 Love Your Neighbor|⥃]]</big></big>***Love Your Neighbor***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Rom.13|Romans 13:8–10]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And when ye reap the harvest<small><small>[^15]</small></small> of your land, thou shalt not wholly reap the corners of thy field, neither shalt thou gather the gleanings of thy harvest. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thou shalt not glean<small><small>[^16]</small></small> thy vineyard, neither shalt thou gather \[every\] grape of thy vineyard; thou shalt<small><small>[^17]</small></small> leave them for the poor and stranger: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye shall not<small><small>[^18]</small></small> steal, neither deal falsely, neither lie one<small><small>[^19]</small></small> to another. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And ye shall<small><small>[^20]</small></small> not swear by my name falsely, neither shalt thou profane<small><small>[^21]</small></small> the name of thy God: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thou shalt not<small><small>[^22]</small></small> defraud thy neighbour, neither rob \[him\]: the wages<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of him that is hired shall not abide with thee all night until the morning. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thou shalt not curse<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the deaf, nor put a stumblingblock before the blind, but shalt fear<small><small>[^25]</small></small> thy God: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment: thou shalt not respect the person of the poor, nor honour the person of the mighty: \[but\] in righteousness shalt thou judge thy neighbour.<small><small>[^26]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thou shalt not go up and down \[as\] a talebearer<small><small>[^27]</small></small> among thy people: neither shalt thou stand<small><small>[^28]</small></small> against the blood of thy neighbour: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thou shalt not hate<small><small>[^29]</small></small> thy brother in thine heart: thou shalt in any wise rebuke<small><small>[^30]</small></small> thy neighbour, and not suffer sin upon him<small><small>[^31]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thou shalt not avenge<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, nor bear any grudge against the children of thy people, but thou shalt<small><small>[^33]</small></small> love thy neighbour as thyself: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.

<big><big>[[Lev.19.19 Keep My Statutes|⥃]]</big></big>***Keep My Statutes***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye shall keep my statutes. Thou shalt not let thy cattle gender<small><small>[^34]</small></small> with a diverse kind: thou shalt not sow thy field with mingled<small><small>[^35]</small></small> seed: neither shall a garment mingled of linen and woollen come upon thee. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And whosoever lieth carnally with a woman, that \[is\] a bondmaid, betrothed to an husband<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, and not at all redeemed, nor freedom given her; she shall be scourged<small><small>[^37]</small></small>; they shall not be put to death, because she was not free. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall bring his trespass offering unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, unto the door of the tabernacle of the congregation, \[even\] a ram for a trespass offering.<small><small>[^38]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the priest shall make an atonement for him with the ram of the trespass offering before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> for his sin which he hath done: and the sin<small><small>[^39]</small></small> which he hath done shall be forgiven him. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And when<small><small>[^40]</small></small> ye shall come into the land, and shall have planted all manner of trees for food, then ye shall count the fruit thereof as uncircumcised<small><small>[^41]</small></small>: three years shall it be as uncircumcised unto you: it shall not be eaten of. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But in the fourth year all the<small><small>[^42]</small></small> fruit thereof shall be holy to praise the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[withal<small><small>[^43]</small></small>\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And in the fifth year shall ye eat of the fruit thereof, that it may yield unto you the increase thereof: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God.<small><small>[^44]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Ye shall not eat \[any thing\] with the blood<small><small>[^45]</small></small>: neither shall ye use<small><small>[^46]</small></small> enchantment, nor<small><small>[^47]</small></small> observe times. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye shall not round the corners of your heads, neither shalt thou mar the corners of thy beard.<small><small>[^48]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye shall not make any cuttings<small><small>[^49]</small></small> in your flesh for the dead, nor print<small><small>[^50]</small></small> any marks upon you: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Do not prostitute<small><small>[^51]</small></small> thy daughter, to cause<small><small>[^52]</small></small> her to be a whore; lest the land fall to whoredom, and the land become full of wickedness. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye shall keep<small><small>[^53]</small></small> my sabbaths, and reverence<small><small>[^54]</small></small> my sanctuary: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.31|31]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Regard not them that have familiar spirits, neither seek after wizards, to be defiled by them: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God.<small><small>[^55]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.32|32]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Thou shalt rise up before the hoary head, and honour the face of the old man, and fear thy God: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^56]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.33|33]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And if<small><small>[^57]</small></small> a stranger sojourn with thee in your land, ye shall not vex him<small><small>[^58]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.34|34]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[But\] the stranger that dwelleth with you shall be unto you as one born among you, and thou shalt love him as thyself; for ye were strangers in the land of Egypt: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God.<small><small>[^59]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.35|35]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Ye shall do no unrighteousness<small><small>[^60]</small></small> in judgment, in meteyard<small><small>[^61]</small></small>, in weight, or in measure. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.36|36]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Just balances<small><small>[^62]</small></small>, just weights<small><small>[^63]</small></small>, a just ephah, and a just hin, shall ye have: I \[am<small><small>[^64]</small></small>\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God, which brought you out of the land of Egypt. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.19.37|37]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore shall ye observe all my statutes, and all my judgments, and do them: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <small><small>[^65]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Lev.18|<< Leviticus 18]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Lev.20|Leviticus 20 >>]]</td></tr></table>
