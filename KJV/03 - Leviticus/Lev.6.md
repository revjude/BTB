<big><big>[[Lev.6.1 Sins Requiring a Guilt Offering|⥃]]</big></big>***Sins Requiring a Guilt Offering***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Lev.5|Leviticus 5:14–19]] <big>†</big> [[Lev.7|Leviticus 7:1–10]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.6.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.6.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If a soul sin, and commit<small><small>[^2]</small></small> a trespass against the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and lie<small><small>[^3]</small></small> unto his neighbour in that<small><small>[^4]</small></small> which was delivered him to keep, or in fellowship<small><small>[^5]</small></small>, or in a thing taken away by violence, or hath deceived<small><small>[^6]</small></small> his neighbour; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.6.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Or have found<small><small>[^7]</small></small> that which was lost, and lieth concerning it, and sweareth<small><small>[^8]</small></small> falsely; in any of all these that a man doeth, sinning therein: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.6.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then it shall be, because<small><small>[^9]</small></small> he hath sinned, and is guilty, that he shall restore that which he<small><small>[^10]</small></small> took violently away, or the thing which he hath deceitfully gotten, or that which was delivered him to keep, or the lost thing which he found, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.6.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Or all that about which he hath sworn falsely; <big><big>[[Lev.6.8 The Burnt Offering|⥃]]</big></big>***The Burnt Offering***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Lev.1|Leviticus 1:1–17]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.6.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, saying,<small><small>[^17]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.6.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Command Aaron and his sons, saying, This \[is\] the law of the burnt<small><small>[^18]</small></small> offering: It \[is\] the burnt offering, because of the burning<small><small>[^19]</small></small> upon the altar all night unto the morning, and the fire of the altar shall be burning in it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.6.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the priest shall put on his linen garment<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and his linen breeches shall he put upon his flesh, and take up the ashes which the fire hath consumed<small><small>[^21]</small></small> with the burnt offering on the altar, and he shall put them beside<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the altar. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.6.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall put off<small><small>[^23]</small></small> his garments, and put on other garments, and carry forth the ashes without<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the camp unto a clean place. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.6.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the fire<small><small>[^25]</small></small> upon the altar shall be burning in it; it shall not be put out: and the priest shall burn wood<small><small>[^26]</small></small> on it every morning, and lay the burnt offering in order upon it; and he shall burn thereon the fat of the peace offerings. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.6.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The fire shall ever be burning upon the altar; it shall never go out.
