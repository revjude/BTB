<big><big>[[Lev.22.1 Restrictions against the Unclean|⥃]]</big></big>***Restrictions against the Unclean***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Speak unto Aaron and to his sons, that they separate themselves from the holy things of the children of Israel, and that they profane not<small><small>[^2]</small></small> my holy name \[in those things\] which they hallow<small><small>[^3]</small></small> unto me: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Say unto them, Whosoever \[he be\] of all your seed among your generations, that goeth unto the holy things, which the children of Israel hallow unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, having his uncleanness upon him<small><small>[^5]</small></small>, that soul<small><small>[^6]</small></small> shall be cut off from my<small><small>[^7]</small></small> presence: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What man soever of the seed<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of Aaron \[is\] a leper<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, or hath a running issue<small><small>[^10]</small></small>; he shall not eat of the holy things<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, until<small><small>[^12]</small></small> he be clean. And whoso toucheth any thing \[that is\] unclean<small><small>[^13]</small></small> \[by\] the dead, or a man whose<small><small>[^14]</small></small> seed goeth from him; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Or whosoever<small><small>[^15]</small></small> toucheth any creeping thing, whereby he may be made unclean, or a man<small><small>[^16]</small></small> of whom he may take uncleanness, whatsoever uncleanness he hath; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The soul which hath touched any such shall be unclean until even, and shall not eat of the holy things, unless he wash his flesh with water.<small><small>[^17]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when the sun is down, he shall be clean, and shall afterward eat of the holy things; because it \[is\] his food.<small><small>[^18]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That which dieth of itself, or is torn \[with beasts\], he shall not eat to defile himself therewith: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^19]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They shall therefore keep mine ordinance, lest they bear sin for it<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and die therefore, if they profane it: I the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> do sanctify them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> There shall no stranger eat \[of\] the holy thing: a sojourner of the priest, or an hired servant, shall not eat \[of\] the holy thing.<small><small>[^21]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But if the priest buy \[any\] soul with his money<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, he shall eat of it, and he that is born in his house: they shall eat of his meat. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If the priest’s daughter also be \[married\] unto a stranger<small><small>[^23]</small></small><small><small>[^24]</small></small>, she may not eat of an offering of the holy things. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But if the priest’s daughter be a widow, or divorced, and have no child, and is returned unto her father’s house<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, as in her<small><small>[^26]</small></small> youth, she shall eat of her father’s meat: but there shall no stranger eat thereof. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And if a man eat \[of\] the holy thing unwittingly, then he shall put the fifth \[part\] thereof unto it, and shall give \[it\] unto the priest with the holy thing.<small><small>[^27]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they shall not profane the holy things of the children of Israel, which they offer unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>;<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Or suffer them to bear the iniquity of trespass, when they eat their holy things: for I<small><small>[^29]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> do sanctify them.<small><small>[^30]</small></small>

<big><big>[[Lev.22.17 Worthy Offerings|⥃]]</big></big>***Worthy Offerings***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Speak unto Aaron, and to his sons, and unto all the children of Israel, and say unto them, Whatsoever<small><small>[^31]</small></small> \[he be\] of the house of Israel, or of the strangers<small><small>[^32]</small></small> in Israel, that will offer his oblation for all his vows<small><small>[^33]</small></small>, and for all his freewill<small><small>[^34]</small></small> offerings, which they will offer unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> for a burnt offering; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Ye shall offer\] at your own will a male without blemish, of the beeves, of the sheep, or of the goats.<small><small>[^35]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[But\] whatsoever hath a blemish, \[that\] shall ye not offer: for it shall not be acceptable for you.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And whosoever offereth a sacrifice of peace<small><small>[^37]</small></small> offerings unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> to accomplish<small><small>[^38]</small></small> \[his\] vow, or a freewill offering in beeves or sheep<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, it shall be perfect<small><small>[^40]</small></small> to be accepted; there shall be no blemish therein. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Blind<small><small>[^41]</small></small>, or broken, or maimed, or having a wen, or scurvy, or scabbed, ye shall not offer these unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, nor make an offering<small><small>[^42]</small></small> by fire of them upon the altar unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Either a bullock or a lamb<small><small>[^43]</small></small> that hath any thing superfluous<small><small>[^44]</small></small> or lacking in his parts, that mayest thou offer \[for\] a freewill offering; but for a vow it shall not be accepted. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye shall not offer unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> that which is bruised, or crushed, or broken<small><small>[^45]</small></small>, or cut; neither shall ye make \[any offering thereof\] in your land. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither from a stranger’s<small><small>[^46]</small></small> hand shall ye offer the bread<small><small>[^47]</small></small> of your God of any of these; because<small><small>[^48]</small></small> their corruption \[is\] in them, \[and\] blemishes \[be\] in them: they shall not be accepted for you. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When a bullock, or a sheep, or a goat, is brought forth, then it shall be seven days<small><small>[^49]</small></small> under the dam; and from the eighth day and thenceforth it shall be accepted for an offering made by fire unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And \[whether it be\] cow or ewe<small><small>[^50]</small></small>, ye shall not kill it<small><small>[^51]</small></small> and her young both in one day. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when ye will offer a sacrifice of thanksgiving unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, offer \[it\] at your own will.<small><small>[^52]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> On the same day it shall be eaten up; ye shall leave none of it until the morrow: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^53]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.31|31]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore shall ye keep my commandments, and do them: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^54]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.32|32]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither shall ye profane my holy name; but I will<small><small>[^55]</small></small> be hallowed among the children of Israel: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> which hallow you<small><small>[^56]</small></small>,<small><small>[^57]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.22.33|33]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That brought you out of the land of Egypt, to be your God: I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <small><small>[^58]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Lev.21|<< Leviticus 21]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Lev.23|Leviticus 23 >>]]</td></tr></table>
