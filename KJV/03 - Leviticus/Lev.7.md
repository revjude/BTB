<big><big>[[Lev.7.11 The Peace Offering|⥃]]</big></big>***The Peace Offering***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Lev.3|Leviticus 3:1–17]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.7.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And this \[is\] the law of the sacrifice of peace offerings, which he shall offer unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^17]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.7.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If he offer it for a thanksgiving<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, then he shall offer with the sacrifice of thanksgiving unleavened cakes mingled with oil, and unleavened wafers<small><small>[^19]</small></small> anointed with oil, and cakes mingled with oil, of fine flour, fried. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.7.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Besides the cakes, he shall offer \[for\] his offering leavened<small><small>[^20]</small></small> bread with the sacrifice of thanksgiving of his peace offerings. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.7.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And of it he shall offer one out of the whole oblation \[for\] an heave<small><small>[^21]</small></small> offering unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, \[and\] it shall be the priest’s<small><small>[^22]</small></small> that sprinkleth the blood of the peace offerings. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.7.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the flesh of the sacrifice of his peace offerings for thanksgiving shall be eaten the same day that it is offered; <big><big>[[Lev.7.22 Fat and Blood Forbidden|⥃]]</big></big>***Fat and Blood Forbidden***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.7.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake unto Moses, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.7.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Speak unto the children of Israel, saying, Ye shall eat no manner of fat<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, of ox, or of sheep, or of goat. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.7.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the fat of the beast<small><small>[^40]</small></small> that dieth of itself, and the fat of that which is torn with beasts, may be used in any other use: but ye shall in no wise eat of it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.7.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For whosoever eateth the fat of the beast, of which men offer an offering made by fire unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, even the soul that eateth \[it\] shall be cut off<small><small>[^41]</small></small> from his people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.7.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover ye shall eat<small><small>[^42]</small></small> no manner of blood, \[whether it be\] of fowl or of beast, in any of your dwellings. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.7.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whatsoever soul \[it be\] that eateth any manner of blood, even that soul<small><small>[^43]</small></small> shall be<small><small>[^44]</small></small> cut off from his people.
