***Aaron’s First Offerings*** ^bsb290

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass on the eighth day<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, \[that\] Moses called Aaron and his sons, and the elders of Israel; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said unto Aaron, Take thee a young<small><small>[^3]</small></small> calf for a sin offering, and a ram<small><small>[^4]</small></small> for a burnt offering, without blemish, and offer \[them\] before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And unto the children of Israel thou shalt speak, saying, Take ye<small><small>[^5]</small></small> a kid<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of the goats for a sin offering; and a calf<small><small>[^7]</small></small> and a lamb, \[both<small><small>[^8]</small></small>\] of the first year, without blemish, for a burnt offering; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Also a bullock<small><small>[^9]</small></small> and a ram for peace offerings, to sacrifice before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; and a meat<small><small>[^10]</small></small> offering mingled with oil: for to day<small><small>[^11]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will appear unto you. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And they brought \[that\] which Moses commanded before the tabernacle of the congregation: and all the congregation<small><small>[^12]</small></small> drew near and stood before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses said, This \[is\] the thing which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> commanded that ye should do: and the glory<small><small>[^13]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall appear unto you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses said unto Aaron, Go unto the altar, and offer thy<small><small>[^14]</small></small> sin offering, and thy burnt offering, and make an atonement for thyself, and for the people: and offer the<small><small>[^15]</small></small> offering of the people, and make an atonement for them; as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> commanded. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Aaron therefore went unto the altar, and slew the calf of the sin offering, which \[was\] for himself.<small><small>[^16]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sons of Aaron brought the blood unto him: and he dipped his finger in the blood, and put \[it\] upon the horns of the altar, and poured out the blood at the bottom of the altar:<small><small>[^17]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the fat<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, and the kidneys, and the caul above the liver of the sin offering, he burnt upon the altar; as the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^19]</small></small></span> commanded Moses. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the flesh and the hide he burnt with fire without the camp.<small><small>[^20]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he slew the burnt offering; and Aaron’s sons presented unto him the blood, which he sprinkled round about upon the altar.<small><small>[^21]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they presented the burnt offering unto him, with the pieces thereof, and the head: and he burnt \[them\] upon the altar. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he did wash the inwards and the legs, and burnt \[them\] upon the burnt offering on the altar.<small><small>[^22]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And he brought the people’s offering, and took the goat, which \[was\] the sin offering for the people, and slew it, and offered it for sin, as the first.<small><small>[^23]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he brought the burnt offering, and offered it according to the manner<small><small>[^24]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he brought the meat<small><small>[^25]</small></small> offering, and took an handful thereof<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, and burnt \[it\] upon the altar, beside the burnt sacrifice of the morning. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He slew also the bullock and the ram \[for\] a sacrifice<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of peace offerings, which \[was\] for the people: and Aaron’s sons presented unto him the blood, which he sprinkled upon the altar round about, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the fat of the bullock and of the ram, the rump, and that which covereth \[the inwards\], and the kidneys, and the caul \[above\] the liver:<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they put<small><small>[^29]</small></small> the fat upon the breasts, and he burnt<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the fat upon the altar: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the breasts<small><small>[^31]</small></small> and the right shoulder Aaron waved \[for\] a wave offering before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; as Moses commanded<small><small>[^32]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Aaron lifted up his hand<small><small>[^33]</small></small> toward the people, and blessed them, and came down from offering of the sin offering, and the burnt offering, and peace offerings. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Moses and Aaron went into the tabernacle of the congregation, and came out<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, and blessed the people: and the glory<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> appeared unto all the people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Lev.9.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And there came a fire<small><small>[^36]</small></small> out from before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and consumed upon the altar the burnt offering and the fat: \[which\] when all the people saw, they shouted<small><small>[^37]</small></small>, and fell on their faces. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Lev.8|<< Leviticus 8]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Lev.10|Leviticus 10 >>]]</td></tr></table>
