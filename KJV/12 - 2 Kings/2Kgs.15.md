$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the thirty<small><small>[^21]</small></small> and eighth year of Azariah king of Judah did Zachariah<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the son of Jeroboam reign over Israel in Samaria six months.<small><small>[^23]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he did \[that which was\] evil in the sight of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, as his<small><small>[^24]</small></small> fathers had done: he departed not from the sins of Jeroboam the son of Nebat, who made Israel to sin. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Shallum the son of Jabesh conspired against him, and smote him<small><small>[^25]</small></small> before the people, and slew him<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, and reigned in his stead.<small><small>[^27]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the rest of the acts of Zachariah, behold, they \[are\] written in the book of the chronicles of the kings of Israel.<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This \[was\] the word<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> which he spake unto Jehu, saying, Thy sons<small><small>[^30]</small></small> shall sit on the throne of Israel unto the fourth \[generation\]. $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Shallum the son of Jabesh began to reign in the nine and thirtieth year of Uzziah<small><small>[^33]</small></small> king of Judah; and he reigned a full month<small><small>[^34]</small></small> in Samaria.<small><small>[^35]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For Menahem the son of Gadi went up from Tirzah<small><small>[^36]</small></small>, and came to Samaria, and smote<small><small>[^37]</small></small> Shallum the son of Jabesh in Samaria, and slew him, and reigned in his stead. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the rest of the acts of Shallum, and his conspiracy which he made, behold, they \[are\] written in the book of the chronicles of the kings of Israel.<small><small>[^38]</small></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the nine<small><small>[^41]</small></small> and thirtieth year of Azariah king of Judah began Menahem the son of Gadi to reign over Israel, \[and reigned\] ten years in Samaria.<small><small>[^42]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he did \[that which was\] evil in the sight of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: he departed not all his days from the sins of Jeroboam the son of Nebat, who made Israel to sin.<small><small>[^43]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[And\] Pul<small><small>[^44]</small></small> the king of Assyria came against the land: and Menahem<small><small>[^45]</small></small> gave Pul a thousand talents of silver, that his hand might be with him to confirm<small><small>[^46]</small></small> the kingdom in his hand.<small><small>[^47]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Menahem<small><small>[^48]</small></small> exacted<small><small>[^49]</small></small> the money of Israel, \[even\] of all the mighty<small><small>[^50]</small></small> men of wealth, of each man<small><small>[^51]</small></small> fifty shekels of silver, to give to the king of Assyria. $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]In the fiftieth year of Azariah king of Judah Pekahiah the son of Menahem began to reign over Israel in Samaria, \[and reigned\] two years<small><small>[^54]</small></small>.<small><small>[^55]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he did \[that which was\] evil in the sight of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: he departed not from the sins of Jeroboam the son of Nebat, who made Israel to sin.<small><small>[^56]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Pekah<small><small>[^57]</small></small> the son of Remaliah, a captain<small><small>[^58]</small></small> of his, conspired<small><small>[^59]</small></small> against him, and smote him in Samaria, in the palace of the king’s house, with Argob<small><small>[^60]</small></small> and Arieh, and with him fifty men of the Gileadites: and he killed him, and reigned in his room.<small><small>[^61]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.15.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the rest of the acts of Pekahiah, and all that he did, behold, they \[are\] written in the book of the chronicles of the kings of Israel.<small><small>[^62]</small></small>
