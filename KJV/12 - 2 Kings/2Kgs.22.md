<big><big>[[2Kgs.22.1 Josiah Reigns in Judah|⥃]]</big></big>***Josiah Reigns in Judah***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Chr.34|2 Chronicles 34:1–2]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Josiah<small><small>[^2]</small></small> \[was\] eight years old<small><small>[^3]</small></small> when he began to reign, and he reigned thirty and one years in Jerusalem. And his mother’s name \[was\] Jedidah, the daughter of Adaiah of Boscath<small><small>[^4]</small></small>.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he did \[that which was\] right<small><small>[^6]</small></small> in the sight of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and walked<small><small>[^7]</small></small> in all the way of David his father, and turned<small><small>[^8]</small></small> not aside to the right hand or to the left.

<big><big>[[2Kgs.22.3 Funding the Temple Repairs|⥃]]</big></big>***Funding the Temple Repairs***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Chr.34|2 Chronicles 34:8–13]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And it came to pass in the<small><small>[^9]</small></small> eighteenth year of king Josiah, \[that\] the king sent Shaphan the son of Azaliah, the son of Meshullam, the scribe, to the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, saying,<small><small>[^10]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Go up to Hilkiah<small><small>[^11]</small></small> the high priest, that he may<small><small>[^12]</small></small> sum the silver<small><small>[^13]</small></small> which is brought into the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, which the keepers<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of the door<small><small>[^15]</small></small> have gathered of the people: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And let them deliver<small><small>[^16]</small></small> it into the hand of the doers of the work, that have the oversight of the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and let them give it to the doers of the work which \[is\] in the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, to repair<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the breaches of the house, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Unto carpenters<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, and builders<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, and masons<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and to buy timber and hewn stone to repair the house. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Howbeit<small><small>[^21]</small></small> there was no reckoning made with them of the money that was delivered into their hand, because they dealt faithfully<small><small>[^22]</small></small>.

<big><big>[[2Kgs.22.8 Hilkiah Finds the Book of the Law|⥃]]</big></big>***Hilkiah Finds the Book of the Law***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Chr.34|2 Chronicles 34:14–21]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Hilkiah the high priest said unto Shaphan the scribe, I have found<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the book of the law in the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. And Hilkiah gave the book to Shaphan, and he read it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Shaphan<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the scribe<small><small>[^25]</small></small> came to the king, and brought the king word again, and said, Thy servants have gathered<small><small>[^26]</small></small> the money that was found in the house, and have delivered it into the hand of them that do the work, that have the oversight of the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Shaphan<small><small>[^27]</small></small> the scribe shewed the king<small><small>[^28]</small></small>, saying, Hilkiah the priest hath delivered me a book. And Shaphan read it before the king. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when the king had heard the words of the book of the law, that he rent<small><small>[^29]</small></small> his clothes. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the king<small><small>[^30]</small></small> commanded Hilkiah the priest, and Ahikam<small><small>[^31]</small></small> the son of Shaphan, and Achbor<small><small>[^32]</small></small> the son of Michaiah<small><small>[^33]</small></small>, and Shaphan the scribe, and Asahiah a servant of the king’s, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Go ye, enquire<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> for me, and for the people, and for all Judah, concerning the words of this book that is found: for great<small><small>[^35]</small></small> \[is\] the wrath of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> that is kindled against us, because our fathers<small><small>[^36]</small></small> have not hearkened unto the words of this book, to do according unto all that which is written concerning us.

<big><big>[[2Kgs.22.14 Huldah’s Prophecy|⥃]]</big></big>***Huldah’s Prophecy***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Chr.34|2 Chronicles 34:22–28]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Hilkiah the priest, and Ahikam, and Achbor, and Shaphan, and Asahiah, went unto Huldah the prophetess<small><small>[^37]</small></small>, the wife of Shallum the son of Tikvah<small><small>[^38]</small></small>, the son of Harhas, keeper of the wardrobe<small><small>[^39]</small></small>; (now she dwelt in Jerusalem in the college<small><small>[^40]</small></small>;) and they communed with her. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And she said unto them, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel, Tell the man that sent you to me,<small><small>[^41]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, Behold<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, I will bring evil upon this place, and upon the inhabitants thereof, \[even\] all the words<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of the book which the king of Judah hath read: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because they have forsaken<small><small>[^44]</small></small> me, and have burned incense unto other gods, that they might provoke me to anger with all the works<small><small>[^45]</small></small> of their hands; therefore<small><small>[^46]</small></small> my wrath shall be kindled against this place, and shall not be<small><small>[^47]</small></small> quenched. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But to the king<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of Judah which sent you to enquire of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, thus shall ye<small><small>[^49]</small></small> say to him, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel, \[As touching\] the words which thou hast heard; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because thine heart<small><small>[^50]</small></small> was tender, and thou hast humbled<small><small>[^51]</small></small> thyself before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, when thou heardest what I spake against this place, and against the inhabitants thereof, that they should become a desolation<small><small>[^52]</small></small> and a curse, and hast rent<small><small>[^53]</small></small> thy clothes, and wept<small><small>[^54]</small></small> before me; I also have<small><small>[^55]</small></small> heard \[thee\], saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.22.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold therefore, I will gather<small><small>[^56]</small></small> thee unto thy fathers, and thou shalt<small><small>[^57]</small></small> be gathered<small><small>[^58]</small></small> into thy grave in peace; and thine eyes shall not see all the evil which I will bring upon this place.
