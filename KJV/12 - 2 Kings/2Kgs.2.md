<big><big>[[2Kgs.2.1 Elijah Taken Up to Heaven|⥃]]</big></big>***Elijah Taken Up to Heaven***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> would take up<small><small>[^2]</small></small> Elijah into heaven by a whirlwind<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, that Elijah went with Elisha<small><small>[^4]</small></small> from Gilgal<small><small>[^5]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Elijah said unto Elisha, Tarry here<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, I pray thee; for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath sent me to Beth–el. And Elisha said \[unto him\], \[As\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^7]</small></small></span> liveth, and \[as\] thy soul liveth, I will not<small><small>[^8]</small></small> leave thee. So they went down to Beth–el<small><small>[^9]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sons<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of the prophets that \[were\] at Beth–el came forth to Elisha, and said unto him, Knowest thou that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will take away thy master<small><small>[^11]</small></small> from thy head to day? And he said, Yea, I know \[it\]; hold ye your peace. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Elijah said unto him, Elisha, tarry here, I pray thee; for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath sent me to Jericho<small><small>[^12]</small></small>. And he said, \[As\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^13]</small></small></span> liveth, and \[as\] thy soul liveth, I will not leave thee. So they came to Jericho. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sons<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of the prophets that \[were\] at Jericho came to Elisha, and said unto him, Knowest thou that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will take away thy master<small><small>[^15]</small></small> from thy head to day? And he answered, Yea, I know \[it<small><small>[^16]</small></small>\]; hold ye your peace. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Elijah said unto him, Tarry, I pray thee, here; for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath sent me to Jordan. And he said, \[As\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> liveth, and \[as\] thy soul liveth, I will not leave thee. And they two went on. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And fifty men<small><small>[^17]</small></small> of the sons of the prophets went, and stood to view afar off<small><small>[^18]</small></small>: and they two stood by Jordan. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Elijah took his mantle<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, and wrapped \[it\] together, and smote the waters, and they were<small><small>[^20]</small></small> divided hither and thither, so that they two went over on dry ground. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when they were gone over, that Elijah said unto Elisha, Ask what<small><small>[^21]</small></small> I shall do for thee, before I be taken away from thee. And Elisha said<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, I pray thee, let a double portion<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of thy spirit be upon me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Thou hast<small><small>[^24]</small></small> asked a hard thing<small><small>[^25]</small></small>: \[nevertheless\], if thou see<small><small>[^26]</small></small> me \[when I am\] taken from thee, it shall be so unto thee; but if not, it shall not be \[so\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, as they still went on, and talked, that, behold, \[there appeared\] a chariot of fire, and horses of fire, and parted them both asunder; and Elijah went up by a whirlwind<small><small>[^27]</small></small> into heaven<small><small>[^28]</small></small>.<small><small>[^29]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Elisha saw \[it<small><small>[^30]</small></small>\], and he cried, My father<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, my father, the chariot of Israel, and the horsemen thereof. <big><big>[[2Kgs.2.15 Elisha Succeeds Elijah|⥃]]</big></big>***Elisha Succeeds Elijah***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when the sons of the prophets which \[were\] to view<small><small>[^38]</small></small> at Jericho saw him, they said, The spirit<small><small>[^39]</small></small> of Elijah doth rest on Elisha. And they came to meet him, and bowed<small><small>[^40]</small></small> themselves to the ground before him. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And they said unto him, Behold now, there be with thy servants fifty strong men<small><small>[^41]</small></small>; let them go, we pray thee, and seek thy master: lest peradventure the Spirit<small><small>[^42]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath taken him up, and cast him upon some mountain<small><small>[^43]</small></small>, or into some valley. <big><big>[[2Kgs.2.19 Elisha Heals the Waters of Jericho|⥃]]</big></big>***Elisha Heals the Waters of Jericho***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the men of the city said unto Elisha, Behold, I pray thee, the situation of this city \[is\] pleasant, as my lord seeth<small><small>[^46]</small></small>: but the water<small><small>[^47]</small></small> \[is\] naught, and the ground barren<small><small>[^48]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Bring me a new cruse, and put salt therein<small><small>[^49]</small></small>. And they brought \[it\] to him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he went forth unto the spring of the waters, and cast<small><small>[^50]</small></small> the salt in there, and said, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, I have healed<small><small>[^51]</small></small> these waters; there shall<small><small>[^52]</small></small> not be from thence any more death or barren \[land\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.2.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So the waters were healed unto this day, according to the saying of Elisha which he spake.
