<big><big>[[2Kgs.14.1 Amaziah Reigns in Judah|⥃]]</big></big>***Amaziah Reigns in Judah***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Chr.25|2 Chronicles 25:1–4]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.14.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the second year of Joash<small><small>[^2]</small></small> son of Jehoahaz king of Israel reigned Amaziah<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the son of Joash king of Judah.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.14.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He was twenty and five years old when he began to reign, and reigned twenty and nine years in Jerusalem. <big><big>[[2Kgs.14.8 Jehoash Defeats Amaziah|⥃]]</big></big>***Jehoash Defeats Amaziah***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Chr.25|2 Chronicles 25:17–24]] ]</sup></small>
