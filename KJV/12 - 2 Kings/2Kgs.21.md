<big><big>[[2Kgs.21.1 Manasseh Reigns in Judah|⥃]]</big></big>***Manasseh Reigns in Judah***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Chr.33|2 Chronicles 33:1–9]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Manasseh \[was\] twelve<small><small>[^2]</small></small> years old when he began to reign, and reigned fifty and five years in Jerusalem. And his mother’s name \[was\] Hephzi–bah<small><small>[^3]</small></small>.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he did<small><small>[^5]</small></small> \[that which was\] evil in the sight of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, after the abominations<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of the heathen, whom the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> cast out before the children of Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he built up again the high places<small><small>[^7]</small></small> which Hezekiah his father had destroyed; and he reared<small><small>[^8]</small></small> up altars for Baal, and made a grove<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, as did Ahab<small><small>[^10]</small></small> king of Israel; and worshipped<small><small>[^11]</small></small> all the host of heaven, and served them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he built<small><small>[^12]</small></small> altars in the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, of which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said, In Jerusalem<small><small>[^13]</small></small> will I put my name. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he built altars for all the host of heaven in the two courts<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he made<small><small>[^15]</small></small> his son pass through the fire, and observed times<small><small>[^16]</small></small>, and used enchantments, and dealt with familiar<small><small>[^17]</small></small> spirits and wizards: he wrought<small><small>[^18]</small></small> much wickedness in the sight of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, to provoke \[him\] to anger.<small><small>[^19]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he set<small><small>[^20]</small></small> a graven image of the grove that he had made in the house, of which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said to David, and to Solomon his son, In this house<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, and in Jerusalem, which I have chosen out of all tribes of Israel, will I put my name for ever:<small><small>[^22]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither will I make<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the feet of Israel move any more out of the land which I gave their fathers; only if they<small><small>[^24]</small></small> will observe to do according to all that I have commanded them, and according to all the law that my servant Moses commanded them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But they hearkened<small><small>[^25]</small></small> not: and Manasseh seduced<small><small>[^26]</small></small> them to do more evil<small><small>[^27]</small></small> than did the nations whom the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> destroyed before the children of Israel.

<big><big>[[2Kgs.21.10 Manasseh’s Idolatries Rebuked|⥃]]</big></big>***Manasseh’s Idolatries Rebuked***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Chr.33|2 Chronicles 33:10–20]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake by his servants the prophets, saying,<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because<small><small>[^29]</small></small> Manasseh king of Judah hath done these abominations, \[and\] hath done wickedly above all<small><small>[^30]</small></small> that the Amorites did, which \[were\] before him, and hath made Judah<small><small>[^31]</small></small> also to sin with his idols: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel, Behold, I \[am\] bringing<small><small>[^32]</small></small> \[such\] evil upon Jerusalem and Judah, that whosoever<small><small>[^33]</small></small> heareth of it, both his ears shall tingle. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will stretch<small><small>[^34]</small></small> over Jerusalem the line of Samaria, and the plummet<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of the house of Ahab: and I will wipe<small><small>[^36]</small></small> Jerusalem as \[a man\] wipeth a dish, wiping \[it\], and turning \[it\] upside down<small><small>[^37]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will<small><small>[^38]</small></small> forsake the remnant<small><small>[^39]</small></small> of mine inheritance, and deliver<small><small>[^40]</small></small> them into the hand of their enemies; and they shall become a prey and a spoil to all their enemies; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because they have done \[that which was\] evil in my sight, and have provoked me to anger, since the day<small><small>[^41]</small></small> their fathers came forth out of Egypt, even unto this day. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover Manasseh<small><small>[^42]</small></small> shed innocent blood very much, till he had filled Jerusalem from one end to another<small><small>[^43]</small></small>; beside his sin<small><small>[^44]</small></small> wherewith he made Judah to sin, in doing \[that which was\] evil in the sight of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Now the rest<small><small>[^45]</small></small> of the acts of Manasseh, and all that he did, and his sin that he sinned, \[are\] they not written in the book of the chronicles of the kings of Judah? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Manasseh slept with his fathers, and was buried<small><small>[^46]</small></small> in the garden of his own house, in the garden of Uzza: and Amon his son reigned in his stead.<small><small>[^47]</small></small>

<big><big>[[2Kgs.21.19 Amon Reigns in Judah|⥃]]</big></big>***Amon Reigns in Judah***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Chr.33|2 Chronicles 33:21–25]] ]</sup></small>

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Amon<small><small>[^48]</small></small> \[was\] twenty and two years<small><small>[^49]</small></small> old when he began to reign, and he reigned two years in Jerusalem. And his mother’s name \[was\] Meshullemeth, the daughter of Haruz of Jotbah.<small><small>[^50]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he did \[that which was\] evil in the sight of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, as his father<small><small>[^51]</small></small> Manasseh did. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he walked in all the way that his father walked in, and served the idols that his father served, and worshipped them: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.21.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he forsook the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of his fathers, and walked not in the way of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^52]</small></small>
