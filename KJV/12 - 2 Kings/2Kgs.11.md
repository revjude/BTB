***The Death of Athaliah***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Chr.23|2 Chronicles 23:12–15]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb919

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.11.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when Athaliah heard the noise of the guard \[and\] of the people, she came to the people into the temple of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^39]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.11.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when she looked, behold, the king stood<small><small>[^40]</small></small> by a pillar<small><small>[^41]</small></small>, as the manner \[was\], and the princes<small><small>[^42]</small></small> and the trumpeters by the king, and all the people<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of the land rejoiced, and blew with trumpets: and Athaliah rent her clothes, and cried, Treason<small><small>[^44]</small></small>, Treason.<small><small>[^45]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.11.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Jehoiada the priest commanded the captains<small><small>[^46]</small></small> of the hundreds, the officers of the host, and said unto them, Have<small><small>[^47]</small></small> her forth without the ranges: and him that followeth<small><small>[^48]</small></small> her kill with the sword. For the priest had said, Let<small><small>[^49]</small></small> her not be slain in the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.11.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they laid hands on her; and she went by the way by the which<small><small>[^50]</small></small> the horses came into the king’s house: and there was she slain<small><small>[^51]</small></small>.
