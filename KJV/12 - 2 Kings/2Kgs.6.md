 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.6.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the sons<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of the prophets said unto Elisha, Behold now, the place<small><small>[^3]</small></small> where we dwell with thee is too strait for us<small><small>[^4]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.6.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let us go, we pray thee, unto Jordan, and take thence<small><small>[^5]</small></small> every man a beam, and let us make us a place there, where we may dwell. $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[2Kgs.6.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Go and spy where<small><small>[^29]</small></small> he \[is\], that I may send and fetch him.
