<big><big>[[1Sam.16.1 Samuel Anoints David|⥃]]</big></big>***Samuel Anoints David***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Samuel, How long<small><small>[^2]</small></small> wilt thou mourn for Saul, seeing<small><small>[^3]</small></small> I have rejected him from reigning over Israel? fill thine horn with oil<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, and go, I will send thee to Jesse<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the Beth–lehemite: for I have provided me a king among his sons.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Samuel said, How can I go<small><small>[^7]</small></small>? if Saul hear \[it\], he will kill me. And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said, Take an heifer<small><small>[^8]</small></small> with thee<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, and say, I am come<small><small>[^10]</small></small> to sacrifice to the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And call Jesse<small><small>[^11]</small></small> to the sacrifice, and I will shew<small><small>[^12]</small></small> thee what thou shalt do: and thou shalt anoint<small><small>[^13]</small></small> unto me \[him\] whom I name unto thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Samuel did that which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> spake, and came to Beth–lehem. And the elders of the town trembled<small><small>[^14]</small></small> at his coming<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, and said, Comest<small><small>[^16]</small></small> thou peaceably? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he said, Peaceably: I am come to sacrifice unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: sanctify yourselves<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, and come with me to the sacrifice. And he sanctified Jesse and his sons, and called them to the sacrifice. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when they were come, that he looked on Eliab<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, and said, Surely<small><small>[^19]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span> anointed \[is\] before him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto Samuel, Look not<small><small>[^20]</small></small> on his countenance, or on the height of his stature; because I have refused him: for \[the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> seeth\] not<small><small>[^21]</small></small> as man seeth; for man looketh<small><small>[^22]</small></small> on the outward appearance<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, but the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> looketh on the heart<small><small>[^24]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Jesse called Abinadab<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, and made him pass before Samuel. And he said, Neither hath the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> chosen this. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Jesse made Shammah<small><small>[^26]</small></small> to pass by. And he said, Neither hath the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> chosen this. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Again, Jesse made seven<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of his sons to pass before Samuel. And Samuel said unto Jesse, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath not chosen these. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Samuel said unto Jesse, Are here all \[thy\] children? And he said, There remaineth<small><small>[^28]</small></small> yet the youngest, and, behold, he keepeth the sheep. And Samuel said unto Jesse, Send and fetch him: for we will not sit down<small><small>[^29]</small></small> till he come hither. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he sent, and brought him in. Now he \[was\] ruddy<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, \[and\] withal of a beautiful countenance<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, and goodly to look to. And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^32]</small></small></span> said, Arise, anoint him<small><small>[^33]</small></small>: for this \[is\] he. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Samuel took the horn of oil, and anointed<small><small>[^34]</small></small> him in the midst of his brethren: and the Spirit<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came upon David from that day forward. So Samuel rose up, and went to Ramah.

<big><big>[[1Sam.16.14 David Serves Saul|⥃]]</big></big>***David Serves Saul***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the Spirit<small><small>[^36]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> departed from Saul, and an evil spirit<small><small>[^37]</small></small> from the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> troubled<small><small>[^38]</small></small> him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Saul’s servants said unto him, Behold now, an evil spirit from God troubleth thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let our lord now command thy servants, \[which are\] before thee<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, to seek out a man, \[who is\] a cunning play<small><small>[^40]</small></small>er on an harp: and it shall come to pass, when the evil spirit from God is upon thee, that he shall play with his hand, and thou shalt be well. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Saul said unto his servants, Provide me now a man that can play well, and bring \[him\] to me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[1Sam.16.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then answered one of the servants, and said, Behold, I have seen a son of Jesse the Beth–lehemite, \[that is\] cunning in playing, and a mighty<small><small>[^41]</small></small> valiant man, and a man of war, and prudent<small><small>[^42]</small></small> in matters<small><small>[^43]</small></small>, and a comely<small><small>[^44]</small></small> person, and the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^45]</small></small></span> \[is\] with him.
