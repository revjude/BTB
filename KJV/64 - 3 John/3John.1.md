***A Greeting from the Elder***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2John.1|2 John 1:1–3]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb3018

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The elder<small><small>[^2]</small></small> unto the wellbeloved<small><small>[^3]</small></small> Gaius, whom<small><small>[^4]</small></small> I love in the truth<small><small>[^5]</small></small>.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Beloved, I wish<small><small>[^7]</small></small> above<small><small>[^8]</small></small> all things that<small><small>[^9]</small></small> thou mayest prosper and be in health, even<small><small>[^10]</small></small> as thy soul prospereth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I<small><small>[^11]</small></small> rejoiced greatly, when<small><small>[^12]</small></small> the brethren came and testified of the truth<small><small>[^13]</small></small> that is in thee, even as thou walkest in the truth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have<small><small>[^14]</small></small> no greater joy than to hear that<small><small>[^15]</small></small> my children walk<small><small>[^16]</small></small> in truth.

***Gaius Commended for Hospitality*** ^bsb3019

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Beloved, thou doest faithfully whatsoever thou doest to the brethren, and to strangers;<small><small>[^17]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which have borne witness of thy charity<small><small>[^18]</small></small> before the church: whom<small><small>[^19]</small></small> if thou bring forward on their journey after a godly sort<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, thou shalt do well<small><small>[^21]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because that<small><small>[^22]</small></small> for his name’s sake they went forth, taking<small><small>[^23]</small></small> nothing of the Gentiles. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> We therefore ought to receive<small><small>[^24]</small></small> such, that we might be fellowhelpers<small><small>[^25]</small></small> to the truth.

***Diotrephes and Demetrius*** ^bsb3020

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I wrote unto the church: but Diotrephes, who loveth<small><small>[^26]</small></small> to have the preeminence among them, receiveth<small><small>[^27]</small></small> us not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore, if I come, I will<small><small>[^28]</small></small> remember his deeds which he doeth, prating<small><small>[^29]</small></small> against us with malicious words: and not content therewith, neither doth he himself receive the brethren, and forbiddeth them that would, and casteth<small><small>[^30]</small></small> \[them\] out of the church. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Beloved<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, follow<small><small>[^32]</small></small> not that which is evil, but that which is good. He that doeth good<small><small>[^33]</small></small> is of God: but he that doeth evil<small><small>[^34]</small></small> hath not seen God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[3John.1.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Demetrius hath good<small><small>[^35]</small></small> report of all \[men\], and of the truth itself: yea, and we<small><small>[^36]</small></small> \[also\] bear record; and ye know that our record is true.

***Conclusion***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2John.1|2 John 1:12–13]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb3021
