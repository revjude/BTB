<big><big>[[Eph.3.1 The Mystery of the Gospel|⥃]]</big></big>***The Mystery of the Gospel***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thi<small><small>[^2]</small></small>s cause I Paul, the<small><small>[^3]</small></small> prisoner of Jesus Christ for<small><small>[^4]</small></small> you Gentiles, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If ye<small><small>[^5]</small></small> have heard of the dispensation<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of the grace of God which is given me to you-ward: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> How that by<small><small>[^7]</small></small> revelation he made known unto me the mystery<small><small>[^8]</small></small>; (as I<small><small>[^9]</small></small> wrote afore<small><small>[^10]</small></small> in few words, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whereby, when ye read, ye may<small><small>[^11]</small></small> understand my knowledge in the mystery<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of Christ) <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which in other<small><small>[^13]</small></small> ages was not made known unto the sons of men, as it<small><small>[^14]</small></small> is now revealed unto his holy apostles and prophets by<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the Spirit; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That the Gentiles<small><small>[^16]</small></small> should be fellowheirs, and of the same<small><small>[^17]</small></small> body, and partakers<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of his promise in Christ by the gospel: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whereof I<small><small>[^19]</small></small> was made a minister, according<small><small>[^20]</small></small> to the gift of the grace of God given unto me by<small><small>[^21]</small></small> the effectual working of his power. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Unto me, who am<small><small>[^22]</small></small> less than the least of all saints, i<small><small>[^23]</small></small>s this<small><small>[^24]</small></small> grace given, that I should preach among the Gentiles the unsearchable<small><small>[^25]</small></small> riches of Christ; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And to<small><small>[^26]</small></small> make all \[men\] see what \[is\] the fellowship<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of the mystery, which from the beginning<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of the world hath been hid<small><small>[^29]</small></small> in God, who created<small><small>[^30]</small></small> all things by Jesus Christ: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To the intent<small><small>[^31]</small></small> that now unto the prin<small><small>[^32]</small></small>cipalities<small><small>[^33]</small></small> and powers in heavenly \[places\] might be known by the church the manifold<small><small>[^34]</small></small> wisdom of God, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> According to the eternal purpose which he purposed in Christ Jesus our Lord:<small><small>[^35]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In whom we have boldness and access with confidence by the faith of him.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore I desire that ye<small><small>[^37]</small></small> faint not at<small><small>[^38]</small></small> my tribulations for you, which is your glory.

<big><big>[[Eph.3.14 Paul’s Prayer for the Ephesians|⥃]]</big></big>***Paul’s Prayer for the Ephesians***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thi<small><small>[^39]</small></small>s cause I bow my knees unto the Father<small><small>[^40]</small></small> of our Lord Jesus Christ, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Of whom the whole<small><small>[^41]</small></small> family in heaven and earth is<small><small>[^42]</small></small> named, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That he would grant you, according<small><small>[^43]</small></small> to the riches of his glory, to be<small><small>[^44]</small></small> strengthened with might by his Spirit in the inner<small><small>[^45]</small></small> man; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That Christ<small><small>[^46]</small></small> may dwell in your hearts by faith; that ye, being<small><small>[^47]</small></small> rooted and grounded<small><small>[^48]</small></small> in love, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> May be able<small><small>[^49]</small></small> to comprehend with<small><small>[^50]</small></small> all saints what<small><small>[^51]</small></small> \[is\] the breadth, and length, and depth, and height; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And to know<small><small>[^52]</small></small> the love of Christ, which passeth<small><small>[^53]</small></small> knowledge, that ye<small><small>[^54]</small></small> might be filled with all the fulness of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now unto him that is able<small><small>[^55]</small></small> to do exceeding<small><small>[^56]</small></small> abundantly above all that we ask or think, according<small><small>[^57]</small></small> to the power that worketh in us, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.3.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Unto him \[be<small><small>[^58]</small></small>\] glory in the church by<small><small>[^59]</small></small> Christ Jesus throughout<small><small>[^60]</small></small> all ages, world without end.
