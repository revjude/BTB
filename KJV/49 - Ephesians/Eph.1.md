<big><big>[[Eph.1.1 Paul’s Greeting to the Ephesians|⥃]]</big></big>***Paul’s Greeting to the Ephesians***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Acts.19|Acts 19:8–12]] <big>†</big> [[Rev.2|Revelation 2:1–7]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.1.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Paul, an<small><small>[^2]</small></small> apostle of Jesus Christ by the will of God, to the saints<small><small>[^3]</small></small> which<small><small>[^4]</small></small> are at Ephesus, and to the faithful<small><small>[^5]</small></small> in Christ Jesus: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.1.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Grace \[be\] to you, and peace, from God our Father, and \[from\] the Lord Jesus Christ.<small><small>[^6]</small></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.1.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Blessed<small><small>[^7]</small></small> \[be\] the God<small><small>[^8]</small></small> and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who<small><small>[^9]</small></small> hath blessed us with all spiritual blessings in heavenly<small><small>[^10]</small></small> \[places<small><small>[^11]</small></small>\] in Christ<small><small>[^12]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.1.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> According as<small><small>[^13]</small></small> he hath chosen us in him before<small><small>[^14]</small></small> the foundation of the world, that<small><small>[^15]</small></small> we should be holy and without<small><small>[^16]</small></small> blame before him in love<small><small>[^17]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.1.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Having predestinated<small><small>[^18]</small></small> us unto<small><small>[^19]</small></small> the adoption of children by<small><small>[^20]</small></small> Jesus Christ to himself, according<small><small>[^21]</small></small> to the good pleasure of his will, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.1.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To the praise<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of the<small><small>[^23]</small></small> glory of his grace, wherein<small><small>[^24]</small></small> he hath made us accepted in the beloved. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.1.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In whom<small><small>[^25]</small></small> we have redemption through his blood, the forgiveness<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of sins, according to<small><small>[^27]</small></small> the riches of his grace; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.1.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Having made<small><small>[^30]</small></small> known unto us the mystery of his will, according<small><small>[^31]</small></small> to his good pleasure which he hath purposed<small><small>[^32]</small></small> in himself: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.1.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That in the<small><small>[^33]</small></small><small><small>[^34]</small></small> dispensation of the fulness of times he might gather together in one all things in Christ, both which are in heaven<small><small>[^35]</small></small>, and which are on earth;
