<big><big>[[Eph.6.1 Children and Parents|⥃]]</big></big>***Children and Parents***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Col.3|Colossians 3:18–21]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Children, obey<small><small>[^2]</small></small> your parents in<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the Lord: for<small><small>[^4]</small></small> this is right. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Honour thy father and mother; (which is the first commandment with promise;)<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That it may be well with thee, and thou mayest live long on the earth.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And, ye<small><small>[^7]</small></small> fathers, provoke not your children to wrath: but<small><small>[^8]</small></small> bring them up in the nurture and admonition of the Lord.

<big><big>[[Eph.6.5 Serving with Honor|⥃]]</big></big>***Serving with Honor***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Col.3|Colossians 3:22–25]] <big>†</big> [[1Tim.6|1 Timothy 6:1–2]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Servants, be<small><small>[^9]</small></small> obedient to them that are \[your\] masters according<small><small>[^10]</small></small> to the flesh, with<small><small>[^11]</small></small> fear and tremblin<small><small>[^12]</small></small>g, in singleness of your heart, as<small><small>[^13]</small></small> unto Christ; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Not with eyeservice<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, as menpleasers; but as the servants of Christ, doing<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the will of God from<small><small>[^16]</small></small> the heart; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> With good<small><small>[^17]</small></small> will doing service, as<small><small>[^18]</small></small> to the Lord, and not to men: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Knowing that whatsoever<small><small>[^19]</small></small> good thing any man doeth, the same shall he receive of the Lord, whether<small><small>[^20]</small></small> \[he be\] bond or free. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And, ye<small><small>[^21]</small></small> masters, do the same<small><small>[^22]</small></small> things unto them, forbearing<small><small>[^23]</small></small> threatening: knowing<small><small>[^24]</small></small> that your Master<small><small>[^25]</small></small> also is in heaven; neither<small><small>[^26]</small></small> is there respect of persons with him.

<big><big>[[Eph.6.10 The Full Armor of God|⥃]]</big></big>***The Full Armor of God***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Finally<small><small>[^27]</small></small>, my brethren, be<small><small>[^28]</small></small> strong in the Lord, and in the power of his might. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Put<small><small>[^29]</small></small> on the whole<small><small>[^30]</small></small> armour of God, that ye may be able<small><small>[^31]</small></small> to stand against the wiles<small><small>[^32]</small></small> of the devil. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For we wrestle<small><small>[^33]</small></small> not against flesh and blood<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, but against principalities<small><small>[^35]</small></small>, against powers, against the<small><small>[^36]</small></small> rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness<small><small>[^37]</small></small> in high<small><small>[^38]</small></small> \[places\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore take<small><small>[^39]</small></small> unto you the whole<small><small>[^40]</small></small> armour of God, that ye may be able to withstand in the<small><small>[^41]</small></small> evil day, and having done all<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, to stand<small><small>[^43]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Stand therefore, having<small><small>[^44]</small></small> your loins girt about with truth, and having on the breastplate<small><small>[^45]</small></small> of righteousness; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And your<small><small>[^46]</small></small> feet shod with the preparation of the gospel<small><small>[^47]</small></small> of peace; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Above all, taking the shield<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of faith, wherewith ye shall be able to quench<small><small>[^49]</small></small> all the fiery darts of the wicked. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And take the helmet<small><small>[^50]</small></small> of salvation, and the sword<small><small>[^51]</small></small> of the Spirit, which<small><small>[^52]</small></small> is the word of God: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praying<small><small>[^53]</small></small> always with all prayer and supplication<small><small>[^54]</small></small> in the<small><small>[^55]</small></small> Spirit, and watching<small><small>[^56]</small></small> thereunto with all perseverance<small><small>[^57]</small></small> and supplication<small><small>[^58]</small></small> for all saints; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And for<small><small>[^59]</small></small> me, that utterance<small><small>[^60]</small></small> may be given unto me, that I<small><small>[^61]</small></small> may open my mouth boldly, to make known the mystery<small><small>[^62]</small></small> of the gospel, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For which I am<small><small>[^63]</small></small> an ambassador in bonds<small><small>[^64]</small></small>: that therein<small><small>[^65]</small></small> I may speak boldly<small><small>[^66]</small></small>, as I ought to speak.

<big><big>[[Eph.6.21 Final Greetings|⥃]]</big></big>***Final Greetings***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Phil.4|Philippians 4:21–23]] <big>†</big> [[2Tim.4|2 Timothy 4:19–22]] ]</sup></small>

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But that<small><small>[^67]</small></small> ye also may know my affairs, \[and\] how I do, Tychicus<small><small>[^68]</small></small>, a beloved<small><small>[^69]</small></small> brother and faithful<small><small>[^70]</small></small> minister in the Lord, shall make known to you all things: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whom I have sent unto you for the same purpose, that ye might know our affairs, and \[that\] he might comfort your hearts.<small><small>[^71]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Peace<small><small>[^72]</small></small> \[be\] to the brethren, and love<small><small>[^73]</small></small> with faith, from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Eph.6.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Grace<small><small>[^74]</small></small> \[be\] with all them that love<small><small>[^75]</small></small> our Lord Jesus Christ in sincerity<small><small>[^76]</small></small>.
