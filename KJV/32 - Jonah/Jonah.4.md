***Jonah’s Anger at the LORD’s Compassion*** ^bsb2020

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But it displeased Jonah exceedingly, and he was very angry.<small><small>[^2]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he prayed<small><small>[^3]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and said, I pray thee, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, \[was\] not this my saying, when I was yet in my country? Therefore I fled<small><small>[^4]</small></small> before unto Tarshish: for I knew that thou \[art<small><small>[^5]</small></small>\] a gracious God, and merciful, slow to anger, and of<small><small>[^6]</small></small> great kindness, and repentest thee of the evil. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore now, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, take<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, I beseech thee, my life from me; for<small><small>[^8]</small></small> \[it is\] better for me to die than to live.

So<small><small>[^14]</small></small> Jonah was exceeding glad<small><small>[^15]</small></small> of the gourd. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.4.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But God prepared<small><small>[^16]</small></small> a worm when the morning rose the next day, and it smote the gourd that it withered<small><small>[^17]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.4.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, when the sun did arise, that God<small><small>[^18]</small></small> prepared a vehement<small><small>[^19]</small></small> east wind; and the sun<small><small>[^20]</small></small> beat upon the head of Jonah, that he fainted, and wished<small><small>[^21]</small></small> in himself to die, and said, \[It is\] better for me to die than to live. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.4.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And God said to Jonah, Doest thou well to be angry<small><small>[^22]</small></small> for the gourd?
