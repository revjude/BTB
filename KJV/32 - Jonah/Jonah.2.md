<big><big>[[Jonah.2.1 Jonah’s Prayer|⥃]]</big></big>***Jonah’s Prayer***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Jonah prayed<small><small>[^2]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> his God out<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of the fish’s belly, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And said, I cried<small><small>[^4]</small></small> by reason of mine<small><small>[^5]</small></small> affliction unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and he heard me; out<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of the belly of hell<small><small>[^7]</small></small> cried I, \[and\] thou<small><small>[^8]</small></small> heardest my voice. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thou<small><small>[^9]</small></small> hadst cast me into the deep, in the midst<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of the seas; and the floods compassed me about: all<small><small>[^11]</small></small> thy billows and thy waves passed over me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then I said<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, I am cast out<small><small>[^13]</small></small> of thy sight; yet I will look again toward<small><small>[^14]</small></small> thy holy temple. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.2.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The waters compassed me about, \[even\] to the soul: the depth closed me round about, the weeds were wrapped about my head.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.2.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I went down to the bottoms<small><small>[^16]</small></small> of the mountains<small><small>[^17]</small></small>; the earth<small><small>[^18]</small></small> with her bars \[was\] about me for ever: yet<small><small>[^19]</small></small> hast thou brought up my life from corruption<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> my God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.2.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When my soul<small><small>[^21]</small></small> fainted within me I remembered<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and my prayer<small><small>[^23]</small></small> came in unto thee, into thine holy<small><small>[^24]</small></small> temple. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.2.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They that observe lying vanities forsake their own mercy.<small><small>[^25]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jonah.2.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will sacrifice<small><small>[^26]</small></small> unto thee with the voice of thanksgiving; I will pay<small><small>[^27]</small></small> \[that\] that I have vowed. Salvation<small><small>[^28]</small></small> \[is\] of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.
