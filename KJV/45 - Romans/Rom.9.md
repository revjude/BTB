<big><big>[[Rom.9.1 Paul’s Concern for the Jews|⥃]]</big></big>***Paul’s Concern for the Jews***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I say<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the truth in Christ, I lie not, my conscience<small><small>[^3]</small></small> also bearing me witness in the Holy Ghost, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That I have great heaviness and continual sorrow in my heart.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I could<small><small>[^5]</small></small> wish that myself were<small><small>[^6]</small></small> accursed<small><small>[^7]</small></small> from Christ for my brethren, my kinsmen<small><small>[^8]</small></small> according to the flesh: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who are Israelites<small><small>[^9]</small></small>; to whom \[pertaineth\] the adoption<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, and the glory<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, and the covenants<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, and the giving<small><small>[^13]</small></small> of the law, and the service<small><small>[^14]</small></small> \[of God\], and the promises<small><small>[^15]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whose \[are\] the fathers<small><small>[^16]</small></small>, and of whom<small><small>[^17]</small></small> as concerning the flesh Christ \[came\], who is<small><small>[^18]</small></small> over all, God blessed<small><small>[^19]</small></small> for ever. Amen<small><small>[^20]</small></small>.

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Not as though<small><small>[^21]</small></small> the word of God hath taken none effect. For they \[are\] not<small><small>[^22]</small></small> all Israel, which are of Israel: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither, because<small><small>[^23]</small></small> they are the seed of Abraham, \[are they\] all children: but, In Isaac<small><small>[^24]</small></small> shall thy seed be called. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That is, They which<small><small>[^25]</small></small> are the children of the flesh, these \[are\] not the children of God: but the children of the promise are counted<small><small>[^26]</small></small> for the seed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For this \[is\] the word of promise, At this time<small><small>[^27]</small></small> will I come, and Sara<small><small>[^28]</small></small> shall have a son. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And not only<small><small>[^29]</small></small> \[this\]; but when<small><small>[^30]</small></small> Rebecca also had conceived by one, \[even\] by our father Isaac; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> (For \[the children<small><small>[^31]</small></small>\] being not yet born, neither having done any good or evil, that the<small><small>[^32]</small></small> purpose of God according<small><small>[^33]</small></small> to election might stand, not of works<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, but of<small><small>[^35]</small></small> him that calleth;) <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> It was said unto her, The elder<small><small>[^36]</small></small> shall serve the younger<small><small>[^37]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As it is written, Jacob<small><small>[^38]</small></small> have I loved, but Esau have I hated<small><small>[^39]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What shall<small><small>[^40]</small></small> we say then? \[Is there\] unrighteousness<small><small>[^41]</small></small> with God? God forbid. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he saith to Moses, I will have<small><small>[^42]</small></small> mercy on whom I will have mercy, and I will have compassion on whom I will have compassion. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So then \[it is\] not of him that willeth, nor of him that runneth, but of God that sheweth mercy.<small><small>[^43]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For<small><small>[^44]</small></small> the scripture saith unto Pharaoh, Even<small><small>[^45]</small></small> for this same purpose have I raised<small><small>[^46]</small></small> thee up, that<small><small>[^47]</small></small> I might shew my power in thee, and that my<small><small>[^48]</small></small> name might be declared throughout all the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore hath<small><small>[^49]</small></small> he mercy on whom he will \[have mercy\], and whom he will he<small><small>[^50]</small></small> hardeneth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou<small><small>[^51]</small></small> wilt say then unto me, Why doth<small><small>[^52]</small></small> he yet find fault? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As he saith also in Osee<small><small>[^67]</small></small>, I will call<small><small>[^68]</small></small> them my people, which were not my people; and her beloved<small><small>[^69]</small></small>, which was not beloved. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it<small><small>[^70]</small></small> shall come to pass, \[that\] in the place where it was said unto them, Ye \[are\] not my people; there shall<small><small>[^71]</small></small> they be called the children of the living God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Esaias<small><small>[^72]</small></small> also crieth concerning Israel, Though<small><small>[^73]</small></small> the number of the children of Israel be as the sand of the sea, a remnant<small><small>[^74]</small></small> shall be saved: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he will finish the work<small><small>[^75]</small></small>, and cut<small><small>[^76]</small></small> \[it\] short in righteousness<small><small>[^77]</small></small>: because a short work will the Lord make upon the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.9.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And as Esaias said before, Except<small><small>[^78]</small></small> the Lord of Sabaoth<small><small>[^79]</small></small> had left us a seed, we had been<small><small>[^80]</small></small> as Sodoma<small><small>[^81]</small></small>, and been made like unto Gomorrha.
