***A Remnant Chosen by Grace*** ^bsb2718

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.11.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I say then, Hath God<small><small>[^2]</small></small> cast away his people? Wot<small><small>[^6]</small></small> ye not what the scripture saith of Elias<small><small>[^7]</small></small>? how he maketh<small><small>[^8]</small></small> intercession to God against Israel, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.11.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Lord<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, they have killed thy prophets, and digged<small><small>[^10]</small></small> down thine altars; and I am left alone, and they seek my life. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.11.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But what saith the answer of God unto him? I have reserved<small><small>[^11]</small></small> to myself seven thousand men, who have not bowed the knee to \[the image of\] Baal<small><small>[^12]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.11.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Even so then at this present<small><small>[^13]</small></small> time also there is a remnant according to the election of grace<small><small>[^14]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.11.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if<small><small>[^15]</small></small> by grace, then \[is it\] no more of works: otherwise grace is no more grace. But if \[it be\] of works, then is it no more grace: otherwise work<small><small>[^16]</small></small> is no more work. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.11.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What then<small><small>[^17]</small></small>? Israel<small><small>[^18]</small></small> hath not obtained that which he seeketh for; but the election<small><small>[^19]</small></small> hath obtained it, and the rest<small><small>[^20]</small></small> were blinded<small><small>[^21]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.11.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> (According as it is written, God<small><small>[^22]</small></small> hath given them the spirit of slumber<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, eyes<small><small>[^24]</small></small> that they should not see, and ears that they should not hear;) unto this day<small><small>[^25]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.11.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And David saith<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, Let their table<small><small>[^27]</small></small> be made a snare, and a trap, and a stumblingblock, and a recompence unto them:<small><small>[^28]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.11.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let their eyes<small><small>[^29]</small></small> be darkened, that they may not see, and bow<small><small>[^30]</small></small> down their back alway.

***The Ingrafting of the Gentiles*** ^bsb2719

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.11.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I say then, Have they stumbled<small><small>[^31]</small></small> that they should fall?
