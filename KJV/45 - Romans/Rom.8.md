<big><big>[[Rom.8.1 Walking by the Spirit|⥃]]</big></big>***Walking by the Spirit***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Ezek.36|Ezekiel 36:16–38]] <big>†</big> [[Gal.5|Galatians 5:16–26]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[There is\] therefore no<small><small>[^2]</small></small>w no condemnation to them which are in<small><small>[^3]</small></small> Christ Jesus, who<small><small>[^4]</small></small> walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the law of the Spirit<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of life in Christ Jesus hath<small><small>[^7]</small></small> made me free from<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the law of sin and death. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For what<small><small>[^9]</small></small> the law could not do, in that it was weak through the flesh, God<small><small>[^10]</small></small> sending his own Son in the<small><small>[^11]</small></small> likeness of sinful flesh, and for sin<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, condemned<small><small>[^13]</small></small> sin in the flesh: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That<small><small>[^14]</small></small> the righteousness of the law might be fulfilled in us, who<small><small>[^15]</small></small> walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they<small><small>[^16]</small></small> that are after the flesh do mind<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the things of the flesh; but they that are after the Spirit the things of the Spirit<small><small>[^18]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For to be carnally minded<small><small>[^19]</small></small> \[is\] death; <big><big>[[Rom.8.12 Heirs with Christ|⥃]]</big></big>***Heirs with Christ***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore, brethren, we are<small><small>[^39]</small></small> debtors, not to the flesh, to live after the flesh. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For if ye live<small><small>[^40]</small></small> after the flesh, ye shall die: but if<small><small>[^41]</small></small> ye through<small><small>[^42]</small></small> the Spirit do mortify the deeds of the body, ye shall live. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For as many as are led<small><small>[^43]</small></small> by the Spirit of God, they are<small><small>[^44]</small></small> the sons of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For ye have not received the spirit<small><small>[^45]</small></small><small><small>[^46]</small></small> of bondage again to fear; but ye have received the Spirit of adoption, whereby we cry, Abba<small><small>[^47]</small></small>, Father. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The Spirit<small><small>[^48]</small></small> itself beareth witness with our<small><small>[^49]</small></small> spirit, that we are the children of God: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.8.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if children<small><small>[^50]</small></small>, then heirs; heirs of<small><small>[^51]</small></small> God, and joint-heirs with Christ; if so be<small><small>[^52]</small></small> that we suffer with \[him\], that we may be also glorified together.

<big><big>[[Rom.8.28 God Works in All Things|⥃]]</big></big>***God Works in All Things***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Eph.1|Ephesians 1:3–14]] ]</sup></small>
