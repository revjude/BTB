<big><big>[[Rom.10.1 The Word Brings Salvation|⥃]]</big></big>***The Word Brings Salvation***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Isa.65|Isaiah 65:1–16]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Brethren, my heart’s<small><small>[^2]</small></small> desire and prayer to God for Israel is, that they might be saved. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I bear them<small><small>[^3]</small></small> record that they<small><small>[^4]</small></small> have a zeal of God, but not<small><small>[^5]</small></small> according to knowledge. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they being ignorant of God’s righteousness<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, and going about to establish<small><small>[^7]</small></small> their own righteousness, have not submitted<small><small>[^8]</small></small> themselves unto the righteousness of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For Christ<small><small>[^9]</small></small> \[is\] the end<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of the law for righteousness to every one that believeth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For Moses describeth the righteousness which is of the law, That the man<small><small>[^11]</small></small> which doeth those things shall live by them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the righteousness<small><small>[^12]</small></small> which is of faith speaketh on this wise, Say not<small><small>[^13]</small></small> in thine heart, Who shall ascend into heaven? (that is, to bring<small><small>[^14]</small></small> Christ down \[from above\]:) <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Or, Who shall descend into the deep? (that is, to bring up<small><small>[^15]</small></small> Christ again from the dead.) <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But what saith it? The word<small><small>[^16]</small></small> is nigh thee, \[even\] in thy mouth, and in thy heart: that is, the word of faith<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, which we preach; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That if<small><small>[^18]</small></small> thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt<small><small>[^19]</small></small> believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For with<small><small>[^20]</small></small> the heart man believeth unto righteousness<small><small>[^21]</small></small>; and with<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the mouth confession is made unto salvation. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the scripture saith, Whosoever<small><small>[^23]</small></small> believeth on him shall not be ashamed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For there is no<small><small>[^24]</small></small> difference between the Jew and the Greek: for the same Lord<small><small>[^25]</small></small> over all is rich<small><small>[^26]</small></small> unto all that call upon him<small><small>[^27]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For whosoever<small><small>[^28]</small></small> shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> How then shall they<small><small>[^29]</small></small> call on him in whom they have not believed? and how shall<small><small>[^30]</small></small> they believe in him of whom they have not heard? and how shall they hear without a preacher? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And how<small><small>[^31]</small></small> shall they preach, except they be sent? as it is written, How beautiful<small><small>[^32]</small></small> are the feet of them that preach the gospel<small><small>[^33]</small></small> of peace, and bring<small><small>[^34]</small></small> glad tidings of good things! <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But they<small><small>[^35]</small></small> have not all obeyed<small><small>[^36]</small></small> the gospel. For Esaias saith, Lord<small><small>[^37]</small></small>, who hath believed our report<small><small>[^38]</small></small>? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So then faith<small><small>[^39]</small></small> \[cometh\] by hearing, and hearing<small><small>[^40]</small></small> by the word of God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I say, Have they<small><small>[^41]</small></small> not heard? Yes verily, their sound<small><small>[^42]</small></small> went into all the earth, and their words unto the ends<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of the world. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I say<small><small>[^44]</small></small>, Did not Israel know? First<small><small>[^45]</small></small> Moses saith, I will provoke you to jealousy by \[them that are\] no people, \[and\] by a foolish<small><small>[^46]</small></small> nation I will anger you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Esaias is very bold<small><small>[^47]</small></small>, and saith, I was found<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of them that sought me not; I was made<small><small>[^49]</small></small> manifest unto them that asked not after me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.10.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But to Israel he saith, All day long<small><small>[^50]</small></small> I have stretched forth my hands unto a disobedient<small><small>[^51]</small></small> and gainsaying people. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Rom.9|<< Romans 9]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Rom.11|Romans 11 >>]]</td></tr></table>
