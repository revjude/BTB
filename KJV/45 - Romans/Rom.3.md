 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.3.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What then<small><small>[^24]</small></small>? are we<small><small>[^25]</small></small> better \[than they\]? Yes, of the Gentiles also:<small><small>[^74]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.3.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Seeing \[it is\] one God, which shall justify the circumcision by faith, and uncircumcision through faith.<small><small>[^75]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Rom.3.31|31]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Do we<small><small>[^76]</small></small> then make void the law through faith?
