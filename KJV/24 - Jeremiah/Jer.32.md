<big><big>[[Jer.32.1 Jeremiah Buys Hanamel’s Field|⥃]]</big></big>***Jeremiah Buys Hanamel’s Field***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The word that came to Jeremiah from the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> in the<small><small>[^2]</small></small> tenth year of Zedekiah king of Judah, which \[was\] the eighteenth<small><small>[^3]</small></small> year of Nebuchadrezzar.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For then<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the king of Babylon’s army besieged Jerusalem: and Jeremiah<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the prophet was shut up in the<small><small>[^7]</small></small> court of the prison, which \[was\] in the king of Judah’s house. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For Zedekiah<small><small>[^8]</small></small> king of Judah had shut him up, saying, Wherefore<small><small>[^9]</small></small> dost thou prophesy, and say, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, Behold<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, I will give this city into the hand of the king of Babylon, and he shall take it; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Zedekiah king of Judah shall not escape out of the hand of the Chaldeans, but shall surely be delivered into the hand of the king of Babylon, and shall speak with him mouth to mouth, and his eyes shall behold his eyes;<small><small>[^11]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall lead Zedekiah to Babylon, and there shall he be until<small><small>[^12]</small></small> I visit him, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: though<small><small>[^13]</small></small> ye fight with the Chaldeans, ye shall not prosper. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And Jeremiah said, The word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, Hanameel the son of Shallum thine uncle shall come unto thee, saying, Buy thee my field that \[is\] in Anathoth<small><small>[^15]</small></small>: for<small><small>[^16]</small></small> the right of redemption \[is\] thine to buy \[it\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Hanameel mine uncle’s son came to me in the court<small><small>[^17]</small></small> of the prison according to the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and said unto me, Buy my field, I pray thee, that \[is\] in Anathoth<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, which \[is\] in the country of Benjamin: for the right of inheritance \[is\] thine, and the redemption \[is\] thine; buy \[it\] for thyself. Then I<small><small>[^19]</small></small> knew that this<small><small>[^20]</small></small> \[was\] the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I bought the field of Hanameel my uncle’s son, that \[was\] in Anathoth, and weighed<small><small>[^21]</small></small> him the money, \[even\] seventeen shekels of silver<small><small>[^22]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I<small><small>[^23]</small></small> subscribed the evidence<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, and sealed<small><small>[^25]</small></small> \[it\], and took<small><small>[^26]</small></small> witnesses, and weighed \[him\] the money in the balances. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So I took the evidence of the purchase, \[both\] that which was sealed \[according<small><small>[^27]</small></small>\] to the law and custom, and that which was open: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I gave the evidence of the purchase unto Baruch<small><small>[^28]</small></small> the son of Neriah<small><small>[^29]</small></small>, the son of Maaseiah, in the sight of Hanameel mine uncle’s \[son\], and in<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the presence of the witnesses that subscribed the book of the purchase, before all the Jews that sat in the court of the prison. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And I charged Baruch before them, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, the God of Israel; Take<small><small>[^31]</small></small> these evidences, this evidence of the purchase, both which is sealed, and this evidence which is open; and put them in an earthen vessel, that they may continue many days. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, the God of Israel; Houses<small><small>[^32]</small></small> and fields and vineyards shall be possessed again in this land.

<big><big>[[Jer.32.16 Jeremiah Prays for Understanding|⥃]]</big></big>***Jeremiah Prays for Understanding***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now when I had delivered the evidence of the purchase unto Baruch the son of Neriah, I prayed<small><small>[^33]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ah<small><small>[^34]</small></small> Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>! behold, thou<small><small>[^35]</small></small> hast made the heaven and the earth by thy great power and stretched out arm, \[and\] there<small><small>[^36]</small></small> is nothing too hard for thee: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shewest<small><small>[^37]</small></small> lovingkindness unto thousands, and recompensest<small><small>[^38]</small></small> the iniquity of the fathers into the bosom of their children after them: the Great<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, the Mighty God, the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^40]</small></small></span> of hosts, \[is\] his name, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Great<small><small>[^41]</small></small> in counsel, and mighty in work<small><small>[^42]</small></small>: for<small><small>[^43]</small></small> thine eyes \[are\] open upon all the ways of the sons of men: to give<small><small>[^44]</small></small> every one according to his ways, and according to the fruit of his doings: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which hast set<small><small>[^45]</small></small> signs and wonders in the land of Egypt, \[even\] unto this day, and in Israel, and among \[other\] men; and hast<small><small>[^46]</small></small> made thee a name, as at this day; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And hast brought<small><small>[^47]</small></small> forth thy people Israel out of the land of Egypt with signs, and with wonders, and with a strong<small><small>[^48]</small></small> hand, and with a stretched out arm, and with great terror; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And hast given them this land, which<small><small>[^49]</small></small> thou didst swear to their fathers to give them, a land<small><small>[^50]</small></small> flowing with milk and honey; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they came in, and possessed<small><small>[^51]</small></small> it; but<small><small>[^52]</small></small> they obeyed not thy voice, neither walked in thy law; they have<small><small>[^53]</small></small> done nothing of all that thou commandedst them to do: therefore<small><small>[^54]</small></small> thou hast caused all this evil to come upon them: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold the mounts<small><small>[^55]</small></small>, they are come unto the city to take it; and the city is<small><small>[^56]</small></small> given into the hand of the Chaldeans, that fight against it, because<small><small>[^57]</small></small> of the sword, and of the famine, and of the pestilence: and what<small><small>[^58]</small></small> thou hast spoken is come to pass; and, behold, thou seest \[it\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thou<small><small>[^59]</small></small> hast said unto me, O Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>, Buy thee the field for<small><small>[^60]</small></small> money, and take witnesses; for the city is given into the hand of the Chaldeans.

<big><big>[[Jer.32.26 The LORD Answers Jeremiah|⥃]]</big></big>***The LORD Answers Jeremiah***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then came the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> unto Jeremiah, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, the God<small><small>[^61]</small></small> of all flesh: is<small><small>[^62]</small></small> there any thing too hard for me? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; Behold<small><small>[^63]</small></small>, I will give this city into the hand of the Chaldeans, and into the hand of Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon, and he shall take it: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Chaldeans, that fight against this city, shall come and set<small><small>[^64]</small></small> fire on this city, and burn it with the houses, upon<small><small>[^65]</small></small> whose roofs they have offered incense unto Baal, and poured out drink offerings unto other gods, to provoke me to anger. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the children<small><small>[^66]</small></small> of Israel and the children of Judah have only done evil before me from<small><small>[^67]</small></small> their youth: for the children of Israel have only provoked me to anger with the work of their hands, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.31|31]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For this city<small><small>[^68]</small></small> hath been to me \[as\] a provocation of mine anger<small><small>[^69]</small></small> and of my fury from the day that they built it even unto this day; that I<small><small>[^70]</small></small> should remove it from before my face, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.32|32]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because of all the evil of the children of Israel and of the children of Judah, which they<small><small>[^71]</small></small> have done to provoke me to anger, they, their kings, their princes, their priests, and their prophets, and the men of Judah, and the inhabitants of Jerusalem. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.33|33]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they have turned<small><small>[^72]</small></small> unto me the back<small><small>[^73]</small></small>, and not the face: though I taught them, rising<small><small>[^74]</small></small> up early and teaching \[them\], yet they have not hearkened to receive instruction. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.34|34]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But they set their abominations in the house, which is called by my name, to defile it.<small><small>[^75]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.32.35|35]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And they built<small><small>[^76]</small></small> the high places of Baal, which<small><small>[^77]</small></small> \[are\] in the valley of the son of Hinnom, to cause<small><small>[^78]</small></small> their sons and their daughters to pass through \[the fire\] unto Molech; which I commanded them not, neither came it into my mind, that they should do this abomination, to cause Judah to sin.
