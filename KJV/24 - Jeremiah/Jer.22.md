 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.22.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; Go<small><small>[^2]</small></small> down to the house of the king of Judah, and speak there this word,<small><small>[^3]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.22.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And say, Hear<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, O king of Judah, that sittest<small><small>[^5]</small></small> upon the throne of David, thou, and thy servants, and thy people that enter<small><small>[^6]</small></small> in by these gates: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.22.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; Execute<small><small>[^7]</small></small> ye judgment and righteousness, and deliver the spoiled out of the hand of the oppressor: and do no wrong<small><small>[^8]</small></small>, do no violence<small><small>[^9]</small></small> to the stranger, the fatherless, nor the widow, neither<small><small>[^10]</small></small> shed innocent blood in this place. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.22.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For if ye do this thing indeed, then<small><small>[^11]</small></small> shall there enter in by the gates of this house kings sitting upon the throne of David<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, riding in chariots and on horses, he, and his servants, and his people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.22.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But i<small><small>[^13]</small></small>f<small><small>[^14]</small></small> ye will not hear these words, I swear by myself, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, that<small><small>[^15]</small></small> this house shall become a desolation.

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.22.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> unto<small><small>[^16]</small></small> the king’s house of Judah; Thou<small><small>[^17]</small></small> \[art\] Gilead unto me, \[and\] the head of Lebanon: \[yet\] surely<small><small>[^18]</small></small> I will make thee a wilderness, \[and\] cities \[which\] are not inhabited. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.22.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I<small><small>[^19]</small></small> will prepare destroyers against thee, every one with his weapons: and they shall cut<small><small>[^20]</small></small> down thy choice cedars, and cast \[them\] into the fire. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.22.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And many nations shall pass by this city, and they shall say every man to his neighbour, Wherefore hath the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> done thus unto this great city?<small><small>[^21]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.22.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then they shall answer, Because they have forsaken the covenant of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> their God, and worshipped other gods, and served them.<small><small>[^22]</small></small>

***A Warning about Jehoiakim*** ^bsb1746

<span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.22.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He judged<small><small>[^37]</small></small> the cause of the poor and needy; then \[it was\] well \[with him\]: \[was\] not<small><small>[^38]</small></small> this to know me?
