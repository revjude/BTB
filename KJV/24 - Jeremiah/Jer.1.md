***The Call of Jeremiah*** ^bsb1695

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The words<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of Jeremiah the son of Hilkiah, of the priests<small><small>[^3]</small></small> that \[were\] in Anathoth<small><small>[^4]</small></small> in the land of Benjamin: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To whom the word<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came in the days<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of Josiah the son of Amon king of Judah, in the thirteenth year of his reign. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> It came also<small><small>[^7]</small></small> in the days of Jehoiakim the son of Josiah king of Judah, unto the end<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of the eleventh year of Zedekiah the son of Josiah king of Judah, unto the carrying away of Jerusalem captive in the fifth<small><small>[^9]</small></small> month. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the word<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Before I<small><small>[^11]</small></small> formed thee in the belly I knew<small><small>[^12]</small></small> thee; and before<small><small>[^13]</small></small> thou camest forth out of the womb I sanctified thee, \[and\] I ordained<small><small>[^14]</small></small> thee a prophet unto the nations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said I, Ah, Lord<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">God</span>! behold, I cannot<small><small>[^16]</small></small> speak: for I \[am<small><small>[^17]</small></small>\] a child. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]But the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto me, Say not, I \[am\] a child: for thou shalt<small><small>[^18]</small></small> go to all that I shall send thee, and whatsoever I command thee thou shalt speak. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be not afraid<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of their faces: for I \[am<small><small>[^20]</small></small>\] with thee to deliver thee, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> put forth his hand, and touched<small><small>[^21]</small></small> my mouth. And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto me, Behold<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, I have put my words in thy mouth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> See, I have<small><small>[^23]</small></small> this day set thee over the nations and over the kingdoms, to root out<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, and to pull down, and to destroy, and to throw down, to build<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, and to plant. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, Jeremiah, what seest thou<small><small>[^26]</small></small>? And I said, I see a rod<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of an almond tree. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> unto me, Thou hast<small><small>[^28]</small></small> well seen: for I will<small><small>[^29]</small></small> hasten my word to perform it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me the second time<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, saying, What seest thou? And I said, I see<small><small>[^31]</small></small> a seething pot; and the face thereof \[is\] toward the north<small><small>[^32]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto me, Out of<small><small>[^33]</small></small> the north an evil shall break forth<small><small>[^34]</small></small> upon all the inhabitants of the land. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, lo, I will call<small><small>[^35]</small></small> all the families of the kingdoms of the north, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; and they<small><small>[^36]</small></small> shall come, and they shall set every one his throne at the entering of the gates of Jerusalem, and against<small><small>[^37]</small></small> all the walls thereof round about, and against all the cities of Judah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.1.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I<small><small>[^38]</small></small> will utter my judgments against them touching all their wickedness, who have<small><small>[^39]</small></small> forsaken me, and have<small><small>[^40]</small></small> burned incense unto other gods, and worshipped<small><small>[^41]</small></small> the works of their own hands.
