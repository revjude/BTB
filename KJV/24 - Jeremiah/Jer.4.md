***A Plea to Return*** ^bsb1702

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If thou wilt return<small><small>[^2]</small></small><small><small>[^3]</small></small>, O Israel, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, return unto me: and if thou wilt put away<small><small>[^4]</small></small> thine abominations out of my sight, then shalt<small><small>[^5]</small></small> thou not remove. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thou shalt swear<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> liveth, in truth<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, in judgment, and in righteousness; and the nations<small><small>[^8]</small></small> shall bless themselves in him, and in him<small><small>[^9]</small></small> shall they glory. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> to the men of Judah and Jerusalem, Break<small><small>[^10]</small></small> up your fallow ground, and sow not among thorns. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.4.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Circumcise yourselves to the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and take<small><small>[^11]</small></small> away the foreskins of your heart, ye men of Judah and inhabitants of Jerusalem: lest<small><small>[^12]</small></small> my fury come forth like fire, and burn that none can quench \[it\], because of the evil of your doings.
