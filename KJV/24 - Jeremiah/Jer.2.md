***Israel Has Forsaken God*** ^bsb1696

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.2.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover the word<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came to me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.2.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Go and cry<small><small>[^3]</small></small> in the ears of Jerusalem, saying, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; I remember thee<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, the kindness<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of thy youth, the love of thine espousals<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, when<small><small>[^7]</small></small> thou wentest after me in the wilderness, in a land \[that was\] not sown. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.2.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Israel \[was\] holiness<small><small>[^8]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, \[and\] the firstfruits<small><small>[^9]</small></small> of his increase: all that<small><small>[^10]</small></small> devour him shall offend; evil shall come upon them, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.2.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear ye<small><small>[^11]</small></small> the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, O house of Jacob, and all the families<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of the house of Israel:

***The Consequence of Israel’s Sin*** ^bsb1697

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.2.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Is\] Israel<small><small>[^43]</small></small> a servant? \[is\] he a homeborn<small><small>[^44]</small></small> \[slave\]? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.2.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And now what hast<small><small>[^57]</small></small> thou to do in the way of Egypt, to drink the waters of Sihor<small><small>[^58]</small></small>? $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.2.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For of<small><small>[^64]</small></small> old time I have broken thy yoke, \[and\] burst thy bands; and thou saidst<small><small>[^65]</small></small>, I will not transgress<small><small>[^66]</small></small>; when upon<small><small>[^67]</small></small> every high hill and under every green tree thou wanderest, playing<small><small>[^68]</small></small> the harlot. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.2.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yet I<small><small>[^69]</small></small> had planted thee a noble vine, wholly<small><small>[^70]</small></small> a right seed: how then art thou turned into the degenerate<small><small>[^71]</small></small> plant of a strange vine unto me? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.2.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For though<small><small>[^72]</small></small> thou wash thee with nitre, and take thee much soap, \[yet\] thine iniquity<small><small>[^73]</small></small> is marked before me, saith the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>.
