but ye have not hearkened. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.25.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath sent unto you all<small><small>[^8]</small></small> his servants the prophets, rising<small><small>[^9]</small></small> early and sending \[them\]; $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.25.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts; Because ye have not heard my words, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.25.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, I<small><small>[^15]</small></small> will send and take all the families of the north, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and Nebuchadrezzar<small><small>[^16]</small></small> the king of Babylon, my servant, and will bring them against<small><small>[^17]</small></small> this land, and against the inhabitants thereof, and against all these nations round about, and will utterly destroy them, and make them an astonishment<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, and an hissing, and perpetual desolations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.25.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover I will take from<small><small>[^19]</small></small> them the voice of mirth<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and the voice of gladness, the voice of the bridegroom, and the voice of the bride, the sound<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of the millstones, and the light of the candle. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.25.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And this whole land shall be a desolation, \[and\] an astonishment; and these nations shall serve the king of Babylon seventy<small><small>[^22]</small></small> years. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.25.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And it shall come to pass, when<small><small>[^23]</small></small> seventy years are accomplished, \[that\] I<small><small>[^24]</small></small> will punish<small><small>[^25]</small></small> the king of Babylon, and that nation, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, for their iniquity, and the land of the Chaldeans, and will make it perpetual<small><small>[^26]</small></small> desolations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.25.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will bring upon that land all my words which I have pronounced against it, \[even\] all that is written in this book, which Jeremiah hath<small><small>[^27]</small></small> prophesied against all the nations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.25.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For many<small><small>[^28]</small></small> nati<small><small>[^29]</small></small>ons and great kings shall serve themselves of them also: and I will recompense them according to their deeds, and according to the works of their own hands.
