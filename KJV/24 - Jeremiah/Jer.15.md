$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.15.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, thou<small><small>[^39]</small></small> knowest: remember<small><small>[^40]</small></small> me, and visit me, and revenge me of my persecutors; take<small><small>[^41]</small></small> me not away in thy longsuffering: know<small><small>[^42]</small></small> that for thy sake I have suffered rebuke. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.15.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thy words were found, and I did<small><small>[^43]</small></small> eat them; and thy word<small><small>[^44]</small></small> was unto me the joy and rejoicing of mine heart: for I am called by thy name<small><small>[^45]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of hosts. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.15.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I sat not<small><small>[^46]</small></small> in the assembly of the mockers, nor rejoiced; I sat alone<small><small>[^47]</small></small> because of thy hand: for<small><small>[^48]</small></small> thou hast filled me with indignation. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.15.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Why is my pain<small><small>[^49]</small></small> perpetual, and my wound<small><small>[^50]</small></small> incurable, \[which\] refuseth to be healed? wilt thou be altogether unto me as a<small><small>[^51]</small></small> liar, \[and as<small><small>[^52]</small></small>\] waters \[that\] fail<small><small>[^53]</small></small>?
