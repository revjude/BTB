***Jeremiah Warns Zedekiah*** ^bsb1780

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And king Zedekiah<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the son of Josiah reigned instead of Coniah<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the son of Jehoiakim, whom Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon made<small><small>[^4]</small></small> king in the land of Judah.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But neither<small><small>[^6]</small></small> he, nor his servants, nor the people of the land, did hearken unto the words of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, which he spake by the prophet<small><small>[^7]</small></small> Jeremiah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Zedekiah the king sent Jehucal the son of Shelemiah and Zephaniah<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the son of Maaseiah the priest to the prophet Jeremiah, saying, Pray<small><small>[^9]</small></small> now unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> our God for us. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now Jeremiah came in and went out among the people: for<small><small>[^10]</small></small> they had not put him into prison. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Pharaoh’s army was come forth out of Egypt: and when the Chaldeans that besieged Jerusalem heard tidings of them, they<small><small>[^11]</small></small> departed from Jerusalem.<small><small>[^12]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Then came the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> unto the prophet Jeremiah, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus<small><small>[^13]</small></small> saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, the God of Israel; Thus shall ye say to the king of Judah, that sent you unto me to enquire of me; Behold, Pharaoh’s army, which is come forth to help you, shall return to Egypt into their own land.<small><small>[^14]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the Chaldeans shall come again, and fight against this city, and take it, and burn it with fire.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; Deceive<small><small>[^16]</small></small> not yourselves<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, saying, The Chaldeans shall surely depart from us: for they shall not depart. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For though<small><small>[^18]</small></small> ye had smitten the whole army of the Chaldeans that fight against you, and there remained \[but\] wounded men<small><small>[^19]</small></small> among them, \[yet<small><small>[^20]</small></small>\] should they rise up every man in his tent, and burn this city with fire.

***Jeremiah Imprisoned*** ^bsb1781

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, that<small><small>[^21]</small></small> when the army of the Chaldeans was broken<small><small>[^22]</small></small> up from Jerusalem for fear of Pharaoh’s army, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Jeremiah went<small><small>[^23]</small></small> forth out of Jerusalem to go into the land<small><small>[^24]</small></small> of Benjamin, to separate himself thence<small><small>[^25]</small></small> in the midst of the people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when he was in the<small><small>[^26]</small></small> gate of Benjamin, a captain of the ward \[was\] there, whose name \[was\] Irijah, the son of Shelemiah, the son of Hananiah<small><small>[^27]</small></small>; and he took Jeremiah the prophet, saying, Thou<small><small>[^28]</small></small> fallest away to the Chaldeans. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said<small><small>[^29]</small></small> Jeremiah, \[It is\] false<small><small>[^30]</small></small>; I fall not away to the Chaldeans. But he hearkened not to him: so Irijah took Jeremiah, and brought him to the princes. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore the princes<small><small>[^31]</small></small> were wroth with Jeremiah, and smote him, and put<small><small>[^32]</small></small> him in prison in the<small><small>[^33]</small></small> house of Jonathan the scribe: for they had made that the prison. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]When Jeremiah was entered into the dungeon<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, and into the cabins<small><small>[^35]</small></small>, and Jeremiah had remained there many days;<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Zedekiah the king sent, and took him out: and the king asked<small><small>[^37]</small></small> him secretly in his house, and said, Is there<small><small>[^38]</small></small> \[any\] word from the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>? And Jeremiah said, There is: for, said he, thou shalt<small><small>[^39]</small></small> be delivered into the hand of the king of Babylon. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover Jeremiah said unto king Zedekiah, What have I offended against thee, or against thy servants, or against this people, that ye have put me in prison?<small><small>[^40]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Where<small><small>[^41]</small></small> \[are\] now your<small><small>[^42]</small></small> prophets which prophesied unto you, saying, The king of Babylon shall not come against you, nor against this land? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore hear now, I pray thee, O my lord the king: let my supplication, I pray thee, be accepted before<small><small>[^43]</small></small> thee; that thou cause me not to return to the house of Jonathan the scribe, lest<small><small>[^44]</small></small> I die there. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.37.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then Zedekiah the king commanded that they should commit Jeremiah into the<small><small>[^45]</small></small> court of the prison, and that<small><small>[^46]</small></small> they should give him daily a piece of bread out of the bakers’ street, until<small><small>[^47]</small></small> all the bread in the city were spent.
