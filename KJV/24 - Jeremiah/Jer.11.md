***The Broken Covenant*** ^bsb1718

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The word that came to Jeremiah from the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear ye the words of this covenant, and speak unto the men of Judah, and to the inhabitants of Jerusalem;<small><small>[^2]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And say thou unto them, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel; Cursed \[be\] the man that obeyeth not the words of this covenant,<small><small>[^3]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which I commanded<small><small>[^4]</small></small> your fathers in the day \[that\] I brought them forth out of the land of Egypt, from the iron<small><small>[^5]</small></small> furnace, saying, Obey<small><small>[^6]</small></small> my voice, and do them, according to all which I command you: so shall ye be<small><small>[^7]</small></small> my people, and I will be your God: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That I may perform<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the oath which I have sworn unto your fathers, to give them a land<small><small>[^9]</small></small> flowing with milk and honey, as \[it is\] this day. Then answered I, and said, So be it<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto me, Proclaim<small><small>[^11]</small></small> all these words in the cities of Judah, and in the streets of Jerusalem, saying, Hear<small><small>[^12]</small></small> ye the words of this covenant, and do them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I earnestly<small><small>[^13]</small></small> protested unto your fathers in the<small><small>[^14]</small></small> day \[that\] I brought them up out of the land of Egypt, \[even\] unto this day, rising early and protesting, saying, Obey my voice. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yet they obeyed<small><small>[^15]</small></small> not, nor inclined their ear, but walked every one in the imagination<small><small>[^16]</small></small> of their evil heart: therefore<small><small>[^17]</small></small> I will bring upon them all the words of this covenant, which I commanded \[them\] to do; but they did \[them\] not. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> said unto me, A conspiracy is found among the men of Judah, and among the inhabitants of Jerusalem.<small><small>[^18]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They are turned<small><small>[^19]</small></small> back to the iniquities<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of their forefathers, which refused to hear my words; and they went after other gods to serve them: the house of Israel<small><small>[^21]</small></small> and the house of Judah have broken my covenant which I made with their fathers. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, Behold, I will bring<small><small>[^22]</small></small> evil upon them, which<small><small>[^23]</small></small> they shall not be able to escape<small><small>[^24]</small></small>; and though they shall cry<small><small>[^25]</small></small> unto me, I will not hearken unto them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then shall the cities of Judah and inhabitants of Jerusalem go<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, and cry unto the gods unto whom they offer incense: but they shall not save them at all in the time of their trouble<small><small>[^27]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For \[according<small><small>[^28]</small></small> to\] the number of thy cities were thy gods, O Judah; and \[according to\] the number of the streets of Jerusalem have ye set up altars<small><small>[^29]</small></small> to \[that\] shameful thing<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, \[even\] altars to burn incense unto Baal. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore pray<small><small>[^31]</small></small> not thou for<small><small>[^32]</small></small> this people, neither lift up a cry or prayer for them: for I will not hear \[them\] in the time that they cry unto me for their trouble<small><small>[^33]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What<small><small>[^34]</small></small> hath my<small><small>[^35]</small></small> beloved to do<small><small>[^36]</small></small> in mine house, \[seeing<small><small>[^37]</small></small>\] she hath wrought lewdness with many, and the holy<small><small>[^38]</small></small> flesh is passed from thee? when thou doest evil<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, then thou rejoicest. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> called thy name, A green<small><small>[^40]</small></small> olive tree, fair, \[and\] of goodly fruit: with<small><small>[^41]</small></small> the noise of a great tumult he hath kindled fire upon it, and the branches of it are broken. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.11.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, that<small><small>[^42]</small></small> planted thee, hath pronounced<small><small>[^43]</small></small> evil against thee, for the evil of the house of Israel and of the house of Judah, which they have done against themselves to provoke me to anger in offering incense unto Baal.
