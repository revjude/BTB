***The Excellence of the Restored Nation*** ^bsb1770

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the<small><small>[^3]</small></small> word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto Jeremiah the second time, while he was yet shut up in the court of the prison, saying,<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> the maker<small><small>[^5]</small></small> thereof, the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> that formed it, to establish it; the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] his name;<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Call<small><small>[^7]</small></small> unto me, and I will answer thee, and shew thee great and mighty<small><small>[^8]</small></small> things, which thou knowest not.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, the God of Israel, concerning the houses of this city, and concerning the houses of the kings of Judah, which are thrown<small><small>[^10]</small></small> down by the mounts, and by the sword; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They come<small><small>[^11]</small></small> to fight with the Chaldeans, but \[it is\] to fill them with the dead bodies of men, whom I have slain in mine anger and in my fury, and for all whose wickedness I have hid<small><small>[^12]</small></small> my face from this city. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, I will bring<small><small>[^13]</small></small> it health<small><small>[^14]</small></small> and cure, and I will cure them, and will<small><small>[^15]</small></small> reveal unto them the abundance of peace and truth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will cause<small><small>[^16]</small></small> the captivity of Judah and the captivity of Israel to return, and will<small><small>[^17]</small></small> build them, as at the first. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will cleanse them from all their iniquity, whereby they have sinned against me; and I will pardon all their iniquities, whereby they have sinned, and whereby they have transgressed against me.<small><small>[^18]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And it shall be to me a name<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of joy, a praise and an honour before<small><small>[^20]</small></small> all the nations of the earth, which shall hear all the good that I do unto them: and they shall fear<small><small>[^21]</small></small> and tremble for all the goodness and for all the prosperity that I procure unto it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; Again there shall be heard in this place, which ye<small><small>[^22]</small></small> say \[shall be\] desolate without man and without beast, \[even\] in the cities of Judah, and in the streets of Jerusalem, that are desolate, without man, and without inhabitant, and without beast, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The voice of joy<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, and the voice of gladness, the voice of the bridegroom, and the voice of the bride, the voice of them<small><small>[^24]</small></small> that shall say, Praise the<small><small>[^25]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts: for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] good; for his mercy \[endureth\] for ever: \[and\] of them that shall bring the sacrifice<small><small>[^26]</small></small> of praise into the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. For I<small><small>[^27]</small></small> will cause to return the captivity of the land, as at the first, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts; Again in this place, which is desolate without<small><small>[^28]</small></small> man and without beast, and in all<small><small>[^29]</small></small> the cities thereof, shall be an habitation of shepherds causing \[their\] flocks to lie down. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the cities of the mountains, in the cities of the vale, and in the cities of the south, and in the land of Benjamin, and in the places about Jerusalem, and in the cities of Judah, shall<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the flocks pass again under the hands of him that telleth \[them\], saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.

***The Covenant with David*** ^bsb1771

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, the days come, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, that I will perform that good thing which I have promised unto the house of Israel and to the house of Judah.<small><small>[^31]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]In those days, and at that time, will I cause the Branch<small><small>[^32]</small></small> of righteousness to grow up unto David; and he<small><small>[^33]</small></small> shall execute judgment and righteousness in the land. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In those days shall Judah<small><small>[^34]</small></small> be saved, and Jerusalem shall dwell<small><small>[^35]</small></small> safely: and this \[is the name\] wherewith she shall be called, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> our righteousness.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; David shall never want<small><small>[^37]</small></small> a man to sit upon the throne of the house of Israel; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither shall the priests the Levites want a man before me to offer burnt offerings, and to kindle meat offerings, and to do sacrifice continually.<small><small>[^38]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto Jeremiah, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; If ye can break my covenant of the day, and my covenant of the night, and that there should not be day and night in their season;<small><small>[^39]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Then\] may<small><small>[^40]</small></small> also my covenant be broken with David my servant, that he<small><small>[^41]</small></small> should not have a son to reign upon his throne; and with<small><small>[^42]</small></small> the Levites the priests, my ministers. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As the host<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of heaven cannot be numbered, neither the sand of the sea measured: so<small><small>[^44]</small></small> will I multiply the seed of David my servant, and the Levites<small><small>[^45]</small></small> that minister unto me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moreover the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came to Jeremiah, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Considerest thou not what this people have spoken, saying, The two<small><small>[^46]</small></small> families which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath chosen, he hath even cast them off? thus<small><small>[^47]</small></small> they have despised my people, that they should be no more a nation before them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; If my<small><small>[^48]</small></small> covenant \[be\] not with day and night, \[and if<small><small>[^49]</small></small>\] I have not appointed the ordinances of heaven and earth; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.33.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then will I<small><small>[^50]</small></small> cast away the seed of Jacob, and David my servant, \[so\] that I will<small><small>[^51]</small></small> not take \[any\] of his seed \[to be\] rulers over the seed of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob: for I will cause their captivity to return, and have<small><small>[^52]</small></small> mercy on them. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Jer.32|<< Jeremiah 32]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Jer.34|Jeremiah 34 >>]]</td></tr></table>
