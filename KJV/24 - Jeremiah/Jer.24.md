***The Good and Bad Figs*** ^bsb1751

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.24.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^2]</small></small></span> shewed me, and, behold, two<small><small>[^3]</small></small> baskets of figs \[were\] set before the temple of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, after<small><small>[^4]</small></small> that Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon had carried away captive Jeconiah the son of Jehoiakim king of Judah, and the princes of Judah, with the carpenters and smiths<small><small>[^5]</small></small>, from Jerusalem, and had brought them to Babylon.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.24.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> One basket<small><small>[^7]</small></small> \[had\] very good figs, \[even\] like the figs \[that are\] first ripe<small><small>[^8]</small></small>: and the other basket \[had\] very naughty<small><small>[^9]</small></small> figs, which could not be eaten, they were so bad<small><small>[^10]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.24.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> unto me, What<small><small>[^11]</small></small> seest thou, Jeremiah? And I said, Figs; the good figs, very good; and the evil, very evil, that cannot be eaten, they are so evil. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.24.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Again the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.24.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, the God of Israel; Like these good figs, so will I acknowledge<small><small>[^12]</small></small> them that are carried away captive<small><small>[^13]</small></small> of Judah, whom I have sent out of this place into the land of the Chaldeans for \[their\] good. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.24.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I will<small><small>[^14]</small></small> set mine eyes upon them for good, and I will bring<small><small>[^15]</small></small> them again to this land: and I will build<small><small>[^16]</small></small> them, and not pull \[them\] down; and I will plant them, and not pluck \[them\] up. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.24.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will give<small><small>[^17]</small></small> them an heart to know me, that I \[am\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and they<small><small>[^18]</small></small> shall be my people, and I will be their God: for they<small><small>[^19]</small></small> shall return unto me with their whole heart. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.24.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And as<small><small>[^20]</small></small> the evil figs, which cannot be eaten, they are so evil; surely thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, So will<small><small>[^21]</small></small> I give Zedekiah the king of Judah, and his princes, and the residue of Jerusalem, that remain in this land, and them<small><small>[^22]</small></small> that dwell in the land of Egypt: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.24.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will deliver them to be removed<small><small>[^23]</small></small> into all the kingdoms of the earth for \[their\] hurt, \[to be\] a<small><small>[^24]</small></small> reproach and a proverb, a taunt and a curse<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, in all places whither I shall drive them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.24.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will send the sword, the famine, and the pestilence, among them, till they be consumed from off the land that I gave unto them and to their fathers. <small><small>[^26]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Jer.23|<< Jeremiah 23]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Jer.25|Jeremiah 25 >>]]</td></tr></table>
