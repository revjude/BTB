***The Prosperity of the Wicked*** ^bsb1720

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.12.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Righteous<small><small>[^2]</small></small> \[art\] thou, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, when I plead with thee: yet let me talk<small><small>[^3]</small></small> with thee of \[thy\] judgments: Wherefore doth<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the way of the wicked prosper? \[wherefore\] are all they happy that deal<small><small>[^5]</small></small> very treacherously? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.12.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou hast<small><small>[^6]</small></small> planted them, yea, they have taken root: they grow<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, yea, they bring forth fruit: thou \[art\] near<small><small>[^8]</small></small> in their mouth, and far from their reins. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.12.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But thou, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, knowest<small><small>[^9]</small></small> me: thou hast seen me, and tried mine heart toward<small><small>[^10]</small></small> thee: pull<small><small>[^11]</small></small> them out like sheep for the slaughter, and prepare them for the day<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of slaughter. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.12.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> How long<small><small>[^13]</small></small> shall the land mourn, and the herbs<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of every field wither, for the wickedness of them that dwell therein? the beasts<small><small>[^15]</small></small> are consumed, and the birds; because they said, He<small><small>[^16]</small></small> shall not see our last end.

***God’s Answer to Jeremiah*** ^bsb1721
