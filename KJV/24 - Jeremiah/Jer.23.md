***David’s Righteous Branch*** ^bsb1748

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.23.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Woe<small><small>[^2]</small></small> be unto the pastors<small><small>[^3]</small></small> that destroy and scatter the sheep of my pasture! saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.23.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel against the pastors that feed my people; Ye have scattered my flock, and driven them away, and have<small><small>[^4]</small></small> not vi<small><small>[^5]</small></small>sited them: behold, I will visit upon you the evil of your doings, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.23.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will gather the remnant of my flock out of all countries whither I have driven them, and will bring them again to their folds; and they shall be fruitful and increase.<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.23.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I<small><small>[^7]</small></small> will set up shepherds over them which shall feed them: and they shall fear no more, nor be dismayed, neither<small><small>[^8]</small></small> shall they be lacking, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.
