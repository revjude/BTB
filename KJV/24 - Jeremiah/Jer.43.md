***Jeremiah Taken to Egypt*** ^bsb1791

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it came to pass, \[that\] when Jeremiah had made<small><small>[^2]</small></small> an end of speaking unto all the people all the words<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> their God, for which the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> their God had sent him to them, \[even\] all these words, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then spake Azariah<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the son of Hoshaiah, and Johanan<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the son of Kareah, and all the<small><small>[^6]</small></small> proud men, saying unto Jeremiah, Thou speakest<small><small>[^7]</small></small> falsely: the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> our God hath not sent thee to say, Go not into Egypt to sojourn there: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Baruch<small><small>[^8]</small></small> the son of Neriah setteth thee on against us, for to deliver<small><small>[^9]</small></small> us into the hand of the Chaldeans, that they might put us to death, and carry us away captives into Babylon. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So Johanan the son of Kareah, and all the captains of the forces, and all the people, obeyed<small><small>[^10]</small></small> not the voice of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, to dwell<small><small>[^11]</small></small> in the land of Judah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But Johanan the son of Kareah, and all the captains of the forces, took<small><small>[^12]</small></small> all the remnant of Judah, that were returned from all nations, whither they had been driven, to dwell in the land of Judah; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Even\] men, and women, and children, and the king’s daughters, and every<small><small>[^13]</small></small> person that Nebuzar–adan the captain of the guard had left with Gedaliah the son of Ahikam the son of Shaphan, and Jeremiah<small><small>[^14]</small></small> the prophet, and Baruch the son of Neriah.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So<small><small>[^16]</small></small> they came into the land of Egypt: for they obeyed not the voice of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: thus came they \[even\] to Tahpanhes<small><small>[^17]</small></small>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then came the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> unto Jeremiah in Tahpanhes, saying,<small><small>[^18]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Take great<small><small>[^19]</small></small> stones in thine hand, and hide them in the clay in the brickkiln<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, which \[is\] at the entry of Pharaoh’s house in Tahpanhes, in the sight of the men of Judah; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And say unto them, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, the God of Israel; Behold, I will send<small><small>[^21]</small></small> and take Nebuchadrezzar the king of Babylon, my servant<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, and will set his throne upon these stones that I have hid; and he shall spread his royal<small><small>[^23]</small></small> pavilion over them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And when he cometh, he shall smite<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the land of Egypt, \[and deliver\] such \[as are\] for death<small><small>[^25]</small></small> to death; and such \[as are\] for captivity to captivity; and such \[as are\] for the sword to the sword. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will kindle a fire in the<small><small>[^26]</small></small> houses of the gods of Egypt; and he shall burn them, and carry them away captives: and he shall array<small><small>[^27]</small></small> himself with the land of Egypt, as a shepherd putteth<small><small>[^28]</small></small> on his garment; and he shall go forth from thence in peace. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.43.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He shall break also the images<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of Beth–shemesh, that \[is\] in the land of Egypt; and the<small><small>[^30]</small></small> houses of the gods of the Egyptians shall he burn with fire. <small><small>[^31]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Jer.42|<< Jeremiah 42]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Jer.44|Jeremiah 44 >>]]</td></tr></table>
