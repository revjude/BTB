***Jeremiah’s Letter to the Exiles*** ^bsb1761

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now<small><small>[^2]</small></small> these \[are\] the words of the letter<small><small>[^3]</small></small> that Jeremiah the prophet sent from Jerusalem unto the residue of the elders<small><small>[^4]</small></small> which were carried away captives, and to the priests, and to the prophets, and to all the people whom Nebuchadnezzar had carried away captive from Jerusalem to Babylon;<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> (After that Jeconiah<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the king, and the queen, and the eunuchs<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, the princes of Judah and Jerusalem, and the carpenters, and the smiths, were departed from Jerusalem;) <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By the hand of Elasah the son of Shaphan<small><small>[^8]</small></small>, and Gemariah<small><small>[^9]</small></small> the son of Hilkiah, (whom Zedekiah king of Judah sent unto Babylon to Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon) saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, the God of Israel, unto all that are carried away captives, whom<small><small>[^10]</small></small> I have caused to be carried away from Jerusalem unto Babylon; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Build ye houses, and dwell \[in them\]; and plant gardens, and eat the fruit of them;<small><small>[^11]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Take ye<small><small>[^12]</small></small> wives, and beget sons and daughters; and take wives<small><small>[^13]</small></small> for your sons, and give your daughters to husbands, that they may bear sons and daughters; that ye may be increased there, and not diminished. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And seek<small><small>[^14]</small></small> the peace of the city whither I have caused you to be carried away captives, and pray<small><small>[^15]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> for it: for in the peace thereof shall ye have peace. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, the God of Israel; Let<small><small>[^16]</small></small> not your prophets and your diviners, that \[be\] in the midst of you, deceive you, neither hearken to your dreams<small><small>[^17]</small></small> which ye cause to be dreamed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For they prophesy falsely<small><small>[^18]</small></small> unto you in my name: I have not sent them, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]For thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, That after<small><small>[^19]</small></small> seventy years be accomplished at Babylon I will<small><small>[^20]</small></small> visit you, and perform my good word toward you, in causing you to return to this place. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I know<small><small>[^21]</small></small> the thoughts<small><small>[^22]</small></small> that I think toward you, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, thoughts of peace, and not of evil, to give you an expected end<small><small>[^23]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then shall ye call upon me, and ye shall go and pray unto me, and I will hearken unto you.<small><small>[^24]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And ye shall<small><small>[^25]</small></small> seek me, and find \[me\], when ye shall search for me with<small><small>[^26]</small></small> all your heart. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will be<small><small>[^27]</small></small> found of you, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and I will turn<small><small>[^28]</small></small> away your captivity, and I will gather you from all the nations, and from all the places whither I have driven you, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; and I will bring you again into the place whence I caused you to be carried away captive. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because ye have said, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath raised us up prophets in Babylon;<small><small>[^29]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Know\] that thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of the king that sitteth upon the throne of David, and of all the people that dwelleth in this city, \[and\] of your brethren that are not gone forth with you into captivity;<small><small>[^30]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts; Behold<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, I will send upon them the sword, the famine, and the pestilence, and will make them like<small><small>[^32]</small></small> vile figs, that cannot be eaten, they are so evil. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will persecute them with the sword, with the famine, and with the pestilence, and will deliver<small><small>[^33]</small></small> them to be removed to all the kingdoms of the earth, to be a curse<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, and an astonishment, and an hissing, and a reproach, among all the nations whither I have driven them: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because they have not hearkened to my words, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, which I sent unto them by my servants the prophets, rising up early and sending \[them\]; but ye would not hear, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^35]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Hear ye therefore the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, all ye<small><small>[^36]</small></small> of the captivity, whom<small><small>[^37]</small></small> I have sent from Jerusalem to Babylon:<small><small>[^38]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, the God of Israel, of Ahab the son of Kolaiah, and of Zedekiah the son of Maaseiah, which<small><small>[^39]</small></small> prophesy a lie unto you in my name; Behold, I will deliver them into the hand of Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon; and he shall slay them before your eyes; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And of them shall be<small><small>[^40]</small></small> taken up a curse by all the captivity of Judah which \[are\] in Babylon, saying, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> make thee like Zedekiah and like Ahab, whom the king of Babylon roasted<small><small>[^41]</small></small> in the fire; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because they have committed villany in Israel, and have<small><small>[^42]</small></small> committed adultery with their neighbours’ wives, and have spoken lying<small><small>[^43]</small></small> words in my name, which I have not commanded them; even I<small><small>[^44]</small></small> know, and \[am\] a witness, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.

***The Message to Shemaiah*** ^bsb1762

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Thus\] shalt thou also speak to Shemaiah<small><small>[^45]</small></small> the Nehelamite<small><small>[^46]</small></small>, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus speaketh the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, the God of Israel, saying, Because<small><small>[^47]</small></small> thou hast sent letters in thy name unto all the people that \[are\] at Jerusalem, and to Zephaniah<small><small>[^48]</small></small> the son of Maaseiah the priest, and to all the priests, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath made thee priest in the stead of Jehoiada the priest, that ye should be officers<small><small>[^49]</small></small> in the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, for every<small><small>[^50]</small></small> man \[that is\] mad, and maketh<small><small>[^51]</small></small> himself a prophet, that thou<small><small>[^52]</small></small> shouldest put him in prison, and in the stocks. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now therefore<small><small>[^53]</small></small> why hast thou not reproved Jeremiah of Anathoth, which<small><small>[^54]</small></small> maketh himself a prophet to you? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For therefore he sent unto us \[in\] Babylon, saying, This \[captivity is\] long: build ye houses, and dwell \[in them\]; and plant gardens, and eat the fruit of them.<small><small>[^55]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And Zephaniah the priest read this letter in the ears of Jeremiah the prophet.<small><small>[^56]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Then came the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> unto Jeremiah, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.31|31]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Send<small><small>[^57]</small></small> to all them of the captivity, saying, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> concerning Shemaiah the Nehelamite; Because<small><small>[^58]</small></small> that Shemaiah hath prophesied unto you, and I sent him not, and he caused you to trust in a lie: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.29.32|32]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; Behold, I will punish<small><small>[^59]</small></small> Shemaiah the Nehelamite, and his seed: he shall<small><small>[^60]</small></small> not have a man to dwell among this people; neither shall he behold<small><small>[^61]</small></small> the good that I will do for my people, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; because he hath taught rebellion<small><small>[^62]</small></small> against the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Jer.28|<< Jeremiah 28]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Jer.30|Jeremiah 30 >>]]</td></tr></table>
