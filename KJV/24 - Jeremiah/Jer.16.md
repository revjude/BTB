<big><big>[[Jer.16.1 Disaster Predicted|⥃]]</big></big>***Disaster Predicted***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The word<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> came also unto me, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt not take thee a wife, neither shalt thou have sons or daughters in this place.<small><small>[^3]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> concerning the sons and concerning the daughters that are born in this place, and concerning their mothers that bare them, and concerning their fathers that begat them in this land; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They shall die<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of grievous deaths; they shall not<small><small>[^6]</small></small> be lamented; neither<small><small>[^7]</small></small> shall they be buried; \[but\] they shall be as dung<small><small>[^8]</small></small> upon the face of the earth: and they shall be consumed<small><small>[^9]</small></small> by the sword, and by famine; and their carcases shall be meat<small><small>[^10]</small></small> for the fowls of heaven, and for the beasts of the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, Enter<small><small>[^11]</small></small> not into the house of mourning<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, neither go to lament nor bemoan them: for I have<small><small>[^13]</small></small> taken away my peace from this people, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, \[even\] lovingkindness and mercies. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Both the great<small><small>[^14]</small></small> and the small shall die in this land: they<small><small>[^15]</small></small> shall not be buried, neither shall \[men\] lament for them, nor cut<small><small>[^16]</small></small> themselves, nor make themselves bald for them: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Neither shall \[men\] tear \[themselves<small><small>[^17]</small></small>\] for them in mourning, to comfort them for the dead; neither shall \[men\] give them the cup<small><small>[^18]</small></small> of consolation to drink for their father or for their mother. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt not also go into the house of feasting, to sit with them to eat and to drink.<small><small>[^19]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, the God of Israel; Behold, I will<small><small>[^20]</small></small> cause to cease out of this place in your eyes, and in your days, the voice of mirth, and the voice of gladness, the voice of the bridegroom, and the voice of the bride. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And it shall come to pass, when thou shalt shew this people all these words, and they shall say unto thee, Wherefore<small><small>[^21]</small></small> hath the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> pronounced all this great evil against us? or what \[is\] our iniquity? or what \[is\] our sin that we have committed against the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> our God? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then shalt thou say unto them, Because<small><small>[^22]</small></small> your fathers have forsaken me, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and have walked<small><small>[^23]</small></small> after other gods, and have served them, and have worshipped them, and have forsaken me, and have not kept my law; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And ye have done worse<small><small>[^24]</small></small> than your fathers; for, behold, ye walk every one after the imagination<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of his evil<small><small>[^26]</small></small> heart, that they may not hearken unto me: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore will I<small><small>[^27]</small></small> cast you out of this land into a<small><small>[^28]</small></small> land<small><small>[^29]</small></small> that ye know not, \[neither\] ye nor your fathers; and there shall ye serve other gods day and night; where I will not shew you favour.

<big><big>[[Jer.16.14 God Will Restore Israel|⥃]]</big></big>***God Will Restore Israel***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore, behold<small><small>[^30]</small></small>, the days come, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, that it shall no more be said, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> liveth, that brought<small><small>[^31]</small></small> up the children of Israel out of the land of Egypt; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> liveth, that brought<small><small>[^32]</small></small> up the children of Israel from the land of the north, and from all the lands whither he had driven them: and I will bring them again into their land that I gave unto their fathers. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Behold, I will send<small><small>[^33]</small></small> for many fishers, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and they shall fish them; and after will I send for many hunters<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, and they shall hunt them from every mountain<small><small>[^35]</small></small>, and from every hill, and out of the holes of the rocks. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For mine eyes \[are\] upon all their ways: they are not hid from my face, neither is their iniquity hid from mine eyes.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And first<small><small>[^37]</small></small> I will recompense their iniquity and their sin double; because they have defiled<small><small>[^38]</small></small> my land, they have filled mine inheritance with the carcases<small><small>[^39]</small></small> of their detestable and abominable things. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, my strength<small><small>[^40]</small></small>, and my fortress, and my refuge in the day of affliction, the Gentiles<small><small>[^41]</small></small> shall come unto thee from the ends of the earth, and shall say, Surely<small><small>[^42]</small></small> our fathers have inherited lies, vanity, and \[things\] wherein<small><small>[^43]</small></small> \[there is\] no profit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Shall a man make gods unto himself, and they \[are\] no gods?<small><small>[^44]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.16.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore, behold, I will this<small><small>[^45]</small></small> once cause them to know, I will cause them to know mine hand and my might; and they<small><small>[^46]</small></small> shall know that my name \[is\] The <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^47]</small></small></span>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Jer.15|<< Jeremiah 15]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Jer.17|Jeremiah 17 >>]]</td></tr></table>
