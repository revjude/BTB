<big><big>[[Jer.26.1 A Warning to the Cities of Judah|⥃]]</big></big>***A Warning to the Cities of Judah***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the beginning of the reign of Jehoiakim the son of Josiah king of Judah came this word from the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, saying,<small><small>[^2]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; Stand<small><small>[^3]</small></small> in the court<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span> house, and speak unto all the cities of Judah, which come to worship in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span> house, all the words<small><small>[^5]</small></small> that I command thee to speak unto them; diminish<small><small>[^6]</small></small> not a word: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If so<small><small>[^7]</small></small> be they will hearken, and turn every man from his evil way, that I<small><small>[^8]</small></small> may repent me of the evil, which I purpose to do unto them because of the evil of their doings. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And thou shalt say unto them, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; If<small><small>[^9]</small></small> ye will not hearken to me, to walk in my law, which<small><small>[^10]</small></small> I have set before you, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To hearken to the words of my<small><small>[^11]</small></small> servants the prophets, whom<small><small>[^12]</small></small> I sent unto you, both rising up early, and sending \[them\], but ye have not hearkened; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then will I<small><small>[^13]</small></small> make this house like Shiloh, and will make this city a curse<small><small>[^14]</small></small> to all the nations of the earth.

<big><big>[[Jer.26.7 Jeremiah Threatened with Death|⥃]]</big></big>***Jeremiah Threatened with Death***

 <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So the priests and the prophets and all the people heard Jeremiah speaking these words in the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Now it came to pass, when Jeremiah had made an end of speaking all that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> had commanded \[him\] to speak unto all the people, that the priests<small><small>[^16]</small></small> and the prophets and all the people took him, saying, Thou shalt surely die. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Why<small><small>[^17]</small></small> hast thou prophesied in the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, saying, This house shall be like Shiloh, and this city shall be desolate without an inhabitant? And all<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the people were gathered against Jeremiah in the<small><small>[^19]</small></small> house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]When the princes<small><small>[^20]</small></small> of Judah heard these things, then they came up from the king’s house unto the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and sat down in the entry<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of the new<small><small>[^22]</small></small> gate of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span> \[house\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then spake the priests and the prophets unto the princes and to all the people, saying<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, This man \[is\] worthy to die<small><small>[^24]</small></small>; for he<small><small>[^25]</small></small> hath prophesied against this city, as ye have heard with your ears. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Then spake Jeremiah unto all the princes and to all the people, saying, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^26]</small></small></span> sent me to prophesy against this house and against this city all the words that ye have heard. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Therefore now amend<small><small>[^27]</small></small> your ways and your doings, and obey the voice of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> your God; and the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will repent<small><small>[^28]</small></small> him of the evil that he hath pronounced against you. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As for<small><small>[^29]</small></small> me, behold, I \[am\] in your hand: do with me as seemeth good and meet unto you<small><small>[^30]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But know ye for certain, that if ye put me to death, ye shall<small><small>[^31]</small></small> surely bring innocent blood upon yourselves, and upon this city, and upon the inhabitants thereof: for of<small><small>[^32]</small></small> a truth the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath sent me unto you to speak all these words in your ears.

<big><big>[[Jer.26.16 Jeremiah Spared from Death|⥃]]</big></big>***Jeremiah Spared from Death***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said the princes and all the people unto the priests and to the prophets; This man \[is\] not worthy to die: for he hath spoken to us in the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> our God.<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then rose<small><small>[^34]</small></small> up certain of the elders of the land, and spake to all the assembly of the people, saying, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Micah<small><small>[^35]</small></small> the Morasthite prophesied in the days of Hezekiah king of Judah, and spake to all the people of Judah, saying, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts; Zion<small><small>[^36]</small></small> shall be plowed \[like\] a field, and Jerusalem<small><small>[^37]</small></small> shall become heaps, and the mountain<small><small>[^38]</small></small> of the house as the high places of a forest.<small><small>[^39]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.26.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Did He<small><small>[^40]</small></small>zekiah king of Judah and all Judah put him at all to death? did he not fear the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and besought the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^41]</small></small></span>, and the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^42]</small></small></span> repented him of the evil which he had pronounced against them? Thus<small><small>[^43]</small></small> might we procure great evil against our souls.
