<big><big>[[Jer.21.1 Jerusalem Will Fall to Babylon|⥃]]</big></big>***Jerusalem Will Fall to Babylon***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The word<small><small>[^2]</small></small> which came unto Jeremiah from the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, when<small><small>[^3]</small></small> king Zedekiah sent unto him Pashur<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the son of Melchiah, and Zephaniah<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the son of Maaseiah the priest, saying,<small><small>[^6]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Enquire<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, I pray thee, of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> for<small><small>[^8]</small></small> us; for Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon maketh war against us; if so be that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will deal with us according<small><small>[^9]</small></small> to all his wondrous works, that he may go up from us. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Then said Jeremiah unto them, Thus shall ye say to Zedekiah: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel; Behold<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, I will turn back the weapons of war that \[are\] in your hands, wherewith ye fight against the king of Babylon, and \[against\] the Chaldeans, which besiege you without the walls, and I<small><small>[^11]</small></small> will assemble them into the midst of this city. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I<small><small>[^12]</small></small> myself will fight against you with an<small><small>[^13]</small></small> outstretched hand and with a strong arm, even in anger, and in fury, and in great wrath. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And I will<small><small>[^14]</small></small> smite the inhabitants of this city, both man and beast: they<small><small>[^15]</small></small> shall die of a great pestilence. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And afterward, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, I will<small><small>[^16]</small></small> deliver Zedekiah king of Judah, and his servants, and the people, and such as are left in this city from the pestilence, from the sword, and from the famine, into the hand of Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon, and into the hand of their enemies, and into the hand of those that seek their life: and he shall<small><small>[^17]</small></small> smite them with the edge of the sword; he shall not spare them, neither have pity, nor have mercy. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And unto this people thou shalt say, Thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; Behold, I set<small><small>[^18]</small></small> before you the way of life, and the way of death. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He that abideth<small><small>[^19]</small></small> in this city shall die by the sword, and by the famine, and by the pestilence: but he that goeth out, and falleth to the Chaldeans that besiege you, he shall live, and his<small><small>[^20]</small></small> life shall be unto him for a prey. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I have<small><small>[^21]</small></small> set my face against this city for evil, and not for good, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: it shall<small><small>[^22]</small></small> be given into the hand of the king of Babylon, and he shall burn it with fire.

<big><big>[[Jer.21.11 A Message to the House of David|⥃]]</big></big>***A Message to the House of David***

 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]And touching the house of the king of Judah, \[say\], Hear ye the word of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>;<small><small>[^23]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O house<small><small>[^24]</small></small> of David, thus saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; Execute<small><small>[^25]</small></small> judgment in the morning<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, and deliver<small><small>[^27]</small></small> \[him that is\] spoiled out of the hand of the oppressor, lest<small><small>[^28]</small></small> my fury go out like fire, and burn that none<small><small>[^29]</small></small> can quench \[it\], because of the evil of your doings. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, I \[am<small><small>[^30]</small></small>\] against thee, O inhabitant<small><small>[^31]</small></small> of the valley<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, \[and\] rock of the plain, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; which say, Who<small><small>[^33]</small></small> shall come down against us? or who shall enter into our habitations? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Jer.21.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I will punish<small><small>[^34]</small></small> you according<small><small>[^35]</small></small> to the fruit of your doings, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and I will kindle a fire in the<small><small>[^36]</small></small> forest thereof, and it shall<small><small>[^37]</small></small> devour all things round about it. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Jer.20|<< Jeremiah 20]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Jer.22|Jeremiah 22 >>]]</td></tr></table>
