<big><big>[[Mal.4.1 The Day of the LORD|⥃]]</big></big>***The Day of the LORD***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Zeph.1|Zephaniah 1:7–18]] <big>†</big> [[1Thess.5|1 Thessalonians 5:1–11]] <big>†</big> [[2Pet.3|2 Peter 3:8–13]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mal.4.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For, behold, the day<small><small>[^2]</small></small> cometh, that shall burn<small><small>[^3]</small></small> as an oven; and all the<small><small>[^4]</small></small> proud, yea, and all that do wickedly, shall be stubble: and the day that cometh shall burn them up, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts, that it<small><small>[^5]</small></small> shall leave them neither root nor branch. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mal.4.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]But unto you that fear<small><small>[^6]</small></small> my name shall the Sun<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of righteousness arise with healing<small><small>[^8]</small></small> in his wings<small><small>[^9]</small></small>; and ye shall<small><small>[^10]</small></small> go forth, and grow up as calves of the stall. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mal.4.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And ye shall tread down<small><small>[^11]</small></small> the wicked; for they shall be ashes under the soles of your feet in the day that I shall do \[this\], saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mal.4.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Remember ye the law<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of Moses my servant, which I commanded unto him in<small><small>[^13]</small></small> Horeb for all Israel, \[with<small><small>[^14]</small></small>\] the statutes and judgments. 

$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mal.4.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Behold, I will<small><small>[^15]</small></small> send you Elijah the prophet before the coming of the great<small><small>[^16]</small></small> and dreadful day of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mal.4.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he shall turn<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the heart of the fathers to the children, and the heart of the children to their fathers, lest<small><small>[^18]</small></small> I come and smite<small><small>[^19]</small></small> the earth with a curse.  <div canonical="true" eID="gen992" osisID="Mal" type="book"/> <div canonical="true" eID="gen28" subType="x-OT" type="bookGroup"/><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Mal.3|<< Malachi 3]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Matt.1|Matthew 1 >>]]</td></tr></table>
