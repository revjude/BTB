$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mal.2.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> [¶]Yet ye say, Wherefore<small><small>[^43]</small></small>? Therefore take<small><small>[^52]</small></small> heed to your spirit, and let none deal treacherously<small><small>[^53]</small></small> against the wife of his youth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Mal.2.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, the God of Israel, saith that he hateth putting away<small><small>[^54]</small></small>: for \[one\] covereth<small><small>[^55]</small></small> violence with his garment, saith the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> of hosts: therefore take heed to your spirit, that ye deal not treacherously.<small><small>[^56]</small></small>
