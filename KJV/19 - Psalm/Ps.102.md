***The Prayer of the Afflicted*** ^bsb1395



<w lemma="strong:H8605">A Prayer</w> <w lemma="strong:H6041">of<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the afflicted</w>, <w lemma="strong:H5848" morph="strongMorph:TH8799">when he is overwhelmed<small><small>[^3]</small></small></w>, <w lemma="strong:H8210" morph="strongMorph:TH8799">and poureth<small><small>[^4]</small></small> out</w> <w lemma="strong:H7879">his complaint</w> <w lemma="strong:H6440">before</w> <w lemma="strong:H3068">the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span></w>.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear<small><small>[^5]</small></small> my prayer, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and let my<small><small>[^6]</small></small> cry come unto thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hide<small><small>[^7]</small></small> not thy face from me in the day \[when\] I am in trouble; incline<small><small>[^8]</small></small> thine ear unto me: in the day<small><small>[^9]</small></small> \[when\] I call answer me speedily. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For my days<small><small>[^10]</small></small> are consumed like smoke<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, and my bones<small><small>[^12]</small></small> are burned as an hearth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My heart<small><small>[^13]</small></small> is smitten, and withered<small><small>[^14]</small></small> like grass; so that<small><small>[^15]</small></small> I forget to eat my bread. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By reason of the voice<small><small>[^16]</small></small> of my groaning my bones cleave to my skin<small><small>[^17]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I am like<small><small>[^18]</small></small> a pelican<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of the wilderness: I am like an owl of the desert. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I watch<small><small>[^20]</small></small>, and am as a sparrow alone<small><small>[^21]</small></small> upon the house top. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Mine<small><small>[^22]</small></small> enemies reproach me all the day; \[and\] they that are mad<small><small>[^23]</small></small> against me are sworn<small><small>[^24]</small></small> against me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I have<small><small>[^25]</small></small> eaten ashes like bread, and mingled<small><small>[^26]</small></small> my drink with weeping, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of thine indignation and thy wrath: for thou hast<small><small>[^28]</small></small> lifted me up, and cast me down. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My days<small><small>[^29]</small></small> \[are\] like a shadow that declineth; and I am withered<small><small>[^30]</small></small> like grass. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But thou<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, shalt endure for ever; and thy remembrance<small><small>[^32]</small></small> unto all generations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou<small><small>[^33]</small></small> shalt arise, \[and\] have mercy upon Zion: for the time to favour her, yea, the set<small><small>[^34]</small></small> time, is come. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thy servants take pleasure in her stones, and favour the dust thereof.<small><small>[^35]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> So the heathen shall fear the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and all the kings of the earth thy glory.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When<small><small>[^37]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall build up Zion, he shall<small><small>[^38]</small></small> appear in his glory. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He will<small><small>[^39]</small></small> regard the prayer of the destitute, and not despise<small><small>[^40]</small></small> their prayer. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> This<small><small>[^41]</small></small> shall be written for the generation to come: and the people<small><small>[^42]</small></small> which shall be created shall praise the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he<small><small>[^43]</small></small> hath looked down from the height<small><small>[^44]</small></small> of his sanctuary; from heaven did the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> behold the earth; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To hear<small><small>[^45]</small></small> the groaning of the prisoner; to loose<small><small>[^46]</small></small> those that are appointed to<small><small>[^47]</small></small> death; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To declare the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> in Zion, and his praise in Jerusalem;<small><small>[^48]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When the people are gathered together, and the kingdoms, to serve the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>.<small><small>[^49]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He weakened<small><small>[^50]</small></small> my strength in the way; he shortened<small><small>[^51]</small></small> my days. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I said<small><small>[^52]</small></small>, O my God, take me not away in the midst of my days: thy years<small><small>[^53]</small></small> \[are\] throughout all generations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Of old hast thou laid the foundation of the earth: and the heavens \[are\] the work of thy hands.<small><small>[^54]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They shall<small><small>[^55]</small></small> perish, but thou shalt endure<small><small>[^56]</small></small>: yea, all of them shall wax old like a garment; as a vesture shalt thou change them, and they shall be changed: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But thou \[art<small><small>[^57]</small></small>\] the same, and thy years<small><small>[^58]</small></small> shall have no end. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.102.28|28]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The children<small><small>[^59]</small></small> of thy servants shall continue, and their seed<small><small>[^60]</small></small> shall be established before thee. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.101|<< Psalm 101]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.103|Psalm 103 >>]]</td></tr></table>
