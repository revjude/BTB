***The LORD Is My Rock***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Sam.22|2 Samuel 22:1–51]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1311

<span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.18.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He delivered me from my strong<small><small>[^47]</small></small> enemy, and from <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.18.27|27]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thou wilt save<small><small>[^69]</small></small> the afflicted people; but wilt darkness. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.18.29|29]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For by thee<small><small>[^74]</small></small> I have run<small><small>[^75]</small></small> through a troop; and by my God<small><small>[^76]</small></small> have I leaped over a wall. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.18.30|30]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[As for\]
