***Be My Rock of Refuge*** ^bsb1364

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In thee, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, do I<small><small>[^2]</small></small> put my trust: let me<small><small>[^3]</small></small> never be put to confusion. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Deliver me in thy<small><small>[^4]</small></small> righteousness, and cause<small><small>[^5]</small></small> me to escape: incline<small><small>[^6]</small></small> thine ear unto me, and save me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be thou my strong habitation<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, whereunto I may continually resort: thou hast<small><small>[^8]</small></small> given commandment to save me; for thou \[art\] my rock<small><small>[^9]</small></small> and my fortress. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Deliver me, O my God, out of the<small><small>[^10]</small></small> hand of the wicked, out of the hand of the unrighteous and cruel man. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thou<small><small>[^11]</small></small> \[art\] my hope, O Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>: \[thou art\] my trust<small><small>[^12]</small></small> from my youth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By thee<small><small>[^13]</small></small> have I been holden up from the womb: thou art<small><small>[^14]</small></small> he that took me out of my mother’s bowels: my praise<small><small>[^15]</small></small> \[shall be\] continually of thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I am as a wonder<small><small>[^16]</small></small> unto many; but thou \[art<small><small>[^17]</small></small>\] my strong refuge.<small><small>[^18]</small></small><small><small>[^19]</small></small><small><small>[^20]</small></small><small><small>[^21]</small></small><small><small>[^22]</small></small><small><small>[^23]</small></small><small><small>[^24]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let my mouth be filled \[with\] thy praise \[and with\] thy honour all the day.<small><small>[^25]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Cast<small><small>[^26]</small></small> me not off in the time of old age<small><small>[^27]</small></small>; forsake me not when<small><small>[^28]</small></small> my strength faileth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For mine enemies speak against me; and they<small><small>[^29]</small></small> that lay wait for<small><small>[^30]</small></small> my soul take<small><small>[^31]</small></small> counsel together, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Saying, God<small><small>[^32]</small></small> hath forsaken him: persecute and take him; for \[there<small><small>[^33]</small></small> is\] none to deliver \[him\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O God<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, be not far from me: O my God, make<small><small>[^35]</small></small> haste for my help. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let them be<small><small>[^36]</small></small> confounded \[and\] consumed that are adversaries to my soul; let them be covered<small><small>[^37]</small></small> \[with\] reproach and dishonour that seek my hurt. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But<small><small>[^38]</small></small> I will hope continually, and will yet praise<small><small>[^39]</small></small> thee more and more. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My mouth<small><small>[^40]</small></small> shall shew forth thy righteousness \[and\] thy salvation all the day<small><small>[^41]</small></small>; for I know<small><small>[^42]</small></small> not the numbers \[thereof\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will go<small><small>[^43]</small></small> in the strength of the Lord <span class="smallcaps">God</span>: I will make<small><small>[^44]</small></small> mention of thy righteousness<small><small>[^45]</small></small>, \[even\] of thine only. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O God, thou hast<small><small>[^46]</small></small> taught me from my youth: and hitherto<small><small>[^47]</small></small> have I declared thy wondrous works. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Now<small><small>[^48]</small></small> also when I am old and grayheaded, O God, forsake me not; until I<small><small>[^49]</small></small> have shewed thy strength<small><small>[^50]</small></small> unto \[this\] generation, \[and\] thy power to every one \[that\] is to come.<small><small>[^51]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thy righteousness<small><small>[^52]</small></small> also, O God, \[is\] very high, who hast<small><small>[^53]</small></small> done great things: O God, who \[is\] like<small><small>[^54]</small></small> unto thee! <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Thou\], which<small><small>[^55]</small></small> hast shewed me great and sore troubles, shalt quicken<small><small>[^56]</small></small> me again, and shalt bring<small><small>[^57]</small></small> me up again from the depths of the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt increase<small><small>[^58]</small></small> my greatness, and comfort<small><small>[^59]</small></small> me on every side. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will also praise thee with the psaltery<small><small>[^60]</small></small>, \[even<small><small>[^61]</small></small>\] thy truth, O my God: unto thee will I sing with the harp, O thou<small><small>[^62]</small></small> Holy One of Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My lips<small><small>[^63]</small></small> shall greatly rejoice when I sing unto thee; and my soul<small><small>[^64]</small></small>, which thou hast redeemed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.71.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My tongue<small><small>[^65]</small></small> also shall talk of thy righteousness all the day long: for they<small><small>[^66]</small></small> are confounded, for they are brought unto shame, that seek my hurt. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.70|<< Psalm 70]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.72|Psalm 72 >>]]</td></tr></table>
