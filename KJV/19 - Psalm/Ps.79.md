$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.79.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O God, the heathen<small><small>[^3]</small></small> are come into<small><small>[^4]</small></small> thine inheritance; thy holy<small><small>[^5]</small></small> temple have they defiled; they have laid<small><small>[^6]</small></small> Jerusalem on heaps. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.79.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The dead bodies of thy servants have they given \[to be\] meat unto the fowls of the heaven, the flesh of thy saints unto the beasts of the earth.<small><small>[^7]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.79.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Their<small><small>[^8]</small></small> blood have they shed like water round about Jerusalem; and \[there<small><small>[^9]</small></small> was\] none to bury \[them\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.79.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> We are become<small><small>[^10]</small></small> a reproach to our neighbours, a scorn<small><small>[^11]</small></small> and derision to them that are round about us. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.79.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> How long<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>?
