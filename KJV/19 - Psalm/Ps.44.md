<big><big>[[Ps.44.1 Redeem Us, O God|⥃]]</big></big>***Redeem Us, O God***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Rom.8|Romans 8:35–39]] ]</sup></small>



To the chief Musician for the sons of Korah, Maschil.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> We have heard<small><small>[^2]</small></small> with our ears, O God, our fathers have told us, \[what\] work thou didst in their days, in the times<small><small>[^3]</small></small> of old. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[How\] thou didst drive out<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the heathen with thy hand, and plantedst them; \[how\] thou didst afflict<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the people, and cast them out. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For<small><small>[^6]</small></small> they got not the land in possession by their own sword, neither did their own arm save them: but thy right<small><small>[^7]</small></small> hand, and thine arm, and the light<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of thy countenance, because<small><small>[^9]</small></small> thou hadst a favour unto them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou art my King<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, O God: command<small><small>[^11]</small></small> deliverances for Jacob. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Through thee<small><small>[^12]</small></small> will we push<small><small>[^13]</small></small> down our enemies: through thy name will we tread<small><small>[^14]</small></small> them under that rise up against us. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I will not trust in my bow, neither shall my sword save me.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But<small><small>[^16]</small></small> thou hast saved us from our enemies, and hast put them<small><small>[^17]</small></small> to shame that hated us. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In God<small><small>[^18]</small></small> we boast all the day long, and praise<small><small>[^19]</small></small> thy name for ever. Selah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But thou hast cast off, and put us to shame; and goest not forth with our armies.<small><small>[^20]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou<small><small>[^21]</small></small> makest us to turn back from the enemy: and they which hate us spoil<small><small>[^22]</small></small> for themselves. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou hast given<small><small>[^23]</small></small> us like sheep \[appointed\] for meat<small><small>[^24]</small></small>; by reason of the enemy<small><small>[^36]</small></small> and avenger. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All this<small><small>[^37]</small></small> is come upon us; yet<small><small>[^38]</small></small> have we not forgotten thee, neither have we dealt<small><small>[^39]</small></small> falsely in thy covenant. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Our heart<small><small>[^40]</small></small> is not turned back, neither have<small><small>[^41]</small></small> our steps<small><small>[^42]</small></small> declined from thy way; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Though<small><small>[^43]</small></small> thou hast sore broken us in the<small><small>[^44]</small></small> place of dragons, and covered us with the<small><small>[^45]</small></small> shadow of death. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If we<small><small>[^46]</small></small> have forgotten the name of our God, or stretched<small><small>[^47]</small></small> out our hands to a strange god; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Shall<small><small>[^48]</small></small> not God search this out? for he knoweth<small><small>[^49]</small></small> the secrets of the heart. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yea<small><small>[^50]</small></small>, for thy sake are we killed<small><small>[^51]</small></small> all the day long; we are counted as sheep for the slaughter. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Awake<small><small>[^52]</small></small>, why sleepest thou, O Lord? arise, cast<small><small>[^53]</small></small> \[us\] not off for ever. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore<small><small>[^54]</small></small> hidest thou thy face, \[and\] forgettest<small><small>[^55]</small></small> our affliction and our oppression? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.25|25]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For our soul is bowed down to the dust: our belly cleaveth unto the earth.<small><small>[^56]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.44.26|26]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Arise for our help<small><small>[^57]</small></small>, and redeem<small><small>[^58]</small></small> us for thy mercies’ sake. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.43|<< Psalm 43]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.45|Psalm 45 >>]]</td></tr></table>
