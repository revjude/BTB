***I Will Set No Worthless Thing before My Eyes*** ^bsb1394



A Psalm of David.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.101.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will sing<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of mercy and judgment: unto thee<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, will I sing. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.101.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will behave<small><small>[^4]</small></small> myself wisely in a perfect way. O when<small><small>[^5]</small></small> wilt thou come unto me? I will walk<small><small>[^6]</small></small> within my house with a perfect<small><small>[^7]</small></small> heart. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.101.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will set<small><small>[^8]</small></small> no wicked thing<small><small>[^9]</small></small> before mine eyes: I hate<small><small>[^10]</small></small> the work of them<small><small>[^11]</small></small> that turn aside; \[it\] shall not<small><small>[^12]</small></small> cleave to me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.101.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> A froward<small><small>[^13]</small></small> heart shall depart from me: I will not know<small><small>[^14]</small></small> a wicked \[person\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.101.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whoso<small><small>[^15]</small></small> privily slandereth his neighbour, him will I cut off: him that hath an high<small><small>[^16]</small></small> look and a proud heart will not I suffer. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.101.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Mine<small><small>[^17]</small></small> eyes \[shall be\] upon the faithful of the land, that they<small><small>[^18]</small></small> may dwell with me: he that walketh in a perfect way<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, he shall serve me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.101.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He that worketh<small><small>[^20]</small></small> deceit shall not dwell within my house: he that telleth lies shall not tarry in my sight<small><small>[^21]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.101.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will early<small><small>[^22]</small></small> destroy all the wicked of the land; that I may cut off<small><small>[^23]</small></small> all wicked doers from the city of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.100|<< Psalm 100]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.102|Psalm 102 >>]]</td></tr></table>
