***A Thankful Heart*** ^bsb1431



\[A Psalm\] <w lemma="strong:H1732">of David</w>.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.138.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will praise<small><small>[^2]</small></small> thee with my whole<small><small>[^3]</small></small> heart: before<small><small>[^4]</small></small> the gods<small><small>[^5]</small></small> will I sing praise unto thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.138.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will worship toward<small><small>[^6]</small></small> thy holy temple, and praise<small><small>[^7]</small></small> thy name for thy lovingkindness and for thy truth: for thou hast<small><small>[^8]</small></small> magnified thy word above all thy name. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.138.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the day<small><small>[^9]</small></small> when I cried thou answeredst me, \[and\] strengthenedst<small><small>[^10]</small></small> me \[with\] strength in my soul. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.138.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All the kings<small><small>[^11]</small></small> of the earth shall praise thee, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, when they hear<small><small>[^12]</small></small> the words of thy mouth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.138.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yea, they shall<small><small>[^13]</small></small> sing in the ways of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: for great<small><small>[^14]</small></small> \[is\] the glory of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.138.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Though<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[be\] high, yet hath he respect unto the lowly: but the proud<small><small>[^16]</small></small> he knoweth afar off<small><small>[^17]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.138.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Though I walk<small><small>[^18]</small></small> in the midst of trouble, thou wilt<small><small>[^19]</small></small> revive me: thou shalt stretch<small><small>[^20]</small></small> forth thine hand against the wrath of mine enemies, and thy right<small><small>[^21]</small></small> hand shall save me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.138.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will perfect<small><small>[^22]</small></small> \[that which\] concerneth me: thy mercy<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, \[endureth\] for ever: forsake<small><small>[^24]</small></small> not the works of thine own hands. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.137|<< Psalm
