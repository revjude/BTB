<big><big>[[Ps.115.1 To Your Name Be the Glory|⥃]]</big></big>***To Your Name Be the Glory*** <small><small>[^2]</small></small> &emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Ps.135|Psalms 135:1–21]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Not unto us<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, not unto us, but unto thy name give glory, for thy mercy<small><small>[^4]</small></small>, \[and\] for thy truth’s sake. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wherefore should the heathen say, Where \[is\] now their God?<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But our<small><small>[^6]</small></small> God \[is\] in the heavens: he hath<small><small>[^7]</small></small> done whatsoever he hath pleased. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Their idols<small><small>[^8]</small></small> \[are\] silver and gold, the work of men’s hands. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They have mouths, but they speak not: eyes have they, but they see not: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They have ears, but they hear not: noses have they, but they smell not: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They have hands, but they handle not: feet have they, but they walk not: neither speak they through their throat. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They that make them are like unto them; \[so is\] every one that trusteth in them.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O Israel<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, trust<small><small>[^11]</small></small> thou in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: he \[is\] their help<small><small>[^12]</small></small> and their shield. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O house of Aaron, trust in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: he \[is\] their help and their shield.<small><small>[^13]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye that fear the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, trust in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: he \[is\] their help and their shield.<small><small>[^14]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath<small><small>[^15]</small></small> been mindful of us: he will bless \[us\]; he will bless the house of Israel<small><small>[^16]</small></small>; he will bless the house of Aaron. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He will bless<small><small>[^17]</small></small> them that fear the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, \[both\] small<small><small>[^18]</small></small> and<small><small>[^19]</small></small> great. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^20]</small></small></span> shall increase you<small><small>[^21]</small></small> more and more, you and your children. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye \[are\] blessed<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> which made<small><small>[^23]</small></small> heaven and earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The heaven<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, \[even\] the heavens, \[are\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord’s</span>: but the earth<small><small>[^25]</small></small> hath he given to the children of men. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The dead<small><small>[^26]</small></small> praise not the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, neither any that go down<small><small>[^27]</small></small> into silence. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.115.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But we will bless the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> from this time forth and for evermore.
