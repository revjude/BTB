<big><big>[[Ps.135.1 Give Praise, O Servants of the LORD|⥃]]</big></big>***Give Praise, O Servants of the LORD*** <small><small>[^2]</small></small> &emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Ps.115|Psalms 115:1–18]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praise ye the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^3]</small></small></span>. Praise ye the name<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; praise \[him\], O ye servants<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye that stand<small><small>[^6]</small></small> in the house of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, in the courts<small><small>[^7]</small></small> of the house of our God, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praise the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; for the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^8]</small></small></span> \[is\] good: sing praises unto his name; for \[it is<small><small>[^9]</small></small>\] pleasant. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^10]</small></small></span> hath chosen Jacob unto himself, \[and\] Israel for his peculiar<small><small>[^11]</small></small> treasure. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I know<small><small>[^12]</small></small> that the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] great, and \[that\] our Lord \[is\] above all gods. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Whatsoever<small><small>[^13]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> pleased, \[that\] did he in heaven, and in earth, in the seas<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, and all deep places. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He causeth<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the vapours to ascend from the ends of the earth; he maketh lightnings<small><small>[^16]</small></small> for the rain; he bringeth<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the wind out of his treasuries. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who smote<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the firstborn of Egypt, both of man and beast<small><small>[^19]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Who\] sent tokens<small><small>[^20]</small></small> and wonders into the midst of thee, O Egypt, upon Pharaoh<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, and upon all his servants. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who smote<small><small>[^22]</small></small> great nations, and slew mighty kings; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Sihon<small><small>[^23]</small></small> king of the Amorites, and Og king of Bashan, and all the<small><small>[^24]</small></small> kingdoms of Canaan: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And gave their<small><small>[^25]</small></small> land \[for\] an heritage, an heritage unto Israel his people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thy name<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, \[endureth\] for ever; \[and\] thy memorial, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, throughout all generations<small><small>[^27]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^28]</small></small></span> will judge his people, and he will repent<small><small>[^29]</small></small> himself concerning his servants. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The idols<small><small>[^30]</small></small> of the heathen \[are\] silver and gold, the work of men’s hands. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They have mouths, but they speak not; eyes have they<small><small>[^31]</small></small>, but they see not; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They have ears, but they hear not; neither is there \[any\] breath in their mouths. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They that<small><small>[^32]</small></small> make them are like unto them: \[so is\] every one that trusteth in them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Bless the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, O house of Israel: bless the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, O house of Aaron:<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Bless the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, O house of Levi: ye that fear the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, bless the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.135.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Blessed be the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> out of Zion<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, which dwelleth<small><small>[^35]</small></small> at Jerusalem. Praise ye the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.134|<< Psalm 134]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.136|Psalm 136 >>]]</td></tr></table>
