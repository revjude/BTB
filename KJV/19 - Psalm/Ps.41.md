<big><big>[[Ps.41.1 Victory over Betrayal|⥃]]</big></big>***Victory over Betrayal*** <small><small>[^2]</small></small> &emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[John.13|John 13:18–30]] ]</sup></small>



To the chief Musician, A Psalm of David.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Blessed<small><small>[^3]</small></small> \[is\] he that considereth the poor<small><small>[^4]</small></small>: the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^5]</small></small></span> will deliver him in time of trouble<small><small>[^6]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will preserve<small><small>[^7]</small></small> him, and keep him alive; \[and\] he shall be blessed<small><small>[^8]</small></small> upon the earth: and thou wilt not<small><small>[^9]</small></small> deliver him unto the will of his enemies. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will strengthen<small><small>[^10]</small></small> him upon the bed of languishing: thou wilt make<small><small>[^11]</small></small> all his bed in his sickness. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I said, <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^12]</small></small></span>, be merciful unto me: heal<small><small>[^13]</small></small> my soul; for I have sinned against thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Mine<small><small>[^14]</small></small> enemies speak evil of me, When shall he die, and his name<small><small>[^15]</small></small> perish? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And if he come to see \[me\], he speaketh<small><small>[^16]</small></small> vanity: his heart gathereth iniquity to itself; \[when<small><small>[^17]</small></small>\] he goeth abroad, he telleth \[it\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All that hate me whisper<small><small>[^18]</small></small> together against<small><small>[^19]</small></small> me: against me do they devise my hurt<small><small>[^20]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> An evil disease<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, \[say they\], cleaveth fast unto him: and<small><small>[^22]</small></small> \[now\] that he lieth he shall rise up no more. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yea<small><small>[^23]</small></small>, mine own familiar friend<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, in whom I trusted, which<small><small>[^25]</small></small> did eat of my bread, hath lifted up<small><small>[^26]</small></small> \[his\] heel against me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But thou, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, be merciful<small><small>[^27]</small></small> unto me, and raise me up, that<small><small>[^28]</small></small> I may requite them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> By this I know that thou favourest me, because<small><small>[^29]</small></small> mine enemy doth not triumph over me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And as for me, thou<small><small>[^30]</small></small> upholdest me in mine integrity, and settest<small><small>[^31]</small></small> me before thy face for ever. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.41.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Blessed<small><small>[^32]</small></small> \[be\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God of Israel from everlasting, and to everlasting. Amen<small><small>[^33]</small></small>, and Amen. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.40|<< Psalm 40]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.42|Psalm 42 >>]]</td></tr></table>
