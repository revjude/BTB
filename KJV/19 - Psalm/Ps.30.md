and unto<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> I made supplication. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.30.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What<small><small>[^25]</small></small> profit \[is there\] in my blood, when I go down to the pit? Shall the dust praise thee? shall it declare thy truth? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.30.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hear<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and have mercy upon me: <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, be thou<small><small>[^27]</small></small> my helper. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.30.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou hast turned<small><small>[^28]</small></small> for me my mourning into dancing<small><small>[^29]</small></small>: thou hast put off my sackcloth, and girded<small><small>[^30]</small></small> me with gladness; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.30.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To the end that \[my\] glory<small><small>[^31]</small></small> may sing praise to thee, and<small><small>[^32]</small></small> not be silent.
