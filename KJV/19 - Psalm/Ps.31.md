***Into Your Hands I Commit My Spirit***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Luke.23|Luke 23:44–49]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1324



<w lemma="strong:H5329" morph="strongMorph:TH8764">To the chief Musician</w>, <w lemma="strong:H4210">A Psalm</w> <w lemma="strong:H1732">of David</w>.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In thee<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, do I put my trust; let me never be ashamed: deliver<small><small>[^3]</small></small> me in thy righteousness.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Bow<small><small>[^5]</small></small> down thine ear to me; deliver<small><small>[^6]</small></small> me speedily: be thou my strong rock<small><small>[^7]</small></small>, for an house<small><small>[^8]</small></small> of defence to save me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thou \[art\] my rock and my fortress; therefore for thy<small><small>[^9]</small></small> name’s sake lead<small><small>[^10]</small></small> me, and guide me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Pull<small><small>[^11]</small></small> me out of the net that they have laid privily for me: for thou \[art\] my strength<small><small>[^12]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Into<small><small>[^13]</small></small> thine hand I commit my spirit: thou<small><small>[^14]</small></small> hast redeemed me, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God<small><small>[^15]</small></small> of truth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have hated<small><small>[^16]</small></small> them that regard lying<small><small>[^17]</small></small> vanities: but I trust in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will<small><small>[^18]</small></small> be glad and rejoice in thy mercy: for<small><small>[^19]</small></small> thou hast considered my trouble; thou hast known<small><small>[^20]</small></small> my soul in adversities; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And hast not shut me<small><small>[^21]</small></small> up into the hand of the enemy: thou hast set<small><small>[^22]</small></small> my feet in a large room. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Have mercy upon me, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, for I am in trouble: mine<small><small>[^23]</small></small> eye is consumed with grief, \[yea\], my soul<small><small>[^24]</small></small> and my belly. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For my life<small><small>[^25]</small></small> is spent with grief, and my years with sighing: my strength<small><small>[^26]</small></small> faileth because of mine iniquity, and my bones<small><small>[^27]</small></small> are consumed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I was<small><small>[^28]</small></small> a reproach among all mine enemies, but especially<small><small>[^29]</small></small> among my neighbours, and a fear<small><small>[^30]</small></small> to mine acquaintance: they that did see me without fled from me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I am forgotten<small><small>[^31]</small></small> as a dead man out of mind: I am like a broken vessel<small><small>[^32]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I have<small><small>[^33]</small></small> heard the slander of many: fear<small><small>[^34]</small></small> \[was\] on every side: while<small><small>[^35]</small></small> they took counsel together against me, they devised to take away my life. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I trusted in thee, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: I said, Thou<small><small>[^36]</small></small> \[art\] my God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My times<small><small>[^37]</small></small> \[are\] in thy hand: deliver<small><small>[^38]</small></small> me from the hand of mine enemies, and from them that persecute me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Make<small><small>[^39]</small></small> thy face to shine upon thy servant: save<small><small>[^40]</small></small> me for thy mercies’ sake. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let me<small><small>[^41]</small></small> not be ashamed, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; for I have called upon thee: let the wicked<small><small>[^42]</small></small> be ashamed, \[and\] let them<small><small>[^43]</small></small> be silent in the grave<small><small>[^44]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let the lying<small><small>[^45]</small></small> lips be put to silence; which speak<small><small>[^46]</small></small> grievous things<small><small>[^47]</small></small> proudly and contemptuously against the righteous. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Oh<small><small>[^48]</small></small>\] how great \[is\] thy goodness, which thou hast laid up<small><small>[^49]</small></small> for them that fear thee; \[which\] thou hast wrought<small><small>[^50]</small></small> for them that trust in thee before the sons of men! <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou shalt hide<small><small>[^51]</small></small> them in the secret of thy presence from<small><small>[^52]</small></small> the pride of man: thou shalt keep them secretly in a pavilion from the strife<small><small>[^53]</small></small> of tongues. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Blessed \[be\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: for he hath shewed me his marvellous<small><small>[^54]</small></small> kindness in a strong city<small><small>[^55]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I said<small><small>[^56]</small></small> in my haste, I am<small><small>[^57]</small></small> cut off from before thine eyes: nevertheless<small><small>[^58]</small></small> thou heardest the voice of my supplications when I cried unto thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O love<small><small>[^59]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, all ye his saints<small><small>[^60]</small></small>: \[for\] the<small><small>[^61]</small></small> <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> preserveth the faithful, and plentifully<small><small>[^62]</small></small> rewardeth the proud doer. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.31.24|24]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be of<small><small>[^63]</small></small> good courage, and he shall<small><small>[^64]</small></small> strengthen your heart, all ye<small><small>[^65]</small></small> that hope in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;"
