To the chief Musician, A Psalm \[and\] Song of David.

\[being\] girded<small><small>[^22]</small></small> with power: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.65.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which<small><small>[^23]</small></small> stilleth the noise<small><small>[^24]</small></small> of the seas, the noise of their waves, and the tumult<small><small>[^25]</small></small> of the people. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.65.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They also that dwell in the<small><small>[^26]</small></small> uttermost parts are afraid<small><small>[^27]</small></small> at thy tokens: thou makest the outgoings<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of the morning<small><small>[^29]</small></small> and evening to rejoice<small><small>[^30]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.65.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou visitest<small><small>[^31]</small></small> the earth, and waterest it<small><small>[^32]</small></small>: thou greatly<small><small>[^33]</small></small> enrichest it with the river<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of God, \[which\] is full of water: thou preparest<small><small>[^35]</small></small> them corn, when thou hast so provided for it. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.65.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou waterest the ridges thereof abundantly: thou settlest the furrows thereof<small><small>[^36]</small></small>: thou makest it soft with showers: thou blessest the springing thereof. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.65.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou crownest<small><small>[^37]</small></small> the year with thy<small><small>[^38]</small></small> goodness; and thy paths<small><small>[^39]</small></small> drop fatness<small><small>[^40]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.65.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They drop<small><small>[^41]</small></small> \[upon\] the pastures of the wilderness: and the little hills rejoice<small><small>[^42]</small></small> on every side. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.65.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The pastures<small><small>[^43]</small></small> are clothed with flocks;
