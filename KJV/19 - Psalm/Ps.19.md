To the chief Musician, A Psalm of David.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.19.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The heavens<small><small>[^2]</small></small> declare the glory of God; and the firmament<small><small>[^3]</small></small> sheweth his handywork. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.19.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Day unto<small><small>[^4]</small></small> day uttereth speech, and night unto<small><small>[^5]</small></small> night sheweth knowledge. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.19.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[There<small><small>[^6]</small></small> is\] no speech nor language, \[where<small><small>[^7]</small></small>\] their voice is not heard. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.19.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Their<small><small>[^8]</small></small> line<small><small>[^9]</small></small> is gone out through all the earth, and their words to the end of the world.
