<big><big>[[Ps.97.1 Let the Earth Rejoice|⥃]]</big></big>***Let the Earth Rejoice***

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.97.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^2]</small></small></span> reigneth; let the earth<small><small>[^3]</small></small> rejoice; and the daughters of Judah rejoiced because<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of thy judgments, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.97.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thou, <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, \[art\] high<small><small>[^20]</small></small> above all the earth: thou art exalted far<small><small>[^21]</small></small> above all gods. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.97.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Ye that<small><small>[^22]</small></small> love the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, hate<small><small>[^23]</small></small> evil: he preserveth<small><small>[^24]</small></small> the souls of his saints; he delivereth<small><small>[^25]</small></small> them out of the hand of the wicked. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.97.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Light<small><small>[^26]</small></small> is sown<small><small>[^27]</small></small> for the righteous, and gladness for the upright in heart. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.97.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Rejoice<small><small>[^28]</small></small> in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, ye righteous; and give thanks<small><small>[^29]</small></small> at the remembrance<small><small>[^30]</small></small> of his holiness. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.96|<< Psalm 96]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.98|Psalm 98 >>]]</td></tr></table>
