Maschil of Asaph.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O God<small><small>[^2]</small></small>, why hast thou cast \[us\] off for ever? \[why\] doth thine<small><small>[^7]</small></small> inheritance, \[which\] thou hast redeemed<small><small>[^8]</small></small>; this mount<small><small>[^9]</small></small> Zion, wherein thou hast dwelt. <span desolations; \[even\] all<small><small>[^12]</small></small> \[that\] the enemy hath done wickedly in the sanctuary. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thine<small><small>[^13]</small></small> enemies roar in the midst of thy congregations; they set<small><small>[^14]</small></small> up their ensigns \[for\] signs. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[A man\] was famous according as he had lifted up axes upon the thick trees.<small><small>[^15]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But now they break down the carved work thereof at once with axes and hammers.<small><small>[^16]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They have cast fire into thy sanctuary<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, they have defiled<small><small>[^18]</small></small> \[by casting down\] the dwelling<small><small>[^19]</small></small> place of thy name to the ground. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They said<small><small>[^20]</small></small> in their hearts, Let us destroy<small><small>[^21]</small></small> them together: they have burned up all the synagogues<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of God in the land. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> We see<small><small>[^23]</small></small> not our signs: \[there is\] no more<small><small>[^24]</small></small> any prophet: neither \[is there\] among us any that knoweth how long. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O God, how long shall the class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For God<small><small>[^28]</small></small> \[is\] my King of old, working<small><small>[^29]</small></small> salvation in the midst of the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou didst divide<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the sea by thy strength: thou brakest<small><small>[^31]</small></small> the heads of the dragons<small><small>[^32]</small></small> in the waters. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou brakest the heads of leviathan<small><small>[^33]</small></small> in pieces, \[and\] gavest him \[to be\] meat<small><small>[^34]</small></small> to the people inhabiting the wilderness. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou didst cleave<small><small>[^35]</small></small> the fountain and the flood<small><small>[^36]</small></small>: thou driedst up mighty rivers<small><small>[^37]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The day<small><small>[^38]</small></small> \[is\] thine, the night also \[is\] thine: thou hast prepared<small><small>[^39]</small></small> the light and the sun. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou hast set<small><small>[^40]</small></small> all the borders of the earth: thou hast made summer<small><small>[^41]</small></small> and winter. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Remember<small><small>[^42]</small></small> this, \[that\] the enemy hath reproached, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and \[that\] the foolish<small><small>[^43]</small></small> people have blasphemed thy name. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O deliver not the soul of thy turtledove<small><small>[^44]</small></small> unto the multitude \[of the wicked\]: forget<small><small>[^45]</small></small> not the congregation of thy poor for ever. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Have<small><small>[^46]</small></small> respect unto the covenant: for the dark<small><small>[^47]</small></small> places of the earth are full of the habitations<small><small>[^48]</small></small> of cruelty. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O let not<small><small>[^49]</small></small> the oppressed return ashamed: let the poor<small><small>[^50]</small></small> and needy praise thy name. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Arise<small><small>[^51]</small></small>, O God, plead thine own cause: remember<small><small>[^52]</small></small> how the foolish man reproacheth thee daily. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.74.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Forget<small><small>[^53]</small></small> not the voice of thine enemies: the
