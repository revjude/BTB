mine ears<small><small>[^20]</small></small> hast thou opened<small><small>[^21]</small></small>: burnt offering and sin offering hast thou not required. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.40.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Then said I, Lo<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, I come: in the<small><small>[^23]</small></small> volume of the book \[it is\] written of me, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.40.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I delight<small><small>[^24]</small></small> to do thy will, O my God: yea<small><small>[^25]</small></small>, thy law \[is\] within my heart<small><small>[^26]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.40.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have preached<small><small>[^27]</small></small> righteousness in the great congregation: lo, I have not<small><small>[^28]</small></small> refrained my lips, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, thou knowest<small><small>[^29]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.40.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I have not hid<small><small>[^30]</small></small> thy righteousness<small><small>[^31]</small></small> within my heart; I have declared thy faithfulness<small><small>[^32]</small></small> and thy salvation<small><small>[^33]</small></small>: I have not concealed thy lovingkindness<small><small>[^34]</small></small> and thy truth from the great congregation. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.40.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Withhold<small><small>[^35]</small></small> not thou thy tender mercies from me, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: let thy<small><small>[^36]</small></small> lovingkindness and thy truth continually preserve me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.40.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For innumerable<small><small>[^37]</small></small> evils have compassed me about: mine<small><small>[^38]</small></small> iniquities have taken hold upon me, so that I am not able to look up; they are<small><small>[^39]</small></small> more than the hairs of mine head: therefore my heart<small><small>[^40]</small></small> faileth<small><small>[^41]</small></small> me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.40.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be<small><small>[^42]</small></small> pleased, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, to deliver me: O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, make<small><small>[^43]</small></small> haste to help me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.40.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let them be ashamed<small><small>[^44]</small></small> and confounded together that<small><small>[^45]</small></small> seek after my soul to destroy it; let them be driven<small><small>[^46]</small></small> backward and put to shame that wish me evil. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.40.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let them be desolate<small><small>[^47]</small></small> for a reward of their shame that say<small><small>[^48]</small></small> unto me, Aha, aha. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.40.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let all<small><small>[^49]</small></small> those that seek thee rejoice and be glad in thee: let such as love<small><small>[^50]</small></small> thy salvation say<small><small>[^51]</small></small> continually, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> be magnified. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.40.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I \[am\] poor<small><small>[^52]</small></small> and needy; \[yet\] the Lord<small><small>[^53]</small></small> thinketh upon me: thou \[art\] my help<small><small>[^54]</small></small> and my deliverer; make<small><small>[^55]</small></small> no tarrying, O my God. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.39|<< Psalm 39]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.41|Psalm 41 >>]]</td></tr></table>
