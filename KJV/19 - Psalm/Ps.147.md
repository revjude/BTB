***It Is Good to Sing Praises*** ^bsb1440

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praise ye the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: for \[it is\] good<small><small>[^2]</small></small> to sing praises unto our God; for \[it is\] pleasant; \[and\] praise<small><small>[^3]</small></small> is comely. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> doth build<small><small>[^4]</small></small> up Jerusalem: he<small><small>[^5]</small></small> gathereth together the outcasts of Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He healeth<small><small>[^6]</small></small> the broken in heart, and bindeth up their wounds<small><small>[^7]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He<small><small>[^8]</small></small> telleth the number of the stars; he calleth them all by \[their\] names. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Great<small><small>[^9]</small></small> \[is\] our Lord, and of great power: his understanding \[is\] infinite<small><small>[^10]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> lifteth up<small><small>[^11]</small></small> the meek: he casteth<small><small>[^12]</small></small> the wicked down to the ground. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Sing unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> with thanksgiving; sing praise upon the harp unto our God:<small><small>[^13]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who covereth<small><small>[^14]</small></small> the heaven with clouds, who prepareth<small><small>[^15]</small></small> rain for the earth, who maketh grass to grow upon the mountains. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He giveth to the beast his food, \[and\] to the young ravens which cry.<small><small>[^16]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He delighteth<small><small>[^17]</small></small> not in the strength of the horse: he taketh<small><small>[^18]</small></small> not pleasure in the legs of a man. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> taketh<small><small>[^19]</small></small> pleasure in them that fear<small><small>[^20]</small></small> him, in those that hope in his mercy. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praise the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, O Jerusalem; praise thy God<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, O Zion. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he hath<small><small>[^22]</small></small> strengthened the bars of thy gates; he hath blessed<small><small>[^23]</small></small> thy children within thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He maketh peace<small><small>[^24]</small></small> \[in\] thy borders, \[and\] filleth<small><small>[^25]</small></small> thee with the finest of the wheat<small><small>[^26]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He sendeth<small><small>[^27]</small></small> forth his commandment \[upon\] earth: his word<small><small>[^28]</small></small> runneth very swiftly. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He giveth<small><small>[^29]</small></small> snow like wool: he scattereth<small><small>[^30]</small></small> the hoarfrost like ashes. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He casteth<small><small>[^31]</small></small> forth his ice like morsels: who can stand<small><small>[^32]</small></small> before his cold? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He sendeth out his word, and melteth them: he causeth his wind to blow, \[and\] the waters flow.<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He sheweth his word<small><small>[^34]</small></small> unto Jacob, his statutes<small><small>[^35]</small></small> and his judgments unto Israel.<small><small>[^36]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.147.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He hath not dealt so<small><small>[^37]</small></small> with any nation: and \[as for his\] judgments, they have not known them. Praise ye the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.146|<< Psalm 146]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.148|Psalm 148 >>]]</td></tr></table>
