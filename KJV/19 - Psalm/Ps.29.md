$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.29.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Give<small><small>[^2]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, O ye mighty<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, give unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> glory and strength. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.29.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Give<small><small>[^4]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> the glory<small><small>[^5]</small></small> due unto his name; yea, the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> sitteth King<small><small>[^29]</small></small> for ever. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.29.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will give<small><small>[^30]</small></small> strength unto his people;
