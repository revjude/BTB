<big><big>[[Ps.148.1 Praise the LORD from the Heavens|⥃]]</big></big>***Praise the LORD from the Heavens***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Ps.33|Psalms 33:1–22]] ]</sup></small>

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praise ye the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^2]</small></small></span>. Praise ye the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> from the heavens: praise him in the heights. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praise ye him, all his angels<small><small>[^3]</small></small>: praise ye him, all his hosts<small><small>[^4]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praise ye him, sun<small><small>[^5]</small></small> and moon: praise him, all ye stars of light. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praise him, ye heavens<small><small>[^6]</small></small> of heavens, and ye waters<small><small>[^7]</small></small> that \[be\] above the heavens. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let them praise the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: for he<small><small>[^8]</small></small> commanded, and they were created. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He hath also<small><small>[^9]</small></small> stablished them for ever and ever: he hath made a decree which shall not pass. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praise the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> from the earth<small><small>[^10]</small></small>, ye dragons<small><small>[^11]</small></small>, and all deeps: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Fire<small><small>[^12]</small></small>, and hail; snow, and vapour; stormy<small><small>[^13]</small></small> wind fulfilling his word: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Mountains<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, and all hills; fruitful trees, and all cedars: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Beasts<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, and all cattle; creeping things, and flying fowl<small><small>[^16]</small></small>: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Kings<small><small>[^17]</small></small> of the earth, and all people; princes, and all judges of the earth: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Both young men<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, and maidens; old men, and children: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let them praise the name of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: for his name<small><small>[^19]</small></small> alone is excellent<small><small>[^20]</small></small>; his glory<small><small>[^21]</small></small> \[is\] above the earth and heaven. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.148.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He also exalteth<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the horn of his people, the praise<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of all his saints; \[even\] of the children of Israel, a people<small><small>[^24]</small></small> near unto him.
