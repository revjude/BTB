***In You My Soul Takes Refuge***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[1Sam.22|1 Samuel 22:1–5]] <big>†</big> [[Ps.108|Psalms 108:1–13]] <big>†</big> [[Ps.142|Psalms 142:1–7]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1350



To the chief Musician, Al–taschith, Michtam of David, when<small><small>[^2]</small></small> he fled from Saul in the cave.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.57.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be<small><small>[^3]</small></small> merciful unto me, O God, be merciful unto me: for my soul<small><small>[^4]</small></small> trusteth in thee: yea, in the shadow<small><small>[^5]</small></small> of thy wings will I make my refuge, until<small><small>[^6]</small></small> \[these\] calamities be overpast. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.57.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will cry unto God most<small><small>[^7]</small></small> high; unto God that<small><small>[^8]</small></small> performeth \[all things\] for me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.57.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He shall send<small><small>[^9]</small></small> from heaven, and save me \[from\] the reproach of him<small><small>[^10]</small></small> that would swallow me up. Selah. God shall send<small><small>[^11]</small></small> forth his mercy and his truth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.57.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My soul \[is\] among<small><small>[^12]</small></small> lions: \[and\] I lie \[even among\] them that are set<small><small>[^13]</small></small> on fire, \[even\] the sons of men, whose<small><small>[^14]</small></small> teeth \[are\] spears and arrows, and their tongue<small><small>[^15]</small></small> a sharp sword. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.57.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be thou<small><small>[^16]</small></small> exalted, O God, above<small><small>[^17]</small></small> the heavens; \[let\] thy glory<small><small>[^18]</small></small> \[be\] above all the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.57.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They have prepared a net<small><small>[^19]</small></small> for my steps; my soul<small><small>[^20]</small></small> is bowed down: they have digged a pit before me, into the midst whereof they are fallen \[themselves\]. Selah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.57.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My<small><small>[^21]</small></small> heart is fixed<small><small>[^22]</small></small>, O God, my heart is fixed: I will<small><small>[^23]</small></small> sing and give praise. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.57.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Awake<small><small>[^24]</small></small> up, my glory<small><small>[^25]</small></small>; awake, psaltery and harp: I \[myself\] will awake early<small><small>[^26]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.57.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will praise thee, O Lord, among the people: I will sing unto thee among the nations.<small><small>[^27]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.57.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For<small><small>[^28]</small></small> thy mercy \[is\] great unto the heavens, and thy truth<small><small>[^29]</small></small> unto the clouds. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.57.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: \[let\] thy glory \[be\] above all the earth. <small><small>[^30]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.56|<< Psalm 56]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.58|Psalm 58 >>]]</td></tr></table>
