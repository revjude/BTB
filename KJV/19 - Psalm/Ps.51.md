$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Have mercy upon me, O God<small><small>[^3]</small></small>, according to thy lovingkindness: according unto the multitude<small><small>[^4]</small></small> of thy tender<small><small>[^5]</small></small> mercies blot<small><small>[^6]</small></small> out my transgressions. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Wash<small><small>[^7]</small></small> me thoroughly from mine iniquity, and cleanse<small><small>[^8]</small></small> me from my sin. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I<small><small>[^9]</small></small> acknowledge my transgressions: and my sin<small><small>[^10]</small></small> \[is\] ever before me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Against<small><small>[^11]</small></small> thee, thee only, have I sinned, and done \[this\] evil<small><small>[^12]</small></small> in thy sight: that thou<small><small>[^13]</small></small> mightest be justified when<small><small>[^14]</small></small> thou speakest, \[and\] be clear when thou judgest. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Behold, I was shapen<small><small>[^15]</small></small> in iniquity; \[that\] the bones<small><small>[^24]</small></small> \[which\] thou hast broken may rejoice. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Hide<small><small>[^25]</small></small> thy face from my sins, and blot<small><small>[^26]</small></small> out all mine iniquities. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Create<small><small>[^27]</small></small> in me a clean<small><small>[^28]</small></small> heart, O God; and take<small><small>[^32]</small></small> not thy holy<small><small>[^33]</small></small> spirit from me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Restore<small><small>[^34]</small></small> unto me the joy<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of thy salvation; and uphold<small><small>[^36]</small></small> me \[with thy\] free<small><small>[^37]</small></small> spirit. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Then<small><small>[^38]</small></small>\] will I teach transgressors thy ways<small><small>[^39]</small></small>; and sinners shall be converted<small><small>[^40]</small></small> unto thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Deliver<small><small>[^41]</small></small> me from bloodguiltiness<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, O God, thou God<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of my salvation: \[and\] my tongue<small><small>[^44]</small></small> shall sing aloud of thy righteousness<small><small>[^45]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O Lord<small><small>[^46]</small></small>, open<small><small>[^47]</small></small> thou my lips; and my mouth<small><small>[^48]</small></small> shall shew forth thy praise. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.51.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thou desirest<small><small>[^49]</small></small> not sacrifice;
