***Be Merciful to Me, O God***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[1Sam.21|1 Samuel 21:8–15]] ]</sup></small> ^bsb1349



To the chief Musician upon Jonath–elem–rechokim, Michtam<small><small>[^2]</small></small> of David, when<small><small>[^3]</small></small> the Philistines took him in Gath.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be<small><small>[^4]</small></small> merciful unto me, O God: for man would swallow<small><small>[^5]</small></small> me up; he fighting daily oppresseth me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Mine enemies<small><small>[^6]</small></small> would daily swallow \[me\] up: for \[they be\] many<small><small>[^7]</small></small> that fight against me, O thou most<small><small>[^8]</small></small> High. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What time I am afraid, I will trust in thee.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In God I will<small><small>[^10]</small></small> praise his word, in God I have<small><small>[^11]</small></small> put my trust; I will not fear what flesh can do unto me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Every day they<small><small>[^12]</small></small> wrest my words: all<small><small>[^13]</small></small> their thoughts \[are\] against me for evil. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They gather<small><small>[^14]</small></small> themselves together, they hide<small><small>[^15]</small></small> themselves, they mark<small><small>[^16]</small></small> my steps, when they wait for my soul. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Shall they escape<small><small>[^17]</small></small> by iniquity? in \[thine<small><small>[^18]</small></small>\] anger cast down the people, O God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou tellest<small><small>[^19]</small></small> my wanderings: put<small><small>[^20]</small></small> thou my tears into thy bottle: \[are they<small><small>[^21]</small></small>\] not in thy book? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When<small><small>[^22]</small></small> I cry \[unto thee\], then<small><small>[^23]</small></small> shall mine enemies turn back: this I know; for<small><small>[^24]</small></small> God \[is\] for me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In God will I praise \[his\] word: in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will I praise \[his\] word.<small><small>[^25]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In God have I put my trust: I will not<small><small>[^26]</small></small> be afraid what man can do unto me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thy<small><small>[^27]</small></small> vows \[are\] upon me, O God: I will<small><small>[^28]</small></small> render praises unto thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.56.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For<small><small>[^29]</small></small> thou hast delivered my soul from death: \[wilt<small><small>[^30]</small></small>\] not \[thou deliver\] my feet from falling, that I may walk<small><small>[^31]</small></small> before God in the light<small><small>[^32]</small></small> of the living?
