<big><big>[[Ps.63.1 Thirsting for God|⥃]]</big></big>***Thirsting for God***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[2Sam.15|2 Samuel 15:30–37]] ]</sup></small>



A Psalm of David, when he was in the wilderness of Judah.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.63.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O God, thou<small><small>[^2]</small></small> \[art\] my God; early<small><small>[^3]</small></small> will I seek thee: my soul<small><small>[^4]</small></small> thirsteth for thee, my flesh<small><small>[^5]</small></small> longeth for thee in a dry and thirsty land, where no water is<small><small>[^6]</small></small>; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.63.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To see<small><small>[^7]</small></small> thy power and thy glory, so \[as\] I have seen thee in the<small><small>[^8]</small></small> sanctuary. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.63.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because<small><small>[^9]</small></small> thy lovingkindness \[is\] better than life, my lips<small><small>[^10]</small></small> shall praise thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.63.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus<small><small>[^11]</small></small> will I bless thee while I live: I will lift<small><small>[^12]</small></small> up my hands in thy name. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.63.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My soul<small><small>[^13]</small></small> shall be satisfied as \[with\] marrow<small><small>[^14]</small></small> and fatness; and my mouth shall praise \[thee\] with joyful<small><small>[^15]</small></small> lips: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.63.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When I remember thee upon my bed, \[and\] meditate on thee in the \[night\] watches.<small><small>[^16]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.63.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Because<small><small>[^17]</small></small> thou hast been my help, therefore<small><small>[^18]</small></small> in the shadow of thy wings will I rejoice. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.63.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My soul<small><small>[^19]</small></small> followeth<small><small>[^20]</small></small> hard after thee: thy<small><small>[^21]</small></small> right hand upholdeth me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.63.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But those \[that\] seek<small><small>[^22]</small></small> my soul, to destroy \[it\], shall go<small><small>[^23]</small></small> into the lower parts of the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.63.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They shall fall<small><small>[^24]</small></small> by the sword: they shall be a portion<small><small>[^25]</small></small> for foxes. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.63.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But<small><small>[^26]</small></small> the king<small><small>[^27]</small></small> shall rejoice in God; every one that sweareth<small><small>[^28]</small></small> by him shall glory: but the mouth<small><small>[^29]</small></small> of them that speak lies shall be stopped. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.62|<< Psalm 62]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.64|Psalm 64 >>]]</td></tr></table>
