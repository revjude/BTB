<span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.113.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He raiseth<small><small>[^14]</small></small> up the poor out of<small><small>[^15]</small></small> the dust, \[and\] lifteth the needy<small><small>[^16]</small></small> out of the dunghill; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.113.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That he may set \[him\] with princes, \[even\] with the princes of his people.<small><small>[^17]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.113.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He maketh<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the barren woman to keep house<small><small>[^19]</small></small>, \[and to be\] a joyful mother of children.
