***Bless the LORD, O My Soul*** ^bsb1396



\[A Psalm\] of David.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Bless<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, O my soul: and all that<small><small>[^3]</small></small> is within me, \[bless\] his holy name<small><small>[^4]</small></small>.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Bless the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, O my soul, and forget not<small><small>[^6]</small></small> all his benefits: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who forgiveth<small><small>[^7]</small></small> all thine iniquities; who healeth<small><small>[^8]</small></small> all thy diseases; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who redeemeth<small><small>[^9]</small></small> thy life from destruction; who crowneth<small><small>[^10]</small></small> thee with lovingkindness and tender mercies; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who satisfieth<small><small>[^11]</small></small> thy mouth with good \[things\]; \[so that\] thy youth<small><small>[^12]</small></small> is renewed like the eagle’s. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> executeth<small><small>[^13]</small></small> righteousness and judgment for all that are oppressed. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He made<small><small>[^14]</small></small> known his ways unto Moses, his acts<small><small>[^15]</small></small> unto the children of Israel. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] merciful<small><small>[^16]</small></small> and gracious, slow<small><small>[^17]</small></small> to anger, and plenteous in mercy<small><small>[^18]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He will not always chide: neither<small><small>[^19]</small></small> will he keep \[his anger\] for ever. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He hath not dealt<small><small>[^20]</small></small> with us after our sins; nor rewarded us according to our iniquities. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For as the<small><small>[^21]</small></small> heaven is high above the earth, \[so\] great is his mercy<small><small>[^22]</small></small> toward them that fear him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> As far as the east<small><small>[^23]</small></small> is from the west, \[so\] far<small><small>[^24]</small></small> hath he removed our transgressions from us. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Like<small><small>[^25]</small></small> as a father pitieth \[his\] children, \[so\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> pitieth them<small><small>[^26]</small></small> that fear him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For he knoweth<small><small>[^27]</small></small> our frame; he remembereth that we \[are\] dust<small><small>[^28]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[As for\] man, his days<small><small>[^29]</small></small> \[are\] as grass: as a flower<small><small>[^30]</small></small> of the field, so he flourisheth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the wind<small><small>[^31]</small></small> passeth over it, and it is gone<small><small>[^32]</small></small>; and the<small><small>[^33]</small></small> place thereof shall know it no more. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But the mercy<small><small>[^34]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] from everlasting to everlasting upon them that fear him, and his righteousness<small><small>[^35]</small></small> unto children’s<small><small>[^36]</small></small> children; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To such<small><small>[^37]</small></small> as keep his covenant, and to those that remember<small><small>[^38]</small></small> his commandments to do them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> hath prepared<small><small>[^39]</small></small> his throne in the heavens; and his kingdom<small><small>[^40]</small></small> ruleth over all. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Bless<small><small>[^41]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, ye his angels, that excel in strength<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, that do his<small><small>[^43]</small></small> commandments, hearkening unto the voice of his word. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Bless ye the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, all \[ye\] his hosts<small><small>[^44]</small></small>; \[ye\] ministers<small><small>[^45]</small></small> of his, that do his pleasure. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.103.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Bless the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, all his works<small><small>[^46]</small></small> in all places of his dominion: bless the <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^47]</small></small></span>, O my soul. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.102|<< Psalm 102]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.104|Psalm 104 >>]]</td></tr></table>
