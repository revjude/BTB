<big><big>[[Ps.66.1 Make a Joyful Noise|⥃]]</big></big>***Make a Joyful Noise***&emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Ps.100|Psalms 100:1–5]] ]</sup></small>



To the chief Musician, A Song \[or\] Psalm.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Make<small><small>[^2]</small></small> a joyful noise unto God, all ye lands<small><small>[^3]</small></small>:<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Sing forth the honour of his name: make his praise glorious.<small><small>[^5]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Say unto God, How terrible<small><small>[^6]</small></small> \[art thou in\] thy works! through<small><small>[^7]</small></small> the greatness of thy power shall thine enemies submit themselves<small><small>[^8]</small></small> unto thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All the earth shall worship thee, and shall sing unto thee; they shall sing \[to\] thy name. Selah.<small><small>[^9]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Come<small><small>[^10]</small></small> and see the works of God: \[he is\] terrible<small><small>[^11]</small></small> \[in his\] doing toward the children of men. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He turned<small><small>[^12]</small></small> the sea into dry \[land\]: they<small><small>[^13]</small></small> went through the flood on foot: there<small><small>[^14]</small></small> did we rejoice in him. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He ruleth<small><small>[^15]</small></small> by his power for ever; his eyes<small><small>[^16]</small></small> behold the nations: let<small><small>[^17]</small></small> not the rebellious exalt themselves. Selah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O bless<small><small>[^18]</small></small> our God, ye people, and make<small><small>[^19]</small></small> the voice of his praise to be heard: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which holdeth<small><small>[^20]</small></small> our soul in life, and suffereth<small><small>[^21]</small></small> not our feet to be moved. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thou, O God, hast proved us<small><small>[^22]</small></small>: thou hast tried<small><small>[^23]</small></small> us, as silver is tried. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou broughtest<small><small>[^24]</small></small> us into the net; thou laidst affliction upon<small><small>[^25]</small></small> our loins. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou hast caused<small><small>[^26]</small></small> men to ride over our heads; we went through<small><small>[^27]</small></small> fire and through water: but thou<small><small>[^28]</small></small> broughtest us out into a wealthy<small><small>[^29]</small></small> \[place\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will go into<small><small>[^30]</small></small> thy house with burnt offerings: I will pay<small><small>[^31]</small></small> thee my vows, <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which my lips have uttered<small><small>[^32]</small></small>, and my mouth<small><small>[^33]</small></small> hath spoken, when<small><small>[^34]</small></small> I was in trouble. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will offer unto thee burnt sacrifices of fatlings<small><small>[^35]</small></small>, with the<small><small>[^36]</small></small> incense of rams; I will offer<small><small>[^37]</small></small> bullocks with goats. Selah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Come<small><small>[^38]</small></small> \[and\] hear, all ye that fear God, and I will<small><small>[^39]</small></small> declare what he hath done for my soul. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I cried<small><small>[^40]</small></small> unto him with my mouth, and he was<small><small>[^41]</small></small> extolled with my tongue. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> If I regard<small><small>[^42]</small></small> iniquity in my heart, the Lord will not hear \[me\]: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[But\] verily God hath heard \[me\]; he hath attended to the voice of my prayer.<small><small>[^43]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.66.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Blessed \[be\] God, which hath not turned away my prayer, nor his mercy from me. <small><small>[^44]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.65|<< Psalm 65]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.67|Psalm 67 >>]]</td></tr></table>
