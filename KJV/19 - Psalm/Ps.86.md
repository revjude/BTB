$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Bow<small><small>[^2]</small></small> down thine ear, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, hear me: for I \[am<small><small>[^3]</small></small>\] poor and needy. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Preserve<small><small>[^4]</small></small> my soul; and attend to the voice of my supplications.<small><small>[^17]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the day of my trouble I will call upon thee: for thou wilt answer me.<small><small>[^18]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Among<small><small>[^19]</small></small> the gods \[there is\] none like unto thee, O Lord; neither<small><small>[^20]</small></small> \[are there any works\] like unto thy works. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All<small><small>[^21]</small></small> nations whom thou hast made shall come and worship before thee, O Lord; and shall glorify<small><small>[^22]</small></small> thy name. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For<small><small>[^23]</small></small> thou \[art\] great, and doest wondrous things: thou \[art\] God<small><small>[^24]</small></small> alone. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Teach<small><small>[^25]</small></small> me thy way, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; I will<small><small>[^26]</small></small> walk in thy truth: unite<small><small>[^27]</small></small> my heart to fear thy name. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will praise<small><small>[^28]</small></small> thee, O Lord my God, with all<small><small>[^29]</small></small> my heart: and I will glorify<small><small>[^30]</small></small> thy name for evermore. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For great<small><small>[^31]</small></small> \[is\] thy mercy toward me: and thou<small><small>[^32]</small></small> hast delivered my soul from the lowest hell<small><small>[^33]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O God<small><small>[^34]</small></small>, the proud are risen against me, and the assemblies<small><small>[^35]</small></small> of violent<small><small>[^36]</small></small> \[men\] have sought after my soul; and have<small><small>[^37]</small></small> not set thee before them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But thou<small><small>[^38]</small></small>, O Lord, \[art\] a God full of compassion, and gracious, longsuffering, and plenteous in mercy<small><small>[^39]</small></small> and truth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O turn<small><small>[^40]</small></small> unto me, and have mercy upon me; give<small><small>[^41]</small></small> thy strength unto thy servant, and save the son<small><small>[^42]</small></small> of thine handmaid. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.86.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Shew me a token for good; that they<small><small>[^43]</small></small> which hate me may see \[it\], and be ashamed: because thou<small><small>[^44]</small></small>, <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, hast holpen me, and comforted me. <small><small>[^45]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.85|<< Psalm 85]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.87|Psalm 87 >>]]</td></tr></table>
