 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.146.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Praise ye the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. Praise the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, O my soul.<small><small>[^2]</small></small><small><small>[^3]</small></small><small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.146.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> While I live<small><small>[^5]</small></small> will I praise the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: I will sing praises unto my God while I have any being. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.146.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Put<small><small>[^6]</small></small> not your trust in princes, \[nor\] in the son of man, in whom \[there is\] no help<small><small>[^7]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.146.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> His breath<small><small>[^8]</small></small> goeth forth, he returneth<small><small>[^9]</small></small> to his earth; in that very day his thoughts<small><small>[^10]</small></small> perish. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.146.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Happy<small><small>[^11]</small></small> \[is he\] that \[hath\] the God<small><small>[^12]</small></small> of Jacob for his help, whose<small><small>[^13]</small></small> hope \[is\] in the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> his God: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.146.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which made heaven<small><small>[^14]</small></small>, and earth, the sea<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, and all that therein \[is\]: which keepeth truth<small><small>[^16]</small></small> for ever: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.146.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Which executeth<small><small>[^17]</small></small> judgment for the oppressed: which giveth food<small><small>[^18]</small></small> to the hungry. The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> looseth<small><small>[^19]</small></small> the prisoners: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.146.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> openeth<small><small>[^20]</small></small> \[the eyes of\] the blind: the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> raiseth<small><small>[^21]</small></small> them that are bowed down: the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> loveth<small><small>[^22]</small></small> the righteous: <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.146.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> preserveth<small><small>[^23]</small></small> the strangers; he relieveth the fatherless and widow: but the way<small><small>[^24]</small></small> of the wicked he turneth upside down. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.146.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall reign<small><small>[^25]</small></small> for ever, \[even\] thy God<small><small>[^26]</small></small>, O Zion, unto all generations.
