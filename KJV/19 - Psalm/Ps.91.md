 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.91.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He that dwelleth<small><small>[^3]</small></small> in the secret place of the most High shall abide<small><small>[^4]</small></small> under<small><small>[^5]</small></small> the shadow of the Almighty. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.91.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will<small><small>[^6]</small></small> say of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, \[He is\] my refuge and my fortress: my God<small><small>[^7]</small></small>;
