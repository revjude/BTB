<span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.145.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> To make known<small><small>[^22]</small></small> to the sons of men his mighty acts, and the glorious majesty of his kingdom. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.145.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thy kingdom<small><small>[^23]</small></small> \[is\] an everlasting kingdom<small><small>[^24]</small></small>, and thy dominion \[endureth\] throughout all generations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.145.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> upholdeth<small><small>[^25]</small></small> all that fall, and raiseth up<small><small>[^26]</small></small> all \[those that be\] bowed down. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.145.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The eyes<small><small>[^27]</small></small> of all wait upon thee<small><small>[^28]</small></small>; and thou givest them their meat in due season. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.145.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thou openest<small><small>[^29]</small></small> thine hand, and satisfiest the desire of every living thing. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.145.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] righteous<small><small>[^30]</small></small> in all his ways, and holy<small><small>[^31]</small></small> in all his works. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.145.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] nigh unto<small><small>[^32]</small></small> all them that call upon<small><small>[^33]</small></small> him, to all that call upon him in truth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.145.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He will fulfil<small><small>[^34]</small></small> the desire of them that fear him: he also will<small><small>[^35]</small></small> hear their cry, and will save them. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.145.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> preserveth<small><small>[^36]</small></small> all them that love him: but all the wicked<small><small>[^37]</small></small> will he destroy. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.145.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My mouth<small><small>[^38]</small></small> shall speak the praise of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: and let all flesh<small><small>[^39]</small></small> bless his holy name for ever and ever. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.144|<< Psalm 144]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.146|Psalm 146 >>]]</td></tr></table>
