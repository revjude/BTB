A Song \[or\] Psalm of David.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O God, my heart<small><small>[^3]</small></small> is fixed; I will<small><small>[^4]</small></small> sing and give praise, even with my glory<small><small>[^5]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Awake<small><small>[^6]</small></small>, psaltery and harp: I \[myself<small><small>[^7]</small></small>\] will awake early. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I will praise<small><small>[^8]</small></small> thee, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, among the people: and I will sing praises unto thee among the nations. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thy mercy<small><small>[^9]</small></small> \[is\] great above the heavens: and thy truth \[reacheth\] unto the clouds<small><small>[^10]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Be thou<small><small>[^11]</small></small> exalted, O God, above the heavens: and thy glory<small><small>[^12]</small></small> above all the earth; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That thy<small><small>[^13]</small></small> beloved may be delivered: save<small><small>[^14]</small></small> \[with\] thy right hand, and answer me<small><small>[^15]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> God hath spoken<small><small>[^16]</small></small> in his holiness; I will rejoice<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, I will divide Shechem<small><small>[^18]</small></small>, and mete out the valley<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of Succoth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Gilead<small><small>[^20]</small></small> \[is\] mine; Manasseh \[is\] mine; Ephraim<small><small>[^21]</small></small> also \[is\] the strength of mine head; Judah<small><small>[^22]</small></small> \[is\] my lawgiver; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Moab<small><small>[^23]</small></small> \[is\] my washpot; over Edom will I cast<small><small>[^24]</small></small> out my shoe; over Philistia<small><small>[^25]</small></small> will I triumph. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead<small><small>[^26]</small></small> me into Edom? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[Wilt\] not \[thou\], O God, \[who\] hast<small><small>[^27]</small></small> cast us off? and wilt not thou, O God, go forth<small><small>[^28]</small></small> with our hosts? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Give<small><small>[^29]</small></small> us help from trouble: for vain<small><small>[^30]</small></small> \[is\] the help of man. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.108.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Through<small><small>[^31]</small></small> God we shall do valiantly: for he \[it is that\] shall tread<small><small>[^32]</small></small> down our enemies. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.107|<< Psalm 107]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.109|Psalm 109 >>]]</td></tr></table>
