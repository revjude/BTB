$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.98.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O sing<small><small>[^2]</small></small> unto the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> a new song; with the harp, and the voice of a psalm.<small><small>[^11]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.98.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> With trumpets<small><small>[^12]</small></small> and sound of cornet make a joyful noise before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, the King<small><small>[^13]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.98.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let<small><small>[^14]</small></small> the sea roar, and the fulness thereof; the world<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, and they that dwell therein. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.98.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let the floods<small><small>[^16]</small></small> clap \[their\] hands: let the hills<small><small>[^17]</small></small> be joyful together <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.98.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Before the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; for he cometh<small><small>[^18]</small></small> to judge the earth: with righteousness<small><small>[^19]</small></small> shall he judge the world, and the people with equity. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.97|<< Psalm 97]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.99|Psalm 99 >>]]</td></tr></table>
