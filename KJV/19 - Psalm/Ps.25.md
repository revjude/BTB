***To You I Lift Up My Soul*** ^bsb1318



\[A Psalm\] of David.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Unto thee, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, do I<small><small>[^2]</small></small> lift up my soul.<small><small>[^3]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O<small><small>[^4]</small></small> my God, I trust in thee: let me not be ashamed, let not<small><small>[^5]</small></small> mine enemies triumph over me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yea, let none that wait<small><small>[^6]</small></small> on thee be ashamed<small><small>[^7]</small></small>: let<small><small>[^8]</small></small> them be ashamed which transgress without<small><small>[^9]</small></small> cause. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Shew me thy ways, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; teach me thy paths.<small><small>[^10]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Lead<small><small>[^11]</small></small> me in thy truth, and teach<small><small>[^12]</small></small> me: for thou \[art\] the God<small><small>[^13]</small></small> of my salvation; on thee<small><small>[^14]</small></small> do I wait all the day. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Remember<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, thy tender mercies<small><small>[^16]</small></small> and thy lovingkindnesses; for they<small><small>[^17]</small></small> \[have been\] ever of old. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Remember<small><small>[^18]</small></small> not the sins<small><small>[^19]</small></small> of my youth, nor my transgressions: according<small><small>[^20]</small></small> to thy mercy remember thou me for thy<small><small>[^21]</small></small> goodness’ sake, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Good<small><small>[^22]</small></small> and upright<small><small>[^23]</small></small> \[is\] the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: therefore will he teach<small><small>[^24]</small></small> sinners in the way. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The meek<small><small>[^25]</small></small> will he guide<small><small>[^26]</small></small> in judgment: and the meek will he teach his way<small><small>[^27]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> All the paths<small><small>[^28]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[are\] mercy<small><small>[^29]</small></small> and truth unto such as keep<small><small>[^30]</small></small> his covenant and his testimonies. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thy<small><small>[^31]</small></small> name’s sake, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, pardon mine iniquity; for it<small><small>[^32]</small></small> \[is\] great. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> What<small><small>[^33]</small></small> man \[is\] he that feareth the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>? him<small><small>[^34]</small></small> shall he teach in the way \[that\] he shall choose. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> His soul<small><small>[^35]</small></small> shall dwell at ease<small><small>[^36]</small></small>; and his seed<small><small>[^37]</small></small> shall inherit<small><small>[^38]</small></small> the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The secret<small><small>[^39]</small></small> of the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[is\] with them that fear him; and he will<small><small>[^40]</small></small> shew them his covenant. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Mine<small><small>[^41]</small></small> eyes \[are\] ever toward the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>; for he shall pluck<small><small>[^42]</small></small> my feet out<small><small>[^43]</small></small> of the net. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Turn<small><small>[^44]</small></small> thee unto me, and have mercy upon me; for I<small><small>[^45]</small></small> \[am\] desolate and afflicted. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The troubles of my heart are enlarged: \[O\] bring thou me out of my distresses.<small><small>[^46]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Look<small><small>[^47]</small></small> upon mine affliction and my pain; and forgive<small><small>[^48]</small></small> all my sins. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Consider<small><small>[^49]</small></small> mine enemies; for they are many; and they hate me with cruel hatred<small><small>[^50]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O<small><small>[^51]</small></small> keep my soul, and deliver me: let<small><small>[^52]</small></small> me not be ashamed; for I put my trust in thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Let integrity and uprightness preserve me; for I wait on thee.<small><small>[^53]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.25.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Redeem Israel, O God, out of all his troubles. <small><small>[^54]</small></small><table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.24|<< Psalm 24]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.26|Psalm 26 >>]]</td></tr></table>
