A Psalm of David.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.141.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, I cry unto thee: make haste<small><small>[^2]</small></small> unto me;
