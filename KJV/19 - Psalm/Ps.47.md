To the chief Musician, A Psalm for<small><small>[^2]</small></small> the sons of Korah.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.47.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O clap<small><small>[^3]</small></small> your hands, all ye people; shout<small><small>[^4]</small></small> unto God with the voice of triumph. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.47.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> most high \[is\] terrible<small><small>[^5]</small></small>; \[he is\] a great<small><small>[^6]</small></small> King over all the earth. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.47.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He shall subdue<small><small>[^7]</small></small> the people under us, and the nations under our feet<small><small>[^8]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.47.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He shall choose<small><small>[^9]</small></small> our inheritance for us, the excellency<small><small>[^10]</small></small> of Jacob whom<small><small>[^11]</small></small> he loved. Selah. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.47.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> God<small><small>[^12]</small></small> is gone up with a shout<small><small>[^13]</small></small>, the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> with the sound<small><small>[^14]</small></small> of a trumpet. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.47.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Sing praises to God<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, sing praises: sing praises unto our King<small><small>[^16]</small></small>, sing praises. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.47.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For God \[is\] the King<small><small>[^17]</small></small> of all the earth: sing<small><small>[^18]</small></small> ye praises with understanding<small><small>[^19]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.47.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> God reigneth<small><small>[^20]</small></small> over the heathen: God sitteth upon the throne<small><small>[^21]</small></small> of his holiness. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.47.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The princes<small><small>[^22]</small></small> of the people are gathered together, \[even\] the people of the God<small><small>[^23]</small></small> of Abraham: for the shields<small><small>[^24]</small></small> of the earth \[belong\] unto God: he is<small><small>[^25]</small></small> greatly exalted. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.46|<< Psalm 46]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.48|Psalm 48 >>]]</td></tr></table>
