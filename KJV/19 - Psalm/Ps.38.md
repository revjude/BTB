<big><big>[[Ps.38.1 Do Not Rebuke Me in Your Anger|⥃]]</big></big>***Do Not Rebuke Me in Your Anger*** <small><small>[^2]</small></small> &emsp;<small><sup>\[ [[Ps.6|Psalms 6:1–10]] ]</sup></small>



A Psalm of David, to bring<small><small>[^3]</small></small> to remembrance.


$\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, rebuke<small><small>[^4]</small></small> me not in thy wrath: neither chasten me in thy hot<small><small>[^5]</small></small> displeasure. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For thine<small><small>[^6]</small></small> arrows stick fast in me, and thy hand<small><small>[^7]</small></small> presseth me sore. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[There is\] no soundness<small><small>[^8]</small></small> in my flesh because of thine anger; neither<small><small>[^9]</small></small> \[is there any\] rest<small><small>[^10]</small></small> in my bones because<small><small>[^11]</small></small> of my sin. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For mine<small><small>[^12]</small></small> iniquities are gone over mine head: as an<small><small>[^13]</small></small> heavy burden they are too heavy for me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My wounds<small><small>[^14]</small></small> stink \[and\] are corrupt because of my foolishness. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I am troubled<small><small>[^15]</small></small>; I am bowed<small><small>[^16]</small></small> down greatly; I go mourning<small><small>[^17]</small></small> all the day long. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For my loins<small><small>[^18]</small></small> are filled with a loathsome \[disease\]: and \[there is\] no<small><small>[^19]</small></small> soundness in my flesh. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> I am feeble and sore broken: I have roared<small><small>[^20]</small></small> by reason of the disquietness of my heart. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Lord<small><small>[^21]</small></small>, all my desire \[is\] before thee; and my groaning<small><small>[^22]</small></small> is not hid from thee. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My heart<small><small>[^23]</small></small> panteth, my strength faileth me: as for the light<small><small>[^24]</small></small> of mine eyes, it also is gone from<small><small>[^25]</small></small> me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> My lovers<small><small>[^26]</small></small> and my friends stand<small><small>[^27]</small></small> aloof from my sore<small><small>[^28]</small></small>; and my kinsmen<small><small>[^29]</small></small> stand afar off<small><small>[^30]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They also that seek after my life lay snares<small><small>[^31]</small></small> \[for me\]: and they that seek my hurt speak<small><small>[^32]</small></small> mischievous things, and imagine deceits all the day long. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But I, as a deaf \[man\], heard not; and \[I was\] as a dumb man \[that\] openeth not his mouth.<small><small>[^33]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Thus I was as a man that heareth<small><small>[^34]</small></small> not, and in whose mouth \[are\] no reproofs. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For in thee<small><small>[^35]</small></small>, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, do<small><small>[^36]</small></small> I hope: thou wilt hear<small><small>[^37]</small></small>, O Lord<small><small>[^38]</small></small> my God. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I said<small><small>[^39]</small></small>, \[Hear me\], lest \[otherwise\] they should rejoice over me: when my foot<small><small>[^40]</small></small> slippeth, they magnify<small><small>[^41]</small></small> \[themselves\] against me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For I \[am\] ready to halt<small><small>[^42]</small></small>, and my sorrow<small><small>[^43]</small></small> \[is\] continually before me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For<small><small>[^44]</small></small> I will declare mine iniquity; I will be sorry<small><small>[^45]</small></small> for my sin. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But<small><small>[^46]</small></small> mine enemies \[are\] lively<small><small>[^47]</small></small>, \[and\] they are strong: and they that<small><small>[^48]</small></small> hate me wrongfully are multiplied. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They also that render<small><small>[^49]</small></small> evil for good are mine adversaries; because<small><small>[^50]</small></small> I follow \[the thing that\] good \[is\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Forsake me not, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>: O my God<small><small>[^51]</small></small>, be not far from me. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.38.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Make<small><small>[^52]</small></small> haste to help me<small><small>[^53]</small></small>, O Lord<small><small>[^54]</small></small> my salvation. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.37|<< Psalm 37]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.39|Psalm 39 >>]]</td></tr></table>
