***The LORD Will Not Forget His People*** <small><small>[^2]</small></small>  ^bsb1387

 $\quad$ <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.1|1]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> God, to whom vengeance belongeth<small><small>[^3]</small></small>; O God, to whom vengeance belongeth, shew thyself.<small><small>[^4]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.2|2]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Lift<small><small>[^5]</small></small> up thyself, thou<small><small>[^6]</small></small> judge of the earth: render<small><small>[^7]</small></small> a reward to the proud. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.3|3]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> <span class="smallcaps">Lord<small><small>[^8]</small></small></span>, how long shall the wicked<small><small>[^9]</small></small>, how long shall the wicked triumph? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.4|4]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> \[How long\] shall<small><small>[^10]</small></small> they utter \[and\] speak hard things? \[and\] all the workers of iniquity boast<small><small>[^11]</small></small> themselves? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.5|5]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They break<small><small>[^12]</small></small> in pieces thy people, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and afflict<small><small>[^13]</small></small> thine heritage. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.6|6]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They slay the widow and the stranger, and murder the fatherless.<small><small>[^14]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.7|7]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Yet they say<small><small>[^15]</small></small>, The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> shall not see, neither shall the God of Jacob regard \[it\]. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.8|8]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Understand, ye brutish<small><small>[^16]</small></small> among the people: and \[ye\] fools<small><small>[^17]</small></small>, when will ye be wise? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.9|9]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He that planted<small><small>[^18]</small></small> the ear, shall he not hear<small><small>[^19]</small></small>? he that formed the eye, shall he not see? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.10|10]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> He that chastiseth<small><small>[^20]</small></small> the heathen, shall not he correct<small><small>[^21]</small></small>? he that teacheth man<small><small>[^22]</small></small> knowledge, \[shall not he know\]? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.11|11]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> The <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> knoweth the thoughts of man, that they \[are\] vanity.<small><small>[^23]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.12|12]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Blessed<small><small>[^24]</small></small> \[is\] the man whom thou chastenest, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, and teachest<small><small>[^25]</small></small> him out of thy law; <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.13|13]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> That thou mayest<small><small>[^26]</small></small> give him rest from the days of adversity, until the pit<small><small>[^27]</small></small> be digged for the wicked. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.14|14]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> For<small><small>[^28]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> will not cast off his people, neither will he forsake<small><small>[^29]</small></small> his inheritance. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.15|15]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But<small><small>[^30]</small></small> judgment shall return unto righteousness: and all<small><small>[^31]</small></small> the upright in heart shall follow it<small><small>[^32]</small></small>. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.16|16]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Who will rise up<small><small>[^33]</small></small> for me against the evildoers? \[or\] who will stand up<small><small>[^34]</small></small> for me against the workers of iniquity? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.17|17]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Unless<small><small>[^35]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> \[had been\] my help, my soul had almost<small><small>[^36]</small></small> dwelt<small><small>[^37]</small></small> in silence. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.18|18]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> When I said, My foot<small><small>[^38]</small></small> slippeth; thy mercy, O <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span>, held me up. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.19|19]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> In the multitude of my thoughts within me thy comforts delight my soul.<small><small>[^39]</small></small> <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.20|20]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> Shall the throne<small><small>[^40]</small></small> of iniquity have fellowship<small><small>[^41]</small></small> with thee, which frameth<small><small>[^42]</small></small> mischief by a law? <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.21|21]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> They gather<small><small>[^43]</small></small> themselves together against the soul of the righteous, and condemn<small><small>[^44]</small></small> the innocent blood. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.22|22]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> But<small><small>[^45]</small></small> the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> is my defence; and my God \[is\] the rock<small><small>[^46]</small></small> of my refuge. <span class="startversemarker">[</span>**[[Ps.94.23|23]]**<span class="endversemarker">]</span> And he<small><small>[^47]</small></small> shall bring upon them their own iniquity, and shall cut them<small><small>[^48]</small></small> off in their own wickedness; \[yea\], the <span class="smallcaps">Lord</span> our God shall cut them off. <table width="100%" border="0"><tr><td style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;" width="1%" align="left">[[Ps.93|<< Psalm 93]]</td><td align="center" style="border:none;">&nbsp;</td><td width="1%" align="right" style="border:none;white-space: nowrap;">[[Ps.95|Psalm 95 >>]]</td></tr></table>
