**LXX Nehemiah 2:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Sanballat the Horonite heard \[of it\], and Tobiah the servant, the Ammonite, and it was a bad \[thing\] to them \[that it\] happened. And they were grieved that a man comes to seek good to the sons of Israel.

When Sanballat the Horonite, and Tobiah the servant, the Ammonite, heard [of it], it grieved them exceedingly that there was come a man to seek the welfare of the children of Israel.
