**LXX Nehemiah 2:12** 

***Nehemiah Surveys the Wall***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I rose up at night, I and a few men with me. And I did not report to a man, for  God put it into my heart  to do \[this\] with  Jerusalem. And there is no beast with me, except the beast which I mounted upon it. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And I arose in the night, I and some few men with me; neither told I [any] man what my God had put in my heart to do at Jerusalem: neither [was there any] beast with me, save the beast that I rode upon.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I set out at night with a few men. I did not tell anyone what my God had laid on my heart to do for Jerusalem. The only animal with me was the one on which I was riding.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
