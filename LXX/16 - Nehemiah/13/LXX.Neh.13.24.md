**LXX Nehemiah 13:24** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And  their sons \[were\] speaking half Ashdodish, and are not knowing to speak Jewish, but \[spoke\] according to \[the\] language of \[this\] people and \[that\] people.

And their children spake half in the speech of Ashdod, and could not speak in the Jews’ language, but according to the language of each people.
