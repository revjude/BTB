**LXX Nehemiah 7:6** 

***The Sons of the Captivity***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
These \[are\] the sons of the place, the ones ascending from the captivity of the resettlement of whom Nebuchadnezzar the king of Babylon resettled, and they returned to Jerusalem, and to Judah, \[every\] man to his own city; 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
These [are] the children of the province, that went up out of the captivity, of those that had been carried away, whom Nebuchadnezzar the king of Babylon had carried away, and came again to Jerusalem and to Judah, every one unto his city;
