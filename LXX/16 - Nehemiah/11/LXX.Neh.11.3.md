**LXX Nehemiah 11:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And these \[are\] the rulers of the place, the ones they settled in Jerusalem, and in \[the\] cities of Judah. \[Every\] man settled in his possession in their cities -- Israelites, the priests, and the Levites, and the Nethinim, and the sons of \[the\] servants of Solomon. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Now these [are] the chief of the province that dwelt in Jerusalem: but in the cities of Judah dwelt every one in his possession in their cities, [to wit], Israel, the priests, and the Levites, and the Nethinims, and the children of Solomon’s servants.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
