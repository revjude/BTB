**LXX Nehemiah 3:1** 

***The Families of Israel Rebuild Jerusalem***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Eliashib the great priest rose up, and  his brethren the priests, and they constructed the sheep gate; they sanctified it, and they set its doors; even unto \[the\] tower of the hundred they sanctified it, unto \[the\] tower of Hananeel. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then Eliashib the high priest rose up with his brethren the priests, and they builded the sheep gate; they sanctified it, and set up the doors of it; even unto the tower of Meah they sanctified it, unto the tower of Hananeel.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
At the Sheep Gate, Eliashib the high priest and his fellow priests began rebuilding. They dedicated it and installed its doors. After building as far as the Tower of the Hundred and the Tower of Hananel, they dedicated the wall.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
