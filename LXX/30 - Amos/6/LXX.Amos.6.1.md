**LXX Amos 6:1** 

***Woe to Israel***

Woe to the ones treating Zion with contempt, and to the ones yielding upon the mountain of Samaria; they harvested \[the\] heads of nations, and \[the\] house of Israel entered themselves,.

Woe to them [that are] at ease in Zion, and trust in the mountain of Samaria, [which are] named chief of the nations, to whom the house of Israel came!
