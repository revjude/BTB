**LXX Amos 7:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it shall be when the devouring the grass of the land should be completed, that I said, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, be kind! Who shall raise up  Jacob, for he is very few? 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it came to pass, [that] when they had made an end of eating the grass of the land, then I said, O Lord GOD, forgive, I beseech thee: by whom shall Jacob arise? for he [is] small.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And when the locusts had eaten every green plant in the land, I said, “Lord GOD, please forgive! How will Jacob survive, since he is so small?”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
