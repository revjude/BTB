**LXX Habakkuk 1:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Pure \[is\] the eye  to not see evil \[things\], and you are not able to look upon evils. Why should you look upon ones disdaining? Shall you remain silent in the impious swallowing down the just?
