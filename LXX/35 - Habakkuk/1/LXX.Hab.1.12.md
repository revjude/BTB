**LXX Habakkuk 1:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Are you not from \[the\] beginning, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  my God,  my holy one? In no way should we die. O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, for judgment -- you have ordered it. And he shaped me  to reprove for his discipline. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[Art] thou not from everlasting, O LORD my God, mine Holy One? we shall not die. O LORD, thou hast ordained them for judgment; and, O mighty God, thou hast established them for correction.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Are You not from everlasting, O LORD, my God, my Holy One? We will not die. O LORD, You have appointed them to execute judgment; O Rock, You have established them for correction.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
