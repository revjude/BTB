**LXX Habakkuk 1:15**

He pulled up consumption with a hook, and drew it with his casting-net, and gathered it in  his dragnets. Because of this he shall be glad and shall rejoice.

They take up all of them with the angle, they catch them in their net, and gather them in their drag: therefore they rejoice and are glad.
