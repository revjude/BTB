**LXX Habakkuk 2:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O, the one giving a drink  to his neighbor a clouded and intoxicating diet of \[lees\], so that he should look upon  their private parts. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Woe unto him that giveth his neighbour drink, that puttest thy bottle to [him], and makest [him] drunken also, that thou mayest look on their nakedness!
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Woe to him who gives drink to his neighbors, pouring it from the wineskin until they are drunk, in order to gaze at their nakedness!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
