**LXX Habakkuk 2:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O, the one overabounding evil wealth \[to\] his house,  to arrange his nest in \[the\] height,  to pull out from \[the\] hand of evils. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Woe to him that coveteth an evil covetousness to his house, that he may set his nest on high, that he may be delivered from the power of evil!
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Woe to him who builds his house by unjust gain, to place his nest on high and escape the hand of disaster!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
