**LXX Lamentations 2:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
<p style="text-align:center;font-weight:600;">ΝΟΥΝ</p>

Your prophets saw for you vanities and folly, and they uncovered not upon  your iniquity,  to turn your captivity; and they beheld for you vain concerns and purgations. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thy prophets have seen vain and foolish things for thee: and they have not discovered thine iniquity, to turn away thy captivity; but have seen for thee false burdens and causes of banishment.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The visions of your prophets were empty and deceptive; they did not expose your guilt to ward off your captivity. The burdens they envisioned for you were empty and misleading.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
