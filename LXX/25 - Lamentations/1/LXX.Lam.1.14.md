**LXX Lamentations 1:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
<p style="text-align:center;font-weight:600;">ΝΟΥΝ</p>

He was vigilant concerning  my acts of impiety; they are closely joined in my hands; they ascended unto  my neck. My strength is weakened, for \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> put griefs in my hands; I shall not be able to stand. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The yoke of my transgressions is bound by his hand: they are wreathed, [and] come up upon my neck: he hath made my strength to fall, the Lord hath delivered me into [their] hands, [from whom] I am not able to rise up.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
My transgressions are bound into a yoke, knit together by His hand; they are draped over my neck, and the Lord has broken my strength. He has delivered me into the hands of those I cannot withstand.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
