**LXX Job 32:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And undertaking, Elihu the \[son of\] Barachel the Buzite, said, Forasmuch as I am younger in time, and you are older; therefore I was still, fearing  to announce to you the higher knowledge of myself. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And Elihu the son of Barachel the Buzite answered and said, I [am] young, and ye [are] very old; wherefore I was afraid, and durst not shew you mine opinion.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
So Elihu son of Barachel the Buzite declared: “I am young in years, while you are old; that is why I was timid and afraid to tell you what I know.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
