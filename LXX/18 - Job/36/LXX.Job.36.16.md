**LXX Job 36:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And from when he beguiled from out of \[the\] mouth of \[the\] enemy, an abyss \[for\] throwing down underneath it, that your table went down full of fatness. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Even so would he have removed thee out of the strait [into] a broad place, where [there is] no straitness; and that which should be set on thy table [should be] full of fatness.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Indeed, He drew you from the jaws of distress to a spacious and broad place, to a table full of richness.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
