**LXX Job 9:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For if I might be just,  my mouth will be impious; and though I might be blameless, I shall turn out perverse. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
If I justify myself, mine own mouth shall condemn me: [if I say], I [am] perfect, it shall also prove me perverse.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Even if I were righteous, my mouth would condemn me; if I were blameless, it would declare me guilty.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
