**LXX Job 1:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> said to the devil, From what place have you come? And the devil answered  \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, \[and\] said, Going around the earth, and walking about the place at hand under heaven.

And the LORD said unto Satan, Whence comest thou? Then Satan answered the LORD, and said, From going to and fro in the earth, and from walking up and down in it.

“Where have you come from?” said the LORD to Satan. “From roaming through the earth,” he replied, “and walking back and forth in it.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
