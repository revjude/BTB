**LXX Hosea 13:1** 

***Rejection and Renewal***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
According to the word Ephraim conceived ordinances himself in Israel, and appointed  to Baal, and died. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
When Ephraim spake trembling, he exalted himself in Israel; but when he offended in Baal, he died.

When Ephraim spoke, there was trembling; he was exalted in Israel. But he incurred guilt through Baal, and he died.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
