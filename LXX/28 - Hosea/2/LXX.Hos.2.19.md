**LXX Hosea 2:19** 

***The LORD Will Espouse Israel to Himself***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I shall espouse you to myself into the eon; and I shall espouse you to myself in righteousness, and in equity, and in mercy, and in compassions.

And I will betroth thee unto me for ever; yea, I will betroth thee unto me in righteousness, and in judgment, and in lovingkindness, and in mercies.

So I will betroth you to Me forever; I will betroth you in righteousness and justice, in loving devotion and compassion.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
