**LXX Hosea 10:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Speaking words, lying excuses; he shall ordain a covenant. Judgment shall rise as wild grass upon an uncultivated field. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
They have spoken words, swearing falsely in making a covenant: thus judgment springeth up as hemlock in the furrows of the field.

They speak mere words; with false oaths they make covenants. So judgment springs up like poisonous weeds in the furrows of a field.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
