**LXX Hosea 10:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Ephraim -- a heifer being taught, loving altercation; but I shall come upon the best  of her neck; I will conduct Ephraim; I will pass silently over Judah; Jacob shall grow in strength against him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And Ephraim [is as] an heifer [that is] taught, [and] loveth to tread out [the corn]; but I passed over upon her fair neck: I will make Ephraim to ride; Judah shall plow, [and] Jacob shall break his clods.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Ephraim is a well-trained heifer that loves to thresh; but I will place a yoke on her fair neck. I will harness Ephraim, Judah will plow, and Jacob will break the hard ground.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
