**LXX 2 Kings 21:1** 

***Manasseh's Wicked Reign in Judah***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Manasseh \[was\] a son \[being\] twelve years \[old\] in  his taking reign, and fifty and five years he reigned in Jerusalem. And \[the\] name  of his mother \[was\] Hephzi-bah.

Manasseh [was] twelve years old when he began to reign, and reigned fifty and five years in Jerusalem. And his mother’s name [was] Hephzibah.

Manasseh was twelve years old when he became king, and he reigned in Jerusalem fifty-five years. His mother’s name was Hephzibah.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
