**LXX 2 Kings 11:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Jehosheba daughter of king Jehoram sister of Ahaziah took  Joash, \[the\] son of her brother, and she stole him \[away\] from \[the\] midst of the sons of the king, of the ones being put to death, him and  his nurse. And she hid him in the inner chamber of the beds from \[the\] face of Athaliah, and he was not put to death. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But Jehosheba, the daughter of king Joram, sister of Ahaziah, took Joash the son of Ahaziah, and stole him from among the king’s sons [which were] slain; and they hid him, [even] him and his nurse, in the bedchamber from Athaliah, so that he was not slain.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But Jehosheba daughter of King Joram, the sister of Ahaziah, took Joash son of Ahaziah and stole him away from among the sons of the king who were being murdered. She put him and his nurse in a bedroom to hide him from Athaliah, and he was not killed.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
