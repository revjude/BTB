**LXX 2 Kings 4:42** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And a man came from out of Baal-shalisha, and he brought to the man  of God first produce of twenty bread loaves of barley, and dried clusters of figs. And he said, Give \[them\] to the people, and let them eat!

And there came a man from Baalshalisha, and brought the man of God bread of the firstfruits, twenty loaves of barley, and full ears of corn in the husk thereof. And he said, Give unto the people, that they may eat.
