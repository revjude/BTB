**LXX 2 Kings 18:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Hezekiah king of Judah sent messengers to the king of the Assyrians, to Lachish, saying, I have sinned, turn from me! What ever you should place upon me I will bear. And the king of \[the\] Assyrians placed \[a tribute\] upon Hezekiah king of Judah of three hundred talents of silver and thirty talents of gold. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And Hezekiah king of Judah sent to the king of Assyria to Lachish, saying, I have offended; return from me: that which thou puttest on me will I bear. And the king of Assyria appointed unto Hezekiah king of Judah three hundred talents of silver and thirty talents of gold.
