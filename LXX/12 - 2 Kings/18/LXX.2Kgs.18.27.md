**LXX 2 Kings 18:27** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Rabshakeh said to them, Is it to  your master, and to you my master sent me to speak  these words? \[Is it\] not to the men, the ones sitting upon the wall,  to eat  their dung, and to drink  their urine with you together?

But Rabshakeh said unto them, Hath my master sent me to thy master, and to thee, to speak these words? [hath he] not [sent me] to the men which sit on the wall, that they may eat their own dung, and drink their own piss with you?
