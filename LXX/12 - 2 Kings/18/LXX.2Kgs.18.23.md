**LXX 2 Kings 18:23** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And now mix in \[with\] my master the king of \[the\] Assyrians, and I will give to you two thousand horses -- if you shall be able to put for yourself a rider upon them. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Now therefore, I pray thee, give pledges to my lord the king of Assyria, and I will deliver thee two thousand horses, if thou be able on thy part to set riders upon them.

Now, therefore, make a bargain with my master, the king of Assyria. I will give you two thousand horses—if you can put riders on them!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
