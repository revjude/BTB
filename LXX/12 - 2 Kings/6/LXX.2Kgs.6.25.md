**LXX 2 Kings 6:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And there was a great famine in Samaria. And behold, they laid siege against it until of which \[the\] head of a donkey was \[worth\] eighty shekels of silver; and a fourth  of a cab of doves dung five shekels of silver. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And there was a great famine in Samaria: and, behold, they besieged it, until an ass’s head was [sold] for fourscore [pieces] of silver, and the fourth part of a cab of dove’s dung for five [pieces] of silver.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
So there was a great famine in Samaria. Indeed, they besieged the city so long that a donkey’s head sold for eighty shekels of silver, and a quarter cab of dove’s dung sold for five shekels of silver.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
