**LXX 2 Kings 23:36** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Jehoiakim \[was\] a son \[being\] twenty and five years \[old\] in  his taking reign, and eleven years he reigned in Jerusalem. And \[the\] name  of his mother \[was\] Zebudah, daughter of Pedaiah of Rumah.

Jehoiakim [was] twenty and five years old when he began to reign; and he reigned eleven years in Jerusalem. And his mother’s name [was] Zebudah, the daughter of Pedaiah of Rumah.
