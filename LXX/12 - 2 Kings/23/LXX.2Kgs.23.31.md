**LXX 2 Kings 23:31** 

***Jehoahaz Reigns in Judah***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Jehoahaz was a son \[being\] twenty and three years \[old\] in  his taking reign; and three months he reigned in Jerusalem. And \[the\] name  of his mother \[was\] Hamutal, daughter of Jeremiah of Libnah. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Jehoahaz [was] twenty and three years old when he began to reign; and he reigned three months in Jerusalem. And his mother’s name [was] Hamutal, the daughter of Jeremiah of Libnah.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Jehoahaz was twenty-three years old when he became king, and he reigned in Jerusalem three months. His mother’s name was Hamutal daughter of Jeremiah; she was from Libnah.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
