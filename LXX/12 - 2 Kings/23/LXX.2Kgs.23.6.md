**LXX 2 Kings 23:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he brought forth the sacred grove \[carving\] from out of \[the\] house of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> outside of Jerusalem unto the rushing stream Kidron. And he incinerated it at the rushing stream Kidron, and \[ground\] it fine as dust. And he tossed  its dust into the tombs of the sons of the people. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he brought out the grove from the house of the LORD, without Jerusalem, unto the brook Kidron, and burned it at the brook Kidron, and stamped [it] small to powder, and cast the powder thereof upon the graves of the children of the people.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He brought the Asherah pole from the house of the LORD to the Kidron Valley outside Jerusalem, and there he burned it, ground it to powder, and threw its dust on the graves of the common people.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
