**LXX 2 Kings 24:17** 

***Zedekiah Reigns in Judah***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] king of Babylon gave reign to  Mattaniah brother  of his father instead of him. And he placed \[the\] name Zedekiah to him.
