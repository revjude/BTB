**LXX 2 Kings 24:8** 

***Jehoiachin Reigns in Jerusalem***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Jehoiachin \[was\] a son \[being\] eighteen years \[old\] in  his taking reign, and three months he reigned in Jerusalem. And \[the\] name \[to\] his mother \[was\] Nehushta daughter of Elnathan of Jerusalem. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Jehoiachin [was] eighteen years old when he began to reign, and he reigned in Jerusalem three months. And his mother’s name [was] Nehushta, the daughter of Elnathan of Jerusalem.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Jehoiachin was eighteen years old when he became king, and he reigned in Jerusalem three months. His mother’s name was Nehushta daughter of Elnathan; she was from Jerusalem.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
