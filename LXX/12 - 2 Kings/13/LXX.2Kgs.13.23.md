**LXX 2 Kings 13:23** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> showed mercy on them, and pitied them, and looked upon them on account of  his covenant, the \[one\] with Abraham, and Isaac, and Jacob. And \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> did not want to utterly destroy them, and he did not throw them from  his face until now.

And the LORD was gracious unto them, and had compassion on them, and had respect unto them, because of his covenant with Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, and would not destroy them, neither cast he them from his presence as yet.
