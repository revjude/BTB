**LXX Song of Solomon 7:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And your throat \[is\] as good wine, going \[to\] my beloved \[man\] in straightness, suiting in my lips and teeth. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the roof of thy mouth like the best wine for my beloved, that goeth [down] sweetly, causing the lips of those that are asleep to speak.

and your mouth like the finest wine. May it flow smoothly to my beloved, gliding gently over lips and teeth.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
