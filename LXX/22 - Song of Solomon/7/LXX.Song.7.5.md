**LXX Song of Solomon 7:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Your head upon you \[is\] as Carmel, and \[the\] braid of your head \[is\] as purple, \[with the\] king being bound by its passing by.

Thine head upon thee [is] like Carmel, and the hair of thine head like purple; the king [is] held in the galleries.

Your head crowns you like Mount Carmel, the hair of your head like purple threads; the king is captured in your tresses.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
