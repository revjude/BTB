**LXX Song of Solomon 6:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Your teeth as herds of the ones being sheared, the ones which ascend from the bath,  all bearing twins, and there is not one being childless among them. Your lips \[are\] as a scarlet string, and  your speech is beautiful. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thy teeth [are] as a flock of sheep which go up from the washing, whereof every one beareth twins, and [there is] not one barren among them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Your teeth are like a flock of sheep coming up from the washing; each has its twin, and not one of them is lost.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
