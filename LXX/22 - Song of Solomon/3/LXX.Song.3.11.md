**LXX Song of Solomon 3:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Come forth and behold, daughters of Zion! unto  king Solomon, unto the crown which his mother crowned him in \[the\] day of his betrothing, and in \[the\] day of \[the\] gladness of his heart. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Go forth, O ye daughters of Zion, and behold king Solomon with the crown wherewith his mother crowned him in the day of his espousals, and in the day of the gladness of his heart.

Come out, O daughters of Zion, and gaze at King Solomon, wearing the crown with which his mother crowned him on the day of his wedding—the day of his heart’s rejoicing.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
