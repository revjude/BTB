**LXX Song of Solomon 5:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
His legs \[are as\] columns of marble, founded upon bases of gold. His appearance as Lebanon, choice as cedars. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
His legs [are as] pillars of marble, set upon sockets of fine gold: his countenance [is] as Lebanon, excellent as the cedars.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
His legs are pillars of marble set on bases of pure gold. His appearance is like Lebanon, as majestic as the cedars.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
