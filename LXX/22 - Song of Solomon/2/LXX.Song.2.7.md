**LXX Song of Solomon 2:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I bound you by an oath, O daughters of Jerusalem, by the powers and by \[the\] strengths of the field, if you should arise and awaken  love until of which \[time\] it should want. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I charge you, O ye daughters of Jerusalem, by the roes, and by the hinds of the field, that ye stir not up, nor awake [my] love, till he please.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
O daughters of Jerusalem, I adjure you by the gazelles and does of the field: Do not arouse or awaken love until the time is right.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
