**LXX Song of Solomon 2:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
As an apple among the trees of the groves, so \[is\] my beloved \[man\] in the midst of the sons. \[To be\] in  his shadow I desired, and I sat down, and his fruit \[was\] sweet in my throat. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
As the apple tree among the trees of the wood, so [is] my beloved among the sons. I sat down under his shadow with great delight, and his fruit [was] sweet to my taste.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Like an apple tree among the trees of the forest is my beloved among the young men. I delight to sit in his shade, and his fruit is sweet to my taste.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
