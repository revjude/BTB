**LXX Song of Solomon 1:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] scent of your perfumes -- your name \[is\] over all the aromatics of perfume being emptied out; on account of this \[the\] young women loved you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Because of the savour of thy good ointments thy name [is as] ointment poured forth, therefore do the virgins love thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The fragrance of your perfume is pleasing; your name is like perfume poured out. No wonder the maidens adore you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
