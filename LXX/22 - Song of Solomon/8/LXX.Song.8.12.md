**LXX Song of Solomon 8:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
My vineyard, mine, \[is\] before me. The thousands to Solomon, and  two hundred to the ones keeping  its fruit. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
My vineyard, which [is] mine, [is] before me: thou, O Solomon, [must have] a thousand, and those that keep the fruit thereof two hundred.

But my own vineyard is mine to give; the thousand shekels are for you, O Solomon, and two hundred are for those who tend its fruit.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
