**LXX Song of Solomon 4:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Awake, O north \[wind\]! And come, O south! and refresh my garden, and let flow my aromatics! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Awake, O north wind; and come, thou south; blow upon my garden, [that] the spices thereof may flow out. Let my beloved come into his garden, and eat his pleasant fruits.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Awake, O north wind, and come, O south wind. Breathe on my garden and spread the fragrance of its spices. Let my beloved come into his garden and taste its choicest fruits.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
