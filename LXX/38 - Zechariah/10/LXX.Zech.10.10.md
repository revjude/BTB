**LXX Zechariah 10:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I will turn them from out of \[the\] land of Egypt; and from \[the\] Assyrians I will take them in; and into the \[land\] of Gilead, and into  Lebanon I will bring them; and in no way should there be left behind of them not even one. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I will bring them again also out of the land of Egypt, and gather them out of Assyria; and I will bring them into the land of Gilead and Lebanon; and [place] shall not be found for them.

I will bring them back from Egypt and gather them from Assyria. I will bring them to Gilead and Lebanon until no more room is found for them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
