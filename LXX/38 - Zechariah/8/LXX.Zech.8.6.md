**LXX Zechariah 8:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Thus says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> almighty, Shall it be impossible in the presence of the remnant  of this people in  those days, \[no\]. Shall it be impossible also before me, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> almighty, \[no\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thus saith the LORD of hosts; If it be marvellous in the eyes of the remnant of this people in these days, should it also be marvellous in mine eyes? saith the LORD of hosts.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
This is what the LORD of Hosts says: “If this is impossible in the eyes of the remnant of this people in these days, should it also be impossible in My eyes?” declares the LORD of Hosts.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
