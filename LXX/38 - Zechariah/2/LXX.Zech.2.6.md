**LXX Zechariah 2:6** 

***The Future Glory of Jerusalem***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Oh, Oh flee from \[the\] land of \[the\] north! says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. For from out of the four winds of the heaven I will gather you, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>.

Ho, ho, [come forth], and flee from the land of the north, saith the LORD: for I have spread you abroad as the four winds of the heaven, saith the LORD.
