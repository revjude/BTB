**LXX Zechariah 13:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O broadsword, awaken against  my shepherds, and against a man, my fellow-countryman! says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> almighty. Strike the shepherd! and the sheep were dispersed. And I will bring  my hand upon the shepherds. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Awake, O sword, against my shepherd, and against the man [that is] my fellow, saith the LORD of hosts: smite the shepherd, and the sheep shall be scattered: and I will turn mine hand upon the little ones.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Awake, O sword, against My Shepherd, against the man who is My Companion, declares the LORD of Hosts. Strike the Shepherd, and the sheep will be scattered, and I will turn My hand against the little ones.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
