**LXX Zechariah 13:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it will be in  that day the prophets shall be disgraced, each at  his vision when  he prophesies; and they shall put on a hair hide covering, because they lied. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it shall come to pass in that day, [that] the prophets shall be ashamed every one of his vision, when he hath prophesied; neither shall they wear a rough garment to deceive:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And on that day every prophet who prophesies will be ashamed of his vision, and he will not put on a hairy cloak in order to deceive.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
