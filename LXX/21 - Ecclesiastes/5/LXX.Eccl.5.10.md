**LXX Ecclesiastes 5:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] one loving silver shall not be filled of silver; \[nor\] one who loved \[the\] offspring in their abundance. And indeed this \[is\] folly. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He that loveth silver shall not be satisfied with silver; nor he that loveth abundance with increase: this [is] also vanity.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He who loves money is never satisfied by money, and he who loves wealth is never satisfied by income. This too is futile.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
