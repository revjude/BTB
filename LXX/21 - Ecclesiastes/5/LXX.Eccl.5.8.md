**LXX Ecclesiastes 5:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
If you should see extortion of \[the\] needy and seizure by lawsuit and judicial right in a place, you should not wonder over the thing; for a high one shall guard above a high one, and high ones over them. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
If thou seest the oppression of the poor, and violent perverting of judgment and justice in a province, marvel not at the matter: for [he that is] higher than the highest regardeth; and [there be] higher than they.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If you see the oppression of the poor and the denial of justice and righteousness in the province, do not be astonished at the matter; for one official is watched by a superior, and others higher still are over them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
