**LXX Ecclesiastes 12:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And O my son guard extra by them!  To make many scrolls there is no limit; and much meditation \[is\] weariness of flesh. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And further, by these, my son, be admonished: of making many books [there is] no end; and much study [is] a weariness of the flesh.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And by these, my son, be further warned: There is no end to the making of many books, and much study wearies the body.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
