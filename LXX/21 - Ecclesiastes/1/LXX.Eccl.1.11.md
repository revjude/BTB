**LXX Ecclesiastes 1:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
There is no remembrance to the first \[things\]; and indeed to the last \[things\] being, there will not be a remembrance of them, with the ones being born at the latter end. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[There is] no remembrance of former [things]; neither shall there be [any] remembrance of [things] that are to come with [those] that shall come after.

There is no remembrance of those who came before, and those yet to come will not be remembered by those who follow after.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
