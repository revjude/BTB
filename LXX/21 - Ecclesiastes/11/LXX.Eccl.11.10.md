**LXX Ecclesiastes 11:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And leave rage from your heart, and pass off wickedness from your flesh! for  youth and  thoughtlessness \[are\] folly. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Therefore remove sorrow from thy heart, and put away evil from thy flesh: for childhood and youth [are] vanity.

So banish sorrow from your heart, and cast off pain from your body, for youth and vigor are fleeting.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
