**LXX Ecclesiastes 6:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
If a man should engender a hundred, and shall live many years, and however abundant will be the days of his years, that  his soul shall not be filled up from goodness, and indeed there be no burial for him. I said, Better for him the miscarriage. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
If a man beget an hundred [children], and live many years, so that the days of his years be many, and his soul be not filled with good, and also [that] he have no burial; I say, [that] an untimely birth [is] better than he.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A man may father a hundred children and live for many years; yet no matter how long he lives, if he is unsatisfied with his prosperity and does not even receive a proper burial, I say that a stillborn child is better off than he.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
