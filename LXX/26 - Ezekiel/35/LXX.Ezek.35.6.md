**LXX Ezekiel 35:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
On account of this, \[as\] I live, says \[the\] Lord \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; Since you sinned unto blood, even blood shall pursue you. And blood you detested, even blood shall pursue you.

Therefore, [as] I live, saith the Lord GOD, I will prepare thee unto blood, and blood shall pursue thee: sith thou hast not hated blood, even blood shall pursue thee.

therefore as surely as I live, declares the Lord GOD, I will give you over to bloodshed and it will pursue you. Since you did not hate bloodshed, it will pursue you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
