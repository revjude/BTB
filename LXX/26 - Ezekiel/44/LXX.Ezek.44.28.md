**LXX Ezekiel 44:28** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it will be to them for an inheritance -- I \[am\] an inheritance to them; and a possession shall not be given to them among the sons of Israel -- I \[am\] their possession. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it shall be unto them for an inheritance: I [am] their inheritance: and ye shall give them no possession in Israel: I [am] their possession.

In regard to their inheritance, I am their inheritance. You are to give them no possession in Israel, for I am their possession.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
