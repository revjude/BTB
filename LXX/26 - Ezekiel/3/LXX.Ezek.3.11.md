**LXX Ezekiel 3:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And proceed, enter unto the captivity, to the sons  of your people! And you shall speak to them, and you shall say to them, Thus says \[the\] Lord, \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; Surely they shall hear, surely they shall give way. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And go, get thee to them of the captivity, unto the children of thy people, and speak unto them, and tell them, Thus saith the Lord GOD; whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Go to your people, the exiles; speak to them and tell them, ‘This is what the Lord GOD says,’ whether they listen or refuse to listen.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
