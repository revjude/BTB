**LXX Ezekiel 14:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
and  these three men \[were\] in \[the\] midst of it; as I live, says Adonai \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, shall sons or daughters be delivered, \[no\]. Only these alone shall be delivered, but the land will be for ruin. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[Though] these three men [were] in it, [as] I live, saith the Lord GOD, they shall deliver neither sons nor daughters; they only shall be delivered, but the land shall be desolate.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
then as surely as I live, declares the Lord GOD, even if these three men were in it, they could not deliver their own sons or daughters. They alone would be delivered, but the land would be desolate.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
