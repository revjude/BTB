**LXX Ezekiel 14:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Son of man,  these men set  their thoughts in  their hearts, and set the punishment of their iniquities before their face. Shall in answering I answer them, \[no\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Son of man, these men have set up their idols in their heart, and put the stumblingblock of their iniquity before their face: should I be inquired of at all by them?
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Son of man, these men have set up idols in their hearts and put wicked stumbling blocks before their faces. Should I consult with them in any way?<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
