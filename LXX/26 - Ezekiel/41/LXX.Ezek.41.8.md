**LXX Ezekiel 41:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the thraels of the house height round about \[was\] a space of the sides equal to the reed of six cubits intervals.

I saw also the height of the house round about: the foundations of the side chambers [were] a full reed of six great cubits.

I saw that the temple had a raised base all around it, forming the foundation of the side rooms. It was the full length of a rod, six long cubits.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
