**LXX Ezekiel 7:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
My eye will not spare over you, nor shall I show mercy. For  I shall impute your way against you, and  your abominations will be in \[the\] midst of you. And you shall know that I \[am\] \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And mine eye shall not spare thee, neither will I have pity: but I will recompense thy ways upon thee, and thine abominations shall be in the midst of thee: and ye shall know that I [am] the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I will not look on you with pity, nor will I spare you, but I will punish you for your ways and for the abominations among you. Then you will know that I am the LORD.’<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
