**LXX Ezekiel 7:7** 

***Punishment Meted Out to Israel***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Behold, \[the\] wreathen \[circle\] is come upon you, the one dwelling the land. The time is come, the day has approached, not with tumult nor with pangs. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The morning is come unto thee, O thou that dwellest in the land: the time is come, the day of trouble [is] near, and not the sounding again of the mountains.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Doom has come to you, O inhabitants of the land. The time has come; the day is near; there is panic on the mountains instead of shouts of joy.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
