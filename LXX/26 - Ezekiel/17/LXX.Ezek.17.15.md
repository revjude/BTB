**LXX Ezekiel 17:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he shall revolt from him  to send messengers unto Egypt,  to give to him horses and many people. Shall he prosper, \[no\]. Shall the one acting opposite be preserved, \[no\]. And shall one violating covenant be preserved, \[no\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But he rebelled against him in sending his ambassadors into Egypt, that they might give him horses and much people. Shall he prosper? shall he escape that doeth such [things]? or shall he break the covenant, and be delivered?
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But this king rebelled against Babylon by sending his envoys to Egypt to ask for horses and a large army. Will he flourish? Will the one who does such things escape? Can he break a covenant and yet escape?’<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
