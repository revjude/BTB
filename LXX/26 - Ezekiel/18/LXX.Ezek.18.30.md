**LXX Ezekiel 18:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Therefore I shall judge you each according to his way, O house of Israel, says Adonai \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. Be restored and turn from all  your impiety, and punishment of iniquity shall not be to you! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Therefore I will judge you, O house of Israel, every one according to his ways, saith the Lord GOD. Repent, and turn [yourselves] from all your transgressions; so iniquity shall not be your ruin.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Therefore, O house of Israel, I will judge you, each according to his ways, declares the Lord GOD. Repent and turn from all your transgressions, so that your iniquity will not become your downfall.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
