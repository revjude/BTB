**LXX Ezekiel 20:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I acted so that  my name  should not be thoroughly profaned before the nations which they are in \[the\] midst of them, to the ones whom I made known to them of their presence,  to lead them from out of \[the\] land of Egypt. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But I wrought for my name’s sake, that it should not be polluted before the heathen, among whom they [were], in whose sight I made myself known unto them, in bringing them forth out of the land of Egypt.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But I acted for the sake of My name, that it should not be profaned in the eyes of the nations among whom they were living, in whose sight I had revealed Myself to Israel by bringing them out of the land of Egypt.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
