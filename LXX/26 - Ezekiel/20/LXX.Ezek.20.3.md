**LXX Ezekiel 20:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O son of man, speak to the elders  of Israel! and you shall say to them, Thus says Adonai \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; Are you come to ask me? As I live, I shall not be answering to you, says Adonai \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Son of man, speak unto the elders of Israel, and say unto them, Thus saith the Lord GOD; Are ye come to inquire of me? [As] I live, saith the Lord GOD, I will not be inquired of by you.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Son of man, speak to the elders of Israel and tell them that this is what the Lord GOD says: Have you come to inquire of Me? As surely as I live, I will not be consulted by you, declares the Lord GOD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
