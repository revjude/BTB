**LXX Ezekiel 30:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In  that day shall come forth messengers hastening to obliterate the hope of Ethiopia; and there will be a disturbance among them in the day of Egypt; for behold, it is come. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
In that day shall messengers go forth from me in ships to make the careless Ethiopians afraid, and great pain shall come upon them, as in the day of Egypt: for, lo, it cometh.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
On that day messengers will go out from Me in ships to frighten Cush out of complacency. Anguish will come upon them on the day of Egypt’s doom. For it is indeed coming.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
