**LXX Ezekiel 36:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they entered unto the nations of which they entered there. And they profaned  my holy name in the saying of them, These \[are\] \[the\] people \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, and they went forth from out of his land. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And when they entered unto the heathen, whither they went, they profaned my holy name, when they said to them, These [are] the people of the LORD, and are gone forth out of his land.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And wherever they went among the nations, they profaned My holy name, because it was said of them, ‘These are the people of the LORD, yet they had to leave His land.’<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
