**LXX Ezekiel 12:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you shall bring forth  your items, items for a day of captivity before their eyes. And you shall go forth at evening before them as a captive goes forth before them. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then shalt thou bring forth thy stuff by day in their sight, as stuff for removing: and thou shalt go forth at even in their sight, as they that go forth into captivity.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Bring out your baggage for exile by day, as they watch. Then in the evening, as they watch, go out like those who go into exile.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
