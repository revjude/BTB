**LXX Ezekiel 24:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O son of man, behold, I take from you the desirable \[things\]  of your eyes in battle array; \[but\] in no way shall you beat your chest, nor in any way shall you weep, nor in any way tears come to you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Son of man, behold, I take away from thee the desire of thine eyes with a stroke: yet neither shalt thou mourn nor weep, neither shall thy tears run down.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Son of man, behold, I am about to take away the desire of your eyes with a fatal blow. But you must not mourn or weep or let your tears flow.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
