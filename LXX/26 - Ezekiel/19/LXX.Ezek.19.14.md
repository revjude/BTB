**LXX Ezekiel 19:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And fire came forth from out of a rod of her choice \[ones\] and devoured her; and a rod of strength was not in her. She is a tribe for a parable of lamentation, and will be for a lamentation. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And fire is gone out of a rod of her branches, [which] hath devoured her fruit, so that she hath no strong rod [to be] a sceptre to rule. This [is] a lamentation, and shall be for a lamentation.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Fire has gone out from its main branch and devoured its fruit; on it no strong branch remains fit for a ruler’s scepter.’ This is a lament and shall be used as a lament.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
