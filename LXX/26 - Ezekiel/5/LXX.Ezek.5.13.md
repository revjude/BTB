**LXX Ezekiel 5:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And my rage shall be exhausted, and  my anger against them. And I shall enjoin, and you shall know, because I \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> have spoken in  my zeal in  my exhausting  my anger against them. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thus shall mine anger be accomplished, and I will cause my fury to rest upon them, and I will be comforted: and they shall know that I the LORD have spoken [it] in my zeal, when I have accomplished my fury in them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And when My anger is spent and I have vented My wrath against them, I will be appeased. And when I have spent My wrath on them, they will know that I, the LORD, in My zeal have spoken.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
