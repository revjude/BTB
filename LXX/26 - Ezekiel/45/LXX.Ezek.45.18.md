**LXX Ezekiel 45:18** 

***The Holidays***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Thus says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  God; In the first month, \[day\] one of the month, you shall take a calf from out of \[the\] oxen, unblemished,  to atone the holy \[place\].

Thus saith the Lord GOD; In the first [month], in the first [day] of the month, thou shalt take a young bullock without blemish, and cleanse the sanctuary:

This is what the Lord GOD says: ‘On the first day of the first month you are to take a young bull without blemish and purify the sanctuary.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
