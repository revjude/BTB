**LXX Ezekiel 45:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the order \[concerning\] the olive oil -- a cup of olive oil out of ten cups, for the ten cups are a homer. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Concerning the ordinance of oil, the bath of oil, [ye shall offer] the tenth part of a bath out of the cor, [which is] an homer of ten baths; for ten baths [are] an homer:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The prescribed portion of oil, measured by the bath, is a tenth of a bath from each cor (a cor consists of ten baths or one homer, since ten baths are equivalent to a homer).<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
