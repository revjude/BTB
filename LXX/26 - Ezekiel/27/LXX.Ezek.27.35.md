**LXX Ezekiel 27:35** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
All the ones dwelling the islands shall be gloomy over you, and  their kings shall be amazed with a change of state, and their countenance shall burst into tears.

All the inhabitants of the isles shall be astonished at thee, and their kings shall be sore afraid, they shall be troubled in [their] countenance.
