**LXX Ezekiel 22:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Of whom \[the\] ones guiding in her midst \[are\] as roaring lions by force prey; devouring souls by domination, and taking honor; and  your widows are multiplied in your midst. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[There is] a conspiracy of her prophets in the midst thereof, like a roaring lion ravening the prey; they have devoured souls; they have taken the treasure and precious things; they have made her many widows in the midst thereof.

The conspiracy of the princes in her midst is like a roaring lion tearing its prey. They devour the people, seize the treasures and precious things, and multiply the widows within her.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
