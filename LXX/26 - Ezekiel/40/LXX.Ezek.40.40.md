**LXX Ezekiel 40:40** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And in back of the overflow of the whole burnt-offerings, of the \[part\] looking to \[the\] north, \[were\] two tables; \[and\] according to \[the\] back of \[the\] other and to the columned porch of the gate \[were\] two tables according to \[the\] east. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And at the side without, as one goeth up to the entry of the north gate, [were] two tables; and on the other side, which [was] at the porch of the gate, [were] two tables.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Outside, as one goes up to the entrance of the north gateway, there were two tables on one side and two more tables on the other side of the gate’s portico.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
