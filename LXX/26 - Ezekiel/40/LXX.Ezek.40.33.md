**LXX Ezekiel 40:33** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
and the vestibules, and the portals, and the pediments according to  these measures; and its windows and the pediments round about. Fifty cubits \[was\] the length, and its breadth twenty-five cubits. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the little chambers thereof, and the posts thereof, and the arches thereof, [were] according to these measures: and [there were] windows therein and in the arches thereof round about: [it was] fifty cubits long, and five and twenty cubits broad.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Its gate chambers, side pillars, and portico had the same measurements as the others. Both the gateway and its portico had windows all around. It was fifty cubits long and twenty-five cubits wide.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
