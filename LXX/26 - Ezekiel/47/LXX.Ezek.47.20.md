**LXX Ezekiel 47:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
This  part of the great sea defines the bounds unto before the entrance of Hamath, unto its entrance. These are the \[borders\] towards \[the\] west.

The west side also [shall be] the great sea from the border, till a man come over against Hamath. This [is] the west side.
