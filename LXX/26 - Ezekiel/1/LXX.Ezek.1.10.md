**LXX Ezekiel 1:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] likeness  of their faces \[was as the\] face of a man, and a face of a lion from out of \[the\] right to the four; and \[the\] face of a calf from out of \[the\] left to the four; and \[the\] face of an eagle to the four. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
As for the likeness of their faces, they four had the face of a man, and the face of a lion, on the right side: and they four had the face of an ox on the left side; they four also had the face of an eagle.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The form of their faces was that of a man, and each of the four had the face of a lion on the right side, the face of an ox on the left side, and also the face of an eagle.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
