**LXX Ezekiel 1:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the sight of the wheels and their action \[was\] as \[the\] sight of Tharsis \[stone\]. And \[there was\] one likeness to the four; and  their work was as if it may be a wheel in a wheel. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The appearance of the wheels and their work [was] like unto the colour of a beryl: and they four had one likeness: and their appearance and their work [was] as it were a wheel in the middle of a wheel.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The workmanship of the wheels looked like the gleam of beryl, and all four had the same likeness. Their workmanship looked like a wheel within a wheel.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
