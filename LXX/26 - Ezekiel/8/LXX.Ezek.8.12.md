**LXX Ezekiel 8:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he said to me, You have seen, O son of man, what the elders of the house of Israel do in darkness, each of them in  their hidden room; because they said, the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> does not see; \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> has abandoned the earth. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then said he unto me, Son of man, hast thou seen what the ancients of the house of Israel do in the dark, every man in the chambers of his imagery? for they say, The LORD seeth us not; the LORD hath forsaken the earth.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Son of man,” He said to me, “do you see what the elders of the house of Israel are doing in the darkness, each at the shrine of his own idol? For they are saying, ‘The LORD does not see us; the LORD has forsaken the land.’”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
