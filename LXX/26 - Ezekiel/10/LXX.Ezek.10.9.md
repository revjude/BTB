**LXX Ezekiel 10:9**

And I beheld, and behold, four wheels stood next to the cherubim, one wheel being next to one cherub, and one wheel being next to one cherub. And the appearance of the wheels \[was\] as \[the\] appearance of carbuncle stone.

And when I looked, behold the four wheels by the cherubims, one wheel by one cherub, and another wheel by another cherub: and the appearance of the wheels [was] as the colour of a beryl stone.
