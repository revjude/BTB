**LXX Ezekiel 32:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I will stretch you upon the earth; \[the\] plains shall be filled. And I shall set upon you all the birds of the heaven; and I will fill up by you all the wild beasts of all the earth. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then will I leave thee upon the land, I will cast thee forth upon the open field, and will cause all the fowls of the heaven to remain upon thee, and I will fill the beasts of the whole earth with thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I will abandon you on the land and hurl you into the open field. I will cause all the birds of the air to settle upon you, and all the beasts of the earth to eat their fill of you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
