**LXX Ezekiel 32:18** 

***Lament over Egypt***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O son of man, lament over the multitude of Egypt, for the daughters down her; \[even\] the nations dead into the depths of the earth, to the ones going down into \[the\] pit. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Son of man, wail for the multitude of Egypt, and cast them down, [even] her, and the daughters of the famous nations, unto the nether parts of the earth, with them that go down into the pit.

“Son of man, wail for the multitudes of Egypt, and consign her and the daughters of the mighty nations to the depths of the earth with those who descend to the Pit:<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
