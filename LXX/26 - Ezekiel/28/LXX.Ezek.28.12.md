**LXX Ezekiel 28:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O son of man, take a lamentation over the ruler of Tyre, and say to him! Thus says Adonai \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; You \[are\] a seal of likeness full of wisdom, and a crown of beauty. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Son of man, take up a lamentation upon the king of Tyrus, and say unto him, Thus saith the Lord GOD; Thou sealest up the sum, full of wisdom, and perfect in beauty.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Son of man, take up a lament for the king of Tyre and tell him that this is what the Lord GOD says: ‘You were the seal of perfection, full of wisdom and perfect in beauty.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
