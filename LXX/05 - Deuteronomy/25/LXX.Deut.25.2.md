**LXX Deuteronomy 25:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
then it will be if the one being impious might be worthy of strokes, they shall sit him before the judges, and they shall whip him before them according to  his impiety. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it shall be, if the wicked man [be] worthy to be beaten, that the judge shall cause him to lie down, and to be beaten before his face, according to his fault, by a certain number.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If the guilty man deserves to be beaten, the judge shall have him lie down and be flogged in his presence with the number of lashes his crime warrants.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
