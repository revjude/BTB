**LXX Deuteronomy 15:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
With supplies you shall provide him from  your sheep, and from  your grain, and from  your wine vat. As \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> your God blessed you you shall give to him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thou shalt furnish him liberally out of thy flock, and out of thy floor, and out of thy winepress: [of that] wherewith the LORD thy God hath blessed thee thou shalt give unto him.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You are to furnish him liberally from your flock, your threshing floor, and your winepress. You shall give to him as the LORD your God has blessed you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
