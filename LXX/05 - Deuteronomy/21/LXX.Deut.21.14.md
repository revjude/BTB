**LXX Deuteronomy 21:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it shall be if you should not want her, you shall send her free, and for sale you shall not sell \[her\] for silver, you shall not disrespect her, for you humbled her. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it shall be, if thou have no delight in her, then thou shalt let her go whither she will; but thou shalt not sell her at all for money, thou shalt not make merchandise of her, because thou hast humbled her.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And if you are not pleased with her, you are to let her go wherever she wishes. But you must not sell her for money or treat her as a slave, since you have dishonored her.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
