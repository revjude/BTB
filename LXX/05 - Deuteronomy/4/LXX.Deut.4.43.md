**LXX Deuteronomy 4:43** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
 Bezer in the wilderness, in the land in the plain  of Reuben; and  Ramoth in Gilead to the of Gadites; and  Golon in Bashan to the Manassites.
