**LXX Deuteronomy 3:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the remainder  of Gilead, and all  Bashan -- \[the\] kingdom of Og, I gave to the half tribe of Manasseh; and all the place round about Argob. All that Bashan shall be considered \[the\] land of Raphaim.

And the rest of Gilead, and all Bashan, [being] the kingdom of Og, gave I unto the half tribe of Manasseh; all the region of Argob, with all Bashan, which was called the land of giants.
