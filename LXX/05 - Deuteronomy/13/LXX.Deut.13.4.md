**LXX Deuteronomy 13:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You shall go after \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> your God, and him you shall fear, and  his commandments you shall keep, and  his voice you shall hearken to, and to him you shall serve, and to him you shall be added to. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Ye shall walk after the LORD your God, and fear him, and keep his commandments, and obey his voice, and ye shall serve him, and cleave unto him.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You are to follow the LORD your God and fear Him. Keep His commandments and listen to His voice; serve Him and hold fast to Him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
