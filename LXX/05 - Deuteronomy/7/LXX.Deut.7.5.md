**LXX Deuteronomy 7:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But thus you shall do to them;  you shall demolish their shrines, and  you shall break their monuments, and  you shall cut down their sacred groves; and  their carvings of the gods you shall incinerate by fire. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But thus shall ye deal with them; ye shall destroy their altars, and break down their images, and cut down their groves, and burn their graven images with fire.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Instead, this is what you are to do to them: tear down their altars, smash their sacred pillars, cut down their Asherah poles, and burn their idols in the fire.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
