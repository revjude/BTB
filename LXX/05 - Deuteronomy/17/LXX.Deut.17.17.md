**LXX Deuteronomy 17:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he shall not multiply to himself wives, that he shall not change over his  heart. And silver and gold he shall not multiply to himself exceedingly. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Neither shall he multiply wives to himself, that his heart turn not away: neither shall he greatly multiply to himself silver and gold.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He must not take many wives for himself, lest his heart go astray. He must not accumulate for himself large amounts of silver and gold.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
