**LXX Deuteronomy 2:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
 For \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  your God blessed you in every work  of your hands. Determine how you went through  that great and dreadful wilderness! Behold, forty years \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  your God \[was\] with you; you did not want of a thing. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For the LORD thy God hath blessed thee in all the works of thy hand: he knoweth thy walking through this great wilderness: these forty years the LORD thy God [hath been] with thee; thou hast lacked nothing.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Indeed, the LORD your God has blessed you in all the work of your hands. He has watched over your journey through this vast wilderness. The LORD your God has been with you these forty years, and you have lacked nothing.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
