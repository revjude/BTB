**LXX Deuteronomy 23:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it will be  towards evening he shall bathe  his body in water; and \[at the\] going down of \[the\] sun, he shall enter into the camp. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But it shall be, when evening cometh on, he shall wash [himself] with water: and when the sun is down, he shall come into the camp [again].
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
When evening approaches, he must wash with water, and when the sun sets he may return to the camp.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
