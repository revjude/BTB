**LXX Deuteronomy 23:1** 

***Social Laws***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
One with crushed testicles shall not enter (nor being cut off) into the assembly of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He that is wounded in the stones, or hath his privy member cut off, shall not enter into the congregation of the LORD.

No man with crushed or severed genitals may enter the assembly of the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
