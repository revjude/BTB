**LXX Deuteronomy 23:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You shall not address peaceable to them, \[nor\] be advantageous to them all  your days into the eon.

Thou shalt not seek their peace nor their prosperity all thy days for ever.

You are not to seek peace or prosperity from them as long as you live.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
