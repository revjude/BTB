**LXX Deuteronomy 28:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> strike you with perplexity, and burning heat, and shivering, and aggravation, and carnage, and wind-blown, and  paleness; and may they pursue you until whenever they should destroy you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The LORD shall smite thee with a consumption, and with a fever, and with an inflammation, and with an extreme burning, and with the sword, and with blasting, and with mildew; and they shall pursue thee until thou perish.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The LORD will strike you with wasting disease, with fever and inflammation, with scorching heat and drought, and with blight and mildew; these will pursue you until you perish.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
