**LXX Deuteronomy 28:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> your God shall multiply you for good \[things\] upon the progeny  of your belly, and upon the progeny  of your cattle, and upon the produce  of your ground, upon the land which \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> swore by an oath \[to\] your fathers to give to you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the LORD shall make thee plenteous in goods, in the fruit of thy body, and in the fruit of thy cattle, and in the fruit of thy ground, in the land which the LORD sware unto thy fathers to give thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The LORD will make you prosper abundantly—in the fruit of your womb, the offspring of your livestock, and the produce of your land—in the land that the LORD swore to your fathers to give you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
