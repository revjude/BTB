**LXX Deuteronomy 28:26**

And your dead will be things devoured by the winged creatures of the heaven, and the wild beasts of the earth; and there will not be  one frightening away.

And thy carcase shall be meat unto all fowls of the air, and unto the beasts of the earth, and no man shall fray [them] away.

Your corpses will be food for all the birds of the air and beasts of the earth, with no one to scare them away.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
