**LXX Deuteronomy 14:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A tenth you shall tithe of all your produce of seed, the produce  of your field year by year. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thou shalt truly tithe all the increase of thy seed, that the field bringeth forth year by year.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You must be sure to set aside a tenth of all the produce brought forth each year from your fields.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
