**LXX Deuteronomy 11:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Not any one withstand against your face. The fear of you and the trembling \[because\] of you \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> your God shall place upon \[the\] face of all the land, upon which ever you should mount upon it, in which manner he spoke to you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
There shall no man be able to stand before you: [for] the LORD your God shall lay the fear of you and the dread of you upon all the land that ye shall tread upon, as he hath said unto you.

No man will be able to stand against you; the LORD your God will put the fear and dread of you upon all the land, wherever you set foot, as He has promised you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
