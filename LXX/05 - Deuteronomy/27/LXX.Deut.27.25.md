**LXX Deuteronomy 27:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Accursed is who ever should take bribes to strike \[the\] life of innocent blood. And all the people shall say, May it be. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Cursed [be] he that taketh reward to slay an innocent person. And all the people shall say, Amen.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
‘Cursed is he who accepts a bribe to kill an innocent person.’ And let all the people say, ‘Amen!’<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
