**LXX Deuteronomy 27:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Accursed \[is\] the one going to bed with \[the\] wife  of his father, for he uncovered \[the\] marriage veil  of his father. And all the people shall say, May it be. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Cursed [be] he that lieth with his father’s wife; because he uncovereth his father’s skirt. And all the people shall say, Amen.

‘Cursed is he who sleeps with his father’s wife, for he has violated his father’s marriage bed.’ And let all the people say, ‘Amen!’<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
