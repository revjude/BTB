**LXX Deuteronomy 22:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But if in a plain a man should find  a girl,  being espoused; and using force should go to bed with her, you shall kill the man, the one going to bed with her only; 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But if a man find a betrothed damsel in the field, and the man force her, and lie with her: then the man only that lay with her shall die:

But if the man encounters a betrothed woman in the open country, and he overpowers her and lies with her, only the man who has done this must die.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
