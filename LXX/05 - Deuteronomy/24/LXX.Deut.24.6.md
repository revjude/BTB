**LXX Deuteronomy 24:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You shall not take for security a millstone nor an upper millstone, for this one takes a life for security. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
No man shall take the nether or the upper millstone to pledge: for he taketh [a man’s] life to pledge.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Do not take a pair of millstones or even an upper millstone as security for a debt, because that would be taking one’s livelihood as security.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
