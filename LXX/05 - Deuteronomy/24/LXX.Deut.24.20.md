**LXX Deuteronomy 24:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if one should pick olives, you shall not turn back to glean  after you; it shall be to the foreigner and to the orphan and to the widow. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
When thou beatest thine olive tree, thou shalt not go over the boughs again: it shall be for the stranger, for the fatherless, and for the widow.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
When you beat the olives from your trees, you must not go over the branches again. What remains will be for the foreigner, the fatherless, and the widow.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
