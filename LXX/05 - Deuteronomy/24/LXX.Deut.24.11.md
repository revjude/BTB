**LXX Deuteronomy 24:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You shall stand outside, and the man of whom  your debt is in, he shall bring forth to you the item of security outside. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thou shalt stand abroad, and the man to whom thou dost lend shall bring out the pledge abroad unto thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You are to stand outside while the man to whom you are lending brings the security out to you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
