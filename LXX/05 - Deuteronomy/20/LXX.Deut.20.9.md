**LXX Deuteronomy 20:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it will be whenever the scribes should cease speaking to the people, that they shall place rulers of the military taking lead of the people.

And it shall be, when the officers have made an end of speaking unto the people, that they shall make captains of the armies to lead the people.

When the officers have finished addressing the army, they are to appoint commanders to lead it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
