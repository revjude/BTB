**LXX Deuteronomy 32:50** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And come to an end in the mountain into which you ascend there! And be added to  your people! in which manner Aaron your brother died in Hor the mountain, and was added to  his people. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And die in the mount whither thou goest up, and be gathered unto thy people; as Aaron thy brother died in mount Hor, and was gathered unto his people:

And there on the mountain that you climb, you will die and be gathered to your people, just as your brother Aaron died on Mount Hor and was gathered to his people.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
