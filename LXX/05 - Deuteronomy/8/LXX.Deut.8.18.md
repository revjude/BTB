**LXX Deuteronomy 8:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you shall remember \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  your God, that he gives to you strength  to produce \[the\] ability, that he should establish  his covenant which he swore by an oath to your fathers as today. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But thou shalt remember the LORD thy God: for [it is] he that giveth thee power to get wealth, that he may establish his covenant which he sware unto thy fathers, as [it is] this day.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But remember that it is the LORD your God who gives you the power to gain wealth, in order to confirm His covenant that He swore to your fathers even to this day.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
