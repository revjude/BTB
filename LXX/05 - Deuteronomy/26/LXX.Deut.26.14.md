**LXX Deuteronomy 26:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I did not eat of them in my grief; I did not yield of them in an unclean \[manner\]; I did not give of them to the one having died; I obeyed the voice of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  my God to do as he gave charge to me. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I have not eaten thereof in my mourning, neither have I taken away [ought] thereof for [any] unclean [use], nor given [ought] thereof for the dead: [but] I have hearkened to the voice of the LORD my God, [and] have done according to all that thou hast commanded me.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I have not eaten any of the sacred portion while in mourning, or removed any of it while unclean, or offered any of it for the dead. I have obeyed the LORD my God; I have done everything You commanded me.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
