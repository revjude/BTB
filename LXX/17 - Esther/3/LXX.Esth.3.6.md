**LXX Esther 3:6** 

***Haman Plots to Destroy the Jews***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he took counsel to remove all  \[the\] Jews under the kingdom of Artaxerxes. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he thought scorn to lay hands on Mordecai alone; for they had shewed him the people of Mordecai: wherefore Haman sought to destroy all the Jews that [were] throughout the whole kingdom of Ahasuerus, [even] the people of Mordecai.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And when he learned the identity of Mordecai’s people, he scorned the notion of laying hands on Mordecai alone. Instead, he sought to destroy all of Mordecai’s people, the Jews, throughout the kingdom of Xerxes.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
