**LXX Esther 9:24** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
of how Haman \[son of\] Hammedatha the Macedonian waged war against them; as he ordained a referendum and \[the\] lot, to obliterate them; 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Because Haman the son of Hammedatha, the Agagite, the enemy of all the Jews, had devised against the Jews to destroy them, and had cast Pur, that [is], the lot, to consume them, and to destroy them;
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For Haman son of Hammedatha, the Agagite, the enemy of all the Jews, had plotted against the Jews to destroy them and had cast the Pur (that is, the lot) to crush and destroy them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
