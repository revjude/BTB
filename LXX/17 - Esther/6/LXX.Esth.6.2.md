**LXX Esther 6:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he found letters  having been written concerning Mordecai, as was reported to the king concerning the two eunuchs of the king, during  their watching and their seeking to put  hands on Artaxerxes. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it was found written, that Mordecai had told of Bigthana and Teresh, two of the king’s chamberlains, the keepers of the door, who sought to lay hand on the king Ahasuerus.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And there it was found recorded that Mordecai had exposed Bigthana and Teresh, two of the eunuchs who guarded the king’s entrance, when they had conspired to assassinate King Xerxes.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
