**LXX Proverbs 11:27** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] one contriving good \[things\] seeks good favor; but \[the one\] seeking evil \[things\], \[evil\] shall overtake him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He that diligently seeketh good procureth favour: but he that seeketh mischief, it shall come unto him.

He who searches out good finds favor, but evil will come to him who seeks it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
