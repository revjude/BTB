**LXX Proverbs 11:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A just son engenders unto life; but \[the\] persecution of \[the\] impious \[is\] unto death. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
As righteousness [tendeth] to life: so he that pursueth evil [pursueth it] to his own death.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Genuine righteousness leads to life, but the pursuit of evil brings death.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
