**LXX Proverbs 11:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A \[man\] lacking of sense sneers at fellow-countrymen; but an intelligent man leads restfully. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He that is void of wisdom despiseth his neighbour: but a man of understanding holdeth his peace.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Whoever shows contempt for his neighbor lacks judgment, but a man of understanding remains silent.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
