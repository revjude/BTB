**LXX Proverbs 11:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
From out of \[the\] fruit of righteousness germinates a tree of life; but \[the\] lives of lawbreakers are removed \[at\] unseasonable times.

The fruit of the righteous [is] a tree of life; and he that winneth souls [is] wise.
