**LXX Proverbs 11:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Perverting ways \[are\] an abomination \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; but all unblemished \[ones\] in \[the\] way \[are\] acceptable to him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
They that are of a froward heart [are] abomination to the LORD: but [such as are] upright in [their] way [are] his delight.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The perverse in heart are an abomination to the LORD, but the blameless in their walk are His delight.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
