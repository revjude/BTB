**LXX Proverbs 11:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In \[the\] mouth of impious \[men\] \[is\] a snare to fellow-countrymen; but \[the\] perception of just \[men\] is prosperous.

An hypocrite with [his] mouth destroyeth his neighbour: but through knowledge shall the just be delivered.

With his mouth the ungodly man destroys his neighbor, but through knowledge the righteous are rescued.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
