**LXX Proverbs 11:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
By \[the\] good \[things\] of just \[men\] a city \[is\] set up; and in \[the\] destruction of \[the\] impious \[there is\] a leap for joy. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
When it goeth well with the righteous, the city rejoiceth: and when the wicked perish, [there is] shouting.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
When the righteous thrive, the city rejoices, and when the wicked perish, there are shouts of joy.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
