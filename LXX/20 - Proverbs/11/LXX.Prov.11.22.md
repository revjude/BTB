**LXX Proverbs 11:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
As a ring of gold in a nose of a pig; so beauty to an evil-minded woman. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[As] a jewel of gold in a swine’s snout, [so is] a fair woman which is without discretion.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Like a gold ring in a pig’s snout is a beautiful woman who lacks discretion.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
