**LXX Proverbs 11:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Where ever insult should enter, dishonor \[is\] also there; but \[the\] mouth of \[the\] humble meditates upon wisdom. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[When] pride cometh, then cometh shame: but with the lowly [is] wisdom.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
When pride comes, disgrace follows, but with humility comes wisdom.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
