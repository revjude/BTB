**LXX Proverbs 28:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A lion hungering, and a wolf thirsting \[is\] the one who \[is\] sovereign of a destitute nation being poor. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[As] a roaring lion, and a ranging bear; [so is] a wicked ruler over the poor people.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Like a roaring lion or a charging bear is a wicked ruler over a helpless people.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
