**LXX Proverbs 28:21** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] one who does not feel respect for persons \[of the\] just, \[is\] not good;  such \[a one\] will deliver over a man for a morsel of bread. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To have respect of persons [is] not good: for for a piece of bread [that] man will transgress.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
To show partiality is not good, yet a man will do wrong for a piece of bread.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
