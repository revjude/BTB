**LXX Proverbs 28:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A vigorous man with impious deeds extorts \[the\] poor as a fierce and unprofitable rain. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A poor man that oppresseth the poor [is like] a sweeping rain which leaveth no food.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A destitute leader who oppresses the poor is like a driving rain that leaves no food.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
