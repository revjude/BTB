**LXX Proverbs 28:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Blessed \[is\] a man who is struck with awe of all \[things\] through veneration; but the hard  of heart shall fall into evils. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Happy [is] the man that feareth alway: but he that hardeneth his heart shall fall into mischief.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Blessed is the man who is always reverent, but he who hardens his heart falls into trouble.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
