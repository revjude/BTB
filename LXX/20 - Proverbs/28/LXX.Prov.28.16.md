**LXX Proverbs 28:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A king lacking revenue \[is\] a great extortioner; but the one detesting injustice shall live a long time. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The prince that wanteth understanding [is] also a great oppressor: [but] he that hateth covetousness shall prolong [his] days.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A leader who lacks judgment is also a great oppressor, but he who hates dishonest profit will prolong his days.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
