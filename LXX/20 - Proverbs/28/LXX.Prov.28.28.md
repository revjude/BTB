**LXX Proverbs 28:28** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In \[the\] places of \[the\] impious \[the\] just moan; but in the destruction of those \[the\] just shall be multiplied.

When the wicked rise, men hide themselves: but when they perish, the righteous increase.
