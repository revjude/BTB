**LXX Proverbs 15:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] uninstructed shall not love the ones reproving him; and with \[the\] wise he shall not consort. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A scorner loveth not one that reproveth him: neither will he go unto the wise.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A mocker does not love to be reproved, nor will he consult the wise.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
