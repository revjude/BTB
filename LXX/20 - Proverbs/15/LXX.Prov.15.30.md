**LXX Proverbs 15:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] eye viewing good gladdens \[the\] heart; and a good reputation fattens \[the\] bones. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The light of the eyes rejoiceth the heart: [and] a good report maketh the bones fat.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The light of the eyes cheers the heart, and good news nourishes the bones.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
