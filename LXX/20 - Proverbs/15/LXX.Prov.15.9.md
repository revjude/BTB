**LXX Proverbs 15:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] ways of \[the\] impious \[are\] an abomination \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; he loves \[the\] ones pursuing righteousness.

The way of the wicked [is] an abomination unto the LORD: but he loveth him that followeth after righteousness.
