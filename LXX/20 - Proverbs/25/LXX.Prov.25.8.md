**LXX Proverbs 25:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Do not fall into a fight quickly! lest you should change your mind at \[the\] last. When ever a friend should berate you, 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Go not forth hastily to strive, lest [thou know not] what to do in the end thereof, when thy neighbour hath put thee to shame.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
do not bring hastily to court. Otherwise, what will you do in the end when your neighbor puts you to shame?<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
