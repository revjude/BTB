**LXX Proverbs 25:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In finding honey, eat  what is enough! lest at any time being overfilled, you should vomit forth. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Hast thou found honey? eat so much as is sufficient for thee, lest thou be filled therewith, and vomit it.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If you find honey, eat just what you need, lest you have too much and vomit it up.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
