**LXX Proverbs 25:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In an ear-ring of gold a very costly sardius \[is\] bound; wise word to a heedful ear. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[As] an earring of gold, and an ornament of fine gold, [so is] a wise reprover upon an obedient ear.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Like an earring of gold or an ornament of fine gold is a wise man’s rebuke to a listening ear.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
