**LXX Proverbs 25:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
As vinegar draws hurtful; so passion falling on a body distresses \[the\] heart. As a moth in a garment, and a worm in wood, so distress of a man hurts \[the\] heart. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[As] he that taketh away a garment in cold weather, [and as] vinegar upon nitre, so [is] he that singeth songs to an heavy heart.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Like one who removes a garment on a cold day or vinegar poured on a wound is one who sings songs to a heavy heart.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
