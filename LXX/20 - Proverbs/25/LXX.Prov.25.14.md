**LXX Proverbs 25:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
As winds, and clouds, and rains \[are\] apparent, so the men boasting over false portions. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Whoso boasteth himself of a false gift [is like] clouds and wind without rain.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Like clouds and wind without rain is the man who boasts of gifts never given.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
