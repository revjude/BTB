**LXX Proverbs 25:28** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
As a city  \[with\] walls having been thrown down and unwalled, so a man who acts with no counsel in anything. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He that [hath] no rule over his own spirit [is like] a city [that is] broken down, [and] without walls.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Like a city whose walls are broken down is a man who does not control his temper.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
