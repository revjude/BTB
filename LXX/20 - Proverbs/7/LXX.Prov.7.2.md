**LXX Proverbs 7:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>

Keep my commandments, and live; and my law as the apple of thine eye.

Keep my commandments and live; guard my teachings as the apple of your eye.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
