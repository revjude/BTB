**LXX Proverbs 13:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
There are the ones enriching themselves, having nothing; and there are the ones abasing themselves with many riches. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
There is that maketh himself rich, yet [hath] nothing: [there is] that maketh himself poor, yet [hath] great riches.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
One pretends to be rich, but has nothing; another pretends to be poor, yet has great wealth.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
