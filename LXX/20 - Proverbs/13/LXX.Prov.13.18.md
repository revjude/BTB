**LXX Proverbs 13:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Poverty and dishonor are removed by instruction; and the one guarding reproofs shall be extolled. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Poverty and shame [shall be to] him that refuseth instruction: but he that regardeth reproof shall be honoured.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Poverty and shame come to him who ignores discipline, but whoever heeds correction is honored.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
