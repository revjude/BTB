**LXX Proverbs 13:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Substance being hastily \[obtained\] with lawlessness becomes less; but the one gathering for himself with piety shall be multiplied. \[The\] just pities and lends. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Wealth [gotten] by vanity shall be diminished: but he that gathereth by labour shall increase.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Dishonest wealth will dwindle, but what is earned through hard work will be multiplied.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
