**LXX Proverbs 13:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] just detest an unjust word; but \[the\] impious man is ashamed, and shall not have an open manner. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A righteous [man] hateth lying: but a wicked [man] is loathsome, and cometh to shame.

The righteous hate falsehood, but the wicked bring shame and disgrace.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
