**LXX Proverbs 13:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
From fruits of righteousness \[the\] good shall eat; but \[the\] lives of lawbreakers will be destroyed unseasonably. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A man shall eat good by the fruit of [his] mouth: but the soul of the transgressors [shall eat] violence.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
From the fruit of his lips a man enjoys good things, but the desire of the faithless is violence.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
