**LXX Proverbs 13:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] just in eating fills up  his soul; but souls of \[the\] impious \[go\] lacking.

The righteous eateth to the satisfying of his soul: but the belly of the wicked shall want.
