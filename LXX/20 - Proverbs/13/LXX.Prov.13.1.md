**LXX Proverbs 13:1** 

***Good Instructions***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
An astute son \[is\] subject to \[his\] father; but an unhearing son \[goes\] unto destruction. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A wise son [heareth] his father’s instruction: but a scorner heareth not rebuke.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A wise son heeds his father’s discipline, but a mocker does not listen to rebuke.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
