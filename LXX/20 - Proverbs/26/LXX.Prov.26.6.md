**LXX Proverbs 26:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The his own ways one sending a word through a foolish messenger shall cause scorn by. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He that sendeth a message by the hand of a fool cutteth off the feet, [and] drinketh damage.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Like cutting off one’s own feet or drinking violence is the sending of a message by the hand of a fool.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
