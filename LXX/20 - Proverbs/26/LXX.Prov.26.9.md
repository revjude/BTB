**LXX Proverbs 26:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Thorn-bushes germinate in \[the\] hand of the intoxicated; and servitude in \[the\] hand  of fools.

[As] a thorn goeth up into the hand of a drunkard, so [is] a parable in the mouth of fools.
