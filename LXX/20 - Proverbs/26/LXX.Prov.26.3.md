**LXX Proverbs 26:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
As a whip to a horse, and a spur to a donkey, so a rod to a lawbreaking nation. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A whip for the horse, a bridle for the ass, and a rod for the fool’s back.

A whip for the horse, a bridle for the donkey, and a rod for the backs of fools!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
