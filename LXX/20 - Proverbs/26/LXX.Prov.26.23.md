**LXX Proverbs 26:23** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Silver given with treachery, \[is\] as a potsherd esteemed. Smooth lips cover a distressed heart.

Burning lips and a wicked heart [are like] a potsherd covered with silver dross.
