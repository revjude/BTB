**LXX Proverbs 26:27** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The one digging a pit \[for his\] neighbor shall fall into it; and the one rolling a stone, rolls it upon himself.

Whoso diggeth a pit shall fall therein: and he that rolleth a stone, it will return upon him.

He who digs a pit will fall into it, and he who rolls a stone will have it roll back on him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
