**LXX Proverbs 23:24** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A just father nourishes well; and his soul is gladdened by a wise son. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The father of the righteous shall greatly rejoice: and he that begetteth a wise [child] shall have joy of him.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The father of a righteous man will greatly rejoice, and he who fathers a wise son will delight in him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
