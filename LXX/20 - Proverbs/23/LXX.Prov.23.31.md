**LXX Proverbs 23:31** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For if in the bowls and the cups you should give  your eyes, afterwards you shall walk more naked than a pestle. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Look not thou upon the wine when it is red, when it giveth his colour in the cup, [when] it moveth itself aright.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Do not gaze at wine while it is red, when it sparkles in the cup and goes down smoothly.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
