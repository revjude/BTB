**LXX Proverbs 23:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Do not reach forth, being needy, to \[the\] rich!  but in your insight be at a distance! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Labour not to be rich: cease from thine own wisdom.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Do not wear yourself out to get rich; be wise enough to restrain yourself.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
