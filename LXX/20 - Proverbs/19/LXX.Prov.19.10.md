**LXX Proverbs 19:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Luxury is not advantageous to a fool, nor \[is it seemly\] if a servant should begin to be in power by insult. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Delight is not seemly for a fool; much less for a servant to have rule over princes.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Luxury is unseemly for a fool—how much worse for a slave to rule over princes!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
