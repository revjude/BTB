**LXX Proverbs 19:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The one showing mercy on \[the\] poor lends to God; and according to  his gift he shall make recompense to him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He that hath pity upon the poor lendeth unto the LORD; and that which he hath given will he pay him again.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Kindness to the poor is a loan to the LORD, and He will repay the lender.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
