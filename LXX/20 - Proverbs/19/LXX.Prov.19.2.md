**LXX Proverbs 19:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And even a soul without higher knowledge \[is\] not good; and the one hastening with the feet sins. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Also, [that] the soul [be] without knowledge, [it is] not good; and he that hasteth with [his] feet sinneth.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Even zeal is no good without knowledge, and he who hurries his footsteps misses the mark.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
