**LXX Proverbs 22:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The one sowing heedlessly harvests bad \[things\]; and \[the\] calamity of his works he shall complete. A happy man and a giver God loves; and \[the\] folly of his works he shall end. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He that soweth iniquity shall reap vanity: and the rod of his anger shall fail.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He who sows injustice will reap disaster, and the rod of his fury will be destroyed.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
