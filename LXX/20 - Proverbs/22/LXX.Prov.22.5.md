**LXX Proverbs 22:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Thistles and snares \[are\] in crooked ways; but the one guarding  his own soul is at a distance from them. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thorns [and] snares [are] in the way of the froward: he that doth keep his soul shall be far from them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Thorns and snares lie on the path of the perverse; he who guards his soul stays far from them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
