**LXX Proverbs 27:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
If you whip a fool in \[the\] midst of a sanhedrin, dishonoring \[him\], in no way his folly be removed. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Though thou shouldest bray a fool in a mortar among wheat with a pestle, [yet] will not his foolishness depart from him.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Though you grind a fool like grain with mortar and a pestle, yet his folly will not depart from him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
