**LXX Proverbs 27:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Perfumes, and wines, and incenses \[the\] heart happy; but \[the\] soul breaks down by adverse incidents. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Ointment and perfume rejoice the heart: so [doth] the sweetness of a man’s friend by hearty counsel.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Oil and incense bring joy to the heart, and the counsel of a friend is sweetness to the soul.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
