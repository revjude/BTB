**LXX Proverbs 27:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
As a bird whenever it flies down from out of  its own nest; so a man \[is\] enslaved whenever he estranges himself from out of  his own places. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
As a bird that wandereth from her nest, so [is] a man that wandereth from his place.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Like a bird that strays from its nest is a man who wanders from his home.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
