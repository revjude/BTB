**LXX Proverbs 12:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Through \[the\] sin of \[the\] lips a sinner falls into snares; but \[the\] just flees from them. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The wicked is snared by the transgression of [his] lips: but the just shall come out of trouble.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
An evil man is trapped by his rebellious speech, but a righteous man escapes from trouble.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
