**LXX Proverbs 12:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A courageous wife \[is\] a crown to her husband; but as \[the\] worm in wood, so an evil doing wife destroys \[her\] husband. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A virtuous woman [is] a crown to her husband: but she that maketh ashamed [is] as rottenness in his bones.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A wife of noble character is her husband’s crown, but she who causes shame is like decay in his bones.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
