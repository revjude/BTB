**LXX Proverbs 12:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Better a man with dishonor serving himself, than one putting value on himself, and feeling want of bread. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[He that is] despised, and hath a servant, [is] better than he that honoureth himself, and lacketh bread.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Better to be lightly esteemed yet have a servant, than to be self-important but lack food.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
