**LXX Proverbs 29:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[With the\] being of many impious, many sins take place; but \[with\] the just, at the falling of those, fear takes place. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
When the wicked are multiplied, transgression increaseth: but the righteous shall see their fall.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
When the wicked thrive, rebellion increases; but the righteous will see their downfall.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
