**LXX Proverbs 29:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A man fond of wisdom gladdens  his father; but \[the\] one tending harlots destroys wealth. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Whoso loveth wisdom rejoiceth his father: but he that keepeth company with harlots spendeth [his] substance.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A man who loves wisdom brings joy to his father, but a companion of prostitutes squanders his wealth.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
