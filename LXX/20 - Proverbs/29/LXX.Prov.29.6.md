**LXX Proverbs 29:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A great snare \[is set\] against a man sinning; but \[the\] just will be in joy and in gladness. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
In the transgression of an evil man [there is] a snare: but the righteous doth sing and rejoice.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
An evil man is caught by his own sin, but a righteous one sings and rejoices.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
