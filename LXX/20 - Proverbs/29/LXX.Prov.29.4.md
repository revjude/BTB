**LXX Proverbs 29:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A just king establishes regions; but a lawbreaking man razes \[them\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The king by judgment establisheth the land: but he that receiveth gifts overthroweth it.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
By justice a king brings stability to the land, but a man who exacts tribute demolishes it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
