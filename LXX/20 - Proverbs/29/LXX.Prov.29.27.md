**LXX Proverbs 29:27** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A just man \[is\] an abomination to an unjust man; and an abomination to \[the\] lawless one \[is\] a straight way. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
An unjust man [is] an abomination to the just: and [he that is] upright in the way [is] abomination to the wicked.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
An unjust man is detestable to the righteous, and one whose way is upright is detestable to the wicked.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
