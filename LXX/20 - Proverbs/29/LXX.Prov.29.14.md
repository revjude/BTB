**LXX Proverbs 29:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A king judging \[the\] poor in truth,  his throne shall be established for a \[good\] testimony.

The king that faithfully judgeth the poor, his throne shall be established for ever.

A king who judges the poor with fairness—his throne will be established forever.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
