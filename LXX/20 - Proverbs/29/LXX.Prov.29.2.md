**LXX Proverbs 29:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In \[the\] lauding \[of the\] just peoples will be glad; but men moan over \[the\] rule of \[the\] impious. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
When the righteous are in authority, the people rejoice: but when the wicked beareth rule, the people mourn.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
When the righteous flourish, the people rejoice, but when the wicked rule, the people groan.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
