**LXX Proverbs 30:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[the\] traces of a flying eagle, and \[the\] ways of a serpent upon a rock, and \[the\] paths of a ship passing through the sea, and \[the\] ways of a man in youth. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The way of an eagle in the air; the way of a serpent upon a rock; the way of a ship in the midst of the sea; and the way of a man with a maid.

the way of an eagle in the sky, the way of a snake on a rock, the way of a ship at sea, and the way of a man with a maiden.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
