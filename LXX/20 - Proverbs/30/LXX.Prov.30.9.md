**LXX Proverbs 30:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
that become not false in being filled up, and should say, Who sees me? Or to be in need \[that\] I shall steal, and I shall swear an oath by the name  of God. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Lest I be full, and deny [thee], and say, Who [is] the LORD? or lest I be poor, and steal, and take the name of my God [in vain].
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Otherwise, I may have too much and deny You, saying, ‘Who is the LORD?’ Or I may become poor and steal, profaning the name of my God.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
