**LXX Proverbs 30:32** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
If you should let go of yourself in gladness, and should stretch out  your hand for a fight you shall be dishonored. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
If thou hast done foolishly in lifting up thyself, or if thou hast thought evil, [lay] thine hand upon thy mouth.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If you have foolishly exalted yourself or if you have plotted evil, put your hand over your mouth.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
