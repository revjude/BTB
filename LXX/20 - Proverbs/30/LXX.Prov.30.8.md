**LXX Proverbs 30:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
appoint \[the\] vain and false word far from me; and riches and poverty you should not give to me; but order up for me the \[things\] necessary and the \[things\] \[to be\] self-sufficient; 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Remove far from me vanity and lies: give me neither poverty nor riches; feed me with food convenient for me:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Keep falsehood and deceitful words far from me. Give me neither poverty nor riches; feed me with the bread that is my portion.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
