**LXX Proverbs 21:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A private present prostrates angers; but the one sparing gifts shall raise up strong rage. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A gift in secret pacifieth anger: and a reward in the bosom strong wrath.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A gift in secret soothes anger, and a covert bribe pacifies great wrath.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
