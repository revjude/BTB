**LXX Proverbs 21:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Better to live upon a corner \[of the housetop\] in the open air, than \[houses\] being whitewashed with injustice, and in a profane house. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[It is] better to dwell in a corner of the housetop, than with a brawling woman in a wide house.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Better to live on a corner of the roof than to share a house with a quarrelsome wife.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
