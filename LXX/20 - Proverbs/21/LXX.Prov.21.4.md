**LXX Proverbs 21:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A high-minded man is bold-hearted in \[his\] insolence; and \[the\] torch of \[the\] impious \[is\] sin.

An high look, and a proud heart, [and] the plowing of the wicked, [is] sin.
