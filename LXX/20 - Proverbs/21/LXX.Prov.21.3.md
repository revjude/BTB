**LXX Proverbs 21:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
To do just \[things\] and to be truthful \[are\] more pleasing to God rather, than a sacrifice of blood.

To do justice and judgment [is] more acceptable to the LORD than sacrifice.
