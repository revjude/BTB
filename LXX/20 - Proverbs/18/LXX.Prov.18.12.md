**LXX Proverbs 18:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Before destruction \[the\] heart of a man is raised up high, and before glory it is humbled.

Before destruction the heart of man is haughty, and before honour [is] humility.

Before his downfall a man’s heart is proud, but humility comes before honor.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
