**LXX Proverbs 18:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
An intelligent attendant calms \[the\] rage of a man; but a faint hearted man, who can endure.

The spirit of a man will sustain his infirmity; but a wounded spirit who can bear?

The spirit of a man can endure his sickness, but who can survive a broken spirit?<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
