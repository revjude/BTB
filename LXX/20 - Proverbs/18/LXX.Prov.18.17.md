**LXX Proverbs 18:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A just man, \[is\] an accuser of himself at \[the\] beginning of speaking; but when ever the opponent demands attention he is reproved. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[He that is] first in his own cause [seemeth] just; but his neighbour cometh and searcheth him.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The first to state his case seems right until another comes and cross-examines him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
