**LXX Proverbs 18:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
To admire \[the\] face of \[the\] impious \[is\] not good; nor \[is it\] sacred to turn aside the just in a judgment. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[It is] not good to accept the person of the wicked, to overthrow the righteous in judgment.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Showing partiality to the wicked is not good, nor is depriving the innocent of justice.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
