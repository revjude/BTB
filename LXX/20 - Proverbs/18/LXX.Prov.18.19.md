**LXX Proverbs 18:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A brother being helped by a brother \[is\] as a high and fortified city; and is strong as a well founded palace. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A brother offended [is harder to be won] than a strong city: and [their] contentions [are] like the bars of a castle.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
An offended brother is harder to win than a fortified city, and disputes are like the bars of a castle.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
