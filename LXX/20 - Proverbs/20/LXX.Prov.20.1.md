**LXX Proverbs 20:1** 

***The Trustworthiness of the Righteous***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Unrestrained wine and outrageous intoxication, and all  being laid waste shall not be wise. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Wine [is] a mocker, strong drink [is] raging: and whosoever is deceived thereby is not wise.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Wine is a mocker, strong drink is a brawler, and whoever is led astray by them is not wise.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
