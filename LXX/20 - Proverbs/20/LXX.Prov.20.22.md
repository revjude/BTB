**LXX Proverbs 20:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You should not say, I will pay back the enemy; but wait on the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> that he should help you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Say not thou, I will recompense evil; [but] wait on the LORD, and he shall save thee.

Do not say, “I will avenge this evil!” Wait on the LORD, and He will save you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
