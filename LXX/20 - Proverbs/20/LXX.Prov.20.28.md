**LXX Proverbs 20:28** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Charity and truth \[are\] a guard to a king, and they will surround his throne in righteousness.

Mercy and truth preserve the king: and his throne is upholden by mercy.

Loving devotion and faithfulness preserve a king; by these he maintains his throne.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
