**LXX Proverbs 24:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For thus you shall perceive wisdom \[in\] your soul; for if you should find it, your decease will be good, and hope shall not forsake you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
So [shall] the knowledge of wisdom [be] unto thy soul: when thou hast found [it], then there shall be a reward, and thy expectation shall not be cut off.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Know therefore that wisdom is sweet to your soul. If you find it, there is a future for you, and your hope will never be cut off.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
