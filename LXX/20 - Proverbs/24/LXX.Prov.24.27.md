**LXX Proverbs 24:27** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Prepare your works for the departure, and make preparations for the field, and go after me! and you shall rebuild  your house. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Prepare thy work without, and make it fit for thyself in the field; and afterwards build thine house.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Complete your outdoor work and prepare your field; after that, you may build your house.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
