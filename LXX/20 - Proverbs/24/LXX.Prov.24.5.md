**LXX Proverbs 24:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A wise man \[is\] better \[than\] a strong man;  a man of intelligence \[than\] one having a great farm. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A wise man [is] strong; yea, a man of knowledge increaseth strength.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A wise man is strong, and a man of knowledge enhances his strength.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
