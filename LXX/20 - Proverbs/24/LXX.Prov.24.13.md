**LXX Proverbs 24:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Eat honey, O son, for \[the\] honeycomb \[is\] good! that your throat should be sweetened.

My son, eat thou honey, because [it is] good; and the honeycomb, [which is] sweet to thy taste:
