**LXX Proverbs 5:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
for she does not come by \[the\] ways of life; but her tracks \[are\] slippery, and not well-known. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Lest thou shouldest ponder the path of life, her ways are moveable, [that] thou canst not know [them].

She does not consider the path of life; she does not know that her ways are unstable.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
