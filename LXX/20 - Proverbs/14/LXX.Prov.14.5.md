**LXX Proverbs 14:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A trustworthy witness does not lie; but a lying witness kindles unjust \[acts\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A faithful witness will not lie: but a false witness will utter lies.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
An honest witness does not deceive, but a dishonest witness pours forth lies.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
