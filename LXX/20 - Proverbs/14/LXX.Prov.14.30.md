**LXX Proverbs 14:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A gentle-minded man \[is\] a healer of \[the\] heart; but a sensitive heart \[is\] a moth for \[the\] bones. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A sound heart [is] the life of the flesh: but envy the rottenness of the bones.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A tranquil heart is life to the body, but envy rots the bones.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
