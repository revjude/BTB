**LXX Proverbs 14:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
All \[things\] \[are\] adverse to a foolish man; but wise lips \[are\] shields for good sense. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Go from the presence of a foolish man, when thou perceivest not [in him] the lips of knowledge.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Stay away from a foolish man; you will gain no knowledge from his speech.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
