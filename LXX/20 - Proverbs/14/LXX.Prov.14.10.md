**LXX Proverbs 14:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] heart of a man is sensitive of his soul's distress; but whenever he should be glad, he shall not intermix insult.

The heart knoweth his own bitterness; and a stranger doth not intermeddle with his joy.
