**LXX Proverbs 14:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
There is a way which seems to be straight by men, but the finalities of it come into \[the\] lower branch of Hades. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
There is a way which seemeth right unto a man, but the end thereof [are] the ways of death.

There is a way that seems right to a man, but its end is the way of death.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
