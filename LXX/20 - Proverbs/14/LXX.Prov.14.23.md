**LXX Proverbs 14:23** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
With every one having concern there is extra; but the pleasure-bent and unfeeling will be with lack. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
In all labour there is profit: but the talk of the lips [tendeth] only to penury.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
There is profit in all labor, but mere talk leads only to poverty.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
