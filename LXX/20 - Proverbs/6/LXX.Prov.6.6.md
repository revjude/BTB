**LXX Proverbs 6:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Be as the ant, O lazy one, and be jealous beholding  his ways; and become \[as\] that one -- wiser! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Go to the ant, thou sluggard; consider her ways, and be wise:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Walk in the manner of the ant, O slacker; observe its ways and become wise.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
