**LXX Proverbs 6:1** 

***Surety for Fools***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O son, if you should guarantee a loan for your friend, you shall deliver up your hand to \[the\] enemy. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
My son, if thou be surety for thy friend, [if] thou hast stricken thy hand with a stranger,
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
My son, if you have put up security for your neighbor, if you have struck hands in pledge with a stranger,<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
