**LXX Proverbs 8:12** 

***Advantage of Wisdom***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I  wisdom encamped with counsel and knowledge; and I called upon reflection.

I wisdom dwell with prudence, and find out knowledge of witty inventions.

I, wisdom, dwell together with prudence, and I find knowledge and discretion.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
