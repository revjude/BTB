**LXX Proverbs 8:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For wisdom \[is\] better \[than\] very costly stones; and every esteemed \[thing\] is not worth her. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For wisdom [is] better than rubies; and all the things that may be desired are not to be compared to it.

For wisdom is more precious than rubies, and nothing you desire compares with her.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
