**LXX Proverbs 8:29** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
in the putting of his restriction to the sea; and \[the\] waters shall not go by his mouth; and as he made the foundations of the earth strong, 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
