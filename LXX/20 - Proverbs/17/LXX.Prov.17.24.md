**LXX Proverbs 17:24** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] countenance of a wise man \[is\] discerning; but the eyes of the fool \[are\] unto \[the\] uttermost parts of \[the\] earth. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Wisdom [is] before him that hath understanding; but the eyes of a fool [are] in the ends of the earth.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Wisdom is the focus of the discerning, but the eyes of a fool wander to the ends of the earth.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
