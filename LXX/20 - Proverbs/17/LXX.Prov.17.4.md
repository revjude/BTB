**LXX Proverbs 17:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A bad \[man\] obeys \[the\] tongue of lawbreakers; and an unjust \[man\] heeds lying lips. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A wicked doer giveth heed to false lips; [and] a liar giveth ear to a naughty tongue.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A wicked man listens to evil lips; a liar gives ear to a destructive tongue.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
