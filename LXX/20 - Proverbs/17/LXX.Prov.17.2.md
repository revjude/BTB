**LXX Proverbs 17:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
An intelligent servant shall prevail over foolish masters; and among brethren he shall divide portions. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A wise servant shall have rule over a son that causeth shame, and shall have part of the inheritance among the brethren.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A wise servant will rule over a disgraceful son and share his inheritance as one of the brothers.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
