**LXX Proverbs 17:28** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
To an unthinking man asking, wisdom shall be imputed; and any dumb \[man\] doing for himself shall seem to be intelligent. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Even a fool, when he holdeth his peace, is counted wise: [and] he that shutteth his lips [is esteemed] a man of understanding.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Even a fool is considered wise if he keeps silent, and discerning when he holds his tongue.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
