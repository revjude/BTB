**LXX Proverbs 3:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Charity and trust -- do not let them fail to you! but affix them upon your neck! write them upon \[the\] tablet of your heart! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Let not mercy and truth forsake thee: bind them about thy neck; write them upon the table of thine heart:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Never let loving devotion or faithfulness leave you; bind them around your neck, write them on the tablet of your heart.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
