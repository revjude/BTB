**LXX Proverbs 3:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You should not be fond of quarreling against a man in vain, so that work not evil against you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Strive not with a man without cause, if he have done thee no harm.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Do not accuse a man without cause, when he has done you no harm.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
