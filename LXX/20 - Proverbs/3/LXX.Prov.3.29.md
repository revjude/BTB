**LXX Proverbs 3:29** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You should not contrive bad \[things\] against your friend, \[the\] one sojourning and relying upon you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Devise not evil against thy neighbour, seeing he dwelleth securely by thee.

Do not devise evil against your neighbor, for he trustfully dwells beside you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
