**LXX Proverbs 16:29** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A man \[who is\] a lawbreaker \[his\] friends to test, and takes them ways not good.

A violent man enticeth his neighbour, and leadeth him into the way [that is] not good.
