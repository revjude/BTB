**LXX Proverbs 16:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
An oracle \[is\] upon \[the\] lips of a king; but in judgment, in no way his mouth be misled. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A divine sentence [is] in the lips of the king: his mouth transgresseth not in judgment.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A divine verdict is on the lips of a king; his mouth must not betray justice.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
