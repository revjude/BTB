**LXX Proverbs 16:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] spring of life \[is\] insight to the ones acquiring; but \[the\] instruction of fools \[is\] evil. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Understanding [is] a wellspring of life unto him that hath it: but the instruction of fools [is] folly.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Understanding is a fountain of life to its possessor, but the discipline of fools is folly.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
