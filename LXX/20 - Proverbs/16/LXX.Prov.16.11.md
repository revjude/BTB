**LXX Proverbs 16:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] crux of \[the\] yoke \[balance scale\] \[is\] righteousness with God;  and his works \[are\] just weights. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A just weight and balance [are] the LORD’S: all the weights of the bag [are] his work.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Honest scales and balances are from the LORD; all the weights in the bag are His concern.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
