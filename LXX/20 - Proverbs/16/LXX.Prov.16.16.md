**LXX Proverbs 16:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Nests of wisdom \[are\] more preferred \[than\] gold; and nests of intelligence more preferred than silver.

How much better [is it] to get wisdom than gold! and to get understanding rather to be chosen than silver!

How much better to acquire wisdom than gold! To gain understanding is more desirable than silver.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
