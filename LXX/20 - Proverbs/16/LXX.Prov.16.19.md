**LXX Proverbs 16:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Better a gentle-minded one with a low estate, than one who divides spoils with \[the\] arrogant. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Better [it is to be] of an humble spirit with the lowly, than to divide the spoil with the proud.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
It is better to be lowly in spirit among the humble than to divide the spoil with the proud.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
