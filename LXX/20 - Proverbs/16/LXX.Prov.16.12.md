**LXX Proverbs 16:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The one doing evils \[is\] an abomination to a king; for with righteousness \[the\] throne of sovereignty is prepared. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[It is] an abomination to kings to commit wickedness: for the throne is established by righteousness.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Wicked behavior is detestable for kings, for a throne is established through righteousness.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
