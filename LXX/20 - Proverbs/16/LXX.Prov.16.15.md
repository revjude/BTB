**LXX Proverbs 16:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] son of a king \[is\] in \[the\] light of life; and the ones acceptable to him \[are\] as a cloud of late \[rain\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
In the light of the king’s countenance [is] life; and his favour [is] as a cloud of the latter rain.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
When a king’s face brightens, there is life; his favor is like a rain cloud in spring.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
