**LXX Proverbs 16:28** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A crooked man spreads evils, and by \[the\] torch of treachery lights a fire for evils, and he parts friends. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A froward man soweth strife: and a whisperer separateth chief friends.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A perverse man spreads dissension, and a gossip divides close friends.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
