<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] beginning of wisdom \[is\] fear of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; and \[the\] counsel of holy ones \[is\] understanding.  For to know \[the\] law is of good consideration.
