**LXX Proverbs 9:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>

Forsake the foolish, and live; and go in the way of understanding.

Leave your folly behind, and you will live; walk in the way of understanding.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
