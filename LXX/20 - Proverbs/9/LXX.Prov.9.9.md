**LXX Proverbs 9:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Give to a wise \[man\] opportunity! and he will be wiser; make \[things\] known to a just \[man\]! and he shall proceed  to receive \[more\].

Give [instruction] to a wise [man], and he will be yet wiser: teach a just [man], and he will increase in learning.

Instruct a wise man, and he will be wiser still; teach a righteous man, and he will increase his learning.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
