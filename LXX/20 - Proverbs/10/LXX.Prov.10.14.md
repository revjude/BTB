**LXX Proverbs 10:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] wise shall hide perception; but \[the\] mouth of \[the\] precipitous approaches destruction.

Wise [men] lay up knowledge: but the mouth of the foolish [is] near destruction.
