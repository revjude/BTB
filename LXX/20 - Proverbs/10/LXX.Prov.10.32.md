**LXX Proverbs 10:32** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] lips of just men drop favors; but \[the\] mouth of \[the\] impious is perverted. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The lips of the righteous know what is acceptable: but the mouth of the wicked [speaketh] frowardness.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The lips of the righteous know what is fitting, but the mouth of the wicked is perverse.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
