**LXX Proverbs 10:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> will not famish a just soul; but \[the\] life of \[the\] impious he will prostrate. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The LORD will not suffer the soul of the righteous to famish: but he casteth away the substance of the wicked.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The LORD does not let the righteous go hungry, but He denies the craving of the wicked.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
