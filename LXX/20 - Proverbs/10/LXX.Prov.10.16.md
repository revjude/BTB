**LXX Proverbs 10:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] works of \[the\] just produce life; but \[the\] fruit of \[the\] impious \[produces\] sins.

The labour of the righteous [tendeth] to life: the fruit of the wicked to sin.
