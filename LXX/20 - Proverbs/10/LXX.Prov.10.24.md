**LXX Proverbs 10:24** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
By destruction an impious man \[is\] carried round about; but \[the\] desire of \[the\] just \[is\] accepted. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The fear of the wicked, it shall come upon him: but the desire of the righteous shall be granted.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
What the wicked man dreads will overtake him, but the desire of the righteous will be granted.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
