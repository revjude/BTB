**LXX Proverbs 10:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A blessing of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> \[is\] upon \[the\] head of \[the\] just; but \[the\] mouth of \[the\] impious he covers \[with\] untimely mourning.

Blessings [are] upon the head of the just: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.

Blessings are on the head of the righteous, but the mouth of the wicked conceals violence.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
