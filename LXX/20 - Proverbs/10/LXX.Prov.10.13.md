**LXX Proverbs 10:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The one\] who brings forth wisdom from \[his\] lips, beats \[the\] heartless man \[with\] a rod. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
In the lips of him that hath understanding wisdom is found: but a rod [is] for the back of him that is void of understanding.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Wisdom is found on the lips of the discerning, but a rod is for the back of him who lacks judgment.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
