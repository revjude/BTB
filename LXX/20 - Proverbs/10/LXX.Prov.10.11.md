**LXX Proverbs 10:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] spring of life \[is\] in \[the\] hand of \[the\] just; but destruction shall cover \[the\] mouth of \[the\] impious. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The mouth of a righteous [man is] a well of life: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The mouth of the righteous is a fountain of life, but the mouth of the wicked conceals violence.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
