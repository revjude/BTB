**LXX Proverbs 10:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] spring of life \[is\] in \[the\] hand of \[the\] just; but destruction shall cover \[the\] mouth of \[the\] impious.

The mouth of a righteous [man is] a well of life: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.
