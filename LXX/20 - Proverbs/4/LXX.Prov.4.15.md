**LXX Proverbs 4:15** 

***The Ways of Lawbreakers Versus the Just***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In what ever place they should encamp, you should not come there. Turn aside from them and alter \[course\]! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Avoid it, pass not by it, turn from it, and pass away.

Avoid it; do not travel on it. Turn from it and pass on by.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
