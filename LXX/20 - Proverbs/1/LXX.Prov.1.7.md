**LXX Proverbs 1:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] beginning of wisdom \[is\] fear of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, and good understanding to all the ones observing it. And piety to God \[is the\] beginning of perception. But wisdom and instruction \[the\] impious shall treat with contempt. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The fear of the LORD [is] the beginning of knowledge: [but] fools despise wisdom and instruction.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The fear of the LORD is the beginning of knowledge, but fools despise wisdom and discipline.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
