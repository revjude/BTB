**LXX Leviticus 10:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Why did you not eat the \[thing\] for the sin \[offering\] in \[the\] holy place? For because it is a holy of holies. This he gave to you to eat, that you should remove the sin of the congregation, and should atone for them before \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Wherefore have ye not eaten the sin offering in the holy place, seeing it [is] most holy, and [God] hath given it you to bear the iniquity of the congregation, to make atonement for them before the LORD?
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Why didn’t you eat the sin offering in the holy place? For it is most holy; it was given to you to take away the guilt of the congregation by making atonement for them before the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
