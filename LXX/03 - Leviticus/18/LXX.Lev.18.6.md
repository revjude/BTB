**LXX Leviticus 18:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] man, a man shall not draw near to any family member of his flesh to uncover their indecency. I \[am\] \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
None of you shall approach to any that is near of kin to him, to uncover [their] nakedness: I [am] the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
None of you are to approach any close relative to have sexual relations. I am the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
