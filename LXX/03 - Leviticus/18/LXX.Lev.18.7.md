**LXX Leviticus 18:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] indecency  of your father, and \[the\] indecency  of your mother you shall not uncover, for she is your mother.  Her indecency you shall not uncover. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The nakedness of thy father, or the nakedness of thy mother, shalt thou not uncover: she [is] thy mother; thou shalt not uncover her nakedness.

You must not expose the nakedness of your father by having sexual relations with your mother. She is your mother; you must not have sexual relations with her.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
