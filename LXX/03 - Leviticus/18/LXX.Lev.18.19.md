**LXX Leviticus 18:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And to a wife in separation of her uncleanness you shall not enter, to uncover  her indecency. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Also thou shalt not approach unto a woman to uncover her nakedness, as long as she is put apart for her uncleanness.

You must not approach a woman to have sexual relations with her during her menstrual period.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
