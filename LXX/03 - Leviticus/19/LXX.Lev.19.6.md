**LXX Leviticus 19:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In which ever day you should sacrifice, it shall be eaten, and in the next morning. And if any should be left behind until \[the\] third day it shall be incinerated by fire. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
It shall be eaten the same day ye offer it, and on the morrow: and if ought remain until the third day, it shall be burnt in the fire.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
It shall be eaten on the day you sacrifice it, or on the next day; but what remains on the third day must be burned up.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
