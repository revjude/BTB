**LXX Leviticus 19:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You shall not go by treachery among  your nation. You shall not rise up together for blood of your neighbor. I \[am\] \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  your God.

Thou shalt not go up and down [as] a talebearer among thy people: neither shalt thou stand against the blood of thy neighbour: I [am] the LORD.

You must not go about spreading slander among your people. You must not endanger the life of your neighbor. I am the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
