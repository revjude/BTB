**LXX Leviticus 23:41** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it a holiday -- the holiday to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> seven days of the year; an eternal law unto  your generations. In the seventh month it a holiday. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And ye shall keep it a feast unto the LORD seven days in the year. [It shall be] a statute for ever in your generations: ye shall celebrate it in the seventh month.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You are to celebrate this as a feast to the LORD for seven days each year. This is a permanent statute for the generations to come; you are to celebrate it in the seventh month.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
