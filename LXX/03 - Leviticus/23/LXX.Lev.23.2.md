**LXX Leviticus 23:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Speak to the sons of Israel! And you shall say to them, The holiday \[feasts\] of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> which you shall call them \[for\] holy convocations -- these are  my holiday \[feasts\].

Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, [Concerning] the feasts of the LORD, which ye shall proclaim [to be] holy convocations, [even] these [are] my feasts.
