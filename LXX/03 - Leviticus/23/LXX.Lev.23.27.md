**LXX Leviticus 23:27** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Also on the tenth  of this seventh month \[is\] a day of making an atonement, it will be a holy convocation to you. And you shall humble  your souls, and you shall bring a whole burnt-offering to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>.

Also on the tenth [day] of this seventh month [there shall be] a day of atonement: it shall be an holy convocation unto you; and ye shall afflict your souls, and offer an offering made by fire unto the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“The tenth day of this seventh month is the Day of Atonement. You shall hold a sacred assembly and humble yourselves, and present a food offering to the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
