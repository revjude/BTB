**LXX Leviticus 23:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[with\] its sacrifice two tenths of fine flour being prepared in olive oil, a sacrifice to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> for a scent of pleasant aroma to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; and its libation -- the fourth of the hin of wine. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the meat offering thereof [shall be] two tenth deals of fine flour mingled with oil, an offering made by fire unto the LORD [for] a sweet savour: and the drink offering thereof [shall be] of wine, the fourth [part] of an hin.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
along with its grain offering of two-tenths of an ephah of fine flour mixed with oil—a food offering to the LORD, a pleasing aroma—and its drink offering of a quarter hin of wine.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
