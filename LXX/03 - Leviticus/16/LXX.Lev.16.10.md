**LXX Leviticus 16:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the young he-goat, upon which the lot of the scapegoat came unto it, he shall set it living before \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, so as to atone for him, so as to send him out as the scapegoat, and to let him go into the wilderness. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But the goat, on which the lot fell to be the scapegoat, shall be presented alive before the LORD, to make an atonement with him, [and] to let him go for a scapegoat into the wilderness.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But the goat chosen by lot as the scapegoat shall be presented alive before the LORD to make atonement by sending it into the wilderness as the scapegoat.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
