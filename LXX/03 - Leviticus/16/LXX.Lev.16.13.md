**LXX Leviticus 16:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he shall place the incense upon the fire before \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. And the vapor of the incense shall cover the atonement-seat, the one upon the testimonies, and he shall not die. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he shall put the incense upon the fire before the LORD, that the cloud of the incense may cover the mercy seat that [is] upon the testimony, that he die not:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He is to put the incense on the fire before the LORD, and the cloud of incense will cover the mercy seat above the Testimony, so that he will not die.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
