**LXX Leviticus 16:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he shall slay the young he-goat, (the one for a sin \[offering\], the one for the people) before \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. And he shall carry  its blood inside the veil. And he shall offer  its blood in which manner he offered the blood of the calf. And he shall sprinkle  its blood on the atonement-seat, in front of the atonement-seat. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then shall he kill the goat of the sin offering, that [is] for the people, and bring his blood within the vail, and do with that blood as he did with the blood of the bullock, and sprinkle it upon the mercy seat, and before the mercy seat:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Aaron shall then slaughter the goat for the sin offering for the people and bring its blood behind the veil, and with its blood he must do as he did with the bull’s blood: He is to sprinkle it against the mercy seat and in front of it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
