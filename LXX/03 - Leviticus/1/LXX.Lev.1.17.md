**LXX Leviticus 1:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he shall break it from the wings, and shall not divide it. And the priest shall place it upon the altar upon the wood  upon the fire -- it is a yield \[offering\] sacrifice scent of pleasant aroma to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he shall cleave it with the wings thereof, [but] shall not divide [it] asunder: and the priest shall burn it upon the altar, upon the wood that [is] upon the fire: it [is] a burnt sacrifice, an offering made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He shall tear it open by its wings, without dividing the bird completely. And the priest is to burn it on the altar atop the burning wood. It is a burnt offering, a food offering, a pleasing aroma to the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
