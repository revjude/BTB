**LXX Leviticus 1:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Speak to the sons of Israel! And you shall say to them, A man of you, if he should bring gift \[offerings\] to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> from the cattle, and from the oxen, and from the sheep -- you shall bring  your gift \[offerings thus\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, If any man of you bring an offering unto the LORD, ye shall bring your offering of the cattle, [even] of the herd, and of the flock.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Speak to the Israelites and tell them: When any of you brings an offering to the LORD, you may bring as your offering an animal from the herd or the flock.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
