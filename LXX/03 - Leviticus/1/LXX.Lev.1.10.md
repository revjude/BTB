**LXX Leviticus 1:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if his gift \[offering\] \[is\] from the sheep to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, or also from the lambs, and of the kids for a whole burnt-offering, he shall bring an unblemished male for it. And he shall place \[his\] hand upon  its head. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And if his offering [be] of the flocks, [namely], of the sheep, or of the goats, for a burnt sacrifice; he shall bring it a male without blemish.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If, however, one’s offering is a burnt offering from the flock—from the sheep or goats—he is to present an unblemished male.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
