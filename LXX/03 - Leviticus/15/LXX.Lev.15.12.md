**LXX Leviticus 15:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And an earthenware item of which ever the one having a seminal emission should have touched, it shall be broken. And every wooden item shall be washed in water, and it will be clean. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the vessel of earth, that he toucheth which hath the issue, shall be broken: and every vessel of wood shall be rinsed in water.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Any clay pot that the man with the discharge touches must be broken, and any wooden utensil must be rinsed with water.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
