**LXX Leviticus 15:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And a wife, if a husband should have gone to bed with her in a marriage-bed of semen, that they shall bathe in water, and they will be unclean until evening.

The woman also with whom man shall lie [with] seed of copulation, they shall [both] bathe [themselves] in water, and be unclean until the even.
