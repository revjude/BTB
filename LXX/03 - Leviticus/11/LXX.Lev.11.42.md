**LXX Leviticus 11:42** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And all  going upon \[the\] belly, and all  going upon fours always, which is numerous in feet among all the crawling things  crawling upon the earth -- you shall not eat it, for it is an abomination to you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Whatsoever goeth upon the belly, and whatsoever goeth upon [all] four, or whatsoever hath more feet among all creeping things that creep upon the earth, them ye shall not eat; for they [are] an abomination.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Do not eat any creature that moves along the ground, whether it crawls on its belly or walks on four or more feet; for such creatures are detestable.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
