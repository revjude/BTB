**LXX Leviticus 8:35** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And upon the door of the tent of the testimony you shall sit seven days -- day and night; and you shall guard the injunctions of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, and you shall not die. For thus \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> gave charge to me. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Therefore shall ye abide [at] the door of the tabernacle of the congregation day and night seven days, and keep the charge of the LORD, that ye die not: for so I am commanded.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You must remain at the entrance to the Tent of Meeting day and night for seven days and keep the LORD’s charge so that you will not die, for this is what I have been commanded.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
