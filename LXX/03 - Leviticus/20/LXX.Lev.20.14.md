**LXX Leviticus 20:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Who ever should take a woman and  her mother, it is a violation of the law; in fire they shall incinerate him and them, and there shall not be a violation of the law among you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And if a man take a wife and her mother, it [is] wickedness: they shall be burnt with fire, both he and they; that there be no wickedness among you.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If a man marries both a woman and her mother, it is depraved. Both he and they must be burned in the fire, so that there will be no depravity among you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
