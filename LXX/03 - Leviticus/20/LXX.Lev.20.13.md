**LXX Leviticus 20:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And who ever should have bedded with a male as the marriage-bed of a woman, both did an abomination; to death let them be put to death! they are liable. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
If a man also lie with mankind, as he lieth with a woman, both of them have committed an abomination: they shall surely be put to death; their blood [shall be] upon them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If a man lies with a man as with a woman, they have both committed an abomination. They must surely be put to death; their blood is upon them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
