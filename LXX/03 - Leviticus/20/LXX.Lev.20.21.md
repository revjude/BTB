**LXX Leviticus 20:21**

And a man who ever should take \[the\] wife  of his brother, it is uncleanness; he uncovered his brother's indecency -- they shall die childless.

And if a man shall take his brother’s wife, it [is] an unclean thing: he hath uncovered his brother’s nakedness; they shall be childless.
