**LXX Leviticus 20:11**

And if anyone should have bedded with \[the\] wife  of his father, to uncover \[the\] indecency of his father, to death let them be put to death! both are liable.

And the man that lieth with his father’s wife hath uncovered his father’s nakedness: both of them shall surely be put to death; their blood [shall be] upon them.
