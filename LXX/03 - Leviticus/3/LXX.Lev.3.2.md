**LXX Leviticus 3:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he shall place  his hands upon the head of the gift \[offering\], and he shall slay it by the doors of the tent of the testimony, and the sons of Aaron the priests shall pour the blood upon the altar of the whole burnt-offerings round about. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he shall lay his hand upon the head of his offering, and kill it [at] the door of the tabernacle of the congregation: and Aaron’s sons the priests shall sprinkle the blood upon the altar round about.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He is to lay his hand on the head of the offering and slaughter it at the entrance to the Tent of Meeting. Then Aaron’s sons the priests shall splatter the blood on all sides of the altar.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
