**LXX Leviticus 3:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he shall bring from the sacrifice  of deliverance a yield \[offering\] to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> -- the fat and the loin, unblemished with the flank he shall remove it, and all the fat  covering up the belly, and all the fat  upon the belly,

And he shall offer of the sacrifice of the peace offering an offering made by fire unto the LORD; the fat thereof, [and] the whole rump, it shall he take off hard by the backbone; and the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that [is] upon the inwards,
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And from the peace offering he shall bring a food offering to the LORD consisting of its fat: the entire fat tail cut off close to the backbone, the fat that covers the entrails, all the fat that is on them,<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
