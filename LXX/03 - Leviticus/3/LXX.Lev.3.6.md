**LXX Leviticus 3:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if his gift \[offering\] sacrifice of deliverance to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> \[is\] of the sheep, he shall bring it as an unblemished male or female. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And if his offering for a sacrifice of peace offering unto the LORD [be] of the flock; male or female, he shall offer it without blemish.

If, however, one’s peace offering to the LORD is from the flock, he must present a male or female without blemish.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
