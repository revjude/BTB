**LXX Leviticus 14:42** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they shall take stones being scraped of another, and they shall substitute for the stones, and other dust \[for plaster\] they shall take, and they shall plaster the house. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And they shall take other stones, and put [them] in the place of those stones; and he shall take other morter, and shall plaister the house.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
So different stones must be obtained to replace the contaminated ones, as well as additional mortar to replaster the house.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
