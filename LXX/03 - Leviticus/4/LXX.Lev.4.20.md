**LXX Leviticus 4:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he shall offer the calf, in which manner he did the calf  for the sin \[offering\] -- so it shall be done. And the priest shall atone for them, and the sin shall be forgiven them. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he shall do with the bullock as he did with the bullock for a sin offering, so shall he do with this: and the priest shall make an atonement for them, and it shall be forgiven them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He shall offer this bull just as he did the bull for the sin offering; in this way the priest will make atonement on their behalf, and they will be forgiven.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
