**LXX Leviticus 4:28** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
and the sin should be made known to him which he sinned by it, then he shall bring the gift \[offering\] -- he shall offer an unblemished female yearling of \[the\] goats for the sin which he sinned. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Or if his sin, which he hath sinned, come to his knowledge: then he shall bring his offering, a kid of the goats, a female without blemish, for his sin which he hath sinned.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
When he becomes aware of the sin he has committed, he must bring an unblemished female goat as his offering for that sin.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
