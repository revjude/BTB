**LXX Leviticus 21:21** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Every man in which there is on him a blemish from the seed of Aaron the priest, shall not approach  to offer the sacrifices \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, that \[has\] a blemish on him; he shall not come forward to bring the gifts of his God. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
No man that hath a blemish of the seed of Aaron the priest shall come nigh to offer the offerings of the LORD made by fire: he hath a blemish; he shall not come nigh to offer the bread of his God.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
No descendant of Aaron the priest who has a defect shall approach to present the food offerings to the LORD. Since he has a defect, he is not to come near to offer the food of his God.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
