**LXX Leviticus 7:24** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] fat of decaying flesh, and \[that\] taken by wild beasts shall be appointed for all work, but for food it shall not be eaten. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the fat of the beast that dieth of itself, and the fat of that which is torn with beasts, may be used in any other use: but ye shall in no wise eat of it.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The fat of an animal found dead or mauled by wild beasts may be used for any other purpose, but you must not eat it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
