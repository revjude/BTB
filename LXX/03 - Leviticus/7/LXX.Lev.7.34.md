**LXX Leviticus 7:34** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For the breast of the increase \[offering\] and the shoulder of the cut-away portion I have taken from the sons of Israel from the sacrifices  of your deliverance \[offering\], and I gave them to Aaron to the priest and \[to\] his sons, an eternal law for the sons of Israel. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For the wave breast and the heave shoulder have I taken of the children of Israel from off the sacrifices of their peace offerings, and have given them unto Aaron the priest and unto his sons by a statute for ever from among the children of Israel.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I have taken from the sons of Israel the breast of the wave offering and the thigh of the contribution of their peace offerings, and I have given them to Aaron the priest and his sons as a permanent portion from the sons of Israel.’”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
