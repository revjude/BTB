**LXX Leviticus 7:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Every one eating fat off the cattle, of which he will bring from them as a yield \[offering\] \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, that soul shall be destroyed from  its people. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For whosoever eateth the fat of the beast, of which men offer an offering made by fire unto the LORD, even the soul that eateth [it] shall be cut off from his people.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If anyone eats the fat of an animal from which a food offering may be presented to the LORD, the one who eats it must be cut off from his people.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
