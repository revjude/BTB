**LXX Leviticus 7:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And meats, whatever as much as touch any unclean \[thing\], shall not be eaten; it shall be incinerated in fire. Every \[one\] clean shall eat \[clean\] meats. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the flesh that toucheth any unclean [thing] shall not be eaten; it shall be burnt with fire: and as for the flesh, all that be clean shall eat thereof.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Meat that touches anything unclean must not be eaten; it is to be burned up. As for any other meat, anyone who is ceremonially clean may eat it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
