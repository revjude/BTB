**LXX Leviticus 27:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if the one sanctifying the field should ransom it, he shall add the fifth part of the money to the value of it, and it will be his. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And if he that sanctified the field will in any wise redeem it, then he shall add the fifth [part] of the money of thy estimation unto it, and it shall be assured to him.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And if the one who consecrated the field decides to redeem it, he must add a fifth to the assessed value, and it shall belong to him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
