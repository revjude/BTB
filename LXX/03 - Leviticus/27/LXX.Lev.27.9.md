**LXX Leviticus 27:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if \[it be\] from the cattle of the ones being offered of them as a gift to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, who ever should give of these to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, it shall be holy.

And if [it be] a beast, whereof men bring an offering unto the LORD, all that [any man] giveth of such unto the LORD shall be holy.

If he vows an animal that may be brought as an offering to the LORD, any such animal given to the LORD shall be holy.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
