**LXX Leviticus 27:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Every tenth of the land, from the seed of the land, and from the fruit of the wood to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, it is holy to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And all the tithe of the land, [whether] of the seed of the land, [or] of the fruit of the tree, [is] the LORD’S: [it is] holy unto the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Thus any tithe from the land, whether from the seed of the land or the fruit of the trees, belongs to the LORD; it is holy to the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
