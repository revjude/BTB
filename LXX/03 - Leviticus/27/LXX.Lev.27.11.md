**LXX Leviticus 27:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if any unclean beast, of which none are offered of them as a gift to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, he shall set the beast before the priest,

And if [it be] any unclean beast, of which they do not offer a sacrifice unto the LORD, then he shall present the beast before the priest:

But if the vow involves any of the unclean animals that may not be brought as an offering to the LORD, the animal must be presented before the priest.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
