**LXX Leviticus 27:5**

And if from five years \[old\] unto twenty years \[old\], the value of the male shall be twenty double-drachmas, and the female ten double-drachmas.

And if [it be] from five years old even unto twenty years old, then thy estimation shall be of the male twenty shekels, and for the female ten shekels.
