**LXX Leviticus 22:24** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Crushed testicles, or being squeezed out, or emasculated, or drawn away -- you shall not bring them to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, even upon  your land you shall not offer \[them\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Ye shall not offer unto the LORD that which is bruised, or crushed, or broken, or cut; neither shall ye make [any offering thereof] in your land.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You are not to present to the LORD an animal whose testicles are bruised, crushed, torn, or cut; you are not to sacrifice them in your land.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
