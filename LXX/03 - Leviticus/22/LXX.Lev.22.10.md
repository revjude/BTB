**LXX Leviticus 22:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And all of another race shall not eat \[the\] holy \[things\]; a sojourner with a priest, or a hireling shall not eat \[the\] holy \[things\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
There shall no stranger eat [of] the holy thing: a sojourner of the priest, or an hired servant, shall not eat [of] the holy thing.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
No one outside a priest’s family may eat the sacred offering, nor may the guest of a priest or his hired hand eat it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
