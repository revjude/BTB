**LXX Leviticus 13:8**

And the priest shall look at him, and behold, \[if\] the spot degenerated in the skin, then the priest shall declare him defiled -- it is leprosy.

And [if] the priest see that, behold, the scab spreadeth in the skin, then the priest shall pronounce him unclean: it [is] a leprosy.
