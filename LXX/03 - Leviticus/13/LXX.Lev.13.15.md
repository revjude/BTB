**LXX Leviticus 13:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the priest shall look at the healthy flesh, and the healthy flesh shall prove him defiled; for it is unclean, it is leprosy. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the priest shall see the raw flesh, and pronounce him to be unclean: [for] the raw flesh [is] unclean: it [is] a leprosy.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
When the priest sees the raw flesh, he must pronounce him unclean. The raw flesh is unclean; it is a skin disease.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
