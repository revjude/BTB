**LXX Leviticus 13:49** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
and the infection should become greenish or reddish in the skin, or in the garment, or in the warp, or in the woof, or in any item of workmanship of skin, it is an infection of leprosy; and he shall show \[it\] to the priest.

And if the plague be greenish or reddish in the garment, or in the skin, either in the warp, or in the woof, or in any thing of skin; it [is] a plague of leprosy, and shall be shewed unto the priest:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
and if the mark in the fabric, leather, weave, knit, or leather article is green or red, then it is contaminated with mildew and must be shown to the priest.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
