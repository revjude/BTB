**LXX Leviticus 13:57** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if it should appear still in the garment, or in the warp, or in the woof, or in any item made of skin, it is leprosy breaking forth; by fire you shall incinerate that which the infection is in. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And if it appear still in the garment, either in the warp, or in the woof, or in any thing of skin; it [is] a spreading [plague]: thou shalt burn that wherein the plague [is] with fire.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But if it reappears in the fabric, weave, or knit, or on any leather article, it is spreading. You must burn the contaminated article.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
