<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
then the priest shall look at the infection. And behold,  \[if\] its appearance be imbedded under the skin, and in it a yellowish thin hair, then the priest shall declare him defiled; it is an outbreak, it is a leprosy of the head or a leprosy of the beard. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then the priest shall see the plague: and, behold, if it [be] in sight deeper than the skin; [and there be] in it a yellow thin hair; then the priest shall pronounce him unclean: it [is] a dry scall, [even] a leprosy upon the head or beard.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
