**LXX Leviticus 13:56** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if the priest should look, and the infection should be faint after  washing it, he shall rip it from the garment, or from the warp, or from the woof, or from the skin. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And if the priest look, and, behold, the plague [be] somewhat dark after the washing of it; then he shall rend it out of the garment, or out of the skin, or out of the warp, or out of the woof:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If the priest examines it and the mildew has faded after it has been washed, he must cut the contaminated section out of the fabric, leather, weave, or knit.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
