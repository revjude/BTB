**LXX Leviticus 13:31** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if the priest should look at the infection of the outbreak, and behold, the appearance is not imbedded under the skin, and \[the\] hair is not yellowish in it, then the priest shall separate the infection of the outbreak seven days. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And if the priest look on the plague of the scall, and, behold, it [be] not in sight deeper than the skin, and [that there is] no black hair in it; then the priest shall shut up [him that hath] the plague of the scall seven days:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But if the priest examines the scaly infection and it does not appear to be deeper than the skin, and there is no black hair in it, the priest shall isolate the infected person for seven days.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
