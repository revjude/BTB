**LXX Leviticus 13:39** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
then the priest shall look; and behold, in \[the\] skin  of his flesh shinings shining forth being white, it is a psoriasis, it broke out in the skin, he is clean. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then the priest shall look: and, behold, [if] the bright spots in the skin of their flesh [be] darkish white; it [is] a freckled spot [that] groweth in the skin; he [is] clean.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
the priest shall examine them, and if the spots are dull white, it is a harmless rash that has broken out on the skin; the person is clean.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
