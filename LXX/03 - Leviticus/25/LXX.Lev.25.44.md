**LXX Leviticus 25:44** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And a boy and a girl, as many as should be to you from the nations, as many as there are round about you -- from them you shall acquire a manservant and a maidservant.

Both thy bondmen, and thy bondmaids, which thou shalt have, [shall be] of the heathen that are round about you; of them shall ye buy bondmen and bondmaids.
