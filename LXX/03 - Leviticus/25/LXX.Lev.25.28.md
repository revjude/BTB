**LXX Leviticus 25:28** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But if his hand should not be well-provided \[with\] the \[thing\] fit so as to repay him, then his sale \[property\] will be to the one acquiring it until the year of the release; and it shall go forth in the release; then he shall go return to  his possession. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But if he be not able to restore [it] to him, then that which is sold shall remain in the hand of him that hath bought it until the year of jubile: and in the jubile it shall go out, and he shall return unto his possession.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But if he cannot obtain enough to repay him, what he sold will remain in possession of the buyer until the Year of Jubilee. In the Jubilee, however, it is to be released, so that he may return to his property.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
