**LXX Leviticus 6:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the remainder of it Aaron shall eat and  his sons. It shall be eaten unleavened in a holy place; in \[the\] courtyard of the tent of the testimony they shall eat it. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the remainder thereof shall Aaron and his sons eat: with unleavened bread shall it be eaten in the holy place; in the court of the tabernacle of the congregation they shall eat it.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Aaron and his sons are to eat the remainder. It must be eaten without leaven in a holy place; they are to eat it in the courtyard of the Tent of Meeting.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
