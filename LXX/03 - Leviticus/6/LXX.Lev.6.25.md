**LXX Leviticus 6:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Speak \[to\] Aaron and \[to\] his sons, saying, This \[is\] the law of the sin \[offering\]. In \[the\] place where they slay the whole burnt-offerings, they shall slay also the one for the sin \[offering\] before \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> -- it is a holy of holies. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Speak unto Aaron and to his sons, saying, This [is] the law of the sin offering: In the place where the burnt offering is killed shall the sin offering be killed before the LORD: it [is] most holy.
