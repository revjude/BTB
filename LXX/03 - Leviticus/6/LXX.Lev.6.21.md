**LXX Leviticus 6:21** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You shall prepare \[it\] upon a frying pan with olive oil. He shall bring it being mixed up, kneaded, \[as\] a sacrifice of pieces; he shall bring a sacrifice for a scent of pleasant aroma \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
In a pan it shall be made with oil; [and when it is] baken, thou shalt bring it in: [and] the baken pieces of the meat offering shalt thou offer [for] a sweet savour unto the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
It shall be prepared with oil on a griddle; you are to bring it well-kneaded and present it as a grain offering broken in pieces, a pleasing aroma to the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
