**LXX Leviticus 6:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] fire upon the altar shall be kept burning upon it, it shall not be extinguished. And the priest shall burn wood upon it in the morning \[by\] morning; and he shall pile upon it the whole burnt-offering. And he shall place upon it the fat of the deliverance \[offering\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the fire upon the altar shall be burning in it; it shall not be put out: and the priest shall burn wood on it every morning, and lay the burnt offering in order upon it; and he shall burn thereon the fat of the peace offerings.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The fire on the altar shall be kept burning; it must not be extinguished. Every morning the priest is to add wood to the fire, arrange the burnt offering on it, and burn the fat portions of the peace offerings on it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
