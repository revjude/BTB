**LXX Leviticus 26:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I will put peace in  your land, and you shall go to bed, and there will not be among you one frightening. And I will destroy \[the\] ferocious wild beasts from  your land, and war shall not go through  your land. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And I will give peace in the land, and ye shall lie down, and none shall make [you] afraid: and I will rid evil beasts out of the land, neither shall the sword go through your land.

And I will give peace to the land, and you will lie down with nothing to fear. I will rid the land of dangerous animals, and no sword will pass through your land.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
