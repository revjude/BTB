**LXX Leviticus 26:35** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
All the days  of its desolation it shall observe the Sabbath, in which it did not observe the Sabbath in  your Sabbaths, when you dwelt it.

As long as it lieth desolate it shall rest; because it did not rest in your sabbaths, when ye dwelt upon it.

As long as it lies desolate, the land will have the rest it did not receive during the Sabbaths when you lived in it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
