**LXX Daniel 5:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the king yelled in strength  to bring the magi, and the Chaldeans, and the astrologers. And he said to the wise \[men\] of Babylon, Who ever should read  this writing, and  should make known its interpretation to me, purple shall be put on \[him\], and the necklace  of gold upon  his neck, and as third in  my kingdom he shall rule.
