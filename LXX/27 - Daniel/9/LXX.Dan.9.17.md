**LXX Daniel 9:17** 

***Daniel Pleads for God's Mercy***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And now, hearken O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  our God, of the prayer  of your servant and  his supplications! And your face appear upon  your sanctified place, the deserted one, because of you O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Now therefore, O our God, hear the prayer of thy servant, and his supplications, and cause thy face to shine upon thy sanctuary that is desolate, for the Lord’s sake.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
So now, our God, hear the prayers and petitions of Your servant. For Your sake, O Lord, cause Your face to shine upon Your desolate sanctuary.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
