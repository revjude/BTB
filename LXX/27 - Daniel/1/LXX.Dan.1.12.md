**LXX Daniel 1:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Test now indeed  your servants ten days; and let there be given to us from \[food of the\] seeds of the earth! and we shall eat \[of it\], and water we shall drink.

Prove thy servants, I beseech thee, ten days; and let them give us pulse to eat, and water to drink.
