**LXX Daniel 11:35** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And of the ones perceiving shall weaken,  to purify them by fire, and  to choose, and  to be uncovered until \[the\] time of \[the\] end. For \[it is\] still for a time. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And [some] of them of understanding shall fall, to try them, and to purge, and to make [them] white, [even] to the time of the end: because [it is] yet for a time appointed.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Some of the wise will fall so that they may be refined, purified, and made spotless until the time of the end, for it will still come at the appointed time.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
