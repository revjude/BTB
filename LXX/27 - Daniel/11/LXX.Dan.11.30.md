**LXX Daniel 11:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they shall enter in it, even the ones coming forth, \[the\] Chittim. And he shall be humbled, and he shall return, and he shall be enraged against holy covenant; and he shall act, and he shall return, and he shall perceive with the ones forsaking holy covenant. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For the ships of Chittim shall come against him: therefore he shall be grieved, and return, and have indignation against the holy covenant: so shall he do; he shall even return, and have intelligence with them that forsake the holy covenant.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Ships of Kittim will come against him, and he will lose heart. Then he will turn back and rage against the holy covenant and do damage. So he will return and show favor to those who forsake the holy covenant.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
