**LXX Daniel 4:37** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Now then I Nebuchadnezzar praise and greatly exalt and glorify the king  of heaven, for all  his works \[are\] true, and  his paths equitable, and all the ones going in pride he is able to humble. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Now I Nebuchadnezzar praise and extol and honour the King of heaven, all whose works [are] truth, and his ways judgment: and those that walk in pride he is able to abase.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Now I, Nebuchadnezzar, praise and exalt and glorify the King of heaven, for all His works are true and all His ways are just. And He is able to humble those who walk in pride.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
