**LXX Daniel 2:34**

You viewed until a stone was shredded from a mountain without hands, and it struck the image upon the feet  of iron and earthenware; and it thinned them out unto completion.

Thou sawest till that a stone was cut out without hands, which smote the image upon his feet [that were] of iron and clay, and brake them to pieces.
