**LXX Daniel 2:1** 

***Nebuchadnezzar's Dream***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In the second year of the kingdom of Nebuchadnezzar, Nebuchadnezzar dreamed a dream, and his spirit was startled, and  his sleep \[went\] from him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And in the second year of the reign of Nebuchadnezzar Nebuchadnezzar dreamed dreams, wherewith his spirit was troubled, and his sleep brake from him.

In the second year of his reign, Nebuchadnezzar had dreams that troubled his spirit, and sleep escaped him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
