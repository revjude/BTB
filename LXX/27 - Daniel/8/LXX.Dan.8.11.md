**LXX Daniel 8:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And unto the commander-in-chief he was magnified, and through him \[the\] sacrifice was disturbed, and the holy place shall be made desolate. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Yea, he magnified [himself] even to the prince of the host, and by him the daily [sacrifice] was taken away, and the place of his sanctuary was cast down.

It magnified itself, even to the Prince of the host; it removed His daily sacrifice and overthrew the place of His sanctuary.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
