**LXX 1 Kings 7:38**

And he made ten pots of brass, having the capacity of forty coos to the one pot, measuring four cubits. And one pot \[was\] upon the one base for the ten bases.

Then made he ten lavers of brass: one laver contained forty baths: [and] every laver was four cubits: [and] upon every one of the ten bases one laver.
