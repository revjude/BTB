**LXX 1 Kings 7:50** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
and the thresholds, and the nails, and the bowls, and the saucers, and the incense pans \[were\] joined of gold. And the doorways of the doors of the innermost house, of the holy  of holies, and the doors of the house of the temple \[were\] of gold. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the bowls, and the snuffers, and the basons, and the spoons, and the censers [of] pure gold; and the hinges [of] gold, [both] for the doors of the inner house, the most holy [place, and] for the doors of the house, [to wit], of the temple.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
the pure gold basins, wick trimmers, sprinkling bowls, ladles, and censers; and the gold hinges for the doors of the inner temple (that is, the Most Holy Place) as well as for the doors of the main hall of the temple.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
