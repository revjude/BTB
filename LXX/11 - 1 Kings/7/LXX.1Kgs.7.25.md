**LXX 1 Kings 7:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And twelve oxen \[were\] underneath the sea -- the three looking to \[the\] north, and the three looking to \[the\] west, and the three looking to \[the\] south, and the three looking to \[the\] east. And the sea \[was\] upon them on top. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
It stood upon twelve oxen, three looking toward the north, and three looking toward the west, and three looking toward the south, and three looking toward the east: and the sea [was set] above upon them, and all their hinder parts [were] inward.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The Sea stood on twelve oxen, three facing north, three facing west, three facing south, and three facing east. The Sea rested on them, with all their hindquarters toward the center.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
