**LXX 1 Kings 7:47** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Solomon the king put \[away\] all the items, because of \[the\] exceeding multitude which there was no weight of the brass.

And Solomon left all the vessels [unweighed], because they were exceeding many: neither was the weight of the brass found out.

Solomon left all these articles unweighed, because there were so many. The weight of the bronze could not be determined.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
