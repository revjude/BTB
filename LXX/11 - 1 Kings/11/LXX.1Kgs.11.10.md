**LXX 1 Kings 11:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
after being given charge to him, for  this word  thoroughly, to not go after other gods. And he did not guard what \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> gave charge to him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And had commanded him concerning this thing, that he should not go after other gods: but he kept not that which the LORD commanded.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Although He had warned Solomon explicitly not to follow other gods, Solomon did not keep the LORD’s command.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
