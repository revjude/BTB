**LXX 1 Kings 9:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Pharaoh king of Egypt ascended and first took  Gezer, and he burnt it by fire, and the Canaanite  dwelling in the city he put to death, and Pharaoh gave it as a dowry to his daughter -- to \[the\] wife of Solomon. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[For] Pharaoh king of Egypt had gone up, and taken Gezer, and burnt it with fire, and slain the Canaanites that dwelt in the city, and given it [for] a present unto his daughter, Solomon’s wife.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Pharaoh king of Egypt had attacked and captured Gezer. He had set it on fire, killed the Canaanites who lived in the city, and given it as a dowry to his daughter, Solomon’s wife.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
