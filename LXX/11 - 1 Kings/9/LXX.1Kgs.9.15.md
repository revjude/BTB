**LXX 1 Kings 9:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And this \[is\] the matter of the plunder which king Solomon brought to build the house of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, and the house of the king, and the wall of Jerusalem, and the Akra  to enclose the barrier of the city of David, and  Hazor, and  Megiddo, and  Gezer. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And this [is] the reason of the levy which king Solomon raised; for to build the house of the LORD, and his own house, and Millo, and the wall of Jerusalem, and Hazor, and Megiddo, and Gezer.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
This is the account of the forced labor that King Solomon imposed to build the house of the LORD, his own palace, the supporting terraces, and the wall of Jerusalem, as well as Hazor, Megiddo, and Gezer.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
