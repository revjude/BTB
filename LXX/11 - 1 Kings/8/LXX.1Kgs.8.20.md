**LXX 1 Kings 8:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> raised up  his word which he spoke. And I am risen up instead of David  my father, and I sat down upon the throne of Israel, as \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> spoke. And I built the house to the name of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  God of Israel. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the LORD hath performed his word that he spake, and I am risen up in the room of David my father, and sit on the throne of Israel, as the LORD promised, and have built an house for the name of the LORD God of Israel.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Now the LORD has fulfilled the word that He spoke. I have succeeded my father David, and I sit on the throne of Israel, as the LORD promised. I have built the house for the Name of the LORD, the God of Israel.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
