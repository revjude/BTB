**LXX 1 Kings 3:21** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I rose up in the morning to nurse  my son, and that one was \[the\] one having died. And behold, I contemplated him in the morning; and behold, he was not  my son whom I gave birth. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And when I rose in the morning to give my child suck, behold, it was dead: but when I had considered it in the morning, behold, it was not my son, which I did bear.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The next morning, when I got up to nurse my son, I discovered he was dead. But when I examined him, I realized that he was not the son I had borne.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
