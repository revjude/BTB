**LXX 1 Kings 16:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The one having died  of Baasha in the city, the dogs shall eat him. And the one having died of his in the plain, the birds of the heaven shall eat him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Him that dieth of Baasha in the city shall the dogs eat; and him that dieth of his in the fields shall the fowls of the air eat.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Anyone belonging to Baasha who dies in the city will be eaten by dogs, and anyone who dies in the field will be eaten by the birds of the air.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
