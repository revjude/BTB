**LXX 1 Kings 21:24** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The one having died  of Ahab in the city, the dogs will eat; and the one having died of his in the plain, the birds of the heaven will eat.

Him that dieth of Ahab in the city the dogs shall eat; and him that dieth in the field shall the fowls of the air eat.
