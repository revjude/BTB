**LXX 1 Kings 1:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And his father did not make a reproach to him at any time, saying, Why \[is it\] that have you done thus? And he \[was\] good  in appearance, exceedingly. And he was born after Absalom. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And his father had not displeased him at any time in saying, Why hast thou done so? and he also [was a] very goodly [man]; and [his mother] bare him after Absalom.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
(His father had never once reprimanded him by saying, “Why do you act this way?” Adonijah was also very handsome, born next after Absalom.)<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
