**LXX 1 Kings 22:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> said to him, In what \[way\]? And he said, I shall go forth, and I shall be a lying spirit in \[the\] mouth of all  his prophets. And he said, You shall deceive, and indeed you will be able. Go forth, and do so! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the LORD said unto him, Wherewith? And he said, I will go forth, and I will be a lying spirit in the mouth of all his prophets. And he said, Thou shalt persuade [him], and prevail also: go forth, and do so.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
