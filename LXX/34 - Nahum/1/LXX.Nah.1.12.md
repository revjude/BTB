**LXX Nahum 1:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Thus says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, who rules many waters, And so they shall be drawn apart, and the report of you shall not be listened to attentively any longer. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thus saith the LORD; Though [they be] quiet, and likewise many, yet thus shall they be cut down, when he shall pass through. Though I have afflicted thee, I will afflict thee no more.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
This is what the LORD says: “Though they are allied and numerous, yet they will be cut down and pass away. Though I have afflicted you, O Judah, I will afflict you no longer.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
