**LXX Nahum 1:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For \[the enemy\] will be made barren unto his foundation, and as a yew tree being twisted he shall be devoured, even as stubble he shall be dried up fully. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For while [they be] folden together [as] thorns, and while they are drunken [as] drunkards, they shall be devoured as stubble fully dry.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For they will be entangled as with thorns and consumed like the drink of a drunkard—like stubble that is fully dry.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
