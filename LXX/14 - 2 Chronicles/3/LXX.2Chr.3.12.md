**LXX 2 Chronicles 3:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the other wing \[was\] five cubits, touching the wing of the other cherub.

And [one] wing of the other cherub [was] five cubits, reaching to the wall of the house: and the other wing [was] five cubits [also], joining to the wing of the other cherub.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The wing of the second cherub also measured five cubits and touched the wall of the temple, while its other wing measured five cubits and touched the wing of the first cherub.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
