**LXX 2 Chronicles 1:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Now give to me wisdom and understanding! and I shall go forth before  this people and enter in. For who shall judge  this your great people?

Give me now wisdom and knowledge, that I may go out and come in before this people: for who can judge this thy people, [that is so] great?
