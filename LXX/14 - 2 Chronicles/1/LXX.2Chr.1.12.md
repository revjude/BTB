**LXX 2 Chronicles 1:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
the wisdom and the understanding I give to you; and wealth, and possessions, and glory I shall give to you, as did not happen likened unto you among the kings  before you, and it will not be thus after you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Wisdom and knowledge [is] granted unto thee; and I will give thee riches, and wealth, and honour, such as none of the kings have had that [have been] before thee, neither shall there any after thee have the like.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
therefore wisdom and knowledge have been granted to you. And I will also give you riches and wealth and honor unlike anything given to the kings before you or after you.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
