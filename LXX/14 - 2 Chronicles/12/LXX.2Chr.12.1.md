**LXX 2 Chronicles 12:1** 

***Rehoboam Abandons the Commandments***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it came to pass as the kingdom of Rehoboam was prepared, and as he was secure, he abandoned the law of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, and all Israel with him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it came to pass, when Rehoboam had established the kingdom, and had strengthened himself, he forsook the law of the LORD, and all Israel with him.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
After Rehoboam had established his sovereignty and royal power, he and all Israel with him forsook the Law of the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
