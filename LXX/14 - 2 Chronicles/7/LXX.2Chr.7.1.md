**LXX 2 Chronicles 7:1** 

***Fire from Heaven***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And as Solomon completed praying, and  fire came down from out of the heaven, and it devoured the whole burnt-offerings and the sacrifices; and \[the\] glory of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> filled the house.

Now when Solomon had made an end of praying, the fire came down from heaven, and consumed the burnt offering and the sacrifices; and the glory of the LORD filled the house.

When Solomon had finished praying, fire came down from heaven and consumed the burnt offering and the sacrifices, and the glory of the LORD filled the temple.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
