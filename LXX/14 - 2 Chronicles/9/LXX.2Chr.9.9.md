**LXX 2 Chronicles 9:9**

And she gave to the king a hundred twenty talents of gold, and aromatics in very great multitude, and valuable stone. And there was not \[any\] according to  those aromatics which \[the\] queen of Sheba gave \[to\] king Solomon.

And she gave the king an hundred and twenty talents of gold, and of spices great abundance, and precious stones: neither was there any such spice as the queen of Sheba gave king Solomon.
