**LXX 2 Chronicles 9:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I did not trust in the words until of which \[time\] I came and beheld \[with\] my eyes. And behold, the half was not reported to me of the magnitude  of your wisdom being added over the hearing which I heard. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Howbeit I believed not their words, until I came, and mine eyes had seen [it]: and, behold, the one half of the greatness of thy wisdom was not told me: [for] thou exceedest the fame that I heard.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But I did not believe the reports until I came and saw with my own eyes. Indeed, not half of the greatness of your wisdom was told to me. You have far exceeded the report I heard.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
