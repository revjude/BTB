**LXX 2 Chronicles 13:13** 

***Jeroboam Sets an Ambush against Judah***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Jeroboam circled  an ambush to come from behind them. And they were in front of Judah, and the ambush \[was\] from the \[places\] behind. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But Jeroboam caused an ambushment to come about behind them: so they were before Judah, and the ambushment [was] behind them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Now Jeroboam had sent troops around to ambush from the rear, so that while he was in front of Judah, the ambush was behind them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
