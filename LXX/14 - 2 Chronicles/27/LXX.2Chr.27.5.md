**LXX 2 Chronicles 27:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he did combat against \[the\] king of \[the\] sons of Ammon, and he prevailed against him. And the sons of Ammon gave to him each year a hundred talents of silver, and ten thousand cors of wheat, and ten thousand of barley. These the king of \[the\] sons of Ammon brought to him each year, and in the second year, and the third. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He fought also with the king of the Ammonites, and prevailed against them. And the children of Ammon gave him the same year an hundred talents of silver, and ten thousand measures of wheat, and ten thousand of barley. So much did the children of Ammon pay unto him, both the second year, and the third.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Jotham waged war against the king of the Ammonites and defeated them, and that year they gave him a hundred talents of silver, ten thousand cors of wheat, and ten thousand cors of barley. They paid him the same in the second and third years.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
