**LXX 2 Chronicles 18:10**

And Zedekiah son of Chenaanah made for himself horns of iron, and he said, Thus says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; With these you shall gore the Syrian until whenever he should be finished off entirely.

And Zedekiah the son of Chenaanah had made him horns of iron, and said, Thus saith the LORD, With these thou shalt push Syria until they be consumed.
