**LXX 2 Chronicles 18:1** 

***Jehoshaphat and Ahab Allied in Marriage***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And there came  to Jehoshaphat still much riches and glory. And he was allied by marriage with the house of Ahab. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Now Jehoshaphat had riches and honour in abundance, and joined affinity with Ahab.

Now Jehoshaphat had an abundance of riches and honor, and he allied himself with Ahab by marriage.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
