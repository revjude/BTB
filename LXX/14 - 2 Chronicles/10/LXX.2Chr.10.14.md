**LXX 2 Chronicles 10:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he spoke to them according to the counsel of the younger men, saying,  My father oppressed  your yoke, and I will add upon it.  My father corrected you with whips, and I will correct you with scorpions.

And answered them after the advice of the young men, saying, My father made your yoke heavy, but I will add thereto: my father chastised you with whips, but I [will chastise you] with scorpions.
