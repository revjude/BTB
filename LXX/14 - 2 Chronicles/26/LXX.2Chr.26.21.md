**LXX 2 Chronicles 26:21** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Uzziah the king was leprous until \[the\] day  of his decease. And he settled in a house for \[the\] sick, \[being\] leprous, for he was severed from \[the\] house of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. And Jotham  his son \[was\] over  his kingdom, to judge the people of the land. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And Uzziah the king was a leper unto the day of his death, and dwelt in a several house, [being] a leper; for he was cut off from the house of the LORD: and Jotham his son [was] over the king’s house, judging the people of the land.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
So King Uzziah was a leper until the day of his death. He lived in isolation, leprous and cut off from the house of the LORD, while his son Jotham had charge of the royal palace and governed the people of the land.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
