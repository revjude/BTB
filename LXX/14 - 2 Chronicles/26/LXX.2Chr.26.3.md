**LXX 2 Chronicles 26:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Uzziah \[was\] a son \[being\] sixteen years \[old\] in  his taking reign. And fifty and two years he reigned in Jerusalem. And \[the\] name  of his mother \[was\] Jecoliah, from Jerusalem. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Sixteen years old [was] Uzziah when he began to reign, and he reigned fifty and two years in Jerusalem. His mother’s name also [was] Jecoliah of Jerusalem.

Uzziah was sixteen years old when he became king, and he reigned in Jerusalem fifty-two years. His mother’s name was Jecoliah; she was from Jerusalem.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
