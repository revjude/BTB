**LXX 2 Chronicles 26:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they stood by against Uzziah the king, and they said to him, \[It is\] not for you, Uzziah,  to burn incense to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, but only to the priests, to the sons of Aaron, to the ones having been sanctified  to burn incense. Go forth from the sanctuary! for you departed from the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, for this will not be to you for glory from \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  God.
