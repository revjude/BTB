**LXX 2 Chronicles 35:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they prepared the whole burnt-offering, to deliver them according to the division, according to \[the\] houses of \[the\] families to the sons of the people,  to offer to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, as it is written in \[the\] book of Moses. And so \[it was\] into the morning. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And they removed the burnt offerings, that they might give according to the divisions of the families of the people, to offer unto the LORD, as [it is] written in the book of Moses. And so [did they] with the oxen.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
They set aside the burnt offerings to be given to the divisions of the families of the people to offer to the LORD, as it is written in the Book of Moses. And they did the same with the bulls.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
