**LXX 2 Chronicles 23:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And came forth Jehoiada the priest, and he gave charge to the commanders of hundreds, and to the chiefs of the force. And said to them, Lead her outside of the house, and go forth after her, and let \[her\] die by \[the\] sword! For the priest said, Let her not die in \[the\] house of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>!
