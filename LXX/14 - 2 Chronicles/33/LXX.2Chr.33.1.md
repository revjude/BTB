**LXX 2 Chronicles 33:1** 

***Manasseh Reigns in Judah***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Manasseh \[was\] a son being twelve years \[old\] in  his taking reign, and fifty-five years he reigned in Jerusalem.
