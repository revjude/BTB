**LXX 2 Chronicles 5:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Nothing was in the ark except \[the\] two tablets which Moses stationed at Horeb, which \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> ordained with the sons of Israel in  their coming forth from out of \[the\] land of Egypt.

[There was] nothing in the ark save the two tables which Moses put [therein] at Horeb, when the LORD made [a covenant] with the children of Israel, when they came out of Egypt.

There was nothing in the ark except the two tablets that Moses had placed in it at Horeb, where the LORD had made a covenant with the Israelites after they had come out of Egypt.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
