**LXX 2 Chronicles 20:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
It is not for you to wage war in this. Stand, and perceive, and see the deliverance of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> with you, O Judah, and Jerusalem! Do not fear nor be terrified tomorrow coming forth for meeting them! for \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> \[is\] with you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Ye shall not [need] to fight in this [battle]: set yourselves, stand ye [still], and see the salvation of the LORD with you, O Judah and Jerusalem: fear not, nor be dismayed; to morrow go out against them: for the LORD [will be] with you.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You need not fight this battle. Take up your positions, stand firm, and see the salvation of the LORD on your behalf, O Judah and Jerusalem. Do not be afraid or discouraged. Go out and face them tomorrow, for the LORD is with you.’”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
