**LXX 2 Chronicles 36:22** 

***The Proclamation of Cyrus***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[In the\] first year of Cyrus king of \[the\] Persians, after the fulfillment of \[the\] saying of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> through \[the\] mouth of Jeremiah, \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> awakened the spirit of Cyrus king of \[the\] Persians. And he exhorted to proclaim in all  his kingdom in writing, saying, 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Now in the first year of Cyrus king of Persia, that the word of the LORD [spoken] by the mouth of Jeremiah might be accomplished, the LORD stirred up the spirit of Cyrus king of Persia, that he made a proclamation throughout all his kingdom, and [put it] also in writing, saying,
