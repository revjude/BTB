**LXX 2 Chronicles 36:8** 

***Jehoiachin Reigns in Judah***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the rest of the words of Jehoiakim, and all which he did, behold, these are written in \[the\] scroll of \[the\] words of the days of the kings of Israel and of Judah. And Jehoiachin his son reigned instead of him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Now the rest of the acts of Jehoiakim, and his abominations which he did, and that which was found in him, behold, they [are] written in the book of the kings of Israel and Judah: and Jehoiachin his son reigned in his stead.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
As for the rest of the acts of Jehoiakim, the abominations he committed, and all that was found against him, they are indeed written in the Book of the Kings of Israel and Judah. And his son Jehoiachin reigned in his place.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
