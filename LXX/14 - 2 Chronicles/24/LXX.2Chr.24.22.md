**LXX 2 Chronicles 24:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Joash the king did not remember the mercy of which Jehoiada his father performed with him, and his son to death. And as he died, he said, \[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> behold even to judge! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thus Joash the king remembered not the kindness which Jehoiada his father had done to him, but slew his son. And when he died, he said, The LORD look upon [it], and require [it].
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Thus King Joash failed to remember the kindness that Zechariah’s father Jehoiada had extended to him. Instead, Joash killed Jehoiada’s son. As he lay dying, Zechariah said, “May the LORD see this and call you to account.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
