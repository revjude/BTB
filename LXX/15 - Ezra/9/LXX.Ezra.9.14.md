**LXX Ezra 9:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
for we turned to efface  your commandments, and allied by marriage to the peoples of the these lands. You should not be provoked by us unto consumption, \[so that\] there should not be \[one\] left over and preserved \[to us\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Should we again break thy commandments, and join in affinity with the people of these abominations? wouldest not thou be angry with us till thou hadst consumed [us], so that [there should be] no remnant nor escaping?
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
shall we again break Your commandments and intermarry with the peoples who commit these abominations? Would You not become so angry with us as to wipe us out, leaving no remnant or survivor?<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
