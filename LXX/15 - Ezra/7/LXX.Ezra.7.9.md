**LXX Ezra 7:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For on \[day\] one of the first month he founded the expedition  from Babylon. And during the first \[day\] of the fifth month he came into Jerusalem, for \[the\] hand of his God was good upon him.

For upon the first [day] of the first month began he to go up from Babylon, and on the first [day] of the fifth month came he to Jerusalem, according to the good hand of his God upon him.

He had begun the journey from Babylon on the first day of the first month, and he arrived in Jerusalem on the first day of the fifth month, for the gracious hand of his God was upon him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
