**LXX Ezra 4:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
So that of the decree you were guarding \[you not be\] remiss to do concerning this, lest at any time an extinction \[of power\] should be multiplied for an evil deed \[to happen to\] kings. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Take heed now that ye fail not to do this: why should damage grow to the hurt of the kings?
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
See that you do not neglect this matter. Why allow this threat to increase and the royal interests to suffer?<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
