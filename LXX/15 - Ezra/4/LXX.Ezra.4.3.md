**LXX Ezra 4:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Zerubbabel said to them, and Jeshua, and the rest of the rulers of the families  of Israel, \[It is\] not for us and you  to build a house \[to\] our God, for we ourselves together shall build to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> God of Israel, as Cyrus the king of \[the\] Persians gave charge to us. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But Zerubbabel, and Jeshua, and the rest of the chief of the fathers of Israel, said unto them, Ye have nothing to do with us to build an house unto our God; but we ourselves together will build unto the LORD God of Israel, as king Cyrus the king of Persia hath commanded us.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But Zerubbabel, Jeshua, and the other heads of the families of Israel replied, “You have no part with us in building a house for our God, since we alone must build it for the LORD, the God of Israel, as Cyrus king of Persia has commanded us.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
