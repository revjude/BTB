**LXX Ezra 5:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But the eyes  of God became upon the captivity of Judah, and they did not cause them to cease work until \[the\] decree  \[was\] carried to Darius. And then it was given to the tribute-gatherer concerning this. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But the eye of their God was upon the elders of the Jews, that they could not cause them to cease, till the matter came to Darius: and then they returned answer by letter concerning this [matter].
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But the eye of their God was on the elders of the Jews, so that they were not stopped until a report was sent to Darius and written instructions about this matter were returned.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
