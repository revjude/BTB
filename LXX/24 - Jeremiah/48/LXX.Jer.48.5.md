**LXX Jeremiah 48:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
for Luhith is filled with weeping. It shall ascend weeping in \[the\] way of Horonaim. You heard a cry of destruction. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For in the going up of Luhith continual weeping shall go up; for in the going down of Horonaim the enemies have heard a cry of destruction.

For on the ascent to Luhith they weep bitterly as they go, and on the descent to Horonaim cries of distress resound over the destruction:<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
