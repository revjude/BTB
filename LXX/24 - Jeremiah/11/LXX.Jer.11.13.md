**LXX Jeremiah 11:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For according to \[the\] number of your cities were your gods, O Judah. And according to \[the\] number of \[the\] streets  of Jerusalem you arranged shrines  to burn incense  to Baal. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For [according to] the number of thy cities were thy gods, O Judah; and [according to] the number of the streets of Jerusalem have ye set up altars to [that] shameful thing, [even] altars to burn incense unto Baal.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Your gods are indeed as numerous as your cities, O Judah; the altars of shame you have set up—the altars to burn incense to Baal—are as many as the streets of Jerusalem.’<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
