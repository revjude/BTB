for you prophesied in the name of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, saying, This house will be as Shiloh, and  this city shall be made desolate of ones dwelling \[it\]. And all the people held an assembly against Jeremiah in \[the\] house of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>.

Why hast thou prophesied in the name of the LORD, saying, This house shall be like Shiloh, and this city shall be desolate without an inhabitant? And all the people were gathered against Jeremiah in the house of the LORD.
