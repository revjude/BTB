**LXX Jeremiah 44:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And as we stopped burning incense to the queen of the heaven, and offering a libation to her of a libation, we made less all, and by broadsword and by famine we failed.

But since we left off to burn incense to the queen of heaven, and to pour out drink offerings unto her, we have wanted all [things], and have been consumed by the sword and by the famine.

But from the time we stopped burning incense to the Queen of Heaven and pouring out drink offerings to her, we have lacked everything and have been perishing by sword and famine.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
