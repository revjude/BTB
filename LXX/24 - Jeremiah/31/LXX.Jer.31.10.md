**LXX Jeremiah 31:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Hear \[the\] words of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, O nations, and announce unto islands  far off! Say! The one winnowing  Israel will gather him, and he shall guard him as the one grazing his flock. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Hear the word of the LORD, O ye nations, and declare [it] in the isles afar off, and say, He that scattered Israel will gather him, and keep him, as a shepherd [doth] his flock.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Hear, O nations, the word of the LORD, and proclaim it in distant coastlands: “The One who scattered Israel will gather them and keep them as a shepherd keeps his flock.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
