**LXX Jeremiah 35:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But \[the\] sons of Jonadab son of Rechab established the commandment  of their father, which he gave charge to them.  But this people heard me not.

Because the sons of Jonadab the son of Rechab have performed the commandment of their father, which he commanded them; but this people hath not hearkened unto me:

Yes, the sons of Jonadab son of Rechab carried out the command their forefather gave them, but these people have not listened to Me.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
