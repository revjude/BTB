**LXX Jeremiah 5:15** 

***The Coming Destruction***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Behold, I bring upon you a nation at a distance, O house of Israel, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. A strong nation, a nation from \[the\] eon, a nation of which you shall not hear the voice  of its language.

Lo, I will bring a nation upon you from far, O house of Israel, saith the LORD: it [is] a mighty nation, it [is] an ancient nation, a nation whose language thou knowest not, neither understandest what they say.
