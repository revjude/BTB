**LXX Jeremiah 8:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they shall gather  of their produce, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. There is no grape on the grapevines, and there are no figs on the fig-trees, and the leaves have flown down. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I will surely consume them, saith the LORD: [there shall be] no grapes on the vine, nor figs on the fig tree, and the leaf shall fade; and [the things that] I have given them shall pass away from them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I will take away their harvest, declares the LORD. There will be no grapes on the vine, nor figs on the tree, and even the leaf will wither. Whatever I have given them will be lost to them.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
