**LXX Jeremiah 30:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>

For I will restore health unto thee, and I will heal thee of thy wounds, saith the LORD; because they called thee an Outcast, [saying], This [is] Zion, whom no man seeketh after.
