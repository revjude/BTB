**LXX Jeremiah 19:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they shall eat the flesh  of their sons, and the flesh  of their daughters; and each shall eat the flesh of his neighbor in the citadel, and in the assault in which their enemies shall assault them, and the ones seeking  their life. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And I will cause them to eat the flesh of their sons and the flesh of their daughters, and they shall eat every one the flesh of his friend in the siege and straitness, wherewith their enemies, and they that seek their lives, shall straiten them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I will make them eat the flesh of their sons and daughters, and they will eat one another’s flesh in the siege and distress inflicted on them by their enemies who seek their lives.’<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
