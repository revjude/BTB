**LXX Jeremiah 43:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you shall say, Thus said \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> of the forces, the God of Israel, Behold, I send \[for\], and I will bring Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon  my servant. And he shall put  his throne upon  these stones which you hid; and he shall lift the shields upon them. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And say unto them, Thus saith the LORD of hosts, the God of Israel; Behold, I will send and take Nebuchadrezzar the king of Babylon, my servant, and will set his throne upon these stones that I have hid; and he shall spread his royal pavilion over them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Then tell them that this is what the LORD of Hosts, the God of Israel, says: ‘I will send for My servant Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon, and I will set his throne over these stones that I have embedded, and he will spread his royal pavilion over them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
