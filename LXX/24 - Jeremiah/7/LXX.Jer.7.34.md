**LXX Jeremiah 7:34** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I will depose from out of \[the\] cities of Judah, and from out of \[the\] corridors of Jerusalem \[the\] voice of ones being glad, and \[the\] voice of ones rejoicing, \[the\] voice of \[the\] groom, and \[the\] voice of \[the\] bride; for all the land will be for desolation. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then will I cause to cease from the cities of Judah, and from the streets of Jerusalem, the voice of mirth, and the voice of gladness, the voice of the bridegroom, and the voice of the bride: for the land shall be desolate.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I will remove from the cities of Judah and the streets of Jerusalem the sounds of joy and gladness and the voices of the bride and bridegroom, for the land will become a wasteland.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
