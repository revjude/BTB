**LXX Jeremiah 6:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Make preparations against her for war! Rise up! for we should ascend upon her at midday. Woe to us, for the day has declined, for the shadows of the day fail. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Prepare ye war against her; arise, and let us go up at noon. Woe unto us! for the day goeth away, for the shadows of the evening are stretched out.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
‘Prepare for battle against her; rise up, let us attack at noon. Woe to us, for the daylight is fading; the evening shadows grow long.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
