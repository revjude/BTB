**LXX Jeremiah 37:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Zedekiah sent and called him. And the king asked him secretly, and said to him, Is there  a word from \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>? And he said, It is into \[the\] hands of \[the\] king of Babylon you shall be delivered up. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then Zedekiah the king sent, and took him out: and the king asked him secretly in his house, and said, Is there [any] word from the LORD? And Jeremiah said, There is: for, said he, thou shalt be delivered into the hand of the king of Babylon.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Later, King Zedekiah sent for Jeremiah and received him in his palace, where he asked him privately, “Is there a word from the LORD?” “There is,” Jeremiah replied. “You will be delivered into the hand of the king of Babylon.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
