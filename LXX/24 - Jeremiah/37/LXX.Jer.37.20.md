**LXX Jeremiah 37:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And now hear indeed O lord, O king! mercy fall for me in front of you! And in no way should you return me unto \[the\] house of Jonathan the scribe, and in no way should I die there. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Therefore hear now, I pray thee, O my lord the king: let my supplication, I pray thee, be accepted before thee; that thou cause me not to return to the house of Jonathan the scribe, lest I die there.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But now please listen, O my lord the king. May my petition come before you. Do not send me back to the house of Jonathan the scribe, or I will die there.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
