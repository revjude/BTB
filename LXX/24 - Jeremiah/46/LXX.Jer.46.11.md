**LXX Jeremiah 46:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Ascend to Gilead, and take balm to the virgin daughter of Egypt! In vain you multiplied your cures; there is no benefit to you.

Go up into Gilead, and take balm, O virgin, the daughter of Egypt: in vain shalt thou use many medicines; [for] thou shalt not be cured.

Go up to Gilead for balm, O Virgin Daughter of Egypt! In vain you try many remedies, but for you there is no healing.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
