**LXX Jeremiah 32:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For the sons of Israel and the sons of Judah were alone doing the wicked \[thing\] in front of my eyes from their youth. \[The\] sons of Israel \[were\] the ones greatly embittering me in the works  of their hands, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For the children of Israel and the children of Judah have only done evil before me from their youth: for the children of Israel have only provoked me to anger with the work of their hands, saith the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the children of Israel and of Judah have done nothing but evil in My sight from their youth; indeed, they have done nothing but provoke Me to anger by the work of their hands, declares the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
