**LXX Jeremiah 32:32** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
because of all the wickedness of the sons of Israel and Judah, which they did to embitter me, they, and  their kings, and  their rulers, and  their priests, and  their prophets, \[the\] men of Judah, and the ones dwelling in Jerusalem. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Because of all the evil of the children of Israel and of the children of Judah, which they have done to provoke me to anger, they, their kings, their princes, their priests, and their prophets, and the men of Judah, and the inhabitants of Jerusalem.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
because of all the evil the children of Israel and of Judah have done to provoke Me to anger—they, their kings, their officials, their priests and prophets, the men of Judah, and the residents of Jerusalem.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
