**LXX Jeremiah 34:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And all the great men heeded, and all the people, the ones entering into the covenant  to send each  his manservant and each  his maidservant free, to not serve any more to them. And they heeded, and let \[them\] go. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Now when all the princes, and all the people, which had entered into the covenant, heard that every one should let his manservant, and every one his maidservant, go free, that none should serve themselves of them any more, then they obeyed, and let [them] go.
