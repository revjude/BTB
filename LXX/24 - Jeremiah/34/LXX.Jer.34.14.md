**LXX Jeremiah 34:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Whenever six years should be fulfilled, you shall send \[free\]  your Hebrew brother, who shall be sold to you; and he shall work for you six years, and \[then\] you shall send him out free. And they did not hear me, and did not lean  their ear. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
At the end of seven years let ye go every man his brother an Hebrew, which hath been sold unto thee; and when he hath served thee six years, thou shalt let him go free from thee: but your fathers hearkened not unto me, neither inclined their ear.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Every seventh year, each of you must free his Hebrew brother who has sold himself to you. He may serve you six years, but then you must let him go free. But your fathers did not listen or incline their ear.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
