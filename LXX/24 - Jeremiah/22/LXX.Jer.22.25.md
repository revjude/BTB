**LXX Jeremiah 22:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I will deliver you up into \[the\] hands of the ones seeking  your life, whom you are cautious from in front of them, even into \[the\] hands of Nebuchadnezzar king  of Babylon, and into \[the\] hands of the Chaldeans. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And I will give thee into the hand of them that seek thy life, and into the hand [of them] whose face thou fearest, even into the hand of Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon, and into the hand of the Chaldeans.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
In fact, I will hand you over to those you dread, who want to take your life—to Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon and to the Chaldeans.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
