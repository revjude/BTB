**LXX Jeremiah 22:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Thus says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, Write up  this man as a man banished by public proclamation! For in no way should there be \[anything\] grown from out of  his seed, a man sitting down upon \[the\] throne of David, ruling again in  Judah. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thus saith the LORD, Write ye this man childless, a man [that] shall not prosper in his days: for no man of his seed shall prosper, sitting upon the throne of David, and ruling any more in Judah.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
This is what the LORD says: “Enroll this man as childless, a man who will not prosper in his lifetime. None of his descendants will prosper to sit on the throne of David or to rule again in Judah.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
