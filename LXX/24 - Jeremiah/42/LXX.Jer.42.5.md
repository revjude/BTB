**LXX Jeremiah 42:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they said  to Jeremiah, Let \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> be to us for just and trustworthy witness! if we do not according to every  word which ever \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> your God should send you to us -- thus we will do. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then they said to Jeremiah, The LORD be a true and faithful witness between us, if we do not even according to all things for the which the LORD thy God shall send thee to us.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Then they said to Jeremiah, “May the LORD be a true and faithful witness against us if we do not act upon every word that the LORD your God sends you to tell us.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
