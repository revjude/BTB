**LXX Jeremiah 24:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Thus says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  God of Israel, As  these better figs, so shall I recognize the Jews being resettled whom I have sent out of  this place into \[the\] land of \[the\] Chaldeans for good. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thus saith the LORD, the God of Israel; Like these good figs, so will I acknowledge them that are carried away captive of Judah, whom I have sent out of this place into the land of the Chaldeans for [their] good.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“This is what the LORD, the God of Israel, says: ‘Like these good figs, so I regard as good the exiles from Judah, whom I have sent away from this place to the land of the Chaldeans.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
