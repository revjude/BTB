**LXX Jeremiah 24:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> said to me, What do you see, Jeremiah? And I said, Figs, the better figs \[are\] exceedingly better, and the bad ones exceedingly bad which cannot be eaten because of their badness. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then said the LORD unto me, What seest thou, Jeremiah? And I said, Figs; the good figs, very good; and the evil, very evil, that cannot be eaten, they are so evil.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Jeremiah,” the LORD asked, “what do you see?” “Figs!” I replied. “The good figs are very good, but the bad figs are very bad, so bad they cannot be eaten.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
