**LXX Jeremiah 2:36** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For you are disdained very much of the repeating a second time  of your ways. And from Egypt you shall be disgraced, as you were disgraced by Assyria. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Why gaddest thou about so much to change thy way? thou also shalt be ashamed of Egypt, as thou wast ashamed of Assyria.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
How impulsive you are, constantly changing your ways! You will be disappointed by Egypt just as you were by Assyria.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
