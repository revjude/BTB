**LXX Jeremiah 2:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Turn  your foot from \[the\] rough way, and  your throat from thirst!  But she said, I will be manly. For I loved strangers, and went after them.

Withhold thy foot from being unshod, and thy throat from thirst: but thou saidst, There is no hope: no; for I have loved strangers, and after them will I go.

You should have kept your feet from going bare and your throat from being thirsty. But you said, ‘It is hopeless! For I love foreign gods, and I must go after them.’<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
