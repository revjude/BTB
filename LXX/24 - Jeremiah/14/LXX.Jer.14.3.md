**LXX Jeremiah 14:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And  her great men sent  their younger ones for water. They came upon the wells and did not find water. They returned  their receptacles empty. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And their nobles have sent their little ones to the waters: they came to the pits, [and] found no water; they returned with their vessels empty; they were ashamed and confounded, and covered their heads.

The nobles send their servants for water; they go to the cisterns, but find no water; their jars return empty. They are ashamed and humiliated; they cover their heads.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
