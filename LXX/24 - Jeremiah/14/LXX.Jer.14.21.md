**LXX Jeremiah 14:21** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Abate, on account of  your name! You should not destroy \[the\] throne of your glory. Remember! you should not efface  your covenant, the one with us! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Do not abhor [us], for thy name’s sake, do not disgrace the throne of thy glory: remember, break not thy covenant with us.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the sake of Your name do not despise us; do not disgrace Your glorious throne. Remember Your covenant with us; do not break it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
