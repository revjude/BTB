**LXX Jeremiah 14:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Since  our sins opposed us, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, do for us because of  your name! For our sins \[are\] many before you. Against you we sinned. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
O LORD, though our iniquities testify against us, do thou [it] for thy name’s sake: for our backslidings are many; we have sinned against thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Although our iniquities testify against us, O LORD, act for the sake of Your name. Indeed, our rebellions are many; we have sinned against You.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
