**LXX Jeremiah 9:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Their tongue \[is\] a piercing arrow; the words of their mouth deceitful; one speaks peaceably \[to\] his neighbor, and in himself he has  hatred. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Their tongue [is as] an arrow shot out; it speaketh deceit: [one] speaketh peaceably to his neighbour with his mouth, but in heart he layeth his wait.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Their tongues are deadly arrows; they speak deception. With his mouth a man speaks peace to his neighbor, but in his heart he sets a trap for him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
