**LXX Jeremiah 9:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And let them take upon you a lamentation! And your eyes lead down tears! And  let your eyelids flow water! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And let them make haste, and take up a wailing for us, that our eyes may run down with tears, and our eyelids gush out with waters.

Let them come quickly and take up a lament over us, that our eyes may overflow with tears, and our eyelids may gush with water.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
