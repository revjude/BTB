**LXX Jeremiah 36:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Perhaps the house of Judah will hear all the bad \[things\] which I devise to do to them; that they should turn from their wicked way; and I will be propitious \[to\] their iniquities, and \[to\] their sins. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
It may be that the house of Judah will hear all the evil which I purpose to do unto them; that they may return every man from his evil way; that I may forgive their iniquity and their sin.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Perhaps when the people of Judah hear about all the calamity I plan to bring upon them, each of them will turn from his wicked way. Then I will forgive their iniquity and their sin.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
