**LXX Jeremiah 36:31** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I will visit against him, and against  his family, and against  his children  their lawlessnesses. And I will bring upon them, and upon the ones dwelling in Jerusalem, and upon \[the\] land of Judah, all the evils which I spoke to them; and they heard not. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And I will punish him and his seed and his servants for their iniquity; and I will bring upon them, and upon the inhabitants of Jerusalem, and upon the men of Judah, all the evil that I have pronounced against them; but they hearkened not.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I will punish him and his descendants and servants for their iniquity. I will bring on them, on the residents of Jerusalem, and on the men of Judah, all the calamity about which I warned them but they did not listen.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
