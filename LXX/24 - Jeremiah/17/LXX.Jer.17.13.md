**LXX Jeremiah 17:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] waiting of Israel, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, all the ones forsaking you, let them be disgraced! Ones revolting, let them be written upon the ground! for they abandoned \[the\] spring of life -- the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
O LORD, the hope of Israel, all that forsake thee shall be ashamed, [and] they that depart from me shall be written in the earth, because they have forsaken the LORD, the fountain of living waters.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
O LORD, the hope of Israel, all who abandon You will be put to shame. All who turn away will be written in the dust, for they have abandoned the LORD, the fountain of living water.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
