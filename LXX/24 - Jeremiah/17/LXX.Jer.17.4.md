**LXX Jeremiah 17:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you shall be forsaken from  your inheritance which I gave to you. And I will make you to serve \[to\] your enemies in the land which you knew not. For you kindled fire in  my wrath; unto the eon it shall blaze. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And thou, even thyself, shalt discontinue from thine heritage that I gave thee; and I will cause thee to serve thine enemies in the land which thou knowest not: for ye have kindled a fire in mine anger, [which] shall burn for ever.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And you yourself will relinquish the inheritance that I gave you. I will enslave you to your enemies in a land that you do not know, for you have kindled My anger; it will burn forever.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
