**LXX Jeremiah 25:1** 

***The Captivity of Judah***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The word  coming to Jeremiah concerning all the people of Judah, in the fourth year  of Jehoiakim son of Josiah king of Judah; it \[was\] \[the\] first year  to Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon;

The word that came to Jeremiah concerning all the people of Judah in the fourth year of Jehoiakim the son of Josiah king of Judah, that [was] the first year of Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon;
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
This is the word that came to Jeremiah concerning all the people of Judah in the fourth year of Jehoiakim son of Josiah king of Judah, which was the first year of Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
