**LXX Jeremiah 13:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>

And if thou say in thine heart, Wherefore come these things upon me? For the greatness of thine iniquity are thy skirts discovered, [and] thy heels made bare.

And if you ask yourself, “Why has this happened to me?” It is because of the magnitude of your iniquity that your skirts have been stripped off and your body has been exposed.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
