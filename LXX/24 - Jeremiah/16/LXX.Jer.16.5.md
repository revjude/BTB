**LXX Jeremiah 16:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Thus says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, You should not enter into their revelry, and you should not go in  to lament, and you should not mourn them. For I have removed  my peace from  this people, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, \[even\] mercy and charity. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For thus saith the LORD, Enter not into the house of mourning, neither go to lament nor bemoan them: for I have taken away my peace from this people, saith the LORD, [even] lovingkindness and mercies.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Indeed, this is what the LORD says: “Do not enter a house where there is a funeral meal. Do not go to mourn or show sympathy, for I have removed from this people My peace, My loving devotion, and My compassion,” declares the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
