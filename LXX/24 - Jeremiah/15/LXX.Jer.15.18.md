**LXX Jeremiah 15:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Why \[do\] the ones grieving me prevail over me?  My wound \[is\] substantial, from where shall I be healed? In becoming, it became to me as false water, not having trust. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Why is my pain perpetual, and my wound incurable, [which] refuseth to be healed? wilt thou be altogether unto me as a liar, [and as] waters [that] fail?
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Why is my pain unending, and my wound incurable, refusing to be healed? You have indeed become like a mirage to me—water that is not there.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
