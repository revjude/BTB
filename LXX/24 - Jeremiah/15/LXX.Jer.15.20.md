**LXX Jeremiah 15:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I will appoint you to this people as a fortified wall of brass. And they shall wage war against you, but in no way shall they be able \[to prevail\] against you; because I am with you  to deliver you, and to rescue you, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And I will make thee unto this people a fenced brasen wall: and they shall fight against thee, but they shall not prevail against thee: for I [am] with thee to save thee and to deliver thee, saith the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Then I will make you a wall to this people, a fortified wall of bronze; they will fight against you but will not overcome you, for I am with you to save and deliver you, declares the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
