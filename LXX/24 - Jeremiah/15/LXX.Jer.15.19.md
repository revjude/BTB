**LXX Jeremiah 15:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Because of this, thus says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, If you should return, and I should restore you, you shall stand before my face. And if you should lead out \[the\] esteemed from \[the\] unworthy, you will be as my mouth. And they shall return to you; and you shall not return to them. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Therefore thus saith the LORD, If thou return, then will I bring thee again, [and] thou shalt stand before me: and if thou take forth the precious from the vile, thou shalt be as my mouth: let them return unto thee; but return not thou unto them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Therefore this is what the LORD says: “If you return, I will restore you; you will stand in My presence. And if you speak words that are noble instead of worthless, you will be My spokesman. It is they who must turn to you, but you must not turn to them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
