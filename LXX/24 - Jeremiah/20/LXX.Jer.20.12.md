**LXX Jeremiah 20:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, trying just \[things\], perceiving \[the\] kidneys and heart; may I behold the punishment by you on them. For to you I uncovered  my pleas in defense. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But, O LORD of hosts, that triest the righteous, [and] seest the reins and the heart, let me see thy vengeance on them: for unto thee have I opened my cause.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
O LORD of Hosts, who examines the righteous, who sees the heart and mind, let me see Your vengeance upon them, for to You I have committed my cause.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
