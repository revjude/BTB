**LXX Jeremiah 3:14** 

***The Gathering of Israel from All Nations***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Return, O revolting sons, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>! For I shall lord over you. And I shall take you, one from out of a city, and two from out of a family, and I will bring you into Zion.

Turn, O backsliding children, saith the LORD; for I am married unto you: and I will take you one of a city, and two of a family, and I will bring you to Zion:

“Return, O faithless children,” declares the LORD, “for I am your master, and I will take you—one from a city and two from a family—and bring you to Zion.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
