**LXX Jeremiah 3:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you had many shepherds for your occasion for stumbling; \[the\] appearance of a harlot became to you; you cast shame to all. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Therefore the showers have been withholden, and there hath been no latter rain; and thou hadst a whore’s forehead, thou refusedst to be ashamed.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Therefore the showers have been withheld, and no spring rains have fallen. Yet you have the brazen look of a prostitute; you refuse to be ashamed.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
