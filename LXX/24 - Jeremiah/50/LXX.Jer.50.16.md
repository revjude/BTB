**LXX Jeremiah 50:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Utterly destroy \[the\] seed from out of Babylon! \[the\] one holding \[the\] sickle in \[the\] time of harvest, from in front \[of the\] Grecian sword. Each unto  his people shall return, and each unto  his land shall flee. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Cut off the sower from Babylon, and him that handleth the sickle in the time of harvest: for fear of the oppressing sword they shall turn every one to his people, and they shall flee every one to his own land.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Cut off the sower from Babylon, and the one who wields the sickle at harvest time. In the face of the oppressor’s sword, each will turn to his own people, each will flee to his own land.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
