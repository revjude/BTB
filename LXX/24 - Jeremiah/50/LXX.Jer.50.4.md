**LXX Jeremiah 50:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
They shook and went; in  those days, and in  that time, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, the sons of Israel shall come; they and the sons of Judah together. Proceeding and weeping they shall go seeking the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> their God. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
In those days, and in that time, saith the LORD, the children of Israel shall come, they and the children of Judah together, going and weeping: they shall go, and seek the LORD their God.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“In those days and at that time, declares the LORD, the children of Israel and the children of Judah will come together, weeping as they come, and will seek the LORD their God.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
