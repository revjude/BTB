**LXX Jeremiah 45:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you seek to yourself great \[things\]. You should not seek. For behold, I bring bad \[things\] upon all flesh, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. But I will give to you  your life for gain in every place wherever you should proceed there. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And seekest thou great things for thyself? seek [them] not: for, behold, I will bring evil upon all flesh, saith the LORD: but thy life will I give unto thee for a prey in all places whither thou goest.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But as for you, do you seek great things for yourself? Stop seeking! For I will bring disaster on every living creature, declares the LORD, but wherever you go, I will grant your life as a spoil of war.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
