**LXX Jeremiah 1:5**

Before  my shaping you in \[the\] belly I knew you; and before  your coming forth from out of \[the\] womb I sanctified you; I appointed you prophet unto nations.

Before I formed thee in the belly I knew thee; and before thou camest forth out of the womb I sanctified thee, [and] I ordained thee a prophet unto the nations.

“Before I formed you in the womb I knew you, and before you were born I set you apart and appointed you as a prophet to the nations.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
