**LXX Jeremiah 1:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For behold, I call together all the kingdoms of \[the\] north, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; and they shall come, and each shall put  his throne upon the thresholds of the gates of Jerusalem, and upon all the walls  round about, and upon all the cities of Judah. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For, lo, I will call all the families of the kingdoms of the north, saith the LORD; and they shall come, and they shall set every one his throne at the entering of the gates of Jerusalem, and against all the walls thereof round about, and against all the cities of Judah.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For I am about to summon all the clans and kingdoms of the north,” declares the LORD. “Their kings will come and set up their thrones at the entrance of the gates of Jerusalem. They will attack all her surrounding walls and all the other cities of Judah.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
