**LXX Jeremiah 12:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Upon every mountain pass in the wilderness \[the\] ones causing misery came. For \[the\] sword of the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> shall devour from \[one\] tip of the land unto \[the other\] tip of the land; there is no peace to all flesh. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The spoilers are come upon all high places through the wilderness: for the sword of the LORD shall devour from the [one] end of the land even to the [other] end of the land: no flesh shall have peace.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Over all the barren heights in the wilderness the destroyers have come, for the sword of the LORD devours from one end of the earth to the other. No flesh has peace.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
