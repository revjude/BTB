**LXX Jeremiah 12:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For even  your brethren and the house  of your father, even they disregarded you; and they yelled out; they assembled at your rear; you should not trust in them when they speak with you good \[words\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For even thy brethren, and the house of thy father, even they have dealt treacherously with thee; yea, they have called a multitude after thee: believe them not, though they speak fair words unto thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Even your brothers—your own father’s household—even they have betrayed you; even they have cried aloud against you. Do not trust them, though they speak well of you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
