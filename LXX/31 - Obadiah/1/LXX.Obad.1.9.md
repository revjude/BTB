**LXX Obadiah 1:9** 

***Esau to be Removed***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And your warriors shall be terrified, the ones from Teman, so that man shall be removed from \[the\] mountain of Esau. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And thy mighty [men], O Teman, shall be dismayed, to the end that every one of the mount of Esau may be cut off by slaughter.

Then your mighty men, O Teman, will be terrified, so that everyone in the mountains of Esau will be cut down in the slaughter.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
