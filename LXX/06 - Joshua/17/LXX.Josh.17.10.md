**LXX Joshua 17:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
From \[the\] south \[it was to\] Ephraim, and towards \[the\] north to Manasseh; and the sea will be their borders, and at Asher they join together towards \[the\] north, and \[with\] Issachar from \[the\] east. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Southward [it was] Ephraim’s, and northward [it was] Manasseh’s, and the sea is his border; and they met together in Asher on the north, and in Issachar on the east.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Ephraim’s territory was to the south, and Manasseh’s was to the north, having the Sea as its border and adjoining Asher on the north and Issachar on the east.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
