**LXX Joshua 18:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Give from out of yours three men from a tribe, and send them; and rising up let them go through the land, and let them diagram it before me! as it shall behoove to divide it. And they came to him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Give out from among you three men for [each] tribe: and I will send them, and they shall rise, and go through the land, and describe it according to the inheritance of them; and they shall come [again] to me.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Appoint three men from each tribe, and I will send them out to survey the land and map it out, according to the inheritance of each. Then they will return to me<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
