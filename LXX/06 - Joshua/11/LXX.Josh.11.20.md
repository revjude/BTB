**LXX Joshua 11:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For it was by \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> to become strong  of their heart to meet for war against Israel, that they should be utterly destroyed; so that no mercy should be given to them, but that they should be utterly destroyed in which manner \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> told to Moses. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For it was of the LORD to harden their hearts, that they should come against Israel in battle, that he might destroy them utterly, [and] that they might have no favour, but that he might destroy them, as the LORD commanded Moses.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For it was of the LORD to harden their hearts to engage Israel in battle, so that they would be set apart for destruction and would receive no mercy, being annihilated as the LORD had commanded Moses.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
