**LXX Joshua 22:23** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if we built a shrine for ourselves, so as to depart from \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, or so as to haul up on it a sacrifice of whole burnt-offerings, or so as to make upon it a sacrifice of deliverance, \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> himself shall inquire. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
That we have built us an altar to turn from following the LORD, or if to offer thereon burnt offering or meat offering, or if to offer peace offerings thereon, let the LORD himself require [it];
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If we have built for ourselves an altar to turn away from Him and to offer burnt offerings and grain offerings on it, or to sacrifice peace offerings on it, may the LORD Himself hold us accountable.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
