**LXX Joshua 24:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And we yelled to \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, and he put a cloud and dimness between you and between the Egyptians; and he brought upon them the sea, and it covered them; and your eyes beheld as much as \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> did in \[the\] land of Egypt; and you were in the wilderness many days. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And when they cried unto the LORD, he put darkness between you and the Egyptians, and brought the sea upon them, and covered them; and your eyes have seen what I have done in Egypt: and ye dwelt in the wilderness a long season.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
