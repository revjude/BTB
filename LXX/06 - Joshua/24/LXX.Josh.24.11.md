**LXX Joshua 24:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you passed over the Jordan and came to Jericho; and the ones dwelling in Jericho waged war against you, the Amorite, and the Perizzite, and the Canaanite, and the Hittite, and the Girgashite, and the Hivite, and the Jebusite; and he delivered them into  your hands. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And ye went over Jordan, and came unto Jericho: and the men of Jericho fought against you, the Amorites, and the Perizzites, and the Canaanites, and the Hittites, and the Girgashites, the Hivites, and the Jebusites; and I delivered them into your hand.
