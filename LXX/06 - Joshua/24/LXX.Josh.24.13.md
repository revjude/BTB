**LXX Joshua 24:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he gave to you \[the\] land upon which you did not tire \[by labor\] upon; and \[he gave\] cities which you did not build, but you settled upon them; and \[he gave\] vineyards and olive groves which you did not plant, \[but\] you shall eat from \[them\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And I have given you a land for which ye did not labour, and cities which ye built not, and ye dwell in them; of the vineyards and oliveyards which ye planted not do ye eat.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
So I gave you a land on which you did not toil and cities that you did not build, and now you live in them and eat from vineyards and olive groves that you did not plant.’<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
