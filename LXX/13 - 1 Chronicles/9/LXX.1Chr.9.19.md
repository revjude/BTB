**LXX 1 Chronicles 9:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Shallum son of Kore, son of Ebiasaph, son of Korah, and  his brethren according to \[the\] house of their fathers, the Korahites, over the works of the service, guarding the thresholds of the tent, and  their fathers over the camp of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> guarding the entrance. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And Shallum the son of Kore, the son of Ebiasaph, the son of Korah, and his brethren, of the house of his father, the Korahites, [were] over the work of the service, keepers of the gates of the tabernacle: and their fathers, [being] over the host of the LORD, [were] keepers of the entry.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Shallum son of Kore, the son of Ebiasaph, the son of Korah, and his relatives from the Korahites were assigned to guard the thresholds of the Tent, just as their fathers had been assigned to guard the entrance to the dwelling of the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
