**LXX 1 Chronicles 20:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the people  in it he led out, and he sawed through with saws, and with adz of iron, and with threshing machines. And thus David did to all the cities \[of the\] sons of Ammon. And David returned and all  his people unto Jerusalem.

And he brought out the people that [were] in it, and cut [them] with saws, and with harrows of iron, and with axes. Even so dealt David with all the cities of the children of Ammon. And David and all the people returned to Jerusalem.
