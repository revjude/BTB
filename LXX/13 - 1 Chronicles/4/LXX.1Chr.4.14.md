**LXX 1 Chronicles 4:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Meonothai engendered  Ophrah. And Seraiah engendered  Joab father of Ge Charashim, for they were fabricators. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And Meonothai begat Ophrah: and Seraiah begat Joab, the father of the valley of Charashim; for they were craftsmen.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Meonothai was the father of Ophrah, and Seraiah was the father of Joab, the father of those living in Ge-harashim, which was given this name because its people were craftsmen.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
