And \[the\] word \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> came unto me, saying, You poured out blood in multitude, and you made great wars; you shall not build a house \[to\] my name, for you poured out much blood upon the earth before me.

But the word of the LORD came to me, saying, Thou hast shed blood abundantly, and hast made great wars: thou shalt not build an house unto my name, because thou hast shed much blood upon the earth in my sight.
