**LXX 1 Chronicles 22:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>

He shall build an house for my name; and he shall be my son, and I [will be] his father; and I will establish the throne of his kingdom over Israel for ever.

He is the one who will build a house for My Name. He will be My son, and I will be his Father. And I will establish the throne of his kingdom over Israel forever.’<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
