**LXX 1 Chronicles 15:29** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it came to pass, and the ark of \[the\] covenant \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> came unto \[the\] city of David, and Michal the daughter of Saul leaned over through the window, and she beheld  king David dancing and playing; and she treated him with contempt in  her soul. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it came to pass, [as] the ark of the covenant of the LORD came to the city of David, that Michal the daughter of Saul looking out at a window saw king David dancing and playing: and she despised him in her heart.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
As the ark of the covenant of the LORD was entering the City of David, Saul’s daughter Michal looked down from a window and saw King David dancing and celebrating, and she despised him in her heart.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
