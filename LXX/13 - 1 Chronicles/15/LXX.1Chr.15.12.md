**LXX 1 Chronicles 15:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he said to them, You \[are\] the rulers of the families of the Levites. Purify yourselves and  your brethren! even to bear the ark of the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  God of Israel, of which I prepared for it. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And said unto them, Ye [are] the chief of the fathers of the Levites: sanctify yourselves, [both] ye and your brethren, that ye may bring up the ark of the LORD God of Israel unto [the place that] I have prepared for it.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
