**LXX 1 Chronicles 21:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Joab said, May \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> add unto  his people as they are a hundred foldly, and the eyes  of my master the king seeing \[it\].
