**LXX 1 Chronicles 29:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And give \[to\] my son Solomon a good heart!  to observe  your commandments, and  your testimonies, and  your orders, and  to lead all unto completion, and  to build the furnishing  of your house which I prepared. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And give unto Solomon my son a perfect heart, to keep thy commandments, thy testimonies, and thy statutes, and to do all [these things], and to build the palace, [for] the which I have made provision.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And give my son Solomon a whole heart to keep and carry out all Your commandments, decrees, and statutes, and to build Your palace for which I have made provision.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
