**LXX 1 Samuel 9:27** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And as they were going down into \[the\] uttermost part of the city, that Samuel said  to Saul, Speak to the young man, and let him go through in front of us! And you stand here today, and hear \[the\] word of God!

[And] as they were going down to the end of the city, Samuel said to Saul, Bid the servant pass on before us, (and he passed on,) but stand thou still a while, that I may shew thee the word of God.
