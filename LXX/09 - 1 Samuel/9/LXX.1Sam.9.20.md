**LXX 1 Samuel 9:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>

And as for thine asses that were lost three days ago, set not thy mind on them; for they are found. And on whom [is] all the desire of Israel? [Is it] not on thee, and on all thy father’s house?

As for the donkeys you lost three days ago, do not worry about them, for they have been found. And upon whom is all the desire of Israel, if not upon you and all your father’s house?”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
