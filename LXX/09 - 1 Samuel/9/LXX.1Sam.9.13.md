**LXX 1 Samuel 9:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
As soon as you enter into the city, thus you shall find him in the city, before his ascending unto Bama  to eat; for in no way the people eat until  his entering. For this one blesses the sacrifice. And after these \[things\] the strangers eat. And now, ascend! for on account of the day you shall find him.
