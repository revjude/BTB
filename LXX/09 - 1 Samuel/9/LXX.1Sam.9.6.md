**LXX 1 Samuel 9:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the servant-lad said to him, Behold, indeed a man of God \[is\] in  this city, and the man is honorable, all what ever he should speak, coming shall be at hand. And now, we should go there, so that he should report to us  our way, upon which we should go upon it. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he said unto him, Behold now, [there is] in this city a man of God, and [he is] an honourable man; all that he saith cometh surely to pass: now let us go thither; peradventure he can shew us our way that we should go.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Look,” said the servant, “in this city there is a man of God who is highly respected; everything he says surely comes to pass. Let us go there now. Perhaps he will tell us which way to go.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
