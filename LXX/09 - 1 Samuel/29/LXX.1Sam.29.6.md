**LXX 1 Samuel 29:6**

And Achish called  David, and said to him, As \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> lives, \[know\] that you \[are\] upright and good in my eyes, and \[so is\] your exiting and  your entering with me in the camp.
