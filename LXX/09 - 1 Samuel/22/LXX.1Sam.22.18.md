**LXX 1 Samuel 22:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the king said  to Doeg, You turn, and encounter  the priests! And Doeg the Edomite turned, and put to death the priests of the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> in  that day -- eighty-five men, all bearing an ephod. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the king said to Doeg, Turn thou, and fall upon the priests. And Doeg the Edomite turned, and he fell upon the priests, and slew on that day fourscore and five persons that did wear a linen ephod.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
So the king ordered Doeg, “You turn and strike down the priests!” And Doeg the Edomite turned and struck down the priests himself. On that day he killed eighty-five men who wore the linen ephod.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
