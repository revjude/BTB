**LXX 1 Samuel 28:20**

And Saul hastened, and fell full stop upon the ground, and feared very much from the words of Samuel. And there was no strength in him any longer, for he did not eat bread all  that day, and all the night.

Then Saul fell straightway all along on the earth, and was sore afraid, because of the words of Samuel: and there was no strength in him; for he had eaten no bread all the day, nor all the night.

Immediately Saul fell flat on the ground, terrified by the words of Samuel. And his strength was gone, because he had not eaten anything all that day and night.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
