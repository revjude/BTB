**LXX 1 Samuel 28:7** 

***Saul and the Oracle in En-dor***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Saul said \[to\] his servants, Seek for me a woman who delivers oracles! and I will go to her, and I will inquire by her. And his servants said to him, Behold, a woman who delivers oracles \[is\] in En-dor.

Then said Saul unto his servants, Seek me a woman that hath a familiar spirit, that I may go to her, and inquire of her. And his servants said to him, Behold, [there is] a woman that hath a familiar spirit at Endor.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Then Saul said to his servants, “Find me a woman who is a medium, so I can go and consult her.” “There is a medium at Endor,” his servants replied.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
