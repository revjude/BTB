**LXX 1 Samuel 23:28** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Saul returned \[to\] not pursue after David, and he went to meet the Philistines. On account of this that place is called, The Portioned Rock.

Wherefore Saul returned from pursuing after David, and went against the Philistines: therefore they called that place Selahammahlekoth.

So Saul broke off his pursuit of David and went to meet the Philistines. That is why that place is called Sela-hammahlekoth.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
