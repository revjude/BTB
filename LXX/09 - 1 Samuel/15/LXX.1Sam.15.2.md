**LXX 1 Samuel 15:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Thus said \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> of hosts, Now I shall punish what Amalek did  to Israel, when he met him in the way ascending out of Egypt.

Thus saith the LORD of hosts, I remember [that] which Amalek did to Israel, how he laid [wait] for him in the way, when he came up from Egypt.
