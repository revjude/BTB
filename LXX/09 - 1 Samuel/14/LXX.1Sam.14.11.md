**LXX 1 Samuel 14:11**

And they entered both unto the support \[camp\] of the Philistines. And the Philistines said, Behold, the Hebrews come forth from out of  their burrows where they hide there.

And both of them discovered themselves unto the garrison of the Philistines: and the Philistines said, Behold, the Hebrews come forth out of the holes where they had hid themselves.
