**LXX 1 Samuel 14:21** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the servants  being yesterday and \[the\] third day \[before\] with the Philistines, the ones ascending into the camp, turned themselves also to be with Israel, of the ones with Saul and Jonathan.

Moreover the Hebrews [that] were with the Philistines before that time, which went up with them into the camp [from the country] round about, even they also [turned] to be with the Israelites that [were] with Saul and Jonathan.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And the Hebrews who had previously gone up into the surrounding camps to join the Philistines now went over to the Israelites who were with Saul and Jonathan.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
