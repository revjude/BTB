**LXX 1 Samuel 25:26** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And now, O my lord, \[as the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> lives, and your soul lives, as \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> restrained you  to not come against innocent blood, and to deliver  your hand for yourself. And now your enemies become as Nabal, and the ones seeking bad \[things\] \[against\] my lord. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Now therefore, my lord, [as] the LORD liveth, and [as] thy soul liveth, seeing the LORD hath withholden thee from coming to [shed] blood, and from avenging thyself with thine own hand, now let thine enemies, and they that seek evil to my lord, be as Nabal.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Now, my lord, as surely as the LORD lives and you yourself live, since the LORD has held you back from bloodshed and from avenging yourself with your own hand, may your enemies and those who seek harm for my lord be like Nabal.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
