**LXX 1 Samuel 2:33** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And a man whom I should not utterly destroy among you from  my altar, I will make his eyes to fail, and his life will flow down, and all \[the\] ones abounding  of your house shall fall by \[the\] broadsword of men.

And the man of thine, [whom] I shall not cut off from mine altar, [shall be] to consume thine eyes, and to grieve thine heart: and all the increase of thine house shall die in the flower of their age.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And every one of you that I do not cut off from My altar, I will cause your eyes to fail and your heart to grieve. All your descendants will die by the sword of men.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
