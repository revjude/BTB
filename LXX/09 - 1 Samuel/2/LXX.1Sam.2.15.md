**LXX 1 Samuel 2:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And before \[the\] burning the fat, the servant of the priest would come. And he said to the man  sacrificing, Give meat to the priest to roast; for in no way shall I take meat from you cooked from out of the kettle.

Also before they burnt the fat, the priest’s servant came, and said to the man that sacrificed, Give flesh to roast for the priest; for he will not have sodden flesh of thee, but raw.

Even before the fat was burned, the servant of the priest would come and say to the man who was sacrificing, “Give the priest some meat to roast, because he will not accept boiled meat from you, but only raw.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
