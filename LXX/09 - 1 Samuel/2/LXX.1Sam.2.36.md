**LXX 1 Samuel 2:36** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it will be everyone  being left behind among  your house shall come to do obeisance to him for an obolus of silver, and for one bread loaf, saying, Throw me aside upon one  of your priesthoods  to eat bread!

And it shall come to pass, [that] every one that is left in thine house shall come [and] crouch to him for a piece of silver and a morsel of bread, and shall say, Put me, I pray thee, into one of the priests’ offices, that I may eat a piece of bread.
