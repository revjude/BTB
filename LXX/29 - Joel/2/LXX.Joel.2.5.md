**LXX Joel 2:5**

As \[the\] sound of chariots upon the tops of the mountains they shall leap, even as \[the\] sound of a flame of fire devouring stubble, and as a vast and strong people being deployed for battle.

Like the noise of chariots on the tops of mountains shall they leap, like the noise of a flame of fire that devoureth the stubble, as a strong people set in battle array.
