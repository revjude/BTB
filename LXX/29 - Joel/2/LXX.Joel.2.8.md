**LXX Joel 2:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And each shall not be at a distance from his brother; they shall go being burdened with their shields, and with  arrows they shall fall, and no way shall they be ended. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Neither shall one thrust another; they shall walk every one in his path: and [when] they fall upon the sword, they shall not be wounded.

They do not jostle one another; each proceeds in his path. They burst through the defenses, never breaking ranks.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
