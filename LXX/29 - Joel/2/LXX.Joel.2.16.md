**LXX Joel 2:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Gather together people! Sanctify an assembly! Choose elders! Gather together infants nursing breasts! \[the\] groom go forth from out of  his bedroom, and \[the\] bride from out of  her nuptial chamber! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Gather the people, sanctify the congregation, assemble the elders, gather the children, and those that suck the breasts: let the bridegroom go forth of his chamber, and the bride out of her closet.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Gather the people, sanctify the congregation, assemble the aged, gather the children, even those nursing at the breast. Let the bridegroom leave his room, and the bride her chamber.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
