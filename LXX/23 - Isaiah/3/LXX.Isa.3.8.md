**LXX Isaiah 3:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>

For Jerusalem is ruined, and Judah is fallen: because their tongue and their doings [are] against the LORD, to provoke the eyes of his glory.

For Jerusalem has stumbled and Judah has fallen because they spoke and acted against the LORD, defying His glorious presence.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
