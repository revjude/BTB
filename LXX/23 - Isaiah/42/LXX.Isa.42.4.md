**LXX Isaiah 42:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
He shall illuminate, and shall not be devastated until whenever he should set judgment upon the earth. And upon  his name nations shall hope.

He will not grow weak or discouraged before He has established justice on the earth. In His law the islands will put their hope.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
