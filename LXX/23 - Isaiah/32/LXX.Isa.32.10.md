**LXX Isaiah 32:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Make mention for \[the\] days of a year in grief, \[yet\] with hope! The gathering of crops has been consumed, the sowing has ceased, and no longer should it come.

Many days and years shall ye be troubled, ye careless women: for the vintage shall fail, the gathering shall not come.

In a little more than a year you will tremble, O secure ones. For the grape harvest will fail and the fruit harvest will not arrive.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
