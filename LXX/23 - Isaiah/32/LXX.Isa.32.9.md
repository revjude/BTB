**LXX Isaiah 32:9** 

***Works of Righteousness Shall Be Peace***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Rise up rich women, and hear  my voice! Listen to my words O daughters in hope! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Rise up, ye women that are at ease; hear my voice, ye careless daughters; give ear unto my speech.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Stand up, you complacent women; listen to me. Give ear to my word, you overconfident daughters.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
