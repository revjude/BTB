**LXX Isaiah 23:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it will be in  that day, Tyre shall be forsaken \[for\] seventy years, as \[the\] time of one king, as \[the\] time of a man. And after seventy years Tyre will be as \[the\] song of a harlot. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it shall come to pass in that day, that Tyre shall be forgotten seventy years, according to the days of one king: after the end of seventy years shall Tyre sing as an harlot.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
At that time Tyre will be forgotten for seventy years—the span of a king’s life. But at the end of seventy years, it will happen to Tyre as in the song of the harlot:<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
