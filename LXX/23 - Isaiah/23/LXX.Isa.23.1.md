**LXX Isaiah 23:1** 

***The Word Concerning Tyre***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The matter of Tyre. Shriek O boats of Carthage! For she perished, and no longer do they come from out of \[the\] land of Chittim; she is led captive. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The burden of Tyre. Howl, ye ships of Tarshish; for it is laid waste, so that there is no house, no entering in: from the land of Chittim it is revealed to them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
This is the burden against Tyre: Wail, O ships of Tarshish, for Tyre is laid waste, without house or harbor. Word has reached them from the land of Cyprus.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
