**LXX Isaiah 23:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
To whom become the ones dwelling in the island likened; traders of Phoenicia passing through the sea; 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Be still, ye inhabitants of the isle; thou whom the merchants of Zidon, that pass over the sea, have replenished.

Be silent, O dwellers of the coastland, you merchants of Sidon, whose traders have crossed the sea.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
