**LXX Isaiah 15:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For the water  of Dimon shall be filled with blood. For I shall bring upon Dimon \[the\] Arabians, and I will lift away the seed of Moab, and Ariel, and the remnant of Adama. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For the waters of Dimon shall be full of blood: for I will bring more upon Dimon, lions upon him that escapeth of Moab, and upon the remnant of the land.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The waters of Dimon are full of blood, but I will bring more upon Dimon—a lion upon the fugitives of Moab and upon the remnant of the land.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
