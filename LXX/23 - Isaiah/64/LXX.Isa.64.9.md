**LXX Isaiah 64:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You should not be provoked to anger with us very much, and our sins should not be remembered for \[a long\] time. And now, look upon \[us\], O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, for we \[are\] all your people! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Be not wroth very sore, O LORD, neither remember iniquity for ever: behold, see, we beseech thee, we [are] all thy people.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Do not be angry, O LORD, beyond measure; do not remember our iniquity forever. Oh, look upon us, we pray; we are all Your people!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
