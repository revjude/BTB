**LXX Isaiah 27:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
There is not her who did not take hold of her; therefore because of this \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> did all as much as he ordered. I am incinerated. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Fury [is] not in me: who would set the briers [and] thorns against me in battle? I would go through them, I would burn them together.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I am not angry. If only thorns and briers confronted Me, I would march and trample them, I would burn them to the ground.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
