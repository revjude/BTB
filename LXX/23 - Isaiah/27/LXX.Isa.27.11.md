**LXX Isaiah 27:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And after much time there will not be in it any green \[thing\] on account of  drying out. O women coming from a spectacle, come. for it is a people having no understanding. On account of this in no way the one making them pity, nor the one shaping them in any way show mercy. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
When the boughs thereof are withered, they shall be broken off: the women come, [and] set them on fire: for it [is] a people of no understanding: therefore he that made them will not have mercy on them, and he that formed them will shew them no favour.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
When its limbs are dry, they are broken off. Women come and use them for kindling; for this is a people without understanding. Therefore their Maker has no compassion on them, and their Creator shows them no favor.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
