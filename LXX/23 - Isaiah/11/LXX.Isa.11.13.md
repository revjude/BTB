**LXX Isaiah 11:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the jealousy of Ephraim shall be removed, and the enemies of Judah shall be destroyed. Ephraim shall not be jealous of Judah, and Judah will not afflict Ephraim.

The envy also of Ephraim shall depart, and the adversaries of Judah shall be cut off: Ephraim shall not envy Judah, and Judah shall not vex Ephraim.
