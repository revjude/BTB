**LXX Isaiah 21:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Because of this my loin was filled with feebleness, and pangs took me as the one giving birth. I transgressed  to not hear, I hurried  to not see. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Therefore are my loins filled with pain: pangs have taken hold upon me, as the pangs of a woman that travaileth: I was bowed down at the hearing [of it]; I was dismayed at the seeing [of it].
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Therefore my body is filled with anguish. Pain grips me, like the pains of a woman in labor. I am bewildered to hear, I am dismayed to see.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
