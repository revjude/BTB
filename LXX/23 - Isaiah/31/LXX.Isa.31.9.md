**LXX Isaiah 31:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>

And he shall pass over to his strong hold for fear, and his princes shall be afraid of the ensign, saith the LORD, whose fire [is] in Zion, and his furnace in Jerusalem.
