**LXX Isaiah 31:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
As birds flying, so \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> of Hosts shield above Jerusalem; and he shall rescue and shall protect and shall deliver.

As birds flying, so will the LORD of hosts defend Jerusalem; defending also he will deliver [it; and] passing over he will preserve [it].
