**LXX Isaiah 17:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And in the day whenever you should plant, you shall be misled;  but if you should sow \[by\] morning it shall bloom for a harvest in whatever day of inheritance; and as a father of a man, you shall choose by lot  for your sons. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
In the day shalt thou make thy plant to grow, and in the morning shalt thou make thy seed to flourish: [but] the harvest [shall be] a heap in the day of grief and of desperate sorrow.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
though on the day you plant you make them grow, and on that morning you help your seed sprout—yet the harvest will vanish on the day of disease and incurable pain.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
