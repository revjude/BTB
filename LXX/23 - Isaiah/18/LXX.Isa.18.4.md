**LXX Isaiah 18:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Because thus said \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> to me; Safety will be in  my city, as \[the\] light of sweltering heat at midday, and it will be as a cloud of dew \[in the\] day of harvest. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For so the LORD said unto me, I will take my rest, and I will consider in my dwelling place like a clear heat upon herbs, [and] like a cloud of dew in the heat of harvest.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For this is what the LORD has told me: “I will quietly look on from My dwelling place, like shimmering heat in the sunshine, like a cloud of dew in the heat of harvest.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
