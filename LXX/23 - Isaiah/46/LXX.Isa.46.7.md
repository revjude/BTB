**LXX Isaiah 46:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
They lift it upon the shoulder, and they go. And if they put it upon  its place, it remains. In no way shall it move, and who ever should yell to it, in no way shall it listen; in no way shall it deliver him from hurt. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
They bear him upon the shoulder, they carry him, and set him in his place, and he standeth; from his place shall he not remove: yea, [one] shall cry unto him, yet can he not answer, nor save him out of his trouble.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
They lift it to their shoulder and carry it along; they set it in its place, and there it stands, not budging from that spot. They cry out to it, but it does not answer; it saves no one from his troubles.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
