**LXX Isaiah 46:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I brought near  my righteousness, in no way \[is it\] at a distance, and the deliverance the one by me, I will not be slow about; I have appointed in Zion deliverance to Israel for glory. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I bring near my righteousness; it shall not be far off, and my salvation shall not tarry: and I will place salvation in Zion for Israel my glory.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I am bringing My righteousness near; it is not far away, and My salvation will not be delayed. I will grant salvation to Zion and adorn Israel with My splendor.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
