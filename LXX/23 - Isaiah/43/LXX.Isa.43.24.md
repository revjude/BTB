**LXX Isaiah 43:24** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Nor you acquired for me a sacrifice of silver, nor the fat  of your sacrifices I desired. But in  your sins you stood in front of me, and in  your iniquities. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thou hast bought me no sweet cane with money, neither hast thou filled me with the fat of thy sacrifices: but thou hast made me to serve with thy sins, thou hast wearied me with thine iniquities.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You have not bought Me sweet cane with your silver, nor satisfied Me with the fat of your sacrifices. But you have burdened Me with your sins; you have wearied Me with your iniquities.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
