**LXX Isaiah 14:12** 

***The Fallen Morning Star***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O how the morning star fell from out of the heaven -- the one rising \[by\] morning; the one earth sending to all the nations was broken unto the. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
How art thou fallen from heaven, O Lucifer, son of the morning! [how] art thou cut down to the ground, which didst weaken the nations!
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
How you have fallen from heaven, O day star, son of the dawn! You have been cut down to the ground, O destroyer of nations.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
