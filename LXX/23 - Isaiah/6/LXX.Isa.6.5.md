**LXX Isaiah 6:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I said, O, I \[am\] miserable, for I am vexed, for being a man and having unclean lips in \[the\] midst of a people having unclean lips I live in. And the king, \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> of Hosts, I beheld with my eyes. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then said I, Woe [is] me! for I am undone; because I [am] a man of unclean lips, and I dwell in the midst of a people of unclean lips: for mine eyes have seen the King, the LORD of hosts.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Then I said: “Woe is me, for I am ruined, because I am a man of unclean lips dwelling among a people of unclean lips; for my eyes have seen the King, the LORD of Hosts.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
