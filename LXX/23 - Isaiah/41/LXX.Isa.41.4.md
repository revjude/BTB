**LXX Isaiah 41:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Who exerted energy and did these \[things\]? He called her -- the one calling her from \[the\] beginning of generations; I  God, \[the\] first and into the coming, I am. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Who hath wrought and done [it], calling the generations from the beginning? I the LORD, the first, and with the last; I [am] he.

Who has performed this and carried it out, calling forth the generations from the beginning? I, the LORD—the first and the last—I am He.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
