**LXX Isaiah 41:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The man was strong -- \[the\] fabricator, and \[the\] brazier beating with a hammer forging together; how long then shall he say, \[The\] coupling is good, they strengthened them with nails; they shall establish them, and it shall not be moved. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
So the carpenter encouraged the goldsmith, [and] he that smootheth [with] the hammer him that smote the anvil, saying, It [is] ready for the sodering: and he fastened it with nails, [that] it should not be moved.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The craftsman encourages the goldsmith, and he who wields the hammer cheers him who strikes the anvil, saying of the welding, “It is good.” He nails it down so it will not be toppled.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
