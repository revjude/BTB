**LXX Isaiah 41:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Behold, I made you as wheels of a wagon threshing -- new, toothed; and you shall thresh \[the\] mountains, and shall thin out \[the\] hills, and make \[them\] as dust. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Behold, I will make thee a new sharp threshing instrument having teeth: thou shalt thresh the mountains, and beat [them] small, and shalt make the hills as chaff.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Behold, I will make you into a threshing sledge, new and sharp, with many teeth. You will thresh the mountains and crush them, and reduce the hills to chaff.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
