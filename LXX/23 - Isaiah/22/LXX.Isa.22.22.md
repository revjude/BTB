**LXX Isaiah 22:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I will put to him the key of \[the\] house of David upon  his shoulder. And he shall open, and there will not be  one locking. And he shall lock, and there shall not be  one opening. And I will give the glory of David to him. And he will rule, and there will not be  one disputing. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the key of the house of David will I lay upon his shoulder; so he shall open, and none shall shut; and he shall shut, and none shall open.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I will place on his shoulder the key to the house of David. What he opens no one can shut, and what he shuts no one can open.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
