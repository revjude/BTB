**LXX Isaiah 60:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Lift your eyes round about, and behold all your children gathering together! All your sons come \[from\] far off, and  your daughters shall be carried upon shoulders. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Lift up thine eyes round about, and see: all they gather themselves together, they come to thee: thy sons shall come from far, and thy daughters shall be nursed at [thy] side.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Lift up your eyes and look around: They all gather and come to you; your sons will come from afar, and your daughters will be carried on the arm.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
