**LXX Isaiah 60:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Herds of camels, and camels of Midian and Ephah shall cover you; come all from out of Sheba bringing gold and frankincense; and they shall announce as good news the deliverance \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The multitude of camels shall cover thee, the dromedaries of Midian and Ephah; all they from Sheba shall come: they shall bring gold and incense; and they shall shew forth the praises of the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Caravans of camels will cover your land, young camels of Midian and Ephah, and all from Sheba will come, bearing gold and frankincense and proclaiming the praises of the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
