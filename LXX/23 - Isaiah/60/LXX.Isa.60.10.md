**LXX Isaiah 60:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And foreigners shall build  your walls, and  their kings shall stand beside you. For on account of my anger I struck you, and on account of mercy I loved you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the sons of strangers shall build up thy walls, and their kings shall minister unto thee: for in my wrath I smote thee, but in my favour have I had mercy on thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Foreigners will rebuild your walls, and their kings will serve you. Although I struck you in anger, yet in favor I will show you mercy.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
