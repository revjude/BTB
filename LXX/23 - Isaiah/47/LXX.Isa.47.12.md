**LXX Isaiah 47:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Stand now! with  your enchantments, and in the abundance of your sorcery! which you learned from your youth -- if you shall be able to derive benefit. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Stand now with thine enchantments, and with the multitude of thy sorceries, wherein thou hast laboured from thy youth; if so be thou shalt be able to profit, if so be thou mayest prevail.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
So take your stand with your spells and with your many sorceries, with which you have wearied yourself from your youth. Perhaps you will succeed; perhaps you will inspire terror!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
