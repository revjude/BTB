**LXX Isaiah 47:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
these shall be your help. You tired in the revolt from  your youth. A man wanders by himself, but to you there will be no deliverance. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thus shall they be unto thee with whom thou hast laboured, [even] thy merchants, from thy youth: they shall wander every one to his quarter; none shall save thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
This is what they are to you—those with whom you have labored and traded from youth—each one strays in his own direction; not one of them can save you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
