**LXX Isaiah 40:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Ascend upon \[the\] high mountain! O one announcing good news of Zion. Raise up high in the strength  of your voice! O one announcing good news of Jerusalem. Raise it up high! Do not fear! Say to the cities of Judah! Behold,  your God. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
O Zion, that bringest good tidings, get thee up into the high mountain; O Jerusalem, that bringest good tidings, lift up thy voice with strength; lift [it] up, be not afraid; say unto the cities of Judah, Behold your God!
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Go up on a high mountain, O Zion, herald of good news. Raise your voice loudly, O Jerusalem, herald of good news. Lift it up, do not be afraid! Say to the cities of Judah, “Here is your God!”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
