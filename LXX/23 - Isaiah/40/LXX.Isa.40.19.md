**LXX Isaiah 40:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[Has\] not \[the\] fabricator made an image? or \[the\] goldsmith having cast gold, gilt it over, and carefully prepared it for a representation? 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The workman melteth a graven image, and the goldsmith spreadeth it over with gold, and casteth silver chains.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
To an idol that a craftsman casts and a metalworker overlays with gold and fits with silver chains?<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
