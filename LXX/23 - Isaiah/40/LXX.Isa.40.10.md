**LXX Isaiah 40:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Behold, \[the\] Lord. \[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> comes with strength, even the arm with dominion. Behold,  his wage \[is\] with him, and the work before him.

Behold, the Lord GOD will come with strong [hand], and his arm shall rule for him: behold, his reward [is] with him, and his work before him.

Behold, the Lord GOD comes with might, and His arm establishes His rule. His reward is with Him, and His recompense accompanies Him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
