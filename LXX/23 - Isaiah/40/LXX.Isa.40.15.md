**LXX Isaiah 40:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Since all the nations were considered as a drop from a pail and as \[the\] crux of a yoke \[balance scale\]; and they are considered as saliva. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Behold, the nations [are] as a drop of a bucket, and are counted as the small dust of the balance: behold, he taketh up the isles as a very little thing.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Surely the nations are like a drop in a bucket; they are considered a speck of dust on the scales; He lifts up the islands like fine dust.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
