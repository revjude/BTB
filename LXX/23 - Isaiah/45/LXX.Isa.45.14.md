**LXX Isaiah 45:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Thus says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> of Hosts, Egypt tired \[for you\], and \[the\] trade of \[the\] Ethiopians and the Sabeans, haughty men shall pass over you, and they will be your servants; and they shall follow after you being tied in manacles, and they shall pass over to you, and shall do obeisance to you, and they shall pray to you, for God is among you, and there is no God besides you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thus saith the LORD, The labour of Egypt, and merchandise of Ethiopia and of the Sabeans, men of stature, shall come over unto thee, and they shall be thine: they shall come after thee; in chains they shall come over, and they shall fall down unto thee, they shall make supplication unto thee, [saying], Surely God [is] in thee; and [there is] none else, [there is] no God.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
This is what the LORD says: “The products of Egypt and the merchandise of Cush, along with the Sabeans, men of stature, will come over to you and will be yours; they will trudge behind you; they will come over in chains and bow down to you. They will confess to you: ‘God is indeed with you, and there is no other; there is no other God.’”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
