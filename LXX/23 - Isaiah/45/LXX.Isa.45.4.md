**LXX Isaiah 45:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Because of  my servant Jacob, and Israel  my chosen, I shall call you \[by\] your name, and favorably receive you; though you did not know me. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For Jacob my servant’s sake, and Israel mine elect, I have even called thee by thy name: I have surnamed thee, though thou hast not known me.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the sake of Jacob My servant and Israel My chosen one, I call you by name; I have given you a title of honor, though you have not known Me.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
