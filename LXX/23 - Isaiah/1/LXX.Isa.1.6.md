**LXX Isaiah 1:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
From feet unto head there is no wholeness in it. Neither wound, nor stripe, nor inflamed wound \[are healed\]; there is no dressing to place upon \[it\], nor oil, nor bandages. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
From the sole of the foot even unto the head [there is] no soundness in it; [but] wounds, and bruises, and putrifying sores: they have not been closed, neither bound up, neither mollified with ointment.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
From the sole of your foot to the top of your head, there is no soundness—only wounds and welts and festering sores neither cleansed nor bandaged nor soothed with oil.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
