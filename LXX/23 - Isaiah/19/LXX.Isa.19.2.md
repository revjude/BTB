**LXX Isaiah 19:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Egyptians shall be roused against Egyptians, and \[every\] man shall wage war against  his brother, and \[every\] man \[against\] his neighbor; city against city, and abode against abode.

And I will set the Egyptians against the Egyptians: and they shall fight every one against his brother, and every one against his neighbour; city against city, [and] kingdom against kingdom.

“So I will incite Egyptian against Egyptian; brother will fight against brother, neighbor against neighbor, city against city, and kingdom against kingdom.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
