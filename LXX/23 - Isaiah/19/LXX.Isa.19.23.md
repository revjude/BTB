**LXX Isaiah 19:23** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[In\] that day there will be a way of Egypt to \[the\] Assyrians; and \[the\] Assyrians shall enter into Egypt, and Egypt will go to \[the\] Assyrians, and the Egyptians shall serve to the Assyrians. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
In that day shall there be a highway out of Egypt to Assyria, and the Assyrian shall come into Egypt, and the Egyptian into Assyria, and the Egyptians shall serve with the Assyrians.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
In that day there will be a highway from Egypt to Assyria. The Assyrians will go to Egypt, and the Egyptians to Assyria. The Egyptians and Assyrians will worship together.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
