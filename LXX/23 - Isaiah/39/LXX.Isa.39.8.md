And Hezekiah said to Isaiah, the word \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> \[is\] good which you spoke; let there be indeed peace and righteousness in  my days!

Then said Hezekiah to Isaiah, Good [is] the word of the LORD which thou hast spoken. He said moreover, For there shall be peace and truth in my days.
