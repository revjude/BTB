**LXX Isaiah 66:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The ones purifying themselves and cleansing themselves in the gardens, and \[the ones\] eating in the thresholds \[the\] meat of a pig and the abominations and the mouse, together shall be consumed, said \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
They that sanctify themselves, and purify themselves in the gardens behind one [tree] in the midst, eating swine’s flesh, and the abomination, and the mouse, shall be consumed together, saith the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Those who consecrate and purify themselves to enter the groves—to follow one in the center of those who eat the flesh of swine and vermin and rats—will perish together,” declares the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
