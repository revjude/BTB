**LXX Isaiah 62:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And no longer shall you be called -- One Being Forsaken. And  your land shall not be called -- Desolation. For you shall be called -- My will; and  your land -- Being Lived In. For \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> took pleasure in you, and  your land shall be lived in together. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thou shalt no more be termed Forsaken; neither shall thy land any more be termed Desolate: but thou shalt be called Hephzibah, and thy land Beulah: for the LORD delighteth in thee, and thy land shall be married.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
No longer will you be called Forsaken, nor your land named Desolate; but you will be called Hephzibah, and your land Beulah; for the LORD will take delight in you, and your land will be His bride.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
