**LXX Isaiah 48:1** 

***Israel Is Hardened***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Hear these \[things\], O house of Jacob! the ones being called by the name Israel, and coming forth from waters of Judah, the ones swearing by an oath to the name of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> God of Israel, ones remembering not with truth, nor with righteousness, 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Hear ye this, O house of Jacob, which are called by the name of Israel, and are come forth out of the waters of Judah, which swear by the name of the LORD, and make mention of the God of Israel, [but] not in truth, nor in righteousness.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Listen to this, O house of Jacob, you who are called by the name of Israel, who have descended from the line of Judah, who swear by the name of the LORD, who invoke the God of Israel—but not in truth or righteousness—<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
