**LXX Isaiah 10:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
the mountains shall be consumed, and the hills, and the forests; and it shall devour from soul unto flesh. And it will be the one fleeing \[will be\] as the one fleeing from a burning flame. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And shall consume the glory of his forest, and of his fruitful field, both soul and body: and they shall be as when a standardbearer fainteth.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The splendor of its forests and orchards, both soul and body, it will completely destroy, as a sickness consumes a man.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
