**LXX Isaiah 10:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But he did not ponder thus, and the soul did not consider thus; but he shall dismiss  his mind even  to utterly destroy nations -- not a few. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Howbeit he meaneth not so, neither doth his heart think so; but [it is] in his heart to destroy and cut off nations not a few.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But this is not his intention; this is not his plan. For it is in his heart to destroy and cut off many nations.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
