**LXX Isaiah 44:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Know that their heart \[is\] ashes! and they err, and no one is able to rescue  his soul. Behold! you shall not say that, \[Is there\] a lie in  my right hand? 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He feedeth on ashes: a deceived heart hath turned him aside, that he cannot deliver his soul, nor say, [Is there] not a lie in my right hand?
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He feeds on ashes. His deluded heart has led him astray, and he cannot deliver himself or say, “Is not this thing in my right hand a lie?”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
