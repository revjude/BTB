**LXX Isaiah 61:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Thus of a second \[time\] they shall inherit the land, and everlasting gladness \[is\] above their head. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For your shame [ye shall have] double; and [for] confusion they shall rejoice in their portion: therefore in their land they shall possess the double: everlasting joy shall be unto them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Instead of shame, My people will have a double portion, and instead of humiliation, they will rejoice in their share; and so they will inherit a double portion in their land, and everlasting joy will be theirs.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
