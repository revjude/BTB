**LXX Isaiah 61:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they shall build up everlasting wildernesses being made quite desolate prior. They shall rise up and shall revive cities of wildernesses having been made quite desolate for generations. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And they shall build the old wastes, they shall raise up the former desolations, and they shall repair the waste cities, the desolations of many generations.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
They will rebuild the ancient ruins; they will restore the places long devastated; they will renew the ruined cities, the desolations of many generations.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
