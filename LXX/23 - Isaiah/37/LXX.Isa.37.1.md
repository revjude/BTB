**LXX Isaiah 37:1** 

***Hezekiah Inquires of The LORD***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it came to pass in  king Hezekiah hearing, \[that\] he split  his garments, and put on sackcloth, and ascended unto the house of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it came to pass, when king Hezekiah heard [it], that he rent his clothes, and covered himself with sackcloth, and went into the house of the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
On hearing this report, King Hezekiah tore his clothes, put on sackcloth, and entered the house of the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
