**LXX Isaiah 26:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, your arm \[is\] high, but they did not know; but in knowing they shall be ashamed; zeal shall take hold of an uninstructed people; and now fire shall devour the adversaries. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
LORD, [when] thy hand is lifted up, they will not see: [but] they shall see, and be ashamed for [their] envy at the people; yea, the fire of thine enemies shall devour them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
O LORD, Your hand is upraised, but they do not see it. They will see Your zeal for Your people and be put to shame. The fire set for Your enemies will consume them!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
