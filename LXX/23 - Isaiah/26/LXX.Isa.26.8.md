**LXX Isaiah 26:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For the way of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> \[is\] equitable; we hoped upon  your name, and upon the remembrance \[of you\]

Yea, in the way of thy judgments, O LORD, have we waited for thee; the desire of [our] soul [is] to thy name, and to the remembrance of thee.

Yes, we wait for You, O LORD; we walk in the path of Your judgments. Your name and renown are the desire of our souls.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
