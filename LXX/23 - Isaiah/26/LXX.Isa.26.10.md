**LXX Isaiah 26:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For the impious ceases; in no way should he learn righteousness upon the earth; no way should he execute truth. Lift away the impious! that he should not behold the glory of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Let favour be shewed to the wicked, [yet] will he not learn righteousness: in the land of uprightness will he deal unjustly, and will not behold the majesty of the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Though grace is shown to the wicked man, he does not learn righteousness. In the land of righteousness he acts unjustly and fails to see the majesty of the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
