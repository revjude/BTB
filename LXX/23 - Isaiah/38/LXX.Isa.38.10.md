**LXX Isaiah 38:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I said in the height  of my days, I shall go to \[the\] gates of Hades; I shall forsake the years  remaining. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I said in the cutting off of my days, I shall go to the gates of the grave: I am deprived of the residue of my years.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I said, “In the prime of my life I must go through the gates of Sheol and be deprived of the remainder of my years.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
