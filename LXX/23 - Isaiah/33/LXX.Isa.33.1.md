**LXX Isaiah 33:1** 

***After Affliction Comes Deliverance***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Woe to the ones causing misery, but no one makes you miserable; and the one disrespecting you, does not disrespect; the ones disrespecting shall be captured, and they shall be delivered up; and as a moth upon a cloak, so they shall be vanquished. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Woe to thee that spoilest, and thou [wast] not spoiled; and dealest treacherously, and they dealt not treacherously with thee! when thou shalt cease to spoil, thou shalt be spoiled; [and] when thou shalt make an end to deal treacherously, they shall deal treacherously with thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Woe to you, O destroyer never destroyed, O traitor never betrayed! When you have finished destroying, you will be destroyed. When you have finished betraying, you will be betrayed.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
