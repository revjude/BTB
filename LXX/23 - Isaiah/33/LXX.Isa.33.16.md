**LXX Isaiah 33:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
This one shall live in a high cave of strong rocks; bread shall be given to him, and  his water \[is\] trustworthy. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He shall dwell on high: his place of defence [shall be] the munitions of rocks: bread shall be given him; his waters [shall be] sure.

he will dwell on the heights; the mountain fortress will be his refuge; his food will be provided and his water assured.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
