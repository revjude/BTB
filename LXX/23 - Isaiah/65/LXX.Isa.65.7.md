**LXX Isaiah 65:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
 their sins, and the ones of their fathers, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, the ones \[that\] burnt incense upon the mountains, and berated me upon the hills. I will recompense  their works into  their bosom. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Your iniquities, and the iniquities of your fathers together, saith the LORD, which have burned incense upon the mountains, and blasphemed me upon the hills: therefore will I measure their former work into their bosom.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
both for your iniquities and for those of your fathers,” says the LORD. “Because they burned incense on the mountains and scorned Me on the hills, I will measure into their laps full payment for their former deeds.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
