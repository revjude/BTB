**LXX Isaiah 5:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Therefore my people became captive because of  their not knowing the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. And a multitude became dead because of hunger and thirst for water.

Therefore my people are gone into captivity, because [they have] no knowledge: and their honourable men [are] famished, and their multitude dried up with thirst.
