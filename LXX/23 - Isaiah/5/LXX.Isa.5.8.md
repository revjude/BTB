**LXX Isaiah 5:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Woe to the ones joining together house to house, and nearing field to field, that  they should remove anything of their neighbor; will you not live alone upon the land? 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Woe unto them that join house to house, [that] lay field to field, till [there be] no place, that they may be placed alone in the midst of the earth!
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Woe to you who add house to house and join field to field until no place is left and you live alone in the land.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
