**LXX Isaiah 5:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For where ten teams of oxen work \[the land will\] produce one clay vessel; and the one sowing six \[large\] measures shall produce three measures. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Yea, ten acres of vineyard shall yield one bath, and the seed of an homer shall yield an ephah.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For ten acres of vineyard will yield but a bath of wine, and a homer of seed only an ephah of grain.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
