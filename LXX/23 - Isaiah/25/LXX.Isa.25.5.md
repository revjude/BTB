**LXX Isaiah 25:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
as faint-hearted men thirsting in Zion, for you shall rescue them from impious men, to whom you delivered us. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thou shalt bring down the noise of strangers, as the heat in a dry place; [even] the heat with the shadow of a cloud: the branch of the terrible ones shall be brought low.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
like heat in a dry land. You subdue the uproar of foreigners. As the shade of a cloud cools the heat, so the song of the ruthless is silenced.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
