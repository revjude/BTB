**LXX Isaiah 63:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I looked, and there was no helper. And I paid attention, and no one assisted. And my arm rescued them, and  my rage attended to it. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And I looked, and [there was] none to help; and I wondered that [there was] none to uphold: therefore mine own arm brought salvation unto me; and my fury, it upheld me.

I looked, but there was no one to help; I was appalled that no one assisted. So My arm brought Me salvation, and My own wrath upheld Me.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
