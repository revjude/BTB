**LXX Isaiah 63:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I remembered the mercy \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; the virtues of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> in all which he recompenses to us. \[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> -- a good judge to the house of Israel; he brings upon us according to  his mercy, and according to the magnitude  of his righteousness. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I will mention the lovingkindnesses of the LORD, [and] the praises of the LORD, according to all that the LORD hath bestowed on us, and the great goodness toward the house of Israel, which he hath bestowed on them according to his mercies, and according to the multitude of his lovingkindnesses.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I will make known the LORD’s loving devotion and His praiseworthy acts, because of all that the LORD has done for us—the many good things for the house of Israel according to His great compassion and loving devotion.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
