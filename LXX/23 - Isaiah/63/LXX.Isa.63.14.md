**LXX Isaiah 63:14**

And as cattle \[led\] through a plain, spirit of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> descended, and guided them. Thus you led  your people to make for yourself a glorious name.

As a beast goeth down into the valley, the Spirit of the LORD caused him to rest: so didst thou lead thy people, to make thyself a glorious name.
