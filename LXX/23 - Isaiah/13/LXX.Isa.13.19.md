And Babylon will be (\[the place\] which is called honorable by \[the\] king of \[the\] Chaldeans) in which manner God eradicated Sodom and Gomorrah.

And Babylon, the glory of kingdoms, the beauty of the Chaldees’ excellency, shall be as when God overthrew Sodom and Gomorrah.
