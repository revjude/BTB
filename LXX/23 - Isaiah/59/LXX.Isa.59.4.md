**LXX Isaiah 59:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
No one speaks just \[things\], nor is there true equity. They rely upon vanities, and they speak empty \[things\]; for they sire misery and give birth to lawlessness. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
None calleth for justice, nor [any] pleadeth for truth: they trust in vanity, and speak lies; they conceive mischief, and bring forth iniquity.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
No one calls for justice; no one pleads his case honestly. They rely on empty pleas; they tell lies; they conceive mischief and give birth to iniquity.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
