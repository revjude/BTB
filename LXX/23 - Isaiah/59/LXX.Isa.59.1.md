**LXX Isaiah 59:1** 

***The Sin of a Nation***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[Is\] not the hand \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> strong  to deliver? or his ear weighed down to not listen?
