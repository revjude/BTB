**LXX Isaiah 52:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For come forth not with disturbance, nor shall you go forth in flight into exile. For \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> shall go before prior of you; and the one assembling you \[is\] the God of Israel. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For ye shall not go out with haste, nor go by flight: for the LORD will go before you; and the God of Israel [will be] your rereward.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For you will not leave in a hurry nor flee in haste, for the LORD goes before you, and the God of Israel is your rear guard.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
