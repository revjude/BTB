**LXX Isaiah 9:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Because the yoke, being situated upon them was removed, even the rod, the one upon  their neck; for the rod of the extractors he effaced as in the day  against Midian.

For thou hast broken the yoke of his burden, and the staff of his shoulder, the rod of his oppressor, as in the day of Midian.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For as in the day of Midian You have shattered the yoke of their burden, the bar across their shoulders, and the rod of their oppressor.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
