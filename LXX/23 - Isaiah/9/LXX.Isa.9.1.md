**LXX Isaiah 9:1** 

***Unto Us a Child Is Born***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Drink this first quickly! Quickly act! O place of Zebulun, the land of Naphtali, and the rest on the coast, and on the other side of the Jordan, Galilee of the nations! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Nevertheless the dimness [shall] not [be] such as [was] in her vexation, when at the first he lightly afflicted the land of Zebulun and the land of Naphtali, and afterward did more grievously afflict [her by] the way of the sea, beyond Jordan, in Galilee of the nations.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Nevertheless, there will be no more gloom for those in distress. In the past He humbled the land of Zebulun and the land of Naphtali, but in the future He will honor the Way of the Sea, beyond the Jordan, Galilee of the nations:<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
