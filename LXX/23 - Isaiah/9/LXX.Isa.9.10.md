**LXX Isaiah 9:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] bricks are fallen, but come, we should dress stones. And we should fell sycamine trees and cedars, and we should build for ourselves a tower. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The bricks are fallen down, but we will build with hewn stones: the sycomores are cut down, but we will change [them into] cedars.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“The bricks have fallen, but we will rebuild with finished stone; the sycamores have been felled, but we will replace them with cedars.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
