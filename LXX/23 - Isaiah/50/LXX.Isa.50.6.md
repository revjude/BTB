**LXX Isaiah 50:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
 My back I have given to whips,  and my jaws for slaps,  and my face I did not turn from \[the\] shame of ones spitting. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I gave my back to the smiters, and my cheeks to them that plucked off the hair: I hid not my face from shame and spitting.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I offered My back to those who struck Me, and My cheeks to those who tore out My beard. I did not hide My face from scorn and spittle.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
