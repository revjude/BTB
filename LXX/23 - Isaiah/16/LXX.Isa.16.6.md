**LXX Isaiah 16:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
We heard the insult of Moab -- insulting exceedingly; the pride and  his insult and  his vehement anger; not so your divination. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
We have heard of the pride of Moab; [he is] very proud: [even] of his haughtiness, and his pride, and his wrath: [but] his lies [shall] not [be] so.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
We have heard of Moab’s pomposity, his exceeding pride and conceit, his overflowing arrogance. But his boasting is empty.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
