**LXX Isaiah 29:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And this scroll will be given into \[the\] hands of a man not having knowledge of letters. And \[one\] shall say to him, Read this! And he shall say, I do not have knowledge of letters. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the book is delivered to him that is not learned, saying, Read this, I pray thee: and he saith, I am not learned.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Or if the scroll is handed to one unable to read, he will say, “I cannot read.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
