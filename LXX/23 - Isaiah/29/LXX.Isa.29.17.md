**LXX Isaiah 29:17** 

***The Deaf-mutes Shall Hear***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
No longer a little \[while\] and Lebanon shall be transposed as the mountain  of fruitful field; and the mountain of fruitful field shall be considered as a forest. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[Is] it not yet a very little while, and Lebanon shall be turned into a fruitful field, and the fruitful field shall be esteemed as a forest?

In a very short time, will not Lebanon become an orchard, and the orchard seem like a forest?<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
