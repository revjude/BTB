**LXX Isaiah 55:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
What do you value for money not in bread \[loaves\], and  your effort not unto fullness? Hearken to me! and you shall eat good \[things\], and shall revel in good \[things\] \[to\] your soul. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Wherefore do ye spend money for [that which is] not bread? and your labour for [that which] satisfieth not? hearken diligently unto me, and eat ye [that which is] good, and let your soul delight itself in fatness.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Why spend money on that which is not bread, and your labor on that which does not satisfy? Listen carefully to Me, and eat what is good, and your soul will delight in the richest of foods.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
