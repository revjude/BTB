**LXX Isaiah 58:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
If you should turn  your foot from the Sabbaths,  \[so as\] to not do  your wants on the holy day, and shall call the Sabbaths delightful, holy \[to\] your God; \[if\] you shall not lift  your foot to work, nor shall you speak a word in anger from out of  your mouth, 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
If thou turn away thy foot from the sabbath, [from] doing thy pleasure on my holy day; and call the sabbath a delight, the holy of the LORD, honourable; and shalt honour him, not doing thine own ways, nor finding thine own pleasure, nor speaking [thine own] words:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
