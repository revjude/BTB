**LXX Isaiah 56:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
See how they all have been blinded! they knew not. All \[are\] dumb dogs, they shall not be able to bark; ones dreaming \[of\] a bed, being fond of slumber. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
His watchmen [are] blind: they are all ignorant, they [are] all dumb dogs, they cannot bark; sleeping, lying down, loving to slumber.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Israel’s watchmen are blind, they are all oblivious; they are all mute dogs, they cannot bark; they are dreamers lying around, loving to slumber.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
