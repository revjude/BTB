**LXX Isaiah 51:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For in  your being delivered, he shall not stop nor pass time. And he shall not kill for hurt, and in no way shall he lack  his bread. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The captive exile hasteneth that he may be loosed, and that he should not die in the pit, nor that his bread should fail.

The captive will soon be freed; he will not die in the dungeon, and his bread will not be lacking.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
