**LXX Isaiah 51:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
 Your sons, the ones being perplexed, the ones sleeping upon \[the\] top of all \[the\] streets, as a half-boiled beet; the ones full of \[the\] rage of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, fainting from \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  God. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thy sons have fainted, they lie at the head of all the streets, as a wild bull in a net: they are full of the fury of the LORD, the rebuke of thy God.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Your sons have fainted; they lie at the head of every street, like an antelope in a net. They are full of the wrath of the LORD, the rebuke of your God.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
