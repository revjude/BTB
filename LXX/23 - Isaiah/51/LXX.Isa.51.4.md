**LXX Isaiah 51:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Hear me! Hear me O my people! and O kings give ear to me! For a law shall go forth from me, and  my judgment for a light of nations. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Hearken unto me, my people; and give ear unto me, O my nation: for a law shall proceed from me, and I will make my judgment to rest for a light of the people.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Pay attention to Me, My people, and listen to Me, My nation; for a law will go out from Me, and My justice will become a light to the nations; I will bring it about quickly.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
