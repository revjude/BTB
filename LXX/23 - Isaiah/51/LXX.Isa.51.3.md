**LXX Isaiah 51:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And now I will comfort you, O Zion. And I comforted all  her desolate \[places\]; and I will make  her desolate \[places\] as a park; and   her western \[places\] as a park of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. They shall find gladness and a leap for joy in her -- acknowledgment and a voice of praise. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For the LORD shall comfort Zion: he will comfort all her waste places; and he will make her wilderness like Eden, and her desert like the garden of the LORD; joy and gladness shall be found therein, thanksgiving, and the voice of melody.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the LORD will comfort Zion and will look with compassion on all her ruins; He will make her wilderness like Eden and her desert like the garden of the LORD. Joy and gladness will be found in her, thanksgiving and melodious song.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
