**LXX Isaiah 7:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it will be in  that day, \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> will whistle for \[the\] flies which shall dominate part of \[the\] river of Egypt, and \[for\] the bee which is in \[the\] place of \[the\] Assyrians. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it shall come to pass in that day, [that] the LORD shall hiss for the fly that [is] in the uttermost part of the rivers of Egypt, and for the bee that [is] in the land of Assyria.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
On that day the LORD will whistle to the flies at the farthest streams of the Nile and to the bees in the land of Assyria.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
