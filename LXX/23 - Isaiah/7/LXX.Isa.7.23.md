**LXX Isaiah 7:23** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it will be in  that day \[that\] every place where ever might be a thousand grapevines worth a thousand shekels,  they shall be uncultivated and for \[the\] thorn-bush. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it shall come to pass in that day, [that] every place shall be, where there were a thousand vines at a thousand silverlings, it shall [even] be for briers and thorns.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And on that day, in every place that had a thousand vines worth a thousand shekels of silver, only briers and thorns will be found.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
