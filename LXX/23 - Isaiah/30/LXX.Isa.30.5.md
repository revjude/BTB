**LXX Isaiah 30:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In folly they labor over a people who shall not benefit them, neither for help, nor for benefit, but for shame, and scorn. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
They were all ashamed of a people [that] could not profit them, nor be an help nor profit, but a shame, and also a reproach.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
everyone will be put to shame because of a people useless to them. They bring neither help nor benefit, but only shame and disgrace.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
