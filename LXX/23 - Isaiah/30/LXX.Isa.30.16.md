**LXX Isaiah 30:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But you said, Upon horses we shall flee. On account of this you shall flee and say, we will be upon light riders; on account of this the ones pursuing you will be nimble. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But ye said, No; for we will flee upon horses; therefore shall ye flee: and, We will ride upon the swift; therefore shall they that pursue you be swift.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“No,” you say, “we will flee on horses.” Therefore you will flee! “We will ride swift horses,” but your pursuers will be faster.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
