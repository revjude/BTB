**LXX Isaiah 30:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Therefore this will be to you --  this sin \[will be\] as a wall of a fortified city falling immediately being captured, of which immediately is at hand the calamitous downfall. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Therefore this iniquity shall be to you as a breach ready to fall, swelling out in a high wall, whose breaking cometh suddenly at an instant.

this iniquity of yours is like a breach about to fail, a bulge in a high wall, whose collapse will come suddenly—in an instant!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
