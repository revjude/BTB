**LXX Judges 6:35** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he sent messengers to all Manasseh, and he called also himself \[others\] after him. And he sent out messengers in Asher, and in Zebulun, and in Naphtali. And they ascended for meeting him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he sent messengers throughout all Manasseh; who also was gathered after him: and he sent messengers unto Asher, and unto Zebulun, and unto Naphtali; and they came up to meet them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Calling them to arms, Gideon sent messengers throughout Manasseh, as well as Asher, Zebulun, and Naphtali, so that they came up to meet him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
