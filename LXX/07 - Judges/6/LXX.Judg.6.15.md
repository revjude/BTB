**LXX Judges 6:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Gideon said to him, \[Be it\] to me, O my Lord, by what \[means\] shall I deliver  Israel? Behold,  my thousand \[is\] humbler in Manasseh, and I am \[the\] lesser in \[the\] house  of my father. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he said unto him, Oh my Lord, wherewith shall I save Israel? behold, my family [is] poor in Manasseh, and I [am] the least in my father’s house.

“Please, my Lord,” Gideon replied, “how can I save Israel? Indeed, my clan is the weakest in Manasseh, and I am the youngest in my father’s house.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
