**LXX Judges 6:11** 

***The Angel Appears unto Gideon***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And an angel \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> came and sat under the oak, the one being in Ophrah, the one of the father of Joash \[of\] Ezri. And Gideon  his son wheat with a rod in a wine-vat,  to flee from \[the\] face of Midian. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And there came an angel of the LORD, and sat under an oak which [was] in Ophrah, that [pertained] unto Joash the Abiezrite: and his son Gideon threshed wheat by the winepress, to hide [it] from the Midianites.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Then the angel of the LORD came and sat down under the oak in Ophrah that belonged to Joash the Abiezrite, where his son Gideon was threshing wheat in a winepress to hide it from the Midianites.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
