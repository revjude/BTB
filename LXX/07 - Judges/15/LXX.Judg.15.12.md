**LXX Judges 15:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they said to him, We came down to tie you,  to deliver you up into \[the\] hands of \[the\] Philistines. And Samson said to them, Swear to me by an oath to not kill me yourselves, that lest at any time you should meet up with me yourselves. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And they said unto him, We are come down to bind thee, that we may deliver thee into the hand of the Philistines. And Samson said unto them, Swear unto me, that ye will not fall upon me yourselves.

But they said to him, “We have come down to arrest you and hand you over to the Philistines.” Samson replied, “Swear to me that you will not kill me yourselves.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
