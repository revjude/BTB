**LXX Judges 20:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they rose up and ascended unto Beth-el, and asked of  God. And the sons of Israel said, Who shall ascend to us guiding to wage war with the sons of Benjamin? And \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> said, Judah shall ascend guiding. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the children of Israel arose, and went up to the house of God, and asked counsel of God, and said, Which of us shall go up first to the battle against the children of Benjamin? And the LORD said, Judah [shall go up] first.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The Israelites set out, went up to Bethel, and inquired of God, “Who of us shall go up first to fight against the Benjamites?” “Judah will be first,” the LORD replied.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
