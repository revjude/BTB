**LXX Judges 19:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it came to pass all the ones seeing said, Neither happened, nor was thus seen from the days of \[the\] ascending of \[the\] sons of Israel from out of \[the\] land of Egypt until  this day. Appoint indeed to yourselves counsel concerning her and speak! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And it was so, that all that saw it said, There was no such deed done nor seen from the day that the children of Israel came up out of the land of Egypt unto this day: consider of it, take advice, and speak [your minds].
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And everyone who saw it said, “Nothing like this has been seen or done from the day the Israelites came out of the land of Egypt until this day. Think it over, take counsel, and speak up!”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
