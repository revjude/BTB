**LXX Judges 4:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Sisera withdrew \[by\] his feet to \[the\] tent of Jael wife of Heber the Kenite; for there was peace between Jabin king of Hazor and between the house of Heber the Kenite.
