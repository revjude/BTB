**LXX Judges 12:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Gilead was first to take the ford of the Jordan  of Ephraim. And it came to pass when the ones coming through safe of Ephraim said to them, We should pass over indeed! That the men of Gilead said to them, \[Are\] you not of Ephraim? And they said, We are not. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the Gileadites took the passages of Jordan before the Ephraimites: and it was [so], that when those Ephraimites which were escaped said, Let me go over; that the men of Gilead said unto him, [Art] thou an Ephraimite? If he said, Nay;
