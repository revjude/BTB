**LXX Judges 12:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I saw that \[there\] was no  one delivering, and I put  my life in  my hand, and I passed over to \[the\] sons of Ammon, and \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> delivered them over into my hand. And why did you ascend against me in  this day to wage war against me? 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And when I saw that ye delivered [me] not, I put my life in my hands, and passed over against the children of Ammon, and the LORD delivered them into my hand: wherefore then are ye come up unto me this day, to fight against me?

When I saw that you would not save me, I risked my life and crossed over to the Ammonites, and the LORD delivered them into my hand. Why then have you come today to fight against me?”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
