**LXX Judges 18:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In their being by \[the\] house of Micah, that they knew the voice of the young man, the Levite, and they turned aside there. And they said to him, Who brought you here? And what do you do here? And what is it here? 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
When they [were] by the house of Micah, they knew the voice of the young man the Levite: and they turned in thither, and said unto him, Who brought thee hither? and what makest thou in this [place]? and what hast thou here?
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And while they were near Micah’s house, they recognized the voice of the young Levite; so they went over and asked him, “Who brought you here? What are you doing in this place? Why are you here?”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
