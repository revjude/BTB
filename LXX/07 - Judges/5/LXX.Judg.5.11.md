**LXX Judges 5:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A sound of the men playing music in the midst of ones making merry. There they shall give righteousness \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. O righteousness grow in strength in  Israel! Then shall go down into  his cities the people of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>.

[They that are delivered] from the noise of archers in the places of drawing water, there shall they rehearse the righteous acts of the LORD, [even] the righteous acts [toward the inhabitants] of his villages in Israel: then shall the people of the LORD go down to the gates.
