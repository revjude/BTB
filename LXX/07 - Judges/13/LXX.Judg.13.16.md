**LXX Judges 13:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the angel \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> said to Manoah, If it should hold me I will not eat from  your bread \[loaves\]. And if you should offer a whole burnt-offering, you shall offer it to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. For Manoah did not know that he \[was\] an angel \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the angel of the LORD said unto Manoah, Though thou detain me, I will not eat of thy bread: and if thou wilt offer a burnt offering, thou must offer it unto the LORD. For Manoah knew not that he [was] an angel of the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And the angel of the LORD replied, “Even if I stay, I will not eat your food. But if you prepare a burnt offering, offer it to the LORD.” For Manoah did not know that it was the angel of the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
