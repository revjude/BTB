<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the angel \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> said to Manoah, If it should hold me I will not eat from  your bread \[loaves\]. For Manoah did not know that he \[was\] an angel \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>.
