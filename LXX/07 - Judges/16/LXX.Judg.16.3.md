**LXX Judges 16:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Samson went to bed until midnight. And he rose up at half the night, and he took hold of the doors of the gate of the city, and the two doorposts, and carried them with the bar, and put \[them\] upon his shoulders, and bore them upon the top of the mountain which is at \[the\] face of Hebron. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And Samson lay till midnight, and arose at midnight, and took the doors of the gate of the city, and the two posts, and went away with them, bar and all, and put [them] upon his shoulders, and carried them up to the top of an hill that [is] before Hebron.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
