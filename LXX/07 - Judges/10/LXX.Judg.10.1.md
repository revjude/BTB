**LXX Judges 10:1** 

***Tola and Jair Judge Israel***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And rose up after Abimelech  to deliver  Israel, Tola son of Phua, son of his uncle, a man of Issachar; and he dwelt in Shamir in mount Ephraim.

And after Abimelech there arose to defend Israel Tola the son of Puah, the son of Dodo, a man of Issachar; and he dwelt in Shamir in mount Ephraim.

After the time of Abimelech, a man of Issachar, Tola son of Puah, the son of Dodo, rose up to save Israel. He lived in Shamir, in the hill country of Ephraim.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
