**LXX Judges 9:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he entered to the house  of his father at Ophrah, and he killed  his brothers, \[the\] sons of Jerubbaal, seventy men upon one stone. But Jotham the younger son of Jerubbaal was left behind, for he was hidden. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he went unto his father’s house at Ophrah, and slew his brethren the sons of Jerubbaal, [being] threescore and ten persons, upon one stone: notwithstanding yet Jotham the youngest son of Jerubbaal was left; for he hid himself.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
