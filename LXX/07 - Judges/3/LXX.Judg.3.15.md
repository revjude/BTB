***Ehud Judges Israel***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the sons of Israel cried out to \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; and \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> raised up to them a deliverer --  Ehud son of Gera, son  of Benjamin, a man ambidextrous. And the sons of Israel sent gifts by his hand  to Eglon king of Moab. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But when the children of Israel cried unto the LORD, the LORD raised them up a deliverer, Ehud the son of Gera, a Benjamite, a man lefthanded: and by him the children of Israel sent a present unto Eglon the king of Moab.
