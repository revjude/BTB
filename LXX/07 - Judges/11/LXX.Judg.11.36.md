**LXX Judges 11:36** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And she said to him, If about me you opened  your mouth to \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, let him do to me in which manner it came forth from out of  your mouth, because \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> did vengeance for you on  your enemies, of the sons of Ammon. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And she said unto him, My father, [if] thou hast opened thy mouth unto the LORD, do to me according to that which hath proceeded out of thy mouth; forasmuch as the LORD hath taken vengeance for thee of thine enemies, [even] of the children of Ammon.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“My father,” she replied, “you have given your word to the LORD. Do to me as you have said, for the LORD has avenged you of your enemies, the Ammonites.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
