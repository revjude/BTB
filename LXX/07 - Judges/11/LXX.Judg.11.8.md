**LXX Judges 11:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the elders of Gilead said to Jephthah, Not so, now we gathered together to you, and you shall go with us, and we shall wage war against the sons of Ammon, and you will be to us as head to all the ones dwelling in Gilead. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the elders of Gilead said unto Jephthah, Therefore we turn again to thee now, that thou mayest go with us, and fight against the children of Ammon, and be our head over all the inhabitants of Gilead.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
They answered Jephthah, “This is why we now turn to you, that you may go with us, fight the Ammonites, and become leader over all of us who live in Gilead.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
