**LXX Judges 14:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the wife of Samson wept to him, and said to him, You detest me, and do not love me, because the riddle which you propounded to the sons  of my people, you did not explain even it to me. And Samson said to her, Behold, to my father and \[to\] my mother I did not report it, and should I report to you? 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And Samson’s wife wept before him, and said, Thou dost but hate me, and lovest me not: thou hast put forth a riddle unto the children of my people, and hast not told [it] me. And he said unto her, Behold, I have not told [it] my father nor my mother, and shall I tell [it] thee?
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Then Samson’s wife came to him, weeping, and said, “You hate me! You do not really love me! You have posed to my people a riddle, but have not explained it to me.” “Look,” he said, “I have not even explained it to my father or mother, so why should I explain it to you?”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
