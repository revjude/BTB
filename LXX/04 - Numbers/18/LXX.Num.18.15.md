**LXX Numbers 18:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And every \[thing\] opening wide \[the\] womb of all flesh, as many \[things\] as they offer \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, from man unto beast, it will be to you; only with ransoms you shall ransom the first-born  of men; and the first-born of the unclean cattle you shall ransom. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Every thing that openeth the matrix in all flesh, which they bring unto the LORD, [whether it be] of men or beasts, shall be thine: nevertheless the firstborn of man shalt thou surely redeem, and the firstling of unclean beasts shalt thou redeem.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The firstborn of every womb, whether man or beast, that is offered to the LORD belongs to you. But you must surely redeem every firstborn son and every firstborn male of unclean animals.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
