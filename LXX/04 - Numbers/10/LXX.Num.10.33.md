**LXX Numbers 10:33** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And they lifted away from the mountain of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, a journey of three days. And the ark of covenant of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> went forth in front of them, a journey of three days, to survey themselves a rest. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And they departed from the mount of the LORD three days’ journey: and the ark of the covenant of the LORD went before them in the three days’ journey, to search out a resting place for them.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
So they set out on a three-day journey from the mountain of the LORD, with the ark of the covenant of the LORD traveling ahead of them for those three days to seek a resting place for them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
