**LXX Numbers 30:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But if in keeping silent he should remain silent at her day by day, then all her vows shall stand to her; and the sets of limits  upon her shall stand to her, for he kept silent at her in the day in which he heard. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But if her husband altogether hold his peace at her from day to day; then he establisheth all her vows, or all her bonds, which [are] upon her: he confirmeth them, because he held his peace at her in the day that he heard [them].
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But if her husband says nothing to her from day to day, then he confirms all the vows and pledges that bind her. He has confirmed them, because he said nothing to her on the day he heard about them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
