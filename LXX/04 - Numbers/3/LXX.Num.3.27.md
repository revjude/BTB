**LXX Numbers 3:27** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[To\] Kohath \[was\] one \[division of\] people for Amram, and one \[division of\] people for Izehar, and one \[division of\] people for Hebron, and one \[division of\] people for Uzziel. These are the peoples  of Kohath according to number. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And of Kohath [was] the family of the Amramites, and the family of the Izeharites, and the family of the Hebronites, and the family of the Uzzielites: these [are] the families of the Kohathites.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
From Kohath came the clans of the Amramites, the Izharites, the Hebronites, and the Uzzielites; these were the clans of the Kohathites.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
