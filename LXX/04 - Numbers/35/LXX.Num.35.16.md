**LXX Numbers 35:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if by an item of iron one should strike him, and he should come to an end -- \[that one\] is a murderer -- unto death the murderer be put to death! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And if he smite him with an instrument of iron, so that he die, he [is] a murderer: the murderer shall surely be put to death.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If, however, anyone strikes a person with an iron object and kills him, he is a murderer; the murderer must surely be put to death.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
