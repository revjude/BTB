**LXX Numbers 35:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you shall draw apart to you for them cities for them; they will be places of refuge to you \[for\] the man-slayer to flee there; any one  striking a soul unintentionally. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then ye shall appoint you cities to be cities of refuge for you; that the slayer may flee thither, which killeth any person at unawares.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
designate cities to serve as your cities of refuge, so that a person who kills someone unintentionally may flee there.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
