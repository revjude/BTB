**LXX Numbers 4:41** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
This \[is\] the numbering of \[the\] people of \[the\] sons of Gershon, every  one officiating in the tent of the testimony, which Moses and Aaron numbered by \[the\] voice of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> by \[the\] hand of Moses. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
These [are] they that were numbered of the families of the sons of Gershon, of all that might do service in the tabernacle of the congregation, whom Moses and Aaron did number according to the commandment of the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
These were counted from the Gershonite clans who served at the Tent of Meeting, whom Moses and Aaron counted at the LORD’s command.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
