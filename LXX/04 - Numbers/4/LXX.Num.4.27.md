**LXX Numbers 4:27** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
By \[the\] mouth of Aaron and  his sons shall be the ministration of the sons of Gershon, according to all  their ministrations, and according to all  their works. And you shall number them by names, and all the works by them. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
At the appointment of Aaron and his sons shall be all the service of the sons of the Gershonites, in all their burdens, and in all their service: and ye shall appoint unto them in charge all their burdens.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
All the service of the Gershonites—all their transport duties and other work—is to be done at the direction of Aaron and his sons; you are to assign to them all that they are responsible to carry.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
