**LXX Numbers 33:38** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Aaron the priest ascended into the mountain by order of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, and he died there in the fortieth year of the exodus of the sons of Israel from out of \[the\] land of Egypt, in the fifth month, \[day\] one of the month. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And Aaron the priest went up into mount Hor at the commandment of the LORD, and died there, in the fortieth year after the children of Israel were come out of the land of Egypt, in the first [day] of the fifth month.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
At the LORD’s command, Aaron the priest climbed Mount Hor and died there on the first day of the fifth month, in the fortieth year after the Israelites had come out of the land of Egypt.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
