**LXX Numbers 28:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And a tenth of fine flour being prepared in olive oil for the one lamb -- a sacrifice scent of pleasant aroma, a yield \[offering\] \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And a several tenth deal of flour mingled with oil [for] a meat offering unto one lamb; [for] a burnt offering of a sweet savour, a sacrifice made by fire unto the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
and a tenth of an ephah of fine flour mixed with oil as a grain offering with each lamb. This is a burnt offering, a pleasing aroma, a food offering to the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
