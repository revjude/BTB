**LXX Numbers 5:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
or a man who ever a spirit of jealousy come upon him, and should be jealous  of his wife, and should stand  his wife before \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, then the priest shall do to her all  this law. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Or when the spirit of jealousy cometh upon him, and he be jealous over his wife, and shall set the woman before the LORD, and the priest shall execute upon her all this law.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
or when a feeling of jealousy comes over a husband and he suspects his wife. He is to have the woman stand before the LORD, and the priest is to apply to her this entire law.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
