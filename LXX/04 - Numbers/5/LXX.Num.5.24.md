**LXX Numbers 5:24** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he shall give to drink to the woman the water of the rebuke of the accursing; and shall enter into her the water of the rebuke of the accursing. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he shall cause the woman to drink the bitter water that causeth the curse: and the water that causeth the curse shall enter into her, [and become] bitter.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He is to have the woman drink the bitter water that brings a curse, and it will enter her and may cause her bitter suffering.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
