**LXX Numbers 9:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And those men said to them, We \[are\] unclean by reason of \[the dead\] soul of a man. Are we then lacking so as to bring the gift \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> according to its time in \[the\] midst of \[the\] sons of Israel? 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And those men said unto him, We [are] defiled by the dead body of a man: wherefore are we kept back, that we may not offer an offering of the LORD in his appointed season among the children of Israel?
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
and said to Moses, “We are unclean because of a dead body, but why should we be excluded from presenting the LORD’s offering with the other Israelites at the appointed time?”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
