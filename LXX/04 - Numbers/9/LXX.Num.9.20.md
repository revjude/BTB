**LXX Numbers 9:20** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it shall be whenever the cloud should become a number of days upon the tent; by \[the\] voice of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> they shall camp, and by order of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> they shall depart. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And [so] it was, when the cloud was a few days upon the tabernacle; according to the commandment of the LORD they abode in their tents, and according to the commandment of the LORD they journeyed.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Sometimes the cloud remained over the tabernacle for only a few days, and they would camp at the LORD’s command and set out at the LORD’s command.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
