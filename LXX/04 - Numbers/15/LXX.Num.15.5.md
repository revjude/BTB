**LXX Numbers 15:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And wine for a libation \[offering\], the fourth of the hin, you shall offer upon the whole burnt-offering, or upon the sacrifice \[offering\] to the one lamb. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the fourth [part] of an hin of wine for a drink offering shalt thou prepare with the burnt offering or sacrifice, for one lamb.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
With the burnt offering or sacrifice of each lamb, you are to prepare a quarter hin of wine as a drink offering.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
