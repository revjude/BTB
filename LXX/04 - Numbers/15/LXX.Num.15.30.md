**LXX Numbers 15:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] soul who shall do \[a thing\] by hand through pride -- of the native born, or of the foreigners --  this one provokes God, and that soul shall be utterly destroyed from out of  its people, 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But the soul that doeth [ought] presumptuously, [whether he be] born in the land, or a stranger, the same reproacheth the LORD; and that soul shall be cut off from among his people.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But the person who sins defiantly, whether a native or foreigner, blasphemes the LORD. That person shall be cut off from among his people.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
