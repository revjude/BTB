**LXX Numbers 14:27** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For how long \[shall I endure\]  this wicked congregation, in which they grumble before me? The grumbling of the sons of Israel, which they grumble concerning you, I have heard. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
How long [shall I bear with] this evil congregation, which murmur against me? I have heard the murmurings of the children of Israel, which they murmur against me.

“How long will this wicked congregation grumble against Me? I have heard the complaints that the Israelites are making against Me.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
