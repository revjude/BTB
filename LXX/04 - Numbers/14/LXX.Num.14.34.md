**LXX Numbers 14:34** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
According to the number of the days, as many as you surveyed the land -- forty days; a day for the year you will take  for your sins -- forty years; and you shall know the rage  of my anger. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
After the number of the days in which ye searched the land, [even] forty days, each day for a year, shall ye bear your iniquities, [even] forty years, and ye shall know my breach of promise.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
In keeping with the forty days you spied out the land, you shall bear your guilt forty years—a year for each day—and you will experience My alienation.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
