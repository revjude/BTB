**LXX Numbers 14:24**

 But my servant Caleb, for another spirit is in him, and he followed after me, I will bring him into the land into which you enter there, and  his seed shall inherit it.

But my servant Caleb, because he had another spirit with him, and hath followed me fully, him will I bring into the land whereinto he went; and his seed shall possess it.
