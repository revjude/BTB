**LXX Numbers 14:29** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In  this wilderness your carcasses shall fall, even all  overseeing you, and the ones being counted of you from twenty years \[old\] and up, as many as grumbled against me.

Your carcases shall fall in this wilderness; and all that were numbered of you, according to your whole number, from twenty years old and upward, which have murmured against me,

Your bodies will fall in this wilderness—all who were numbered in the census, everyone twenty years of age or older—because you have grumbled against Me.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
