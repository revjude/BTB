**LXX Numbers 16:37** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Speak to Eleazar the son of Aaron the priest, Do away with the censers from \[the\] midst of the ones having been incinerated, and  sow this alien fire there! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Speak unto Eleazar the son of Aaron the priest, that he take up the censers out of the burning, and scatter thou the fire yonder; for they are hallowed.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Tell Eleazar son of Aaron the priest to remove the censers from the flames and to scatter the coals far away, because the censers are holy.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
