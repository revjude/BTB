**LXX Numbers 16:33**

And these went down, and all as many as is to them, alive into Hades; and the earth covered them, and they were destroyed from out of \[the\] midst of the congregation.

They, and all that [appertained] to them, went down alive into the pit, and the earth closed upon them: and they perished from among the congregation.
