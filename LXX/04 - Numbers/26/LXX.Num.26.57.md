**LXX Numbers 26:57** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] sons of Levi according to their peoples -- \[to\] Gershon, \[the\] people the Gershonites; \[to\] Kohath, \[the\] people the Kohathites; \[to\] Merari, \[the\] people the Merarites. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And these [are] they that were numbered of the Levites after their families: of Gershon, the family of the Gershonites: of Kohath, the family of the Kohathites: of Merari, the family of the Merarites.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Now these were the Levites numbered by their clans: The Gershonite clan from Gershon, the Kohathite clan from Kohath, and the Merarite clan from Merari.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
