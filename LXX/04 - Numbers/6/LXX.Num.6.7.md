**LXX Numbers 6:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Unto father and unto mother, and to brother, and to sister, he shall not be defiled by them in their dying; for his vow of God \[is\] upon him, upon his head. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He shall not make himself unclean for his father, or for his mother, for his brother, or for his sister, when they die: because the consecration of his God [is] upon his head.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Even if his father or mother or brother or sister should die, he is not to defile himself, because the symbol of consecration to his God is upon his head.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
