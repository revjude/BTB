**LXX Numbers 23:21** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
There shall not be trouble in Jacob, nor misery appear in Israel. \[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  his God \[is\] with him, the honorable ones of \[the\] rulers \[are\] with him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He hath not beheld iniquity in Jacob, neither hath he seen perverseness in Israel: the LORD his God [is] with him, and the shout of a king [is] among them.

He considers no disaster for Jacob; He sees no trouble for Israel. The LORD their God is with them, and the shout of the King is among them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
