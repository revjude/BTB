**LXX Numbers 11:31** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And a wind went forth from \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, and carried over mother-quail from the sea, and it put \[them\] upon the camp, a days journey here \[on this side\], and a days journey here \[on that side\], round about the camp, about two cubits from the earth. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And there went forth a wind from the LORD, and brought quails from the sea, and let [them] fall by the camp, as it were a day’s journey on this side, and as it were a day’s journey on the other side, round about the camp, and as it were two cubits [high] upon the face of the earth.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Now a wind sent by the LORD came up, drove in quail from the sea, and brought them near the camp, about two cubits above the surface of the ground, for a day’s journey in every direction around the camp.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
