**LXX Numbers 34:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the boundaries of the west will be to you -- the great sea shall define the bounds. This will be to you the boundaries of the west.

And [as for] the western border, ye shall even have the great sea for a border: this shall be your west border.
