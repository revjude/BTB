**LXX Numbers 21:1** 

***The Canaanites War with Israel***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And king Arad the Canaanite heard, the one dwelling by the wilderness, that Israel came \[by the\] way to Atharin; and he waged war against Israel, and they carried away captive some of them \[into\] captivity. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And [when] king Arad the Canaanite, which dwelt in the south, heard tell that Israel came by the way of the spies; then he fought against Israel, and took [some] of them prisoners.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
When the Canaanite king of Arad, who lived in the Negev, heard that Israel was coming along the road to Atharim, he attacked Israel and captured some prisoners.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
