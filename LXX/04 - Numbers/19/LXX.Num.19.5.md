**LXX Numbers 19:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you shall incinerate her before him, and  her skin, and  her meats, and  her blood with  her dung shall be incinerated.

And [one] shall burn the heifer in his sight; her skin, and her flesh, and her blood, with her dung, shall he burn:

Then the heifer must be burned in his sight. Its hide, its flesh, and its blood are to be burned, along with its dung.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
