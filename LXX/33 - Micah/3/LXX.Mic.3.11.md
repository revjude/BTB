**LXX Micah 3:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
 Her leaders judge with bribes, and  her priests answer with a wage, and  her prophets \[are\] divining with silver; but upon the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> they rest upon, saying, is not \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> among us? In no way bad \[things\] come upon us. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The heads thereof judge for reward, and the priests thereof teach for hire, and the prophets thereof divine for money: yet will they lean upon the LORD, and say, [Is] not the LORD among us? none evil can come upon us.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Her leaders judge for a bribe, her priests teach for a price, and her prophets practice divination for money. Yet they lean upon the LORD, saying, “Is not the LORD among us? No disaster can come upon us.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
