**LXX Micah 6:15** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You shall sow, but you shall not reap; you shall compress \[the\] olive, but in no way should you anoint \[with\] olive oil; and wine, and in no way shall you drink; and \[the\] laws of my people shall be obliterated. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thou shalt sow, but thou shalt not reap; thou shalt tread the olives, but thou shalt not anoint thee with oil; and sweet wine, but shalt not drink wine.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You will sow but not reap; you will press olives but not anoint yourselves with oil; you will tread grapes but not drink the wine.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
