He explained to you, O man, what \[is\] good or what \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> requires from you; but only  to execute equity, and to love mercy, and to be prepared  to go with your God.

He hath shewed thee, O man, what [is] good; and what doth the LORD require of thee, but to do justly, and to love mercy, and to walk humbly with thy God?
