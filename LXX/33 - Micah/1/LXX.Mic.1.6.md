**LXX Micah 1:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And I will make Samaria for a storehouse of fruits of \[the\] field, and for planting a vineyard. And I will tear down in chaos  her stones; and  her foundations I will uncover. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Therefore I will make Samaria as an heap of the field, [and] as plantings of a vineyard: and I will pour down the stones thereof into the valley, and I will discover the foundations thereof.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Therefore I will make Samaria a heap of rubble in the open field, a planting area for a vineyard. I will pour her stones into the valley and expose her foundations.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
