**LXX Micah 7:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Rejoice not against me, O my enemy! for I have fallen and I will rise up. For if I shall sit in the darkness, \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> will give light to me. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Rejoice not against me, O mine enemy: when I fall, I shall arise; when I sit in darkness, the LORD [shall be] a light unto me.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Do not gloat over me, my enemy! Though I have fallen, I will arise; though I sit in darkness, the LORD will be my light.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
