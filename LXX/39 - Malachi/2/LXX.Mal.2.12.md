**LXX Malachi 2:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> will utterly destroy the man  doing these \[things\], until whenever he should be humbled from out of \[the\] tents of Jacob, and from out of \[the\] ones bringing a sacrifice to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> almighty. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The LORD will cut off the man that doeth this, the master and the scholar, out of the tabernacles of Jacob, and him that offereth an offering unto the LORD of hosts.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
As for the man who does this, may the LORD cut off from the tents of Jacob everyone who is awake and aware—even if he brings an offering to the LORD of Hosts.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
