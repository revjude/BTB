**LXX Malachi 2:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But if by detesting, you should send \[her\] forth, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  God  of Israel, then impiety shall cover over  your thoughts, says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  God almighty. And \[keep\] guard in  your spirit, and in no way should you abandon \[them\], 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For the LORD, the God of Israel, saith that he hateth putting away: for [one] covereth violence with his garment, saith the LORD of hosts: therefore take heed to your spirit, that ye deal not treacherously.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“For I hate divorce,” says the LORD, the God of Israel. “He who divorces his wife covers his garment with violence,” says the LORD of Hosts. So guard yourselves in your spirit and do not break faith.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
