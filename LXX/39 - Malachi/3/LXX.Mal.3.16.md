**LXX Malachi 3:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The ones fearing the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> spoke these \[things\] each to  his neighbor. And \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> heeded, and hearkened, and wrote a scroll of memorial before him to the ones fearing the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, and venerating his name. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Then they that feared the LORD spake often one to another: and the LORD hearkened, and heard [it], and a book of remembrance was written before him for them that feared the LORD, and that thought upon his name.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
At that time those who feared the LORD spoke with one another, and the LORD listened and heard them. So a scroll of remembrance was written before Him regarding those who feared the LORD and honored His name.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
