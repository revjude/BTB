**LXX 2 Samuel 12:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And a traveller came to the rich man. And he spared  to take from  his flock, and from  his herd,  to prepare for the stranger coming to him. And he took the ewe-lamb of the needy man; and he prepared it for the man  coming to him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And there came a traveller unto the rich man, and he spared to take of his own flock and of his own herd, to dress for the wayfaring man that was come unto him; but took the poor man’s lamb, and dressed it for the man that was come to him.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Now a traveler came to the rich man, who refrained from taking one of his own sheep or cattle to prepare for the traveler who had come to him. Instead, he took the poor man’s lamb and prepared it for his guest.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
