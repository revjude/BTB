**LXX 2 Samuel 3:29** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Let \[it\] arrive upon \[the\] head of Joab, and upon all the house  of his father! and may there not cease to be one from the house of Joab having gonorrhea, and leprous, and holding a stave, and falling by \[the\] broadsword, and lacking bread loaves. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Let it rest on the head of Joab, and on all his father’s house; and let there not fail from the house of Joab one that hath an issue, or that is a leper, or that leaneth on a staff, or that falleth on the sword, or that lacketh bread.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
May it whirl over the head of Joab and over the entire house of his father, and may the house of Joab never be without one having a discharge or skin disease, or one who leans on a staff or falls by the sword or lacks food.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
