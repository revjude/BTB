**LXX 2 Samuel 23:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And a man who shall labor among them, full of iron and wooden spear, and in fire burning, they shall be burnt in their shame. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But the man [that] shall touch them must be fenced with iron and the staff of a spear; and they shall be utterly burned with fire in the [same] place.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The man who touches them must be armed with iron or with the shaft of a spear. The fire burns them to ashes in the place where they lie.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
