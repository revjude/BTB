**LXX 2 Samuel 14:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the Tekoahite woman said to the king, O my master, O king, upon me \[be\] the iniquity, and upon the house  of my father! and the king and  his throne \[be\] innocent! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the woman of Tekoah said unto the king, My lord, O king, the iniquity [be] on me, and on my father’s house: and the king and his throne [be] guiltless.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But the woman of Tekoa said to the king, “My lord the king, may any blame be on me and on my father’s house, and may the king and his throne be guiltless.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
