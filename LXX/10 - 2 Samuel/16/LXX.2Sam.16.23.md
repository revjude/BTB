**LXX 2 Samuel 16:23** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the counsel of Ahithophel, which he consulted in the first days, \[was\] in which manner \[as if\] he should ask anything by \[the\] word  of God; thus \[was\] all the counsel  of Ahithophel, both \[to\] David and \[to\] Absalom. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the counsel of Ahithophel, which he counselled in those days, [was] as if a man had inquired at the oracle of God: so [was] all the counsel of Ahithophel both with David and with Absalom.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Now in those days the advice of Ahithophel was like the consultation of the word of God. Such was the regard that both David and Absalom had for Ahithophel’s advice.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
