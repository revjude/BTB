**LXX 2 Samuel 19:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[to\] Amasa you shall say, you not of my bone and of my flesh? And now, thus God do to me, and thus add \[more\], be not ruler of \[the\] force before me all the days instead of Joab. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And say ye to Amasa, [Art] thou not of my bone, and of my flesh? God do so to me, and more also, if thou be not captain of the host before me continually in the room of Joab.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And say to Amasa, ‘Aren’t you my flesh and blood? May God punish me, and ever so severely, if from now on you are not the commander of my army in place of Joab!’”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
