**LXX 2 Samuel 6:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And David rose up and went, and all the people  with him, and \[some\] of the rulers of Judah,  to lead from there the ark  of God, upon which the name of the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> of the forces is called upon, \[the\] one sitting upon the cherubim upon it. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And David arose, and went with all the people that [were] with him from Baale of Judah, to bring up from thence the ark of God, whose name is called by the name of the LORD of hosts that dwelleth [between] the cherubims.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
And he and all his troops set out for Baale of Judah to bring up from there the ark of God, which is called by the Name—the name of the LORD of Hosts, who is enthroned between the cherubim that are on it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
