**LXX Zephaniah 2:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Seek the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> all \[you\] humble ones of \[the\] earth! Work equity, and seek righteousness, and answer them \[accordingly\]! so that you should be sheltered in \[the\] day of \[the\] wrath of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Seek ye the LORD, all ye meek of the earth, which have wrought his judgment; seek righteousness, seek meekness: it may be ye shall be hid in the day of the LORD’S anger.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Seek the LORD, all you humble of the earth who carry out His justice. Seek righteousness; seek humility. Perhaps you will be sheltered on the day of the LORD’s anger.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
