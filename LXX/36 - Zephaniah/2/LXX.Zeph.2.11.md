**LXX Zephaniah 2:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> shall appear against them, and he shall utterly destroy all the gods of the nations of the earth. And they shall do obeisance to him, each from  his place -- all the islands of the nations. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The LORD [will be] terrible unto them: for he will famish all the gods of the earth; and [men] shall worship him, every one from his place, [even] all the isles of the heathen.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The LORD will be terrifying to them when He starves all the gods of the earth. Then the nations of every shore will bow in worship to Him, each in its own place.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
