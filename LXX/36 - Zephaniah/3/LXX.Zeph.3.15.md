**LXX Zephaniah 3:15** 

***The LORD in the Midst***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> removed  your offences; he has ransomed you from out of \[the\] hand of your enemies. \[The\] king of Israel -- \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> in \[the\] midst of you. You shall not see \[the\] bad \[things\] any longer. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The LORD hath taken away thy judgments, he hath cast out thine enemy: the king of Israel, [even] the LORD, [is] in the midst of thee: thou shalt not see evil any more.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The LORD has taken away your punishment; He has turned back your enemy. Israel’s King, the LORD, is among you; no longer will you fear any harm.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
