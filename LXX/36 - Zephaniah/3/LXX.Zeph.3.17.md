**LXX Zephaniah 3:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  your God among you; the mighty one shall deliver you; he shall bring upon you gladness, and shall revive you in  his affection; and he shall be glad over you with delight as in \[the\] day of \[the\] holiday \[feast\]. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The LORD thy God in the midst of thee [is] mighty; he will save, he will rejoice over thee with joy; he will rest in his love, he will joy over thee with singing.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The LORD your God is among you; He is mighty to save. He will rejoice over you with gladness; He will quiet you with His love; He will rejoice over you with singing.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
