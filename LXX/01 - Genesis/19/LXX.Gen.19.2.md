**LXX Genesis 19:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he said, Behold lords, turn aside to the house  of your servant, and rest up, and wash  your feet! that rising early you may go forth into  your way. And they said, No, but in the square we will rest up. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And he said, Behold now, my lords, turn in, I pray you, into your servant’s house, and tarry all night, and wash your feet, and ye shall rise up early, and go on your ways. And they said, Nay; but we will abide in the street all night.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
and said, “My lords, please turn aside into the house of your servant; wash your feet and spend the night. Then you can rise early and go on your way.” “No,” they answered, “we will spend the night in the square.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
