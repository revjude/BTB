**LXX Genesis 30:40** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Jacob drew apart the lambs. And he set a ram white-mixed before the sheep, and every colored \[one\] among the lambs. And he parted to himself flocks for himself, and did not mix them into the flocks of Laban. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And Jacob did separate the lambs, and set the faces of the flocks toward the ringstraked, and all the brown in the flock of Laban; and he put his own flocks by themselves, and put them not unto Laban’s cattle.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Jacob set apart the young, but made the rest face the streaked dark-colored sheep in Laban’s flocks. Then he set his own stock apart and did not put them with Laban’s animals.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
