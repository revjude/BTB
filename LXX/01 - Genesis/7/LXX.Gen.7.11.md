**LXX Genesis 7:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In the six hundredth year in the life  of Noah, the second month, seventh and twentieth of the month, \[in\] this day, all the springs of the abyss tore, and the torrents of the heaven were opened.

In the six hundredth year of Noah’s life, in the second month, the seventeenth day of the month, the same day were all the fountains of the great deep broken up, and the windows of heaven were opened.

In the six hundredth year of Noah’s life, on the seventeenth day of the second month, all the fountains of the great deep burst forth, and the floodgates of the heavens were opened.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
