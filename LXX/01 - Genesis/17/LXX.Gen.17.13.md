**LXX Genesis 17:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
By circumcision he shall be circumcised -- the native-born servant  of your house, and the \[one\] bought with silver. And my covenant will be upon  your flesh for an eternal covenant. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He that is born in thy house, and he that is bought with thy money, must needs be circumcised: and my covenant shall be in your flesh for an everlasting covenant.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Whether they are born in your household or purchased, they must be circumcised. My covenant in your flesh will be an everlasting covenant.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
