**LXX Genesis 41:36** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the foods being guarded shall be for the land in the seven years of the famine, which will be in \[the\] land of Egypt, and the land shall not be obliterated in the famine. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And that food shall be for store to the land against the seven years of famine, which shall be in the land of Egypt; that the land perish not through the famine.

This food will be a reserve for the land during the seven years of famine to come upon the land of Egypt. Then the country will not perish in the famine.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
