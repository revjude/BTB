**LXX Genesis 43:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I will look out for him. You seek him from out of my hand. If I do not lead him to you, and set him before you, I will be sinning against you all the days.

I will be surety for him; of my hand shalt thou require him: if I bring him not unto thee, and set him before thee, then let me bear the blame for ever:

I will guarantee his safety. You may hold me personally responsible. If I do not bring him back and set him before you, then may I bear the guilt before you all my life.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
