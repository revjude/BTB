**LXX Genesis 36:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For it was \[that\] their  possessions \[were\] many  to live together. And the land of their sojourning was not able to bear them, because of the multitude  of their possessions.

For their riches were more than that they might dwell together; and the land wherein they were strangers could not bear them because of their cattle.
