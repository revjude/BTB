**LXX Psalm 36:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
They shall be intoxicated from \[the\] fatness of your house; and you shall water them of the rushing stream of your delicacy. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
They shall be abundantly satisfied with the fatness of thy house; and thou shalt make them drink of the river of thy pleasures.

They feast on the abundance of Your house, and You give them drink from Your river of delights.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
