**LXX Psalm 36:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
He reasons lawlessness upon  his bed; he renders to every way not good; and evil he did not loath. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He deviseth mischief upon his bed; he setteth himself in a way [that is] not good; he abhorreth not evil.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Even on his bed he plots wickedness; he sets himself on a path that is not good; he fails to reject evil.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
