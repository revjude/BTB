**LXX Psalm 34:1** 

***The LORD Rescues from Afflictions***

A psalm \[to\] David, when he changed  his countenance before Abimelech, and he loosened him, and he went forth. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I will bless the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> at all time; always  his praise \[is\] in  my mouth.
