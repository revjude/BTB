**LXX Psalm 50:21** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
These \[things\] you did, and I kept quiet. You undertook lawlessness, for \[you thought\] I will be likened to you. I will reprove you, and I will stand your sins in front of your face. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
These [things] hast thou done, and I kept silence; thou thoughtest that I was altogether [such an one] as thyself: [but] I will reprove thee, and set [them] in order before thine eyes.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You have done these things, and I kept silent; you thought I was just like you. But now I rebuke you and accuse you to your face.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
