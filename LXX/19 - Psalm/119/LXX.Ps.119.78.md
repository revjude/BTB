**LXX Psalm 119:78** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[the\] proud be shamed! for unjustly they acted lawlessly against me. But I shall meditate in  your commandments. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Let the proud be ashamed; for they dealt perversely with me without a cause: [but] I will meditate in thy precepts.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
May the arrogant be put to shame for subverting me with a lie; I will meditate on Your precepts.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
