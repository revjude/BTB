**LXX Psalm 119:116** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Take hold of me according to  your oracle; and enliven me! for you should not put me to shame of  my expectation. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Uphold me according unto thy word, that I may live: and let me not be ashamed of my hope.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Sustain me as You promised, that I may live; let me not be ashamed of my hope.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
