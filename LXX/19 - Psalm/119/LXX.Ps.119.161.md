**LXX Psalm 119:161** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Rulers pursued me without charge; and from  your words my heart was timid. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
SCHIN. Princes have persecuted me without a cause: but my heart standeth in awe of thy word.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Rulers persecute me without cause, but my heart fears only Your word.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
