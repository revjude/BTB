**LXX Psalm 55:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For if an enemy berated me, I would have endured. And if the one detesting me spoke great words against me, I would have hidden from him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For [it was] not an enemy [that] reproached me; then I could have borne [it]: neither [was it] he that hated me [that] did magnify [himself] against me; then I would have hid myself from him:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For it is not an enemy who insults me; that I could endure. It is not a foe who rises against me; from him I could hide.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
