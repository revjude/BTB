**LXX Psalm 148:1** 

***Let the Heavens and Earth Praise The LORD***

Alleluiah. of Haggai and Zachariah. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Praise the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> from out of the heavens! Praise him in the highest! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Praise ye the LORD. Praise ye the LORD from the heavens: praise him in the heights.

Hallelujah! Praise the LORD from the heavens; praise Him in the highest places.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
