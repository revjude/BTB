**LXX Psalm 148:14** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he shall exalt \[the\] horn of his people. A hymn to all  his sacred ones; to the sons of Israel; to a people approaching to him.

He also exalteth the horn of his people, the praise of all his saints; [even] of the children of Israel, a people near unto him. Praise ye the LORD.

He has raised up a horn for His people, the praise of all His saints, of Israel, a people near to Him. Hallelujah!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
