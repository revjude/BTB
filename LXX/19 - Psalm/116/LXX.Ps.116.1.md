**LXX Psalm 116:1** 

***The LORD Listens to the Afflicted***

Alleluiah. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I loved that shall listen to the voice  of my supplication. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I love the LORD, because he hath heard my voice [and] my supplications.

I love the LORD, for He has heard my voice—my appeal for mercy.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
