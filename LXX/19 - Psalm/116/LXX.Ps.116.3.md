**LXX Psalm 116:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] pangs of death compassed me; \[the\] dangers of Hades found me; I found affliction and grief. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The sorrows of death compassed me, and the pains of hell gat hold upon me: I found trouble and sorrow.

The ropes of death entangled me; the anguish of Sheol overcame me; I was confronted by trouble and sorrow.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
