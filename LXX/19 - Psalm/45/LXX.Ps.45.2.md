**LXX Psalm 45:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[More\] beautiful in beauty than the sons  of men. Favor was poured out on your lips. On account of this God blessed you into the eon. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thou art fairer than the children of men: grace is poured into thy lips: therefore God hath blessed thee for ever.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You are the most handsome of men; grace has anointed your lips; therefore God has blessed you forever.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
