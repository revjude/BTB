**LXX Psalm 101:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The one speaking ill in private of the ones near him -- this one I drove out. Proud eye and insatiable heart -- with this one I ate not. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Whoso privily slandereth his neighbour, him will I cut off: him that hath an high look and a proud heart will not I suffer.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Whoever slanders his neighbor in secret, I will put to silence; the one with haughty eyes and a proud heart, I will not endure.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
