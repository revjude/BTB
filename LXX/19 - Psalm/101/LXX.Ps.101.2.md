**LXX Psalm 101:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I shall strum, and I shall perceive in an unblemished way. When shall you come to me? I traveled in \[the\] innocence of my heart; in \[the\] midst  of my house. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I will behave myself wisely in a perfect way. O when wilt thou come unto me? I will walk within my house with a perfect heart.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I will ponder the way that is blameless—when will You come to me? I will walk in my house with integrity of heart.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
