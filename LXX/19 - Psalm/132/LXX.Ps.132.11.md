\[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> swore by an oath \[to\] David in truth, and in no way shall he annul it, \[saying\], I will establish upon your throne from \[the\] fruit of your belly.

The LORD hath sworn [in] truth unto David; he will not turn from it; Of the fruit of thy body will I set upon thy throne.
