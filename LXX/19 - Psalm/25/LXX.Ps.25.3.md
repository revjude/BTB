**LXX Psalm 25:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For all the \[ones\] remaining \[with\] you in no way shall be disgraced. the ones acting lawlessly without cause be ashamed! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Yea, let none that wait on thee be ashamed: let them be ashamed which transgress without cause.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Surely none who wait for You will be put to shame; but those who engage in treachery without cause will be disgraced.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
