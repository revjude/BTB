**LXX Psalm 71:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
the ones slandering my soul be shamed and fail! the ones seeking bad \[things\] for me put on shame and remorse! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Let them be confounded [and] consumed that are adversaries to my soul; let them be covered [with] reproach and dishonour that seek my hurt.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
May the accusers of my soul be ashamed and consumed; may those who seek my harm be covered with scorn and disgrace.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
