**LXX Psalm 71:1** 

***The LORD is Our Endurance***

 A psalm to David  of \[the\] sons of Jonadab, and of the ones originally being taken captive. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Upon you, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, I hoped. May I not be disgraced into the eon. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
In thee, O LORD, do I put my trust: let me never be put to confusion.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
In You, O LORD, I have taken refuge; let me never be put to shame.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
