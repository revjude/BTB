**LXX Psalm 71:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
From \[the\] womb I stayed upon you; from \[the\] belly of my mother you are my shelterer. My singing praise \[is\] always about you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
By thee have I been holden up from the womb: thou art he that took me out of my mother’s bowels: my praise [shall be] continually of thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I have leaned on You since birth; You pulled me from my mother’s womb. My praise is always for You.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
