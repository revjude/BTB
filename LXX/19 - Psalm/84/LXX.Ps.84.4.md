**LXX Psalm 84:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Blessed \[are\] the ones dwelling in  your house; into the eons of the eons they shall praise you. PAUSE.

Blessed [are] they that dwell in thy house: they will be still praising thee. Selah.
