**LXX Psalm 93:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>

The LORD on high [is] mightier than the noise of many waters, [yea, than] the mighty waves of the sea.

Above the roar of many waters—the mighty breakers of the sea—the LORD on high is majestic.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
