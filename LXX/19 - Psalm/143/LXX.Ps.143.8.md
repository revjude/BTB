**LXX Psalm 143:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Cause your mercy \[to be\] audible to me in the morning! for upon you I hoped. Make known to me, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, \[the\] way in which I shall go! for to you I lifted  my soul. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Cause me to hear thy lovingkindness in the morning; for in thee do I trust: cause me to know the way wherein I should walk; for I lift up my soul unto thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Let me hear Your loving devotion in the morning, for I have put my trust in You. Teach me the way I should walk, for to You I lift up my soul.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
