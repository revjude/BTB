**LXX Psalm 143:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you should not enter into judgment with  your servant, for all \[the\] living do justice not before you.

And enter not into judgment with thy servant: for in thy sight shall no man living be justified.

Do not bring Your servant into judgment, for no one alive is righteous before You.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
