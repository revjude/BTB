**LXX Psalm 74:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Rise up, O God, adjudicate  your cause! Remember  your scorning by the fool \[the\] entire  day!

Arise, O God, plead thine own cause: remember how the foolish man reproacheth thee daily.
