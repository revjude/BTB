**LXX Psalm 74:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Lift up  your hands against  their pride unto completion! \[for\] as much as the enemy did wickedly in  your holy place. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Lift up thy feet unto the perpetual desolations; [even] all [that] the enemy hath done wickedly in the sanctuary.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Turn Your steps to the everlasting ruins, to everything in the sanctuary the enemy has destroyed.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
