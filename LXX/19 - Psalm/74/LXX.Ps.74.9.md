**LXX Psalm 74:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
 We did not see our signs; there is not yet a prophet; and \[one of\] us does not know any longer. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
We see not our signs: [there is] no more any prophet: neither [is there] among us any that knoweth how long.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
There are no signs for us to see. There is no longer any prophet. And none of us knows how long this will last.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
