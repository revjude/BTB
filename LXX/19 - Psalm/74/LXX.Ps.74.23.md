**LXX Psalm 74:23** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You should not forget the voice  of your servants. The pride of the ones detesting you ascended continually. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Forget not the voice of thine enemies: the tumult of those that rise up against thee increaseth continually.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Do not disregard the clamor of Your adversaries, the uproar of Your enemies that ascends continually.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
