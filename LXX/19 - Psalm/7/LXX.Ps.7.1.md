**LXX Psalm 7:1** 

***The LORD Rescues the Innocent***

A psalm \[to\] David which he sang to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, because of the words of Cush son of Benjamin. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  my God, upon you I hope. Deliver me from all the ones pursuing me, and rescue me! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Shiggaion of David, which he sang unto the LORD, concerning the words of Cush the Benjamite. O LORD my God, in thee do I put my trust: save me from all them that persecute me, and deliver me:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite. O LORD my God, I take refuge in You; save me and deliver me from all my pursuers,<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
