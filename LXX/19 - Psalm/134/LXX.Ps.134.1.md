**LXX Psalm 134:1** 

***Bless The LORD***

An ode of the steps. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Behold indeed, bless the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> all \[you\] servants of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, the ones standing in \[the\] house of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, in \[the\] courtyards of \[the\] house of our God in the night! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A Song of degrees. Behold, bless ye the LORD, all [ye] servants of the LORD, which by night stand in the house of the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A song of ascents. Come, bless the LORD, all you servants of the LORD who serve by night in the house of the LORD!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
