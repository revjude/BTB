**LXX Psalm 111:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] beginning of wisdom \[is\] fear of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; and good understanding to all the ones observing it. The praise of him abides into the eon of the eon. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The fear of the LORD [is] the beginning of wisdom: a good understanding have all they that do [his commandments]: his praise endureth for ever.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom; all who follow His precepts gain rich understanding. His praise endures forever!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
