**LXX Psalm 73:28** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But for me  to cleave to  God is good; to put my hope in the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>;  for me to declare all  your praises at the gates of the daughter of Zion. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But [it is] good for me to draw near to God: I have put my trust in the Lord GOD, that I may declare all thy works.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
But as for me, it is good to draw near to God. I have made the Lord GOD my refuge, that I may proclaim all Your works.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
