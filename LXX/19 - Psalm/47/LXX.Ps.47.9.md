**LXX Psalm 47:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Rulers of peoples are brought together with the God of Abraham. For  by God the strong ones of the earth were lifted up exceedingly. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The princes of the people are gathered together, [even] the people of the God of Abraham: for the shields of the earth [belong] unto God: he is greatly exalted.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The nobles of the nations have assembled as the people of the God of Abraham; for the shields of the earth belong to God; He is highly exalted.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
