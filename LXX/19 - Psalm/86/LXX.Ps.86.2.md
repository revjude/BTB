**LXX Psalm 86:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Guard  my soul, for I am sacred! Deliver  your servant, O my God! the one hoping upon you.

Preserve my soul; for I [am] holy: O thou my God, save thy servant that trusteth in thee.
