**LXX Psalm 59:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[For the\] sin of their mouth, \[and the\] word of their lips, even let them be seized in  their pride! even for cursing and lying \[which\] they shall declare.

[For] the sin of their mouth [and] the words of their lips let them even be taken in their pride: and for cursing and lying [which] they speak.

By the sins of their mouths and the words of their lips, let them be trapped in their pride, in the curses and lies they utter.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
