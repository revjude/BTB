**LXX Psalm 95:1** 

***The LORD Remembers His People***

A praise of an ode \[to\] David. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Come, we should make exultation to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; we should shout \[to\] God  our deliverer. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
O come, let us sing unto the LORD: let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Come, let us sing for joy to the LORD; let us shout to the Rock of our salvation!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
