**LXX Psalm 128:1** 

***Happy Are the Ones Fearing The LORD***

An ode of the steps. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Blessed \[are\] all the ones fearing the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; the ones going in  his ways. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A Song of degrees. Blessed [is] every one that feareth the LORD; that walketh in his ways.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A song of ascents. Blessed are all who fear the LORD, who walk in His ways!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
