**LXX Psalm 37:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Submit to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, and entreat him! Be not provoked to jealousy in the prospering by a man doing unlawfulness in his way! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Rest in the LORD, and wait patiently for him: fret not thyself because of him who prospereth in his way, because of the man who bringeth wicked devices to pass.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Be still before the LORD and wait patiently for Him; do not fret when men prosper in their ways, when they carry out wicked schemes.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
