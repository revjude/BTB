**LXX Psalm 149:1** 

***The LORD Takes Pleasure in His People***

Alleluiah. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Sing to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> a new song,  of his praise in \[the\] assembly of \[the\] sacred ones!
