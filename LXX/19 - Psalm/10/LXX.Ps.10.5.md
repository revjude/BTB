**LXX Psalm 10:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
his ways are profane at all time; your judgments are taken away from in front of him; he shall dominate all his enemies. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
His ways are always grievous; thy judgments [are] far above out of his sight: [as for] all his enemies, he puffeth at them.

He is secure in his ways at all times; Your lofty judgments are far from him; he sneers at all his foes.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
