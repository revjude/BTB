**LXX Psalm 12:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
the ones having said,  We will magnify our tongues;  our lips are for us; who is our lord?

Who have said, With our tongue will we prevail; our lips [are] our own: who [is] lord over us?
