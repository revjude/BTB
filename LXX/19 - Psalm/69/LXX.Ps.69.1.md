**LXX Psalm 69:1** 

***The LORD Delivers the Aching Soul***

To the director; for the changings; a psalm \[to\] David. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Deliver me, O God! for \[the\] waters entered into my soul. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician upon Shoshannim, [A Psalm] of David. Save me, O God; for the waters are come in unto [my] soul.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David. Save me, O God, for the waters are up to my neck.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
