**LXX Psalm 69:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I tired crying out; my throat is sore. My eyes failed from  my hope upon  my God. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I am weary of my crying: my throat is dried: mine eyes fail while I wait for my God.

I am weary from my crying; my throat is parched. My eyes fail, looking for my God.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
