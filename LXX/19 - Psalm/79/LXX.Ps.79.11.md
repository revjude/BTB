**LXX Psalm 79:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
the moaning of the ones being shackled enter before you! According to the greatness  of your arm protect the sons of the ones being put to death! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Let the sighing of the prisoner come before thee; according to the greatness of thy power preserve thou those that are appointed to die;
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
May the groans of the captives reach You; by the strength of Your arm preserve those condemned to death.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
