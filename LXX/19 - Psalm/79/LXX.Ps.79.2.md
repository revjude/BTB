**LXX Psalm 79:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
They made the decaying flesh  of your servants foods for the winged creatures of the heaven; the flesh  of your sacred ones for the wild beasts of the earth. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The dead bodies of thy servants have they given [to be] meat unto the fowls of the heaven, the flesh of thy saints unto the beasts of the earth.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
They have given the corpses of Your servants as food to the birds of the air, the flesh of Your saints to the beasts of the earth.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
