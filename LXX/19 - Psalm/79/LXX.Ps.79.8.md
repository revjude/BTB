**LXX Psalm 79:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You should not remember our lawless deeds of old. Quickly your compassions be first to take us! for exceedingly poor. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
O remember not against us former iniquities: let thy tender mercies speedily prevent us: for we are brought very low.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Do not hold past sins against us; let Your compassion come quickly, for we are brought low.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
