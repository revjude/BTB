**LXX Psalm 43:1** 

***Acknowledge God with the Heart***

A psalm \[to\] David. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Judge for me, O God, and adjudicate  my cause, from a nation \[which is\] not sacred! rescue me from a man unjust and deceitful!
