**LXX Psalm 99:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And \[the\] honor of \[the\] king loves equity. You prepared straight judgment; and you established righteousness in Jacob. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The king’s strength also loveth judgment; thou dost establish equity, thou executest judgment and righteousness in Jacob.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The mighty King loves justice. You have established equity; You have exercised justice and righteousness in Jacob.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
