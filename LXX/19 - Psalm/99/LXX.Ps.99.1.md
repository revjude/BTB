**LXX Psalm 99:1** 

***The LORD is Holy***

A psalm \[to\] David. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> reigned. peoples be angry! \[He is\] the one sitting upon the cherubim. the earth be shaken! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The LORD reigneth; let the people tremble: he sitteth [between] the cherubims; let the earth be moved.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The LORD reigns; let the nations tremble! He is enthroned above the cherubim; let the earth quake!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
