**LXX Psalm 27:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Do not deliver me into \[the\] souls of ones afflicting me! For unjust witnesses rose up against me; for unjustness lied to herself. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Deliver me not over unto the will of mine enemies: for false witnesses are risen up against me, and such as breathe out cruelty.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Do not hand me over to the will of my foes, for false witnesses rise up against me, breathing out violence.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
