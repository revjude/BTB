**LXX Psalm 27:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
If a camp should deploy against me, my heart shall not fear. If war should rise up against me, in this I will be hopeful. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Though an host should encamp against me, my heart shall not fear: though war should rise against me, in this [will] I [be] confident.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Though an army encamps around me, my heart will not fear; though a war breaks out against me, I will keep my trust.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
