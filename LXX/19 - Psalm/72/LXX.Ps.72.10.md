**LXX Psalm 72:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Kings of Tarshish, and the islands shall bring gifts. \[The\] kings of \[the\] Arabians and Sheba will come forward \[with\] gifts.

The kings of Tarshish and of the isles shall bring presents: the kings of Sheba and Seba shall offer gifts.

May the kings of Tarshish and distant shores bring tribute; may the kings of Sheba and Seba offer gifts.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
