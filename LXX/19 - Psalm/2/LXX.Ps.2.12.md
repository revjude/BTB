**LXX Psalm 2:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Grab instruction! lest at any time you should provoke \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> to anger, and you shall perish from \[the\] just way, whenever his rage should be kindled quickly. Blessed \[are\] all the ones yielding upon him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Kiss the Son, lest he be angry, and ye perish [from] the way, when his wrath is kindled but a little. Blessed [are] all they that put their trust in him.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Kiss the Son, lest He be angry and you perish in your rebellion, when His wrath ignites in an instant. Blessed are all who take refuge in Him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
