**LXX Psalm 144:12** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
whom  their sons \[are\] as newly planted, secure in  their youth;  their daughters being bedecked, being adorned sumptuously as \[the\] likeness of a temple; 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
That our sons [may be] as plants grown up in their youth; [that] our daughters [may be] as corner stones, polished [after] the similitude of a palace:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Then our sons will be like plants nurtured in their youth, our daughters like corner pillars carved to adorn a palace.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
