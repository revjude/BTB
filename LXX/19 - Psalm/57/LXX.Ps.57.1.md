**LXX Psalm 57:1** 

***The LORD Rescues***

To the director; do not utterly destroy; \[to\] David; for an inscription on a monument; in  his running away from \[the\] face of Saul into the cave. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Show mercy on me, O God, show mercy on me! For my soul yielded upon you. And in the shadow  of your wings I shall hope, until of which \[time\] lawlessness should go by.
