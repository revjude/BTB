**LXX Psalm 41:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
May \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> guard him, and enliven him, and bless him in the land; and may he not deliver him into \[the\] hands of his enemies. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The LORD will preserve him, and keep him alive; [and] he shall be blessed upon the earth: and thou wilt not deliver him unto the will of his enemies.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The LORD will protect and preserve him; He will bless him in the land and refuse to give him over to the will of his foes.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
