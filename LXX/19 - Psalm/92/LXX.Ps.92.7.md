**LXX Psalm 92:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In the rising up of the sinners as grass, and all the ones working lawlessness look on, that thus they shall be utterly destroyed into the eon of the eon. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
When the wicked spring as the grass, and when all the workers of iniquity do flourish; [it is] that they shall be destroyed for ever:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
that though the wicked sprout like grass, and all evildoers flourish, they will be forever destroyed.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
