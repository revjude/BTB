**LXX Psalm 92:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For you gladdened me, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, by  your action; and in the works  of your hands I shall exult.

For thou, LORD, hast made me glad through thy work: I will triumph in the works of thy hands.

For You, O LORD, have made me glad by Your deeds; I sing for joy at the works of Your hands.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
