**LXX Psalm 67:1** 

***God Guides the Nations***

To the director; among hymns; a psalm of an ode; \[to\] David. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
 God, may he pity us, and bless us, to shine  his face upon us, and to show mercy on us; 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician on Neginoth, A Psalm [or] Song. God be merciful unto us, and bless us; [and] cause his face to shine upon us; Selah.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm. A song. May God be gracious to us and bless us, and cause His face to shine upon us, Selah<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
