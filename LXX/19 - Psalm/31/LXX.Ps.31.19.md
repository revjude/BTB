**LXX Psalm 31:19** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
How abundant \[is\] the multitude  of your graciousness, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, of which you hid to the ones fearing you. You brought it to completion to the ones hoping upon you, before the sons  of men.

[Oh] how great [is] thy goodness, which thou hast laid up for them that fear thee; [which] thou hast wrought for them that trust in thee before the sons of men!

How great is Your goodness which You have laid up for those who fear You, which You have bestowed before the sons of men on those who take refuge in You!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
