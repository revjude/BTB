**LXX Psalm 137:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
my tongue cleave \[to\] my throat if I do not remember you; if I do not prefer  Jerusalem as in \[the\] beginning  of my gladness. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
If I do not remember thee, let my tongue cleave to the roof of my mouth; if I prefer not Jerusalem above my chief joy.

May my tongue cling to the roof of my mouth if I do not remember you, if I do not exalt Jerusalem as my greatest joy!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
