**LXX Psalm 88:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You distanced from me the ones knowing me. You made me an abomination to them. I have been delivered up, and have not gone forth. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thou hast put away mine acquaintance far from me; thou hast made me an abomination unto them: [I am] shut up, and I cannot come forth.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You have removed my friends from me; You have made me repulsive to them; I am confined and cannot escape.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
