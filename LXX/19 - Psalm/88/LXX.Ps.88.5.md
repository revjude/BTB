**LXX Psalm 88:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
free among \[the\] dead, as ones slain sleeping in \[the\] tomb, whom you do not remember any longer; and they were thrusted away from out of your hand. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Free among the dead, like the slain that lie in the grave, whom thou rememberest no more: and they are cut off from thy hand.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I am forsaken among the dead, like the slain who lie in the grave, whom You remember no more, who are cut off from Your care.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
