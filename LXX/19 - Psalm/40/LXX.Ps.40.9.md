**LXX Psalm 40:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I announced good news -- righteousness in \[the\] great assembly. Behold,  in no way should I restrain my lips, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, you know.

I have preached righteousness in the great congregation: lo, I have not refrained my lips, O LORD, thou knowest.

I proclaim righteousness in the great assembly; behold, I do not seal my lips, as You, O LORD, do know.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
