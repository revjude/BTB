**LXX Psalm 75:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The earth is melted away and all the ones dwelling in it. PAUSE. I solidified  its columns.

The earth and all the inhabitants thereof are dissolved: I bear up the pillars of it. Selah.
