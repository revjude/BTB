**LXX Psalm 133:1** 

***The Blessing of Unity***

An ode of the steps. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Behold, indeed what \[is\] good or what \[is\] delightful, none other than the dwelling of \[the\] brethren together. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A Song of degrees of David. Behold, how good and how pleasant [it is] for brethren to dwell together in unity!
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A song of ascents. Of David. Behold, how good and pleasant it is when brothers live together in harmony!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
