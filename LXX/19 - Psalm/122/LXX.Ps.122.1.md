**LXX Psalm 122:1** 

***Confess the Name of The LORD***

An ode of the steps. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I shall be glad over the ones saying to me, We shall go into \[the\] house \[of the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A Song of degrees of David. I was glad when they said unto me, Let us go into the house of the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A song of ascents. Of David. I was glad when they said to me, “Let us go to the house of the LORD.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
