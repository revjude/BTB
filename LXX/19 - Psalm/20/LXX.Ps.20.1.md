**LXX Psalm 20:1** 

***The Name of The LORD is a Shield***

To the director; a psalm \[to\] David. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> heed you in \[the\] day of affliction! the name of the God of Jacob shield you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician, A Psalm of David. The LORD hear thee in the day of trouble; the name of the God of Jacob defend thee;
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. May the LORD answer you in the day of trouble; may the name of the God of Jacob protect you.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
