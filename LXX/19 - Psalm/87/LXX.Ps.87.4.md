**LXX Psalm 87:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I shall make mention of Rahab and Babylon to the ones knowing me; and behold, \[the\] Philistines and Tyre, and \[the\] people of \[the\] Ethiopians; these were there. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon to them that know me: behold Philistia, and Tyre, with Ethiopia; this [man] was born there.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“I will mention Rahab and Babylon among those who know Me—along with Philistia, Tyre, and Cush—when I say, ‘This one was born in Zion.’”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
