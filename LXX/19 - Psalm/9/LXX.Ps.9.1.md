**LXX Psalm 9:1** 

***The LORD Abides unto the Eon***

To the director; for the private \[things\] of the son; a psalm \[to\] David. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I shall make acknowledgment to you, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, with my entire heart. I shall describe all  your wonders. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician upon Muthlabben, A Psalm of David. I will praise [thee], O LORD, with my whole heart; I will shew forth all thy marvellous works.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David. I will give thanks to the LORD with all my heart; I will recount all Your wonders.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
