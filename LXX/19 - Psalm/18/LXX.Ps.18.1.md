**LXX Psalm 18:1** 

***The LORD is Our Deliverance***

To the director; to the servant of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; \[to\] David; what he spoke to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; the words  of this ode, in \[the\] day in which the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> rescued him from out of \[the\] hand of all  his enemies, and from out of \[the\] hand of Saul. And he said; 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I shall love you, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, of my strength.
