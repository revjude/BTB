**LXX Psalm 18:1** 

***The LORD is Our Deliverance***

To the director; to the servant of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; \[to\] David; what he spoke to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>; the words  of this ode, in \[the\] day in which the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> rescued him from out of \[the\] hand of all  his enemies, and from out of \[the\] hand of Saul. And he said; 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I shall love you, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, of my strength. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician, [A Psalm] of David, the servant of the LORD, who spake unto the LORD the words of this song in the day [that] the LORD delivered him from the hand of all his enemies, and from the hand of Saul: And he said, I will love thee, O LORD, my strength.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the choirmaster. Of David the servant of the LORD, who sang this song to the LORD on the day the LORD had delivered him from the hand of all his enemies and from the hand of Saul. He said: I love You, O LORD, my strength.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
