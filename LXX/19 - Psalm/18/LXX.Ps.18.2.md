**LXX Psalm 18:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> \[is\] my firmament, and my refuge, and my rescuer. My God \[is\] my helper, and I will hope upon him; my defender and horn of my deliverance, and my shielder.

The LORD [is] my rock, and my fortress, and my deliverer; my God, my strength, in whom I will trust; my buckler, and the horn of my salvation, [and] my high tower.
