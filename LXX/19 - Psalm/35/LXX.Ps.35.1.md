**LXX Psalm 35:1** 

***The LORD Pleads Our Cause***

A psalm \[to\] David. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Adjudicate, O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, the ones wronging me! Wage war \[against\] the ones waging war \[against\] me! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[A Psalm] of David. Plead [my cause], O LORD, with them that strive with me: fight against them that fight against me.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Of David. Contend with my opponents, O LORD; fight against those who fight against me.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
