**LXX Psalm 97:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
All the ones doing obeisance to the carvings were shamed; the ones boasting in  their idols. all his angels do obeisance to him! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Confounded be all they that serve graven images, that boast themselves of idols: worship him, all [ye] gods.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
All worshipers of images are put to shame—those who boast in idols. Worship Him, all you gods!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
