**LXX Psalm 44:1** 

***God is Our Deliverer***

To the director; to the sons of Korah; for contemplation. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O God, we heard with our ears, and  our fathers announced to us \[the\] work which you worked in  their days, in ancient days. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician for the sons of Korah, Maschil. We have heard with our ears, O God, our fathers have told us, [what] work thou didst in their days, in the times of old.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah. We have heard with our ears, O God; our fathers have told us the work You did in their days, in the days of old.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
