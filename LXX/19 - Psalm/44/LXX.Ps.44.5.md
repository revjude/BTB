**LXX Psalm 44:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In you  we will gore our enemies, and in  your name we will treat with contempt the ones rising up against us. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Through thee will we push down our enemies: through thy name will we tread them under that rise up against us.

Through You we repel our foes; through Your name we trample our enemies.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
