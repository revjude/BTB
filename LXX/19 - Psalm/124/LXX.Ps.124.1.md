**LXX Psalm 124:1** 

***Our Help is in the Name of The LORD***

An ode of the steps. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Unless it be that \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> was with us -- Israel now say!

A Song of degrees of David. If [it had] not [been] the LORD who was on our side, now may Israel say;

A song of ascents. Of David. If the LORD had not been on our side—let Israel now declare—<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
