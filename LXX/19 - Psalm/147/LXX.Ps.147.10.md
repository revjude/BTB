**LXX Psalm 147:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Not by the might of the horse does he want; nor by the lower legs  of a man does he think well of. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He delighteth not in the strength of the horse: he taketh not pleasure in the legs of a man.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He does not delight in the strength of the horse; He takes no pleasure in the legs of a man.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
