**LXX Psalm 147:1** 

***Great is Our LORD***

Alleluiah. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Praise the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, for a psalm \[is\] good! be delicious praise \[to\] our God. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Praise ye the LORD: for [it is] good to sing praises unto our God; for [it is] pleasant; [and] praise is comely.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Hallelujah! How good it is to sing praises to our God, how pleasant and lovely to praise Him!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
