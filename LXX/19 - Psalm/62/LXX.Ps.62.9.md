**LXX Psalm 62:9** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But the sons of men \[are\] vain; the sons of men \[are\] false \[weights\] in \[the\] yoke \[balance scales\]  to do wrong. They \[are\] of folly all together. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Surely men of low degree [are] vanity, [and] men of high degree [are] a lie: to be laid in the balance, they [are] altogether [lighter] than vanity.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Lowborn men are but a vapor; the exalted are but a lie. Weighed on the scale, they go up; together they are but a vapor.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
