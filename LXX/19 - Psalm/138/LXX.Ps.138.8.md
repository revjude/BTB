**LXX Psalm 138:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> shall recompense for me. O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>,  your mercy \[is\] into the eon. The works  of your hands you should not ignore. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The LORD will perfect [that which] concerneth me: thy mercy, O LORD, [endureth] for ever: forsake not the works of thine own hands.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The LORD will fulfill His purpose for me. O LORD, Your loving devotion endures forever—do not abandon the works of Your hands.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
