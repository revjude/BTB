**LXX Psalm 138:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I shall do obeisance towards your holy temple, and I shall make acknowledgment \[to\] your name concerning  your mercy and  your truth; for you magnified your holy name above all. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
I will worship toward thy holy temple, and praise thy name for thy lovingkindness and for thy truth: for thou hast magnified thy word above all thy name.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
I bow down toward Your holy temple and give thanks to Your name for Your loving devotion and Your faithfulness; You have exalted Your name and Your word above all else.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
