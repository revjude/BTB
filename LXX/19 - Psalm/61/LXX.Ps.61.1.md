**LXX Psalm 61:1** 

***Fear God and Abide unto the Eon***

To the director; among \[the\] hymns \[to\] David; a psalm.

To the chief Musician upon Neginah, [A Psalm] of David. Hear my cry, O God; attend unto my prayer.
