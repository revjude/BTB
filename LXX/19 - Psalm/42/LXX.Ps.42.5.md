**LXX Psalm 42:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Why are you dejected, O my soul? and why do you disturb me? Hope upon  God! for I shall acknowledge to him \[the\] deliverance  of my person. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and [why] art thou disquieted in me? hope thou in God: for I shall yet praise him [for] the help of his countenance.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Why are you downcast, O my soul? Why the unease within me? Put your hope in God, for I will yet praise Him for the salvation of His presence.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
