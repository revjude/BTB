**LXX Psalm 42:1** 

***The Disturbed Soul Thirsts for God***

To the director; for contemplation to the sons of Korah. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In which manner the stag longs after the springs of the waters, so my soul longs after you, O God. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician, Maschil, for the sons of Korah. As the hart panteth after the water brooks, so panteth my soul after thee, O God.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah. As the deer pants for streams of water, so my soul longs after You, O God.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
