**LXX Psalm 42:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Why are you dejected, O my soul? and why do you disturb me? Hope upon  God! for I shall acknowledge to him \[the\] deliverance  of my person, and  my God. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted within me? hope thou in God: for I shall yet praise him, [who is] the health of my countenance, and my God.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Why are you downcast, O my soul? Why the unease within me? Put your hope in God, for I will yet praise Him, my Savior and my God.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
