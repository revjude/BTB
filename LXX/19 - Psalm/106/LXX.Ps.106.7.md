**LXX Psalm 106:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
 Our fathers in Egypt did not perceive  your wonders, they remembered not the magnitude  of your mercy; and they rebelled \[while\] ascending unto the red sea.

Our fathers understood not thy wonders in Egypt; they remembered not the multitude of thy mercies; but provoked [him] at the sea, [even] at the Red sea.

Our fathers in Egypt did not grasp Your wonders or remember Your abundant kindness; but they rebelled by the sea, there at the Red Sea.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
