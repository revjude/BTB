**LXX Psalm 94:1** 

***The LORD Recompenses Evil***

A psalm \[to\] David; fourth of Sabbath. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] God of acts of vengeance. \[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  God of acts of vengeance spoke openly.
