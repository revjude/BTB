**LXX Psalm 102:13** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
In your rising up you shall pity  Zion, for \[it is\] time  to pity it, for \[the\] time comes. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thou shalt arise, [and] have mercy upon Zion: for the time to favour her, yea, the set time, is come.

You will rise up and have compassion on Zion, for it is time to show her favor—the appointed time has come.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
