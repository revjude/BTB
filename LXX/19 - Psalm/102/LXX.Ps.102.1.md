**LXX Psalm 102:1** 

***The Discouraged Hope in The LORD***

A prayer to the poor whenever he is discouraged, and he should pour out his supplication. before \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, listen to  my prayer, and  come my cry unto you! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
A Prayer of the afflicted, when he is overwhelmed, and poureth out his complaint before the LORD. Hear my prayer, O LORD, and let my cry come unto thee.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD. Hear my prayer, O LORD; let my cry for help come before You.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
