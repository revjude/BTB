**LXX Psalm 70:1** 

***The LORD is My Helper***

To the director; \[to\] David; for remembrance in  my being delivered by \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
O God, for  my help heed \[me\]! O <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, hasten to help me! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician, [A Psalm] of David, to bring to remembrance. [Make haste], O God, to deliver me; make haste to help me, O LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the choirmaster. Of David. To bring remembrance. Make haste, O God, to deliver me! Hurry, O LORD, to help me!<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
