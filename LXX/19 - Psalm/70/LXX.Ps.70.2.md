**LXX Psalm 70:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
the ones seeking my life be ashamed and feel remorse! the ones wanting bad \[things\] for me be turned to the rear and be disgraced! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Let them be ashamed and confounded that seek after my soul: let them be turned backward, and put to confusion, that desire my hurt.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
May those who seek my life be ashamed and confounded; may those who wish me harm be repelled and humiliated.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
