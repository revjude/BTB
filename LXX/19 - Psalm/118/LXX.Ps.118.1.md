**LXX Psalm 118:1** 

***The Mercy of The LORD is unto the Eon***

Alleluiah 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Make acknowledgment to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, for \[he is\] good, for into the eon \[is\] his mercy! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
O give thanks unto the LORD; for [he is] good: because his mercy [endureth] for ever.

Give thanks to the LORD, for He is good; His loving devotion endures forever.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
