**LXX Psalm 109:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And he put on \[the\] curse as a cloak, and it entered as water into  his insides, and as oil in  his bones.

As he clothed himself with cursing like as with his garment, so let it come into his bowels like water, and like oil into his bones.

The cursing that he wore like a coat, may it soak into his body like water, and into his bones like oil.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
