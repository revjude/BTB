**LXX Psalm 109:30** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
I will make acknowledgment to the <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> -- exceedingly. With  my mouth and in \[the\] midst of many I will praise him.

I will greatly praise the LORD with my mouth; yea, I will praise him among the multitude.
