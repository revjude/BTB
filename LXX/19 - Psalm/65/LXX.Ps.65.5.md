**LXX Psalm 65:5** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Wonderful in righteousness -- heed us, O God  our deliverer! \[He is\] the hope of all the ends of the earth, and of the ones in \[the\] sea afar; 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[By] terrible things in righteousness wilt thou answer us, O God of our salvation; [who art] the confidence of all the ends of the earth, and of them that are afar off [upon] the sea:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
With awesome deeds of righteousness You answer us, O God of our salvation, the hope of all the ends of the earth and of the farthest seas.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
