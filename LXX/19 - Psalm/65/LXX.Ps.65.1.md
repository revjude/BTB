**LXX Psalm 65:1** 

***The Earth is The LORD's***

To the director; a psalm of an ode; \[to\] David. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
A hymn is becoming to you, O God, in Zion; and to you \[the\] vow shall be rendered in Jerusalem. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician, A Psalm [and] Song of David. Praise waiteth for thee, O God, in Sion: and unto thee shall the vow be performed.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song. Praise awaits You, O God, in Zion; to You our vows will be fulfilled.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
