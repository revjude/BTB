**LXX Psalm 58:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
They shall be treated with contempt as water going by. He shall stretch tight  his bow until of which \[time\] they shall be weakened. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Let them melt away as waters [which] run continually: [when] he bendeth [his bow to shoot] his arrows, let them be as cut in pieces.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
May they vanish like water that runs off; when they draw the bow, may their arrows be blunted.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
