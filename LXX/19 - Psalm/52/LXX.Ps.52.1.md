**LXX Psalm 52:1** 

***God Demolishes the Deceitful***

To the director; for contemplation; \[to\] David, in the coming of Doeg the Edomite, and announcing  to Saul, and telling to him \[that\] David went to the house of Ahimelech. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Why do you boast in evil, O mighty \[man\], \[with\] lawlessness \[the\] entire  day? 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician, Maschil, [A Psalm] of David, when Doeg the Edomite came and told Saul, and said unto him, David is come to the house of Ahimelech. Why boastest thou thyself in mischief, O mighty man? the goodness of God [endureth] continually.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the choirmaster. A Maskil of David. After Doeg the Edomite went to Saul and told him, “David has gone to the house of Ahimelech.” Why do you boast of evil, O mighty man? The loving devotion of God endures all day long.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
