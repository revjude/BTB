**LXX Psalm 52:8** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
But I \[am\] as a fruitful olive tree in the house  of God. I hoped upon the mercy  of God into the eon, and into the eon of the eon.

But I [am] like a green olive tree in the house of God: I trust in the mercy of God for ever and ever.
