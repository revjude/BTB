**LXX Psalm 110:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
\[The\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> swore by an oath, and shall not repent, \[saying\], You are a priest unto the eon according to the order of Melchisedek. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The LORD hath sworn, and will not repent, Thou [art] a priest for ever after the order of Melchizedek.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The LORD has sworn and will not change His mind: “You are a priest forever in the order of Melchizedek.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
