**LXX Psalm 110:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
He shall judge among the nations; he shall fill up with corpses; he shall fracture in pieces heads upon \[the\] earth -- many.

He shall judge among the heathen, he shall fill [the places] with the dead bodies; he shall wound the heads over many countries.

He will judge the nations, heaping up the dead; He will crush the leaders far and wide.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
