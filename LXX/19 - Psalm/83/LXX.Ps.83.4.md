**LXX Psalm 83:4** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
They said, Come, for we should utterly destroy them from out of \[the\] nations; for in no way the name of Israel be remembered any longer. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
They have said, Come, and let us cut them off from [being] a nation; that the name of Israel may be no more in remembrance.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
saying, “Come, let us erase them as a nation; may the name of Israel be remembered no more.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
