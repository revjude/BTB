**LXX Psalm 64:1** 

***Be Glad in The LORD***

To the director; a psalm \[to\] David. 

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Hear, O God,  my voice! in  my beseeching to you. Rescue my soul from \[the\] fear of \[the\] enemy! 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
To the chief Musician, A Psalm of David. Hear my voice, O God, in my prayer: preserve my life from fear of the enemy.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. Hear, O God, my voice of complaint; preserve my life from dread of the enemy.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
