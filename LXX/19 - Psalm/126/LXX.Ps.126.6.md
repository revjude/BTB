**LXX Psalm 126:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
The ones going went and wept throwing  their seed; but the ones coming shall come in exultation, lifting  their sheaves. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
He that goeth forth and weepeth, bearing precious seed, shall doubtless come again with rejoicing, bringing his sheaves [with him].
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
He who goes out weeping, bearing a trail of seed, will surely return with shouts of joy, carrying sheaves of grain.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
