**LXX Psalm 89:7** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
 God \[is\] being glorified in \[the\] counsel of holy ones; he is great and fearful over all the ones surrounding him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
God is greatly to be feared in the assembly of the saints, and to be had in reverence of all [them that are] about him.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
In the council of the holy ones, God is greatly feared, and awesome above all who surround Him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
