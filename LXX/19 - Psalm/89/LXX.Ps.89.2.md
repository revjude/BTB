**LXX Psalm 89:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
For you said, Into the eon mercy shall be built up. Your truth shall be prepared in the heavens. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
For I have said, Mercy shall be built up for ever: thy faithfulness shalt thou establish in the very heavens.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For I have said, “Loving devotion is built up forever; in the heavens You establish Your faithfulness.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
