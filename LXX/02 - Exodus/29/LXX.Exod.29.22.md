**LXX Exodus 29:22** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you shall take from the ram  \[its\] fat, both the fat  covering up the belly, and the lobe of the liver, and the two kidneys, and the fat  upon them, and the right shoulder -- for this is a consecration. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Also thou shalt take of the ram the fat and the rump, and the fat that covereth the inwards, and the caul [above] the liver, and the two kidneys, and the fat that [is] upon them, and the right shoulder; for it [is] a ram of consecration:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Take the fat from the ram, the fat tail, the fat covering the entrails, the lobe of the liver, both kidneys with the fat on them, and the right thigh (since this is a ram for ordination),<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
