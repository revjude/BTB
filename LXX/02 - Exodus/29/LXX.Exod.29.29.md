**LXX Exodus 29:29** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the apparel of the holy place which is Aaron's, shall be \[to\] his sons' after him, for them to be anointed in them, and to perfect  their hands.

And the holy garments of Aaron shall be his sons’ after him, to be anointed therein, and to be consecrated in them.

The holy garments that belong to Aaron will belong to his sons after him, so they can be anointed and ordained in them.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
