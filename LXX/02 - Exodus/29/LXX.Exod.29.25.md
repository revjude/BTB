**LXX Exodus 29:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you shall take them from  their hands, and you shall offer \[them\] upon the altar of the whole burnt-offering as a scent of pleasant aroma before \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> -- it is a yield \[offering\] \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And thou shalt receive them of their hands, and burn [them] upon the altar for a burnt offering, for a sweet savour before the LORD: it [is] an offering made by fire unto the LORD.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Then take them from their hands and burn them on the altar atop the burnt offering as a pleasing aroma before the LORD; it is a food offering to the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
