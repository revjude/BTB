**LXX Exodus 29:37** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Seven days you shall cleanse the altar, and sanctify it. And the altar will be a holy of the holies; all  touching the altar shall be sanctified. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Seven days thou shalt make an atonement for the altar, and sanctify it; and it shall be an altar most holy: whatsoever toucheth the altar shall be holy.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
For seven days you shall make atonement for the altar and consecrate it. Then the altar will become most holy; whatever touches the altar will be holy.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
