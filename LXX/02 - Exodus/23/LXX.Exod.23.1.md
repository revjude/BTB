**LXX Exodus 23:1** 

***Judicial Conduct***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
You shall not welcome a vain report. You shall not assent together with the unjust to become an unjust witness. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Thou shalt not raise a false report: put not thine hand with the wicked to be an unrighteous witness.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“You shall not spread a false report. Do not join the wicked by being a malicious witness.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
