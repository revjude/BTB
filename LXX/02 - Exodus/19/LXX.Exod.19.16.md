**LXX Exodus 19:16** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And it came to pass  on the third day, happening towards dawn, and there were voices, and lightnings, and overcast clouds upon mount Sinai; \[the\] voice of the trumpet sounded greatly, and all the people in the camp were terrified.

And it came to pass on the third day in the morning, that there were thunders and lightnings, and a thick cloud upon the mount, and the voice of the trumpet exceeding loud; so that all the people that [was] in the camp trembled.
