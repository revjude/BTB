**LXX Exodus 6:14** 

***The Chiefs of Israel***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>

These [be] the heads of their fathers’ houses: The sons of Reuben the firstborn of Israel; Hanoch, and Pallu, Hezron, and Carmi: these [be] the families of Reuben.
