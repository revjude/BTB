**LXX Exodus 6:6** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Proceed, say to the sons of Israel! saying, I \[am\] \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>, and I will lead you from the domination of the Egyptians, and I will rescue you from  their slavery, and I will ransom you with a high arm and great judgment. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Wherefore say unto the children of Israel, I [am] the LORD, and I will bring you out from under the burdens of the Egyptians, and I will rid you out of their bondage, and I will redeem you with a stretched out arm, and with great judgments:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Therefore tell the Israelites: ‘I am the LORD, and I will bring you out from under the yoke of the Egyptians and deliver you from their bondage. I will redeem you with an outstretched arm and with mighty acts of judgment.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
