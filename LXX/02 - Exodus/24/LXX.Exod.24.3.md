**LXX Exodus 24:3** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And Moses entered and described to the people all the words  of God, and the ordinances. And all the people answered in one voice, saying, All the words which \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span> spoke, we will do, and we will hearken.

And Moses came and told the people all the words of the LORD, and all the judgments: and all the people answered with one voice, and said, All the words which the LORD hath said will we do.

When Moses came and told the people all the words and ordinances of the LORD, they all responded with one voice: “All the words that the LORD has spoken, we will do.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
