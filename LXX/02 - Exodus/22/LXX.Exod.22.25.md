**LXX Exodus 22:25** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if money should be lent with interest to the brother \[that is\] destitute close to you, you shall not be coercing him, you shall not interest upon him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
If thou lend money to [any of] my people [that is] poor by thee, thou shalt not be to him as an usurer, neither shalt thou lay upon him usury.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If you lend money to one of My people among you who is poor, you must not act as a creditor to him; you are not to charge him interest.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
