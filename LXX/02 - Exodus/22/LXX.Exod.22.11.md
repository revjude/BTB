**LXX Exodus 22:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
there shall be an oath  of God between both, \[swearing that\] assuredly he had not done wickedly to partake altogether of the deposit put in care \[by his\] neighbor, and thus his owner shall receive him favorably, and he shall not pay. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
[Then] shall an oath of the LORD be between them both, that he hath not put his hand unto his neighbour’s goods; and the owner of it shall accept [thereof], and he shall not make [it] good.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
an oath before the LORD shall be made between the parties to determine whether or not the man has taken his neighbor’s property. The owner must accept the oath and require no restitution.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
