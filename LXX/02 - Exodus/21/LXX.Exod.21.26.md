**LXX Exodus 21:26** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And if any should strike the eye  of his man-servant, or the eye  of his female attendant, and he should blind; he shall send them free on account  of their eye. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And if a man smite the eye of his servant, or the eye of his maid, that it perish; he shall let him go free for his eye’s sake.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
If a man strikes and blinds the eye of his manservant or maidservant, he must let the servant go free as compensation for the eye.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
