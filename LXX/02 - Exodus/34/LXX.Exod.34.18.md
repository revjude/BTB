**LXX Exodus 34:18** 

***Concerning the Holidays***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the holiday of the unleavened \[breads\] you shall guard; seven days you shall eat unleavened \[breads\], just as I gave charge to you, at the time in \[the\] month of the new \[produce\]. For in the month of the new \[produce\] you came forth out of Egypt. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
The feast of unleavened bread shalt thou keep. Seven days thou shalt eat unleavened bread, as I commanded thee, in the time of the month Abib: for in the month Abib thou camest out from Egypt.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You are to keep the Feast of Unleavened Bread. For seven days at the appointed time in the month of Abib, you are to eat unleavened bread as I commanded you. For in the month of Abib you came out of Egypt.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
