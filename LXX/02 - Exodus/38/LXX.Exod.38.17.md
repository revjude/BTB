**LXX Exodus 38:17** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And the bases, the ones of their posts \[were of\] brass, and  their hooks \[were of\] of silver, and  their tips being silver plated in silver; and the posts being silver plated in silver, all the posts of the courtyard. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And the sockets for the pillars [were of] brass; the hooks of the pillars and their fillets [of] silver; and the overlaying of their chapiters [of] silver; and all the pillars of the court [were] filleted with silver.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
The bases for the posts were bronze, the hooks and bands were silver, and the plating for the tops of the posts was silver. So all the posts of the courtyard were banded with silver.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
