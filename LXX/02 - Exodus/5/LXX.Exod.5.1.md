**LXX Exodus 5:1** 

***Moses and Aaron Before Pharaoh***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And after these \[things\] Moses and Aaron entered to Pharaoh, and they said to him, Thus says \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  God of Israel. Send forth  my people! that they may solemnize a holiday to me in the wilderness. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And afterward Moses and Aaron went in, and told Pharaoh, Thus saith the LORD God of Israel, Let my people go, that they may hold a feast unto me in the wilderness.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
After that, Moses and Aaron went to Pharaoh and said, “This is what the LORD, the God of Israel, says: ‘Let My people go, so that they may hold a feast to Me in the wilderness.’”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
