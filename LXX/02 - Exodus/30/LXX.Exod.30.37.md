**LXX Exodus 30:37** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
According to  this composition you shall not make to you yourselves -- it shall be a sanctified \[thing\] to you \[to the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And [as for] the perfume which thou shalt make, ye shall not make to yourselves according to the composition thereof: it shall be unto thee holy for the LORD.

You are never to use this formula to make incense for yourselves; you shall regard it as holy to the LORD.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
