**LXX Exodus 30:21** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
they shall wash \[their\] hands and  their feet in water, that they should not die. And it will be to them an eternal law, to him and  his generations after him. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
So they shall wash their hands and their feet, that they die not: and it shall be a statute for ever to them, [even] to him and to his seed throughout their generations.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Thus they are to wash their hands and feet so that they will not die; this shall be a permanent statute for Aaron and his descendants for the generations to come.”<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
