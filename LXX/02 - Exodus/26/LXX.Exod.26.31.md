**LXX Exodus 26:31** 

***The Veil***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you shall make a veil from out of blue, and purple, and scarlet being twined, and linen being spun. You shall make it a woven work \[with\] cherubim. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And thou shalt make a vail [of] blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine twined linen of cunning work: with cherubims shall it be made:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
Make a veil of blue, purple, and scarlet yarn, and finely spun linen, with cherubim skillfully worked into it.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
