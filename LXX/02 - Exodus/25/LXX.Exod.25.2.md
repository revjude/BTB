**LXX Exodus 25:2** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Speak to the sons of Israel! And take to me first-fruits of all the things which seem \[good\] in the heart! You shall take the first-fruits of mine. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
Speak unto the children of Israel, that they bring me an offering: of every man that giveth it willingly with his heart ye shall take my offering.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
“Tell the Israelites to bring Me an offering. You are to receive My offering from every man whose heart compels him.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
