**LXX Exodus 25:23** 

***The Table of the Bread Loaves***

<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And you shall make a table of incorruptible woods, of two cubits \[being\] the length, and a cubit the breadth, and a cubit and a half the height.

Thou shalt also make a table [of] shittim wood: two cubits [shall be] the length thereof, and a cubit the breadth thereof, and a cubit and a half the height thereof.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
You are also to make a table of acacia wood two cubits long, a cubit wide, and a cubit and a half high.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
