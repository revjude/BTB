**LXX Exodus 12:11** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
And thus shall you eat it -- \[with\] your loins girded, and the sandals on  your feet, and  your staff in  your hands; and you shall eat it with haste -- it is \[the\] passover of \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
And thus shall ye eat it; [with] your loins girded, your shoes on your feet, and your staff in your hand; and ye shall eat it in haste: it [is] the LORD’S passover.
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
This is how you are to eat it: You must be fully dressed for travel, with your sandals on your feet and your staff in your hand. You are to eat in haste; it is the LORD’s Passover.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
