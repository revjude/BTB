**LXX Exodus 12:18** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
Commencing the fourteenth day  of the first month, from evening you shall eat unleavened \[breads\] until \[the\] first and twentieth day of the month, until evening.

In the first [month], on the fourteenth day of the month at even, ye shall eat unleavened bread, until the one and twentieth day of the month at even.
