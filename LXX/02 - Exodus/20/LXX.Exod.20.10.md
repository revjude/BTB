**LXX Exodus 20:10** 
<small><sub>**LXX:**</sub></small>
but the seventh day \[is a\] Sabbath to \[the\] <span class="smallcaps">lord</span>  your God. You shall not do on it any work -- you, and  your son, and  your daughter,  your servant, and  your maidservant,  your ox, and  your beast of burden, and all your cattle, and the convert -- the \[one\] sojourning among you. 
<small><sub>**KJV:**</sub></small>
But the seventh day [is] the sabbath of the LORD thy God: [in it] thou shalt not do any work, thou, nor thy son, nor thy daughter, thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thy cattle, nor thy stranger that [is] within thy gates:
<small><sub>**BSB:**</sub></small>
but the seventh day is a Sabbath to the LORD your God, on which you must not do any work—neither you, nor your son or daughter, nor your manservant or maidservant or livestock, nor the foreigner within your gates.<small><sub>**MT:**</sub></small>
